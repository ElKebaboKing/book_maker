Chapter 1700: People from Wuliang Sea!

“I never expected that I would actually find another weak Eternal Sea!”
Qin Yu was greatly excited in his heart.
Wuliang Sea was an Eternal Sea, and Yuan Sea was also an Eternal Sea.
And the Eternal Seas he knew of were not just one, but each and every one of them was extremely powerful. This was the first time he had ever seen an Eternal Sea like Yuan Sea, whose strongest beings were only a few legendary realm lords.
Such an Eternal Sea was bound to be very young. Although the resources born from it might not be many, once such an Eternal Sea was controlled and one’s own power developed, then in the future even if some precious resources were born, such as Eternal Supreme Treasures, they could all fall into his hands.
This was a huge opportunity!
What’s more, there was still a possibility of obtaining two Eternal Supreme Treasures.
The Qin family, which was powerful in Wuliang Sea, did not have many Eternal Supreme Treasures either. And as a direct descendant young master of the Qin family, he had only been granted one Eternal Supreme Treasure. If he could obtain a few more Eternal Supreme Treasures, his strength would increase greatly.
“This time my luck is not bad. Luckily I seized Dark Mist Lord in time. Otherwise, this opportunity would not have fallen into my hands, and those perfected realm or even domain realm legendary lords would probably all want to come here.” Qin Yu thought to himself.
From Dark Mist Lord’s mouth, he learned that the several legendary realm lords in Yuan Sea’s time-space territory were relatively weak, and almost all of them were ordinary legendary lords; at most, there was only one initial realm legendary lord.
And this time he had brought eighteen ordinary legendary lords and three initial realm lords, plus himself, a top initial realm legendary lord capable of rivaling a perfected realm legendary lord. That was enough to occupy Yuan Sea and make the many Eternal realm beings submit.
Once Yuan Sea was fully occupied, he would then establish a time-space staircase to connect it with Wuliang Sea. Then traveling between Yuan Sea and Wuliang Sea would be very easy, and he could even summon experts from the Qin family at any time to garrison this place and ensure that no other force would seize it away.
Another stretch of time passed.
On this day.
Dark Mist Lord brought Qin Yu and these legendary lords to an area outside the time-space territory, where one could sense one time-space territory after another.
“It really is another Eternal Sea, but its structure is somewhat different from Wuliang Sea. The resources of Yuan Sea are mainly concentrated in the time-space territories, using Yuan Sea’s origin energy to nourish the time-space territories, whereas our Wuliang Sea birthed countless Wuliang Islands on its own, and the Wuliang Islands float on Wuliang Sea.”
Qin Yu murmured.
Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh!!!!
These legendary realm lords instantly crossed this stretch of void and descended into the time-space territory.
At this moment, Qin Yu ordered, “Dark Mist Lord, take me to the headquarters of your lord alliance.”
Dark Mist Lord said in a low voice, “Young Master Qin Yu, shouldn’t we go kill Ye Tian? This person has five clones. If we let him escape, that would be bad.”
“You want to disobey my order? Besides, a mere Immortal realm lord is not worth mentioning. If I handle my own matters well, I will casually help you eliminate him. If you say one more word of nonsense, you know the consequences.” Qin Yu said impatiently.
Dark Mist Lord’s expression changed drastically, and he immediately did not dare speak anymore.
Although Qin Yu had violated his promise, his strength was too weak, and he simply could not resist Qin Yu. Even if Qin Yu really did not help him, he could do nothing about it.
In fact, Qin Yu did not care at all about the so-called promise, after all Dark Mist Lord had not much value to him now.
However, Ye Tian definitely had to be killed. After all, there was a very high possibility that Ye Tian possessed an Eternal Supreme Treasure capable of enhancing one’s strength; otherwise, there was no way he could defeat Dark Mist Lord, a legendary realm lord, while still at the Immortal realm level.
But first, he had to control the lord alliance before dealing with Ye Tian.
Also.
As for the Eternal Supreme Treasure born in Yuan Sea, whoever it ended up in could be found out as long as he controlled the lord alliance here.
Right now, controlling the lord alliance was the most urgent matter.
Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh!!!
Qin Yu led the many legendary realm lords and shuttled to the headquarters of the lord alliance.
Boom!!!!
Wave after wave of legendary realm lord aura spread out, instantly making the many Eternal realm beings in the lord alliance tremble endlessly.
At this time, Holy Peak Lord, Ice Seal Lord, Great Wilderness Lord, Extreme Light Lord, and Six Suns Lord all appeared.
“So many legendary lords!!!”
Holy Peak Lord’s expression changed drastically, looking utterly incredulous.
When had the time-space territory ever had so many legendary lords? And they were all unfamiliar faces, definitely not from the time-space territory.
Moreover, Holy Peak Lord also spotted a familiar face among these legendary lords—Dark Mist Lord.
This almost made it possible to guess that these legendary lords were brought here by Dark Mist Lord, and might have come from a place like Yuan Sea, such as Wuliang Sea.
After all, there were several relics in the time-space territory that came from Wuliang Sea, so these people were very likely from Wuliang Sea.
“You are the five legendary lords of the Yuan Sea time-space territory lord alliance, right? From today onward, your time-space territory lord alliance will serve me. I come from the Qin family of Wuliang Sea. My name is Qin Yu, also known as True Yu Lord, but I prefer people to call me Young Master Qin Yu.”
Qin Yu said.
“Serve you?”
The five great legendary lords immediately showed a trace of anger on their faces, but because of Qin Yu and the others’ strength, they did not act rashly.
At this time, Ice Seal Lord secretly informed Ye Tian.
The time-space territory side was not without the power to fight back, after all these legendary lords who came from Wuliang Sea had not brought all of their true bodies and clones; they had certainly only brought one body each.
And on the time-space territory side, with the five great lords plus Ye Tian’s copy body, they were not inferior to Qin Yu’s side in terms of numbers.
As long as Ye Tian could defeat Qin Yu, then they would have the right to speak.
In short, serving Qin Yu was impossible.
As legendary realm lords, Ice Seal Lord and the others did not want to serve anyone else.
-
Blood Bone Territory.
Ice Seal Lord’s clone descended here, and Ye Tian sensed it immediately.
“Ice Seal Lord, you seem a little flustered. Did something happen?” Ye Tian asked.
Ice Seal Lord hurriedly said, “Something huge has happened. A group of legendary lords has come from Wuliang Sea, and Dark Mist Lord is among them. We suspect that Dark Mist Lord found Wuliang Sea. We searched for endless ages and never found Wuliang Sea, but unexpectedly Dark Mist Lord did. Now these legendary lords from Wuliang Sea should have been brought here by Dark Mist Lord. The leader is a legendary lord named Qin Yu, titled True Yu Lord. His strength is probably very formidable, and we are no match for him.”
“Delay them for a while. I’ll be there immediately!”
Ye Tian said.
This matter was related to him, so he could not ignore it.
“This time, it seems I’ll need to unleash my true strength!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
