After returning, he began cultivating the Golden Thread Sacred Technique while also consuming Sacred Body Fruits to temper his Eternal Indestructible Sacred Body.
The effect of the Sacred Body Fruits was indeed excellent. If he relied solely on self-cultivation, reaching major completion would require an unimaginable amount of time.
But with the fruits, Ye Tian estimated that it would take only about one hundred thousand years to reach minor completion, and roughly three hundred thousand years to reach major completion.
Of course, this required continuously consuming Sacred Body Fruits, which would consume vast quantities.
Without the Battle Sect’s support, such progress would have been impossible.
In the blink of an eye, one hundred years passed.
On this day,
Ye Tian finally mastered the Golden Thread Sacred Technique.
Of course, this was achieved within a time formation, so the actual time spent was far greater than a hundred years.
For others, such time would be insignificant, yet Ye Tian had already mastered a Sacred Technique.
In the Dark Forbidden Domain,
Ye Tian tested the Golden Thread Sacred Technique. With a casual motion, a golden thread streaked across the void.
Rip!!!!
The forbidden void was sliced open as if it were cloth.
“Terrifying power… to tear apart the forbidden void so easily. This should be enough to easily kill a sixth-layer Unity Realm half-step Domain Ancestor!”
Ye Tian muttered.
And this was only the entry level. Once the Sacred Technique reached minor mastery, its power would become incomparably terrifying, elevating his strength to an even greater level.
After that, Ye Tian returned to seclusion and continued cultivating the Sacred Technique.
Thus, ten thousand years passed.
After ten thousand years of cultivation, Ye Tian finally advanced the Golden Thread Sacred Technique to minor mastery.
For Supreme Domain Ancestors, this would be unbelievable—but for Ye Tian, it seemed almost effortless.
This was the power of Sacred Talent!
However, even at minor mastery, Ye Tian could not use the technique frequently. A single use consumed about ten percent of his total power.
Thus, in battle, he could only use it occasionally. If used repeatedly, he would exhaust himself before killing the enemy.
Through testing, Ye Tian determined that a minor-mastery Golden Thread Sacred Technique was comparable to a peak full-power strike of an eighth-layer Unity Realm half-step Domain Ancestor.
If, in the future, his Eternal Indestructible Sacred Body reached minor completion, combined with this Sacred Technique, his combat power would become even more terrifying.
And he had not even used Extreme Dao Power yet. If he did, he might already be able to fight the most powerful eighth-layer Unity Realm half-step Domain Ancestors.
After reaching minor mastery, the speed of improvement slowed significantly. Advancing to major completion would require a very long time.
Thus, Ye Tian shifted his focus back to his Eternal Indestructible Sacred Body.
After more than a hundred thousand years, he finally cultivated it to the minor-completion stage.
At this point, his overall combat power was comparable to a ninth-layer Unity Realm half-step Domain Ancestor.
And what did that mean?
In the Dark Sacred Domain, even some newly advanced Domain Ancestors only possessed such combat power.
In contrast, in ordinary realms, newly advanced Domain Ancestors might only have peak eighth-layer combat power—some even inferior to Ye Tian’s current strength.
In other words, Ye Tian could now contend with—and even defeat—newly advanced Domain Ancestors of the Daoshan Realm and the Shentian Realm.
