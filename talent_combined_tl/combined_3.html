But under the power of a sacred technique, Time Domain Ancestor’s time domain technique was far too weak and could not affect the temporal flow of the sacred technique at all. His time domain technique was instantly broken... Before Time Domain Ancestor could escape, he too was cut in two by the Golden Silk sacred technique and perished on the spot.
Myriad Worlds Domain Ancestor should also have been unable to block the Golden Silk sacred technique, but he had once fortuitously obtained a life-saving treasure.
At the moment his body collapsed and his soul was annihilated, that life-saving treasure protected his soul and reconstructed his body.
Myriad Worlds Domain Ancestor’s life was preserved. He survived.
But that life-saving treasure was a one-time item. He had managed to survive now, but if Ye Tian attacked again, he would still certainly die.
One strike had slain Golden Cauldron Domain Ancestor, Time Domain Ancestor, and Destruction Domain Ancestor. These were three of the absolute strongest top-tier Domain Ancestors. If Myriad Worlds Domain Ancestor had not possessed that life-saving treasure, then even this existence known as the strongest expert of Divine Heaven Realm would have been instantly killed by that one strike as well.
“Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor, spare my life!”
Myriad Worlds Domain Ancestor immediately dropped to his knees and begged for mercy.
Ye Tian’s strength far exceeded his imagination. Even if he himself broke through to the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm, he likely still would not be Ye Tian’s opponent.
Ye Tian had only just reached the ordinary Domain Ancestor realm, so why was his strength so terrifying?
This was simply not normal!
The other top-tier Domain Ancestors were also dumbfounded. Golden Cauldron Domain Ancestor, Destruction Domain Ancestor, and Time Domain Ancestor were all incredibly powerful. To kill an ordinary top-tier Domain Ancestor, they probably only needed a single move. They stood far above normal top-tier Domain Ancestors, practically invincible existences within Divine Heaven Realm.
Yet such invincible beings had been casually slaughtered by Ye Tian as if he were chopping vegetables.
At once, one top-tier Domain Ancestor after another lowered their proud heads, not daring to meet Ye Tian’s gaze.
“Get up!”
Ye Tian did not kill Myriad Worlds Domain Ancestor. He was not some bloodthirsty maniac.
The fact that Myriad Worlds Domain Ancestor had managed to preserve his life under that strike proved that he had the qualification to keep living.
“From this day onward, the Mysterious Void Forbidden Realm shall remain exactly as it is.
As for the territories of Dao Mountain Realm, aside from the territories of the human race and Black Wind Mountain, I will not interfere. You may occupy the rest as you wish, but excessive slaughter is forbidden.
In short, if any of you dare set your sights on the Mysterious Void Forbidden Realm, then the fate of these three top-tier Domain Ancestors today will be your fate as well!”
Ye Tian declared.
“Yes!”
The many top-tier Domain Ancestors all responded in unison. Not one of them dared disagree.
