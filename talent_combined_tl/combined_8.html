Chapter 1550: Fourth-tier Invincible!

After consuming the Xumi Golden Fruit, Ye Tian clearly felt his physical body strengthening at an incredible speed, and his soul became increasingly condensed, as if a Soul Tribulation Realm Supreme had been promoted to an Invincible.
Moreover, countless flashes of inspiration appeared, and his comprehension was also constantly increasing.
Ye Tian felt that any treasures he had taken in the past were trash compared to the Xumi Golden Fruit.
Time passed slowly, and Ye Tian continued to improve as if there were no limit.
After an unknown amount of time, Ye Tian found that the energy of the Xumi Golden Fruit was exhausted, and the changes in his body had come to an end.
At this time, he tested the changes in his body.
His physical body had strengthened tenfold, his soul had strengthened thirtyfold, and his comprehension had strengthened eightfold.
This was an enhancement of his foundation rather than an increase in cultivation, which was terrifying. It meant that Ye Tian's combat power at the same rank would be forcibly raised by several levels in the future.
Especially the soul, which was very difficult to improve. To promote to a Second-tier Invincible, an Invincible who promoted from the Physical Body Tribulation Realm must also raise their soul to the corresponding Tribulation Realm, and Ye Tian felt that his soul was almost at that level.
In other words, there would be no difficulty for him to promote to a Second-tier Invincible in the future.
Just for this point alone, the Xumi Golden Fruit being priced at one billion contribution points was not expensive at all; it was even very cheap. However, even with the contribution points, one might not necessarily be able to purchase it because the Xumi Golden Fruit was too rare. Even the Tianfeng Sea might not have it and might require a queue, and as for how long one would have to wait, that was hard to say.
"It's a pity the next Xumi Golden Fruit requires a wait of thirty thousand Tribulation Eras. Perhaps by then, I will have already reached the First-tier Invincible realm, and the Xumi Golden Fruit will be of no use to me. However, with such a Xumi Golden Mountain, more and more geniuses will be born in my Human Race in the future. It must be well protected!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Furthermore, he always felt that the Xumi Golden Mountain should have more functions than just nurturing the Xumi Golden Fruit. It was just that his current knowledge was too limited, and regarding treasures at the level of Eternal Source Objects, very few secrets would naturally be leaked.
Once his strength became greater and his status in the Tianfeng Sea higher, he might be able to learn more secrets.
With his comprehension enhanced, the speed at which Ye Tian perfected the Myriad Source Return to Void increased.
In the blink of an eye, another Great Tribulation Epoch passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had already perfected the second level of the Myriad Source Return to Void to the Consummate stage, and his main body had also used a large number of top-grade Tribulation Stones to cultivate to the second level's Consummate stage. His strength was much greater than before.
With the second level of Myriad Source Return to Void at Consummate and his cultivation reaching the limit of a Fifth-tier Invincible, Ye Tian could only continue to progress by creating Invincible Ancestral Techniques.
And now, Ye Tian already had several Invincible Ancestral Techniques on the verge of being created; he had just been holding back from creating them.
"Now I can create these Invincible Ancestral Techniques one by one!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Before long, the second Invincible Ancestral Technique was created.
This Invincible Ancestral Technique was a defensive one, combining multiple defensive Supreme Ancestral Techniques. It was a top-tier defensive Invincible Ancestral Technique named the Source Astral Shield.
After creating this Invincible Ancestral Technique and fusing with it, Ye Tian's physical body underwent a qualitative change.
Next, Ye Tian created a speed-type Invincible Ancestral Technique—Extreme Shadow!
Following the Extreme Shadow technique, Ye Tian's physical body underwent another qualitative change. Through the combination of the three Invincible Ancestral Techniques with his physical body, along with his cultivation reaching the limit of a Fifth-tier Invincible and his Invincible Tribulation Body being incomparably powerful, he completed the transition from a Fifth-tier Invincible to a Fourth-tier Invincible.
"Finally promoted to a Fourth-tier Invincible!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
A Fourth-tier Invincible is not only much stronger than a Fifth-tier Invincible but can also perfectly exert the power of Invincible Ancestral Techniques, whereas a Fifth-tier Invincible can only exert half of the power; this is the difference between the two.
Moreover, a Fourth-tier Invincible can vaguely sense the rules of the Eternal Heaven and Earth, while a Fifth-tier Invincible cannot.
After becoming a Fourth-tier Invincible, Ye Tian could continue to increase his cultivation and continue to practice the third level of the Myriad Source Return to Void.
The path from a Fourth-tier Invincible to a Third-tier Invincible is a process of accumulation. Strictly speaking, a Third-tier Invincible is the pinnacle of an Invincible, while Second-tier and First-tier Invincibles are two additional realms that represent levels far exceeding the normal limits of an Invincible.
To become a Third-tier Invincible, one must reach the Sixth Tribulation Realm during the Supreme Tribulation Crystal stage and must cultivate the Invincible Tribulation Body to the Consummate stage that an ordinary person can reach. Of course, this restriction does not exist for an Invincible who has practiced the Invincible Tribulation Body method.
Furthermore, one needs to master even more Invincible Techniques. A mere three Invincible Techniques are certainly not enough; at the very least, more than ten Invincible Techniques involving multiple rules are required.
Becoming a Third-tier Invincible grants a special ability, and this ability can be used to identify whether one has reached the Third-tier Invincible level.
This ability is the capacity to absorb energy from the Source Sea!
No matter how strong Ye Tian's strength was, even if he was stronger than an ordinary Third-tier Invincible, he still could not be considered a Third-tier Invincible.
After becoming a Fourth-tier Invincible, Ye Tian had his duplicate bodies begin to hunt those powerful Tribulation Beasts. Even Thousand-Tribulation Beasts were no match for him, and he could easily kill them.
A single Thousand-Tribulation Beast could earn at least one million contribution points, and there were many Thousand-Tribulation Beasts.
Thus, in a short time, Ye Tian hunted many Thousand-Tribulation Beasts and unknowingly accumulated over one hundred million contribution points.
Due to the multiple kills, the duplicate body had not fallen once, and thus it had accumulated a significant amount of Tribulation Beast resentment aura.
One day.
A terrifying Tribulation Beast aura crossed many foreign heavens and earths.
Ye Tian immediately realized that this was definitely a Hundred-Thousand-Tribulation Beast, so he instantly self-annihilated this duplicate body.
"I was careless. I shouldn't have kept using the same duplicate body to hunt Tribulation Beasts without replacing it. I must be careful next time. After hunting some Tribulation Beasts, I should disperse the duplicate body and copy a new one; otherwise, it's easy to attract the attention of a Hundred-Thousand-Tribulation Beast!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
-----------------------
Tianlin Realm, Wufeng Mountain.
Ye Tian arrived at the Tianfeng Sea headquarters, preparing to apply to become an Elite Member.
To become an Elite Member of the Tianfeng Sea, two conditions must be met: first, accumulated contribution points must reach one hundred million, a goal Ye Tian had already achieved; second, cultivation must reach the Fourth-tier Invincible realm.
Of course, there are Fifth-tier Invincibles who have become Elite Members, but all of them have either huge backgrounds or have made astonishing contributions. For example, the Tianfeng Sea occasionally issues certain tasks; once these tasks are completed, one can become an Elite Member even if their cultivation is insufficient.
However, such tasks are very rare.
