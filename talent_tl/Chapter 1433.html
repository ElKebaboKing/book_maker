Chapter 1432: Breaking Through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor Realm
!
Tens of millions of years passed.
Ye Tian ventured into one forbidden zone after another within the Holy Soul Realm. Relying on his combat power as a four-step Invincible Domain Ancestor, he obtained great benefits from various forbidden areas.
Furthermore, he frequently used his duplicate bodies to perform evolutions, evolving various Ancestral Techniques.
After all, his Ancestral Techniques were only at the Initial stage. If he could raise them to the Small Success stage, his strength would receive a terrifying boost.
Under normal circumstances, raising an Ancestral Technique to the Small Success stage would require a long period of bitter cultivation.
However, if he used evolution to improve them, it would be countless times faster.
Through repeated evolutions, the Holy Soul of Ye Tian's duplicate bodies collapsed time and again. Fortunately, the final results were indeed quite good.
Ancestral Techniques such as the Gold Silk Ancestral Technique, the Void Slash Ancestral Technique, and the Space Escape Ancestral Technique were all raised to the Small Success stage by Ye Tian. However, if he wanted to raise them to the Great Success stage, it would still take a long time even with the evolution trait.
The Holy Soul Realm was nearing its closing day. At that time, even if Ye Tian did not want to leave, once the Original Ancestral World sealed itself off, everyone would be forcibly kicked out. This was the rule of this world, and even an Invincible Domain Ancestor could not violate it.
At this moment, Ye Tian was organizing his gains.
He had no interest in organizing ordinary treasures; he only sorted through the more precious ones.
Star Soul Sand, Holy Gold Sand, and Space Holy Talent Crystals were the treasures he had obtained at the very beginning.
From the later forbidden zones, he had obtained many good things.
For example, the Ancestral Blood Grass, which could allow one's Holy Body to easily reach the limit realm; the Void Soul Stone, a supreme treasure that could instantly increase one's comprehension by a thousandfold; and the Holy Blood Marrow, which could reshape one's foundation.
Especially the Holy Blood Marrow—this item was of little use to Ye Tian, but for his family members, it was a supreme treasure.
After all, his family members initially lacked resources and inheritances, so their foundations were not very good. Although they were considered geniuses compared to ordinary Domain Ancestors, their talent and strength were only average compared to the true Holy Sons of the Dark Holy Domain.
But the Holy Blood Marrow could reshape one's foundation, allowing one to become a monstrous genius in a single step.
Previously, the Holy Blood Marrow was always out of stock on the Battle Gate's treasury exchange list; the Battle Gate simply didn't have any.
Even many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were searching for Holy Blood Marrow, which showed its preciousness.
However, it could not be found.
This time, Ye Tian found ten pieces of Holy Blood Marrow in a forbidden zone. To obtain these ten pieces of Holy Blood Marrow, he fought for a long time with a terrifying existence in that forbidden zone, one that was at least as strong as a four-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. He used his duplicate body to forcibly cast the Five Elements Seal to trap the opponent, while his main body went to steal the Holy Blood Marrow. Only then did he manage to snatch them away.
Of course, he did not kill the opponent, nor could he.
Besides these treasures, there were many other treasures in great quantities. Once brought back to the human race, they would definitely cause the human race's strength to soar.
The only pity was that there were too few Time Holy Talents in the Holy Soul Realm, so he did not manage to fuse a Time Ancestral Talent. This was the only regrettable point.
Another few tens of thousands of years passed.
A repulsive force arrived.
Swish swish swish!!!! One by one, the Peak Domain Ancestors were sent out.
When Ye Tian regained his senses, he found that he had already appeared outside the Original Ancestral World. Then, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Battle Gate brought Ye Tian back to prevent experts from other factions from attacking him.
"Let's go, back to the Battle Ancestor World!"
The Fire Pattern Battle Ancestor of the Battle Gate spoke.
In the Battle Ancestor World, Ye Tian and a group of Peak Domain Ancestors had returned.
This time, one-tenth of the Battle Gate's Peak Domain Ancestors had fallen forever in the Holy Soul Realm, while some Holy Lands had lost all of their Peak Domain Ancestors, suffering even greater losses.
Of course, as long as one person returned, that faction would not lose out.
On this day.
The Fire Pattern Battle Ancestor gathered Ye Tian and the others, mainly to announce one thing: they hoped Ye Tian and the others would trade a portion of their treasures and resources to the Battle Gate, as many resources from the Holy Soul Realm were lacking within the Battle Gate.
Of course, this was not a free trade; they could obtain even more precious resources from the Battle Gate in return.
Ye Tian traded some Star Soul Sand and a small amount of Holy Gold Sand to the Battle Gate, and also traded many other resources one by one.
Subsequently, Ye Tian exchanged for an incredibly precious treasure from the Battle Gate—the Starry Holy Stele! The sole function of the Starry Holy Stele was to open a Starry Barrier. Once opened, even a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor would be unable to break through this barrier's defense in a short amount of time.
Ye Tian was also worried that a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor might take action against him or the Profound Void Holy Realm, which was why he exchanged for the Starry Holy Stele.
This way, if a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor attacked, he could promptly activate the Starry Holy Stele to block the enemy's attack and then request the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Battle Gate to intervene.
However, the Starry Holy Stele could only be activated three times in total. After three times, it would turn into an ordinary holy stele.
But even so, the value of the Starry Holy Stele was extremely high, and Ye Tian only barely managed to exchange for this one treasure.
Using so many rare treasures to exchange for a single Starry Holy Stele, Ye Tian felt it was very much worth it.
Afterward, Ye Tian left the Battle Ancestor World and returned to the Profound Void Holy Realm.
Now wealthy and influential, Ye Tian very extravagantly used a Ten Thousand Void Star Leaf and soon arrived at the Dao Mountain Domain, returning to the Profound Void Holy Realm.
In the Profound Void Holy Realm.
Ye Tian manifested the Starry Holy Stele and linked it with the origin of the Profound Void Holy Realm, ensuring that the Starry Holy Stele could be activated at any time.
"With the Starry Holy Stele, I don't need to worry about safety issues. Next, it's time to charge into the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He had already reached the limit of the Peak Domain Ancestor realm, and even his Holy Soul had completed its second qualitative change. Therefore, there was no need to continue accumulating in the Peak Domain Ancestor realm.
Choosing an auspicious day, Ye Tian entered the Holy Stele World to charge into the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
For Ye Tian, breaking through from Peak Domain Ancestor to Invincible Domain Ancestor was very simple. He had already condensed a Holy Soul, and it was a Holy Soul that had undergone a second qualitative change, making the breakthrough to Invincible Domain Ancestor extremely easy.
Boom!!!!! The shackles were broken, and his Peak Domain Body began to transform toward an Invincible Domain Body.
Countless amounts of Chaos Origin Energy surged into Ye Tian's body, assisting him in completing the transformation of his Domain Body.
At the same time, Ye Tian's Holy Soul also underwent a change, becoming stronger and stronger.
Furthermore, a brand-new Holy Talent was born deep within Ye Tian's Holy Soul. This was his Natal Holy Talent, and it was also the Holy Talent that suited Ye Tian best.
After a long time, the breakthrough was complete.
And the first thing Ye Tian did was not to check his strength, but to see what that Natal Holy Talent was.
