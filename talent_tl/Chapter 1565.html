Chapter 1564: Becoming a Son of the Void!

"It's time to become a Son of the Void!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he used his duplicated body to head to the Tianfeng Sea in the Tianlin Realm to meet the Tianfeng Source God.
When he met the Tianfeng Source God, the Tianfeng Source God gave Ye Tian a token.
"Ye Tian, this token is the treasure for traveling to the Void Source Palace. Otherwise, with your speed, it would likely take a very long time to fly to the Void Source Palace, but using this token will allow you to arrive at the Void Source Palace instantly," the Tianfeng Source God introduced.
Ye Tian took the token and said gratefully, "Thank you, Chief."
Subsequently, he refined the token and activated it.
Boom!!!!!
An unimaginable spatial power enveloped Ye Tian, instantly piercing through the distance of the Endless Source Sea, bringing him to a magnificent divine palace that defied description.
This was the Void Source Palace.
When he arrived at the Void Source Palace, a grand voice entered his soul.
"Examinee, do you wish to undergo the Son of the Void trial?"
Ye Tian replied, "Yes!"
Then, the grand voice continued, "Entering the Void Tower shortly!"
"The examinee needs to pass the first floor of the Void Tower to obtain the title of Son of the Void. Passing the second floor grants the title of Divine Son of the Void, and passing the third floor grants the title of Lord of the Void."
What?
There were also titles like Divine Son of the Void and Lord of the Void?
Why hadn't he heard of them, and why hadn't the Tianfeng Source God mentioned them? Could it be they didn't know?
No, it was impossible for them not to know; perhaps no one had ever achieved them.
Precisely because no one had achieved them, the Tianfeng Source God had not mentioned them.
Boom!!!
Ye Tian was sent into the first floor of the Void Tower by the Void Source Palace and soon encountered his opponents.
Densely packed Tribulation Beasts appeared, all of them comparable to the realm of an ordinary third-tier Invincible. Such Tribulation Beasts were very weak; even an average third-tier Invincible from an Eternal Name-level power could easily kill them, let alone Ye Tian.
However, their numbers were too great, numbering in the tens of thousands. How could those third-tier Invincibles contend with so many Tribulation Beasts?
"No wonder it's so difficult to become a Son of the Void; few third-tier Invincibles can manage it!"
Ye Tian sighed.
Kill!!!
Ye Tian heavily injured or even killed the Tribulation Beasts one by one, but more of them swarmed forward.
Therefore, he had to constantly deploy Invincible defense techniques and utilize his speed advantage, pushing his speed-type Invincible Ancestral Techniques to the Extreme.
It could be said that if a third-tier Invincible had any weaknesses, even if their strength was great, they would likely find it difficult to pass such a trial.
Ye Tian's strength was much greater than Luo Ge's; even Luo Ge had passed the first floor of the Void Tower to become a Son of the Void.
Thus, Ye Tian could naturally pass the first floor of the Void Tower, and it would be quite easy.
As time passed, all the Tribulation Beasts on the first floor were killed.
But Ye Tian discovered that the killed Tribulation Beasts did not produce Tribulation Blood, Tribulation Bones, or Source Rule Tribulation Crystals; instead, they turned into a surge of energy that was absorbed by the Void Source Palace.
Clearly, the Void Source Palace possessed the method to create Tribulation Beasts and could recycle them to continue creating more.
Otherwise, if Invincibles came one after another for the trial, where would the Void Source Palace get so many Tribulation Beasts for them to kill?
"Examinee, you have passed the first floor of the Void Tower and obtained the title of Son of the Void. Do you wish to proceed to the second floor trial?" the grand voice descended.
"Yes!"
Ye Tian replied.
He also wanted to know just how difficult the second floor trial of the Void Tower was, and why no Invincible had ever passed it to become a Divine Son of the Void.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian was transported to the second floor of the Void Tower, and he soon saw his opponents.
The opponents on the second floor were still Tribulation Beasts, but they were clearly not ordinary ones. Judging by their aura, they were still third-tier Invincibles, yet they were far more powerful than the Tribulation Beasts on the first floor.
Furthermore, the number of Tribulation Beasts on the second floor was immense, far exceeding those on the first floor.
The slaughter began!
Ye Tian found it difficult to kill a Tribulation Beast in a single blow; it took at least several moves to kill one. With so many Tribulation Beasts besieging him, he couldn't focus his full strength on just one.
Gradually, he realized he could only hold out; there was simply no way to kill so many Invincible Tribulation Beasts.
Soon, Ye Tian gave up.
This level was simply impossible to pass, leaving one in utter despair...
At this moment, he also understood why other Invincibles did not mention the Divine Son of the Void; it was because none could be born.
Emerging from the Void Tower, Ye Tian arrived at the main hall of the Void Source Palace.
At this time, the Void Source Palace transmitted some information to him, giving him a deeper understanding of the palace and the benefits of being a Son of the Void.
Using the Void Source Palace for travel was just one of the benefits; additionally, he could use the Void Tower to train his combat power, entering it repeatedly for a small fee, which was paid in Source Rule Tribulation Crystals.
"No wonder Luo Ge's combat experience is so rich; it seems he frequently enters the Void Tower to cultivate," Ye Tian speculated.
Furthermore.
Only a Son of the Void could contemplate the Void Source Stele. This was the greatest opportunity within the Void Source Palace, an opportunity that even Supreme Beings coveted immensely.
But if one was not a Son of the Void, even a Supreme Being could not enter the Void Source Palace to contemplate the Void Source Stele.
The time a Son of the Void could spend contemplating the Void Source Stele was very limited and required queuing; only a Divine Son of the Void did not need to queue.
Every ten Sons of the Void could contemplate the Void Source Stele for about one Great Tribulation Era. Of course, a Son of the Void also had to pay a price, which was Void Points.
Void Points could not be exchanged for resources; they could only be obtained through a few specific channels.
First, by becoming a Son of the Void, the Void Source Palace rewarded 1,000 points. Second, by becoming a Supreme Being, one could obtain 10,000 points. Third, points could be gifted by other Sons of the Void. Fourth, by completing missions from the Void Source Palace.
Basically, if one wanted to obtain a large number of points, they had to rely on the Void Source Palace's missions.
"Artifact Spirit, list the missions of the Void Source Palace!"
Ye Tian requested.
The grand voice, which was the Artifact Spirit, listed several missions.
[Mission: Kill the Source Sea lifeform, the Devouring Source Dragon. One Devouring Source Dragon is worth 100,000 points.]
[Mission: Find Void Source Crystals. One Void Source Crystal is worth 100 points.]
[Mission: Condense the Void Source Body. Reward: 30,000 points.]
...
Ye Tian looked at the missions one by one, feeling very tempted, but these missions were all too difficult; not a single one was easy.
