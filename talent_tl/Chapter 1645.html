Chapter 1644 A Chance That Comes Once Every One Hundred Thousand Eternal Years Has Been Found!

On the Sea of Silence, one corpse after another floated on the surface.
There were corpses of Supreme Beings, corpses of half-step Eternal Realm experts, and even some corpses of Invincible Beings.
“Strange, where did so many corpses come from?”
Ye Tian was puzzled.
It was not as if he had never been to the Sea of Silence before. He had been here earlier, but the Sea of Silence had contained nothing at all.
In the past, there had indeed been many Supreme Beings and even half-step Eternal Realm experts who had fallen in the Sea of Silence, but after these experts fell, they quickly disappeared.
This was also one of the Sea of Silence’s secrets. As for the reason, no one knew.
But now, the corpses of these experts had surfaced.
At once, Ye Tian sensed the Sea of Silence’s eeriness; it seemed that some special change had taken place here.
He did not think too much about it, and immediately collected the Void Balls of these experts.
The resources accumulated by these experts added up to a considerable amount. Although there might not be many resources useful to him, leaving so many resources to the human race was still not bad.
Before long, Ye Tian had collected all the Void Balls of these experts.
As for the corpses of these experts, he sent them away to be buried in a blessed land.
……
“I’m afraid something has happened to the Sea of Silence. The Void Source Hall can’t stay here anymore!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Moreover, for him, the Sea of Silence was not a forbidden land anymore; this was also true for those powerful extreme experts.
Therefore, there was no need for the Void Source Hall to remain in the Sea of Silence; it could be directly taken back to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Whoosh!
The enormous Void Source Hall entered Ye Tian’s body, and Ye Tian flew out of the Sea of Silence, quickly returning to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
After placing the Void Source Hall in the Dark Forbidden Domain, Ye Tian’s clone body once again came to the Sea of Silence, watching the changes in the Sea of Silence at all times.
As time passed, the void in the Sea of Silence gradually fluctuated, and the fluctuations came from a deeper void.
And those experts who had fallen in the Sea of Silence back then had been swallowed up by the unknown void; now that the void was fluctuating, those corpses appeared again.
However, before the changes in the Sea of Silence were completely over, Ye Tian found it difficult to do anything.
He tried to follow the void fluctuations into that unknown void, but an unknown force blocked him, preventing him from entering at all.
He had already reached the level of a Five Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm expert, and yet this force could easily block him. It was clear that if there really was a chance here, it would be extremely extraordinary.
“Could the once-in-one-hundred-thousand-eternal-years chance that those Eternal Realm lords want to find be right here?”
Ye Tian guessed.
If it really were here, then the Sea of Silence would probably become a battlefield!
Fortunately, the Dark Forbidden Domain was relatively far from the Sea of Silence. If they were close, even a little bit of battle ripple from an Eternal Realm expert would probably cause an eternal world to collapse.
In the blink of an eye, another kalpa era passed.
The void fluctuations in the Sea of Silence became increasingly intense, even extending beyond the Sea of Silence, but because there were no living beings nearby, the news had not yet spread.
But Ye Tian was very clear that this place would eventually be discovered.
This day.
The changes in the Sea of Silence reached a certain limit, and endless golden light enveloped the entire Sea of Silence, even covering the territories around the Sea of Silence.
Ye Tian saw a golden tree within the golden light. This golden tree did not seem very large, but its radiance actually suppressed time and space, suppressed everything, as if it were the source of all things, and as if it were the essence of everything.
And around the golden tree was an endless land, but because the distance was too far and the scenery around it did not possess an aura as powerful as the golden tree’s, it could not be projected over, and Ye Tian could not see it clearly.
“This is absolutely a chance that only arises once every one hundred thousand eternal years!”
Ye Tian did not say another word and directly charged toward the depths of that unknown void.
Perhaps Eternal Realm experts would arrive very soon, but if he did not fight for such a chance, he would regret it for the rest of his life.
This time, the unknown force did not hinder Ye Tian.
Ye Tian followed a golden road deeper into that unknown void, and soon arrived at that void location.
And just at that moment, a strange force struck, several times stronger than the eerie force of the Sea of Silence.
Ye Tian ignored this force, stepped off the golden road, and landed on a stretch of land. .........
Faintly, he saw the golden tree in the distant distance, but it was still relatively far from here.
Moreover, the eerie force here was constantly affecting him.
Added to that, the suppression here was terrifying, so it would probably take Ye Tian some time to reach the golden tree.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian moved quickly, wanting to reach the golden tree sooner.
But very soon, his expression changed.
“How can this be?”
The more distance he moved, the stronger the impact of the strange force became.
“I’m afraid I won’t be able to reach the golden tree!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
But after advancing for a stretch, Ye Tian was no longer worried.
Because he had discovered a treasure.
“This is the Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass!”
Ye Tian said in shock.
The Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass was an incomparably precious resource in the source sea, and this was true even for half-step Eternal Realm experts. It could increase the strength of the body, improve cultivation, and also enhance the Eternal Body.
If someone’s Eternal Body had not yet reached the Perfected realm, then just a small section of the Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass would be enough to raise their Eternal Body to the Perfected realm.
It could be said that there was nothing on Ye Tian’s person whose value could compare to the Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass, not even the Void True Liquid.
Even if an extreme half-step Eternal Realm expert saw this stalk of Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass, they would probably be extremely excited.
“Hahaha, it seems that aside from that golden tree, there are other resources here. Even if I can’t reach the location of that golden tree, obtaining these resources is already enough!”
Whoosh!
A clone condensed and immediately took this stalk of Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass back to the outside world.
He did not want to fall here and let the resource be left behind.
The clone’s speed was very fast. It quickly left this place, and after leaving the Sea of Silence, it rapidly traversed back to the Dark Forbidden Domain, then delivered the Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass to the main body’s hands.
After that, the clone continued toward the Sea of Silence.
Not long after, Ye Tian found some more resources. Although they were not as good as the Void Scale Golden Leaf Grass, they were not far off, and the clone took on the role of a porter.
Not long after.
The changes in the Sea of Silence also drew the attention of other half-step Eternal Realm experts.
