Chapter 1439: Secrets Known Only to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors
!
At this moment, the Qingyuan Holy World had already attracted the prying eyes of many powerful experts.
They were all watching the spectacle, and there were even many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors among them.
It was at this time that Jinchen Holy Ancestor fled.
"A Supreme-level Domain Ancestor is fleeing! Jinchen Holy Ancestor is simply a disgrace among Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, actually being forced to run by an Invincible Domain Ancestor!"
"I wonder if Jinchen Holy Ancestor can escape. If he can't, he might be the first Supreme-level Domain Ancestor in the Dark Holy Region to be slain by an Invincible Domain Ancestor. His name will certainly go down in the history of the Dark Holy Region!"
"I reckon Jinchen Holy Ancestor won't be able to escape. Myriad Law Domain Ancestor knows speed-type Ancestral Techniques; how can Jinchen Holy Ancestor get away?"
...."Trying to escape?"
Ye Tian sneered.
"Five Elements Seal!"
With a wave of his hand, Ye Tian directly locked down the void.
The current Five Elements Seal was no longer a Holy Technique, but an Ancestral Technique.
Under the Ancestral Technique Five Elements Seal, this patch of void was directly sealed off, as if isolated from the source of the Great Dao. No matter how terrifying Jinchen Holy Ancestor's life-saving abilities were, he could not break through the Five Elements Seal.
Rumble!!!! Jinchen Holy Ancestor frantically tore through time and space, attempting to escape, but it was useless; he could not break out.
"Void Slash!"
A terrifying spatial Ancestral Technique attacked, shattering Jinchen Holy Ancestor's layers of defense and pulverizing one side of his body.
Recovering again and again, and having his physical body pulverized again and again.
As time passed, Jinchen Holy Ancestor's injuries grew increasingly severe.
Half an hour later, Jinchen Holy Ancestor discovered that his Supreme Domain Body had been worn away by seventy percent. He was already in a state of being heavily injured and on the verge of death.
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, spare me!"
Jinchen Holy Ancestor panicked.
He did not want to perish. He was a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor with endless longevity. Even though a Domain World would eventually face a Great Destruction, as a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, he was nearly immortal.
Therefore, he naturally did not want to die! However, Ye Tian ignored Jinchen Holy Ancestor completely and continued his assault.
After a while longer, Jinchen Holy Ancestor's Supreme Domain Body completely collapsed.
And Jinchen Holy Ancestor's soul was also annihilated.
At this point, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor had fallen! Along with the fall of Jinchen Holy Ancestor, the vast territories of the Qingyuan Domain mourned together, and blood rain fell incessantly.
After slaying Jinchen Holy Ancestor, Ye Tian stepped into the Qingyuan Holy World and destroyed the headquarters of the Qingyuan Holy Land. One by one, the Domain Ancestors there were like ants, directly suppressed and killed by a single thought from Ye Tian.
Thus, the Qingyuan Holy Land was wiped off the map.
....Soon, the news of Ye Tian slaying a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor single-handedly and destroying the Qingyuan Holy Land spread throughout the entire Dark Holy Region.
Battle Gate.
Five Thunders Domain Ancestor, Guhai Domain Ancestor, and others were dumbfounded when they learned of this event.
"What? Myriad Law Domain Ancestor killed a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor?"
Guhai Domain Ancestor was dazed.
He felt that it wasn't that long ago—only a few billion years or so. To him, this amount of time was merely the duration of a single secluded meditation session.
The Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, Ye Tian, who was once very weak and required his help, had now surpassed him. Moreover, he had slain a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, placing him far above himself.
"An Invincible Domain Ancestor killing a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor... how is that possible?"
Five Thunders Domain Ancestor was also dazed.
He had condensed a Half-step Ancestral Body and comprehended Ancestral Techniques, making him incredibly monstrous—to some extent, even more so than some Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
But in terms of actual combat power, he could at most exchange a move or two with the weakest, newly promoted Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. In a real fight, he wouldn't be able to defeat one.
And Jinchen Holy Ancestor of the Qingyuan Holy Land was a mid-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. It was said he was injured, but by now, his injuries should have mostly recovered.
If Ye Tian could kill Jinchen Holy Ancestor, he could probably crush him with ease!
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor is truly terrifying!"
Five Thunders Domain Ancestor lamented.
Ye Tian did not know the evaluation other Invincible Domain Ancestors had of him. At this moment, he had returned to the Xuanxu Holy World in the Daoshan Domain.
After Ye Tian returned, he went to the Battle World Tower with doubts and met Fire-Pattern War Ancestor.
"Fire-Pattern War Ancestor, I have some matters to consult you about!"
Ye Tian said very seriously.
"Oh? What is it?"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor asked.
"Fire-Pattern War Ancestor, while I was fighting Jinchen Holy Ancestor, he brought out something that injured me. If it weren't for my Undying Ancestral Technique, I'm afraid I would have been heavily wounded!"
Ye Tian described that piece of paper, or rather, the talisman. He particularly described the phantom image in detail, even manifesting it for Fire-Pattern War Ancestor to see.
At this moment, Ye Tian noticed that Fire-Pattern War Ancestor's expression became very solemn.
"Ye Tian, regarding that phantom, it is actually a great secret of the Dark Holy Region—no, it should be said, the Dark Forbidden Region. It's a secret even greater than those of the Dark Destruction Region. Basically, only Supreme-level Domain Ancestors are qualified to know it, and orders have been given not to leak it.
Now that you have slain Jinchen Holy Ancestor, you are indeed qualified to know this secret!"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor said seriously.
"What secret?"
Ye Tian was shocked.
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor said, "You should have heard that Jinchen Holy Ancestor was injured and had been healing.
Although Jinchen Holy Ancestor had a home-field advantage in the Qingyuan Holy World and could exert stronger power, in reality, a Holy World doesn't provide much of a boost to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, and the arrays of a Holy Land aren't of much help either. Otherwise, Jinchen Holy Ancestor would have fled into the Qingyuan Holy World long ago.
Rumors outside say that Jinchen Holy Ancestor was hiding from a rival Supreme-level Domain Ancestor and didn't dare leave the Qingyuan Holy World.
But if there really were such a rival Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, the Qingyuan Holy World wouldn't be able to stop them; they would have charged in long ago!"
"That's true, it doesn't make sense!"
Ye Tian only now realized this problem.
For someone with his level of strength, the Xuanxu Holy World no longer provided much of a boost.
But ordinary Domain Ancestors didn't know this and thought that a Holy World still provided a massive boost to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors. Thus, those outside rumors were simply unrealistic.
"Then how was Jinchen Holy Ancestor heavily injured?"
Ye Tian asked.
"He was... heavily injured by the kind of terrifying existence you described, which is why he had to return to the Qingyuan Holy World to heal.
He likely sealed the aura of that terrifying existence and utilized it. I must say, Jinchen Holy Ancestor was also something of a genius!"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor said.
"What exactly is it?"
Ye Tian was extremely curious.
"The Nirvana Race!"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor said, his eyes seemingly revealing a trace of fear.
To be able to make Fire-Pattern War Ancestor feel fear showed just how terrifying this so-called Nirvana Race was!
