Chapter 1500: Trading with the Bone Race Again!

Ye Tian did not concern himself with the matter of the fourth Dark Holy Land.
Ultimately, a portion of the fourth Dark Holy Land's territory was occupied by the human race, and other forces did not dare to say anything.
Even if the human race were to completely occupy the fourth Holy Land, the other Ancestors would have nothing to say; after all, the human race was indeed too powerful.
......
"My cultivation progress is getting slower and slower!"
Ye Tian said helplessly.
Although his duplicated bodies were still working hard to collect resources from the Saint Realm, it was still not enough for his use.
Consequently, the cultivation speed of his main body was becoming slower and slower, and many resources were now almost useless to him, making it difficult to increase his strength.
"Perhaps I can try trading with the Bone Race!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
The Bone Race was very powerful and possessed countless resources. If he traded with them, he could obtain many precious resources that would be very helpful in increasing his strength.
On this day.
Ye Tian's main body personally descended upon the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
When Ye Tian appeared in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, the entire area began to tremble.
Immediately, the experts of the Bone Race fell into a state of panic.
"A Supreme Ancestor!!!!"
They were terrified.
A Supreme Ancestor was a powerful existence capable of rivaling a Bone Emperor; they had no way to resist.
Furthermore, the Bone Emperor was about to descend, and his strength would be reduced, so he might not necessarily be able to contend with a Supreme Ancestor.
This was a true disaster!
Of course, many members of the Bone Race also recognized Ye Tian. He was the top-tier Ancestor who had invaded the Bone Sea Forbidden Land multiple times many ages ago, but now he had ascended to the rank of Supreme Ancestor.
"Respected Supreme Ancestor, you signed an agreement with our Bone Emperor not to invade the Bone Sea Forbidden Land again!" a Bone King said, suppressing the terror in his heart.
"I am not here to make an enemy of your Bone Race; I have simply come to discuss a trade!"
Ye Tian spoke.
A trade?
Many experts of the Bone Race were filled with doubt, not knowing what Ye Tian wanted to trade.
In the past, they had never traded with the Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Domain because, in their eyes, the creatures of the Dark Forbidden Domain were mere ants to be slaughtered at will.
When the day of the Great Calamity arrived, what resources would they not have? Everything would be theirs to harvest. Therefore, they looked down on the weak Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Domain and naturally would not trade with them.
Moreover, trading was equivalent to strengthening the power of the Dark Forbidden Domain. If a large number of top-tier Ancestors were born, wouldn't they be creating trouble for themselves?
Thus, experts from other foreign worlds would not trade with the Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Domain unless the Dark Forbidden Domain could produce something so good that the foreign experts could not refuse.
But now that Ye Tian had stepped into the Supreme Ancestor realm, he naturally had the qualifications to trade with the Bone Race.
"Respected Supreme Ancestor, allow us to report this to the Bone Emperor. We cannot make decisions regarding trade!"
The Bone King said hurriedly.
"Very well!"
Ye Tian nodded.
He wasn't planning to trade with these Bone Kings anyway; mere Bone Kings couldn't produce anything truly good, and they wouldn't be of much help to him.
After all, with his current Supreme Ancestor realm, even Bone King Marrow Beads were of little help to him.
Before long, a wisp of the Bone Emperor's consciousness passed through the portal and arrived at the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
This conscious avatar did not possess much power, but the cost to send it was small. Since he wasn't here to fight, there was no need to pay the massive price to descend with his true body.
"What business do you have with me?"
The Bone Emperor of the Bone Race asked indifferently, though he was inwardly shocked.
Back then, he wasn't surprised that Ye Tian could cultivate to the peak of the top-tier Ancestor level. Although Ye Tian's combat power was astonishing and he had created so many Ultimate Ancestral Defensive Techniques, as long as he couldn't step into the Supreme Ancestor realm, he could not be compared to an existence like himself.
But how much time had passed? Ye Tian had actually stepped into the Supreme Ancestor realm, and judging by his aura, he hadn't just broken through; he had already stabilized his realm long ago.
Breaking through to the Supreme Ancestor realm was not something that could be achieved through bitter cultivation alone. One had to leave the Dark Forbidden Domain, travel to another foreign world, comprehend the rules of that world, and then walk the path of that foreign world. Only by merging the two paths could one break the shackles and step into the Supreme Ancestor realm.
He didn't know how many gates the Dark Forbidden Domain had.
Perhaps Ye Tian got lucky and passed through a gate, and the foreign world on the other side just happened to be in the midst of a Great Calamity.
Regardless of the situation, it was enough to prove that Ye Tian's luck and talent were both incredibly heaven-defying; otherwise, he could not have stepped into the Supreme Ancestor realm.
Now that Ye Tian had reached the Supreme Ancestor realm, at least within the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, the Bone Race could no longer do anything to him.
"I wish to trade some resources with the Bone Race. I know that the resources of the Dark Forbidden Domain are very useful to your Bone Race, and similarly, some of your Bone Race's resources are very helpful to me. Wouldn't it be better for us to trade with each other?" Ye Tian suggested.
"Our Bone Race is currently not lacking in resources!"
The Bone Emperor intended to refuse.
He did not want to strengthen the power of the Dark Forbidden Domain; it brought him no benefit.
"Bone Emperor, don't be so quick to refuse. I presume the Bone Race is unwilling to offend a Supreme Ancestor? Furthermore, I am willing to use resources from the Dark Forbidden Domain as well as resources from other foreign worlds to trade with the Bone Race. Additionally, I have already taken over another foreign world. Although it is a very weak one, the Dark Forbidden Domain will birth more Supreme Ancestors in the future. While I have signed an agreement with your Bone Race and will not act against the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, others have not signed such an agreement. If the Bone Race is willing to trade with me, I can guarantee that other Supreme Ancestors will not lay a hand on the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, and its existence can be preserved permanently. How about it?" Ye Tian proposed.
"What? Your Dark Forbidden Domain has already taken over a foreign world?"
The Bone Emperor found it hard to believe.
However, it was certain that the Dark Forbidden Domain had at least established a base in another foreign world; otherwise, it would have been impossible for Ye Tian to break through to the Supreme Ancestor realm.
It was foreseeable that the Dark Forbidden Domain would definitely birth more Supreme Ancestors in the future.
If that were truly the case, the Dark Forbidden Domain could indeed completely destroy the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
Now, suppressing the rise of the Dark Forbidden Domain was no longer possible; it was better to trade with them.
"Fine, I agree!"
The Bone Emperor responded.
Next, Ye Tian traded with this Bone Emperor of the Bone Race.
The Bone Emperor brought out many resources, and Ye Tian refined and tested them one by one, only trading for resources that were significantly helpful to him.
After a round of trading, Ye Tian brought out a massive amount of Dark Forbidden Domain resources. Since these resources were of little help to him—one could even say they were useless—it was essentially recycling waste.
In exchange, he obtained a massive amount of useful resources from the Bone Race, and the same was true for the Bone Race.
Finally.
"Bone Emperor, does your Bone Race possess any cultivation secrets regarding the Bone Emperor level? I have only just stepped into the Supreme Ancestor realm and do not understand this level very well. If you have any, I can offer some resources in exchange," Ye Tian asked.
"I can, but I will only trade some basic information with you. As for truly useful secrets, I will not trade those," the Bone Emperor said.
Soon after, Ye Tian used some resources to trade for a manual from the Bone Emperor. This manual was naturally inscribed by the Bone Emperor on the spot, and it recorded some information regarding the Bone Emperor level.
Whether it was a Bone Emperor or a Supreme Ancestor, they were actually at the same level and naturally shared common points.
With the trade completed, Ye Tian happily left the Bone Sea Forbidden Land and returned to the Profound Void Ancestral World.
