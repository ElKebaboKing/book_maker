Chapter 1594: Legendary Eternal Body!

Ye Tian sent out a vast amount of resources, eternal source items, and Ancient Star Stones, having his main body bring them back to the Void Source Hall.
Subsequently, he began to announce his seclusion. No one was allowed to disturb him.
Then, Ye Tian annihilated this body of his.
After all, this duplicate body belonged to the Ancient Star Race and could not leave the Ancient Star Domain, while a Supreme Being could not enter the Ancient Star Domain.
Therefore, he could only leave in this manner.
...
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian's main body, duplicate body, and clone all appeared here.
The duplicate body Ye Tian copied and the newly condensed clone were both at the First Tier Supreme Being realm. Only with consistent cultivation levels could the fusion of Eternal Bodies be carried out.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian began to conduct fusions of the duplicate body and the clone over and over again.
One was the Endless Eternal Body, and the other was the Ancient Star Eternal Body.
Regarding the fusion of Eternal Bodies, Ye Tian was initially very unfamiliar with it. Even a powerhouse like the Star Ancestor had little experience with the fusion of Eternal Bodies, so Ye Tian had to figure everything out on his own.
Repeated failures did not discourage Ye Tian; instead, they allowed him to accumulate sufficient experience.
However, as the number of failures increased, the potential and foundation of both the duplicate body and the clone were significantly affected.
Until the duplicate body and clone could no longer bear the cost of fusion due to multiple attempts, he directly collapsed his duplicate body and clone, re-condensed the duplicate body, cultivated it to break through, and transformed it into a clone.
Then, he once again conducted Eternal Body fusion experiments with the duplicate body and the clone.
It could be said that every time he re-condensed a duplicate body and created a clone, the resource cost was immense. But Ye Tian could afford it. After all, he had harvested all the resources of the Ancient Star Race, which was equivalent to obtaining all the assets of an entire Eternal Heaven and Earth!
Such staggering wealth was enough for him to waste time and again.
Moreover, the resources left behind by the Void Source Ancestor were also plentiful, so he was not afraid of consuming resources.
Unknowingly, over a hundred Great Tribulation Eras passed.
Ye Tian had consumed countless resources and attempted the process an unknown number of times. By now, he had absolute experience in the fusion of Eternal Bodies. In the entire Source Sea, one would likely be unable to find another Supreme Being with equivalent experience.
On this day.
Ye Tian began another fusion. This time, he had great confidence that he could succeed.
"Let's begin!"
The two bodies turned into two masses of energy and began to fuse together. The Ancient Star Eternal Body and the Endless Eternal Body were being homogenized bit by bit, transforming into the same fused entity.
Time flowed by in this manner. After an unknown amount of time, the two Eternal Bodies finally completed their fusion.
At the same time, the two bodies also merged into a single body.
The fused Eternal Body was extremely powerful, but it still did not exceed the scope of an Upper Eternal Body.
Ye Tian naturally knew the reason. It was because the Ancient Star Eternal Body was not in a Perfected state. After all, he did not want to waste resources cultivating the Ancient Star Eternal Body to a Perfected state only to perform fusions over and over again.
But now that the experiment had succeeded, he prepared to truly cultivate the Ancient Star Eternal Body to a Perfected state from the very beginning, advancing step by step to the Supreme Being realm before attempting to fuse it with the duplicate body of a Perfected Endless Eternal Body Supreme Being.
And wanting to refine the Ancient Star Eternal Body to the most Perfected degree was no small feat; it would take a long time.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian devoted all his time to perfecting the Ancient Star Eternal Body, improving it step by step and fixing every single flaw.
As long years passed, the flaws of the Ancient Star Body became fewer and fewer, and the power of the Ancient Star Eternal Body became increasingly formidable.
Finally, on this day, the Ancient Star Eternal Body was flawless, without a single defect remaining.
At this moment, the Ancient Star Eternal Body was a truly Perfected Upper Eternal Body. When combined with the flawless Middle Eternal Body, the Endless Eternal Body, the new Eternal Body would inevitably break the shackles of the Upper Eternal Body and advance to a new Eternal Body grade once fused.
After a long time, Ye Tian's Ancient Star Race clone grew to the Supreme Being realm, consuming many resources.
Subsequently, Ye Tian once again merged the Ancient Star Race clone and the duplicate body together.
However, a small error occurred this time, but the impact was not significant. Ye Tian summarized his experience, and after recovering from the injuries to the Ancient Star Race clone and the duplicate body, he continued the fusion.
In this way, after repeated attempts, he once again successfully fused the Ancient Star Race clone and the duplicate body together.
It had to be said that after the fusion, the brand-new Eternal Body formed this time indeed surpassed the Upper Eternal Body.
And this kind of Eternal Body that surpassed the Upper Eternal Body also had a name within the Source Sea: the Legendary Eternal Body. It meant it belonged to legends, and almost no Supreme Being had ever seen it...
Having succeeded only once, Ye Tian would not immediately let his main body attempt it.
He needed a one-hundred-percent success rate and would not allow any errors. Even if it meant wasting many eternal source items and resources, he would not hesitate.
Fortunately, the eternal source item known as the Ancient Star Stone was indeed terrifying. It allowed Ye Tian to repeatedly draw upon its source without consuming too much, so he could continue to produce an Ancient Star Race Supreme Being clone.
After Ye Tian collapsed the fused body, he continued to create another Supreme Being clone and performed the Eternal Body fusion once more.
This time, Ye Tian only failed three times before successfully fusing the two Eternal Bodies.
Subsequently, Ye Tian continued this fusion process.
After multiple fusions, Ye Tian could already ensure a one-hundred-percent success rate. Only then did he prepare to let the Ancient Star Race body fuse with his main body.
...
Void Source Hall, Source Space.
After Ye Tian adjusted his state, he began the fusion.
Whether it was the main body or the Ancient Star Race body, their cultivation was at the First Tier Supreme Being realm, and both possessed Perfected Eternal Bodies.
The fusion began. There was not a single bit of rejection between the two bodies during the process, and the fusion proceeded very smoothly.
After a long time, the two bodies perfectly fused into one, and the two Eternal Bodies also completely transformed into a brand-new Eternal Body.
"Let's still call this Legendary Eternal Body the Endless Eternal Body. The name 'Ancient Star' belongs to the Star Ancestor after all, and I don't like using that name!"
Ye Tian murmured.
With the brand-new Endless Eternal Body, this Legendary Eternal Body, Ye Tian's strength was more than just a little bit stronger than before!
In terms of combat power at the same level, he surpassed the Void Source Race by an unknown margin.
Furthermore, the Endless Eternal Body fused with the Ancient Star Eternal Body possessed terrifying survival capabilities.
At this moment, Ye Tian possessed a near-truly undying body. Even a Supreme Being whose strength far surpassed his own would have no way to kill him.
Although he was only at the First Tier Supreme Being realm, his true combat power was at least not weaker than the previous Third Tier Supreme Being Ancient Star Race body.
It was conceivable that once he grew to the Third Tier Supreme Being realm, his combat power could at least rival some Sixth or Seventh Tier Supreme Beings.
