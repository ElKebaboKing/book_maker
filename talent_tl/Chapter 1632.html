Chapter 1631 The mastermind has been found!

And when this mysterious force invaded Lanyue World, Ye Tian notified Source God Pavilion, but it would take time for the Peak experts of Source God Pavilion and the Peak experts of other forces to rush to Lanyue World.
The mysterious force had already used some formations in the area near Lanyue World to interfere with the void, and in addition, Lanyue World had been sealed off.
Even Peak half-step Eternal Realm experts could not use source artifacts to perform spatial travel and descend directly near Lanyue World; they could only descend to the territory not far from Lanyue World, and then fly over at the fastest speed.
By then, it was already too late.
Whizz whizz whizz！！！！
As Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the Peak half-step Eternal Realm experts of other forces descended here, they discovered that Lanyue World had already shattered, countless living beings had vanished, Lanyue Pavilion had completely disappeared, and Lanyue Source Ancestor had also fallen.
“One step too late!”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor muttered.
Immediately after, one after another half-step Eternal Realm experts left this place.
Not long after, Ye Tian’s copy body descended into Source God Pavilion again.
Inside Source God Pavilion.
Ye Tian met with the Twelve Ancestors, and everyone sat down and started talking.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor directly asked, “Endless Ancestor, you said before that you were sure you could investigate the other party’s identity. Have you found it out now?”
“I have!”
Ye Tian nodded.
Under the investigation of the Copy Talent, that Peak expert simply could not hide his identity.
“Which force is it?”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor asked urgently.
Ye Tian said, “I’m not too sure which force, but I know who that Peak expert who made the move is.”
“Who is it?”
The other Twelve Ancestors were also curious.
“Star Demon Source Ancestor!”
Ye Tian revealed.
“It’s him. Are you sure?”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor frowned.
“Yes, I can guarantee it 100%. As for whether you believe it or not, that has nothing to do with me.” Ye Tian said.
“Do you know who Star Demon Source Ancestor is?”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor asked.
“I don’t know.” Ye Tian only learned the other party’s identity from the Copy Talent’s investigation, but who this Peak half-step Eternal Realm expert actually was, he really did not know.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor began explaining Star Demon Source Ancestor’s origins.
“Star Demon Source Ancestor is one of the oldest half-step Eternal Realm experts in Source Sea, even older than many Eternal Realm experts, and he has not appeared for a very long time. At the same time, he is also the master of Star Demon Mountain, and under his command are dozens of half-step Eternal Realm experts serving him. More importantly, one of Star Demon Source Ancestor’s disciples has advanced to the Eternal Realm.”
Star Demon Source Ancestor has a disciple in the Eternal Realm?
Ye Tian finally understood why Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others were frowning, because the other party’s background was too great.
How powerful is an Eternal Realm expert? The Twelve Ancestors did not dare offend such a being.
Therefore, if they wanted to deal with Star Demon Source Ancestor, they had to consider the Eternal Realm expert behind him.
Moreover, although Star Demon Source Ancestor had destroyed many forces, none of those forces had Eternal Realm backing. Would those forces that did have Eternal Realm backers really offend an Eternal Realm expert for the sake of the destroyed forces?
Besides, Star Demon Source Ancestor himself was not weak either.
“Star Demon Source Ancestor’s strength is Three-Extreme Realm, we are no match for him!”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor shook his head.
It was obvious that he also did not dare go looking for trouble with Star Demon Source Ancestor.
“Twelve Ancestors, how are you going to handle this matter?”
Ye Tian asked.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others were silent for a while before making a decision.
“We will spread the news about Star Demon Source Ancestor, but we will not make a move. After all, we cannot kill Star Demon Source Ancestor, and we also cannot afford to offend Star Demon Source Ancestor.”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor said helplessly.
“I understand!”
Ye Tian said nothing more, nor would he persuade Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others.
After all, asking them to go kill a Peak expert they could not kill, and this Peak expert also had an Eternal Realm behind him, no half-step Eternal Realm expert had that kind of courage.
Afterwards, Ye Tian left.
He knew there was no way to count on Source God Pavilion for this matter.
However, Source God Pavilion still spread the news in a discreet manner.
Gradually, the entire Source Sea learned about Star Demon Source Ancestor’s matter.
After all, in this era of Source Sea, the number of Peak experts did not exceed three digits, only around eighty or ninety.
Removing those with no suspicion left an even smaller number.
Therefore, after Star Demon Source Ancestor was exposed, many forces believed it.
-------------
Star Demon Mountain.
Star Demon Source Ancestor also learned of the news from outside and was immediately puzzled.
“Strange, my movements were very fast, there’s no way I could have been exposed. Who exactly leaked it?”
Star Demon Source Ancestor was very surprised.
Although the news had been exposed, it was nothing to him. There were not many half-step Eternal Realm experts who dared to deal with him, after all, one of his disciples was an Eternal Realm expert.
However, once the news was leaked, he would definitely be watched. If he wanted to make a move again, then it would be somewhat troublesome.
“The power absorbed by the Bloodthirst Pearl is still not enough to allow me to advance to the Eternal Realm. I still need to destroy a few more forces!”
Star Demon Source Ancestor thought to himself.
Immediately afterward, many forces appeared in his mind, and in the end he locked onto one target.
Ancient God Palace!
This was also a fairly strong force, considered an alliance force, with six half-step Eternal Realm experts. Among them, the strongest was only an upper-tier half-step Eternal Realm expert, not a Peak expert.
Although with his strength it would take a bit of time to kill an upper-tier half-step Eternal Realm expert, he had already been exposed, so there was no need to worry about speed anymore. Even if the destruction of Ancient God Palace was discovered, not necessarily anyone would dare to attack him.
Ancient God Palace was not weak. Destroying Ancient God Palace, and then destroying one or two more forces, would be enough to gather the power.
On this day.
Star Demon Source Ancestor set out from Star Demon Mountain with eighteen half-step Eternal Realm experts, heading for Ancient God Palace.
Very quickly.
Ancient God Palace was surrounded by Star Demon Source Ancestor. The Time-Space Hourglass Pearl sealed the void, and even half-step Eternal Realm experts had no way to escape from here.
Black Fiend Sacred Ancestor of Ancient God Palace, upon seeing Star Demon Source Ancestor and so many half-step Eternal Realm experts, immediately became terrified.
“Star Demon Source Ancestor, spare us, I am willing to hand over all the treasures and resources!”
Black Fiend Sacred Ancestor panicked.
The other experts of Ancient God Palace also panicked. Today was truly a dead end!
“Attack!”
The half-step Eternal Realm experts of Star Demon Mountain all made their move, slaughtering countless experts. All of these experts from Ancient God Palace were swallowed by the blood river transformed from the Bloodthirst Pearl, leaving not even corpses behind.
As for Black Fiend Sacred Ancestor, he was targeted by Star Demon Source Ancestor, and the two sides fought, or rather, Black Fiend Sacred Ancestor was completely suppressed and had no ability to resist at all.
The only thing Black Fiend Sacred Ancestor could do was try his best to delay time, perhaps some Peak expert would come to rescue him?
At the same time.
Source God Pavilion and other top major forces also received the news that Star Demon Source Ancestor had invaded Ancient God Palace, and the news was absolutely confirmed.
This also completely established the fact that Star Demon Source Ancestor was the mastermind, but many Peak experts still did not dare to make a move.
Not long after, Star Demon Source Ancestor killed Black Fiend Sacred Ancestor, destroyed Ancient God Palace, and was just about to leave.
Suddenly.
This area of Source Sea changed, and they instantly arrived in an ice world.
This ice world had even frozen the origin of Source Sea, as if it could freeze everything.
This was a heaven-shaking grand formation, and the enemy had set it up successfully in just a short time.
“Limit Ice Ancestor, it’s you? Are you going to make a move against me?”
Star Demon Source Ancestor said in terror.
Limit Ice Ancestor, one of the six Peak experts of this era in Source Sea, was a seventh-tier half-step Eternal Realm expert, far surpassing his strength.
Such an expert had actually made a move against him, and had even secretly set up a grand formation. This was clearly not wanting to let him escape!
Crick crick crick！！！！
The many half-step Eternal Realm experts from Star Demon Mountain were frozen in place. Although they had not yet died, once they were frozen for too long and their bodies were frozen and shattered, then they would truly die.
