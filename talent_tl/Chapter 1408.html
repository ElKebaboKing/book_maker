Chapter 1407: Confronting the Invincible Domain Ancestors
!
After Saint Lord Qingfeng decided to seize the Saint-grade ore vein in the Profound Void Saint Realm, he immediately arranged for two Invincible Domain Ancestors to go to the Profound Void Saint Realm.
This time, not many Domain Ancestors were needed; the focus was on elite forces.
If Supreme Domain Ancestors were sent, it would be easy to expose them, and they wouldn't be of much use.
Furthermore, the two Invincible Domain Ancestors arranged this time rarely appeared, so the outside world wouldn't know they were Invincible Domain Ancestors from Qingyuan Holy Land.
Even if discovered, the outside world might not be able to trace it back to Qingyuan Holy Land, making it safer.
On this day.
The two Invincible Domain Ancestors from Qingyuan Holy Land set off for the Profound Void Saint Realm in the Daoshan Domain.
The strength of these two Invincible Domain Ancestors was not weak; although they hadn't reached the level of Bloodgang Domain Ancestor, they were much stronger than the Domain Ancestors on the Supreme Ranking.
Therefore, Saint Ancestor Qingfeng believed that if these two Invincible Domain Ancestors made a move, they could definitely seize that Saint-grade ore vein, and it wouldn't matter even if Ye Tian discovered them.
The Invincible Domain Ancestors were very fast, and they had been traveling at their maximum speed.
In just a few thousand years, these two Invincible Domain Ancestors arrived at the Daoshan Domain.
After they arrived at the Daoshan Domain, they fully concealed their aura.
Although both of them were very confident in their strength, the situation in the Daoshan Domain was quite complex, with many Domain Ancestors from the Dark Saint Realm having come here, and Qingyuan Holy Land was not actually a powerful force.
Compared to other forces, Qingyuan Holy Land was just a bit bolder this time.
Therefore, they still needed to be careful to avoid any mishaps that could lead to the failure of the plan, which would be undesirable.
"Domain Ancestor Wansheng, let's go directly to the location of the Saint-grade ore vein, use the sacred artifact Qingyuan World Pearl to intercept the void where the ore vein is located, and then leave!"
Domain Ancestor Mili said.
"Alright!"
Domain Ancestor Wansheng nodded.
Immediately, Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili headed to the Profound Void Saint Realm. They did not directly tear through the void to enter the Profound Void Saint Realm, as that would be too obvious.
Therefore, they followed ordinary cultivators through the various world entrances of the Profound Void Saint Realm, entering the Profound Void Saint Realm. This way, they blended into the interior of the Profound Void Saint Realm, and no one knew that two Invincible Domain Ancestors had infiltrated.
Soon, Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili arrived at the location of the Saint-grade ore vein.
At this time, they saw the grand formation covering the Saint-grade ore vein and the Human Race Domain Ancestors guarding it.
"There are no Supreme Domain Ancestors guarding it; these Domain Ancestors are too weak, posing no threat! Should we kill them or not?"
Domain Ancestor Wansheng asked.
"Kill them. If we don't, it will be difficult to intercept this void. If these Domain Ancestors attack, it will also cause spatial tremors, which will slow down the Qingyuan World Pearl's speed in intercepting this void, thus wasting time. I don't want to encounter Domain Ancestor Wanfa, and we can't kill him either, so it's best to avoid Domain Ancestor Wanfa!"
Domain Ancestor Mili suggested.
"Alright, then let's directly attack with soul attack secret techniques!"
Domain Ancestor Wansheng said.
It was naturally very easy for Invincible Domain Ancestors to kill a group of weaklings, even those with top-tier Domain Ancestors.
Boom!!!!! A terrifying soul wave swept across the Saint-grade ore vein.
At this moment, the Human Race Domain Ancestors guarding the Saint-grade ore vein seemed to be frozen in place, their souls annihilated one by one, even the top-tier Domain Ancestors were no exception, falling one after another.
It was at this moment that Domain Ancestor Mili took out the Qingyuan World Pearl.
"Collect!"
With a ripping sound!!!! The void near the Saint-grade ore vein was forcibly stripped from the Profound Void Saint Realm by the Qingyuan World Pearl. A large expanse of void directly detached from this area, continuously shrinking, and was sealed inside the Qingyuan World Pearl.
"It's a success! Quickly tear through the Profound Void Saint Realm's world barrier and leave!"
Domain Ancestor Wansheng said excitedly.
Human Race, Human Realm.
Ye Tian's main body was cultivating, and the moment the Saint-grade ore vein's formation was broken, he sensed it.
Moreover, as the Lord of the Profound Void Saint Realm, he knew the location of the Saint-grade ore vein. He immediately communicated with the Primordial Creation Well, and his gaze peered into the area of the Saint-grade ore vein.
Boom!!!! The void rippled open, and Ye Tian's gaze crossed the void, seeing the two Invincible Domain Ancestors—Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili.
The Saint-grade ore vein had been taken by them, and at this moment, these two Invincible Domain Ancestors were preparing to break through the world barrier and leave.
If it were an ordinary Saint Realm Lord, they might not have had enough time to stop them, as it would take time to rush there.
Furthermore, a Saint Realm Lord of the Supreme Domain Ancestor level would also be unable to prevent two Invincible Domain Ancestors from leaving.
However, Ye Tian was not an ordinary Saint Realm Lord; he also controlled the Primordial Creation Well, and his degree of control over the Saint Realm was unparalleled.
The Saint-grade ore vein had disappeared, and two Invincible Domain Ancestors appeared there. Clearly, the result was the demise of the Human Race Domain Ancestors guarding the Saint-grade ore vein.
"I didn't expect anyone would actually dare to attack the Saint-grade ore vein!"
Ye Tian was extremely furious.
Rumble!!!! Ye Tian fully mobilized the power of the Profound Void Saint Realm, holding nothing back.
"Suppress!"
Ye Tian, carrying the power of the Profound Void Saint Realm, suppressed Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili, while he himself quickly teleported over.
At this moment.
Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili were about to tear through the Profound Void Saint Realm's world barrier when a surge of world power suppressed them. This world power far surpassed the level of a Supreme Domain Ancestor; although it was far from enough to kill them, it did obstruct them, preventing them from instantly breaking through the Profound Void Saint Realm's world barrier and leaving.
Bang bang!! Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili were forcibly knocked down during their attempt to teleport away. The two stood in the void, exchanged glances, and knew that they had been discovered by Ye Tian, and that Ye Tian had actually suppressed them from afar, successfully stopping them.
"It seems we must face Domain Ancestor Wanfa!"
Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili frowned.
After all, the Saint Lord's order was for them not to provoke Domain Ancestor Wanfa, and certainly not to kill him. Otherwise, the matter would become too big; if the War Gate fully investigated, Qingyuan Holy Land might not be able to conceal it, and once the truth was exposed, Qingyuan Holy Land would be in trouble.
Therefore, they could not kill Ye Tian; they would ultimately severely injure him before leaving.
Swish! Ye Tian tore through space and descended, appearing before Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili.
"You two stole my Saint-grade ore vein and killed our Human Race Domain Ancestors..."
Ye Tian said, carrying immense fury.
