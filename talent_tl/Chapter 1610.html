Chapter 1609: Innate Eternal Life!

“What if an Innate Eternal Life was born in Fugu River?”
Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor speculated.
“What? You mean Innate Eternal Life?”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor said in disbelief.
The other twelve ancestors were also shocked by this guess. Innate Eternal Life is an extremely rare form of life. Once born, it possesses an Eternal body, and if it reaches adulthood, it is enough to match a half-step Eternal Realm expert.
Moreover, the chance for Innate Eternal Life to break through to the Eternal Realm is much greater than that of a half-step Eternal Realm expert.
According to rumors, among the mighty experts in the Eternal Realm, half are Innate Eternal Life.
This shows just how formidable Innate Eternal Life is.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor quickly asked again, “Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor, Innate Eternal Life is so rare. Why would you make such a guess?”
Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor said, “I’m not guessing randomly. First of all, if Innate Eternal Life truly comes into the world, the commotion will be enormous and attract many powerful experts. The last time Innate Eternal Life appeared, it drew in many half-step Eternal Realm experts, and they even fought over it, causing three half-step Eternal Realm experts to fall. In the end, a mighty Eternal Realm expert appeared and took that Innate Eternal Life away. If the Eternal Tribulation Race discovered that Innate Eternal Life was about to be born, they would occupy Fugu River and then transform it into an Eternal Tribulation Domain. Then they would pay a huge price to seal off the Eternal Tribulation Domain. In that case, outsiders would not be able to get in, and they would also be unable to discover the phenomenon and commotion of Innate Eternal Life being born.”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor shook his head and said, “That’s only speculation, not evidence.”
Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor smiled and said, “Indeed, it’s not evidence, and it’s not even really related. But before Fugu River was occupied by the Eternal Tribulation Race, a subordinate stationed there sent me a message saying that a strange place had been discovered somewhere in Fugu River, as if some lifeform was gestating there. I didn’t pay much attention at the time, because it was just a small matter, so I ignored it. Looking back now, could it have been Innate Eternal Life in gestation, and my subordinate, lacking knowledge, didn’t know about Innate Eternal Life at all, so he only reported to me that it was a special lifeform?”
“If we add this piece of information, then it really could be Innate Eternal Life.”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor nodded.
At this moment, another one of the twelve ancestors, Bei Yang Yuan Ancestor, asked, “Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor, do you know the exact location of that special lifeform?”
“I don’t know!” Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor shook his head. “Because I didn’t pay attention, I never asked for the location. If I had known the location, I would have immediately sent Supreme Beings to investigate the information, and I wouldn’t be here speculating.”
“If Innate Eternal Life really exists, then the Eternal Tribulation Race will definitely spare no effort to occupy Fugu River. As for the Supreme Beings under us, there aren’t many of them. Even if we issue a bounty mission, many Supreme Beings are very protective of their lives and may not be willing to risk their lives for that little bit of resources. In the end, the Eternal Tribulation Race will suffer heavy losses, but if they obtain that Innate Eternal Life, all those losses will be made up for.” Zhenhe Sword Ancestor said.
“Then what should we do?” Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor asked.
After thinking for a while, one of the twelve ancestors, Saint Ya Yuan Ancestor, suggested, “Just spread the news that there is a high possibility of Innate Eternal Life in Fugu River, and even publicize the message your subordinate sent you. I believe some people will believe it and even take risks to enter Fugu River. Many forces will probably also send people to investigate whether it is true or false. In that case, the Eternal Tribulation Race can forget about successfully taking Fugu River.”
“This method works. I agree!” Feng Gu Yuan Ancestor immediately agreed.
Thus.
Next, the twelve ancestors spread this news, and it quickly spread throughout the major forces of the Source Sea.
--------------------
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian also received a message from the Source God Pavilion and was immediately shocked.
“Innate Eternal Life?”
Was this guess reliable?
He was not too sure, but if it were true, then it could explain why the Eternal Tribulation Race was acting this way.
When Ye Tian shared the matter of Innate Eternal Life with the hall spirit of Void Source Hall, the hall spirit revealed some information.
“Master, in fact, the previous master also fell because of Innate Eternal Life!”
the spirit said.
“Wasn’t it said that he was pursued by a great enemy and eventually fell?”
Ye Tian asked in confusion. .........
The spirit explained, “In fact, back then the master and that great enemy were fighting over Innate Eternal Life. If not for the master, that half-step Eternal Realm expert really might have successfully taken away Innate Eternal Life, but the master stopped him in time. In the end, the master was severely injured and gave up the struggle. Later, it was said that a mysterious Eternal Realm expert appeared and, in an instant, took that Innate Eternal Life away. That great enemy lost Innate Eternal Life and blamed all of it on the master, so he came to hunt down the master while he was severely injured and healing. The master knew he wouldn’t survive, so he threw Void Source Hall into a forbidden land and fled, but in the end he was still killed by the other side. However, the master also severely injured the other side at the cost of his life.”
“So that’s how it was!”
Ye Tian understood.
Soon after, he became even more curious and asked, “Why does everyone want to seize Innate Eternal Life?”
The spirit said, “When Innate Eternal Life reaches adulthood, it can grow to the half-step Eternal Realm. If one uses special methods to possess an Innate Eternal Life, then once successful, one can grow into a half-step Eternal Realm expert in the future, and even has a certain chance of breaking through to the Eternal Realm! Of course, the success rate of possessing Innate Eternal Life is too low, so most people who want to seize Innate Eternal Life do not do it for this reason. In fact, the body of Innate Eternal Life contains extremely high innate Eternal rules. If one possesses an Innate Eternal Life, one can continuously comprehend the innate Eternal rules, which is of some help for future breakthroughs in realm and for charging toward the Eternal Realm. Many forces want to seize Innate Eternal Life with the goal of confining it and letting the Supreme Beings comprehend and cultivate from it. Moreover, the innate Eternal crystal core of Innate Eternal Life also has the effect of assisting Ninth-Level Supreme Beings in breaking through to the half-step Eternal Realm.”
Hearing these benefits from the spirit, Ye Tian finally understood why everyone wanted Innate Eternal Life.
At this moment, he felt somewhat excited.
If the Source God Pavilion’s speculation was true, and he found Innate Eternal Life, did that mean he could also replicate Innate Eternal Life?
Thinking of this, he immediately sent his clone to search and investigate for Innate Eternal Life in Fugu River.
