Chapter 1402: The Tables Turn, Ye Tian's Terrifying Power
!
"Power of Eternity!"
Ye Tian enlightened.
Once the Undying Saint Body of Eternity was raised to its extreme realm, the Power of Eternity would be born.
The Power of Eternity could grant Ye Tian even greater explosive power, and it could also elevate Ye Tian's defense to a terrifying degree, making it an eternally unbreakable defense.
In short, once he possessed the Power of Eternity, it would be difficult for Ye Tian to fall.
After the Undying Saint Body of Eternity broke through, Ye Tian's strength was far greater than before.
At this moment, because Ye Tian had just broken through, his aura could not be fully contained, reaching an incredibly terrifying level, which startled even Ancestor Qingyang.
"This.... it's just the Saint Body breaking through from the perfect to the extreme realm, how could his aura surge so much?"
Ancestor Qingyang was dumbfounded.
"Never mind!"
Ancestor Qingyang continued to activate the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn, aiming it at Ye Tian, unleashing a full-power strike.
The power of this strike even carried the power of his Saint Soul! Ancestor Qingyang's Saint Soul and main body merged, transforming into a colossal giant, who then thrust the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn forward.
This sword shattered space and time, capable of annihilating everything.
Yet Ye Tian showed no fear.
"Golden Body Saint Art!"
"Saint Armor Saint Art!"
"Power of Eternity!"
....Layers of defense stacked up, forming a thick Eternal Shield.
Boom!!!! The Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn descended, piercing Ye Tian's Eternal Shield.
"Ancestor Wanfa definitely can't block the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn!"
Ancestor Quezhan, from afar, said coldly.
Ancestor Xuegang, hidden in the shadows, was also a bit nervous, but he couldn't help Ye Tian.
If Ye Tian weren't just a clone, he would truly be in a panic.
In the eyes of the War Gate, Ye Tian's talent was recognized by all high-ranking members; his future achievements were immense, with a high probability of reaching the Supreme Ancestor level.
The War Gate did not lack Invincible Ancestors, but it specifically lacked Supreme Ancestors.
Whether in the Dark Saint Domain or the Dark Destruction Domain, Supreme Ancestors were extremely rare, truly top-tier existences.
Therefore, in the eyes of the War Gate, one Ye Tian was more precious than ten thousand top-tier powerful Ancestors.
If Ye Tian were to fall, the War Gate's loss would be immense.
Fortunately, it was a clone; if Ye Tian's true body were involved, the War Gate would never allow him to challenge the Ghost Fox Organization like this.
Of course, Ancestor Xuegang also didn't want this clone of Ye Tian to fall here, as it would leave a knot in Ye Tian's heart.
"I hope Ancestor Wanfa can withstand this strike!"
Ancestor Xuegang murmured.
Under everyone's gaze, the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn collided with the Eternal Shield.
Crack!!!! The Eternal Shield cracked, forming a fissure, but it was merely a crack; the Eternal Shield did not shatter.
"He blocked it!"
Ye Tian breathed a sigh of relief.
Although he had just used the Time Shard Saint Art to glimpse a future scene and knew that the Eternal Shield could block the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn.
But now that it was truly blocked, he finally relaxed.
"How could he block it? This was a full-power strike from the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn!"
Ancestor Qingyang was bewildered.
He had planned to heavily injure Ye Tian with one sword, or after killing Ye Tian, immediately unleash the "Fruitless Annihilation" karmic Saint Art to kill Ye Tian's true body across endless territories.
But he never expected that even a full-power strike from the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn couldn't heavily injure Ye Tian, so the Fruitless Annihilation Saint Art couldn't possibly kill Ye Tian, let alone his true body! "You just attacked me, now it's my turn!"
Ye Tian sneered.
At this moment, Ancestor Qingyang felt a crisis, and it was a death crisis.
"Not good!"
Ancestor Qingyang was greatly alarmed.
Immediately, he wanted to escape.
After all, he had already condensed his Saint Soul; if Ancestor Quezhan hadn't come to find him, he would have stabilized his newly broken-through Saint Soul realm and then proceeded to challenge the Invincible Ancestor realm.
Once he reached the Invincible Ancestor realm and obtained a natal Saint Talent most suitable for him, he could then cultivate a Saint Art most suitable for him, and further cultivate that Saint Art to the perfect realm, or even the extreme realm.
In that case, his strength would reach a terrifying level, and perhaps in a short time, he could even reach the level of the Ten Emperors of the Ghost Fox Organization, not inferior to Ancestor Quezhan.
Therefore, he didn't want to die, at least not in this meaningless battle.
He still had an infinite future, how could he die here! At this moment, he regretted it! If he had known this, he shouldn't have agreed to Ancestor Quezhan's request to take on this mission.
With his status and the qualification of having condensed a Saint Soul, even if he refused, Ancestor Quezhan's anger wouldn't do anything to him.
After all, he was about to reach the Invincible Ancestor realm and would be one of the Ten Emperors in the future. Could Ancestor Quezhan do anything to him? No! Therefore, even if he refused, he would only suffer a minor punishment for now, which wouldn't affect him much.
"Quick, rush into the Thunder Island branch! Ancestor Wanfa won't be able to kill his way in either!"
Ancestor Qingyang said.
"Escape?"
Ye Tian sneered and unleashed the Five Elements Seal.
After his strength greatly increased, Ye Tian's Five Elements Seal became many times more terrifying, instantly sealing the void, making it difficult for Ancestor Qingyang to break through the blockade.
If he could still use the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn, perhaps he could tear apart the Five Elements Seal with one strike.
But right now, he couldn't continue to use the Projection Sword of the Grand Dawn.
"Eternal Saint Art!"
Ye Tian froze time, and also froze Ancestor Qingyang.
Next, Ye Tian fully unleashed the Void Slash Saint Art.
Poof! The Void Slash Saint Art struck Ancestor Qingyang's body.
At this moment, Ancestor Qingyang finally broke through the Eternal Saint Art and woke up, but he couldn't avoid Ye Tian's Void Slash Saint Art.
With a bang! Ancestor Qingyang was directly sent flying, the power of the Void Slash permeated his body, injuring him, and the injuries were not light.
If it had been before, Ye Tian's Void Slash Saint Art wouldn't have been able to injure Ancestor Qingyang at all.
Ye Tian's strike being able to injure Ancestor Qingyang meant that Ancestor Qingyang was in danger.
Next, Ye Tian went all out, unleashing one Saint Art after another.
Although the same Saint Art could not be cast continuously, casting different Saint Arts consecutively was not affected.
Under the barrage of Saint Arts, Ancestor Qingyang was dazed, his injuries growing heavier and heavier, while his counterattacks couldn't harm Ye Tian.
In this way, Ancestor Qingyang's injuries gradually approached critical levels.
If this continued, Ancestor Qingyang might fall.
Whoosh! An aura of an Invincible Ancestor appeared, and it was extremely terrifying, even stronger than Ancestor Xuegang's aura.
He was Ancestor Quezhan of the Ghost Fox Organization.
As soon as Ancestor Quezhan appeared, Ancestor Xuegang also appeared, fearing that Ancestor Quezhan would attack Ye Tian.
In terms of strength, Ancestor Xuegang was indeed no match for Ancestor Quezhan, and there was even a certain probability that he would be killed by the opponent, but he had to step forward; this was the mission given to him by the Supreme Ancestors of the War Gate.
"Ancestor Quezhan, what are you doing?"
Ancestor Xuegang blocked Ancestor Quezhan and asked.
