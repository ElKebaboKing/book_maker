Chapter 1516: The Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain is Here!

With a single thought, Jie Zu traveled instantly to the outskirts of the human race's Profound Void Ancestral World. Today, the Profound Void Ancestral World had already surpassed a standard Ancestral World, reaching the level of a Supreme Ancestral World. Both its cultivation environment and resources far exceeded those of naturally born Ancestral Worlds.
This place had completely developed into the core of the human race, while the human ancestral worlds within those Dark Holy Lands had become branch locations.
Furthermore, through Ye Tian's refinements, even an Ancestor would find it difficult to pry into the slightest detail of the Profound Void Ancestral World.
When a thought from Jie Zu arrived, the human Ancestors naturally sensed it.
"Jie Zu, what business brings you here?"
A human Ancestor asked.
"I wish to see the Myriad Law Ancestor!" Jie Zu stated.
"Very well, I will go and inquire. As for whether Lord Myriad Law Ancestor will see you, I cannot guarantee it!" the human Ancestor replied.
After all, even these human Ancestors had not seen the Myriad Law Ancestor for a long time.
Soon, the human Ancestor went to see the Myriad Law Ancestor.
In truth, Ye Tian naturally knew Jie Zu had arrived. Since he was not currently in a state of total secluded meditation, he was aware of the situation in the outside world.
He could even roughly guess Jie Zu's purpose, which was why he hesitated over whether to meet him.
"Forget it, I might as well see him."
Ye Tian's avatar appeared outside the Profound Void Ancestral World to meet Jie Zu.
"Myriad Law Ancestor!"
Jie Zu said respectfully.
This was the first time he had seen the Myriad Law Ancestor in a very long time.
At this moment, he was somewhat alarmed.
Because the aura on the Myriad Law Ancestor's body far surpassed his imagination; it was simply not an aura that a top-tier Ancestor could possess.
A Supreme Ancestor!
Jie Zu realized it instantly.
Clearly, Ye Tian had indeed stepped into the Supreme Ancestor realm, just as the rumors suggested.
"Jie Zu, why have you sought me out?" Ye Tian asked.
Jie Zu said, "Myriad Law Ancestor, the Ancestors of our Dark Forbidden Domain are currently holding a meeting to discuss how to deal with the Great Calamity. As their representative, I wish to ask if the human race has the capability to withstand the calamity. If the human race is capable, everyone wishes to join the human race and become a subordinate force."
"Jie Zu, go back and tell them that the human race does not have the capability to withstand the Great Calamity!" Ye Tian spoke plainly.
"Myriad Law Ancestor, is the human race truly unable to withstand the calamity?" Jie Zu asked seriously.
Ye Tian said, "Once the calamity arrives, the Battle Sect can come to the human territory for refuge, but other forces cannot."
"Why?"
Jie Zu wanted to know the reason.
"Because the human race cannot protect all forces!" Ye Tian sighed. "You do not know how powerful those foreign forces are. Supreme Ancestors are divided into three levels: the first level, the second level, and the third level. Above the Supreme Ancestors are the Invincible Ancestors. Six of the foreign forces possess Invincible Ancestors, and these six forces are the ones behind the six Great Forbidden Lands. The human race is no match for them. If we truly tried to protect all forces, we would have to face all those opponents. When the calamity arrives, those foreign forces must slaughter and clear the entire Dark Forbidden Domain. With my ability, protecting this area of the human race is my limit."
"I understand. Thank you, Myriad Law Ancestor!"
Jie Zu said gratefully.
Subsequently, this thought of Jie Zu's returned to his main body.
...
"Jie Zu, what did the Myriad Law Ancestor say?"
One Ancestor after another asked, appearing incredibly urgent.
Jie Zu sighed. "The human race says they cannot protect us. They are also not certain they can survive the calamity. If they accept us, they would have to face threats from even more foreign forces, so they have declined!"
"Damn the human race!"
"I shouldn't have expected the Myriad Law Ancestor to agree!"
"Forget it, I'll just find a safe place to hide!"
Afterward, the Ancestors departed one by one.
Some Ancestors even headed toward the various Forbidden Lands, attempting to join those foreign forces.
However, those Ancestors never appeared again.
As time passed, the Great Calamity of the Dark Boundary grew closer and closer.
On this day.
Ye Tian recalled the human race from various Dark Holy Lands. One Ancestor after another took action, either personally bringing back humans from various territories or opening space-time gates to let the humans travel through them to the Daoshan Boundary Domain.
The number of humans in the entire Dark Forbidden Domain was too vast; it took a long time to relocate them all.
To settle these humans, the human Ancestors built a massive number of worlds in the areas surrounding the Daoshan Boundary Domain.
It took several boundary years to finally complete the migration of the entire human race.
At this point, the territory centered around the Profound Void Ancestral World was the human race's sole domain; all other territories were abandoned.
Some small forces, unaware of the situation, were excited to see the human race abandon so many territories and even Ancestral Worlds. They rushed to occupy these Ancestral Worlds and territories, not knowing that when the calamity descended, these lands would be of no use at all.
While the human race was preparing, the Ancestors of other forces were also preparing.
They all abandoned their organizations because they simply couldn't protect them, and each went into hiding.
If they were lucky, some Ancestors might still survive.
Calamity Black Mountain.
One Ancient Ancestor after another, radiating the aura of calamity, appeared. Ancient Ancestors were equivalent to Ancestors, and the Ancient Ancestors of Calamity Black Mountain not only possessed powerful physical bodies but were also skilled in curses. Even from a vast distance, they could curse someone to death using just a single strand of hair.
Even if the opponent possessed many clones and a main body, as long as they had one clone, they could instantly kill all of the opponent's clones.
Thus, the Ancient Ancestors of Calamity Black Mountain were very powerful and terrifying.
Before long, three third-level Supreme Ancient Ancestors crossed over from the other side of the Rank Two Gate.
Dozens of Supreme Ancient Ancestors and tens of thousands of ordinary Ancient Ancestors—Calamity Black Mountain had mobilized a great deal of power this time.
Moreover, they could dispatch even more experts at any time.
Besides Calamity Black Mountain, other foreign forces were also ready, waiting for the Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain to arrive.
BOOM!!!!
The Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain arrived. The rules of the entire Dark Forbidden Domain underwent a subtle change, no longer repelling foreign experts.
Having already experienced a Great Calamity in the Holy Domain Heaven and Earth, Ye Tian was naturally very familiar with the changes of the calamity.
"...Activate the grand array!"
Ye Tian commanded.
Instantly, within the human territory, countless grand arrays were activated. Myriad Domain Ancestors stood as guardians of the formations at their respective posts, while the many Ancestors of the human race also maintained the arrays.
Ye Tian had already issued orders forbidding any Ancestor from going out to snatch Source Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian produced a duplicated body. His main body sat in wait within the human race, while his duplicated body went out to seize Source Rule Tribulation Crystals. If any foreign experts invaded the human race, this duplicated body could return to provide support at any time.
