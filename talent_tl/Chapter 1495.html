Chapter 1494: Three Great Calamities, the Third Dark Era
"How is it possible? How can you possess such terrifying strength?"
The Holy Ancestor spoke in disbelief.
He was a top-tier Ancestor, and not one who had just broken through. His strength was immense.
In the Dark Forbidden Zone, a top-tier Ancestor was the... limit. Even the strongest among them shouldn't be able to heavily injure him in a single strike.
But Ye Tian had done it. A single blow had left him severely wounded. If Ye Tian attacked a few more times, he feared he would be killed outright.
"Could it be that you have stepped into the realm of a Supreme Ancestor?"
The Holy Ancestor asked in horror.
But he immediately dismissed the thought, saying, "Impossible. You couldn't have reached the level of a Supreme Ancestor. No one in the Dark Forbidden Zone can reach that level!"
"I naturally haven't reached the level of a Supreme Ancestor."
Ye Tian stared at the Holy Ancestor. "But killing a top-tier Ancestor like you only takes a few moves."
The Holy Ancestor was terrified. Once, he had looked down upon everything; aside from the Destruction Ancestor, he feared no one.
But now, he was afraid of Ye Tian.
How long had it been? Ye Tian had grown from an ant to a level where even he had to look up.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, what exactly do you want?"
The Holy Ancestor asked.
Ye Tian gazed at him and said, "I said before, I am looking for the ancient Ancestor behind you. Don't deny it, or you die!"
The Holy Ancestor dared not deny it any longer and hurriedly asked, "What exactly do you want with my Master?"
Master? Ye Tian had already suspected as much, and now he finally had a definitive answer from the Holy Ancestor's mouth.
As expected, that ancient Ancestor was the Holy Ancestor's Master.
Ye Tian said, "Rest assured, I am only looking for your Master to ask some questions, not to cause him trouble. After all, I don't even know him. There is no enmity between us, so there's no need to deal with him!"
"I need to contact my Master. If he is unwilling to see you, there is nothing I can do. After all, I do not know his exact location!"
The Holy Ancestor said.
"No problem!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Subsequently.
The Holy Ancestor began to contact his Master, waiting slowly.
Before long, the Holy Ancestor replied, "Master is willing to see you, but only through an incarnation!"
"That's fine!"
Ye Tian agreed.
At that moment.
An incarnation of an Ancestor appeared before Ye Tian. It took the form of a middle-aged man wearing a simple, ancient black robe. His aura was extremely powerful.
Through this incarnation, Ye Tian roughly judged the other party's strength. He had indeed reached the peak of the top-tier Ancestor realm. Although much weaker than Ye Tian, he was far stronger than the Holy Ancestor.
"Greetings, Ancestor of Myriad Laws!"
The Black-Robed Ancestor was polite.
"May I ask how to address your Excellency?"
Ye Tian said indifferently.
The Black-Robed Ancestor said, "Call me Baipo!"
"Ancestor Baipo, I would like to inquire about some matters. I wonder if that is possible?"
Ye Tian asked politely.
"No problem!"
Ancestor Baipo agreed.
Immediately, Ye Tian and Ancestor Baipo isolated themselves from the prying eyes of other Ancestors and began to communicate privately.
Ye Tian also knew that if he hadn't threatened the Holy Ancestor, this Ancestor Baipo would certainly not have appeared.
"Ancestor Baipo, I want to know what the Great Calamity is all about."
Ye Tian asked.
"You know about the Great Calamity as well?"
Ancestor Baipo was somewhat surprised.
"Indeed!"
Ye Tian nodded. "I learned of it from the Bone Race in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. The Bone Race came from the other side of the Gate. They stay in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land and can only enter the Dark Forbidden Zone during a Great Calamity. Currently, they dare not set foot in the Dark Forbidden Zone. Furthermore, based on the information I have, the Dark Forbidden Zone has experienced Great Calamities before. Forces like the Bone Race have invaded, yet no such information has been passed down in the Dark Forbidden Zone. I presume ancient Ancestors like you must know many secrets."
Ancestor Baipo said in great shock, "I didn't expect the Ancestor of Myriad Laws to know so much and even obtain information from the Bone Race. It truly surprises me. Some information can indeed be told to you. The Dark Forbidden Zone has indeed experienced Great Calamities. As far as I know, there have been three in total, and I have only experienced one."
"In terms of Domain Years, 120,000 Domain Years make one Domain Epoch. 108 Domain Epochs count as one Dark Era Year. After nine Dark Era Years, a Great Calamity arrives. We are currently in the third Dark Era Year. There are still six Dark Era Years left before the next Great Calamity."
As soon as Ancestor Baipo finished speaking, Ye Tian was startled and said, "Dark Holy Lands!"
"Exactly. Each time a Dark Holy Land is born, it represents the passing of one Dark Era Year. Three Dark Eras have passed since the last Great Calamity. Therefore, the Ancestor of Myriad Laws does not need to worry too much about the next Great Calamity."
Ancestor Baipo said with a smile.
"Then what is the deal with the Gate? Passing through the Gate allows one to enter other worlds similar to the Dark Forbidden Zone. In the Dark Forbidden Zone, one cannot break through to become a Supreme Ancestor. Ancestor Baipo should know about this, right?"
Ye Tian asked.
"I do indeed know!"
Ancestor Baipo nodded. "It seems you know this as well, which surprises me. Even among our batch of Ancestors who survived one Great Calamity, only a very few vaguely know a little about it."
Ye Tian shared the information he knew: "According to what I know, one cannot break through to a Supreme Ancestor in the Dark Forbidden Zone. Only by entering other worlds can one break through. However, if another world is not undergoing a Great Calamity, anyone entering it will be erased by that world's laws. One must wait until the other world enters a Great Calamity to cross over. Only then is there hope of reaching the Supreme Ancestor realm. I want to know one thing: does any Ancestor in the Dark Forbidden Zone control a Gate?"
"No!"
Ancestor Baipo said with certainty, "I can tell you for sure that no Ancestor in the Dark Forbidden Zone controls a Gate. After all, we have only gone through three Great Calamities, while some worlds have even survived ten or even a hundred. Compared to other worlds, the Dark Forbidden Zone is still too young and thus cannot compete with other powerful worlds. If we controlled a Gate, perhaps we would have hope of giving birth to a Supreme Ancestor. But based on the information I currently have, not a single Supreme Ancestor has been born yet. Of course, this is just what I know. Some Ancestors passed through unstable Gates and disappeared thereafter. Perhaps those Ancestors became Supreme Ancestors, but it is more likely they perished upon crossing."
"Unstable Gates? What are those?"
Ye Tian was curious.
Ancestor Baipo did not hide it; this was information that could be easily discovered, so there was no harm in telling Ye Tian.
Thus, he said, "In the Dark Forbidden Zone, shortly after every Dark Era, a Gate appears. This Gate only appears for a short time and then dissipates; it does not exist eternally. We have yet to find a way to make such a Gate permanent. Some Ancestors, unable to withstand the ravages of time and wishing to promote to Supreme Ancestor, would risk entering another world. If that world happened to be in a Great Calamity, they might luckily survive. But the probability is too low. I dare not gamble!"
"So, you also have no way to enter other worlds to pursue Supreme Ancestor combat power?"
Ye Tian frowned.
"Yes, no way!"
Ancestor Baipo shook his head. "We are too weak. If a force like the Bone Race were willing to share the method of establishing a permanent Gate, then we would have hope of becoming Supreme Ancestors. But they certainly wouldn't be willing. After all, once they hand over such a method, we would have a way to enter their world, potentially threatening their home world."
Ye Tian was somewhat disappointed.
Suddenly, Ye Tian asked again, "Do you know which force is the weakest—far weaker than the Bone Race—yet possesses a forbidden land in our Dark Forbidden Zone?"
"What do you intend to do?"
Ancestor Baipo was curious.
"Just tell me."
Ye Tian said.
Baipo, also fearing Ye Tian would cause trouble for his disciple, spoke: "According to what I know, the Bone Race is at a middle-tier level among these forces. The weakest force is called the Haitian Holy Sect. This is a sect of exotic beasts. They possess a forbidden land in our Dark Forbidden Zone called the Haitian Forbidden Land. However, it is no less dangerous than the Bone Sea Forbidden Land because this forbidden land is covered by seawater with powerful corrosive properties. Only the exotic beasts of the Haitian Holy Sect can survive in it. Once we Ancestors enter, our strength is reduced by more than half, and we cannot even sustain long-term combat. Furthermore, at least a thousand exotic beasts are stationed inside that forbidden land, with six or seven among them having strength comparable to top-tier Ancestors. According to my guesses, this force does not have many beasts comparable to the Supreme Ancestor realm—at most one or two."
"Thank you!"
Ye Tian thanked him.
