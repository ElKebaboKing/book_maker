Chapter 1563: The War Ends, Seclusion Once More!

Qiuye Source Ancestor, one of the three Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm, possessed strength far exceeding that of Tianfeng Source God. With the title of Source Ancestor, he stood above ordinary Source Gods.
Neither Tianfeng Source God nor the Nether Hall Master dared to act presumptuously in front of Qiuye Source Ancestor.
"As Supreme Beings, why are you slaughtering each other in the Tianlin Realm?"
Qiuye Source Ancestor questioned sternly.
Supreme Beings were not permitted to fight at will within the Eternal Heavens. Otherwise, if left unchecked, there was a risk of it evolving into something like the Yuanji Realm, which had turned into the Yuanji Abyss. One day, the Tianlin Realm would also become the Tianji Abyss.
As one of the three oldest Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm, Qiuye Source Ancestor naturally did not wish to see this happen.
"Qiuye Source Ancestor, Tianfeng Source God killed thirteen First-Class Invincibles of my Nether Hall, and even killed a Son of the Void!"
The Nether Hall Master stated.
Tianfeng Source God hurriedly said, "It was the Nether Hall that first moved against our Tianfeng Sea. I only acted in retaliation."
"Enough. I know a thing or two about your previous matters. Things have already reached this point. Regardless of what grudges you hold, let it end here today. As for the war between you, let it end as well, otherwise the impact will be too great!" Qiuye Source Ancestor commanded.
The Nether Hall Master was somewhat unwilling, but he still responded, "Yes!"
He did not dare to disagree, for Qiuye Source Ancestor possessed the strength to kill him.
Having become a Supreme Being, he stood nearly at the peak of the Source Sea; he did not wish to fall.
Tianfeng Source God naturally agreed as well. He had already wanted to end it, and besides, he was very happy to have caused the Nether Hall such heavy losses this time.
As for attacking Invincibles in the future, he would no longer do so, otherwise Qiuye Source Ancestor likely would not let him off.
...
Jinhe Heaven.
The Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea and the Nether Hall received the news and knew that both sides had ceased hostilities.
Thus, the Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea left Jinhe Heaven one by one and returned to the Tianlin Realm.
They had to return to Tianfeng Sea to submit their missions and claim their contribution points.
Ye Tian also submitted his missions. This time, he had slain many Invincibles of the Nether Hall, including several Third-Class Invincibles. Killing a single Third-Class Invincible rewarded 1 billion contribution points, and adding the bounties from other Invincibles...
In the end, Ye Tian received a reward of nearly ten billion contribution points.
It could be said that Ye Tian was now almost not lacking in contribution points, enough to purchase countless resources.
Not long after his return, the leader of Tianfeng Sea, the current Tianfeng Source God, summoned him.
"Tianfeng Source God, this newly promoted Supreme Being, is summoning me?"
Ye Tian was somewhat puzzled, but he still went.
Soon, he met Tianfeng Source God.
"Greetings, Leader!"
Ye Tian said respectfully.
Tianfeng Source God looked at Ye Tian and said with a smile, "Ye Tian, you are very good. I heard that you fought back and forth with the Nether Hall's Son of the Void, Luo Ge. Do you want to become a Son of the Void?"
Ye Tian hurriedly said, "Leader, I do not know much about the Sons of the Void, so I do not know how to answer."
"That is true." Tianfeng Source God also knew that many Invincibles did not understand the Sons of the Void, as it was merely a title.
Thus, he explained, "The title of Son of the Void is not evaluated by our organizations, but by the Void Source Hall."
Through Tianfeng Source God's explanation, Ye Tian finally gained a general understanding.
It turned out that in a mysterious place within the Source Sea, there was an infinitely grand temple—the Void Source Hall.
A Son of the Void was an evaluation obtained by passing the trials of the Void Source Hall. Once one became a Son of the Void, they would receive many benefits.
At this time, Tianfeng Source God spoke again, "The Source Sea is vast. There is more than just the Tianlin Realm; there are other Eternal Heavens as well. Within those Eternal Heavens, there are many Sons of the Void. The Void Source Hall can ignore the distances of the Source Sea and communicate with every Eternal Heaven. Once you become a Son of the Void, you can travel to other Eternal Heavens through the Void Source Hall in the future, saving countless amounts of time. Furthermore, more benefits can only be known once one is qualified as a Son of the Void."
"I want to participate in the trial, but not now!" Ye Tian said, "I have only recently been promoted to a Third-Class Invincible and still have room for improvement. If I go to participate now, I cannot guarantee a hundred percent chance of becoming a Son of the Void."
"Very well. When you wish to take the trial, come find me then!" Tianfeng Source God promised.
"Thank you, Leader!"
Ye Tian said gratefully.
Shortly after, he left Tianfeng Sea.
...
In the following years, Ye Tian began to cultivate diligently, striving to reach the Third-Class Invincible Limit, at which point he would participate in the Son of the Void trial.
As time passed, Ye Tian continued to create one Invincible Ancestral Technique after another. Although this did not increase his strength by much, every bit added up!
He also purchased many precious resources to assist in cultivating Wan Yuan Returning to the Void. His duplicated bodies spared no expense to perfect Wan Yuan Returning to the Void, collapsing an unknown number of times and consuming many resources to be condensed again and again.
After his strength increased, the resources required to duplicate a body also increased somewhat, but in reality, more of the resources came from the Source Sea.
Only after becoming a Source Sea lifeform did Ye Tian learn that while his duplicated bodies had previously been copied countless times without paying any price, where did that energy come from?
Now he knew that the energy came from the origin energy within the Source Sea.
However, after his strength grew, even if the duplication process drew energy from the Source Sea, it could not be condensed instantly; it still required some auxiliary resources.
Of course, compared to other Invincibles who possessed special clones, the price Ye Tian paid was practically nothing.
For other Invincibles with special clones, once a clone fell, the main body would also be severely injured, requiring endless years to recover. Moreover, an immense price was required to condense the clone again, with each condensation enough to consume a significant portion of their net worth.
Unknowingly, several hundred Great Tribulation Eras passed in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
And on this day, Ye Tian's Wan Yuan Returning to the Void finally reached the Fourth Layer Perfected Stage. His cultivation had also reached the Third-Class Invincible Limit, and he had mastered many Invincible Ancestral Techniques.
Compared to the past, Ye Tian's strength had grown significantly.
If he were to fight Luo Ge again, he could easily defeat him. However, killing Luo Ge would still be somewhat difficult. After all, at the level of a Third-Class Invincible, unless the strength gap was too vast, one could not be certain of a kill.
He had been able to slay other Third-Class Invincibles because those Third-Class Invincibles were too weak.
Those who could become a Son of the Void were themselves among the top existences of the Tianlin Realm's Third-Class Invincibles. For him to kill such a Third-Class Invincible was naturally fraught with difficulties.
