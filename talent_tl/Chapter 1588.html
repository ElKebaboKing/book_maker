Chapter 1587: The Four Seas Mirror, the Ancient Star Race's Action!

Inside Juemen's Void Sphere, there were five portions of eternal resources, which was quite a significant amount.
After all, Juemen himself had already refined many eternal resources to condense an Eternal Tribulation Body, and the other first-class Invincibles of the Crystal Gate also required eternal resources. Yet, in the end, five portions of eternal resources still remained, showing just how abundant the Crystal Gate's eternal resources were.
From this, one could imagine that the eternal resources in the Ancient Star Domain were simply too numerous.
Having obtained five portions of eternal resources this time, Ye Tian was now only one portion of eternal resources away from his goal.
However, his target couldn't be just one more portion; what if it was still not enough?
After all, he was only estimating and wasn't truly certain that he only needed that many eternal resources. Therefore, it wouldn't be wrong to prepare a few more portions.
Moreover, in the future, his relatives would have hope of breaking through to the Supreme Being realm, and they would also need eternal resources.
If he prepared more now, there would be enough for them when the time came.
To prevent this duplicated body from falling, he left the Ancient Star Domain once again to deliver these five portions of eternal resources into the hands of his main body.
Afterward, he returned to the Ancient Star Domain.

Ancient Star Domain, at the Ancient Star Race's clan meeting.
The ten great Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race had all gathered here. Their strength stood above all other Supreme Beings, making them the truly invincible existences of the Ancient Star Race.
This time, the ten great Supreme Beings focused their discussion on one matter: the recent frequent killings of the Ancient Star Race's first-class Invincibles. Although those invaded were only small factions of the Ancient Star Race, the frequency was far too high.
Furthermore, they hadn't killed many outside Invincibles in return.
If things continued to develop this way, the Ancient Star Race clansmen would fall into panic, and it would lead to even more outside Invincibles flooding into the Ancient Star Domain. This was not a good thing for either the Ancient Star Domain or the Ancient Star Race.
"Gu Chen, tell us, what should be done now? we cannot let the outside Invincibles continue their invasion like this. If we don't deter them, we might be in trouble if countless outside Invincibles flood in."
The speaker was Donghe, one of the ten great Supreme Beings.
Gu Chen was also one of the ten great Supreme Beings and was specifically the general person in charge of intercepting outside Invincibles.
Gu Chen pondered for a moment before speaking: "Once outside Invincibles hide themselves, it is very difficult for us to detect them. We can only lock onto them and perform a blockade the moment they take action, but that is often half a step too slow. Only those outside Invincibles with insufficient strength or those unlucky enough to run directly into our race's Supreme Beings can be killed. But this is not a solution. I propose using the clan-suppressing Eternal Source Item—the Four Seas Mirror!"
"Use the Four Seas Mirror?"
The other Supreme Beings frowned one by one, seemingly somewhat in disagreement.
Donghe said, "Gu Chen, if we use the Four Seas Mirror to shroud the entire Ancient Star Domain, it will rapidly consume the Four Seas Mirror's source. Moreover, we would need to maintain the Four Seas Mirror constantly to lock onto the positions of those outsiders. Even if we kill all those outside Invincibles, the Four Seas Mirror's source would likely be mostly exhausted and it would fall into a slumber. The loss would be too great!"
Gu Chen smiled and said, "Actually, it's not too much of a loss. These outside Invincibles are all quite wealthy. Even if they didn't bring many treasures in, killing them all can compensate for some of the losses. Many resources from the outside are not found in our Ancient Star Domain, and we can only plunder outside resources through this method. Additionally, if we can deter the outside practitioners, fewer Invincibles will enter the Ancient Star Domain, and we can enjoy peace for a long time."
Immediately, the ten great Supreme Beings expressed their opinions one after another, discussing the matter for a long time.
In the end, they still decided to activate the Four Seas Mirror.
Inside an ancient palace of the Ancient Star Race, Gu Chen, Donghe, and the others arrived. In the void here stood a black mirror tens of thousands of meters high.
This black mirror was the Four Seas Mirror, an Eternal Source Item belonging to the Ancient Star Race.
Any Eternal Source Item has a special effect, and the Four Seas Mirror's special effect was monitoring and locking on.
Gu Chen personally activated the Four Seas Mirror. Soon, the mirror released countless rays of light that slowly covered the entire Ancient Star Domain.
Rumble!!!!!
The Four Seas Mirror's source was rapidly consumed, and an infinitely vast image appeared upon its surface.
Soon, the Four Seas Mirror locked onto the outsiders one by one and displayed their locations.
"Donghe, lead the team yourself and kill these outsiders. I will stay here to maintain the Four Seas Mirror!" Gu Chen suggested.
"Alright!"
Donghe nodded.
Whoosh!
Donghe tore through the void and departed.
Soon, the Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race set out one by one. Since there were no Supreme Beings among the outsiders, the outcome was obvious.
On an unnamed star, a young master was drinking fine wine with an Ancient Star Race beauty in a restaurant.
Suddenly.
The young master's figure was frozen in place, and in the next moment, his body crumbled.
At that moment, his original aura emanated; he was not of the Ancient Star Race but an Invincible from the outside.
Unfortunately, though he had hidden well and even changed his aura, he couldn't hide from the Four Seas Mirror. Having been locked onto by the Four Seas Mirror, a Supreme Being had arrived and instantly obliterated him.
This scene played out in various places across the Ancient Star Domain as countless outsiders were struck down.
In the starry sky of the Ancient Star Domain.
A powerful outside first-class Invincible was being hunted by an Ancient Star Race Supreme Being. If not for his powerful life-saving abilities and the treasures bestowed by his elders, he truly wouldn't have been able to escape.
Suddenly.
Another Supreme Being intercepted him from ahead.
"Damn it, I must leave!"
This first-class Invincible tore a golden piece of paper. In the next moment, a giant mouth swallowed him, and he escaped to the outside world, vanishing without a trace.
"That outsider escaped. Forget it, let's continue hunting the other outside Invincibles!"
The Ancient Star Race Supreme Being said in a low voice.
--------------------------
"Someone is observing me?"
Ye Tian's expression shifted as he sensed something was wrong.
This situation felt very much like being locked onto by something.
If he hadn't guessed wrong, he had been discovered.
The next moment.
Rumble!!!!
A Supreme Being tore through the void and arrived. Upon appearing, he directly unleashed an Invincible Technique to kill Ye Tian.
Clearly, the opponent had come prepared.
"Since I'm exposed, I won't hide anymore!"
Ye Tian struck with a Void-Shattering Supreme Technique, directly hitting the Supreme Being and sending him flying.
That Supreme Being was sent flying an unknown distance. His physical body cracked, and his internal organs were shattered. Although it wasn't a major injury, for a Supreme Being to be injured by an Invincible in a single strike was simply too incredible.
After injuring this Supreme Being, Ye Tian's gaze turned toward a palace on the star. This was the headquarters of the most powerful force on this star, and there were bound to be eternal resources here.
Previously, he had wanted to slowly plot for eternal resources, but now there was no time.
He would just snatch them directly!.
