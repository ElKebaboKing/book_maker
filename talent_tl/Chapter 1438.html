Chapter 1437: Killing Over a Thousand Invincible Domain Ancestors in One Strike!
!
Qingyuan Holy Land was located in the Qingyuan Domain of the Dark Holy Territory.
The Dark Holy Territory was vast and divided into many domains. For example, the Battle Gate was located in the Battle Domain, and the Battle Ancestor World was... the center of the Battle Domain.
The Qingyuan Domain was the territory of the Qingyuan Holy Land, one of the smaller domains. Many subordinate forces were under the Qingyuan Holy Land, including numerous city-level forces situated within the Qingyuan Domain.
On this day.
Ye Tian’s duplicated body arrived at the Qingyuan Domain. He crossed several giant cities and reached the Qingyuan Holy World.
At this time.
The Qingyuan Holy World was very lively. At the various entrances to the Holy World, practitioners were flying in and out in a bustling scene.
When Ye Tian appeared there, he was immediately noticed, but no one paid him any mind.
After all, many people came to the Qingyuan Holy World. The Qingyuan Holy Land did not completely blockade the world, so they simply assumed Ye Tian was like everyone else.
Suddenly! A long spear appeared in Ye Tian’s hand, and he thrust it fiercely toward the Qingyuan Holy World.
Boom!!!! The Qingyuan Holy World was actually pierced through by Ye Tian’s spear. This strike even penetrated deep into the interior of the Qingyuan Holy World, easily obliterating a starry sky continent within.
Furthermore, countless regions of the Qingyuan Holy World began to crack and collapse; this was all the impact of Ye Tian’s single strike.
"What on earth is going on?"
One by one, the Invincible Domain Ancestors of the Qingyuan Holy World were startled. Even Holy Lord Qingfeng of the Qingyuan Holy World was alarmed. Their divine senses swept across the void, extending outside the Qingyuan Holy World to see Ye Tian, who had attacked.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor, it's you!"
Holy Lord Qingfeng appeared, followed by a group of Invincible Domain Ancestors—all of whom belonged to the Qingyuan Holy Land.
One Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, over a dozen Third-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, dozens of Fourth-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, Fifth-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors... a dense crowd of Invincible Domain Ancestors formed a surging and overwhelming aura.
Ye Tian took a quick count; there were about a thousand Invincible Domain Ancestors. Even though the Qingyuan Holy Land had declined, it still possessed many Invincible Domain Ancestors.
This was the foundation of a Holy Land! "They are only slightly more numerous than the Invincible Domain Ancestors of our human race!"
Ye Tian didn't think much of these Invincible Domain Ancestors at all. Despite there being over a thousand of them, if he chose to act, he could slaughter them all in an instant.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor, although you are an Invincible Domain Ancestor of the Battle Gate, one of the Ten Great Holy Gates, isn't it unseemly to provoke our Qingyuan Holy Land like this?"
Holy Lord Qingfeng questioned him.
In reality, he knew exactly why Ye Tian had come, but he could not back down.
"Holy Lord Qingfeng, you previously dispatched two Invincible Domain Ancestors to our human race to seize a Saint-level mineral vein and even asked the Weeping Blood Emperor to save them. Do you really think I wouldn't dare to provoke your Qingyuan Holy Land?"
Ye Tian said coldly.
"Nonsense, I never did such a thing!"
Holy Lord Qingfeng flatly denied it.
"This is your Qingyuan Holy Land's Qingyuan World Bead. Back then, that ancestor used this treasure to intercept the space where the Saint-level mineral vein was located.
The Qingyuan World Bead is a sacred object of your Qingyuan Holy Land and is only held by you, the Holy Lord. Does that not prove the matter?"
Ye Tian held up the Qingyuan World Bead and questioned him again.
Holy Lord Qingfeng did not panic at all and quickly explained: "I did indeed bestow the Qingyuan World Bead upon a talented disciple for his use. I didn't expect that disciple to be killed outside. It seems the murderer who killed our Qingyuan Holy Land disciple was the one who tried to steal the human race's Saint-level mineral vein.
I thank the Wanfa Domain Ancestor for returning the Qingyuan World Bead. I will certainly be more careful next time!"
"Holy Lord Qingfeng, so you're going to deny it to the death?"
Ye Tian sneered.
"I didn't do it, so what is there to admit?"
Holy Lord Qingfeng said.
"Holy Lord Qingfeng, do you know what the truth of this world is?"
Ye Tian asked.
"What?"
Holy Lord Qingfeng felt a sense of foreboding.
Ye Tian sneered: "It is that strength is everything. I don't need you to admit it. I have presented the evidence, and that is enough. As for your rebuttals, they are meaningless.
Because I am stronger than you, and the Battle Gate behind me is my backing.
Today, I give your Qingfeng Holy Land a choice: execute the participants of that plan—remember, all participants and executors—and I shall leave today!"
Holy Lord Qingfeng was furious: "Wanfa Domain Ancestor, do not go too far! No matter how weak our Qingyuan Holy Land is, it is still a Holy Land. A mere Invincible Domain Ancestor like you has no right to threaten us!"
The reason he was so furious was that he himself was the executor.
How could he possibly execute himself? "Then you shall die!"
Ye Tian struck.
Holy Lord Qingfeng was not slow either, immediately sending a divine transmission for all the Invincible Domain Ancestors to attack.
Boom!!!!! Holy Lord Qingfeng directly activated the sacred object of the Qingyuan Holy Land—the Qingyuan Void-Suppressing Tower!
This was a top-tier Saint Weapon formed by the fusion of dozens of top-tier sacred objects.
Boom!!!! The Qingyuan Void-Suppressing Tower pressed down. Combined with the simultaneous attacks of so many Invincible Domain Ancestors, including Holy Lord Qingfeng, a Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, and over a dozen Third-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, it could definitely suppress a First-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
And Ye Tian had only recently broken through to become an Invincible Domain Ancestor; could he possibly rival a First-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor? "Mere rabble!"
Ye Tian thrust his spear.
With a thunderous boom, the Qingyuan Void-Suppressing Tower was directly knocked flying by Ye Tian’s spear.
Many Invincible Domain Ancestors coughed up blood as they were slammed against the world barrier of the Qingyuan Holy World. Even Holy Lord Qingfeng was sent flying hundreds of millions of miles, vomiting blood incessantly.
They couldn't even withstand a single ordinary strike.
"Quickly, retreat into the Holy World!"
Holy Lord Qingfeng transmitted urgently.
Right at that moment, Ye Tian unleashed an Ancestral Technique—Golden Silk!
Streaks of golden light pierced through the void, easily skewering the Invincible Domain Ancestors one by one.
Every Invincible Domain Ancestor hit had their physical body disintegrated, falling and dying instantly. No matter how many life-saving treasures they had, they could not preserve their lives.
In the blink of an eye, of the over a thousand Invincible Domain Ancestors, only a dozen or so remained. They were slightly faster and managed to enter the Qingyuan Holy World in time.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor, you have destroyed the foundation of our Qingyuan Holy Land!!!!"
Holy Lord Qingfeng shrieked.
"If you hadn't plotted against me and killed the Domain Ancestors of our human race, today's events would never have happened!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
"We request the Supreme Elder to emerge from seclusion!"
"We request the Supreme Elder to emerge from seclusion!"
"We request the Supreme Elder to emerge from seclusion!"
The surviving Invincible Domain Ancestors of the Qingyuan Holy Land roared, their voices shaking the Qingyuan Holy World.
The next moment.
The aura of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor permeated the air. At this moment, countless practitioners looked toward the depths of the Qingyuan Holy Land.
The Supreme Elder of the Qingyuan Holy Land, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, Saint Ancestor Jinchen, had appeared!
