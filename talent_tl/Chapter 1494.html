Chapter 1493: One Punch Heavily Injures the Holy Ancestor
!
The Bone Emperor was hesitant; he was indeed afraid.
He could not guard this place forever. Not only would it be a great burden on himself, but it would also be a heavy burden on the Bone Sea Forbidden Ground.
Guarding it for a few thousand years was fine, but any longer would be dangerous.
And a mere few thousand years was practically nothing to beings of their level. Once he left and Ye Tian came to cause trouble, the Bone Sea Forbidden Ground would not only suffer heavy losses, but if Ye Tian managed to destroy the Gate, it would be disastrous.
Although a top-tier Ancestor might not necessarily be able to destroy the Gate—the probability was very, very low—the Bone Emperor did not dare to gamble.
Furthermore, if the Bone Race members in the Bone Sea Forbidden Ground were all killed, even if the Gate wasn't destroyed, it would still mean the destruction of the Bone Race's foundation in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Therefore, he absolutely could not completely offend Ye Tian.
"Fine, I agree to this deal!"
The Bone Emperor agreed.
He had no choice but to agree, otherwise, the Bone Race would suffer heavy losses.
As for whether Ye Tian would reach the level of an Ultimate Ancestor after obtaining the method to do so, he wasn't worried. Reaching the level of an Ultimate Ancestor was not that simple; it wasn't something that could be achieved just by having monstrous talent.
Immediately, the two made a Life Oath. Although it might not be absolutely binding, it at least had some effect.
Moreover, the Bone Emperor did not dare to violate it, otherwise, the Bone Race would not be able to withstand Ye Tian's desperate retaliation.
Afterward.
Ye Tian returned to the Profound Void Holy Realm and brought back the Ultimate Emperor Artifact.
This item wasn't of much use to him, so it was better to use it for the trade.
"This Ultimate Emperor Artifact is yours!"
Ye Tian tossed the Ultimate Emperor Artifact over.
At the same time, the Bone Emperor handed over a bead, which contained the secrets of how a top-tier Ancestor could break through to the level of an Ultimate Ancestor.
"Pleasure doing business with you!"
Ye Tian smiled.
"Hmph!"
The Bone Emperor snorted coldly, not feeling pleased at all.
Whoosh! Ye Tian left the Bone Sea Forbidden Ground and likely wouldn't return in the future, while the Bone Emperor also passed through the Gate and left the Bone Sea Forbidden Ground.
Back in the Profound Void Ancestral Realm.
Ye Tian carefully browsed the information recorded in the bead and gradually understood the key to breaking through to the Ultimate Ancestor level.
Whether it was an Ultimate Ancestor or a Bone Emperor, they actually belonged to the same level; it was just that the names were different.
A top-tier Ancestor was almost the limit of the world within the Dark Forbidden Domain. No matter how one practiced, it was impossible to break through; this was the restriction of the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Never mind that Ye Tian had comprehended many Ultimate Ancestral Techniques; even if he created hundreds or thousands of them, he still wouldn't be able to break through.
No one could break the shackles within a single world.
However, if one left the Dark Forbidden Domain, they could break the shackles and become an Ultimate Ancestor.
It was just like how if a Bone King of the Bone Race entered the Dark Forbidden Domain during its Great Catastrophe, they would have a chance to break the shackles and become a Bone Emperor.
In the endlessly distant past, the Bone Race had given birth to several Bone Emperors within the Dark Forbidden Domain.
New Ancestors like Ye Tian did not know about such things, and neither did the Realm Ancestor or War Ancestor, but... those ancient Ancestors who were in deep slumber knew.
But outside of the Great Catastrophe period, even a Bone Emperor would not dare to step into the Dark Forbidden Domain, otherwise, they would surely die.
Similarly, if Ye Tian dared to step into a world similar to the Dark Forbidden Domain, he would be instantly obliterated. He could only go there when that other world entered its own Great Catastrophe.
But to go there, one must use a Gate.
Controlling a Gate was the only way to travel to other worlds.
The Bone Race controlled a Gate. How they obtained it was unknown; only the Bone Emperors of the Bone Race would know.
"To control a Gate and also know when the world on the other side enters a Great Catastrophe before being able to cross over... both of these conditions are extremely harsh!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Furthermore, to make the breakthrough, one's true body must go in person.
The reason the Bone Race dared to reveal this secret was that they weren't afraid of him using their Gate to break through.
After all, if his true body went over, it would be exactly what the Bone Race wanted. At that time, the Bone Race would definitely dispatch multiple Bone Emperors to kill him.
Besides, if the Bone Race's side hadn't entered a Great Catastrophe, he wouldn't dare to go over. Going over would mean... death.
"Based on the Bone Race's situation, the six forbidden grounds of the Dark Forbidden Domain should be bases established by six powerful worlds. Once the Dark Forbidden Domain enters a Great Catastrophe, the experts of these forces will enter the Dark Forbidden Domain to snatch resources and attempt to break through to the level of an Ultimate Ancestor."
Ye Tian speculated.
However, based on the current situation, it would likely be a very long time before the Dark Forbidden Domain entered its next Great Catastrophe.
He couldn't stand being unable to improve his cultivation and strength for such a long period.
"I must find an ancient Ancestor to ask about the situation!"
Ye Tian murmured.
But who should he look for? He didn't know a single ancient Ancestor, and he didn't even know where they were.
The Dark Forbidden Domain was so vast; it wasn't just a small place like the Bone Sea Forbidden Ground. If a top-tier Ancestor hid themselves in slumber, he wouldn't be able to find them.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian thought of the Holy Ancestor.
The Holy Ancestor was very likely the disciple of an ancient Ancestor and definitely had a way to contact that ancient Ancestor.
In the past, the Holy Ancestor's strength was not much different from his.
But now, Ye Tian didn't think much of the Holy Ancestor's strength; he could kill him within a few moves.
"Then I'll take a trip to the Dark Ancestral Realm!"
Ye Tian's duplicated body tore through time and space, instantly arriving at the Dark Holy Domain.
Immediately, the Realm Ancestor, War Ancestor, and other Ancestors sensed him.
"Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws!"
"Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws!"
One by one, the Ancestors greeted Ye Tian.
"I'm looking for the Holy Ancestor!"
Ye Tian responded to these Ancestors.
"Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws, why are you looking for the Holy Ancestor?"
The Realm Ancestor was curious.
"To ask him about something!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
The Realm Ancestor was curious but didn't ask further.
At this moment, Ye Tian arrived outside the Dark Ancestral Realm.
The Holy Ancestor naturally knew Ye Tian had arrived, but he felt an ill omen, as if he were already in a dangerous situation.
"Holy Ancestor, come out!"
Ye Tian said.
BOOM!!!! The Holy Ancestor walked out of the Dark Ancestral Realm, his gaze fixed on the Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws, speaking with some wariness: "Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws, what do you want with me?"
"I want to find the ancient Ancestor behind you. Tell me where he is."
Ye Tian questioned in a commanding tone.
"What Ancestor behind me? I don't know what you mean, Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws."
The Holy Ancestor shook his head.
"Speak, or die!"
Ye Tian threatened.
"Die?"
The Holy Ancestor said coldly, "Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws, even if you have a top-tier Ancestor clone plus your true body, you can't do anything to me even if you work together!"
"Is that so?"
Ye Tian struck.
He threw a single punch, using nearly eighty percent of his strength.
The Holy Ancestor's expression changed drastically. An Innate Ancestral Artifact-level defensive armor appeared on his body, and he exerted his full strength to perform an Ultimate Ancestral Technique to resist Ye Tian.
However, it was useless!!! CRACK!!!!!! Under the punch, the Innate Ancestral Artifact cracked, and the Holy Ancestor's physical body was nearly blown apart. The terrifying power annihilated countless amounts of his life force.
With this one punch, the Holy Ancestor was heavily injured and on the verge of death! At the same time, the entire Dark Ancestral Realm cracked, and countless living beings were annihilated by the shockwaves of the strike.
It could be said that the Dark Holy Sect was heavily damaged by the aftershocks and was on the verge of being wiped out.
At this moment.
The Realm Ancestor was shocked, and the Ancestors who were secretly watching were also dumbfounded.
