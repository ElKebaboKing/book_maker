Chapter 1568: Eternal Source Object: Sun of All Things!

Although Ye Tian, upon becoming a Void God Son, could not view the data of other Void God Sons, he was aware of their total number.
In the Void Source Pavilion, it wasn't that there were no Void God Sons; rather, Ordinary Void Sons simply lacked the qualifications to know their identities. Furthermore, many Void God Sons were too ancient and had no need for the Void Source Monument, leading other Void Sons to believe the Void Source Pavilion had no Void God Sons.
"Thirty-nine Void God Sons!"
Ye Tian checked and was somewhat surprised.
It had to be said that the number of Void God Sons was quite significant, but when considering that this was the total number from the entire Source Sea, it wasn't many. It likely required the birth of multiple Eternal Heaven and Earths to produce a single Void God Son.
Afterward, he checked the data for the Void Sons.
The number of Void Sons was vast, totaling 59,813 people—nearly sixty thousand Void Sons.
Had he not become a Void God Son, it would have been difficult for Ye Tian to imagine that there were so many Void Sons within the Source Sea.
"Investigate Autumn Leaf Source God!"
Ye Tian exercised his authority to investigate the Autumn Leaf Source God.
Soon, he found the results: the Autumn Leaf Source God was indeed a Void Son and had not become a Void God Son.
Clearly, although the Autumn Leaf Source God had condensed a Void Source Body, the flaws in that Void Source Body were somewhat significant. Combined with a foundation that wasn't particularly profound, this prevented him from passing the second floor of the Void Tower, though he was likely very close to the level of a Void God Son.
...
Source Sea, Yuanji Abyss.
A first-class Invincible was cultivating bitterly on a certain level when he suddenly opened his eyes.
"Another Void God Son has actually been born. How long has it been since the last one? I remember I was the thirty-sixth Void God Son, and in the 300,000 Kalpa Eras since then, not a single Void God Son had been born."
The man murmured.
At the same time, Void God Sons from all corners of the Source Sea learned that another Void God Son had emerged.
However, they did not know the identity or the realm of this Void God Son, making any attempt to investigate extremely difficult.
Of course, they were in no hurry to know his identity and saw no need to investigate.
Meanwhile, back at the Void Source Pavilion.
Ye Tian also began selecting his Source Artifact and Eternal Source Object. These were his rewards; although he wasn't in a rush to take them, there was no reason to wait.
For the Source Artifact, Ye Tian did not plan to use a defensive one. While defensive Source Artifacts provided excellent survival capabilities, he already possessed three life-saving opportunities, and even a Supreme Being could not kill him. Thus, there was no need to choose a defensive Source Artifact.
As for offensive Source Artifacts, while they were very good, Ye Tian's current cultivation had not yet reached the Supreme Being realm. While an offensive Source Artifact would help, it wouldn't multiply his strength several times over. Furthermore, he wouldn't let his duplicated bodies carry an offensive Source Artifact into battle; if a duplicated body fell, the Source Artifact would be lost.
Considering all these factors, Ye Tian decided to choose an escape-type Source Artifact.
This way, if he truly encountered danger in the future, his main body might not even need to use a life-saving opportunity and could instead escape directly using the artifact.
"Artifact Spirit, filter for escape-type Source Artifacts!"
Ye Tian commanded.
*Whoosh!!!*
Projections of various Source Artifacts appeared before Ye Tian. There were roughly dozens of escape-type Source Artifacts here.
Golden Thunder Source Wings: Instantly increases speed by dozens of times, ignores seals. The flaw is the extreme consumption of power; after use, there is a period of weakness.
Black Aurora Shuttle: Increases speed by a hundred times, ignores seals, low consumption, but the direction cannot be controlled.
Phantom Thousand Machines: Forms countless shadows that enter different space-times, making it very difficult for enemies to discover the true body.
...
Ye Tian looked at the artifacts one by one, feeling incredibly tempted.
If all these Source Artifacts were his, his survival capabilities would be terrifying; even a Supreme Being with many tricks would find it impossible to kill him.
However, he could only choose one.
After picking for a long time, Ye Tian chose the Black Aurora Shuttle.
Although its direction could not be controlled and it could only carry him away in one direction, as long as he could escape, he would have enough time to activate his Void God Son Badge and cross the vast distance of the Source Sea to return to the Void Source Pavilion.
Therefore, the Black Aurora Shuttle was more important and the most suitable for him.
"Artifact Spirit, I choose the Black Aurora Shuttle!"
Ye Tian made his choice.
*Hum!*
The projection of the Black Aurora Shuttle became increasingly solid until the actual artifact, resembling a black shuttle, fell into Ye Tian's hand, becoming his Source Artifact.
Next, Ye Tian began selecting an Eternal Source Object.
The Void Source Pavilion's Artifact Spirit listed many Eternal Source Objects, but they were all ranked after the top one thousand. Their value could not compare to the Xumi Golden Mountain.
By now, he knew that every Eternal Source Object possessed a certain unique value. For instance, the Xumi Golden Mountain could produce Xumi Golden Fruits, and the Soul Green Gold of the Heavenly Wind Sea could temper soul power and enhance soul strength.
Ye Tian intended to pick an Eternal Source Object that could increase the strength of the human race, as only then could the race become truly powerful.
Suddenly.
An Eternal Source Object caught Ye Tian's attention.
Eternal Source Object: Sun of All Things!
On the surface, this Eternal Source Object appeared to be just a sun, but it was a quite extraordinary object. It constantly radiated the Sun Power of All Things, which could slowly alter the bloodline, physical body, and potential of surrounding living beings. However, it was only helpful to weak beings and had almost no effect on those at the Ancestor level or above.
Furthermore, the Sun of All Things could not produce any good items; treasures like the Xumi Golden Fruit were out of the question.
Moreover, because the Sun of All Things constantly radiated power, it consumed its own energy. It required frequent energy replenishment; otherwise, after a long period, it would fall into dormancy and become an ordinary sun.
Because of this, the Sun of All Things was relatively average among Eternal Source Objects, ranking only 3,987th.
However, for a race, the Sun of All Things was a supreme treasure that far surpassed other Eternal Source Objects.
"Artifact Spirit, I choose the Sun of All Things!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
With the Sun of All Things, the human race's potential and life essence would likely rival the powerful races of the Eternal Heaven and Earths in the future.
By then, after a long passage of time, the human race would likely produce many Invincibles and truly become a powerful race capable of marching into the Source Sea, rather than just an ordinary race of a foreign heaven and earth.
For Ye Tian, the value of the Sun of All Things was enough to surpass the Eternal Source Object Xumi Golden Mountain.
