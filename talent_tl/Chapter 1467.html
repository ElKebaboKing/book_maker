Chapter 1466: The Birth of the Primordial Ancestral Realm, About to Impact the Ancestor Realm!
One hundred billion years, one hundred billion years, passed just like that.
Over the long years, the overall strength of the Dark Soul Domain grew rapidly, but the growth rate of Supreme-level Soul Ancestors became slower and slower. Later on, it was difficult for even one Supreme-level Soul Ancestor to be born in ten billion years. Additionally, Supreme-level Soul Ancestors fought among themselves, and occasionally one or two would fall, causing the number of Supreme-level Soul Ancestors in the Dark Soul Domain to stabilize at a certain figure, occasionally dropping by one or two.
With the help of a nearly invincible Supreme-level Soul Ancestor like Ye Tian, the strength of the human race developed very quickly. With sufficient resources, the human race produced a large number of Invincible Domain Ancestors and even ten Supreme-level Soul Ancestors. This was an opportunity for the human race.
But after the birth of ten Supreme-level Soul Ancestors, it became difficult for the human race to produce more. This was not only related to the end of the Dark Soul Domain's benefits but also to providence.
For the same force, once a dozen or so Supreme-level Soul Ancestors have been born, it becomes very difficult to continue producing more.
Of course, if there are enough resources and opportunities, more Supreme-level Soul Ancestors can still be born, but it will definitely take a long time.
At this time.
Ye Tian had already fully comprehended the Space Ancestor Law and could step into the Ancestor Realm at any time with its help.
Besides him, the strongest Supreme-level Soul Ancestor in the Dark Soul Domain was a mid-stage Supreme-level Soul Ancestor, a native resident, who was far from being Ye Tian's match and was like an ant in Ye Tian's eyes.
"It's coming soon. The one trillion-year mark is approaching. At that time, the Dark Soul Domain will no longer be able to restrict Supreme-level Domain Ancestors from entering. Moreover, the first Ancestral Realm will be born, and a large number of innate items will appear—specifically, innate items of the Ancestral Realm.
Furthermore, the first Ancestral Realm born in the Dark Holy Land is... the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
When the Primordial Ancestral Realm is born, the Primordial Ancestral Spring will inevitably appear. Soaking in the Primordial Ancestral Spring will rapidly increase one's cultivation and even allow a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor's body to be tempered a second time. This is a truly great opportunity!"
Ye Tian murmured.
At that time, his main body could also enjoy the opportunities of the Primordial Ancestral Realm, allowing his Supreme Domain Body to undergo a second transformation. This was the only chance in the Dark Holy Land; Ancestral Realms born afterward would no longer produce the Primordial Ancestral Spring.
In the distant past, when the Dark Holy Domain was first born, the Primordial Ancestral Realm was shattered. But even now, the Ancestors still craved the opportunities of the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
It was clear how precious the birth of the Primordial Ancestral Realm was; countless Supreme-level Domain Ancestors would fight for it.
Ye Tian could not deny that his strength was indeed very powerful, but that didn't mean the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of other forces were weak.
Various major forces had hidden their strength. In the Silent Spacetime, those Supreme-level Domain Ancestors didn't seem to have any monstrously talented individuals, but who knew if there were any truly exceptional ones? Moreover, if there were peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors who had long reached the level where they could become Ancestors but had been refraining from breaking through, once they entered the Dark Soul Domain, they might immediately break through to become Ancestors. In this way, they would be confident in controlling the Dark Soul Domain and even preventing other Supreme-level Soul Ancestors from impacting the Ancestor Realm in the future.
Back then, the Dark Soul Domain gave birth to the Saint.
Additionally, it was the first time the Dark Soul Domain learned that another Dark Holy Land would be born outside the Dark Destruction Domain. They were completely unprepared, and the Dark Holy Domain rose.
But this time is different. The Dark Destruction Domain and the Dark Holy Domain are fully prepared, vowing to take over the Dark Soul Domain. Therefore, it is almost unrealistic for the native residents of the Dark Soul Domain to want to be the first to give birth to an Ancestor and completely control the Dark Soul Domain.
...."The major forces really do have peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors who can become Ancestors very quickly, but it will be difficult for them to achieve that in the Dark Soul Domain; it will take quite some time. After all, they are Domain Ancestors, not Soul Ancestors.
But my duplicate body is different; it is a Supreme-level Soul Ancestor. Therefore, becoming an Ancestor in the Dark Soul Domain will be many times easier!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Soon, the one trillion-year deadline was approaching.
Outside the Dark Soul Domain.
A large number of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors arrived, and even some unfamiliar Supreme-level Domain Ancestors appeared, clearly the hidden trump cards of various major forces.
Some of these Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were too ancient, some even older than the Ancestors themselves. Each of their auras was incredibly terrifying. Clearly, they had all formed Ancestral Bodies and cultivated them to a high level, and they were terrifying existences who mastered powerful Ancestral Techniques.
As a peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, Ye Tian naturally had to enter the Dark Soul Domain as well.
It could be said that almost all Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were going to enter the Dark Soul Domain to compete for this opportunity.
One could imagine that the Primordial Ancestral Realm would likely fall into a terrifying war this time, which might even cause the Primordial Ancestral Realm of the Dark Soul Domain to crack.
This day.
"It's here!"
Ye Tian said in a low voice.
Boom!!!! The Dark Soul Domain underwent a transformation, and the rules changed dramatically.
At the same time, in the central region of the Dark Soul Domain, an incredibly massive world was born in one location.
This world was the Primordial Ancestral Realm! As soon as the Primordial Ancestral Realm was born, many Supreme Soul Ancestors of the Dark Soul Domain descended here. Ye Tian's duplicate body, Soul Ancestor Qing Ye, also arrived here.
Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh! One by one, Supreme-level Domain Ancestors from the outside world stepped into the Dark Soul Domain.
Many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors exuded the aura of the peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor stage, far surpassing ordinary Supreme-level Soul Ancestors.
Moreover, these Supreme-level Soul Ancestors had cultivated for a short time and had not mastered Ancestral Techniques or Ancestral Bodies, so they were naturally far from being able to contend with these ancient peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
For a moment, many Supreme-level Soul Ancestors in the Dark Soul Domain were terrified, fearing they would be wiped out.
From the birth of the Primordial Ancestral Realm to the birth of the Primordial Ancestral Spring, some time was needed in between.
Therefore, this period was an opportunity for the Supreme-level Soul Ancestors to increase their strength.
Consequently, many Supreme-level Soul Ancestors instantly fled into the Primordial Ancestral Realm and hid, searching for innate items that could help them quickly increase their strength.
Ye Tian's main body also arrived at the Primordial Ancestral Realm and began searching for various innate items.
Meanwhile, his duplicate body began to impact the Ancestor Realm.
At this time, the benefits of being the first to reach the Ancestor Realm were too great; there was no need to wait for the birth of the Primordial Ancestral Spring.
He believed that there were likely other peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors also preparing to impact the Ancestor Realm.
