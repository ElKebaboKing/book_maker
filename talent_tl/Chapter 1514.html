Chapter 1513: Twelve Supreme Ancestral Techniques!

Nine supreme ancestral techniques—this number was an absolutely staggering figure for many supreme-level existences.
Generally, even a third-tier Supreme Primordial Ancestor would be unable to master nine supreme ancestral techniques.
Under normal circumstances, once one's cultivation reached a point where it was difficult to improve further, mastering four or five supreme ancestral techniques would be equivalent to the third tier of a Supreme Primordial Ancestor.
Yet, Ye Tian had mastered a full nine supreme ancestral techniques; this number was truly terrifying.
In the years that followed, besides refining countless resources to accumulate cultivation, Ye Tian spent the rest of his time comprehending and creating supreme ancestral techniques.
Long years passed, and one domain epoch after another went by.
Finally, the Dark Forbidden Domain passed its seventh Dark Era.
By this time, Ye Tian's strength had surged. Not only had his cultivation reached a level where further progress was difficult, but he had also mastered a total of twelve supreme ancestral techniques.
Twelve supreme ancestral techniques allowed Ye Tian's strength to far surpass that of an ordinary third-tier Supreme Primordial Ancestor, reaching an incredible level, though he was still definitely no match for an Invincible Ancestor.
"It's becoming harder and harder to fuse ultimate ancestral techniques that are compatible with the Sacred Domain and the Bone Clan worlds. It's taking more and more time. Now, creating just one more supreme ancestral technique would take at least an entire Dark Era, and there are only two Dark Eras left until the Great Calamity."
Ye Tian frowned.
Therefore, he could no longer continue practicing in such a step-by-step manner.
"I must obtain Source Rule Calamity Crystals from other foreign worlds!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
However, Source Rule Calamity Crystals were incredibly precious; how could other factions possibly trade them to him?
Even if he traded item for item, exchanging one Source Rule Calamity Crystal for another, it wouldn't be easy. After all, no faction wanted to see another grow stronger.
Those factions would rather slowly collect Source Rule Calamity Crystals over long ages than trade them away.
Furthermore, Source Rule Calamity Crystals would eventually be consumed; they couldn't be used indefinitely.
Additionally, once experts from many factions obtained a Source Rule Calamity Crystal, they might refine it directly to increase their cultivation instead of using it for comprehension.
"Trading with the Bone Clan again probably won't work. Their world's Source Rule Calamity Crystals are useless to me now, and even if they have crystals from other foreign worlds, they won't trade them. If I ask, they'll likely just say they don't have any," Ye Tian thought.
He thought for a moment and prepared to trade with other Nameless-grade or Named-grade foreign powers in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
If these factions were unwilling, he would destroy their forbidden lands and their gates.
There were dozens of forbidden lands in the Dark Forbidden Domain, which meant there were dozens of foreign powers. Factions like the Bone Clan were ranked relatively high.
There were also many foreign powers behind forbidden lands that were only equivalent to the level of the Sea Heaven Sacred Sect. He could easily threaten them.
Of course, these weaker factions might not necessarily have Source Rule Calamity Crystals; it would require a bit of luck.
"If they don't have Source Rule Calamity Crystals, then I'll just rob them of some resources. I'm sure they'll be willing to give them up!" Ye Tian said coldly.
......
Golden Sun River Forbidden Land.
This was a base established by a foreign power called the Golden Sun River Palace. It resembled a massive river and was one of the bottom ten among the dozens of forbidden lands in the Dark Forbidden Domain. It wasn't very dangerous, and even top-tier ancestors could enter and leave safely.
This forbidden land produced a treasure called Golden Sun River Beads, which were one of the treasures born from the collision of rules.
Any base established by a foreign power would produce some treasures under the collision of rules between two different worlds. Exactly what treasures would be born depended on luck.
Golden Sun River Beads could be used to restore energy and heal wounds, making them decent items.
On this day.
Ye Tian arrived at the Golden Sun River Forbidden Land, but he had no interest in the Golden Sun River Beads. He was here for the Golden Sun River Palace.
Boom!!!
Ye Tian stepped inside, causing a disturbance in the rules.
Immediately, the experts of the Golden Sun River Palace took notice and launched an attack on Ye Tian at once.
Hands covered in scales reached out across the void, striking toward Ye Tian.
Hmph!
Ye Tian snorted coldly, and a terrifying force directly shattered those hands.
"Get lost!!!"
Ye Tian shouted, and the entire void of the Golden Sun River Forbidden Land shattered. One by one, the experts of the Golden Sun River Palace were heavily injured and spat out blood.
"A Supreme Primordial Ancestor!!!!"
The experts of the Golden Sun River Palace were horrified.
They only had a few top-tier River Ancestors stationed here, who were only equivalent to top-tier ancestors. How could they be a match for Ye Tian?
· ·········Seeking Flowers··· ·····
Immediately, they wanted to go for reinforcements.
Ye Tian did not stop them, letting them seek help.
After all, he wanted to speak with the Supreme River Ancestor of the Golden Sun River Palace. These top-tier River Ancestors were not qualified to talk to him.
Soon.
The Supreme River Ancestor of the Golden Sun River Palace crossed through the gate and arrived at the Golden Sun River Forbidden Land.
Rumble!!!
A figure with a human torso and a fish tail flew out from the gate.
The experts of the Golden Sun River Palace were similar to a merman race, living in river water, which was why the Golden Sun River base was like a river.
"Lord River Ancestor Lingze, a Supreme Primordial Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Domain is attacking!!!"
... .. 0
The top-tier River Ancestors cried out for help.
River Ancestor Lingze saw Ye Tian and originally intended to threaten him, but the more he looked, the more something felt wrong.
Terror!
True, absolute terror!
He had never seen such a terrifying expert. Although the aura on Ye Tian's body was concealed, the slight trace of aura he emitted made Lingze feel as if he were facing a true nightmare.
Clearly, Ye Tian was no ordinary Supreme Primordial Ancestor.
"Respected Excellency, I believe there might be some misunderstanding between us?" River Ancestor Lingze said with a smile.
Ye Tian said coldly, "Misunderstanding? I am a Supreme Primordial Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain, and you are a foreign power, an invader. Do you really think there's a misunderstanding?"
Ye Tian approached River Ancestor Lingze, no longer suppressing his cultivation aura.
Immediately, River Ancestor Lingze finally understood Ye Tian's level of strength.
The third tier of the Supreme Primordial Ancestor realm!
Heavens, the entire Golden Sun River Palace didn't even have a second-tier Supreme River Ancestor; they only had a few first-tier Supreme River Ancestors.
To the Golden Sun River Palace, an expert like Ye Tian was absolutely an existence they had to look up to.
It was only because Ye Tian was not an expert from their foreign world; otherwise, they would have all knelt and surrendered immediately. How would they dare to offend him?
"Respected powerhouse, since you didn't strike immediately, I assume you didn't come to destroy this base? If there is anything you need, please just say it!" River Ancestor Lingze asked.
"I want to trade for a Source Rule Calamity Crystal from your Golden Sun River Palace!"
Ye Tian demanded.
