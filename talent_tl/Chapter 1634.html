Chapter 1633: Spirit Crane Lord and Lianyi Lord!

“Spirit Crane Lord!”
The Extreme Ice Ancestor felt Spirit Crane Lord’s aura and immediately frowned.
She had once had dealings with Spirit Crane Lord, but at that time, Spirit Crane Lord was only at half-step Eternal Realm and had merely come to visit her in the posture of a junior.
Of course, the two of them had only met once.
But she had never expected that Spirit Crane Lord would actually fortunately become an Eternal Realm existence.
Although Spirit Crane Lord had not broken through to Eternal Realm from the Extreme half-step Eternal Realm, he had advanced through a normal breakthrough, and it had already been countless years since he reached Eternal Realm. His strength was naturally terrifying, far beyond Extreme Ice Ancestor.
“Run!”
Extreme Ice Ancestor did not want to fight an Eternal Realm existence. Even if Spirit Crane Lord’s strength in Eternal Realm was not considered powerful, he was still not someone she could contend with.
“Extreme Ice Ancestor, you actually dare to kill this Lord’s master! You’re courting death~!!!”
A big hand covering the endless void of the Source Sea suppressed her.
Layers of domains shattered and collapsed, but Extreme Ice Ancestor escaped through the void in time.
Boom!!!!!
Spirit Crane Lord appeared and pursued Extreme Ice Ancestor.
Very quickly, Extreme Ice Ancestor was caught up.
Even though she was a seven-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm expert, escaping from an Eternal Realm expert’s pursuit was far too difficult.
Unless she hid in certain special regions, she would be able to evade an Eternal Realm expert’s pursuit.
For example, the Eternal Tribulation Race’s supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain. This world was extremely special; even Eternal Realm experts found it very difficult to enter, and they had to pay a huge price.
Otherwise, the Eternal Tribulation Race would no longer exist.
Aside from the supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain, there were also some special places that restricted Eternal Realm experts from entering, or would cause their strength to plummet.
And Extreme Ice Ancestor wanted to hide in such a place, but unfortunately she was a little too slow and was caught up to by Spirit Crane Lord.
Spirit Crane Lord threw a punch, and his body, fused with the Innate Eternal Rules, did not need to mobilize any Innate Eternal Rule power. It stood above the Innate Eternal Rules and shattered all rules.
Extreme Ice Ancestor activated the Eternal origin weapon, Endless Ice Sand, forming layer upon layer of ice defenses.
However.
With that punch descending, all things fell into silence.
The countless grains of ice sand transformed from the Endless Ice Sand Eternal origin weapon were all shattered and Annihilated, and then the punch pierced through many of Extreme Ice Ancestor’s defensive treasures and struck her body.
Crack!!!!
Extreme Ice Ancestor’s body shattered, with half of her body directly Annihilated, making it difficult to recover, while the other half was also covered in countless cracks.
If she took another blow, Extreme Ice Ancestor would probably fall.
“No wonder you’re a seven-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm expert. You actually survived one of my blows without dying. Unfortunately, as long as you do not enter Eternal Realm, you will always be an ant!”
Spirit Crane Lord struck again with a punch, wanting to completely erase Extreme Ice Ancestor.
But at that moment, a slender jade hand blocked Spirit Crane Lord’s punch and directly sent him flying.
Whoosh!
A beautiful figure appeared before Extreme Ice Ancestor, smiling as she looked at Spirit Crane Lord.
“Spirit Crane Lord, let’s let this matter end here. Star Demon Source Ancestor killed too many Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts, and even killed Extreme Ice Ancestor’s descendants. Extreme Ice Ancestor killing Star Demon Source Ancestor is not wrong!”
The woman spoke.
“Greetings, Lianyi Lord. Does Lianyi Lord really intend to step in for a half-step Eternal Realm expert?” Spirit Crane Lord asked.
“That’s right!” Lianyi Lord said with a smile. “I am protecting Extreme Ice Ancestor, and besides, why would Star Demon Source Ancestor have Blood Devouring Beads in his possession? The method for refining this item is only possessed by us Eternal Realm experts, and using Blood Devouring Beads to assault the pseudo-Eternal Realm is also a forbidden method that is not allowed to be spread. If Spirit Crane Lord insists on killing Extreme Ice Ancestor, I’m afraid I’ll have to report this to the Lord Alliance.”
Spirit Crane Lord’s expression changed, then he immediately changed his face and smiled. “Lianyi Lord is joking. This is just a small matter. I’ll leave at once.”
Whoosh!
Spirit Crane Lord turned and left.
“Many thanks, Lianyi Lord!”
Extreme Ice Ancestor said gratefully.
“Extreme Ice Ancestor, your talent is very good, and you have hope of becoming an Eternal Realm expert. Last time I invited you to become my follower, but you refused. What about this time?” Lianyi Lord invited her again.
“Lianyi Lord, I am willing to become your follower!”
Extreme Ice Ancestor agreed.
“Good, then I’ll take you with me!”
Lianyi Lord waved her hand, took Extreme Ice Ancestor with her, and disappeared.

Star Demon Source Ancestor fell, Spirit Crane Lord appeared and pursued Extreme Ice Ancestor, only to be rescued by another Eternal Realm expert, Lianyi Lord.
This dramatic scene quickly spread, and many people were not very familiar with Spirit Crane Lord and Lianyi Lord, especially Lianyi Lord, whom almost no half-step Eternal Realm experts had any impression of.
However, with Star Demon Source Ancestor’s fall, this was a great thing for many factions; at the very least, they no longer needed to worry every day and could pass their days in fear.
However, Ye Tian had a different feeling.
That is, half-step Eternal Realm experts also had no sense of security. Facing an Eternal Realm existence, even half-step Eternal Realm experts were nothing more than ants.
Of course, Eternal Realm experts rarely appeared in the Source Sea, unless a special situation like this time involved something that drew out Eternal Realm existences.

Otherwise, even if a half-step Eternal Realm expert fought until death, he might not necessarily encounter an Eternal Realm powerhouse.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian diligently cultivated and raised his strength, gradually forgetting about this matter.
One Tribulation Era after another passed, and Ye Tian’s cultivation continued to improve, but he was still very far from the eight-Supreme Being Limit.
Thus, over the long years, he would occasionally go to various Eternal Heavens for training, and would even occasionally accompany his wife Xiao Yue as they roamed among many treasured lands.
Meanwhile, the human race was also developing at a rapid pace, giving rise to quite a few Supreme Beings, and had already surpassed the Tianlin Realm.
Now, even the Tianlin Realm had to rely on the Dark Forbidden Domain, and even take the Dark Forbidden Domain as its backing.
In the blink of an eye, hundreds of thousands of Tribulation Era years passed.
At this point, Ye Tian had finally cultivated to the eight-Supreme Being Limit and was qualified to attempt breaking through to the nine-Supreme Being realm, which was also the greatest dream of many Supreme Beings in their entire lives.
This breakthrough posed no difficulty at all, after all, Ye Tian had already comprehended the Innate Eternal Rules, so naturally there was no difficulty in charging toward the nine-Supreme Being realm.
Very quickly, Ye Tian broke through to the nine-Supreme Being realm, and his strength increased somewhat again.
With Ye Tian’s current strength, he was already enough to be comparable to top-tier half-step Eternal Realm experts. If he borrowed a finger of an Eternal Realm expert, he would probably be able to unleash attacks comparable to the Extreme realm, but his peak strength was still somewhat short of the true Extreme realm.
