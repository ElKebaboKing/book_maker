Chapter 1454: The Ji Mie Clan are Leeks, This is the Truth
!
Ye Tian picked all 108 Star Forbidden Fruits, intending to use some for himself and bestow the rest upon his relatives.
However, it would take an even longer period for this Star Forbidden Tree to produce a second batch of Star Forbidden Fruits. Thus, he did not continue cultivating the tree there and instead departed.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian summoned Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, his wife Xiao Yue, and other relatives to his side.
"Father!"
"Husband!"
"Brother!"
After they arrived, they called out to him.
"You've come!"
Ye Tian said with a smile, then took out the Star Forbidden Fruits one by one.
"These are Star Forbidden Fruits. Just one is enough to raise your cultivation to the limit of an Invincible Territory Ancestor. Take them and refine them; it will save you countless years of bitter cultivation.
Otherwise, with your current cultivation speed, reaching the limit of the Invincible Territory Ancestor realm would likely take an incredibly long time!"
Ye Tian explained.
"Star Forbidden Fruits? To think there's such a good thing!"
Ye Xingchen and the others said with great excitement.
Soon, each of them took one Star Forbidden Fruit. They didn't take more, as one was indeed enough for each person.
Afterward, they returned to begin refining the Star Forbidden Fruits.
With the help of the Star Forbidden Fruits, the four of them—Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, Xiao Yue, and Ye Yu—successively raised their cultivation to the limit of the Invincible Territory Ancestor realm within a million years.
Their strength also increased significantly, approaching the combat power of a First-Step Invincible Territory Ancestor.
Once they achieved some results in mastering the limits of the physical body, they would definitely possess the combat power of a First-Step Invincible Territory Ancestor.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian was also refining the Star Forbidden Fruits. Although the fruits weren't of immense help to him, he couldn't ignore the sheer quantity! One Star Forbidden Fruit was enough to push his progress from the middle stage of the Supreme-level Territory Ancestor realm toward the late stage by about three percent.
Ye Tian's speed in refining the Star Forbidden Fruits was much faster than Ye Xingchen and the others; he could refine one fruit in nearly ten thousand years.
Thus, now that a million years had passed, Ye Tian had refined all 100 Star Forbidden Fruits, leaving only the final four to be reserved for the geniuses of the human race in the future.
What Ye Tian didn't know was that in the border spacetime of the Dark Forbidden Domain, six Ancestors were opening a very important meeting.
Within a void palace, six Ancestors sat.
Among these six Ancestors, two came from the Dark Holy Domain, three from the Dark Destruction Domain, and one was the leader of a certain Supreme Sect within the Dark Forbidden Domain.
These six Ancestors were no ordinary Ancestors; they were top-tier Ancestors who stood above the average, with strength comparable to an Eight-Mark Ji Mie Clan member.
"Everyone, another cycle has turned. The third Dark Holy Land is about to arrive. We have delayed at this border for far too long!"
The speaker was the Spacetime Ancestor of the Dark Destruction Domain, the same one who had constructed the Spacetime Gate. His strength was incredibly formidable, far surpassing that of an ordinary Ancestor.
"Indeed, the value of the third Dark Holy Land is much higher than this place!"
The speaker was the World Ancestor, an Ancestor from the Battle Sect.
Others believed the Battle Sect had only one Ancestor, but in reality, there were two: the Battle Ancestor and the World Ancestor. The Battle World Tower was actually the World Ancestor's sacred object.
The Battle Ancestor's strength was only comparable to a top-tier Seven-Mark Ji Mie Clan member, while the World Ancestor was the second Ancestor of the Battle Sect, yet his strength surpassed the Battle Ancestor, reaching the combat power of an Eight-Mark Ji Mie Clan member, second only to the first Ancestor of the Dark Holy Domain—the Holy Ancestor.
The Holy Ancestor's strength was actually comparable to a Nine-Mark Ji Mie Clan member, much stronger than even the Spacetime Ancestor.
In a one-on-one fight, if the Spacetime Ancestor didn't run, he might even be killed by the Holy Ancestor.
Of course, the Dark Destruction Domain also had an Ancestor comparable to a Nine-Mark Ji Mie Clan member: the Destruction Ancestor, known as the source of endless destruction.
With the Destruction Ancestor and three top-tier Ancestors in one sect, plus many ordinary Ancestors, the Dark Holy Domain's strength was actually slightly inferior.
Of course.
The potential of the Dark Holy Domain was very high. Had it not been born so late, it wouldn't be inferior to the Dark Destruction Domain.
There are currently two Dark Holy Lands in the Dark Forbidden Domain. The first is the Dark Destruction Domain, and the second is the Dark Holy Domain. This is something many cultivators are unaware of, as they assume the Dark Destruction Domain was born later.
However, when a Dark Holy Land is born, there is a protection period during which Ancestors cannot enter. Otherwise, the Dark Holy Domain would have long been destroyed or occupied by the Dark Destruction Domain.
If Ye Tian knew the true strength of these Ancestors, he would be very confused. Their side possessed two Ancestors comparable to Nine-Mark Ji Mie Clan members, while the Ji Mie Clan only had one. Why had they never defeated the Ji Mie Clan and could only barely hold them back?
There was only one reason: Ji Mie Treasures! Once the Ji Mie Clan was wiped out, this special spacetime would cease to exist, making it difficult for Ji Mie Treasures to continue being born.
The Ji Mie Treasures were valuable to them, and presiding over this place was very safe, allowing them to harvest "leeks" time and time again.
This was the real reason why the Ji Mie Clan continued to exist without being exterminated.
As for the occasional fall of a Supreme-level Territory Ancestor, it meant nothing to them. What did the death of a few Supreme-level Territory Ancestors matter? When these factions fought each other, many Supreme-level Territory Ancestors fell, so this loss was negligible.
Of course.
Such secrets could not be told to the Supreme-level Territory Ancestors, otherwise, the impact would be too great.
Furthermore, the Supreme-level Territory Ancestors might not fight the Ji Mie Clan seriously. If they relied solely on these Ancestors, they wouldn't be able to intercept all the Ji Mie Clan members. If some escaped, it would truly be a major problem.
After all, the Ji Mie Clan grew very quickly. If given enough resources and territory, they could truly produce several Nine-Mark Ji Mie Clan members.
Thus, this was not a scene they wished to see.
But things were different now. The third Dark Holy Land was about to be born; this was a rule of the Dark Forbidden Domain and something the Ancestors had calculated.
As for where the third Dark Holy Land would appear, they could not calculate it and could only wait silently.
But one thing was very clear: once the third Dark Holy Land was born, the Supreme-level Territory Ancestors could not remain here; they had to contribute to the struggle for the third Dark Holy Land.
At that time, these factions would no longer be able to cooperate.
Therefore, they had to crush the Ji Mie Clan and destroy this place. They could no longer leave it be, or their forces would be tied down, and many things would be impossible to accomplish.
