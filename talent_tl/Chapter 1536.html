Chapter 1535: Purchasing Intelligence!

Ye Tian could kill a 28th Tribulation beast on his own, so naturally, he wouldn't seek cooperation with other Invincible Ones; otherwise, he would have to share the profits with them.
Thus, on this day, he dispatched his duplicate body along with his Invincible Ancestor clone to enter this foreign world named Jiuhe.
Jiuhe World.
As soon as Ye Tian arrived, he immediately attacked the Tribulation Beast.
Rumble!!!
The battle erupted. Ye Tian used two Invincible-level combat forces to besiege this 28th Tribulation beast. The duplicate body was the strongest, while the Invincible Ancestor clone was the weakest.
Although the clone was decent and didn't occupy a duplicate body slot, it functioned as an individual entity that needed to cultivate on its own; it couldn't be as powerful as the main body like the duplicate body was.
Of course, having an extra clone was still beneficial.
Although Ye Tian's strength was greater than this Tribulation Beast's, the difference was limited. Therefore, it took a long time to besiege the beast. The duplicate body even went to any lengths, repeatedly inflicting heavy damage on the beast at the cost of sustaining severe injuries itself.
Eventually, the duplicate body collapsed and perished, but the Tribulation Beast was also on the brink of death and was subsequently slain by Ye Tian's clone.
This was a one-for-one trade. For an ordinary person, it would naturally be unprofitable, but Ye Tian didn't care about the death of this duplicate body; after all, it only cost some resources to replace.
In reality, if Ye Tian had been more careful, the duplicate body wouldn't have perished, as its strength was indeed greater than the Tribulation Beast's.
However, Ye Tian didn't want to waste time. If the time for the Great Tribulation in Jiuhe World passed, he wouldn't be able to kill the beast and would have to wait for another Great Tribulation Era.
That was why he chose this "life-for-a-life" method to quickly end the battle.
Crack!!!
The body of the 28th Tribulation beast disintegrated, turning into a large amount of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, Tribulation Blood, and Tribulation Bone.
Eighty thousand Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, ten portions of Tribulation Blood, and six portions of Tribulation Bone.
Although the value of the Tribulation Blood and Bone wasn't as high as those from a 37th Tribulation beast, the value of these resources was still immense, far exceeding the harvest Ye Tian had gained from killing the 37th Tribulation beast previously.
After all, this time the harvest belonged entirely to him alone.
Ye Tian collected these items, even taking the entire carcass of the Tribulation Beast, and returned to the Dark Forbidden Zone.
...
"Hahaha, the harvest is massive! Hunting Tribulation Beasts alone is much more satisfying!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
However, his current strength was limited; he could only kill weaker Tribulation Beasts. Once he encountered a truly powerful one, he wouldn't be able to kill it no matter what.
But this harvest was so significant that it was enough to push his strength a bit further. Perhaps it would be enough to rival the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity and the Wanyuan Sword Ancestor, those two Invincible Ones.
Subsequently, Ye Tian began to absorb and refine those ten portions of Tribulation Blood. Although each portion was less effective than the blood of a 37th Tribulation beast, there was a large quantity!
As portion after portion of Tribulation Blood was consumed and refined, Ye Tian's cultivation rose rapidly, and his strength grew steadily.
Finally, after a long time, all ten portions of Tribulation Blood were refined.
At this moment, Ye Tian felt a vague bottleneck. If he couldn't comprehend an Invincible Ancestral Technique to cause a qualitative change in his physical body, he wouldn't be able to increase his cultivation quickly.
In other words, at his current level, he could only go to the Eternal World to meditate on the world rules there and attempt to comprehend an Invincible Ancestral Technique.
"It's time to head to the Eternal World. My current strength is not inferior to the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity, which is equivalent to stepping over the threshold of a Fifth-Grade Invincible combat power, though I am not yet a true Fifth-Grade Invincible One."
Ye Tian murmured.
However, before heading to the Eternal World, he wanted to learn more about the situation there from the Firmament Sword Tomb power.
Although the Firmament Sword Tomb had destroyed the Tier 2 Gate connecting to the Dark Forbidden Zone, Ye Tian knew which foreign world the Firmament Sword Tomb was located in.
After all, he knew the locations of countless nearby foreign worlds and naturally understood the locations of those six super-tier powers.
In fact, there were more than six super-tier powers among the nearby foreign worlds; there were many, such as the Blood Mist Alliance, which was also a super-tier power.
However, Ye Tian had not made contact with the other super-tier powers, temporarily only dealing with these six.
On this day.
Through another foreign world, Ye Tian contacted a base of the Firmament Sword Tomb rather than entering their home world directly, which would have been equivalent to declaring war.
"I am looking for your Firmament Sword Tomb's Wanyuan Sword Ancestor!"
Ye Tian spoke.
The powerhouses of the Firmament Sword Tomb immediately went to notify their headquarters, and soon an Invincible One descended upon the base.
This Invincible One was not the Wanyuan Sword Ancestor, but another Invincible One of the Firmament Sword Tomb—the Shifang Sword Ancestor.
The Shifang Sword Ancestor was also one of the Invincible Ones who had cooperated with Ye Tian to hunt the Tribulation Beast last time. His strength wasn't particularly great, but his current aura was slightly stronger than before, though not by much.
After all, other Invincible Ones couldn't absorb and refine Tribulation Power as quickly as Ye Tian. Therefore, even if the Shifang Sword Ancestor had obtained Tribulation Blood, his refining speed was slow and his utilization rate wasn't high, so it was impossible for his strength to skyrocket all at once.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws!"
The Shifang Sword Ancestor said politely.
"Shifang Sword Ancestor, is Wanyuan Sword Ancestor not here?" Ye Tian asked casually.
"He is not!" Shifang Sword Ancestor replied. "Wanyuan Sword Ancestor has left the Firmament Sword Tomb for the Eternal World. At his level, it is very difficult to increase his strength; he must comprehend an Invincible Sword Technique to undergo a transformation. Last time, he obtained Tribulation Blood and Bone, so he went to the Eternal World to sell them in exchange for treasures that could help him, thereby attempting to comprehend an Invincible Sword Technique."
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian understood.
It was difficult for Wanyuan Sword Ancestor's cultivation to improve further, and refining Tribulation Blood would be a waste, so selling it directly was the best choice.
"Shifang Sword Ancestor, I wish to learn about the situation in the Eternal World, such as the route and some details within the Eternal World. I wonder if the Firmament Sword Tomb can inform me?" Ye Tian asked.
Shifang Sword Ancestor smiled and said, "Ancestor of Myriad Laws, whether in the Eternal World or in the various foreign worlds, we follow the principle of equivalent exchange. If you want information, you must pay a certain amount in treasures; otherwise, our Firmament Sword Tomb will not provide any intelligence!"
Ye Tian didn't feel like haggling with the Shifang Sword Ancestor. Moreover, he was now an Invincible One and wouldn't do something as beneath him as capturing the Firmament Sword Tomb's powerhouses to threaten the Shifang Sword Ancestor; that would be too degrading.
Thus, Ye Tian chose to pay some Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals to purchase the information.
In the end, Ye Tian paid 3,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals and obtained a route map to the Eternal World from the Shifang Sword Ancestor, as well as some specific intelligence regarding the Eternal World, including some of the powers within and things to watch out for.
For Ye Tian, using Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals to buy this information was naturally worth it.
