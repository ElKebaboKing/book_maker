Chapter 1512: Super-Rank Power!

The Cangyu Bone Emperor slowly revealed the powers behind the six great forbidden zones of the Dark Forbidden Domain, while Ye Tian listened intently.
The Cangyu Bone Emperor said, "The powers behind the six forbidden zones of the Dark Forbidden Domain are stronger than our Bone Race. The gates established by those six forbidden zones are Second-Tier Gates!"
"What, Second-Tier Gates!"
Ye Tian was incredibly surprised.
He knew very well what a Second-Tier Gate represented; the gate established by the Blood Mist Alliance was a Second-Tier Gate.
Although he didn't know how to establish a Second-Tier Gate, any power capable of doing so was bound to be immensely powerful.
The Sanctuary Heaven and Earth only had one Second-Tier Gate, yet the Dark Forbidden Domain actually possessed six. This was simply unbelievable.
Immediately, he asked, "Why are there six Second-Tier Gates in the Dark Forbidden Domain? I have been to other foreign heavens and earths, and the one I visited before only had one Second-Tier Gate."
The Cangyu Bone Emperor did not answer directly, but said evasively, "The Dark Forbidden Domain seems to be somewhat different from other foreign heavens and earths. As for how it is different, I am not sure, but it is significant enough for six powerful forces to open Second-Tier Gates."
Ye Tian didn't know if the Cangyu Bone Emperor truly didn't know or was intentionally withholding information, but he didn't press further.
The Cangyu Bone Emperor continued, "Those six foreign powers are capable of establishing Second-Tier Gates and possess many Supreme-level existences, even those of the third tier. There are even rumors that there are Invincible-level existences among those six major powers. Of course, we have never been to the foreign realms of those six powers, so we cannot know more detailed information. However, based on several interactions, the most powerful among them should be Calamity Black Mountain, followed by the Firmament Sword Tomb. The other four major powers are likely comparable to each other."
Subsequently, the Cangyu Bone Emperor provided a piece of information that surprised Ye Tian.
Calamity Black Mountain once deployed six third-tier Supreme-level existences to fight a great war against other forces right within the Dark Forbidden Domain.
As for why the battle broke out, the Bone Race did not know, because they were too weak to dare approach.
Of course, this was the Cangyu Bone Emperor's way of putting it.
"Ancestor of Ten Thousand Laws, I might as well provide you with some data!" the Cangyu Bone Emperor said. "Among the many foreign heavens and earths, major powers are divided into grades. Those without Supreme-level existences aren't worth mentioning. Any power that possesses Supreme-level existences but lacks a second-tier Supreme-level existence is called an Unnamed Rank power, meaning they aren't even qualified to have their names known, representing weakness. A power that possesses a second-tier Supreme-level existence and has more than three Supreme-level existences in total is called a Named Rank power, indicating they have potential worth noting. A power that possesses a third-tier Supreme-level existence and has more than ten Supreme-level existences is called a Great Rank power. And a power that possesses an Invincible-level existence is called a Super-Rank power. Above Super-Rank powers, it is said there are also Saint-Rank powers and Eternal-Rank powers. Those six major powers belong to the Super-Rank category; their strength is unfathomable!"
Unnamed Rank, Named Rank, Great Rank, Super-Rank...
Such a classification was very apt and simple, but it also presented the truth of the law of the jungle: without strength, one didn't even have the qualification to be remembered.
"According to this classification, our Human Race can only be considered an Unnamed Rank power!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Although he had reached the third tier of the Supreme Ancestor level, the Human Race had no other Supreme Ancestors besides him, so they didn't even count as a Named Rank power.
Furthermore, the Human Race's foundation was too shallow; not only was he the only Supreme Ancestor, but even ordinary Ancestors were incredibly scarce.
Of course, this was also because the Human Race had not yet survived a single Great Tribulation. It was said that once a tribulation was passed, providence would skyrocket, and cultivation would become much easier.
Once they survived a Great Tribulation, the Human Race would have the chance to give birth to many Ancestors.
Afterward, Ye Tian spoke with the Cangyu Bone Emperor for a while longer before departing.
Although he now had a general understanding of the strength of the six major powers, the pressure had actually increased.
Six Super-Rank powers, each possessing at least one Invincible-level existence, and each power having more than just one or two third-tier Supreme-level existences comparable to him.
Such powers would be very difficult for him to contend with.
"It seems I must raise my strength to a level nearly invincible among the Supreme-level before the Great Tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Domain arrives."
Ye Tian thought secretly.
Having learned the strength of the six major powers, he no longer had any intention of barging into the six forbidden zones.
At the very least, for now, only the Bone Race knew of his strength, and the Bone Race had no way to contact the people of those six major powers. Therefore, those six powers did not yet know that a third-tier Supreme Ancestor had been born in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
If they knew, the six major powers might dispatch even more Supreme-level existences during the Dark Forbidden Domain's Great Tribulation.
Therefore, there were benefits to hiding his strength and keeping a low profile.
...
Trading for a Source Rule Tribulation Crystal from the Bone Race this time brought many benefits to Ye Tian. At the very least, he could easily create several more Supreme Ancestral Techniques.
After all, it was becoming increasingly difficult for him to fuse Ultimate Ancestral Techniques that harmonized with the rules of the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth, requiring a long time. However, fusing with a Source Rule Tribulation Crystal that harmonized with the Bone Race's world was much easier.
"For the next Ultimate Ancestral Technique, I'll choose the Extreme Arm Ultimate Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian decided.
Thus, with the help of the Bone Race's Source Rule Tribulation Crystal, he began to comprehend the Extreme Arm Ultimate Ancestral Technique that harmonized with the Bone Race's world rules. It didn't take long for him to successfully create the Extreme Arm Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
Next, he began to fuse the two types of Extreme Arm Ultimate Ancestral Techniques.
In the blink of an eye, one Domain Epoch passed.
"I've finally succeeded in the fusion. I didn't expect that fusing an Ultimate Ancestral Technique harmonized with the Bone Race's world rules for the first time would be so easy. With the Evolution characteristic plus the Copying Body, if I have enough different Source Rule Tribulation Crystals from various worlds, I'm afraid I could skyrocket my strength to a terrifying degree in a short time!"
Ye Tian thought.
Successfully creating the Extreme Arm Supreme Ancestral Technique was equivalent to possessing a seventh Supreme Ancestral Technique, causing Ye Tian's body to undergo another transformation and his strength to increase once more.
Next, Ye Tian began attempting to create an eighth Supreme Ancestral Technique.
This time, the Ultimate Ancestral Technique he chose was the Golden Body Ultimate Ancestral Technique. Having an extra Supreme Defensive Ancestral Technique would at least make his life-preserving capabilities stronger.
To create the eighth Supreme Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian spent one and a half Domain Epochs.
For the ninth Supreme Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian still chose a defensive Supreme Ancestral Technique, which took him two Domain Epochs this time.
