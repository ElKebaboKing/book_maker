Chapter 1650 Eternal Compression Secret Art!

“Voidless Ancestor, I want to invite you to join the Tower of Doom.”
said True Martial Source Ancestor, a six-Domain half-step Eternal realm expert.
True Martial Source Ancestor was one of the Extreme realm half-step Eternal realm experts Ye Tian had made contact with, and the Tower of Doom was not a force within Maple Leaf Domain, but an organization that spanned multiple spacetime domains.
This organization did not have Eternal realm experts. Or rather, once a half-step Eternal realm expert broke through to the Eternal realm, they would automatically leave the Tower of Doom.
Because the Tower of Doom was a loose organization for those seeking to break through to the Eternal realm; it could also be said to be a mutual aid organization, with the ultimate goal of becoming an Eternal realm expert.
Therefore, once one broke through to the Eternal realm, they had to leave the Tower of Doom, otherwise the nature of the Tower of Doom would change.
In fact, there was another reason: the eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts of the Tower of Doom also did not want some ordinary half-step Eternal realm expert to break through to the Eternal realm and then immediately step on their heads to lead them.
For Ye Tian, this organization was pretty good.
So, Ye Tian agreed to join the Tower of Doom.
On this day.
True Martial Source Ancestor brought Ye Tian to the branch of the Tower of Doom in Maple Leaf Domain.
Here, Ye Tian saw many half-step Eternal realm experts at the Extreme realm level.
After all, for many half-step Eternal realm experts, the organizational model of the Tower of Doom was still very attractive, and they did not need to pay much of a price.
The Tower of Doom in Maple Leaf Domain did not have any eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts; there were only seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts, and there were quite a few of them—twenty-three in total.
Now that Ye Tian had joined the Tower of Doom, it could be said that there were twenty-four seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts.
“Just the Extreme realm experts in this Maple Leaf Domain branch of the Tower of Doom far surpass those in Source Sea!”
Ye Tian could not help but sigh.
This showed that Source Sea really was not very suitable for half-step Eternal realm cultivation, but if an eight-Domain expert wanted to break through to the nine-Domain level, they still had to go to Source Sea.
There was no specific division of positions within the Tower of Doom. On the surface, everyone had the same position, but privately, those seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts would still ignore ordinary half-step Eternal realm experts, and even the half-step Eternal realm experts of the first, second, and third Domain levels were not qualified to communicate with them.
Ye Tian had only just joined the Tower of Doom, so naturally he could not immediately squeeze into the upper circle of the Tower of Doom, but through True Martial Source Ancestor he still learned quite a few things.
For example, in Maple Leaf Domain, the strongest member of the Tower of Doom was the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm expert—Sang Ze.
Seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts were also divided into strong and weak, and Ye Tian belonged to the kind who had just broken through to the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm, while Sang Ze belonged to that top-tier group of seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts.
After joining the Tower of Doom, he discovered that the scripture treasury of the Tower of Doom also recorded the secrets of how to break through from the seven-Domain level to the eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm, and he could not obtain such secret records in Source Sea.
Basically, once one’s strength reached the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm, they would touch the boundary barrier.
This was also called the First Boundary Barrier.
And the barrier between the eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm and the nine-Domain half-step Eternal realm was called the Second Boundary Barrier.
Both boundary barriers were very difficult to break through, but the Second Boundary Barrier was more than a hundred times more difficult than the First Boundary Barrier.
After all, among the countless spacetime domains, there were still quite a few eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts, but nine-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts were far too rare—there might not even be any in this era.
To break the First Boundary Barrier, the secret records of the Tower of Doom gave two solutions.
The first solution was to first break through the barrier of the Eternal body. Those who could cultivate to the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm basically all possessed an Eternal body, and had cultivated the Eternal body to the Perfected realm.
And Perfected was only the apparent Limit, but it could still be further improved, only it was extremely difficult.
Above Perfected was the Transcendence realm.
An Eternal body in the Transcendence realm was a realm that had broken through the Limit; once the Eternal body reached this realm, the First Boundary Barrier could be broken through.
But the precondition was that your strength had already touched the First Boundary Barrier.
If you were only an ordinary seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm expert or six-Domain half-step Eternal realm expert, then even if you raised the Eternal body to the Transcendence realm, you still would not be able to break the First Boundary Barrier.
The second solution was to comprehend the Innate Eternal Rule to an unimaginable height, and then use the Innate Eternal Rule to forcibly break the First Boundary Barrier.
However, the second solution was even harder.
Basically, those eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts all broke through by using the first method.
The strength gap between the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm and the eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm was huge, even as different as heaven and earth.
One eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm expert could even deal with one hundred seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts alone, and top-tier eight-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts could even fight weaker Eternal realm experts.
“The First Boundary Barrier, Transcendence realm Eternal body!”
Ye Tian shook his head.
At present, he also had no way to break the First Boundary Barrier.
However, if he wanted to continue improving his strength and raise it to the peak of the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm, that he could still do.
In fact, within spacetime domains, there were quite a few secret arts for increasing willpower; it was just that most of them were not as powerful as the willworld.
In addition, the half-step Eternal power of the Extreme realm could still be further compressed, infinitely approaching true Eternal power.
Otherwise, the Extreme realm experts within the spacetime domains would simply be unable to reach the peak level of the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm, much less charge at the First Boundary Barrier.
And Ye Tian’s half-step Eternal power had never been compressed before, yet it had already risen to the combat strength of the seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm; if word of this got out, I’m afraid hardly anyone would believe it.
However, this could not be blamed on Ye Tian for not compressing it; in fact, he really did not know.
Source Sea did indeed have several seven-Domain half-step Eternal realm experts, but those few could not possibly tell him this secret, and the Extreme realm members of Source God Pavilion likewise did not know this secret.
But now that he knew, it was not too late. Once his half-step Eternal power successfully compressed, his strength would surely increase rapidly.
However, compressing half-step Eternal power was not an easy task and required an Eternal Compression Secret Art.
Moreover, different Eternal Compression Secret Arts could compress to different degrees.
For example, some Eternal Compression Secret Arts could only compress half-step Eternal power by five times, while the most top-tier Eternal Compression Secret Arts could compress it by a hundred times.
But even higher-level Eternal Compression Secret Arts were incomparably precious, and the Tower of Doom did not have them. Unless one joined some Eternal spacetime domains and pledged allegiance to those Eternal realm experts, one could obtain top-tier Eternal Compression Secret Arts.
As for the Eternal Compression Secret Art currently publicly sold by the Tower of Doom, it was an eighteen-times compression art; even so, one still had to pay some price to obtain it.
“Then I’ll first cultivate the Eternal Compression Secret Art with eighteen-times compression!”
Ye Tian decided.
