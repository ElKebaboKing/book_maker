Chapter 1511: The Third Level of the Supreme Ancestor!

In just one domain epoch, Ye Tian had mastered another Supreme Ancestral Technique. At this rate, it wouldn't take long for him to step into the third level of the Supreme Ancestor.
"I have already mastered two Supreme Ancestral Techniques. For the third one, I'll choose the Eternal Supreme Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian decided.
This Supreme Ancestral Technique was a Time Supreme Ancestral Technique, which required comprehending another Eternal Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
Thus, Ye Tian continued to contemplate the Origin Rule Calamity Crystal of the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth, repeatedly using his duplicated bodies to trigger the derivation trait, causing them to collapse time and again.
Under these circumstances, Ye Tian quickly created another Eternal Ultimate Ancestral Technique that aligned with the rules of the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth.
Next, he began to fuse the two Eternal Ultimate Ancestral Techniques to create an Eternal Supreme Ancestral Technique.
This time, he took a bit longer.
As for the reason, Ye Tian knew it well.
Using the same Sanctuary Heaven and Earth to create Supreme Ancestral Techniques would become increasingly difficult unless he switched to a different foreign heaven and earth.
However, Ye Tian currently only had the Origin Rule Calamity Crystal of the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth, making it easiest to comprehend its rules. Even if it took some time, it didn't matter much.
After two domain epochs, Ye Tian successfully fused the two Eternal Ultimate Ancestral Techniques into one Supreme Ancestral Technique.
With this, Ye Tian had mastered three Supreme Ancestral Techniques at once, and his strength skyrocketed.
Furthermore, over these years, Ye Tian continuously refined resources from the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth. His cultivation rose steadily, and his physical body underwent three transformations. Combined with the amplification of the three Supreme Ancestral Techniques, his strength was far greater than when he had first broken through to the Supreme Ancestor level.
However, Ye Tian was still a bit short of the third level of the Supreme Ancestor.
In reality, the limit for every Supreme Ancestor was different.
In terms of actual combat power, Ye Tian wasn't far from the third level, but he was still far from his own limit.
Once he truly reached the third level of the Supreme Ancestor, he would be much more powerful than other third-level experts.
In the following years, Ye Tian continued to create Supreme Ancestral Techniques.
To create the fourth Supreme Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian spent nearly ten domain epochs.
And to create the fifth, he spent thirty domain epochs.
After spending another sixty domain epochs, Ye Tian finally created the sixth Supreme Ancestral Technique.
Meanwhile, the Dark Forbidden Realm had passed its sixth Dark Era. Only three Dark Eras remained before the fourth great tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Realm would arrive.
......
"Now I have mastered six Supreme Ancestral Techniques: Tyrant Strength, Streamer, Sacred Armor, Void Slash, Eternal, and God-Suppressing. Combined with the countless resources I've refined from the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth, my cultivation has risen high enough. With my current strength, although I haven't reached my limit, I am even more powerful than an average third-level Supreme Ancestor. I can now be considered at the third level of the Supreme Ancestor!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He wasn't sure if this level of strength was enough to protect the human race, but he wanted to improve even more.
The threats facing the Dark Forbidden Realm were no less than those of the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth. Even the Bone Race might be stronger than the Myriad Beast Saint Sect of the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth.
And the Bone Race wasn't actually the greatest threat to the Dark Forbidden Realm; the real threats were the forces behind the six major forbidden zones.
Even now, Ye Tian had not attempted to enter those six forbidden zones. The top ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Realm also knew very little about them.
Calamity Black Mountain, Destruction Swamp, Boundless Wall, Firmament Sword Tomb, Illusory Dream World, and Infinite Demon Sea.
These were the six forbidden zones, and also six major powers.
They were likely no weaker than the so-called Blood Mist Alliance. It was just that the Dark Forbidden Realm was a newly born forbidden realm, protected by the Supreme Rules and unable to be modified.
The Sanctuary Heaven and Earth was targeted by the Blood Mist Alliance because its protection period had ended.
But the Dark Forbidden Realm was too young, so there was no immediate worry about it being occupied.
However, without enough strength, it would still be destroyed. Those powerful forces definitely did not want to see the Dark Forbidden Realm rise.
"I must understand the general strength of those six major forces; otherwise, I will be very passive when the great tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Realm arrives!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Seeking information from the top ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Realm yielded almost nothing.
He thought of one power—the Bone Race!
The Bone Race had entered the Dark Forbidden Realm many times, so they should logically know about these six major forces.
Thus, Ye Tian arrived at the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, intending to trade with the Bone Race again and inquire about the six major forces.
--------------
Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
The Bone Race was very pleased with Ye Tian's arrival.
But since Ye Tian was powerful, the Bone Race naturally didn't want to offend him.
Soon, a Bone Emperor of the Bone Ancestor condensed a wisp of consciousness and descended upon the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, why have you come here again?"
The Bone Emperor said with some annoyance.
"Bone Emperor Cang Yu, I wish to trade some resources with your Bone Race and also ask about the forces behind the six forbidden zones of the Dark Forbidden Realm," Ye Tian said.
"Not interested in trading!"
Bone Emperor Cang Yu refused.
"What if it's an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal?" Ye Tian took out an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal from the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth and said, "You should be interested in such a good thing, right?"
"An Origin Rule Calamity Crystal? This isn't from your Dark Forbidden Realm. Where did you get it?"
Bone Emperor Cang Yu said in shock.
"Regardless of where it came from, you must be interested. I want to use this Origin Rule Calamity Crystal to trade for one from your foreign heaven and earth. This is a mutually beneficial trade!" Ye Tian said with a smile.
Since he had two Origin Rule Calamity Crystals from the Sanctuary Heaven and Earth, trading one wouldn't affect him much. If he could exchange it for one from the Bone Race's world, it would be a great deal!
Bone Emperor Cang Yu stared at the crystal in Ye Tian's hand and nodded. "This Origin Rule Calamity Crystal is indeed one our Bone Race doesn't have. We are willing to trade."
Subsequently.
Bone Emperor Cang Yu descended in person to the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone and completed the trade with Ye Tian.
"Bone Emperor Cang Yu, can you tell me about the six forbidden zones?"
Ye Tian asked.
"I can tell you, but what treasure can you offer in exchange?" Bone Emperor Cang Yu asked.
"How about my friendship?"
Ye Tian revealed his pressure.
Boom!!!!!
The pressure belonging to the third level of the Supreme Ancestor spread out, making Bone Emperor Cang Yu tremble.
He could sense that Ye Tian could easily kill him in an instant, even preventing him from escaping.
"The third level of the Supreme Ancestor? How is this possible? You were clearly only at the level of having just broken through to the Supreme Ancestor before. How could you have stepped into the third level so quickly???" Bone Emperor Cang Yu was stunned.
Only a few Dark Eras had passed. For Supreme-level beings, a few Dark Eras wasn't a long time.
Many Supreme-level foreign experts had gone through multiple great tribulations without being able to improve their strength much, yet Ye Tian's progress was too illogical.
Knowing Ye Tian's strength, Bone Emperor Cang Yu's attitude changed, and he immediately answered Ye Tian's question.
"Alright, I can tell you the general situation of those six forces!" Bone Emperor Cang Yu said.
