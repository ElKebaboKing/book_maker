Chapter 1657: The Eternity Realm Lords Who Are Tracking Them!

Everyone passed through the spacetime passage and crossed one spacetime territory after another, quickly arriving at the outermost spacetime territory—Silent Territory.
Silent Territory was a territory with an owner; it was the domain of the Silent Lord, but she had no ambition. Anyone could come to her territory, and she did not really manage affairs either. She seemed not to care about anything. Otherwise, she would not have chosen the Silent Territory, so far away from the Source Sea.
Silent Territory was one of the spacetime territories farthest from the Source Sea. Beyond that distance, spacetime territories would no longer be born, because if one was too far from the Source Sea, it would no longer count as existing by attaching itself to the Source Sea, and naturally there would be no such thing as a spacetime territory.
“Entering the Void Region from here, we’ll have to traverse a very long distance afterward. You must be mentally prepared!”
Gu He, a Domain Ancestor, reminded them.
“It’s not like this is our first time entering the Void Region. Gu He, Domain Ancestor, let’s set off quickly!”
One Eightfold Realm expert urged hurriedly.
“Go!”
Everyone immediately tore apart the spacetime barrier of Silent Territory and entered the Void Region, then headed deeper into it.
Not long after they left, one Eternity Realm expert after another appeared.
They looked in the direction Gu He, Domain Ancestor, and the others had gone, then chased after them while chatting among themselves.
“That old fellow Gu He, Domain Ancestor, actually knows the location of the Abyssless Land. Rumor has it that the Abyssless Land is one of the nests of the Void Beasts, and it contains countless precious resources. It’s a pity that back then, when the Immortal Lords fought the Void Beasts in a decisive battle, they had no time to deal with the Abyssless Land, and the Abyssless Land actually moved away, leaving no trace of where it went.”
“If we obtain some benefits in the Abyssless Land, maybe we’ll have a chance to advance into Immortality Realm Lords.”
“We also need to be careful of this group of Eightfold Realm half-step Eternity Realm experts. Their strength isn’t weak either, and there are so many of them. We’ll have to join forces too, so we don’t get bullied by a bunch of half-step Eternity Realm experts!”
“Of course!”
……
Time slowly passed. At first, some Eightfold Realm experts could still endure the environment of the Void Region, but as time went on, they too grew somewhat irritable. The fact that even Eightfold Realm experts could develop irritability showed just how terrifying the environment deep within the Void Region was.
Of course, the ones truly affected were only those Eightfold Realm experts who had not yet condensed a second-tier Eternity Will. For experts like Ye Tian, who were Eightfold Realm half-step Eternity Realm experts, the impact was very small and could almost be ignored.
However, once they ventured deeper into the Void Region, even second-tier Eternity Will began to feel somewhat uncomfortable.
At this point, one of the Eightfold Realm experts asked, “Gu He, Domain Ancestor, how much farther is it? If this goes on, I won’t be able to endure it.”
“Not far now!” Gu He, Domain Ancestor, replied.
After another few days, they finally arrived at their destination.
“We’re here!”
Gu He, Domain Ancestor, pointed to a shadowy area ahead and said.
“It really is the Abyssless Land!”
One Eightfold Realm expert after another became quite excited.
“Everyone, be careful. To enter the Abyssless Land, we must wait. Only when the Abyssless Land is about to move can we enter it; otherwise, it’s impossible to get in at any other time.” Gu He, Domain Ancestor, reminded them.
“We know!” The other Eightfold Realm experts nodded.
Immediately, the many Eightfold Realm experts sat cross-legged around the Abyssless Land and waited quietly.
Very quickly, Gu He, Domain Ancestor, changed expression and looked into the distance.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
The auras of one Eternity Realm expert after another appeared, without any concealment.
“Eternity Realm Lords!”
Gu He, Domain Ancestor, looked somewhat ugly. He had not wanted the Eternity Realm Lords to know, but it was still exposed.
The Eightfold Realm experts present had signed contracts, so they should not have exposed it. Therefore, it could only have been the Eightfold Realm experts who left that revealed the news. But how did these Eternity Realm Lords lock onto his location?
Logically speaking, after they traversed one spacetime territory after another, the Eternity Realm Lords would not have had time to track them at all!
Suddenly.
Gu He, Domain Ancestor, saw an Eternity Realm expert and immediately understood.
“Silent Lord!”
Not only Gu He understood, the other Eightfold Realm experts understood as well.
They had left from the Silent Lord’s territory, so naturally the Silent Lord knew about it.
They had just not expected the Silent Lord to immediately notify the other lords.
And as they flew through the Void Region, their auras would leak out, making them very conspicuous. However, after a period of time, these auras would be turned into nothingness by the Void Region, leaving no trace at all.
But these lords had tracked them in time, so of course they could still follow their trail.
At this moment, there were only thirty-three Eternity Realm Lords who had arrived, not a very large number, but among them were not a few Peak Immortality Realm Lords.
Although Eightfold Realm half-step Eternity Realm experts could be comparable to weaker Eternity Realm experts—that is, weaker Immortality Realm experts—and even Gu He, Domain Ancestor, who had touched the barrier of the second boundary limit, could at most be comparable to ordinary Immortality Realm experts, they still could not defeat Peak Immortality Realm Lords.
Their only advantage was that they had greater numbers, while the arriving Eternity Realm Lords were relatively few.
“Silent Lord, I really thought you didn’t care about anything?”
Gu He, Domain Ancestor, mocked.
The Silent Lord did not care at all about Gu He, Domain Ancestor’s mockery, and said with a smile, “I truly don’t care about many things, but as for the Abyssless Land, I can’t possibly not care. I also want to become an Immortality Realm Lord, but many great opportunities have already been occupied by those Immortal Lords, and the Abyssless Land is my chance.”
Gu He, Domain Ancestor, did not want to fight either. After all, they were not in an advantageous position, but they were not easy to bully either.
So Gu He, Domain Ancestor, proposed, “After we enter, how about we avoid interfering with each other? The Abyssless Land is, after all, one of the nests of the Void Beasts. There may still be Void Beasts in it now. We are not your obstacle or enemy.”
“Rest assured. As long as you don’t cause us trouble, we won’t cause you trouble either. Our goal is simply to find opportunities!” the Silent Lord said with a smile.
That was what was said, but everyone still harbored a certain hostility toward one another.
In fact, the relationship between the Eightfold Tower and the Immortality Realm Lords was very poor; this was common knowledge in the spacetime territories.
Because Eternity Realm Lords had almost all once been ordinary half-step Eternity Realm experts, not peak half-step Eternity Realm experts, whereas if an Eightfold Realm half-step Eternity Realm expert could break through to Eternity Realm, they would almost always become an Immortality Realm Lord upon breaking through.
Eightfold Realm experts looked down on ordinary half-step Eternity Realm experts, and once they broke through to Eternity Realm, they also looked down on ordinary Immortality Realm Lords.
Now, these Immortality Realm Lords naturally wanted to take advantage of the fact that Eightfold Realm experts could not break through and come cause trouble for them.
The only thing that made Gu He, Domain Ancestor, and the others let out a breath of relief was that this time there were no Immortal Lords or Eternal Lords involved; otherwise, there would be nothing for them to share at all.
And those Eternity Realm Lords also would not notify Immortal Lords or Eternal Lords.
In the blink of an eye, several tribulation-era years passed.
On this day, the Abyssless Land began to tremble.
It was about to move!.
