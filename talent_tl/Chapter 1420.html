Chapter 1419: The Second Sacred Monument Slot
!
"The Primordial Ancestral Realm has come to a close!"
Ye Tian received this news from the War Gate.
After such a long time, the ten Supreme Sacred Gates and numerous Sacred Lands had maneuvered against one another—essentially a game played between the Sacred Gates—and a final result had been reached.
As for what that result was, Ye Tian wasn't entirely clear.
In truth, there wasn't much he understood about the specifics.
He only knew that after the Holy War, the War Gate had obtained many slots to enter the Primordial Ancestral Realm. However, having entry slots only represented an increased opportunity to obtain treasures; it didn't guarantee that one would actually acquire a massive amount of them.
For instance, some factions might only send in one person, but if that person was strong enough, they could still obtain many treasures.
But if there were many people, working together also provided sufficient standing.
However, judging by the tone of several Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, the War Gate's harvest was quite substantial.
Yet, one Invincible Domain Ancestor had fallen, and three Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were heavily injured.
The fallen Invincible Domain Ancestor was also one of the Thirty-Six War Emperors, with strength second only to the Five Thunders Domain Ancestor. Falling in the Primordial Ancestral Realm was simply an unpredictable turn of events.
Perhaps before long, the War Gate would select a new Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor to be ranked among the Thirty-Six War Emperors.
These matters had nothing to do with Ye Tian. What he cared about was another matter—an opportunity belonging to the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
This opportunity was reserved for Paramount Domain Ancestors; only Paramount Domain Ancestors were permitted to enter. If the opportunity was sufficient, it would be enough for Ye Tian to condense a Sacred Soul.
This opportunity was a special world within the Primordial Ancestral Realm—the Sacred Soul Realm! The Sacred Soul Realm was a unique world capable of birthing a type of creature called Sacred Soul Beasts. Once a Sacred Soul Beast was hunted, one could absorb Sacred Soul power to strengthen their own Sacred Soul.
These Sacred Soul Beasts could not be taken out of the Sacred Soul Realm; they could only be born there due to the realm's unique laws.
Additionally, the Sacred Soul Realm produced many treasures and even Sacred Items. Almost all Sacred Items born in the Sacred Soul Realm were soul-type Sacred Items. When paired with a Sacred Soul, they could unleash terrifying soul attacks to annihilate an enemy's soul.
For example, the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit that Ye Tian had consumed previously was a treasure that could also be found within the Sacred Soul Realm.
It was easy to imagine that the Sacred Soul Realm was an ultimate treasure land for Paramount Domain Ancestors.
However, the Primordial Ancestral Realm only opened once in a very long age, and the Sacred Soul Realm would only open along with it.
Previously, many Paramount Domain Ancestors had been striving to strengthen their Soul Sacred Light precisely to prepare for entering the Sacred Soul Realm.
Otherwise, so many factions wouldn't have sent their Paramount Domain Ancestors into the Sacred Monument Worlds of the Profound Void Sacred Realm to strengthen their Soul Sacred Light and increase their power.
The stronger one's power, the better their chances of survival upon entering the Sacred Soul Realm.
Moreover, many Sacred Soul-level Paramount Domain Ancestors from the ten Sacred Gates had likely been suppressing their cultivation, refusing to break through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, just to prepare for this opportunity in the Sacred Soul Realm.
Ye Tian didn't dare claim his strength was the absolute strongest. After all, there were many monstrous geniuses in the Dark Sacred Territory, such as those with Sacred Talents who had already condensed Sacred Souls, coupled with the gifts bestowed by their parents.
They possessed many trump cards and startling secret treasures.
Consequently, they also posed a threat to Ye Tian.
However, once he condensed a Sacred Soul, his strength would skyrocket, and he would fear nothing! "When the Sacred Soul Realm opens, I must go. It's useless for a clone to go and improve Soul Sacred Light; if the main body doesn't improve, my cultivation still won't progress!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
This was also a flaw of the duplicated bodies. If a duplicated body improved its cultivation and the main body improved along with it, how wonderful that would be! However, such things could only be wished for.
Ye Tian needed to head to the Primordial Ancestral Realm. With his main body away from the Profound Void Sacred Realm, he was worried about the safety of the humans there.
Thus, Ye Tian withdrew his duplicated body from the War Ancestral World and then condensed a duplicated body again within the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
This way, the duplicated body would guard the Profound Void Sacred Realm, while his main body left the Profound Void Sacred Realm to head toward the Dark Sacred Territory.
Ye Tian didn't use those treasures for rapid travel. In fact, he could easily afford them, but there was no need to use them.
He spent tens of thousands of years traveling to reach the Dark Sacred Territory and arrived at the War Ancestral World.
There was still some time before the Sacred Soul Realm opened; otherwise, many Paramount Domain Ancestors who were still roaming the Dark Forbidden Territory would also need time to return.
Ye Tian took advantage of this time to enter various treasure lands of the War Gate. Although he had entered these places before, it had always been with a duplicated body, meaning the cultivation gains were essentially wasted.
But now that his main body was here, the cultivation improvements were real and solid!
In the blink of an eye, another several hundred thousand years passed.
By this time, Ye Tian's Soul Sacred Light had improved further, now accounting for roughly twenty-one percent of his total soul.
Such an improvement was quite fast, but it was achieved with the help of many treasure lands and numerous resources. Going forward, many of the War Gate's treasure lands would no longer be of much use to him.
On this day.
An order was issued by the War Gate: Ye Tian's application for a Sacred Monument slot had been approved.
The War Gate governed over a dozen Sacred Realms in total. Combined with the Sacred Monuments in the Ancestral Realm, they possessed quite a few Sacred Monuments. However, over the long ages, the Sacred Monument slots had naturally been carved up until not many remained.
It was also because Ye Tian had previously contributed many slots from the Profound Void Sacred Realm's Sacred Monument to the War Gate that his application was approved this time, though he only obtained one Sacred Monument slot.
This Sacred Monument slot was located in the Red Maple Sacred Realm under the War Gate's jurisdiction. This Sacred Realm was born five Domain Eras ago.
A Domain Era is the time from the birth of an ordinary domain to its Great Destruction, roughly equivalent to one hundred million universe years—a universe year being the lifespan of an ordinary universe.
From this, one could imagine how long five Domain Eras were.
After such a long time, the Sacred Monument slots of the Red Maple Sacred Realm had not yet been exhausted; only three slots remained.
And now, Ye Tian was taking one of them.
The War Gate gave this slot to Ye Tian in the hope that he could increase his strength before entering the Sacred Soul Realm, thereby obtaining more benefits within it.
It would still be a long time before the Sacred Soul Realm opened, so there was enough time for Ye Tian to cultivate in the Red Maple Sacred Realm's Sacred Monument World for a long period.
After entering the Red Maple Sacred Realm, Ye Tian entered its Sacred Monument World and began his cultivation.
Just like that, tens of millions of years passed.
By this time, Ye Tian's Soul Sacred Light had increased significantly.
"The Soul Sacred Light now occupies thirty-three percent of the soul!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Improving from twenty-one percent to thirty-three percent was a massive leap. Although it seemed small compared to his previous progress, it was normal. After all, the stronger the Soul Sacred Light became, the harder it was to increase it further.
If the rate of improvement remained the same, wouldn't four or five Sacred Monument Worlds be enough for a Paramount Domain Ancestor to birth a Sacred Soul? That was impossible!
With a soul as powerful as Ye Tian's, if he wanted to completely transform his soul and birth a Sacred Soul, he would likely need dozens of Sacred Monument Worlds, or even more.
In other words, if not for the opportunity of the Sacred Soul Realm, he would likely need an endless amount of time to birth a Sacred Soul.
It had to be said that Ye Tian's luck was good, as he happened to encounter the opening of the Primordial Ancestral Realm's Sacred Soul Realm!
