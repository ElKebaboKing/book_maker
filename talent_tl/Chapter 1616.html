Chapter 1615 Dark Forbidden Domain finally becomes an Eternal Heaven!

Dark Forbidden Domain.
Endless source sea origin energy surged back into the Dark Forbidden Domain. At this time, the Dark Forbidden Domain was like a balloon rapidly expanding, yet it would not explode, as if it could expand without limit.
And the living beings within the Dark Forbidden Domain were blessed as well; they also obtained tremendous benefits, and their cultivation bases surged one after another.
Almost all the living beings in the Dark Forbidden Domain were human race, and long before Ye Xingchen was assaulting the Supreme Being realm, the Human Race Sacred Hall had already sent out a message that all human race members in other foreign heavens and worlds must enter the Dark Forbidden Domain to enjoy this opportunity.
Thus, the Dark Forbidden Domain was densely packed with humans.
As the Dark Forbidden Domain continued to expand, endless Eternal Continent lands were born, and boundless resources were being nurtured.
The process of the Dark Forbidden Domain advancing into an Eternal Heaven would take a very long time, roughly a great tribulation cycle.
Of course, before the Dark Forbidden Domain was completely stabilized, Supreme Beings could not enter it either.
Moreover, even after it stabilized, the Eternal Heaven would have a protection period, during which only the native Supreme Beings of the Eternal Heaven would be allowed to enter, and outsiders would not be permitted.
This protection period lasted one hundred thousand tribulation eras, meant to prevent other Supreme Beings from entering the newly born Eternal Heaven to plunder resources.
Because newly born Eternal Heavens would give birth to large amounts of Eternal resources, and even many resources that could rapidly enhance the cultivation of Supreme Beings. This was also why the three Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm were so much stronger than the other Supreme Beings.
Because they had occupied the precious resources produced at the birth of the Tianlin Realm, their strength naturally improved very quickly, and they thus stood above the other Supreme Beings.
The Dark Forbidden Domain advancing into an Eternal Heaven could no longer be concealed.
Ye Tian had previously hidden the Dark Forbidden Domain, but now the formation was broken apart as the Dark Forbidden Domain advanced, and it could no longer be hidden.
Some nearby foreign heavens and worlds immediately had Invincible Beings sense the matter of the Dark Forbidden Domain's advancement, and thus the news quickly spread to the Tianlin Realm, which was not far from the Dark Forbidden Domain.
At the first moment, one Supreme Being after another from the Tianlin Realm rushed toward the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Even the three Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm had come.
Tianlin Source Ancestor, Qiuyè Source Ancestor, Jianheng Source Ancestor.
These three Supreme Beings were the undisputed three great Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm. Among them, Tianlin Source Ancestor was a sixth-level Supreme Being, while Qiuyè Source Ancestor and Jianheng Source Ancestor were both fifth-level Supreme Beings. Their strength completely crushed the other Supreme Beings, and they could slaughter an ordinary Supreme Being with a single hand.
……
“Which foreign heaven and world is this?”
Tianlin Source Ancestor asked in confusion.
He only secluded himself for a bit longer, so how did an Eternal Heaven suddenly appear nearby?
“Lord Tianlin Source Ancestor, this should be the Dark Forbidden Domain, under the Endless Ancestor!”
Tianfeng Source God spoke up.
Endless Ancestor was also Ye Tian’s current title. After becoming a Supreme Being, he planned to use the title Endless Ancestor.
Endless Ancestor?
Tianlin Source Ancestor had never heard of this title, but he very much wanted to see him.
“Tianfeng Source God, can you contact Endless Ancestor?” Qiuyè Source God asked.
“Yes!”
Tianfeng Source God immediately contacted Ye Tian.
In truth, Ye Tian had been paying close attention to the situation in the Dark Forbidden Domain from the shadows. After receiving Tianfeng Source God’s transmission, he immediately came out.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian walked out from the void and looked at the many Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm.
In the past, he could be considered to belong to the Tianlin Realm, but now he belonged to the Dark Forbidden Domain, this Eternal Heaven.
“Fifth-level Supreme Being!!!!”
Tianfeng Source God widened his eyes, somewhat unable to believe it.
It had only been how long since he last saw Ye Tian, and Ye Tian had already advanced to this realm?
How difficult was it for a Supreme Being to raise their realm? Which one of them did not spend countless tribulation eras, and some even remained forever stuck in one realm without being able to break through.
Yet Ye Tian, without a sound, had advanced to the peak of fifth-level Supreme Being. It seemed that before long, he would have the hope of advancing to sixth-level Supreme Being.
Tianlin Source Ancestor looked at Ye Tian and felt a fatal threat.
In his perception, Ye Tian was very strong, stronger than him.
“Impossible, he is only a fifth-level Supreme Being. How could he be stronger than me? It should be an illusion!”
Tianlin Source Ancestor thought inwardly.
However, he would not underestimate Ye Tian. He greeted him very politely: “Greetings, Endless Ancestor!”
“And you are...”
Ye Tian did not know how to address him. Fortunately, Tianfeng Source God introduced him to Ye Tian, letting Ye Tian learn Tianlin Source Ancestor’s title.
“Greetings, Tianlin Source Ancestor!”
Ye Tian said politely.
Tianlin Source Ancestor smiled and said, “Endless Ancestor, our two Eternal Heavens are relatively close. We can cooperate more in the future.”
“Naturally!”
Ye Tian nodded slightly.
Then the two chatted for a while.
Of course, Tianlin Source Ancestor did not stay long. He only came to take a look and, incidentally, confirm the situation here.
He did not want to become enemies with Ye Tian either; there was no benefit in that.
It was fortunate that he did not have such an idea, or he would have been in great despair.
Unknowingly, a great tribulation cycle passed.
At this time, the Dark Forbidden Domain had completely become a side of Eternal Heaven.
And Ye Tian’s family members all returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain. Although Ye Tian’s true body had not returned, his clone body had come back.
Ye Xingchen and Ye Jinpeng, these two Supreme Beings, naturally also returned, and some Invincible Beings of the human race in the Tianlin Realm also returned to the Eternal Heaven. Even some Invincible Beings who had joined Tianfeng Sea were not retained by Tianfeng Sea; Tianfeng Source God did not dare retain them either!
Since the Dark Forbidden Domain had only recently advanced into an Eternal Heaven, the entire human race was still somewhat chaotic.
Thus, the upper echelons of the human race were dividing regions and managing the human race separately. Many, many human race members obtained large tracts of fiefdoms.
Moreover, the human race encouraged the establishment of various forces, using those forces to manage the many regions. Otherwise, relying solely on the Human Race Sacred Hall to manage everything simply would not be enough.
It took as many as several dozen great tribulation cycles before the Dark Forbidden Domain was finally completely stabilized.
After that, Ye Tian personally established countless teleportation formations for the human race, otherwise ordinary teleportation formations simply could not be used to teleport within an Eternal Heaven. In addition, an Eternal Heaven was simply too vast; unless one had reached the level of Ancestor, there was no way to fly very far at all.
With the Dark Forbidden Domain stabilized, Ye Tian no longer needed to worry about it.
After that, he began collecting all kinds of rare resources in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
His countless clones were scattered throughout the Dark Forbidden Domain, and whenever important resources were born, he would take them away.
Most of the resources were of no use to him at all, but they could be stored in the Sacred Hall. A small portion was given to Ye Jinpeng and Ye Xingchen and the others to use; they had only just advanced to first-level Supreme Being realm.
They were in urgent need of these resources, and Ye Jinpeng and Ye Xingchen no longer cultivated either; they were all searching for resources suitable for Supreme Beings to use.
It could be said that this era was their best era, and also their opportunity.
In this way, the Dark Forbidden Domain ushered in an era of rapid human race development, and countless Invincible Beings were born.
