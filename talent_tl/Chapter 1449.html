Chapter 1448: The Terrifying Talent of the Nirvana Clan
!Copy!
After understanding the situation here and the strength of the Nirvana Clan, Ye Tian felt nothing but solemnity.
The Nirvana Clan's strength was too terrifying, and they were very difficult to kill because once a member of the Nirvana Clan was too heavily injured, they could simply retreat.
Furthermore, the Nirvana Clan could absorb Nirvana energy during battle, whereas these Domain Ancestors had no way to absorb energy. In a long-term battle, those of the same level simply couldn't defeat the Nirvana Clan.
Additionally, their consumption was too great. If they didn't have the resources of an entire Dark Forbidden Domain as backup, they wouldn't be able to sustain such endless ages of fighting.
"War Ancestor Wanfa, your mission is... to guard this area. Once the Nirvana Clan invades from this area, as long as the opponent's strength does not exceed your limit, you cannot flee, nor can you call for help, understood?"
War Ancestor Blood Dragon said.
"I understand!"
Ye Tian nodded.
A few days later, Ye Tian flew out of the Sanctuary Continent and headed to an area in front of the Spacetime Corridor.
There were a total of six Supreme-level Domain Ancestors stationed in this area, the strongest of whom was at the peak of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor stage.
But this... peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor was not a cultivator, but a Taboo Creature.
When Ye Tian arrived here and saw this Taboo Creature, he was instantly shocked.
He recognized this... Taboo Creature at the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor level.
"Ancestor Peng!"
Ye Tian called out the other's name.
Before he had become a Half-step Domain Ancestor, the Profound Void Sacred Realm had not yet appeared. At that time, the world was called the Profound Void Ancient Realm, which was not even as good as a Domain Realm. In a Taboo Treasure Land, Ancestor Peng's consciousness had descended upon a Taboo Treasure.
He had fought with Ancestor Peng's phantom, and in the end, Ancestor Peng, seeing his high potential, passed on the Extreme Path Inheritance to him.
To the current him, the Extreme Path Inheritance was nothing, but back then his strength was very weak, and the Extreme Path Inheritance had been of immense help to him.
Therefore, Ancestor Peng had shown him kindness.
"Greetings, Ancestor Peng!"
Ye Tian said.
"Oh? Looking at your eyes, you know me?"
Ancestor Peng asked curiously.
"Ancestor Peng, you once taught me the Extreme Path Inheritance, do you remember?"
Ye Tian asked.
"Extreme Path Inheritance?"
Ancestor Peng thought back for a moment and finally remembered Ye Tian. "You are that... little fellow from some small realm? Back then, I saw you were somewhat interesting; in a place like a small realm, you could actually rival the geniuses in the Dark Sanctuary, so I casually gave you a copy of the Extreme Path Inheritance. I didn't expect you to grow to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor so quickly!"
At this moment, Ancestor Peng was incredibly shocked.
He had praised Ye Tian back then, saying that Ye Tian had a chance to become a being like him.
But that was just a joke.
At that time, Ye Tian's talent only gave him a bit of interest, but there were countless people who surpassed Ye Tian. Some Sacred Talent possessors were incredibly monstrous from birth, yet in the end, they still couldn't break through to become Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
Therefore, he didn't think Ye Tian had a chance to become a being like himself.
If calculated by probability, Ye Tian probably only had a one-in-a-billion chance of becoming a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor like him.
But now, Ye Tian had done it, and it hadn't even taken that long.
"Ancestor Peng's Extreme Path Inheritance laid a good foundation for me back then, thank you, Ancestor Peng!"
Ye Tian said gratefully.
"Hahaha, I just did it on a whim!"
Ancestor Peng laughed.
Immediately, the human and the Taboo Creature chatted for a long time before parting ways, returning to their respective posts to guard the area at all times and prevent any Nirvana Clan members from sneaking in.
Time slowly passed. Ye Tian guarded this place for tens of thousands of years without encountering a single Nirvana Clan member. However, the Nirvana Clan appeared in other places, but he couldn't leave his post, so he didn't go to watch.
One day.
While Ye Tian was cultivating, he suddenly sensed a special fluctuation.
"The Nirvana Clan is here; it's a Nirvana Clan squad."
Ancestor Peng's voice came through.
Boom!!! More than twenty Nirvana Clan members surged forward, led by a Six-Mark Nirvana Ancestor.
A Six-Mark Nirvana Ancestor was comparable to a top-tier existence among Half-step Primordial Ancestors. Ancestor Peng was far from being a match; once it charged over, they would be finished.
Suddenly.
A figure emerged; it was a Half-step Primordial Ancestor who had arrived just in time—War Ancestor Vajra, a Half-step Primordial Ancestor from the Myriad Things Sacred Sect.
Rumble!!! War Ancestor Vajra's strength was terrifying, far exceeding that of an ordinary Half-step Primordial Ancestor. He immediately blocked that Seven-Mark Nirvana Ancestor.
Meanwhile, Ancestor Peng blocked two Four-Mark Nirvana Clan members, and the other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors each chose two or more Nirvana Clan members to fight. After all, they were outnumbered, which was unavoidable, and nearby Supreme-level Domain Ancestors also arrived to support them in time.
Ye Tian chose one Two-Mark Nirvana Clan member and three One-Mark Nirvana Clan members.
But during the exchange, Ye Tian's Copy Talent enveloped them.
Nirvana Clan: Cha Bai. Ancestral Talents: Strength Ancestral Talent, Speed Ancestral Talent, Defense Ancestral Talent, Nirvana Ancestral Talent, Time Ancestral Talent, Space Ancestral Talent... Seeing such talents, Ye Tian couldn't help but secretly exclaim how heaven-defying they were.
With so many Ancestral Talents, no wonder they were such monsters.
In the area covered by his Copy Talent, the Nirvana Clan members he inspected all possessed these Ancestral Talents; they were all exactly the same.
And Ye Tian didn't even have the Time Ancestral Talent.
These Nirvana Clan members didn't have comprehension-type Ancestral Talents, so their comprehension was likely not high. Otherwise, with so many Ancestral Talents, each of them could be as monstrous as he was.
"Time Ancestral Talent, I don't even have it, and now I can copy it directly!"
Ye Tian immediately copied the Time Ancestral Talent.
Furthermore, he also copied the Nirvana Ancestral Talent. Although he didn't know what it was used for, copying it couldn't be wrong.
As for the other Ancestral Talents, he thought about it and copied them all as well, in case they could be fused into higher-level talents in the future. Boom!!! The battle began.
Ye Tian did not hide his strength; hiding his strength here was useless.
He directly unleashed the Eternal Imperishable Ancestral Body and performed the Void Slash Ancestral Technique.
Immediately, his Void Slash Ancestral Technique struck one of the One-Mark Nirvana Clan members, directly annihilating its body.
"Not good, he is an existence comparable to a top-tier Supreme-level Domain Ancestor!"
That Two-Mark Nirvana Clan member said in terror.
Instantly killing a One-Mark Nirvana Ancestor with one move was something only an existence like Ancestor Peng could do.
They had been careless. They didn't expect that Ye Tian's cultivation was only at the early stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm, yet his combat power was so terrifying, leading to the direct fall of a One-Mark clansman.
