Chapter 1548: A Golden Mountain That Cannot Be Spied Upon!

With Ye Tian's strength, finding the Yuhua World was not difficult. The Yuhua World was bound to be not very far from the Blood Mist World, so as long as he toured all the surrounding foreign worlds, he would have hope of finding it.
Thus, Ye Tian began to cross one foreign world after another.
Time passed slowly, and before he knew it, several hundred million years had gone by. During this period, Ye Tian searched many foreign worlds and conducted investigations, which took quite a bit of time.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian descended upon a foreign world.
This foreign world had almost no living beings and was incredibly silent. However, through his understanding of the Yuhua World and an Origin Rule Tribulation Crystal of the Yuhua World obtained from within the Blood Mist Alliance...
At this moment, Ye Tian briefly contemplated the rules of this foreign world and found them to be exactly the same as that Origin Rule Tribulation Crystal.
Clearly, this was the Yuhua World.
Soon, Ye Tian found a supreme powerhouse and captured him.
"Speak, where is Ancestor Mingli Mist?"
Ye Tian said coldly.
That supreme powerhouse clearly recognized Ye Tian and couldn't help but roar, "Wanfa Ancestor, do not be so arrogant! Ancestor Mingli Mist is right here. I have already notified him; he will definitely kill you!"
In his eyes, Ancestor Mingli Mist was clearly an invincible existence who only needed to guard this place and was temporarily unable to leave, which was why Ye Tian had been able to invade the Blood Mist Alliance.
But he had no idea of Ye Tian's terror or Ancestor Mingli Mist's wariness; otherwise, how would he dare to speak like that!
...
Somewhere in the Yuhua World.
Ancestor Mingli Mist received the message from his subordinate, and his expression shifted instantly.
In reality, he had vaguely sensed an Invincible descending upon the Yuhua World, but he didn't dare to investigate for fear of attracting that Invincible's attention.
He had hoped this Invincible was just passing through, but after receiving this message, he knew who the visitor was.
Wanfa Ancestor, Ye Tian!
"Damn it, he actually found his way here!" Ancestor Mingli Mist said nervously.
He really did not want to fight Ye Tian. Although Ye Tian had killed three Invincibles of the Blood Mist Alliance, he didn't feel much anger. Compared to the opportunity in the Yuhua World, the fall of three Invincibles was nothing.
Once he obtained this opportunity and his strength rose to a fourth-grade or even third-grade Invincible, killing Ye Tian would naturally be an easy task.
But now, he didn't want any accidents to occur.
However, it seemed he could no longer hide.
An Invincible's divine sense was far from being able to probe an entire foreign world, but there were many ways to detect powerhouses within one. Unless he had an extremely powerful secret technique to hide himself, he would be unable to stay hidden.
Just then, Ye Tian took out a compass-like treasure. This was a special item purchased from the Tianfeng Sea that could detect any hidden living beings within a foreign world. Even an Invincible would find it hard to evade this compass's detection.
Buzz!!!!
The compass released a wave that covered the entire foreign world at extreme speed.
"Found him!"
Ye Tian's gaze locked onto an Invincible's position through the compass. Then, with a single thought, he pierced through time and space to look toward that infinitely distant land.
Boom!!!!
With one step across time and space, he arrived at the place where Ancestor Mingli Mist was located.
Here was a golden mountain, and upon the mountain stood a palace. Ancestor Mingli Mist was inside that palace.
"Eh, this golden mountain..."
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
When he had pierced through time and space to spy on this place from afar just now, he hadn't noticed this golden mountain, yet now it had appeared.
Immediately, he used his divine sense to probe the golden mountain, only to find that the space in front of him was empty. Where was any golden mountain?
"No, it's that divine sense and all forms of scrying techniques are unable to observe this golden mountain. It can only be seen with the naked eye at close range!" Ye Tian said in shock.
Once upon a time, when he was still relatively weak, he had encountered similar treasures, but that was when he was very weak.
Now, he had already reached the level of a fifth-grade Invincible, yet he still couldn't spy on this golden mountain. This showed just how precious this mountain was, or perhaps it was related to some staggering treasure to have such an effect.
At this moment, he completely understood.
Why Ancestor Mingli Mist didn't show himself, and why he wanted to destroy those gates to prevent the Yuhua World from being exposed—it was all for the opportunity here.
The opportunity here was enough to make even an Invincible go mad, willing to hide it at any cost.
"This opportunity is great, but now it's mine!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Boom!!!!
Ancestor Mingli Mist walked out of the palace, his gaze looking toward Ye Tian with complexity.
··········
"Wanfa Ancestor, I am surprised. I thought you were just a newly ascended Invincible, but I didn't expect you to already be a fifth-grade Invincible!" Ancestor Mingli Mist said indifferently.
"Ancestor Mingli Mist, what is this golden mountain?"
Ye Tian pointed at the golden mountain and asked.
"It is merely a small place of opportunity!" Ancestor Mingli Mist replied. "Wanfa Ancestor, since you have already become a fifth-grade Invincible, you should have visited the Eternal World known as the Tianlin Realm. I am a member of the Nether Corridor, a super-tier power in the Tianlin Realm. The Nether Corridor possesses many second-grade and third-grade Invincibles, ranking within the top hundred among the countless super-tier powers in the Tianlin Realm. Have you thought it through? Are you willing to offend such a colossus for a small opportunity?"
...
Ancestor Mingli Mist did not want to fight Ye Tian, so he brought up the Nether Corridor to intimidate him.
However.
Ye Tian gave a cold laugh and said, "Nether Corridor? Is it very powerful? I happen to be a high-level member of the Tianfeng Sea!"
"The Saint-tier Tianfeng Sea? Don't try to fool me!" Ancestor Mingli Mist didn't believe Ye Tian's words at all.
At that moment, Ye Tian took out his badge belonging to the Tianfeng Sea and said, "This is my Tianfeng Sea badge. If you still don't believe it, then it doesn't matter. In short, today you must die!"
Ancestor Mingli Mist started to believe him, but he wouldn't admit it.
Moreover, it was impossible for him to give up this opportunity.
"Wanfa Ancestor should have only just ascended to a fifth-grade Invincible. Furthermore, the Yuhua World is currently not in a Great Tribulation period, so he is being suppressed by the world's rules. His strength is greatly diminished; I still have hope of winning!" Ancestor Mingli Mist thought to himself.
Immediately, he made his move!
Ye Tian also moved at the same time.
As soon as he struck, Ye Tian unleashed a top-tier Invincible Ancestral Technique—the Heavenly Pole Combat Technique.
Kill!!!!!
Boom!!!!
The void trembled as the two fifth-grade Invincibles immediately clashed in a life-and-death struggle.
The moment they engaged, Ye Tian discovered that Ancestor Mingli Mist's Invincible Tribulation Body was too fragile, far inferior to his own.
Clearly, Ancestor Mingli Mist had never cultivated an Invincible Tribulation Body manual, which was why he was so fragile.
Even though Ye Tian was suppressed by the rules of the Yuhua World and could only exert a small portion of his strength, he still easily crushed Ancestor Mingli Mist.
After a few moves, Ancestor Mingli Mist's Invincible Tribulation Body was blasted apart by Ye Tian.
