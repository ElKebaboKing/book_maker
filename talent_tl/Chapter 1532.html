Chapter 1531: Instantly Killed by a Calamity Beast!

After breaking through to become an Invincible Ancestor, Ye Tian's perception broke through the shackles of the Dark Forbidden Domain, and he could even vaguely sense the regions beyond it.
"That is..."
Ye Tian sensed the aura of the Source, but it was an unowned Source, and an incredibly chaotic one at that.
At this moment, Ye Tian clearly realized that he could tear through the void of the Dark Forbidden Domain and travel to the regions outside.
This was an instinct.
Back when he was a Physical Calamity Realm powerhouse, no matter how strong he was, he never had this feeling. This was an ability unique to Invincible beings.
Immediately, Ye Tian's Invincible Ancestor clone tore through the Dark Forbidden Domain and stepped into the region beyond.
*Whoosh!*
As Ye Tian stepped into the ocean of chaotic Source, he immediately realized where he was.
The Source Sea!
The Source Sea was the origin of all Sources. Foreign worlds were born from the Source Sea and annihilated within it. Aside from Invincible beings and those above them, no other living creatures could survive in the Source Sea, nor could they even reach it.
The Source Sea rejected all beings below the Invincible level. Even a powerhouse, even with the help of an Invincible being, could not traverse foreign worlds to reach the Source Sea; they would be directly repelled.
Standing in the Source Sea, although Ye Tian could survive, his strength was heavily suppressed, as if he had instantly become an ordinary being, unable to exert any powerful offensive force.
Of course, the same applied to other beings.
Ye Tian instinctively tried to absorb the Source within the Source Sea, but found he couldn't. The Source here was not only chaotic but also acted as a single entity; a mere Invincible being could not absorb the Source of the Source Sea at all.
It was at this moment that Ye Tian sensed carefully and actually felt a calling—a call coming from the Eternal Heaven and Earth.
"Too far!"
Ye Tian shook his head.
If he traveled through the Source Sea, his current speed was too slow, and the Eternal Heaven and Earth was too far away. It would likely take an incredibly long time to reach it.
Therefore, traveling through the Source Sea while his strength was still relatively low was not a wise choice. Furthermore, the Source Sea was not necessarily without danger, and he didn't dare to cross it recklessly.
However, having vaguely sensed the location of the Eternal Heaven and Earth, he could tear through the void from various foreign worlds into the Source Sea to map out a route.
Basically, the positions of foreign worlds connected to one another within the Source Sea were not too distant, so it was possible to find a route given enough time. Then, he could traverse through foreign worlds one by one and eventually reach the Eternal Heaven and Earth.
Moreover, an Invincible being did not need to rely on Second-Order Gates. As long as they knew the coordinates of another foreign world, they could tear open a temporary gate themselves to reach it.
For example, the Invincible beings of the six Super-Famous level forces wouldn't need a gate if they wanted to enter the Dark Forbidden Domain. They could directly tear through the barriers between two foreign worlds with their true bodies to cross over, though it would consume some of their power.
After becoming an Invincible Ancestor, Ye Tian went into seclusion for a period to stabilize his realm.
Afterward, he began refining various resources to increase his cultivation, but found the progress too slow.
It was as if those resources had suddenly become useless to him.
Upon careful thought, Ye Tian understood.
An Invincible Ancestor had almost surpassed the scope of a single foreign world itself, so the resources born within that world had very little effect on him. Now, aside from Calamity Stones and Calamity Blood, other resources were of no use to him.
If he could enter the Eternal Heaven and Earth, many of the resources there should still be of great help to him.
"The Calamity Stones in my hands are exhausted. To significantly increase my cultivation, a mere few tens of thousands of Calamity Stones are definitely not enough. First-Calamity blood probably won't help much either. Only the blood from a Second-Calamity beast or an even stronger one can significantly boost my cultivation!"
Ye Tian muttered.
Calamity Stones were too difficult to find. Unless he could endure long ages, waiting for foreign worlds to face the end of their Calamity Eras and be destroyed, he wouldn't be able to collect enough. Other Invincible beings might be able to wait, but his cultivation speed was too fast. The time he had spent cultivating didn't even amount to a fraction of theirs, so he didn't have the patience to wait.
Therefore, he currently had only one option: to hunt Calamity Beasts and obtain their blood to increase his cultivation. This blood could even help the human race produce more Physical Calamity Realm powerhouses.
It was difficult for powerhouses to find Calamity Beasts, but it wasn't so hard for an Invincible being.
One day.
Ye Tian condensed an Invincible Ancestor duplicate body and tore through the barrier between the Dark Forbidden Domain and the Yuanmo Heaven and Earth. He descended into the Yuanmo Heaven and Earth because it was currently in the midst of a Great Calamity, so his duplicate's strength would not be suppressed.
Next, Ye Tian's thoughts transformed into individual thought-clones, heading to various parts of the Yuanmo Heaven and Earth to search for the Calamity Beast's location.
After reaching the Invincible Ancestor level, his detection range and mastery over the void far exceeded those of a powerhouse. Thus, he had a ninety percent chance of finding the Calamity Beast's hiding place before the Great Calamity ended.
Time passed slowly.
Finally, as the Great Calamity of the Yuanmo Heaven and Earth was nearing its end, Ye Tian found the Calamity Beast's sleeping place.
Then, Ye Tian's duplicate body descended, tearing through the space-time node with a single strike and discovering the Calamity Beast.
*Boom!!!*
The Calamity Beast's slumber was disturbed. It woke up and glared at Ye Tian in rage.
Then, the beast attacked.
It was relatively easy to identify the strength of a Calamity Beast. A First-Calamity beast had only one Calamity Mark, but this beast possessed 1,795 marks, meaning it was a 1,795-Calamity beast.
This meant that the Yuanmo Heaven and Earth had been destroyed at least 1,795 times, leading to the birth of such a beast.
Ye Tian didn't know exactly how powerful a 1,795-Calamity beast was, but he was about to find out.
The Calamity Beast fixed its gaze on Ye Tian, and he found himself unable to move.
Then, the beast lunged with a claw.
With a *bang*, Ye Tian's duplicate body was instantly killed without any power to resist.
Back in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian's main body sensed the fall of his duplicate and exclaimed in shock, "The Calamity Beast of the Yuanmo Heaven and Earth is actually this powerful!!!"
He finally understood why the Invincible beings of the six Super-Famous level forces didn't hunt Calamity Beasts themselves—because they were too terrifying.
This was a beast that had only survived over a thousand Calamity Eras; how terrifying would one be if it had survived a hundred thousand?.
