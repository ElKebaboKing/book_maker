Chapter 1532: A Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast!

"Fortunately, I sent a duplicate body. If my main body had gone looking for the Calamity Beast, I would definitely be dead!"
Ye Tian felt a surge of relief.
Other Invincible Ones did not possess duplicate bodies like his. They could only enter otherworld realms in their true forms to search for Calamity Beasts. If they were unlucky enough to encounter a relatively powerful Calamity Beast, they wouldn't even have time to escape.
For instance, the Calamity Beast from just now possessed strength far superior to Ye Tian's. A single glance had pinned his body in place, rendering him unable to move.
According to Ye Tian's estimation, that Calamity Beast's strength was at least comparable to a Third-tier Invincible Ancestor. Compared to it, his strength as a Sixth-tier Invincible Ancestor was practically like that of an ant.
"I have no choice but to give up on the Yuanmo Realm!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He selected another otherworld realm. In any case, the fall of a duplicate body only meant a loss of some resources, which was nothing significant. If he got lucky and encountered a relatively weak Calamity Beast—for example, one below the ten-calamity level—he would have a certain hope of slaying it.
As an Invincible Ancestor, Ye Tian could easily enter various otherworld realms without worrying about being obliterated by their foreign laws.
Each otherworld realm was connected to many others, and countless such realms linked together to form an otherworld realm network.
Therefore, within a very short period, he could find many otherworld realms and determine what stage they were in. Once these realms entered a Great Calamity period, he would enter them to search for Calamity Beasts.
Essentially, every short interval, an otherworld realm would enter a Great Calamity period, and Ye Tian would take action.
It had to be said that weak Calamity Beasts were still very rare. The birth of a One-Calamity Beast in the Dark Forbidden Zone was an incredibly rare occurrence. It was precisely because it was so rare that the supreme experts of the six super-tier forces had set their sights on it.
However, the Invincible Ones looked down on a mere One-Calamity Beast, which was why they hadn't come to the Dark Forbidden Zone to hunt it.
A Calamity Beast that had experienced too few Calamity Eras was not only weak in strength but also contained very little Calamity Power in its Calamity Blood. Naturally, it wasn't of much help to Invincible Ones.
Ye Tian tried time and time again, repeatedly encountering powerful Calamity Beasts. Most of those he met were over a thousand calamities, and occasionally he even encountered those over ten thousand calamities.
Of course, there was one time he encountered a beast of over a hundred calamities, but he was still far from being its match. The opponent's combat power was at least comparable to a top-tier Fifth-tier Invincible Ancestor and could easily inflict heavy injuries on him.
There were also several otherworld realms that surprisingly had no Calamity Beasts. Clearly, the beasts had been hunted, and since a new Calamity Era hadn't passed yet, no new Calamity Beast had been born.
He discovered that these realms had many "Gates," even several Second-tier Gates. Obviously, various forces were keeping a close eye on these special realms, waiting for them to enter a new Calamity Era and birth a new Calamity Beast before striking.
Unknowingly, the Dark Forbidden Zone had already passed six Dark Calendars, with only three remaining before the arrival of the Great Calamity.
Regarding the Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Zone, he was no longer worried. Instead, he continued his search for Calamity Beasts.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian found a Calamity Beast that wasn't overwhelmingly powerful.
A Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast. Its strength was only equivalent to a Sixth-tier Invincible Ancestor, but it was comparable to the very peak of that tier, not much weaker than a Fifth-tier Invincible Ancestor.
Ye Tian was naturally not its match, but he was able to leave easily.
A Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast was quite rare, and its strength was relatively weak, meaning there was hope of hunting it.
However, he couldn't hunt it alone.
Even if his main body, duplicate body, and Invincible Ancestor avatar all attacked together, they wouldn't be able to kill a Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast.
"I can only find people to cooperate with!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Currently, only two forbidden lands of super-tier forces remained in the Dark Forbidden Zone: the Firmament Sword Tomb and the Calamity Black Mountain.
As for the other four super-tier forces, they had long since destroyed their Second-tier Gates. Although Ye Tian knew which otherworld realms their headquarters were located in, he didn't want to go looking for them.
The more Invincible Ones involved in the cooperation, the smaller his own harvest would be.
Therefore, he only planned to cooperate with the Firmament Sword Tomb and the Calamity Black Mountain. These two forces were powerful enough; only by cooperating with them would there be hope of slaying that Calamity Beast.
If he cooperated with the other four super-tier forces, they might not be able to handle that beast.
------------
Dark Forbidden Zone, Firmament Sword Tomb Forbidden Land.
Ye Tian arrived here and directly asked the supreme experts guarding the place to contact their Invincible One.
Soon, an Invincible One from the Firmament Sword Tomb descended.
Upon his arrival, the Invincible One was incredibly surprised to see that Ye Tian had reached the Invincible Ancestor realm.
"I didn't expect that not long after this Dark Forbidden Zone was born, an Invincible Ancestor would emerge, and one who achieved it in such a short time. It's truly unexpected!" the Firmament Sword Tomb Invincible One—Shangxu Sword Ancestor—spoke.
"Just good luck!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Shangxu Sword Ancestor added, "Since an Invincible One has been born in your Dark Forbidden Zone, our Firmament Sword Tomb will naturally show respect. We will withdraw this Second-tier Gate immediately."
He assumed this was Ye Tian's purpose and spoke preemptively.
However, Ye Tian shook his head and said, "Shangxu Sword Ancestor, my purpose in coming to you is not that. Rather, I wish to cooperate with your Firmament Sword Tomb to slay a Calamity Beast!"
"Slay a Calamity Beast?" Shangxu Sword Ancestor was slightly startled. "Calamity Beasts are very powerful. Many are even comparable to Fourth-tier, Third-tier, or even Second-tier Invincible Ones. We wouldn't dare go looking for them; there's a ninety percent chance of being killed instantly."
Ye Tian quickly said, "Shangxu Sword Ancestor, I have intelligence on the beast's strength. This time, we are dealing with a Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast. I am not its match alone. I wonder if your Firmament Sword Tomb is interested?"
"A Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast?"
Shangxu Sword Ancestor became interested.
Although a Calamity Beast of this level was powerful and infinitely close to a Fifth-tier Invincible One, there was at least a hope of slaying it.
"I... our Firmament Sword Tomb agrees!" Shangxu Sword Ancestor consented.
"I will also seek cooperation from the Calamity Black Mountain," Ye Tian added.
"No problem!" Shangxu Sword Ancestor nodded. "The leader of our Firmament Sword Tomb is in an Eternal Realm and cannot be contacted for the time being. Cooperating with the Calamity Black Mountain will give us a greater certainty of slaying that beast. However, can you ensure your intelligence is correct?"
"Naturally. I went there personally; how could it be false? When the time comes, I will strike first, and you can follow behind. If the intelligence is wrong, you can withdraw immediately!" Ye Tian guaranteed.
"Then I have no issues!"
Shangxu Sword Ancestor said.
Subsequently, Ye Tian contacted the Calamity Black Mountain. After speaking with their Invincible One, they were also willing to take action.
After all, the value of a Thirty-Seven Calamity Calamity Beast far exceeded that of a One-Calamity Beast. Even for Invincible Ones, it held great value.
Ye Tian chose a date and united eight Invincible Ones from the two super-tier forces, Firmament Sword Tomb and Calamity Black Mountain. Adding in Ye Tian's two Invincible-level combatants—his duplicate body and his Invincible Ancestor avatar—it was equivalent to a total of ten Invincible Ones being deployed.
