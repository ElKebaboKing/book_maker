Chapter 1517: Slaying Supreme-level Beings!

Dark Forbidden Domain.
Dozens of forbidden lands, from each of which a large number of foreign powerhouses emerged, entering various parts of the Dark Forbidden Domain to wipe out countless living beings.
Even the Three Thousand Evil Tribes of the Dark Destruction Domain were like ants before these foreign powerhouses, possessing no power to resist at all.
The Three Thousand Evil Tribes lived for destruction, but now, they were being destroyed by foreign powerhouses.
Dark Destruction Domain, Dark Sacred Domain, Dark Soul Domain...
Countless beings in one Dark Sacred Land after another were wiped out, and endless resources were taken by these foreign powerhouses.
The entire Dark Forbidden Domain was filled with slaughter; or rather, the creatures of the Dark Forbidden Domain were being massacred. Even ordinary Ancestors would be easily struck down if discovered by foreign powerhouses.
In a short time, almost all ordinary creatures in the Dark Forbidden Domain were killed, and many Ancestors had also fallen.
Outside the Daoshan Realm.
An Ancestor wanted to flee into the Human Race's territory, but was blocked by the Human Race's grand array.
"Ancestor Wanfa, save me!"
One Ancestor cried out for help.
However, the Human Race's Ancestors had no intention of saving him.
After all, the most important thing for the Human Race was to survive this Great Tribulation. Once they passed it, the Human Race was almost certain to rise and soar.
Therefore, nothing else mattered; surviving the Great Tribulation was the only priority.
This Ancestor had no connection to the Human Race, so they naturally wouldn't offend a foreign power for his sake.
Boom!!!!
A Supreme-level foreign powerhouse descended and killed that Ancestor with a single hand.
This Supreme-level foreign powerhouse looked at the Human Race's territory in this area of the Daoshan Realm, saw the densely packed arrays, hesitated for a moment, and did not act immediately, instead summoning other foreign powerhouses.
Previously, although Ye Tian had broken into many forbidden lands, there were some he had never entered. Thus, the powers behind those forbidden domains had no idea of Ye Tian's strength.
Foreign powerhouses who knew of Ye Tian's strength wouldn't dare invade the Human Race's territory after making even a slight inquiry.
But foreign powers ignorant of Ye Tian's strength, seeing the Human Race's territory, assumed the resources here were abundant and naturally wanted to invade.
This Supreme-level foreign powerhouse belonged to a Great Name-level foreign power—Chaotian Pavilion.
Soon, powerhouses comparable to Supreme Ancestors arrived outside the Human Race's territory.
In a short while, nine Supreme-level beings gathered here, including one at the second tier of the Supreme level.
"Long Mo, why have you summoned us here?"
A Supreme-level being from Chaotian Pavilion asked.
The Supreme-level powerhouse named Long Mo spoke: "This is the territory of the Human Race, the strongest power in the Dark Forbidden Domain. I suspect they have Supreme Ancestors among them, so I did not act immediately!"
"The Human Race? This power didn't exist during the last Great Tribulation. It must be a newly risen power. Within the span of a single Great Tribulation era, it's difficult to even leave the Dark Forbidden Domain. Even with opportunities, birthing one Supreme Ancestor is likely the limit! And any Supreme Ancestor born would likely have inherited a legacy to advance, making their combat power weak and not worth mentioning!"
"Exactly, what does a mere Human Race amount to? With our Chaotian Pavilion's strength, we can easily wipe them out!"
"Let's act quickly. If the six Ultra Name-level powers like Calamity Black Mountain take action, we won't even get a scrap of the benefits!"
These Supreme-level beings discussed briefly and decided to attack the Human Race.
Rumble!!!!!
One Supreme-level aura after another spread out, enveloping the entire Human Race territory.
Under the pressure of these Supreme-level beings, the arrays began to tremble.
These Supreme-level powerhouses didn't want to wipe out the Human Race all at once, otherwise, many resources would be destroyed along with them, which would be a great loss.
Therefore, they planned to first kill the Human Race's Ancestors, then wipe out the souls of countless clansmen with a single thought. That way, they could collect the Human Race's resources and treasures without damaging them.
However, just as they were attacking the Human Race's arrays, a towering figure appeared outside the territory.
Ye Tian stood there, his gaze fixed on the powerhouses from Chaotian Pavilion.
Their attacks were directly blocked by Ye Tian, failing to even touch the Human Race's arrays.
In the next moment.
Ye Tian struck. Combining the Tyrant Strength Supreme Ancestral Technique with the Extreme Arm Supreme Ancestral Technique, he threw a punch.
This punch seemed to extinguish everything, causing the Supreme-level beings of Chaotian Pavilion to fall into terror.
"Run!"
They were terrified.
Ye Tian's strike was too terrifying, exceeding their imagination. They had never seen such a terrifying powerhouse; even a third-tier Supreme-level being probably wouldn't have such a powerful aura.
Crack, crack, crack!!!!
One Supreme-level being after another had their bodies shattered by Ye Tian's punch, completely annihilated.
In the blink of an eye, only one second-tier Supreme-level being remained.
This Supreme-level being wanted to tear through space-time to escape, but Ye Tian had already sealed it.
Unable to escape through space-time, he could only rely on speed. However, how could his speed match Ye Tian's? He was easily overtaken.
"Eternal Supreme Ancestral Technique!"
Terrifying time rules enveloped this second-tier Supreme-level being from Chaotian Pavilion. Under the time-type Eternal Supreme Ancestral Technique, time was frozen. Although he could perceive the solidification of time, he couldn't react. His entire body was as if pinned, uncontrollable.
In the next moment.
Ye Tian's palm descended, directly slaying this Supreme-level being.
With this, the Supreme-level beings of Chaotian Pavilion were completely wiped out.
This was naturally a massive loss for Chaotian Pavilion; it was unknown how long it would take for them to recover their strength.
After slaying these Supreme-level beings from Chaotian Pavilion, Ye Tian collected the treasures on their bodies.
"Oh? There are actually three Source Rule Calamity Crystals!"
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
These Source Rule Calamity Crystals were naturally not from the Sacred Domain Heaven and Earth, but from other foreign worlds. They were carried by these Supreme-level beings, who never expected to fall in the Dark Forbidden Domain; otherwise, they would never have brought these three crystals to their deaths.
With these three foreign Source Rule Calamity Crystals, Ye Tian had made a huge profit.
Ye Tian's action of slaying the Supreme-level beings of Chaotian Pavilion did not attract much attention, as there were no other Supreme-level beings in this area. Thus, the news did not leak.
In the following days, Supreme-level beings would occasionally attack the Human Race's territory, only to be slain by Ye Tian.
Gradually, the news leaked, and the Human Race's strength was exposed.
And for some unknown reason, the Supreme-level beings of the six Ultra Name-level powers were not keen on searching for opportunities, but were instead looking for something.
Ye Tian was also curious about this intelligence.
"What exactly are those six major powers looking for? Could it be that the mysterious treasure is the true goal of the six major powers!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
