Chapter 1604 Half-Step Eternal Technique!

This day.
Ye Tian continued to fuse the cloned body with the finger of an Eternal Realm mighty figure. After such a long period of fusion, he gradually came to understand this finger more and more, gaining more and more insights.
The Eternal rule contained in this finger of an Eternal Realm mighty figure was Destruction. What he was comprehending was the endless Eternal rule, which contained all kinds of Eternal rules, so the Destruction rule was also of great use to him.
Moreover, the rules influenced each other. The Destruction rule could also give rise to the rule of life, and the rule of life nurtured all things and could in turn give birth to other rules.
Therefore, even just comprehending the Eternal rule could greatly enhance Ye Tian’s endless Eternal rule.
Let alone the fact that this was a finger from the Eternal Realm, which could help him better understand the life structure of the Eternal Realm and was of tremendous help to his cultivation of the Eternal Body.
In fact, the Eternal Body was a body that was gradually refined and improved step by step into the Eternal Realm. If the Eternal Body reached Perfected, then one could possess the body of the Eternal Realm. Although it could not exert much power, the strength it displayed was still far beyond that of Supreme Beings of the same stage.
This fusion went relatively smoothly because he had already mastered the knack of fusion, and coupled with his profound comprehension of the Destruction Eternal rule, no rejection problem appeared from the very beginning.
One could see Ye Tian’s power seeming to turn into billions of filaments, merging into this finger and slowly assimilating it.
No one knew how much time had passed before the fusion finally succeeded.
This was also the first time Ye Tian had succeeded in fusion!
“It’s done. Next, I only need to try a few more times, and then I can ensure a 100% fusion success rate!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
However, before the next fusion, Ye Tian intended to test what kind of attack he could unleash with this finger of the Eternal Realm.
So, Ye Tian came to the Source Sea.
His gaze pointed toward a distant region, and he activated his right index finger, executing a peerless ancestral technique of finger arts.
“Break!”
A terrifying force erupted, and that patch of the Source Sea shattered like glass.
After unleashing this strike, Ye Tian felt as if his body was about to split apart. Even with the recovery and life-saving power of his endless Eternal Body, he was almost completely Annihilated.
But in the end, Ye Tian still survived, though he was severely injured.
“The price is too great. If other twofold Supreme Beings or even threefold Supreme Beings and fourfold Supreme Beings were to luckily fuse this finger of the Eternal Realm, then if they dared to borrow it to launch an attack, they would certainly die without a doubt!” Ye Tian murmured.
He estimated the power of this attack, and it was very likely at the level of an ordinary eightfold Supreme Being, second only to the realm of a ninefold Supreme Being.
This was still because his own strength was too weak, so he could not display too much power.
If he advanced to the realm of a threefold Supreme Being, then with the power of this strike, he would probably be able to unleash combat strength comparable to that of a ninefold Supreme Being.
Of course, this move could only be used as a trump card.
Moreover, Ye Tian decided not to let his true body fuse this finger. It would be enough for the cloned body to fuse it. Otherwise, if his true body were severely injured, it would affect his attacks, whereas there would be no need to worry if it were a clone.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian disassembled the cloned body, condensed it anew, and started the fusion process again.
Just like that, the success rate of fusion grew higher and higher. After a great kalpa era of research, Ye Tian had already secured a 100% success rate, and only then did he stop the fusion experiments.
Afterward, he began using this finger to comprehend the Eternal rules, comprehend the life structure of the Eternal Realm, and even attempt to create a Eternal technique.
Of course, the true Eternal techniques were too terrifying in power, and he found it very difficult to create one. But with the help of this finger from the Eternal Realm, he could create a peerless technique containing a trace of Eternal technique power, which could also be called a Half-Step Eternal Technique.
According to what the artifact spirit said, Half-Step Eternal Realm experts basically possessed many Half-Step Eternal techniques, and the most outstanding among them had even mastered multiple Eternal techniques.
Therefore, he still had a great chance of creating a Half-Step Eternal Technique.
This closed-door cultivation lasted quite a long time. Ye Tian comprehended for a full kalpa epoch.
During this period, the Dark Forbidden Domain fell into a great destruction, but the human race had other foreign lands, so all humans were transferred to other foreign lands.
In this great destruction, a tribulation beast was born again in the Dark Forbidden Domain, but it was slain by a human Invincible.
.........
After the great destruction, the Dark Forbidden Domain entered the next kalpa epoch.
One kalpa epoch was almost a very short time to other Supreme Beings, but Ye Tian’s cultivation speed had always been too fast, so he felt that this period of seclusion was very long.
A full kalpa epoch of seclusion instead allowed Ye Tian to make progress in the Eternal Body.
The Eternal Body was very difficult to improve unless one had treasures like the Eternal Tribulation Crystal, which could raise the Eternal Body.
But there was another way to improve the realm of the Eternal Body, and that was to actively enhance the Eternal Body.
Basically, other Supreme Beings could not do this, but Ye Tian did it.
He had comprehended this finger of the Eternal Realm, and his comprehension of the Eternal Body was too profound. Thus, he broke through the shackles and forcibly raised the Eternal Body from the late Entry Stage to the Peak Entry Stage, but this was already the Limit. To raise it to the Minor Stage was almost impossible without relying on treasures like the Eternal Tribulation Crystal.
But such an improvement was already enough.
At the same time, Ye Tian also successfully created a Half-Step Eternal Technique. This was a Destruction Half-Step Eternal Technique, completely modeled after this finger of the Eternal Realm.
Ye Tian named this Half-Step Eternal Technique Destruction Severing Finger. Once he borrowed this finger of the Eternal Realm, the power unleashed would definitely be no less than that of a true Eternal technique.
Right now, with the realm of a twofold Supreme Being, Ye Tian could activate this finger and unleash an attack comparable to that of a ninefold Supreme Being, but it would only be a single strike.
After that, Ye Tian began trying to control this finger so that it would not erupt with its full power—for example, by unleashing power comparable to that of a sixfold Supreme Being or a sevenfold Supreme Being. In that case, would the consumption and cost be smaller, allowing him to unleash multiple attacks?
However, this finger of the Eternal Realm was too advanced, and controlling it was rather difficult.
Ye Tian tried for a long time, but could only manage to unleash attacks at the level of a sevenfold Supreme Being, and he could do it ten times. He could not control it any further down.
Moreover, after a kalpa epoch of bitter cultivation, Ye Tian’s cultivation had finally reached the Limit of a twofold Supreme Being, and he was preparing to make preparations for charging into the realm of a threefold Supreme Being.
