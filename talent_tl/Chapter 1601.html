Chapter 1600: Second Supreme Being!

The two Eternal Ancient Pine Leaves turned into streaks of light and entered Ye Tian’s soul.
At this moment, Ye Tian felt incomparable joy in his soul, and his comprehension, as well as his grasp of the Eternal Law, seemed to have instantly risen to an incredibly unbelievable level.
Afterward, Ye Tian also took some other auxiliary treasures to help himself break through to the Second Supreme Being realm.
Time slowly passed—
Although Ye Tian had about a seventy to eighty percent confidence in his breakthrough, it was not a hundred percent certain, so he remained extremely calm and did not allow his state of mind to fluctuate in the slightest.
Much later, Ye Tian’s body began to change.
Countless source sea origin power poured into Ye Tian’s body. This was a sign of breaking through the shackles and absorbing external energy to promote the body’s transformation.
Simply put, Ye Tian successfully broke through to the Second Supreme Being realm.
After that, Ye Tian spent a little more time stabilizing his realm.
At this point, the time was about right.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian entered the Source God Pavilion.
……
Source God Pavilion, a trading space.
One Supreme Being after another entered here, and soon a total of ninety-one Supreme Beings gathered.
At this time, the Supreme Being who had discovered the Eternal Tribulation Domain, Storm Source God, said, “Since everyone has arrived, let’s set off!”
The many Supreme Beings nodded, then followed Storm Source God and, with the help of Source God Pavilion’s shifting ability, directly moved to a region of the source sea.
The many Supreme Beings followed Storm Source God and continuously traversed the source sea. Before long, they arrived at a place where space and time were distorted.
There were many places in the source sea where space and time were distorted; this was a common type of region in the source sea.
These distorted places of space and time often gave birth to some unique void lands, but the area of distortion here was very small, and if one did not approach it, it was almost impossible to notice.
Moreover, this place should not have existed for a very long time.
“This is it! Follow me in together!”
Storm Source God said softly.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
One Supreme Being after another entered this area, and soon, under Storm Source God’s lead, they spotted a black rift.
This black rift was the entrance to the Eternal Tribulation Domain.
“It really is the entrance to the Eternal Tribulation Domain!”
A Third Supreme Being recognized it at a glance.
Another Third Supreme Being nodded slightly. “It is indeed the Eternal Tribulation Domain. I once went to the entrance of a high-level Eternal Tribulation Domain. Although I didn’t dare go in, the aura was the same. This Eternal Tribulation Domain is indeed only at the initial level.”
“Let’s go in together. Once we enter, this entrance will become even more unstable, and in a short time it won’t be able to let in any more Supreme Beings; otherwise the entrance will disappear, and then it will be somewhat troublesome for us to leave.” Storm Source God said.
The other Supreme Beings nodded, then one by one entered the Eternal Tribulation Domain.
Ye Tian also followed them into this Eternal Tribulation Domain, and at the moment he entered, he immediately unleashed several defensive Supreme Techniques to prevent himself from being attacked.
However, he was not attacked.
Clearly, no one from the Eternal Tribulation Race was guarding this entrance.
This was also very normal; after all, it had never been discovered, so the Eternal Tribulation Race naturally did not pay much attention to it. Besides, if it really were discovered, guarding here would instead be very dangerous.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
After entering the Eternal Tribulation Domain, the Supreme Beings dispersed at once, concealing their auras and figures as they infiltrated various parts of the Eternal Tribulation Domain.
Ye Tian also concealed his aura and slowly traversed toward a certain direction.
The goal of this entry was very simple: find some members of the Eternal Tribulation Race, kill them, and obtain the Eternal Tribulation Crystal Stones on them.
Very soon, Ye Tian spotted a member of the Eternal Tribulation Race.
The other party was traveling through the void like an ancient star, and Ye Tian happened to run into it.
This member of the Eternal Tribulation Race was not weak, roughly comparable to a First-Tier Peak Supreme Being.
Ye Tian discovered it, and it also discovered Ye Tian.
“A cultivator from the outside world!”
The first thing this member of the Eternal Tribulation Race did upon seeing Ye Tian was to flee, not to kill him.
After all, Ye Tian’s cultivation had now reached the Second Supreme Being realm; it did not think itself a match, so it chose to run.
But it could not escape.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian crossed the void with one hand, and with only a single strike, he shattered this member of the Eternal Tribulation Race’s body, extinguishing all vitality. Only some tribulation blood, origin law tribulation crystals, tribulation bones, and a one-person-high Eternal Tribulation Crystal Stone remained.
Ye Tian waved his hand and collected these things, then continued searching for other members of the Eternal Tribulation Race.
At the same time, the members of the Eternal Tribulation Race in this Eternal Tribulation Domain learned that intruders had broken in and immediately assembled to resist the outside Supreme Beings.
The Supreme Beings and the Eternal Tribulation Race began fighting in this Eternal Tribulation Domain. One by one, members of the Eternal Tribulation Race fell, but some Supreme Beings also fell into the Eternal Tribulation Race’s trap, were surrounded on all sides, and were ultimately besieged to death.
And Ye Tian’s strength was very great; when he encountered several members of the Eternal Tribulation Race, he easily killed them all.
On this day.
Ye Tian encountered a member of the Eternal Tribulation Race at the Second Supreme Being level, and with only a single strike he heavily wounded the other party to the point of near death. If he struck a second time, this member of the Eternal Tribulation Race would surely die.
But at this moment, a scroll suddenly manifested in the void, intending to draw Ye Tian in.
This scroll was not an ordinary scroll, but a source weapon.
Ye Tian was seen fully urging the Endless Eternal Body, unleashing multiple power-type Supreme Ancestor Techniques, and then layering on Top of that the use of Shattering Void.
“Break!”
　443　Ye Tian threw a punch, and entire regions shattered; even that scroll collapsed as well.
This strike had already surpassed a Third Supreme Being, coming close to the full-force strike of a Fifth Supreme Being.
And inside the scroll, three members of the Eternal Tribulation Race were directly killed.
“Not good, this Supreme Being is too powerful, all together!”
One member of the Eternal Tribulation Race after another appeared, numbering around several dozen.
Ye Tian knew that member of the Eternal Tribulation Race was bait, but he was not the least bit afraid.
With his strength, he was practically an Invincible existence in this low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain. Even if he encountered a large number of members of the Eternal Tribulation Race, even if he might not necessarily be able to defeat them, no one could stop him if he wanted to leave.
“Kill!”
A great battle erupted.
Ye Tian was too perfect in every aspect; whether speed, strength, or defense, he far surpassed these members of the Eternal Tribulation Race. Although among them there were also geniuses comparable to the Children of the Void, compared with Ye Tian, the gap was too great.
In just a short while, some members of the Eternal Tribulation Race died at Ye Tian’s hands.
And Ye Tian did not want to continue fighting either, after all, he did not know how many members of the Eternal Tribulation Race there were in this Eternal Tribulation Domain. The fall of this copied body was a small matter, but if the Eternal Tribulation Crystal Stones he had obtained were lost, then the loss would be far too great.
Thus, Ye Tian broke out of the encirclement and soon disappeared.
