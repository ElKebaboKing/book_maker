Chapter 1457: Ultimate Ancestral Talent
!
"So many Nirvana treasures!"
Ye Tian was extremely excited to see these dense clusters of Nirvana treasures.
Moreover, among them were many high-level and top-tier Nirvana treasures, whose effects were far more powerful than Nirvana crystals.
"I'll fuse the Ancestral Talents of the Nirvana Race first. Once the fusion is successful, there might be unexpected results!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Subsequently, in the following days, Ye Tian began to fuse these Ancestral Talents, constantly merging those of the same category together.
One Ancestral Talent, fused! Ten Ancestral Talents, fused! A hundred Ancestral Talents, fused! .... Ye Tian had copied almost all the Ancestral Talents of the Nirvana Race, and now he was merging them all. Although it would take a vast amount of time, Ye Tian could clearly sense that the several types of Ancestral Talents he had copied and fused were constantly evolving and strengthening.
Strength Ancestral Talent! Speed Ancestral Talent! Defense Ancestral Talent! Nirvana Ancestral Talent! Time Ancestral Talent! These five major Ancestral Talents were all becoming stronger at an incredible speed.
In the blink of an eye, a hundred million years passed, and within the time acceleration array, Ye Tian had spent an even more localized and lengthy period of time.
Finally, when nearly all of these Ancestral Talents were fused, Ye Tian's Strength Ancestral Talent underwent a qualitative change.
Boom!!!!! Deep within his soul, the mark of Ye Tian's Strength Ancestral Talent seemed to be evolving into a world—a world belonging to the talent of strength.
After a long time, this miniature world was born.
"Ultimate Ancestral Talent!"
Ye Tian murmured.
With the birth of this miniature Strength Ancestral Talent world, his Strength Ancestral Talent transformed into an Ultimate Ancestral Talent. Although it was still an Ancestral Talent, it had reached the ultimate peak of the grade, essentially becoming a different level of Ancestral Talent.
Furthermore, this Ultimate Ancestral Talent was different from ordinary ones. Even if an ordinary Ancestral Talent was activated, it could at most unleash power comparable to a Saint Technique; if it couldn't be properly utilized, it was almost useless.
Much like the Nirvana Race—although every one of them possessed an Ancestral Talent, their comprehension was poor. Consequently, they couldn't create Ancestral Techniques and could only purely activate their Ancestral Talents for attacks. Thus, they could only unleash the power of a Saint Technique at most; otherwise, they wouldn't have been wiped out by the Progenitors.
An Ultimate Ancestral Talent, however, could unleash power surpassing an Ancestral Technique, though it was only the power of a single strike.
For example, once the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent erupted, it was equivalent to mobilizing the power of the entire Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent world for a single, terrifying explosive strike.
But for Ye Tian, with the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent, he could easily cultivate various Strength Ancestral Techniques to the Perfection stage.
In the future, he even had the hope of deducing Strength Ancestral Techniques to the level of Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. At that point, he might be able to kill Progenitors while at a lower level.
"Let's bring out the other Ultimate Ancestral Talents one by one!"
Ye Tian murmured.
With continuous fusion, the Ultimate Defense, Ultimate Speed, Ultimate Nirvana, and Ultimate Time Ancestral Talents were born one after another.
Additionally.
Ye Tian discovered that after his Nirvana Ancestral Talent reached the Ultimate level, his speed in refining Nirvana treasures became faster, and his utilization rate was higher, reaching a level of nearly 100% utilization.
With his Ultimate Nirvana Ancestral Talent and the Nirvana treasures he had obtained, he had enough to push his cultivation to the peak of the Supreme Realm Ancestor level, which was also the limit he could reach.
For him, it was impossible to take the Half-Step Progenitor route, so the next step was... Progenitor. As for how long it would take him to become a Progenitor, he didn't know; it would likely be a very long time.
After all, he currently had no clue at all.
"Let's raise my cultivation first; I'll worry about future matters later!"
Ye Tian continued his secluded cultivation and began refining that massive amount of Nirvana treasures.
Piece after piece of Nirvana treasures were refined by Ye Tian, causing his cultivation to advance continuously.
One hundred million years! One billion years! In the blink of an eye, three billion years passed.
To... Supreme Realm Ancestors, this amount of time really wasn't much, and to... Progenitors, it was merely the time it took to take a nap.
At this time, under the accumulation and refining of Nirvana treasures, Ye Tian finally raised his cultivation to the peak of the Supreme Realm Ancestor level, reaching a point where his cultivation could progress no further.
More importantly, Ye Tian now began to use Nirvana treasures to temper his Ancestral Body.
Nirvana treasures also had an effect on tempering the Ancestral Body, but he had been focusing on: raising his cultivation and had set aside the tempering of his Ancestral Body. Thus, he could now continue using Nirvana treasures for that purpose.
All along, Ye Tian's Ancestral Body had been at the Small Success stage.
Of course.
The Ancestral Body could raise its realm through evolution to complete rapid cultivation, but if there were resources like Nirvana treasures, the speed of progress would naturally be even faster.
Thus.
Ye Tian worked in synchronization with his duplicated body. The duplicated body evolved the Ancestral Body, while the original body used Nirvana treasures to temper it. This allowed him to push the Ancestral Body toward the Great Success stage more quickly.
In the blink of an eye, another billion years passed.
However, Ye Tian's Nirvana treasures were exhausted, and his Ancestral Body had only been deduced to the peak of the Small Success stage. He was still one step away from the Great Success stage, but this step was extremely difficult to cross.
"It's enough. To have increased my strength to this point is already very fast!"
Ye Tian murmured.
In the following days, Ye Tian exchanged for some more Strength, Defense, Speed, and Time-type Saint Techniques from the Battle Gate.
Afterward, he began practicing these Saint Techniques, easily elevating them to Ancestral Techniques and then effortlessly cultivating those Ancestral Techniques to the Perfection stage.
In reality, practicing more Ancestral Techniques didn't provide a significant boost for... Ye Tian.
But the more Ancestral Techniques he practiced, the deeper his insight into that specific path became, which would help Ye Tian deduce Ancestral Techniques into Ultimate Ancestral Techniques in the future.
It was precisely because of this that Ye Tian practiced Saint Techniques and Ancestral Techniques so frantically instead of wasting time.
On this day, a message came from the Battle Gate, cutting off Ye Tian's thoughts of continuing his secluded cultivation.
"So that's how it is..."
Ye Tian was shocked.
Only now did he finally learn why the Progenitors had so suddenly invaded and wiped out the Nirvana Race.
It was because the third Dark Holy Land of the Dark Forbidden Zone was about to be born.
"As expected, the Nirvana Race were just leeks. The birth of the third Dark Holy Land is many times more important than the Nirvana Race. That's why the Progenitors didn't want to continue guarding the Nirvana Race's borders and acted to wipe them out! The Supreme Realm Ancestors were just small soldiers on the front lines, and uninformed ones at that!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian thought of something. Where was the third Dark Holy Land? "Could it be that the Dao Mountain Territory where I am located is... the third Dark Holy Land?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
"No!"
Ye Tian shook his head. "Dark Holy Lands are places like the Dark Destruction Realm and the Dark Holy Realm. They are born naturally, precious lands evolved from the rules of the Dark Forbidden Zone. The Dao Mountain Territory was created by me using the Creation Primordial Well; it cannot be the third Dark Holy Land.
However, the Dao Mountain Territory might develop into the fourth Dark Holy Land in the future, but that would require a very long time."
Thinking this, he breathed a sigh of relief.
Otherwise, if the Dao Mountain Territory really were the third Dark Holy Land, he would have no choice but to move; he wouldn't be able to withstand that immense pressure.
