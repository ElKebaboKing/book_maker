Chapter 1560: Son of the Void! Nether Hall's Response!

Jinhe World.
This was a branch belonging to the Nether Hall. At this moment, a large number of Invincibles were gathered here, including many third-grade Invincibles.
Many Invincibles from the Tianfeng Sea had also come here, preparing to attack Jinhe World and wipe out this branch.
Of course, the Nether Hall was also an Eternal-level power, and its strength was not weak.
For the Tianfeng Sea to want to wipe out this branch, it was naturally fraught with difficulties.
But for many Invincibles, as long as they could behead a few Invincibles, it would be a huge gain!
As for whether they could wipe out Jinhe World, that was not important.
In fact, the Tianfeng Sea had already invaded Jinhe World over a dozen times, and each time they had returned without success.
On this day, the Tianfeng Sea organized yet another invasion operation.
...
Nether Hall, Jinhe Branch.
At this time, several hundred third-grade Invincibles of the Nether Hall were gathered here. Normally, having fourth-grade Invincibles guarding this place would be enough, but now that the Nether Hall was at war with the Tianfeng Sea, this branch had been assigned so many third-grade Invincibles.
A single branch was not important and could be replaced at any time; it didn't have much value.
But for the Nether Hall, if a branch was uprooted, it would be equivalent to falling into a disadvantage in the war against the Tianfeng Sea.
Therefore, they had to protect this branch at all costs.
Rumble!!!
The entire Jinhe Branch was trembling. This was the Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea killing their way in.
The Invincibles of the Nether Hall naturally would not sit and wait for death. One by one, they rushed out of the branch and fought with the Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea in the void.
Region after region of Jinhe World was smashed to pieces, and the battle was incredibly tragic.
At this moment, Ye Tian arrived here.
He saw an Invincible from the Nether Hall and casually shattered the opponent's body with a single punch, killing him in one blow.
Afterward, he collected the opponent's mangled body and continued to search for other Invincibles of the Nether Hall.
After Ye Tian had killed six Invincibles of the Nether Hall, he was discovered by a third-grade Invincible of the Nether Hall.
Immediately, a third-grade Invincible came toward Ye Tian.
"A third-grade Invincible of the Tianfeng Sea actually sneak-attacks fourth and fifth-grade Invincibles. How shameless!" the Nether Hall's third-grade Invincible, Beihe, said angrily.
"It's mutual. Didn't you also send third-grade Invincibles to surround and kill fourth-grade Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea before? Moreover, your Nether Hall's Supreme Beings even attacked Invincibles. Who would you say is more shameless?" Ye Tian mocked.
"You..."
Beihe was so angry he was speechless, so he stopped talking.
Kill!
Beihe performed an Invincible Technique—Ice River!
Instantly, this entire area was directly frozen, and even the rules of the Foreign Domain World were frozen, showing the power of this Invincible Technique.
But Ye Tian's body moved, directly shattering the Ice River. He immediately performed his own Space Invincible Ancestral Technique—Shattering the Void!
How terrifying was Ye Tian's strength? His physical body alone far surpassed other third-grade Invincibles.
He cultivated the top-tier Invincible Tribulation Body, the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void, and had cultivated every layer to the most perfected state. With these layers stacked, even a third-grade Invincible of the Nether Hall who cultivated a top-tier Invincible Tribulation Body was far inferior to him in terms of their physical tribulation body.
Furthermore, the Shattering the Void technique Ye Tian created was a top-tier Space Invincible Technique, making its power even more terrifying.
Boom!
Shattering the Void directly annihilated the area where Beihe was. Layer after layer of Invincible defensive techniques on his body were shattered. Even the many defensive treasures and defensive tribulation artifacts on his person could not block the Shattering the Void move at all and were all directly pulverized.
However, this third-grade Invincible had, after all, entered the ranks of third-grade Invincibles long ago and was not even that far from being a second-grade Invincible. Thus, he was not killed in one hit but was only heavily injured, with half of his body disappearing, making it difficult to recover in a short time.
"How can you be so powerful?"
Beihe was dazed.
This third-grade Invincible of the Tianfeng Sea was so terrifying that he was practically comparable to those few Sons of the Void of the Nether Hall.
Son of the Void was the title for the top geniuses of the Nether Hall, each of whom was Invincible among those of the same rank. To this day, the entire Nether Hall only had six Sons of the Void, distributed across different realms of Invincibles. The Son of the Void among the third-grade Invincibles only had the strength Ye Tian had just displayed.
"Could this person be a Son of the Void of the Tianfeng Sea? Is the Tianfeng Sea willing to send out a Son of the Void? Aren't they afraid their Son of the Void will perish?"
Beihe said in disbelief.
He didn't think too much; right now, his only thought was to escape.
However, he could no longer escape and could only hope that other third-grade Invincibles could come to his rescue in time.
Ye Tian performed a speed-type Invincible Ancestral Technique, quickly catching up to Beihe, and used another Invincible Ancestral Technique—Tianji Combat Technique.
With a crack, Beihe's body was once again beaten into fragments. After merging back together, Beihe's body was even more mangled.
"Die!"
Ye Tian pulverized Beihe's last spark of life and collected his shattered head as proof for submitting the mission in the future.
Just like that, a third-grade Invincible perished at Ye Tian's hands.
Jinhe Branch.
Musen, the third-grade Invincible commanding this war, sensed Beihe's aura dissipate, and his expression suddenly changed.
"Not good, Beihe has fallen!"
Hearing Musen's words, the other Invincibles were shocked.
Beihe was a third-grade Invincible. Even if he encountered a more powerful third-grade Invincible, escaping should not have been a problem.
Moreover, Beihe had only just asked for reinforcements. How was he killed so quickly?
Unless Beihe had encountered multiple top-tier third-grade Invincibles who joined forces to kill him.
Not long after, another third-grade Invincible of the Nether Hall perished.
By this time, the Nether Hall already knew that the Tianfeng Sea had deployed an incredibly powerful third-grade Invincible who could solo-kill a third-grade Invincible.
Helpless, the Nether Hall requested reinforcements from the Nether Hall headquarters; otherwise, even more third-grade Invincibles would be killed.
...
Tianlin Realm, Nether Hall Headquarters.
The first-grade Invincibles of the Nether Hall were discussing how to handle the matter of Jinhe World.
Either retreat or fight.
Ultimately, the Hall Master of the Nether Hall issued an order to dispatch the Nether Hall's third-grade Invincible Son of the Void, letting the Son of the Void face the opponent and turn the tide of the battle.
On the Tianfeng Sea's side, they soon received news that the Nether Hall had dispatched a Son of the Void.
Tianfeng Sea Headquarters.
Qingyang Supreme and other first-grade Invincible Supremes were discussing the Nether Hall's move.
"Why did the Nether Hall suddenly send out a Son of the Void? Aren't they afraid we will pay a huge price to behead their Son of the Void?" a Supreme asked in confusion.
Qingyang Supreme explained, "According to our information, it's because a third-grade Invincible of our Tianfeng Sea killed several of their third-grade Invincibles. This third-grade Invincible is estimated to have the combat power of a Son of the Void, so the Nether Hall could only send out their third-grade Invincible Son of the Void."
Qingyang Supreme's words left the Supremes present dazed.
Had their Tianfeng Sea sent out a Son of the Void beforehand?
That wasn't right!
The Tianfeng Sea only had five Sons of the Void, and they didn't have a Son of the Void at the third-grade Invincible realm!.
