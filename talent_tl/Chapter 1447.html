Chapter 1446: Finally Broke Through, Supreme Realm Ancestor
!
Physical control 362 times! Physical control 363 times! ....At this time, Ye Tian was frantically deducing and calculating. His duplicated bodies collapsed time and again, only to condense once more, as his degree of physical control grew higher and higher.
As long as his physical control reached 365 times, he would charge toward the ninth transformation of the Invincible Realm Body, thereby stepping directly into the Supreme Realm Body stage.
At that time, he would be able to break through to the Supreme Realm Ancestor level.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian achieved the perfect, ultimate control of 365 times physical control.
With a single thought, the Invincible Realm Body transformed.
Rumble!!!!! The ninth transformation of the Invincible Realm Body arrived, and countless amounts of energy surged into Ye Tian's body.
Densely packed, the various precious resources covering the Holy Monument World all turned into lakes of energy, pouring into Ye Tian's body to help him complete the transformation of the Invincible Realm Body.
Time passed, ten thousand years by ten thousand years. This transformation lasted for a full million years. It was unknown how many resources Ye Tian had consumed... it was likely enough to allow hundreds of Invincible Realm Bodies to promote to Supreme Realm Ancestors.
Finally, on this day, the Invincible Realm Body completely finished its ninth transformation, evolving into a Supreme Realm Body.
Furthermore, Ye Tian's Holy Soul and Realm Power began to undergo a qualitative change, skyrocketing frantically.
Another few million years passed before Ye Tian finally completed his breakthrough.
Since he broke through within a time array, not much time had passed in the outside world.
"My cultivation has broken through; I am now a Supreme Realm Ancestor. My current strength is more than a hundred times greater than before. If I were to fight the Black-Marked Saint Ancestor again, I could probably crush him with one hand!"
Ye Tian said with full confidence.
As for exactly how powerful he had become, that was hard to say.
He certainly couldn't compare to a Progenitor, and might not even match a Half-step Progenitor, but compared to the top Supreme Realm Ancestors, he could definitely hold his own in a fight...
Supreme Realm Ancestors are divided into the early stage, middle stage, late stage, and peak stage. After reaching the peak of the Supreme Realm Ancestor level, one could choose to create their own Progenitor Law or use the Progenitor Law of a fallen Progenitor to charge toward the Progenitor realm, or fuse a fragmented Progenitor Law to reach the Half-step Progenitor realm.
The Progenitor realm was still quite far from Ye Tian. His current goal was to raise his cultivation to the middle stage of the Supreme Realm Ancestor level—specifically, tempering his Supreme Realm Body to the middle stage. This step was extremely difficult.
Because there were too few treasures in the Dark Forbidden Zone that could help a Supreme Realm Ancestor temper their Supreme Realm Body, almost all Supreme Realm Ancestors had to temper their bodies slowly, making their cultivation progress very sluggish.
For example, the Black-Marked Saint Ancestor and the Golden Star Saint Ancestor had cultivated for an unknown number of realm epochs just to reach the middle stage of the Supreme Realm Ancestor level. This showed how difficult it was to increase one's cultivation at this level.
Ye Tian tried cultivating for a million years before pausing, as there was almost no progress.
"The Supreme Realm Body is hard to improve, but the Ancestral Body can still progress!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
There were no shortcuts for the Supreme Realm Body; it could only be tempered step by step unless one found treasures capable of tempering it, but such treasures were hard to come by.
The Ancestral Body was different. It was somewhat similar to Ancestral Techniques and could be improved through deduction and derivation. If one understood the mysteries of the Ancestral Body, they could progress rapidly.
It was just like how he had understood the mystery of promoting a Holy Body to an Ancestral Body, allowing him to easily break through the Holy Body stage.
Currently, Ye Tian's Ancestral Body was only at the entry level. If he deduced and derived it repeatedly, it would be enough to push it to the minor achievement stage.
As for Ancestral Techniques, Ye Tian could no longer improve them because almost all of his Ancestral Techniques had reached perfection.
"From the looks of it, the most promising area for improvement is... the Ancestral Body. Once I deduce the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body to the minor achievement stage, my strength will increase significantly again!"
Ye Tian murmured.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian continuously used his duplicated bodies to deduce and derive the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body.
After breaking through to the Supreme Realm Ancestor level, Ye Tian's Holy Soul was many times stronger than before, so the results of his deductions were naturally better.
Through repeated derivations, Ye Tian's understanding of the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body grew deeper and deeper.
Ten million years! One hundred million years! One billion years! Ten billion years! After a full thirty billion years of bitter cultivation and countless derivations, Ye Tian finally deduced the Ancestral Body to the minor achievement stage.
On this day.
Ye Tian began cultivating according to the results of his deductions, and his Ancestral Body soon broke through.
Rumble!!!!! Ye Tian's physical body began to undergo a qualitative change. The higher the level of the Ancestral Body, the stronger the physical body naturally became.
"Hahaha, I've become several times stronger again!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
However, deducing the Ancestral Body to the grand achievement stage would be incomparably difficult.
After the breakthrough, Ye Tian was in a good mood and came out of seclusion.
After thirty billion years, the situation in the Daoshan Realm had changed significantly.
Now, the human race had completely occupied the Daoshan Realm. As for the original factions, they were too weak. Even though Ye Tian hadn't cleared them out, as the human population grew, even small human factions could sweep everything aside. Those non-human factions simply had no room to survive.
Some factions that had produced Ultimate Realm Ancestors were forced to leave the Daoshan Realm. Some went to the Dark Holy Realm, while others went to different realms. Only a few remained as subordinates to human factions, occupying small areas to survive.
The only faction that was doing reasonably well was Black Wind Mountain.
Black Wind Mountain had been granted a large territory by the humans. Additionally, some human martial artists had joined Black Wind Mountain, making it effectively half a human faction. It was doing quite well. The original three mountain lords of Black Wind Mountain had also become Ultimate Realm Ancestors. Ye Tian's former master, Dao Ancestor Xutian, was now Realm Ancestor Xutian. Having received a grain of Star Soul Sand from Ye Tian, he had become an Invincible Realm Ancestor, making him quite powerful in a small place like the Daoshan Realm.
For instance, Ye Tian's old friend Ouyang Jiu'er had also become an Invincible Realm Ancestor. Naturally, this was thanks to Ye Tian's favor; otherwise, she wouldn't have had the potential to reach the Invincible Realm Ancestor stage. In short, all of Ye Tian's old acquaintances were doing well now.
"My strength has been improving too slowly lately. It's time to go to the edge of the Dark Forbidden Zone and clash with the Nirvana Clan... perhaps I'll gain even more!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Of course, his true body wouldn't go. He would only send a duplicated body. Even if it died in battle, he wouldn't feel distressed.
