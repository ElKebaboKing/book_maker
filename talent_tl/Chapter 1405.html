Chapter 1404: Ancestral Skill
!!!!!
Boom!!!!! The Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor transformed into countless afterimages, each possessing a certain attack power. The countless afterimages, each wielding a black saber, slashed towards the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor.
Rip!!!!!! Countless voids shattered. The power of this strike reached an incredibly terrifying level; it was feared that tens of thousands of invincible domain ancestors would be instantly annihilated.
This was the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor's attack power! However, the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor remained very calm, a fiery red barrier appearing on his body, with mysterious patterns displayed on the red barrier.
Once this fiery red barrier appeared, no matter how the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor attacked, he couldn't do anything to the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor.
No damage, this is awkward! "Impossible!"
The Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor was struck down, unable to believe this scene.
He had always considered his strength to be no weaker than the Fire-Patterned Emperor Ancestor, but now he couldn't even defeat a clone of the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor; he refused to believe it.
"Grand Dawn Sword!"
The Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor summoned this sacred artifact, which was among the top hundred sacred artifacts on the Sacred Artifact Ranking, possessing incredibly terrifying power.
As soon as the Grand Dawn Sword appeared, the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor's aura surged.
"Grand Dawn Sword, you've even used that. It seems you're really serious about fighting, so don't blame me for not giving your father face!"
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor became serious.
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor transformed into a terrifying giant standing atop the Roaring Sea, seemingly the size of a domain realm, and his aura also reached a terrifying level.
Immediately, the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor pointed a finger towards the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor.
And the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, holding the Grand Dawn Sword, stabbed towards the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor. The Grand Dawn Sword collided with one of the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor's fingers.
The next instant, the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor's Grand Dawn Sword was sent flying, and the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor himself was struck flying an unknown number of territories away, almost knocked out of the Roaring Sea's range.
At this moment, the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor continuously coughed blood, and his entire body began to crack. Such injuries were not light for him, but rather quite severe.
"Ancestral Skill, impossible, how could you have mastered an Ancestral Skill!!!"
The Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor roared.
At this moment, the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor returned to his original form, stepping across the void to appear before the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, saying, "Is it strange that I've mastered an Ancestral Skill? Although you became a Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor earlier than me, you have never entered the Sacred Gate. You don't know the Sacred Gate's foundation, nor do you know that the Supreme-tier Domain Ancestors of the Sacred Gate are far beyond comparison to a rogue Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor like you.
If you had fully accepted your father's tutelage, perhaps you would still be qualified to fight me today. Unfortunately, you gave all that up, chose to cultivate diligently on your own, and mistakenly believed your strength was immense, unaware that other Supreme-tier Domain Ancestors simply didn't want to fight you, not wishing to offend your father!"
"I surrender!"
The Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor said dejectedly.
The Fire-Patterned Emperor Ancestor had mastered an Ancestral Skill; he couldn't possibly defeat the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor unless he far surpassed the Fire-Patterned Emperor Ancestor in other aspects, but that... was simply unrealistic.
"Swear an oath that from today onwards, you will no longer target the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor. Additionally, the Ghost Organization needs to make compensation: one Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit, how about it?"
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor said.
"Alright!"
The Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor gritted his teeth and said.
Swearing an oath not to target the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor was nothing to him, but losing one Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit was a huge loss.
One Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit could completely create an invincible Domain Ancestor, and its effect was many times more precious than the Soul Saint Realm Flower, having a significant enhancing effect on Soul Saint Light, whereas the Soul Saint Realm Flower offered little help in enhancing Soul Saint Light.
Moreover, the Ghost Fox Organization lost multiple Four-Star 44 Kings this time.
It can be said that the Ghost Fox Organization suffered heavy losses this time.
Whoosh! Ye Tian was teleported over, appearing before the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor.
At this moment, after the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor swore his oath and dropped one Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit, he instantly vanished.
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, this is the Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor's Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit. It was a fortuitous encounter he once obtained in a treasure land. Now, he probably doesn't have many left. Making him lose one, he's probably been heartbroken for a long time!"
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor said with a smile.
"Many thanks, Lord Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said gratefully.
Immediately, Ye Tian accepted the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit.
"Lord Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor, what you just displayed wasn't a Sacred Skill, was it?"
Ye Tian asked curiously.
He was very familiar with Sacred Skills, having mastered multiple Sacred Skills and cultivated each to its extreme realm. If it were any unfamiliar Sacred Skill, he would be able to detect it.
But the technique displayed by the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor was definitely not a Sacred Skill; it was far too powerful compared to Sacred Skills, undoubtedly something superior to them.
However, he didn't understand anything about things beyond Sacred Skills, and the Battle Gate couldn't find any information either.
In reality, many secrets concerning the Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor level and above are not allowed to be spread. Unless one learns them directly from a Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor, it is very difficult to know.
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor was silent for a moment before speaking, "Alright, I can tell you. That is an Ancestral Skill, which is superior to Sacred Skills! Ancestral Skills are extremely difficult to master; generally, only Progenitors can master them, and among Supreme-tier Domain Ancestors, those who can master Ancestral Skills are relatively few."
"Are there any conditions required to master an Ancestral Skill? Does it perhaps require some kind of Ancestral Talent?"
Ye Tian asked curiously.
"There's no such thing as Ancestral Talent; Sacred Talent is... the highest talent!"
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor said with a smile, "Progenitors can easily master Ancestral Skills. If Domain Ancestors below the Progenitor level want to master Ancestral Skills, there are only two ways: one is to break the shackles of a Sacred Skill at its extreme realm, in which case the Sacred Skill will transform into an Ancestral Skill.
The second method is to completely master a single Progenitor Rune. A complete Progenitor Path is divided into 300 Progenitor Runes; any Progenitor Path is like this. And a complete Progenitor Law can connect all 300 Progenitor Runes, thereby forming a complete Progenitor Path.
If we Supreme-tier Domain Ancestors do not have Progenitor Law, it is very difficult to reach the Progenitor level, but there is hope of mastering a single Progenitor Rune. Through a Progenitor Rune, one can cultivate the corresponding Ancestral Skill, thereby mastering an Ancestral Skill.
Of course, it sounds easy, but mastering it is too difficult. Even I cultivated for a long time before mastering this Ancestral Skill!"
"If I want to obtain an Ancestral Skill from the Battle Gate, what should I do?"
Ye Tian was extremely curious about Ancestral Skills.
After all, he had already cultivated Sacred Skills to their extreme, so he naturally yearned for Ancestral Skills.
"Wait until you become an invincible Domain Ancestor in the future, then we can talk about it. For now, Ancestral Skills are too distant for you!"
The Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor said no more and left.
"An invincible Domain Ancestor, huh!"
Ye Tian murmured to himself.
Immediately, he also put aside the matter of Ancestral Skills. After all, his most important task now was to condense his Saint Soul, not to cultivate some Ancestral Skill.
Even if he truly obtained an Ancestral Skill, he didn't know how many years it would take to master it! Therefore, currently, obtaining an Ancestral Skill would instead delay his cultivation. That's why the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor didn't tell him, to prevent him from delaying himself.
Whoosh! Ye Tian also left the Roaring Sea and returned to the Battle Ancestor Realm.
