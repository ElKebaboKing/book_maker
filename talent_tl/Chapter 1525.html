Chapter 1524: The Second-Order Gate, Successfully Constructed!

"The first Dark Era of the Dark Forbidden Domain has passed!"
Ye Tian murmured to himself.
Shortly after, an illusory gate appeared somewhere within the Dark Forbidden Domain. This illusory gate was the target Ye Tian had been waiting for.
An illusory gate would not exist for very long. Therefore, before it could dissipate, one had to completely stabilize it and then prepare countless precious materials to forge it into a physical gate.
Ye Tian possessed the construction methods for not only a First-Order Gate but also a Second-Order Gate.
The cost of building a Second-Order Gate was ten times that of a First-Order Gate, but with Ye Tian's wealth and resources, he naturally had the capability to construct one.
The location of an illusory gate could not be changed. Consequently, the Human Race had to establish a branch base at its location, or they could even move their headquarters there.
However, the Human Race was bound to possess many gates in the future, so there was no need to relocate the headquarters. Dispatching a few Ancestors to guard the site would suffice for daily operations.
At the site of the illusory gate, Ye Tian brought out countless materials and began smelting them, integrating them into the illusory structure.
Time flowed slowly, and in the blink of an eye, ten thousand years had passed.
By now, the illusory gate had transformed into a tangible, real gate, brimming with a powerful aura. This was a Second-Order Gate forged entirely from countless materials, yet it was still missing one final component: Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
A First-Order Gate did not require Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, but a Second-Order Gate required ten. This was the reason why ordinary forces could not build Second-Order Gates—the price was simply too high. Even a typical Great-name level force would find it difficult to produce ten Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Only Super-name level forces could easily bring out ten Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals without feeling the slightest bit of heartache.
Swish! Swish! Swish!
Under Ye Tian's control, the Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals merged into the Second-Order Gate one by one. Gradually, the gate began to take its final shape and was on the verge of completely connecting to another world.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian forcibly modified the channel, inputting the world coordinates of the Xiyue Clan, thereby linking to the world where the Xiyue Clan resided.
After all, his goal was to enter the Xiyue Clan's world.
According to his intelligence, the Xiyue Clan's world had only one-tenth of a Dark Era remaining before it entered its Great Tribulation period. Thus, after building this Second-Order Gate, he wouldn't have to wait very long.
As the coordinates were modified, the Second-Order Gate began to pierce through to the Xiyue Clan's world.
Within the Xiyue Clan's world.
Boom! ! ! ! !
The void split open, and a Second-Order Gate slowly took form.
The appearance of a Second-Order Gate could not possibly be hidden from the Xiyue Clan.
Swish! Swish! Swish!
One after another, the powerhouses of the Xiyue Clan arrived at the scene. Seeing the Second-Order Gate, they attempted to destroy it.
However, their attacks were completely useless.
"Stop attacking. This is a Gate. It has just pierced into our world. Under the protection of the rules of the other world, we cannot destroy it while it is forming. We can only hope to destroy the gate after it has fully materialized," a supreme powerhouse of the Xiyue Clan spoke.
After a long time, the Second-Order Gate was completely formed, signifying that the Dark Forbidden Domain and the Xiyue Clan's forbidden land were now fully connected.
At that moment, the rules emanating from the Second-Order Gate began to influence the surrounding area, transforming it into a forbidden zone.
"Not good, this is a Second-Order Gate!"
The expressions of the Xiyue Clan powerhouses changed drastically.
The Xiyue Clan's world did not possess a Second-Order Gate, but that did not mean they failed to recognize one.
The entire Xiyue Clan possessed a total of thirteen supreme powerhouses, including three who were at the second level. Their strength was quite formidable.
At this moment, these supreme powerhouses gathered together, intent on destroying the Second-Order Gate.
An additional First-Order Gate meant little to the Xiyue Clan, but if a Second-Order Gate appeared, the Xiyue Clan's future days would be difficult!
Boom! ! ! ! !
The many supreme powerhouses attacked the Second-Order Gate, attempting to destroy it before the opposing force could descend. Once the other side dispatched a group of supreme powerhouses, they would have no hope of destroying such a gate.
Just then, a hand reached through from the other side of the gate, transforming into a colossal hand that easily blocked the attacks of all the supreme powerhouses.
In the next instant.
Ye Tian's figure stepped through the Second-Order Gate, officially entering the Xiyue Clan's world.
While remaining within the forbidden zone, he would not be affected by the rules of the Xiyue Clan's world.
Furthermore, a Second-Order Gate allowed supreme powerhouses to pass through freely without paying any price. He had descended here with his full combat power. . . . . . . . . .
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws!"
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws!"
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws!"
The supreme powerhouses of the Xiyue Clan cried out in disbelief.
Retreat! Retreat! Retreat!
The Xiyue Clan's supreme powerhouses hurriedly backed out of the forbidden zone, not daring to take a single step inside.
Currently, the Xiyue Clan's world was not in its Great Tribulation period, so Ye Tian could not step out of the forbidden zone.
At this moment, the Xiyue Clan's supreme powerhouses were extremely bewildered.
Wasn't the Ancestor of Myriad Laws a supreme ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain? How did he suddenly build a Second-Order Gate and arrive in the Xiyue Clan's world?
Setting aside how Ye Tian obtained the construction method for a Second-Order Gate, even if he was lucky enough to find it, how did he obtain the coordinates for the Xiyue Clan?
One usually had to personally step into the Xiyue Clan's world and spend a vast amount of time calculating the coordinates based on the position of the Dark Forbidden Domain!
For the Dark Forbidden Domain to possess the coordinates of the Xiyue Clan's world seemed illogical no matter how they looked at it!
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, what do you intend to do?"
One of the Xiyue Clan's supreme powerhouses asked.
"It's simple. I'm establishing a base on your side. You were able to establish a base in the Dark Forbidden Domain, so why can't I?" Ye Tian sneered.
The Xiyue Clan supreme powerhouse said, "We have already destroyed that gate in the Dark Forbidden Domain and will no longer travel to your domain!"
"That is because you fear me. Otherwise, how could you possibly destroy a First-Order Gate you built yourselves?" Ye Tian mocked.
Then, Ye Tian added, "Rest assured, my Human Race is currently weak and has no interest in invading your world. I only wish to use your world for cultivation. As long as you do not disturb me, I won't bother with you."
"We hope the Ancestor of Myriad Laws is a man of his word!"
The Xiyue Clan supreme powerhouse replied.
Subsequently, all of the Xiyue Clan's forces in this area were withdrawn. They no longer provoked Ye Tian, as they simply could not afford to.
Especially since the Great Tribulation of the Xiyue Clan's world was approaching. If they provoked Ye Tian and he slaughtered his way into the Xiyue Clan's headquarters, no one would be able to stop him.
Time passed slowly.
Finally, the Great Tribulation of the Xiyue Clan's world arrived.
