Chapter 1691 Eight-Step Immortal Realm Lord!

“The Bloodbone Domain is finally about to advance into an Eternal Spacetime Domain!”
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Many Eternal years ago, he had traded for the Heaven-Made Stele from Lord Bing Han, and placed the Heaven-Made Stele at the origin of the Bloodbone Domain, causing the Bloodbone Domain to continuously grow and evolve. However, advancing into an Eternal Spacetime Domain was not something that could be done all at once; it required a certain amount of time.
And for an Immortal Realm Lord, that amount of time was not too long.
Now, the Bloodbone Domain had finally completed the last step and ushered in its final major qualitative change.
Boom!!!!!
The Bloodbone Domain swelled continuously like a balloon, endlessly drawing vast amounts of origin energy from the Source Sea.
Moreover, the Bloodbone Domain was also extracting the energy from that section of bone within the Bloodbone Domain.
At this moment.
One of Ye Tian’s clone bodies was inside the Bloodbone Domain, observing its transformation.
He saw the Bloodbone Domain continuously growing larger, while also giving birth to large amounts of rare resources. One after another, mines, heavenly materials and earthly treasures, and rare lifeforms were born successively, and the blood-colored power contained within the bone was also absorbed by the Bloodbone Domain, making it a rather special Eternal Spacetime Domain.
After a long while, the transformation of the Bloodbone Domain was complete, and it had already become a true Eternal Spacetime Domain. The resources contained in this newly born Eternal Spacetime Domain were incomparably abundant.
Ye Tian did not plan to collect these resources. He intended to bring a group of human Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts from the Dark Forbidden Domain to cultivate here, while also gathering some resources along the way.
In that way, these human powerhouses would grow rapidly, and they could also help him manage the Eternal Spacetime Domain.
“Huh, that suspected bone of a Legendary Realm Lord-level lifeform has shrunk a lot, and it has become much more fragile as well.”
Ye Tian came to the bottom of the Black Sea, sensing that piece of bone from a Legendary Realm Lord-level lifeform, and muttered.
It could be said that this bone of a Legendary Realm Lord had already ceased to affect the Bloodbone Domain; even if it were destroyed, it would not cause any impact on the Bloodbone Domain.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian relocated some human beings from the Source Sea Dark Forbidden Domain to the Eternal Spacetime Domain, and also opened a spacetime channel to ensure that humans could freely move in and out of the Bloodbone Spacetime Domain and the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Only then would he not need to manage these matters himself; otherwise, if any humans wanted to return and needed his help, he would not have that much free time.
After dealing with all of this, Ye Tian finally began to cultivate in earnest, striving to raise his cultivation to the Seven-Step Immortal Realm Lord as soon as possible.
With the help of the Hunyuan Token, the speed at which Ye Tian’s will and body fused became faster and faster. Finally, after dozens of Eternal years, the degree of fusion between Ye Tian’s body and will exceeded seventy percent, and he stepped into the Seven-Step Immortal Realm Lord level.
With his cultivation breakthrough, Ye Tian’s strength also increased considerably. Combined with the Hunyuan Token, he even had hope of fighting the weakest Legendary Realm Lord for a bit.
Of course, the gap in strength between an Immortal Realm Lord and a Legendary Realm Lord was far too great. Ye Tian’s strength was still somewhat below the six guild masters of the spacetime domain, but he no longer needed to fear their combat power; he now had genuine self-protection ability.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian’s main goal was not only to improve his cultivation, but also to create all kinds of top-tier Legendary Eternal Skills.
Only top-tier Legendary Eternal Skills, with the help of the Hunyuan Token, could display the power of a Half-Step Legendary Eternal Skill.
At present, he only had two top-tier Legendary Eternal Skills, Void Step and Ten Thousandfold Void Space. His other Legendary Eternal Skills were still rather ordinary, and he planned to refine and modify those ordinary Legendary Eternal Skills so that they would become top-tier Legendary Eternal Skills.
And when creating and perfecting Legendary Eternal Skills, the Eternal Hunyuan Domain forged by the Hunyuan Token could provide excellent assistance and reference.
Thus, Ye Tian continuously attempted to perfect his Legendary Eternal Skills within the Eternal Hunyuan Domain, and one Eternal year after another passed.
Unknowingly, one hundred thousand Eternal years had passed.
For Ye Tian, this was also an incomparably long period of seclusion.
During this time, the number of human half-step Eternal Realm experts increased greatly, and even several Extreme experts were born. Meanwhile, Ye Tian’s family members, with his help, had stepped into the Eternal Life Realm Lord level.
But compared with Ye Tian, their progress was negligible.
“An Eight-Step Immortal Realm Lord, dozens of top-tier Legendary Eternal Skills, 99,999 will worlds, and I’m about to break through to one hundred thousand will worlds. My strength is far greater than before.” Ye Tian said excitedly.
99,999 will worlds was equivalent to the peak of third-tier Eternal will. Once he broke through to 100,000 will worlds, the will worlds would undergo a transformation and become fourth-tier Eternal will.
And fourth-tier Eternal will was the will level of a Legendary Realm Lord. At present, only six Legendary Realm Lords had reached fourth-tier in will, while the other Immortal Realm Lords were all only at third-tier.
If his will broke through, that would also be a massive increase in strength for Ye Tian, perhaps even allowing his strength to match that of the six guild masters directly.
However, opening up this final will world was also incomparably difficult. Ye Tian tried many times, but never succeeded, and many of his clone bodies were even shattered in the process.
After countless attempts, Ye Tian realized one problem: the Will Worlds secret technique was not good enough and had major flaws. For him to reach the peak of third-tier will was already far too heaven-defying; he could rely on clone bodies to try again and again, but others could not.
Now, he had to refine the Will Worlds secret technique anew before it would be possible to break through the will bottleneck and advance to fourth-tier will.
Ye Tian had also obtained quite a few will cultivation secret techniques, and had even found some will technique inheritances in the Azure Void Ruins, but many of those will cultivation secret techniques were not suitable for him. Moreover, the foundation of his Will Worlds had already been established, so it was not suitable to casually switch techniques. Thus, he could only draw upon the essence of other techniques to perfect the Will Worlds secret technique.
And so, Ye Tian began to perfect the Will Worlds secret technique.
In any case, with clone bodies as experiments, Ye Tian could repeatedly refine and modify the Will Worlds secret technique, and thus his rate of improvement was very fast.
In just dozens of Eternal years, Ye Tian successfully raised the Will Worlds secret technique by several levels.
The new Will Worlds secret technique imitated the Source Sea, transforming the inner world into a will Source Sea, and other will worlds into spacetime domains surrounding the entire Source Sea will world. Such a will world would be extremely stable, and it would be very easy to open up new will worlds.
Of course, transforming the inner world into a will Source Sea was a very crucial step. Once such a will world took shape, it would become an incomparably terrifying will world.
He planned to merge all of his will into such a will world and go all out to open up a will Source Sea.
During this process, many will worlds would probably collapse, but as long as he succeeded in opening up the will Source Sea, he would be able to recover quickly.
This was a very bold move; if not for the clone bodies, Ye Tian would not dare to try it.
