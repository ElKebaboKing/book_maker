Chapter 1575: Researching Compatibility, Success!

"This is the place!"
Ye Tian looked at the island in front of him, confirmed the specific location, and said with certainty.
This kind of island was a relatively common place in the Source Sea, but the vast majority of islands had no value, producing almost no resources. However, for ordinary cultivators, every blade of grass and tree on the island was a treasure. After all, influenced by the source energy of the Source Sea, the plants that could survive here were naturally extraordinary. But for an Invincible, they were of no use, so no one came to pick them.
Ordinary cultivators could not enter the Source Sea, resulting in the islands of the Source Sea being almost ignored.
However, such an island had given birth to an Eternal Source Item.
At this time, the island was already covered by a grand array set up by Jin Zu, hiding the aura of the Eternal Source Item. Even for Ye Tian, it was difficult to sense the aura of the Eternal Source Item. If he hadn't obtained Jin Zu's memories, he wouldn't have thought that an Eternal Source Item was here.
*Swoosh!*
Ye Tian stepped onto the island, tore open a portal according to the method in Jin Zu's memories, and walked inside the grand array.
As soon as he entered the mysterious grand array, he saw a thunder stone emitting black lightning.
Ye Tian truly didn't know what this Eternal Source Item was before, because Jin Zu didn't know either. After all, before an Eternal Source Item was fully born, no one could calculate the specific Eternal Source Item.
Now that Ye Tian saw this thunder stone, he finally recognized it.
"It's actually the Black Thunder Stone, ranked 199th among Eternal Source Items!"
Ye Tian was quite excited.
The value of the Black Thunder Stone was incredibly high. Although its rank was not as high as Eternal Source Items like the Xumi Golden Mountain, they were both in the top 100s, so the gap wasn't very large.
Moreover, the Black Thunder Stone could release Thunder Source Power, which could be used for body tempering. Although the effect of tempering the constitution was not as good as the Xumi Golden Fruit, it could temper the body continuously!
Of course, using the Black Thunder Stone for body tempering continuously would consume its source power.
Additionally, tempering the body with the Black Thunder Stone would injure the Invincible themselves, so a large amount of healing items needed to be prepared; otherwise, it wouldn't have a very good effect.
"I was just thinking about tempering my physical body. The Black Thunder Stone can at least allow my physical body to improve a bit more," Ye Tian thought.
Thus, he immediately took the Black Thunder Stone and then went to the other two locations where Eternal Source Items were located.
Unfortunately, those two Eternal Source Items had not yet been fully nurtured, so Ye Tian could only choose to give up and wait to come back for them in the future.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian's original body obtained the Black Thunder Stone and began to use it to temper his constitution, preparing many healing treasures. Some were resources exchanged from the Sky Wind Sea, and some were purchased from chambers of commerce under other forces.
Subsequently, Ye Tian's original body began his closed-door cultivation.
At the same time, Ye Tian began to slaughter large numbers of Tribulation Beasts, using them to exchange for resources, and then purchasing treasures that could temper his physical body and soul.
In this way, long years passed.
Unknowingly, several hundred Great Tribulation Eras had gone by.
At this time, several Invincibles had been born in the human race, one of whom was Ye Jinpeng, who was already a Fifth-class Invincible.
Compared to Ye Tian, Ye Jinpeng's subsequent progress speed was naturally slow, but compared to other Invincibles, such a speed was terrifying.
During these several hundred Great Tribulation Eras, Ye Tian had spent almost his entire net worth. Furthermore, the countless Tribulation Beasts hunted over these hundreds of Great Tribulation Eras were an astronomical number, all of which were spent to purchase various treasures no less than the Xumi Golden Fruit.
Initially, these treasures had a good effect on him, but after eating too many treasures similar to the Xumi Golden Fruit, the effect became worse and worse.
Ye Tian's Endless Source Body and cultivation had both improved to the Extreme of a Second-class Invincible, and there was no way to continue improving.
Additionally, Ye Tian had also created Supreme Ancestral Techniques. Through the physical transformation caused by the Supreme Ancestral Techniques, his strength increased significantly once again.
He had created more than one Supreme Ancestral Technique; there were several.
To create top-tier Supreme Ancestral Techniques, Ye Tian frequently went to comprehend the Void Source Tablet, spending all the points he had.
Now, Ye Tian's strength was far, far greater than before.
But even so, when Ye Tian went to challenge the third floor of the Void Tower, he ultimately failed.
"There's only one last way left: researching compatibility," Ye Tian thought.
On this day.
Ye Tian cloned Ye Jinpeng's body and began to study Ye Jinpeng's soul.
This was equivalent to studying himself. He didn't even need to care about soul damage; if the soul collapsed, he could just clone it again.
Therefore, even Ye Jinpeng himself wouldn't dare to do this, but Ye Tian did.
This was equivalent to having infinite materials. No one else had such means. Even if the Void Source Ancestor were still alive, he wouldn't be able to research compatibility like Ye Tian.
At first, Ye Tian scrapped Ye Jinpeng's soul several times without researching anything.
But on the fifth time, Ye Tian finally discovered the uniqueness of Ye Jinpeng's soul.
If the soul were decomposed into countless soul particles, then general soul particles were all the same. However, deep within Ye Jinpeng's soul, there were a very small number of soul particles that were different from the others. These few soul particles were very compatible with the source fluctuations of the Eternal Soul Wood.
It was precisely because of the existence of these special soul particles that Ye Jinpeng could achieve 100% compatibility with the Eternal Soul Wood source, allowing him to easily absorb the power of the Eternal Soul Wood source to temper his soul strength.
In the following days, Ye Tian began to study these special soul particles to change his own soul structure and construct these special soul particles.
This step was very, very difficult, but Ye Tian kept researching, even using his 413 cloned bodies' souls to study, failing time and time again.
Finally, after researching for a full three Great Tribulation Eras and failing countless times, Ye Tian finally succeeded.
Of course, after the first success, Ye Tian didn't immediately let his original body try. Instead, he continued to research until he succeeded every time, and only then did he let his original body's soul begin to try.
Deep within the original body's soul.
Ye Tian selected a small soul area and chose a few soul particles within it, treating these soul particles like stars in the starry sky. Then, he used the Eternal Soul Rule to transform these soul stars step by step from the root. After years of evolution, they finally turned into those soul particle stars of Ye Jinpeng.
During the evolution process, not a single step could be wrong. This was also why Ye Tian had spent three Great Tribulation Eras.
Now, Ye Tian's compatibility with the Eternal Soul Wood was also 100%. He could absorb the source of the Eternal Soul Wood to enhance his soul strength.
However, he didn't have any Eternal Soul Wood right now. The bit of Eternal Soul Wood source on Ye Jinpeng was too little; he didn't care for it. Even if he did, he wouldn't snatch the opportunity of his own junior.
Therefore, he set his sights on Jin Zu's corpse and the Eternal Soul Wood on it.
Although Longze was incomparably dangerous—anyone who wasn't a Supreme Being would almost certainly die upon entering—Ye Tian had already thought of a way.
