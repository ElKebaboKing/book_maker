Chapter 1601: The Entry Late-Stage Endless Eternal Body!

After Ye Tian broke out of the encirclement, he did not stop there, but continued searching for the Eternal Tribulation Race.
Once he was surrounded, he would immediately break out of the encirclement, absolutely never lingering in battle.
At this moment, he had roughly figured out the strength of this Eternal Tribulation Domain; it was not very powerful.
Moreover, this Eternal Tribulation Domain had already been discovered. If they waited for the entrance to stabilize, the next batch of Supreme Beings would enter here.
Even if the entrance disappeared, those powerful Supreme Beings of the eighth or ninth level could completely tear open an entrance and let the Third Stage Supreme Beings enter here.
By then, the Eternal Tribulation Race in this Eternal Tribulation Domain would simply be unable to hold them back, and this Eternal Tribulation Domain would absolutely be destroyed.
Therefore, he had to seize this opportunity now and gain as many benefits as possible.
As time passed, more and more Supreme Beings poured in here, and the Eternal Tribulation Race completely fell into a disadvantage.
This day.
A large group of Eternal Tribulation Race members set up a great formation and tore open another exit, leaving this Eternal Tribulation Domain.
They knew very well that if they did not leave, they would be slain by the experts outside.
After all, the Eternal Tribulation Race and those living beings grown in other domains and the Eternal Heaven were mortal enemies; only some special lifeforms in the Source Sea would not deliberately target them, but some special lifeforms of the Source Sea also wanted the Eternal Tribulation Crystals on them.
It had to be said that the Eternal Tribulation Race was simply a moving treasure vault; everyone wanted to take a bite out of them.
Rumble!!!
The Eternal Tribulation Race successfully tore open a gap, and a large number of Eternal Tribulation Race members rushed out.
There were over ten thousand Eternal Tribulation Race members here, and they were also all the remaining Eternal Tribulation Race members in this Eternal Tribulation Domain.
……
Source Sea.
The Eternal Tribulation Race rushed out, and very quickly they were discovered.
A large number of Supreme Beings began hunting down the Eternal Tribulation Race, and Ye Tian also came out of the Eternal Tribulation Domain at this time.
Just as he was about to follow the other Supreme Beings to pursue this group of Eternal Tribulation Race members, a change occurred.
“You actually dare to attack my race, courting death!”
A black giant hand that covered a vast stretch of Source Sea territory tore through the Source Sea void and reached in.
The Source Sea was incomparably stable; the fact that it could be torn apart showed just how terrifying the other party’s strength was.
Those Third and Fourth Stage Supreme Beings could not withstand the collision with this black giant claw; their Eternal Tribulation bodies shattered one after another, turning into corpses.
Then countless streaks of black light pierced through the void and blasted toward the Supreme Beings one by one.
Many Supreme Beings simply could not contend with these black lights and were shot dead one after another.
Although Ye Tian was relatively far away, to such a powerhouse, that distance was nothing at all. Thus, a streak of black light also locked onto him and blasted over.
Ye Tian did everything he could, unleashing one defensive Supreme Technique after another, turning them into layers of light shields.
The black light pierced through layer after layer of light shields, and in the end it was blocked by Ye Tian’s Endless Eternal Body, but he was still injured.
The power of this strike was enough to kill a Fifth Stage Supreme Being. If not for the fact that his Endless Eternal Body had fused with the Ancient Star Eternal Body and its life-preserving ability was too powerful, he would not have been able to withstand the black light’s attack either.
“This mysterious powerhouse should also be from the Eternal Tribulation Race; its strength is definitely above an Eighth Stage Supreme Being, and it may even be a Ninth Stage Supreme Being!”
Ye Tian guessed.
Rumble!!!!
A powerful Supreme Being appeared and instantly blocked that Eternal Tribulation Race powerhouse. The two sides began fighting fiercely.
But after only a few exchanges, that Eternal Tribulation Race powerhouse wrapped up the remaining Eternal Tribulation Race members and disappeared without a trace. Clearly, it did not dare delay any longer; otherwise, if it were surrounded by multiple powerhouses, it would not be able to escape either.
The surviving Supreme Beings all sighed in relief, but there were truly too many Supreme Beings who had fallen this time.
“Entering an Eternal Tribulation Domain without an organization really is too dangerous!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
The Eternal Tribulation Race in this Eternal Tribulation Domain was indeed not powerful, but the Eternal Tribulation Race had experts. Once they got in touch with some powerhouse for help, Third Stage Supreme Beings and even Fourth or Fifth Stage Supreme Beings were just ants.
If a Ninth Stage Supreme Being were in charge, only then could a low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain be easily taken down.
“It’s time to leave!”
Ye Tian immediately left, distancing himself from this area before returning to the Void Source Hall.
After returning, Ye Tian took out those Eternal Tribulation Crystals.
“182 Eternal Tribulation Crystals, not a bad harvest!”
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Even a single Eternal Tribulation Crystal was already very valuable, let alone so many of them. However, most of these Eternal Tribulation Crystals came from Eternal Tribulation Race members with the strength of First Stage Supreme Beings, so their grade was not very high, and their value was not as great as the Eternal Tribulation Crystals from stronger Eternal Tribulation Race members.
But for Ye Tian, so many Eternal Tribulation Crystals were enough to advance his Endless Eternal Body somewhat.
Afterward, Ye Tian began secluded cultivation to absorb and refine the Eternal Tribulation Crystals.
The energy contained within the Eternal Tribulation Crystals was extremely vast, so completely absorbing and refining them would take a very long time. However, Ye Tian’s Eternal Ancestor Tribulation could take effect, increasing his absorption and refining speed by well over tens of thousands of times.
Thus, in merely ten thousand years, Ye Tian could completely refine one Eternal Tribulation Crystal.
As the energy of the Eternal Tribulation Crystals poured into his body, the Endless Eternal Body seemed like a seed that had absorbed nourishment and was growing vigorously; this was a situation that had never happened before.
As the Endless Eternal Body improved, Ye Tian’s strength also improved accordingly.
However, the improvement brought by one Eternal Tribulation Crystal was not obvious.
So Ye Tian continued refining Eternal Tribulation Crystals.
Gradually, one Eternal Tribulation Crystal after another was absorbed and refined by Ye Tian, and his Endless Eternal Body was also growing rapidly.
More than ten million years later, Ye Tian had completely refined all the Eternal Tribulation Crystals, and his strength was much greater than before.
“Pity, I still haven’t reached the Minor Endless Eternal Body level!”
Ye Tian sighed.
Perhaps if it were someone else, their Eternal Body would already have reached Minor Stage long ago; if it were an ordinary Eternal Tribulation body, it might even have directly reached Major Stage or even Perfected Stage.
But his Endless Eternal Body was too powerful, so it had only advanced to the Entry late-stage realm, not even reaching Entry Peak.
However, even having improved to this extent, he could still easily dominate others’ Minor Stage mid-tier Eternal Bodies.

Starry Ancient Domain.
Star Ancestor was now one hundred percent certain that Xu Chen had betrayed him and betrayed the Ancient Star Race; at the same time, he was also preparing to lay everything bare.
“Xu Chen!”
Star Ancestor roared.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian appeared and arrived at the Ancient Star Tower.
“Ancestor, what is it?”
Ye Tian pretended to know nothing and asked.
