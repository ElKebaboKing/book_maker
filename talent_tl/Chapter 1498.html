Chapter 1497: The Primordial Ancestor Law Compatible with the Holy Realm
!
Knowing the key to breaking through to the Strongest Primordial Ancestor, Ye Tian began to comprehend the rules of the Holy Realm within the Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
For him, the rules of this unfamiliar Holy Realm were vastly different from those of the Dark Forbidden Zone. Furthermore, the comprehension of this body was inferior to his original body, and he could not use the "Evolution" ability to comprehend the rules; everything had to rely on himself.
Therefore, it would be extremely difficult for Ye Tian to create a Primordial Ancestor Law belonging to the Holy Realm.
Most importantly, it was too difficult for the two worlds to communicate.
Ye Tian currently had no way to contact his duplicated body, so it was unclear exactly when he would be able to create a Primordial Ancestor Law.
Holy Realm, Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
In the blink of an eye, several domain years had passed.
By this time, Ye Tian's duplicated body had already integrated into the Sea-Sky Holy Sect under the identity of He Yuan. Not a single... Holy Beast suspected him; he was virtually no longer at risk of exposure.
Additionally, Ye Tian occasionally obtained some fragmented Holy Laws from the Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
Holy Laws were similar to the Primordial Ancestor Laws of the Dark Forbidden Zone; only by merging with a Holy Law could one become a Holy Beast.
Ye Tian could not merge with the Holy Laws, but he could use them as a reference to understand and master the rules of this world, which was a huge help in creating his own Primordial Ancestor Law.
With the status of this body, it was not difficult to obtain some fragmented Holy Laws, and he could even manage to get some complete Holy Laws.
Unlike the Dark Forbidden Zone, there were many Holy Laws in the Holy Realm. Although they were still very precious, they were not difficult to obtain.
Because Holy Beasts in the Dark Holy Realm frequently fell and died, there were many masterless Holy Laws.
Furthermore, some of the strongest Holy Beasts could also create other Holy Laws, enriching the number of Holy Laws in the Holy Realm.
Therefore, there were many Holy Laws here, and the difficulty of obtaining them was naturally low.
Unfortunately, the Holy Laws here were not suitable for the human race; otherwise, Ye Tian would have prepared to bring a batch of Holy Laws back to the human race to create a batch of Primordial Ancestors.
Profound Void Ancestral World.
Ye Tian's original body could not progress and could only continuously create Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, occasionally creating Primordial Ancestor Laws.
He walked the path of the Endless Primordial Ancestor, so creating a Primordial Ancestor Law for a single path was possible. Although he had created some before, some paths had not been created yet, as the Endless Primordial Ancestor extended into many paths.
Theoretically, he could create countless Primordial Ancestor Laws.
Of course, this was quite difficult.
Perhaps at the beginning, he could create some Primordial Ancestor Laws, but later on, it became more and more difficult, even requiring countless domain years to create a single one.
In the blink of an eye, a hundred domain years passed. Ye Tian created three Primordial Ancestor Laws and placed them in the Human Race Holy Temple. Only Supreme-level Domain Ancestors with very significant contributions were eligible to exchange for them and merge to become Primordial Ancestors.
After that, another hundred domain years passed, and he created one more Primordial Ancestor Law. Clearly, creating Primordial Ancestor Laws was much harder than before.
Later on, Ye Tian needed many domain years to create a single Ancestral Law.
Domain years passed one after another. Unknowingly, the first domain cycle of the Third Dark Calendar passed.
The first domain cycle lasted for a full twelve [units], and Ye Tian had effectively cultivated for 120,000 domain years. But the total number of Primordial Ancestor Laws he created was only twenty-three. Now, it would likely take one or even several domain eras to create a single Primordial Ancestor Law.
Several more domain eras passed. Although Ye Tian created more Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, his overall strength did not improve much. However, the strength of the human race improved significantly, and it had now become the number one power in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
In terms of the number of Primordial Ancestors alone, the human race had already exceeded double digits. This was naturally thanks to the Primordial Ancestor Laws Ye Tian created, which increased the number of human Primordial Ancestors. There are currently thirteen Primordial Ancestors.
This was only because Ye Tian did not give out Primordial Ancestor Laws casually; one had to make a great contribution to exchange for them. Otherwise, the number of human Primordial Ancestors would have surely exceeded twenty.
Additionally, under Ye Tian's cultivation, his wife, son, daughter, and sister—the four of them—had all stepped into the Elite Primordial Ancestor realm and were only one step away from being Top-tier Primordial Ancestors.
If the duplicated body could bring back enough resources from the Sea-Sky Holy Sect, it would be enough to push their strength to Top-tier Primordial Ancestor combat power.
....Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
Ye Tian's duplicated body was still bitterly cultivating and comprehending to create a Primordial Ancestor Law. Unfortunately, the original body was not here, so the difficulty of creation increased countless times. Thus, he had not yet created a single Primordial Ancestor Law.
In fact, if he were creating an ordinary Primordial Ancestor Law, he might have succeeded already.
But what Ye Tian wanted to create was an Endless Primordial Ancestor Law that fit the rules of the Holy Realm, so the difficulty was very high.
He also followed a path similar to the Dark Forbidden Zone, starting by creating basic Primordial Ancestor Laws for Gold, Wood, Water, Fire, Earth, Time, Space, and so on... and then combining them to create the Endless Primordial Ancestor Law.
Now, after several domain eras, he still had not created a single Primordial Ancestor Law, but he was close to actually succeeding.
He was currently creating a Gold Primordial Ancestor Law. This law was not ordinary; it was definitely at the top tier among Primordial Ancestor Laws.
Unlike the Gold Primordial Ancestor Law of the Dark Forbidden Zone, this law was completely compatible with the Holy Realm. However, Ye Tian also based it on the original body's physical structure and cultivation path, making it extremely suitable for the original body, though perfect compatibility was impossible.
After all, the original body was a creature of the Dark Forbidden Zone, while this was a Primordial Ancestor Law transformed from the rules of the Holy Realm, so it naturally could not be perfectly compatible.
One day.
Ye Tian's duplicated body laughed aloud because the Gold Primordial Ancestor Law had been successfully created. This was a good start.
"It took five domain eras to create one Primordial Ancestor Law. Creating twelve Primordial Ancestor Laws certainly cannot be done in sixty domain eras, but it can definitely be done within the time of one Dark Calendar!"
Ye Tian thought.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian entered another long period of bitter cultivation, almost ignoring external affairs.
Even when some Holy Beasts invited Ye Tian to certain treasure lands or ruins to hunt for treasures, Ye Tian declined them all.
After all, it was not easy for this duplicated body to enter the Holy Realm. There would not be such a good opportunity next time. Therefore, this duplicated body absolutely could not fall. As long as there was a hint of danger, he would not do it.
Domain years passed one after another. In the blink of an eye, 107 domain eras had passed since the last birth of the Dark Soul Realm, equivalent to more than ten million domain years. What a long period of time.
Over such a long period, Ye Tian not only created the twelve basic Primordial Ancestor Laws of the Holy Realm but also merged them to create the Endless Primordial Ancestor Law compatible with the Holy Realm.
In other words, Ye Tian's first goal was accomplished.
"First, apply to enter the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land to guard it for a period. Once my duplicated body enters the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land, I can connect with the original body and share memories and information. At that time, the original body will also obtain the Endless Primordial Ancestor Law of the Holy Realm. Then, I'll send the Holy Realm resources accumulated by the duplicated body over the long years to the original body. Then, the original body can impact the realm of the Strongest Primordial Ancestor!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he applied to the two strongest Holy Beasts of the Sea-Sky Holy Land to enter the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land.
Many Holy Beasts of the Sea-Sky Holy Sect frequently entered the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land to inspect the situation, so Ye Tian's application naturally received a positive response quickly. Of course, it was not a permanent guard duty, but a return after a period of inspection.
