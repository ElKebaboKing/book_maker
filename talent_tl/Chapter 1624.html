“A bone segment like this is very valuable; take it away!”
Ye Tian swallowed this section of bone into his belly space and stored it away.
Subsequently, he began searching for other bone fragments. As time slowly passed, one bone fragment after another was collected by Ye Tian.
Just these bone fragments alone would fetch many precious resources if sold in the future; even a Half-Step Eternal realm expert would take an interest in them.
The only thing Ye Tian found somewhat regrettable was that the Eternal realm power contained within these bones was too thin, not even as good as the Eternal realm finger in Ye Tian’s possession.
Of course, the main reason was also that the power of this arm had been used to hatch Black Mist Worms. One arm was enough to hatch these nearly endless Black Mist Worms, making many Half-Step Eternal realm experts dread them immensely.
From this, it could be seen that the owner of that Eternal realm finger was far inferior to the owner of this arm.
After a long time, Ye Tian collected all the bones from this arm.
Suddenly.
Repulsive force came, and the Black Mist River began to expel countless Black Mist Worms outward.
Although Ye Tian was powerful, he still could not resist the Black Mist River’s repulsion.
No, strictly speaking, it was not the Black Mist River’s repulsion, but the repulsion of the special space where the Black Mist River existed.
From this, it could be seen that the Primordial Sea was very special; otherwise, it would have been impossible for an Eternal Tribulation power’s arm to become a Black Mist River in this state.
Ye Tian’s strength and realm were still far from being able to glimpse the true mysteries of the Primordial Sea.
After being expelled, the Black Mist River disappeared.
Meanwhile, the number of Black Mist Worms increased quite a bit, and after dispersing, the density of the surrounding black mist also became thinner.
As the years passed, the Primordial Sea changed little.

Ye Tian swallowed the Eternal Purple-Heart Flower, began refining it, and charged toward the seventh layer of Supreme Being realm.
Not long after, Ye Tian successfully broke through to the seventh layer of Supreme Being realm.
Breaking through from the sixth layer of Supreme Being realm to the seventh layer of Supreme Being realm was also a huge transformation, with a tremendous increase in strength.
At this point, Ye Tian could easily defeat his former self.
After another period of time, a piece of good news came from the human race: another Supreme Being had been born.
The one who broke through this time was still Ye Tian’s relative—his wife, Xiao Yue.
Under Ye Tian’s diligent cultivation, Xiao Yue had also lived up to expectations and broken through to the Supreme Being realm. Although she was not a powerful Supreme Being and her combat strength was not very strong, she was nonetheless a genuine Supreme Being.
Thus, the human race suddenly had four Supreme Beings.
Moreover, the number of first-tier Invincible Beings in the human race gradually increased; everything was developing in a good direction.
On this day, however, something that happened in the Primordial Sea left Ye Tian full of confusion and shock.
Primordial Sea.
Ye Tian, as usual, was searching for resources in the Primordial Sea.
After all, resources in the Primordial Sea were continuously produced, and thus resources would often appear there.
But suddenly, the void of the Primordial Sea split open in two places, and two hands reached in.
One hand was covered in scales, not like the hand of a humanoid lifeform, while the other looked like the hand of a humanoid lifeform—fair and youthful, yet filled with a terrifying aura.
The two hands immediately grabbed a large number of Black Mist Worms. These Black Mist Worms tried to corrode the two hands, but they simply could not shake them.
The two hands easily carried away many of these Black Mist Worms, grabbing again and again.
No one knew how long had passed when a voice came through.
“Enough, just take these Black Mist Worms; don’t take too many!”
“Fine, we’ll come again next time to take more!”
As soon as the words fell, the hands of the two powerhouses withdrew from the Primordial Sea, and the void fissures healed as well.
Throughout the entire process, Ye Tian did not dare move even a little. He even tried his best to hide himself inside other Black Mist Worms, letting countless Black Mist Worms cover him so he would not be easily discovered.
Only after those two mysterious beings disappeared did Ye Tian finally let out a breath of relief.
“Those two should be Eternal realm experts!”
