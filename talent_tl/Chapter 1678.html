Chapter 1677 Hunting the Void Beasts, Strength Increased!

The three Eternal Realm beings on the twelfth layer were naturally somewhat stronger, but Ye Tian still fought through arduous slaughter and ultimately defeated the three Eternal Realm beings, passing the twelfth layer.
However, on the thirteenth layer, Ye Tian still failed, mainly because the gap in combat strength was somewhat large.
But Ye Tian felt that if he continued to increase the fusion between his will and body, he would be able to defeat the three Eternal Realm beings on the thirteenth layer.
“This time, after consecutively passing the eleventh layer and the twelfth layer, I obtained ten tribulation yuan of comprehension time. I should have a chance to go one step further. Then, if I create some top-tier will Eternal Skills, I should be able to easily pass the thirteenth layer of the True Void Legend Tower.”
Ye Tian muttered.
Then.
He entered the True Void Legend Land, where he began cultivating and continuously fusing his will with his body.
Just as he started cultivating, he unexpectedly wanted to try fusing his will with the innate eternal rules.
Once he tried, it was astonishing. He immediately discovered that in the True Void Legend Land, the speed at which his will fused with the innate eternal rules was many times faster.
“This...”
Ye Tian understood a little.
The True Void Dao Mark Tree in the True Void Legend Land was actually a sacred tree for fusing will with innate eternal rules, but it also had an effect on the fusion of will and body.
Next, Ye Tian used the True Void Legend Land to begin fusing his will with the innate eternal rules, but he did not give up on the process of fusing his will with his body. After all, if he wanted to become stronger and break through to the Eternal Realm or even the level of a legendary lord as soon as possible, then it would be better to fully fuse his will with his body.
Therefore, the fusion of his will with his body was more important to him at present.
Unknowingly, ten tribulation yuan of time passed.
At this point, the degree of fusion between Ye Tian’s will and body had reached 16%, but the progress of fusion between his will and the innate eternal rules was also quite good. All of the will worlds he had opened up had already completely fused with the innate eternal rules.
However, if he were to use a standard to describe the progress of fusion between his will and the innate eternal rules, he could not give one.
After all, even the will within those will worlds and the innate eternal rules were not fully and completely fused; only a little had fused, as if the will worlds themselves could not fully bear it either.
But compared with before, his strength had still increased quite a bit.
After that, Ye Tian continued to create will Eternal Skills. It took him a full several dozen Eternal Years to perfect the will Eternal Skills he had previously created one by one to the level of top-tier will Eternal Skills, and their threat level even surpassed ordinary legendary Eternal Skills.
Finally, this day came.
Ye Tian passed the thirteenth layer in the True Void Legend Tower and obtained another five tribulation yuan of cultivation comprehension time.
However, five tribulation yuan of cultivation time did not help much. In the True Void Legend Land, Ye Tian only increased the degree of fusion between his will and body from 16% to 17%.
“Unless I can have endless Eternal Years of comprehension time, it is nearly impossible to completely fuse my will and body through the True Void Legend Land!”
Ye Tian came to understand.
If he truly wanted to quickly fuse his will and body, he probably needed other opportunities.
“Maybe I can go hunt Void Beasts!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
His current strength was already extremely powerful, comparable to a formidable Immortal Realm expert, and he also had legendary Eternal Skills and will Eternal Skills tailored to Void Beasts. Even against a half-step Immortal Realm expert, the damage he dealt to Void Beasts might not be lower.
Moreover, he possessed multiple copy bodies, so even if one fell, it could be quickly recondensed, and the cost paid was not great.
Therefore, hunting Void Beasts was profitable for him.
As for other Eternal Realm experts, even if they had clones, they would not dare take such a risk; losing a clone would be a huge loss.
“Then let’s go hunt Void Beasts. With three copy bodies attacking together to hunt First-Tier Void Beasts, given my current strength, I should be able to instantly kill a First-Tier Void Beast,” Ye Tian estimated.
On this day.
Ye Tian left the range of the spacetime territory, and the three copy bodies plunged deep into the void region, all the while keeping the copy talent activated at every moment.
In a place like the void region, the range covered by the copy talent was much broader than the range he could sense by himself. If he encountered an extremely powerful Void Beast or a large-scale Void Beast pack, he would temporarily retreat and avoid contact with them.
After traversing for a long time, Ye Tian still had not found a single Void Beast. Clearly, after their previous defeat and retreat, the Void Beasts had withdrawn very deep, making it impossible to find even one here.
Just like that, Ye Tian flew for a long time before finally sensing the existence of a Void Beast.
“Second-Tier Void Beast, no good. I can’t instantly kill it. This place is probably close to the Void Beasts’ territory; if I can’t instantly kill it, I won’t be able to escape in time.”
Ye Tian gave up on this target, then left the area and resumed searching for other Void Beasts.
Very soon.
Ye Tian found a First-Tier Void Beast. The other party was slowly roaming through the void region.
“It’s this one!”
Ye Tian immediately activated his Void True Body, entering a void state so that he would not be easily discovered.
After drawing close to this Void Beast, Ye Tian immediately made his move.
“Void-penetrating Secret Sword!”
With a hiss!
The illusory Void-penetrating Secret Sword directly pierced through this First-Tier Void Beast, striking it so heavily that it was left critically wounded and on the verge of death.
Next, Ye Tian’s second attack arrived immediately.
Will Severing Void Sword!
This sword directly split the Void Beast in two, and its vitality was completely severed.
This Void Beast had fallen!
Swish!
Ye Tian collected the Void Beast’s corpse and moved at extremely high speed, while the three copy bodies dispersed and left separately. In this way, even if they encountered a heavy encirclement, they would not be wiped out completely.
If they were wiped out completely, he would not be able to condense copy bodies here again and would have to send copy bodies over once more, which would undoubtedly waste time.
Fortunately, his speed was fast enough, and he did not encounter pursuit from Void Beasts.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian hunted Void Beasts continuously in this void region, and he also devoted one copy body to cultivating in a not-too-distant area, continuously refining Void Beast materials to increase the fusion between his will and body.
Just like that, Ye Tian hunted one Void Beast after another, and the degree of fusion between his will and body kept rising, which meant his own strength kept becoming stronger as well.
18% fusion!
19% fusion!
20% fusion!
...
Ye Tian had been very smooth at first, but as the number of vanished Void Beasts grew, it finally drew the attention of the Void Beast ruler in this area.
This Void Beast ruler was a fourth-tier Void Beast, comparable to the most top-tier Immortal Realm lords, an existence second only to a legendary lord.
It commanded many Void Beasts, and noticing that many of its subordinates had gone missing, it was not very concerned, but it still went to investigate.
Thus.
Groups of Void Beasts appeared in this area, and this brought trouble to Ye Tian’s hunting.
