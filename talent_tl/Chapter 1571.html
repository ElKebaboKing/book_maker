Chapter 1570: The Influence Brought by the Void God Son!

Having become a second-class Invincible, and with his main body having entered the Void Source Palace, Ye Tian was not worried about his identity being discovered.
He prepared to meditate on the Void Source Tablet; with his current points, he could afford to meditate for a very long period of time.
Moreover, even while meditating on the Void Source Tablet, he could hide his identity. Others would only know that a Void God Son occupied a meditation slot for the Void Source Tablet, but they would not know who that Void God Son was.
Thus, Ye Tian put on a Tribulation Tool mask capable of concealing his aura. After waiting for some time, the previous batch of Void Sons meditating on the Void Source Tablet emerged.
"Enter!"
In an instant, Ye Tian followed the next batch of Void Sons into the space for meditating on the Void Source Tablet.
......
"Artifact Spirit, why did I not enter?"
A Void Son roared within the Void Source Palace.
He had finally waited through a long period of time and gathered 500 points, and it was finally his turn to meditate on the Void Source Tablet. He hadn't expected that while the other nine Void Sons had entered, he alone was left behind.
At this moment, the Artifact Spirit of the Void Source Palace sent a transmission: "A Void God Son has taken your slot. You can only wait until the next Great Tribulation Era!"
"What? A Void God Son!!!"
This Void Son was shocked.
He knew of the title 'Void God Son,' a monstrous genius who stood above the Void Sons, but he had never seen one. He had even thought that Void God Sons did not exist.
But he never expected that a Void God Son would appear and even take his slot, forcing him to wait for another Great Tribulation Era.
Was this luck, or misfortune?
Before the Void Source Tablet.
The other nine Void Sons saw Ye Tian, and their expressions shifted one after another.
Previously, ten Void Sons had been waiting to enter, and Ye Tian was not among them; instead, there had been another Void Son. That Void Son couldn't have suddenly put on a mask, as there was no need for it.
In other words, Ye Tian had suddenly jumped the queue to enter this place.
"He is a Void God Son!"
This thought emerged in the minds of the nine Void Sons.
"Is Your Excellency a Void God Son?"
One Void Son inquired.
Ye Tian ignored them, instead entering a small space to begin meditating on the Void Source Tablet.
Seeing that Ye Tian was so cold and wearing a mask, clearly not wanting to reveal his identity, the other Void Sons said nothing more and also went to meditate on the Void Source Tablet.
This time, Ye Tian's primary goals for meditating on the Void Source Tablet were several. The first was to comprehend more Eternal Rules, which would be of immense benefit for creating Supreme Ancestral Techniques in the future. The second was to continue researching the Endless Source Physique.
Although he had created the Endless Source Physique, he had only created the method for condensing it. His application of the Endless Source Physique was still very superficial. Therefore, he needed to continue meditating on the Void Source Tablet, attempting to combine the Endless Source Physique with various Invincible Ancestral Techniques to exert even more powerful effects.
In a flash, one Great Tribulation Era passed.
The other nine Void Sons were all teleported away, leaving only Ye Tian behind.
He had no time limit for meditating on the Void Source Tablet. If he wished to meditate, he could continue indefinitely, unless multiple Void God Sons were meditating here simultaneously, which would then limit a Void God Son's meditation time.
But now, with only Ye Tian as the sole Void God Son meditating here, he naturally wouldn't be asked to leave.
Soon, another nine Void Sons arrived, one of whom was the Void Son whose slot had been taken by Ye Tian last time.
Seeing that Ye Tian was already here meditating on the Void Source Tablet, this Void Son was not surprised at all.
He had long known that a Void God Son had taken his slot. It was perfectly normal for this Void God Son not to have left and to continue meditating here.
However, the other Void Sons were bewildered.
"Who is this Void Son?"
They asked in confusion.
"He should be a Void God Son!" the Void Son who knew the situation messaged the others.
"What? A Void God Son!"
The nine Void Sons were incomparably shocked.
Outside.
As the nine Void Sons emerged from the Void Source Tablet space, they quickly began spreading the news that a Void God Son was meditating on the Void Source Tablet in the Void Source Palace. This news spread rapidly.
Immediately, the Void Sons of various Eternal Heavens and Earths became aware of it.
In the Heaven Scale Realm.
Qiu Ye Source God also heard the news regarding a Void God Son appearing in the Void Source Palace to meditate on the Void Source Tablet.
"A Void God Son... unfortunately, I was only a step away from becoming a Void God Son. However, my Void Source Physique was not Perfected, and once the foundation is set, it cannot be changed."
"This Void God Son should be a newly ascended Void God Son. Those ancient Void God Sons no longer look highly upon the opportunities within the Void Source Palace. For them, the greatest benefit of the Void Source Palace is using it for travel. The Void Source Tablet is of little help to them, and the Void Source Palace has no other opportunities."
"Unless they can become the Void Lord of the Void Source Palace, only then would they obtain staggering opportunities. But becoming a Void Lord is something that almost cannot exist."
Qiu Ye Source God knew quite a bit about Void God Sons and even knew one personally. That person's strength was much greater than his own, and thus, from that Void God Son's mouth, he had learned some secrets regarding Void God Sons.
The Void Source Palace was indeed one of the great opportunities of the Source Sea, but in the entire Source Sea, opportunities that rivaled or even surpassed the Void Source Palace were not non-existent.
It was just that an ordinary Invincible could not learn too many secrets about the Source Sea. Only a Supreme Being could come into contact with many of those secrets.
A Supreme Being was the true starting point of the Source Sea.
---------------
One Great Tribulation Era after another passed. The batches of Void Sons meditating on the Void Source Tablet changed wave after wave, while Ye Tian remained there meditating the entire time, making many Void Sons envious beyond measure.
If it were them, where would they get so many points!
Unknowingly, Ye Tian had meditated for a full twenty-plus Great Tribulation Eras. By this time, his comprehension of the Eternal Rules had reached a terrifying level. Creating Invincible Ancestral Techniques was now as simple for him as creating ordinary martial techniques.
He had already created countless Invincible Ancestral Techniques. Creating more now offered him little help.
After such a long period of meditation, his cultivation had not increased much, but his control over the Endless Source Physique had reached a state of being Perfected. He could even perfectly combine Invincible Ancestral Techniques with the Endless Source Physique to exert his most powerful strength.
"It is time to leave. Continuing to meditate here would just be a waste of time!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He emerged from that place and entered the Void Tower.
He needed a long period of combat to thoroughly integrate his insights and gains.
Dark Forbidden Domain.
On this day, a major event occurred within the Human Race.
