Chapter 1617 Primordial Sea!

A minute passed.
Ye Tian and Wuyuan Source Ancestor both stopped fighting. The result was very obvious: Ye Tian passed the assessment.
Of course, whether he could be selected still depended on the Twelve Ancestors’ choice.
“Endless Ancestor, your strength is truly powerful, far beyond my imagination. I believe the Twelve Ancestors will definitely choose you as an inner-circle member!”
Wuyuan Source Ancestor said in a certain tone.
“I hope so!”
Ye Tian smiled faintly and did not worry at all. Even if he could not enter the Source God Pavilion inner circle this time, it was no big deal. After all, he would only be missing one opportunity, and it would not affect him much.
Not long after, all the applicants’ assessments came to an end.
And the battles of every applicant who passed were all reviewed once by the Twelve Ancestors. When they saw the battle scene between Ye Tian and Wuyuan Source Ancestor, each and every one of them was shocked.
They all understood Wuyuan Source Ancestor’s strength, yet a Sixteenth Supreme Being could suppress Wuyuan Source Ancestor. How was that possible?
Unless it was a half-step Eternal Realm powerhouse who had cut down their cultivation after reaching that level, only then could they possess such battle strength.
But it was obvious that Ye Tian was not such a half-step Eternal Realm powerhouse, otherwise Wuyuan Source Ancestor should have been able to tell.
Whoosh!
Wuyuan Source Ancestor was summoned before the Twelve Ancestors.
Fenggu Source Ancestor asked, “Wuyuan Source Ancestor, how did Endless Ancestor feel to you?”
Wuyuan Source Ancestor thought for a moment before saying, “That Endless Ancestor is monstrously gifted. Not only did he create an Eternal technique, but what he condensed should also be an Eternal Body, and at the very least, an upper-level Eternal Body. Moreover, his foundation is incomparably solid, and his comprehension of Eternal laws also surpasses that of ordinary half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses.”
Wuyuan Source Ancestor’s account was about the same as the Twelve Ancestors’ guesses.
After Wuyuan Source Ancestor left, the Twelve Ancestors had made their decision.
“This Sixteenth Supreme Being called Endless Ancestor has potential that may surpass us. I suggest taking him into the Source God Pavilion inner-circle member ranks.”
“Naturally, he should be taken in. We must be fair; he is indeed extremely outstanding and qualified to become one of the inner-circle members.”
“Then let’s choose him!”
The Twelve Ancestors unanimously passed it and chose Ye Tian to become a Source God Pavilion inner-circle member.
……
Source God Pavilion.
In the misty void, black thrones appeared one after another. Each throne had a person seated on it; these black thrones were occupied by Source God Pavilion inner-circle members.
Among them, there were twelve golden thrones, and the ones seated on them were the Twelve Ancestors.
At this moment, one throne was empty, but very soon a figure appeared and sat down on it.
The one who arrived was Ye Tian.
“Welcome, new member!”
“Welcome, new member!”
“Welcome, new member!”
Starting with the Twelve Ancestors, one inner-circle member after another offered their welcome.
“Greetings, everyone!”
Ye Tian also greeted them.
At this time, Fenggu Source Ancestor tossed over a mass of information: “Endless Ancestor, these are some records regarding the inner-circle members. After you absorb them, you’ll understand.”
Ye Tian nodded, took hold of the mass of information, and quickly learned about some matters concerning the inner-circle members.
For example, the inner-circle members of the Source God Pavilion controlled the birthplaces of certain special resources, and now Ye Tian also knew about them.
Most importantly, the Source God Pavilion inner-circle members could go to the Primordial Sea.
The Primordial Sea was a special blessed land, the place where the source of the Source Sea was born. Every drop of seawater there was condensed from boundless Source Sea origin energy.
However, the Primordial Sea was located deep within a special Source Sea spacetime. Without a special guide, it was impossible to find the Primordial Sea at all.
Because of this, ordinary Supreme Beings simply did not know of the Primordial Sea’s existence, and had not even heard of it.
Only some powerful factions knew about the Primordial Sea, and the Source God Pavilion had the means to enter it. Every inner-circle member could enter the Primordial Sea.
For example, this time, just after Ye Tian became a Source God Pavilion inner-circle member, the Source God Pavilion was preparing to enter the Primordial Sea.
At the meeting.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor said, “The origin tide of the Primordial Sea is about to begin this time, and it is the best opportunity to enter the Primordial Sea. However, after entering the Primordial Sea, I will not be able to protect you, and the likelihood of perishing is rather high. If you do not want to go, you may stay behind. Once we come out of the Primordial Sea, we will hold a private exchange meeting, and everyone can trade the treasures and resources they need.”
“Zhenhe Sword Ancestor, I won’t be going this time!”
One Eight Supreme Being said.
Those few Seven Supreme Beings also immediately declared that they would not enter the Primordial Sea, clearly lacking confidence in themselves.
“Endless Ancestor, are you going?” Zhenhe Sword Ancestor looked at Ye Tian. “Your potential is very high, so I suggest you don’t go this time. You can go next time.”
“Zhenhe Sword Ancestor, I’m going!” Ye Tian said. “I have a special avatar. This time, I’ll enter the Primordial Sea with my avatar, so even if it perishes, the loss won’t be great.”
Special avatar!
Many Supreme Beings were immediately full of envy upon hearing this.
The stronger the path of cultivation one walked, the harder it was to possess a special avatar. Moreover, many avatars had limited potential and simply could not become Supreme Beings. Thus, having such a powerful avatar was truly exceedingly difficult.
Generally speaking, the stronger one’s cultivation, the fewer powerhouses there were who possessed avatars.
“A special avatar!”
Upon hearing this, Zhenhe Sword Ancestor no longer tried to persuade Ye Tian.
With a special avatar, Ye Tian was not afraid of perishing at all, so he naturally should go to the Primordial Sea and try his luck.
This time, a total of nine Supreme Beings were unwilling to go to the Primordial Sea, while all the other Supreme Beings would go.
This time, not only was Ye Tian entering the Primordial Sea for the first time, but many Supreme Beings had also joined the Source God Pavilion inner-circle member ranks long ago, yet had never been to the Primordial Sea before. Thus, Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others began explaining the specific situation of the Primordial Sea.
For example, after entering the Primordial Sea, one must leave before the origin tide ends; otherwise, one would be trapped in the Primordial Sea and would certainly perish.
To this day, no one trapped in the Primordial Sea has ever been able to survive.
Because a half-step Eternal Realm powerhouse had once tested this with a special avatar and failed to survive. Once the origin tide ended, the Primordial Sea would release a terrifying black mist that would devour even half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses.
Therefore, one had to leave before the origin tide ended.
Second, the Primordial Sea would give birth to many treasures. Eternal source objects were only ordinary among them; Eternal resources were everywhere, and there were also many resources helpful for breakthroughs from Sixteenth Heavens to Seventeenth Heavens and even Eighteenth Heavens.
Moreover, there were also many resources there that could enhance Eternal Bodies.
Not only that, the Primordial Sea also had some treasures that would drive even half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses crazy.
The Twelve Ancestors listed a treasure catalog and gave one to each member, lest anyone fail to recognize a treasure and miss it. That would truly be a pity!.
