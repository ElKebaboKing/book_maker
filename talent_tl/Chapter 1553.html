Chapter 1552: Supreme Qingyang, Escort Mission!

"So many Fourth-tier Invincibles!"
Ye Tian scanned the surroundings and found that a full ten thousand Fourth-tier Invincibles had gathered here. Although he wasn't sure exactly how many Fourth-tier Invincibles there were in Tianfeng Sea, seeing the current situation, it likely far exceeded ten thousand. Otherwise, it would be impossible for so many Invincibles to accept a mission that was almost certain death.
Soon, a First-tier Invincible descended upon the area.
"This is Tianfeng Sea's Supreme Qingyang!"
A Third-tier Invincible introduced.
"Greetings, Supreme Qingyang!"
Numerous Invincibles spoke respectfully one after another.
The highest-ranking members of Tianfeng Sea were Supreme-level members, and Supreme-level members were ranked only below the several leaders of Tianfeng Sea; they were also referred to as Supremes.
For example, if Ye Tian were promoted to a Supreme-level member, he would also be called Supreme Wanfa.
In Tianfeng Sea, to become a Supreme-level member, one had to be at least a First-tier Invincible.
Supreme Qingyang looked at the many Invincibles and said, "Since you have all accepted this mission, there is one thing that must be stated in advance: regarding the mission content, no one is allowed to disclose it. Anyone who dares to violate this will be considered a traitor to Tianfeng Sea. Additionally, every Invincible must swear an oath under the Eternal Source Object Oath Stone. Once the oath is made, even for a clone, the oath will spread to the main body. Therefore, if any of you dare to leak secrets, you will definitely perish. If you are unwilling to swear the oath, then leave now!"
As soon as he finished speaking, some Invincibles left, clearly unwilling to swear the oath or having other concerns, or perhaps they already regretted it and now had an excuse.
In the end, there were still over eight thousand Invincibles willing to swear the oath, including Ye Tian.
Subsequently, Supreme Qingyang took out the Eternal Source Object Oath Stone.
The Eternal Source Object Oath Stone was merely the lowest grade of Eternal Source Object; its value was not high, and it was not even as good as a Rule Heavenly Object.
While the grade of an Eternal Source Object was high, if it had no use, its value was naturally not great. The Oath Stone could not increase strength or provide benefits; it could only make people swear oaths. Even if its grade was high, its value remained low.
However, if an oath was sworn on the Oath Stone, even if one was far from the Tianlin Realm, they would still be bound by the Oath Stone.
After all, the effect of the Eternal Source Object Oath Stone covered the entire Source Sea; no matter where one fled, they could not escape the Oath Stone's effect.
Before long, everyone had sworn the oath.
"Supreme Qingyang, we have all sworn the oath. What exactly is this mission?"
One Invincible asked.
Supreme Qingyang said, "Your mission is to escort an item. I plan to divide you into small teams, with ten Invincibles per team. I will give each of you a route, and once you reach the destination, the mission will be complete!"
"It's that simple? Could there be powerful experts lying in ambush along the way?" An Invincible realized this and asked on the spot.
"Correct, there will indeed be ambushes!" Supreme Qingyang nodded.
At this time, Ye Tian asked, "Supreme Qingyang, since there will be ambushes, why only dispatch Fourth-tier Invincibles for this mission instead of Third-tier or higher Invincibles, or even First-tier Invincibles?"
The other Invincibles also looked toward Supreme Qingyang, equally curious.
Supreme Qingyang smiled and said, "That's a good question, but dispatching Third-tier or higher Invincibles would be even more dangerous. The biggest difference between a Fourth-tier Invincible and a Third-tier Invincible lies in their aura. Third-tier Invincibles can absorb the source power of the Source Sea, and in reality, they are already considered Source Sea lifeforms. Their aura is worlds apart from a Fourth-tier Invincible. In the eyes of powerful experts, it is very difficult for Third-tier or higher Invincibles to hide themselves; even if they hide in an Otherworld Domain, they are like a ball of fire hidden in the darkness. Conversely, if a Fourth-tier Invincible hides in an Otherworld Domain, even a First-tier Invincible would find it difficult to discover them. Additionally, when native Invincibles of the Tianlin Realm enter the Otherworld Void, their strength is heavily suppressed, especially for Third-tier or higher Invincibles. If they enter an Otherworld Domain, not only will their strength be severely suppressed, but they also cannot stay for long. As for Second-tier or higher Invincibles, whether they are creatures of an Otherworld Domain or an Eternal Domain, they can no longer enter an Otherworld Domain. This is a restriction of the Source Sea's rules; only a Supreme Being has a way to break it, but even a Supreme Being cannot stay in an Otherworld Domain for long. Furthermore, Supreme Beings will not participate in this mission, so you can rest assured."
Ye Tian hearing this finally understood. Second-tier and higher Invincibles were actually unable to enter Otherworld Domains.
In that case, once he became a Second-tier Invincible in the future, he probably wouldn't be able to stay in the Dark Forbidden Domain anymore.
From this rule, dispatching Fourth-tier Invincibles to travel through various Otherworld Domains was indeed the safest method. If they traveled through the Source Sea, dispatching weaklings wouldn't work; they would need to dispatch at least First-tier Invincibles, and the enemies' strength would likely be very great. Tianfeng Sea didn't dare to dispatch First-tier Invincibles, otherwise, if they lost too many First-tier Invincibles, Tianfeng Sea might drop in rank among the Saint-Name level forces.
Suddenly.
An Invincible asked curiously, "Supreme Qingyang, isn't it said that if an Otherworld Domain gives birth to three Invincibles, that Otherworld Domain will be promoted to an Eternal Domain? If one cannot continue cultivating in an Otherworld Domain after becoming a Second-tier Invincible, how can an Otherworld Domain be promoted?"
Supreme Qingyang said, "Although this question has nothing to do with the mission, I'll tell you anyway. Even if one leaves their own Otherworld Domain, they still leave an imprint within it. As long as that Otherworld Domain has not completely undergone Annihilation, if a total of three Supreme Beings are born from it, the Source Sea will pour in endless source power to facilitate the Otherworld Domain's evolution into an Eternal Domain. If an Otherworld Domain truly gives birth to a Supreme Being, that Supreme Being would likely do their best to extend the lifespan of the Otherworld Domain, from a hundred thousand kalpa-cycles to several hundred thousand or even several million kalpa-cycles. Back then, with the help of Supreme Beings, the Tianlin Realm's lifespan was extended to five million kalpa-cycles, and eventually, three Supreme Beings were born, and it became an Eternal Domain."
After that, no one asked any more questions unrelated to the mission.
And from beginning to end, no one asked what the mission item was. Since Supreme Qingyang didn't say, it must involve secrets that were too important to be disclosed.
Finally, Ye Tian and the others were divided into teams, and they received a route as well as the mission's final destination.
Everyone had a box in their hands, but in reality, only one of these boxes was real, while the others were fakes.
In other words, only one team possessed the real item, while the other teams were all decoys.
