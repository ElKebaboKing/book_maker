Chapter 1546: Slaying Three Invincible Ones!

Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor and the others were well aware that the Ancestor of Myriad Laws occupying a foreign realm and provoking the Blood Mist Alliance was far less important than the truly major matter facing the alliance.
That matter was enough to change the destiny of the Blood Mist Alliance and was the most urgent priority at hand.
The Sacred Realm.
Ye Tian had cleared the entire Sacred Realm. Even the most deeply hidden Supreme Beings and the Sacred Beasts below that level had all been found and wiped out by him one by one.
This was a necessary step in occupying a realm. Only when the living beings of a foreign realm were completely annihilated could one easily seize the authority of that world. If many living beings or several Supreme Beings remained, it would be nearly impossible to seize authority or transform the entire Sacred Realm.
"Let the transformation begin,"
Ye Tian murmured.
Boom!!!
Under the control of secret techniques, the Second-tier Gate that Ye Tian had specifically established began to expand its base, encroaching upon the entire territory of the Sacred Realm.
Occupying a foreign realm required the use of a Gate. A Gate essentially utilized loopholes in the rules to cross between foreign realms; therefore, it could also use those loopholes to change part of a foreign realm's rules or even assimilate them.
Of course, the prerequisite was possessing the secret techniques to occupy a foreign realm's rules.
Such secret techniques were absolute secrets within foreign realms, but in an Eternal Realm like the Tianlin Realm, they were not particularly precious. The Tianfeng Sea had various such secret techniques, and they were quite cheap. Thus, Ye Tian had purchased several such techniques to handle various special situations.
At this rate, it would only take several billion years for the Second-tier Gate to completely transform the entire Sacred Realm, making the rules of this foreign realm similar to those of the Dark Forbidden Zone.
This way, the living beings of the Dark Forbidden Zone could enter the Sacred Realm to live.
Even if the Second-tier Gate were destroyed, the rules of the Sacred Realm would remain unchanged and would not revert.
Time passed slowly, and the speed at which the Sacred Realm was being transformed grew faster and faster, even far exceeding the flight speed of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian remained stationed near the Second-tier Gate connecting the Dark Forbidden Zone and the Sacred Realm to prevent anyone from destroying it. Once the Second-tier Gate was destroyed, all efforts would be wasted, just as had happened to the Blood Mist Alliance before.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian sensed Invincible Ones approaching, and more than one.
At first, he thought they were just Invincible Ones passing through.
After all, if an Invincible One were traveling to the Tianlin Realm, they would pass through various foreign realms. Passing through the Sacred Realm was quite normal.
As long as they didn't provoke him, he wouldn't stop these passing Invincible Ones.
But soon, he realized these Invincible Ones were not just passing through.
Three Invincible Ones descended here together and were searching for him. They quickly locked onto the location of the Second-tier Gate and his position.
"People looking for trouble?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He hadn't offended any Invincible Ones, so logically, no Invincible One should be coming to trouble him.
After a brief thought, he roughly guessed who these people were.
The Blood Mist Alliance!
Several Supreme Beings of the Sacred Realm had been here earlier, unwilling to leave, and had been blasted to death by him.
Therefore, it was very likely that the Blood Mist Alliance, a super-tier power, had come to find trouble with him. And since they had dispatched three Invincible Ones at once, it was clear how much importance the Blood Mist Alliance placed on him.
Rumble!!!
The three Invincible Ones descended not far from Ye Tian, their eyes fixed on him.
If an Invincible One did not take action or reveal their aura, it was difficult to know their realm and strength.
Ye Tian's speed of progress was too fast, so he had learned a secret technique to hide his aura early on. He was worried that walking through the Tianfeng Sea would attract unnecessary trouble and attention due to his rapid increase in strength. Therefore, he habitually hid his aura, only revealing the realm of an Invincible One, but it was difficult for others to know his true strength.
In the eyes of others, Ye Tian was definitely an Invincible One, but it was hard to confirm whether his aura was at the level of a Sixth-rank or Fifth-rank Invincible One.
The three Invincible Ones of the Blood Mist Alliance naturally did not believe Ye Tian was a Fifth-rank Invincible One. After all, Ye Tian had only recently broken through to the Invincible level; he was bound to be a Sixth-rank Invincible One, and a very weak one at that.
"Are you the Ancestor of Myriad Laws?"
The leader of the Blood Mist Alliance, Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor, asked coldly.
"I am the Ancestor of Myriad Laws. Are you perhaps the Invincible Ones of the Blood Mist Alliance?" Ye Tian said.
"Correct!" Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor nodded. "You actually killed the Supreme Beings of our Blood Mist Alliance; this is a provocation against us. Furthermore, you dare to occupy the Sacred Realm. This Sacred Realm was already designated as the next foreign realm for our alliance to occupy. You are seizing the territory of the Blood Mist Alliance. You are also an Invincible One, and cultivation is not easy. If you are willing to compensate our Blood Mist Alliance with one million Source Rule Calamity Crystals, then we shall let this matter go!"
One million Source Rule Calamity Crystals was nothing to a slightly wealthy Invincible One, but for a newly ascended Invincible One, it was definitely a massive sum.
Therefore, the demand from Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor and the others was incredibly excessive.
"Heh!"
Ye Tian gave a cold laugh.
A mere million Source Rule Calamity Crystals was indeed nothing to him; he could easily produce them.
But why should he give them to the Blood Mist Alliance?
"I am in a very bad mood right now. If you get lost immediately, I won't kill you. If you stay here any longer, then stay here forever!" Ye Tian threatened directly.
"Seeking death!"
The three Invincible Ones of the Blood Mist Alliance were furious and immediately attacked.
They intended to instantly kill this Invincible One. After all, the three of them had cultivated for a long time and were definitely at the top among ordinary Sixth-rank Invincible Ones, far surpassing the average.
However, they had encountered Ye Tian.
Puchi!
Ye Tian struck with his blade. The Mid-grade Calamity Tool saber slashed through the bodies of the three Invincible Ones.
In an instant, the physical bodies of the three Invincible Ones were cut in two, and a terrifying power began to erode their flesh.
In the next moment, their bodies were being annihilated and their souls were collapsing, completely unable to recover.
"No!"
Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor cried out in despair.
Then, Ye Tian struck with another slash, completely erasing their lives.
Three Invincible Ones died at Ye Tian's hands like mere ants.
----------------------
In a foreign realm controlled by the Blood Mist Alliance, Mingli Mist Ancestor was stationed there when he suddenly felt a surge of palpitations and panic.
"Could something have happened to Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor and the others? It shouldn't be possible, right?" Mingli Mist Ancestor thought.
He did not possess a communication item capable of transmitting across two foreign realms. Such items were extremely precious and not something he could own, so he had no way to contact Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor and the others.
However.
Once Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor and the others perished, some of the treasures they left at the Blood Mist Alliance headquarters would become ownerless.
Consequently, the Blood Mist Alliance soon learned that Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor had fallen, and this news was immediately relayed to Mingli Mist Ancestor.
"This is bad, we've provoked a formidable enemy!"
Mingli Mist Ancestor's pupils shrank violently.
