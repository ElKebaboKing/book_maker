Chapter 1616 Regular Combat Power: Top-Tier Eight-Stage Supreme Being!

In the blink of an eye, the Black Dark Forbidden Domain had passed through over a thousand kalpa-era periods.
By now, the human race had produced many powerhouses at the Invincible level; there were even many third-tier Invincible ones, but second-tier Invincible ones were still relatively rare.
And after experiencing over a thousand kalpa-era periods, Ye Tian’s cultivation had not only reached the extreme of the five-stage Peak Supreme Being, he had also created another Eternal technique, called the Shattering Void Sword. It fused various Eternal laws and was an incomparably powerful Eternal technique, much stronger than the Annihilation Finger.
Strictly speaking, the Annihilation Finger was meant to activate a sliver of a finger from a powerhouse of the Eternal realm; in reality, it was not very powerful. The Shattering Void Sword, on the other hand, was the Eternal technique Ye Tian created for regular combat power.
He had originally also wanted to create an Eternal technique for defense or speed, but felt that he currently did not really need one.
Having the Endless Eternal Body, his ability to preserve his life far surpassed other Supreme Beings, so he did not need a defensive Eternal technique to save his life. As for a speed-type Eternal technique, it was not very necessary either; at least it was not as important as an offensive Eternal technique.
“It’s time to break through to the six-stage Supreme Being. I’ve stayed at the five-stage Supreme Being for too long!”
Ye Tian murmured.
Regarding the attempt to reach the six-stage Supreme Being, he was now very confident, and thus he did not go collect resources to assist the breakthrough.
Afterward, he directly chose to break through.
After a long period of time, Ye Tian successfully shattered the bottleneck and advanced to the six-stage Supreme Being realm, with his strength surging once again by a large margin.
From the six-stage Supreme Being onward, it would truly be very difficult to break through a single realm in the future. It was often not something talent alone could determine; unless one was a congenital Eternal Life, it would be very hard to break through the shackles. One needed a very long span of time and many resources to break through the bottleneck.
For example, Tianlin Source Ancestor had spent who knew how many tens of thousands of kalpa-era periods, and still had not broken through to the seven-stage Supreme Being realm to this day.
Among the countless Eternal heavens in the Source Sea, the number of Supreme Beings below the six-stage Supreme Being was large, but the number of Supreme Beings at the seven-stage Supreme Being and above was extremely small.
Any seven-stage Supreme Being held an incomparably high status, and was almost considered a top existence in the Source Sea.
After Ye Tian became a six-stage Supreme Being, he also knew that in the short term he had no way to charge into the seven-stage Supreme Being realm; the difficulty would be extremely, extremely great.
However, he still needed to make preparations for breaking through to the seven-stage Supreme Being in the future.
This day.
He, as usual, came to the Source God Pavilion. The Source God Pavilion’s transactions had brought him many benefits; whenever he lacked some treasure, he could come here to trade for it, without having to spend long years searching for something.
Suddenly.
The Twelve Ancestors of the Source God Pavilion announced a piece of news: the Source God Pavilion was going to invite one inner-circle member.
Aside from the Twelve Ancestors, below them were 108 inner-circle members, and outside the inner-circle members were a large number of outer-circle members.
Outer-circle members had no real constraints and could trade freely, but they could not obtain many of the benefits.
Inner-circle members, however, could not only receive guidance from the Twelve Ancestors, but also enjoy some special treatment and benefits, which was why everyone wanted to become an inner-circle member.
However, inner-circle members were almost fixed; the number of 108 inner-circle members would not change unless another inner-circle member fell, and only then would one be invited from among the outer-circle members.
Of course, to become an inner-circle member, one still had to undergo an assessment. Only after passing the assessment would one truly become an inner-circle member.
……
“Master, I suggest you become an inner-circle member of the Source God Pavilion. The Twelve Ancestors know many secrets, and there are some resources that only the Twelve Ancestors know about. Back then, the previous master was invited to become one of the inner-circle members. And within the Source God Pavilion, aside from the Twelve Ancestors, there are also some Half-Step Eternal realm experts among the inner-circle members, though they are extremely low-key々〃.”
The artifact spirit suggested.
“Then let’s give it a try!”
Ye Tian nodded.
Afterwards, Ye Tian applied for the inner-circle member assessment.
He felt that with his current strength, he should be enough to serve as an inner-circle member of the Source God Pavilion, right?
Source God Pavilion.
After Ye Tian’s application passed, he immediately entered an assessment area.
Whoosh!
A eight-stage Supreme Being appeared before Ye Tian; this person was a member of the Source God Pavilion’s inner circle.
“Six-stage Supreme Being?”
Seeing Ye Tian’s cultivation realm, the other party was somewhat surprised. A mere six-stage Supreme Being dared to apply to become an inner-circle member of the Source God Pavilion?
Among the Source God Pavilion’s inner-circle members, the topmost were Half-Step Eternal realm experts, the majority were nine-stage Supreme Beings, a minority were eight-stage Supreme Beings, and only a very few were seven-stage Supreme Beings.
As for six-stage Supreme Beings, there had practically never been a precedent for becoming inner-circle members.
“Primordial Ancestor, your assessment is to withstand my attacks for one minute. If you succeed, you will enter the waiting selection pool. The Twelve Ancestors will choose based on the combat situations of all of you applicants; whether you can ultimately be selected will depend on luck.” After speaking, the other party added, “By the way, my name is Wuyuan Source Ancestor!”
“．‖ Wuyuan Source Ancestor, make your move!”
Ye Tian said indifferently.
Boom!!!!
The two exchanged blows.
Wuyuan Source Ancestor specialized in fist techniques, and each punch unleashed terrifying attacks, shaking the Source God Pavilion’s void continuously.
In Ye Tian’s eyes, Wuyuan Source Ancestor was a very powerful eight-stage Supreme Being, but not among the most top-tier eight-stage Supreme Beings, simply because Wuyuan Source Ancestor did not even know a single Half-Step Eternal technique. He was merely extremely skilled in many Supreme techniques, having reached the level of using them as he pleased.
“Shattering Void Sword!”
Ye Tian held a top-grade source weapon longsword and, with one sword strike, tore through endless laws as it landed on Wuyuan Source Ancestor.
With a thunderous boom!
The two were actually evenly matched.
No, it should be said that Ye Tian held the upper hand.
At this moment, a sword wound appeared on Wuyuan Source Ancestor’s body and would not disperse at all.
“How is this possible? You actually created an Eternal technique! And you are merely a six-stage Supreme Being, yet your combat power can actually match a top-tier eight-stage Supreme Being!”
Wuyuan Source Ancestor was shocked.
Ye Tian, however, was not surprised at all. When he was at the five-stage Supreme Being, his regular combat power was already enough to match weaker eight-stage Supreme Beings.
Now that his cultivation had risen to the six-stage Supreme Being realm, his combat power was naturally terrifying, almost comparable to Tianxu Calamity Ancestor after self-severing his cultivation.
It was just that Tianxu Calamity Ancestor possessed all kinds of powerful secret treasures and weapons, whereas he did not, so his regular combat power still could not match a nine-stage Supreme Being.
However, once he used the finger of an Eternal realm powerhouse, he would probably be able to unleash a strike comparable to a weaker Half-Step Eternal realm expert.
“Wuyuan Source Ancestor, do we still need to continue fighting?”
Ye Tian asked.
“Of course. I need to test your strength, and that is also the rule set by the Twelve Ancestors!”
Wuyuan Source Ancestor said.
Boom boom boom!!!齐!
