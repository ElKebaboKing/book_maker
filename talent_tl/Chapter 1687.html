Chapter 1686 Black Mountain Prison, Immortal Realm Prisoners, Legendary Realm Prisoners!

“What deal?”
Ye Tian was somewhat curious, and a little expectant as well.
Lord Binghan said, “Lord Endless, you obtained these inheritances. They should have been obtained from some relic, right? I want to know the location of that relic.”
“The location of the relic.”
Ye Tian understood Lord Binghan’s intention. She wanted to go to that relic and obtain some benefits, but in fact there were not many treasures left there. At the very least, he had not found any treasures there.
Thus, Ye Tian said, “Lord Binghan, that relic has no resources. It is merely a battlefield world. Inside, it is almost entirely ruins, with only some fragments of Eternal Realm corpses and building fragments. I only found some useless inheritances there. A relic like that has no value. Even so, does Lord Binghan still want to trade for this piece of information?”
“Yes, even if it is only ruins, I still want to go and take a look.” Lord Binghan nodded.
“Then what can I get?” Ye Tian asked.
Lord Binghan pondered for a moment, then said, “Lord Endless, I obtained information from here, so naturally I will give you a piece of information as well. For example, the location of another relic, and that relic still has some opportunities. It has great value even for Immortal Realm lords. Immortal Realm lords entering it may even obtain quite a few opportunities.”
“Alright, I am willing to trade!”
Ye Tian agreed.
Immediately after, Ye Tian told Lord Binghan the location of the Quiet Void Relic world, and roughly explained some of the situation there.
“It is actually within the Void Beast territory!” Lord Binghan was dumbfounded. She had thought the relic was in some unfamiliar void region far away from the Source Sea and the Time-Space territory, but she never expected it to be within the Void Beast territory.
She stared at Ye Tian closely and examined him carefully.
It was hard for her to imagine how Ye Tian had gotten there, because there were fifth-tier Void Beasts there, and Immortal Realm lords had no chance of entering that relic at all. Unless they condensed one clone after another to probe it, but who would dare waste so many resources condensing clone after clone? The price to pay was far too great.
However, for her, she was not afraid of fifth-tier Void Beasts.
She could completely force her way into that relic world, and fifth-tier Void Beasts would be unable to stop her. Unless many fifth-tier Void Beasts were mobilized to besiege her, but the Void Beasts could not know the situation in advance, so such a scenario practically did not exist.
Lord Binghan also kept her word and told Ye Tian about another relic.
After the trade was completed, Ye Tian left the Extreme Ice Pavilion.
Afterward, he prepared to head to the relic Lord Binghan had told him about.
That relic was not in the Time-Space territory; otherwise, it would have long since been impossible to keep hidden, and almost all Immortal Realm beings would know about it.
However, that relic was not far from the Time-Space territory. Yet the void region was too vast, boundless and endless, and Immortal Realm beings were very unaccustomed to the environment of the void region. Moreover, there were no rules here, so the range they could probe would not be very wide. This caused a relic in the void region to be very difficult for Immortal Realm beings to discover.
If there were no specific location, Ye Tian would have no way to find that relic.
Afterward, Ye Tian went alone to that relic, without informing the other Immortal Realm beings.
As for what this relic contained, and what dangers there were, Ye Tian knew nothing at all. But at most he would lose a copied body, so he did not care in the slightest.
Very quickly, Ye Tian entered the void region and used Void Step, traversing the void region at extremely high speed. He soon arrived at the location of that relic.
……
Somewhere in the void region, a black continent floated here. But if one did not approach, then from afar, this place appeared completely void, with no trace of any continent at all.
When Ye Tian drew near to this continent, the surrounding void distorted, and threads of Innate Eternal Laws actually appeared. Then he stepped onto this black continent.
“Space-time reversal; this black continent is not simple!”
Ye Tian murmured.
He looked around, sensing the power in all directions, as if he were bound by layers upon layers of space-time. Every bit of his perception that extended outward had to shatter layer after layer of space-time.
He lightly punched the void, yet it only caused faint ripples.
Clearly, the suppression of space-time and all things here had reached a terrifying degree.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!
Ye Tian traversed a distance in one step and quickly arrived at a black mountain range in the center of the black continent. Within this black mountain range were many black mountains, and each black mountain looked rather strange.
When Ye Tian approached the black mountains and saw the ancient Stele on each of them, he then realized that these were not black mountains at all, but rather prisons.
Each black mountain was a prison binding enemies. Precisely because of this, the restraint and suppression of foreign objects here were so severe, and the black mountains themselves were the most terrifying seal, suppressing enemies and preventing them from ever emerging.
Which power had arranged this, Ye Tian had no way to investigate, but he quickly understood the meaning behind Lord Binghan’s words. This place was also a place of opportunities for Immortal Realm lords, where one could obtain some opportunities, and those opportunities were the enemies imprisoned within these prisons.
【Lord Qishui, a three-step Immortal Realm lord, once assassinated an Immortal Realm being during a great battle, causing the battle situation to fail. He has been suppressed here for countless ages, and is used for the disciples of Black Mountain to temper Eternal techniques. Immortal Realm lord disciples may enter, and their strength will be suppressed to the same realm as Lord Qishui. They must not kill Lord Qishui, or contribution points will be deducted.】
【Lord Chiming, a six-step Immortal Realm lord, betrayed Black Mountain and became a traitor to Black Mountain. He has been suppressed here, and is used for the disciples of Black Mountain to temper Eternal techniques. Immortal Realm lord disciples may enter……】
【Lord Xueqiong, a one-step Legendary Realm lord, once ambushed and killed disciples of Black Mountain, and was captured and suppressed here for countless ages, used for the disciples of Black Mountain to temper Eternal techniques. Legendary Realm lord disciples may enter……】
……
Ye Tian was extremely shocked when he saw the information recorded on each Stele.
Those suppressed and imprisoned here were all lords above the Immortal Realm, and there were even Legendary Realm lords, not just one.
Even Legendary Realm lords could be imprisoned, which showed how powerful this so-called Black Mountain was. But now Black Mountain may have declined, or else this place would not have become a relic, and the so-called Black Mountain force would not have appeared to this day.
“Black Mountain does not allow disciples to kill these prisoners, but now that Black Mountain no longer exists, I can kill these prisoners. If I kill them, I can obtain the resources and treasures on them. This should be the opportunity Lord Binghan mentioned, but……”
Ye Tian was very clear that if these prisoners were still alive, they must have experienced endless years of battle, and had once fought against who knew how many heaven-defying Immortal Realm beings. Under the condition of equal cultivation and strength, it would probably be extremely difficult to kill them.
The Immortal Realm beings in the Time-Space territory probably could not win, and Lord Binghan and the other Legendary Realm lords probably could not defeat those Legendary Realm prisoners either.
Ye Tian did not know whether this black continent had any other opportunities, but he was extremely interested in these enemies imprisoned within the prisons, and even had the desire to fight them one or two times.
Then, Ye Tian chose an Immortal Realm lord prisoner.
Lord Qishui!.
