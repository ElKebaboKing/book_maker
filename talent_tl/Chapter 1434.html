Chapter 1433: Innate Ancestral Talent
: Copy!
[Innate Ancestral Talent: Copy] Seeing the words "Ancestral Talent," Ye Tian was stunned.
"An Ancestral Talent, not a Holy Talent?"
Ye Tian originally thought he would give birth to an Innate Holy Talent, but he didn't expect it to be an Innate Ancestral Talent.
"Could it be related to the second qualitative change of my Holy Soul?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
"No!"
Ye Tian shook his head. "It's definitely not just the second qualitative change of the Holy Soul. In the Dark Holy Domain, there are people who have achieved the second qualitative change, but none have ever possessed an Innate Ancestral Talent.
It should be related to my large number of Ancestral Talents. I fused too many Holy Talents, which in turn fused into various Ancestral Talents. This directly affected my Holy Soul, causing the most suitable Innate Holy Talent to evolve into an Innate Ancestral Talent!"
The Innate Ancestral Talent 'Copy' was indeed related to his copying ability; it was a talent perfectly suited for him.
Ye Tian's consciousness connected with the Innate Ancestral Talent 'Copy,' and he learned the effect of this Ancestral Talent.
This 'Copy' was different from his Copy Talent; it copied skills.
For example, Holy Skills and Ancestral Skills—he could copy them and make them his own.
This ability was terrifying, second only to his Copy Talent! It could even be said that the birth of this 'Copy' Ancestral Talent was influenced by his original Copy Talent.
Without the existence of the Copy Talent, he wouldn't have been able to form the Innate Ancestral Talent 'Copy.'
"This way, I won't need to exchange for Ancestral Skills anymore!"
Ye Tian thought.
Of course, while this ability seemed incredible, Ye Tian wouldn't copy too many Ancestral Skills; ordinary Ancestral Skills weren't very useful to him.
Moreover, with his vast number of Ancestral Talents and the Evolution trait, he could fully deduce Holy Skills to the level of Ancestral Skills. Thus, the Innate Ancestral Talent 'Copy' was merely the icing on the cake.
Besides, there were very few Domain Ancestors in the Dark Holy Domain who knew Ancestral Skills, so he likely wouldn't find many good ones to copy anyway.
"If I get the chance in the future, I'll copy a few good Ancestral Skills!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Now that he had just broken through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, it was a time for rapid growth, so he did not end his seclusion but continued to cultivate.
Tempering the Invincible Domain Body was extremely difficult; reaching the limit would likely take a very long time.
However, he had gained too many benefits from the Holy Soul Realm. Many of those resources could be used to temper the Invincible Domain Body, so he wasn't worried about his cultivation speed.
But for now, he wanted to improve the strength of the Human Race.
For instance, he had obtained Spatial Holy Talent Crystals from the Holy Soul Realm, which could grant nine people the Spatial Holy Talent.
Additionally, he had obtained a lot of Star Soul Sand and Holy Gold Sand, which could easily create Invincible Domain Ancestors.
"Son, daughter, Yue'er, Xiao Yu, come here!"
Ye Tian sent a message to his family.
Soon, Xiao Yue, Ye Xingchen, Ye Hai, and Ye Yu arrived.
"Husband!"
"Father!"
"Brother!"
They called out.
"This Holy Blood Marrow can reshape your foundation. After taking it, your foundation will be reshaped to rival the top monstrous geniuses!"
Ye Tian took out the Holy Blood Marrow and handed it to the four of them.
"Holy Blood Marrow!"
They hadn't heard of it, but they knew it was extremely precious just by the name.
They didn't stand on ceremony with Ye Tian; they each took it and consumed the Holy Blood Marrow in front of him.
Boom!!!! The four of them began to transform; their entire beings started to undergo a metamorphosis.
From their physical bodies to their souls and then to their origins, everything was changing. This was what it meant to truly reshape one's foundation.
While some treasures could cause a qualitative change in the physical body, they couldn't change the soul or the origin; that wouldn't be called reshaping the foundation.
After reshaping their foundations, they would have a period of adaptation. During this time, as long as they worked hard, they could improve rapidly.
Days passed, and a full ten thousand years went by before the four of them completed their foundation reshaping.
At this time, Ye Tian stripped some Holy Talents from himself and gave them to the four of them.
In this way, they also became Holy Talent Users.
In the past, he hadn't dared to do this for fear of leaking the existence of Holy Talents, as the impact would have been too great.
But now he wasn't afraid. He had obtained the Spatial Holy Talent Crystals from the Holy Soul Realm. He would choose a few human geniuses and grant them the Spatial Holy Talent.
This way, other forces would discover that he had obtained such a treasure that could grant Holy Talents. Although they would be envious, since Ye Tian would have already used it, they naturally couldn't seize it.
In that case, they would also believe that the Holy Talents of Xiao Yue and the others were created using treasures like the Spatial Holy Talent Crystals.
At that time, they would only think that Ye Tian's luck was extraordinary and would never suspect his Copy Talent.
In the following days, Ye Tian began to select suitable candidates from among the human geniuses, placing character as the top priority and even personally conducting multiple assessments.
After a million years and numerous assessments, he selected eight human geniuses to be granted the Spatial Holy Talent.
The Human Race Holy Temple.
Eight human geniuses arrived here, witnessed by many human Domain Ancestors.
Ye Tian took out the Spatial Holy Talent Crystal and said, "This treasure was obtained by me from the Holy Soul Realm. I'm not sure of its exact name, so I call it the Spatial Holy Talent Crystal. Today, I will use it to grant the eight of you a Spatial Holy Talent.
Once you obtain the Spatial Holy Talent, you will become Holy Talent Users, and your talent will instantly leap above countless geniuses!"
Holy Talent Users!!! Upon hearing this, the eight human geniuses were immediately ecstatic.
They naturally knew what Holy Talent Users were! There weren't many Holy Talent Users in the Dark Holy Domain... only the descendants of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors had a hope of being born with a Holy Talent! They never expected that ordinary cultivators like themselves would one day have the chance to obtain a Holy Talent.
The other human Domain Ancestors were extremely envious, wishing they could seize the Spatial Holy Talent Crystal, but they didn't dare. It was Ye Tian's treasure, and they had no right to it.
Boom!!! Ye Tian activated the Spatial Holy Talent Crystal, and eight beams of light erupted from it, shooting into the bodies of the eight human geniuses.
Instantly, the souls of the eight human geniuses felt as if they were being remodeled by a terrifying, supreme power. Each of them was in great pain, but they all endured it.
Days passed, and it took a full few months before the Spatial Holy Talent was completely born within them.
At this moment, they sensed their souls slightly and realized the existence of the Spatial Holy Talent, making them all incredibly excited.
Immediately, they thanked Ye Tian one after another before withdrawing.
Afterward, Ye Tian went to the Battle World Tower and contacted the Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor.
He planned to trade the Spatial Holy Talent Crystal to the Battle Sect to prove that the human race's Spatial Holy Talents truly came from the crystal and were not from a fabricated origin.
This was why he had left one remaining slot for the Spatial Holy Talent.
After such a transaction, the Battle Sect would not suspect him, and other forces wouldn't either.
