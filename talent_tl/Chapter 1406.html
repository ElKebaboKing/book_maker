Chapter 1405: Soul Holy Light Greatly Increases
!
After Ye Tian returned to the War Ancestor Realm, his main body began to assault the ultimate realm of the Eternal Undying Saint Body.
The clone body had already elevated the Eternal Undying Saint Body to its ultimate realm, so for the main body to want to elevate the Eternal Undying Saint Body to its ultimate realm was naturally countless times simpler.
Thus, as soon as the main body heard, it began to assault the ultimate realm of the Eternal Undying Saint Body, and before long, the shackles were broken.
Human Realm.
Ye Tian had now elevated the Eternal Undying Saint Body to its ultimate realm, and then he went into seclusion once more.
Although he had cultivated the Eternal Undying Saint Body to its ultimate realm, he still needed to properly stabilize this realm.
If he didn't stabilize it properly, then the foundation of the Eternal Undying Saint Body would encounter a slight problem. Previously, he had broken through to this realm during battle, not only failing to consolidate it in time but also continuously fighting with Ancestor Qingyang, which led to issues with the Eternal Undying Saint Body.
However, that was a clone body, so it didn't matter how much it was put through; but the main body, could not be put through such turmoil.
In the blink of an eye, tens of thousands of years passed, and by this time, Ye Tian had completely stabilized the Eternal Undying Saint Body.
"The Eternal Undying Saint Body has been stabilized; it's time for the clone body to hand over the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit to the main body."
However, this clone body of Ye Tian was not prepared to return to the Profound Void Saint Realm; he wanted to keep this clone body in the War Ancestor Realm for cultivation.
If the clone body didn't return to the Profound Void Saint Realm, how would it hand over the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit to the main body? Ye Tian had already thought of a method, which was to enter the War Realm Tower.
The War Realm Tower could connect the Dark Saint Domain and the Profound Void Saint Realm. As long as Ye Tian's clone body and main body entered the War Realm Tower simultaneously, they could meet each other, and thus the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit could be handed over to the main body.
Next, Ye Tian headed to the War Realm Tower. The War Realm Tower was not located in the War Ancestor Realm, because too many cultivators from the Dark Saint Domain needed to enter the War Realm Tower, so it naturally couldn't be established in the War Ancestor Realm. Otherwise, wouldn't it require opening up the War Ancestor Realm, allowing countless outsiders to enter? The War Realm Tower was established in a giant city quite far from the War Ancestor Realm. This giant city was called War Realm City, and it belonged to the War Gate.
War Realm City was not a city-level power; it was merely a city specifically used for communication and trade.
Soon, Ye Tian arrived at War Realm City.
After entering War Realm City, Ye Tian directly entered the War Realm Tower.
At the same time, Ye Tian's main body also emerged from the Human Realm and directly entered the War Realm Tower of the Profound Void Saint Realm.
Inside the War Realm Tower.
Ye Tian's main body met his clone body. The clone body handed the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit to the main body, and then they separated.
After obtaining the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit, Ye Tian's main body left the War Realm Tower and returned to the Human Realm.
Human Realm.
"I wonder how much this one Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit can increase my Soul Holy Light..."
Ye Tian was very curious about this.
Immediately, he swallowed the Ice Crystal Lotus Fruit in one gulp and began refining it by circulating the Boundless Domain Law.
Rumble!!!! The energy of the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit erupted directly.
Within Ye Tian's soul, the tempered soul caused Ye Tian's soul to continuously transform into Soul Holy Light.
"What terrifying speed of improvement."
Ye Tian said, greatly astonished.
Subsequently, Ye Tian continued to circulate the Boundless Domain Law to temper his soul. With the help of the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit's energy, Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light continuously grew.
Days passed, and in the blink of an eye, tens of thousands of years had gone by.
By this time, Ye Tian had completely refined the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit.
"Compared to before, my Soul Holy Light has increased by more than 10 times."
Ye Tian was greatly astonished by the effect of the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit.
Compared to the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit, the effect of the Soul Saint Realm Flower was quite ordinary.
It must be said that obtaining the Ice Crystal Snow Lotus Fruit from the Ghost Fox Organization this time was undoubtedly a great opportunity. Otherwise, relying on his own cultivation, he didn't know how many years it would take to increase his Soul Holy Light to its current level.
Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light increased by 10 times, and his own combat power increased by at least several times.
If Ye Tian were to fight Ancestor Qingyang with his current combat power, he could easily defeat Ancestor Qingyang.
Yet, even so, Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light was still very far from condensing a Saint Soul.
After all, Ye Tian's soul was extremely powerful, far surpassing that of ordinary supreme domain ancestors. Therefore, for him to want to transform his entire soul into Soul Holy Light would require more time, and the difficulty would naturally be greater.
But once Ye Tian completely condensed a Saint Soul, his strength would also reach an incomparably terrifying level.
In contrast, a Saint Soul-level supreme domain ancestor like Ancestor Qingyang was merely a relatively ordinary Saint Soul-level supreme domain ancestor.
Dark Saint Domain, War Ancestor Realm.
Ye Tian's clone body continuously cultivated here, often entering various cultivation treasure lands.
The clone body did not need to enhance its Soul Holy Light; it specialized solely in cultivating the Saint Skill, Time Fragments.
Ye Tian's Saint Skill, Time Fragments, had only just barely reached the perfect realm, and was still quite far from the ultimate realm! If he were to cultivate in the Profound Void Saint Realm, the cultivation speed would naturally be far inferior to when he was in the Profound Void Saint Realm. If he cultivated in the War Ancestor Realm, he could enter many cultivation treasure lands, and the cultivation speed would naturally be very fast.
Therefore, he chose to leave the clone body in the War Ancestor Realm to enjoy its resources, thereby elevating the Saint Skill, Time Fragments, to its ultimate realm.
The following days were quite uneventful. Ye Tian continuously cultivated Soul Holy Light, but unfortunately, the Soul Saint Realm's help to him was diminishing. Perhaps before too long, the Soul Saint Realm would no longer be of much help to him.
By then, he would have to enter other Soul Saint Realms or rely on other opportunities to accelerate the cultivation of Soul Holy Light.
Otherwise, he feared it would take an endlessly long time for him to condense a Saint Soul.
Just like many supreme domain ancestors in the War Ancestor Realm, who had cultivated for endless years, relying on the War Gate's resources, they still couldn't condense a Saint Soul, and didn't even know how many more years it would take.
Were the War Gate's resources few? No, they were many, far surpassing ordinary powers.
Unfortunately, many of the War Gate's resources could not regenerate, and many opportunities were gone after being used once. If distributed, they couldn't cultivate many... supreme domain ancestors with Saint Souls.
Therefore, Ye Tian did not count on the War Gate to help him condense a Saint Soul; he still needed to rely on himself.
"I can only hope that the Profound Void Saint Realm will give birth to some opportunities that can help me quickly condense a Saint Soul!"
Ye Tian thought to himself:.
On this day.
The Profound Void Saint Realm gave birth to another holy artifact. The grade of this holy artifact was decent; although not as good as those on the Holy Artifact Ranking, it was much better than ordinary holy artifacts.
In the years that followed, almost every hundred thousand years, the Profound Void Saint Realm would give birth to a holy artifact, which greatly astonished many domain ancestors.
However, most of the holy artifacts fell into Ye Tian's hands; he certainly wouldn't complain about having too few holy artifacts.
Unconsciously, ten million years passed.
