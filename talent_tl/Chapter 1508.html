Chapter 1507: The Ambition of the Blood Mist Alliance!

How precious were the Origin Rule Calamity Crystals? Not only were they useful to Ultimate Ancestors, but they could even create one.
Therefore, once foreign experts heard news of an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal, they would naturally fight for it at any cost.
At this moment.
Ye Tian also received the news. With a single thought, he traversed time and space to arrive at the scene.
By the time he arrived, several Ultimate-level foreign experts had already descended.
Currently, several Ultimate-level foreign experts were besieging one individual, who had clearly seized the corpse of a top-tier Sacred Beast.
Under the siege of multiple Ultimate-level foreign experts, this individual was heavily wounded.
However, at that moment, two more Ultimate-level foreign experts arrived. One of them had even reached the second tier of the Ultimate level. Together, the three Ultimate-level foreign experts fended off the siege from the others.
"A step too late!"
Ye Tian shook his head.
His strength was roughly equal to that of the second-tier Ultimate-level foreign expert, so he had no way to kill them or seize their Origin Rule Calamity Crystal.
Soon, the three foreign experts fled. The others did not pursue them, knowing there was little hope. Unless two or more third-tier experts intervened, there was no chance of stopping them; otherwise, they would surely escape back to their base in a short time and send the Origin Rule Calamity Crystal back to their own foreign world.
Ye Tian wasn't surprised at all that he failed to snatch the first Origin Rule Calamity Crystal.
His strength had not yet reached the level where he could sweep through everything, so it was normal that he couldn't seize it.
However, through this encounter, he gained a general understanding of the strength of these foreign experts.
Most Ultimate-level foreign experts were only comparable to first-tier Ultimate Ancestors. Second-tier Ultimate Ancestors were rare, accounting for only about one-tenth, and he had yet to see a single third-tier Ultimate Ancestor.
Perhaps among the many foreign worlds, third-tier Ultimate Ancestors were incredibly scarce, all working hard to break through to the Invincible Ancestor level, and wouldn't necessarily participate.
The foreign power corresponding to the only Rank 2 Gate in the Sacred Domain World might possess third-tier Ultimate Ancestors, but they might not necessarily deploy them.
Without third-tier Ultimate Ancestors, Ye Tian wasn't as worried.
His biggest fear was the appearance of an Invincible Ancestor; if that happened, he could forget about competing for even a single Origin Rule Calamity Crystal.
……
Sacred Domain World, Blood Mist Forbidden Zone.
This was the forbidden zone where the only Rank 2 Gate in the Sacred Domain World was located, established by the foreign power known as the Blood Mist Alliance.
The Blood Mist Alliance was not actually a single organization but a coalition of multiple forces. Strictly speaking, it was formed by several powers from the Blood Mist World specifically to invade other foreign worlds.
Such an alliance was actually very difficult to form. For instance, in the Sacred Domain World, the three Great Sacred Sects would not unite; after all, they could each open their own Gates to plunder resources from other foreign worlds, so there was no need to join forces.
After all, experts who reached the level of Ultimate Ancestor were extremely proud and would not easily unite.
But the Blood Mist Alliance had united, combining the power of an entire world. Naturally, there was a truly powerful figure suppressing them, which made them very cohesive. Otherwise, they would have long since schemed against each other during missions, leading to the alliance's collapse.
The Blood Mist Alliance was terrifying, which was why the three Great Sacred Sects of the Sacred Domain World and other foreign powers would generally unite to oppose them.
Otherwise, the Blood Mist Alliance would have long ago wiped out the three Great Sacred Sects and the bases of other foreign powers, seizing all the Origin Rule Calamity Crystals for themselves.
Yet this time, the Blood Mist Alliance had not dispatched many foreign experts. For example, they hadn't paid much attention to the competition for the first Origin Rule Calamity Crystal.
If anyone were to come here, they would find that many Ultimate-level foreign experts had gathered.
Within the blood-colored mist, figures sat upon thrones. There were about dozens of them, all at the Ultimate Ancestor level, with no shortage of second-tier experts among them.

Suddenly.
Boom!!!!
A terrifying figure emerged, clearly surpassing the second tier and reaching the third tier, infinitely close to the level of an Invincible Ancestor.
"Greetings, Lord Baigui!"
"Greetings, Lord Baigui!"
"Greetings, Lord Baigui!"
One by one, the foreign experts stood up and bowed respectfully.
True Emperor Baigui, one of the three Vice Alliance Leaders of the Blood Mist Alliance, a third-tier Ultimate True Emperor.
"True Emperor" corresponded to the Ancestor level; it was their title for it.
An Ultimate True Emperor was an Ultimate Ancestor; only the name was different, while the path was similar.
True Emperor Baigui looked at the many True Emperors and spoke: "Our Blood Mist Alliance has completely digested another foreign world and destroyed all Gates not belonging to the alliance, gaining 100% control over that world. Now, our Blood Mist Alliance has sufficient strength to invade the Sacred Domain World. This Sacred Domain World has already passed its 'newbie period.' Unlike some newborn foreign worlds where we cannot completely transform them due to the protection of rules, the Sacred Domain World does not have this issue. Once we gain complete control here, we can begin the transformation to make this world adapt to our Blood Mist Alliance. At that time, our Blood Mist Alliance will have one more foreign world."
"Lord Baigui, when do we strike?" an Ultimate True Emperor asked.
"Wait a bit longer. During the late stages of the Great Calamity, when the source of the Sacred Domain World is weak, it will be easier for us to attempt the transformation. Furthermore, let them slaughter more living beings to clear out the Sacred Domain World for us; there's no rush to act. None of these forces have third-tier Ultimate True Emperors; once I take action, they won't be able to stop me at all. Moreover, in a while, another third-tier Ultimate True Emperor will arrive. With two third-tier Ultimate True Emperors combined with over ten second-tier Ultimate True Emperors, no force will be able to contend with us!" True Emperor Baigui said.
"Yes!"
The many True Emperors responded one by one.
At this time, the other foreign powers and the native Ultimate Sacred Beasts of the Sacred Domain World were not necessarily aware of the Blood Mist Alliance's ambitions and were still searching for Origin Rule Calamity Crystals.
Ye Tian was also unaware of the Blood Mist Alliance's plans, but even if he knew, he wouldn't be worried.
After all, no matter how bad the situation got, the Blood Mist Alliance currently had no way to invade the Dark Forbidden Zone.
Besides, given his rate of progress, by the time the Great Calamity hit the Dark Forbidden Zone, he might have already advanced to the third tier of the Ultimate Ancestor level and wouldn't fear the Blood Mist Alliance at all.
In the blink of an eye, another few billion years passed, and the second Origin Rule Calamity Crystal appeared.
