Chapter 1527: Initial Clash with the Invincible!

Illusory Dream World, Forbidden Land.
Ye Tian entered this place and immediately sensed the situation of this forbidden land, as well as the strength of the members of the Illusory Dream World guarding it.
Five Supreme Beings and thousands of Ancestors.
"It seems the Illusory Dream World values the Dark Forbidden Domain quite highly, actually dispatching so many experts!"
Ye Tian said with slight surprise.
At this moment.
The Supreme Beings of the Illusory Dream World also saw Ye Tian, and their expressions changed drastically one after another.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws!"
It wasn't that they didn't know Ye Tian's strength; it was precisely because they knew his strength that they had dispatched five Supreme Beings, one of whom had even reached the third level.
But they also knew very well that relying on just five Supreme Beings, they couldn't possibly stop Ye Tian, and even relying on the formations here would be useless.
Of course, they didn't know Ye Tian's purpose; if he hadn't come to fight, there was no need to be nervous.
However—
Ye Tian directly attacked!
Boom!!!!
The power of the Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm erupted in full, and a terrifying aura and pressure crushed all the experts here.
Those members of the Illusory Dream World below the level of Supreme Being didn't even have the ability to move, and the five Supreme Beings were also trembling all over, their hearts filled with incomparable horror.
"Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm! How is this possible?"
They simply couldn't believe it.
After all, the Illusory Dream World had previously clashed with Ye Tian in the Dark Forbidden Domain. Although Ye Tian's strength was far greater than an ordinary third-level Supreme Being, he definitely hadn't crossed a single physical body tribulation back then; otherwise, his aura couldn't have been hidden.
Once a Supreme Being reaches the third level, it becomes very difficult to increase their strength. They can only cross physical body tribulations or soul tribulations, gradually advancing toward the realm of the Invincible.
However, absorbing tribulation power to temper the physical body was too slow and time-consuming.
Many third-level Supreme Beings spent thousands of Great Calamity Eras and still found it difficult to cross five physical body tribulations to gain the qualification to charge for the Invincible realm.
But from the last time Ye Tian hadn't crossed a tribulation until now, only a few Dark Calendar periods had passed, and he had suddenly crossed seven physical body tribulations. This was too abnormal!
Ye Tian was already very powerful to begin with, and now that he had crossed seven physical body tribulations, one could imagine to what extent his strength had increased.
Thus, the moment Ye Tian attacked, the five Supreme Beings of the Illusory Dream World wanted to flee, having no intention of fighting at all.
Unfortunately, their strength was too weak; they couldn't escape at all.
"Five Elements Sealing!"
This was one of Ye Tian's Supreme Ancestral Techniques, which had also evolved step by step from a Saint Technique. Now that it had reached the level of a Supreme Ancestral Technique, even Supreme Beings would find it difficult to break through the lock of the Five Elements Sealing.
Buzz!!!!
This entire forbidden land area was directly locked down, making it impossible for these Supreme Beings and those below them to escape.
"Seal!"
The Five Elements Sealing continued to shrink, turning into individual seals that suppressed the power of each expert. Even the five Supreme Beings were quickly sealed, their full strength unable to be exerted, meaning they had no way to escape.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, what are you doing? Do you want to start a war with our Illusory Dream World? The strength of the Illusory Dream World is far beyond your imagination. You are seeking death!"
One Supreme Being said angrily.
"Oh, the strength of the Illusory Dream World is far beyond my imagination? I have already reached the Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm, do I still need to fear your Illusory Dream World?" Ye Tian sneered.
That Supreme Being threatened, "Our Illusory Dream World possesses three Invincible Illusion Ancestors, and our leader is far beyond ordinary Invincible Ancestors. Compared to our Illusory Dream World, you are simply an ant!"
"Oh, thank you for informing me of the Illusory Dream World's strength!" Ye Tian said with a smile.
"You... did that on purpose!" That Supreme Being was stunned, then became furious. He had unintentionally revealed the strength of the Illusory Dream World. Although it wasn't a huge deal, he was very annoyed.
"Go and inform the Illusory Dream World that I want to make a deal with them!" Ye Tian released a top-tier Illusion Ancestor and let him go to inform the Illusory Dream World headquarters.
The other party immediately traversed the Rank 2 Gate, leaving this forbidden land and heading toward the world where the Illusory Dream World was located.
Not long after.
A terrifying aura forcibly traversed the Rank 2 Gate, attempting to cross the domains.
Clearly, the opponent was an Invincible who surpassed the Supreme Beings—an Invincible Illusion Ancestor of the Illusory Dream World.
After all, Supreme Beings could easily pass through a Rank 2 Gate, and only Invincibles needed to pay a price to cross one.
Crack!!!
The void vibrated!
A figure forcibly crossed the Rank 2 Gate and descended upon this forbidden land.
"Pupil Ancestor, save us!"
The Illusion Ancestors of the Illusory Dream World cried out for help.
Pupil Ancestor, one of the three giants of the Illusory Dream World, in the realm of an Invincible Illusion Ancestor, possessed incomparable strength.
However, by forcibly descending this time, his strength had dropped by at least thirty percent, but he still possessed the combat power of an Invincible Illusion Ancestor.
At this moment, seeing Ye Tian holding so many Illusion Ancestors of the Illusory Dream World captive, he was instantly enraged.
Immediately, he wanted to use illusion techniques to attack Ye Tian and kill him outright.
Countless illusions swept in, but Ye Tian held firm to his heart and let out a furious roar: "Begone!"
Countless illusions shattered!
At this time, Pupil Ancestor released a pupil technique attack, which transformed into a sharp blade and slashed toward Ye Tian. It looked like an ordinary pupil technique, but it was extremely terrifying, capable of slashing not only the physical body but also the soul.
However.
Ye Tian threw a punch. With the power of the Extreme Arm Supreme Ancestral Technique combined with the Tyrant Strength Supreme Ancestral Technique, plus the power attached to the Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm, the power of this punch was terrifying to the extreme.
The void in front of him was directly annihilated by the punch, and even Pupil Ancestor's pupil technique attack was shattered. However, this attack also left a bloodstain on Ye Tian's fist, counting as a wound to Ye Tian.
"You actually possess Invincible Ancestor combat power!"
Pupil Ancestor was extremely surprised.
Although a Supreme Being of the Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm was powerful, they were definitely still far from the Invincible level. But now Ye Tian possessed Invincible Ancestor combat power; clearly, his foundation was too deep, and he had created many, many Supreme Ancestral Techniques at the Supreme Being level, which allowed him to possess such formidable combat power.
Moreover, those at the Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm had a seventy to eighty percent chance of promoting to an Invincible; unless their luck was extremely poor, they wouldn't die during the breakthrough process.
He couldn't kill such an expert. Even if the leader arrived, once the Ancestor of Myriad Laws fled into the Dark Forbidden Domain, even if those Invincibles didn't fear the rules of the Dark Forbidden Domain, most of their strength would still be suppressed. At that time, they wouldn't be able to do anything to Ye Tian and might even face the risk of falling.
"I cannot offend the Ancestor of Myriad Laws!" Pupil Ancestor thought to himself.
Thus, Pupil Ancestor stopped attacking and instead asked, "Ancestor of Myriad Laws, what do you want? How can you release these members?"
Ye Tian pondered for a moment and stated his demands.
