Chapter 1445: Compensation, Causality-type Ancestral Weapon!
!
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, release your Immortal Holy Sect's Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor? What is the meaning of this?"
War Ancestor was also somewhat bewildered.
His avatar presided over the War Ancestor Realm, and he usually didn't pay much attention to outside affairs, so he didn't know what had happened.
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor explained the matter between the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor and Ye Tian.
After listening, War Ancestor couldn't help but sneer, "Immortal Primordial Ancestor, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor from your Immortal Holy Sect tried to snatch a Forbidden Ancestral Spring from a Domain Ancestor of my War Sect and got captured. He deserved it! And yet you have the nerve to ask me to release him?"
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor hurriedly said, "War Ancestor, there's no use saying more. Let's talk terms. Besides, if the Dark Holy Domain loses a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, its strength will also weaken by a fraction. There's no need to be so absolute!"
"I cannot make the decision on this matter. I can communicate with the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor. As for whether he is willing to release him, that is his business!"
War Ancestor stated.
"Fine!"
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor nodded.
....Profound Void Holy Realm.
A peculiar fluctuation appeared in the War World Tower.
A strand of will crossed the endless space-time directly from the War World Tower of the Dark Holy Domain and descended upon the War World Tower of the Profound Void Holy Realm.
This strand of will descended directly upon Ye Tian's residence.
"Who is it?"
Ye Tian asked solemnly.
"It is I, War Ancestor!"
The figure of War Ancestor emerged.
Although it was merely a thought, Ye Tian felt an incomparable power, as if a single thought from War Ancestor could suppress and kill him.
This was the strength of a Primordial Ancestor! "Greetings, War Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said respectfully.
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, did you capture the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor of the Immortal Holy Sect?"
War Ancestor asked.
"I did!"
Ye Tian admitted.
War Ancestor said, "The Immortal Primordial Ancestor of the Immortal Holy Sect came to find me. He wants to ransom the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor. If you are willing, state your conditions. If you are not, it doesn't matter even if you kill him. The War Sect does not fear the Immortal Holy Sect!"
Ye Tian pondered for a moment.
If he killed the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor, he would completely become an enemy of the Immortal Holy Sect and offend the Immortal Primordial Ancestor.
Moreover, killing the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor wouldn't be of much help to him.
If he released him, he could obtain some benefits.
"War Ancestor, I choose to release the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said.
"State your conditions, and I will relay them to the Immortal Primordial Ancestor."
War Ancestor said indifferently.
Ye Tian thought about what kind of treasure to demand. Ordinary treasures were useless to him, and given the value of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, he naturally wouldn't ask for something common.
After thinking it over, Ye Tian chose a treasure that would be helpful to him.
"I want a Causality-type Ancestral Weapon!" Ye Tian requested.
Ancestral Weapons were the weapons of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors. Ordinary sacred items and weapons were of almost no help to them, while Ancestral Weapons could enhance their combat power.
Of course, not all Supreme-level Domain Ancestors possessed Ancestral Weapons.
Rumor had it that Ancestral Weapons were forged from special materials, and only the Primordial Ancestors knew the specific forging process.
Thus, Ancestral Weapons were forged by Primordial Ancestors, and a very small number of Half-step Primordial Ancestors.
"A Causality-type Ancestral Weapon? That is quite a rare thing!"
War Ancestor smiled.
Ye Tian chose a Causality-type Ancestral Weapon because he wanted to protect himself.
Although he had cloned bodies, what if an enemy used a cloned body to kill his main body across space? The cloned bodies could not sever their causal connection with the main body. Therefore, if he possessed a Causality-type Ancestral Weapon, he could sever the causal link between his main body and his clones, completely mending his final flaw.
At that time, his cloned bodies could roam freely, and even a Primordial Ancestor skilled in Causality Ancestral Techniques would be unable to kill his main body from an infinite distance.
"I will go talk to the Immortal Primordial Ancestor. As for whether he is willing, I am not sure!"
War Ancestor laughed.
On the other side.
War Ancestor's will spoke, "Immortal Primordial Ancestor, the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor has spoken. One Causality-type Ancestral Weapon in exchange for the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor!"
"A Causality-type Ancestral Weapon? He really dares to ask! How rare is such a thing? Only the Causality Primordial Ancestor can forge them.
There are no more than ten in the entire Dark Holy Domain, and he actually wants one!"
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor was furious.
"Immortal Primordial Ancestor, the message has been delivered. Whether you are willing is your business.
At worst, you can ask the Causality Primordial Ancestor to forge one, even if it costs you a bit!"
War Ancestor said with a smile.
"Fine, fine, I'll give it to him. But it can only be the most basic Causality-type Ancestral Weapon. Wait ten years, and I will deliver it.
During this period, he must not harm the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor!"
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor said.
"No problem!"
War Ancestor agreed.
In the blink of an eye, ten years passed.
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor tossed a small orb to War Ancestor; this small orb was the Causality-type Ancestral Weapon.
War Ancestor then had Ye Tian enter the War Ancestor Realm and handed the orb to him.
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, this is the Causality-type Ancestral Weapon. I have checked it; the Immortal Primordial Ancestor did not tamper with it.
Now that you have this Ancestral Weapon, release the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor!"
War Ancestor said.
"Yes!"
Ye Tian replied.
Subsequently, Ye Tian released the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor.
When the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor regained his freedom, he breathed a sigh of relief and fled without looking back, fearing that Ye Tian might regret it and capture him again.
....Profound Void Holy Realm.
Ye Tian began to refine this Causality-type Ancestral Weapon.
It took several thousand years before he finally succeeded in refining it.
This Causality-type Ancestral Weapon was called the Void Fruit; that was its name.
With the Void Fruit, Ye Tian could shield the causal connection between his cloned bodies and his main body, while the actions of the cloned bodies remained unaffected.
"A very good Causality-type Ancestral Weapon. With this weapon, my shortcoming is truly mended!"
Ye Tian said happily.
It seemed that due to the incident with the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor, the Immortal Holy Sect had completely given up on the Forbidden Ancestral Spring.
Since this matter had involved Primordial Ancestors, even other factions did not dare to send stronger Supreme-level Domain Ancestors; otherwise, it would be bad if it triggered a war.
Furthermore, Ye Tian was indeed strong enough. Even the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor had been captured, making him likely comparable to an ordinary late-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
Among the various major factions, many of those with such strength were stationed at the borders of the Dark Forbidden Zone to resist the Annihilation Clan.
Therefore, the matter was dropped, and no one else tried to target the Forbidden Ancestral Spring.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian remained in bitter cultivation, mastering his physical body and continuously completing transformations of the Invincible Domain Body.
Three transformations of the Invincible Domain Body! Four transformations!
With each transformation, Ye Tian's strength grew significantly.
Finally, after nearly ten billion years, Ye Tian was about to complete the ninth transformation of the Invincible Domain Body.
