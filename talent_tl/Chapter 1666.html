Chapter 1665: The Legendary Lord and the Fifth-Stage Void Beast!

One Void Beast after another emerged from the depths of the Void Region, and there were many of them, causing all the Eternal Realm and Half-Step Eternal Realm experts to be terrified.
“Not good, run!”
One Eternal Realm expert after another fled at the fastest speed possible.
Ye Tian also did not dare linger here. After all, he had not yet fused those Immortal Ancestral Talents; if these three replicated bodies were to fall, then this replication would be wasted.
At this time, the Void Sword Lord, who was in the Abyssless Land, also sensed trouble. He immediately tore through the Abyssless Land and came out from it.
But just as he came out, he was immediately locked onto by the Void Beasts.
Boom!!!!!!
A humanoid Void Beast, far larger than the others, shattered the void and appeared beside the Void Sword Lord, throwing a punch at him.
The Void Sword Lord was sent flying with one punch, his body cracked, and the Eternal Origin Weapon armor on him was shattered as well.
“Fourth-Stage Void Beast!”
The Void Sword Lord’s expression changed drastically.
A Fourth-Stage Void Beast was far more powerful than a Third-Stage Void Beast, comparable to an old-fashioned Immortal Realm lord.
Although the Void Sword Lord was powerful, among the Immortal Realm he was not very strong; he was only an ordinary Immortal Realm lord, and he really was no match for a Fourth-Stage Void Beast.
More importantly, he could not injure a Fourth-Stage Void Beast, whereas a Fourth-Stage Void Beast could injure him.
He did not know whether there was only one Fourth-Stage Void Beast. Even if there was only one, if it cooperated with many Third-Stage Void Beasts, it would still have a chance of killing him.
As an Eternal Realm existence of the Immortal Realm, he did not want to fall here.
“Damn it, how can so many Void Beasts appear here? This place is not too far from the Source Sea, and it has not gone too deep into the Void Region. Normally, Void Beasts would not come here in large numbers!”
The Void Sword Lord thought inwardly.
He did not have time to consider why the Void Beasts had appeared; he had to shake off this Fourth-Stage Void Beast, or he would truly be in danger.
Suddenly.
The Void Sword Lord split into countless sword lights, piercing through space-time as he fled. Each sword light was a portion of his power; as long as even one sword light escaped, he could recover in a short time. This was also one of his life-saving methods.
The Fourth-Stage Void Beast tried to intercept the Void Sword Lord, but only managed to intercept a small portion of the sword lights.
Just then, three more Fourth-Stage Void Beasts appeared, all striking across space at the Void Sword Lord.
Instantly, part of the Void Sword Lord’s sword lights were shattered.
In the distance, the Void Sword Lord’s many sword lights gathered again.
But at this moment, his face was extremely pale, clearly seriously injured.
“Four Fourth-Stage Void Beasts, what are the Void Beasts trying to do?”
The Void Sword Lord did not dare delay and fled at the fastest speed.
And those four Fourth-Stage Void Beasts already possessed a very high level of intelligence. They simply did not pursue the other Eternal Realm and Half-Step Eternal Realm experts. To them, those were merely ants, not worth the effort of chasing. Killing the Void Sword Lord, however, was worthwhile.
Suddenly.
Another terrifying aura swept over, making even the Void Sword Lord shiver all over.
“Fif... Fifth-Stage Void Beast!!!!”
The Void Sword Lord sensed the aura of death.
He was also an Immortal Realm lord without a clone. His Void Sword Eternal Path was incomparably overbearing, which was why he had not been able to condense a clone until now. Thus, he was currently in his true body; if the true body fell, then he would truly be dead.
“I only came to gather some resources for my newly promoted concubine, so how did I end up in a life-and-death crisis!”
The Void Sword Lord regretted it immensely.
He had absolutely no intention of fighting a Fifth-Stage Void Beast. Even several million Eternal Years ago, only one Fifth-Stage Void Beast had appeared, and after being heavily injured, it fled the Spacetime Territory and entered the depths of the Void Region.
How rare Fifth-Stage Void Beasts were, and yet one had actually appeared here.
An Immortal Realm lord simply could not contend against a Fifth-Stage Void Beast. In the Endless Spacetime Territory, only a Legendary Lord above the Immortal Realm lord could truly contend with a Fifth-Stage Void Beast.
And within the Endless Spacetime Territory, there were a total of six Legendary Lords; they were also the six chairmen of the Lord Alliance, and almost never dealt with matters.
This time, a Fifth-Stage Void Beast had appeared. The Void Sword Lord knew he could not withstand it, and could only hope that one of the six chairmen would come. Otherwise, he feared he would not be able to escape.
“I sent out a distress signal long ago. I hope the Lord Alliance can come to provide support in time!”
The Void Sword Lord thought inwardly.
The speed of the Fifth-Stage Void Beast was too fast; in an instant, it caught up to the Void Sword Lord.
Its palm, covered in black fur, came down in a slap. The Void Sword Lord did everything he could to block this strike, and also activated some life-saving items. Only then did he barely withstand the blow, but he was still badly injured.
Just then, the Void Sword Lord used one of his trump cards.
He took out a small black sword. After activating this black sword, his aura weakened, but the small sword released boundless sword light, and each strand of sword light was comparable to the full-force strike of a peak Undying Realm expert.
The endless sword light was so terrifying that it erased this stretch of the Void Region.
However, the Fifth-Stage Void Beast turned into void. It seemed to be there, yet it did not exist on the levels of void, law, or will, as if it truly did not exist at all, leaving no trace in heaven and earth.
Thus, the Void Sword Lord’s trump card failed to injure the Fifth-Stage Void Beast at all.
“It didn’t even injure it!”
The Void Sword Lord was shocked.
He had never fought a Fifth-Stage Void Beast, so he simply did not understand its strength.
He had thought his trump card would be enough to injure a top-tier Immortal Realm lord, perhaps even injure a Fifth-Stage Void Beast, or at least buy him some time. He had not expected it to be completely futile.
Buzz!!!!
Space-time solidified, the Void Region fell still, and all particles began to shatter.
The Void Sword Lord felt his entire body’s particles annihilating bit by bit, while all his power was being sealed, unable to move even slightly. Even his Third-Stage Eternal Will began to collapse, cave in, and annihilate.
“The void law domain of a Fifth-Stage Void Beast, a force that can turn everything into void!”
The Void Sword Lord was in despair.
He could not escape at all and could only wait for death.
Just as nearly ninety percent of his body had been annihilated, the void law domain of the Fifth-Stage Void Beast was shattered, and a hand reached in, directly grabbed the Void Sword Lord, and took him away.
In the distance.
The Void Sword Lord reappeared, standing beside a young man.
“Many thanks, Lord Six Yang!”
The Void Sword Lord said respectfully.
Lord Six Yang was one of the six Legendary Lords of the Endless Spacetime Territory, and also one of the six great chairmen of the Lord Alliance.
Only after seeing Lord Six Yang did the Void Sword Lord finally breathe a sigh of relief.
His life was saved!
