Chapter 1538: Invincible Calamity Body Method, Ye Tian's Luck!

The headquarters of Heavenly Wind Sea was not located in Absolute Martial City. As for its actual location, Ye Tian naturally couldn't find out, but Heavenly Wind Sea did have a branch in Absolute Martial City, along with quite a few businesses.
Therefore, Ye Tian headed directly to the Heavenly Wind Sea branch in Absolute Martial City.
Upon arriving at the Heavenly Wind Sea branch, Ye Tian directly stated his intention to join.
At this time, a sixth-grade Invincible from Heavenly Wind Sea received Ye Tian.
"You want to join our Heavenly Wind Sea?"
The sixth-grade Invincible, Mo Ji, looked at Ye Tian and asked.
"Yes!" Ye Tian nodded slightly.
Mo Ji said, "Follow me. We need to test what level of Flesh Calamity Realm you reached when you promoted to the Invincible level!"
Ye Tian followed Mo Ji to a testing area within the Heavenly Wind Sea branch.
Soon, they arrived before a massive stone mirror.
Mo Ji pointed at the stone mirror and said, "This is a high-grade Calamity Tool—the Cave Calamity Mirror. It can peer into how many Flesh Calamities an Invincible has passed. Of course, before the test, you need to pay 100 Source Rule Calamity Crystals. This is to prevent certain Invincibles who don't meet the requirements from intentionally coming to test and wasting our time. Once the test is complete, if you meet the requirements, the 100 Source Rule Calamity Crystals will be refunded to you!"
Ye Tian didn't find anything wrong with this, so he directly took out a hundred Source Rule Calamity Crystals and handed them to Mo Ji.
Subsequently, the test began.
Hum!!!
The Cave Calamity Mirror released a beam of light that enveloped Ye Tian, and soon, streaks of black light began to emerge.
One streak of black light represented one calamity!
Five streaks!
Six streaks!
Seven streaks!
When he saw the seven streaks of black light, Mo Ji was somewhat surprised: "This Invincible has quite good talent, a breakthrough from the Seven-Calamity Flesh Realm."
As for the Eight-Calamity Realm, he didn't dare to think about it, because those in the Eight-Calamity Flesh Realm were considered geniuses even in the Tianlin Realm. They would have been recruited by Saint-Name level powers back when they were still in the Supreme Flesh Calamity Realm; it was impossible for them to wait until they were Invincibles to join Heavenly Wind Sea.
But immediately after, the eighth streak of black light emerged.
Eight-Calamity Flesh Realm!
This time, Mo Ji's expression changed, filled with immense shock.
However, it hadn't ended yet.
The ninth streak of black light also appeared, and only then did the test conclude.
"Nine... Nine-Calamity Flesh Realm!"
Mo Ji's pupils shrank violently, utterly stunned.
It wasn't that he had never seen Nine-Calamity Flesh Realm monsters before, but every single one of them was an existence he looked up to. Even within Heavenly Wind Sea, Nine-Calamity Flesh Realm geniuses were not numerous!
Then, he realized that Heavenly Wind Sea had picked up a treasure.
Immediately, he notified the higher-ups of the Heavenly Wind Sea branch, lest this genius be snatched away by other powers.
The very second Mo Ji sent the notification, a figure appeared.
"Greetings, Lord Long He!"
Mo Ji bowed respectfully upon seeing the man in front of him.
Long He, the person in charge of the Heavenly Wind Sea branch in Absolute Martial City, was at the third-grade Invincible level, and his strength was incredibly formidable.
"Mo Ji, is it this person?"
Long He asked with some excitement.
"Yes, Lord Long He!" Mo Ji replied.
Long He stared at Ye Tian and said seriously, "Can you test one more time?"
Ye Tian guessed that his talent was too high and had caught the attention of the Heavenly Wind Sea higher-ups, which was a good thing.
Thus, Ye Tian replied, "I can!"
Subsequently, Ye Tian tested once more, and nine streaks of black light emerged in succession.
"It really is an Invincible promoted from the Nine-Calamity Flesh Realm!"
Long He said excitedly.
Immediately, he declared, "Congratulations, you can directly become a member of Heavenly Wind Sea!"
"Don't I need to complete an entry mission?" Ye Tian asked in confusion.
"No need!" Long He answered. "Those missions are prepared for ordinary Invincibles. For a genius like you, there's no need to complete those missions at all. Moreover, many missions are very time-consuming and even somewhat dangerous. It's just that there are too many Invincibles who want to join Heavenly Wind Sea. If we didn't have such restrictions, countless Invincibles without potential would join, and Heavenly Wind Sea would have to spend resources to nurture them, which simply isn't worth it."
Ye Tian nodded, finding this very logical.
A Saint-Name level power like Heavenly Wind Sea didn't need those ordinary sixth-grade Invincibles to increase its strength. Therefore, having a few more or a few less Invincibles was irrelevant to them.
What Heavenly Wind Sea needed were geniuses who could truly enhance their strength and prestige, or even bring benefits to the organization, rather than those who only took resources from it.
"Follow me!"
Long He said.
Afterward, he led Ye Tian to the internal area of the Heavenly Wind Sea branch.
There, he handled the procedures for Ye Tian to join Heavenly Wind Sea and prepared a Heavenly Wind Sea member badge.
The members of Heavenly Wind Sea were divided into five levels: Ordinary Member, Senior Member, Elite Member, Glory Member, and Supreme Member.
Most members were Ordinary Members, a few were Senior Members, and only those at the Elite level and above were considered part of the high-level management.
For example, Long He was one of the Elite Members.
And as soon as Ye Tian joined Heavenly Wind Sea, he was a Senior Member, which was preferential treatment for top-tier geniuses.
Of course, Ye Tian's status as a Senior Member still needed verification; he had to wait for the headquarters to send someone to investigate before it could be finalized.
Soon, several people arrived from the headquarters to conduct an assessment of Ye Tian.
The content of the assessment was simple, similar to the previous test.
· ········· Seeking Flowers··· ·····
Not long after, Ye Tian's status as a Senior Member was confirmed.
……
"Ye Tian, now that you are officially a Senior Member of Heavenly Wind Sea, you probably don't know much about us yet. Let me tell you about the situation of Heavenly Wind Sea."
Long He gave a general overview of Heavenly Wind Sea.
From its strength, occupied territories, to benefits and so on, he provided a detailed description of everything.
"Ye Tian, although you became a Senior Member as soon as you joined, if you want to be promoted in the future, you must complete various missions. Through these missions, you earn contribution points to advance step by step. Only by advancing can you gain access to more secret knowledge and exchange for rarer treasures—treasures that cannot be bought in the outside world," Long He said.
Ye Tian listened intently throughout, naturally gaining a deeper understanding of Heavenly Wind Sea.
... ...... .......
A few days later, a reward belonging to Ye Tian arrived. It was a reward that all top-tier geniuses received. Perhaps for those powerhouses, this reward wasn't worth much, but it was something Ye Tian desperately needed.
A mid-grade defensive Calamity Tool, a mid-grade saber-type Calamity Tool, a portion of Thirty-Calamity Blood, and a Calamity Body Token.
The Calamity Body Token was a token that could be used to exchange for an Invincible Calamity Body Method from Heavenly Wind Sea.
"Isn't the Invincible Calamity Body something one possesses after breaking through to the Invincible level? Why is there an Invincible Calamity Body Method?" Ye Tian was somewhat confused, so he asked Long He on the spot.
Long He replied, "Ye Tian, the Invincible Calamity Body after an Invincible's breakthrough is just an ordinary Invincible Calamity Body. No matter how powerful your previous body was or how terrifying the Ancestral Body you condensed, the essence of the Calamity Power you absorb is not much different from that of other Invincibles. Because your Invincible Calamity Body is essentially the same as others', just slightly stronger. However, there are higher-level Invincible Calamity Bodies that are distinct from the ordinary ones. For example, the Blood River Calamity Body that I cultivate has a physical limit three times that of an ordinary person of the same rank. Perhaps at the sixth-grade Invincible level, three times doesn't seem like much, but what if you reach the fifth-grade, fourth-grade, or even third-grade Invincible level?"
Long He didn't continue, but Ye Tian could already imagine it. An Invincible who cultivated a higher-level Invincible Calamity Body would easily crush ordinary Invincibles as their cultivation level increased.
Even if they were both third-grade Invincibles, Long He could definitely insta-kill those who only possessed an ordinary Invincible Calamity Body.
Therefore, for an Invincible, the quality of the Invincible Calamity Body was incredibly important.
At this moment, Ye Tian felt extremely lucky that he had joined Heavenly Wind Sea. Otherwise, if he had blindly cultivated and promoted to the fifth-grade Invincible realm with an ordinary Invincible Calamity Body, he would likely have been thrashed by the fifth-grade Invincibles of those Saint-Name level powers.
