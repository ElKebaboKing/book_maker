Chapter 1669: The Fall of a Fifth-Tier Void Beast!

The Void Region at the center of the battlefield.
Great arrays surrounded the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts. These arrays almost split this stretch of the Void Region from the Void Region outside, turning it into a battlefield between the six legendary lords and the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts.
At this moment, the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts were not the least bit panicked.
One of the fifth-tier Void Beasts sneered, “What use is it for the six of you legendary lords to set up these arrays? Could it be that you still want to surround and kill us? In terms of numbers, we outnumber you!”
“Are you sure your numbers are greater?”
Lord Six-Sun laughed.
The next moment.
A second Lord Six-Sun appeared.
It was a clone!
Then, the other five legendary lords also revealed their clones one after another. Among them, Lord Dark Mist actually had two clones, and Lord Iceflame actually had three clones.
In this way, there were suddenly fifteen legendary lords here.
Fifteen against seven, naturally the lord alliance held the advantage.
“Clones!”
The fifth-tier Void Beasts naturally knew about clones, but these six legendary lords had never displayed theirs, causing them to forget about them.
Lord Six-Sun sneered, “Back then, the six of us merely used clones to surround and attack a single fifth-tier Void Beast. We just didn’t expect that your fifth-tier Void Beasts would actually possess the ability to turn into Void Light. With billions upon billions of strands of Void Light escaping into the depths of the Void Region, we failed to intercept you in time, so we didn’t send out our true bodies. But now, these arrays were specifically created to restrain your escape method of turning into Void Light. You have no way to get away.”
The fifth-tier Void Beasts still did not panic.
“Hmph, so what? We don’t need to escape at all. It’s extremely difficult for your legendary lords to injure us, while we can injure you. Even if you have the numerical advantage, the final victory will still be ours.” The fifth-tier Void Beast said confidently.
“Is that so?”
Lord Sacred Peak took out an object, causing the fifth-tier Void Beasts’ expressions to change.
It was a blood-colored spear!
Yet the fifth-tier Void Beasts sensed the aura of a fifth-tier Void Beast from that blood-colored spear, and one of them was especially furious.
“Damn legendary lords, you actually refined the remnants I left behind back then into a Void Saint Weapon!!!!”
The fifth-tier Void Beast roared in fury.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!
The other legendary lords also took out their own Void Saint Weapons, all of which had been refined using the remnants of fifth-tier Void Beasts.
Such Void Saint Weapons could be called legendary Void Saint Weapons, and they could inflict a certain degree of damage on fifth-tier Void Beasts.
However, these were not the trump cards they relied on to kill the fifth-tier Void Beasts. Merely relying on these, at most they could gain the upper hand and heavily injure these seven Void Beasts. As for killing them, it would probably require an incomparably long period of time.
Suddenly.
Lord Sacred Peak threw out a seed.
This seed was their true trump card.
As the seed fell, it transformed into a tree, and this tree kept growing larger, affecting the surrounding Void Region.
The Void Region was covered by vast amounts of land, flowers, grass, trees, and mountain ranges, and it spread at a terrifying speed.
Almost in the blink of an eye, this stretch of the Void Region actually gave birth to a temporal-spatial land. It was not some fake temporal-spatial land, but a real one, containing all kinds of innate eternal laws.
“Void Beasts have a clear weakness: they are suited to the environment of the Void Region. The Void Region doesn’t have many laws. If we must name one, it would be the Void law. The Void law is difficult to sense, and we do not include it among the eternal laws; it belongs to your Void Beasts’ laws. Once your Void Beasts enter a world with a large number of eternal laws, your strength will be suppressed, and even your ethereal state will be weakened somewhat.”
“Now, we have created this temporal-spatial land, which is equivalent to weakening your strength, even weakening it several times over.”
After Lord Sacred Peak finished speaking, all seven fifth-tier Void Beasts panicked.
The environment of the Void Region was suitable for them to fight in, but now the world had changed, and their strength was greatly weakened.
Although they could destroy this temporal-spatial land, it would take some time, and these legendary lords would not give them that time.
“Kill!”
The six legendary lords controlled their clones and true bodies, each holding a legendary Void Saint Weapon, and charged toward the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts.
The seven fifth-tier Void Beasts sensed a fatal crisis and, regardless of the cost, tried to tear a path out of here and escape.
An astonishing battle began!!!
……
Outside the arrays.
A large number of Immortal Realm lords formed layers of protective circles here, blocking other Void Beasts from rescuing the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts and buying time for the six great chairmen.
And all of this had little to do with Ye Tian. He was currently still unable to participate in such a battle.
He could only lead the Eight Extremes Building’s Eight Extremes Realm experts in killing more first-tier Void Beasts, nothing more.
Year after year passed, and the battle between the temporal-spatial land and the Void Beasts gradually drew to a close.
This day.
On the battlefield where the lord alliance’s legendary lords were suppressing the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts, those arrays and the temporal-spatial land that had been created were still torn apart by the seven fifth-tier Void Beasts, but the fifth-tier Void Beasts were in a miserable state.
Two fifth-tier Void Beasts were killed, and the other fifth-tier Void Beasts were all heavily injured.
The moment they escaped, the five surviving fifth-tier Void Beasts immediately used the Void Light ability. Their bodies transformed into billions upon billions of strands of Void Light, ignoring the obstacles of the Void Region, merging into the Void law, and fleeing into the depths of the Void Region at a speed that surpassed everything.
Even the legendary lords had no way to stop them, but they could still try their best to intercept them and leave behind more remnants of the fifth-tier Void Beasts.
After the fifth-tier Void Beasts fled, the legendary lords began to target the third- and fourth-tier Void Beasts.
Lord Six-Sun reached into the Void Region, grabbed a fourth-tier Void Beast, and killed it in a very short time.
As the six legendary lords took action, the third- and fourth-tier Void Beasts fell one after another, with escape being nothing more than a luxury.
Meanwhile, the other Undying Realm lords and Immortal Realm lords began hunting the first-tier Void Beasts, which would end the battle more quickly and also yield greater gains.
More time passed, and the Void Beasts were almost completely wiped out, with only a small number escaping back into the depths of the Void Region.
The legendary lords also did not dare pursue into the depths of the Void Region, so this war ended just like that. The surviving Eternal Realm experts all gained a great deal, and the Eight Extremes Building’s harvest was also considerable.
At the very least, for them, obtaining some Void Beast materials was already a huge gain.
Since they could no longer find first-tier Void Beasts, Ye Tian’s temporary team disbanded.
Afterwards, Ye Tian began sorting through his gains.
The accumulated Void Beast materials were roughly equivalent to the materials from three complete Void Beasts. If he had acted alone, he wouldn’t even have been able to kill one Void Beast, let alone three.
It could be said that this harvest was enormous.
“It’s time to use the Void Beast materials to improve my strength!”
Ye Tian murmured.
