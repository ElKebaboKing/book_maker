Chapter 1525: Physical Body Five-Tribulation Realm Supreme Powerhouse!

As the Great Tribulation of the Xiyue Clan descended, Ye Tian did not invade the Xiyue Clan's world.
Perhaps he could have plundered some resources during this tribulation, or even snatched some Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, but he no longer looked down upon such small gains.
Instead of spending time searching for resources, he would rather focus on his cultivation.
This time, his goal was to put all his effort into cultivation and strive to cross several more Physical Body Tribulations.
Thus, Ye Tian's true body descended upon the Xiyue Clan's world.
When the Great Tribulation arrived, Ye Tian built a cultivation site in an area outside that forbidden land, where he activated his Eternal Talent: Tribulation to absorb the tribulation power.
During the previous Great Tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Domain, he had obtained the Eternal Talent: Tribulation, but by then, the tribulation had already been underway for a long time. Consequently, he didn't have enough time to cultivate, which was why he had only crossed two Physical Body Tribulations.
But this time was different. He had been here absorbing tribulation power since the very beginning of the Xiyue Clan's Great Tribulation, so his progress would certainly be greater.
Of course, the Physical Body Tribulations became increasingly difficult as they progressed. Especially after the fifth Physical Body Tribulation, the amount of tribulation power required to complete another one was immense.
However, many Physical Body Tribulation Realm supreme powerhouses would attempt to break through to the Invincible Realm once they completed their fifth Physical Body Tribulation. There was no need to pursue a higher realm, as the time and energy required were too great, and it was easy for accidents to occur, leading to their fall in a foreign world.
Since Ye Tian did not plunder resources or fight for Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, the Xiyue Clan and other foreign forces did not come looking for trouble with him. Any supreme powerhouse who truly sought trouble with him would surely be doomed.
Time flew by like a shuttle.
On this day.
With the help of the Eternal Talent: Tribulation, Ye Tian finally began his third Physical Body Tribulation.
Ye Tian's physical body endured an unimaginable impact. Countless lightning wind-blades were born from every cellular world, attempting to destroy his body. However, Ye Tian's physical body was too powerful; in the end, the tribulation failed to destroy him and instead made his body even stronger.
"Physical Body Three-Tribulation Realm!"
Ye Tian said with joy.
However, he also felt that his absorption and refinement of the tribulation power were too crude.
No matter how powerful the Eternal Talent: Tribulation was, it was only a talent. For example, no matter how powerful an Ancestral Talent was, it could not compare to a created Ancestral Technique. One had to create and comprehend techniques derived from the talent's power to push that power to its limit.
If he could create a secret technique that complemented the Eternal Talent: Tribulation, he might be able to absorb and refine tribulation power even faster, which would speed up his cultivation significantly.
Over the years, Ye Tian had been attempting to create such a secret technique.
Furthermore, he repeatedly used his copied bodies to overdraw his Ancestral Soul and activate his deduction abilities. As a result, the process of creating this secret technique went very smoothly, and he was now at the final threshold.
He needed to absorb and refine tribulation power while simultaneously attempting to modify this secret technique to finally perfect it.
Only during a Great Tribulation could he quickly perfect it. Otherwise, relying solely on his usual meditation, he wouldn't be able to comprehend much at all. Without practical cultivation, it was impossible to create such a secret technique.
And this Great Tribulation of the Xiyue Clan's world was a perfect opportunity.
Finally, as the Great Tribulation approached its later stages, Ye Tian finally created a secret technique that could rapidly absorb and refine tribulation power. Its effectiveness was at least ten times greater than simply using the Eternal Talent: Tribulation alone.
This secret technique could barely be considered an Invincible-level secret technique, and Ye Tian named it the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique!
Through the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique, Ye Tian's speed in absorbing and refining tribulation power surged, using the power to rapidly temper his physical body.
Finally, he ushered in his fourth Physical Body Tribulation. Once he crossed it, he would be a Physical Body Four-Tribulation Realm supreme powerhouse.
Before long, Ye Tian successfully crossed the tribulation and entered the ranks of the Physical Body Four-Tribulation Realm powerhouses.
"According to my cultivation speed, by the time the Xiyue Clan's Great Tribulation ends, I should be confident in stepping into the Physical Body Five-Tribulation Realm!"
Ye Tian said with full confidence.
Without the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique, he naturally wouldn't be able to achieve this, but now it was possible.
Just like that, the Xiyue Clan's Great Tribulation was gradually coming to an end. Various foreign forces returned to their own worlds, while Ye Tian continued to absorb tribulation power to temper his body, preparing to charge into the Physical Body Five-Tribulation Realm.
Perhaps in the eyes of the Xiyue Clan, Ye Tian was an eccentric. After all, they did not know the secrets of supreme powerhouses striving for the Invincible Realm, and they had no idea that one needed to absorb tribulation power to temper the body.
Otherwise, they would have known just how terrifying Ye Tian was.
In such a short time, Ye Tian had stepped into the Physical Body Four-Tribulation Realm. For any other supreme powerhouse, this wouldn't be possible even after countless Great Tribulation Eras.
Finally, when only tens of millions of years remained in the Xiyue Clan's Great Tribulation, Ye Tian ushered in his fifth Physical Body Tribulation. Without much pressure, he crossed this tribulation very easily and stepped into the Physical Body Five-Tribulation Realm.
After entering the Physical Body Five-Tribulation Realm, Ye Tian felt a sensation—or rather, he sensed a vague barrier of shackles.
That was the barrier between a supreme powerhouse and an Invincible One.
At this point, if he entered a foreign world that was not in a period of Great Tribulation and repeatedly attempted to clash his physical body against the rules of that foreign world, and if he could adapt to those rules and cause a qualitative change in his body, then there would be hope of breaking those vague shackles and stepping into the Invincible Realm.
However, Ye Tian did not have that intention for now.
A mere Physical Body Five-Tribulation Realm was nothing. His goal was higher; he wanted to charge into the Invincible Realm with a perfect foundation.
"The Xiyue Clan's world is currently of little value to me. It's time to find a second foreign world that is about to enter a Great Tribulation!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Ye Tian returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain and once again used his copied bodies to infiltrate other foreign worlds to figure out the dates of their Great Tribulations.
Soon, he discovered another foreign world that was about to enter a Great Tribulation.
This foreign world was named the Mingheng World, and its strength was not very great, similar to the Xiyue Clan's world.
More importantly, the supreme powerhouses of the Mingheng World were not very decisive and had not immediately destroyed their Rank One Gate, and the timing of their Great Tribulation was also grasped by Ye Tian.
Thus, Ye Tian waited silently. Once the Mingheng World's Great Tribulation arrived, his true body would forcibly enter the Mingheng World through the Rank One Gate. That way, he could directly occupy this Rank One Gate and turn it into his own, just like the Rank One Gate of the Sea Sky Holy Sect.
After a dozen or so Domain Eras, the Great Tribulation of the Mingheng World arrived.
On this day.
Ye Tian left the Mystic Void Ancestral World and arrived at the forbidden land leading to the Mingheng World.
