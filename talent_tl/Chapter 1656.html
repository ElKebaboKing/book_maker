Chapter 1655: Void Beast!

Ye Tian’s strength had improved. Although he was still not as strong as a Stage Eight Supreme Being like True Yang Holy Ancestor, he was definitely not far off.
With his current strength, he was about on par with the weakest Eternal Being.
However, Ye Tian still could not break through the spacetime barriers of the Source Sea and freely travel between the Source Sea and the spacetime territory.
Breaking through the spacetime barrier was not a matter of strength, but of rules. Only when an Eternal Being completely fused with Eternal rules would they no longer be blocked by the Source Sea barrier. As for a half-step Eternal Being, even if their strength reached the level of a Stage Nine half-step Eternal Being, they still could not break through the Source Sea barrier.
This day.
Ye Tian condensed a clone in the Source Sea and sent the clone to the Primordial Sea.
He had always been very curious about the Primordial Sea. He had not been able to enter this place before because his strength was insufficient, but now that he had reached Stage Eight half-step Eternal Being battle strength, he could naturally force his way into the Primordial Sea and also force his way out.
With a ripping sound!
Ye Tian entered the Primordial Sea, and his powerful aura made countless black mist insects within the Primordial Sea not dare approach.
Ye Tian had no interest in these black mist insects. Instead, he looked toward that severed arm in a certain part of the Primordial Sea. The severed arm had been sealed within a patch of void, and ordinary Extreme experts could not probe its location at all; even Stage Seven half-step Eternal Beings could not detect it.
Yet Ye Tian could see the black mist river formed by that severed arm, and he could sense the most primal aura of the arm.
“This is not the arm of an ordinary Eternal Being lord. It is between Immortal Stage and Undying Stage, so it should be the arm of a half-step Undying Stage lord!” Ye Tian estimated.
It was obvious that the one who could cut off the arm of such an Eternal Being powerhouse was, if not an Undying Stage expert, then at least a half-step Undying Stage expert.
Ye Tian probed for a while and gained little, but he still took away all the resources there.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian sensed two powerful auras rapidly approaching, so he immediately left.
Rumble!!
Two Eternal Beings arrived at the Primordial Sea.
“We’re one step too late. Someone broke into this place; it should be a Stage Eight half-step Eternal Being,” one of the Eternal Beings guessed.
“It should be some Stage Eight expert from the spacetime territory who came here trying to break through to Stage Nine and happened to discover the Primordial Sea, thinking it was some sort of treasure land.”
“Many Eternal Beings and Stage Eight experts in the spacetime territory know the origin of the Primordial Sea. It’s just that one Stage Eight expert didn’t know. But that person will definitely investigate and learn about it now, and won’t dare come again. Let’s go!”
The two Eternal Beings quickly left without investigating further.
-------------------
Eight Extremes Tower.
Ye Tian’s clone body returned here from the God Sound Territory and informed True Yang Holy Ancestor.
Very quickly, True Yang Holy Ancestor also came to the Eight Extremes Tower.
The two of them began chatting casually.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian asked, “True Yang Holy Ancestor, do you know about the Source Sea Primordial Sea?”
“Primordial Sea?”
True Yang Holy Ancestor thought for a moment, then nodded and said, “I know a little. What’s wrong?”
“I am from Source Sea life, and the Primordial Sea is a treasure land of the Source Sea, but it seems to have traces of spacetime territory forces there, so I was a bit curious and asked about it, lest I offend a force from the spacetime territory,” Ye Tian explained.
“Endless Grand Ancestor, we really cannot offend the force behind the Primordial Sea,” True Yang Holy Ancestor said gravely. “As far as I know, the Primordial Sea used to be just an ordinary treasure land, but later it was modified by Phoenix Lord and used to cultivate black mist insects. It is the hatching base of the black mist insects. Moreover, Phoenix Lord once declared to countless spacetime territories that no one was allowed to interfere with the Primordial Sea; otherwise, it would mean making an enemy of him.”
“Phoenix Lord!”
Ye Tian knew this lord; he was one of the Undying Stage lords, a powerhouse standing at the true peak of countless spacetime territories.
Under Phoenix Lord were many Eternal Beings serving him, so the force controlled by Phoenix Lord was extremely powerful.
“What does Phoenix Lord cultivate those black mist insects for?”
Ye Tian was somewhat puzzled.
“For experiments!” True Yang Holy Ancestor said. “This is information that has spread from outside. It is said that Phoenix Lord cultivates large numbers of black mist insects in the Primordial Sea, then periodically sends them to the Phoenix Territory for secondary cultivation, helping these black mist insects advance to half-step Eternal Being, so that he can harvest countless half-step Eternal Being experimental materials. However, there is also another saying: that Phoenix Lord is researching the power of the Void Beast.”
“Void Beast?”
This was the first time Ye Tian had heard of it.
True Yang Holy Ancestor introduced it: “The Void Beast is a kind of creature that drifts outside the Source Sea. It always stays in the void region, and there aren’t many of them. They were long ago killed off by those Undying Stage lords. I’m afraid only in the depths of the void region far away from the Source Sea could one find traces of Void Beasts. But not many Eternal Beings dare go there; it is too far from the Source Sea, making it impossible to distinguish directions, and it is easy to get lost. It is said that strong experts who get lost in the depths of the void region almost never return.”
“That severed arm in the Primordial Sea was the arm of a Void Beast?”
Ye Tian asked in surprise.
“Yes!” True Yang Holy Ancestor nodded. “It is said that when Phoenix Lord was still at half-step Undying Stage, he once fought a powerful Void Beast. In the end, he severed one of its arms, but that Void Beast did not die and instead escaped. What happened later is not something we know.”
“So that’s how it is!”
Ye Tian understood.
Phoenix Lord had cut off a part of a Void Beast’s arm and thrown it into the Primordial Sea. Later, he discovered that black mist insects could reproduce by relying on the Void Beast’s arm, so he used that to research black mist insects.
As for whether black mist insects were used for anything else, that was not clear.
“Back then I even collected some bones of the Void Beast. Luckily Phoenix Lord doesn’t know about this, otherwise he might have come looking for trouble,” Ye Tian thought.
Now that he knew the Primordial Sea was Phoenix Lord’s territory, Ye Tian did not plan to continue probing the Primordial Sea. Otherwise, if he really provoked Phoenix Lord, he would not be able to contend against him, and even the Eight Extremes Tower would not help him.
However, one thing could be certain: the Void Beast was probably not simple, otherwise a powerhouse like Phoenix Lord, an Undying Stage expert, would not have studied the black mist insects derived from Void Beast flesh and blood.
In fact, Ye Tian was also very curious about the Void Beast.
What kind of talent would such a lifeform possess? If his Copying Talent copied the Void Beast’s talent, wouldn’t that be equivalent to obtaining a great opportunity?
“Forget it. I should cultivate properly and strive to first touch the second barrier wall.”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
The Eight Extremes Tower often organized gatherings, and they often discussed the Dao together.
Ye Tian also often attended, and gradually became familiar with the other Stage Eight experts in the Eight Extremes Tower.
Since they could become Stage Eight experts, everyone’s goal was only one thing: to advance to Stage Nine, or to advance to Eternal Being.
This day.
A Stage Eight half-step Eternal Being powerhouse from the Eight Extremes Tower, who had touched the second barrier wall, invited many Stage Eight experts to participate in a meeting.
When Ye Tian arrived at the Eight Extremes Tower, he was immediately very shocked.
What appeared before him was actually an elderly man exuding a decayed aura, as if his life was nearly over.
And he was the Stage Eight expert of the Eight Extremes Tower who had touched the second barrier wall—Gu Heyuan Ancestor!.
