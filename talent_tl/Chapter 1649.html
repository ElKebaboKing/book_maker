Dark Forbidden Domain.

Gradually, Ye Tian’s strength reached the Six Peak Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm level.
This time, he planned to charge into the Seven Peak Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm level before coming out of seclusion.
And the resources he had obtained this time were enough to let him reach the Seven Peak Realm level, more than enough in fact.
Of course, there were also some treasures that were not suitable for improving strength and had other uses; he planned to keep them, or give them to the human race as their foundation.
Unknowingly, several more kalpa eras passed.

He had successfully reached the Seven Peak Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm level.

Their comprehension of the Innate Eternal Laws might even be deeper than Ye Tian’s, and their cultivation foundation had also reached an inconceivable degree.

Therefore, compared with these people, Ye Tian did not have much advantage. If he did not have other opportunities, it would also be very hard for him to reach the Eight Peak Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm level.
