Chapter 1625 You all, I'm afraid, cannot leave!

Outside the Dark Forbidden Domain.
One after another ninefold Supreme Beings appeared here. Suddenly, a human Third-Tier Invincible stepped out of the Dark Forbidden Domain and was immediately seized by one of the ninefold Supreme Beings.
“Seniors, what are you going to do?”
The human Third-Tier Invincible was very panicked. The auras of these people far surpassed his imagination; even a wisp of their aura suppressed him to the point that he could not move. They were certainly Supreme Beings above Invincible, and not ordinary Supreme Beings at that.
He had also met several Supreme Beings of the human race before. Aside from the mysterious Endless Ancestor, the auras of the others were far below these few.
These few were probably top-notch existences among Supreme Beings. Why would such powerful figures seize him?
He was very puzzled, unable to understand how exactly he had offended them, or whether the human race had offended them?
“Send word to your human race and have the Endless Ancestor come here.”
One of the ninefold Supreme Beings ordered.
The human Third-Tier Invincible immediately realized they were enemies of the Endless Ancestor, and he did not dare disobey their order. He hurriedly sent a message to the upper echelons of the human race, and then had the upper echelons send word to the Supreme Beings.
As for him, he had no way to contact a Supreme Being directly.
Very quickly, the human race’s Supreme Beings all learned of the situation, but they did not leave the Dark Forbidden Domain directly. After all, the other side had come specifically for Ye Tian, so they were certainly incomparably powerful Supreme Beings.
And they were too weak; they did not yet have the qualifications to contend against such powerful Supreme Beings. If they went out, they would merely be giving the enemy one more captive.
“No one of the human race is permitted to leave the Dark Forbidden Domain. A great enemy, a Supreme Being, has arrived outside!”
The human race’s Supreme Beings all sent out messages, and even shut down one Tier-three gate after another. At the same time, they contacted Ye Tian, who had returned not long ago and was in cultivation.
“Father, several Supreme Beings have appeared outside the Dark Forbidden Domain.”
Ye Xingchen came to pay his respects to Ye Tian.
“Xingchen, I know. Go down. You all cannot get involved in this matter.” Ye Tian murmured.
In fact, he had already learned of it immediately, and had even guessed the reason.
These Supreme Beings were probably here for the Primordial Tree. The Primordial Tree’s value was too high; even a single Primordial Leaf was enough to drive many Supreme Beings mad!
These Supreme Beings coming to snatch his Primordial Tree was very normal. Even without proof, it was still worth the risk.
Ye Tian could have ignored them, but if he did not go, they might become even more aggressive. Moreover, once the Dark Forbidden Domain was no longer sheltered by the Source Sea, the Supreme Beings outside would come in. If a great battle broke out then, it would instead sweep into the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Therefore, he planned to deal with this matter directly and let the outside forces know that Ye Tian was not someone to be trifled with either.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian took one step and crossed out of the Dark Forbidden Domain, arriving at the Source Sea outside, where he also saw five ninefold Supreme Beings.
These ninefold Supreme Beings were all at the peak of the ninefold Supreme Being realm, only one step away from the Half-Step Eternal realm. But without some heaven-defying opportunity, they would find it hard to break through to the Half-Step Eternal realm in their entire lives.
It was precisely because of this that they longed so much for the opportunity represented by the Primordial Tree.
“Five ninefold Supreme Beings!”
Ye Tian let out a breath of relief.
If it had been five Half-Step Eternal experts, he really would not have been able to establish his authority.
However, Half-Step Eternal experts were inherently rare. Only top loose organizations like the Source God Pavilion could have so many Half-Step Eternal experts.
In truth, many of the Source God Pavilion’s experts belonged to other factions, and in a sense, overlapped with some other forces.
But Ye Tian was certain that these five ninefold Supreme Beings were definitely from the same force as the inner-circle members of the Source God Pavilion; otherwise, they would not have been able to receive the news so quickly.
“What do you mean by this? Why have you seized one of my human race’s Invincible ones?”
Ye Tian asked.
One of the ninefold Supreme Beings said in a deep voice, “Endless Ancestor, we hear that you possess a Primordial Tree. Hand it over.”
“Primordial Tree?” Ye Tian sneered. “Who told you that I have a Primordial Tree?”
“Who said it is not important. The point is, if you do not hand it over, not only will this human Invincible perish, but after your Dark Forbidden Domain passes its protection period, we will destroy this Eternal Heaven and Earth.” The other side threatened.
“Are you threatening me?”
Ye Tian said coldly.
He was very clear that even if he denied it, the other side would not believe him; even if he swore an oath, they would not believe him either.
After all, he possessed the ability to manifest clones, and this information was already no secret. By then, even if he made an oath, it would only affect this body; the main body would not be affected.
Therefore, they would not believe his oath.
All five of them wore robes and masks that concealed their auras, but their identities could not hide from Ye Tian at all.
Through the probing of the Copying Talent and his knowledge of their names, Ye Tian quickly locked onto their identities.
“The five from Golden Orchid Mountain, are you really going to make enemies of me?”
Ye Tian asked.
“Our identities were exposed?”
The five ninefold Supreme Beings were very surprised.
They had not made a move yet; if they had, then it would have been impossible to completely conceal their aura and Half-Step Eternal techniques, so being guessed would have been normal.
But now, Ye Tian had recognized them with just one glance, which shocked them somewhat.
“You lot, aren’t you afraid I’ll take revenge on Golden Orchid Mountain, on your Eternal Heaven and Earth?” Ye Tian said.
“Not afraid!”
One of them smiled and said, “If we obtain your Primordial Tree, at worst we’ll abandon Golden Orchid Mountain. As for the Eternal Heaven and Earth behind us, destroy it if you want. Anyway, we’ll take our close people with us; what do the life and death of everyone else have to do with us? But you should care a great deal about the human race and the Dark Forbidden Domain, right?”
Ye Tian was silent for several dozen seconds before saying, “How about this: I will fight you all. If you win, I will hand over the Primordial Tree. If you lose, you are not to make any further plans for the Primordial Tree. How about it?”
“No problem!”
The five ninefold Supreme Beings immediately agreed.
However, they still did not release that human Third-Tier Invincible, instead sealing him up and tossing him into one of their void orbs.
After that, the battle began.
Boom!!!!!
Aside from not using the finger of the Eternal realm, Ye Tian displayed battle power between the ninefold Supreme Being realm and the Half-Step Eternal realm.
In truth, his regular battle power was already sufficient to fight a weaker Half-Step Eternal expert for a round or two, equivalent to the weakest Half-Step Eternal expert.
And these five ninefold Supreme Beings did not have Half-Step Eternal battle power, so naturally they were inferior to Ye Tian in his normal state.
If Ye Tian did not hide his strength, he was afraid he would scare them.
At the same time, he had another plan.
The moment they exchanged blows, the five Supreme Beings’ expressions changed drastically, and they were completely suppressed by Ye Tian.
Several hours later, the five Supreme Beings had already begun to suffer minor injuries.
“Retreat first. Once we gather more strength, we can come threaten the Endless Ancestor again!” one ninefold Supreme Being proposed.
“Agreed!”
“Agreed!”
The other ninefold Supreme Beings agreed.
Thus, they said in unison, “We admit defeat!”
“Release that Third-Tier Invincible. He is of no value to you. You wouldn’t go so far as to become mortal enemies with me over one human Invincible, would you?”
Ye Tian said.
“Release him!”
They freed that Invincible.
And after Ye Tian grabbed that human Invincible with one hand, he threw him into the Dark Forbidden Domain.
After that, Ye Tian intercepted the five ninefold Supreme Beings.
“You lot, I’m afraid, cannot leave!”
Ye Tian sneered.
