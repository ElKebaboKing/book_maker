Chapter 1483: Human Ancestors Born One After Another!
!
It had been many years since Ye Tian passed the Ancestor Methods to Xiao Yue and the others. Originally, they wouldn't have been able to fuse with the Ancestor Methods so quickly, but Ye Tian had frequently guided them over these years.
After all, these Ancestor Methods were created by Ye Tian himself, making him the person who understood them best. To help his family members become Ancestors faster, Ye Tian provided frequent guidance, which shortened the time required for them to become Ancestors by many times.
Otherwise, it would have taken them at least a hundred Domain Years to have any hope of fusing with an Ancestor Method and becoming an Ancestor.
Another Domain Year passed. On this day, Ye Tian's wife, Xiao Yue, was about to successfully fuse with the Light Ancestor Method.
Once the fusion was successful, Xiao Yue would become a Light Ancestor.
At this time.
Xiao Yue and the others had all returned to the Profound Void Ancestral World for secluded cultivation. With Ye Tian's true body protecting them, her breakthrough to the Ancestor realm would absolutely not be disturbed.
On this day.
Boom!!!!! Xiao Yue successfully fused with the Light Ancestor Method and was breaking through to the Ancestor realm. Countless amounts of energy surged into her body, and the entire Dao Mountain Domain and even the surrounding Dark Forbidden Zone were enveloped in Ancestral phenomena.
An event as significant as an Ancestor's breakthrough shook time and space, making it impossible to hide. Many Ancestors sensed it.
Immediately, the wills of various Ancestors descended upon the Profound Void Ancestral World across time and space. They did not dare to barge directly into the Profound Void Ancestral World, but they could perceive the situation.
"In the Profound Void Ancestral World, someone has broken through to the Ancestor realm. Who is it?"
The various Ancestors were incredibly shocked.
Wasn't Ye Tian the only monstrous talent in the human race? Even the Green Leaf Ancestor was just Ye Tian's clone.
Therefore, there shouldn't have been a second Supreme-level Domain Ancestor in the human race qualified to charge for the Ancestor realm! Although the human race currently had many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, and even some at the peak of that level.
But these Ancestors knew very well how difficult it was to reach the Ancestor realm. One either had to create their own Ancestor Method or fuse with the masterless Ancestor Method of a fallen Ancestor.
However, there were only so many fallen Ancestors, and their Ancestor Methods had almost all been fused by others. Thus, obtaining a brand-new Ancestor Method was far too difficult.
As for creating one's own Ancestor Method, not everyone was as monstrously talented as Ye Tian.
Furthermore, even Ancestors themselves couldn't necessarily create a new Ancestor Method because they couldn't break free from their own Ancestral Path. Trying to create an Ancestor Method different from one's own Ancestral Path was simply too difficult.
Even the Saint Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor had no way to create a second Ancestor Method, so they had no way to help others become Ancestors.
Consequently, they couldn't understand how the human race had suddenly birthed another Ancestor.
"Jie Zu, do you know who it is from the human race that broke through to the Ancestor realm?"
The Eternal Cult Leader asked curiously.
Jie Zu shook his head: "To be honest, I am also very shocked. Within the human race, only the Myriad Law Ancestor is an Ancestor of our Battle Sect, while the other humans are not... disciples of the Battle Sect. Therefore, I don't know much about the human race.
But from what I know, the other peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the human race are not qualified to become Ancestors. Thus, there is only one possibility: the Myriad Law Ancestor obtained a masterless Ancestor Method and allowed a peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of the human race to fuse with it and step into the Ancestor realm."
"What incredible luck!"
The Destruction Ancestor said with a hint of jealousy. A complete, masterless Ancestor Method was something even he couldn't get his hands on.
Although he was confident he could kill a weaker Ancestor, that Ancestor wouldn't just produce an Ancestor Method for him. Unless an Ancestor was willing, it was impossible to obtain their Ancestor Method.
However, there weren't many Ancestors willing to produce one.
In the blink of an eye, several million years passed.
By this time, Xiao Yue had successfully broken through to the Ancestor realm. Although her combat strength wasn't particularly great, she was a true Ancestor.
After her successful breakthrough, Xiao Yue made an appearance and gave herself an Ancestral name: Moon Ancestor! Only then did the other Ancestors realize that the person who had broken through was the wife of the Myriad Law Ancestor, Ye Tian.
A husband and wife both being Ancestors—this was an incredibly rare occurrence.
Many Ancestors had never married, and some who had found their wives had long since fallen, or their strength was so low that they couldn't endure the long passage of time and had entered a self-imposed slumber.
Thus, these Ancestors were extremely envious of Ye Tian.
But there was nothing they could do; jealousy was useless.
The human race had birthed a new Ancestor, and combined with Ye Tian being equivalent to three Ancestors, it was as if they now possessed four Ancestors.
With Ye Tian's strength, the human race was almost comparable to the Destruction Saint Race.
Another half a Domain Year passed. On this day, the human race once again became the focus of many Ancestors.
Simply because the human race had birthed yet another Ancestor! Boom!!!! Ye Xingchen broke through and was stepping into the Ancestor realm. Countless amounts of forbidden energy surged into his body, helping him temper his physique and condense an Ancestral Body.
Soon, the Ancestors arrived again, each one dumbfounded.
"How can this be? How has the human race birthed another Ancestor? It's impossible to have luckily obtained another masterless Ancestor Method. There is definitely something wrong with the human race!!!"
The Spacetime Ancestor said in shock.
"Could it be that the human race has mastered a method to break through to the Ancestor realm without an Ancestor Method?"
One Ancestor speculated.
"Impossible. An Ancestor Method is the only way to become an Ancestor; otherwise, it cannot be done. Perhaps the human race's luck is truly just that good, and they obtained two masterless Ancestor Methods!"
Another Ancestor remarked.
At this point, many Ancestors were driven mad with jealousy. If it weren't for Ye Tian's immense strength, they truly would have barged into the Profound Void Ancestral World to investigate the human race's secrets.
Millions of years later, Ye Xingchen officially succeeded in his breakthrough, stepping into the Ancestor realm and calling himself the Star Ancestor.
Shortly thereafter, Ye Xingyue also broke through to the Ancestor realm, calling herself the Star-Moon Ancestor.
Ye Yu was slightly slower, but two Domain Years later, she also stepped into the Ancestor realm, calling herself the Rain Ancestor.
From that point on, all of Ye Tian's family members had stepped into the Ancestor realm.
With the human race birthing several Ancestors in succession, anyone could see that the human race was not normal.
Ye Tian had no way to explain. If he said he had created many Ancestor Methods, would others believe him? They wouldn't believe it at all! Even if they did believe it, the other Ancestors would only become more terrified, because if he could continuously create Ancestor Methods, it meant he could continuously create Ancestors. That was even more frightening! On this day.
Ye Tian and Jie Zu were chatting together.
Abruptly.
Jie Zu warned: "Myriad Law Ancestor, you must be careful of the Saint Ancestor!"
"Be careful of the Saint Ancestor? Why?"
Ye Tian frowned.
Jie Zu remained silent for a moment before finally saying: "The Saint Ancestor is not simple. Moreover, with your human race birthing Ancestors one after another, the Saint Ancestor will feel that your race holds a great secret that might be of huge help to his own strength. Even if it isn't helpful to him, if he masters your secret, he might be able to continuously create Ancestors, allowing the Dark Saint Sect to become a power that surpasses the Destruction Saint Race.
Therefore, he might make a move against you."
"I am not afraid of a mere Saint Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said confidently.
After all, his strength had reached the level of a top-tier Ancestor, and he was at the peak of that level. Ordinary top-tier Ancestors were far from being his match.
"No, the Saint Ancestor's own strength might not be that great, but he knows even more terrifying top-tier Ancestors!!!"
Jie Zu revealed a massive secret.
