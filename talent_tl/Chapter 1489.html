Chapter 1488: Great Harvest, Bone General Bone Beads
!
Xuanxu Ancestral Realm.
Ye Tian returned with a smile on his face.
Although he wouldn't be able to copy a Bone Soldier to sneak into the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone again, the harvest this time was significant, making his long-term planning worthwhile.
"Eighteen Bone Beads, 120,000 Ancestral Bone Stones... I wonder if these Ancestral Bone Stones will be effective for me."
Ye Tian took out an Ancestral Bone Stone and absorbed and refined it.
"There's some effect, but it's far inferior to the Bone Beads. By my estimation, the cumulative effect of 120,000 Ancestral Bone Stones is probably only equivalent to one Bone Bead!"
Ye Tian estimated.
From this perspective, the Bone Beads were naturally more effective! However, keeping the Ancestral Bone Stones was useless, and he couldn't go back to the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone to buy more Bone Beads anyway. "I'll just refine them all!"
Ye Tian decided.
One by one, the Ancestral Bone Stones were consumed under Ye Tian's refinement. As the years passed, in just tens of thousands of years, all these Ancestral Bone Stones were refined by Ye Tian.
Consequently, Ye Tian's cultivation improved slightly, and his strength grew.
In the following days, Ye Tian continued to refine the Bone Beads one by one.
As each Bone Bead was refined, Ye Tian's strength grew more and more powerful.
Furthermore, Ye Tian discovered that he was far from reaching his limit. Although his combat power seemed to have reached the peak of a Top-tier Ancestor, he could still continue to improve, showing that his foundation was far stronger than that of other Top-tier Ancestors.
In the blink of an eye, hundreds of thousands of years passed.
By now, all eighteen Bone Beads had been refined by Ye Tian.
And now, Ye Tian's strength had increased several times over.
His previous strength had already reached an incredibly terrifying level; increasing it several times over now made it truly formidable.
"I can enter the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone again. Moreover, my strength has increased several times over this time. Those Bone Kings likely don't know this. I can hide a portion of my strength and then suddenly erupt; perhaps I can kill more Bone Soldiers and obtain many Bone Beads!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Once he obtained more Bone Beads, his strength would reach an incredibly terrifying level, allowing him to sweep through the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
In short, he was going to enter the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone this time to harvest even more Bone Beads.
On this day.
Ye Tian's copy body arrived outside the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone and, without the slightest hesitation, entered directly.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian took the initiative to enter the depths of the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone. At this moment, twenty-five Bone Kings appeared—likely all the Bone Kings in the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
Simultaneously, a large number of Bone Generals and Bone Soldiers formed squads and charged toward Ye Tian.
"Damned Ancestor, you actually didn't die! Even if you didn't, killing this special clone of yours will still cause you a massive loss!"
The Bone Kings said angrily.
In their view, Ye Tian's body was so special—just like his original body—that the price paid for it must have been enormous.
With their twenty-five Bone Kings plus so many Bone Generals and Bone Soldiers, it was enough to blast Ye Tian to death without allowing him to kill a single Bone Soldier.
Rumble!!!! Under the continuous siege, Ye Tian appeared to be completely overwhelmed, even suffering significant injuries, as if he had fallen into a desperate situation.
At this point, the Bone Kings let down their guard, believing Ye Tian was powerless to change the outcome and was certain to die.
Right then.
Ye Tian suddenly erupted with his full combat power, broke through the Bone Kings' encirclement, and charged toward the Bone Soldiers.
Combining the Extreme Arm Ultimate Ancestral Technique with the Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique, and further utilizing the Flowing Light and Golden Wing Ultimate Ancestral Techniques—two speed-type ultimate ancestral techniques—he charged forward at extreme speed and unleashed a peak strike.
Boom!!!! Under the single strike, three Bone Soldiers were unable to dodge and had their bodies shattered instantly, dying on the spot and leaving behind three Bone Beads.
With a wave of his hand, Ye Tian collected the three Bone Beads and continued to slaughter other Bone Soldiers.
Although the Bone Soldiers were comparable to ordinary Ancestors, the gap between them and Ye Tian was too vast. They couldn't withstand a full-strength blow; after all, Ye Tian's strength was dozens of times greater than that of an average Top-tier Ancestor.
"Stop him!!!"
The Bone Kings shouted urgently.
If Ye Tian were allowed to kill too many Bone Soldiers and harvest a large number of Bone Beads, he could use them to increase his strength, and they would no longer be able to deal with him.
If that happened, they would be in big trouble.
"All Bone Soldiers, evacuate this place!"
The Bone Kings issued the order.
No matter how strong Ye Tian was, he couldn't kill a Bone General in a single blow; at most, he could heavily injure one, requiring several moves to finish them off.
Therefore, the Bone Generals could stay, but the Bone Soldiers could not.
However, the Bone Soldiers were too slow, while Ye Tian was too fast—much faster than even the Bone Kings. Since the Bone Kings couldn't trap Ye Tian immediately, they lost the opportunity to surround and kill him.
Bang bang bang!!!! One Bone Soldier after another was blasted apart by Ye Tian, turning into Bone Beads.
In a short while, Ye Tian had harvested over thirty Bone Beads.
By this time, many Bone Soldiers had already disappeared, evidently having fled.
It wasn't suitable to pursue the Bone Soldiers now, as they had surely fled back to their lair, which was protected by Bone Race formations. If he went there, the Bone Kings, coordinated with the formations, could easily kill him.
So, he began to attack the Bone Generals.
However, the Bone Generals were numerous, and he was being besieged by twenty-five Bone Kings.
It was difficult for him to kill a Bone General. Ultimately, at the cost of being heavily injured, he managed to kill two Bone Generals and escaped the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
If Ye Tian's original body had also entered, the original plus the copy body might have been able to kill more Bone Generals and a large number of Bone Soldiers. But Ye Tian didn't dare take the risk. After all, the Bone Race's strength was mysterious and unpredictable; they could mobilize more Bone Kings from the other side of the gate, or perhaps even more powerful Bone Race members.
At that point, he would be in danger.
Therefore, he only dared to send his copy body. A smaller harvest didn't matter as long as his own safety was ensured.
After emerging from the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, Ye Tian's copy body delivered the harvest to his original body and then immediately dispelled itself.
"Two Bone General Bone Beads and thirty-three Bone Soldier Bone Beads... the harvest this time is massive!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
Once he refined all these Bone Beads, those twenty-five Bone Kings would definitely be unable to stop him. If the Bone Race had no reinforcements, he would be strong enough to sweep through the entire Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
Just then, in a majestic hall deep within the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, the twenty-five Bone Kings were discussing the matter regarding Ye Tian.
