Chapter 1606: Wind Source Gold Crystal Vein, Strike!

Having slain so many Eternal Tribulation Race members, it was enough for Ye Tian to exchange for sufficient resources to help him break through to the third Supreme Being realm, or even the fourth Supreme Being realm. Thus, he prepared to leave and no longer continue taking risks.
After all, this body was still carrying a finger of an Eternal realm expert, and that could not be lost; otherwise, the loss would be too great.
After that.
Ye Tian prepared to leave Wind Ancient River, but at that moment, he sensed a huge fluctuation of battle; the fighting there was probably extremely intense.
“A large-scale battle?”
Ye Tian guessed.
He was wondering whether to go take part, or simply ignore that large-scale battle and choose to leave directly?
“Go take a look. What if there’s a great opportunity? Besides, with my strength, as long as I’m not surrounded by multiple six Supreme Beings, I won’t fear danger, and I can even leave easily!” Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he moved in covertly.
Very quickly, he arrived at this battlefield.
A large number of Eternal Tribulation Race members and Supreme Beings were slaughtering each other here. There were even quite a few six Supreme Beings, and the battle was unimaginably tragic; even five Supreme Beings could only count as slightly stronger cannon fodder, and would fall here if they were not careful.
And at this moment, many Eternal Tribulation Race members were guarding an island. Dense arrays protected that place, and although quite a few arrays had been damaged, the Supreme Beings were unable to force their way in for the time being.
Clearly, the Supreme Beings’ target was this island.
Suddenly, a six Supreme Being holding a top-tier source weapon joined the battlefield, and suddenly burst forth with battle power comparable to a top-tier seven Supreme Being. He was clearly an extraordinary genius, probably no weaker than the Void Divine Son.
In an instant, three Eternal Tribulation Race members at the six Supreme Being level were heavily injured.
Then, this six Supreme Being took out a defensive source weapon and, under the protection of many Supreme Beings, charged toward that island.
He took out a formation-breaking source weapon, and with a single strike, directly pierced through all the dense formations.
With a tearing sound!
The island was shattered, and a massive mine vein connected beneath the island to the bottom of Wind Ancient River was exposed.
This was not a Wind Source Crystal Sand mine vein, but rather...
“My heavens, it’s actually a Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein!”
Even Ye Tian was tempted.
Although Wind Source Gold Crystal belonged to the same vein as Wind Source Crystal Sand, their values were worlds apart. Wind Source Gold Crystal could not only be used to refine source weapons, but could even have Wind Source Gold Liquid extracted from it, and this material was of enormous help to an Eternal Body, its value far exceeding even that of Eternal Tribulation Crystal by a great deal.
Just how many Wind Source Gold Crystals could be born from such a Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein? It was simply unimaginable.
One could imagine how immense the value of this Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein was; even half-step Eternal realm experts would be tempted!
However, mining the Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein was also rather troublesome.
The Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein was too sturdy. Naturally, the difficulty of separating out Wind Source Gold Crystal was very high, and the mine vein was connected to Wind Ancient River. It was impossible to uproot the vein in one go, even for a half-step Eternal realm expert, unless a true Eternal realm expert made a move.
The Eternal Tribulation Race had long discovered this mine vein and had been secretly mining it all along, but the efficiency was not high.
Even so, the Eternal Tribulation Race had still mined a great deal of Wind Source Gold Crystal and made a huge profit!
At this moment, many Supreme Beings had already rushed into the Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein, and they were not thinking of obtaining too much Wind Source Gold Crystal. Even if they could get a large piece of Wind Source Gold Crystal, that would already be a tremendous harvest.
Rumble, rumble, rumble!!!!
One Supreme Being after another bombarded the mine vein, not caring at all whether some Wind Source Gold Crystal would be damaged.
Occasionally, a piece of Wind Source Gold Crystal would be blasted out, and if a Supreme Being snatched it, they would immediately choose to flee, not thinking about a second piece of Wind Source Gold Crystal at all.
Ye Tian did not make a move immediately. Aside from that finger of an Eternal realm expert, his true strength was not very formidable. His normal combat power was insufficient, so he could not sustain prolonged fighting. He could not possibly only seize a few pieces of Wind Source Gold Crystal, could he?
Therefore, he was waiting for an opportunity.
At this time, not only were the Supreme Beings fighting over Wind Source Gold Crystal, but once the Eternal Tribulation Race saw that they could no longer stop it, they also began fighting the Supreme Beings for Wind Source Gold Crystal.
It had to be said that the Eternal Tribulation Race still had quite a large number of members, so they did snatch quite a bit of Wind Source Gold Crystal, but many Wind Source Gold Crystals were also destroyed. After all, the Supreme Beings’ mining methods were too brutal, directly bombarding with supreme techniques, and nearly seventy to eighty percent of the Wind Source Gold Crystal was wasted.
But if they did not use this method, then mining even one piece of Wind Source Gold Crystal would take a very long time. Right now, where would they find that much time to spare?
Time slowly passed, and this Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein was already torn apart and utterly miserable.
However, many Wind Source Gold Crystals had still been mined away.
Still, many Supreme Beings fled after snatching Wind Source Gold Crystal, and at this moment, the Eternal Tribulation Race held the advantage.
Helplessly, the other Supreme Beings did not dare remain here any longer and chose to flee.
The Eternal Tribulation Race was also worried that their own Wind Source Gold Crystal would be snatched by the Supreme Beings, so they had to send these Wind Source Gold Crystals out of Wind Ancient River at the first possible moment.
Thus.
Those Eternal Tribulation Race members who had snatched Wind Source Gold Crystal immediately took out their Wind Source Gold Crystal and handed it to a six Supreme Being. This six Supreme Being was very powerful, the leader here, and an existence comparable to the Void Divine Son.
Just as this Eternal Tribulation Race member had collected the Wind Source Gold Crystal and was about to leave this place and return to the Eternal Tribulation Race headquarters, Ye Tian made his move.
“．‖Good opportunity!”
Ye Tian immediately activated that finger of an Eternal realm expert and unleashed the Destruction Severing Finger.
This time, although he did not fully activate that finger of an Eternal realm expert, he still activated nearly more than half of its power, unleashing a strike comparable to the battle power of a top-tier eight Supreme Being.
“No!”
That Eternal Tribulation Race member and the Eternal Tribulation Race members around him were all dumbfounded.
This was a strike comparable to the eight Supreme Being level. Had a eight Supreme Being entered Wind Ancient River?
But how could that be possible?
Wind Ancient River currently only allowed six Supreme Beings to enter!
They could not block it!!!!
The other Eternal Tribulation Race members, because they were gathered together, had no time to escape. One after another, they turned to ash, while that Eternal Tribulation Race member comparable to the Void Divine Son tried his best to resist this strike, but he was still slightly lacking.
With a loud boom!
This Eternal Tribulation Race member’s body collapsed, his life force was annihilated, and he turned into a corpse.
Swish, swish, swish!
Ye Tian collected their corpses and treasures, forcibly enduring the injuries in his body as he left this place at extremely high speed.
Very quickly, Ye Tian arrived at one of Wind Ancient River’s exits and returned to the outside world.
