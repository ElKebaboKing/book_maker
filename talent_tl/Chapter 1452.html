Chapter 1451: I'm Suited for Patrol
!
Ye Tian accelerated time, and ten thousand years quickly passed, allowing him to condense another duplicate body.
Subsequently.
This duplicate body of Ye Tian headed to the War Ancestor Realm, asking the War Ancestor to send him to the Saint Realm Continent at the edge of the Dark Forbidden Zone.
....Saint Realm Continent.
When Ye Tian descended here once again, several Supreme-level Domain Ancestors who thought he had fallen were shocked to see him.
After explanations from other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, they finally understood the reason and were all envious of Ye Tian's cloning technique.
Afterward, Ye Tian returned to the place he was stationed.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor, you really didn't perish!"
Peng Ancestor said happily upon seeing Ye Tian.
"No, the one who fell last time was merely one of my clones!"
Ye Tian explained.
"Hahaha, if the Desolation Race finds out, they'll probably be fuming. They sacrificed a Six-Mark Desolation member and still failed to kill you!"
Peng Ancestor and the others laughed heartily.
Peng Ancestor and the rest did not inquire about the secrets of Ye Tian's clones. After all, even if they asked, Ye Tian wouldn't tell them, and it would only serve to offend him.
Thus, they tactfully skipped over that question.
Afterward, life returned to normal.
In the blink of an eye, several thousand more years passed. The Desolation Race still didn't know Ye Tian wasn't dead because they hadn't attacked this particular line of defense again.
One day.
A small squad of the Desolation Race attacked Ye Tian's defensive line, and the two sides engaged in battle.
Ye Tian once again copied the talents of the Desolation Race. After all, this newly condensed duplicate body did not have the Desolation Ancestral Talent, so he had to copy it again.
During this skirmish, Ye Tian did not kill any members of the Desolation Race.
Consequently, the news that he wasn't dead was bound to be exposed.
....Desolation Race Territory.
A Four-Mark Desolation member reported the news of Ye Tian being alive to Ao Long.
"Lord Ao Long, that Forbidden Zone creature we sacrificed a Six-Mark member to kill last time has appeared again!"
The Four-Mark Desolation member reported.
"What? Are you sure?"
Ao Long said in disbelief.
"Certain. We exchanged blows with him; he is definitely still alive, and his strength is just as formidable!"
The Four-Mark Desolation member confirmed.
"How could he not be dead? His physical body was clearly annihilated, yet he's still alive? Could it be an avatar? No, if it were an avatar, we wouldn't fail to recognize it; it must be a true body! Besides, the clones of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors are usually very weak, and in this region of space-time, it's difficult for clones to exert their full strength. We would only need to slightly affect space-time to sever the connection, and their clones would be rendered useless."
Ao Long muttered to himself.
It was precisely because of this that those Supreme-level Domain Ancestors didn't bother spending effort to condense clones, as they were essentially useless.
In the following days, the Desolation Race attacked the defensive line where Ye Tian was located time and again, testing him repeatedly. They even isolated space-time to see if Ye Tian was a clone. In the end, no matter what they did, they couldn't affect Ye Tian's combat power.
Clearly, this body of Ye Tian's was not a clone, but a true body.
They couldn't understand how Ye Tian could still be alive, but they didn't dare to sacrifice the life of another Six-Mark Desolation member to test it, so they could only ignore him...
...and treat Ye Tian as an ordinary Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian copied many Ancestral Talents from the Desolation Race and exchanged the merit he earned from guarding this place for a stalk of ordinary Desolation Grass. This was a plant that contained concentrated desolation energy; to others, it was a poisonous weed, but to Ye Tian, it was a supreme treasure.
Afterward, Ye Tian requested leave to return, intending to bring this stalk of Desolation Grass back. More importantly, he wanted to send the Ancestral Talents he had copied onto his duplicate body back to his main body, as only then would they truly belong to him.
Otherwise, if the duplicate body died in battle, the Ancestral Talents would be lost again.
Profound Void Holy World.
Ye Tian's duplicate body returned.
"Desolation Ancestral Talent, bestow!"
"Time Ancestral Talent, bestow!"
Ye Tian's duplicate body bestowed each of the copied Ancestral Talents. He could fully integrate these talents, making his original Ancestral Talents even more powerful.
Of course, the Desolation Ancestral Talent and the Time Ancestral Talent were the most important, as these were talents Ye Tian did not possess before.
Once all the Ancestral Talents were bestowed, Ye Tian's duplicate body returned to the Saint Realm Continent.
For the Primordial Ancestors, sending someone into the Saint Realm Continent was just a matter of a single thought. Thus, they weren't afraid of the trouble, especially since it only happened once in a long while and didn't tax their minds at all.
Not long after.
Ye Tian fully integrated all the Ancestral Talents bestowed by the duplicate body.
"Desolation Grass, I wonder how effective it is?"
Ye Tian swallowed the Desolation Grass, which was rich in desolation energy, in one gulp.
Boom!!!!! Desolation energy erupted within Ye Tian's body. Then, under the influence of the Desolation Ancestral Talent, it began to temper Ye Tian's Ancestral Body and Supreme Domain Body.
After an unknown amount of time, the desolation energy within Ye Tian's body was completely refined by him.
"My Domain Body has strengthened quite a bit, and my Ancestral Body has also made some slight progress. It's a pity this was just the lowest grade of desolation treasure. If I had enough desolation treasures, my strength could probably improve rapidly, and my cultivation might even quickly rise to the middle stage of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Unfortunately, desolation treasures were very rare. Although he could exchange for them from the Primordial Ancestors, he needed merit.
Guarding the border of the Dark Forbidden Zone only earned a small amount of merit. He was only able to exchange for the Desolation Grass because he had killed several One-Mark Desolation members last time; otherwise, he would have needed to guard the place for countless ages to accumulate enough merit for it.
After all, for the Primordial Ancestors, desolation treasures could also improve their own cultivation, so they naturally wouldn't let others exchange for them easily.
Of course, even if Ye Tian had enough merit, if he kept exchanging for desolation treasures, the Primordial Ancestors would eventually discover his secret of being able to absorb desolation energy.
"The best choice is to go out and find desolation treasures myself! I should talk to the Primordial Ancestors about switching to a patrol mission.
With my strength and my immortal nature, patrolling is what suits me best. And only by patrolling is it easy to encounter desolation treasures!"
Ye Tian decided.
Thus, Ye Tian sought out the War Ancestor.
"War Ancestor, I want to take on a patrol mission. Staying stationed in that one area doesn't suit me!"
Ye Tian volunteered.
"Patrol!"
The War Ancestor thought about it carefully and realized it made sense. Although patrolling required at least the strength of a Half-Step Primordial Ancestor, while Ye Tian's strength wasn't quite there yet, the gap wasn't too large.
Moreover, it didn't matter if Ye Tian's clone died. A patroller could act as a rescue force; even if they encountered an attack from a Primordial Ancestor, Ye Tian could stall for a brief moment, buying time for other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors to escape.
Therefore, Ye Tian was indeed more suited for patrolling.
"Fine, I agree. I'll speak with the other Primordial Ancestors later and have you assigned as one of the patrollers!"
The War Ancestor agreed.
