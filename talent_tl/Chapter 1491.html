Chapter 1490: Twenty Ultimate Defensive Ancestral Techniques
!
Xuanxu Ancestral Realm.
Ye Tian's cultivation continued to rise, and his combat power grew stronger and stronger, as if there were no end to it.
However, Ye Tian knew very well that he was fast approaching the limit of a top-tier Ancestor.
Furthermore, even the Bone General Bone Beads were no longer providing a significant boost to his strength. Perhaps after refining these six Bone General Bone Beads, refining more would only yield a third or even a fifth of the original effect.
"With my current cultivation and strength, if I were to refine Bone Soldier Bone Beads, the effect would likely be negligible,"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
This was just like Nirvana treasures; if one refined too many, further refinement would have no effect.
It was similar to how an ordinary person develops resistance after taking too much of a certain medicine; the same applied to Ancestors.
"Regardless, I'll refine all six Bone General Bone Beads first, and then continue to snatch more!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Millions of years passed. Under Ye Tian's full effort, all six Bone General Bone Beads were completely refined.
By this time, Ye Tian's strength had received a massive boost, becoming much more powerful than before.
Consequently, Ye Tian once again used a duplicate body to break into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
....Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
As soon as Ye Tian entered, he sensed a large number of Bone King auras.
"Sixty Bone Kings! The Bone Race actually dispatched so many experts!!!"
Ye Tian was surprised.
Boom!!!! Sixty Bone Kings crossed over and surrounded Ye Tian, attacking him from all sides.
During the battle, one of the Bone Kings sacrificed a bone sword.
The moment this bone sword appeared, Ye Tian felt the aura of death.
"What kind of weapon is this?"
Although Ye Tian did not panic, he was shocked.
It didn't matter if the duplicate body died, but he had never seen such a terrifying weapon in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
He was certain that the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor definitely did not possess such a thing; otherwise, they would have used it the last time they dealt with him.
Had they used such a weapon, he definitely wouldn't have been able to contend with the Destruction Ancestor last time and would have been instantly killed.
"Die, Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain!"
The Bone King roared.
Boom!!!! The bone sword transformed into a beam of light and pierced toward Ye Tian.
At this moment, time and space were frozen. Even a powerful force could not break the spatial-temporal lock; it was as if no power could contend with this... bone sword.
"Break for me!!!!"
Ye Tian roared inwardly, mobilizing all his power and activating one Ultimate Ancestral Technique after another. Only then did he manage to break free from the constraints.
However, he still could not dodge the bone sword.
Puchi!!!! The bone sword pierced through Ye Tian's body, but it did not kill him; it only heavily wounded him.
After unleashing this strike, that Bone King's aura became incredibly weak, clearly the price for using the bone sword.
But at this moment, Ye Tian was on the verge of death and could not take the opportunity to kill this Bone King.
The other Bone Kings seized the chance and immediately attacked Ye Tian, while one Bone King escorted the weakened one away from the battlefield.
Before long, Ye Tian's duplicate body was blasted apart and killed.
...."I didn't expect that after becoming so powerful, I would still be killed,"
Ye Tian said speechlessly.
He also understood very well that the reason he was killed was not because there were a dozen more Bone Kings, but because of that... bone sword.
That was definitely a powerful weapon used at the level of a Supreme Ancestor. Only such a weapon could heavily wound him to the point where even recovery-type Ancestral Techniques couldn't heal his injuries in time.
Ye Tian did not continue to send duplicate bodies. If he couldn't block that... bone sword, no matter how many times he sent a duplicate, he wouldn't be able to obtain bone beads and would only be killed over and over again.
"I must strengthen my defense!"
Ye Tian thought.
He had always felt his defense was very strong, yet even with two defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques stacked, he was still pierced by the bone sword.
"Two defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques can't contend with that bone sword, but what if there were three or even four?"
Ye Tian pondered.
Defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques were easy to stack, whereas offensive ones were indeed difficult, if not impossible, to stack.
Therefore, if he could create stackable defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, he might be able to withstand the attack of that... bone sword.
"In the coming years, I will focus on creating defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques!"
Ye Tian decided.
He had plenty of experience in creating Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, and with his current cultivation level, the speed at which he could create them was naturally faster.
According to Ye Tian's estimation, he could create at least two to three defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques per Realm Year.
As he progressed, he might even be able to create more.
Although activating Ultimate Ancestral Techniques consumed a lot of energy—and unleashing multiple ones at once would drain a large portion of his Ancestral Power—as long as he could block a single strike from the bone sword, he could kill several Bone Generals in a short amount of time.
Time flowed slowly as Ye Tian continued to create defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques.
Meanwhile, the Bone Kings of the Bone Race, seeing that no Ancestor had entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Land for a long time, assumed that Ye Tian had suffered heavy losses. They believed that the death of his clone must have come at a great cost, requiring a long time to recover, and thus they all breathed a sigh of relief.
As time passed, some Bone Kings returned to the Bone Race.
In the blink of an eye, ten Realm Years had passed.
By now, the Bone Kings in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land had already forgotten about Ye Tian.
However, no one knew how many... defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques Ye Tian had created during this long period.
"Hahaha, eighteen defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques! It's a pity I can't seem to create any more; it's as if some unseen force is limiting me from creating more defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Because of this limitation, he had only created eighteen new ones. Adding the two he already knew, that made a total of twenty defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques.
Most of these defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques were shield-type techniques that could be fused and stacked.
Now, with twenty defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, even a strike from that... bone sword wouldn't be able to hurt him, let alone the attacks of the Bone Kings.
If he kept all twenty defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques active, although he could only fight for a short... while before exhausting his Ancestral Power, he would likely be invincible during that time.
"This time, I must obtain enough bone beads, and maybe even a few Bone King Bone Beads!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
