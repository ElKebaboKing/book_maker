Chapter 1421: All Holy Soul Beasts Have Holy Talents
!!!!
The Holy Soul Realm.
One by one, the Supreme Domain Ancestors shuttled in, but because it was a random teleportation, everyone's landing location was different.
Ye Tian appeared in a forest within the Holy Soul Realm. He was the only Supreme Domain Ancestor around, and he didn't sense the presence of any others.
Clearly, he was quite far from the other Supreme Domain Ancestors.
Boom!!!! A powerful aura flew in from the distance.
"A top-tier Domain Ancestor-level creature!"
Ye Tian sensed it.
"Copy Talent, Investigate!"
Race: Holy Soul Wolf Race
Bloodline Talent: Supreme Grade
Source Power Talent: Super God-level Limit
Source Body Talent: Super God-level Limit
Source Speed Talent: Super God-level Limit
Holy Talent: Colossal Strength
....From the investigation of his Copy Talent, this Holy Soul Wolf possessed a Holy Talent.
"What's going on?"
Ye Tian was stunned.
Why did this Holy Soul Wolf have a Holy Talent? Could it be the descendant of a Transcendent Domain Ancestor? "No, the Holy Soul Realm has opened more than once, and there are no Transcendent Domain Ancestors here. Otherwise, the Primordial Ancestors wouldn't dare let us juniors in!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Therefore, the Holy Soul Beasts here were, at their weakest, merely at the level of powerful Invincible Domain Ancestors; there wouldn't be any Transcendent Domain Ancestors.
So, how could Holy Soul Beasts with Holy Talents be born here? "Strange, so strange!"
Ye Tian muttered.
Of course, there were too many mysteries in the Dark Forbidden Zone. As the first ancestral realm of the Dark Holy Domain, it was normal for the Primordial Ancestral Realm to have some special circumstances.
"Copy Colossal Strength Holy Talent!"
Ye Tian succeeded.
Immediately, with a wave of his hand, the Golden Silk Holy Technique tore through the Holy Soul Wolf's body, killing it instantly.
A... Holy Soul Wolf with a Holy Talent had fallen. If this were the outside world, the death of such a... Holy Talent possessor would likely cause an earth-shattering sensation.
But here, it didn't cause a single ripple.
Upon the beast's death, a wisp of Holy Soul Power surged into Ye Tian's body and was absorbed by his soul.
Ye Tian then began to refine this Holy Soul Power, strengthening his Soul Holy Light.
A few months later, Ye Tian had completely refined the Holy Soul Power, and his Soul Holy Light had grown slightly stronger.
"It really works. If I kill enough Holy Soul Beasts, the progress of my Soul Holy Light will be very fast. In this Holy Soul Realm, I might actually be able to fully condense a Holy Soul!"
Ye Tian muttered.
Whoosh! Ye Tian shuttled through the air and soon discovered another... Holy Soul Beast. This one was stronger, at the level of a Supreme Domain Ancestor, and it also possessed a Holy Talent.
[Holy Talent: Meteor] This was a speed-type Holy Talent.
"Copy Meteor Holy Talent!"
Ye Tian succeeded and also slew this Holy Soul Beast.
Afterward, he absorbed the Holy Soul Power from the dead beast to strengthen his Soul Holy Light.
It had to be said that a Holy Soul Beast at the Supreme Domain Ancestor level contained more Holy Soul Power, and the effect was better.
But at this moment, Ye Tian was still puzzled as to why both Holy Soul Beasts he encountered had Holy Talents. "Could it be that the Holy Soul Beasts are a race of Holy Talents?"
Ye Tian thought.
If that were the case, he could copy countless Holy Talents. Moreover, Holy Talents could be fused together. Previously, he hadn't fused them because he had copied too few, and fusing them wouldn't have been very useful.
But if he had countless Holy Talents, once fused, would a talent above the Holy level be born? Although the Transcendent Domain Ancestors of the Battle Sect had said that Holy Talents were the... limit, they might not know everything. Perhaps they just hadn't seen a talent above the Holy level. Maybe something like an Ancestral Talent really existed?
"Hahaha, this place really is a blessed land for me! Even if I can't form a talent beyond the Holy level this time, by integrating so many Holy Talents, it will be much easier for me to practice Holy Techniques!"
Ye Tian muttered.
Subsequently, Ye Tian hunted Holy Soul Beasts to absorb Holy Soul Power and strengthen his Soul Holy Light while simultaneously copying Holy Talents.
Just as he suspected, all the Holy Soul Beasts here possessed Holy Talents.
The only pity was that these Holy Soul Beasts didn't know Holy Techniques; they could only use their Holy Talents to exert their power, which was a complete waste of the talent.
Of course, at the same level, the strength of a Holy Soul Beast still crushed ordinary cultivators. Only true geniuses could contend with a Holy Soul Beast of the same rank.
For example, the Supreme Domain Ancestor Holy Soul Beast Ye Tian encountered earlier probably had the combat power of a second-tier Supreme Domain Ancestor. A weak Supreme Domain Ancestor would truly be killed in an instant by such a beast.
Unfortunately, in Ye Tian's eyes, these Holy Soul Beasts were like ants, easily killed in an instant.
As time passed, Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light grew stronger and stronger, and he copied more and more Holy Talents.
One day, several decades later.
Ye Tian saw a giant black tree that towered into the clouds.
Three fruits grew on this giant black tree. Ye Tian could tell at a glance that these three fruits were extraordinary, not ordinary fruits.
"Could this be..."
Ye Tian thought of the name of a treasure.
"Black Scale Holy Fruit. Rumor has it that a single Black Scale Holy Fruit can create an Invincible Domain Ancestor, and there are three here, enough to create three Invincible Domain Ancestors! Moreover, for a... Supreme Domain Ancestor who has birthed Soul Holy Light, it can also strengthen that light!"
Ye Tian muttered.
In terms of value, a single Black Scale Holy Fruit was no less valuable than a Holy Soul Realm Flower.
Previously, Ye Tian had consumed the Holy Soul Realm Flower, which was originally intended for his wife. Now that he saw these Black Scale Holy Fruits, he could give them to her to help her step into the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm in the future.
"These Black Scale Holy Fruits belong to me!"
Ye Tian flew over.
Just then, he sensed the presence of a stone python.
Through his Copy Talent, he discovered that this stone python was also a Holy Soul Beast and possessed a Holy Talent called Petrification.
Furthermore, its realm was not that of a Supreme Domain Ancestor, but an Invincible Domain Ancestor.
And not far from the stone python, three humanoid stone statues stood there.
"Three Supreme Domain Ancestors have been petrified!"
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
The Supreme Domain Ancestors who could enter this place were naturally not weak; even the worst among them likely had first-tier Supreme Domain Ancestor strength. He hadn't expected such Supreme Domain Ancestors to be killed.
This showed that the stone python's strength was terrifying, likely possessing the combat power of a nine-step Invincible Domain Ancestor or higher.
The stone python didn't attack Ye Tian. Perhaps as long as one didn't approach its territory, it wouldn't strike.
"Someone is coming!"
Ye Tian sensed it.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!! Two Supreme Domain Ancestors appeared. They were very unfamiliar; Ye Tian had never seen them before.
But through his Copy Talent investigation, he learned of their identities.
"Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Dark Holy Sect!"
