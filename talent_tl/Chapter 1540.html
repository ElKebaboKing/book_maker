Chapter 1539: Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void, Top-grade Tribulation Stones!

Ye Tian took his Tribulation Body Token to the resource exchange of the Tianfenghai branch. Here, as long as one had contribution points, any treasure could be exchanged.
Of course, senior members could only exchange for treasures available to their rank; certain items reserved for elite-level members and above were inaccessible to senior members.
The Tribulation Body Token, however, did not require any contribution points and could be used to exchange for any single Tribulation Body cultivation technique.
Ye Tian browsed through the various Tribulation Body cultivation techniques—
Rising Sun Tribulation Body!
Gold Spirit Tribulation Body!
Eternal Sun Tribulation Body!
Windfall Tribulation Body!
The catalog of techniques scrolled past as Ye Tian browsed. Most of the Tribulation Body techniques were quite average, but they had low cultivation difficulty, low risk, and required few resources, making them easy to master.
Conversely, those more formidable Tribulation Bodies were extremely difficult to start and even harder to improve. Even for monstrous geniuses, it was highly likely they wouldn't be able to perfectly cultivate the Tribulation Body to the completion stage, forcing them to promote prematurely, resulting in an incomplete Tribulation Body.
Therefore, the best Tribulation Body was the one that suited oneself; it wasn't necessarily the case that the more powerful it was, the better.
Ye Tian naturally understood this principle, but he would not choose an average Tribulation Body. Instead, he intended to select a powerful technique that was also suitable for him.
After a long time, Ye Tian finished browsing all the Tribulation Body techniques in Tianfenghai.
"I'll choose this one!"
Ye Tian's gaze fixed on the technique called the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void Tribulation Body, and he immediately used his token to exchange for it.
When Ye Tian emerged after exchanging for the technique, Long He came over to inquire, "Ye Tian, which Tribulation Body technique did you choose?"
"Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void!"
Ye Tian replied truthfully.
"What? You actually chose that technique?" Long He was greatly shocked.
The Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void was known as one of the three great Tribulation Bodies of Tianfenghai. It seemed powerful, but the difficulty of cultivation was immense; even the descendants of first-class Invincibles might not dare to practice it.
The entry requirements for the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void required proficiency in multiple paths of rules, the comprehension of rules from various foreign realms, and a vast amount of Tribulation Blood—and it couldn't be just one type, but blood from different foreign realms, as Tribulation Blood also contained the rules of its respective realm.
Most terrifyingly, the cultivation speed of the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void was too slow, at most one-tenth the progress of other Tribulation Bodies, or even lower.
Furthermore, this technique would frequently hit bottlenecks and was prone to causing cultivation deviation, leading to the collapse of the physical body.
This was because the technique was somewhat incomplete, mainly because even its creator had never successfully cultivated it.
In reality, those top-tier Tribulation Body techniques all had some flaws to varying degrees because so few Invincibles practiced them, making it hard to perfect them. Moreover, every Invincible's body was different, so the flaws were naturally different as well.
Therefore, when practicing a top-tier Tribulation Body technique, one had to grope their way through and perfect it themselves.
But how many Invincibles could ultimately cultivate a top-tier technique to the completion stage?
"Yes, I've decided to practice the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void!" Ye Tian said firmly.
Long He tried to persuade him, "Ye Tian, don't just look at how powerful this technique is. Do you understand the difficulty and the flaws? It's better to practice an average technique. It might not be as strong as a top-tier one, but it's safe, and there's plenty of experience to draw from so you won't go astray!"
Ye Tian shook his head and said, "Lord Long He, I've already made up my mind. Besides, I have confidence!"
Seeing this, Long He stopped trying to dissuade him.
As for Ye Tian saying he had confidence, he didn't believe it for a second.
Having joined Tianfenghai, Ye Tian not only became a senior member instantly but also received a manor as a residence. In Juewu City, the cost of obtaining a manor was not low; one couldn't buy one without a hundred thousand Source Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Subsequently, Ye Tian renamed the manor "Ye Residence" and began his cultivation there.
His purpose in coming here was to comprehend the rules of the Tianlin Realm to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique. Thus, there was no difference between his duplicate body and his main body; once the duplicate created an Invincible Ancestral Technique, it was as good as the main body creating it.
Then...
Ye Tian began to receive the inheritance of the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void technique. It took a long time before the inheritance was finally complete.
Afterward...
Ye Tian's main body began practicing the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void, but he immediately felt the difficulty of this technique.
He cultivated bitterly for an entire domain year, yet there was almost no progress.
"It seems I must use a large amount of Tribulation Blood to make rapid progress with the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Moreover, since the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void was somewhat incomplete, for safety's sake, he had to let his duplicate body practice it first to perfect it step by step. Thus, the amount of Tribulation Blood needed would likely be even greater.
For an ordinary person, obtaining so much Tribulation Blood would be impossible.
But Ye Tian still had hope of acquiring a large amount.
However, using Tribulation Blood for cultivation wasn't actually the best way. Tribulation Blood was mild and expensive, but the tribulation power within wasn't particularly high. Many treasures contained more tribulation power than blood, but the power within them was incredibly chaotic and unsuitable for Invincibles to refine.
However, Ye Tian possessed the Eternal Talent: Tribulation and the Tribulation Transformation Secret Art, allowing him to refine even violent tribulation power.
Therefore, he planned to hunt Tribulation Beasts and exchange their blood for resources containing violent tribulation power. This was the most logical way to cultivate.
And Tianfenghai had such a resource: Top-grade Tribulation Stones.
The Tribulation Stones Ye Tian had traded for before were just ordinary low-grade stones. Above them were middle-grade, high-grade, and top-grade stones.
Among them, the tribulation power in a top-grade stone was even higher than that of thousand-tribulation blood, but the power inside was too chaotic for Invincibles to use. Thus, a top-grade stone was only worth about twenty-tribulation blood.
Consequently.
Ye Tian went to the Tianfenghai branch and exchanged the thirty-tribulation blood rewarded by Tianfenghai directly for top-grade stones, obtaining three of them.
To Ye Tian, these three top-grade stones were equivalent to three portions of thousand-year Tribulation Blood; their value was unimaginable.
Afterward.
Ye Tian's duplicate body used one top-grade stone to practice the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void. If there were any flaws or problems, he could perfect them in time without affecting his main body.
In this way, Ye Tian began his formal cultivation of the Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void.
