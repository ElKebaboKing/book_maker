Chapter 1667: The True Value of Void Beasts!

The Holy Peak Lord spoke as the representative.
“This time, the Void Beast side has sent a large number of Void Beasts, but they are still within our control. With the strength of the Lord Alliance, it is still possible to defeat them. Moreover, by killing these Void Beasts, we can also obtain more Void Beast materials, enough to make up for the losses. Right now, we should discuss the encirclement plan and strive to kill a few fifth-stage Void Beasts.”
After the Holy Peak Lord finished, the Void Sword Lord asked, “Lord Holy Peak, fifth-stage Void Beasts have strength comparable to Legendary Lords, and their ability to preserve their lives is extremely strong. Do the six chairmen have a way to kill them?”
“Naturally, we do!”
The Holy Peak Lord was extremely confident.
Then, the Holy Peak Lord said again: “I know many of you Lords have been secretly studying Void Beasts, but your strength has not yet reached the level of Legendary Lords after all, so your research on Void Beasts is not very thorough. However, the six of us chairmen have discovered a tremendous use for Void Beasts, and we can announce it, even spread it throughout the entire Space-Time Territory. Once it is spread, I’m afraid many Eternal Realm experts who do not wish to join the battle will take action.”
Although the Lord Alliance had gathered most of the Eternal Realm experts in the Space-Time Territory, there were still some Eternal Realm experts who had not joined the Lord Alliance. Although they could not enjoy some of the powers and benefits of the Lord Alliance, those people did not care.
Therefore, this time the six chairmen had no way to force them.
But once the benefits of Void Beasts were made public, that would be enough to attract them.
In that way, the encirclement plan could be carried out much better, and many Void Beasts would have nowhere to escape.
At this moment, the Phoenix Lord urgently asked, “Lord Holy Peak, we only know that the core of a Void Beast helps us temper our will, and that the bones and fur of Void Beasts can be used to refine Void Sacred Soldiers. Void Sacred Soldiers can injure Void Beasts more effectively. What other uses are there?”
The other Lords were also curious, waiting for the Holy Peak Lord’s announcement.
The Holy Peak Lord no longer hid it and said, “Void Beasts are a very special species. They seem to be unable to use any Eternal Rule, but their attack power can easily shatter and melt innate Eternal Rules. Moreover, they also possess a kind of etherealization ability, and this ability is the highest level of application of the Void Eternal Rule. In addition, we discovered that Void Beasts definitely possess will, yet we cannot sense their will. Therefore, it is very likely that their innate will and bodies are perfectly fused, with every particle of their bodies being will. Because of this, our will-based attacks also have a very hard time hurting them. As for breaking through from the Immortal Realm to the Undying Realm, one needs the body and will to become compatible, and that means a perfect fusion. A slight fusion is only Half-Step Undying Realm; once the fusion exceeds ten percent, one can break through the boundary and enter the Undying Realm. If the fusion reaches one hundred percent, one can attempt to break through to the Legendary Lord realm. And we have found a way to improve the fusion of will and body. This is also the achievement you have all longed to study. And the medium for this research is the Void Beast. If we announce this achievement, do you think there will still be any Lord who would not go kill Void Beasts?”
The Phoenix Lord, Void Sword Lord, and the other Immortal Realm Lords were all shocked.
They had indeed studied it in secret, but there had been little success. The Phoenix Lord was the Immortal Realm Lord who had studied it the deepest, yet he still had not found a result. But the six chairmen had actually done so.
“Six chairmen, are you really willing to make it public?”
The Phoenix Lord was somewhat incredulous.
If he had discovered it himself, he would absolutely not tell anyone.
The Six Sun Lord said firmly, “Of course we are willing to make it public. Mainly because this secret is of little use to us, and once it is made public, it may even give birth to a seventh Legendary Lord. The more Legendary Lords there are, the safer our Space-Time Territory and Source Sea will be. From this Void Beast invasion, we can infer that deep within the Void Beast ranks there are probably more than seven fifth-stage Void Beasts. If a large number of fifth-stage Void Beasts invade, we won’t be able to stop them. That is why we are willing to make it public and enhance the strength of the Space-Time Territory. Otherwise, we would not be so selfless.”
“Many thanks to the six chairmen!”
Many Immortal Realm Lords thanked them.
In truth, for the six chairmen, whether there were one more chairman or several more chairmen did not have much of an impact on them. What they wanted was eternal stability, to control the Space-Time Territory for countless Eternal years without being threatened. That was what they hoped for.
But this time, the Void Beasts had somewhat threatened their stability, which was why they chose to reveal the true value of Void Beasts.
Of course.
They also needed a large number of Eternal Realm experts to tie down the other Void Beasts, so that they themselves could focus on dealing with the seven fifth-stage Void Beasts.
Very quickly.
The meeting ended, and nearly a hundred Immortal Realm Lords prepared to fully assist the six chairmen, helping them split the battlefield and, at critical moments, stop those Void Beasts from rescuing the seven fifth-stage Void Beasts.
Not long after, the value of Void Beasts for exploitation was also publicized by the six chairmen. A large number of Eternal Realm experts learned of this secret, and even the Eight Extremes Tower learned of it.
Since the Eight Extremes Tower knew, Ye Tian naturally learned of it immediately as well.
---------------------
“Void Beasts can actually enhance will, and they can also promote the fusion of will and body, allowing one to step into the Undying Realm!”
Ye Tian was shocked.
No wonder the Phoenix Lord wanted to study Void Beasts; their value was simply too high.
Moreover, this secret was announced by the six chairmen, who held the highest status in the Lord Alliance, so its credibility was naturally very high.
“I was just worrying about how hard it would be to improve my will in the future, yet Void Beasts can help me improve it. However, with my current strength, I still can’t kill a Void Beast!” Ye Tian thought.
Void Beasts have strength comparable to Eternal Realm Lords, and they are very hard to injure.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian thought of something: although the etherealization Unparalleled Ancestor Talent is very powerful, Void Beasts have not deeply studied the etherealization Unparalleled Ancestor Talent. They only use the etherealization ability by instinct and their own racial characteristics.
However, he possessed a legendary Ancestor Talent-level etherealization talent. Although it would be very difficult to create a Legendary Eternal Skill related to the etherealization talent, it was not difficult to create a normal Eternal Skill focused on attacking the Void talent.
Once he created such an Eternal Skill, he would be able to inflict some damage on Void Beasts. Then, combined with the many Eight Extremes experts from the Eight Extremes Tower, there would be some hope of besieging and killing a first-stage Void Beast.
“Create such an Eternal Skill as quickly as possible!”
Ye Tian began to accelerate time to comprehend and create it, and the Lord Alliance would not launch a true full-scale assault on the Void Beasts so soon, so there was still plenty of time left for Ye Tian.
Ye Tian’s etherealization talent level was too high, so creating a normal Eternal Skill was very fast.
Not long after, an Eternal Skill was created.
Ye Tian named this Eternal Skill Void-Piercing Secret Sword, meaning to pierce through the power of etherealization.
Once this Eternal Skill is used, it can form a nearly illusory small sword that can injure Void Beasts in their etherealized state.
