Chapter 1530: Finally Breaking Through, Invincible Ancestor!

"It's the limit!"
Ye Tian had a feeling that the Physical Nine Tribulations Realm should be the limit; there was simply no such thing as a Tenth Tribulation Realm.
Moreover, no matter how he used Tribulation Stones to temper his body, he could not progress even a single bit; at the level of the Physical Tribulation Realm, he had already reached the pinnacle.
At this point, his only option was to head to a foreign world and use its rules to influence his body. By repeatedly contending against the rules of the foreign world, he could force his body to undergo a qualitative change, and then, with the help of a large number of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, step into the Invincible Realm.
Of course, exposing the physical body to the rules of a foreign world, especially during a non-Great Tribulation period, could turn one into ashes in an instant if one wasn't careful, and even many protective treasures might not be effective.
Furthermore, Ye Tian didn't have any life-saving treasures on him.
As for trading for treasures from other super-tier forces, that was also unrealistic.
"I'll start by using a duplicate body to resist the rules of the foreign world and give it a try. Even if I fail, it'll only be the loss of one duplicate body!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
---------------
This time, Ye Tian chose to enter the Xiyue Tribe World. He had constructed a Second-Tier Gate between the Xiyue Tribe World and the Dark Forbidden Domain, so entering the Xiyue Tribe World at his current level required no cost.
*Whoosh!*
Ye Tian passed through the Second-Tier Gate and arrived at the base in the Xiyue Tribe World.
Inside the base, Ye Tian looked out at the Xiyue Tribe World, sensing the rules of the outside world.
At this time, the Xiyue Tribe World was not in a Great Tribulation period. Once he entered, he would inevitably face the impact of the rules. Ordinary powerhouses, even those at the third level of the Supreme Realm, would be turned to ashes in an instant with no chance of survival.
Ye Tian was currently a powerhouse in the Physical Nine Tribulations Realm, but whether he could withstand the rules of the Xiyue Tribe World was still unknown.
At this moment.
Some Ancestors of the human race were also here. They all looked toward Ye Tian, waiting for him to enter the Xiyue Tribe World.
They knew Ye Tian was going to attempt to enter the Invincible Realm, so they all wanted to observe. Perhaps in the distant future, they too would have a chance to strike for the Invincible Realm!
Ye Tian had no worries. After all, this was just a duplicate body with no risk to his life, so he naturally didn't hesitate at all.
*Boom!!!*
Ye Tian left the base and stepped into the Xiyue Tribe World.
Immediately, the rules of the Xiyue Tribe World sensed Ye Tian as a foreign intruder, and endless rules crushed down upon him.
*Crack!!!!*
Ye Tian's body cracked. Even with his body at the Physical Nine Tribulations Realm, he couldn't withstand the rules of the foreign world. His body fractured instantly, endless blood flowing out only to be annihilated.
Ye Tian exerted all his strength to cast various defensive Supreme Ancestral Techniques, but they shattered one after another. These techniques couldn't protect him at all because this was a test by the rules against the very foundation of the physical body, something no Supreme Ancestral Technique could block.
Soon, Ye Tian's body could no longer withstand the suppression of the rules. He returned to the base just in time, so this duplicate body wasn't completely annihilated, though it was on the brink of death.
Inside the base.
Ye Tian's duplicate body sat there, sensing the condition within his body to comprehend the gains from this confrontation with the rules of the Xiyue Tribe World.
Soon, he had some small gains.
For example, some parts of his body had turned directly to ash during the confrontation; clearly, those parts could not evolve and needed to be discarded. Meanwhile, some parts had actually undergone slight changes and become stronger, so those areas needed focused tempering.
Afterward, Ye Tian entered the Xiyue Tribe World again to confront the rules once more, but this time the confrontation was very brief before he returned to the base.
Through repeated confrontations, Ye Tian gained a deeper understanding of the mysteries of the physical body.
But eventually, this duplicate body collapsed, unable to be restored.
Shortly after, Ye Tian spent more resources to condense another duplicate body and entered the Xiyue Tribe World again, comprehending the mysteries of the physical body through repeated struggles against the world.
One Realm Era!
Ten Realm Eras!
A hundred Realm Eras!
...
Ye Tian didn't know how many duplicate bodies he had condensed, but many resources from the Dark Forbidden Domain and those seized from other foreign worlds were almost entirely exhausted by him.
However, he finally succeeded.
Through countless condensations of duplicate bodies and countless confrontations with foreign world rules to comprehend physical mysteries, he successfully organized an evolution path suited to himself, allowing his body to undergo a qualitative change under the influence of foreign rules without being harmed.
On this day.
Under the influence of the Xiyue Tribe World's rules and Ye Tian's control, the duplicate body's physical form constantly changed. Some parts were discarded and replaced with a brand-new body. Through these repeated changes, the rules of the Xiyue Tribe World could no longer harm Ye Tian's body.
Moreover, with the consumption of countless resources, Ye Tian's body grew stronger and stronger.
Finally, the body underwent a qualitative change.
*Rumble!!!!*
Countless rules of the Xiyue Tribe World merged into Ye Tian's body, helping it complete its final qualitative change.
It was at this moment that Ye Tian took out a large number of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Ten!
A hundred!
A thousand!
In the end, Ye Tian consumed over three thousand Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals to achieve a complete breakthrough.
At the moment of breakthrough, Ye Tian's body completely transformed into an Invincible Tribulation Body, and his cultivation stepped into the Invincible Ancestor realm.
Of course, this was the breakthrough of Ye Tian's duplicate body, not his original body.
With the breakthrough of this duplicate body, Ye Tian found that he could permanently solidify this body to become a new clone, but the clone in the Haitian Forbidden Land had to be disbanded.
For Ye Tian, the Heyuan Sacred Beast clone in the Haitian Forbidden Land was too weak and couldn't help him much, so it was better to just disband it.
Thus, Ye Tian disbanded the Heyuan Sacred Beast clone and solidified this duplicate body into a permanent clone.
This way, Ye Tian could let this Invincible Ancestor clone attempt to head to the Eternal World, while his original body could remain in the Dark Forbidden Land to ensure the safety of the Dark Forbidden Domain.
After the duplicate body broke through, Ye Tian finally let his original body attempt it.
Not long after, his original body also completely stepped into the Invincible Ancestor realm.
Thus, Ye Tian was finally considered a true Invincible Ancestor. His strength far surpassed ordinary Invincible Ancestors; even the leader of the Boundless Wall was no longer his match, and his strength was likely infinitely close to a Fifth-Tier Invincible Being.
