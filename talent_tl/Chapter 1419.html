Chapter 1418: Sealing Off the Profound Void Sacred Realm
!
The Profound Void Sacred Realm held many opportunities, and as time passed, even more would be born in the future.
If the Profound Void Sacred Realm were to become an Ancestral Realm, the opportunities would be even greater, potentially even giving birth to opportunities and treasures helpful to Ancestors.
However, it would take time for the Profound Void Sacred Realm to grow. It was unknown how many ages it would take for the current Sacred Realm to evolve into an Ancestral Realm, and Ye Tian could not wait forever; that would be far too long.
He wanted to use opportunities to condense a Sacred Soul, and he could not rely on the Profound Void Sacred Realm for that; he had to seek out new opportunities.
In the blink of an eye, another million years passed.
By this time, the effects of the Sacred Tablet World had become mediocre for many Domain Ancestors, and for some, it had almost no effect at all.
Aside from the Domain Ancestors of the Human Race, many Domain Ancestors from other factions had left the Sacred Tablet World.
For instance, Saintess Qingyi and others had left the Profound Void Sacred Realm and returned to the Dark Sacred Domain.
For them, continuing to stay in the Profound Void Sacred Realm was undoubtedly a waste of time, as the Sacred Tablet was almost the greatest opportunity a Sacred Realm could offer; any subsequent opportunities would be quite ordinary.
.
They had no idea that the Profound Void Sacred Realm could be promoted to an Ancestral Realm, and thus they had no further expectations for it, naturally choosing not to stay.
The Domain Ancestors of the Battle Gate also left one by one, and even the eight Invincible Domain Ancestors of the Battle Gate departed.
After all, there was no longer any necessity for them to remain, and there were many matters to attend to in the Dark Sacred Domain, as well as opportunities that belonged to them there. It was already quite impressive that they had stayed in the Profound Void Sacred Realm for so long.
Over the next few hundred thousand years, the Domain Ancestors of other factions were almost entirely gone, with only a few remaining in the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
The Human Realm.
Ye Tian was currently listening to a report from the Sacred Hall.
"Lord Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, this is the progress report for the Human Race's Domain Ancestors!"
Blue Universe Domain Ancestor, a newly promoted Supreme Domain Ancestor of the Human Race and one of the guardians of the Sacred Hall, reported.
Ye Tian looked it over and was very satisfied with the progress of the Human Race's Domain Ancestors.
The Human Race now possessed approximately one million Domain Ancestors, over thirty thousand top-tier Domain Ancestors, and five hundred Supreme Domain Ancestors. All five hundred of these individuals had been promoted through the Sacred Tablet World.
In the Sacred Tablet World, their soul tempering speed was very fast, and their progress was immense. Coupled with a lack of shortage in cultivation resources and the fact that the Sacred Tablet World could amplify their cultivation speed, they were able to promote to Supreme Domain Ancestors one by one.
With 500 Supreme Domain Ancestors, ordinary sects did not even have such a scale.
Some weaker sects only had a few dozen Supreme Domain Ancestors and one or two Invincible Domain Ancestors, and that was it.
One could imagine how great the Human Race's current progress was.
Having grown so much in such a short time, the Human Race now had the capability to completely control the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
On this day.
In the Human Sacred Hall, Ye Tian gathered several hundred Supreme Domain Ancestors and held a Human Race meeting.
One by one, the Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Human Race arrived, each filled with curiosity, not knowing what this meeting would be about.
Boom!!!!! Ye Tian descended into the Sacred Hall, his gaze sweeping over the Supreme Domain Ancestors.
"This meeting is regarding the control of the Profound Void Sacred Realm. The Profound Void Sacred Realm is now our Human Race's territory, but because there weren't many Human Domain Ancestors before, and there were many Domain Ancestors from other factions in the Daoshan Territory, we couldn't completely seal off the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
But now, with a million Human Domain Ancestors, I propose sealing off the Profound Void Sacred Realm, just like those Sacred Lands and Sacred Gates in the Dark Sacred Domain; outsiders are not permitted to enter the headquarters."
Ye Tian proposed.
At this moment, Qinghe Domain Ancestor, one of the Human Race's Supreme Domain Ancestors, spoke up: "Lord Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, once we seal off the Profound Void Sacred Realm, other factions will surely resist. Moreover, the Battle Realm Tower in the Profound Void Sacred Realm is a place of opportunity for many Domain Ancestors and Half-step Domain Ancestors. If we seal the Profound Void Sacred Realm and they cannot enter the Battle Realm Tower, I fear they will unite to suppress our Human Race in the regions outside the Profound Void Sacred Realm!"
Ye Tian smiled and said: "They won't dare to suppress us; they barely have any Supreme Domain Ancestors, so how would they dare suppress our Human Race.
Furthermore, I am not forbidding them from entering the Battle Realm Tower.
We can open a world passage directly to the Battle Realm Void, which will remain a public area.
Except for the Battle Realm Void, all other places will be sealed, and outsiders will not be permitted to enter.
I am doing this for the sake of the Human Race. Otherwise, when treasures are born in the future, other factions will come to snatch them, and it might even attract Domain Ancestors from the Dark Sacred Domain, which would not be a good thing.
If we seal the Profound Void Sacred Realm, other Domain Ancestors will have no way to perceive what treasures have been born here. Even if an astounding opportunity appears, it will belong only to our Human Race!"
"I agree with Lord Myriad Law Domain Ancestor's suggestion!"
"I agree as well!"
"Agreed!"
Many Supreme Domain Ancestors were in full agreement. Many opportunities originally belonged to the Human Race, only to be snatched away by Domain Ancestors of other factions, which had left them quite frustrated.
Previously, it was because the Human Race's strength was weak and they had no choice.
But now, the Domain Ancestors of the Dark Sacred Domain were almost all gone, and those remaining were mostly weaklings. Therefore, they had the strength to seal off the Profound Void Sacred Realm and monopolize the various future opportunities of the realm.
With the majority of the Domain Ancestors in agreement, Ye Tian issued the order.
At the same time, Ye Tian began to utilize the power of the Profound Void Sacred Realm to construct massive arrays in various parts of the realm to seal it off. Even a Supreme Domain Ancestor would not be able to easily enter the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
Soon, many Domain Ancestors from the Daoshan Territory began to send messages to Ye Tian.
"Alliance Leader, why are we no longer allowed to enter the Profound Void Sacred Realm?"
"Alliance Leader, why are you driving us Domain Ancestors out of the Profound Void Sacred Realm?"
"Alliance Leader, such actions by the Human Race are a bit excessive!"
....Regarding the messages from these Domain Ancestors, Ye Tian had only one sentence: This is my command! No matter how dissatisfied or angry those Domain Ancestors were, Ye Tian continued to execute the plan.
There was no other way; others did not know the situation of the Profound Void Sacred Realm, but Ye Tian knew it very well.
The Profound Void Sacred Realm was constantly growing, and it was feared that even more opportunities would be born in the future. If the realm were not sealed, this secret would likely leak out.
Originally, the Sacred Tablet was the greatest opportunity of a Sacred Realm, but for the Profound Void Sacred Realm, the Sacred Tablet was definitely not its greatest opportunity.
Therefore, taking advantage of the departure of the Dark Sacred Domain's Domain Ancestors, he quickly sealed the Profound Void Sacred Realm so that opportunities would not leak out. Even if more great opportunities were born, it would remain an internal matter for the Human Race.
As time slowly passed, after Ye Tian had completely sealed the Profound Void Sacred Realm, other Domain Ancestors were unable to enter.
At first, they made a fuss, but they gradually came to accept it.
Additionally, there were some opportunities in the Daoshan Territory itself, and even quite a few natural domain worlds and precious treasure lands were born, which also attracted the attention of these Domain Ancestors.
In this way, another few million years passed.
On this day.
A message came from the Battle Gate that made Ye Tian extremely excited.
