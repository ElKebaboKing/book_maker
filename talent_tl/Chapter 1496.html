Chapter 1495: Wiping Out the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone, Occupying a Gate
!
Ye Tian had obtained a considerable amount of intelligence from the mouth of the Hundred Souls Ancestor, though the Hundred Souls Ancestor feigned ignorance regarding certain details.
Ye Tian was well aware that the Hundred Souls Ancestor certainly hadn't revealed everything. Only one piece of information was truly useful to Ye Tian: the intelligence regarding the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone.
Perhaps to the Hundred Souls Ancestor, this information was of little use, but to Ye Tian, it was incredibly valuable.
The Sea-Sky Holy Sect was weak, and consequently, the strength of the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone was also weak.
He could completely wipe out the strange beasts in the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone, then use his Duplication Talent to copy one of them. Through that... beast, he could enter the world where the Sea-Sky Holy Sect was located, thereby entering another realm to seek the secrets of breaking through.
This method was not applicable to the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone because the Bone Race was too powerful; they could dispatch a Bone Emperor to descend at any moment.
If he dared to duplicate a fallen Bone King, he would definitely be discovered.
However, the strange beasts within the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone were different. Their numbers were very sparse. If he charged to the other side of the gate, slaughtered those beasts, and wiped them out in a short period, the Sea-Sky Holy Sect wouldn't be able to react in time.
"Although the seawater of the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone will restrict a portion of my strength, I am too powerful. Suppressing a fraction of it is nothing."
With this thought, Ye Tian sent his duplicated body toward the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone.
Swish! In an instant, Ye Tian arrived outside the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone.
Then, he stepped into the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone and was enveloped by countless gallons of seawater. This water constantly eroded his physical body and suppressed about thirty percent of his strength.
A mere thirty percent suppression was nothing; he remained invincible among top-tier Ancestors.
"An outside Ancestor has broken in, kill him!"
The strange beasts of the Sea-Sky Holy Sect rushed over.
These beasts came in all sorts of bizarre shapes, but every one of them was massive in size.
From the aura on their bodies, Ye Tian roughly judged their strength. Most were at the level of ordinary Ancestors, with a few elite Ancestors, and only five comparable to top-tier Ancestors—even weaker than the strength the Hundred Souls Ancestor had disclosed.
Ye Tian did not reveal his full strength immediately. Otherwise, if these beasts became terrified, they might immediately send for reinforcements, which would make it easy for him to be exposed.
Therefore, he initially displayed very weak strength, barely reaching the level of a top-tier Ancestor.
Consequently, Ye Tian allowed himself to be "injured" by their attacks on purpose, while constantly drawing closer to the location of the gate during the battle.
The Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone was not as vast as the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone. From this, it was evident that the Sea-Sky Holy Sect's strength was inferior to the Bone Race, which was why the forbidden zone they opened in the Dark Forbidden Domain was not large.
These strange beasts were completely unaware of Ye Tian's objective; they had no idea his target was the gate.
"There are two beasts stationed at the edge of the gate. One... beast is comparable to an elite Ancestor, and the other's strength is likely on par with a top-tier Ancestor, but not a powerful one—just an average top-tier Ancestor. Against such a top-tier Ancestor, even if my strength is suppressed, I can kill them in just a few moves."
Ye Tian thought to himself:.
"It's about time!"
Ye Tian suddenly erupted with full power. Layers of Ultimate Defensive Ancestral Techniques were displayed, and the attacks of those strange beasts were all blocked, unable to harm Ye Tian in the slightest.
Several beasts were instantly blasted into blood mist, perishing on the spot, allowing Ye Tian to charge out directly toward the gate.
At this moment, those beasts finally realized that Ye Tian was a pig eating a tiger, intentionally disguising his strength, and that his target was their gateway.
"Quickly, go for help! This Ancestor wants to destroy... the gate!"
The beasts roared one after another.
The two beasts guarding the gate immediately understood and prepared to traverse the gate to seek reinforcements.
However, they were too late.
At that moment, Ye Tian performed an Ultimate Ancestral Technique—the Eternal Ultimate Ancestral Technique. This was a Time Ultimate Ancestral Technique, which, combined with the Dream Butterfly Ultimate Ancestral Technique, directly froze time and space.
If these two beasts' strength were comparable to Ye Tian's, they would certainly have the power to break through the space-time freeze, but their strength was lacking. Thus, they were directly pinned down. They might have been able to break the space-time lock, but it would require a bit of time.
And Ye Tian would not give them that time.
At this moment, Ye Tian rushed over at maximum speed. With the boost of two speed-type Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, he charged directly to the gateway, where no one could stop him.
Upon reaching the gate, Ye Tian crushed the beast comparable to an elite Ancestor with one hand, killing it in a single blow.
Then.
Ye Tian sent the other beast flying with a single punch.
He stood right there at the gate, not allowing any beast to pass through.
This place belonged to him now! "Damn it, he doesn't want to destroy the gate; he wants to occupy our gate! Charge through with everything you've got!"
The strange beasts spared no expense to perform forbidden arts, lunging toward Ye Tian one after another.
And at this time.
Ye Tian's main body arrived and stepped into the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone.
If it were the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, Ye Tian's main body would not dare to enter, but he dared to enter the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone.
The gate could not transmit messages, so the events happening here could not pass through. Under normal circumstances, the Sea-Sky Holy Sect on the other side of the gate would not send beasts over, so they wouldn't know immediately.
Therefore, it was very safe for his main body to enter here.
Moreover.
With his current strength, even if a beast comparable to the Strongest Ancestor truly descended, he could easily leave.
As Ye Tian's main body entered the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone, he coordinated with his duplicated body to begin a massacre of these beasts.
For those weaker beasts, Ye Tian killed almost one with every strike.
In a short while, only five beasts remained.
The strength of these five beasts was comparable to top-tier Ancestors, but they were still no match for Ye Tian. The strongest among them was comparable to a very powerful top-tier Ancestor, yet its strength was still much weaker than Ye Tian's.
Soon, the other four beasts had been killed by Ye Tian, leaving only this last powerful beast.
This beast had many life-saving methods and was quite powerful, so it managed to hold out for a long time.
But in the end, this beast was still killed by Ye Tian.
Of course, Ye Tian's consumption was also quite high.
It had to be said that if the strength of the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone had been any greater, it would have been much more difficult for him to wipe out these beasts.
"The beasts of the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone have been completely wiped out by me. The Sea-Sky Holy Sect in the other world will definitely not be able to sense their deaths."
Ye Tian murmured.
He dispelled the duplicated body and then accelerated time.
Soon, the duplication ability could be used again.
This time, Ye Tian duplicated that strongest beast.
Boom!!!! A massive beast, resembling an ape but possessing six arms, appeared.
Compared to the previous beast, the one Ye Tian duplicated was identical, even down to the soul.
Back then, the Bone Race members Ye Tian duplicated were recognized by the origin of the Dark Forbidden Domain as genuine Bone Race members, which was why they had obliterated the duplicated bodies.
Clearly, even the origin of the Dark Forbidden Domain could not distinguish the truth of Ye Tian's duplicated bodies.
Therefore, this duplicated body could definitely enter the gate without being recognized.
Afterward, Ye Tian plundered all the resources of the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone, creating the illusion that the zone's resources had been raided.
Next, Ye Tian acted to heavily injure the duplicated body, putting it in a state of being near death. Otherwise, if it had no injuries at all while passing through the gateway, it would be suspected by the Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
This duplicated body not only had a heavily damaged physical body, but even its soul was in a state on the verge of collapsing, though it wouldn't actually shatter.
"That should be about enough!"
Ye Tian thought to himself:.
