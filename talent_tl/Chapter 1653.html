Chapter 1652: Eight Extremes Tower!

“The effect is pretty good!”
Ye Tian was very satisfied with his cultivation on True Intent Mountain. His willpower was slowly improving. Although the improvement was very slight, once it was combined with the secret art known as Will World, it became quite good.
Occasionally, Ye Tian’s clone would cultivate together with the copied body, beginning to cultivate Will World and gradually perfecting this secret art.
Of course, the clone was still in Source Sea, not on True Intent Mountain.
After all, there were many powerful beings on True Intent Mountain, and if he condensed a clone there, it would surely draw their attention.
So Ye Tian had the clone stay in the Dark Forbidden Domain, where it could also assist the copied body in cultivating Will World.
As time passed, the will suppression Ye Tian endured grew stronger and stronger.
Although Ye Tian could cultivate here for an eternal year, others could as well, but no one would last until the end of an eternal year.
After some eon years had passed, Ye Tian felt that his will had improved quite a bit, so he let his clone begin trying to open up Will Worlds.
At first, the Will Worlds naturally failed, but after many attempts, Ye Tian still found the correct place to open up the Will Worlds.
In this way, the true body and the copied body began opening up Will Worlds.
Just like that, the number of Ye Tian’s Will Worlds kept increasing.
Before he knew it, Ye Tian had already cultivated on True Intent Mountain for a full several hundred thousand eon years.
At this time, even True Yang Sacred Ancestor was shocked.
“He actually cultivated at the summit of True Intent Mountain for so long!!!!”
True Yang Sacred Ancestor felt that even he could not cultivate at the summit of True Intent Mountain for such a long time. Afterward, he simply would not be able to withstand the will suppression of True Intent Mountain; if he forcibly remained there, his will would run into problems.
Yet now Ye Tian had stayed there for so long, and there did not seem to be any problem at all.
It was obvious that Ye Tian’s willpower was much stronger than his. After long years of bitter cultivation, his own will had only barely stepped into second-tier eternal will.
But compared with Ye Tian now, the gap was enormous.
“I’m afraid he’s not even as good as some eternal realm wills!”
True Yang Sacred Ancestor guessed.
Another several hundred thousand eon years passed.
And Ye Tian had already bitterly cultivated here for an eternal year, with his will greatly improved.
By now, he had opened up nearly a thousand Will Worlds in total during this period.
At present, his Will Worlds had reached 6277.
However, toward the later stages, opening up each Will World became more difficult. Even if he cultivated on True Intent Mountain for endless years in the future, he still would not be able to raise the number of Will Worlds to 10000. He would probably need a better place for tempering the will before there would be hope of surpassing 10000.
Of course, Ye Tian was already very satisfied with this bitter cultivation.
The improvement of his will had increased Ye Tian’s strength quite a bit.
Moreover, over such a long span of years, Ye Tian’s true body had not been doing nothing; it had been bitterly cultivating the Wheel Disc secret art and compressing his half-step eternal power.
In addition, aside from testing the cultivation of Will World, Ye Tian’s clone had also spent the rest of its time creating eternal compression secret arts.
He was not creating them out of thin air, but using the Wheel Disc secret art as the core, absorbing the essence of other eternal compression secret arts, and gradually perfecting and improving the Wheel Disc secret art.
By now, the effect of the Wheel Disc secret art had improved greatly, and he had perfected it to the extent of 33 times.
In other words, the Wheel Disc secret art could now compress half-step eternal power by up to 33 times, making its effect far stronger than before.
Under the new Wheel Disc secret art, Ye Tian successfully compressed his half-step eternal power eightfold, which was already a very good degree of compression.
Eightfold compression increased the number by nearly a thousand Will Worlds, directly raising Ye Tian’s strength by a great deal.
By now, Ye Tian could already sense the existence of the first boundary barrier, but he still had not truly touched the first world barrier.
He felt that as long as he compressed his half-step eternal power to ten times, he would reach the first boundary barrier and thus gain the qualification to charge at the first boundary barrier.
“It’s time to go out!”
Ye Tian stood up and flew out of True Intent Mountain.
Just after flying out of True Intent Mountain, a figure blocked Ye Tian’s way.
“A strong expert, a true strong expert!”
Ye Tian sensed danger; the other party was much stronger than him, and he was no match.
Combined with the situation investigated by his copy talent, he immediately knew who the person before him was.
The master of the Shaoyang territory, a half-step eternal realm expert of the Eight Extremes Realm—True Yang Sacred Ancestor.
“Greetings, True Yang Sacred Ancestor!”
Ye Tian said politely.
True Yang Sacred Ancestor was not surprised that Ye Tian could guess his identity, and instead invited him, “Does Your Excellency have any interest in coming to my True Yang Palace for a sit-down?”
“It would be my honor!”
Ye Tian smiled and said.
Immediately after, Ye Tian followed True Yang Sacred Ancestor to True Yang Palace, which was True Yang Sacred Ancestor’s residence and place of cultivation.
“May I ask how Your Excellency is addressed?”
True Yang Sacred Ancestor asked.
“Endless Ancestor!”
Ye Tian introduced himself.
“Endless Ancestor, I see that you are probably close to reaching the half-step eternal realm of the Eight Extremes Realm, right?” True Yang Sacred Ancestor said.
“I’m still far from it!” Ye Tian shook his head. “From Seven Extremes Realm to Eight Extremes Realm, one must break through the first boundary barrier. I may not necessarily be able to break it.”
“Hahaha!!” True Yang Sacred Ancestor laughed and said, “With your realm of second-tier eternal will, Endless Ancestor, breaking the first boundary barrier should naturally be very easy.”
“Second-tier eternal will can help break the first boundary barrier?”
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised; he truly did not know this.
“Of course it can!” True Yang Sacred Ancestor nodded. “It’s just that in Extreme realm, very few people raise their will to second-tier eternal will before breaking the first boundary barrier. I barely reached second-tier eternal will by relying on breaking the first boundary barrier, which greatly increased my willpower, and then cultivating bitterly on True Intent Mountain for countless years. Ordinary half-step eternal realm experts of the Eight Extremes Realm still have will at the first tier!”
Upon hearing this, Ye Tian was instantly extremely excited.
So that meant that as long as he truly touched the level of the first boundary barrier and charged at it a little, he would have a great chance of breaking through the first boundary barrier and stepping into the half-step eternal realm of the Eight Extremes Realm?
He also finally understood why True Yang Sacred Ancestor had invited him as a guest; it turned out he was being regarded as a future half-step eternal realm expert of the Eight Extremes Realm.
Afterward, Ye Tian chatted with True Yang Sacred Ancestor and learned many secrets, greatly broadening his knowledge.
When True Yang Sacred Ancestor learned that Ye Tian had joined the Doomsday Tower, he could not help but say, “Endless Ancestor, there’s really no need to join the Doomsday Tower. In fact, even those few Eight Extremes Realm experts in the Doomsday Tower don’t really manage the Doomsday Tower. Although the Doomsday Tower is a loose force with little constraint, it simply cannot bring you any benefits. I suggest you join the Eight Extremes Tower instead. The Eight Extremes Tower is also a loose force, with extremely high entry requirements. Only half-step eternal realm experts of the Eight Extremes Realm are qualified to join, and its influence is very great. Even many eternal realm experts do not dare offend the Eight Extremes Tower. Moreover, the eternal realm experts who came out of the Eight Extremes Tower are all true powerhouses among eternal realm experts, and from time to time they will bestow some opportunities upon the Eight Extremes Tower. It can be said that more than half of the Eight Extremes Realm experts who did not pledge themselves to eternal realm experts have joined the Eight Extremes Tower, and those few Eight Extremes Realm experts in your Doomsday Tower are also members of the Eight Extremes Tower.”
After weighing things up, Ye Tian decided to join the Eight Extremes Tower.
But that would have to be after he truly stepped into the half-step eternal realm of the Eight Extremes Realm, not now.
