Chapter 1634 The Thirteenth Ancestor of the Source God Pavilion!

“With my current strength, although I’m no match for the six Extreme experts among the twelve ancestors, I should be able to defeat the other six.”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He wanted to become the thirteenth ancestor of the Source God Pavilion.
Although the twelve ancestors had disappointed him before, refusing to deal with the Star Demon Source Ancestor, thinking about it carefully, that was actually normal. The twelve ancestors themselves were not as strong as the Star Demon Source Ancestor. If they dared to attack the Star Demon Source Ancestor, it would be no different from courting death. Even if they joined forces with other Extreme experts to besiege the Star Demon Source Ancestor, the final outcome would still be a miserable defeat.
After all, even someone like the Ice Ancestor of Ultimate Darkness, a Half-step Eternal Realm expert at the Seven Extreme Stage, was killed by the Star Demon Source Ancestor. If not for the rescue of Leader Lian Yi, she would have been killed by the Star Demon Source Ancestor’s disciple, Leader Ling He.
It was easy to see that, without Eternal Realm backing, forcing oneself into the spotlight would only mean death.
Therefore, the Source God Pavilion’s twelve ancestors made the right choice.
Perhaps to other forces, this seemed selfish or even cruel, but reality was just like that.
If Ye Tian were to choose, and if he were not strong enough, he would not step forward either.
Besides, becoming the thirteenth ancestor of the Source God Pavilion would bring many benefits. He could learn more secrets, and it might even greatly help him in his assault on the Eternal Realm.
……
Source God Pavilion.
Ye Tian came here and applied to the twelve ancestors to become the thirteenth ancestor.
In fact, some of the Half-step Eternal Realm members within the inner circle of the Source God Pavilion also wanted to become the thirteenth ancestor, but they all failed one after another. And once they failed, they would not be able to apply for this assessment again for a very long time.
Very soon, all twelve ancestors received Ye Tian’s application information.
“The Endless Ancestor wants to apply to become the thirteenth ancestor?”
True River Sword Ancestor and the others were extremely shocked.
What realm was the Endless Ancestor in, to actually dare apply for the thirteenth ancestor?
“Should we reject him? What realm is he even in? This is simply absurd!” Wind Ancient Source Ancestor shook his head and said.
“Any inner circle member has the qualification to apply to become the thirteenth ancestor, and we have no requirement for cultivation level. We only require strength and background. As long as it is not a force with huge conflict with the Source God Pavilion, we accept them! And the Endless Ancestor meets the standards. We have no reason to reject him.” True River Sword Ancestor said.
“Then agree to it. It won’t take much time anyway.” Wind Ancient Source Ancestor also agreed.
With all twelve ancestors in agreement, the assessment for the thirteenth ancestor was opened.
The assessment was very simple: Ye Tian would choose three of the twelve ancestors, and as long as he could hold out for one hour under any one of the twelve ancestors without being obviously at a disadvantage, he would qualify to become the thirteenth ancestor.
Boom!!!!
Within a void space inside the Source God Pavilion.
Ye Tian and the twelve ancestors appeared one after another.
“Endless Ancestor, are you really going to take the thirteenth ancestor assessment?” True River Sword Ancestor asked.
“Yes!”
Ye Tian nodded.
“Then choose three of the twelve ancestors!” True River Sword Ancestor said.
Ye Tian swept his gaze over the twelve ancestors and chose, “I choose Golden Chen Source Ancestor, Ten-Eyed Source Ancestor, and Eight-Faced Source Ancestor!”
The three Half-step Eternal Realm experts he chose were all not Extreme experts. Among the twelve ancestors, they were three of the weaker Source Ancestors, though all still possessed top-tier Half-step Eternal Realm strength, just not too far from the Extreme level.
True River Sword Ancestor and the others were not surprised by Ye Tian’s choice at all. If Ye Tian had chosen Extreme experts to challenge, that would have been strange.
The first to make a move was Golden Chen Source Ancestor. As soon as Golden Chen Source Ancestor attacked, he displayed incomparably terrifying strength, but he did not go all out, fearing that if he killed Ye Tian in one move, that would be far too disrespectful.
But very quickly, his expression changed.
Because Ye Tian’s strength was not weak; he actually took his punch with ease.
“Endless Ancestor, I won’t hold back anymore!”
Golden Chen Source Ancestor unleashed one Eternal technique after another, each strike erupting with his strongest offensive power.
Rumble rumble rumble!!!!
It was as if endless fists had appeared in the infinite void, each one seemingly capable of shattering endless spacetime.
Ye Tian also used one defensive Eternal technique after another to block Golden Chen Source Ancestor’s attacks, and from time to time he would also strike back at Golden Chen Source Ancestor.
But only after exchanging blows with Golden Chen Source Ancestor did Ye Tian truly realize that there was indeed a difference between top-tier Half-step Eternal Realm and top-tier Half-step Eternal Realm. His normal combat strength still was not Golden Chen Source Ancestor’s match.
As one of the Source God Pavilion’s twelve ancestors, naturally none of them were weak.
If he did not use his trump card, he simply could not become the thirteenth ancestor.
With a booming sound!
Ye Tian was sent flying, and Golden Chen Source Ancestor’s voice rang out: “Endless Ancestor, your strength is pretty good. You have reached the level of an ordinary top-tier Half-step Eternal Realm expert, but even so, you still cannot become the thirteenth ancestor. Come challenge again when your cultivation has reached the Ninth Supreme Being Extreme!”
“Golden Chen Source Ancestor, you are indeed very strong, but unfortunately this is not my full strength!”
As Ye Tian spoke, he used the Destruction Finger. .........
Boom!!!!
The aura of the Eternal Realm spread out from Ye Tian’s finger.
“An Eternal Realm aura!” True River Sword Ancestor and the others sensed it too.
In the next instant.
The Destruction Finger was unleashed, carrying the power of an Eternal Realm finger as it erupted forth. With one finger, it crushed the Eternal rules, and with inconceivable force it shattered Golden Chen Source Ancestor’s defense, directly pressing a small hole into Golden Chen Source Ancestor’s body.
Golden Chen Source Ancestor was injured!
“Stop, the assessment is over!”
True River Sword Ancestor shouted.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian stopped attacking and said, “Twelve ancestors, does that mean I’ve passed the assessment?”
“Passed!”
True River Sword Ancestor nodded, but he was still curious about the Eternal Realm aura Ye Tian had released: “Endless Ancestor, may I ask what that Eternal Realm aura from your finger just now was? Was it some kind of special treasure?”
Ye Tian explained, “I fused with a finger segment from an Eternal Realm expert, allowing me to unleash a sliver of Eternal Realm power and erupt with formidable strength, but it is no longer of much help to me now!”
As he spoke, Ye Tian summoned that finger segment.
“It really is a finger segment from an Eternal Realm expert!”
True River Sword Ancestor and the other twelve ancestors were all extremely shocked.
A Ninth Supreme Being dared to fuse with a finger segment from an Eternal Realm expert—his courage was simply too great.
If they knew that Ye Tian had successfully fused with a finger segment from an Eternal Realm expert while only at the Second Supreme Being realm, they would probably be completely dumbfounded.
Although this finger segment was not Ye Tian’s, he had already successfully fused with it and could exert its power, so naturally it counted as part of Ye Tian’s combat strength.
Borrowing outside objects was also strength, and the twelve ancestors of the Source God Pavilion recognized that as well.
At this moment, True River Sword Ancestor announced, “Endless Ancestor, from today onward, you are the thirteenth ancestor of the Source God Pavilion. We will announce it to the public.”
“Mm!”
Ye Tian nodded.
