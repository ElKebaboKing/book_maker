Chapter 1542: Nine Yang Fish, Three Eternal Rules!

"Since I've joined the Tianfeng Sea, I naturally intend to use their Rule Heavenly Objects to comprehend the rules of the Eternal Heaven and Earth. It would be foolish not to take advantage of such conditions, wouldn't it?"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Therefore, he would not choose to rely on endless years to slowly comprehend them; that was merely a desperate measure taken by ordinary Invincibles.
Of course, even as a high-level member of the Tianfeng Sea with the qualifications to comprehend their Rule Heavenly Objects, the required contribution points were not small.
Even for the most ordinary Rule Heavenly Object, comprehending it for one Realm Year required one contribution point. One Realm Epoch was equivalent to 120,000 contribution points, and comprehending for one Dark Era would require over 10 million points.
Thus, wanting to comprehend a Rule Heavenly Object for a long period was a luxury; many Invincibles simply did not have that many contribution points.
Ye Tian's accumulated contribution points had reached several hundred thousand, and he had hardly spent any of his current points. After all, purchasing several hundred Extreme Grade Tribulation Stones didn't cost much—only a few tens of thousands of points.
Additionally, he had purchased some other treasures for his family, such as exchanging for Emperor Blood Souls and other items, which cost a bit more.
But now, he still had 260,000 contribution points remaining.
This amount was enough for him to comprehend for a long time, perhaps even enough to grasp an Eternal Heaven and Earth Rule in one go, thereby creating an Invincible Ancestral Technique and stepping into the rank of a Fifth-tier Invincible.
-------------380-
Tianlin Realm.
Ye Tian's duplicated body arrived here once again and proceeded to the headquarters of the Tianfeng Sea—Wufeng Mountain.
Of course, given Ye Tian's status, he could only enter the outer perimeter of Wufeng Mountain. To enter the inner perimeter, one needed at least the status of an Elite member.
At this time, Ye Tian arrived at Nine Yang Mountain within Wufeng Mountain, where there was a Rule Heavenly Object known as the Nine Yang Fish.
The Nine Yang Fish contained three Eternal Heaven and Earth Rules: Power, Defense, and Speed. These were the most fundamental yet most important rules. Consequently, many Invincibles came to comprehend this specific Rule Heavenly Object, and it was also the most suitable for Ye Tian.
The cost to comprehend the Nine Yang Fish was higher than that of ordinary Rule Heavenly Objects; one Realm Year cost three contribution points.
On Nine Yang Mountain.
Ye Tian stood before a Third-order Gate. After showing his badge to the Invincible guarding the area, he was allowed to enter.
Strictly speaking, the Nine Yang Fish was not on Nine Yang Mountain itself, but in a special space-time opposite it. One had to pass through the Third-order Gate to travel to that space-time to comprehend the Nine Yang Fish; otherwise, it was impossible to enter.
The Tianfeng Sea placed great importance on every Rule Heavenly Object. For instance, near the Third-order Gate, Ye Tian sensed many Invincibles, several of whom possessed auras far beyond his imagination—they could definitely kill him in an instant.
*Whoosh!*
Ye Tian passed through the Third-order Gate and arrived in a special space-time.
Here, numerous lotus platforms floated in the void. Some platforms were already occupied by Invincibles sitting cross-legged, comprehending the Eternal Rules.
In the center of this space-time, a massive black fish spanning hundreds of millions of miles was writhing, but its body was bound by chains, preventing it from leaving.
"This is the Nine Yang Fish?"
Ye Tian was surprised.
From its appearance, this Rule Heavenly Object did not seem like a dead object, but rather a true living being.
However, Ye Tian used his Copy Talent to investigate and found no talents at all, proving that this giant black fish was not a living creature.
In reality, when most Rule Heavenly Objects have existed for a long time, their rules manifest into forms, often taking the shape of living creatures. However, they remain Rule Heavenly Objects—treasures, not lifeforms.
The Nine Yang Fish was sealed. It was impossible to comprehend the Eternal Rules it carried from elsewhere; one had to sit on a lotus platform and establish a connection with the Nine Yang Fish through the platform to comprehend the rules on its body.
Ye Tian chose an empty lotus platform, sat down, and immediately extended his consciousness through the platform to the Nine Yang Fish.
*Boom!!!*
Ye Tian's consciousness observed the entire Nine Yang Fish. In his perception, the fish was like the incarnation of rules—Eternal Rules that far surpassed the rules of the Alien Realms. The so-called "Great Daos" of the Alien Realms were like child's play here, not even comparing to a single shred of these rules.
Power, Speed, Defense.
As Ye Tian perceived these three Eternal Rules, he truly felt the vastness of the Eternal Rules.
Between the three, Ye Tian unhesitatingly chose the Power Rule first. Speed and Defense could increase combat power to an extent, but they were more for survival. Ye Tian didn't need to worry about survival as much; he usually fought with his duplicated bodies. Even if a duplicated body perished, it wouldn't have much impact. Therefore, Power was more beneficial to him.
If he could comprehend the Eternal Power Rule, he could attempt to create a Power-type Invincible Ancestral Technique, which would significantly boost his combat strength.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian began to comprehend the Eternal Power Rule.
Compared to other Invincibles, Ye Tian's advantage wasn't particularly large. Although he possessed the Ultimate Power Ancestral Talent, it didn't help much with comprehending the Eternal Power Rule unless he could obtain an Eternal Power Talent, which would allow him to grasp it quickly.
"Perhaps I really do have a chance to obtain the Eternal Power Talent!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
There might not be anyone with an Eternal Power Talent in the Tianlin Realm, but there were certainly Invincibles with the Ultimate Power Ancestral Talent. Although they were extremely rare, if he spent enough time searching, he could definitely find many of them.
If he copied and merged a large number of Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents, he might be able to fuse them into an Eternal Power Talent.
"I'll try comprehending here first and see the results!"
Ye Tian thought.
Years passed, and Ye Tian, like the other Invincibles, continued to comprehend the Eternal Rules, but his progress was not fast.
However, this was quite normal. Even with Rule Heavenly Objects, the Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea still needed a long time to promote to Fifth-tier Invincibles. Many even spent vast amounts of time completing missions just to accumulate contribution points.
For an Invincible, let alone one or two Dark Eras, even the span of a Great Calamity Epoch was considered a very short time. To an Invincible, only a Kalpa Era was considered a relatively long period.
Later, he conversed with other Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea and gained a general understanding of how long it took them to comprehend Eternal Rules.
Generally, even if an Invincible comprehended Rule Heavenly Objects continuously, it would take at least three Dark Eras. Those with exceptionally high comprehension still needed one Dark Era. After all, since they had all reached the rank of Invincible, the gap in their natural comprehension wasn't that massive.
But to comprehend continuously for three Dark Eras, how many contribution points would that require? Very few Invincibles possessed that many points.
Consequently, these Invincibles relied on accumulating points over long periods. Once they gathered some, they would come to comprehend for a while.
Thus, many Invincibles in the Tianfeng Sea practiced for thousands or even tens of thousands of Kalpa Eras before breaking through to the Fifth-tier Invincible realm.
"If I were to comprehend for an entire Dark Era, based on the cost of the Nine Yang Fish, I would need at least 30 million contribution points. I only have 260,000. It seems I was overthinking; a mere 260,000 points won't help me comprehend the Eternal Rules in one go," Ye Tian thought.
Of course.
He could go out and hunt Tribulation Beasts repeatedly to earn massive amounts of contribution points before returning to the Nine Yang Fish, but finding Tribulation Beasts also took time.
Alternatively, he could take on missions that offered large amounts of points at once, but those weren't missions he could complete alone. Unless he had someone in the Tianfeng Sea to carry him, he couldn't earn enough points that way, and it wouldn't necessarily be faster than hunting Tribulation Beasts himself.
"It's more reliable to try merging a large number of Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents into an Eternal Power Talent!"
Ye Tian thought.
