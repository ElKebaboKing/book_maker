Chapter 1654: Divine Sound Domain, the Eternal Body’s Transcendence!

“Where to?”
Ye Tian asked curiously.
“Divine Sound Domain!”
said the True Yang Supreme Being.
Divine Sound Domain?
Ye Tian had never heard of it, but that was normal. After all, there were too many space-time Domains, and many Domains were not famous.
True Yang Supreme Being led Ye Tian through several teleports and arrived at a place called Fire Spirit Domain.
“Invincible Ancestor, let us tear open the space-time barrier of Fire Spirit Domain and enter the Void Region,” said True Yang Supreme Being.
“Why?”
Ye Tian was somewhat puzzled.
True Yang Supreme Being explained, “Because Divine Sound Domain does not have any space-time passage, we cannot enter Divine Sound Domain through a space-time passage. We must fly over from the Void Domain. Divine Sound Domain is a space-time Domain completely under our Eight Extremes Tower, and it is also one of our benefits. Many Eternal Realm experts want to seize this space-time Domain, but they were forcefully suppressed by several Immortal Realm Domains that walked out from our Eight Extremes Tower. Otherwise, a space-time Domain like this would not have fallen to our Eight Extremes Tower.”
Ye Tian was full of curiosity about Divine Sound Domain. Could there be some astonishing chance inside?
However, he did not ask too much. Once he entered Divine Sound Domain, he would know what was going on.
Ye Tian and True Yang Supreme Being tore open the space-time barrier of Fire Spirit Domain and entered the Void Domain.
Once they entered the Void Domain, all laws could no longer be sensed, but the laws within their own bodies could still be used. Their strength was not weakened much, but they could not engage in prolonged battle here, and it was difficult to recover.
Because in the Void Domain, it was very hard to borrow the source power of the Source Sea.
Moreover, the Void Domain was filled with an atmosphere of nothingness—silence, gloom, suffocation, despair. All kinds of negative emotions surged into one’s heart.
An ordinary Supreme Being would probably go mad if they dared enter the Void Domain, but Ye Tian and True Yang Supreme Being had extremely powerful wills, so they did not care about the environment here.
Whoosh!
They flew through the Void Region at extremely high speed. Occasionally, they would see faint strands of light; those were one space-time Domain after another, and in the far distance there was a huge source of light. However, the light from that source could not extend very far. If not for Ye Tian’s powerful strength, he would also not have been able to sense the existence of that huge source of light.
That huge source of light was the Source Sea!
Very soon, after they reached the position of a faint source of light, their senses gradually became infinitely vast, and they sensed a space-time Domain—Divine Sound Domain.
“Let’s go in!”
True Yang Supreme Being casually tore open the space-time barrier of Divine Sound Domain and entered Divine Sound Domain.
Ye Tian followed him in as well.
The moment they entered Divine Sound Domain, the power of dozens of wills crossed through the void and peered at their arrival.
“These are members of the other Eight Extremes Tower branches; no need to mind them!”
True Yang Supreme Being introduced.
Ye Tian nodded. He did not sense any malice, so he did not care either.
In a flash, those wills all withdrew.
At this moment.
Ye Tian was feeling the differences of Divine Sound Domain. This Domain was filled with a strange sound, a sound that seemed like muttering in sleep and yet also like some kind of song, an abstruse sound formed entirely from the laws of sound.
Ye Tian sat cross-legged, sensing the profundity of this sound.
The strange power transformed from the sound seeped into his body, as though it were difficult to block this sound’s invasion.
Very quickly, he felt somewhat uncomfortable.
At first, he had wanted to resist this power and repair his hidden injuries.
But then, he abruptly realized something was wrong, and his expression immediately turned to surprise.
“Hahaha, Invincible Ancestor, have you discovered the benefits of Divine Sound Domain?” True Yang Supreme Being laughed.
Ye Tian nodded and said, “Divine Sound Domain can let us detect the flaws of the Eternal Body. No, that should not be called flaws, but rather it lets us detect the more perfect aspects. Moreover, the sound of Divine Sound Domain will stimulate our Eternal Body. Under this kind of stimulation and confrontation, we can gradually perfect the Eternal Body, and even find a path of transcendence.”
“Correct!” True Yang Supreme Being said. “Divine Sound Domain is a blessed land that can allow the Eternal Body to complete transcendence. Even an Eternal Body that has already transcended can become somewhat more perfected and stronger here, which is why many members of the Eight Extremes Tower treat this place as a cultivation ground. And your Eternal Body, Invincible Ancestor, has not yet transcended. This place is perfectly suited for your cultivation. Compared with the other members of the Eight Extremes Tower, only you can truly obtain a breakthrough here.”
“Thank you, True Yang Supreme Being, for bringing me here!”
Ye Tian said gratefully.
True Yang Supreme Being waved his hand and said, “No need to thank me. This is a place every member of the Eight Extremes Tower can come to. Even if I didn’t bring you here, it wouldn’t be long before you learned of this place. .........Next, find a place in Divine Sound Domain to cultivate. I hope you can raise your Eternal Body to the transcendent realm as soon as possible.”
“I also hope to break through as soon as possible!”
Ye Tian said expectantly.
Very soon, True Yang Supreme Being left this place. After all, True Yang Supreme Being’s Eternal Body had already transcended, and he did not know how many years he had cultivated here, so this place was of no help to him anymore.
After True Yang Supreme Being left, Ye Tian casually found a mountain peak, placed a palace there, and began bitter cultivation inside the palace.
If the copied body were to raise the Eternal Body to the transcendent realm, then the main body would be able to copy it soon after and also cultivate the Eternal Body to the transcendent realm.
As the Divine Sound of Divine Sound Domain entered his body, the flaws existing in Ye Tian’s boundless Eternal Body were revealed one by one.
Perhaps in Ye Tian’s eyes, these were not flaws, but manifestations that were perfect to the extreme. However, in the process of resisting the Divine Sound, if these places were not perfect, then they were simply not acceptable.
This was also why the transcendent realm existed.
Transcendence meant transcending perfection and forging a more perfect Eternal Body; that was the transcendent realm.
As time passed, Ye Tian gradually comprehended the transcendent meaning of the boundless Eternal Body, but to truly complete transcendence would probably require a very long period of time. It was not something that could be finished in just a little while.
At the same time.
Ye Tian’s main body was also continuously compressing the half-step Eternal power and perfecting the layered secret technique.
Unknowingly, ten Eternal years passed.
For Ye Tian, ten Eternal years was also an extremely long period of time.
On this day, with the help of Divine Sound Domain, Ye Tian finally raised the boundless Eternal Body to the transcendent realm.
The moment the boundless Eternal Body broke through to the transcendent realm, the body’s power surged many times over, and Ye Tian’s strength also surged.
Moreover, during these ten Eternal years, Ye Tian perfected the layered secret technique to the extent of sixty-five times, and compressed the half-step Eternal power to twenty times.
Now, his overall strength was much stronger than before, and he was no longer comparable to a newly advanced Eight Extremes Realm expert.
