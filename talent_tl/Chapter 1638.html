Chapter 1637: The Will Impact of Rift Wind Valley!

Ye Tian possessed the map of the Seamless Domain, and his own strength was not weak either. Moreover, there were also some half-step Eternal Realm experts heading to Rift Wind Valley, so the pressure on him was not very great.
Very quickly, he broke out of the encirclement of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons and arrived at Rift Wind Valley.
As soon as he entered Rift Wind Valley, a gust of wind blew over.
The Space-Time Dragon Pythons did not dare approach Rift Wind Valley one by one. They all retreated, or lingered outside Rift Wind Valley, but simply refused to go in.
Ye Tian also sensed the strangeness of Rift Wind Valley.
Boom!!!!!!
Waves of gentle breeze swept over and blew onto Ye Tian's will, as if his will were enduring endless pounding. This feeling was extremely painful.
Moreover, the longer the gentle breeze blew, the stronger the impact on his will became.
This was only the outer area of Rift Wind Valley. If it were the inner area, the impact would probably be even more severe.
Inside Rift Wind Valley, one had to withstand will impact; outside Rift Wind Valley, one had to endure the siege of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons.
Therefore, no one dared to stay here for too long. Otherwise, their will would be severely injured, their strength would greatly decline, and they might even find it difficult to resist the attacks of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons.
Ye Tian walked step by step toward the depths of Rift Wind Valley, feeling the will impact brought by the gentle breeze, and actually quietly enjoyed it.
“Compared with other Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts, I have hardly encountered any major setbacks. Although my willpower is not weak, compared with those half-step Eternal Realm experts, it is still somewhat lacking. Perhaps Rift Wind Valley can make up for the deficiency in my will!” Ye Tian muttered.
Very quickly, he arrived at a relatively suitable location, sat down cross-legged, and silently endured the impact on his will.
At the same time, he let the copied body begin copying out another body to serve as a clone.
Whoosh!
Another Ye Tian appeared.
Ye Tian let the other Ye Tian go to Seamless Mountain. He also did not want to waste the opportunities in Seamless Mountain, so one copied body tempered his will in Rift Wind Valley while one clone went to seek opportunities, without delaying anything at all.
Whoosh!
The clone moved, breaking out of Rift Wind Valley, and then entering the encirclement of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons.
Although Ye Tian's strength was not very great, in terms of life-saving ability, he was even more formidable than Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others.
After becoming a nine-realm Supreme Being, his life-saving ability was almost comparable to Star Ancestor's, perhaps even a bit stronger. There were too few half-step Eternal Realm existences who could kill him.
Back then, Star Ancestor was sealed by a half-step Eternal Realm expert, and that half-step Eternal Realm expert was also one of the top half-step Eternal Realm experts, not far from the Extreme realm. No matter what method the other side used, they could not kill Star Ancestor, and could only seal him.
Therefore, even if ten Extreme experts surrounded and killed Ye Tian together, it would still be very difficult to kill him.
Unless a seven-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm expert like Wuji Ice Ancestor made a move against him, there would be hope of killing him, and it would take quite some time.
So Ye Tian was not afraid at all of being surrounded and killed by the Space-Time Dragon Pythons.
After a round of fighting, Ye Tian finally reached Seamless Mountain.
Seamless Mountain was very large. Though called a mountain, it was actually a mountain range. Many blessed lands and buildings stood here.
Ye Tian did not hesitate at all and directly rushed toward one of the mountain peaks.
The Twelve Ancestors had revealed one piece of information: there was a place on Seamless Mountain that was extremely suitable for nine-realm Supreme Beings, where one could rapidly increase one's cultivation.
This was East Return Mountain on Seamless Mountain. On East Return Mountain, there was a special array that could combine the power of this space-time territory and convert various energies into a special kind of energy.
This energy was called Seamless Liquid. Seamless Liquid could not be preserved in the outside world for very long, so it could not be taken away and had to be refined promptly.
Seamless Liquid was likely a special energy once developed by the Seamless Domain, extremely easy to absorb and refine, surpassing any kind of energy in the Source Sea, and even worth far more than many priceless treasures that could raise one's cultivation.
If one refined Seamless Liquid, cultivation could be raised in a short time, though it would not help half-step Eternal Realm experts much.
But for nine-realm Supreme Beings, the help was immense.
That was why Ye Tian came to East Return Mountain.
After entering East Return Mountain, Ye Tian discovered that he was the only one here; the other half-step Eternal Realm experts had not come.
Very quickly, Ye Tian arrived at a rather ruined great hall. This was East Return Hall.
Although the great hall was in utter disrepair, the array could still be used.
Ye Tian activated the array here, then took out countless resources and materials.
This array could not produce Seamless Liquid out of thin air; Ye Tian had to provide the resources himself.
When countless resources surged into the core of the great array, the array began to convert energy.
As time slowly passed, a droplet of green water began to appear in the void; this green droplet was Seamless Liquid.
Ye Tian swallowed Seamless Liquid in one gulp and began refining it.
Boom!!!!
The energy of Seamless Liquid erupted, rapidly merging into Ye Tian's body, helping him temper his body and raise his cultivation.
“Such a powerful effect, pity this is not my true body. Even if the copied body increases its cultivation, it cannot feed back to the true body!”
Ye Tian muttered.
He knew full well that raising the copied body's cultivation would not help much, but he still came here. Mainly because his current strength was too weak, and he had to raise his cultivation a bit before he could obtain good things on Seamless Mountain.
Of course, his true body could also come here, but Ye Tian was unwilling to take the risk.
After all, the opportunities in the Seamless Domain had not yet reached the point where he needed to gamble his life. Besides, if he wanted to reach the Extreme of nine-realm Supreme Beings, it would just take a bit more time.
So he did not care too much.
After a long while, Ye Tian's cultivation increased greatly, and his body also became much stronger. Although he was still some distance away from the Extreme of nine-realm Supreme Beings, his ordinary combat strength was probably enough to match Gold Chen Source Ancestor among the Twelve Ancestors.
“About enough. It's time to go to another place on Seamless Mountain.”
Ye Tian muttered.
Whoosh!
His body disappeared from East Return Mountain, and very soon he arrived at Seamless Tower on Seamless Mountain.
Seamless Tower was a test tower once built by the Seamless Domain Lord for disciples or servants. The tower had nine levels, and for every level passed, one could obtain the corresponding reward.
Now, although the Seamless Domain Lord had fallen, Seamless Tower still remained, and there were still some rewards inside.
At this moment.
Outside Seamless Tower, strands of Space-Time Dragon Pythons were shuttling back and forth.
After Ye Tian arrived here, he forcibly charged into Seamless Tower and came to the hall inside it.
Here, he saw a few familiar faces.
“Zhenhe Sword Ancestor, Fenggu Source Ancestor, you're here too!”
Ye Tian greeted them.
“Endless Ancestor, you've come too!”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and Fenggu Source Ancestor said with a smile.
