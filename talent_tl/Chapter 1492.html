Chapter 1491: Sweeping Through the Bone Sea Forbidden Land
!
Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
It was very peaceful here. Bone soldiers and bone generals were cultivating, and even the Bone Kings were in meditation. There was no sense of tension, as if they had forgotten about Ye Tian's previous invasion of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
Furthermore, there were only thirty Bone Kings remaining here now; the other Bone Kings had already returned to the Bone Race.
On this day.
Ye Tian arrived at the Bone Sea Forbidden Land once again. As soon as he entered, his incompatible aura was naturally detected by the Bone Kings; no matter how he tried to hide, it was useless.
"Damn it, that Ancestor has actually come again!!!"
The Bone Kings roared in anger.
Boom!!!! Thirty Bone Kings moved to intercept Ye Tian, while a large number of bone generals formed battle arrays to block him. As for the bone soldiers, they fled as far away as they could.
Additionally, some bone soldiers returned to the Bone Race to seek reinforcements. Otherwise, relying solely on these Bone Kings, they would be unable to do anything to Ye Tian and might even lose more of their kinsmen.
Rumble!!!! Thirty Bone Kings besieged Ye Tian. They did not use the bone sword because it had already been sent back to the Bone Race.
After all, that was a Supreme Imperial Artifact and did not belong to them. Last time it was a temporary loan; it was impossible for it to be kept here indefinitely.
Without the Supreme Imperial Artifact, how could they deal with Ye Tian? Very quickly, Ye Tian discovered that they did not have that... bone sword, and he became instantly exhilarated.
Immediately, he began to deal with those bone generals.
"Kill!"
A battle array was blasted apart, and bone generals were heavily wounded one after another.
Then, with a squeeze of his hand, Ye Tian crushed a bone general into powder, leaving behind only a single bone bead, which he instantly collected.
Bang bang bang!!!! The bone generals had no power to fight back at all. They were blown apart one by one, turning into bone beads.
The Bone Kings were beyond furious. Bone generals were the high-level combat power of the Bone Race; losing even one was a significant loss, yet they were losing so many now.
Suddenly.
A Bone King was heavily wounded by a full-strength strike from Ye Tian. He was sent flying, the bones on his body cracking as if he were falling apart.
Although the cracks in the bones healed instantly, the injuries did not recover nearly as fast.
Next, Ye Tian aimed directly at this Bone King, attacking him with all his might. Under the successive attacks, this Bone King could no longer hold on and was in a state of being heavily wounded and near death.
The other Bone Kings wanted to stop him, but they couldn't.
Ye Tian's current strength was too powerful; it was simply not something they could withstand.
Moreover, at this moment, Ye Tian had deployed three defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, completely ignoring the attacks of these Bone Kings as he desperately sought to slay one.
"Sha Bai, run!"
The other Bone Kings shouted urgently.
Sha Bai was the heavily wounded and dying Bone King.
However, his speed was too slow.
Ye Tian's speed was much faster than his—whenever he tried to flee, Ye Tian would intercept him.
Finally.
Under a punch from Ye Tian, this bone body completely shattered and could no longer heal, turning into a single bone bead.
This bone bead bore golden patterns and was far larger than an ordinary bone bead.
This was a Bone King's bone bead! With the fall of a Bone King, the bone generals were terrified, and even several Bone Kings panicked.
Bone Kings also feared falling. It was said that since they broke into this place, only a few had fallen when the Bone Sea Forbidden Land was first established, or during the Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain when they clashed with other powerful forces.
As for clashing with the Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Domain, almost no Bone King had ever fallen.
But now, a Bone King had fallen at the hands of an Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Domain.
"No one is allowed to flee! Besiege him with everything you have and wait for reinforcements to arrive!"
A Bone King commanded.
The bone generals had no choice and did not dare to flee. They desperately tried to stop Ye Tian, fearing that he would invade the Gate. Once the Gate was destroyed, they would be... the sinners of the Bone Race! Even though they knew very well that Ye Tian craved bone beads and that staying here meant death for the bone generals, they had to block Ye Tian even if it meant using their lives to pile up a defense.
Rumble!!!! One bone general after another continued to fall, and the Bone Kings were constantly injured, completely unable to contend with Ye Tian.
At this moment, they were also extremely anxious, wanting reinforcements to arrive as soon as possible.
Otherwise, the Bone Sea Forbidden Land would likely suffer the slaughter of a large number of bone generals and even Bone Kings.
Just then.
One Bone King after another passed through the Gate and arrived at the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. One of the Bone Kings held a bone sword—it was the Supreme Imperial Artifact of the Bone Race.
"Kill that Ancestor!"
The arriving Bone Kings roared in anger.
The Bone King holding the bone sword said nothing and began to activate the sword, vowing to use this... Supreme Imperial Artifact to blast Ye Tian to death.
Last time, they had... used this... Imperial Artifact to heavily wound Ye Tian, leaving him on the verge of death, and ultimately he was unable to contend with them and was killed in the siege.
They were certain they could achieve the same result this time. After all, a top-tier Ancestor could not contend with a Supreme Imperial Artifact.
Kill! The bone sword turned into a streak of light and lunged toward Ye Tian.
At this moment.
Ye Tian instantly activated those defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques.
A total of twenty defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques turned into layers of protective shields to guard Ye Tian.
Crack!!!! The bone sword pierced down, and layer after layer of shields were punctured.
But when it pierced through the tenth layer of shields, it finally came to a halt.
Ye Tian had blocked a strike from the Supreme Imperial Artifact.
Meanwhile, the Bone King who had activated the Supreme Imperial Artifact was now incredibly weak.
Ye Tian performed the Flowing Light Ultimate Ancestral Technique and approached that Bone King. Two Bone Kings tried to protect the weakened one, but unfortunately, they couldn't stop Ye Tian and were directly blasted away.
As for that incredibly weak Bone King, he had no power to resist and was killed by a single strike from Ye Tian.
A second Bone King had fallen! As for the Supreme Imperial Artifact, it also fell into Ye Tian's hands.
"Impossible! The Supreme Imperial Artifact actually couldn't wound him?"
The Bone Kings were filled with terror.
In their eyes, even an ordinary Supreme Ancestor level combatant wouldn't be able to take a hit from a Supreme Imperial Artifact without being injured!
In reality, to block that strike, Ye Tian had indeed consumed one-tenth (10%) of his Ancestral Power; only then did he manage to block the Supreme Imperial Artifact.
Next, Ye Tian deactivated some of the defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques; otherwise, he wouldn't be able to maintain his combat strength for long.
Soon, ten minutes passed.
During this time, Ye Tian slaughtered a large number of bone generals and killed yet another Bone King, meaning he had killed three Bone Kings in total.
Meanwhile, more Bone Kings from the Bone Race were arriving as reinforcements, and perhaps even more powerful members of the Bone Race would descend.
Coupled with the fact that the Ancestral Power in Ye Tian's body was severely depleted and his combat strength was declining sharply, if he fought much longer, his power would likely be exhausted.
Consequently, Ye Tian attempted to leave.
"Leave our race's Supreme Imperial Artifact behind!!!!"
Those Bone Kings went into a frenzy, but Ye Tian ignored them completely.
Whoosh! Protected by twenty layers of defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, Ye Tian broke through the encirclement and left the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
And at the moment he left, he seemed to sense a suffocating aura, and even the bone sword he had stowed away seemed as if it wanted to break through space and depart.
But once Ye Tian returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain, the bone sword settled down, and only a faint, indistinct roar of fury echoed out, shaking the entire Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
