Chapter 1573: Compatibility with the Source of Eternal Source Items!

Fifty thousand points in the Void Source Hall was no small amount; even for a Supreme Being, collecting such points was extremely difficult.
Moreover, many powerful Supreme Being Void Sons also required points, as the Void Source Stele was still useful to them.
Additionally, some Void Sons had top-tier Supreme Beings as backers or elders, and they would request their elders to obtain points for them. This was their chance.
If they obtained 50,000 points, it would be enough for them to rise completely.
The reason Ye Tian was willing to pay a reward of 50,000 points was that he currently had too many points. He had gained 30,000 points for condensing the Void Source Body, 30,000 points for clearing the second floor of the Void Tower, and another 10,000 points for becoming a Void Divine Son.
From these alone, he had obtained a total of 70,000 points. Although he had spent some, even if he paid out another 50,000 points, he would still have plenty left for his own use.
Furthermore, the knowledge contained within that mysterious consciousness was bound to be astonishing. If it were a truly top-tier Supreme Being, he would have no way to kill them, and even if he could, he would have no chance to capture their consciousness. Once they fell, the consciousness would quickly dissipate, and even if forcibly condensed, one could not obtain much memory.
Therefore, this special mysterious consciousness was of immense value, enough for him to pay 50,000 points.
Tianlin Realm.
Qiu Ye Domain Ancestor also learned of the 50,000-point bounty. It had to be said that he also needed 50,000 points; even if he didn't need them himself, his organization had several Void Sons who could use them for cultivation.
"A pity, I do not possess a Soul Source Tool!"
Qiu Ye Domain Ancestor shook his head and said.
Many Void Sons or Supreme Beings were like Qiu Ye Domain Ancestor, deeply desiring the 50,000 points but lacking a Soul Source Tool, making it impossible to obtain them.
Time slowly passed, and finally, on the tenth day.
A Void Son arrived at the Void Source Hall and sought to contact Ye Tian through the artifact spirit of the Void Source Hall.
"Finally, someone has contacted me!"
Ye Tian was somewhat excited and immediately met with that Void Son.
Since they were meeting within the Void Source Hall, no matter how powerful the other party was, they could not threaten him.
Ye Tian still wore a mask to hide his aura, unwilling to reveal his true identity, but the other party did not hide theirs.
Thus, Ye Tian recognized him immediately.
Void Son — Bai Lun!
This was a Void Son at the Supreme Being realm, and his strength naturally far surpassed Ye Tian's.
Ye Tian and Void Son Bai Lun did not waste words and proceeded directly to the trade.
Bai Lun produced a treasure that looked like a drop of azure water and introduced it: "This is the Source Tool Water Soul. Once activated, it can instantly freeze soul consciousness and strip away the opponent's memories. However, if the opponent's strength is greater than yours, you still won't be able to easily strip their consciousness."
"What if their strength far surpasses mine, but only soul fragments remain?" Ye Tian asked.
"That can be done!"
Bai Lun nodded.
"Very well, then let us trade!" Ye Tian said.
Soon, Ye Tian transferred 50,000 points to the other party, and the Source Tool Water Soul fell into Ye Tian's hands.
The trade completed, Bai Lun departed.
Ye Tian then began to refine the Source Tool within the Void Source Hall. With his current realm as a Second-tier Invincible, refining a Source Tool was much simpler than in the past.
After a period of time, Ye Tian finished refining the Water Soul Source Tool and could control it with ease.
Additionally, he found some Tribulation Beasts to test the Water Soul Source Tool, ensuring he could use it proficiently.
Afterward, he gave the Water Soul Source Tool to his Invincible Progenitor clone. The Invincible Progenitor clone shared the same soul as the main body, but its cultivation had remained at the Third-tier Invincible realm; thus, using the Water Soul Source Tool was no problem at all.
......
Xumi Golden Mountain.
Ye Tian brought the Water Soul Source Tool here and entered the consciousness space of the mysterious consciousness.
Upon seeing Ye Tian, the mysterious consciousness barked again, "Damned fellow, you've come again. I've told you, unless you merge me with that descendant of yours, I will not tell you anything you want to know."
"I came this time to negotiate with you. I can agree to your request, but..."
Ye Tian spoke.
"But what?" the mysterious consciousness asked excitedly.
At the very moment the mysterious consciousness was excited, Ye Tian struck.
He had deliberately triggered the excitement of the mysterious consciousness because he feared it would instantly cause its own consciousness to undergo Annihilation, leading to the failure of his attempt.
By triggering its excitement, it would not instantly cause its soul to undergo Annihilation; even if it reacted, it would need a moment to shift its mindset.
Within the consciousness space, a drop of water appeared, instantly freezing the entire space.
The figure formed by the mysterious consciousness was also frozen, its expression still carrying a hint of rage, but it was too late to do anything else.
"Extract memory!"
Ye Tian activated the Water Soul Source Tool, which transformed into countless white silk-like rays of light that surged into the depths of the mysterious consciousness, extracting massive amounts of memory. These were transmitted to Ye Tian's hands, turning into clusters of memory fragments.
As time passed, the Water Soul Source Tool extracted more and more memories, but Ye Tian's consumption was also great, and the mysterious consciousness was resisting.
It had to be said that the mysterious consciousness's former strength was indeed formidable. Even with only a fragmented consciousness remaining, it could still shake the Water Soul Source Tool. Had Ye Tian's strength not been great enough and his mastery over Eternal Rules not been profound enough, he truly might not have been able to suppress it.
After an unknown amount of time, the mysterious consciousness finally collapsed, and Ye Tian could no longer continue extracting memories.
"Success!"
Ye Tian looked at the large amount of memories before him, feeling satisfied.
In the following years, he began to absorb these memories bit by bit, learning the identity of this mysterious consciousness and various secrets.
Many useless memories were entirely discarded by him, leaving only the useful ones.
Of course, these memories were not the complete set, but from them, Ye Tian had already obtained the secrets he wanted to know.
For instance, the reason his descendant Ye Jinpeng could merge with the source of an Eternal Source Item was that Ye Jinpeng's soul aptitude was special—he was a natural Source Compatibilist.
Normal living beings only have a compatibility of about 1% with Eternal Source Items. Only after cultivating to the First-tier Invincible realm, creating a Supreme Skill, and then condensing an Eternal Tribulation Body can one raise the compatibility to 30%.
Once this level of compatibility is reached, combined with one's own powerful strength, one can then absorb the source of an Eternal Source Item to forcibly break through to the Supreme Being realm.
However, there is a type of being that is naturally 100% compatible with the source of an Eternal Source Item. As long as they find a suitable Eternal Source Item, they can easily absorb it and break through to the Supreme Being realm. They do not even need to condense an Eternal Tribulation Body or create a Supreme Skill beforehand; they can slowly master those after breaking through to the Supreme Being realm.
Ye Jinpeng belonged to the type that had soul compatibility with a soul-type Eternal Source Item—the Eternal Soul Wood. This was a powerful Eternal Source Item that ranked among the top thirty. That mysterious person had used the source of the Eternal Soul Wood to preserve their soul consciousness, thereby searching for a soul compatibilist for the Eternal Soul Wood.
Only in this way could that mysterious person be reborn and even surpass their Peak.
In fact, such beings compatible with Eternal Source Items were not rare in the Source Sea, but they might never encounter an Eternal Source Item with 100% compatibility in their entire lives, and thus could never discover this special constitution.
It had to be said that Ye Jinpeng was quite lucky, but Ye Tian was even luckier, because the memories of that mysterious person contained many secrets and opportunities.
