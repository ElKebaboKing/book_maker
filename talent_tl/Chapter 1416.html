Chapter 1415: Void Territory!

Chapter 1415: Void Territory
!
"Ancestor Wanfa, I am Ancestor Qingshan from Daoyuan Holy Land, and I wish to purchase a Holy Tablet slot. I wonder if it's possible?"
Ancestor Qingshan teleported in front of Ye Tian and asked.
"As long as you can afford the price, I can naturally sell you a slot!"
Ye Tian said.
Immediately, he stated his price.
Unlike Saintess Qingyi, Saintess Qingyi had only paid a small price to obtain a Holy Tablet slot back then, but that was because Ye Tian needed the Jade Purifying Bottle at the time, which was why he agreed to the transaction with Saintess Qingyi.
But now it was different; even if he were given the Jade Purifying Bottle, he shouldn't expect to get a Holy Tablet slot.
And Ancestor Qingshan was not a member of the War Gate, so Ye Tian's asking price was extremely high.
Just for holy artifacts, six were needed, along with numerous resources and treasures.
After hearing this, Ancestor Qingshan was instantly dumbfounded and couldn't help but say, "Ancestor Wanfa, isn't this price too high? Even if I sold all my treasures, I wouldn't be able to buy a single slot!"
"If you can't afford it, then leave!"
Ye Tian waved his hand.
"You..."
Ancestor Qingshan was extremely annoyed.
Unfortunately, he was not Ye Tian's opponent; he was merely a top-tier Ancestor, not even an ant in front of Ye Tian.
Immediately, Ancestor Qingshan left.
It must be said that almost none of the top-tier Ancestors here could afford a slot, and they all returned disappointed.
....Ten days later, other Holy Sects from the Dark Holy Domain sent many Ancestors who arrived at the Daoshan Realm. They all used secret treasures to rush over at extreme speed, fearing any delay. Once the slots were gone, they would have no way to compete for them.
Boom!!!!! Ancestors descended upon the Profound Void Holy Realm, appearing near the Holy Tablet.
The first to arrive were the Ancestors of the Firmament Holy Sect, led by three Invincible Ancestors.
"Ancestor Wanfa, our Firmament Holy Sect needs some Holy Tablet slots, what do you say?"
One of the Invincible Ancestors said with a smile.
This was clearly a threat, using the combat power of an Invincible Ancestor to threaten Ye Tian.
Although Ye Tian's strength was formidable, ordinary Invincible Ancestors were far from being his match. However, the Invincible Ancestor speaking was not an ordinary Invincible Ancestor.
At this moment, this Invincible Ancestor slightly revealed a wisp of aura, as if suppressing all ages, far surpassing ordinary Invincible Ancestors.
"Second-step Invincible Ancestor!"
Ye Tian murmured in his heart.
He was far from being a match for such an Invincible Ancestor.
"Ancestor Moxie, are you threatening our War Gate?"
Ancestor Guhai and others appeared, confronting the three Invincible Ancestors of the Firmament Holy Sect.
"Eight War Emperors, the War Gate is truly generous!"
Ancestor Moxie of the Firmament Holy Sect said solemnly.
This time, they had only sent three enemy Ancestors, but they didn't expect the War Gate to be even more generous, sending eight.
However, it was also true that although the Holy Tablet did not belong to the War Gate, Ye Tian was an Ancestor of the War Gate, so the War Gate naturally had many Holy Tablet slots and would not neglect them.
"Ancestor Guhai, this Holy Tablet has 100 slots. Our Firmament Holy Sect doesn't need many, how about 30 slots?"
Ancestor Moxie proposed.
"Thirty slots? You're dreaming!"
Ye Tian cursed directly.
"You..."
Ancestor Moxie was furious, wishing he could kill Ye Tian.
A mere top-tier Ancestor dared to be so disrespectful to him! "Ancestor Wanfa's opinion is our opinion!"
Ancestor Guhai and others immediately said.
Just then, another group of Ancestors descended.
They were the Ancestors of the Myriad Things Holy Sect. The Myriad Things Holy Sect had dispatched four Invincible Ancestors, all of whom were at the second step or above.
As time passed, Ancestors from various Holy Sects descended, and even many Holy Lands dispatched third-step Invincible Ancestors.
A large number of Invincible Ancestors descended with their juniors, putting considerable pressure on humanity and the War Gate.
At this moment, the War Gate was also ready to open the War Realm Tower at any time, allowing many War Emperors of the War Gate to descend.
Of course, this was a last resort; they would only make this choice if these Holy Sects collectively attacked and besieged them.
"Ancestor Guhai, since we've come, we can't return empty-handed. Give up some slots!"
Ancestor Xuehen from the Dark Holy Sect spoke.
Ye Tian spoke up: "Esteemed Invincible Ancestors, it is possible to get slots, but they must be purchased. It's impossible to obtain them for free.
Even some of the War Gate's top-tier Ancestors must purchase slots if they want them!"
"Purchasing slots, ridiculous!"
Many Invincible Ancestors were very displeased.
If they had to purchase slots, wouldn't their coming here as Invincible Ancestors be a complete waste of time?
Their goal was... to obtain some slots for free; that was their demand.
"Ancestor Wanfa, let me handle this!"
Ancestor Wulei, one of the eight Invincible Ancestors of the War Gate, spoke.
"Then I'll trouble Ancestor Wulei!"
Ye Tian knew very well that his standing was too low; these Invincible Ancestors completely ignored him, and he couldn't threaten them.
In a word, it was still a matter of strength.
If he possessed the combat power of a first-step Invincible Ancestor, and with the help of the Profound Void Holy Realm's power, he should be able to unleash combat power surpassing a first-step Invincible Ancestor, easily suppressing these troublesome Invincible Ancestors.
At that time, who would dare to say another word? "Ancestor Wulei, what do you want to say?"
Ancestor Xuehen said.
Ancestor Wulei was very low-key and rarely made a move, so although others knew that the War Gate had such an Invincible Ancestor, they didn't know much about him.
At this moment, Ancestor Wulei said: "Everyone, in the Dark Forbidden Zone, strength is paramount. If you want to obtain Holy Tablet slots for free, then show your strength. If any of you are confident in defeating me, I can persuade the other seven Invincible Ancestors of the War Gate not to stop you.
But if you lose, then you cannot interfere. How about it?"
"Ancestor Wulei, this..."
Ancestor Guhai and others frowned, feeling that this was simply giving away slots to other Holy Sects for free! Ye Tian didn't speak. He felt that since Ancestor Wulei dared to say this, he must have absolute confidence in himself, otherwise, he would lose face.
Moreover, Ancestor Wulei was far from simple; he was a Sacred Gifted, and possessed three Sacred Gifts, one of which should be his innate Sacred Gift.
Such an Invincible Ancestor was extremely powerful, perhaps having reached the combat power of a first-step Invincible Ancestor.
"Ancestor Wulei, you are too arrogant. Then I shall experience your strength!"
Ancestor Xuehen said.
"Enter my Void Territory to fight, so as not to damage the Profound Void Holy Realm!"
Ancestor Guhai of the War Gate said.
"Alright!"
"Alright!"
Ancestor Wulei and Ancestor Xuehen both agreed.
The Void Territory was a special fragment that could transform into a domain. Fighting within it made it almost impossible to cause significant damage.
Of course, it could also be used to suppress others.
But Ancestor Guhai certainly wouldn't dare to control the Void Territory to suppress Ancestor Xuehen, as that would be a foul.
Just then, Ancestor Guhai flipped his palm, and a small rhombus-shaped fragment appeared.
"Go!"
Ancestor Guhai waved his hand.
This fragment transformed into a vast territory, covering this area, and Ancestor Wulei and Ancestor Xuehen both flew into this Void Territory.
