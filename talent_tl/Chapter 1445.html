Chapter 1444: Sealing the Dark Holy Ancestor, the Primordial Ancestor Steps In
!
Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Ye Tian had been guarding the Taboo Spring during this time; regardless... it was also a suitable place for cultivation.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian sensed the fluctuations of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
"As expected, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor has crossed the Dark Forbidden Zone!"
Ye Tian immediately sealed off the Xuanxu Holy Realm, and his duplicated body promptly walked out of the Taboo Ancestral Spring, while his original body remained to guard the place. This ensured that the Xuanxu Holy Realm would not be stolen away.
Boom!!!! Outside the Xuanxu Holy Realm, Ye Tian stopped a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor who was traveling through space with a single hand.
Boom!!! Ye Tian threw a punch, directly knocking the opponent out of space-time.
"Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor, it's you. Does your Immortal Holy Sect intend to snatch the Taboo Ancestral Spring?"
Ye Tian questioned.
"Indeed, Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, you should hand over the Taboo Ancestral Spring. Although your strength is comparable to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, I am no ordinary Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. You cannot stop me alone.
If you stand in my way, be careful that I don't trample your Xuanxu Holy Realm today!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor threatened.
"Then let us have a battle and see whether my Xuanxu Holy Realm is trampled by you, or if I slay you today!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
"Arrogant!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor was furious.
Boom!!!!! A black light emerged from the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor's body, transforming into a strange stone statue.
This was his special Ancestral Technique—Stone Statue True Body! By performing this Ancestral Technique, as long as the technique was not broken, all damage dealt by the enemy would be transferred to the stone statue, while his original body remained unharmed.
Furthermore, the Stone Statue True Body could amplify his physical strength and physical defense by tenfold. Thus, this Ancestral Technique was extremely terrifying and was his primary reliance.
"Void Slash!"
Without a word, Ye Tian directly unleashed the space Ancestral Technique, Void Slash, at the Consummate Realm.
Rip! Hundreds of millions of territories of space-time shattered. The terrifying Void Slash seemed capable of annihilating all Great Dao space-time as it struck the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor directly.
Crack! The stone statue behind the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor cracked instantly, a massive fissure appearing that was difficult to heal.
"The Void Slash Ancestral Technique, and it's at the Consummate Realm? How is that possible? How could you possibly raise an Ancestral Technique to the Consummate Realm?"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor was dumbfounded.
Back then, the images of Ye Tian's battle with the Golden Dawn Holy Ancestor had spread, and he had seen them.
Ye Tian indeed knew many Ancestral Techniques, but they were all at the Minor Success stage.
Raising an Ancestral Technique to Minor Success was manageable, but raising it to Great Success or the Consummate Realm was an exaggerated level of difficulty.
He had rarely seen any Domain Ancestors who could cultivate an Ancestral Technique to the Consummate Realm.
Moreover, Ye Tian's current cultivation was different.
Back then, Ye Tian had not fully mastered his body, but now he had not only gained complete control but had also completed two Invincible Domain Body transformations.
"This... how can there be such a rapid rate of improvement? Even if one practiced bitterly within a Time Array, given the best resources, and even with a Primordial Ancestor-level being personally guiding them, it would be impossible to have such a fast rate of cultivation progress!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor was completely stunned, unable to believe it at all.
Abruptly! Streaks of golden light transformed into silk threads, continuously cutting his body.
Each golden thread felt like the full-force strike of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. Under the attack of so many golden threads, he had no way to resist.
At the same time, Ye Tian's Void Slash Ancestral Technique struck from time to time, causing his stone statue to crack continuously.
Crack! The Stone Statue True Body was about to shatter. Once this Ancestral Technique broke, his physical body would be unable to withstand Ye Tian, and his Supreme-level Domain Body would soon be ground away by Ye Tian.
At this moment, the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor gritted his teeth and took out a small gray orb.
He crushed the gray orb, and a gray light shield immediately protected him. No matter how powerful Ye Tian's Void Slash was, it could not shake this gray light shield.
"Can't break it?"
Ye Tian frowned.
Immediately, he directly performed the Five Elements Seal Ancestral Technique.
"Seal!"
The Five Elements Seal directly extracted the power of the Five Elements Taboo Great Dao in this area, forcibly suppressing the gray light shield. Layers of seals compressed this area into a small ball, which appeared in Ye Tian's hand.
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor was sealed within this Five Elements Seal ball. Although Ye Tian could not break the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor's gray light shield, the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor could not get out either.
In the future, he could attack that gray light shield over and over again. After a long period of attacks, he didn't believe the gray light shield wouldn't break.
Void Slash! Void Slash! Void Slash! Ye Tian attacked repeatedly; regardless... he wasn't afraid of wasting time.
Inside the gray light shield.
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor also knew he had been sealed by Ye Tian, and sensing Ye Tian's continuous attacks on the gray light shield, he grew anxious.
This gray light shield was transformed from that small gray orb, which was a special sacred object he had occasionally obtained while traveling the Dark Forbidden Zone. It could withstand the full-force strike of a top-tier Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, and could even block the full-force strike of a Half-step Primordial Ancestor.
Even for himself, if he were to attack this gray light shield, it would take at least hundreds of thousands of years of attacks to have any hope of breaking it.
But at this moment, he felt that Ye Tian's attacks were too terrifying. This gray light shield would last ten years at most.
"Call for help, I must call for help!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor panicked.
In a certain territory of the Immortal Holy Sect, the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor had a weaker avatar stationed there.
This avatar immediately headed to the Immortal Ancestral Realm.
"Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor, what is the situation?"
Several Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Immortal Holy Sect asked.
"My true body has been sealed by the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor. His strength is many times greater than before, and he even possesses Ancestral Techniques at the Consummate Realm.
I must ask the Primordial Ancestor to save me, otherwise I will surely die!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor said urgently.
"What? You've been sealed?"
The Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were shocked.
"Immortal Primordial Ancestor, please save me!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor knelt at the entrance of the Immortal Primordial Ancestor's palace and pleaded.
"I understand!"
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor's voice came through. "I am a Primordial Ancestor, after all; it is inconvenient for me to take action against an Invincible Domain Ancestor.
How about this, I will go find the Battle Ancestor of the Battle Sect and have him persuade the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor to release you!"
"Thank you, Immortal Primordial Ancestor!"
The Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor hurriedly expressed his gratitude.
Boom!!!!! This avatar of the Immortal Primordial Ancestor condensed a sliver of will, crossed the long river of space-time, and arrived at the Battle Ancestor Realm.<
Immediately, the Battle Ancestor sensed it and met with this sliver of the Immortal Primordial Ancestor's will.
"Immortal Primordial Ancestor, what is it? Why have you suddenly come to find me?"
The Battle Ancestor asked curiously.
"Battle Ancestor, have your Battle Sect's Myriad Law Domain Ancestor release the Black-Patterned Holy Ancestor. You name the conditions!"
The Immortal Primordial Ancestor said.
