Chapter 1582: I am the Master of the Void Source Hall!

Void Source Hall, Source Space.
Ye Tian began his closed-door cultivation, merging his Physical Body Tribulation Realm and Soul Tribulation Realm.
The Physical Body Tribulation Realm and Soul Tribulation Realm could not be merged all at once to immediately promote him to a First-tier Invincible.
Ye Tian was in the Nine Tribulations Physical Body Realm and Nine Tribulations Soul Realm, so he needed to merge them nine times, one tribulation realm at a time.
When the degree of fusion between the physical body and soul reached the level of one tribulation realm, it would trigger a Fusion Tribulation. After all, merging the Soul Tribulation Realm and Physical Body Tribulation Realm was an act that defied the rules, so he naturally had to endure tribulations. Without sufficient foundation, anyone who dared to forcibly merge them would definitely perish.
Because they lacked sufficient accumulation and foundation, Second-tier Invincibles had no possibility of promoting to First-tier Invincibles.
As time passed and a vast amount of resources were consumed, Ye Tian finally merged his Physical Body Tribulation Realm and Soul Tribulation Realm to the level of one tribulation.
At that moment, he encountered his first Fusion Tribulation.
Boom!!!
Endless flames, lightning, and wind blades enveloped every particle of Ye Tian's body, attempting to annihilate his entire being.
However, Ye Tian's body was indestructible. Even a single particle was difficult to wear down, and the first Fusion Tribulation could not harm Ye Tian in the slightest.
Soon, the first Fusion Tribulation passed.
This was a good start!
Basically, after passing one Fusion Tribulation, Ye Tian's strength increased significantly. He was no longer considered a Second-tier Invincible, but he had not yet reached the realm of a First-tier Invincible.
This realm was equivalent to a half-step First-tier Invincible; it was almost certain he would become a First-tier Invincible, it was only a matter of time.
The fusion process was irreversible, meaning Ye Tian had to complete the fusion. Otherwise, if the delay was too long, a conflict would arise between the physical body and soul, preventing him from exerting his full strength.
Because of this, Second-tier Invincibles attempting to reach the First-tier Invincible realm would choose to cultivate in seclusion in a safe place for countless ages. The moment they emerged would be the time they broke through to become First-tier Invincibles.
One Great Tribulation Era after another passed, and Ye Tian completed one Fusion Tribulation after another.
The ninth Fusion Tribulation was the most difficult. Since Ye Tian's physical body and soul were too powerful, his ninth Fusion Tribulation was also many times more terrifying than those of other Second-tier Invincibles.
During the ninth Fusion Tribulation, Ye Tian was indeed injured, but only slightly.
Ultimately, he still passed the ninth Fusion Tribulation.
...
"I've broken through!"
Ye Tian felt the transformation of his body. At this moment, his physical body and soul were merged together. Every particle was both a physical particle and a soul particle; it was such a unique state.
A First-tier Invincible had almost no weaknesses. If it were a Second-tier Invincible, if an enemy used a soul attack and the attack power was strong enough, they could annihilate the Second-tier Invincible's soul in an instant.
But for a First-tier Invincible, the physical body was the soul, and the soul was the physical body. The two were one. Whether it was the physical body or the soul, there were no more weaknesses. Trying to kill them instantly was extremely difficult.
Moreover, every casual strike from a First-tier Invincible contained both soul and physical attacks.
At this moment, Ye Tian had broken through to the First-tier Invincible realm. His strength was likely sufficient to rival a Supreme Being like Tianfeng Source God.
As for Ordinary Supreme Beings, he could heavily injure them with a wave of his hand.
At this moment, Ye Tian felt that he was no longer a weakling in the entire Source Sea and possessed the qualifications to speak with other top-tier existences.
--------------------
Tianlin Realm.
Ye Jinpeng had spent many years adventuring here and, through Ye Tian's introduction, had also joined the Tianfeng Sea. After all, in the beginning, having an Eternal-name tier power as a backer was indeed much safer.
By now, Ye Jinpeng had already completely refined the Eternal Soul Wood Source within his soul. His soul talent was very high, and his physical body talent was not bad either, so he had already broken through to the Third-tier Invincible realm.
The next step was to raise his Physical Body Tribulation Realm to attempt to reach the Second-tier Invincible realm, but this step would take a long time.
After all, he did not have the same opportunities as Ye Tian.
On this day.
Ye Tian's duplicate body arrived at the Tianlin Realm and summoned his descendant, Ye Jinpeng.
"The Old Ancestor is summoning me?"
Ye Jinpeng immediately went over.
Soon, he met the Old Ancestor, Ye Tian.
"Jinpeng pays respects to the Old Ancestor!"
Ye Jinpeng said respectfully.
"Very good, you've already reached the Third-tier Invincible realm!" Ye Tian said with satisfaction.
"Compared to the Old Ancestor, I am still far behind!" Ye Jinpeng said modestly.
Ye Tian smiled slightly and asked, "Jinpeng, do you want to become a Son of the Void?"
"I do!" Ye Jinpeng nodded repeatedly, but then said dejectedly, "I know the Old Ancestor is a Son of the Void, and I also want to become one. I previously went to the Void Source Hall with a few friends to take the assessment on the first floor of the Void Tower, but I failed miserably. The difficulty of becoming a Son of the Void is just too high!"
"Let's go, I'll take you to the Void Source Hall!"
Ye Tian grabbed Ye Jinpeng with one hand and instantly crossed an infinite distance to arrive at the Void Source Hall.
Ye Jinpeng had been to the Void Source Hall once, so he was not unfamiliar with it.
"Old Ancestor, why did you bring me here? Even if I take the assessment on the first floor of the Void Tower again, I won't pass!" Ye Jinpeng asked in confusion.
"Artifact Spirit, come out!"
Ye Tian commanded.
Whoosh!
The Artifact Spirit appeared and said respectfully, "Master!"
"Make arrangements for this junior of mine to become a Son of the Void!" Ye Tian ordered.
"Yes!"
The Artifact Spirit replied.
Immediately, the Artifact Spirit made Ye Jinpeng a Son of the Void and gave him the rewards associated with the title, with the same treatment as any other Son of the Void.
It wasn't until he truly became a Son of the Void that Ye Jinpeng snapped out of his daze.
"Old Ancestor, what is going on?"
Ye Jinpeng was shocked.
A single word from the Old Ancestor made him a Son of the Void?
He also knew a lot about the Sons of the Void; even those Eternal-name tier powers only had a few, and one would only occasionally be born over endless ages. This showed how high the status of a Son of the Void was.
"Because the Void Source Hall is mine. I am the Master of the Void Source Hall!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
He was no longer afraid of being exposed. His true body stayed in the Void Source Hall, and his own strength rivaled a Supreme Being. Even a Supreme Being at the level of the Void Source Ancestor would have no way to kill him while he was inside the Void Source Hall across a Forbidden Land.
Therefore, he was virtually impossible to kill, so what was there to fear?
"What? The Old Ancestor is the Master of the Void Source Hall!!!!"
Ye Jinpeng was truly stunned.
