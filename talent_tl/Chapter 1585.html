Chapter 1584: Stealing Thunder Source Liquid!

Upon hearing Ye Tian's words, the Spirit Tool knew what Ye Tian was planning to do, so it reminded him: "Master, those forbidden lands are incomparably dangerous. Some of them could even cause powerful Supreme Beings to fall. Master, you really cannot go there."
"You should know that I have special clones, so my main body doesn't need to go," Ye Tian said.

Hearing this, the Spirit Tool felt slightly more at ease and said: "Master, some forbidden lands are forbidden lands for Invincibles, but not for Supreme Beings. Therefore, the possibility of those forbidden lands possessing Eternal resources is not very high. According to what I know, the forbidden lands that might possess Eternal resources are these..."
The Spirit Tool listed the names of various forbidden lands one by one, while also explaining the basic situation of each forbidden land.

To Ye Tian's surprise, the forbidden land of Dragon Marsh was among them.
But it made sense. A forbidden land like Dragon Marsh was very dangerous; even Gold Ancestor felt it was incomparably dangerous, and powerful Supreme Beings did not dare to step into it lightly. Therefore, it indeed might contain Eternal resources.

Furthermore, within Dragon Marsh, creatures like the Dragon Weasel actually relied on their bloodline. They could become Second-class Invincibles or even First-class Invincibles upon reaching adulthood. Thus, they did not follow the path of cultivation and had no need for Eternal resources to condense an Eternal Tribulation Body.
Consequently, even if creatures like the Dragon Weasel saw Eternal resources, they wouldn't be very interested.

Therefore, there might be unowned Eternal resources within Dragon Marsh. He prepared to enter Dragon Marsh once again to search.
On this day.
Ye Tian headed to the Dragon Marsh forbidden land, but not with his main body. Instead, he sent his duplicate body, the Dragon Weasel, into Dragon Marsh.

The Dragon Weasel body he duplicated was only a Second-class Invincible. Its strength was indeed not great, and it was difficult for him to exert very powerful strength, but it was absolutely safe within the Dragon Marsh forbidden land. Thus, strength was of secondary importance.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian entered the Dragon Marsh forbidden land and began searching for Eternal resources. Initially, he searched the outer areas of the Dragon Marsh forbidden land and did not enter the core area, as the core area was very dangerous.

Years passed, and Ye Tian didn't know how long he had been searching. He had found many valuable treasures, but he hadn't seen a trace of Eternal resources.
"I must go to the core area!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
The place most likely to give birth to Eternal resources was the core area of the Dragon Marsh forbidden land. If he searched in the outer areas, he feared he could search for an endless amount of time without any harvest.

Thus, Ye Tian advanced toward the core area of the Dragon Marsh forbidden land.
At this time, he sensed a terrifying aura and pressure. The opponent's aura was definitely at the level of a Supreme Being, but he wasn't clear about the opponent's identity or what kind of creature it was.
But soon, Ye Tian saw this powerful and mysterious creature.

Rumble!!!!!
In the core area, within an endless Thunder Marsh, a creature resembling a Thunder Dragon lay there sleeping. The aura on its body was incomparably terrifying.
"This is..."
Ye Tian didn't recognize this creature, but the Spirit Tool should know it.

Inside the Void Source Palace.
Ye Tian summoned the Spirit Tool, manifested the appearance of this creature, and asked: "Spirit Tool, do you know what creature this is?"
The Spirit Tool stared at the image of this creature and was instantly shocked: "Master, this is a Thunder Source Ancient Dragon, one of the most ancient creatures in the Source Sea. They are at the Supreme Being realm from birth. Even without cultivating, their strength is incomparably powerful. They are Eternal existences and are almost impossible to kill. Master, in the area near a Thunder Source Ancient Dragon, an Eternal resource will definitely be born—the Eternal resource, Thunder Source Liquid! Moreover, there is likely more than one portion of Thunder Source Liquid."

"Thunder Source Ancient Dragon!"
Ye Tian could tell from the Spirit Tool's tone just how powerful this creature was. It was likely even more terrifying than a powerful Supreme Being like Gold Ancestor, and it was definitely an overlord within the Dragon Marsh forbidden land.
Perhaps because the Dragon Weasel was too weak and was a creature belonging to Dragon Marsh, Ye Tian's approach in the form of the Dragon Weasel did not wake the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon.

Otherwise, a single breath from the opponent would have been enough to blast him to death.
Ye Tian slowly searched for Thunder Source Liquid in the area where the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon was located. Because he was extremely careful, his investigation speed was very slow.
After searching for a full several hundred years, Ye Tian finally found one portion of Thunder Source Liquid here.

"This portion of Thunder Source Liquid is inside the Thunder Marsh. Once I enter the Thunder Marsh, I'm afraid I'll be detected by the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon!" Ye Tian pondered.
He didn't take this portion of Thunder Source Liquid for the time being but continued to search for more.
Tens of thousands of years passed in a flash, and Ye Tian finished searching the entire area.

In the end, he discovered three portions of Thunder Source Liquid. Two of them were not within the Thunder Marsh where the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon resided, so he took both of those portions.
Afterward.
Ye Tian slowly retreated, causing no disturbances, and gradually left Dragon Marsh. Returning to the outside world, he delivered the two portions of Thunder Source Liquid to his main body.

Next, Ye Tian continued to Dragon Marsh in the form of the Dragon Weasel. He prepared to take a risk and try to collect that last portion of Thunder Source Liquid. If he failed, he would only lose this duplicate body, which wasn't much of a loss.
If he succeeded, he would obtain another portion of Thunder Source Liquid, which would be of great significance to him.
At the edge of the Thunder Marsh.

Ye Tian stepped inside and instantly arrived at the location of the Thunder Source Liquid. He reached out to grab it, intending to leave immediately.
But just then, a voice exploded in Ye Tian's ear.
"Little guy, are you trying to steal my Thunder Source Liquid? Although this thing is useless to me, it is mine after all!"
This was the voice of the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon.

Boom!!!!
Ye Tian saw the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon fully awaken. Its eyes, filled with infinite lightning, stared at him, but no anger could be seen.
"Ancestor!"
Ye Tian called out with great shamelessness.
After all, in terms of bloodline, the body of this Dragon Weasel was indeed a race generated from the derivation of the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon's aura. Therefore, calling the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon 'Ancestor' was not a problem.

"Since you called me Ancestor, I'll give the Thunder Source Liquid to you. However, it won't be of much help to you, at most helping you temper your body a bit," the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon said.
"Thank you, Ancestor!" Ye Tian thanked it.
Immediately, Ye Tian left the Thunder Marsh with the Thunder Source Liquid and returned to the outer area of Dragon Marsh.

Just as he neared the outer area of Dragon Marsh, Ye Tian suddenly sprinted out of Dragon Marsh at his fastest speed and was instantly brought back to the Void Source Palace by his main body.
At this moment, the Thunder Source Ancient Dragon also perceived it.
Boom!!!
The Thunder Source Ancient Dragon rushed out from within Dragon Marsh, but it was too late to catch Ye Tian.

"Damned thief!!!!"
The Thunder Source Ancient Dragon roared in anger.
It now realized that its so-called descendant was definitely being directed by a practitioner to steal Thunder Source Liquid from it.
Although it didn't care about the Thunder Source Liquid, it had been deceived, and that was the reason for its anger.
However, it had no idea that its so-called bloodline descendant was also a fake.

Inside the Void Source Palace.
Ye Tian looked at the third portion of Thunder Source Liquid in his hand and revealed a smile.
