Chapter 1675: Eternal Will Technique, Another Path?

Ye Tian found an empty spot here and sat down cross-legged, silently comprehending the sword dao aura in this place.
The sword dao aura here seemed to be within reach, yet impossible to truly capture. Only Eternal Will could faintly perceive a bit of it, and therefore, for many Eternal Realm experts, without a long period of comprehension, they simply could not gain much benefit.
Thus, many Eternal Realm experts would send a clone or their true body here to comprehend it, so as not to delay their own cultivation.
Those without clones were the ones who had no time to waste here.
Compared with other Eternal Realm experts, Ye Tian’s greatest advantage was his talent. His talent far surpassed that of other Eternal Realm experts, and he even possessed a legendary ancestor aptitude, while the six legendary lords did not have a legendary ancestor aptitude.
Therefore, Ye Tian could obtain more benefits from this sword, and his comprehension speed was much faster, far beyond what other Eternal Realm lords could compare to.
In the years that followed, this clone body of Ye Tian cultivated here.
For him, the clone body and the true body were unquestionably the same; as long as one body improved, the others improved as well.
Time slowly passed, and Ye Tian continued to comprehend the sword dao aura here. Gradually, he gained results. Combined with the layered secret technique and other Eternal compression secret techniques, and based on his own situation, he slowly attempted to create a higher-level Eternal compression secret technique.
Although compressing half-step Eternal power had its limit, Ye Tian had not yet reached that limit, far from it.
“The sword dao aura here is too terrifying. Just comprehending a tiny bit of it feels like seeing a world on another level. If I could comprehend even one ten-thousandth of this sword dao aura, I would already benefit greatly!”
Ye Tian could not help but exclaim.
One Eternal year after another passed, and Ye Tian gradually created a fairly good Eternal compression secret technique.
This Eternal compression secret technique imitated the sword dao aura here, using a sword dao aura-like force to compress the half-step Eternal power within the body, and thus its compression effect far surpassed the layered secret technique.
At first, this secret technique was only a rough prototype, but it was gradually perfected by Ye Tian.
During the process of perfecting it, Ye Tian shattered many clone bodies, but in the end, he still succeeded in perfecting it.
He named this Eternal compression secret technique the Sword Pressure Secret Technique.
According to Ye Tian’s estimate, the Sword Pressure Secret Technique could compress half-step Eternal power by about 200 times, and he judged that 200 times was still not the limit, but with his current attainments, he could only create it to this extent.
Unless he spent endless ages, he might be able to create an even better Eternal compression secret technique, but there was no real need.
After the Sword Pressure Secret Technique was created, Ye Tian’s true body began cultivating it, compressing the half-step Eternal power within his body.
Boom!!!!
An illusory sword shadow appeared above Ye Tian’s head, then projected into countless particles within his body. Those half-step Eternal powers seemed to be under the endless suppression of the sword shadow, like a mass of water being compressed into a single drop.
Crack, crack, crack!!!
The half-step Eternal power in his body was suppressed and compressed by the sword shadow, constantly undergoing qualitative change in a special manner, becoming even more condensed half-step Eternal power.
During this process, Ye Tian’s body also endured unimaginable pressure.
However, Ye Tian had long since perfected this secret technique, so most of this pressure was directed at the half-step Eternal power. The burden on the body was not very great, was bearable, and even had some effect of tempering the body.
As time went on, the condensation level of the half-step Eternal power within Ye Tian’s body reached a very strong degree.
90 times!
95 times!
100 times!
105 times!
……
Once the half-step Eternal power was compressed beyond 120 times, the speed slowed considerably. But after nearly 100 Eternal years, Ye Tian’s half-step Eternal power was still compressed to 150 times, and by then the Sword Pressure Secret Technique had also been perfected by him to the point where it could ultimately compress 220 times.
Compressing the half-step Eternal power to this degree also improved Ye Tian’s strength considerably.
Yet Ye Tian was very clear that even so, his strength had not undergone a qualitative change. It was still somewhat difficult for him to pass the eleventh level of the True Nothing Legend Tower.
Not long after, he went to test it.
The result was still a narrow defeat; he remained somewhat short of clearing the eleventh level. These gaps were not something that could be crossed simply by compressing the half-step Eternal power within his body.
At this point, Ye Tian thought of Eternal Will techniques.
His will was actually very powerful. By now, he had already opened up more than thirty thousand will worlds, making him quite strong even among third-tier Eternal Will users.
But with powerful will and no Eternal Will technique, it was difficult to fully bring out the advantage of that will.
“If I can create an Eternal Will technique, my strength will surely undergo a transformation!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
On this day.
Ye Tian chatted with Star-Gazing Sword Ancestor about knowledge related to Eternal Will techniques.
“Star-Gazing Sword Ancestor, what is your view on Eternal Will techniques?”
Ye Tian asked.
“Very powerful, with limitless potential!” Star-Gazing Sword Ancestor evaluated.
“Do you think we have a chance to create an Eternal Will technique?” Ye Tian asked again.
“Too difficult!” Star-Gazing Sword Ancestor shook his head. “I know that you, Unending Ancestor, very much want to create an Eternal Will technique. So do I. So do other Eternal Realm experts above the Undying Realm. Even the six legendary lords are the same. But for countless Eternal years, everyone has failed.”
“Not a single one succeeded?”
Ye Tian was surprised.
Star-Gazing Sword Ancestor shook his head and said, “As far as I know, no. The Eternal Realm experts in the True Nothing Legend Tower do possess Eternal Will techniques, but they have left behind no inheritance regarding Eternal Will techniques. We can only fight those Eternal Realm experts; there is no way to create an Eternal Will technique on our own. It can even be said that Eternal Will techniques belong to a system that is incompatible with our cultivation system. We don’t even have an entry-level inheritance. How could we create it?”
Then he added: “Perhaps some Eternal Realm expert has created one, but simply won’t say so. Still, an Eternal Realm expert did put forward such a conjecture: the fusion of will and innate Eternal rules is the basic condition for creating an Eternal Will technique. But for us half-step Eternal experts, we need to fuse innate Eternal rules with the body in order to break through and become Eternal Realm experts. As for Undying Realm lords, they need to fuse the body with will in order to become Immortal Realm experts, or even legendary lords. There has never been any need to fuse will with innate Eternal rules. Moreover, fusing innate Eternal rules with will is far too dangerous; one misstep can cause the will to shatter. I think that will and innate Eternal rules simply cannot be fused. This is a path that cannot be walked, and no one dares to try.”
“Will and innate Eternal rules fused together?”
Hearing this, Ye Tian suddenly felt that this might be the correct path, but no one dared to walk it.
