Chapter 1430: Holy Soul Traits
——Indestructible, Evolution!
Although Holy Gold Sand was much smaller in size than Star Soul Sand, it far surpassed it in both quality and energy content.
As Ye Tian refined the Holy Gold Sand, it turned into golden light that showered over his Holy Soul, tempering it.
"My Holy Soul is truly being tempered and is strengthening rapidly. As expected of the legendary Holy Gold Sand!"
Ye Tian was excited.
This was a treasure that even the War Sect couldn't provide him, yet he had obtained 520 grains. This was more than enough to undergo a second qualitative change of his Holy Soul, with plenty to spare.
As time passed, Ye Tian consumed one grain of Holy Gold Sand after another.
His Holy Soul was far stronger than those of others, so achieving a second qualitative change naturally required more Holy Gold Sand. Fortunately, he had obtained a large amount; otherwise, he wouldn't have been able to sustain such consumption.
One grain! Ten grains! A hundred grains! It wasn't until Ye Tian had consumed 365 grains of Holy Gold Sand that his Holy Soul finally underwent its qualitative change.
Boom!!! Ye Tian's Holy Soul was covered in gold, and endless holy light radiated throughout his entire soul sea.
At the same time, Ye Tian's soul sea seemed to be expanding.
The soul sea appeared boundless, but in reality, the area perceived by the soul had limits. This expansion was a massive stroke of luck.
As the soul sea expanded, Ye Tian's Holy Soul also continued to strengthen. Densely packed rule patterns emerged on the Holy Soul. After a long time, the qualitative change was complete.
"My Holy Soul has strengthened fivefold!"
Ye Tian sensed it and murmured.
But that wasn't the main benefit; his Holy Soul had actually gained two additional traits.
Normally, a second qualitative change of the Holy Soul would only produce one trait, but his Holy Soul was likely too powerful, resulting in an extra one.
"Indestructible!"
"Evolution!"
These were the two traits of Ye Tian's Holy Soul.
The first trait, Indestructible, meant that Ye Tian's Holy Soul could not be destroyed. Unless a special method was used or the opponent's strength far exceeded Ye Tian's imagination, his Holy Soul could not be obliterated. This was one of the traits of the Holy Soul—Indestructible! The second trait, Evolution, was also incredibly powerful, but its side effects were significant.
Evolution allowed him to... deduce, derive, and upgrade any cultivation technique, Holy Technique, or Ancestral Technique. However, it consumed Holy Soul Source. Consuming even a little required a long time to recover. If too much was consumed, the Holy Soul Source would be severely damaged, causing heavy injury to the Holy Soul and potentially hindering future growth.
For others, they could only use Evolution once in a long while. It was helpful, but not game-breaking. This was normal; if it were too heaven-defying, it would be unnatural.
But for Ye Tian, the Evolution trait was terrifying! "I can clone my body. If I use the clone's Holy Soul to evolve techniques at any cost, that would be incredible! Even if the clone's Holy Soul collapses, it won't affect my main body. I can just clone myself again!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
With this trait, his rise would be terrifyingly fast. In the future, he might even be able to deduce his own Ancestral Law.
After all, no matter how powerful the Copy Talent was, it couldn't deduce an original Ancestral Law, but the Evolution trait could.
Of course, without the Copy Talent, the Evolution trait would be practically useless! "My clone in the War Ancestor Realm is an old one and doesn't have the Evolution trait. I must dispel it!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, Ye Tian's clone informed the higher-ups of the War Ancestor Realm and then directly dispelled itself to avoid any misunderstandings.
Afterward, Ye Tian spent ten thousand years inside a time acceleration formation. Once his copying ability was available again, he cloned himself once more.
As expected, the Copy Talent did not disappoint. The new clone not only possessed the Holy Soul but also both Holy Soul traits.
Then, Ye Tian had the clone begin the Evolution process.
"Evolve!"
Ye Tian's target this time was the Flowing Light Holy Technique. Perhaps he could use this evolution to upgrade it directly to an Ancestral Technique.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian's Holy Soul Source began to drain, but countless flashes of inspiration and insights flooded his mind. Under the effect of Evolution, the Flowing Light Holy Technique was constantly being refined and improved, evolving toward a higher level.
Time passed slowly, and the consumption of Holy Soul Source grew.
Before he knew it, one percent of his Holy Soul Source had been consumed. This was already a significant amount, enough to affect one's foundation.
But Ye Tian ignored it and continued the evolution.
After a long time, ten percent of the Holy Soul Source was gone! Twenty percent! Thirty percent! Ye Tian kept evolving, not caring about this clone at all.
Soon, fifty percent of the Holy Soul Source was consumed.
If it were a normal soul, it would surely be on the verge of collapse. However, Ye Tian's Holy Soul possessed the Indestructible trait, so even if ninety percent of the source was consumed, it wouldn't crumble or die; it would only suffer irreversible soul damage.
After another period of time, over ninety percent of Ye Tian's Holy Soul Source was exhausted. The Indestructible trait protected his Holy Soul from being annihilated.
At this moment, Ye Tian finally opened his eyes and smiled.
"Ninety-three percent of the Holy Soul Source is gone, but I've finally realized the key to upgrading a Holy Technique to an Ancestral Technique. However, I'll need to clone myself one more time and use the Holy Soul Evolution again to be certain of evolving the Flowing Light Holy Technique into an Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Thus, he dispelled the clone himself. Once the copying ability recovered, he cloned himself again and began the second evolution.
This time, after consuming only seventy percent of the Holy Soul Source, Ye Tian's Flowing Light Holy Technique was deduced into the Flowing Light Ancestral Technique.
"The Flowing Light Holy Technique has become the Flowing Light Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian performed the Flowing Light Ancestral Technique; its speed was far greater than the Holy Technique version! In the following days, Ye Tian continuously used the Evolution trait to deduce his Holy Techniques. Under this process, one Holy Technique after another was transformed into an Ancestral Technique.
However, the Space and Time Holy Techniques couldn't be upgraded to Ancestral Techniques for the time being. This was because Ye Tian had copied too few Space and Time Holy Talents to transform them into Ancestral Talents. Without Ancestral Talents, even the powerful Evolution trait struggled to upgrade Space and Time Holy Techniques unless he spent a vast amount of time on them.
He didn't have the time to evolve them over and over again. Moreover, time within the Holy Soul Realm was precious and could not be wasted.
With the boost from various Ancestral Techniques, Ye Tian's strength became incredibly formidable. Combined with the second qualitative change of his Holy Soul, his combat power now rivaled a Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
A Paramount Domain Ancestor rivaling a Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor—no one would believe it if told, but Ye Tian had truly achieved it.
"Next, it's time to check out those forbidden areas recorded by the War Sect!"
Ye Tian ended his seclusion and murmured.
