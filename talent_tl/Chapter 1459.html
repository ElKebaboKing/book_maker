Chapter 1458: The Third Dark Holy Land, It's Simply Made For Me
!
As the news of the imminent birth of the third Dark Holy Land spread, the entire Dark Holy Domain was in a frenzy.
The third Dark Holy Land—this was a brand-new holy land comparable to the Dark Holy Domain itself. The key was the countless resources within; it was absolutely the stuff of dreams for innumerable cultivators.
Moreover, while such a holy land was of great importance to the Ancestors, it was also an incredibly precious opportunity for ordinary Domain Ancestors. If they could enter the third holy land and seize some resources, they might be able to rise to prominence.
For instance, those Invincible Domain Ancestors who had been stuck at that level for countless ages might find a supreme treasure within the third holy land that could forcibly help them break through their shackles and promote them to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
As for physical body control, they could completely bypass it for now. Once they became Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, they could slowly master their bodies later; that was also an option.
Furthermore, many Invincible Domain Ancestors didn't care about foundations or the like; they would be satisfied just to become Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
In short, once the third Dark Holy Land was born, the endless Domain Ancestors of the entire Dark Holy Domain would all want to fight for a chance at fortune.
On this day.
The War Gate opened a meeting. It was initiated by the two Ancestors of the War Gate, and all Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were required to participate.
Thus, Ye Tian arrived at the War Ancestor World immediately.
Inside the War Ancestor Hall of the War Ancestor World.
One by one, Supreme-level Domain Ancestors appeared, and Ye Tian entered as well.
Here, he saw several familiar figures, all of whom were Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the War Gate, as well as some Half-step Ancestors.
"Fifty-eight Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and thirteen Half-step Ancestors... is this the true strength of the War Gate?"
Ye Tian was quite surprised.
This was more Supreme-level Domain Ancestors than had participated in the previous invasion of the Silent Clan, which showed that some had not taken part last time.
After all, any Saint Gate needed to maintain its strength and could not afford to send everyone out at once.
Moreover, Ye Tian was certain that the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors here were likely not all of them. There might be others roaming the Dark Forbidden Zone, or perhaps some were being kept hidden.
Therefore, the total number of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and Half-step Ancestors in the War Gate was definitely over a hundred.
If one Saint Gate possessed over a hundred Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, then among the ten Great Saint Gates of the Dark Holy Domain, the number of experts at or above the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor rank would be over a thousand. Adding in the other holy lands, loose organizations, and rogue Supreme-level Domain Ancestors...
It could be said that there were at least two thousand experts at or above the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor rank in the Dark Holy Domain, perhaps even more.
Meanwhile, the Dark Destruction Domain had many more Supreme-level Domain Ancestors than the Dark Holy Domain. After all, it was the first Dark Holy Land and had been born in a much more ancient era, so it likely had three to four thousand Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
Adding in the scattered Supreme-level experts across the Dark Forbidden Zone...
The total number of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors in the entire Dark Forbidden Zone was at least ten thousand.
Yet last time, when all the major forces moved out, they had only mobilized a little over three thousand Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
"During the last invasion of the Silent Clan, all the major forces were hiding their strength. They really are playing it safe!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
If the third Dark Holy Land was born this time, the number of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors in the entire Dark Forbidden Zone would likely increase significantly again.
Soon, the meeting began.
The one speaking was the World Ancestor, as he was the strongest.
"I have summoned you all here today to discuss the birth of the third Dark Holy Land."
The World Ancestor said solemnly, "When the third Dark Holy Land is born, many worlds will be created. Of course, it's estimated that Ancestor Worlds won't be born at the start, and the range won't be too large.
Furthermore, due to the restrictions of the laws, Ancestors are not allowed to enter the Holy Land at the beginning. Even Supreme-level Domain Ancestors will have no way to enter.
According to convention, during the first hundred million years, only Half-step Domain Ancestors are allowed to enter.
After a hundred million years, and within a trillion years, only those below Peerless Domain Ancestors are allowed to enter.
Within one Domain Year, Supreme-level Domain Ancestors will be allowed to enter. As for the Ancestors, it will likely take a hundred Domain Years before they can enter the third Dark Holy Land.
If no Ancestor is born within the third Dark Holy Land within those hundred Domain Years, it will be successfully invaded by us, and we will occupy it.
But if an Ancestor is born there, we will be unable to invade and can only snatch some resources.
Just like the Dark Holy Domain back then—if the Saint Ancestor hadn't become an Ancestor within a hundred Domain Years, our Dark Holy Domain would have also fallen and become a part of the Dark Destruction Domain."
"World Ancestor, why does the birth of a single Ancestor protect the Holy Land? The Dark Destruction Domain and the Dark Holy Domain have many Ancestors, and with the strength of the Saint Ancestor or the Destruction Ancestor, they should be able to instantly kill an ordinary Ancestor!"
A Supreme-level Domain Ancestor asked curiously.
The other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors also looked toward the World Ancestor. They were not privy to this secret and were equally curious.
"Because the first Ancestor born in a Dark Holy Land becomes the Lord of the Holy Land. They can mobilize the power of the Dark Holy Land to exert the combat power of a Strongest Ancestor.
Of course, once they leave the Dark Holy Land, their strength immediately reverts to its original form.
Whether it is the Saint Ancestor or the Destruction Ancestor, although their strength is very close to a Strongest Ancestor, they are still not quite there. Therefore, they cannot kill a Lord of the Holy Land within their own domain!"
The World Ancestor explained.
"So that's how it is!"
Many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors came to an understanding.
At this moment.
Ye Tian was internally very excited.
When the third Dark Holy Land is born, other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors cannot enter, and neither can the Ancestors.
However, he could enter!
Once he copied the body of a Half-step Domain Ancestor, he could enter the third Dark Holy Land. At that time, who could compete with him? Wouldn't the greatest benefits there belong to him? Moreover, he could rapidly increase his cultivation in that third Dark Holy Land and might even have the hope of becoming an Ancestor there.
If he didn't care about his foundation, he really could quickly cultivate to the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor rank and then rely on that Space Ancestor Law to become an Ancestor.
The Space Ancestor Law was an Ancestor Law he had obtained, and it was very complete. Furthermore, the Ancestor who created this Space Ancestor Law had fallen, so he could completely use that law to become an Ancestor.
However, his main body looked down on the Space Ancestor Law, as it was someone else's law. Relying on such a law to become an Ancestor would make it very difficult to become a powerhouse like the Saint Ancestor in the future.
But this was just a copied body; it wouldn't affect his main body.
Furthermore, if a copied body became an Ancestor, it would even benefit his main body. At the very least, he would understand the mysteries of an Ancestor, providing insights for when he eventually created his own Ancestor Law to become an Ancestor in the future.
It could be said that the birth of the third Dark Holy Land was a massive opportunity practically tailored for him, and perhaps even a great opportunity for the rise of the Human Race.
