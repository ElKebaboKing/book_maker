Chapter 1441: Entering the Realm of Time Hundreds of Thousands of Times!
!
In the Dark Holy Region, there were many such sinful Domain Ancestors.
Ye Tian went to the Dark Holy Region and spent some time capturing a large number of evil Domain Ancestors. He sealed them within his internal world, bestowed Ancestral Talents upon them, copied their bodies, left a mark within the Copy Talent, and then reclaimed the Ancestral Talents.
This was also one of the abilities of the Copy Talent.
However, when Ye Tian bestowed his talents upon his own relatives, he naturally would not take them back.
Therefore, this was the first time he had ever performed such an act of reclaiming a talent.
In this manner, Ye Tian bestowed almost all of his talents and then took them back.
By doing this, he could copy many people's bodies, with each body possessing one or two Ancestral Talents.
On this day.
Ye Tian arrived at the War Ancestor Realm, at the entrance of the Time Hall.
Having slain a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, Ye Tian's status within the War Gate had soared, making him equivalent to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
Therefore, he ordered the two Domain Ancestors stationed at the Time Hall to leave; for the coming period, he would personally guard the Time Hall.
They did not dare to disobey Ye Tian's orders and complied accordingly.
Entrance to the Time Hall:
Ye Tian sealed off the area and then created a copied Domain Ancestor body.
This copied body possessed the Space Ancestral Talent. Thus, after entering the Realm of Time, this body specialized in practicing the Void Slash and Void Escape Ancestral Techniques, continuing until it was nearly assimilated by time before exiting.
"Go in!"
Ye Tian's copied body stepped into the Realm of Time. Ye Tian took responsibility for the energy consumption required to operate the Realm of Time, pouring a vast amount of various energy treasures into it to sustain its operation.
Boom! As soon as the copied body entered the Realm of Time, it began its cultivation, constantly comprehending and training the Void Slash and Void Escape Ancestral Techniques.
An unknown number of billions of years passed. When this copied body was nearly half-eroded by time, it withdrew.
Otherwise, if the copied body did not come out, the Realm of Time would remain isolated from the outside world, and the insights could not be brought out.
As soon as the copied body emerged, countless insights flooded in.
Boom! Ye Tian gained a much deeper understanding of the Void Slash and Void Escape Ancestral Techniques. In reality, for the main body, only a single moment had passed, yet he could significantly increase the realm of these techniques. This was incredibly cost-effective.
However, even after billions of years of hard cultivation in the Realm of Time, raising an Ancestral Technique to the Great Success stage was no easy feat, let alone reaching the Perfection stage.
Furthermore, his body within the Realm of Time lacked the Evolution trait, so he had no way to use it to deduce techniques. The speed of cultivation was naturally extremely slow, relying entirely on the accumulation of endless years.
"It's fine, I'll just condense another copied body!"
Ye Tian manipulated time, constantly accelerating it. While only a short while passed outside, Ye Tian lived through ten thousand years, and the Copy Talent could be used again.
Subsequently, Ye Tian copied another body and entered the Realm of Time.
In this way, one body after another entered the Realm of Time to comprehend the Void Slash and Void Escape Ancestral Techniques.
Ten copied bodies! A hundred copied bodies! It took several hundred copied bodies to finally train the Void Slash and Void Escape Ancestral Techniques to the Perfection stage.
Afterward, Ye Tian began practicing other Ancestral Techniques.
Once he ran out of copied bodies, he would go capture more Domain Ancestors, bestow talents, copy their bodies, and then kill them.
Using this method, Ye Tian's Ancestral Techniques reached the Perfection stage one by one.
"Hundreds of thousands of Domain Ancestors now!"
During this period, Ye Tian had captured hundreds of thousands of Domain Ancestors, and their fate was naturally death.
The Dark Holy Region did not lack Domain Ancestors. Hundreds of thousands of them didn't even cause a ripple there. Moreover, he only killed those who deserved to die, so he did not attract the attention of others.
He copied bodies hundreds of thousands of times and entered the Realm of Time hundreds of thousands of times.
Although not much time had passed in the outside world, Ye Tian had indeed spent an unimaginably long time within the Realm of Time. Even with his mental state, he had nearly collapsed.
Had it been back when he was a Peak Domain Ancestor, he wouldn't have dared to do this; his soul definitely wouldn't have been able to withstand such a long period of continuous comprehension.
But now his Holy Soul was incredibly powerful, and his mental state was naturally high, which was why he dared to do it.
Even so, Ye Tian felt incredibly mentally exhausted.
Fortunately, his efforts were not in vain. Almost all of his Ancestral Techniques had been trained to the Perfection stage.
Now, his strength had increased to a terrifying degree.
If he were to face a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor like the Golden Star Holy Ancestor, he could kill them in just a few moves.
"I can't continue practicing for now. I need to travel the Dark Holy Region for ten million years, enjoy myself everywhere, and relax my mind!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Thus, in the following years, Ye Tian spent his time eating, drinking, and having fun, completely forgetting about cultivation and everything else.
Sometimes, he even sealed his own memories and strength, living as an ordinary person.
Just like that, tens of millions of years passed.
In a small city within the Dark Holy Region.
Ye Tian stood in a small courtyard, his heart filled with peace.
At this moment, he unsealed his memories. His heart was no longer restless, nor did he feel any resistance toward cultivation.
"I've finally recovered. I can't do that next time. Besides, the Realm of Time is no longer useful to me!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He took a step forward, left the place, and soon returned to the War Ancestor Realm.
Then, he left an avatar in the War Ancestor Realm. As for his copied bodies, he would not leave them here. He still needed to use them for repeated evolution and deduction of body control in his future cultivation, so it wasn't convenient to leave them here.
His avatar's combat power was comparable to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. While it might not match the Fire Pattern War Ancestor's avatar, it certainly wouldn't be much weaker.
After Ye Tian returned to the Profound Void Holy Realm, he continued to master his body.
He had only mastered his body by a few dozen times; he was still far from 365-fold body control. Only by completely mastering his body 365 times could he break the limit of the Invincible Domain Body and complete one Invincible Domain transformation. After nine transformations, it would evolve to the level of a Supreme-level Domain Body.
In the blink of an eye, billions of years passed.
After such a long period of hard cultivation and countless evolutions of his copied bodies, he finally raised his body control to an extremely high level.
And he was now only one step away from 365-fold body control!
