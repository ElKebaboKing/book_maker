Chapter 1506: Resource Scramble!

Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect.
This was the most powerful of the three major holy sects in the Holy Domain World. It possessed five Supreme Holy Beasts, one of whom had reached the second level of the Supreme Holy Beast realm. There were even more Holy Beasts below the Supreme level, numbering several times more than those of the Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
Furthermore, the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect occupied the region with the most abundant resources in the Holy Domain World.
Currently, the experts from the outworlds had not yet moved to occupy the territory of the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect. This was a form of tacit understanding, or even a kind of cooperation; they would first wipe out the territories of smaller factions.
It could be said that during the early or middle stages of the Great Tribulation, the three major holy sects faced almost no danger.
At this time, although the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect was preparing for war, the overall atmosphere remained peaceful.
On this day.
The Violent Ape Holy Beast arrived at the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect and was immediately sensed by the sect's Supreme Holy Beasts.
"Violent Ape, what are you doing here?"
Voices enveloped the Violent Ape Holy Beast from all directions.
The Violent Ape Holy Beast immediately pleaded, "Wan Yuan Holy Beast, the Gate of our Sea-Sky Holy Sect has been occupied by a Supreme Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Domain. The opponent entered through the Gate and, in cooperation with our sect's He Yuan Holy Beast, killed the Wolf Bone Holy Beast. Now, the Sea-Sky Holy Sect has fallen into his hands. That Ancestor has reached the second level of the Supreme Ancestor realm. While he hasn't yet fully established his foundation, I ask the Wan Yuan Holy Beast to help me drive him away. Otherwise, if he establishes a Forbidden Land in our Holy Domain World, we will be in deep trouble!"
Swish!
Figures emerged one after another, all of them massive exotic beasts. They were the Supreme Holy Beasts of the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect.
One of them had six eyes and was covered in black scales.
This was the Wan Yuan Holy Beast.
"Violent Ape, you are truly useless, letting someone seize even your Gate. But right now, we cannot help you. A second-level Supreme Ancestor plus a Supreme Holy Beast is a very powerful force. If we go to war with them, what if outworld experts invade our Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect? We cannot take such a risk for your Sea-Sky Holy Sect. After the Great Tribulation, the opponent must leave. At that time, we will help you kill He Yuan Holy Beast and reclaim the Sea-Sky Holy Sect."
Wan Yuan Holy Beast suggested.
"Fine!"
The Violent Ape Holy Beast agreed helplessly.
Without the help of the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect, he could not kill He Yuan Holy Beast on his own.
In the days that followed, the Violent Ape Holy Beast stayed within the Ten Thousand Beasts Holy Sect.
...
After Ye Tian occupied the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land, he dispatched a large number of humans to renovate it. Even the various Ancestors of the human race were sent to guard the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land.
One of Ye Tian's avatars also stayed near the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land. If any top-tier Ancestors from the Dark Forbidden Domain came to invade, he would naturally be able to take action immediately.
Subsequently, a human Ancestor frequently entered and exited the Gate, bringing back vast amounts of resources from the Sea-Sky Holy Sect.
The total amount of resources possessed by the Sea-Sky Holy Sect was beyond the human race's imagination.
The human race had only existed for a few Dark Eras, while the Sea-Sky Holy Sect had existed for several Great Tribulation cycles. They had even invaded the Dark Forbidden Domain before, and the resources in their treasury were an astronomical figure.
Moreover, the Sea-Sky Holy Sect's territory was vast, with many resource-rich lands. Ye Tian now sent people to mine them extensively, transporting everything back to the human race.
After all, once the late stage of the Great Tribulation arrived, the Sea-Sky Holy Sect might not be able to be held. He naturally wanted to bring the resources back to the human race.
At the same time, the many Holy Arts and other cultivation techniques and secret methods owned by the Sea-Sky Holy Sect were also brought back to the human race, almost turning the Sea-Sky Holy Sect into an empty shell.
The duration of the Great Tribulation was not long, lasting a total of one Domain Year.
In the first hundred billion years, outworld forces would not invade the three major holy sects. Thus, Ye Tian took this opportunity to aggressively seize resources from other factions, naturally doing so under the identity of an outworld expert.
Consequently, Ye Tian's duplicated body personally took action, crossing one territory after another to seize the resources of many factions. If anyone resisted, he would kill them directly.
Countless resources and countless ancient texts all fell into Ye Tian's hands.
During this period, he also encountered some outworld experts. Everyone maintained a tacit understanding and did not fight, as now was not the time for conflict.
The entire Holy Domain World was filled with wailing, allowing Ye Tian to witness the horror of the Great Tribulation.
This was only the Holy Domain World; because it possessed several Supreme Holy Beasts, it could still preserve some territory. If it were the Dark Forbidden Domain, the past was even more terrifying, with everything being wiped out directly.
Year after year passed. By the later stages, basically all the relatively large factions had been annihilated, and the resources of the Holy Domain World began to grow scarce.
Even so, Ye Tian did not waste any resources. Even those useless to him were collected; at the very least, they could be used to nurture experts of the human race.
Gradually, a hundred billion years passed.
A hundred billion years was indeed not long, but it marked the transition into the middle stage of the Great Tribulation.
At this time, it was highly likely that Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals would be born in the Holy Domain World.
As for where the Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals would appear, it was hard to say; it depended on luck.
Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals would be born within a short period, and because the Holy Domain World was so vast, even those with weak cultivation might obtain one, while powerful Supreme Holy Beast-level experts might not obtain a single one.
Of course, once an Origin Rule Tribulation Crystal was obtained, unless no one saw it, the news would leak, and the possessor would be hunted by a large number of experts. Without sufficient strength, it was very difficult to keep the crystal.
On this day.
News arrived that a top-tier Holy Beast rogue cultivator in the Holy Domain World had obtained an Origin Rule Tribulation Crystal and was currently being pursued.
-------------------
In a silent void filled with the scent of blood.
A top-tier Holy Beast resembling a minotaur was hiding inside a small world, completely concealing its aura and diving deep beneath the rocky earth of the small world.
Normally, hiding like this would make it very difficult to be discovered.
However, this was the Great Tribulation.
BOOM!!!!
An outworld expert comparable to a Supreme Holy Beast arrived here. Seeing this solitary cluster of small worlds, he struck out with a wave of his hand. Countless small worlds were shattered, and this stretch of space-time was pulverized. Many hidden spaces also vanished into nothingness.
Naturally, the countless living beings inside were annihilated.
Under such an attack, that top-tier Holy Beast could no longer hide and was exposed.
"Hahaha, I didn't expect you to be hiding here. You can't run now!"
The outworld expert said excitedly.
CRACK!!!!
The top-tier Holy Beast was heavily injured by a single strike from the opponent, and the Innate Holy Artifact on its body was directly shattered.
After the second strike, this top-tier Holy Beast was killed.
Just as he was about to collect the corpse of the top-tier Holy Beast, another outworld expert appeared.
Instantly, a battle between outworld experts broke out.
