Chapter 1626 All Killed!

Ye Tian blocked these five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings, which instead made them stunned.
“Endless Ancestor, you won’t let us leave? Ridiculous, could it be that you still have the confidence to keep us here?”
The five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings were very puzzled. Although Ye Tian’s strength was somewhat stronger than theirs, it was not that much stronger.
If they wanted to leave, Ye Tian simply could not stop them.
What right did Ye Tian have to stop and threaten them?
“Endless Ancestor, move aside immediately!”
The five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings said coldly.
“You actually dare to threaten the Dark Forbidden Domain and me, then perish here. I hope you have the ability to create incarnations, otherwise today you will really die!”
After speaking, Ye Tian made his move.
This time, he did not hold back, but displayed all of his combat power in full.
Boom!!!!!!
The perfected Endless Eternal Body fully burst forth with power, and one after another extremely powerful Eternal arts were also unleashed.
Buzz buzz buzz!!!!
Countless terrifying sword qi tore apart the laws and slashed toward the five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings. It was clearly the Eternal art, Void-Splitting Sword, unleashed with full force.
The current Void-Splitting Sword had been further perfected by Ye Tian and was even more terrifying than before, and it had been cultivated to a very profound level.
The power of this sword had already reached the level of the weakest half-step Eternal Realm power.
The five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings blocked this sword with all their might, yet they were still severely injured.
“Power of half-step Eternal Realm!”
The expressions of the five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings changed drastically.
How could a Seventh-Level Supreme Being unleash power comparable to a Ninth-Level Supreme Being?
Although it was the power of the weakest half-step Eternal Realm, it could not be compared with those top half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses at all, but they still could not defeat it!
They were not those monstrous geniuses whose Ninth-Level Supreme Being strength was enough to match half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses!
“Run!”
This thought immediately arose in the minds of the five Ninth-Level Supreme Beings.
At once, they used their trump cards and fled at the fastest speed.
For example, one Ninth-Level Supreme Being took out a top-grade source artifact that could pierce through the Source Sea void and cross the void over long distances, thereby escaping from here.
Another Ninth-Level Supreme Being used a secret treasure that could temporarily increase speed a hundredfold, but it could only be used once.
In short, they all tried every possible means to save their lives.
If they stayed here, they would not survive at all.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian also used his own trump card and unleashed the Destroying Finger, activating the power of the Eternal Realm finger bone.
Immediately, a strike comparable to a powerful half-step Eternal Realm directly shattered the Source Sea void. A large area collapsed and shattered on the spot, and three Ninth-Level Supreme Beings who did not escape in time were directly crushed into fragments, their vitality completely erased.
A single strike killed them instantly, and none of their trump cards could save their lives.
This was the difference between an ordinary top Ninth-Tier Peak powerhouse and combat power at the level of a powerful half-step Eternal Realm; the gap was simply too great.
At this time, another Ninth-Level Supreme Being was severely injured, and one more had pierced through the void and escaped.
“I already said, you cannot escape!”
Ye Tian murmured.
After another Destroying Finger strike blasted the heavily injured Ninth-Level Supreme Being to death, he immediately activated the Void Source Hall, locked onto the other party’s position, and began the pursuit.
……
Somewhere in the Source Sea.
The last of the five great Ninth-Level Supreme Beings, Qi Xue Source Ancestor, used a source artifact to pierce through the void and arrive at a certain region, then finally breathed a sigh of relief.
He felt that Ye Tian would not be able to catch up to him in time; by the time Ye Tian reached here, he would already have left.
“Leave this place first!”
Qi Xue Source Ancestor was just about to depart.
Suddenly, a powerful force appeared and suppressed this stretch of void.
Then Ye Tian’s body stepped out from the void.
“How could this be…”
Qi Xue Source Ancestor was stunned.
He had already roughly guessed why Ye Tian had caught up so quickly: he must have possessed some special source artifact or other treasure that could perform long-distance void teleportation and lock onto a position.
Moreover, during their battle, Ye Tian had silently locked onto the positions of all five of them, so when he used the source artifact to flee, he was already locked on.
Otherwise, Ye Tian would not know where he had teleported to, and would not be able to pursue him.
“So from the very beginning, he wanted to kill all five of us!”
Qi Xue Source Ancestor trembled inwardly.
“Endless Ancestor, I’ll tell you who wants to deal with you, just don’t kill me!”
Qi Xue Source Ancestor begged for mercy.
“No need, I can find out!”
Ye Tian activated the Destroying Finger and killed Qi Xue Source Ancestor with one strike.
After returning to the Dark Forbidden Domain, Ye Tian entered the Source God Pavilion and requested to meet Feng Gu Source Ancestor.
Very quickly, Feng Gu Source Ancestor met Ye Tian.
“Endless Ancestor, what do you seek me for?”
Feng Gu Source Ancestor asked.
Ye Tian hurriedly said, “Lord Feng Gu Source Ancestor, I want to ask, if there is a conflict between inner circle members of the Source God Pavilion and they kill each other, will the Source God Pavilion intervene?”
Feng Gu Source Ancestor understood Ye Tian’s meaning and directly said, “If an inner circle member asks for help, the twelve ancestors will make one move, but only one move; this is the welfare treatment of every inner circle member.”
“Feng Gu Source Ancestor, I understand!”
Ye Tian slightly nodded, then left immediately.
The twelve ancestors would make a move, but the premise was that Feng Gu Source Ancestor and the other twelve ancestors received the distress message and could arrive in time.
What if that inner circle member did not have time to ask for help?
Therefore, relying on the protection of the Source God Pavilion was of no real use at all.
For other Supreme Beings, the Source God Pavilion was just a loose organization; it was not bad for trading, but it would not truly help a member free of charge.
Afterward.
Ye Tian contacted other inner circle members and used some treasures and resources to exchange for a piece of information: which member had a relationship with Jinlan Mountain?
This intelligence was not hard to investigate, so Ye Tian naturally found it.
Among the many inner circle members, there was only one member belonging to Jinlan Mountain.
This person was Ge Fei Source Ancestor!
Ge Fei Source Ancestor was a Ninth-Level Supreme Being of Jinlan Mountain, and also one of its founders.
After receiving the news, Ye Tian immediately came to Jinlan Mountain.
Source Sea, Jinlan World.
This was the Eternal Heaven and Earth, while Jinlan Mountain was the ruling force of the Jinlan Heaven and Earth.
When Ye Tian descended here and used his copying talent to investigate Jinlan Mountain, he did not find Ge Fei Source Ancestor.
Immediately afterward, he captured a Supreme Being from Jinlan Mountain and interrogated him.
In the end, these Supreme Beings also could not contact Ge Fei Source Ancestor.
Clearly, Ge Fei Source Ancestor had run away.
Ye Tian did not kill these Supreme Beings of Jinlan Mountain. They were unaware, and killing them was useless, but Ye Tian still seized Jinlan Mountain’s treasury, counting it as their punishment.
In addition, if Ge Fei Source Ancestor was found in the future, he would pursue and kill him immediately, giving the other party no chance to survive.
Somewhere in the Source Sea void.
Ge Fei Source Ancestor appeared here, and at this moment he looked flustered and helpless.
“Damn it, the Endless Ancestor’s strength is actually so terrifying. Even Qi Xue Source Ancestor and the others have fallen. Luckily, I escaped fast enough, otherwise I would have been dead for sure!”
“I can’t return to Jinlan World in the future, and I also can’t enter the Source God Pavilion. I must find a place to hide.”
Ge Fei Source Ancestor muttered to himself.
Very quickly, he disappeared from this area, without a trace.
