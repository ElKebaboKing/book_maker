Chapter 1580: The Second Growth of the Endless Source Physique!

"Artifact Spirit, I remember the Void Source Palace purchases Devouring Source Dragons for 100,000 points each. Why are they so expensive? Do Devouring Source Dragons have some special use?"
Ye Tian asked the Artifact Spirit.
After all, he hadn't heard the name of the Devouring Source Dragon in the Artifact Spirit's report just now, so he was curious. Could it be that the Void Source Palace hadn't managed to purchase a single Devouring Source Dragon?
The Artifact Spirit replied, "Master, the value of the Devouring Source Dragon is very high. It can devour alien worlds and the origin of Eternal Source Objects, and it is closely related to the Void Source Monument. The Void Source Monument is an Eternal Source Object that was eventually born after a long period of evolution in a place where a large number of Devouring Source Dragons were killed in ancient times. The Void Source Monument has always been provided for the Sons of the Void to comprehend, and it consumes its own origin in the process. Devouring Source Dragons can quickly replenish the origin consumption of the Void Source Monument. It is precisely because the Void Source Palace has been purchasing Devouring Source Dragons that the origin of the Void Source Monument has always remained above ninety percent."
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian realized.
"What about Void Source Crystals? Why does the Void Source Palace purchase this treasure?" Ye Tian asked again.
The Artifact Spirit explained, "The operation of the Void Tower requires Void Source Crystals. The interior of the Void Tower contains a grand array that can continuously condense Tribulation Beasts, and the energy consumed is provided by Void Source Crystals; otherwise, the Void Tower would not be able to operate. Additionally, Void Source Crystals have another important use that few people know about: they can allow the Void Source Physique to undergo a second growth. However, a large quantity is required; otherwise, they are of little use."
"The Void Source Physique can undergo a second growth?"
Ye Tian was surprised.
"Yes!" the Artifact Spirit said. "According to the previous master's settings, one must undergo the second growth of the Void Source Physique to have any hope of clearing the third floor of the Void Tower. However, Master is an exception, having actually passed the third floor of the Void Tower before the Void Source Physique had even undergone its second growth. If Master completes the second growth of the Void Source Physique, your combat power at the same level will even surpass that of the previous master."
"So the combat power standard set by the Void Source Ancestor for the third floor of the Void Tower was the requirement for the Void Source Physique to complete its second growth!"
Ye Tian understood.
No wonder he felt the requirements for the third floor of the Void Tower were so perverted; he had spent so much effort and time to pass, and there was even a hint of luck involved.
If he had known about this method earlier, he might have...
"Even if I had known this method earlier, I wouldn't have been able to collect so many Void Source Crystals!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
"Artifact Spirit, roughly how many Void Source Crystals do I need to complete the second growth of my Endless Source Physique?" Ye Tian asked.
The Artifact Spirit came over, examined Ye Tian's body, and said in surprise, "Master, your Endless Source Physique is much stronger than an ordinary Void Source Physique. The number of Void Source Crystals required will likely be quite high; I estimate you will need more than fifty thousand. The Void Source Palace has been collecting Void Source Crystals to prepare for the Master. Currently, there are over one hundred and thirty thousand Void Source Crystals, which is enough for Master to complete the second growth!"
"To think it requires more than fifty thousand Void Source Crystals!"
Ye Tian was stunned.
Previously, he had sent his duplicated bodies to roam for many years and only managed to obtain a single Void Source Crystal by luck. Now, he needed more than fifty thousand Void Source Crystals to allow the Endless Source Physique to complete its second growth. Without relying on the Void Source Palace, he would have been fundamentally unable to let the Endless Source Physique undergo its second growth.
"I'm afraid even the top-tier forces would find it difficult to collect so many Void Source Crystals. Only the Void Source Palace is special, having collected them over countless ages to amass such a quantity," Ye Tian murmured.
Immediately, he ordered the Artifact Spirit to bring all the Void Source Crystals.
He wanted to absorb the Void Source Crystals to complete the second growth of the Endless Source Physique. Once completed, his strength would undergo another qualitative change.
"Yes, Master!"
The Artifact Spirit entered the treasury of the Void Source Palace and brought all the Void Source Crystals.
"Artifact Spirit, you may leave for now. I will be in secluded cultivation here. Do not disturb me unless there is something important!"
Ye Tian commanded.
"Yes, Master!"
The Artifact Spirit left.
Origin Space.
Ye Tian was in secluded cultivation here, activating his Endless Source Physique to absorb the Void Source Crystals one by one.
One, two, three...
The Void Source Crystals were absorbed and refined by Ye Tian one after another, but the Endless Source Physique showed no change, and his strength did not increase.
If the Artifact Spirit hadn't told him that absorbing more than fifty thousand Void Source Crystals would complete the second growth of his Endless Source Physique, he wouldn't have absorbed and refined them like this!
It could be said that even if a certain Son of the Void happened to obtain so many Void Source Crystals, without knowing the reason, they would never dare to absorb and refine them in this manner. Otherwise, if they were wasted, it would be enough to cause heart-wrenching pain!
Time slowly passed, and in the blink of an eye, one Dark Era had gone by.
Over such a long period, Ye Tian had refined a full 50,000 Void Source Crystals. The second growth had not yet arrived, but he could already feel the Endless Source Physique becoming somewhat stronger.
"I estimate it will take more than 50,000 Void Source Crystals. The Artifact Spirit was only giving an estimate and couldn't be precise down to a specific number," Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he continued to absorb and refine the Void Source Crystals. In any case, he had many Void Source Crystals and was not afraid of the consumption.
51,000! 52,000! 53,000!
It wasn't until the 60,001st Void Source Crystal that Ye Tian's Endless Source Physique finally underwent a fundamental, essential transformation.
Boom!!!!!
The Endless Source Physique began to frantically absorb origin energy, and energy from the Source Sea also flooded into Ye Tian's body, helping him complete the second growth.
After an unknown amount of time, Ye Tian's transformation was complete.
At this moment, his physical body and soul were more than ten times stronger; this was the second growth.
It could be said that although Ye Tian was still at the second-tier Invincible realm, his strength was enough to easily insta-kill those first-tier Invincibles. Even those first-tier Invincibles who had already condensed an Eternal Tribulation Physique were no match for him.
Ye Tian felt that his strength was even sufficient to contend with weaker Supreme Beings. This was the strength increase brought about by the second growth of the Endless Source Physique.
"Congratulations, Master, on completing the second growth of your physical body!" The Artifact Spirit appeared and congratulated him. "Master, when the previous master completed the second growth of the Void Source Physique, the increase in physical body and soul was sevenfold. Master's physical body and soul increase has reached at least elevenfold. In the future, Master will definitely be even more powerful than the previous master, and there is even hope of stepping into a realm above the Supreme Being."
"No need for praise. The realm above the Supreme Being is too far away for me. Right now, I only want to advance to the first-tier Invincible realm!"
Ye Tian said.
At this time, the Artifact Spirit suggested, "Master, for a second-tier Invincible to become a first-tier Invincible, the Physical Tribulation Realm and the Soul Tribulation Realm must merge. Since Master has completed the second growth of the Endless Source Physique, your physical body and soul far exceed those of other second-tier Invincibles, making the merger more difficult. The Void Source Palace does not have a suitable treasure land for Master to charge into the first-tier Invincible realm, but there is a treasure land in the Source Sea that is very suitable for Master and can save Master a lot of time."
"What place?"
Ye Tian asked curiously.
The Artifact Spirit said, "Yin-Yang Qi Mountain!"
