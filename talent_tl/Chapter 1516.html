Chapter 1515: Relatives Become Supreme Primordials!

Creating Primordial Laws was not difficult for Ye Tian. Moreover, the Primordial Laws practiced by his relatives—Xiao Yue, Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, and Ye Yu—were all created by him. Therefore, he understood their laws perfectly. He only needed to create corresponding Primordial Laws based on their bodies and Primordial Bodies combined with Otherworld rules.
Previously, Ye Tian had already created a batch of Primordial Laws and fused them to create his own Endless Primordial Law.
Now, he only needed to modify them slightly to make them compatible with his family members before passing these Otherworld Primordial Laws to them.
When the time came, they would follow the steps, using the Otherworld Primordial Laws to ascend to the Primordial realm once more on a second path. After reaching the level of Top-tier Primordials, they would combine the two Primordial Laws and fuse them to become Supreme Primordials.
In just a few dozen realm years, Ye Tian created Primordial Laws that were perfectly suited to his relatives' Primordial Bodies.
Subsequently, Ye Tian taught the four Primordial Laws to them one by one, guiding their cultivation and providing a massive amount of resources.
Under Ye Tian's guidance and the supply of vast resources, Xiao Yue and the others used the Otherworld Primordial Laws to step into the Primordial realm again on their second path. Shortly after, they returned to the Top-tier Primordial level.
With both Primordial Laws elevated to the Top-tier Primordial level, their strength increased significantly, already surpassing other Top-tier Primordials.
Next, they pushed both Primordial Laws to the absolute limit of the Top-tier Primordial level. They only needed to fuse the two laws to break through to the Supreme Primordial realm—the realm that countless Primordials dreamed of.
With Ye Tian's guidance, even though they hadn't created the laws themselves, it wouldn't take them too long to advance to the Supreme Primordial realm.
If Ye Tian hadn't helped, it might have taken them several Dark Eras to reach that level.
But with his guidance, it wouldn't even take one Dark Era.
As time passed, countless cultivators throughout the Dark Forbidden Zone began to grow anxious because news of the Great Calamity was spreading.
The current Dark Forbidden Zone was no longer what it once was. The number of Primordials had increased tenfold, and the Human Race had even more, reaching triple digits. Among them were those who had advanced by creating their own Primordial Laws, as well as those who used the laws Ye Tian had created.
With hundreds of Primordials, such a number was enough to make any force find it difficult to compete.
The Human Race no longer took other forces seriously; their only goals were the other Otherworld Heavens and Earths.
Furthermore, the Human Race possessed the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth as a resource point, allowing them to obtain many resources. Consequently, the Human Primordials had ample resources, and one by one, they ascended to the Top-tier Primordial level. Some had even surpassed the Destruction Primordial and the Saint Ancestor.
Finally, with less than ten realm epochs remaining before the Great Calamity, Ye Tian's wife, Xiao Yue, was the first to advance to the Supreme Primordial realm.
Following her, Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, and Ye Yu successively advanced to the Supreme Primordial realm.
At this point, the Human Race possessed four Supreme Primordials.
If categorized by power, the Human Race could at least be rated as a Ranked-tier force. Moreover, with a powerhouse like Ye Tian present, as long as he did not fall, even those Renowned-tier forces would not dare to offend the Human Race.
...
On this day.
The heads of many forces outside the Human Race held a meeting together. The leaders were the Destruction Saint Clan and various Saint-Gate level forces from the Dark Holy Lands, such as the Saint Ancestor.
The current strengths of the Destruction Primordial, Spacetime Primordial, World Ancestor, and Saint Ancestor were also much greater than in the past. They had reached the peak of the Top-tier Primordial realm, not inferior to those ancient Primordials.
Unless it was a Top-tier Primordial possessing a Supreme Ancestral Artifact, they could not be easily crushed.
Supreme Ancestral Artifacts were like the Supreme Imperial Weapons of the Bone Clan; their value was incredibly high. Even for a Supreme Primordial, they were quite precious.
Ye Tian had wiped out the Sea-Sky Holy Sect and, combined with the various materials he had collected, had only managed to refine a few Supreme Ancestral Artifacts in total.
Therefore, aside from the Human Race, other forces in the Dark Forbidden Zone did not possess Supreme Ancestral Artifacts. To them, such items were legends and not counted as regular combat power.
"Destruction Primordial, what is the main purpose of this meeting? The Great Calamity is approaching. Although none of us have experienced it, based on the limited intelligence available, the calamity is terrifyingly horrific. Even for Primordials, it is a narrow escape from death. We must be prepared!" a Top-tier Primordial asked.
The Destruction Primordial smiled and said, "What preparation? When the Great Calamity arrives, our strength will be like that of ants. Unless we hide, but even then, it is easy to be found; it almost entirely depends on luck. If your luck is good, you can survive by hiding. If your luck is bad, you die instantly!"
"Perhaps if we unite, we might have a chance to survive!" an Elite Primordial suggested.
"Unite? No matter how we unite, we cannot contend with a single Supreme Primordial. And when the Great Calamity comes, do you know how many Supreme Primordial-level experts there will be? Even those ancient Primordials who survived one calamity still don't dare to show their faces, fearing their hiding spots will be exposed. Uniting is useless. Currently, there are only two ways!" the Destruction Primordial said.
"What ways?"
The many Primordials were curious.
The Destruction Primordial said, "The first way is to seek refuge with an Otherworld force. If they accept us, we have a hope of surviving. The second way is to find a place we believe is very hidden, such as a special spacetime node, and then use ultimate concealment-type Ancestral Techniques. If luck is on our side, we won't be discovered. But Supreme Primordials are very powerful; whether we can hide from them, I cannot say for sure."
Seek refuge? Hide?
They had thought of these two methods, but no one had spoken them aloud.
Now, the Destruction Primordial had said it.
"Neither of these two methods seems very reliable!" a Primordial remarked.
"Naturally they aren't reliable, but there's no other way. Who told us to be too weak to advance to the Supreme Primordial realm?" the Saint Ancestor sighed.
"What about the Human Race?"
Suddenly, a Primordial asked.
The Human Race?
Currently, the Human Race had many Primordials and was the most powerful force. The Myriad Law Primordial was even more mysterious and had almost stopped interacting with them; they hadn't seen him for a very long time.
Rumor had it that the Myriad Law Primordial had reached the Supreme Primordial realm, but it couldn't be confirmed.
"World Ancestor, the Myriad Law Primordial is one of the Primordials of our War Gate. Has he actually advanced to the Supreme Primordial realm? Does the Human Race have the confidence to survive this calamity?" the Spacetime Primordial looked at the World Ancestor and asked.
The World Ancestor smiled bitterly. "The Myriad Law Primordial stopped contacting us much a long time ago. Although he was once a member of the War Gate, his core is with the Human Race. The Human Race is so powerful now, how would we dare to treat him as just a member of the War Gate anymore? Therefore, we don't know much about the Human Race or the Myriad Law Primordial."
The Saint Ancestor said, "World Ancestor, you can contact the Myriad Law Primordial and ask how the Human Race plans to deal with this calamity. If the Human Race is confident in surviving, I am willing to join them!"
"Yes, yes, yes, we are also willing to join the Human Race!"
One Primordial after another spoke up quickly.
With the Great Calamity approaching, they were all afraid of death and wanted to live.
"I'll ask then," the World Ancestor said helplessly.
