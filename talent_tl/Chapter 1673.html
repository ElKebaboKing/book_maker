Chapter 1672: The True Void Legend Tower!

Chapter 1672: The True Void Legend Tower!【Please subscribe in full】

After Ye Tian and Star-Observing Sword Ancestor sat down, Ye Tian began exchanging dao insights with Star-Observing Sword Ancestor, which was beneficial to both sides.
Very soon.
Both sides gained something from the exchange. For example, in Ye Tian’s research on will, Star-Observing Sword Ancestor was not as strong. After all, Ye Tian had carried out countless experiments using his replicated bodies, thereby cultivating the secret art known as the Will World, so he naturally had unimaginable comprehension of will.
In addition, Ye Tian’s comprehension of Eternal Skills was also far beyond what Star-Observing Sword Ancestor could compare with.
However, Star-Observing Sword Ancestor surpassed Ye Tian in compressing half-step eternal power and in the development of the Eternal Body, which benefited Ye Tian greatly.
“Star-Observing Sword Ancestor, I have now passed the enhancement period of the Nine Extremes Stage. At present, I want to improve the fusion of will and body, but it is very difficult to improve. Apart from using Void Beast materials, are there any other ways to increase the degree of fusion?” Ye Tian asked sincerely.
“Of course there are!” Star-Observing Sword Ancestor said. “In fact, I also didn’t know before that Void Beast materials could help the body and will fuse. In the past, there were roughly two ways to speed up the fusion of body and will. The first was to use some rare resources to accelerate the fusion of will and body, but such resources were too rare, and once they appeared, those Immortal Realm lords would take action to seize them. The second way was to challenge the True Void Legend Tower!”
“True Void Legend Tower?”
This was the first time Ye Tian had heard of such a place, so he was somewhat puzzled.
Then Star-Observing Sword Ancestor said, “The True Void Legend Tower is one of the most mysterious places in the Time-Space Territory. It is located within a relic and is only open to Eternal Realm cultivators above the Immortal Realm. However, Nine Extremes Stage cultivators are also qualified to enter, because Nine Extremes Stage cultivators can step into the Eternal Realm at any time, and their combat power is not weaker than that of ordinary Immortal Realm cultivators, so they are also allowed to enter the True Void Legend Tower. There are a total of ninety-nine floors in the True Void Legend Tower. Every time you clear one floor, you can obtain the chance to enter the True Void Legend Land to cultivate, and the True Void Legend Land has a mysterious sacred tree—the True Void Dao Mark Tree. The True Void Dao Mark Tree can rapidly fuse our will and body. If one were to cultivate beside the True Void Dao Mark Tree all the time, there would even be hope of stepping into the Legendary Lord realm. Unfortunately, the time we can cultivate there is too short. Right now, I have only cleared the ninth floor and obtained dozens of Tribulation Eras of comprehension time.”
“The True Void Legend Tower is actually this mysterious!”
Ye Tian was quite surprised, and at the same time a little eager, wanting to go in and take a look.
Without Void Beast materials, the True Void Legend Tower was now the treasure land where he could rapidly promote the fusion of body and will.
The relic housing the True Void Legend Tower was sealed off layer by layer by the Lord Alliance. It was said that the true bodies of the six legendary chairmen usually cultivated in that relic.
Therefore, no lord dared to forcibly break into the relic privately; otherwise, it would be courting death.
Ye Tian also did not need any credential. With his Nine Extremes Stage cultivation, he could just go over directly and would not be stopped.
And the location of that relic had also been shared with Ye Tian by Star-Observing Sword Ancestor.
Not long after, Ye Tian left the Eight Extremes Tower and went directly to the relic where the True Void Legend Tower was located.
-
“Formation!”
Ye Tian sensed an incomparably huge formation in the Void Region; this formation directly enveloped this area.
Clearly, this was a great formation set up by the Lord Alliance, meant to conceal this relic.
Ye Tian passed through this formation and sensed strands of will crossing the Void Region to probe this place, but these wills did not linger on him. If it had been an Eight Extremes Stage cultivator, it would probably have triggered the suppression of these wills.
Very soon, Ye Tian arrived at the relic’s location.
“So this is the relic!”
Ye Tian saw a shattered Void Region. The key point was that this area was so badly shattered, yet the Void Region’s restoration ability was unable to restore it?
One must know that even when a Legendary Lord fought a fifth-tier Void Beast and shattered the Void Region to an extreme degree, the Void Region could still be restored to its original state in a short time.
But this could not be restored. Just from this alone, one could tell that this relic was too mysterious.
Within the shattered Void Region, countless building fragments drifted about, but there were still quite a few intact buildings.
And in the most central area, an ancient tower stood there.
If Ye Tian had not guessed wrong, that tower was the True Void Legend Tower.
He walked into the relic, and a terrifying force acted on him, as if it wanted to crush his body.
Ye Tian resisted with all his might before he finally held it back.
“I finally understand why only Nine Extremes Stage and Eternal Realm cultivators above the Immortal Realm are allowed to enter here!”
Ye Tian suddenly understood.
If an Eight Extremes Stage or Immortal Stage cultivator dared to come here, they simply would not be able to withstand the suppression here. And the opportunities in this relic were indeed too precious; if there were no restrictions, many Immortal Stage lords or Eight Extremes Stage cultivators would probably risk entering here.
Therefore, the Lord Alliance had made such restrictions.
Moreover, Eight Extremes Stage and Immortal Stage cultivators entering the True Void Legend Tower would not be of much use, as they were not yet at the level where body and will could be fused, and with their strength, it would also be very difficult for them to clear many floors of the tower.
Ye Tian walked within this relic, as if the surrounding void were countless shattered mirrors, and his body traversed this shattered void, from time to time enduring impacts from the void fragments.
After a long while, he finally reached the True Void Legend Tower.
After entering here, the suppression on his body disappeared.
At the very least, the void inside the True Void Legend Tower was normal; otherwise, there would be no way to challenge the tower.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian entered the True Void Legend Tower and soon arrived at its first floor.
Buzz！！！！
Five Eternal Realm figures wearing black battle armor appeared in the True Void Legend Tower, and their cultivation was all at the level of ordinary Immortal Realm cultivators, yet they gave off a very unusual feeling.
Their coordination was perfect, but their strength was still far too far below Ye Tian’s.
Very quickly, Ye Tian defeated them.
After all, these Eternal Realm figures were not true Eternal Realm cultivators. Once their physical bodies were shattered, they could not recover. Otherwise, it would have taken Ye Tian a bit more effort to deal with them.
Second floor!
Third floor!
Fourth floor!
Ye Tian advanced all the way through, and only when he reached the seventh floor did he feel pressure.
The seventh floor still had only three Immortal Realm lords, but they had reached the peak of the Immortal Realm lord level, and they had also mastered will-attack methods. These were not ordinary will-attack methods, but will Eternal Skills!
This was an Eternal Skill Ye Tian had never seen before!.
