Chapter 1679 Two Cultivation Systems, the Legendary Realm!

The ruin world.
This place was in complete disrepair, but it had not been utterly drained; there were still quite a few resources here.
Thus, Ye Tian began collecting resources here.
It had to be said that although the resources here were scarce, their quality was very high. In no time at all, Ye Tian had gathered enough resources to condense many clone bodies multiple times.
So, he immediately condensed the other two clone bodies.
As for the remaining clone body, he left it hidden in the outside world, so that if he could not get out, all the clone bodies would not be trapped here. After all, the clone body outside had no resources left on it and could no longer condense any other clone bodies.
This day.
Ye Tian discovered a corpse, and it was actually the corpse of a powerhouse from the Eternal Stage.
It was the corpse of a woman, wearing battle armor, but the armor was already extremely damaged. The area where she had been was directly turned into nothingness and still had not recovered to this day. Moreover, there was a three-inch sword wound on her body, piercing straight through her.
That was only the visible wound.
Ye Tian’s divine sense probed over and saw that every particle in her body had lost all power of will, and every particle retained traces of sword aura. Clearly, she had been killed by a powerful Eternal Stage sword cultivator using an Eternal sword technique, erasing both her power of will and her life.
“Huh!”
Ye Tian cried out in surprise.
He discovered that this corpse was somewhat special. Although the other party had already fallen, from the corpse one could still see some traces of her cultivation.
Perhaps other Eternal Stage experts would not be able to see the situation of this corpse, but he could.
This corpse had definitely once fused innate Eternal rules with will; he had done the same, so he could sense it.
From the aura emitted by the corpse, this was merely the corpse of a Lord at the Immortal Stage, yet it had achieved the fusion of will and innate Eternal rules. Clearly, the system she cultivated was not the system of Source Sea and the Time-Space Territory, but rather the cultivation system behind this relic, the True Void Legend Pagoda.
Perhaps within that system, a Half-Step Eternal Stage cultivator could attempt to fuse will with innate Eternal rules, and many Eternal Stage cultivators would possess will Eternal techniques.
“This corpse is highly valuable to me. I can study it carefully; it will be of immense help to my fusion of will and innate Eternal rules!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he took away this corpse.
Unfortunately, there were no treasure resources on her body. Clearly, after she was killed, the resources on her person had already been taken away.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian found quite a few corpses in this ruin world, all of them corpses of Eternal Stage cultivators, and all of them bore traces of the fusion of will and innate Eternal rules.
Therefore, Ye Tian took all of these corpses away.
Ye Tian searched this ruin world for a long time, but never saw a living Eternal Stage cultivator, and even living beings were very few. There were only some Half-Step Eternal Stage lives, but unfortunately they were trapped in this ruin world, with no path forward, and thus could never break through to become Eternal Stage.
At last, this day arrived.
Ye Tian came to the center of the ruin world, where he saw a field of ruins.
The battle here back then must have been incomparably tragic. Ye Tian even sensed many auras that made him tremble in fear; he did not even dare to approach them.
Moreover, there were also many fragments of Eternal Stage corpses here, such as a finger bone, an arm bone. Over the long years, these corpse fragments lost the support of power, and because they were not complete corpses, they turned into one treasure land after another.
For example, one of the mountain ranges was actually formed from a section of bone.
It had to be said that because so many Eternal Stage experts had fallen here, the resources born from it were extraordinarily abundant.
Thus, Ye Tian began collecting the resources here.
A long time later.
Ye Tian had collected all the resources that could be gathered here, which could be considered a great harvest.
After that.
He began to take the risk of venturing deep into this battlefield ruin. What he most wanted to collect was inheritances; even the most worthless inheritance could at least provide him with some guidance, and at the very least save him countless years of research.
However, there were far too many terrifying traces here. Some of them had existed for countless Eternal years, and could instantly kill Ye Tian outright.
According to Ye Tian’s speculation, the existence that left behind such terrifying traces must have surpassed the level of a legendary Lord. If one slowly comprehended and cultivated here, enormous benefits could be obtained.
Of course, Ye Tian had no mind to slowly comprehend things here right now.
He sent one clone body deep into the shattered void, into the depths of the battlefield, where he saw countless void fragments and palace fragments, and felt deeply moved.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian triggered a force, and a tiny thread rose up in the void, directly sweeping across Ye Tian’s body.
The next moment.
Ye Tian’s clone body directly disintegrated, collapsing without the slightest ability to resist.
Outside the battlefield ruins.
Ye Tian sensed that his clone body had fallen, and immediately condensed another clone body, sending it once again into the battlefield ruins.
Just like that, Ye Tian tried again and again. Even though he fell many times, he still penetrated deep into the battlefield ruins.
-
Somewhere in the battlefield ruins.
A finger bone floated there, and a ring was worn on the finger bone, with a void orb embedded in the ring.
However, this void orb was also severely damaged.
“There’s actually still a void orb left behind!”
Ye Tian was excited.
Even no matter how damaged such a void orb was, perhaps a small piece of void heaven and earth still remained intact within it, which meant there would be some good things inside.
Thus, he took this finger bone away and returned by the same route, fearing that some accident would occur and cause the finger bone and void orb to be lost.
Very soon.
The clone body returned, and Ye Tian began to stabilize the void before slowly opening the void orb.
Boom!!!
A small patch of broken void appeared in Ye Tian’s perception, but this broken void seemed as though it could shatter at any moment.
Ye Tian extracted all the treasures from this broken void at the fastest speed, and the void orb, influenced by Ye Tian’s power, completely shattered.
“There are quite a lot of treasures!”
Ye Tian said joyfully.
Clearly, these things were rather crude. Back then, the victor of the great battle must have overlooked this void orb, otherwise it would not have fallen to him.
This should be the treasure of an Immortal Stage Lord, and only a small portion of it at that. Therefore, for Ye Tian, these resources were not too many, but they were not few either, enough to greatly increase his net worth.........
But what Ye Tian truly cared about was that there were also quite a few inheritances and ancient texts here; these things were the most precious to him.
Ye Tian slowly accepted these inheritances and browsed through some of the ancient texts, gradually obtaining quite a lot of things.
“Jingxu!”
From the inheritances and reference texts, it could be understood that this Immortal Stage Lord came from a place called Jingxu, and it was very likely that this was this ruin world.
In addition.
Ye Tian also learned about the other party’s cultivation system, which indeed differed somewhat from Source Sea.
For example, after cultivating to the Half-Step Eternal Stage, they could attempt to fuse will with Eternal rules or innate Eternal rules. Once the fusion reached a certain degree, they could break through to the Eternal Stage.
And they divided the Eternal Stage into will stage, Immortal Stage, Immortal Stage, legendary stage, Legendary Realm, and other realms.
If they wanted to cultivate to the legendary stage, they had to turn all particles in their entire body into will-rules, with every body particle containing will-rules power formed by the fusion of will and innate Eternal rules.
On the other hand, here in the Time-Space Territory, only after will and the body were 100% fused could one charge toward the realm of legendary Lord.
However, in Jingxu’s side, an Eternal Stage cultivator who wanted to charge toward a Legendary Realm Lord had to completely fuse will-rules power with the body, and this step was extremely difficult.
“I never expected that in the end, their cultivation system would still walk our path, and our path, after reaching the legendary Lord stage, must then walk their path. In that case, once everyone reaches the Legendary Realm, it will be the same. The only difference is that they have the method of fusing will with the body, while we do not have the method of fusing will with innate Eternal rules!”
Ye Tian muttered.
He also obtained a secret technique inheritance that could be used to attempt the fusion of will and innate Eternal rules. This secret technique was called the Chenxu Technique!
However, after Ye Tian’s clone body cultivated it on the Time-Space Territory side, he found that this secret technique was useless.
Suddenly, Ye Tian understood something.
“Different heavens and earth!!!”
Ye Tian thought.
On the Source Sea side, the path of fusing will with innate Eternal rules was something that vastly surpassed the heaven and earth where Jingxu existed, and on Jingxu’s side, the path of fusing will with the body was probably also extremely difficult.
This was the result brought about by different heavens and earth.
“Looks like I must become an Eternal Stage expert by taking the path of fusing will and body first, and only after that, use will power and innate Eternal rules to completely fuse and reach the Legendary Realm.” Ye Tian thought.
Although he did not gain much this time, he did clarify the path ahead.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian continuously searched for treasures in this Jingxu battlefield ruin, while his clone bodies remained here, constantly comprehending and cultivating.
Gradually, the good things Ye Tian obtained grew more and more numerous, and some of them even helped improve the fusion of will and innate Eternal rules.
In the blink of an eye, one thousand Eternal years passed just like that.
This day.
Constellation Sword Ancestor came to inform Ye Tian that he was preparing to break through to the Eternal Stage and wanted Ye Tian to observe.
