Chapter 1410: Weeping Blood Emperor Arrives, Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor Appears
!
Seeing that Saint Lord Qingfeng had agreed to his request, the Weeping Blood Emperor immediately smiled very happily.
For him, this was just a small task, easily accomplished.
Once completed, he would obtain the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus and Phoenix Blood Soul Fruit he had long dreamed of.
In the past, he had tried to trade with Saint Lord Qingfeng, but unfortunately, Saint Lord Qingfeng didn't have them, because the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus and Phoenix Blood Soul Fruit were indeed extremely rare, almost impossible to find in the outside world, and he couldn't produce the treasures Saint Lord Qingfeng needed.
Therefore, he had never obtained these two treasures from Saint Lord Qingfeng.
“Then let's make a trip to the Profound Void Saint Realm!”
The Weeping Blood Emperor murmured.
Immediately, he took out a treasure—the Myriad Void Star Leaf! This was a relatively rare treasure that allowed one to travel through the void at an extremely terrifying speed, but because it was a one-time consumable item, its value wasn't too great; an ordinary sacred artifact could basically be exchanged for ten such Myriad Void Star Leaves.
So, he didn't feel any heartache.
Immediately, the Weeping Blood Emperor activated the Myriad Void Star Leaf.
Boom!!!! The Myriad Void Star Leaf transformed into a ball of light, enveloping the Weeping Blood Emperor. In an instant, the Weeping Blood Emperor traversed endless void, crossing the Dark Saint Domain in the blink of an eye, then passing through the River of Taboos, flying towards the Dao Mountain Domain.
At the Weeping Blood Emperor's speed, if he maintained it, he could reach the Dao Mountain Domain in just a few days.
However, a few hours later, the power of the Myriad Void Star Leaf was exhausted.
Thus, the Weeping Blood Emperor replaced it with another Myriad Void Star Leaf and continued forward.
Just like that, one Myriad Void Star Leaf after another was consumed. If it were any other Domain Ancestor, they would probably be heartbroken, but the Weeping Blood Emperor felt no pain at all.
He also wanted to rush to the Dao Mountain Domain as quickly as possible. Saint Lord Qingfeng had told him the detailed situation. If he arrived a step late, causing Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng to fall, then he would have gone for nothing, and wouldn't even dream of getting the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus and Phoenix Blood Soul Fruit.
Therefore, wasting more Myriad Void Star Leaves didn't matter.
For him, the value of the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus exceeded that of more than ten ordinary sacred artifacts, perhaps even far more than ten.
In the blink of an eye, three days passed.
On this day.
The Weeping Blood Emperor arrived at the Dao Mountain Domain.
“This domain is extraordinary, even better than some of the large domains I've visited!”
The Weeping Blood Emperor murmured.
He wasted no time, traversing at the fastest speed to the Profound Void Saint Realm.
Profound Void Saint Realm.
Ye Tian was still: fighting with those two invincible Domain Ancestors. At this moment, these two invincible Domain Ancestors were already severely wounded, but still hadn't fallen.
It must be said that invincible Domain Ancestors were too difficult to kill. Even if his combat power was much stronger than these two invincible Domain Ancestors, it would still take a considerable amount of time to kill them.
Therefore, he had to wear down these two invincible Domain Ancestors until they died.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian's expression changed drastically as he sensed the arrival of a terrifying aura.
As the master of the Profound Void Saint Realm, Ye Tian's perception extended beyond just the Profound Void Saint Realm. After all, the entire Dao Mountain Domain had undergone transformation under the influence of the Primordial Well of Creation, so Ye Tian could perceive some situations in the Dao Mountain Domain.
What's more, this powerful individual was not... concealing his aura, and was very close to the Profound Void Saint Realm. Ye Tian couldn't help but sense him! “An invincible Domain Ancestor, and one far surpassing Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng!”
Ye Tian said gravely.
Such an invincible Domain Ancestor, even he couldn't defeat! Regarding... the classification of invincible Domain Ancestors, he also had some understanding.
He estimated that his strength could at most be considered a top-tier four-step invincible Domain Ancestor, and this was only with the blessing of the Profound Void Saint Realm. If he didn't rely on the power of the Profound Void Saint Realm, he would at best be a six-step invincible Domain Ancestor.
And the newcomer definitely surpassed a four-step invincible Domain Ancestor, likely possessing the strength of a three-step invincible Domain Ancestor.
“According to the investigation by the Replication Talent, Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng should be from the Azure Origin Holy Land. Could the newcomer be a three-step invincible Domain Ancestor from the Azure Origin Holy Land?”
Ye Tian speculated.
However, he wasn't certain.
Rip!!!! The world barrier of the Profound Void Saint Realm was torn open like paper by a bloody figure.
That bloody figure instantly locked onto the battlefield of Ye Tian and the two invincible Domain Ancestors, stepping across to descend into this area.
The moment the Weeping Blood Emperor appeared, one supreme Domain Ancestor after another almost stood up.
“Oh no, it’s the Weeping Blood Emperor!”
“Weeping Blood Emperor, why is he here?”
“We're doomed, the Weeping Blood Emperor isn't going to kill us, is he?”
For a time, everyone was terrified.
Even Domain Ancestor Qingyu of the Battle Gate was extremely nervous.
If it were invincible Domain Ancestors from other forces, they wouldn't dare to attack them, but the Weeping Blood Emperor was different. This... invincible Domain Ancestor was a two-step invincible Domain Ancestor, and his clone was also a three-step invincible Domain Ancestor. He had killed countless disciples from major forces, and many disciples of the Saint Gate had also fallen by his hand.
Therefore, the Weeping Blood Emperor wasn't afraid of their identities at all, unless they were Ancestor disciples. Only then might the Weeping Blood Emperor be wary and not dare to act.
After all, if an Ancestor wanted to kill someone, even an invincible Domain Ancestor like the Weeping Blood Emperor would surely die, unable to escape or hide, unless he truly abandoned the Dark Saint Domain completely and fled into the remote areas of the Dark Forbidden Zone.
In that case, the Ancestors would not easily find him.
But if he truly offended an Ancestor, he would likely be intercepted before even escaping the Dark Saint Domain.
Domain Ancestor Qingyu was not... an Ancestor disciple, and therefore had no qualifications to make the Weeping Blood Emperor wary.
At this moment, the Weeping Blood Emperor didn't even look at Domain Ancestor Qingyu and the others, but instead looked at Ye Tian.
“A supreme Domain Ancestor actually crushed two five-step invincible Domain Ancestors, interesting!”
The Weeping Blood Emperor developed a strong interest in Ye Tian, and also a sense of jealousy.
If he possessed Ye Tian's heaven-defying talent, why would he still fear supreme-level Domain Ancestors! “First, complete the mission!”
The Weeping Blood Emperor made his move, intending to tear apart Ye Tian's Five Elements Seal to rescue both Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili.
Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili were also a bit bewildered. Looking at the Weeping Blood Emperor's stance, it seemed he was going to save them! But they didn't know the Weeping Blood Emperor! “Could it be that the Saint Lord asked the Weeping Blood Emperor to intervene?”
This thought arose in the minds of Domain Ancestor Wansheng and Domain Ancestor Mili.
For a time, they were overjoyed.
If the Weeping Blood Emperor made a move, they would definitely be rescued! A mere Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, no matter how heaven-defying, couldn't contend against the Weeping Blood Emperor! However.
Just then, a figure blocked the Weeping Blood Emperor.
“Weeping Blood Emperor, what are you doing?”
Seeing this figure, the Weeping Blood Emperor's expression changed drastically.
“Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, why is it you?”
The Weeping Blood Emperor said in disbelief.
Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor was an invincible Domain Ancestor of the Battle Gate, known as one of the Thirty-Six Battle Emperors of the Battle Gate.
What were the Thirty-Six Battle Emperors? They were the thirty-six most top-tier invincible Domain Ancestors of the Battle Gate, each with strength no less than a two-step invincible Domain Ancestor. Their strength was even greater than that of Domain Ancestor Xuegang.
Domain Ancestor Xuegang had a great reputation, but in reality, he was only a three-step invincible Domain Ancestor, and his status within the Battle Gate wasn't particularly high. He merely often handled some minor affairs of the Battle Gate, hence his high recognition within the Battle Gate.
As for Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, few people knew of him, as he was often in seclusion or undergoing trials in the outside world.
