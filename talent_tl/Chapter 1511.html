Chapter 1510: The Second Supreme Ancestral Technique!

"The Second-stage Gate has been slightly damaged. If we want to repair it, it will take a very long time. Until it is fixed, we cannot transform the Sacred Domain. Transforming the Sacred Domain must use the Second-stage Gate as its foundation," the White Turtle True Emperor said angrily.
"There’s no other way. We won't be able to completely occupy the Sacred Domain during this Great Calamity. We can only wait for the next one!"
another third-tier Supreme True Emperor said helplessly.
In short, the Blood Mist Alliance had miscalculated this time.
Originally, they felt that a mere Sacred Domain didn't have many powerful experts. By threatening other foreign forces, they believed they could completely wipe out the three major Sacred Sects within the Sacred Domain. Once the three major sects were destroyed, they planned to use a supreme treasure to radiate across the entire Sacred Domain, locate all the hidden Sacred Beasts, and slaughter them all.
Then, they would begin the process of transforming the Sacred Domain, continuously expanding the Blood Mist Forbidden Zone to cover countless territories.
Although they wouldn't be able to completely transform the Sacred Domain during this single Great Calamity, they could have at least transformed nearly half of it.
Once half was transformed, they could have resisted the rules of the Sacred Domain and seized a portion of its authority.
At that point, even outside the period of a Great Calamity, the True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance could enter the Sacred Domain without being erased by its rules.
If that happened, the Sacred Domain would effectively fall into their hands.
But now, all of this had been ruined.
Of course, they could still execute this plan again during the next Great Calamity and ensure its perfect completion, but delaying it by an entire Great Calamity cycle was a massive loss for the Blood Mist Alliance.
Once the White Turtle True Emperor returned, he would definitely be punished by the Leader of the Blood Mist Alliance.
"Forget it. Things have come to this. Repairing the Second-stage Gate takes too long; we won't be able to fix it before this Great Calamity in the Sacred Domain ends!" the White Turtle True Emperor sighed.
……
Ten Thousand Beasts Sacred Sect.
Ye Tian was still somewhat surprised that the Wanyuan Sacred Beast possessed such means, sacrificing its own life to dismantle the Blood Mist Alliance's plan and kill many of their Supreme True Emperors.
He could sense that if the Wanyuan Sacred Beast had targeted him, his duplicate body would have been instantly annihilated as well.
After all, his duplicate body was a creature of the Dark Forbidden Domain. Once the power of the calamity receded, he would also be erased by the rules of the Sacred Domain.
*If I also mastered this kind of technique, I would truly have nothing to fear from other foreign forces in the Dark Forbidden Domain.*
Subsequently, Ye Tian wanted to trade for this secret technique with the few surviving Supreme Sacred Beasts of the Ten Thousand Beasts Sacred Sect. Unfortunately, the other Supreme Sacred Beasts of the sect did not possess this technique at all; they didn't even know the Wanyuan Sacred Beast had such a secret skill.
Thus, Ye Tian could only give up on the idea.
With the Blood Mist Alliance suffering heavy losses, the experts from other foreign forces began to return one after another, continuing their search for Source Rule Calamity Crystals within the Sacred Domain.
Ye Tian returned to the Sea-Sky Sacred Sect. Although it had been turned into ruins and all the disciples of the Sea-Sky Sacred Sect had perished, he didn't particularly care about that.
He rebuilt a base, set up arrays, and with the Heyuan Sacred Beast as the core, established a new Sacred Sect. He no longer used the name Sea-Sky Sacred Sect, but instead named it the Profound Void Sacred Sect.
In the blink of an eye, more years passed.
By this time, the Great Calamity of the Sacred Domain had finally come to an end.
Before the calamity ended, the foreign experts departed one after another, passing through the gates to leave the Sacred Domain and return to their own foreign worlds. Otherwise, if they left too late, they might accidentally be erased by the Sacred Domain's rules, which would be a foolish way to die.
-----------------
Ye Tian's duplicate body returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain, while his clone, the Heyuan Sacred Beast, stayed behind to guard the Profound Void Sacred Sect and the forbidden area, keeping watch over the gate.
On the other side, the Sea-Sky Forbidden Zone in the Dark Forbidden Domain was once again managed by the human race. A large number of human experts were stationed there, and the place was transformed into a forbidden zone suitable for human cultivation.
Not long after, the fifth Dark Era of the Dark Forbidden Domain arrived, and the fifth Dark Sacred Ground was born.
This meant that in four more Dark Eras, the Dark Forbidden Domain would face its own Great Calamity.
For Ye Tian, the duration of four Dark Eras was still quite long; he had enough time to increase his strength.
With his abilities and talents, reaching the third tier of a Supreme Ancestor should not be a problem.
"Over such a long time, I have continuously searched for Ancestral Talents. Every Original Ancestral World within the Dark Sacred Grounds has an Ancestral Talent race. Combined with my last trip to the Sacred Domain, where my duplicate body copied many, many Ancestral Talents, all these talents have now been fused. This has allowed me to give birth to many Ultimate Ancestral Talents. For now, I don't need to worry about Ultimate Ancestral Talents. It is time to create several Supreme Ancestral Techniques and strive to step into the third tier of the Supreme Ancestor realm."
Ye Tian murmured to himself.
Soon after, he took out a Source Rule Calamity Crystal from the Sacred Domain and began to comprehend the rules of that world.
Then, his duplicate body began to burn Ancestral Talents, activating the Evolutionary Trait to deduce the Space Ultimate Ancestral Technique—Void Slash.
Void Slash was once an Ancestral Technique, but after he fused numerous Space Ancestral Talents into an Ultimate Space Ancestral Talent, he had recently deduced this technique to the level of an Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
Therefore, he only needed to create a version of Void Slash that was compatible with the rules of the Sacred Domain. Then, he could attempt to fuse the two versions of the Ultimate Ancestral Technique Void Slash to form a single Supreme Ancestral Technique: Void Slash.
Using the Evolutionary Trait to comprehend and create the Void Slash Ultimate Ancestral Technique, his progress was incredibly fast.
Before long, the duplicate body's Ancestral Talents were burned out, and it completely shattered.
Next, Ye Tian spent some resources to condense another duplicate body and once again used it to activate the Evolutionary Trait to create the Ultimate Ancestral Technique Void Slash.
"Such incredible speed. Back then, it took me ten Domain Years to create an Ultimate Ancestral Technique, but it took nearly an entire Dark Era to fuse two Ultimate Ancestral Techniques into a Supreme Ancestral Technique. Although that was created by the Heyuan Sacred Beast, whose talent is somewhat inferior, even if my main body were to create and fuse them, it would take at least dozens of Domain Epochs. Now, by sacrificing duplicate bodies and repeatedly activating the Evolutionary Trait, I estimate that in at most one Domain Epoch, I can fuse the two Ultimate Ancestral Techniques of Void Slash." Ye Tian thought with full confidence.
In a very short amount of time, an Ultimate Ancestral Technique compatible with the rules of the Sacred Domain was created by Ye Tian.
Next, he began to fuse the two versions of the Ultimate Ancestral Technique Void Slash, which was the real challenge.
As his duplicate bodies collapsed and were annihilated time and time again, finally, after many annihilations and the consumption of a vast amount of resources, approximately one Domain Epoch passed. Ye Tian finally fused the two Ultimate Ancestral Techniques of Void Slash, resulting in the Supreme Ancestral Technique: Void Slash.
When the Supreme Ancestral Technique Void Slash was created, Ye Tian's physical body fused with it, undergoing a massive transformation.
BOOM!!!!!
Ye Tian's physical body transformed, as countless resources were turned into energy to facilitate the process.
After a long time, the physical transformation was complete, and Ye Tian's strength had become even more powerful.
Possessing two major Supreme Ancestral Techniques, Ye Tian's current strength was likely very close to that of a third-tier Supreme Ancestor expert.
