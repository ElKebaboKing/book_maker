Chapter 1647 The Battle of the Eternal Realm!

Countless golden runes appeared, and under the control of the Xuan Jade Lord, they sealed this Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree.
Although the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree could unleash strange shocks, it was after all just a tree, not an Eternal Realm powerhouse, so it had no way to resist the seal of an Eternal Realm.
Very quickly.
The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree was sealed.
And at the very moment it was sealed, the strange shock instantly disappeared.
The Xuan Jade Lord immediately took away the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree, then prepared to flee.
“Xuan Jade Lord, hand over the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree!!!”
One Eternal Realm Lord after another blocked the Xuan Jade Lord.
Even though the Xuan Jade Lord was somewhat stronger than the other Eternal Realm powerhouses, he still could not withstand so many Eternal Realm powerhouses, and was thus blocked.
Rumble!!!!
A great battle erupted!
Buzz!!!!!!
The terrifying aftershock of the Eternal Realm clash swept in all directions, and countless treasures and resources were shattered into pieces by the aftershock of the Eternal Realm.
The place where Ye Tian was located was not far from where the Eternal Realm powerhouses were fighting, and he was affected immediately.
“The speed of the Eternal Realm powerhouses is too fast, right? They took away that golden tree so quickly. If they had been a little later, I could still have found some resources. What a pity!”
Ye Tian said helplessly.
At once, he swallowed his clone into his inner world, using the copied body to withstand the aftermath of the Eternal Realm battle.
In this way, even if the copied body was annihilated in the end, the clone would not be annihilated as well.
“Run!”
Ye Tian quickly moved through space, but his body was still struck by the aftermath of the Eternal Realm battle until it cracked open. Had it not been for the fact that the recovery power of the legendary Eternal Body, the Endless Eternal Body, was too powerful, even to some extent more terrifying in terms of life-preserving ability than a Seven Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm powerhouse.
Otherwise, he would have already been annihilated by that wave just now.
Not only Ye Tian was fleeing, but the other Peak powerhouses were also fleeing.
Ye Tian and the others had not yet escaped this stretch of the void-heaven and earth when it cracked apart.
And the golden path was also torn apart, with countless terrifying aftershocks sweeping through the world.
Some Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses watching from the outside were the first to bear the brunt, directly struck into ashes.
Boundless expanses of territory began to collapse, and some nearby alien heavens and even Eternal heavens could no longer withstand the aftermath of the Eternal Realm battle.
Although the rules of the Source Sea were protecting the alien heavens and the Eternal heavens, they still could not withstand the shock of Eternal Realm powerhouses.
Moreover, under such circumstances, traversing space was simply impossible.
At this moment, Ye Tian had already burst out of the Sea of Stillness, but he still had not moved far away from the aftermath of the Eternal Realm battle.
His physical body had already been torn apart quite badly, but overall there was still no major problem.
However, the reason he became like this was that he was too close to the Eternal Realm powerhouses, and had not received any information that they were making a move, so he had not fled immediately.
As for those Peak powerhouses from the Space-Time Territory, they had basically received the news in advance. Even if it was only one second earlier, that was enough to save their lives.
Therefore, those Peak powerhouses from the Space-Time Territory had hardly suffered any losses, but the Peak powerhouses from the Source Sea who had not taken refuge under the Eternal Realm Lords were miserable.
Very soon, Ye Tian’s copied body finally moved far away from the battlefield of the Eternal Realm battle.
Then the clone came out of the copied body’s inner world; it was also injured, but nothing serious.
After that, the clone returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain, and only then did it breathe a sigh of relief.
……
The Xuan Jade Lord was injured, and quite badly at that.
Surrounded and attacked by so many Lords, he simply could not hold them off.
“Are you really going to stop me? This is only my clone. One of you may not have a clone, so be careful—I might fight to the death and take one of you down with me!”
the Xuan Jade Lord threatened.
The Cloud Crane Lord smiled and said, “Xuan Jade Lord, threats are useless. The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree is far too valuable. Whoever gets it will rise. Do you think we’ll give up?”
Not a single Lord was willing to give up; all of them wanted to kill the Xuan Jade Lord’s clone and seize the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree.
Boom!!!!
The Xuan Jade Lord was blasted away by wave after wave of attacks, his physical body shattered, as if he were about to fall.
And at that moment, a figure tore through the void and descended, forcibly entering the battlefield.
He swung a sword across the sky, shattering many Eternal Realm attacks, then came to the Xuan Jade Lord’s side.
The Xuan Jade Lord thought that a powerful Lord had come to save him, but when he saw who it was, his expression changed.
“Void Sword Lord, you actually came!”
The Xuan Jade Lord’s expression changed drastically.
This person could not possibly have come to save him; there was only one possibility, and that was also for the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree.
The Void Sword Lord said nothing and struck with a sword.
The Xuan Jade Lord simply could not withstand it; no method was of any use.
Puff!!!!
This sword shattered the Xuan Jade Lord’s many defenses, annihilating every single cell completely, and even his will was utterly destroyed.
With just one sword strike, this clone of the Xuan Jade Lord was completely fallen.
Although it was because the Xuan Jade Lord was already heavily injured, even without being injured, three sword strikes would have been enough to kill him.
“Lord Void Sword, if you join in, isn’t that against the rules?”
Lord Linghe said somewhat unhappily.
The other Eternal Realm Lords also expressed dissatisfaction, believing that the Void Sword Lord should not have interfered.
Within the Lord Alliance, there was an unwritten rule: Eternal Realm Lords like the Void Sword Lord should not participate in the chance encounter that comes once every one hundred thousand eternal years.
This was an opportunity fought over by ordinary Eternal Realm Lords, and besides, it was of no use to Eternal Realm powerhouses like the Void Sword Lord.
Precisely because of this, they were qualified to snatch it; otherwise, where would they have the chance to obtain such an opportunity?
“This Lord has snatched it—what can you do about it?”
The Void Sword Lord snorted coldly, then took the sealed Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree from the corpse of the Xuan Jade Lord’s clone.
Throughout the entire process, none of the other Eternal Realm Lords dared to make a move, allowing the Void Sword Lord to take the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree away.
It was not until the Void Sword Lord had completely disappeared that these Lords voiced their anger.
“Damn it, the Void Sword Lord openly broke the unspoken rules. Too hateful!”
“The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree indeed has the qualifications to draw the attention of a strong being like the Void Sword Lord, but the Void Sword Lord definitely isn’t doing it for himself. The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree isn’t of much use to him; his Time-Space Lord has already been upgraded to an Eternal Time-Space Territory. He’s probably doing it for his little lover, Lord Qingxue.”
“Humph, Lord Qingxue’s talent isn’t good, yet he forcibly raised her to the Eternal Realm. Now he’s even prepared the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree for her—she may end up surpassing us.”
“There’s nothing we can do. Who told Lord Qingxue to hook up with the Void Sword Lord? At the Eternal Realm level, the only Lord who can still be influenced by love and affection is probably the Void Sword Lord.”
“Let’s go. Since we didn’t seize the opportunity this time, after we return, we’ll report the intelligence to the Lord Alliance. We must have the Lord Alliance sanction the Void Sword Lord.”
“The most miserable one should be the Xuan Jade Lord. He lost a clone and also lost the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree. He’s probably going to go mad. We must not provoke him recently!”
Very soon, these Eternal Realm Lords all left the Source Sea one by one.
And this area gradually returned to calm.
