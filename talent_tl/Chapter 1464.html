Chapter 1463: The Armies of the Three Great Evil Races are Completely Wiped Out!
!
Like other forces, Ye Tian also dispatched many top-tier human Domain Ancestors to the Dark Soul Realm. However, limited by the number of slots, he could only send thirty thousand top-tier Domain Ancestors.
Once these thirty thousand top-tier Domain Ancestors entered the Dark Soul Realm, approximately a small portion of them would be able to break through to the level of Supreme Domain Ancestor.
In this way, it could increase the strength of the humans in the Shifang Saint Realm and also alleviate some of Ye Tian's pressure.
After these top-tier Domain Ancestors flooded into the Dark Soul Realm, there was no internal fighting among them, but they encountered opposition from the local native forces.
After all, the native forces had also risen by now, and they possessed the home-field advantage, being favored by the providence of the Dark Soul Realm, making cultivation much easier. Moreover, many monstrous geniuses had been born among them, who were not at all weaker than the Domain Ancestors from the Dark Saint Domain, the Dark Destruction Domain, or the Eternal Cult.
After a period of fighting, neither side could do anything to the other, so they called it a day.
However, once these top-tier Domain Ancestors broke through to become Supreme Domain Ancestors, the Dark Soul Realm would usher in a new round of contention.
In the blink of an eye, another tens of millions of years passed.
At this time, the first batch of Supreme Domain Ancestors was born.
Outside the Shifang Saint Realm, groups of Domain Ancestors and Soul Ancestors arrived.
Leading them were three Saint-Talented ones from the evil races. One of them was... the Scorpion Holy Son, while the other two were from the Demon Prison Race and the Dream Race respectively.
To be able to lead the top-tier Domain Ancestors of the three races into the Dark Soul Realm, one could imagine how powerful their strength was. They were bound to far surpass ordinary people, belonging to the top ranks even among Saint-Talented ones.
Now, these three Saint-Talented ones had all stepped into the ranks of Supreme Domain Ancestors. Furthermore, with the treasures bestowed by their Ancestors, they had forcibly raised their cultivation to the peak of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm within the Dark Soul Realm.
Of course, if they wanted to condense Soul Saint Light and then condense a Saint Soul, that would require a long period of time.
If they didn't care about their foundation, the Ancestors also had ways to forcibly push them into the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
However, fighting for a temporary advantage... was useless. Even if a few Invincible Domain Ancestors were born, it wouldn't change anything.
Moreover, no Saint-Talented one was willing to waste their talent and destroy their own foundation to forcibly break through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor level.
This time.
The Scorpion Holy Son, also known as the Scorpion Domain Ancestor, and the other two Saint-Talented ones led tens of thousands of Supreme Domain Ancestors to the Shifang Saint Realm. Their goal was to clear out the humans and occupy the Shifang Saint Realm; this was also one of the orders given to them by the Ancestors.
"Xiefeng Domain Ancestor, Mingying Domain Ancestor, in a moment, the three of us Saint-Talented ones will strike together to assassinate the leader of the humans in the Shifang Saint Realm, Soul Ancestor Qingye. We must strive to kill him instantly."
The Scorpion Domain Ancestor proposed.
"No problem, this is but a simple matter. That Soul Ancestor Qingye was only a Half-step Domain Ancestor back then. Although his cultivation improved quickly after coming to the Dark Soul Realm and he is now a Supreme Soul Ancestor, his cultivation time is short. He has the cultivation level but likely hasn't mastered many powerful soul techniques. Even if he is a Saint-Talented one, he probably hasn't even mastered a single Saint Technique to the Perfected stage.
Furthermore, the power of Saint Techniques from the Dark Saint Domain drops significantly when used by a Soul Ancestor, and there are probably very few Saint Techniques suitable for Soul Ancestors. It's too late for him to learn them now.
Meanwhile, all three of us Saint-Talented ones have mastered two Perfected Saint Techniques. Killing a Supreme Soul Ancestor is naturally very simple!"
The Mingying Domain Ancestor said with full confidence.
"Still, be careful. This is an order issued by the Ancestors."
The Scorpion Domain Ancestor reminded.
The Mingying Domain Ancestor and the Xiefeng Domain Ancestor nodded. They then led the numerous Supreme Domain Ancestors and a large number of top-tier Domain Ancestors to attack the Shifang Saint Realm.
With their strength, they could simply crush their way through.
.... "Lord Soul Ancestor Qingye, a large number of ancestors are attacking our Shifang Saint Realm!"
A human Supreme Domain Ancestor reported to Ye Tian.
Soul Ancestor Qingye was... this cloned body. No one knew this was Ye Tian's clone, not even the humans.
Furthermore, Ye Tian was currently using entirely new methods, so no one could guess his identity.
"Let them enter the Shifang Saint Realm. When the time comes, you all activate the grand array of the Shifang Saint Realm. Do not allow a single person to escape!"
Ye Tian ordered.
"Lord Soul Ancestor Qingye, the opponent's numbers are too great, I worry..."
This human Supreme Domain Ancestor clearly didn't know Ye Tian's true strength, hence his concern.
"Rest assured, I can kill them all. You just need to assist!"
Ye Tian said indifferently.
"Yes!"
The Supreme Domain Ancestor nodded.
The Supreme Domain Ancestors and top-tier Domain Ancestors of the three evil races easily broke into the Shifang Saint Realm and then charged toward the human headquarters.
BOOM!!!!! The Shifang Saint Realm was sealed off, and human Supreme Domain Ancestors appeared one after another.
Ye Tian stood at the very front, his gaze directly meeting the Supreme Domain Ancestors of the three evil races.
Neither side spoke; they clashed immediately.
"Kill!"
The three Saint-Talented ones—the Scorpion Domain Ancestor, Mingying Domain Ancestor, and Xiefeng Domain Ancestor—targeted Ye Tian immediately.
RUMBLE!!!! Three Perfected Saint Techniques were unleashed, sealing off the void around Ye Tian from all directions.
"Soul Ancestor Qingye, be careful! Those are three Saint-Talented ones!!!!"
The other human Supreme Domain Ancestors cried out in worry.
However, Ye Tian gave a cold smile: "Three Saint-Talented ones? Merely ants!"
With a wave of Ye Tian's hand, countless golden threads of soul appeared.
"Saint Technique—Soul Golden Silk!"
This was not the Soul Golden Silk of the Perfected stage, but the Soul Golden Silk of the Limit stage.
Moreover, the Soul Golden Silk Saint Technique Ye Tian had created was a top-tier Saint Technique, very close to an Ancestor Technique.
However, Ye Tian had not yet created a Soul Golden Silk at the Ancestor Technique level. After all, it wasn't good to be too high-profile right now. Otherwise, with Ye Tian's attainment in practicing a large number of Perfected stage Ancestor Techniques, it wouldn't be difficult to push the Soul Golden Silk to the Ancestor Technique level.
Puff puff puff!!!! The three Saint-Talented ones were sliced apart, while their three Saint Technique attacks landed on Ye Tian's body, only to be blocked by Ye Tian's Spirit Light Saint Armor, failing to harm him in the slightest.
And the Saint Technique Spirit Light Saint Armor had also been cultivated to the Limit stage by Ye Tian.
"Not good, a Saint Technique at the Limit stage! How is this possible? How can the humans still have such a monstrous Saint-Talented one? Could he be a descendant of the Myriad Law War Ancestor?"
But they felt something was wrong.
The Myriad Law War Ancestor did indeed have descendants, but they were born before he became a Supreme Domain Ancestor, and none of them were Soul Ancestor Qingye! Although there were treasures in the Dark Forbidden Zone that could grant someone a Saint Talent, they were too rare, and the Saint-Talented ones created that way were definitely not as monstrous as true Saint-Talented ones.
Soul Ancestor Qingye had only become a Soul Ancestor for a hundred million years. What could such a short time achieve? Could it be that Soul Ancestor Qingye had already mastered Limit stage Saint Techniques when he was a Half-step Domain Ancestor? That was absolutely impossible; the Dark Forbidden Zone had never produced such a monstrous existence.
Then there was only one possibility: Soul Ancestor Qingye had found a treasure in the Dark Soul Realm that could rapidly assist in comprehending Saint Techniques, which allowed him to raise his Saint Techniques to the Limit stage in a short time.
At this moment.
The Scorpion Domain Ancestor and the others felt immense regret, but it was already too late.
Puff puff puff!!!! Under the Soul Golden Silk, the three Saint-Talented ones couldn't resist at all.
In a short while, they were all slain by Ye Tian.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian single-handedly swept through the armies of the three evil races. A large number of Supreme Domain Ancestors and top-tier Domain Ancestors were slaughtered by him alone.
Some Supreme Domain Ancestors and top-tier Domain Ancestors tried to escape, but they were trapped by the human Supreme Domain Ancestors and the grand array. Not a single one escaped.
With this, the armies of the three evil races were completely annihilated.
