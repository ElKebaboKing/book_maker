Chapter 1588: Infiltrating as a Member of the Ancient Star Race!

Boom!
Ye Tian charged directly at the great hall. The hall's defensive formation couldn't stop his attack at all.
Crack!
The hall's formations all shattered, and a large number of Ancient Star Race experts were instantly turned to ashes.
Three first-class Invincibles tried to resist Ye Tian, but they were struck into nothingness by a single blow. They couldn't withstand even a casual strike from him; the gap between the two sides was too great.
The entire hall was smashed by Ye Tian. His hands directly shattered the void, reaching into a treasury within the hall. He uprooted the treasury and took all the void spheres left by those first-class Invincibles.
"Go!"
Ye Tian directly tore through the barrier of the Starry Ancient Domain, intending to leave.
Not far away, that Invincible saw this scene and was incredibly furious.
"Damn outsider!"
He tried his best to stop Ye Tian, but his strength was insufficient, and he was once again sent flying by Ye Tian.
At this moment, Ye Tian finally succeeded in tearing the Starry Ancient Domain's barrier and left.
A few breaths after Ye Tian left, several Supreme Beings arrived, but unfortunately, they were a step too late.
Donghe, as one of the ten Supreme Beings, also arrived with a dark expression.
When he heard that a first-class Invincible could wound a Supreme Being with one blow, he realized this person had the strength to kill an ordinary Supreme Being!
Even a genius like the Void Divine Son definitely didn't have such combat power.
"The opponent is likely the most monstrous genius from the outside world. How does such a genius dare to enter our Starry Ancient Domain?" Donghe was very puzzled.
But now that the opponent had left, he couldn't kill them even if he wanted to.
......
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian returned and began to organize his gains from this trip.
He opened the treasury and quickly found six portions of Eternal resources. Combined with the five portions he had obtained previously, he had gained a total of eleven portions this time.
The Void Source Hall itself had sixteen portions of Eternal resources, and he had obtained three portions from the Dragon Pool.
In other words, he now possessed a total of thirty portions of Eternal resources.
With thirty portions, Ye Tian didn't need to worry about Eternal resources for the time being. Once his cultivation reached the Limit of a first-class Invincible and his Boundless Source Body reached its current Limit, he could start condensing the Boundless Eternal Tribulation Body.
"The Starry Ancient Domain and the Ancient Star Race hold a great opportunity, perhaps even greater than the Void Source Hall. Now that I don't lack Eternal resources, I can investigate this opportunity!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He had already made a plan. He prepared to replicate the body of a weak Ancient Star Race member and send it into the Starry Ancient Domain to rise step by step. This way, he wouldn't be discovered. Otherwise, directly replicating the body of a first-class Invincible would definitely be exposed quickly.
Because replicating the body of a living first-class Invincible would be easily detected. If he replicated a fallen first-class Invincible, it would be as if the person had suddenly resurrected. The Ancient Star Race wasn't stupid and would naturally think he was an impostor.
Therefore, only by replicating a very weak Ancient Star Race member would he avoid drawing attention.
Previously, during the battle on an Ancient Star Race planet, many weak members were affected, and some had perished. He planned to replicate one of those weak, ordinary members who had no backing, and then bestow the Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent upon this body.
This way, this replicated body could rise quickly.
"Let's do it this way!"
Ye Tian decided.
He transferred the Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent from his replicated body to his Invincible Ancestor avatar, then disintegrated the replicated body. He then replicated the body of a weak Ancient Star Race youth and stripped the Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent from the Invincible Ancestor avatar to merge it into the Ancient Star Race body.
Thus, this Ancient Star Race replicated body possessed the Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent.
On this day.
Ye Tian's main body arrived at the Starry Ancient Domain. With one hand, he tore the barrier and sent the Ancient Star Race replicated body into the domain, positioning it on a planet.
......
Whoosh!
Ye Tian's replicated body appeared directly on an Ancient Star Race planet. His current identity was that of an Ancient Star Race youth; his true identity would never be discovered.
Boom!!!!
A Supreme Being arrived at this planet. His divine sense covered a vast territory of planets and naturally swept over Ye Tian's Ancient Star Race body. However, the opponent didn't notice anything and only thought Ye Tian was a genuine member of the Ancient Star Race.
"Next, I'll cultivate on this planet. With the aptitude of this body's Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent, I can raise my cultivation in a short time!"
Ye Tian said confidently.
Soon, Ye Tian integrated into the environment of this planet. As his cultivation continued to rise, his fame began to spread.
Furthermore, he chose to join the peripheral forces of a major power.
Just like that, after billions of years, Ye Tian cultivated to the Ancestor Realm, which is the Ancestor realm of the human race.
Unknowingly, one Great Tribulation Era passed.
Ye Tian's name had already resounded throughout the Starry Ancient Domain because he had broken through to the Invincible realm. His foundation was far stronger than other Invincibles, and his combat power was terrifying. This was even with Ye Tian intentionally hiding most of his strength; otherwise, it would have been enough to shock the ten Supreme Beings.
As his strength increased, Ye Tian gradually came into contact with the high-level members of the Starry Ancient Domain.
After several more Great Tribulation Eras, Ye Tian's cultivation rose to the fourth-class Invincible realm.
Finally, on this day, he was invited to enter the holy land of the Ancient Star Race—Ancient Star.
......
"Am I about to come into contact with the true opportunity of the Ancient Star Race?"
Ye Tian murmured.
On this day, he was personally picked up by a first-class Invincible and soon arrived at the holy land of the Ancient Star Race—Ancient Star.
Ancient Star was the name of the holy land and the origin of the Ancient Star Race's name.
When Ye Tian arrived, he found many geniuses of the Ancient Star Race gathered here. There were many fourth-class, third-class, second-class, and first-class Invincibles, totaling about a hundred thousand geniuses.
Of course, compared to the total number of experts in the Ancient Star Race, this was just a drop in the ocean.
After all, obtaining a quota to enter the holy land was extremely difficult. Without being a true genius, one simply couldn't enter.
Suddenly.
A powerful Supreme Being arrived.
When this Supreme Being arrived, many holy land guards respectfully said, "Greetings, Lord Donghe!"
Donghe!
One of the ten Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race, a truly powerful figure who stood far above other Supreme Beings.
Donghe looked at the many Ancient Star Race geniuses present and said, "Everyone, you may be wondering why you have entered the holy land this time. But I tell you, this is an opportunity. Whether you can obtain an astonishing opportunity depends on how well you grasp this chance. Of course, many clansmen have been here before, some more than once, but every clansman must cherish every opportunity. After all, each clansman only has five chances in their lifetime."
Soon, Donghe revealed a secret.
After Ye Tian finished listening, he was instantly shocked.
