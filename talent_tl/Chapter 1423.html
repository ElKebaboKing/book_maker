Chapter 1422: Slaying the Stone Python
!
Before coming to the Holy Soul Realm, Ye Tian had also looked into the Supreme Domain Ancestors on the rankings of the various Holy Sects in the Dark Holy Sanctuary. Unless they were hidden Supreme Domain Ancestors, he recognized everyone on the rankings.
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors he sensed were not on the Dark Holy Sect's rankings, so their strength likely wasn't that great.
"Black Scale Holy Fruits!"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect also spotted the Black Scale Holy Fruits, and looks of greed immediately appeared on their faces.
This item was of high value, and they naturally desired it. Refining it themselves would be a waste; they could trade it to those Supreme Domain Ancestors who were struggling to break through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor level, and it would definitely fetch a high price.
These two Supreme Domain Ancestors also noticed Ye Tian, but they were somewhat uncertain of his strength.
Although they were very confident in their own power, none of the Supreme Domain Ancestors entering the Holy Soul Realm this time were weak. Even some Supreme Domain Ancestors from certain Holy Lands possessed immense strength, not necessarily weaker than those on the rankings, and perhaps even stronger than themselves.
Therefore, it was better to be cautious.
"Your Excellency, there are three Black Scale Holy Fruits. How about one for each of us?"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors asked.
"Let's deal with this Stone Python first!"
Ye Tian suggested.
"Fine!"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect also sensed the Stone Python and discovered the three petrified Supreme Domain Ancestors.
However, they estimated that those three must have been relatively weak Supreme Domain Ancestors. As members of the Dark Holy Sect, their own strength was very close to the combat power of those on the rankings, so they did not fear the Stone Python.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian and the other two attacked the Stone Python together.
Just then, the Stone Python struck.
Swish! A flash of white light passed by, and the attacks of the three were directly petrified.
At the same time, the Stone Python erupted with a terrifying aura, shattering the void with a strike as it lunged toward them.
"Not good, this Stone Python's strength is comparable to a Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor!"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect were stunned.
A Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor was not weak. Although Supreme Domain Ancestors like them could leapfrog levels to kill weaker Invincible Domain Ancestors, they had no way to contend with a Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Previously, they thought the Holy Soul Beasts of this world did not understand Holy Techniques and that their combat power would be inferior to geniuses of the same rank; even if they became Invincible Domain Ancestors, they shouldn't be a threat.
But they realized they had miscalculated; this Stone Python's strength was terrifying.
"Retreat!"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect made a split-second decision.
However, just as they wanted to flee, Ye Tian deployed the Five Elements Sealing, locking down the void.
Suddenly, the two of them could not escape.
"What is the meaning of this, Your Excellency? Why are you blocking us? If you don't run, you'll die!"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect roared in anger.
"No, the ones dying are you!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
If they were Supreme Domain Ancestors from other factions, he might have let it go, but they were from the Dark Holy Sect. The relationship between the Battle Gate and the Dark Holy Sect was terrible; even the Fire-Patterned Battle Ancestor had said that if he encountered Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect, he should kill them all if possible.
Therefore, he would not let these two go.
"No!"
The two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect frantically attacked Ye Tian, but before they could land many hits, the Stone Python's petrification talent covered them.
The first time, they resisted the petrification talent, but they used up their life-saving treasures.
The second time, they couldn't hold out and were directly petrified, their life force gradually withering away until they died.
....Outside the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
In a patch of darkness, a figure sat perched.
He was the Progenitor of the Dark Holy Sect—the Dark Progenitor. Of course, this was merely a clone, not his true body.
The Dark Progenitor opened his eyes. He sensed that the soul tablets of two Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Sect had shattered. He couldn't help but mutter, "Two Supreme Domain Ancestors dead. They fell so shortly after entering. What useless trash!"
He didn't care much; the two deceased were not on the rankings. No matter how many such Supreme Domain Ancestors fell, he wouldn't feel the sting.
Ye Tian didn't actually need to use the Stone Python's power to kill those two; he could have killed them easily. But he did so to gauge the Stone Python's strength.
"Combat power of a Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, not bad at all!"
Ye Tian smiled, not panicking in the slightest.
Long ago, when he fought the two Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors from the Qingyuan Holy Land, he had suppressed and killed them.
Although he had used the power of the Profound Void Holy Realm back then, his true strength had definitely reached the level of a seven or eight-step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Now, his Soul Holy Light had increased significantly, and his combat power had definitely stepped into the level of a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Using the strength of a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor to kill a Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor was not particularly difficult.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian struck, using the Void Slash Holy Technique to continuously bombard the Stone Python's massive body.
Crack, crack, crack!!!! The scales on the Stone Python's body shattered under the assault of the Void Slash, and countless amounts of blood sprayed out.
The Stone Python's wounds healed continuously at a very fast rate. This wasn't because it possessed a special recovery method, but rather the instinctive recovery ability of an Invincible Domain Ancestor.
The recovery speed of an Invincible Domain Ancestor was very fast. Unless the disparity in strength was truly massive, killing one would take a long time.
"Kill!"
Countless golden threads surrounded the Stone Python. This was Ye Tian's Golden Silk Holy Technique. Although its power wasn't as great as the Void Slash, it could provide a continuous stream of attacks, which was more effective here.
Under Ye Tian's barrage of various Holy Techniques, the Stone Python had no way to resist. Its petrification ability couldn't affect Ye Tian either; it was directly blocked by several of Ye Tian's defensive Holy Techniques at the Limit Realm. Even if a sliver of petrification power invaded Ye Tian's body, it would be restored by his Indestructible Holy Technique.
Thus, its methods were useless against Ye Tian.
Several months later, this Stone Python was ground to death by Ye Tian.
"A pity. This Stone Python's strength was actually very close to a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor. If it knew a petrification Holy Technique, its power might even exceed a Six-Step. If it truly practiced a petrification technique to a very high level, its combat power would likely jump to the level of a four or five-step Invincible Domain Ancestor instantly!"
Ye Tian evaluated.
However, there are no "ifs." The Holy Soul Beasts of this world cannot leave, but it is thanks to the rules of this world that they can be born with Holy Talents.
Thus, there are gains and losses.
But for Ye Tian, this world was... a place of truly great opportunity.
"I must strive to copy countless Holy Talents as soon as possible and see if a qualitative change occurs after merging them!"
After picking the three Black Scale Holy Fruits, Ye Tian left the area.
