Chapter 1659 The True Yang Holy Ancestor’s avatar was killed!

“The Void Beasts are here!”
In the void outside the cave, one Void Beast after another came shuttling over.
The dozens of Eight Extremity Stage experts all had grave expressions. These were all equivalent to Lords of the Eternal Realm, and even they could not contend with them; they could only delay them for a period of time.
“First use sealing-type Eternal Origin weapons to seal off the void, then use formations to hold them back for a bit, and then use defensive Eternal Arts to block these Void Beasts!”
The many Eight Extremity Stage experts began to buy time in this way.
But very quickly, dozens of Void Beasts had already gathered outside. It could be seen that the actions of the Eight Extremity Stage experts cutting down the Void Gold Essence Tree had completely enraged these Void Beasts.
Crack!
An Eternal Origin weapon that was sealing off the void directly shattered, and then the other Eternal Origin weapons also shattered one after another.
The great formations also did not hold out for long before breaking one by one.
At this point, the Eight Extremity Stage experts had to face these Void Beasts directly.
The Eight Extremity Stage experts joined forces to unleash a series of defensive Eternal Arts, forming layer upon layer of light shields that sealed the entrance tightly.
Meanwhile, the Void Beasts kept bombarding the entrance, countless laws collapsing as this stretch of void caved in.
Some of the weaker Eight Extremity Stage experts, whose bodies were also rather frail, were already unable to hold on and their physical bodies began to crack, so they could only forcibly take some pills to recover from their injuries.
The only Eight Extremity Stage expert under relatively light pressure was Ye Tian.
Ye Tian’s Endless Eternal Body was a legendary Eternal Body, and he had also reached the Transcendent realm. His ability to save his life was arguably the number one among Eight Extremity Stage experts; even if he faced multiple Void Beasts alone, it was enough for him to preserve his life.
Thus, he was not the least bit panicked.
“Ancestor Gu He, hurry up. We’re about to reach our limit!”
True Yang Holy Ancestor and the other Eight Extremity Stage experts sent transmissions to Ancestor Gu He and the other five Eight Extremity Stage half-step Eternal Realm experts, urging them to speed up.
Finally, just when the Eight Extremity Stage experts were about to completely collapse, a crack echoed throughout the nest.
The Void Gold Essence Tree broke!
“Collect it!”
Ancestor Gu He waved his hand and directly took away the Void Gold Essence Tree. Then he sent a message to the many Eight Extremity Stage experts: “The Void Gold Essence Tree is already in hand. After we break out of the encirclement, we can then divide it into pieces of Void Gold Essence Wood.”
“Good!”
The other Eight Extremity Stage experts also agreed.
After all, they were still surrounded by the Void Beasts right now, and they did not have that much time to continue cutting up the Void Essence Wood.
So, they had to break out of the encirclement first.
The Eight Extremity Stage experts were indeed no match for this group of Void Beasts, but breaking out of the encirclement was still possible.
After paying a certain price, the dozens of Eight Extremity Stage experts finally managed to break out.
Then, they concealed themselves on a small island and set up a concealment formation, temporarily making them safe.
“Ancestor Gu He, divide the Void Gold Essence Tree into pieces of Void Gold Essence Wood.”
An Eight Extremity Stage expert hurriedly urged.
They were also afraid that Ancestor Gu He would go back on his word; what if he ran off?
“Alright!”
Ancestor Gu He did not have any intention of reneging. He took out the entire Void Gold Essence Tree, then began cutting it up. After some time, he finally cut it into many pieces.
“Let’s begin the distribution!”
According to the agreement, Ancestor Gu He directly took one-tenth, while the rest was divided equally. After all, everyone had contributed, and at this time there was no room to argue about who was stronger or weaker; otherwise, there would be no way to divide it.
Very soon, Ye Tian also obtained a piece of Void Gold Essence Wood, and its size was quite large.
With so much Void Gold Essence Wood, it was enough to refine quite a bit of Void Gold Essence Liquid. Although it could help with fusing innate Eternal Laws, it would at most increase the chance by 5%.
But if enough similar treasures were collected, then the usefulness would be huge.
For example, Ancestor Gu He had lived for nearly 10 million Eternal Years after all. Ye Tian did not believe he had no other treasures or resources that could help him break through.
After the distribution was completed, Ancestor Gu He said, “Everyone, I only know that the Land of the Abyss has this one resource, the Void Gold Essence Wood. Now that we’ve obtained it, continuing to seek more Void Gold Essence Wood won’t be of much use. So I suggest we split up and search for treasures separately, lest we only obtain one type of treasure yet be unable to divide it, causing conflicts between us. What do you think?”
“Agreed!”
True Yang Holy Ancestor was the first to agree.
“I agree too!”
Ye Tian said.
How many good things could he get by following the main group? Even if he encountered some extremely good treasure, he would not be able to monopolize it alone.
Moreover,
he could also copy a clone of himself, allowing his two bodies to act separately. If he followed the other Eight Extremity Stage experts, it would be inconvenient for him to copy his own body.
So he was the person who most wanted to act separately.
“Agree!”
“Agree!”
Most of the Eight Extremity Stage experts agreed.
And some Eight Extremity Stage experts were not confident in their own strength, and they were also injured, so they planned to act in teams.
Just like that, this team split up here.
------------------------
Ye Tian traveled far away, and his copying talent still had not detected any other Eight Extremity Stage experts or Eternal Realm beings, so he began copying his own body.
Very quickly, another body was copied out.
Immediately, the two bodies moved off in two different directions.
In this way, his chances of finding treasures became much greater.........
Along the way, regardless of what resource it was, as long as it was of some use, he directly collected it.
No matter what, after resources accumulated to a certain extent, they would still have some value.
Occasionally, he encountered Void Beasts, but he did not fight them to the death; instead, he chose to shake them off.
Just like that, time slowly passed.
Deep within the Land of the Abyss.
True Yang Holy Ancestor arrived here and found a mineral vein.
“It’s actually an Indestructible Earth Spirit Stone vein. Integrating this kind of mineral vein into the spacetime territory will help the territory grow!”
The Land of the Abyss said excitedly.
The value of this mineral vein was enough to equal half of his net worth.
Just as he was about to take away this mineral vein, a black, hair-covered hand reached out from the void and grabbed at him.
True Yang Holy Ancestor’s expression changed drastically, and he used an Eternal Art to protect himself.
However, the power of this large hand was far too terrifying. With just one strike, it tore through his Eternal Art defense, pierced through the Eternal Origin armor he wore, and shattered his Eternal Body.
True Yang Holy Ancestor was covered in countless cracks, as though he was about to fall apart completely in the next moment.
He kept shuttling through space while trying his best to recover his injuries.
However, all of it was useless.
With a thunderous boom!
He was directly shaken out of his spacetime shuttling, and then a humanoid Void Beast appeared, punching toward him.
“True Yang Extinguishing World Fist!”
True Yang Holy Ancestor gathered all his strength, mobilized all his innate Eternal Laws, and unleashed a fist at the pinnacle of mastery.
Fist clashed with fist!
The other side was unharmed, while True Yang Holy Ancestor was directly blasted apart by a single punch. Only a trace of his will remained, but very quickly even that will was directly erased by the other side.
Shaoyang Territory.
In a certain stretch of void, True Yang Holy Ancestor’s true body opened his eyes, and within them was nothing but regret.
“I should have left. This time I’ve suffered a huge loss. The Void Gold Essence Wood is gone, and this avatar of mine has also perished. If I want to condense this avatar again, it will require a very long time, and I’ll also have to pay a very precious amount of resources!”
True Yang Holy Ancestor sighed.
However, he was also thankful it had been an avatar. If it had been his true body, then he would have perished completely this time.
“The other Eight Extremity Stage experts are probably in a miserable state. There’s actually an Immortal Void Beast hidden in the Land of the Abyss, and it even has some intelligence!” True Yang Holy Ancestor muttered.
Then, he sent messages to those Eight Extremity Stage experts in the Eight Extremities Tower who possessed the ability to use avatars.
As for whether their avatars were willing to withdraw, that was none of his business.
In any case, he had done his duty.
Very quickly, the other Eight Extremity Stage experts in the Land of the Abyss learned that the Land of the Abyss had a Void Beast comparable to a Immortal Realm lord.
