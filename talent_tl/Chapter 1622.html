Chapter 1621: Huge Harvest!

Void Source Hall.
After Ye Tian returned, he immediately began to fuse those Eternal Ancestor Talents.
As time slowly passed, one Eternal Ancestor Talent after another was fused.
After many years, Ye Tian fused a succession of Undying Ancestor Talents.
When Ye Tian’s true body obtained these Undying Ancestor Talents, its potential became even greater.
And if he were to create Eternal Skills in the future, it would become even easier.
In particular, the Immortal Undying Ancestor Talent—this Undying Ancestor Talent was somewhat related to the Endless Eternal Body, because the Endless Eternal Body had fused the Ancient Star Eternal Body, and the Ancient Star Eternal Body actually possessed the power of immortality, which was why it could have such powerful life-preserving ability.
And now, Ye Tian had obtained the Immortal Undying Ancestor Talent, which would help him cultivate the Endless Eternal Body.
The fusion of talents had already been completed, and Ye Tian began cultivating the Endless Eternal Body.
This time, he had obtained Yuanqing Eternal Stone and quite a few other resources that would help improve the Endless Eternal Body, so naturally he had to cultivate the Endless Eternal Body and strive to raise it to the Perfected realm.
Immediately after, Ye Tian began refining Yuanqing Eternal Liquid.
That large chunk of Yuanqing Eternal Stone quickly yielded some Yuanqing Eternal Liquid, while some other resources also refined into substances that would help improve the Endless Eternal Body.
“Begin cultivating the Endless Eternal Body!”
Ye Tian swallowed a mouthful of Yuanqing Eternal Liquid, and the Endless Eternal Body began absorbing the energy of the Yuanqing Eternal Liquid.
At this moment, the Immortal Undying Ancestor Talent played a certain role, greatly increasing Ye Tian’s utilization rate of the Yuanqing Eternal Liquid, which surprised Ye Tian a little.
As the Yuanqing Eternal Liquid was continuously consumed, Ye Tian’s Endless Eternal Body kept improving.
Gradually, the Endless Eternal Body advanced to the peak of the late Major realm.
By this point, Ye Tian had already consumed a great deal of substances, and he was only one step away from the Perfected realm of the Endless Eternal Body, but this one step was very difficult to complete and required an enormous amount of resources. Fortunately, Ye Tian was lucky; before those substances were completely exhausted, his Endless Eternal Body finally reached Perfected.
Once the Endless Eternal Body reached Perfected, Ye Tian’s strength surged dramatically.
Moreover, the resources Ye Tian had obtained from the Primordial Sea this time were enough to support his cultivation to the Seventh Supreme Being Stage, and they could easily help him break through his shackles, but it was still somewhat difficult to冲击 the Eighth Supreme Being Stage.
On the other hand, Ye Tian’s Black Mist Worm clone in the Primordial Sea was searching for various resources.

Primordial Sea.
At this point, the place was already covered with Black Mist Worms, and no matter where Ye Tian went, he could command the other Black Mist Worms; he was the king among the Black Mist Worms.
All kinds of precious resources were collected by Ye Tian and stored in a void he had opened up within his body.
On this day.
Ye Tian discovered an incomparably precious treasure—Void True Liquid.
Void True Liquid appeared in a void-like liquid state, with obscure fluctuations of Eternal Rule, but its main characteristic was that when it came into contact with Eternal Rule, it would turn into a truly void state.
In other words, attacks from Eternal Rule could not touch it. Once external Eternal Rule touched it, it would completely vanish, as if it did not exist in heaven and earth, or within Eternal Rule; even a half-step Eternal realm expert could not detect its existence.
And once the Eternal Rule attack moved away from where it was, it would appear again.
Thus, identifying Void True Liquid was still quite easy.
“To think I actually found Void True Liquid. If those Ninth Supreme Being experts knew about it, they would probably go mad!”
Ye Tian muttered.
He immediately put away this portion of Void True Liquid and stored it carefully.
Void True Liquid could help any Ninth Supreme Being expert冲击 the half-step Eternal realm, and for some Ninth Supreme Being experts who had used treasures similar to Primordial Source Liquid, it was their last hope.
Ye Tian did not need Void True Liquid all that much, but the human race certainly had people who did—for example, his relatives needed it.
He did not think his relatives could cultivate to the half-step Eternal realm on their own; in the end, they would probably still have to rely on Void True Liquid.
Therefore, even one portion of Void True Liquid was still not enough for him.
After obtaining Void True Liquid, Ye Tian’s luck afterwards seemed to become better and better.
Not long after, Ye Tian once again obtained an extremely precious treasure in the Primordial Sea—Primordial Tree!
Primordial Leaves are treasures produced by the Primordial Tree; one Primordial Leaf can carry an Supreme Being into the Primordial Sea, but the Primordial Tree had ten leaves, and the Primordial Tree itself likely had special uses as well.
Unfortunately, Ye Tian did not know the uses of the Primordial Tree.
However, he still collected the ten leaves.
As for the Primordial Tree, he did not take it away, but left it here. It would not be too late to come back after figuring out the Primordial Tree’s use.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian obtained many, many resources, including quite a few that were useful even to half-step Eternal realm experts.
In the entire Primordial Sea, there was no Supreme Being competing with Ye Tian; this place was his home ground.
And the Black Mist Worms had little intelligence, so they did not need those resources.
At this moment, Ye Tian was thinking that if the Black Mist Worms could come out, how wonderful that would be!
Then, he would not have to worry at all about the resources needed to cultivate to the half-step Eternal realm, and he could even help the human race nurture multiple half-step Eternal realm experts.
Long years passed.
Ye Tian had almost visited most of the Primordial Sea, and in fact many resources had already been collected by the Supreme Beings who had entered before him. After all, there had been many Supreme Beings entering this place, and they collected things very quickly.
However, the amount of resources Ye Tian found was still terrifying, far exceeding Ye Tian’s expectations.
Among them were two drops of Void True Liquid, one Primordial Tree, one Eternal Undying Sacred Flower, three Eternal Comprehension Flowers, and 109 Immortal Primordial Stones.
These were among the treasures he harvested the most of.
The Eternal Comprehension Flower could make even half-step Eternal realm experts enter a state of sudden enlightenment, and its value was great.
The Eternal Undying Sacred Flower could heal all injuries; even fatal injuries to a half-step Eternal realm expert could be healed by the Eternal Undying Flower.
Immortal Primordial Stone was an incomparably hard material that could be used to forge Eternal Source Weapons, and after half-step Eternal realm, few people possessed Eternal Source Weapons.
And with so many Immortal Primordial Stones, it was enough to forge many Eternal Source Weapons.
As for the other resources, they were simply innumerable.
More importantly, these were treasures missed by Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal realm experts.
As long years passed, the Primordial Sea would continue to give birth to more treasures, and then Ye Tian could harvest another wave of resources.
Suddenly.
On this day, some changes occurred in the Primordial Sea. Many, many Black Mist Worms were drawn by some kind of attraction and flew toward a certain location in the Primordial Sea together.
Ye Tian could resist this pull, but he still followed over.
He very much wanted to know what exactly this so-called pull came from?.
