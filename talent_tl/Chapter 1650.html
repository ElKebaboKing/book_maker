Chapter 1649 Maple Leaf Domain, Initial Entry!

Among the half-step Eternal Realm cultivators in the Source Sea, the most common way to go to the time-space Domains was to take refuge under some time-space lord and have that lord bring them out of the Source Sea.
But after years of investigation, Ye Tian also learned of another method: entering the time-space Domains through a time-space island. This was also an opportunity the Lord Alliance gave the half-step Eternal Realm cultivators of the Source Sea to enter the time-space Domains.
Among the countless time-space Domains, many time-space lords did not have lords of their own, and instead they were public time-space Domains that allowed any half-step Eternal Realm cultivator to enter.
However, the time-space Domains were only open to Extreme cultivators, and even ordinary Extreme cultivators still could not enter the time-space Domains; they had to pass an assessment before they could leave the Source Sea.
In addition, even after passing the assessment, one still had to pay a certain fee to the administrator of the time-space island as a toll.
Otherwise, they would still be stopped.
This day.
Ye Tian's clone body came to the time-space island. In fact, the time-space island was made by remodeling a fragment of a time-space Domain, and the entrance was very hidden.
Ye Tian entered the time-space island and was immediately stopped by two puppets.
These were not ordinary puppets, but puppets whose strength was comparable to a Six-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm cultivator.
“I am here to participate in the time-space island assessment, to go to the time-space Domains!”
Ye Tian stated his purpose.
“Come with me!”
One puppet brought Ye Tian to a tower within the time-space Domain.
Here, Ye Tian needed to undergo an assessment.
In fact, as long as one's strength was at the Five-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm, there was a very high chance of passing the assessment, but not many people wanted to go to the time-space Domains.
After all, with an eternal lifespan, cultivating in the Source Sea at the Five- or Six-Extreme level was extremely comfortable, and if one happened to find a great opportunity, no one would compete with them for it.
And after going to the time-space Domains, it was relatively difficult to find good opportunities, and the chance of falling was also very high.
Very soon.
The time-space island arranged Ye Tian's assessment: he would fight against a puppet with Five-Extreme strength. Ye Tian easily defeated the opponent, thereby passing the assessment.
In front of the time-space passage of the time-space island, an old man stood guard there.
He was not a puppet, but an Extreme half-step Eternal Realm existence, and his strength was very powerful.
Ye Tian estimated that his strength had at least reached the Seven-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm, and may even have reached the Eight-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm.
“To enter the time-space Domains, you need to pay resources worth one hundred thousand Time-Space Coins.”
The old man said coldly.
Time-Space Coins were a currency of the time-space Domains, and Ye Tian knew this.
So, he took out some resources and quickly gathered enough to make up one hundred thousand Time-Space Coins.
“This time-space passage can lead to three time-space Domains: Green Marsh Domain, Hundred Snow Domain, and Maple Leaf Domain. They are all relatively large and publicly ownerless domains among the time-space Domains. Choose one, and I will activate the time-space passage to send you there.” the old man introduced.
“Maple Leaf Domain!”
Ye Tian chose casually.
Rumble!!!!!
The time-space passage opened, forming a huge time-space vortex.
Ye Tian stepped into the time-space vortex and was immediately pulled by a powerful force; his entire body directly shattered the time-space rules of the Source Sea, even shattering the Source Sea barrier, and entered another world.
……
Darkness, void, emptiness, infinity.
This was the void region between the Source Sea and the time-space Domains. There were no rules here, no energy, everything was void.
Ye Tian was in the time-space passage and felt the suffocating pressure of the void region; even his body felt somewhat uncomfortable.
But very soon, he arrived at the end of the time-space passage—the Maple Leaf Domain.
He truly knew nothing at all about the situation in the Maple Leaf Domain.
Therefore, after arriving here, the first thing he needed to do was familiarize himself with the environment here and understand the situation of the time-space Domains.
Maple Leaf Domain, Heavenly Imperial City.
This city connected one time-space Domain after another, and was a rather famous city in Maple Leaf Domain.
After Ye Tian settled down in Maple Leaf City, he began to learn about the situation in Maple Leaf Domain.
After some time, Ye Tian roughly understood the situation here.
Maple Leaf Domain was not very large. In terms of area, it was only about as big as a foreign world, and compared with the Source Sea, it was simply not worth mentioning.
But although the time-space Domains were small in area, the cultivation environment here was even better than in the Eternal Heaven and Earth. Just a bit of resources, if placed in the Eternal Heaven and Earth, would be enough to make many Supreme Beings compete for them.
Moreover, the races born in the time-space Domains were all very powerful, and they could easily cultivate to the Supreme Being level. .........
Of course.
In the time-space Domains, becoming a half-step Eternal Realm cultivator was extremely difficult.
What surprised Ye Tian was that the living beings in the time-space Domains could not exist eternally; their lifespans were limited.
For example, the Supreme Beings of the time-space Domains had a lifespan limit of one hundred thousand Eternal Years. Although that was a very long span of time, there was ultimately a limit, whereas many ordinary powerhouses in the Source Sea had infinite lifespans.
“This may be the flaw of the time-space Domains!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In the time-space Domains, even half-step Eternal Realm cultivators could only live for ten million Eternal Years; only Eternal Realm lords were truly eternal.
Perhaps it was precisely this limitation that prevented the time-space Domains from facing an extinction crisis due to the expansion of living beings.
In addition, because struggles in the time-space Domains were very frequent, each time-space Domain could maintain a balanced operation.
Maple Leaf Domain had many half-step Eternal Realm cultivators, and even Extreme powerhouses, with the strongest being three Eight-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses.
Eight-Extreme half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses held status second only to ordinary Eternal Realm experts. In Maple Leaf Domain, they possessed very high prestige and power, and many half-step Eternal Realm cultivators had taken refuge under them.
Besides these, Ye Tian also learned about the division of the time-space Domains.
Time-space Domains were divided into ordinary time-space Domains and Eternal time-space Domains, and Maple Leaf Domain was an ordinary time-space Domain, though it was considered quite good among ordinary time-space Domains.
However, Maple Leaf Domain had not produced top-tier resources, so it was not valued by newly ascended Eternal Realm lords, and to this day it was still a publicly ownerless domain.
Among the endless time-space Domains, there were many such ordinary time-space Domains, and there were also quite a few damaged time-space Domains.
But Eternal time-space Domains were not many, only a little over a hundred. Every Eternal time-space Domain lord was an incomparably terrifying existence among Eternal Realm experts.
For Ye Tian, Maple Leaf Domain was fairly suitable for him and could serve as his starting place.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian began to make friends with one Extreme after another. Only by making many Extreme acquaintances would he have the chance to come into contact with some opportunities and intelligence.
Finally, on this day, an Extreme invited Ye Tian to join a relatively free faction.
