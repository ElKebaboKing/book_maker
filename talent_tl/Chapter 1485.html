"The Holy Ancestor knows even more terrifying top-tier Ancestors?"
Ye Tian said in shock.
"Yes!"
World Ancestor nodded. "Not many Ancestors know this secret. The Destruction Ancestor knows, a few Ancestors of the Dark Holy Gate know, and then there's me and the War Ancestor."
"Could it be a sleeping top-tier Ancestor?"
Ye Tian asked curiously.
World Ancestor said solemnly, "Exactly, it is indeed a sleeping top-tier Ancestor. According to our guesses, back when the Holy Ancestor was able to become the first Ancestor of the Dark Holy Domain and created his own Ancestor Law to do so, he must have received guidance from a top-tier Ancestor.
Otherwise, how could he have created his own Ancestor Law in a mere few dozen domain years?"
Ye Tian nodded. Even for a genius like him, with the Ultimate Ancestral Talent, it would take a long time to create an Ancestor Law.
The Holy Ancestor of the past was definitely not as monstrous as he was, yet he could create his own Ancestor Law in just a few dozen domain years; this was definitely abnormal.
After all, the cultivation system of the Dark Holy Domain had only just been established back then, and the Dark Destruction Domain could not have provided guidance for it. Therefore, becoming an Ancestor was even harder then. Creating an Ancestor Law was equivalent to creating something from nothing; one could imagine how immense the difficulty was! However, under such circumstances, the Holy Ancestor was able to create an Ancestor Law and become an Ancestor, which was naturally abnormal.
"World Ancestor, is there any evidence? If it's just a guess, I'm afraid it's not enough... right?"
Ye Tian said.
"Naturally, it's not based on guesswork alone!"
World Ancestor continued, "Once, when the Dark Destruction Domain invaded the Dark Holy Domain again, I had just promoted to Ancestor. The Holy Ancestor had just lost the power boost from the Dark Holy Domain, and the Dark Holy Domain was at its weakest. The Holy Ancestor was no match for the Destruction Ancestor, and the Destruction Ancestor brought out a terrifying treasure capable of slaying an Elite Ancestor. At that time, the Holy Ancestor was still an Elite Ancestor. Other Ancestors were busy in battle and didn't pay attention to the fight between the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor, but I was different. I have a special method that allows me to perceive the battles and any slight fluctuations throughout the entire Dark Holy Domain.
At that moment, the strike from the treasure unleashed by the Destruction Ancestor was blocked by a single finger."
"I did not sense it wrongly; it was indeed a finger. Although it was fleeting, it was definitely a finger, and then it vanished.
Subsequently, the Destruction Ancestor retreated and did not dare to invade the Dark Holy Domain again.
Since then, the battles between the Dark Destruction Domain and the Dark Holy Domain have not involved Ancestors; they have all been minor skirmishes!"
"Previously, I was too weak to perceive the strength of that finger's owner, but I know now. That was definitely a top-tier Ancestor, and one many times more powerful than top-tier Ancestors like the Destruction Ancestor or the Holy Ancestor.
That person acted for the sake of the Holy Ancestor, so they likely have a deep relationship. Thus, I suspect he guided the Holy Ancestor and might even be the Holy Ancestor's master, the most ancient top-tier Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain."
After World Ancestor finished speaking, Ye Tian began to take it somewhat seriously.
Although his current strength had reached the level of the highest top-tier Ancestor combat power and wasn't far from the limit of a top-tier Ancestor,
he was very clear that he was likely not yet invincible at the top-tier Ancestor level.
Some of the most ancient top-tier Ancestors likely had combat power that had reached a point where no further progress was possible.
He naturally wouldn't deliberately make an enemy of such a top-tier Ancestor, but the key was that those top-tier Ancestors might become his enemies.
After all, the Human Race had produced Ancestors one after another and cultivated an Ancestral World, and his own strength was comparable to a top-tier Elite Ancestor as soon as he broke through. All of this defied the understanding of the Ancestors.
All Ancestors knew he possessed a great secret; they just weren't confident in dealing with him.
But if one day those top-tier Ancestors united, he would truly be in some danger.
If not for World Ancestor's reminder this time, he really would have thought he was perfectly safe and nearly invincible.
"Although I feel I've reached the peak of a top-tier Ancestor, my strength can actually still improve a lot. But if I just cultivate slowly, the speed of improvement won't be very fast. My current rate of improvement is already very slow unless I obtain a great opportunity!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Great opportunities were rare. Currently, there were almost no opportunities in the Dark Forbidden Domain, except for those forbidden zones.
"The six major forbidden zones are too dangerous for me, but there are records of other forbidden zones where Ancestors have fallen, yet some have also escaped and survived.
Those Ancestors might not have obtained good things, but it doesn't matter if my duplicate bodies perish, as long as I don't encounter methods that can wipe out my original body through a duplicate body."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In short, there was a sliver of risk, but the risk was not great.
Besides, he couldn't just stay huddled here cultivating forever. If he did, he would almost never be able to break the shackles of a top-tier Ancestor and charge into the level of a Supreme Ancestor.
In the following days, Ye Tian began to communicate with various Ancestors to gather information about the forbidden zones.
Those Ancestors might not reveal all the information, but they would at least disclose a little. Combined with the information from War Ancestor and World Ancestor, Ye Tian gained a better understanding of these forbidden zones.
Below the six major forbidden zones, there were dozens of forbidden zones that were dangerous even for Ancestors, but there were only eight that were considered very dangerous even for top-tier Ancestors. Other forbidden zones posed no danger to top-tier Ancestors, only to ordinary and Elite Ancestors.
Therefore, even if ordinary forbidden zones had good things, they had long since been scavenged.
Thus, Ye Tian's targets were those eight top-tier forbidden zones.
After some screening, Ye Tian chose one of them—the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
This forbidden zone contained many mysterious bones, which was how it got its name.
The Bone Sea Forbidden Zone was extremely dangerous. Even top-tier Ancestors did not dare to go deep; they only dared to linger on the outskirts for a few breaths. This way, they wouldn't perish. If they went even a little deeper or stayed a bit longer, they would likely be in danger.
Within the Bone Sea, there existed a special kind of creature—Bone Soldiers.
These Bone Soldiers were somewhat like the Bone Race of the three thousand evil races in the Dark Destruction Domain, but the difference in strength between the two was like heaven and earth.
An ordinary Bone Soldier possessed the combat power of an ordinary Ancestor, and there were many such Bone Soldiers, at least a thousand of them. There were also Bone Generals and Bone Kings.
Among them, Bone Generals possessed the combat power of an Elite Ancestor, and Bone Kings possessed the combat power of a top-tier Ancestor.
With so many Ancestor-level entities, once surrounded, even a top-tier Ancestor would have no chance of escaping.
As for what opportunities existed in the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, only one is currently known: the Bone Beads.
Once, the Destruction Ancestor had sneaked into the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, killed a Bone Soldier, and obtained a Bone Bead. According to the news revealed by the Destruction Ancestor, Bone Beads could increase one's cultivation, but no one dared to go in to hunt Bone Soldiers.
Because even the Destruction Ancestor had returned heavily injured and almost didn't make it back.
"Bone Beads can increase cultivation; they suit me. If my duplicate bodies can kill more Bone Soldiers and bring back some Bone Beads, then I can increase my strength in a short period of time!"
Ye Tian thought.
