Chapter 1603 A Finger of a Eternal Stage Expert!

A true great opportunity?
Ye Tian immediately felt somewhat excited. After all, Star Ancestor had previously given him quite a few good things, such as treasures like the Primordial Source Liquid. But now, since he had spoken the three words “great opportunity,” it was probably even more precious than the Primordial Source Liquid, and perhaps even more valuable than the Ancient Star Stone, an Eternal Source Object.
Very soon, Star Ancestor’s true body sent out something from the place of seal.
Ye Tian looked over and found that it was a finger.
This finger was sealed layer upon layer. It looked plain and ordinary, but since even Star Ancestor called it a great opportunity, that meant this finger was no simple thing.
This finger was about three meters long, which meant that the life form it came from was not small. The finger was purple in color, covered with countless patterns, and it was not the finger of any creature Ye Tian knew.
So after looking at it for a while, Ye Tian still could not identify it.
“Star Ancestor, what is this finger?”
Ye Tian could not help asking.
Star Ancestor’s consciousness touched this finger, somewhat reluctant to part with it, but still handed it to Ye Tian.
“Xu Chen, this finger is the most important opportunity I have ever obtained. In fact, the reason I was able to create the Ancient Star Body has something to do with it. It is a finger of a Eternal Stage life form, containing a sliver of Eternal Stage power. If it were not sealed, just the aura of its power would be enough to shatter the entire Starry Sky Ancient Domain.” Star Ancestor explained.
“Then why don’t you use this finger to break open the Starry Sky Ancient Domain?”
Ye Tian asked curiously.
Star Ancestor said with a dark expression, “If I could do that, would I still need to cultivate you? This finger was sealed by another Eternal Stage expert, so there is no way to lift the seal. Even if the seal were really broken, with Eternal Stage power, it might launch indiscriminate attacks and Annihilate my body. At that point, trying to save myself would turn into suicide instead.”
“What use does it have for me?” Ye Tian asked.
“Ignorant!” Star Ancestor said disdainfully. “A finger of a Eternal Stage expert, though sealed, can still be comprehended. Even obtaining a little insight from it is a heaven-defying opportunity. In the Source Sea, you simply cannot find a single Eternal Stage being. If other Supreme Beings learned that you had a finger of a Eternal Stage expert in your hands, I’m afraid even half-step Eternal Stage experts would come looking for trouble.”
“What do you mean, there isn’t a single Eternal Stage expert in the Source Sea?”
Ye Tian was very curious.
“I don’t know either!” Star Ancestor seemed unwilling to say more, and changed the subject. “Perhaps when you have the chance to rise to half-step Eternal Stage, you may know. Asking me now is useless.”
Ye Tian could not tell whether Star Ancestor did not want to say it, or truly did not know.
But no matter what, this finger of a Eternal Stage expert was indeed a true great opportunity.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian took this finger and left.
After Ye Tian left, Star Ancestor thought inwardly, “Xu Chen, once the contract is signed, you cannot violate it. When you rescue me, I will grant you death. This Eternal Stage finger is indeed real, and it is useful for comprehending Eternal Stage laws. It can even give you many insights and help you raise your cultivation realm faster, but the faster your cultivation realm rises, the sooner you must rescue me!”
If not for the contract, he would never have given this finger of incalculable value to Ye Tian.
In fact, he was also extremely anxious.
His great enemy might descend at any time, so he did not want to keep delaying. Escaping from his predicament as soon as possible was the most urgent matter.
---------------------
After obtaining a finger of Eternal Stage, Ye Tian immediately decided to take it out of the Starry Sky Ancient Domain.
He copied himself from when he was at the First-Tier Supreme Being realm. This copied body entered the Starry Sky Ancient Domain, obtained the finger of Eternal Stage from his clone, and then left the Starry Sky Ancient Domain, silently taking away this treasure.
It could be said that Star Ancestor’s plan had completely come to nothing. If he knew about it, he would probably have wanted to die.
Once inside the Void Source Hall, Ye Tian immediately handed the finger of Eternal Stage to the artifact spirit.
After all, the Void Source Hall artifact spirit was the artifact spirit of Void Source Ancestor, a half-step Eternal Stage expert. It had broad knowledge and might even know more than Star Ancestor in some areas, so perhaps it knew more secrets regarding Eternal Stage.
“Artifact spirit, what do you think this is?”
Ye Tian took out the finger.
The artifact spirit stared closely at it and cried out in shock, “Master, this is the finger of a Eternal Stage expert, isn’t it? Where did you get it?”

Ye Tian briefly explained Star Ancestor’s situation.
After hearing it, the artifact spirit could not help but smile and say, “Master, if this Star Ancestor knew that his Eternal Stage finger had been completely lost, he would probably be heartbroken to death.”
“Artifact spirit, Star Ancestor said this Eternal Stage finger is very valuable. Does it have any other uses?”
Ye Tian asked.
“There is another very important use!” the artifact spirit said. “Eternal Stage experts are unimaginably terrifying beings. Even my former master, Void Source Ancestor, had never seen one. They are too mysterious; in fact, no one even knows how many Eternal Stage experts exist, but they definitely do exist, because there are relics left behind by Eternal Stage experts in the Source Sea. This finger of Eternal Stage can be fused into a finger. Others would not dare to fuse it, because if the fusion fails, they will die without a doubt. But Master can use a copied body to fuse this finger of Eternal Stage, and if you try countless times, there will eventually be a day of success. Once it succeeds, Master will have a killer trump card. For example, you could use this finger of Eternal Stage to unleash a finger technique-type supreme skill, enough to leap levels and blast top-tier Supreme Beings to death. Moreover, once Master fuses this finger, it will also be easier to comprehend more mysteries and benefits.”
.... .... ...
“Yes, this method is indeed suitable for me!”
Ye Tian nodded.
It had to be said, the artifact spirit was really clever; he had not thought of this method at all.
Afterwards, Ye Tian found a secret technique for fusing foreign objects in the inheritance of Void Source Ancestor, which could be used to attempt fusion with this finger of a Eternal Stage expert.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian had his copied body fuse with this finger of a Eternal Stage expert.
The first fusion.
Boom!!!!
The copied body directly shattered, and the fusion failed.
After that, Ye Tian copied another body and began the second fusion.
It was obvious that the second fusion still failed.
Again and again, the fusion failed again and again.
For Ye Tian, these repeated failures were nothing; he only lost a little resources.
