Chapter 1591: Supreme Being!

Ancient Star Clan, Ancient Star Stone space.
Ye Tian came here once again and began to use the Ancient Star Stone source to make a breakthrough.
At the same time, the Ancient Star Clan had prepared various types of Eternal Source Item sources.
Unfortunately, the Ancient Star Clan was sealed here, so the Eternal Source Items they could find were very few. If it weren't to nurture Ye Tian, they would not have brought out so many Eternal Source Item sources.
Boom!!!!!
A massive amount of Ancient Star Stone source surged into Ye Tian's body, being absorbed and refined by him, causing his body to begin changing.
At this moment, Ye Tian absorbed the sources of other Eternal Source Items into his body, making the changes in his body increasingly violent and his aura increasingly powerful.
This was the process of promoting from an Invincible to a Supreme Being.
The Ancient Star Eternal Body condensed by this body of Ye Tian's was an Upper-rank Eternal Body; therefore, once he broke through, the increase in strength would be immense.
Inside the Ancient Star Tower.
Star Ancestor's consciousness saw that Ye Tian was breaking through and was very gratified.
Ye Tian's foundation was very solid. Although the condensed Ancient Star Eternal Body was not perfectly flawless, it was second only to his former self.
Little did he know, if Ye Tian wanted to condense a flawless Ancient Star Eternal Body, he could have done so. However, it would require repeatedly using duplicated bodies to attempt cultivation and perfect it time and again. Since he was cultivating here with the Ancient Star Clan, he naturally didn't have such an opportunity.
Consequently, Ye Tian's Ancient Star Eternal Body actually still had dozens of flaws, but it was still extremely powerful.
In terms of strength, this body's combat power was inferior to his main body, and the gap was still quite large. After all, the foundation and the degree of perfection of the Ancient Star Body were lacking, but it shouldn't be far from the combat power of the Void Lord.
However, Ye Tian didn't care about this duplicated body at all. He would certainly use it for research in the future, so he naturally wasn't that dedicated.
"Break through. Once you step into the 7th-layer Supreme Being realm, you can help me break a sliver of the seal. Then, I can strike with full force from within the seal and break it open!" Star Ancestor thought to himself.
Time passed slowly. After a long period, Ye Tian finally completed his breakthrough.
……
"I've broken through!"
Ye Tian murmured.
At this moment, this duplicated body of his had already reached the Supreme Being realm. Although it was only the 1st-layer Supreme Being realm, in terms of strength, it likely surpassed the ten great Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Clan.
However, he was in no hurry to fall out with the Ancient Star Clan for now.
After all, his foundation in the Ancient Star Clan was currently too weak. Even if he could defeat the ten great Supreme Beings, he still couldn't manage to rule the entire Ancient Star Clan.
Therefore, he would slowly take control of the Ancient Star Clan and collect their Eternal resources.
Additionally, Ye Tian annihilated his clone left in the Dark Forbidden Domain and made the Ancient Star Clan body his new clone.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian seemed like a different person in the Ancient Star Clan, focusing on power and collecting resources. This gradually caused dissatisfaction among many Supreme Beings, but Star Ancestor didn't care. He didn't truly consider the Ancient Star Clan as his descendants at all.
The Ancient Star Clan was merely descendants evolved from his aura. All he needed was an assistant who could help him escape his predicament. Thus, no matter how excessively Ye Tian acted, he would not be the slightest bit unhappy.
As time passed, Ye Tian collected many Eternal resources, and the strength of this duplicated body was also progressing rapidly.
As for Ye Tian's main body, it had long since condensed the Endless Eternal Tribulation Body. His cultivation had reached the Extreme, and he could become a Supreme Being at any time.
Ye Tian didn't plan to delay. Anyway, the fusion of Eternal Bodies could also be carried out in the Supreme Being realm.
He now needed to raise his main body's cultivation to the Supreme Being realm so that his strength would be powerful enough to obtain more benefits.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian began to charge toward the Supreme Being realm. He directly expelled all the Void Sons from the Void Source Hall, no longer allowing these Void Sons to comprehend the Void Source Tablet.
Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh!!!
One Void Son after another was comprehending the Void Source Tablet when they suddenly found themselves interrupted, and then they were teleported out of the Void Source Hall.
They were extremely confused. They wanted to enter the Void Source Hall but found that they couldn't get in no matter what.
"What's going on?"
These ten Void Sons were filled with doubt.
Immediately, they began to contact other Void Sons. The other Void Sons were the same; they couldn't enter the Void Source Hall at all.
Soon, a powerful Supreme Being spread a piece of news: the Void Source Hall had a master.
Once this news came out, a large number of Void Sons in the Source Sea were shocked.
For the longest time, the Void Source Hall had been masterless, but they hadn't expected it to have a master now.
When the Void Source Hall was masterless, they still dared to go in and comprehend the Void Source Tablet. Now that it had a master, even if they were allowed in, they wouldn't dare to enter!
Soon, the Void Sons were all very curious about who exactly had taken control of the Void Source Hall.
Unfortunately, they couldn't find out.
------------------------
Throughout the Source Sea.
One Void Divine Son after another learned that the Void Source Hall had a master, and they were all very surprised.
The Void Source Hall had existed for such a long time, yet it had only produced dozens of Void Divine Sons. However, not a single Void Divine Son had been promoted to Void Lord, so they couldn't control the Void Source Hall. But now, a Void Divine Son had suddenly become a Void Lord and taken control of the Void Source Hall?
Who was this Void Lord?
However, they didn't think too much about it. Even if they couldn't become the Void Lord, they could still continue to grow stronger in the future. They weren't necessarily inferior to that mysterious Void Lord.
Inside the Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian arrived before the Void Source Tablet and began to absorb the Void Source Tablet according to the secret technique of the Void Source Ancestor, while also taking out a Void Source Heart.
After all, his main body was not like the Ancient Star Clan body, which was 100% compatible with the Ancient Star Stone source.
Therefore, if he wanted to forcibly raise it to the level of 100% compatibility, he had to use the Void Source Heart.
Boom!!!!!
The Void Source Heart was placed at Ye Tian's heart and began to be absorbed and refined, causing the compatibility between his body and the Void Source Tablet to instantly reach 100%.
Buzz!
Ye Tian's body felt the call of the Void Source Tablet's source. This was the manifestation of reaching 100% compatibility.
Thus, Ye Tian immediately absorbed and refined the source of the Void Source Tablet.
As a massive amount of the Void Source Tablet's source was absorbed and refined by Ye Tian, he could now accommodate the sources of other Eternal Source Items.
There were hundreds of Eternal Source Items in the Void Source Hall, even more than the Eternal Source Items owned by the Ancient Star Clan.
Consequently, Ye Tian did not lack the sources of Eternal Source Items.
One after another, the sources of Eternal Source Items were absorbed and refined by Ye Tian, making his foundation more and more terrifying. Furthermore, although his Endless Eternal Body was only ranked as a Middle-rank Eternal Body, it was the most perfect Eternal Body. To a certain extent, it was much stronger than the Ancient Star Eternal Body, which had dozens of flaws.
Therefore, once Ye Tian broke through to the Supreme Being realm, his combat power would be incomparably terrifying.
A massive amount of resources was delivered to Ye Tian's side under the control of the artifact spirit, providing energy for his breakthrough to the Supreme Being realm.
Unknowingly, a long time passed.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian completed his breakthrough.
He had become a Supreme Being.
At this time, the Dark Forbidden Domain seemed to undergo some changes. The source of the Dark Forbidden Domain suddenly became much stronger. This was because Ye Tian had broken through to the Supreme Being realm, and the Dark Forbidden Domain received feedback.
But the breakthrough of just one person was far from enough to cause a qualitative change in the Dark Forbidden Domain. It would still require two more Invincibles to break through to the Supreme Being realm before the Dark Forbidden Domain would transform into an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
