Chapter 1630: The enemy’s identity has been uncovered!

Zhenhe Sword Ancestor said, “We went to investigate the ruins of the Dragon Scale Sacred World. The enemy left very quickly, and so there were some clues left behind. The other side clearly used another Eternal Source Weapon—the Blood Devouring Bead. This Eternal Source Weapon is a forbidden weapon, prohibited from use by Eternal Realm experts, because it can devour Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts, refine their pure power, and thus help oneself break through to the next realm. Based on our speculation, some mysterious force is preparing this time to use the Blood Devouring Bead to devour a large number of Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts in order to break through to the Eternal Realm.”
“Can the Eternal Realm be broken through like that?”
Someone was very puzzled.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor continued, “Of course it isn’t that easy. One must also prepare all kinds of other precious and rare resources, and even then the success rate is less than one in ten. Moreover, even if the breakthrough succeeds, it still does not count as a true Eternal Realm. It can only be considered a pseudo-Eternal Realm, possessing the strength of the Eternal Realm but not the power of the Eternal Realm. A true Eternal Realm can fuse with the Eternal Rule, and after breaking through and cultivating for some time, one can fuse with the innate Eternal Rule and wield the true power of the innate Eternal Rule. However, if one breaks through using a method like the Blood Devouring Bead, one cannot fuse with the innate Eternal Rule, and can even fail to perfectly fuse with an ordinary Eternal Rule.”
“But for some half-step Eternal Realm experts at the Extreme Stage, once they see no hope of breaking through, using this method to step into the Eternal Realm is enough so long as they gain the power of the Eternal Realm. They do not care about the future.”
“Furthermore, once one breaks through to the Eternal Realm, even if it is a flawed Eternal Realm, the actual battle strength is still that of a true Eternal Realm. And the Eternal Realm power gained after breaking through from the Extreme Stage is terrifying; some top-tier Eternal Realm experts who break through normally may not necessarily be stronger than them.”
After Zhenhe Sword Ancestor finished speaking, many Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts became rather worried about their own safety.
If that mysterious force set its sights on them, how could they possibly resist an Extreme Stage powerhouse!
Seeing many Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts panic, Fenggu Domain Ancestor said, “Everyone need not worry too much. The various major forces of Source Sea are already investigating. Although that mysterious force has destroyed many powers, some forces had Supreme Beings with avatars, and their true bodies did not perish—only the avatars were killed. Through the information from these people, we will gradually collect the origins of that mysterious force, find them, and then join forces to wipe out this force.”
At this time, many Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts prepared to leave their own forces, either to stay at Source God Pavilion forever, or go to the safest place, or hide themselves.
Only then would they avoid being destroyed by that mysterious force.
As for Ye Tian, although he did not think that a small force like the Dark Forbidden Domain would be targeted by such a mysterious force, he also did not want to sit and wait for death.
If he were truly unlucky, then the Dark Forbidden Domain would be finished.
Therefore, what he most hoped for was that the mysterious force would be found.
After the meeting ended, Ye Tian stayed behind on his own initiative and said, “Twelve Ancestors, I possess the ability to create avatars, and I can help with the investigation. Even if I fall, it is nothing serious.”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor pondered for a moment, then nodded and said, “Endless Ancestor, if you want to help, that is fine. Your cultivation is at the Eighth Supreme Being level, so it is not easy to attract attention. In contrast, even if we possess the ability to create avatars, our avatars are still half-step Eternal Realm experts at the Extreme Stage. If we appear, that mysterious force may not necessarily make a move. But you, on the other hand, would not draw attention. You really can help.”
Fenggu Domain Ancestor said, “Endless Ancestor, if you truly want to help, I will send you to one force—the Moon-Gathering Pavilion. This force’s headquarters is located in Moon-Gathering World. Moon-Gathering Pavilion has only one half-step Eternal Realm expert, but it has many, many Supreme Beings, and there are even thirty-three Ninth Supreme Beings. Thus, this force is very likely to be targeted. At present, ordinary half-step Eternal Realm experts do not dare to help, and even top-tier Eternal Realm experts do not dare to go help Moon-Gathering Pavilion. If a half-step Eternal Realm expert at the Extreme Stage appears in Moon-Gathering Pavilion, the enemy will not show up. You go to Moon-Gathering Pavilion, and if the enemy appears, notify us immediately. However, the enemy’s strength is terrifying, so it is very likely we will not make it in time.”
At this, Ye Tian guaranteed, “Twelve Ancestors, I may not be able to block the enemy, but once the enemy appears, I have a way to investigate their identity.”
“Really?”
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others were shocked.
Based on their understanding, the enemy concealed their aura, and even altered it. Moreover, when an Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm expert made a move, they almost instantly killed those ordinary or powerful half-step Eternal Realm experts and Supreme Beings.
Even if those people were only avatars and their true bodies were still alive, they had no way of knowing the other side’s identity at all.
Yet Ye Tian was confident he could investigate the other side’s identity. Was that possible?
“Endless Ancestor, what method do you have? Could you explain it?” Zhenhe Sword Ancestor asked.
Ye Tian shook his head and said, “This is my secret. Please do not ask, Twelve Ancestors. But I can guarantee that once the enemy appears, their identity will definitely be exposed. It would be useless even if they kill me instantly.”
The Twelve Ancestors smiled happily, and then immediately had Ye Tian rush to Moon-Gathering Pavilion.

Moon-Gathering World.
Ye Tian arrived here and stayed in a giant city near Moon-Gathering Pavilion’s headquarters.
Throughout Moon-Gathering Pavilion, only Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor knew of Ye Tian’s arrival, but she did not care about the arrival of merely an Eighth Supreme Being and paid little attention to it at all.
At present, Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor only hoped that the mysterious enemy would not target her.
As for fleeing, Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor had no such intention. Moon-Gathering Pavilion was her everything; she would not run away.
Time slowly passed, and Ye Tian’s copied body remained here, silently waiting.
Finally, on this day, figures of imposing grandeur appeared around Moon-Gathering World.
Suddenly, a bead floated above Moon-Gathering World.
This was the Space-Time Sand Bead!
Buzz!!!!!
The entire Moon-Gathering World was completely cut off from the outside world. Even communication artifacts could not transmit messages outward, and the connection between true body and avatar also became extremely weak. If the avatar wanted to send information to the true body, there would even be a certain delay.
Boom!!!!
Moon-Gathering World’s world barrier was torn apart. At the same time, a blood-colored bead turned into a blood river, spanning across Moon-Gathering World.
Countless living beings were instantly absorbed into the blood river, and even ordinary Supreme Beings had no power to resist.
Boom!!!!
Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor appeared, angrily staring outside Moon-Gathering World.
“Who exactly are you??”
However, what came in response was a finger pointed through the void.
This was a finger strike at the Extreme Stage. No matter how Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor defended, her body was crushed by that single finger.
Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor still wanted to recover her body, but strands of afterimages of fingers came slaughtering over, instantly blasting her body apart.
In almost a single breath, Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor was attacked many times, and her vitality was completely shattered. Her残躯 was absorbed by the blood river, or rather, by the Blood Devouring Bead.
Then, a huge hand directly smashed the Moon-Gathering Pavilion headquarters to pieces. Those Ninth Supreme Beings were almost like ants, with absolutely no ability to resist.
Ye Tian also saw that finger, but did not stop it, because he could not stop it.
Of course, his Endless Eternal Body had terrifying life-preserving ability. Even though his current cultivation was only at the Eighth Supreme Being level, his life-preserving ability should still be somewhat stronger than Moon-Gathering Domain Ancestor’s.
But that was useless. Once that Extreme Stage powerhouse got serious, he would still be killed within one or two breaths.
In any case, he had already used his Copy Talent to investigate this Extreme Stage powerhouse’s identity, so there was no need to expose himself.
Thus.
Ye Tian annihilated this copied body himself, and the suicide of an Eighth Supreme Being naturally did not attract the attention of that Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm expert.
