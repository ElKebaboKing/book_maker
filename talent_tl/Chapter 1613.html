Chapter 1612: The Eternal Realm Has Made Its Move!

The copying talent instantly enveloped the place where that innate eternal lifeform was located, detected the other party’s talent, and the copying talent memorized the other party’s body.
In the future, Ye Tian could completely copy the body of this innate eternal lifeform.
In addition.
The talent of this innate eternal lifeform was indeed extremely formidable; its entire body was made of eternal ancestral talents, showing just how powerful its talent was. No wonder it was an innate eternal lifeform.
Power Eternal Ancestral Talent!
Speed Eternal Ancestral Talent!
Defense Eternal Ancestral Talent!
……
Ye Tian already possessed these eternal ancestral talents, so he did not pay them much mind. But there was one eternal ancestral talent Ye Tian did not have, and that was the Undying Eternal Ancestral Talent.
This meant that this innate eternal lifeform possessed undying ability; once it grew up, it would be very hard to kill.
Of course, it had only just been born now. Although it was comparable to an Invincible, its strength was very weak and it could still be easily killed.
Suddenly.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor locked onto Ye Tian.
“You still dare to come closer? Seeking death!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor made his move.
He held a long spear and thrust it at Ye Tian, but he did not go all out.
In his view, Ye Tian was only a Third-Tier Invincible, not worthy of him using his full strength.
Therefore, the power of this strike was only at the Seventh-Tier.
However, this strike was blocked by Ye Tian with a single finger, not harming Ye Tian in the slightest.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor’s expression changed. Without another word, he immediately attacked with full power; he did not want Ye Tian to ruin this operation.
Boom!!!
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor attacked with all his strength, endless spacetime shattered, and that terrifying strike seemed as if it was about to Annihilation this boundless expanse.
To Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor, distance was no longer anything. Unless Ye Tian escaped beyond the range of his perception, he could not avoid this strike.
This was an attack power of the Ninth-Tier.
Ye Tian also did not hold back, directly activating the power of that Eternal Realm expert’s finger and unleashing the battle strength of a Ninth-Tier Invincible.
In an instant, a huge finger appeared in the void, as if it stood above all rules, descending directly before Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor and pressing down with a single finger.
With a loud boom!!!!
Ye Tian’s attack collided with Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor’s, causing a spacetime tide; even Fenggu River began to shake violently, and countless regions collapsed.
Crack, crack, crack!!!!
Numerous cracks appeared on Ye Tian’s body. This was the injury caused by forcibly activating the power of an Eternal Realm expert’s finger. However, he only looked a little ferocious; in truth, his injuries were not very serious.
After all, his Endless Eternal Body had now reached the Major realm, and his cultivation had also reached the Third-Tier Invincible realm, so the price he paid for activating the Eternal Realm expert’s finger was somewhat lighter.
But he knew very clearly that even so, he was still no match for Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor.
Even though Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor had cut away his cultivation, with the addition of various treasures, the battle strength he could actually unleash still reached the Ninth-Tier.
Moreover, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor could not only unleash Ninth-Tier battle strength once; he could do so every time.
“Go!”
Ye Tian did not want to continue fighting. He had already achieved his goal, so there was no need to keep taking risks.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian fled rapidly, and Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor struck again, but this was blocked by Ye Tian’s second Ninth-Tier Invincible attack, and he quickly left this place.
If Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor wanted to pursue and kill Ye Tian, Ye Tian probably would not be able to escape.
However, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor did not dare take the risk.
His current goal was to take the innate eternal lifeform back to the Eternal Tribulation Domain, not to kill Ye Tian.
He could tell that Ye Tian possessed a finger from an Eternal Realm expert, and that for some unknown method he had fused with that finger, allowing him to unleash its power and thus erupt with Ninth-Tier battle strength.
However, compared with the innate eternal lifeform, an Eternal Realm expert’s finger was worth far less.
Therefore, he would not take the risk for the sake of the Eternal Realm finger.
Immediately.
He seized the newly born innate eternal lifeform and was about to leave Fenggu River.
However, at this moment, a sudden change occurred.
A fair hand crossed spacetime and came forth; even Fenggu River’s rules found it difficult to restrict the arrival of this hand.
One could see the rules that restricted those above the Sixth-Tier Invincible realm in Fenggu River shattering inch by inch, the entire rule chains of Fenggu River collapsing, the rules of the entire void thrown into chaos, all of them unable to stop this hand’s arrival.
And when this hand arrived, the Eternal Tribulation Race and the Invincibles of Fenggu River were all frozen in place, practically unable to move.
Even Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor found it very hard to move; before this hand, he seemed like nothing more than an ant.
“Eternal Realm!!!!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor was unwilling.
Just one step short!
If not for Ye Tian stopping him at that moment, perhaps he would already have left with the innate eternal lifeform.
Of course, perhaps that Eternal Realm expert had been hiding in the dark all along.
If that was the case, then no matter what, he could not take this innate eternal lifeform away.
“Why? Why are you coming to snatch the innate eternal lifeform?”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor questioned.
“Noisy!”
Along with a crisp female voice, a finger descended, and with just one finger it heavily injured Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor and left him critically wounded and dying.
The next instant, the innate eternal lifeform disappeared.
It must be said that the Eternal Tribulation Race suffered too grievous a loss.
Not only had they lost a large number of clansmen, but Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor had even cut away his own cultivation for the sake of the innate eternal lifeform, only for it to still be snatched away at the critical moment by a mysterious Eternal Realm expert.
It could be said that the Eternal Tribulation Race lost miserably.
But the real winner, aside from that Eternal Realm expert, was Ye Tian.
At this time.
Ye Tian left Fenggu River and returned to the Void Source Hall.
Then, he activated layer upon layer of array restrictions in the Void Source Hall, and only then did he解除 the copied body, before copying the body of that innate eternal lifeform.
He did not want to painstakingly copy an innate eternal lifeform only for it to be snatched away by an Eternal Realm expert—that would be far too awkward.
Inside the Void Source Hall, a huge one-horned creature appeared; this was precisely the copied body of the innate eternal lifeform that Ye Tian had condensed.
Ye Tian existed in the body of the innate eternal lifeform and immediately sensed how extraordinary this body was.
Putting aside those eternal ancestral talents, just the countless innate eternal rules contained within its body were enough to allow Ye Tian to comprehend the innate eternal rules at every moment; his progress in the eternal rules was improving at an unbelievable speed.
Moreover, the body structure of the innate eternal lifeform was entirely worth studying by Ye Tian. This was simply an innate eternal body!
“In the years to come, I’ll carefully study and comprehend this innate eternal lifeform!”
Ye Tian murmured.
