Chapter 1459: Selection of the Human Race's 100,000 Geniuses
!
While the Supreme Domain Ancestors were contemplating the third Dark Holy Land, the World Ancestor continued, "The birth of the third Dark Holy Land will initially involve a struggle between Half-step Domain Ancestors. Although the grade of the treasures born at the beginning... won't be very high, it is still a great opportunity.
Therefore, every major power has a quota for how many Half-step Domain Ancestors they can send in.
Each of you Supreme Domain Ancestors has 100,000 slots. You can send 100,000 Half-step Domain Ancestors, or you can train them yourselves.
If you can keep the benefits and opportunities these Half-step Domain Ancestors obtain under your control, those opportunities will belong to you as well.
After all, since you cannot enter the Dark Holy Land yourselves, you can only rely on those Half-step Domain Ancestors to seize the opportunities!"
"Yes!"
The Supreme Domain Ancestors responded one after another.
As for after a hundred million years, they could also send a certain number of top-tier Domain Ancestors into it.
It would only be after a trillion years, within one Realm Year, that Supreme Domain Ancestors like them would have the chance to take the stage.
"Go back and prepare. According to the calculations of us Progenitors, the third Dark Holy Land will be born in approximately 3 million years!"
The World Ancestor reminded them.
Subsequently, the Supreme Domain Ancestors went back to prepare.
The two Progenitors of the Battle Gate also needed to make some preparations for their future struggle for the opportunities within the third Dark Holy Land.
....In the Profound Void Holy Realm, the Human Race.
Ye Tian immediately began a selection process covering the billions of Half-step Domain Ancestors within the human race. Countless Half-step Domain Ancestors across the human territories would undergo rounds of assessments and battles to select those with the highest talent, strongest combat power, and greatest potential.
For this battle, Ye Tian specifically obtained a projection of the Battle Realm Tower from the Battle Gate to be used for the human race's selection.
And so.
The human race's internal selection began.
Although many human Half-step Domain Ancestors did not understand the significance of this selection, this was an order issued by the strongest of the human race and the creator of the human rise, the Myriad Law War Ancestor. It was bound to be a truly great opportunity.
Consequently, countless human Half-step Domain Ancestors went wild, cultivating hard for this selection. After all, this selection was set to last for 300 years, meaning even those with slightly lower cultivation or those who had just broken through to the Half-step Domain Ancestor realm could participate.
As long as they performed well, they could receive rewards from the human race to rapidly increase their cultivation.
In short, the selection rules set by Ye Tian would not miss a single genius.
Soon, the million-year period was nearing its end.
At this time, the 100,000 human geniuses who had emerged from a million years of slaughter were born.
What no one knew was that Ye Tian had killed a human Half-step Domain Ancestor who had committed a capital crime, replicated that person's body, and then bestowed some ancestral talents upon this body.
In this way, this body's talent remained a monstrous talent second only to his original body, and its cultivation was at the Half-step Domain Ancestor realm, possessing high growth potential.
Furthermore, Ye Tian spent a huge price to reshape and even modify this body.
Most importantly, the origin of this body could be fully traced; even the Progenitors would not be able to see any problems.
Thus, Ye Tian's replicated body, along with the other 99,999 geniuses, would enter the third Dark Holy Land shortly.
On this day.
In the Profound Void Holy Realm, before the Human Holy Palace.
The 100,000 geniuses gathered here, and Ye Tian appeared as well.
"Greetings, Lord Myriad Law War Ancestor!"
The human geniuses said respectfully.
Ye Tian looked at these 100,000 human geniuses and said, "You may be very curious as to why I held such a selection process, and you might even be guessing what kind of rewards there will be.
However, I tell you, there are no rewards, only one great opportunity!"
Opportunity? The 100,000 geniuses all became curious. What kind of opportunity would make the human race go to such great lengths to hold a selection for a million years?
"You should all know about the Dark Holy Realm and the Dark Destruction Domain from the human library. Now, the third Dark Holy Land of the Dark Forbidden Zone is being born. It is filled with endless resources. Obtaining any treasure at random can easily allow you to promote to a Domain Ancestor. If you obtain enough opportunities, there is even hope of promoting to a Supreme Domain Ancestor in the future."
Ye Tian spoke truthfully.
Boom!!!! The 100,000 geniuses were excited.
What a massive opportunity this was! They never expected that they would be able to obtain it.
An opportunity to become a Supreme Domain Ancestor could not be found in the Profound Void Holy Realm at all.
In the entire human race, after countless years, there was currently only one Supreme Domain Ancestor, Ye Tian. Even the ten Great Holy Gates of the Dark Holy Realm did not have many Supreme Domain Ancestors! Once one became a Supreme Domain Ancestor, they could create a Holy Land-level power.
Ye Tian gave some instructions and also warned that this opportunity was extremely dangerous; one could fall if they were not careful. If they were afraid, they could withdraw.
However, no one withdrew.
If they withdrew, they might never have such an opportunity again for the rest of their lives.
For them, having raised their cultivation to Half-step Domain Ancestor and having cultivated for long years, they had lived long enough. Their goals were the Domain Ancestor realm and the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm. Now that such a great opportunity was before them, how could they refuse out of fear of death?
Time passed slowly, and another several tens of thousands of years went by.
On this day.
The World Ancestor sent a message: the third Dark Holy Land was about to be born, and all major powers were to be prepared.
....Deep within the Dark Forbidden Zone.
In an area far away from the Dark Holy Realm and the Dark Destruction Domain, a massive vortex was generated. The area of this vortex far exceeded that of a large Great Realm. It wasn't very large at first, but it gradually expanded to a size comparable to the Dark Holy Realm.
At this time.
In the center of this vortex, worlds were being born, and continents were evolving.
Countless domains were born one by one, and later, the first Holy Realm was born.
At the same time, these worlds also gave birth to a large number of innate beings and ancient primordial chaos gods and demons.
As for Taboo Creatures, they had not yet been born. After all, Taboo Creatures were comparable to Domain Ancestors, so they naturally wouldn't be born so quickly; otherwise, how would the ordinary beings of this third world survive?
Suddenly.
A figure emerged here, and a massive Gate of Space-Time appeared behind him.
He was the Spacetime Progenitor of the Dark Destruction Domain.
Next, a giant golden tower appeared, and a person walked out from within the tower—it was the World Ancestor of the Battle Gate.
Not long after the World Ancestor arrived, the Holy Progenitor also arrived, and the Destruction Progenitor came as well.
In terms of speed, the Spacetime Progenitor and the World Ancestor were the fastest, but in terms of strength, the Holy Progenitor and the Destruction Progenitor were the most powerful.
One Progenitor after another arrived, their gazes fixed upon this third Dark Holy Land.
"What a familiar scene!"
The Destruction Progenitor grinned, revealing a cruel smile.
If he could, he would even want to wipe out all the living beings here and transform it into a second Destruction Domain.
Unfortunately, he couldn't do it at the moment. Even if he could, the practitioners of the Dark Holy Realm would surely stop him.
