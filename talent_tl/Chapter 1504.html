Chapter 1503: Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals!

Not only were the two Supreme Saint Beasts of the Sea-Sky Saint Sect stunned, but the other Saint Beasts were also dumbfounded.
He Yuan Saint Beast had been very low-key for the past few Dark Eras, with little presence, and had shown almost no signs of a breakthrough. How did he suddenly break through?
One must know that many Saint Beasts had better talent than He Yuan Saint Beast and faster cultivation speeds, and had reached the limit of Top-tier Saint Beasts long ago, even entering the Dark Forbidden Zone twice, yet they still hadn't broken through.
On what basis did He Yuan Saint Beast break through?
Immediately, some Top-tier Saint Beasts became jealous.
Of course, many disciples and some Saint Beasts of the Sea-Sky Saint Sect were still very excited. After all, the addition of a Supreme Saint Beast to the Sea-Sky Saint Sect meant the sect's strength had grown even greater, and their lives would naturally be much easier.
Furthermore, the Great Tribulation was approaching. The stronger the Sea-Sky Saint Sect was, the more hope they had of survival.
After all, the Great Tribulation was terrifying, and for the Sea-Sky Saint Sect, it was also a massive challenge.
"Ensure He Yuan Saint Beast's safety, and do not let anyone disturb—him!"
The two Supreme Saint Beasts, Wolf Bone Saint Beast and Violent Ape Saint Beast, ordered.
However, they had no idea that this was not their Sea-Sky Saint Sect's He Yuan Saint Beast, but merely Ye Tian's duplicated body. If they knew, they would definitely kill Ye Tian's duplicated body at any cost.
As time passed, combined with the refining of a large amount of resources, Ye Tian's duplicated body continued to transform, its physique becoming stronger and stronger.
After a long period of time, the Supreme Saint Beast body of Ye Tian's duplicated body, He Yuan Saint Beast, had completely finished its transformation.
At this point, this duplicated body had stepped into the Supreme Saint Beast realm.
When the duplicated body He Yuan Saint Beast stepped into the Supreme Saint Beast realm, it actually established a faint connection with the main body.
Previously, the duplicated body was too weak, and the connection could not cross the Gate. Thus, there was no way to connect with the main body; every time, it had to pass through the Gate and enter the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land before it could contact the main body.
But now, there was no need to worry.
"The connection is very faint, but at least I can connect. It only takes about several decades to connect once!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Soon, several decades passed, and the duplicated body connected with the main body once.
The moment they connected, the Duplication Talent underwent a change; strictly speaking, the ability of the duplicated body changed.
"I can re-solidify a duplicated body?"
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
Previously, his duplicated body, Ancestor Qing Ye, did not occupy a duplicated body slot and was permanently solidified as a special clone.
Unfortunately, this clone had disregarded its foundation for the sake of promotion back then and had fused with someone else's Ancestral Law. Thus, its potential was very limited. Although it had now reached the level of a Top-tier Ancestor, wanting to advance to a Supreme Ancestor would be a hundred times more difficult than for others.
Therefore, this clone's potential was lacking, and it was no longer of much use.
After all, in the Dark Forbidden Zone, Ye Tian's main body could suppress everything. What use was a mere Top-tier Ancestor clone? It wasn't even as useful as an incarnation.
"Then I'll give up the Ancestor Qing Ye clone and make He Yuan Saint Beast the permanently solidified clone. That way, my main body can free up the duplicated body slot, allowing me to use duplicated bodies repeatedly and burn Ancestral Souls to comprehend Ultimate Ancestral Techniques and fuse Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. My cultivation speed will increase many times over!"
Ye Tian planned.
*Crack!!!!*
Dark Soul Domain.
Ancestor Qing Ye's clone shattered, and its aura dissipated.
Immediately, the Ancestors of the Human Race sensed it and became panicked.
But soon, Ye Tian sent a message, which calmed the Human Race's Ancestors.
With the dissipation of the Ancestor Qing Ye clone, He Yuan Saint Beast, far away in the Sea-Sky Saint Sect of the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth, became the permanently solidified clone.
At this moment, Ye Tian finally breathed a sigh of relief.
"The duplicated body doesn't need to be maintained constantly; in the future, it can be used as a consumable to constantly overdraw potential to comprehend Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. With the Evolution ability and the continuously condensing duplicated bodies, I can definitely comprehend a second Supreme Ancestral Technique in a short time. Of course, the prerequisite is to first occupy the Sea-Sky Saint Sect; otherwise, I won't be able to comprehend the rules in the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth in peace."
Ye Tian murmured.
Before long, only several decades remained before the Great Tribulation of the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth.
-----------------
Saint Realm Heaven and Earth, Sea-Sky Saint Sect.
Ye Tian's clone, He Yuan Saint Beast, was with Wolf Bone Saint Beast and Violent Ape Saint Beast, discussing the time of the Great Tribulation.
At this time, Ye Tian had just come out of seclusion, so he didn't understand many things.
"The Great Tribulation is coming; this time, our Saint Realm Heaven and Earth will face great trouble," the Violent Ape Saint Beast lamented.
Ye Tian didn't say much; after all, he wasn't the real He Yuan Saint Beast, and it wouldn't be good if he said the wrong thing.
Therefore, he only listened, occasionally speaking up to say some unimportant words.
Gradually, Ye Tian gained some understanding of this Great Tribulation.
The Great Tribulation faced by the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth was not as terrifying as the one in the Dark Forbidden Zone. The Dark Forbidden Zone was too weak; during the first three Great Tribulations, there was no resistance at all. Even Top-tier Ancestors would hide and not dare to show their faces, allowing experts from other foreign realms to plunder resources.
· ········· Seeking Flowers ··· ·····
But the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth was different. The Saint Realm Heaven and Earth possessed Supreme Saint Beasts, and more than one. It even had a Supreme Saint Beast of the second level, so it naturally had a certain degree of resistance.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian spoke up: "Wolf Bone Saint Beast, Violent Ape Saint Beast, why not destroy those Gates in the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth? Top-tier Ancestors find it hard to invade those forbidden lands, but we Supreme Saint Beasts should have enough strength to destroy their forbidden lands and shatter their Gates, right?"
Wolf Bone Saint Beast smiled and said: "He Yuan Saint Beast, you've just broken through to the Supreme Saint Beast level, so you don't understand some things. True, we do have the strength to destroy a forbidden land, but those forces have already locked onto the coordinate position of the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth and can establish a Gate again. When they establish a Gate next time, I'm afraid they will seek frantic revenge. Moreover, destroying a Gate is not simple. Gates are divided into three tiers. A First-tier Gate is the weakest and only allows Top-tier Saint Beasts to enter; Supreme Saint Beasts can also pass through by paying a price, but it will cause damage to the Gate and require many resources to repair. A Second-tier Gate allows Supreme Saint Beasts to pass, and even Invincible Saint Beasts can enter by paying a price. A Third-tier Gate is the legendary Gate, comparable to a Gate naturally born between heaven and earth; that kind of Gate allows Invincible Saint Beasts to enter, and even Peerless Saint Beasts can pass through by paying a price."
... .. 0
"Is our Sea-Sky Saint Sect's Gate a First-tier Gate?"
Ye Tian asked.
"Yes!" Wolf Bone Saint Beast nodded. "We don't have the construction method for a Second-tier Gate, but the Saint Realm Heaven and Earth does have one Second-tier Gate. The force corresponding to that Second-tier Gate is terrifying; the three Great Saint Sects alone cannot stop them. Therefore, our three Great Saint Sects have allied with some foreign forces. we won't destroy their Gates, but during the Great Tribulation, they need to join forces with us to resist the force where that Second-tier Gate is located. This is the ultimate reason why we don't destroy some Gates."
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian understood.
Next, Wolf Bone Saint Beast and Violent Ape Saint Beast revealed another secret, which was the purpose of those foreign forces' frantic desire to invade other realms.
All along, Ye Tian thought the reason those foreign forces invaded was for resources and to comprehend foreign rules to break through to the level of Supreme Ancestor, Supreme Saint Beast, or Bone Emperor, but that was only part of the reason. The more important reason was for the Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals!
