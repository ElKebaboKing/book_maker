Chapter 1557: Harvest, Space Eternal Ancestral Talent!

Sovereign Tianfeng crushed one of the boxes, revealing an incredibly massive azure crystal. This azure crystal was the Eternal Source Substance, Mind Azure Gold.
"Protect me while I refine the Mind Azure Gold!"
Sovereign Tianfeng said.
"Alright!"
The other first-class Invincibles nodded.
Once Sovereign Tianfeng broke through to the Supreme Being realm, it would only bring them benefits and no harm.
Perhaps in the future, with Sovereign Tianfeng's help, they would also have hope of ascending to become Supreme Beings.
Like the top-ranked Eternal-name level forces in the Tianlin Realm, they all started with the birth of their first Supreme Being, and later, with that Supreme Being's assistance, more Supreme Beings were born, causing the forces themselves to grow stronger and stronger.
Sovereign Tianfeng began to extract the substance from within the Mind Azure Gold. Ordinary Invincibles could not extract the internal substance of an Eternal Source Substance at all, but first-class Invincibles who had created Supreme Being Techniques and condensed Eternal Tribulation Bodies could use a Supreme Being Technique as a guide and the Eternal Tribulation Body as a medium to draw in the internal substance of the Eternal Source Substance, absorbing and refining it to break through their shackles and ascend to become Supreme Beings.
Of course, doing this would not completely destroy the Eternal Source Substance. Once the internal substance of an Eternal Source Substance was consumed, it could slowly recover after a long period of time.
If creating a single Supreme Being destroyed an Eternal Source Substance, there wouldn't be so many Eternal Source Substances in the Source Sea; they would have been exhausted long ago.
As time passed, Sovereign Tianfeng extracted more and more of the Mind Azure Gold's substance.
The Nether Hall did not dispatch a large number of first-class Invincibles to interrupt Sovereign Tianfeng's breakthrough, because interrupting was useless; he could simply continue the breakthrough later.
Moreover, the Nether Hall was unwilling to suffer heavy losses.
Additionally, if Sovereign Tianfeng once broke through to the Supreme Being realm and the Nether Hall's first-class Invincibles had not yet withdrawn, they would likely all be killed.
At this time.
The first-class Invincibles of the Nether Hall were all waiting for the Nether Hall Master to issue orders—whether to leave or to continue staying here.
......
Tianlin Realm, Nether Hall.
Several first-class Invincibles were reporting to the Nether Hall Master.
"Hall Master, according to the news, the Tianfeng Sea has already delivered the Mind Azure Gold to Sovereign Tianfeng's hands. With Sovereign Tianfeng's talent, aptitude, and potential, the hope of him successfully breaking through is over ninety percent!" an Invincible said.
"A bunch of useless trash!"
The Nether Hall Master said angrily.
He had been keeping a close eye on the high-level members of the Tianfeng Sea in the Tianlin Realm. Any Invincible above the third-class could forget about traveling through the Source Sea to the Yuanji Abyss; if one came out, he would kill one.
Therefore, he could only leave the task of intercepting the Mind Azure Gold to those Invincibles of the Nether Hall.
He hadn't expected them to still mess it up.
Once Sovereign Tianfeng truly broke through, even if his strength was inferior, they would both be Supreme Beings. In a situation where the strength gap was not very large, he would be completely unable to kill the other party.
This time was truly a huge loss, creating a great enemy for himself and also causing the Nether Hall and the Tianfeng Sea to become completely hostile.
"Hall Master, please calm your anger!"
Several first-class Invincibles said in fear.
"Get out!"
The Nether Hall Master roared.
The several first-class Invincibles hurriedly retreated, fearing that if they left a step too late, they would continue to be scolded or punished by the Nether Hall Master.

Unknowingly, hundreds of millions of years passed.
On this day.
The Yuanji Abyss shook, and a powerful aura seemed to pierce through the entire Yuanji Abyss.
"Someone is impacting the Supreme Being realm!"
Many Invincibles said in shock.
Soon, they guessed it was the leader of the Tianfeng Sea, Sovereign Tianfeng, who was breaking through; otherwise, it couldn't be such a coincidence.
The breakthrough process was very fast. In a short while, Sovereign Tianfeng had already crossed the bottleneck, entering the Supreme Being realm. Although his realm was not yet stable, he was already a true Supreme Being.
At the very moment he broke through to become a Supreme Being, he was repelled out of the Yuanji Abyss.
If he dared to resist this repulsion, he would definitely be completely suppressed by the Yuanji Abyss.
"Not good, run!"
Many Invincibles in the Yuanji Abyss suddenly realized what was happening and immediately began to flee toward the outside of the Yuanji Abyss.
Because just now, Sovereign Tianfeng had broken through to become a Supreme Being, and the aura of a Supreme Being had transmitted from the depths of the Yuanji Abyss, causing many Abyss Tribulation Beasts in the depths to be frightened, and they all rushed out from the depths of the Yuanji Abyss.
Consequently, the Invincibles in the outer layers of the Yuanji Abyss suffered. Once they encountered those incredibly terrifying Abyss Tribulation Beasts, they would likely be eaten as snacks.
In an instant, many Invincibles died tragically at the hands of the Abyss Tribulation Beasts.
It must be said that these people were too unlucky!
Invincibles with the ability to create clones were somewhat better off, only losing a clone, but Invincibles without clones truly died.
But Invincibles with clones were too rare, almost negligible.
Since all the Invincibles were fleeing, who would bother to warn others?
Furthermore, transmitting messages in the Yuanji Abyss was not very convenient, and the speed of the Abyss Tribulation Beasts was too fast, resulting in the Invincibles in the outermost layer of the Yuanji Abyss not receiving the news immediately.
Ye Tian was located not far from a void passage, with many other Invincibles nearby.
Abruptly.
An incredibly massive Abyss Tribulation Beast tore through the void and descended upon this layer. Its terrifying aura made many Invincibles tremble with fear.
"Not good, this is an Abyss Tribulation Beast comparable to over 100,000 tribulations!"
An Invincible said in terror.
Such an Abyss Tribulation Beast was comparable to a first-class Invincible; how could they possibly rival it?
"Flee!"
Ye Tian did not hesitate and prepared to flee immediately.
At the same time, he instinctively activated his Copy Talent, enveloping this Abyss Tribulation Beast.
Suddenly, he saw an incredible talent.
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Tribulation]
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Space]
This was an Abyss Tribulation Beast that possessed two Eternal Ancestral Talents, and one of them was actually the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent.
Although Ye Tian possessed many Eternal Ancestral Talents, he currently lacked only two: the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent and the Time Eternal Ancestral Talent.
His Eternal Ancestral Talents were all obtained through copying and fusion, but Invincibles possessing Space and Time Ultimate Ancestral Talents were too few, so they had not yet fused into Eternal Ancestral Talents.
However, this time, he could directly copy the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent.
"Copy!"
Without a second word, Ye Tian began the copy process.
Of course, copying an Eternal Ancestral Talent required a bit of time, but once the copying began, even if he moved far away, the copying process would not be interrupted at all; this was a result he had verified through experimentation.
Thus, he wanted to flee from here to avoid being killed by this Abyss Tribulation Beast.
However, this Abyss Tribulation Beast was too powerful; many Invincibles were wiped out almost instantly.
In terms of speed, Ye Tian was far inferior to the opponent. If he didn't have special means, he feared he wouldn't be able to escape either.
At the critical moment.
Ye Tian fully activated the Eternal Ancestral Talent: Tribulation. This was a talent derived from Tribulation Beasts.
When this talent was activated, combined with Ye Tian deliberately mimicking the aura of a Tribulation Beast, the Abyss Tribulation Beast actually mistook Ye Tian for a Tribulation Beast for a moment. Consequently, the claw that was originally attacking Ye Tian was retracted.
Tribulation Beasts do not kill Tribulation Beasts; this was an instinct.
But when Ye Tian fled into the void passage and left the Yuanji Abyss, the Abyss Tribulation Beast suddenly realized it had been tricked. It immediately became incredibly infuriated and wanted to rush over to kill Ye Tian.
However, it was already too late.
