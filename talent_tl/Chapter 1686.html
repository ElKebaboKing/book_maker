Chapter 1685: A Transaction with Lord Binghan!

Ye Tian had become the lord of the Blood Bone Domain, and naturally wanted to evolve it into an Eternal Space-Time Domain.
Only in this way would it match his strength and movement technique, and it could also give birth to a large amount of resources.
After all, compared with an Eternal Space-Time Domain, an ordinary space-time domain was far inferior in both area and cultivation environment, and only an Eternal Space-Time Domain was suitable for an Immortal Domain Lord to cultivate in. For an Immortal Domain Lord, staying in an ordinary space-time domain was completely slowing down cultivation speed.
At this moment.
Ye Tian was cultivating in the Blood Bone Domain, and his own body could even absorb the origin energy of the source sea, feeding it back into the Blood Bone Domain. This was one of the abilities of an Immortal Domain Lord.
If this continued for countless Eternal years, the Blood Bone Domain would truly evolve into an Eternal Space-Time Domain.
However, Ye Tian did not want to wait that long.
He planned to trade for a treasure with other Immortal Domain Lords to hasten the Blood Bone Domain’s evolution.
For ordinary Eternal Realm experts, such a treasure was bound to be incomparably precious, but for those top-tier Immortal Domain Lords, it was not too precious.
After all, an Eternal Realm lord could only refine one space-time domain, so having extra treasures of this kind was of little use, and they could not improve their strength either, so they could be traded away.
An Eternal Space-Time Domain, the Extreme Ice Domain.
This was the domain of Lord Binghan, one of the six chairmen. This domain was hundreds of thousands of times larger than the Blood Bone Domain, and Lord Binghan also used it as one of the trading places of the Lord Alliance. Many, many Eternal Realm experts could enter the Extreme Ice Giant City here to conduct trades.
With a legendary lord guarding it, no Eternal Realm expert would dare cause trouble here.
On this day.
Ye Tian arrived at the Extreme Ice Domain and entered the Extreme Ice Giant City.
Quite a few Eternal Realm lords had set up shops here, with a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert serving as the shopkeeper to manage the stores here. Some Immortal Domain Lords even had ordinary Eternal Life Realm lords manage their shops here, so it was quite lively.
However, in normal times, not many Immortal Domain Lords came here to buy things.
After entering Extreme Ice Giant City, Ye Tian did not enter those shops, because there was no way they would have what he needed. He went straight to the Extreme Ice Pavilion in Extreme Ice Giant City, which was Lord Binghan’s property. Only Immortal Domain Lords or Half-Step Immortal Domain Lords were qualified to enter here.
After every lord above Half-Step Immortal Domain Lord entered here, they could look at the purchasing information and purchase requests posted here. It was equivalent to providing a fair and just trading platform for powerhouses above Half-Step Immortal Domain Lord.
After entering the Extreme Ice Pavilion, Ye Tian browsed the various purchase missions and purchase requests here, and there were also several purchase requests for treasures that could evolve an ordinary space-time domain into an Eternal Space-Time Domain.
Clearly, in recent years, quite a few more people had advanced to Immortal Domain Lord, or perhaps some Half-Step Eternal Realm experts also hoped to obtain an Eternal Space-Time Domain.
“What a pity that the Eternal Insight Gold Sea Tree was snatched away by Lord Void Sword back then, and he had already used it up long ago. Otherwise, I could have traded for it from him,” Ye Tian thought to himself.
Then.
Ye Tian posted a purchase request and put forward his bargaining chip. If the chip were not enough, then even if other Immortal Domain Lords possessed such a treasure, they would not take it out for trade.
Ordinary treasures could not attract those Immortal Domain Lords, so Ye Tian planned to take out a treasure that would move many Immortal Domain Lords.
A secret art inheritance of the fusion of will and innate Eternal Rules!
In space-time domains, many Eternal Realm experts believed that will and innate Eternal Rules could not be fused. If such a secret art appeared, it would inevitably cause a sensation.
Of course.
Such a secret art could not be cultivated, because it was not suitable for space-time domains; it could only be used as a reference.
Even so, the value of such a secret art was still extremely high.
Thus.
Ye Tian immediately posted this purchase request, and once it was posted, it would be sent to all Extreme Ice Pavilion members.
Not long after, three Eternal Realm experts contacted Ye Tian.
Two Immortal Domain Lords, and one legendary lord.
“Lord Binghan actually contacted me!”
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised by Lord Binghan’s contact. After thinking it over carefully, he decided to first meet and talk with Lord Binghan. After all, the treasure that could help an ordinary space-time domain advance was not precious at all to Lord Binghan, so her asking price would not be too high. As for the other Immortal Domain Lords, they might offer a very high price, and then the secret art might not necessarily be fully exchanged for, and they might even put forward other conditions.
So the one he most wanted to trade with was Lord Binghan.
The top floor of the Extreme Ice Pavilion.
Ye Tian and Lord Binghan came here. This was not Ye Tian’s first time meeting Lord Binghan, but it was the first time they were exchanging items.
“Six-Step Immortal Domain Lord, Lord Endless’s cultivation speed is truly fast. I remember Lord Endless had only just broken through to Eternal Realm not long ago, yet unexpectedly he has crossed into the realm of Six-Step Immortal Domain Lord so quickly,” Lord Binghan said with some surprise.
“Just some chance encounters,”
Ye Tian said modestly.
Soon, the two of them got to the main point.
Lord Binghan said, “Lord Endless, you have a secret art inheritance of the fusion of will and innate Eternal Rules. That kind of secret art cannot be cultivated, right?”
Ye Tian answered truthfully, “That’s right. That kind of secret art cannot be cultivated, but it can be used for reference, so its value is still very great.”
He knew Lord Binghan must have obtained a similar inheritance before, otherwise she would not have asked this.
He himself could obtain such an inheritance, so it was not strange at all for Lord Binghan, as a legendary lord, to have obtained one as well.
“Indeed, it can be used for reference, but I need to browse it first to confirm whether it is genuine. If it is real, I can trade with you and give you a treasure that can evolve a space-time domain into an Eternal Space-Time Domain,” Lord Binghan said.
At this time, Ye Tian handed over to Lord Binghan one inheritance technique he had obtained from the Silent Void Battlefield ruins.
Of course, the inheritance he gave was not complete. After all, the formal transaction had not yet begun, so naturally he would not give out the complete inheritance.
Lord Binghan browsed through it and confirmed its authenticity.
“It is indeed a true inheritance, and one I do not have. However, such an inheritance cannot be cultivated. In fact, for ordinary Eternal Realm experts, it has no value at all. I need five such inheritances,” Lord Binghan requested.
“Five inheritances? No problem!”
Ye Tian immediately took out five inheritances, and this time they were all complete inheritances.
Seeing that Ye Tian had really taken out five inheritances, Lord Binghan took a stone stele from her void sphere. It looked very small, but that was because Lord Binghan had shrunk the void where it was located; its true size was actually very large.
“This is the Heaven-Made Stele. Merging it into the source of a space-time domain can promote the evolution of the space-time domain. In addition, it can also strengthen the defense of the space-time domain,” Lord Binghan said.
“Good, let’s trade!”
Ye Tian and Lord Binghan exchanged the items and officially completed the transaction.
After the trade was completed, Lord Binghan suddenly smiled and said, “Lord Endless, I want to carry out another kind of transaction with you.”.
