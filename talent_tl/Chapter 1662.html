Chapter 1661 Black Giant Island, Using the Immortal Realm Lords!

These Immortal Realm lords all rushed toward the depths of the Abyssless Land, wanting to search for resources inside.
Very quickly, they encountered Immortal Realm Void Beasts.
Boom!!!
The slaughter began.
They had thought there would not be many Immortal Realm Void Beasts, but they had underestimated the Void Beasts of the Abyssless Land.
More than ten Void Beasts comparable to the Immortal Realm appeared, and this place was suited for Void Beasts to fight. Although the Immortal Realm lords could suppress these Void Beasts, it was somewhat difficult to kill them.
At this moment.
One Immortal Realm lord appeared, grabbed a Void Beast comparable to the Immortal Realm with one hand, and directly crushed it, but that Void Beast turned into formlessness and reassembled in the distance, though it was still injured.
“Greetings, Lord Chenhai!”
“Greetings, Lord Chenhai!”
“Greetings, Lord Chenhai!”
Many Immortal Realm lords spoke respectfully one after another.
Lord Chenhai was not an ordinary Immortal Realm lord. Moreover, he was very close to the Immortal Realm. Although he had not yet become a half-step Immortal Realm lord, his true strength was not weaker than a half-step Immortal Realm lord, and even an Immortal Realm expert would find it very difficult to kill him.
Thus, in the countless temporal lands, Lord Chenhai was a very special Eternal Realm lord.
But the real reason no one dared to deal with Lord Chenhai was not his strength, but that he had three incarnations—incarnations that truly had the same strength as his main body and could freely enter other places without restriction.
Including his three incarnations and his main body, he had a total of four bodies, each located in different places across the countless temporal lands, making it very difficult to find all four of them.
Once, an Immortal Realm expert joined forces with eight Immortal Realm lords to carry out an encirclement and kill against Lord Chenhai.
The eight Immortal Realm lords temporarily trapped two of Lord Chenhai’s bodies, while that Immortal Realm expert personally dealt with another body.
At first, they only thought Lord Chenhai had three bodies, and they had paid a great price to investigate the locations of those three bodies before launching this encirclement.
But the final result was that all three of Lord Chenhai’s bodies were killed, while he also desperately took down two Immortal Realm lords.
Countless years later, Lord Chenhai returned and assassinated the six surviving Immortal Realm lords one by one. By sacrificing the fall of his three incarnations and luring that Immortal Realm expert into a perilous death trap, he finally severely injured that Immortal Realm expert.
Even today, that Immortal Realm expert had not fully recovered from his injuries.
Precisely because of this, Lord Chenhai’s reputation in the countless temporal lands soared.
So when these Immortal Realm lords saw Lord Chenhai, they were extremely respectful and did not dare offend him in the slightest.
Lord Chenhai nodded slightly, then began dealing with these Void Beasts.
The reason he came here was simple: he also came to seek opportunities in order to break through to the Immortal Realm.
He still had an Immortal Realm enemy, and if he could break through to the Immortal Realm, then he would have sufficient confidence to kill that enemy.
Otherwise, he was afraid he would simply be unable to kill the other party.
With Lord Chenhai’s joining, these Void Beasts simply could not hold them back and were quickly heavily injured.
These Void Beasts had some intelligence, and thus feared perishing; one by one, they fled.
“These Void Beasts are really hard to kill!”
Lord Chenhai shook his head and said.
Unfortunately, he was already at the Immortal Realm, and such Void Beasts were of no help to him. Otherwise, he would really have gone to any lengths to kill some Void Beasts.
Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh!!!
One Immortal Realm lord after another went deeper into the Abyssless Land in search of the so-called opportunities.
And not long after they left, a figure appeared, none other than Ye Tian.
“Those Immortal Realm lords heavily injured the Immortal Realm Void Beasts. Those Immortal Realm Void Beasts probably won’t come to stop me for now!”
Ye Tian muttered to himself.
He followed the pull of his replication talent and ventured deeper into the Abyssless Land.
Very soon, the attraction of that mysterious treasure to Ye Tian grew stronger and stronger. He should not be far from the treasure’s location.
“We’re here!”
Ye Tian arrived at a huge black island.
The treasure attracting him was on this black island, but just as he stepped onto it, a streak of black light attacked him.
The black light was too fast, leaving Ye Tian no time to react; this clone of his was instantly killed.
--------------------
“That was just now...”
Ye Tian’s expression turned grave.
That black light shot out from deep within the black island. He had not been able to dodge it; it was as if even his will had been frozen, leaving him no time to react at all.
And because the distance was too far, his replicated body could not cover that place either.
But he could roughly guess that the one who attacked him should be a Void Beast, and not an ordinary one. If it was not comparable to a half-step Immortal Realm expert, then it was comparable to a true Immortal Realm expert.
His strength was too far from the Immortal Realm, so he could not estimate the other party’s strength.
If he did not deal with that mysterious Void Beast, he would have no way to go onto that black island and search for the treasure he needed.
“I have to make use of those Immortal Realm lords!”
Ye Tian thought.
So, he spread the word about the black island and the astonishing treasure on it, informing the other Eight Extremes Realm experts in the Eight Extremes Tower.
This news definitely could not be concealed, and would even spread into the ears of those Immortal Realm lords.
By then, those Immortal Realm lords would likely head to that black island, which meant they would clash with that mysterious Void Beast.
Once they fought, Ye Tian would have his chance.
Very quickly, the news spread.
“A black island, with many precious resources on it, and also a powerful Void Beast?”
Lord Chenhai also learned of this news. After pondering for a moment, he then prepared to go take a look.
The news recorded the location of that black island. He was only going to have a look, so there was no loss; if it was true, then he would profit.
Whoosh!
Lord Chenhai traversed the void and quickly arrived at that black island.
At the same time, other Immortal Realm lords also arrived here one after another, but faintly sensed danger.
After all, these Immortal Realm lords were much stronger than Ye Tian, so naturally they could detect that something was off, but not to the point of leaving immediately.
Once Lord Chenhai arrived too, they all felt at ease.
At the very least, with Lord Chenhai here, even if that mysterious Void Beast was very powerful, they would not be unable to handle it.
Lord Chenhai showed no concern and stepped directly onto the black island. The other Immortal Realm lords followed as well, and even several Peak Eternal Realm lords came, seemingly wanting to join the excitement and look for some opportunities.
The moment they entered the black island, streaks of black light attacked them.
Those several Peak Eternal Realm lords could not dodge and were killed in one strike. Their Eternal bodies were smashed to pieces, and the Eternal origin weapons on them also turned into countless fragments.
As for those Immortal Realm lords, they also reacted and tried to block the black light, but were all blasted backward, each with ugly expressions, clearly having suffered serious injuries.
Only Lord Chenhai easily blocked the black light attack.
However, this was merely the other party’s ordinary Void Beast.
Boom!!!!!!
A nearly transparent Void Beast appeared, but when it attacked, its arm would turn black, and the black light came shooting out from its fingers.
“Not good, it’s a Tier 3 Void Beast!”
An Immortal Realm lord’s expression changed drastically.
