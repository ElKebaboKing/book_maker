Chapter 1526: Preparing to Negotiate with Super-Famous Level Forces

As soon as Ye Tian entered the Mingheng World, he immediately began to take action against the Mingtian Alliance that guarded the gate.
Although he had suffered backlash from forcibly traversing the Tier 1 Gate, was wounded by the laws, and had consumed a significant amount of power, his strength remained incredibly terrifying. He far surpassed ordinary third-tier powerhouses, and thus, he easily destroyed the Mingtian Alliance and slaughtered their supreme powerhouses.
Afterward, Ye Tian began to refine the gate, turning it into his own, and then established a base within the Mingheng World.
This way, even after the great calamity of the Mingheng World passed, he could dispatch human powerhouses to guard the base, ensuring that this Tier 1 Gate would not be snatched back.
Having finished all this, Ye Tian began to absorb and refine Calamity Power to temper his physical body.
He was currently a powerhouse in the Physical Body Five-Calamity Realm. His goal this time was not overly ambitious; he only needed to reach the Physical Body Six-Calamity Realm. After all, after the Five-Calamity Realm, every subsequent stage was incredibly difficult, requiring far more Calamity Power and time than before.
Therefore, he did not expect to be able to raise his strength to the Physical Body Seven-Calamity Realm in one go.
Time passed slowly, and in the blink of an eye, the great calamity of the Mingheng World reached its late stages.
On this day.
Ye Tian finally tempered his physical body to a new limit, ushering in his sixth physical body calamity.
Boom!!!!
After his body underwent repeated cycles of destruction and recovery, and after surviving the most terrifying lightning calamity, it finally completed a qualitative change, stepping into the Physical Body Six-Calamity Realm.
"This tribulation was relatively easy; I only suffered a few minor injuries. It seems I am still far from my limit!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Everyone has a limit. Many people lack a sufficient foundation and cannot even reach the Physical Body Five-Calamity Realm; attempting a forced breakthrough would only result in death.
Thus, no matter how powerful a force was, or how many resources and how much time they had, once they touched their own limit, they could no longer continue to undergo physical body calamities.
For example, most Invincible Ones were only at the Physical Body Five-Calamity Realm back then and did not dare to attempt the sixth calamity because the fifth was their limit.
But for Ye Tian, passing the sixth physical body calamity was not even difficult, so he could continue using Calamity Power to temper his body.
Next, Ye Tian cultivated in the Mingheng World for another period of time. It wasn't until the great calamity of the Mingheng World was about to end that he left. As for the base in the Mingheng World, Ye Tian dispatched several human Ancestors to guard it.
After all, the Mingheng World had suffered heavy losses during this calamity, and he had killed many of its supreme powerhouses. They wouldn't dare to move against the human base!
Therefore, dispatching a few Ancestors to guard it was sufficient.
---------------
Time flew by.
Five Dark Eras had already passed in the Dark Forbidden Zone. During this time, Ye Tian entered multiple foreign worlds, and even visited the Holy Region World during another of its great calamities. Using the Calamity Power of these foreign worlds, he continuously tempered his physical body.
However, the further he progressed, the harder it became to temper his body. To this end, he had refined his Calamity-Neutralizing Secret Technique several times, yet the great calamity of a single foreign world was no longer enough to raise him by a full calamity realm.
Now, by utilizing the Calamity Power from multiple great calamities, he had barely managed to raise his realm to the Physical Body Seven-Calamity Realm, making his strength much greater than before.
In terms of actual combat power, he was definitely not weaker than an ordinary Invincible Ancestor.
However, moving from the Physical Body Seven-Calamity Realm to the Eight-Calamity Realm was too difficult. He had already visited two foreign worlds and still hadn't reached the Eight-Calamity Realm.
Clearly, relying solely on pure Calamity Power to reach the Physical Body Eight-Calamity Realm was too difficult.
"If I could obtain Calamity Blood, perhaps I could raise my cultivation faster!"
Ye Tian thought.
After all, it was difficult for ordinary supreme powerhouses to absorb Calamity Power directly; they could only rely on Calamity Blood. This showed the value of Calamity Blood. Even the blood of a One-Calamity beast might be enough to push him directly from the Seven-Calamity Realm to the Eight-Calamity Realm.
"It's a pity about that portion of Calamity Blood from before!"
Ye Tian couldn't help but sigh.
However, if given the choice again, he would still choose to trade that Calamity Blood for the construction method of a Tier 2 Gate and the secret knowledge of how to become an Invincible One. Otherwise, he still wouldn't know how to strike for the Invincible Ancestor level, and he wouldn't have been able to reach his current Physical Body Seven-Calamity strength.
But the problem at hand was how to obtain another portion of Calamity Blood.
Calamity Beasts were extremely powerful. A One-Calamity beast was comparable to a weaker Invincible Ancestor. If it were a beast of two, three, or more calamities, even an Invincible Ancestor wouldn't dare provoke it; to do so would be seeking death.
Whenever a foreign world was destroyed, a Calamity Beast would be born.
Perhaps the Holy Region World had a Calamity Beast, but it might not be a One-Calamity beast, or it might have already been slain.
However, many foreign worlds had experienced multiple destructions. In those cases, if the Calamity Beast hadn't been killed, it would be absolutely terrifying. Even Super-Famous Level forces wouldn't dare provoke such a beast.
The reason the Dark Forbidden Zone was targeted was that it had only experienced one world destruction. That Calamity Beast was only a One-Calamity beast. The six Super-Famous Level forces knew this information, which was why they dared to plot against it. Otherwise, they wouldn't have the courage to lay a finger on a Calamity Beast!
……
The Dark Forbidden Zone.
Today, the Human Race already possessed over several thousand Ancestors. Their strength could be described as incredibly formidable, and their clansmen were spread throughout the entire Dark Forbidden Zone. Among other forces, only the Battle Gate had some room to survive; the others had no living space at all.
At the same time, the number of forbidden lands in the Dark Forbidden Zone had dwindled. Previously, there were dozens, but now only about a dozen remained, including the base forbidden lands of the six Super-Famous Level forces.
This was naturally due to Ye Tian, which led many foreign forces to abandon the Dark Forbidden Zone.
This was a good thing; at least in the future, the Dark Forbidden Zone would not suffer invasions from multiple foreign forces.
Once the Dark Forbidden Zone was free of any foreign gates, the Human Race could completely control the entire zone and focus all their efforts on invading other foreign worlds.
"It's time to show my hand to the six Super-Famous Level forces. At the very least, I need to trade for some knowledge or resources. If nothing else, I must destroy their Tier 2 Gates!"
Ye Tian thought.
On this day.
Ye Tian arrived at the forbidden land of the "Illusory Dream World," a Super-Famous Level force. He intended to capture members of this forbidden land to use as leverage for negotiations. Otherwise, he had nothing of value to trade with a Super-Famous Level force.
As for whether he would offend the Illusory Dream World, he didn't care in the slightest.
