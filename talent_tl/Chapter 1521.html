Chapter 1520: Tribulation Blood! Transaction!

Ka ka ka!!!
Under the siege of numerous supreme-level experts, the Tribulation Beast gradually began to show signs of fatigue.
The Tribulation Beast was not without intelligence; its intelligence was simply very low.
Thus, seeing itself at a disadvantage, it attempted to flee.
Many experts pursued it relentlessly, but the Tribulation Beast's defense was too terrifying. If it hadn't lacked powerful offensive techniques, the many supreme-level experts present would not have been able to defeat it at all.
The Tribulation Beast could not leave the Dark Forbidden Domain, so it could only flee within its boundaries, unable to shake off these experts from the outer realms.
Finally, in a silent stretch of the void.
The Tribulation Beast fought for its life, engaging in a desperate struggle with the many supreme-level beings.
Boom!!!!
A battle formation was torn apart. Six supreme experts of the first tier were blasted to death, and other supreme experts were heavily injured.
Of course, this Tribulation Beast was also heavily wounded again by the other supreme experts, making its injuries even more severe.
Subsequently, one battle formation after another collapsed, rendering them unusable.
Consequently, everyone began attacking the Tribulation Beast with their individual strength.
Before long, the number of fallen supreme experts from the outer realms reached double digits, and many more were severely injured.
These supreme experts were on the brink of death and dared not intervene further, distancing themselves one by one.
In the end, only the third-tier supreme experts from the six super-tier forces remained to besiege the Tribulation Beast.
Compared to the beginning, the Tribulation Beast's current strength was less than a third of its peak, and its injuries were incredibly heavy. Its scale-covered body was in a wretched state, with countless scales shattered, and the Tribulation Beast's blood flowed continuously, unable to heal.
"Give it one more push!"
The supreme experts roared.
At this moment, every supreme expert was extremely excited. Once the Tribulation Beast fell, the benefits would be immense.
Ye Tian was also among them, keeping a low profile and not deliberately showing off his strength.
Moreover, the current goal of the six super-tier forces was to slay the Tribulation Beast, so they naturally wouldn't bother with him.
Not long after, the Tribulation Beast's strength dropped to an extremely low level. Its body was riddled with holes, and it could no longer recover from its wounds or withstand the attacks of the supreme experts.
Finally, it succumbed to its heavy injuries and perished.
The moment the Tribulation Beast died, its physical body began to disintegrate.
A large number of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals flew out, along with a mass of black blood that radiated a terrifying aura.
The supreme experts immediately rushed to snatch that mass of black blood; this was their true target.
Of course, the Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals were also their targets, but their primary objective was naturally the black blood, followed by the crystals.
Every supreme expert craved this mass of black blood. Ye Tian naturally knew it was a treasure and stopped hiding his strength.
Boom!!!!
Ye Tian displayed his full combat power, grabbing the black blood at maximum speed before beginning to snatch the Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
In the void, giant hands reached out to grab the crystals one by one, with the majority falling into Ye Tian's hands.
At this moment, how could these supreme experts not understand!
Ye Tian hadn't been injured at all; he had hidden his strength. His true power might not equal the Tribulation Beast's peak, but he possessed at least a third of its strength.
Meanwhile, they were all heavily injured with greatly reduced combat power. How could they compete with Ye Tian?
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, hand over the Tribulation Blood! That is not something you can snatch!!!!"
The supreme experts surrounded Ye Tian, threatening him.
"Get lost!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
The six super-tier forces wasted no more words and attacked Ye Tian together.
At this moment, Ye Tian displayed nearly invincible strength, almost sweeping everything aside.
However, he did not kill the supreme experts of the six super-tier forces. After all, with his current strength, he was not yet enough to contend with these six forces. If he killed these supreme experts and the six invincible powerhouses behind them took action, he would have no choice but to hide and wait for the Great Tribulation to pass.
Therefore, unless it was absolutely necessary, he would not seek his own death.
In the void of the Dark Forbidden Domain.
One by one, the supreme experts were heavily injured, either lying down, kneeling, or trembling as they all stared at Ye Tian.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, you are truly remarkable to have created so many supreme ancestral techniques. No wonder your strength is so terrifying. But as long as you haven't become an Invincible Ancestor, you have no right to be an enemy of the super-tier forces!" one supreme expert said.
Ye Tian sighed, "I didn't want to be your enemy either; it was you who insisted on being mine. I am curious, what use does this so-called Tribulation Blood have? Why do you all want to fight for it?"
"That is a secret!"
The supreme experts of the six super-tier forces seemed unwilling to speak.
Ye Tian smiled and said, "If you tell me, perhaps I can trade the Tribulation Blood with you!"
Trade the Tribulation Blood?
The supreme experts of the six super-tier forces were somewhat moved. If they could trade for it, that would naturally be for the best.
"What do you want?"
A representative of the six super-tier forces asked.
Ye Tian pondered for a moment and said, "I want to know how to break through to become an Invincible Ancestor. Your six super-tier forces should have invincible-level beings, so you should know this secret, right? Each of you communicate with me; whoever offers a price that interests me, I will give the Tribulation Blood to them!"
Immediately, the supreme experts communicated with Ye Tian, stating their offers.
In reality, one portion of Tribulation Blood could at most be consumed by one person. If it were divided, the effect would be lost, as this was only the blood of a First-Tribulation Beast.
Ultimately, a third-tier supreme expert from the Firmament Sword Tomb proposed a trade that Ye Tian found hard to refuse.
The manufacturing method for a Tier-2 Gate, the secret of how a supreme expert breaks through to become an invincible one, and additionally, ten Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals. These ten crystals were not from the same outer realm heaven and earth, but belonged to ten different ones, which were very difficult to collect.
"Supreme expert of the Firmament Sword Tomb, Wuhai Sword Ancestor, we can make the trade!"
Ye Tian chose Wuhai Sword Ancestor.
Seeing Ye Tian trade with Wuhai Sword Ancestor, the other supreme experts were incredibly envious.
The key profound secret of a supreme expert breaking through to an invincible one wasn't much, but the manufacturing method for a Tier-2 Gate was extremely rare. Few among the supreme experts knew it. While some present did know, they had sworn oaths not to reveal it. Therefore, only a manufacturing method obtained by chance could be used for trade.
Coincidentally, Wuhai Sword Ancestor had once wiped out a force and obtained a Tier-2 Gate manufacturing method, which he now used for the trade.
Ye Tian handed the Tribulation Blood to Wuhai Sword Ancestor, and in return, he received the Tier-2 Gate manufacturing method, the secret of breaking through to an Invincible Ancestor, and the ten Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
"Farewell!"
Ye Tian departed.
Meanwhile, Wuhai Sword Ancestor quickly retreated under the protection of the other supreme experts from the Firmament Sword Tomb. Several battles broke out during the process, but in the end, Wuhai Sword Ancestor successfully returned to the Firmament Sword Tomb.
Of course, the other forces were not without gains; they also harvested some Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals and materials from the Tribulation Beast.
Xuanxu Ancestral Realm.
After Ye Tian returned, he immediately began to study the secrets in his mind regarding how to break through to become an Invincible Ancestor. Such secrets were like a legacy; they could only be obtained through inheritance and could not be spoken or written down.
Soon, Ye Tian finished receiving the inheritance and finally understood how a Supreme Ancestor should break through to become an Invincible Ancestor.
