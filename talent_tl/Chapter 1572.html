Chapter 1571: The Ye Family Genius Fused with the Source of an Eternal Source Object!

Human Race, Ye Family.
The Ye family was the number one clan of the Human Race, a bloodline descended from Ye Xingchen's line. As Ye Tian's only son, Ye Xingchen had always practiced hard and thus did not have many children, but he couldn't stop the Ye family members below him from being very prolific.
Furthermore, as long as a Ye family member's talent was decent, they could almost all achieve immortality; thus, the number of Ye family members gradually increased.
In this era, a truly monstrous genius was born in the Ye family.
This Ye family genius had a high seniority, not far from Ye Xingchen's generation. He was the great-grandson of Ye Xingchen's third son and a direct descendant of the Ye family. In a short time, he cultivated to the Domain Ancestor realm and broke through to the Peak Supreme Domain Ancestor realm early on.
Moreover, at the Peak Supreme Domain Ancestor realm, his combat power was not weaker than an ordinary Ancestor.
And now, this Ye family genius had broken through to the Ancestor realm.
Only by becoming an Ancestor was one qualified to be the backbone of the Human Race. If one did not become an Ancestor, they were not qualified to receive the attention of the high-level humans.
No matter how monstrous a genius was, if they could not become an Ancestor, it was meaningless.
Even in this current era, becoming an Ancestor still held some difficulty.
As soon as the Ye family genius became an Ancestor, he challenged a top Elite Ancestor and won in a single battle. His record was not inferior to Ye Tian's in the past.
Of course, Ye Tian lacked inheritances back then, whereas the Human Race now did not lack them, making it much easier to increase strength. Thus, the two could not be compared.
Even so, this matter caused a sensation throughout the entire Human Race.
Many high-level humans believed this Ye family genius had the hope of becoming an Invincible Ancestor. Even Ye Xingchen came out of seclusion to personally summon this Ye family descendant.
---------------
Ye Jinpeng was somewhat nervous. This time, the Ye family's ancestor Ye Xingchen wanted to see him; this was his great-grandfather.
Although Great-grandfather Ye Xingchen's strength was not as great as the Ye family's true ancestor, Ye Tian, he was still incredibly powerful, being a Sixth-tier Invincible Ancestor, far beyond what other high-level humans could compare to.
Soon, Ye Xingchen arrived.
"Greetings, Great-grandfather!"
Ye Jinpeng kowtowed.
"Jinpeng, you are very good!" Ye Xingchen praised.
"Thank you for the praise, Great-grandfather!" Ye Jinpeng was quite excited.
Ye Xingchen continued, "Your talent is much better than mine. The reason I could become a Sixth-tier Invincible Ancestor was entirely due to my father's help. Without my father's help, I'm afraid even becoming an Ancestor would have been difficult. However, the Human Race's current cultivation environment is much better than before, so becoming an Ancestor is no longer hard. But Ordinary Ancestors, Elite Ancestors, and top Ancestors are just the starting point. Even Supreme Ancestors are just the beginning. Only by becoming an Invincible Ancestor are you qualified to go to the Eternal Heaven and Earth."
Ye Jinpeng listened intently.
Ye Xingchen continued, "With my talent, I'm afraid I cannot guide you much. But our Ye family has never given birth to a genius with talent like yours. I plan to notify my father and have him guide you."
"Have the Ancestor guide me?"
Ye Jinpeng was incredibly excited. He had grown up listening to the legends of the Ancestor. If not for the Ancestor, there would be no Ye family today, let alone the current glory of the Human Race.
Legend had it that Ancestor Ye Tian's strength far surpassed Great-grandfather Ye Xingchen's, and even in the Eternal Heaven and Earth, he was an incomparably powerful existence.
"Thank you, Great-grandfather!"
Ye Jinpeng said gratefully.
Soon, Ye Xingchen sent a message to Ye Tian's Invincible Ancestor avatar stationed in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
-------------
"The Ye family has produced a genius with a very fast cultivation speed and an incredibly solid foundation, who could defeat a top Elite Ancestor just after breaking through to the Ancestor realm?"
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
He indeed needed to meet such a talented junior. Perhaps under his nurturing, this Ye family junior would be qualified to become a Supreme Ancestor in the future.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian shuttled through space and arrived before Ye Xingchen.
"Father, this is Jinpeng, Qi'er's great-grandson!" Ye Xingchen introduced.
Qi'er's great-grandson?
Ye Tian naturally knew his grandson, Ye Qi. Ye Qi's great-grandson was his direct bloodline descendant, making the relationship even closer.
Thus, Ye Tian carefully sized up Ye Jinpeng.
But with this look, he noticed something was wrong.
"Xingchen, you go out first!"
Ye Tian ordered.
Ye Xingchen sensed something was amiss but didn't ask anything and left immediately.
At this moment, Ye Tian placed a hand on Ye Jinpeng's shoulder and probed his body.
"As I thought!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Even if Ye Jinpeng were a fool, he would know his secret had been discovered by the Ancestor, and he suddenly became worried.
He hadn't always been such a genius. When he was very weak, he had accidentally encountered a ball of light and fused with it. This ball of light would occasionally transmit information to him, guiding his cultivation direction.
Under the guidance of this ball of light, he was able to advance all the way to the Ancestor realm.
However, this ball of light wouldn't answer too many of his questions; he had to figure out many things on his own.
But he felt that this ball of light was likely a great opportunity with an incredibly mysterious origin. After all, even after stepping into the Ancestor realm, he was completely baffled by it. Even his great-grandfather, at the Sixth-tier Invincible Ancestor realm, couldn't discover the light cluster inside him.
But he never expected that the Ancestor would discover it.
Since the Ancestor had discovered it, Ye Jinpeng didn't dare to hide it anymore. Even if the Ancestor really wanted to seize his opportunity, there was nothing he could do.
"Ancestor, I confess!" Ye Jinpeng hurriedly said. "The reason I became such a genius isn't that my talent is so good, but because I obtained a light cluster!"
Ye Tian released his hand and asked, "Do you know what that light cluster is?"
"I'm not quite sure!" Ye Jinpeng shook his head.
Ye Tian said, "I just temporarily sealed that light cluster. The consciousness inside it doesn't know what happened yet, so he cannot hear our conversation for now. In reality, it should be the source of some mysterious Eternal Source Object, but the remnant consciousness of a powerhouse is trying to be reborn using this Eternal Source Object source. This mysterious Eternal Source Object might be compatible with your physique and soul, which is why it chose you. Strictly speaking, the stronger you become, the more that powerhouse's consciousness will recover until one day it completely occupies your consciousness or fuses with it. This can be considered another form of rebirth. After all, in a strict sense, the other party has already fallen and can only use this method to continue their conscious existence. At that time, if you are the dominant consciousness, you will still be you. But if the other party is the dominant consciousness, you will no longer be yourself. And everything you've experienced is likely not worth mentioning compared to them; the greater possibility is that they will be the dominant consciousness."
In fact, Ye Tian was also somewhat surprised. At the Ancestor level, possession was almost impossible, but that mysterious Eternal Source Object source was currently assimilating Ye Jinpeng's soul source, providing the conditions for conscious possession.
"...What? Ancestor, is what you're saying true?"
Ye Jinpeng panicked.
"I'll take you somewhere!"
Ye Tian grabbed Ye Jinpeng and headed toward the core area of the Dark Forbidden Domain, where the Wanwu Yang was located.
There was a great terror there!
Ye Jinpeng was very nervous. He knew there was a treasure land in the center of the Human Race, but every time he wanted to go there, the light cluster in his body would transmit an emotion of great terror. Thus, he had never been to the Wanwu Yang in the Human Race's core area.
But at this moment, he felt strange; he actually didn't feel the emotion of great terror.
"We're here!"
Ye Tian stood with Ye Jinpeng not far from the Eternal Source Object, the Wanwu Yang.
"This is the secret of the Human Race's strength in this era—the Eternal Source Object, Wanwu Yang. I brought back the Wanwu Yang, which caused a qualitative change in the bloodlines, bodies, and souls of the Human Race members. Even a child's strength is comparable to powerful martial artists of the past."
"Feel carefully. Is the source aura of the Wanwu Yang similar to the light cluster in your body?"
Ye Tian asked.
Ye Jinpeng truly felt it.
"It really is almost the same!"
Ye Jinpeng was shocked.
He now believed what the Ancestor said. Moreover, compared to the source of the Wanwu Yang, the light cluster in his body was very weak.
The Ancestor could even get his hands on the Wanwu Yang, so it was naturally impossible for him to be coveting Jinpeng's opportunity.
At this time, Ye Tian gave Ye Jinpeng two choices: "Jinpeng, I am giving you two options to choose from. Your ability to fuse with the source of an Eternal Source Object is something that has almost never happened. Even I haven't heard of a weak living being being able to fuse with an Eternal Source Object source. Even if it's just a little bit, it is a massive opportunity. Because of this, your physique has become very special, somewhat similar to a Source Body, though currently far inferior. If you are willing to keep this source, there will be many unknowns in the future, but there is also a great possibility that you will advance by leaps and bounds and become a truly powerful existence. The second choice is to let me destroy the light cluster in your body. However, in either case, I will extract that mysterious remnant consciousness from your body so it won't affect you!"
"Ancestor, I choose the first option!"
Ye Jinpeng decided.
