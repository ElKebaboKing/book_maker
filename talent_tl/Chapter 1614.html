Chapter 1613: Ye Jingpeng Enters Supreme!

Ye Tian was in seclusion, comprehending the body of the Innate Eternal Life, and Fenggu River also underwent new changes.
The Eternal Tribulation Race had failed to seize the Innate Eternal Life. If they continued to stay in Fenggu River, they would probably suffer even greater losses.
Moreover, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor had been severely injured and could not remain here; otherwise, there would be a risk of death.
Thus, the members of the Eternal Tribulation Race in Fenggu River withdrew one after another, and Fenggu Source Ancestor once again took control of Fenggu River, turning it back into a treasured land. The formations laid out by the Eternal Tribulation Race were destroyed one by one.
In this way, Fenggu River could no longer be transformed into an Eternal Tribulation Domain.
Supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor returned and came before Tianzai Tribulation Ancestor, saying shamefully, “Tianzai Tribulation Ancestor, I failed!”
Tianzai Tribulation Ancestor also knew what had happened. Although he was furious, he also knew this could not be blamed on Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor.
Moreover, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor had already cut his own cultivation to take part in this operation. If he were punished again, it would likely arouse the dissatisfaction of many members of the Eternal Tribulation Race.
Therefore, Tianzai Tribulation Ancestor said, “This cannot be blamed on you. Go back and heal properly. Let this matter rest!”
Let it rest!
Beyond that, what else could they do?
Could they still oppose an Eternal Realm being?
The Eternal Tribulation Race did not have an Eternal Realm, which was also why they had never dared to go out. If they had an Eternal Realm expert, they would not need to fear anything.
Gradually, the matter of the Innate Eternal Life passed.
……
Three Tribulation Yuan years passed.
At this time, the human race was developing rapidly, and a large number of Invincible beings had already been born, while Ye Jingpeng had already cultivated to the level of a First-Tier Invincible being.
Moreover, under the supply of resources from Ye Tian, his wife, children, and younger sister had also all cultivated to the level of First-Tier Invincible beings.
Now, Ye Jingpeng was condensing an Eternal Tribulation Body. Once he succeeded, Ye Tian would prepare to hand the Eternal Source Object—the Eternal Soul Wood—to him, helping him charge into the Supreme Being realm.
Compared with their progress, Ye Tian’s progress was the truly remarkable one.
He had comprehended the Innate Eternal Life for a full three Tribulation Yuan years; during this period, his grasp of the eternal rules had already surpassed many half-step Eternal Realm experts, and he had also created one half-step Eternal skill after another.
Not only that, but his insight into the Eternal Body had also reached an inconceivable level. Without any auxiliary resources, he had forcibly raised the Endless Eternal Body from the early Major stage to the peak of the mid Major stage.
It could be said that his current strength was far greater than before.
“It’s time to charge into the fourfold Supreme Being realm!”
Ye Tian murmured.
He took out the Immortal Source Heart Liquid, and after drinking it in one gulp, he very quickly charged into the fourfold Supreme Being realm, without feeling the slightest bottleneck along the way.
In fact, even without consuming the Immortal Source Heart Liquid, he probably could have broken through very easily, but for the sake of greater safety, he still used the Immortal Source Heart Liquid.
Fourfold Supreme Being was much stronger than threefold Supreme Being, and the gap between fourfold Supreme Being and threefold Supreme Being was even greater than the gap between threefold Supreme Being and twofold Supreme Being.
Especially for Ye Tian, this breakthrough caused his strength to soar by many times in an instant.
Now, even without borrowing the finger of an Eternal Realm expert, his conventional combat strength could reach the level of a sevenfold Supreme Being.
If he used that severed finger of an Eternal Realm expert, his combat strength would likely soar to the top-tier ninefold realm, and he could use it more than ten times.
Several dozen Great Calamity Epochs later, news came from Ye Jingpeng’s side.
Now, Ye Jingpeng had successfully condensed an Eternal Tribulation Body and could charge toward the Supreme Being realm with an incomparably profound foundation.
……
“Ancestor!”
Ye Jingpeng came before Ye Tian and said respectfully.
Ye Tian took out the Eternal Soul Wood and handed it to Ye Jingpeng, saying, “This is Jin Ancestor’s Eternal Soul Wood. I extracted Jin Ancestor’s memories and found it in a forbidden land. The bit of origin you absorbed before belonged to the origin of the Eternal Soul Wood. You can perfectly absorb its origin and charge into the Supreme Being realm. Take it!”
“Thank you, Ancestor!” Ye Jingpeng said excitedly.
Supreme Being!
This was the realm he had longed for day and night. Once he broke through to this realm, he would be among the top tier in the Tianlin Realm, fully capable of being compared with Tianfeng Source God.
Ye Jingpeng took the Eternal Soul Wood and went into seclusion in the Void Source Hall to charge toward the Supreme Being realm.
For him, this place was safer than the Tianlin Realm.
Time slowly passed.
Ye Tian did not pay much attention to Ye Jingpeng’s breakthrough, because this was a breakthrough that was one hundred percent guaranteed to succeed. If Ye Jingpeng could still fail to break through like this, then he would truly be useless, enough to break records.
Finally, on this day, Ye Jingpeng succeeded in his breakthrough and stepped into the Supreme Being realm.
After Ye Jingpeng broke through, the origin of the Dark Forbidden Domain once again surged upward by a small margin.
“He’s broken through. If we cultivate one more Supreme Being, then the Dark Forbidden Domain should be able to become an Eternal Heaven and Earth!”
Ye Tian murmured.
The next one most likely to break through was Ye Xingchen. Ye Tian planned to let Ye Xingchen fuse the power of the Ancient Star Stone and let him condense an Ancient Star Body, even if it was a defective Ancient Star Body.
For this, he needed to prepare countless precious resources to help Ye Xingchen grow; otherwise, Ye Xingchen simply would not be able to comprehend the Ancient Star Body, and even cultivating for billions of Tribulation Yuan years would be useless.
In order to let Ye Xingchen successfully condense the Ancient Star Body, Ye Tian also re-fused and produced an Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Gift, and bestowed it upon Ye Xingchen.

This was already the utmost that Ye Tian could do.
However, according to Ye Tian’s expectations, for Ye Xingchen to break through to the Supreme Being realm would require at least one hundred Tribulation Yuan years, and that was only if countless resources were supplied; otherwise, it would take at least tens of thousands of Tribulation Yuan years or even more.
As for Ye Xingchen’s cultivation plan, Ye Tian had already arranged it.
As for the rest of the time, Ye Tian no longer needed to worry about it.
Before he knew it, another ten Tribulation Yuan years passed, and Ye Tian had also raised his cultivation to the peak of the fourfold Supreme Being realm. The next step was to charge into the fivefold Supreme Being realm.
The Void Source Hall happened to have a treasure suitable for breaking through to the fivefold Supreme Being realm. Ye Tian had never been willing to use it before, after all, using it at a low level would have been too wasteful.
This treasure was the Azure Lihuo Flame Crystal.
After that, Ye Tian used the Azure Lihuo Flame Crystal to break through, and similarly, he very easily broke through to the fivefold Supreme Being realm.
After breaking through to the fivefold Supreme Being realm, Ye Tian’s strength surged once again.
With his current strength, even his conventional combat power was enough to rival a relatively weak eightfold Supreme Being.
