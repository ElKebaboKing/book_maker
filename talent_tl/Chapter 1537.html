Chapter 1536: Tianqi Realm, Eternal Realm!

The route map obtained from the trade with the Ten Directions Sword Ancestor recorded the coordinates of various exotic realms. He only needed to follow these coordinates, directly tear through the barriers of each exotic realm, and descend upon them to reach an Eternal Realm.
According to the instructions on the route map, he needed to traverse 298 exotic realms to reach the one closest to the Eternal Realm. At that time, he would enter the Source Sea and from there enter the Eternal Realm. After all, ordinary Invincible Ones could not tear through the barrier of an Eternal Realm and could only enter through the Source Sea.
Ye Tian memorized this route map and sent his duplicate body to the Eternal Realm. This way, even if it perished, his own life would not be at risk.
Ye Tian's speed was very fast. He crossed one exotic realm after another and arrived at the exotic realm closest to the Eternal Realm in just a few days.
This exotic realm was named Bogu. Ye Tian entered the Source Sea directly from the Bogu Realm and immediately saw an incomparably massive phantom. This phantom was an Eternal Realm.
Ye Tian could only vaguely sense the existence of the Eternal Realm, so the distance between them was still very far.
Thus, it took Ye Tian several years to fly to the vicinity of this Eternal Realm.
At this time, he discovered several gateways around the phantom.
"Those are Tier 3 Gates!"
Ye Tian guessed.
Because he could build Tier 2 Gates himself, and Tier 2 Gates were far inferior to these gates, it was clear they were Tier 3 Gates.
Tier 3 Gates could accommodate Invincible Ones, so all Invincible Ones could enter the Eternal Realm through them.
As for Supreme Ones, why would they need Tier 3 Gates? They could likely enter the Eternal Realm with ease.
Therefore, it was enough for the Eternal Realm to build these Tier 3 Gates.
According to the intelligence from the Ten Directions Sword Ancestor, entering the Eternal Realm was not without a price; a certain amount of Source Rule Tribulation Crystals had to be paid.
Although the Eternal Realm was good, it had no Great Tribulations, so it could not produce Source Rule Tribulation Crystals. Thus, the Eternal Realm used the collection of Source Rule Tribulation Crystals as a toll.
In this way, the Eternal Realm could obtain countless Source Rule Tribulation Crystals without paying any price.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian flew to a Tier 3 Gate and was immediately stopped by an Invincible One.
This Invincible One's aura was terrifying, far exceeding Ye Tian's imagination. He was clearly an incredibly powerful Invincible One.
"Stranger, are you paying one Source Rule Tribulation Crystal as a toll, or are you applying for a permanent Eternal Token?" the Invincible One asked.
"Applying for a permanent Eternal Token!" Ye Tian said.
If one did not apply for an Eternal Token, they would have to pay one Source Rule Tribulation Crystal every time they entered or exited the Eternal Realm.
Once or twice might not be much, but if one entered and exited dozens, hundreds, or even thousands of times, the cost in Source Rule Tribulation Crystals would be significant.
Applying for an Eternal Token only cost 1,000 Source Rule Tribulation Crystals. Therefore, most Invincible Ones would apply for one. Only some newly ascended Invincible Ones who did not have many crystals would choose to pay the single crystal toll.
Soon, Ye Tian completed the procedures, paid 1,000 Source Rule Tribulation Crystals, and received an Eternal Token.
Afterward, Ye Tian entered this Eternal Realm.
-------------------
This Eternal Realm was known as the Tianqi Realm. The Tianqi Realm was incredibly vast, divided into hundreds of thousands of regions. Each region had countless giant trading cities. No one knew how many Invincible Ones lived here; many were outsiders who resided here permanently.
However, living in the Tianqi Realm was not without a price. One Source Rule Tribulation Crystal had to be paid every Great Tribulation Era. This applied to everyone, and no one could avoid it.
If one tried to hide to avoid paying, the consequences would be miserable. No one could hide from it.
Although Ye Tian had obtained an Eternal Token, it only waived the entry and exit fees. If he stayed in the Tianqi Realm for a Great Tribulation Era, he would still have to pay one Source Rule Tribulation Crystal.
After entering the Tianqi Realm, Ye Tian kept a very low profile. He did not use his divine sense to probe the surroundings. If he provoked a powerful and ill-tempered Invincible One, they might attack him.
The ones that should never be provoked in the Tianqi Realm were the eight Great Eternal-rank powers. Each Eternal-rank power possessed at least one Supreme One.
Below the eight Eternal-rank powers were over a hundred Saint-rank powers. The requirements to become a Saint-rank power were also very high; they had to possess a First-class Invincible One.
As for Transcend-rank powers, they were too numerous to count.
Basically, as long as one did not provoke the Eternal-rank or Saint-rank powers, newly ascended Invincible Ones were relatively safe. Fighting did occur in the Tianqi Realm, but it was rare, especially inside the giant trading cities where fighting was prohibited.
Ye Tian came to the Tianqi Realm this time not only to comprehend an Invincible Ancestral Technique but also to join a power.
Although he possessed the Copying Talent, it would be difficult to obtain certain heritage knowledge and grow without a power to rely on.
Knowledge was extremely important. If he had not threatened or infiltrated various exotic powers to obtain pieces of knowledge, he would not have been able to enter the Invincible Realm, or even become a Paramount Being.
Furthermore, joining a power meant having a backer. Before fully growing up, he would not have to worry about safety issues.
Just like when he was weak and joined the War Sect. The War Sect helped him a lot and protected him. Without the War Sect's protection, he would have died countless times already; the Copying Talent could not save his life every time!
But he also knew very well that the Eternal-rank and Saint-rank powers of the Tianqi Realm would not easily recruit Invincible Ones. Only those weak Transcend-rank powers would be eager to recruit Invincible Ones to expand their influence.
Ye Tian had no interest in Transcend-rank powers. His minimum requirement was a Saint-rank power, and ideally, he could join an Eternal-rank power.
Tianqi Realm, Juewu District, Juewu City.
This was a fairly famous giant trading city in the Tianqi Realm, where many powers had branches.
Ye Tian came here, first, to trade away the Tribulation Bones he had, and second, to purchase some knowledge and inquire about how to join those Saint-rank or Eternal-rank powers.
After arriving at Juewu City, he found that the cultivation level here was very high. Naturally, there were not many Invincible Ones, but there were many cultivators comparable to Ancestors, and the weakest were comparable to Domain Ancestors.
Soon, Ye Tian arrived at a shop in Juewu City.
