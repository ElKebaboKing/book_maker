Chapter 1629: A Half-Step Eternal Realm at the Extreme Stage!

Ye Tian swallowed this Eternal Enlightenment Flower, and his comprehension instantly broke through some kind of shackle, rising to an unimaginably high level.
Ye Tian took this opportunity to begin comprehending the innate Eternal rules within the innate Eternal lifeform, and his understanding of the Eternal rules improved at an incredible speed.
Gradually, Ye Tian’s Eternal rules began undergoing a qualitative transformation.
But at this moment, the effect of the first Eternal Enlightenment Flower faded.
So, Ye Tian immediately took the second Eternal Enlightenment Flower and continued comprehending the innate Eternal rules.
It was only when the second Eternal Enlightenment Flower was about to lose its effect that Ye Tian finally comprehended the innate Eternal rules.
Buzz!!!!
The innate power Eternal rules were engraved onto Ye Tian’s body, faintly seeming as if they were about to merge with him, but the fusion failed. After all, he was not a true Eternal realm being yet, so he had no way to completely fuse with the Eternal rules, let alone the innate Eternal rules.
But even being merely engraved upon his own body allowed Ye Tian to grasp the innate power Eternal rules, and his strength underwent a heaven-shaking qualitative transformation.
“There’s still one Eternal Enlightenment Flower left. I won’t use it for now; I’ll keep it.”
Ye Tian muttered.
Moreover, having now mastered the innate power Eternal rules—just a single innate Eternal rule—Ye Tian had also broken through his shackle and stepped into the level of innate Eternal rules.
From now on, it would be much easier for him to comprehend other innate Eternal rules, equivalent to mastering one method and applying it to all methods.
Afterward, Ye Tian comprehended for some more time. His strength still improved somewhat, but he was still far from the Ninth Supreme Being Stage.
However, his normal combat strength was already crushing that of an ordinary Half-Step Eternal Realm, and within the Half-Step Eternal Realm, he could be considered quite outstanding.
Moreover, the finger of an Eternal realm expert was no longer of much help to him; at most, it could only increase his strength severalfold.
If he advanced to the Ninth Supreme Being Stage, then the finger of an Eternal realm expert would completely lose its usefulness to him.
But this was a good thing, enough to prove that Ye Tian was growing stronger and stronger.
On this day.
An enormous event occurred in the Source Sea: the top major force of the Source Sea, the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall, was destroyed, and even the Dragon Scale Sacred World was obliterated.
The key point was that after the Dragon Scale Sacred World was destroyed, other forces discovered it only afterward; before that, not a single plea for help had been transmitted.
If some ordinary force had been wiped out like this, that would have been one thing, but the key was that the Dragon Scale Sacred World was not weak—it was a true top major force, possessing three Half-Step Eternal Realm beings, dozens of Ninth Supreme Beings, and over one hundred thousand Supreme Beings. It was a truly ancient force.
Very quickly, this news spread, and even Ye Tian heard about it.
“The Dragon Scale Sacred Hall was actually destroyed!”
Ye Tian was very surprised.
And after the spirit of the Void Source Hall learned of it, it was also incomparably shocked.
“Master, the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall is a very ancient force. Although it has never given birth to an Eternal realm existence, it has existed for longer than the previous master, Void Source Ancestor. Although the founder of the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall fell long ago, the Sea Ancestor of the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall is very powerful, much stronger than Void Source Ancestor. Such a force was actually destroyed without a sound; I’m afraid this involves an even more powerful existence, and it may even involve an Eternal realm being.” The spirit said.
“Forget it. This has nothing to do with us. Naturally, other forces will investigate it.”
Ye Tian muttered.
Some more time passed, and the impact brought by the destruction of the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall gradually faded.
But on this day, yet another force was destroyed.
This force was the Myriad Ancient Sect, a force that had risen through a sect. It had originally been a sect force in an Eternal Heaven and Earth, but later a powerful prodigy was born there. Rising all the way, that prodigy elevated the Myriad Ancient Sect to a level comparable to the major forces of the Source Sea, and even occupied a blessed land in the Source Sea, where a brand-new Myriad Ancient Sect was established.
The Myriad Ancient Sect was not as powerful as the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall, but it still had one Half-Step Eternal Realm being, and there were also many Supreme Beings in the sect.
Yet even so, the Myriad Ancient Sect was still completely annihilated.
In the days that followed, one force after another was silently destroyed; among them were even quite a few forces without Half-Step Eternal Realm beings, possessing only Eighth or Ninth Supreme Beings, and yet they were still targeted.
Originally, everyone thought this mysterious black hand was only targeting the top major forces with Half-Step Eternal Realm beings, but now even slightly weaker forces were suffering, so all forces were thrown into panic.
Thus, all the forces joined together to investigate.
On this day, the twelve ancestors of Source God Pavilion convened an inner-circle meeting, and Ye Tian also received a summons.
Source God Pavilion.
Ye Tian arrived here and sat in his seat.
Very soon, the other inner-circle members appeared one by one, but Ge Fei Source Ancestor did not come.
The twelve ancestors did not care about one or two missing inner-circle members.
Seeing that almost all the inner-circle members had arrived, True River Sword Ancestor spoke: “I believe everyone has heard the news about the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall and the many forces that were wiped out, right?”
“We have heard!”
“Right now, many forces are quite worried, and some even don’t know what to do.”
“True River Sword Ancestor, do you know who the mysterious black hand is?”
“If I find out who the enemy is, I will definitely destroy them.”
“Forget it. The Dragon Scale Sacred Hall was killed without a sound; how could an ordinary Half-Step Eternal Realm be a match for that?”
……
True River Sword Ancestor waved his hand, telling everyone to stop talking, and then said, “Based on my understanding, the black hand behind this used an Eternal origin weapon—the Time-Space Constant Sand Bead—to seal off the Eternal Heaven and Earth, preventing any information from being transmitted out. Only then did they move to destroy those forces. To be able to wipe out a force like the Dragon Scale Sacred Hall in such a short time, either an Eternal realm being made a move, or one Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm joined forces with several top Half-Step Eternal Realm beings.”
“An Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm? There shouldn’t be many of those in the entire Source Sea, right?”
An ordinary Half-Step Eternal Realm said in a low voice.
“Indeed, there aren’t many Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm beings in the entire Source Sea!” Wind Ancient Source Ancestor nodded. “Among us twelve ancestors of the Source Sea, only six are Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm beings, but even within the Extreme Stage there are differences.”
Extreme Stage!
Ye Tian also knew this division of levels: those who had mastered innate Eternal rules and whose strength far surpassed top Eternal realm experts were Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm beings.
It was said that Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm beings were further divided into First Extreme Stage, Second Extreme Stage, Third Extreme Stage...
The highest was the Ninth Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm, but such a Half-Step Eternal Realm could even defeat ordinary Eternal realm beings; in this era, they practically did not exist.
The twelve ancestors of Source God Pavilion possessed six Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm beings, but they were all at the First Extreme Stage. As for whether they had hidden strength, that was unknown.
But Source God Pavilion was a loose organization with no real constraints, so it posed no threat to many top major forces.
At this moment.
Some inner-circle members even suspected whether the twelve ancestors were behind this, but thinking about the twelve ancestors’ personalities, they probably would not do such a thing. However, if this matter was not resolved for a day, the fear in their hearts could not be erased.
At this time, True River Sword Ancestor provided another important clue, causing all the inner-circle members’ expressions to change in shock.
