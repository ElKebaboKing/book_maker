Chapter 1687 Prisoners with Astonishing Strength!

The ancient Stele was both a warning and an introduction, and also the entrance into the prison.
Ye Tian’s will connected with the Stele, and his body was quickly swallowed by it.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian entered a void similar to a void area, but it was even more desolate than the void area. Yet there was still a trace of energy here, which allowed prisoners to keep their bodies in Peak condition. However, if they wanted to cultivate and increase their cultivation, that was absolutely impossible.
When Ye Tian entered here, he saw a tall Eternal Realm lord. He was bound by strands of invisible chains and seemed to have fallen into a state of self-dormancy.
But now that Ye Tian had entered, those chains vanished, and that change awakened the Eternal Realm lord.
This Eternal Realm lord should be the Qishui Lord mentioned on the Stele.
“After so many ages, another Immortal Realm lord has finally come in. I’m so bored!!!!”
Then, Qishui Lord revealed a ferocious cold smile, his voice echoing through the void, as if extremely chilling.
Of course, for an Immortal Realm lord, this was not frightening at all.
But the aura emitted by Qishui Lord was incomparably condensed, even close to frenzied. He was definitely not an ordinary three-step Immortal Realm lord.
Even though this place was not suitable for cultivation, it did not affect comprehension of Eternal Techniques. No one knew how many Eternal Techniques Qishui Lord had comprehended.
But soon, Ye Tian knew. The moment the other party made a move, it was with a legendary Eternal Technique.
Puff!
A black hand shattered the void and appeared at Ye Tian’s location; it was clearly Qishui Lord making his move.
But this hand did not hit Ye Tian. Ye Tian promptly used Void Step to dodge.
“Will Severing Void Sword!”
Ye Tian did not hesitate at all and immediately used this move.
This will Eternal Technique had an extremely fast activation speed, but Qishui Lord’s reaction was too quick; he even dodged the Will Severing Void Sword.
At this point, Ye Tian no longer underestimated Qishui Lord.
If it were any other Immortal Realm lord, they would already have been heavily injured by a single strike from him.
Although Qishui Lord was not as fast as Ye Tian, his battle experience was far too rich. Every move was a legendary Eternal Technique, and almost every legendary Eternal Technique had been created for battle, perfectly coordinated, without the slightest error.
However, with Ye Tian’s Void True Body and Void Step, Qishui Lord had no hope of injuring him.
At this point, Ye Tian could have used Myriad Layers of Void to heavily injure Qishui Lord, but Qishui Lord was indeed a very good sparring opponent, so he chose not to do that. Moreover, he could learn many things from Qishui Lord.
In such a prison, it was impossible to temper oneself indefinitely. After a certain amount of time, one would be transferred out of the prison, and then be unable to enter again for a short period. This was the rule of Mount Mo. Unless this heaven and earth were broken, there was no way to change such a rule.
Fortunately, there were many prisons here, and there were quite a few Immortal Realm lord-level prisoners, so Ye Tian was not short of opponents.
Among these opponents, some were slightly weaker, while some were extremely strong.
However, only Ye Tian, an Eternal Realm being, had come here; there were no other Immortal Realm lords from the Time-Space Territory.
Perhaps there were other Immortal Realm lords here, but if their strength was too weak, they would easily be heavily injured by the prisoners here, and thus would not serve any tempering purpose at all, so they would not remain in Mount Mo’s prison.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian sent out another clone body to explore the black continent and discovered six Immortal Realm lords.
Besides Mount Mo’s prison, this black continent also had several other opportunities. For example, there was a blessed land here that could improve willpower, which was also somewhat effective for Immortal Realm lords; there was also a place where legendary realm lords cultivated, where traces left by legendary realm lords remained and were worth comprehending.
Ye Tian went to take a look at all of those places, but for him, the most valuable place was still Mount Mo’s prison.
In the years that followed, one of Ye Tian’s clone bodies remained in Mount Mo’s prison, battling and sparring with these Immortal Realm prisoners, gradually perfecting his legendary Eternal Techniques, allowing these legendary Eternal Techniques to fit together more perfectly, thereby coordinating better and unleashing even more powerful effects from his own strength.
Moreover, from them, Ye Tian sensed the true application of will Eternal Techniques, an experience that many secret art inheritances simply could not teach.
Year after year of Eternal Years passed, and Ye Tian’s strength grew stronger and stronger. Even though his cultivation still remained at the Six-Step Immortal Realm lord level, his combat strength had increased several times over.
After several thousand Eternal Years, Ye Tian no longer knew how many battles he had experienced; they were practically innumerable.
By this point, continuing to fight these prisoners was no longer necessary. He was preparing to kill these prisoners and obtain the treasures on them.
Even though these Immortal Realm prisoners had been captured by Mount Mo, Mount Mo could not possibly have thoroughly stripped them of all treasures. Some treasures were hidden very deeply, and unless they were killed, there was simply no way to find them.
Therefore, killing these Immortal Realm prisoners was hugely profitable as long as one found even a single treasure, because it was certainly no ordinary treasure.
On this day.
Ye Tian entered the prison void of Qishui Lord, preparing to finish Qishui Lord off.
“You’ve come again!”
Qishui Lord looked at Ye Tian coldly.
The battle began again.
But this time, Ye Tian directly used Myriad Layers of Void and heavily injured Qishui Lord on the spot.
Immediately afterward, Ye Tian went all out. Even though his cultivation was suppressed to the same level as Qishui Lord’s, the three-step Immortal Realm lord level, Ye Tian’s combat power was far, far stronger than Qishui Lord’s.
Throughout the entire process, Qishui Lord was completely crushed by Ye Tian and had almost no ability to resist.
Very quickly, Qishui Lord was heavily injured.
The key point was that Mount Mo no longer existed, so there would be no powerful experts from Mount Mo stepping in to stop him.
Until the moment of death, Qishui Lord never begged for mercy. In the end, he fell at Ye Tian’s hands and became a corpse.
After killing Qishui Lord, Ye Tian began searching for treasures within his body.
“There really is a treasure!”
Ye Tian found it.
Whoosh!
A drop of water flew out from Qishui Lord’s body and landed in Ye Tian’s hand.
Although this drop of water was not an Eternal treasure, it was far more precious than weapons like the Void Holy Weapon. It could be called an Eternal wonder treasure.
Such an Eternal wonder treasure was not considered a weapon; it could only be counted as one of the strange objects, an object at the Eternal Realm level.
After Ye Tian refined this drop of water, he finally understood its use.
[Kui Tian Pearl: Can nourish willpower and restore willpower strength]
Such a treasure was quite good. For example, if the will of an Eternal Realm expert were heavily injured and one wanted to recover, it would be somewhat troublesome. But the Kui Tian Pearl could restore such injuries.
“Not bad. I really don’t have such a treasure. Getting this Eternal wonder treasure this time is a huge gain!”
Ye Tian said excitedly.
Afterwards, he began targeting the second Immortal Realm prison.
