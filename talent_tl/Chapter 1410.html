Chapter 1409: Saint Lord Qingfeng's Plea for Help, Crying Blood Emperor
!
As time passed, the injuries of Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng grew heavier.
Of course, they had many life-saving methods, and being invincible domain ancestors, it would be difficult to kill them unless there was a significant power gap.
For... Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng, they had no way to escape now. If they couldn't escape, the ultimate outcome would be to be worn down to death by Ye Tian.
"Let's call for help!"
Ancestor Mili suggested.
Ancestor Wansheng agreed. There was no other way; Ye Tian's Five Elements Seal was too terrifying. While fighting Ye Tian, they couldn't spare attention to break the Five Elements Seal, and they couldn't defeat Ancestor Wanfa in the Profound Void Sacred Realm, so they could only call for help.
Thus, they used special communication artifacts to send messages to the Qingyuan Sacred Land ancestors in the Daoshan Realm, and then had the Qingyuan Sacred Land ancestors try to contact the Saint Lord of Qingyuan Sacred Land.
The Dark Forbidden Zone isolated communications, and it was extremely far from Qingyuan Sacred Land, making communication impossible.
Only by entering the War Realm Tower or using rare formations to forcibly connect with Qingyuan Sacred Land could they barely manage to send a message back.
Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng were currently in battle and couldn't contact Qingyuan Sacred Land directly, so they could only send messages to the Qingyuan Sacred Land ancestors in the Daoshan Realm, asking them to try and contact Qingyuan Sacred Land.
Qingyuan Sacred Land.
Saint Lord Qingfeng received a message from a sacred land ancestor, learning about the situation in the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
At first, he didn't believe the source of the news.
But soon, after multiple confirmations, he finally ascertained that the news was true.
"How is that possible? Ancestor Wanfa's strength is actually that powerful—that's too exaggerated!"
Saint Lord Qingfeng was dumbfounded.
The two invincible domain ancestors he had summoned were Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, not weak by any means. Even he himself only possessed the strength of a Two-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Upon learning this news, Saint Lord Qingfeng was very hesitant, unsure of what to do.
Should he rescue them? If he did, he would definitely have to send out top-tier invincible domain ancestors, otherwise, Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng couldn't be saved at all. Moreover, certain treasures would have to be used, or there wouldn't be enough time for rescue.
And once top-tier invincible domain ancestors were dispatched, the secret of Qingyuan Sacred Land's involvement would be exposed.
After all, the Qingyuan Sacred Land's invincible domain ancestors above Three-Step were all known to the outside world; once they acted, their identities would inevitably be revealed.
This way, they would completely offend Ancestor Wanfa and the War Gate.
Even if the War Gate didn't destroy their Qingyuan Sacred Land, a casual suppression would make life difficult for Qingyuan Sacred Land, and it might even lose its title as a sacred land.
If it lost its sacred land title, other forces would not let Qingyuan Sacred Land off the hook.
By then, Qingyuan Sacred Land would surely be annihilated.
But if they didn't rescue them, Qingyuan Sacred Land would suffer a great loss, not only losing two invincible domain ancestors but also the sacred artifact, the Qingyuan World Pearl.
And once the Qingyuan World Pearl fell into Ancestor Wanfa's hands, if Ancestor Wanfa investigated, he would be able to find out that this sacred artifact, the Qingyuan World Pearl, belonged to Qingyuan Sacred Land.
By then, Qingyuan Sacred Land would still be exposed.
"We must save Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng, but it cannot be our renowned invincible domain ancestors from Qingyuan Sacred Land!"
Saint Lord Qingfeng thought to himself.
After pondering for a few seconds, he thought of someone.
A rogue cultivator—the Crying Blood Emperor! The Crying Blood Emperor was also a legendary figure; his talent wasn't exceptional, but relying on numerous fortuitous encounters, coupled with his ruthlessness, he seized opportunities from many peerless prodigies and killed quite a few Sacred Gate disciples, thus allowing him to grow to his current state.
The Crying Blood Emperor's strength was formidable; he was also a Two-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
However, the Crying Blood Emperor was on the wanted lists of multiple Sacred Gates, pursued by invincible domain ancestors and even supreme domain ancestors from the Sacred Gates.
However, the Crying Blood Emperor was extremely difficult to kill. His true form was a river of blood, cultivating into a human form from the blood river and growing to this day. No one knew where his true form was; usually, only his clones were dispatched.
The strength of these clones was inferior to his true form, possessing only the power of a Three-Step Domain Ancestor. But even if a clone fell, it could form another clone after a period of recuperation.
It was precisely because of the unique nature of his clones that he could seize countless opportunities and wasn't afraid of being hunted by others.
For instance, many invincible domain ancestors had killed him many times, but they had only killed his clones.
Only supreme-level domain ancestors proficient in karmic sacred techniques could easily kill the Crying Blood Emperor.
However, the Crying Blood Emperor's clones were not that easy to find, and supreme-level domain ancestors wouldn't idly spend vast amounts of time hunting down an invincible domain ancestor.
Therefore, the Crying Blood Emperor had lived to this day.
What no one knew was that Saint Lord Qingfeng knew the Crying Blood Emperor.
Or rather, he had cooperated with the Crying Blood Emperor multiple times and had even ventured into the Dark Forbidden Zone, so their relationship was decent.
Of course, asking the Crying Blood Emperor to help him for free was something the Crying Blood Emperor would definitely not do.
But as long as he paid a price, the Crying Blood Emperor would absolutely be willing to do it, and only he dared to openly offend the disciples of the Sacred Gate.
Saint Lord Qingfeng was seen holding a communication artifact and sending a message to the Crying Blood Emperor.
Communication in the Dark Sacred Domain was relatively easier; as long as the Crying Blood Emperor was still in the Dark Sacred Domain, he could contact him.
"Saint Lord Qingfeng, what is it?"
The Crying Blood Emperor's voice sounded from the communication artifact.
"Crying Blood Emperor, there's something I'd like your help with!"
Saint Lord Qingfeng said directly.
"Speak!"
The Crying Blood Emperor said.
"Two of our Qingyuan Sacred Land's invincible domain ancestors are in danger in the Profound Void Sacred Realm, trapped by the War Gate's supreme domain ancestor, and are at risk of falling. The War Gate doesn't know they are our Qingyuan Sacred Land's ancestors, and I don't want to expose the existence of Qingyuan Sacred Land, so I'd like you to go to the Profound Void Sacred Realm for rescue.
If successful, I am willing to give you the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus!"
Saint Lord Qingfeng requested.
"Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus!!!!"
The Crying Blood Emperor's voice was slightly agitated. "I didn't expect Saint Lord Qingfeng to be so generous, actually paying such a huge price for two invincible domain ancestors. It seems what those two invincible domain ancestors did was not simple.
Don't worry, I won't care what those two invincible domain ancestors did, or what treasures they obtained. Given our relationship, I wouldn't covet anything from your Qingyuan Sacred Land.
However, after all, this time we're offending the War Gate. The Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus alone is not enough for me to act. I also need three Phoenix Blood Soul Fruits from that mysterious cave we once ventured into.
If you agree, I will head to the Profound Void Sacred Realm in the Daoshan Realm at the fastest speed."
"Alright, I agree!"
Saint Lord Qingfeng agreed without hesitation.
In reality, even if the Crying Blood Emperor demanded more outrageous things, he would still agree, otherwise Qingyuan Sacred Land would be in complete trouble.
