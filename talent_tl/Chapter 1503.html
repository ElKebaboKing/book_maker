Chapter 1502: Another Breakthrough, the Second Level of the Ultimate Ancestor!

After obtaining the two Overpowering Paramount Ancestral Techniques, Ye Tian began to work hard on merging them.
In reality, the essence of the two Paramount Ancestral Techniques was the same; they simply aligned with different laws of heaven and earth.
One belonged to the laws of the Dark Forbidden Zone, and the other belonged to the laws of the Sanctuary World.
Therefore, merging these two Paramount Ancestral Techniques was not an easy task.
As time slowly passed, Ye Tian gradually grasped the mysteries of the fusion.
First of all, to merge these two Paramount Ancestral Techniques, they had to be tailored to oneself, fused based on his own being.
For example, even if he merged these two Paramount Ancestral Techniques to create an Ultimate Ancestral Technique, and his cloned body, the Heyuan Sacred Beast, obtained the memories of that Ultimate Ancestral Technique, the clone would still be unable to perform it. This was unless the cloned body also merged two Paramount Sacred Arts that suited its own body to break through the shackles and comprehend an Ultimate Sacred Art.
The cloned body, the Heyuan Sacred Beast, was currently not even an Ultimate Sacred Beast, so it naturally had no way to comprehend an Ultimate Sacred Art.
However, Ye Tian was now thinking of helping the cloned body, the Heyuan Sacred Beast, break through its shackles and promote it to an Ultimate Sacred Beast.
Of course, he had to wait until his main body stepped into the second level of the Ultimate Ancestor before helping the cloned body break through.
In fact, he didn't really need to waste time doing this. After all, once his main body reached the Ultimate Ancestor realm, he could simply clone the body of an Ultimate Sacred Beast.
But Ye Tian felt that if the cloned body achieved a self-breakthrough to become an Ultimate Sacred Beast, it might be like when Ancestor Qingye broke through—the cloned body might become permanently fixed as an avatar and no longer occupy a slot for cloned bodies.
If that were the case, it would be equivalent to having an extra permanent avatar at the Ultimate Sacred Beast level.
This was an attempt; if it truly failed, it would merely be a waste of some time.
One Domain Era after another passed.
Unknowingly, nearly a hundred Domain Eras had gone by, almost a full Dark Era, and by this time, the Great Tribulation of the Sanctuary World was approaching.
On this day.
Ye Tian was finally about to comprehend an Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
"It's so difficult. I didn't expect that merging two Overpowering Paramount Ancestral Techniques would be even harder than breaking through to become an Ultimate Ancestor. I thought that with my realm as an Ultimate Ancestor, it wouldn't take long to merge them, but it actually took nearly a full Dark Era."
Ye Tian said with some surprise.
However, he finally comprehended it.
Boom!!!!
The two Overpowering Paramount Ancestral Techniques merged, transforming into an Overpowering Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
The moment the Overpowering Ultimate Ancestral Technique was successfully comprehended, a terrifying power surged within Ye Tian's body.
At this moment, Ye Tian's physical strength began to skyrocket, comparable to the transformation when he broke through from a Top-tier Ancestor to an Ultimate Ancestor.
The transformation of his power caused his body to continuously absorb energy from the outside world, but this energy was still not enough.
Thus, Ye Tian used all the energy he had collected during this period to complete the power transformation.
It took several Domain Years for this transformation to be completely finished.
"I didn't expect that comprehending an Ultimate Ancestral Technique would actually trigger a transformation of power. My current strength has increased by too much!"
Ye Tian clenched his fist, and the void itself began to shatter. He couldn't help but feel astonished.
At this moment, he finally understood why the Ultimate Ancestors were divided into three levels. Once one comprehends an Ultimate Ancestral Technique, they belong to the second level.
After all, once an Ultimate Ancestral Technique is mastered—no matter which kind it is—one's strength will skyrocket to a terrifying degree.
"With my strength soaring, if I perform the Overpowering Ultimate Ancestral Technique again, my attack power will far exceed what it was before. Even an ordinary Ultimate Ancestor or Ultimate Sacred Beast would likely be heavily wounded by a single strike from me!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
At this point, there were only a few Domain Eras left until the Sanctuary World's tribulation.
By now, all the major forces in the Sanctuary World were preparing for this Great Tribulation.
After Ye Tian stabilized his realm, he began to create a Sacred Art based on the body of his clone, the Heyuan Sacred Beast.
Ye Tian's cloned body, the Heyuan Sacred Beast, had promoted to a Sacred Beast using the Six-Armed Vajra Technique, and this Sacred Art had also been dedicated to the Haitian Sacred Sect.
Later, Ye Tian found this Sacred Art in the Haitian Sacred Sect's Sacred Art Treasury; otherwise, there really would have been no way to charge toward the Ultimate Sacred Beast level.
Now that he had the Sacred Art for the cloned body, he just needed to create a Sacred Art that aligned with the laws of the Dark Forbidden Zone.
Ye Tian's main body's realm was extremely high, having stepped into the second level of the Ultimate Ancestor, so creating a version of the Six-Armed Vajra Technique that suited the Dark Forbidden Zone was not very difficult.
It only took a dozen or so Domain Years for Ye Tian to successfully create the Dark Forbidden Zone version of the Six-Armed Vajra Technique.
Shortly after.
The cloned body, the Heyuan Sacred Beast, arrived at the Haitian Forbidden Land. It instantly shared memories with the main body, obtained this Six-Armed Vajra Technique, and then returned to the Haitian Sacred Sect.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian's cloned body began practicing the Six-Armed Vajra Technique from the Dark Forbidden Zone, using this Sacred Art to charge toward becoming a Sacred Beast of the second path.
Soon, the second path also stepped into the Sacred Beast level.
The countless resources Ye Tian had collected from the Dark Forbidden Zone had long been delivered to the cloned body. Now, the cloned body used these resources to cultivate rapidly. The second path progressed quickly, stepping into the Top-tier Sacred Beast level, and then rapidly advanced to the limit of a Top-tier Sacred Beast.
With both paths having reached the limit of a Top-tier Sacred Beast, it meant that this cloned body of Ye Tian's could now attempt to break through to the Ultimate Sacred Beast level.
Normally, merging two Sacred Arts would take at least dozens of Domain Eras, but these two Sacred Arts were far inferior to the Endless Ancestor Law, so the difficulty of fusion was not high.
Additionally, Ye Tian's current realm was very high—he was a second-level Ultimate Ancestor. Although the cloned body's realm was still at the Top-tier Sacred Beast level, it possessed the memories of the main body. Therefore, in terms of insight and comprehension, it far surpassed any Top-tier Sacred Beast.
Thus, Ye Tian felt he had hope to completely elevate this cloned body to the Ultimate Sacred Beast level within the next few Domain Eras—specifically, before the day the Great Tribulation arrived.
"Let's begin!"
Ye Tian devoted all his efforts to comprehension, working hard to merge the two Sacred Arts.
One Domain Year after another flowed by, and unknowingly, one Domain Era passed.
At this point, Ye Tian felt his comprehension was nearly complete. Perhaps with one more Domain Era of cultivation, he would be certain of completely merging the two Sacred Arts.
Finally, another Domain Era passed.
On this day, Ye Tian completely merged the two Sacred Arts, and the two Sacred Beast bodies also began to fuse, transforming toward an Ultimate Sacred Beast body.
Haitian Sacred Sect.
The gazes of two Ultimate Sacred Beasts pierced through infinite space simultaneously, looking toward the place where the Heyuan Sacred Beast was in seclusion.
"What's happening? The Heyuan Sacred Beast is breaking through? He's about to step into the Ultimate Sacred Beast level?"
The two Ultimate Sacred Beasts were dumbfounded.
