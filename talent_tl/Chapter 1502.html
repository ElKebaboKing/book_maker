Chapter 1501: The Three Levels of Supreme Ancestors!

Ye Tian was in no hurry to refine the resources from the Bone Race; instead, he began studying the manual obtained from the Bone Emperor.
This manual recorded some information regarding the Bone Emperors, such as their classifications.
The cultivation of a Bone Emperor was generally divided into three levels. The first level consisted of those who had just broken through to become a Bone Emperor. Although such a Bone Emperor was much stronger than a Bone King, the difference wasn't overly exaggerated. If there were enough Bone Kings, it was even possible to wear down and kill a first-level Bone Emperor.
The second level consisted of Bone Emperors who had mastered a Supreme Ancestral Technique. The Bone Race also practiced Ancestral Techniques, and their terminology was similar.
An Ultimate Ancestral Technique was almost the most powerful technique in a given world, representing the "ultimate," or the limit.
However, above Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, there were still Supreme Ancestral Techniques. The manual did not mention the secrets of Supreme Ancestral Techniques or how to cultivate them; after all, the Bone Emperor would not teach Ye Tian how to cultivate a Supreme Ancestral Technique.
In short, a Bone Emperor who mastered a Supreme Ancestral Technique and raised their cultivation to a certain level could be ranked in the second level.
Then there was the third level, which belonged to the peak realm of Bone Emperors. They mastered multiple Supreme Ancestral Techniques, and their cultivation had reached the absolute limit, unable to advance further, standing only a single step away from the next realm.
Above the Bone Emperor was the Bone Saint, a classification equivalent to the invincible Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Zone.
"I should still belong to the first level of Supreme Ancestors. I must master a Supreme Ancestral Technique before I can step into the second level. However, my foundation is extremely deep, and I cultivate the Endless Ancestor Path, so my true combat power should be close to the second level!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
He had some guesses about how to master a Supreme Ancestral Technique, but he wasn't certain.
"In the future, I'll have my cloned body, the He Yuan Holy Beast, consult the two Supreme Holy Beasts of the Sea Sky Holy Sect. They might know!" Ye Tian murmured.
In the following days, Ye Tian began refining the resources from the Bone Race, and his strength continued to improve.
In the blink of an eye, several Realm Eras passed in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
By this time, Ye Tian had long since finished refining the resources from the Bone Race. He wanted to trade with them again, but unfortunately, the Bone Race began to act perfunctorily. Consequently, he couldn't trade for many resources from them. On the other hand, from the Sea Sky Holy Sect's side, his cloned body would send over resources from time to time, ensuring he never lacked cultivation materials.
Additionally, he frequently sent resources from the Dark Forbidden Zone to his cloned body, the He Yuan Holy Beast, for cultivation. As a result, the strength of this cloned body improved quite rapidly and was now not far from the limit of a top-tier Holy Beast.
One day.
Ye Tian's cloned body, the He Yuan Holy Beast, went to visit one of the Supreme Holy Beasts of the Sea Sky Holy Sect—the Wolf Bone Holy Beast.
"Greetings, Lord Wolf Bone Holy Beast!"
Ye Tian's cloned body, the He Yuan Holy Beast, said respectfully.
"He Yuan, why have you come to see me?"
The Wolf Bone Holy Beast asked.
"I wish to ask Lord Wolf Bone Holy Beast a few questions," Ye Tian said.
"Speak, what is it?"
The Wolf Bone Holy Beast said indifferently.
"Lord Wolf Bone Holy Beast, I want to know how one can comprehend a Supreme Holy Technique!"
Ye Tian asked.
"Holy Technique" was the term used in the Holy Realm for Ancestral Techniques; a Holy Technique here was equivalent to an Ancestral Technique in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
And a Supreme Holy Technique was equivalent to a Supreme Ancestral Technique.
Upon hearing the name "Supreme Holy Technique," the Wolf Bone Holy Beast's voice turned cold. "He Yuan, this is not knowledge you should be concerned with. You are merely a top-tier Holy Beast. You will find out once you step into the rank of Supreme Holy Beast."
"Lord Wolf Bone Holy Beast, I am merely curious,"
Ye Tian hurriedly said.
Afterward, under his persistent requests, the Wolf Bone Holy Beast finally spoke: "There is no harm in telling you. To comprehend a Supreme Holy Technique, one must go to an Otherworld to comprehend an Ultimate Holy Technique of the same type, and then fuse our Holy Realm's Ultimate Holy Technique with the Otherworld's similar Ultimate Holy Technique. It is the same method as breaking through to become a Supreme Holy Beast. However, if one cannot step into the level of a Supreme Holy Beast, there is no way to fuse two Ultimate Holy Techniques."
"Lord Wolf Bone Holy Beast, once two Ultimate Holy Techniques from different worlds are fused, can one break the shackles of Ultimate Holy Techniques and comprehend a Supreme Holy Technique?" Ye Tian asked excitedly.
"Correct!" The Wolf Bone Holy Beast nodded. "However, in our Holy Realm, only one Supreme Holy Beast has ever comprehended a Supreme Holy Technique. You should put such thoughts out of your mind!"
"Yes!"
Ye Tian nodded and then withdrew.
...
"From the information obtained from the Wolf Bone Holy Beast, the Holy Realm likely only has one Supreme Holy Beast at the second level. No, that Supreme Holy Beast who comprehended a Supreme Holy Technique might have even reached the third level. However, the strength of the Holy Realm is still not that great. Once I become stronger in the future, there is a high hope of completely occupying this Holy Realm," Ye Tian thought to himself.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian's cloned body began a period of secluded meditation to comprehend and create an Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
The Ultimate Ancestral Technique he prepared to create was the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique. Once he created the Holy Realm's version of the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique, he could fuse it with the Dark Forbidden Zone's version, giving him hope of fusing them into the Supreme Ancestral Technique: Overlord Strength.
At that time, his strength would receive a terrifying boost, instantly stepping into the second level of Supreme Ancestors.
However, this cloned body did not possess the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent. Trying to create the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique would be extremely difficult.
He could spend a vast amount of time creating and comprehending it, but it might take more than a single Dark Era. By then, he wouldn't be able to step into the second level of Supreme Ancestors before the Great Tribulation of the Holy Realm.
"I'll first bestow the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent to the cloned body. Once the cloned body creates the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique, I'll take the talent back."
Ye Tian planned.
But this plan carried a risk: a bestowed talent could not be replicated. This meant Ye Tian would temporarily lose the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent. If the cloned body, the He Yuan Holy Beast, were to perish, he would lose the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent forever.
In that case, he would have to search for a large number of Strength Ancestral Talents to replicate and slowly fuse them back into an Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent, which would undoubtedly waste a lot of his time, and he might not even be able to obtain it again.
However, compared to the potential gains, it was worth the risk.
One day.
Ye Tian's cloned body entered the Sea Sky Forbidden Land once again. Subsequently, his main body infiltrated the Sea Sky Forbidden Land, instantly bestowed the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent to the cloned body, and then the main body left the forbidden land.
Having obtained the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent, the cloned body He Yuan Holy Beast's comprehension for creating a Strength-type Ultimate Ancestral Technique increased many times over.
Next, Ye Tian's cloned body remained in the Sea Sky Forbidden Land to comprehend the laws of strength of this world and create the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
With the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent as an aid, the cloned body's speed of comprehension was very fast.
He estimated that it wouldn't be long before he could create the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
As expected.
In just ten Realm Years, the cloned body successfully created the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
If he hadn't had the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent, he would likely have needed countless times more time to create it.
He did not enter the Sea Sky Forbidden Land again immediately, as too short an interval would arouse suspicion.
After several Realm Eras had passed, Ye Tian's cloned body finally applied to enter the Sea Sky Forbidden Land.
Once the cloned body returned to the Sea Sky Forbidden Land, memories were shared. Ye Tian's main body naturally obtained the cloned body's memories and the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique from the Holy Realm.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian's main body infiltrated the Sea Sky Forbidden Land again, stripped the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent from the cloned body, and reclaimed the talent.
Thus, Ye Tian's plan was complete.
With two versions of the Overlord Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique in hand, once they were fused, he would be able to step into the second level of Supreme Ancestors.
