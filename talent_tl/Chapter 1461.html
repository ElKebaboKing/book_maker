Chapter 1460: Dark Soul Domain, Soul Dao
!
"This Dark Holy Land seems a bit special!"
The Holy Ancestor murmured.
"It is indeed a bit special!"
The Destruction Ancestor also noticed it.
Every Dark Holy Land has a unique cultivation system. For example, the Dark Destruction Domain absorbs destruction energy from countless regions of the Dark Forbidden Zone, using destruction as its cultivation system. Thus, the Path of Destruction prevails in the Dark Destruction Domain. Coupled with their natural bloodline advantages, they can grow simply by destroying everything.
The Dark Holy Domain, on the other hand, is a holy land for cultivators, focusing on cultivation and the comprehension of Taboo Great Daos.
But this third Dark Holy Land was completely different; the rules here were very peculiar.
"The rules here lean toward the soul!"
The Holy Ancestor said in surprise.
The soul! The other Ancestors investigated and deduced one by one.
Indeed, the rules of this Dark Holy Land leaned toward the Path of the Soul.
It was normal for some worlds to lean toward soul rules, but for an entire Dark Holy Land to lean toward the Path of the Soul meant that the mainstream cultivation path of this Dark Holy Land was... the Soul Dao.
The mainstream of the Dark Destruction Domain was the Bloodline Dao, while the mainstream of the Dark Holy Domain was the Physical Body Dao—for instance, Domain Ancestors increased their cultivation by tempering their Domain Bodies.
If the mainstream was the Soul Dao, then one must increase their soul realm to increase their cultivation level.
"Interesting. In a Dark Holy Land where the Soul Dao is mainstream, many of the treasures born within might be items that increase the soul realm, or perhaps treasures that can temper the souls of us Ancestors.
It is difficult for our physical bodies to improve further, but if we temper our souls, our strength will see a certain degree of growth!"
The Time-Space Ancestor said excitedly.
The other Ancestors were also very excited. Unfortunately, they could not enter; otherwise, they would have charged into this third Dark Holy Land immediately.
"What should we name this Dark Holy Land?"
The various Ancestors prepared to name this holy land.
"Let's call it the Dark Soul Domain!"
The Holy Ancestor named it casually.
"Since the Holy Ancestor has named this Dark Holy Land, then let it be called the Dark Soul Domain!"
The other Ancestors all agreed.
Once the location of the Dark Soul Domain was confirmed, they each established teleportation points there.
On the side of the Dark Destruction Domain, the Time-Space Ancestor established a permanent Time-Space Gate.
On the side of the Dark Holy Domain, the World Ancestor condensed a sub-tower of the War World Tower and opened permissions, allowing passage through the War World Tower.
Previously, using the War World Tower to travel consumed too much energy, which was why the World Ancestor had disabled that function, making the War World Tower unusable for travel. Only the Wu Ancestor of the War Gate had a small amount of permission to temporarily travel between two places, but even then, he could not stray too far from the range of the War World Tower.
Back then, the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor had used this to reach the Profound Void Holy Realm to help Ye Tian.
But now, permissions were opened so that anyone could travel between the two places via the War World Tower, though this was only a temporary opening to send the half-step Domain Ancestors of the Dark Holy Domain over.
On the side of the Eternal Cult, a massive teleportation array was also constructed to bring their half-step Domain Ancestors over.
In reality, the Ancestors could have sent the half-step Domain Ancestors over one by one, but the speed would be too slow.
The Ancestors could not send too many people at once because their divine thoughts could not envelop everyone. After all, crossing such a vast distance imposed great restrictions; if they forced people through, those half-step Domain Ancestors would decompose into nothingness midway.
Just as when they sent Supreme Domain Ancestors to the Holy Domain Continent at the edge of the Dark Forbidden Zone, the Ancestors had to send them one by one rather than all at once.
Otherwise, if an Ancestor could carry countless half-step Domain Ancestors and easily cross endless territories, it would be unrealistic.
The Ancestors themselves could cross endless time and space with a single thought and descend anywhere in the Dark Forbidden Zone because their own thoughts were eternal and indestructible and their bodies were too powerful, but others could not do the same.
Soon.
One by one, the half-step Domain Ancestors of the Dark Holy Domain passed through the War World Tower and arrived at the Dark Soul Domain.
Ye Tian also went directly to the Dark Soul Domain through the War Ancestor Realm of the Profound Void Realm. This was because the World Ancestor had opened permissions, making it very convenient.
BOOM!!!!! Led by Ye Tian's main body, one hundred thousand human Domain Ancestors descended upon the edge of the Dark Soul Domain.
But when Ye Tian appeared, many Ancestors were surprised.
The peak stage of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm! They saw through Ye Tian's cultivation level, but previously, Ye Tian was only at the middle stage of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm! How much time had passed, and Ye Tian's cultivation had already reached the peak stage? This speed of improvement was far too fast! The Ancestors of the Dark Destruction Domain all wanted to eliminate Ye Tian, but since the many Ancestors of the Dark Holy Domain were present, they naturally could not do such a thing.
"Let the half-step Domain Ancestors enter!"
The Ancestors spoke.
Swish swish swish!!!! Countless half-step Domain Ancestors were thrown into the Dark Soul Domain by the Supreme Domain Ancestors.
Ye Tian also waved his hand, enveloping the one hundred thousand human geniuses and sending them into the Dark Soul Domain. Furthermore, he sent them to a remote region rather than the core area.
The Human Race's foundation was still relatively low, and their innate racial advantages were not great. There was no need to compete with other forces at the half-step Domain Ancestor stage, so developing in a remote region was fine.
Besides, with his duplicated bodies present to help the Human Race rise within the Dark Soul Domain, it would be quite easy and didn't require a prime territory.
Before long, countless half-step Domain Ancestors flooded into the Dark Soul Domain.
Meanwhile, the various Ancestors remained there to cultivate, not leaving the area.
They even meditated on the rules of the Dark Soul Domain, which was a form of cultivation for them as well.
Dark Soul Domain, a remote territory.
The one hundred thousand half-step Domain Ancestor geniuses of the Human Race descended here. They began to familiarize themselves with the environment and immediately occupied a Domain Realm.
Ye Tian had long since instructed them that after entering the Dark Soul Domain, the Human Race must unite, stay low-profile at the beginning, cultivate diligently, and not wander aimlessly.
The Dark Soul Domain had existed for a long time, so many races had already appeared here, and the cultivation system had begun to take shape.
"Soul Dao!"
The Human Race gathered a lot of information, including the cultivation system of this world.
The practitioners of this world did not value the physical body; they only valued the soul, believing the soul to be the foundation. If the physical body was destroyed, it could be completely reconsolidated, but if the soul was annihilated, then one truly perished.
When Ye Tian saw the Soul Dao, he immediately felt that this path had immense potential.
Moreover, to become an Ancestor in the Dark Soul Domain, one must also cultivate the Soul Dao.
