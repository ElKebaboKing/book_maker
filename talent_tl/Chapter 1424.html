Chapter 1423: Ancestral Talent, Ancestral Technique
!
In the days that followed, Ye Tian searched for various resources and treasures while copying Saint Talents, and hunted Saint Soul Beasts to absorb Saint Soul power to strengthen his Soul Saint Light.
It had to be said that Ye Tian's strength was formidable, and thus his harvest was naturally plentiful.
Furthermore, there were so many Saint Soul Beasts in the Saint Soul Realm that there were naturally many Saint Talents, completely exceeding his imagination.
In the blink of an eye, ten thousand years passed.
By this time, Ye Tian didn't even know how many... Saint Talents he had copied. The Copy Talent panel was densely packed with Saint Talents, and he had fused them one by one.
On this day, Ye Tian prepared to fuse a specific category of Saint Talents to see if he could trigger a transformation.
"I'll fuse strength-type Saint Talents then!"
Ye Tian decided.
Because strength-type Saint Talents were relatively common, many Saint Soul Beasts possessed them.
Fusing Saint Talents took a long time. To avoid wasting time, Ye Tian took out a Saint Object containing time rules and activated it with full force, supported by countless energy treasures, initiating a time acceleration of ten million years to one.
Of course, under these conditions, it was difficult for him to cultivate, but he didn't intend to; he only wanted to accelerate the fusion of Saint Talents.
Two Saint Talents fused! Three Saint Talents fused! Four Saint Talents fused! ....Ten! A hundred! A thousand! How could an ordinary Saint Talent user have so many Saint Talents? Even if an Ancestor had the greatest of opportunities, they wouldn't be able to give birth to so many Saint Talents. Besides, Ancestors looked down on Saint Talents and naturally wouldn't spend effort researching how to create them.
Only a monster like Ye Tian, who possessed the Copy Talent, could have such Saint Talents and even fuse them together.
Unknowingly, Ye Tian had spent a billion years fusing in the time-accelerated environment, while a hundred years had passed in the outside world.
By now, Ye Tian had fused over a hundred thousand strength-type Saint Talents, yet he still hadn't caused a qualitative change in the Saint Talent.
However, he felt that the new, unknown strength-type Saint Talent had reached an extremely powerful level, far exceeding ordinary Saint Talents, and was on the verge of a qualitative change.
"Continue fusing!"
After fusing a dozen more strength-type Saint Talents, the unknown strength-type Saint Talent finally completed its qualitative change.
Boom!!!!! Deep within his soul, Ye Tian's unknown Saint Talent, formed from the fusion of over a hundred thousand strength-type Saint Talents, was transforming. The talent mark expanded continuously, gradually turning into a single character—Strength! The talent mark taking the form of a character was something no one would believe if told, yet it happened.
At the same time, Ye Tian sensed the status of this Strength Saint Talent.
[Ancestral Talent: Strength] "There really is an Ancestral Talent. Perhaps this is the only one in the Dark Saint Domain!"
Ye Tian mused.
Of course, it was hard to say for sure; they might possess more secrets and might even be able to research Ancestral Talents.
It had to be said that after obtaining the Ancestral Talent, Ye Tian discovered that the power of the Ancestral Talent alone was comparable to a Saint Technique at the limit realm.
"If I continue to cultivate a strength-type Saint Technique, is it possible to break its shackles and turn it into an Ancestral Technique?"
Ye Tian wondered.
If he could truly achieve that, his strength would reach a terrifying level.
Next, Ye Tian continued to fuse other Saint Talents.
He had copied many of each category of Saint Talent. For instance, although he didn't dare provoke certain Saint Soul Beast groups, he could copy a large number of Saint Talents at once, so he was not lacking in Saint Talents at all.
Soon, one Ancestral Talent after another was born.
[Ancestral Talent: Speed] [Ancestral Talent: Defense] [Ancestral Talent: Gold] [Ancestral Talent: Fire] .... Ye Tian possessed many Ancestral Talents, but the only pity was that he couldn't form a Space Ancestral Talent or a Time Ancestral Talent. This was because there were fewer Saint Soul Beasts with Space and Time Saint Talents. After searching for so long, he had only encountered a few dozen Time Saint Talent beasts and over a hundred Space Saint Talent beasts.
Such a small number of Saint Talents simply couldn't trigger a qualitative change.
Therefore, if he wanted to give birth to Space and Time Ancestral Talents, he could only accumulate them slowly.
Having birthed so many Ancestral Talents, Ye Tian began to re-cultivate those Saint Techniques, specifically focusing on two: the Saint Armor Technique and the Gold Silk Technique.
These two Saint Techniques were not very difficult to cultivate, making it easier to break their shackles and become Ancestral Techniques.
Another few hundred thousand years passed, and Ye Tian's Soul Saint Light had reached fifty percent. This meant half of his soul had turned into Soul Saint Light. Consequently, Ye Tian's strength increased significantly again, reaching the peak of the Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, very close to the Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
If he could condense a Saint Soul, he could definitely reach the combat power of a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Alternatively, if he cultivated a Saint Technique to the level of an Ancestral Technique, even if it was just the entry-level, he would definitely possess the combat power of a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
On this day.
Ye Tian used an... Invincible Domain Ancestor-level Saint Soul Beast to practice his Gold Silk Technique. He didn't kill this beast, which was only an Eight-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, but only sparred with it.
Puchi puchi puchi!!!! Strands of gold silk sliced at the Saint Soul Beast, while the beast continuously attacked Ye Tian, the two of them trading blows.
At the same time, Ye Tian kept his Gold Ancestral Talent active, sensing the power of the Ancestral Talent and the Gold Silk Technique.
At some point, Ye Tian seemed to fall into an epiphany, his consciousness extending into the source of the Saint Technique.
At this moment, he saw rivers of gold.
"Gold, sharpness, Ancestral Talent, origin, unity..."
In a sudden flash, Ye Tian seemed to grasp the essence of gold and finally understood a hint of the mysteries of the Gold Ancestral Talent.
It had to be said that the Gold Ancestral Talent was not just a talent; it was more like a guide, leading Ye Tian to comprehend deeper realms, second only to the effect of the Gold Ancestor Law.
However, in terms of comprehending Saint Techniques and Ancestral Techniques, the Gold Ancestral Talent far surpassed the so-called Ancestor Law.
Boom!!!!! The Gold Silk Technique transformed; it was no longer a Saint Technique.
Now, the Gold Silk Technique could be called the Gold Silk Ancestral Technique! "Gold Silk Ancestral Technique, Kill!"
A streak of golden light instantly flashed through the void, slicing the Invincible Domain Ancestor Saint Soul Beast in front of him into two pieces.
"As expected of an Ancestral Technique, the attack power is terrifying!"
Ye Tian was very satisfied.
Having comprehended the Gold Silk Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian's strength surged, reaching the combat power of a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor. As long as he continued to cultivate the Gold Silk Ancestral Technique to the minor completion stage, or obtained more Ancestral Techniques, his strength would likely reach the limit of a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, second only to the combat power of a Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
By then, he would likely be able to sweep through the Saint Soul Realm.
