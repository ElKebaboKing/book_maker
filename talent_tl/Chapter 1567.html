Chapter 1566: Endless Source Body!

Void Source Palace.
Ten Void Sons arrived here. At this time, Ye Tian discovered that among the other ten Void Sons, there were only five third-tier Invincibles, two second-tier Invincibles, one first-tier Invincible, and two Supreme Beings.
The ten Void Sons did not speak and were soon teleported by the artifact spirit of the Void Source Palace to the space where the Void Source Monument was located.
In a vast void space, a giant Void Source Monument stood.
Near the Void Source Monument, there were ten small spaces, each representing a seat.
Swish swish swish!!!
Ye Tian appeared in one small space, while the other Void Sons entered the other small spaces.
Upon entering this small space, Ye Tian clearly sensed its extraordinary nature. The endless Eternal Rules seemed to be presented right before him, as if he were inside the Void Source Monument itself, constantly sensing the Eternal Rules contained within it.
"Comprehend!"
Ye Tian immediately sat cross-legged and began to comprehend the Eternal Rules.
Comprehending the Void Source Monument once required 500 points. In other words, without any other point income, the points obtained just by becoming a Void Son were only enough to comprehend it twice.
Therefore, for him, any opportunity to comprehend the Void Source Monument was incredibly precious.
He even wanted to directly condense a Void Source Body through this single opportunity for comprehension.
For others, a single comprehension of the Void Source Monument naturally offered no chance of condensing a Void Source Body. But Ye Tian was different; he possessed a large number of Eternal Ancestral Talents. Comprehending for the duration of an entire Great Tribulation Era was already an incredibly long period for him.
Thus, he felt he had a great chance of directly condensing a Void Source Body.
Time slowly passed—
Time Eternal Rule, Space Eternal Rule, Gold Eternal Rule, Wood Eternal Rule, Water Eternal Rule...
Each Eternal Rule grew at an incredible speed, and his progress far surpassed that of the other Void Sons.
It had to be said that the combination of Eternal Ancestral Talents and the Void Source Monument was truly wonderful.
Furthermore, as his Eternal Rules advanced, and with the help of the Void Source Monument, Ye Tian's comprehension of the Invincible Tribulation Body grew deeper and deeper, gradually allowing him to see the flaws in the Wan Yuan Gui Xu.
Previously, he felt that the Wan Yuan Gui Xu was an impressive Invincible Tribulation Body, and he had perfected it step by step, making his physical body far stronger than that of other Invincibles.
But now, he felt that the Wan Yuan Gui Xu was relatively ordinary. If he spent some time, he could create multiple top-tier Invincible Tribulation Body techniques comparable to the Wan Yuan Gui Xu.
Long ages passed, equivalent to about half a Great Tribulation Era.
By this time, Ye Tian's attainment in various Eternal Rules had surpassed his previous level by countless times.
He decided to begin creating a Void Source Body technique.
In fact, even if every Void Son eventually created a Void Source Body, they would all be different from one another.
Some Void Sons' Void Source Bodies were relatively ordinary, while others were incredibly powerful.
Ye Tian intended to condense a top-tier Void Source Body, and he had the potential to do so.
After all, a Void Son like him who possessed a large number of Eternal Ancestral Talents was likely unique. Therefore, his single comprehension of the Void Source Monument was equivalent to others comprehending it countless times.
For example, his current understanding of Eternal Rules was superior even to those Supreme Beings.
If even he could not create a top-tier Void Source Body, the other Void Sons would likely have no hope at all.
One Dark Calendar year passed, and Ye Tian had initially created a Void Source Body technique.
Thus, he had his duplicate body in the outside world begin practicing this Void Source Body technique to identify its flaws.
This was also why Ye Tian had his main body comprehend the Void Source Monument while leaving his duplicate body outside.
Because he did not dare to experiment with his main body, he could only use the duplicate body for testing. If the duplicate body practiced here and failed, causing it to collapse or suffer major issues, wouldn't that waste this comprehension opportunity?
Soon, the results of the cultivation came out.
"As expected, there are flaws!"
Ye Tian murmured.
The initially created Void Source Body had roughly over ten thousand flaws. His current goal was to perfect and remedy these flaws, thereby making the Void Source Body flawless.
Another Dark Calendar year passed. By this time, Ye Tian had already remedied many flaws, with only about 100 flaws remaining. However, the subsequent flaws became increasingly difficult to fix.
Left with no choice, Ye Tian continued to comprehend the Void Source Monument while simultaneously making refinements.
As time passed, the remaining time grew shorter, yet many loopholes still remained in the Void Source Body.
Finally, on this day, the time for comprehending the Void Source Monument came to an end.
"The comprehension time for all Void Sons has ended!"
Buzz!
Ye Tian found himself being moved out by the artifact spirit of the Void Source Palace.
"What a pity!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Time was somewhat insufficient; he still had six flaws that had not been perfected and remedied.
Perhaps for other Void Sons, being able to create a Void Source Body would already be immensely satisfying, and they wouldn't care about a mere six flaws.
However, Ye Tian intended to continue perfecting it.
Although perfecting the Void Source Body without the help of the Void Source Monument would be difficult and require an immense amount of time, he could still persevere by using his duplicate bodies to conduct experiments over and over again.
Before leaving the Void Source Palace, he applied to comprehend the Void Source Monument again. However, since he had already comprehended it once, the time he needed to wait in line would be much longer; he was already scheduled for 126 Great Tribulation Eras later.
In other words, there were still 1,260 Void Sons ahead of him in the queue.
After coming out of the Void Source Palace, Ye Tian immediately entered secluded cultivation again, researching and perfecting the Void Source Body every day.
One Great Tribulation Era after another passed. Unknowingly, the human race had already occupied many foreign worlds, and countless ancestors had been born. Even Ye Tian's wife and children had successively entered the Tribulation Realm, each preparing to cross the physical body tribulation to become Invincibles.
With Ye Tian's assistance, their hope of becoming Invincibles was naturally very high.
Another dozen or so Great Tribulation Eras passed. Xiao Yue became a sixth-tier Invincible, and Ye Xingchen and Ye Xingyue also successively became sixth-tier Invincibles. However, their talent was limited, so they only became sixth-tier Invincibles by achieving the sixth-tier Tribulation Realm.
For them, this was already very satisfying.
In the sixty-eighth Great Tribulation Era after Ye Tian returned from the Void Source Palace, he finally perfected and remedied the last flaw of the Void Source Body.
He had successfully created the most flawless and perfect top-tier Void Source Body.
He named it the Endless Source Body!
