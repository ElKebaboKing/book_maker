Chapter 1504: The Great Calamity Has Arrived!

When the Wolf Bone Saint Beast and the Violent Ape Saint Beast mentioned the Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, Ye Tian asked curiously, "What exactly are Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, and what use do they have?"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast explained, "Origin Rule Calamity Crystals are treasures that are only born during a Great Calamity of Heaven and Earth. They are extremely rare and precious. They can help a top-tier Saint Beast comprehend the rules of a world much faster. Otherwise, we could only stay in a foreign world for a very short time. How could we possibly create Saint Arts that are compatible with a foreign world? There simply wouldn't be enough time. Only by obtaining an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal is there hope of becoming an Ultimate Saint Beast..."
At this point, he cast a curious glance at the Heyuan Saint Beast, showing a look of wonder, and the Violent Ape Saint Beast looked curious as well.
If Ye Tian didn't know about the Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, how did he break through to become an Ultimate Saint Beast?
Previously, the two of them had assumed that Ye Tian had been lucky enough to obtain an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal during the last Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain, and had spent those long ages secretly comprehending the rules of the Dark Forbidden Domain to create compatible Saint Arts, thus allowing him to break through to the Ultimate Saint Beast realm.
But looking at it now, that didn't seem to be the case.
For a moment, they were extremely curious.
Ye Tian also knew that Wolf Bone and the others were suspicious, but he didn't care.
This clone of his was also at the Ultimate Saint Beast realm; he didn't fear Wolf Bone and the others, and they wouldn't make a move against him because of this.
At this moment.
Ye Tian finally understood how foreign experts achieved their breakthroughs. Foreign experts didn't have special duplicated bodies like he did, which allowed him to infiltrate foreign lands and constantly comprehend their rules.
They could only infiltrate a foreign world to comprehend its rules during a Great Calamity, but the duration of a calamity wouldn't be very long. In such a short time, no matter how monstrous one's talent was, it was impossible to fully comprehend foreign rules!
And once they left the foreign world and returned to their own, there would be no hope of creating Saint Arts or Progenitor Arts compatible with that foreign world.
However, if one obtained an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal, they could continue to comprehend the rules of that foreign world even after leaving it.
"So you're saying that if one obtains a foreign Origin Rule Calamity Crystal, even without entering that foreign world, there's hope of becoming an Ultimate Saint Beast?"
Ye Tian asked.
"Yes!"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast nodded.
At this point, Ye Tian felt a surge of interest in the foreign Origin Rule Calamity Crystals. If he could obtain one, he could bring it back to the Human Race for his relatives to study. Once they fully comprehended it, they could step into the Ultimate Progenitor realm.
"The foreign Origin Rule Calamity Crystals must be useful to us Ultimate Saint Beasts as well, right?"
Ye Tian asked again.
"Of course they are!" The Wolf Bone Saint Beast said with a smile. "Our goal is to become second-tier Ultimate Saint Beasts, so we must create Ultimate Saint Techniques compatible with the foreign rules, and then fuse two Ultimate Saint Techniques to break the shackles and comprehend a Paramount Saint Technique. Therefore, the foreign Origin Rule Calamity Crystals are still of immense help to us. Furthermore, they can greatly increase our cultivation. As long as we don't completely refine the crystal, it can slowly recover. Thus, a single foreign Origin Rule Calamity Crystal is enough to make even Ultimate Saint Beasts fight to the death. Normally, the number of crystals born during a calamity is uncertain; there might only be a few, or there might be dozens. There is evidence suggesting that the more slaughter there is, the more crystals will eventually be born."
"The more slaughter, the more Origin Rule Calamity Crystals?" Ye Tian frowned, realizing what this implied. "So, is this why those foreign forces slaughter so frenziedly and destroy everything during every calamity?"
"Yes!"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast nodded and then said, "Aside from our three Great Saint Sects, other forces simply cannot survive. After the calamity, I'm afraid only the territories of our three Great Holy Lands will still have living beings; other regions will likely be nearly annihilated. Only after the calamity passes will those regions slowly give birth to new life and worlds."
Ye Tian now understood completely.
In the Dark Forbidden Domain, various Dark Holy Lands were born—the Dark Destruction Domain, the Dark Saint Domain, the Dark Soul Domain... these Dark Holy Lands emerged, one for every Dark Era, with nine Dark Holy Lands born over nine Dark Eras.
But why were the Dark Holy Lands from the previous three calamities gone, with not even a trace of ruins or the worlds from before the calamities remaining?
Now, he knew. The previous Dark Holy Lands and their many worlds had all been wiped out by foreign experts.
After the calamity, the Dark Forbidden Domain became an empty world, waiting to be nurtured anew.
The Holy Region could at least preserve the three Great Saint Sects, but the Dark Forbidden Domain previously had no Ultimate Progenitors and thus couldn't protect any location. Those top-tier Progenitors were all busy trying to hide; why would they bother protecting those Dark Holy Lands!
"No wonder those ancient Progenitors aren't keen on occupying Dark Holy Lands or developing their forces. It's all in vain; even if they develop them, they'll just be wiped out by foreign experts."
Ye Tian realized suddenly.
However, this time was different. With him guarding the Dark Forbidden Domain, he could at least protect the Human Race's territory.
Ye Tian chatted with the two Ultimate Saint Beasts for a long time.
Soon, the Great Calamity of the Holy Region arrived.
Buzz buzz buzz!!!
The rules of the entire Holy Region underwent a subtle change. The world became filled with the aura of slaughter, and it no longer suppressed foreign experts.
One by one, figures emerged from the various forbidden lands across the Holy Region. The weakest among them were comparable to ordinary Progenitors.
As for those below the Progenitor level, there was no point in them coming. They couldn't even cross the distance of a single world, and they were too weak to even qualify as cannon fodder.
At the same time.
In the areas surrounding the Sea-Sky Saint Sect, countless experts gathered, seeking the protection of the sect. As for the weak, there was no way to look after them.
Countless experts traversed the various regions of the Holy Region, and endless living beings were turned to ash in an instant.
Countless resources were seized by them, becoming the energy for the foreign experts to grow stronger.
In the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian established contact with his clone, the Heyuan Saint Beast. Knowing that the Great Calamity of the Holy Region had arrived, he split off a duplicated body.
However, it took some time to condense a duplicated body at the Ultimate Progenitor level, and it required a significant amount of energy—clearly a limitation.
Before long, this duplicated body was condensed, possessing the same combat power as the original body.
"Go!"
The duplicated body crossed over to the Sea-Sky Forbidden Land and easily slaughtered all the exotic beasts within.
He intended to pass through the gate, enter the Holy Region, and take the opportunity to occupy the Sea-Sky Saint Sect.
