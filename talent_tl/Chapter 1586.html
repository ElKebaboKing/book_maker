Chapter 1585: Ancient Star Domain!

Obtaining three portions of Thunder Source Liquid at once was equivalent to three portions of eternal resources. If other first-class Invincible ones knew about this, they would definitely be incredibly envious.
These three portions of Thunder Source Liquid alone were enough to make many first-class Invincible ones fight to the death.
Of course, for Ye Tian, three portions of Thunder Source Liquid were still not enough; he was still short by six portions of eternal resources.
"There might be other eternal resources in Longze, but I've already angered the Ancient Thunder Source Dragon. It's better not to go to Longze anymore!" Ye Tian murmured.
Immediately, he began to select one from the other forbidden lands. After choosing for a long time, Ye Tian finally made his decision.
"I'll go to the Ancient Star Domain!" Ye Tian decided.
The Ancient Star Domain was a special forbidden land with a great reputation throughout the Source Sea; it was also a confined forbidden land.
The living beings of the Ancient Star Domain could not leave, meaning they were bound, but a powerful race lived within it—the Ancient Star Race!
The Ancient Star Race had unique talent in physical cultivation, though their soul talent was somewhat lacking.
But even so, the Ancient Star Race still had many Supreme Beings.
Despite being bound within the Ancient Star Domain, the Ancient Star Race was still able to produce Supreme Beings, which showed that the Ancient Star Domain definitely contained many eternal resources.
Indeed, many Invincible ones had broken into the Ancient Star Domain and snatched eternal resources, but even more Invincible ones had fallen within it.
Based on Ye Tian's understanding of the Ancient Star Domain, the Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race were not very powerful. After all, being bound to a forbidden land meant they lacked many resources. Being able to cultivate to the Supreme Being realm was already quite good, and it was difficult to advance further within that realm.
If Supreme Beings from the outside world could enter the Ancient Star Domain, they could naturally sweep through it. Unfortunately, Supreme Beings could not enter, and even existences like the Void Source Ancestor could not enter the Ancient Star Domain.
When Ye Tian told the artifact spirit that he wanted to enter the Ancient Star Domain, the artifact spirit cautioned, "Master, the Ancient Star Domain is very mysterious. The previous master studied it for a while and even sent people there, gaining some harvests. The Ancient Star Domain likely has mysterious restrictions placed by an even more ancient and powerful existence, which disallows Supreme Beings from entering. Perhaps that person has already surpassed the Supreme Being realm. It is certain that there are inheritances within the Ancient Star Domain, but only the Ancient Star Race is allowed to enter. It is precisely because of those inheritances that the Ancient Star Race is so powerful. Otherwise, how could a mere forbidden land of such a small scale give birth to Supreme Beings? Master, you have special clones and do not fear falling. You can try to spy on the Ancient Star Race's inheritances when the time comes; perhaps you can obtain a great opportunity!"
"We'll see," Ye Tian said indifferently.
-------------------
Ancient Star Domain.
It was like a transparent world surrounded by the Source Sea. Any Invincible one could enter the Ancient Star Domain from various locations, provided they could break through its barrier. Without the strength of a second-class Invincible one, it was impossible to break this barrier. As for coming out, even a second-class Invincible one might not be enough; only a first-class Invincible one was safe.
Therefore, only the top second-class Invincible ones and first-class Invincible ones dared to enter the Ancient Star Domain.
Ye Tian's duplicated body arrived here, chose a location at random, and immediately struck the barrier of the Ancient Star Domain.
With a tearing sound, the barrier of the Ancient Star Domain was ripped open, and Ye Tian instantly stepped inside.
Next, Ye Tian activated a secret treasure and departed at extreme speed, piercing through the void to distance himself from this area of the Ancient Star Domain. Otherwise, once the experts of the Ancient Star Race arrived, he would be besieged by them.
Meanwhile, inside a great hall of the Ancient Star Race, a ten-zhang-tall member of the Ancient Star Race opened his eyes. His gaze seemed to pierce through layers of void to look at a certain place outside.
"Another outsider has entered. These outsiders are truly too greedy!!!" this member of the Ancient Star Race said angrily.
He was an ancestor of the Ancient Star Race, which meant he was at the Supreme Being level.
*Whoosh!* He quickly rushed to the place where Ye Tian had entered, but when he arrived, he could no longer see any sign of Ye Tian.
This situation was normal; it was like this every time.
However, once an outsider's tracks were exposed and they were tracked down by the experts of the Ancient Star Race, they would be surrounded and killed immediately.
……
"The environment of the Ancient Star Domain is somewhat similar to a starry sky environment. There are actually stars here!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, these stars were no ordinary stars. Each star was incredibly massive and contained terrifying power. Moreover, the minerals and resources on each star were very abundant, with a value no less than that of a barren exotic world.
Ye Tian sensed the rules of the Ancient Star Domain, wanting to know why so many eternal resources were born here, but no matter how he investigated, he gained nothing.
No matter how he looked at it, this place seemed very average, almost ordinary!
"Almost all eternal resources are controlled by the Ancient Star Race. If I want to snatch eternal resources, I can only take them from the hands of the Ancient Star Race!" Ye Tian thought.
It was very difficult to snatch eternal resources from the Ancient Star Race. So why did so many first-class Invincible ones still want to enter the Ancient Star Domain to snatch them? Were they looking for death?
No, it was because the rewards were too great.
Once successful, the snatched eternal resources would not be just one or two portions, but many.
Most eternal resources were indeed kept in the ancestral land of the Ancient Star Race, but the Ancient Star Race was similar to outside forces and was not a monolith. There was also competition here, divided into various power organizations.
Therefore, some small forces also possessed eternal resources. If one wiped out a small force, it was possible to obtain some eternal resources.
Similarly, Ye Tian also wanted to find a small force of the Ancient Star Race to wipe them out and obtain their eternal resources.
Soon, Ye Tian descended near a star and used his Copying Talent to investigate the situation on this star.
*Buzz!* The Copying Talent enveloped the entire star, and the talent situations of countless Ancient Star Race members were detected by Ye Tian.
"Eh?" Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
He actually discovered that many members of the Ancient Star Race possessed an ultimate ancestral talent called the Ancient Star Body. This was clearly a physical body talent, and it was likely the reason why the Ancient Star Race was so outstanding in physical cultivation.
Ye Tian immediately copied these Ancient Star Body talents. If he absorbed enough of them, he could fuse them into an eternal ancestral talent.
Ye Tian certainly did not lack eternal ancestral talents, but that did not mean he didn't need more. Perhaps fusing an Ancient Star Body at the eternal ancestral talent level would bring unexpected benefits.
Immediately, Ye Tian was in no hurry to find eternal resources; he would copy these Ancient Star Body talents first.
He had made his plan: he would copy and fuse a total of two Ancient Star Body eternal ancestral talents—one for his original body and one for his duplicated body.
He prepared to duplicate the body of an Ancient Star Race member and bestow the Ancient Star Body eternal ancestral talent upon it.
Of course, this was a preparation for later, intended for spying on the secrets of the Ancient Star Race.
