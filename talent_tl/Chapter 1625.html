Chapter 1624 The Primordial Tree Heart That Can Be Refined Into a Clone!

“I know another use for the Primordial Tree!”
True River Sword Ancestor said.>
As soon as his words fell, Ye Tian and the other Supreme Beings’ gazes were drawn to True River Sword Ancestor, waiting for his explanation.>
True River Sword Ancestor said: “The Primordial Leaf can bring people into the Primordial Sea, but in fact the Primordial Leaf was born from the Primordial Tree, and the Primordial Tree was born from the Primordial Sea. As for how the Primordial Leaf was brought out of the Primordial Sea in the first place, let’s temporarily assume it was brought out by the Eternal Realm and not dwell on that for now. However, some Primordial Trees have also been discovered, and after many fights and snatchings, our Source God Pavilion once obtained a Primordial Tree. Through our research, we discovered that the Primordial Tree heart can be refined into a special clone, and it is even useful to those at the half-step Eternal Realm. In other words, if one obtains a Primordial Tree heart, it can be refined into a half-step Eternal Realm clone. Moreover, if the Primordial Tree heart exists within the body, then even if the clone perishes, it can be condensed again at the cost of some price. For Supreme Beings or half-step Eternal Realm experts without the ability to create clones, the Primordial Tree heart is extremely valuable. But as far as I know, the Primordial Tree heart is useless to those in the Eternal Realm; otherwise, all the Primordial Trees would have long since been taken away by the Eternal Realm and would not have been left for us.”
“Condense a clone!”
Ye Tian was shocked.>
This ability was incredibly powerful. For Supreme Beings without the ability to create clones, this was simply an invaluable treasure.>
Although he possessed body duplication and did not need a Primordial Tree heart, his family did!>
They did not have the ability to create clones and did not dare to venture out rashly; otherwise, if they accidentally perished, they would truly be gone. But if they had a Primordial Tree heart and could refine clones, then there would be nothing to fear.>
Having learned of this use, Ye Tian felt delighted inside, but he did not show it.>
Afterward, he remained silent.>
Later, everyone exchanged intelligence, and the Twelve Ancestors also announced the opening date of a blessed land. However, the value of this blessed land was not high; it was suitable for Seventh or Eighth Supreme Beings to enter, but the gains would not be very great.>
Ye Tian had little interest in such a blessed land, so he did not plan to participate.>
Very quickly.>
The meeting ended, and the Supreme Beings all left one after another.>
When all the inner-circle members had left, the Twelve Ancestors began discussing again.>
“The Nirvana Origin Ancestor suddenly asked about the Primordial Tree. Do you think he obtained a Primordial Tree?”
Fenggu Source Ancestor asked.>
True River Sword Ancestor said, “There is a more than half chance that he found a Primordial Tree in the Primordial Sea and brought it out. If that is really the case...”
“True River Sword Ancestor, do not entertain such an idea. The Nirvana Origin Ancestor possesses the ability to create clones, and as for his potential, you are very clear on that as well. In the future, he may even have hope of surpassing us. Even if he cannot enter the Eternal Realm, his combat power is too powerful, and he may even become invincible at the half-step Eternal Realm. Such a person cannot be made an enemy.” One of the Twelve Ancestors, Black-White Source Ancestor, reminded him.>
“Hahahaha!!” True River Sword Ancestor laughed a few times and said, “You are all underestimating me, aren’t you? I would not snatch the Nirvana Origin Ancestor’s Primordial Tree. Indeed, I do not have the ability to create clones, but at my level, I am all striving to break through to the Eternal Realm, and a clone merely gives me one more life-saving ability. It is not of much use to cultivation. Moreover, once one reaches the Eternal Realm, the Primordial Tree has no real use. Even the Eternal Realm looks down on it, so my vision is not that low. If it were a treasure that could allow even those in the Eternal Realm to condense clones, then I would be tempted.”
“However, although we would not be tempted, there is no guarantee that other Supreme Beings or half-step Eternal Realm experts would not be. Perhaps they would not act personally, but they could invite other experts to take action. Although the Nirvana Origin Ancestor is very powerful, his cultivation has only just broken through to the Seventh Supreme Being. At most, his combat power can rival an ordinary Ninth Supreme Being. If multiple top Ninth Supreme Beings or a half-step Eternal Realm expert were to be deployed, the Nirvana Origin Ancestor would be in trouble.”
“Then should we help him?”
“I think that even if other Supreme Beings or half-step Eternal Realm experts make a move, they would not try to kill the Nirvana Origin Ancestor, because the Nirvana Origin Ancestor has the ability to create clones. No one knows where his true body is hidden, and he is almost impossible to kill. But the Nirvana Origin Ancestor’s identity has already been investigated clearly: he is a Supreme Being from the newly born Eternal Heaven’s Dark Forbidden Domain. If the enemy uses the Dark Forbidden Domain as leverage and threatens the Nirvana Origin Ancestor, he might hand over the Primordial Tree.”
“Yes, as long as it is not a matter of life and death, we should not interfere; otherwise, wouldn’t we become their hired thugs?”
After such a discussion, the Twelve Ancestors felt that the Nirvana Origin Ancestor really might not be able to keep the Primordial Tree.>
Of course, there was another possibility: the Nirvana Origin Ancestor might not have a Primordial Tree at all, and they were simply overthinking it.>
