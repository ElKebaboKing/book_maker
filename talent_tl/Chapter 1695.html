Chapter 1694: All Six Bodies Join the Battle!

Dark Mist Domain.
This was the Eternal Time-Space Domain of Dark Mist Lord, and one of the most top-tier Eternal Time-Space Domains. There were quite a few Eternal Realm lords here, as well as countless Half-Step Eternal Realm experts. It was also regarded by other Eternal Realm experts as one of the safest places in the time-space domains.
After all, with one of the six legendary lords, Dark Mist Lord, personally overseeing it, who would dare invade this place?
Even during the two wars between the time-space domains and the Void Beasts, Dark Mist Domain was never affected.
But today, Dark Mist Domain was invaded.
Rumble!!!!
The time-space barrier of Dark Mist Domain was torn apart, and an intent-slash void-breaking sword composed of will and innate Eternal rules crossed the void and struck the core area of Dark Mist Domain, which was also its headquarters, the cultivation ground of Dark Mist Lord.
Dark Mist Lord’s true body was not stationed in Dark Mist Domain. In fact, he was still cultivating in a ruin.
And his two clones had already gone to the Source Sea, which meant Dark Mist Domain simply could not withstand Ye Tian.
Crack!
In Dark Mist Domain, the divine mountain where Dark Mist Lord cultivated was shattered by a single strike, and the subordinates surrounding it all suffered the erosion of the intent-slash void-breaking sword.
Waves of sword light of will swept across the bodies of one Half-Step Eternal Realm expert after another. Even Seven-Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm experts or Eight-Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm experts could not withstand even a trace of that sword light of will, and each of them turned to dust.
“No!”
A Peak Immortal Realm lord revealed a look of despair and did everything he could to resist the waves of sword light of will. This was only the aftershock of the intent-slash void-breaking sword’s attack, yet he still could not withstand it.
The Peak Immortal Realm lord’s body was blasted into countless fragments by the sword light of will, his vitality completely dissipated, and his will was Annihilated. He had already fallen and died.
Not only this Peak Immortal Realm lord, but many other Immortal Realm lords also fell and died one after another.
Perhaps they had clones, but the one left here was definitely the true body. In their eyes, Dark Mist Domain was the safest place, and if the true body stayed here, it naturally would not fall. But who could have imagined that Dark Mist Domain would be invaded?
These Immortal Realm lords could not withstand Ye Tian’s sword light of will, but Dark Mist Lord’s subordinates also had two Immortal Realm lords.
These two Immortal Realm lords were cultivated by Dark Mist Lord, so their true bodies were also cultivating in Dark Mist Domain, while their clones managed their own time-space domains.
At this moment, they too had suffered the attack of the intent-slash void-breaking sword.
The Two-Step Immortal Realm lord was directly severely injured and on the verge of death, while the Seven-Step Immortal Realm lord was also heavily injured.
However, Ye Tian’s attack just now had not been aimed at them. If it had been, they would not have been able to withstand it.
“Quick, notify Lord Dark Mist!”
The two Immortal Realm lords immediately sent a message to Dark Mist Lord.
Then they tried to persuade Ye Tian.
“Lord Endless, you’ve invaded Dark Mist Domain. Aren’t you afraid of Lord Dark Mist’s fury? Leave at once, and you still have a chance to live,” the Two-Step Immortal Realm lord urged.
Void Step!
Ye Tian stepped in front of the Two-Step Immortal Realm lord and struck out with the Intent Extreme Punch.
Boom!
The Two-Step Immortal Realm lord was directly blasted apart by one punch, and the Intent Extreme Punch was an intent legendary Eternal technique. This Two-Step Immortal Realm lord did not know any intent defense Eternal techniques at all, and his will was not very strong either, so Ye Tian directly Annihilated his will.
Thus, this Two-Step Immortal Realm lord fell.
In the True Void Legend Land.
Inside the Eternal treasure time-space projection palace where the aura of sword dao was being comprehended, a number of Immortal Realm lords and the legendary lord Ice-Tough Lord were cultivating and comprehending the aura of sword dao there.
Suddenly, the body of one Immortal Realm lord collapsed and vanished between heaven and earth.
This scene immediately drew the attention of the other Immortal Realm lords, and even Ice-Tough Lord looked over.
“Why did Yuze Lord’s body suddenly collapse?”
“That’s Yuze Lord’s clone. It should be Yuze Lord himself dissipating his own clone, right?”
“Dissipating his own body? How is that possible? The cost of re-condensing a clone is enormous. How could Yuze Lord be so foolish as to dissipate his own body?”
“Yuze Lord is a subordinate lord of Dark Mist Lord. His true body should be in Dark Mist Domain. Could it be that Yuze Lord can actually fall?”
“I’ve received news that Dark Mist Domain has been invaded.”
“Who invaded Dark Mist Domain? Void Beasts?”
“Not Void Beasts, it’s Lord Endless!”
“What? Lord Endless? How dare he invade Dark Mist Domain? Lord Endless is only an Immortal Realm lord. Isn’t he afraid of being killed by Dark Mist Lord?”
“Come, let’s go take a look. Something huge must have happened!”
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
One Immortal Realm lord after another went to watch the commotion, and one of Ice-Tough Lord’s clones also headed to Dark Mist Domain. She was also extremely curious about this. .........
……
Dark Mist Domain.
The Seven-Step Immortal Realm lord was already critically injured and on the verge of death. He wanted to escape, but simply could not. The difference in strength between the two sides was too great.
“A Thousandfold Void!”
Ye Tian used this move.
Boom!
Layers upon layers of void compressed in, and the Seven-Step Immortal Realm lord turned into a gigantic body of hundreds of millions of zhang to resist the suppression of the Thousandfold Void, but in an instant he was crushed into dust, and even the Thousandfold Void itself turned into countless fragments.
The Seven-Step Immortal Realm lord had also fallen.
And all of this happened in a very short time. The speed was too fast, and even Dark Mist Lord had no time to rescue him.
Just after this Seven-Step Immortal Realm lord fell, Dark Mist Lord’s true body rushed back from a ruin somewhere.
“Lord Endless, you’ve gone too far!!!!”
Dark Mist Lord said angrily.
“Too far? I never provoked you, yet you wanted to kill me, and you even wanted to target my Dark Forbidden Domain. Since you dared to do that, then you must bear my fury!” Ye Tian said coldly.
Without another word, the two immediately began slaughtering each other in the Void Region.
Rumble rumble rumble!!!!!
The aftermath of their battle even affected Dark Mist Domain. Dark Mist Lord tried to move farther away from Dark Mist Domain and lure Ye Tian to a more distant place to fight. Ye Tian did not care about that.
His goal was not to destroy Dark Mist Domain. Having already killed so many Eternal Realm experts in Dark Mist Domain, that was enough. In fact, he now even wanted to kill Dark Mist Lord.
Of course, he knew that would be very difficult, but he could try.
The Ye Tian who came here had two copied bodies, so he was fighting two against one and had a slight advantage.
Unfortunately, there was only one Yuanmeng Token, so it could not be equipped on either copied body.
What Ye Tian wanted to do now was first eliminate one of Dark Mist Lord’s clones. Then he would have the hands free to deal with Dark Mist Domain’s true body and the other clone.
Very quickly.
Ye Tian’s true body also made a move. In the Source Sea, he joined the two copied bodies in attacking one of Dark Mist Lord’s clones.
This was three against one, and with the help of the Yuanmeng Token, it completely crushed this clone of Dark Mist Lord.
