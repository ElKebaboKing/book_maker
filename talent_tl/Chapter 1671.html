Chapter 1670: Breaking Through the Second Boundary Barrier!

The materials of the Void Beast could enhance willpower, could also be used to refine Void Saint Weapons, and could even merge willpower and the body. However, he was still only at the half-step Eternal Realm, so the fusion of willpower and body was still very far away from him.
Refining Void Saint Weapons was not very helpful to him either, so he only wanted to use the Void Beast materials to enhance his willpower.
Among the secrets revealed by the six great chairmen, there was naturally a specific method for tempering and increasing willpower. First, one needed to extract Void Essence from the Void Beast materials, then merge willpower into the Void Essence. The power within the Void Essence would then merge into the willpower, effectively tempering and enhancing it.
Then.
Ye Tian began refining the Void Beast materials, extracting drop after drop of Void Essence.
Then his true body used the Void Essence to temper and enhance his own willpower. As time passed, his willpower grew stronger and stronger, and the world of will was perfected time and again by him, so the number of condensed worlds of will naturally kept increasing.
Unknowingly, he had already condensed 9,000 worlds of will, and there was still plenty of Void Essence left.
At this rate, he had hope of condensing 10,000 worlds of will, thereby allowing his Eternal Will to undergo a transformation to the third stage.
9,191 worlds of will!
9,200 worlds of will!
9,300 worlds of will!
……
In the blink of an eye, several Eternal Years passed. After countless attempts, after his countless cloned bodies shattered, and after step by step perfecting the secret art of the world of will, Ye Tian finally succeeded in condensing 9,999 worlds of will.
At this moment, he only needed one more world of will to condense, and his Eternal Will would transform, reaching the third stage.
After more attempts, the 10,000th world of will was finally opened up by Ye Tian.
Boom!!!!!
The ten thousand worlds of will within his body interconnected, triggering a tremendous qualitative change.
His will was transforming, rising, allowing Ye Tian’s will to undergo a huge leap and transformation.
A long while later, the transformation was complete.
By now, Ye Tian’s will was more than ten times stronger than before, and his control over his body far surpassed what it had been before, enough to unleash strength far beyond his previous level.
In the dark, Ye Tian had already touched the second boundary barrier.
But if he truly fought, he would definitely be able to defeat Lord Gu He, because his will had reached the third stage, and his will attacks were enough to make a ninth extreme realm being like Lord Gu He unable to fully display his strength at all.
After touching the second boundary barrier, Ye Tian’s goal was to break through to the Nine Extreme Realm.
At present, his half-step Eternal power could still continue to be compressed, and if he created a Legendary Eternal Technique, his strength would surge again, so there was a great possibility of breaking through the second boundary barrier.
By now, he had already traded for many Eternal compression secret techniques from other eighth extreme realm beings. With his innate comprehension and repeated experiments, he had already perfected the stacking secret technique to the limit of being able to compress up to eighty times.
Of course, this was the theoretical limit, not something that could truly be achieved.
But Ye Tian still had a great chance of compressing his half-step Eternal power to forty times. With the Legendary Eternal Technique as support, he did not believe he would be unable to break the second boundary barrier.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian compressed his half-step Eternal power while also creating a Legendary Eternal Technique.
The first Legendary Eternal Technique he wanted to create was naturally related to the Voidification legendary ancestral gift, so that it would greatly enhance his ability to preserve his life.
Since only in the Source Sea was there hope of breaking through to the Nine Extreme Realm, Ye Tian left only one cloned body in the Eight Extreme Tower of the Time-Space Territory, while all the other cloned bodies were in the Dark Forbidden Zone of the Source Sea, helping his true body raise its strength together.
One Eternal Year after another passed, and Ye Tian’s half-step Eternal power grew more and more condensed through compression.
Moreover, on this day, Ye Tian’s Legendary Eternal Technique was finally created.
This time, the Legendary Eternal Technique Ye Tian created was called Void True Body. Once activated, the body would enter a state of voidification, and it would be even more thoroughly voidified than an ordinary Void Beast, as if it did not exist within the rules and had completely turned into void.
It could be said that once Void True Body was used, even a Immortal Realm lord would not be able to harm him in the slightest.
However, an Undying Realm lord could still injure him, though killing him would be somewhat difficult, unless one of the most top-tier Undying Realm lords made a move.
After creating Void True Body, Ye Tian clearly felt the second boundary barrier tremble a little, but in the end, it still did not break.
Clearly, he needed to continue increasing his strength.
Thus, Ye Tian began creating a Legendary Eternal Technique related to the power legendary ancestral gift.
After ten Eternal Years, Ye Tian finally created his second Legendary Eternal Technique—Eternal Supreme Fist!
This was an Eternal Technique that brought the power to its extreme, ranked among the Legendary Eternal Techniques, and could fully unleash Ye Tian’s strength.
Compared with Void True Body, Eternal Supreme Fist was the one that could greatly increase his strength.
After he created Eternal Supreme Fist, cracks appeared in the second boundary barrier, as if increasing his strength a bit more would allow him to completely break this boundary barrier.
“Breaking the second boundary barrier is indeed too difficult. I’ve already raised my Eternal Will to the third stage and created two Legendary Eternal Techniques, yet I still haven’t broken the second boundary barrier. No wonder so many eighth extreme realm beings have no way to break it.”
Ye Tian muttered to himself.
There was no choice; he could only continue creating Legendary Eternal Techniques.
This time, he planned to create a defensive Legendary Eternal Technique. Combined with Void True Body, his ability to preserve his life would become even stronger, and naturally his combat power would also become stronger.
About ten more Eternal Years later, a defensive Legendary Eternal Technique was finally created by Ye Tian, and he named it Eternal Wall!
Eternal Wall was equivalent to forming an invisible defensive barrier all around. It could not be seen by the naked eye, but its defensive power was unbelievably terrifying. It could not only defend against ordinary attacks, but even will attacks could be blocked.
After this Legendary Eternal Technique, Eternal Wall, was created, the cracks in the second boundary barrier became more and more numerous.
“I won’t create any more Legendary Eternal Techniques for now. First, I’ll compress my half-step Eternal power to 40 times, then create a speed-type Legendary Eternal Technique. There’s definitely hope of breaking the shackle in one go and advancing to the Nine Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm!”
Ye Tian planned.
Just like that, in the Eternal Years that followed, he continuously worked to compress his half-step Eternal power, making it increasingly close to true Eternal power.
Ten Eternal Years!
Twenty Eternal Years!
Thirty Eternal Years!
Unknowingly, a hundred Eternal Years passed.
At this point, Ye Tian’s half-step Eternal power had finally been compressed to 40 times, and the second boundary barrier had nearly been completely shattered.
