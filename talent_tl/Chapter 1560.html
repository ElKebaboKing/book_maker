Chapter 1559: Tianfeng Sea and Netherworld Hall Have Gone to War!

Ye Tian felt an endless amount of source energy pouring into his body from the Source Sea, a scene somewhat similar to when Tianfeng Exalt broke through to become a Supreme Being.
However, compared to the commotion of Tianfeng Exalt's breakthrough, his was merely a small affair.
Soon, the breakthrough was complete.
At this moment, Ye Tian was truly a 3rd-grade Invincible, and his life level had undergone a transformation.
"I should be considered a Source Sea creature now!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
At the same time, Ye Tian discovered that he was being suppressed by the Yuhua World to the point where he could only exert extremely weak strength.
This was a situation that all 3rd-grade Invincibles would experience.
3rd-grade Invincibles were considered Source Sea creatures; even if they were born in a foreign world, it was very difficult for them to return to their own foreign world. This was the suppression and rejection of powerful life forms by the foreign worlds.
However, as a 3rd-grade Invincible, Ye Tian could still stay in the Dark Forbidden Domain and the Yuhua World, though it was just difficult to exert much of his strength.
But once he promoted to a 2nd-grade Invincible, he would have no way to stay in a foreign world.
Of course, for Ye Tian, this was not much of a problem.
He could completely have his Invincible clone garrison the Yuhua World, as long as he did not let the Invincible clone advance to the 2nd-grade Invincible realm.
...
Time passed.
Ye Tian gradually stabilized his 3rd-grade Invincible realm and created several more Invincible Ancestral Skills, making his strength even more powerful.
Additionally, he began the cultivation of the fourth stage of Myriad Source Return to Void, perfecting the fourth stage step by step while refining various resources to increase his cultivation base.
One day.
Ye Tian began to create a space-type Invincible Ancestral Skill. After all, he had already obtained the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent. If he did not create a space-type Invincible Ancestral Skill, would that not be a waste of this Eternal Ancestral Talent?
After spending some time, Ye Tian finally succeeded in creating it.
Ye Tian named this space-type Invincible Ancestral Skill "Void Shatter." It was an offensive space-type Invincible Ancestral Skill and was currently the most powerful offensive Invincible Ancestral Skill in Ye Tian's hands.
Now, Ye Tian's next cultivation goal was to promote to a 2nd-grade Invincible.
To become a 2nd-grade Invincible, one's physical body must feed back into the soul, and then the soul must be cultivated through the Soul Tribulation Realm to reach the Invincible realm, condensing a Soul Invincible Tribulation Body. When the Soul Invincible Tribulation Body reached the same level as the Physical Invincible Tribulation Body, one would reach the 2nd-grade Invincible realm.
Ye Tian had consumed the Xumi Golden Fruit, and his soul had transformed to a level comparable to a Soul Invincible, but it was only equivalent to a 6th-grade Invincible.
However, he was not yet considered a true Soul Invincible Tribulation Body because his soul had not crossed the tribulation.
Ye Tian was currently in no hurry to cross the tribulation. His physical body had not yet reached the Peak Extreme of the 3rd-grade Invincible realm, and there was still a lot of room for improvement. To rush for the 2nd-grade Invincible realm now would undoubtedly be a waste of potential.
Thus, he resisted the temptation of quickly promoting to a 2nd-grade Invincible and settled down to cultivate Myriad Source Return to Void, seeking to raise Myriad Source Return to Void to the Peak stage of the fourth stage as soon as possible.
----------------
"The Tianfeng Sea is summoning me!"
Ye Tian received a transmission from the Tianfeng Sea branch, requesting him to immediately head to the Tianfeng Sea headquarters in the Tianlin Realm.
Immediately, Ye Tian rushed to the Tianlin Realm.
Soon, Ye Tian arrived at the Tianfeng Sea headquarters.
Here, Ye Tian saw many Invincibles. Densely packed Invincibles stood on a mountain peak, waiting for instructions from the high-level members of the Tianfeng Sea.
When the number of Invincibles reached nearly a million, Ye Tian truly witnessed the power of an old Sacred Name grade power, or rather, a newly promoted Eternal Name grade power. And this was likely still just the tip of the iceberg for the Tianfeng Sea.
Boom!!!!
Qingyang Exalt descended, his gaze sweeping over all the Invincibles.
"I am Qingyang Exalt of the Tianfeng Sea. I have summoned you all here today to announce one thing: from this day forward, the Tianfeng Sea officially declares war on the Netherworld Hall!"
Qingyang Exalt announced.
Upon hearing this news, the many Invincibles were instantly shocked.
The Tianfeng Sea had actually declared war on the Netherworld Hall.
Previously, they had all heard about the conflict between Tianfeng Exalt and the Netherworld Hall, but they thought it had passed. After all, the Tianfeng Sea had made no moves over these years.
But now, the real move had come.
In reality, during these years, Tianfeng Exalt had been in closed-door cultivation to stabilize his realm. Now that his realm was stable, he had come to find trouble with the Netherworld Hall.
Qingyang Exalt continued, "From today onwards, the 6th-grade to 3rd-grade Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea will carry out retaliation against the various branches of the Netherworld Hall outside the Tianlin Realm. Killing a 6th-grade Invincible of the Netherworld Hall rewards 100,000 contribution points; killing a 5th-grade Invincible rewards 5 million contribution points; killing a 4th-grade Invincible rewards 100 million contribution points; killing a 3rd-grade Invincible rewards 1 billion contribution points..."
Foreign worlds only allowed a maximum of 3rd-grade Invincibles to enter, so there were no rewards for killing 2nd-grade Invincibles.
For many Invincibles, contribution points were never enough. Therefore, such rewards could definitely attract them, even to the point of risking their lives.
In fact, once this mission was issued, for Invincibles born in foreign worlds, they had to accept it even if they did not want to.
After all, if the Tianfeng Sea could issue this mission, the Netherworld Hall could naturally issue it as well.
When the time came, the Invincibles of the Netherworld Hall would also come to hunt the Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea in the foreign worlds. Perhaps his information had already fallen into the hands of the Netherworld Hall.
With the Netherworld Hall's capabilities, they might be able to find the location of the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Unless he hid the Dark Forbidden Domain right now, but there was no time left.
"If I had known, I would have hidden the Dark Forbidden Domain earlier!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Subsequently, Ye Tian accepted the mission. Taking a piece of intelligence, he left the Tianlin Realm and immediately returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Then, he began to set up arrays in the Dark Forbidden Domain. Although time was short, what if the Invincibles of the Netherworld Hall did not come to the Dark Forbidden Domain?
In that case, would the Dark Forbidden Domain not remain unaffected?
To ensure the safety of the Dark Forbidden Domain, Ye Tian destroyed all the gates of the Dark Forbidden Domain, severing the connection between other foreign worlds and the Dark Forbidden Domain.
As the years passed, the Tianfeng Sea and the Netherworld Hall fought in countless foreign worlds. A large number of Invincibles fell, and the fighting on both sides was extremely tragic.
Finally, on this day.
Ye Tian successfully finished setting up the grand array, concealing the entire Dark Forbidden Domain.
"It seems I overestimated myself. I have no reputation in the Tianfeng Sea, so it's impossible for the Invincibles of the Netherworld Hall to specifically investigate me and search for my Dark Forbidden Domain," Ye Tian said as he came to a realization.
With the Dark Forbidden Domain hidden, the risk of exposure here was nearly zero.
Next, Ye Tian prepared to go and hunt the Invincibles of the Netherworld Hall.
For him, killing the Invincibles of the Netherworld Hall was much faster for earning contribution points than killing Tribulation Beasts.
