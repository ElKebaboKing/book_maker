Chapter 1429: Condensing the Holy Soul, Strength Skyrockets
!
Time passed slowly. Holy Gold Sand stopped appearing for a while, but Star Soul Sand continued to appear at a rate of one per day.
Ye Tian was the strongest and fastest, so he was often able to snatch the Star Soul Sand.
A hundred years later, on this day, a ripple of spatial fluctuation occurred.
"Holy Gold Sand is about to appear!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Sure enough.
A grain of Holy Gold Sand appeared, and it was relatively close to Ye Tian. Thus, Ye Tian appeared beside the Holy Gold Sand at his fastest speed and forcibly collected it. The Supreme Domain Ancestors of various Holy Sects, including those ranked first and second on the Supreme List, wanted to snatch the Holy Gold Sand, but Ye Tian severely injured them one by one.
After that, they didn't dare to try and snatch it anymore, and the Holy Gold Sand fell into Ye Tian's hands.
Next, almost every hundred years, Holy Gold Sand would appear once.
But Ye Tian didn't manage to snatch it every time. If it was far from him, some Supreme Domain Ancestors had peculiar methods and could take the Holy Gold Sand in an instant. Of course, they would pay a price, but compared to obtaining the Holy Gold Sand, that price was nothing.
Ye Tian was somewhat helpless about this; he also wanted to snatch all the Holy Gold Sand, but it wasn't realistic.
Time passed slowly in this manner, snatching Star Soul Sand and Holy Gold Sand.
A thousand years! Ten thousand years! A hundred thousand years! Three hundred thousand! A million years! On this day, many Supreme Domain Ancestors discovered that this treasure land was no longer producing Star Soul Sand. Day after day passed, but Star Soul Sand never appeared.
Moreover, they found that the void of this treasure land had become much more fragile.
"This treasure land is ruined!"
many Supreme Domain Ancestors lamented.
They waited for another few hundred years and finally had to accept the fact that this treasure land was ruined. Not only could Star Soul Sand no longer be born, but Holy Gold Sand had also completely ceased to appear.
In a million years, a total of 1,000 grains of Holy Gold Sand were born. Almost all the Supreme Domain Ancestors on the Supreme List had come here, so the competitive pressure was immense. Yet, in the end, Ye Tian still snatched 521 grains of Holy Gold Sand, showing his strength.
Most of the other Holy Gold Sand was snatched by the top ten Supreme Domain Ancestors on the Supreme List, while a few were taken by some lucky individuals.
In addition to several thousand grains of Holy Gold Sand, Ye Tian also obtained nearly ten million grains of Star Soul Sand.
What did ten million grains of Star Soul Sand represent? It was enough to create ten million Invincible Domain Ancestors! Of course, he wouldn't be so foolish as to use them to cultivate ten million ordinary Invincible Domain Ancestors. There was no need for that, and the War Sect wouldn't cultivate them that way either.
Moreover, he also needed a large amount of Star Soul Sand to strengthen his Holy Soul Light.
During these million years, Ye Tian had to constantly watch for the birth of Star Soul Sand and Holy Gold Sand, so he had very little time to cultivate. His cultivation time was very fragmented, but his soul had improved a lot under the influence of the Star Soul Sand. Now, the Holy Soul Light occupied sixty percent of his soul.
"Next, I'll go into seclusion for a while. Only by condensing the Holy Soul can my strength skyrocket and allow me to go to some of the forbidden areas of the Holy Soul Realm!"
Ye Tian murmured.
There were many opportunities in the Holy Soul Realm. According to the War Sect, some forbidden areas in the Holy Soul Realm contained immense opportunities. Unfortunately, some places were too dangerous; without strength comparable to the peak of an Invincible Domain Ancestor or the combat power of a Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, one must never go there.
Those who entered the Holy Soul Realm were all Supreme Domain Ancestors; how could their combat power be that high? That was why those places were classified as forbidden areas.
But once Ye Tian condensed his Holy Soul and his strength surged, those places would no longer be forbidden areas, but his treasure lands! Ye Tian found a place and devoted himself to refining the Star Soul Sand.
It had to be said that the effect of the Star Soul Sand was indeed excellent.
While refining the Star Soul Sand, Ye Tian's Holy Soul Light grew rapidly.
At Ye Tian's refining speed, he could refine one grain of Star Soul Sand in almost a year. Ten thousand grains of Star Soul Sand could increase his Holy Soul Light from sixty percent to seventy percent, though the subsequent increases might be more difficult.
But according to Ye Tian's estimation, he probably only needed a hundred thousand grains of Star Soul Sand to have a 100% certainty of condensing the Holy Soul.
Refining a hundred thousand grains of Star Soul Sand would take him a hundred thousand years.
To be able to condense a Holy Soul in a mere hundred thousand years was extremely fast.
Ye Tian was refining the Star Soul Sand within a Time Array. In the outside world, only about a year would have passed.
From this, one could see how awesome the effect of the Star Soul Sand was! Grain after grain of Star Soul Sand was refined by Ye Tian, and eventually, a hundred thousand grains were consumed.
On this day.
Ye Tian was in the breakthrough stage. His Holy Soul Light almost filled his entire soul, with only a sliver of soul left that hadn't been completely transformed.
Thus, Ye Tian refined another grain of Star Soul Sand. Under its effect, the last sliver of his soul was also transformed into Holy Soul Light.
Finally, the qualitative change began!!! Countless rays of Holy Soul Light began to compress. It was as if Ye Tian's Holy Soul Light, which seemed to span hundreds of millions of feet in his soul sea, was gradually undergoing a qualitative change toward the size of a normal human body under infinite compression.
As the Holy Soul Light compressed, an incredibly terrifying soul pressure emanated from Ye Tian.
After a long time, Ye Tian's Holy Soul was finally condensed.
Once the Holy Soul was formed, Ye Tian clearly felt that his strength had improved significantly.
By activating the Holy Soul, his combat power would be terrifying. Furthermore, the power of the Holy Soul was nearly infinite and could recover quickly, allowing for sustained combat.
"My current strength should be comparable to the combat power of a peak Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor!"
Ye Tian estimated.
He didn't think his strength could cross the boundaries of Five-Step and Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors in one go; that was too difficult.
Above the Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor level, the strength gap between each step was enormous.
According to Ye Tian's estimation, unless he cultivated his Ancestral Techniques to the Minor Achievement stage or underwent a second qualitative change of his Holy Soul, it would be impossible to break through the combat power shackles and reach the level of a Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
"Cultivating Ancestral Techniques takes a long time, and I don't have time for that right now. However, as for the Holy Soul, I can definitely push it to undergo a second qualitative change!"
Ye Tian thought.
He still had many grains of Holy Gold Sand in his hands. Once he refined them, he could trigger a second qualitative change in his Holy Soul. The Holy Soul would then become even more terrifying, and it was said that special traits would be born, some of which were heaven-defying.
"Then let's refine the Holy Gold Sand!"
Ye Tian took out a grain of Holy Gold Sand and swallowed it into his soul sea, and his Holy Soul began to absorb its power.
