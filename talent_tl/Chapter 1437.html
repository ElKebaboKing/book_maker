Chapter 1436: Destroying the Incarnation of the Fire Pattern War Ancestor!
!
Once the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body was completely condensed, Ye Tian began to practice his control over it.
He had never practiced physical body control before, so he could currently only be considered to have a 1x level of control.
The so-called control training was... mobilizing the power of the body's countless billions of cells, treating almost every cell as a world to be controlled. The stronger the physical body, the stronger the cellular worlds, and the difficulty of controlling the physical body to a 365x level naturally became greater.
Since Ye Tian had condensed the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body, his physical form was incredibly terrifying. The difficulty of controlling such a body to the 365x level far exceeded the imagination of other Invincible Domain Ancestors.
However, Ye Tian had a cheat code: the Evolution trait.
He could use the Evolution trait to deduce physical body control and even use his Duplicate Bodies for experimentation. It didn't matter how recklessly he pushed things; since it wasn't his main body, if a Duplicate Body was damaged, he could simply condense a new one.
Therefore, with the Evolution trait and Duplicate Bodies, the speed at which Ye Tian mastered his body would be extremely fast.
Because of this, he wasn't worried at all.
"Let's begin mastering the body!"
Ye Tian began a long period of repeated control exercises, constantly evolving and deducing, gaining a deeper and deeper understanding of his physical form.
Ten thousand years! A million years! A hundred million years! By this time, Ye Tian had improved from a 1x body control level to a 13x level. Such a rapid rate of improvement far surpassed anyone else.
"Continue!"
After another billion years, Ye Tian's body control level rose to 62x.
The further he progressed, the more difficult it became to control the body.
If anyone else possessed a body as terrifying as Ye Tian's, it would likely take them endless billions of years to achieve full 365x control.
Thus, having a body that was too powerful was not always a good thing.
With his physical body control level reaching 62x, Ye Tian's combat power had already reached an incredibly terrifying level.
"No need to rush the cultivation anymore; it's time to deal with the Qingyuan Holy Land!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Back then, the Qingyuan Holy Land had sent two Invincible Domain Ancestors to seize the human race's Saint-level mineral vein and had killed a number of human Domain Ancestors.
He still remembered this matter clearly.
Furthermore, the Qingyuan Holy Land had even requested the Weeping Blood Emperor to come to the rescue. If it hadn't been for the intervention of the War Gate's Invincible Domain Ancestor, the Gu Hai Domain Ancestor, the two Invincible Domain Ancestors from the Qingyuan Holy Land would have been rescued, and even the human race's Saint-level mineral vein would have been snatched away.
He might have even been in mortal danger.
Therefore, he had always kept this matter in mind.
Previously, his strength was too weak to resolve the issue with the Qingyuan Holy Land.
Now, his strength was already incredibly formidable, and he had the power to settle this small trouble.
Of course, before preparing to deal with the Qingyuan Holy Land, Ye Tian first reported to the War Gate.
....War World Tower.
"Fire Pattern War Ancestor, I wish to deal with the Qingyuan Holy Land!"
Ye Tian met with an incarnation of the Fire Pattern War Ancestor.
"Oh? Why do you want to deal with the Qingyuan Holy Land?"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor asked curiously.
"Back then, the Qingyuan Holy Land sent people to steal our race's Saint-level mineral vein and killed many of our human Domain Ancestors. They even sent the Weeping Blood Emperor to the rescue. Fortunately, the Gu Hai Domain Ancestor stepped in to help, which resolved the crisis!"
Ye Tian said.
"I have some recollection of that matter. So it was the Qingyuan Domain Ancestor's doing!"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor was somewhat surprised.
He then added: "Ye Tian, the Qingyuan Holy Land does, after all, have a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. Although that Supreme-level Domain Ancestor's strength isn't exceptionally great, his cultivation has reached the mid-stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm. He isn't... a newly promoted Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. Are you sure you can handle him?"
"Fire Pattern War Ancestor, I wonder if the War World Tower can withstand a battle between Supreme-level Domain Ancestors?"
Ye Tian asked.
"Do you wish to spar with me?"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor laughed.
"Fire Pattern War Ancestor, that is exactly my intention!"
Ye Tian nodded.
"Rest assured, the War World Tower is more than capable of withstanding a battle between Supreme-level Domain Ancestors."
With a wave of the Fire Pattern War Ancestor's hand, the two of them arrived in the deepest void of the War World Tower.
"This incarnation of mine also possesses Supreme-level Domain Ancestor combat power. Whatever abilities you have, show them!"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor said.
"Fine!"
Ye Tian was not polite at all and immediately activated his Ancestral Body.
Boom!!!!! The Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body erupted, and its terrifying aura caused even the Fire Pattern War Ancestor's expression to change.
"The Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body! You actually broke the Saint Body limit, and you even skipped the half-step Ancestral Body process to reach the Ancestral Body level directly!!!"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor said in shock.
He was dumbfounded! As a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, although he had broken the Saint Body limit, he was still currently at the half-step Ancestral Body level, albeit infinitely close to a true Ancestral Body.
He never expected that the little fellow he had met not long ago would condense an Ancestral Body in such a short time—and the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body at that, the number one Ancestral Body in the Dark Saint Realm, far surpassing ordinary Ancestral Bodies!
But the surprises continued!
Ye Tian activated the Golden Body Ancestral Technique and the Ancestral Armor Ancestral Technique, maxing out his defensive power.
Then, with a wave of his hand, countless golden rays shot toward the Fire Pattern War Ancestor.
This was the Ancestral Technique: Golden Silk!
"The Golden Silk Ancestral Technique! You actually upgraded the Golden Silk Saint Technique to the Ancestral Technique level!"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor was shocked once again.
Not daring to be careless, the Fire Pattern War Ancestor directly erupted with the power of his Ancestral Body and activated his Ancestral Technique—the Fire Pattern War Body, transforming into a massive form the size of a realm.
However, Ye Tian's Golden Silk Ancestral Technique pierced through his defenses and wounded him.
Yet, the surprises didn't end there. With his physical body control at 62x, Ye Tian activated the Extreme Strength Ancestral Technique and, holding a top-tier Saint Weapon spear, struck at the Fire Pattern War Ancestor.
With a loud bang!
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor's body was sent flying, and countless voids were annihilated as their battle shook the War World Tower.
At this moment, countless Domain Ancestors and half-step Domain Ancestors within the War World Tower were terrified, not knowing what was happening.
Soon, the Domain Ancestors of the War Gate informed the others that two Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were sparring, which finally put everyone's fears to rest.
At the same time, they were very curious about which two Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were sparring....
"Void Slash!"
Another of Ye Tian's Ancestral Techniques erupted, instantly slicing through the Fire Pattern War Ancestor's body.
Following that, under Ye Tian's continuous attacks, the Fire Pattern War Ancestor was actually being dominated.
The power of an incarnation was limited, and before long, the Fire Pattern War Ancestor's incarnation could no longer sustain itself and completely collapsed.
A moment later, the Fire Pattern War Ancestor's true body descended.
"Ye Tian, your strength has indeed reached the level of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, and you are quite a bit stronger than the average Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. That Supreme-level Domain Ancestor from the Qingyuan Holy Land is indeed no match for you! This time, you have a valid reason, and since you are dealing with the Qingyuan Holy Land as an Invincible Domain Ancestor, even if you wipe them out, the other Saint Gates and Holy Lands won't say anything.
As for other matters, do not worry; I will handle them!"
The Fire Pattern War Ancestor said.
"Thank you, Fire Pattern War Ancestor!"
Ye Tian expressed his gratitude.
Afterward, Ye Tian left the War World Tower, condensed a Duplicate Body, and sent it toward the Qingyuan Holy Land!
