Chapter 1544: Breakthrough, Becoming a Fifth-Tier Invincible Ancestor!

Juewu City.
Ye Tian began his secluded cultivation in his manor, starting to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique.
Of course, an Invincible Ancestral Technique was not created out of thin air; there had to be a direction.
Ye Tian planned to create a top-tier Invincible Ancestral Technique based on his Supreme Ancestral Techniques combined with the Power Eternal Rule. For instance, he looked to techniques like the Overpowering Strength Supreme Ancestral Technique and the Extreme Arm Supreme Ancestral Technique, which involved the aspect of power.
By drawing on the strengths of these Supreme Ancestral Techniques and incorporating his insights into the application of the Power Eternal Rule, he would eventually forge them into an Invincible Ancestral Technique.
Comprehending an Eternal Rule was a difficult task, but the process from comprehending the rule to creating an Invincible Ancestral Technique also required a significant amount of time, though success was ultimately guaranteed.
With Ye Tian's comprehension, his level of insight into the Power Eternal Rule, and his possession of the Power Eternal Ancestral Talent, creating an Invincible Ancestral Technique naturally wouldn't take too long.
In the blink of an eye, ten domain eras passed.
On this day.
Ye Tian finally succeeded in creating an Invincible Ancestral Technique, and it was one that far surpassed those of ordinary fifth-tier Invincible Ones. After all, he had seen the Invincible Ancestral Techniques of the Invincible Ones from the Heavenly Wind Sea, and they were far inferior to the one he had created.
Ye Tian named this Invincible Ancestral Technique the Heavenly Limit Combat Technique. It was an extreme development of power that utilized the Power Eternal Rule; without a sufficiently high level of insight into the Power Eternal Rule, one simply could not perform this technique.
Once the Heavenly Limit Combat Technique was unleashed, physical strength would surge many times over, and the entire body would become like a weapon of slaughter, crushing everything in its path.
The moment he mastered the Heavenly Limit Combat Technique, Ye Tian had a moment of clarity. He could break through at any time with the help of this technique, but he could only allow either his main body or his duplicated body to break through; he could not have both break through simultaneously. This was a restriction of the rules.
Consequently.
Ye Tian allowed his main body to undergo the breakthrough.
Boom!!!!
Under the influence of the Heavenly Limit Combat Technique, his main body began to transform. This was equivalent to merging with the Invincible Ancestral Technique, causing his physical body to undergo a qualitative change.
Suddenly, under the influence of a mysterious power, countless strands of source power descended from the Source Sea into the Dark Forbidden Domain, surging into Ye Tian's body to help him complete his transformation.
The source power of the Source Sea was almost impossible to refine, but at this moment, under that mysterious influence, it turned into source power that was very easy to refine. This was the benefit brought by a breakthrough in realm.
Breaking through from a sixth-tier Invincible One to a fifth-tier Invincible One brought a massive increase due to the qualitative change. Furthermore, Ye Tian practiced the top-tier Invincible Calamity Body technique, Ten Thousand Origins Returning to the Void, and had reached the first layer of the Perfect Calamity Body at the Great Circle stage. Combined with the fact that the Invincible Ancestral Technique he created was a top-tier one.
Thus, with this breakthrough, Ye Tian's strength underwent an earth-shaking change.
After a long time, Ye Tian finally completed his breakthrough and officially stepped into the realm of a Fifth-Tier Invincible Ancestor.
"My current strength far exceeds that of an ordinary Fifth-Tier Invincible Ancestor. It is said that the mysterious leaders of the Calamity Black Mountain and the Firmament Sword Tomb are fifth-tier Invincible Ones, but if I wanted to kill them, I could probably crush them with a casual wave of my hand!" Ye Tian murmured.
This wasn't arrogance, but a fact.
When he was a sixth-tier Invincible One, his strength was already comparable to a powerful fifth-tier Invincible One. Now that he had broken through to the fifth tier and created a top-tier Invincible Ancestral Technique, his strength had increased by an unknown number of times. He was likely not much weaker than a weaker fourth-tier Invincible One.
The requirement to become a fourth-tier Invincible One was to raise one's cultivation to a very high level and create at least three Invincible Techniques. Only then could one be called a fourth-tier Invincible One.
Cultivation was the easiest to increase; it just required slow accumulation over long years. The difficult part was the three Invincible Techniques.
Moreover, these three techniques could not be of the same type. If they were of the same type—for example, if they were all power-based—then such an Invincible One could not become a fourth-tier Invincible One. Even if they created ten techniques of the same type, they could only be called a pseudo-fourth-tier Invincible One.
This was because creating a second technique of the same type provided little help in increasing strength and made it difficult for the physical body to undergo another qualitative change. Only by comprehending other Eternal Rules—comprehending three or more—and creating three different types of Invincible Techniques could one trigger three qualitative changes in the physical body and finally step into the realm of a fourth-tier Invincible One.
Moving from a fifth-tier to a fourth-tier Invincible One required even more time, and some might never reach it in their entire lives.
This was because some Invincible Ones only had an affinity for one type of Eternal Rule and none for others. Furthermore, having spent endless years and countless resources just to comprehend one Eternal Rule, the idea of comprehending two more was almost an extravagant hope.
Thus, many fifth-tier Invincible Ones in the Heavenly Wind Sea practiced for countless ages without being able to advance to the fourth tier.
But for Ye Tian, advancing to the fourth tier was truly not difficult.
He possessed multiple Eternal-level talents, allowing him to easily comprehend various Eternal Rules. This was his advantage.
Currently, Ye Tian's difficulty lay instead in accumulating cultivation and practicing the Ten Thousand Origins Returning to the Void; these two aspects would take a long time.
"I'll take it slow. First, I'll condense a new duplicated body and have it start practicing the second layer of Ten Thousand Origins Returning to the Void, perfecting it step by step, while my main body focuses on increasing cultivation. Then, I'll occasionally go to Nine Yang Mountain to study the Nine Yang Fish," Ye Tian planned.
However, before doing all that, Ye Tian prepared to take action against some Calamity Beasts...
Previously, he had been constantly searching for Calamity Beasts and had encountered some that weren't too powerful, but he couldn't kill them back then. Now that his cultivation had reached the fifth tier, he naturally had the confidence to slay them.
Basically, as long as it was a beast of one or two hundred Calamities, he was confident he could kill it. As for beasts around five hundred Calamities, he could only manage a stalemate, and he was no match for those over a thousand Calamities.
Consequently.
Ye Tian had his duplicated body begin to target these Calamity Beasts.
Bao'an World.
Ye Tian descended here. The Calamity Beast here was only at 118 Calamities. Although Bao'an World was not currently undergoing a Great Calamity, even though Ye Tian's strength was suppressed significantly by the world, he still had the confidence to slay this beast.
He didn't want to wait until the Bao'an World's Great Calamity to deal with this beast; that would require waiting a long time, and he didn't have that much patience.
Boom!!!!
After Ye Tian descended, the rules and source of Bao'an World suppressed him, and his strength was suddenly weakened by at least seventy percent, leaving him with only thirty percent of his power.
But even with only thirty percent, he still far surpassed an ordinary fifth-tier Invincible One.
Soon, Ye Tian found the Calamity Beast and shattered the space-time node where it resided with a single strike.
Crack!!!
The massive Calamity Beast appeared, staring angrily at Ye Tian. It recognized him; last time he had come to disturb it and had been heavily injured. It hadn't expected him to return.
Seeing the Calamity Beast, Ye Tian said nothing and directly activated his Invincible Ancestral Technique, the Heavenly Limit Combat Technique.
Instantly, Ye Tian's physical strength surged, and his entire body underwent a massive change, transforming into an incredibly terrifying existence.
Next, Ye Tian held a mid-grade Calamity Tool long saber and slashed at the beast.
With a squelch!
The Calamity Beast's body was slashed by Ye Tian, leaving a massive wound from which countless amounts of blood flowed.
Kill!
The two sides began to fight fiercely.
Before long, relying on his invincible Calamity Body and the Calamity Tool saber in his hand, Ye Tian forcibly bludgeoned the Calamity Beast to death.
This was merely the beginning.
In the following days, Ye Tian continued to hunt Calamity Beasts, slaying a total of thirteen in a short period.
As for other Calamity Beasts, he wasn't confident, unless he went further into the Source Sea domains to find other worlds.
But for Ye Tian, these current gains were enough to allow him to cultivate for a long time without worrying about a lack of resources.
Thus, Ye Tian paused his hunting of Calamity Beasts.
