Chapter 1523: Seeking the Great Tribulation of an Otherworldly Realm!

When a great tribulation will occur in an otherworldly realm is a very hidden matter. Unless one has established a base in another otherworldly realm and mastered its secrets, it is very difficult to know when that realm will enter a great tribulation; otherwise, it is nearly impossible to predict.
For example, he did not know when the Bone Race's realm would enter its great tribulation.
The only otherworldly realm whose tribulation date he knew was the Sanctuary Realm, but there were still several Dark Eras of time remaining. He did not want to wait too long. After all, he did not wish to encounter the current situation again during the next Dark Forbidden Zone tribulation, where the entire Dark Forbidden Zone was looted clean.
He wanted the human race to completely control the Dark Forbidden Zone and sweep through all the forbidden lands, making the various major forces afraid to enter the Dark Forbidden Zone again.
Thinking it over, Ye Tian came up with two methods.
The first method was to use the same approach he used to enter the Sanctuary Realm: first wipe out the bases of those forces in the Dark Forbidden Zone, then copy the body of one of the otherworldly experts to enter their realm to gather information and confirm how much time remained before their next great tribulation.
The second method was to directly threaten these forces, demanding they inform him when their tribulation arrived, or else he would directly destroy their bases.
But Ye Tian calculated that the second method was likely unrealistic.
After all, no one wanted a force far stronger than themselves to enter their realm; in that case, the entire force would likely be occupied or wiped out.
Therefore, once he used the second method, these forces would likely abandon the Dark Forbidden Zone and directly destroy their gates.
After all, if they destroyed the gate, they could simply wait for an illusory gate to appear again, fix it, enter another otherworldly realm, and open a new gate. That way, they would not be threatened by the Dark Forbidden Zone.
After all, the current Dark Forbidden Zone was very powerful, and many small forces had already actively abandoned this place. Currently, the number of forbidden lands in the Dark Forbidden Zone had already decreased by about a dozen.
For example, the otherworldly force that had attacked Ye Tian back then—the Chaotian Pavilion—destroyed its gate shortly after those supreme experts fell, and the forbidden land where the Chaotian Pavilion was located vanished directly.
After thinking for a while, Ye Tian made his decision: "Then I will adopt the first method!"
------------------
Not long after, Ye Tian directly targeted an otherworldly force slightly weaker than the Bone Race—the Wind-Rain Sect.
In an instant, he wiped out the Wind-Rain Sect's base in the Dark Forbidden Zone, then copied the body of one of the Wind-Rain Sect experts and passed through the gate into the otherworldly realm where the Wind-Rain Sect was located.
The otherworldly realm where the Wind-Rain Sect was located was called the Universal Realm. The Wind-Rain Sect occupied an incredibly massive world within the Universal Realm and was the most powerful force there, possessing six supreme experts.
Ye Tian entered the Universal Realm in the guise of a Wind-Rain Sect expert. He immediately called for help and used a perfect reason to explain why he had survived.
That was, Ye Tian had spared him to bring back a message: the Wind-Rain Sect must pay a portion of resources and disclose the time of the great tribulation's arrival before they would be allowed to continue maintaining their base in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
After Ye Tian said this, he thought the Wind-Rain Sect would consider it for a while, but to his surprise, the several supreme experts of the Wind-Rain Sect began to deliberate immediately.
"What should we do? An incredibly powerful supreme ancestor has been born in the Dark Forbidden Zone, far exceeding an ordinary third-tier supreme ancestor. Once we tell him the time of our world's great tribulation, if he breaks in, none of us will be able to stop him. When the time comes and he wipes out our Wind-Rain Sect, everything will be over!"
"We cannot tell him about the time of our Universal Realm's tribulation. Even if he makes a life-bound oath, we cannot believe him. For an expert like him, no oath is effective."
"Abandon this gate. When the Universal Realm's illusory gate descends again, we will fix it and rebuild an eternal gate. Although it will cost many resources and materials, at least the Wind-Rain Sect will not face any threat!"
"Mm, then let us abandon this gate!"
All six supreme experts of the Wind-Rain Sect agreed to abandon the gate.
Subsequently, the six supreme experts arrived before the Wind-Rain Sect's gate and attacked it together.
A first-tier gate was not very sturdy. Top-tier ancestor-level peak experts could damage the gate, and a single strike from a supreme expert could destroy it. With the six experts acting together, they directly smashed the gate into countless cracks, making it almost unusable.
They continued to attack because what they wanted was to completely destroy the gate, meaning it would vanish entirely.
Boom! Boom! Boom!!!!
One attack after another fell, and soon this first-tier gate turned into countless fragments and vanished.
· ········· Seeking flowers ··· ·····
Seeing this, Ye Tian's copied body could not help but marvel inwardly: "How decisive!"
It had to be said, this was indeed the best method.
"I wonder if I can communicate with the main body in the Dark Forbidden Zone?"
Ye Tian's copied body tried to make contact.
Back in the Sanctuary Realm, he had tried to make contact but failed, only succeeding once the copied body reached the supreme expert level.
But things were different now. The main body's strength was much greater, second only to the Invincibles. Thus, the connection was deep. Perhaps even if this copied body only rivaled a top-tier ancestor, it could still sense the main body!
Time passed slowly, and in the blink of an eye, hundreds of thousands of years went by.
... ..... 0
On this day, the copied body established a sliver of connection with the main body and could transmit some simple messages, but that was enough.
By this time, Ye Tian's copied body already knew the timing of the Universal Realm's great tribulation. Calculating by the nine Dark Eras of the Dark Forbidden Zone, only five Dark Eras had passed in the Universal Realm, and there were still four Dark Eras before the great tribulation would arrive. Thus, there was still plenty of time.
Ye Tian not only transmitted the time but also, through the connection between the main body and the copied body, mapped out the coordinates of the Universal Realm in the following days.
This way, when the Universal Realm's great tribulation was about to arrive in the future, he could build a gate to travel there and absorb and refine those tribulation forces.
"The Universal Realm still has four Dark Eras before its great tribulation. It is not the best target. Let's keep looking!"
Ye Tian abandoned this copied body, letting it disintegrate on its own, and then set his sights on a second otherworldly force.
In this way, Ye Tian tried again and again, finally finding an otherworldly realm very close to its great tribulation.
The force in this otherworldly realm was called the Xiyue Tribe. They had also destroyed their gate, but that did not matter. Ye Tian already knew the coordinates of this otherworldly realm. He prepared to build a gate connecting to this realm, and a second-tier gate at that.
He already had the method to build a second-tier gate. He only needed to wait for the first Dark Era of the Dark Forbidden Zone to pass. At the moment the illusory gate appeared, he would completely fix it, modify the coordinates, and establish a second-tier gate connecting to that otherworldly realm.
