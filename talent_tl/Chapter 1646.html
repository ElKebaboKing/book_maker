Chapter 1645: Lords of the Eternal Realm Descend One After Another!

A half-step Eternal Realm expert arrived here and discovered the changes in the Sea of Silence.
“This is……”
The half-step Eternal Realm expert, Qianyu Domain Ancestor, stared in shock at that golden tree, immediately sensing that this was surely a heaven-shaking opportunity, and it might even be the kind of opportunity that was said to be born only once every one hundred thousand Eternal years recently.
“I’m very likely the first one!”
Qianyu Domain Ancestor was incomparably excited and immediately flew toward the depths of the void where that golden tree was located.
However, an eerie force came surging in.
“Not good!”
Only then did he remember the terror of the Sea of Silence, and that the place where the golden tree was located was not within the Sea of Silence, but was very likely the true source of the Sea of Silence.
He had been blinded by the opportunity and had not thought of this problem at all.
Boom!!!!!
Under the impact of that eerie force, Qianyu Domain Ancestor’s will was destroyed, his consciousness gradually faded away, and his body turned into a corpse floating on the golden road.
Not long after, the anomaly here was completely discovered and spread throughout the entire Source Sea.
Qingming Saint Ancestor of the True Extreme Palace and Qi Yuan Domain Ancestor from the Space-Time Territory, as well as other extreme experts, also arrived here.
At the same time, a large number of extreme experts descended upon the area.
Of course, most of the extreme experts here came from various Space-Time Territories, while the extreme experts native to the Source Sea were relatively few.
“Found it. This is the true opportunity that is born only once every one hundred thousand Eternal years!”
The extreme experts were absolutely certain.
Immediately after, they notified the lords of the Space-Time Territories.
Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!!!
One extreme expert after another wanted to rush in, otherwise once the Eternal Realm lords descended, how would it still be their turn?
When one extreme expert after another charged into the golden road, they soon turned into corpses, and only a Five-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm expert did not fall.
However, under the impact of that eerie force, his whole body trembled, clearly suffering tremendous pain as well.
Seeing this scene, the weaker extreme experts no longer dared to enter.
So many extreme experts had fallen at once; when had something like this happened in the Source Sea in all these years?
“Get down. Before our Lord Xuanyu arrives, none of you are allowed to go in!”
A Seven-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm expert made his move, extending a hand into the golden road, grabbing that Five-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm expert directly, and throwing him to a faraway place.
Boom rumble!!!!!
A group of Six- and Seven-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm experts blocked the area, not allowing any stronger expert to enter.
Very soon, the Eternal Realm lords descended.
Northern Sea Lord, Xuanyu Lord, Lianyi Lord, Linghe Lord……
One lord after another descended. The aura of each lord made those half-step Eternal Realm experts tremble uncontrollably, not daring to meet their gaze at all.
When Lianyi Lord saw that golden tree, his eyes lit up brilliantly.
“If I’m not mistaken, this should be the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree. The opportunity born this time is actually it!”
“Hahaha, it really is the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree. The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree can give birth to Eternal Knowledge Golden Fruits; it can enhance our wills and even help break through bottlenecks and shackles. The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree also has another enormous function, and that is upgrading Space-Time Lords, turning ordinary Space-Time Lords into Eternal Space-Time Territories. The resources born from an Eternal Space-Time Lord are beyond imagination. If I can snatch the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree, I will certainly become a top existence among the Eternal Realm!”
“What a joke. You can also snatch the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree? It will definitely belong to me!”
“The Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree belongs to me!”
The lords immediately started arguing.
They did not waste time continuing the argument, and all of them charged into the golden road at once, entering the unknown depths of the void and heading toward the place where the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree was located.
Suddenly.
Voices sounded from within.
“You lot can come in too. There are some good things here, and they’re useful to you as well!”
The lords reminded their servants through transmission.
“There are other good things?”
The extreme experts became excited and all rushed in.
As far as they knew, the opportunity born one hundred thousand Eternal years ago had only one treasure and no other treasures, which was why it had only caused slaughter among the Eternal Realm experts, while the half-step Eternal Realm experts had gained no benefits at all and instead been affected, suffering heavy losses in the fighting.
“We came at the right time this time. Hurry and go in!”
A Six-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm expert said excitedly.
In the Space-Time Territories, many Seven-Extreme, and even Eight-Extreme, half-step Eternal Realm experts were unwilling to obey the orders of the Eternal Realm and come here to search for the once-in-one-hundred-thousand-Eternal-years opportunity, and thus they were unable to descend into the Source Sea.
So, those experts had lost this opportunity this time.
Basically, half-step Eternal Realm experts above the Four-Extreme Realm had all passed through the golden road, but even if Four-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm experts paid the price to get through it, they could only barely walk onto the land in the depths of the void and could not go deeper at all. Whether they could find good things depended entirely on luck.
As for Three-Extreme Realm half-step Eternal Realm experts, they did not dare enter at all. Once they stepped onto the golden road, they would be blasted to death by the eerie force before they could go far.
Outside the golden road.
Zhenhe Sword Ancestor and the others from the Source God Pavilion stood there, all sighing endlessly and not daring to go in at all.
“Forget it. This opportunity has nothing to do with us. Registration is more important. Let’s go!” Zhenhe Sword Ancestor suggested.
“Mm, let’s go. I wonder if the Endless Ancestor has gone in?” Fenggu Domain Ancestor asked curiously.
“The Endless Ancestor has never appeared, so he probably didn’t go in either?” Zhenhe Sword Ancestor was not sure.
However, no matter what, this opportunity had nothing to do with them anymore.

“Undying Golden Sun Flower, another treasure that drives even extreme experts crazy!”
Ye Tian was incomparably excited.
In such a short time, he had already found dozens of treasures, and most of them were treasures he did not recognize.
Boom rumble rumble!!!!!
Auras of one Eternal Realm after another came from the distance, not the least bit concealed.
“The Eternal Realm lords have come in!”
Ye Tian immediately concealed his aura, even hiding himself with a second-tier will.
As long as the Eternal Realm lords did not investigate carefully, he could still conceal himself.
The true target of those Eternal Realm lords was only that Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree, so they did not investigate other places.
They also did not want to waste time and end up having that Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree snatched away.
Therefore, they did not discover Ye Tian’s traces.
However, when these Eternal Realm experts approached the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree, their speed all slowed a great deal, and the eerie force also affected them.
Ye Tian was interested in the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree, but he knew he could not compete with the Eternal Realm experts for it, so his target was only the other resources.
Compared with the other extreme experts, his will was comparable to that of an Eternal Realm expert, so he could go to more places.
Therefore, he could obtain more resources.
“About time. Let’s have the clone take these resources back!”
Ye Tian murmured.
