Chapter 1408: Fight! Fight! Fight!

Chapter 1408: Fight
! Fight! Fight!
"I'll go first, Wansheng, you hold him back!"
Ancestor Mili said.
He didn't want to waste time. If he did, what if the Myriad Dao Mountain Domain hidden an invincible domain ancestor? Although the possibility wasn't high, he didn't want any mistakes.
Therefore, he would first leave with the Saint-grade ore vein, and then go to Qingyuan Holy Land; this was the safest method.
As for Ancestor Wansheng, he was an invincible domain ancestor himself and could leave easily.
"Alright!"
Ancestor Wansheng nodded.
The Saint-grade ore vein was the most important; they truly couldn't waste time here.
After all, severely injuring Ye Tian without killing him, making it impossible for Ye Tian to pursue them, would also take some time.
Therefore, having him block Ye Tian was the best plan.
Whoosh! Ancestor Mili, carrying the Qingyuan World Pearl, was about to teleport away, either through the various world entrances of the Profound Void Holy Realm, or by tearing through the world barrier while Ancestor Wansheng held Ye Tian back.
Although they were blocked by the world's power just now, if they attacked repeatedly, they could still break through the world barrier and leave the Profound Void Holy Realm.
Therefore, Ancestor Mili wasn't worried about not being able to leave.
Unfortunately, they still underestimated Ye Tian.
"Just the two of you, and you think you can leave?"
Ye Tian fully activated the power of the Profound Void Holy Realm to empower himself. At this moment, his strength reached an incredibly terrifying level of amplification.
Through the Time Shard Saint Technique, he glimpsed a corner of the future, knowing that the strength of these two invincible domain ancestors wasn't too terrifying and wouldn't threaten him. Only then did he dare to intercept them; otherwise, he wouldn't have rushed forward so rashly.
"Five Elements Seal!"
Ye Tian instantly sealed the void, blocking Ancestor Mili.
It would be extremely difficult for Ancestor Mili to break through the Five Elements Seal and escape.
After all, he wouldn't give them time to break the Five Elements Seal. Coupled with the effect of the Primordial Well of Creation, Ye Tian could mobilize all the power of the Profound Void Holy Realm, making it even harder for them to leave.
"First, severely injure him!"
Seeing that he couldn't leave for now, Ancestor Mili said coldly.
Boom!!!! The two invincible domain ancestors attacked, unleashing the same Saint Technique—Azure Wind Giant Hand! As invincible domain ancestors of Qingyuan Holy Land, they could only acquire a few types of Saint Techniques, because Qingyuan Holy Land had very few Saint Techniques.
Only top holy lands and the Ten Great Holy Gates possessed many techniques. For invincible domain ancestors like them, acquiring even one Saint Technique was quite good.
Two Azure Wind Giant Hands covered a section of the void, targeting Ye Tian. Such an attack intensity was enough to easily capture any supreme domain ancestor.
If Ye Tian didn't have the Profound Void Holy Realm, he would struggle to contend with these two invincible domain ancestors with his own strength. After all, these two invincible domain ancestors were not weak; although not as strong as Ancestor Xuegang, they could definitely withstand several moves under Ancestor Xuegang's attack.
Ye Tian was confident in killing ordinary invincible domain ancestors, but he couldn't contend with these two invincible domain ancestors.
But now, with the amplification of the Profound Void Holy Realm, his combat power soared many times over, so he naturally didn't fear these two invincible domain ancestors.
He could even wear down these two invincible domain ancestors to death.
"Golden Body Saint Technique!"
"Saint Armor Saint Technique!"
"Body Guard Saint Technique!"
....Ye Tian unleashed one Saint Technique after another, fighting against Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng.
Ye Tian held a blade-type holy artifact, a holy artifact born in the Profound Void Holy Realm previously, which now served as his personal blade.
He saw him slash out with his blade, combining it with the Void Slash Saint Technique, unleashing a terrifying strike.
Pfft!!!! Ancestor Mili was sent flying by a single slash, a blade mark almost piercing through his physical body.
This slash injured him! "How is this possible? He's only a supreme domain ancestor, how could he injure me with one slash?"
Ancestor Mili was in disbelief.
It wasn't that he hadn't encountered geniuses; even many geniuses who advanced to invincible domain ancestors could be suppressed by him with one hand.
After all, he was a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor! Invincible Domain Ancestor was just a realm, initially without specific divisions. But as countless invincible domain ancestors found themselves unable to advance to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, the Dark Holy Domain began to categorize invincible domain ancestors into different tiers.
For example, those closest to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, almost just one step away from advancing to invincible domain ancestors, these types of invincible domain ancestors possessed incredibly terrifying combat power, known as One-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors. Those slightly weaker in combat power were called Two-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, and finally, there were Nine-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors.
Under normal circumstances, those who could advance to invincible domain ancestors were all geniuses with strong combat power. As for those invincible domain ancestors who barely advanced using some special treasures, after cultivating to the peak of the invincible domain ancestor realm, they would roughly be... Nine-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors.
And some newly advanced invincible domain ancestors, if their combat power was inferior to a Nine-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, could only be called ordinary invincible domain ancestors.
And he, as a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, was already very powerful, yet he was actually injured by a supreme domain ancestor.
This indicated that even if Ye Tian's strength hadn't reached the combat power of a Four-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, it had definitely reached the combat power of a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Of course, they could also see that Ye Tian was leveraging the power of the Profound Void Holy Realm, but his inherent combat power was definitely not weak either.
"Eternal Saint Technique!"
Time was frozen. Ye Tian, with the fastest speed, slashed at Ancestor Wansheng, directly severing Ancestor Wansheng's head.
However, in an instant, Ancestor Wansheng regenerated his head.
Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng couldn't injure Ye Tian at all, while any of Ye Tian's attacks could injure both of them.
But an invincible domain ancestor is... an invincible domain ancestor, possessing an invincible domain body, their physical body is practically immortal and indestructible. Even if Ye Tian's strength was great, it would be difficult to obliterate their physical bodies and souls.
As time passed little by little, the injuries of Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng grew heavier and heavier.
Fight! Fight! Fight! Ye Tian fought like a madman, displaying almost all of his strength, like a terrifying supreme demon god, striking fear into the hearts of both Ancestor Mili and Ancestor Wansheng.
This battle also attracted many domain ancestors. One by one, domain ancestors arrived in this area and witnessed the great battle.
Immediately, they were greatly shocked by Ye Tian's combat power.
"Ancestor Wanfa's strength has actually reached such a level!!!!"
Ancestor Qingyu said in shock.
Although Ye Tian utilized the power of the Profound Void Holy Realm, even with Ye Tian's own strength alone, he could probably easily defeat her.
Saintess Qingyi was also extremely shocked. There was a time when she could still fight Ye Tian, but now the gap between them was too vast.
Although she had now also advanced to a supreme domain ancestor, compared to Ye Tian, she was simply not on the same level.
