Chapter 1628 Refining an Eternal Source Weapon!

The densely packed black fog insects had become Ye Tian’s food, and this way of devouring made him increasingly terrifying at the half-step Eternal Realm. Even he did not know how powerful he had become.
However, he was very clear that only by becoming stronger could he leave this place.
In fact, after the source tide of the Primal Sea ended, it was not impossible to leave. It was just that, upon leaving, one would be killed by the black fog insects, which was why the top half-step Eternal Realm experts outside did not dare remain in the Primal Sea. Naturally, Ye Tian’s black fog insect clone was not afraid of black fog insects.
“Almost there!”
Ye Tian felt that it was difficult to further improve his strength, and there was no need to continue cultivating. He could try leaving the Primal Sea.
Boom!!!!
The void of the Primal Sea was shattered.
Ye Tian forcibly shattered the void of the Primal Sea. His not-so-huge body burned part of its body and power in exchange for even more terrifying strength, drilling his way out toward the outside world.
Space shattered, and time and space surged.
Ye Tian followed the connection with his true body and headed toward the Source Sea.
Very quickly.
Somewhere in the Source Sea, the void split open, and a black fog insect drilled out. At this moment, its whole body was already almost shattered. In order to break out of the Primal Sea, this clone was nearly ruined.
At this time, Ye Tian’s copy body had long been waiting here. It immediately took the countless resources from the black fog insect clone, then the copy body immediately fled, leaving only the black fog insect clone behind.
He did not dare take the black fog insect clone away. If it attracted other Eternal Realm experts, even the Void Source Hall would not be able to protect him.
Therefore, it was better to be careful. After the copy body left, Ye Tian immediately severed all aura, allowing this black fog insect clone to completely fall and perish, and its body also began to Annihilation.
In this way, even if an Eternal Realm expert came, nothing could be found out.
As expected.
A mysterious Eternal Realm expert, his whole body shrouded in darkness, appeared here and wanted to investigate something, but found nothing at all.
After that, this mysterious Eternal Realm expert left.
To him, this was only a trivial matter, not worth investigating.
……
“I’m back!”
Ye Tian let out a sigh of relief.
Immediately after, he took out those resources, and was instantly overjoyed.
These resources, when accumulated together, were enough to arouse greed in any half-step Eternal Realm expert.
“I now have a lot of Undying Primal Stones in my hands; I can forge an Eternal Source Weapon for myself. The weapons I’ve been using have all been top-grade source weapons, and they can no longer keep up with my strength.”
Ye Tian murmured.
In the following days, he began forging an Eternal Source Weapon.
Of course, he first spent a lot of materials tempering his weapon-refining skill.
In fact, he often refined source weapons for the human race. Now, refining ordinary source weapons was very simple for him; it was just that the success rate for refining top-grade source weapons was not very high.
But this time, he had gained too much. The materials for refining top-grade source weapons, he had obtained in endless quantities, and he was not afraid of consumption at all.
Time passed, and in the blink of an eye, dozens of tribulation eras had gone by.
During these dozens of tribulation eras, Ye Tian almost day and night tempered his weapon-refining level, and finally he had nearly a one hundred percent certainty of refining top-grade source weapons.
So, he began refining Eternal Source Weapons.
With so many Undying Primal Stones, it was enough for him to waste them, and there was no fear of failing.
The first refinement failed; Ye Tian wasted one Undying Primal Stone.
But he did not feel distressed and continued refining.
Just like that, Ye Tian failed at refining again and again, but his experience became more and more abundant, and his mistakes became fewer and fewer.
Finally, after wasting seventeen Undying Primal Stones, Ye Tian finally succeeded in refining an Eternal Source Weapon, a sword.
Ye Tian named this weapon the Endless Eternal Sword!
Afterwards.
Ye Tian refined another Eternal Source Weapon-level full-body armor, which consumed a full six Undying Primal Stones.
A set of full-body armor, a sword.
These two kinds of Eternal Source Weapons increased Ye Tian’s strength by at least more than thirty percent.
Afterward, Ye Tian bestowed some of the source weapons he had refined upon the Supreme Beings of the human race. After all, these things were of little use to him.
With Eternal Source Weapons in hand, Ye Tian no longer refined Eternal Source Weapons.
He called his wife, Xiao Yue.
“Husband!” Xiao Yue came over.
“Yue’er, this is the heart of the Primal Tree. Use it to refine a special clone. The clone and the true body will have the same strength, and it can also cultivate, with mutual sharing of insights.”
Ye Tian took out the heart of the Primal Tree and handed it to Xiao Yue.
Such a good thing—he only had one right now, so he would only give it to his wife, not even his son, let alone anyone else.
“It can be used to refine a clone?”
Xiao Yue was extremely excited.
She did not know the value of the heart of the Primal Tree; otherwise, she would not have accepted it so readily.
After that, Xiao Yue took the heart of the Primal Tree away to refine a clone.
Not long after, Xiao Yue’s clone was successfully refined, with no real difference from the true body.

Xiao Yue said excitedly.
“Go ahead. If you encounter an utterly desperate danger, just Annihilate the clone directly. If the clone falls, it’s no big deal. You can spend some resources to condense it again, so don’t be reluctant!” Ye Tian reminded her.
“Husband, I understand!”
Xiao Yue nodded.
Afterward, Ye Tian no longer bothered with his wife Xiao Yue and let her go out to train as she pleased.
As for himself, he went into seclusion once more.
This time, he again copied the body of a Innate Eternal Life Form, and at the same time his true body began to consume Eternal Comprehension Flowers.
He had three Eternal Comprehension Flowers. Even half-step Eternal Realm experts would be incomparably eager to obtain one.
Moreover, Ye Tian possessed the Immortal Ancestor Gift, allowing him to bring out the effects of the Eternal Comprehension Flower to a greater extent.
The benefits he gained from absorbing one Eternal Comprehension Flower were absolutely comparable to the benefits other half-step Eternal Realm experts would gain from absorbing dozens or even hundreds of Eternal Comprehension Flowers.
This time, Ye Tian planned to use these three Eternal Comprehension Flowers to condense innate Eternal rules.
Eternal rules were divided into ordinary Eternal rules and innate Eternal rules. For example, the Eternal rules engraved within an Innate Eternal Life Form’s body were innate Eternal rules, which was what made Innate Eternal Life Forms so valuable and so immensely promising.
And for ordinary lifeforms, wanting to condense innate Eternal rules was almost impossible, unless they entered the Eternal Realm; only then could they condense innate Eternal rules and grasp them.
Although Innate Eternal Life Forms possessed innate Eternal rules within their bodies, in reality they had not mastered them. Only after breaking through to the Eternal Realm could they control them freely. This was also one of their flaws.
But compared with other lifeforms, Innate Eternal Life Forms unquestionably had too great an advantage.
“Among half-step Eternal Realm experts, only those half-step Eternal Realm experts who have grasped innate Eternal rules are truly nearly invincible. Half of the Twelve Ancestors have grasped innate Eternal rules, which is why they can stand above other half-step Eternal Realm experts and make countless top great powers of the Source Sea fear them.”
Ye Tian murmured.
His comprehension of Eternal rules was already very deep, but he was still a little short of condensing innate Eternal rules.
Therefore, he planned to rely on the three Eternal Comprehension Flowers he had obtained this time; he was absolutely hopeful of succeeding in one go.
This time, the innate Eternal rule he intended to condense was strength!
Because he possessed the Immortal Ancestor Gift of strength, he had the greatest chance of condensing the strength innate Eternal rule.
