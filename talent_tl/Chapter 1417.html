Chapter 1416: Gu Heng, the Domain Ancestor whose Invincible Domain Body underwent three transformations
!
The Void Territory.
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor and Blood Scar Domain Ancestor began to fight. Originally, the battle's impact from an Invincible Domain Ancestor would be terrifying, but within the Void Territory, the fluctuations were confined to a certain range.
Blood Scar Domain Ancestor transformed into countless blood-colored figures. This was a special combat avatar, but it couldn't exist for long. However, it was terrifying in battle. If those of the same rank had no way to counter it, they would be instantly killed.
"This is Blood Scar Domain Ancestor's famous Saint Skill—Blood Shadow. It's a Saint Skill he created himself; others cannot learn it!"
Someone commented.
Facing the Blood Shadow Saint Skill, other Two-step Invincible Domain Ancestors would be very solemn. But Five Thunder Domain Ancestor merely smiled faintly, clasped his hands together, and his entire body began to expand, transforming into an earth-shattering Thunder Body.
Countless blood shadows struck Five Thunder Domain Ancestor's Thunder Body. However, countless blood shadows were annihilated by the Thunder Body's thunder power, and the Thunder Body was not shattered.
"No, this isn't the Thunder Extreme Saint Body. The Thunder Extreme Saint Body isn't this powerful!"
Blood Scar Domain Ancestor was dumbfounded.
Soon, he noticed a clue.
"Your Thunder Extreme Saint Body has broken its limits."
Blood Scar Domain Ancestor said solemnly.
If the Saint Body breaks its limits, the Saint Body will gradually transform towards an Ancestral Body.
This is just like Saint Skills; once a Saint Skill breaks its limits, it will transform into an Ancestral Skill.
The same applies to Saint Bodies! However, for a Saint Body to transform into an Ancestral Body, the difficulty is greater. Even if it breaks its limits, it's impossible to transform into an Ancestral Body all at once.
At this moment, Five Thunder Domain Ancestor smiled and said, "Blood Scar Domain Ancestor, my Saint Body is still far from reaching the Ancestral Body realm, but it already possesses a trace of Ancestral Body characteristics, which is the Half-step Ancestral Body realm! With your strength, you probably can't completely break through my Five Thunder Saint Body's defense!"
"Half-step Ancestral Body, impressive. I am indeed not your opponent. You must have reached the combat power of a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor, right? I am no match!"
Blood Scar Domain Ancestor directly conceded defeat.
In reality, he only possessed the combat power of a Two-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. Although infinitely close to a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor, he was not yet one.
Blood Scar Domain Ancestor's concession meant that Blood Scar Domain Ancestor would no longer interfere.
"Is there anyone else who wishes to spar?"
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor asked.
"I'll give it a try!"
Mo Xie Domain Ancestor of the Azure Sky Sacred Gate spoke.
"Mo Xie Domain Ancestor, please proceed!"
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor said calmly.
Mo Xie Domain Ancestor flew into the Void Territory, and began to engage Five Thunder Domain Ancestor.
Mo Xie Domain Ancestor was seen holding a black longsword. This was his top-tier Saint Weapon—the Mo Xie Sword. It was specially forged for him by a Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor. Its power was terrifying, comparable to the top hundred weapon-type Saint Artifacts on the Saint Artifact Ranking.
"Ten Thousand Swords!!!"
Countless sword lights slashed towards Five Thunder Domain Ancestor. Each sword light contained immense power.
"One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor!"
Many Invincible Domain Ancestors had previously heard rumors that Mo Xie Domain Ancestor was merely a Two-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. However, the strength he now displayed reached that of a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. And not just an ordinary One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor; he had probably already been promoted to a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor long ago.
"He hides his true depth!"
Many Invincible Domain Ancestors thought to themselves.
At this moment, Five Thunder Domain Ancestor did not sit idly by. Instead, he also unleashed his Saint Skill—Five Thunder Seal! Boom!!!!! Five thunder seals appeared, blasting towards Mo Xie Domain Ancestor's countless sword lights.
Instantly, the Five Thunder Seals and Mo Xie Domain Ancestor's Ten Thousand Swords Saint Skill collided, and both were annihilated in the collision.
Mo Xie Domain Ancestor's sword qi was completely annihilated. However, one of Five Thunder Domain Ancestor's Five Thunder Seals remained. This single Five Thunder Seal tore through space-time, slammed into Mo Xie Domain Ancestor's body, and instantly severely wounded him.
If Invincible Domain Ancestors were truly to fight to the death, it would likely take a very long time.
Therefore, Mo Xie Domain Ancestor would not renege and insist on fighting to the death.
At this moment, he was injured. Furthermore, his strongest attack could not harm Five Thunder Domain Ancestor. This already counted as a loss.
"I concede!"
Mo Xie Domain Ancestor conceded.
"Anyone else?"
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor asked.
Many Invincible Domain Ancestors fell silent. Most of them were Two-step Invincible Domain Ancestors. There were indeed some One-step Invincible Domain Ancestors, but they believed their strength was inferior to Five Thunder Domain Ancestor's. If they forced themselves to fight, they would not only lose but also expose their own strength, which was completely unnecessary.
Just as everyone was about to give up, an Invincible Domain Ancestor spoke.
"Five Thunder Domain Ancestor, I'll challenge you!"
Everyone looked over, only to see that it was an Invincible Domain Ancestor from the Immortal Sacred Gate—Gu Heng Domain Ancestor! Gu Heng Domain Ancestor was very low-key, and his specific strength was unknown.
At this moment, for Gu Heng Domain Ancestor to dare challenge Five Thunder Domain Ancestor, his strength must have definitely reached the combat power of a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
"Gu Heng Domain Ancestor, make your move!"
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor said, still unperturbed.
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor entered the Void Territory, and the aura on his body began to spread.
Rumble!!!!! An incredibly ancient and powerful aura suppressed all ages, far surpassing the momentum of a Two-step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Moreover, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor's physique was actually filled with supreme majesty. This was not an Invincible Domain Body.
"You've broken the limits of your Domain Body!"
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor was surprised.
"Indeed, I have already broken through the Domain Body's progression, and am transforming towards a Supreme Domain Body. The shackles between Invincible Domain Ancestor and Supreme Domain Ancestor can no longer hinder me.
For an Invincible Domain Ancestor to transform into a Supreme Domain Ancestor, nine transformations are required. And I have already completed three transformations.
You should know what three transformations represent. Although I haven't surpassed the scope of a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor, I subtly exceed the combat power of a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. If you only have the strength you just showed, I'm afraid you won't be able to defeat me!"
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor said.
"Congratulations, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor. It seems your Immortal Sacred Gate is about to produce another Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor!"
Five Thunder Domain Ancestor congratulated.
"Congratulations, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor!"
"Congratulations, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor!"
"Congratulations, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor!"
One after another, Invincible Domain Ancestors offered their congratulations, their tones filled with envy.
Within the Dark Sacred Domain, there was no shortage of Invincible Domain Ancestors whose strength surpassed Gu Heng Domain Ancestor's. There were even monstrous talents whose combat power exceeded that of a One-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. But what did that matter? As long as they couldn't break the limits of the Invincible Domain Body, they couldn't be promoted to a Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor.
An Invincible Domain Ancestor was almost considered the limit of Domain Ancestors. Under normal circumstances, an Invincible Domain Body could hardly break its shackles. This was also why Supreme-tier Domain Ancestors were so rare.
The Sacred Gate also had no good way to help Invincible Domain Ancestors cultivate a Supreme Domain Body. Everything depended on fortuitous encounters, or endless years of grinding.
It could be said that no Invincible Domain Ancestor had a hundred percent certainty of breaking through to the Supreme-tier Domain Ancestor realm. They could only rely on a certain amount of luck.
And now, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor had already broken through the shackles. By cultivating step by step, he could break through to the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm.
At this moment, many Invincible Domain Ancestors looked at Five Thunder Domain Ancestor, eager to know if Five Thunder Domain Ancestor would concede.
However, Five Thunder Domain Ancestor was not flustered in the slightest. Instead, he revealed a smile.
Clearly, Five Thunder Domain Ancestor still had complete confidence in himself.
