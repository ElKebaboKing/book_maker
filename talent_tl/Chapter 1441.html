Chapter 1440: I Am Simply a Genius!
!
After a long silence, the Fire-Pattern War Ancestor finally spoke: "The Nirvana Clan is an existence that brings extinction to all things. They do not seem to exist under the Great Dao; they transcend time, space, and rules. From the moment they are born, they are comparable to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, and the elites among them are even comparable to Primordial Ancestors!"
"What!"
Ye Tian's eyes widened, his face filled with disbelief.
Comparable to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors at birth, with elites rivaling Primordial Ancestors.
Was the Nirvana Clan truly that terrifying? If so, what was the point of cultivation? How could anyone possibly compare to the Nirvana Clan? No wonder even the Fire-Pattern War Ancestor was so fearful; this Nirvana Clan was simply too monstrous! "If the Nirvana Clan is so terrifying, why haven't I heard of them? Logically, they should have invaded the Dark Holy Region long ago!"
Ye Tian asked in confusion.
"Because of the resistance of the Primordial Ancestors!"
The Fire-Pattern War Ancestor said, "Where do you think the Primordial Ancestors are? Do you really think they are in the Dark Holy Region? No, those remaining in the Dark Holy Region are merely the incarnations of the Primordial Ancestors.
Even during the last struggle for the Primordial Ancestral World, it was only the incarnations of the Primordial Ancestors who acted.
The true bodies of the Primordial Ancestors are far more powerful than their incarnations, and they are busy holding back the Nirvana Clan.
Furthermore, some of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors from the Ten Great Holy Sects are also helping to resist the Nirvana Clan. Even certain Holy Lands must dispatch Supreme-level Domain Ancestors to guard the front; whichever Holy Land is called upon must send someone.
For instance, the Golden Star Holy Ancestor of the Qingyuan Holy Land was chosen. He returned heavily injured and has been healing ever since."
"In fact, there are more Supreme-level Domain Ancestors in the Dark Holy Region than you know. Even the number of Primordial Ancestors isn't just ten, but twenty-three. Moreover, the Primordial Ancestors of the Dark Destruction Region must also go to guard the front, and they have even more Primordial Ancestors than we do.
Our Dark Holy Region and the Dark Destruction Region can kill each other all we want; as long as it doesn't involve the Primordial Ancestor level, it doesn't really matter, even if some Supreme-level Domain Ancestors fall.
But when it comes to resisting the Nirvana Clan, there is no room for negotiation—we must resist.
Otherwise, if we fail to hold them back, both the Dark Holy Region and the Dark Destruction Region will be finished.
Additionally, the Dark Forbidden Region is vast. There are also some rogue Primordial Ancestors and rogue Supreme-level Domain Ancestors. Once their tracks are discovered, they must go to guard the front, or they will inevitably be hunted down by all the Primordial Ancestors.
Back then, some Holy Sects suspected there was a Primordial Ancestor behind you, which is why they tested you time and again. When they found no evidence, they eventually dropped the matter!"
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian understood completely.
"Where is the Nirvana Clan?"
Ye Tian asked this final question.
"At the edge of the Dark Forbidden Region, in the Endless Space-Time Corridor. The Dark Forbidden Region seems boundless, but in reality, when the Primordial Ancestors travel through space-time, they can reach any part of it. The capabilities of the Primordial Ancestors far exceed those of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors. Even if a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor flies for billions upon billions of eons, they would never reach the end of the Dark Forbidden Region, but the Primordial Ancestors can reach the end by walking through space-time.
Long ago, while traveling through the depths of space-time, the Primordial Ancestors discovered a dark area in the Endless Space-Time Corridor. That area had permeated into the depths of space-time, which puzzled the Primordial Ancestors greatly. After investigating, they finally discovered the existence of the Nirvana Clan.
A great war broke out; one Primordial Ancestor fell, and six were heavily injured.
Afterward, the Primordial Ancestors gathered together, and only then did they manage to hold back the Nirvana Clan."
"The Nirvana Clan seems to possess no wisdom and cannot be communicated with. Through calculations, it was learned that if the Nirvana Clan isn't stopped, they will extinguish everything, returning all things to one and bringing space-time back to its starting point. The Nirvana Clan can affect and permeate space-time—that is the most terrifying part!"
The Fire-Pattern War Ancestor said with a hint of dread.
Then, he calmed down again, saying: "Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, you don't need to worry. The Primordial Ancestors have held back the Nirvana Clan, so for now, there are no issues.
If the Dark Holy Region gives birth to more Primordial Ancestors, there might even be hope of wiping out the Nirvana Clan in the future!"
"Fire-Pattern War Ancestor, I'm not worried!"
Ye Tian had a very good mindset.
He believed he would definitely become a Primordial Ancestor and might even be able to wipe out the Nirvana Clan single-handedly in the future.
"That's good then!"
The Fire-Pattern War Ancestor was relieved.
He was afraid that after learning about the Nirvana Clan, Ye Tian would lose his motivation to progress and give up on himself; that would be bad.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian asked again: "Fire-Pattern War Ancestor, what is the deal with the Mirror of Time? Why is a person only allowed to enter once in their lifetime? Is it really impossible to enter a second time?"
"The Mirror of Time?"
The Fire-Pattern War Ancestor smiled and said: "That thing was actually forged from a core fragment of a Nirvana Clan Primordial Ancestor's corpse. The Nirvana Clan can erode space-time, so the Mirror of Time can both influence and erode space-time. If you enter a second time, you have already left an imprint within the Mirror of Time.
After all, during the first entry, almost everyone stays until they are at their limit before leaving. The second time, the impact is even greater; you will be directly unable to resist the erosion of space-time and will be assimilated by time, or rather, assimilated by the power of the Nirvana Clan.
The Mirror of Time cannot assimilate a Primordial Ancestor, but it is also useless to them, or simply doesn't work on them.
And those below the Primordial Ancestor level have no way to resist the erosion of time, which is why it can only be entered once!"
"So that's the reason."
Ye Tian understood.
In other words, the first time he entered the Mirror of Time, it remembered him. Entering a second time would subject him to the erosion of time, which those below the Primordial Ancestor level could almost never resist; they would one hundred percent be eroded and wouldn't even be able to withdraw in time.
But now that Ye Tian understood the principle behind the Mirror of Time, it actually wasn't that mysterious.
If he used his Copy Talent to copy someone else's body and then entered the Mirror of Time, he would be equivalent to a different person.
He would only need to pay the cost to open the Mirror of Time; it wouldn't matter how many times he used it.
However, once he stepped into the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm, the Mirror of Time would likely be of little use to him, as ordinary cultivation insights can hardly be improved rapidly there.
Aside from providing a long duration of time, the Mirror of Time had no other special benefits.
"Then I'll use the Mirror of Time to practice Ancestral Techniques. I'll strive to use that endless stretch of time to practice each Ancestral Technique to the Great Success or even the Perfection stage."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, when copying someone else's body, that person must also possess Ancestral Talents. Otherwise, a body without Ancestral Talents would gain nothing from entering the Mirror of Time; even after practicing for endless years, they wouldn't be able to improve their Ancestral Techniques!
For others, this was an unsolvable problem, but for Ye Tian, it was simple.
He could capture some sinful Domain Ancestors, bestow upon them various Ancestral Talents, then kill them and copy their bodies. The bodies copied this way would then possess Ancestral Talents.
"I am simply a genius!"
Ye Tian couldn't help but praise his own plan.
Subsequently, he began to put this plan into motion.
Once the plan succeeded, he could practice his Ancestral Techniques to the Perfection stage in a short amount of time, and his strength would skyrocket many times over.
