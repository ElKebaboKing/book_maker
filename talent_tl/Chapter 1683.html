Chapter 1682: Combat Power Second Only to a Legendary Lord!

“Copy!”
Ye Tian spent some resources to copy a body of a third-tier Nihility Beast.
At that time, he could completely hide a copied body inside the third-tier Nihility Beast’s body, and then hunt other Nihility Beasts, while easily evading the pursuit of other Nihility Beasts.
Moreover, Ye Tian could switch to another Nihility Beast body again and again. Even a fifth-tier Nihility Beast would be unable to find his existence.
In this way, he could hunt a large number of Nihility Beasts in a short period of time.
……
Somewhere in the Nihility region.
A first-tier Nihility Beast was roaming aimlessly when a third-tier Nihility Beast suddenly appeared.
This third-tier Nihility Beast was none other than Ye Tian.
When the first-tier Nihility Beast saw the third-tier Nihility Beast, it conveyed a sense of reverence and then silently prepared to avoid it.
But at that moment, the third-tier Nihility Beast that Ye Tian had copied immediately attacked the first-tier Nihility Beast. At the same time, the copied body also shuttled out from within the third-tier Nihility Beast’s body and used an intent eternal technique to attack this third-tier Nihility Beast.
Almost in an instant, that first-tier Nihility Beast was instantly killed by Ye Tian.
Then, Ye Tian collected the corpse of that first-tier Nihility Beast and once again entered the third-tier Nihility Beast’s body.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian used this method to kill many first-tier Nihility Beasts, and even occasionally targeted second-tier Nihility Beasts, but he was never caught. After all, no one would have thought that a fierce beast would contain a third-tier Nihility Beast.
After hunting many Nihility Beasts, Ye Tian prepared to rest for a period of time and refine the materials from these Nihility Beasts to enhance the fusion of his intent and body. Once the materials were used up, he would continue hunting Nihility Beasts.
As time passed, the degree of fusion between Ye Tian’s intent and body grew higher and higher.
However, first-tier Nihility Beasts were no longer of much help to Ye Tian. If he were to step into the Lord level of the Four-Step Immortal Realm, first-tier Nihility Beasts would probably be of no use at all. By then, he would have to rely on second-tier or even third-tier Nihility Beasts.
After many long years, Ye Tian’s Nihility Beast materials were completely exhausted.
So on this day, he began hunting Nihility Beasts again.
At this time, although the fifth-tier Nihility Beasts knew that a powerful being was hunting Nihility Beasts, they had no way to deal with it.
At first, they could still more or less ignore such losses, but as a large number of second-tier Nihility Beasts fell, they became anxious.
And whenever Ye Tian’s third-tier Nihility Beast was exposed, he would immediately switch to another third-tier Nihility Beast.
As time went on, the fifth-tier Nihility Beasts even suspected that many third-tier Nihility Beasts were participating in such hunts, and in the end they had no choice but to restrain their subordinates and gather them all together, forbidding them from going out privately.
In this way, Ye Tian really had no way to continue hunting.
But.
He could not hunt here, but the Nihility region was not limited to these Nihility Beasts alone. There were also other Nihility Beasts.
Therefore, Ye Tian would never lack Nihility Beast materials.
One Eternal Year after another passed, and Ye Tian’s strength also grew stronger and stronger.
After 300 Eternal Years, the degree of fusion between Ye Tian’s intent and body had reached sixty percent, and at this point even second-tier Nihility Beasts were of no use to him.
His cultivation had reached the Lord level of the Six-Step Immortal Realm, but his true strength was probably even stronger than a Nine-Step Immortal Realm Lord.
Because, with the help of the Eternal Treasure fragment, Ye Tian had created two of the most top-tier legendary eternal techniques.
One was Nihility Step, which allowed him to instantly cross vast stretches of the Nihility region; in terms of speed, even a Legendary Lord was probably inferior to him.
This was a high-level application of the Eternal Spatial Dao. Without that Eternal Treasure fragment, Ye Tian would also have found it very difficult to create such a top-tier legendary eternal technique.
The second legendary eternal technique was Myriad Layers of Nihility, a spatial legendary eternal technique involving intent. It created endless layers of void to suppress enemies; if the enemy’s strength was relatively weak, they would be directly crushed into nothingness.
In order to create these two legendary eternal techniques, Ye Tian spent a full 300 Eternal Years. And this was with the help of the Eternal Treasure fragment; if he had relied on himself alone, he would simply not have been able to create such legendary eternal techniques.
It could be imagined how powerful these two legendary eternal techniques were, far surpassing ordinary legendary eternal techniques.
With these two top-tier legendary eternal techniques, plus other legendary eternal techniques and a large number of intent eternal techniques, even though his cultivation had only reached the Lord level of the Six-Step Immortal Realm, he had already far surpassed other Immortal Realm Lords.
Many Immortal Realm Lords probably did not even know a single legendary eternal technique, or at most only one or two.
“In the Source Sea Time-Space Territory, my strength is probably second only to the six Legendary Lords!”
Ye Tian murmured.
This day.
Ye Tian prepared to leave the depths of the Nihility region; there was no need to continue staying here.
First- and second-tier Nihility Beasts were of little use to him, and if he wanted to kill third-tier Nihility Beasts, he simply could not achieve instant annihilation. And once third-tier Nihility Beasts were given time, fifth-tier Nihility Beasts would descend.
So, he did not plan to continue hunting Nihility Beasts.
Relying on Nihility Beast materials could indeed improve the fusion of intent and body, but it was very difficult to use Nihility Beast materials to break through to the Legendary Lord realm.
The six Legendary Lords of the Time-Space Territory absolutely did not break through by relying on Nihility Beast materials.
The Time-Space Territory also had many unknown opportunities, some of which might not be inferior to opportunities like the Nihility Beasts.
This day.
Ye Tian’s copied body in the relic world rushed out of the relic world, and the other Nihility Beasts simply could not intercept it.
Afterward, this copied body hid within a third-tier Nihility Beast’s body and evaded the pursuit of the fifth-tier Nihility Beasts.
Then, this third-tier Nihility Beast took Ye Tian farther and farther away from this area, shuttling toward the Source Sea Time-Space Territory.
Ye Tian used Nihility Step, and his body crossed the vast Nihility region. Before long, he returned to the Time-Space Territory and also successfully brought back that Eternal Treasure fragment.
Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian placed the Eternal Treasure fragment here, and used the Eternal Treasure fragment to create a treasure space. Only the true core members of the human race had the chance to enter here to cultivate, while his true body had also been guarding this place all along, constantly cultivating with the Eternal Treasure fragment.
He did not want to carry such an Eternal Treasure fragment on a copied body. If it were to fall in the Time-Space Territory or some other treasure land, wouldn’t the Eternal Treasure fragment be lost?
Therefore, placing such an Eternal Treasure fragment in the Dark Forbidden Domain was undoubtedly the safest method.
This day.
Ye Tian prepared to select a Time-Space Territory in the Time-Space Territory as his own territory. After all, if in the future the human race gave birth to a Half-Step Eternal Realm or an Extreme Realm, they could also be brought into the Time-Space Territory to cultivate. Therefore, he must have a piece of Time-Space Territory that belonged to him.
After all, every Lord in the Time-Space Territory had their own Time-Space Territory, and he had already become an Immortal Realm Lord. If he did not have a Time-Space Territory of his own, that would be far too strange.
