Chapter 1688: The Boundless Sea far more powerful than the Source Sea!

The second prisoner Ye Tian chose was Lord Chiming.
Afterwards, Ye Tian entered Lord Chiming’s prison cell and fought with Lord Chiming. Very soon, he heavily injured Lord Chiming, and not long after that, he killed Lord Chiming.
On Lord Chiming, Ye Tian found no treasures at all.
Not all prisoners had treasures on them, and Lord Chiming happened to be one who did not have such treasures.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian killed prisoner after prisoner, and one Stele after another collapsed, meaning the prisoners inside had fallen.
Roughly one out of every three or four of these prisoners had rather good treasures on them, which made Ye Tian’s wealth grow more and more. However, there were only about a thousand or so Immortal Realm lord-level prisoners, and the Eternal treasures Ye Tian could truly obtain were probably only about three or four hundred.
Even so, his wealth was probably already enough to surpass the six guild leaders.
Unfortunately, he could not beat those legend realm lord prisoners; otherwise, his gains would have been even greater.
After a long time, the number of Immortal Realm lord falls had already reached two hundred.
If other Immortal Realm lords knew that Ye Tian had killed two hundred Immortal Realm lord prisoners, they would absolutely find it unbelievable.
The Source Sea Time-Space Territory only had about a hundred Immortal Realm lords, yet Ye Tian had killed two hundred Immortal Realm prisoners here. What an astonishing number that was.
But from this, it could also be seen that Mo Shan was far too powerful a force, having imprisoned so many Immortal Realm lords and legend realm lords, far beyond what the Time-Space Territory could compare to.
Mo Shan, Jingxu, True Void Legend Land—these places definitely did not belong to the Time-Space Territory, but as for where they actually belonged, Ye Tian really did not know.
Of course, he was not looking into that for now. He only wanted to obtain more resources and opportunities.
In another prison cell.
Ye Tian, as usual, prepared to kill the Immortal Realm prisoner here. This prisoner was called Lord Yuyi, a female lord whose appearance was not much different from that of the human race.
Very soon, Ye Tian fought fiercely with her and displayed all of his strength, easily heavily injuring her.
Just as Ye Tian was about to kill her, Lord Yuyi actually begged for mercy.
“Don’t kill me!”
Lord Yuyi pleaded.
This was the first time Ye Tian had seen a prisoner beg for mercy. The prisoners before had all been mentally unhinged and simply impossible to communicate with, while Yuyi had never spoken much during their previous fights, yet now she was begging for mercy.
Instantly, Ye Tian became interested and stopped fighting.
“Among so many prisoners in the Mo Shan prison, you are the only one who has begged for mercy,” Ye Tian said.
Lord Yuyi said, “I don’t want to die, and I can feel that this time you truly intend to kill me, so I can only beg for mercy. As long as you don’t kill me, I’ll agree to any condition.”
Ye Tian was silent for a moment, and a decision formed in his heart.
“I’ll ask you some things. If you answer truthfully, I won’t kill you.”
“Okay!”
Lord Yuyi hurriedly agreed.
Ye Tian asked, “I want to know the origins of Mo Shan.”
“I’ll tell you!” Lord Yuyi recounted the origins of Mo Shan one by one.
From Yuyi’s words, Ye Tian learned what kind of force Mo Shan was.
Mo Shan was a very ancient force, and Lord Yuyi was not clear about Mo Shan’s current situation. She only spoke of the Mo Shan she knew about.
Mo Shan had three legend realm lords, many legendary realm lords, and as for Immortal Realm lords, there were simply too many. To join a force like Mo Shan, the entry requirement was at least Eternal Realm or eighth-level half-step Eternal Realm; otherwise, one was not qualified to join Mo Shan at all.
Moreover, Mo Shan was absolutely ruthless toward traitors. Once someone joined Mo Shan, they were no longer allowed to join any other force; otherwise, they would definitely be hunted down by Mo Shan. There had even once been an extremely powerful legendary realm lord, an existence second only to legend realm lords, who betrayed Mo Shan and was still killed by Mo Shan’s legend realm lords.
Just as Ye Tian had guessed, Mo Shan was not a force of the Time-Space Territory, and Lord Yuyi also did not know anything about the Time-Space Territory. Both she and Mo Shan came from another place—the Boundless Sea.
The Boundless Sea was the world where Mo Shan existed, and Mo Shan was only one of many forces in the Boundless Sea. There were quite a few forces comparable to Mo Shan, and even forces even stronger than Mo Shan.
And within the Boundless Sea, legend realm lords were the true peak existences. Legendary realm lords could not even be considered top-tier combat strength, but they were still overlords of a region, while Immortal Realm lords could only be called invincible within a small area.
“Do you know Jingxu?”
Ye Tian asked.
“Jingxu?” Lord Yuyi recalled for a moment, then nodded. “I’ve heard of it. Jingxu’s strength is far weaker than Mo Shan; they are not on the same level. Jingxu should not have any legend realm lords, but it does have a half-step legend realm lord, which is why it has some fame in the Boundless Sea, though not much.”

“As expected, Jingxu and Mo Shan both belong to the same place. Back then, there should have been a great war erupting in the Boundless Sea, which caused the two places, Jingxu and Mo Shan, to directly traverse time and space and arrive here, becoming ruins here,” Ye Tian thought to himself.
“Then that True Void Legend Tower, have you heard of it?” Ye Tian asked.
“I have!”
Lord Yuyi immediately replied, “The True Void Legend Tower is the emblematic assessment tower of the True Void Sacred Land in the Boundless Sea. The True Void Sacred Land is a top force that stands above Mo Shan; it has more than ten legend realm lords alone, and is by no means comparable to Mo Shan.”

“Now this place is no longer in the Boundless Sea. Do you know how to go to the Boundless Sea?” Ye Tian asked again.
He would definitely want to go to a higher world in the future, so the Boundless Sea was certainly a very good choice.
“So it really is no longer in the Boundless Sea. Back then, I did sense a terrifying shock in the prison cell, and thought Mo Shan had been destroyed by enemies, but I never waited for the enemies of Mo Shan to arrive. If I had not used a secret technique to keep my will and mind from being affected, I might not have been able to endure that endless loneliness and may have gone mad as well. As for how to return to the Boundless Sea, I really don’t know. If this place truly detached from the Boundless Sea, then it would have entered a void region, and I cannot locate the Boundless Sea,” Lord Yuyi said, shaking her head.
Ye Tian also did not truly expect Lord Yuyi to be able to guide him to the Boundless Sea; he was only asking. Since Lord Yuyi could not help him now, he gave up on the idea. Perhaps when his strength became even greater, he would have a chance to find the Boundless Sea.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian continued communicating with Lord Yuyi and learned many secrets.
After a long time, Ye Tian finally left Lord Yuyi’s prison cell, and he also kept his promise and did not kill Lord Yuyi.
However, he would not release Lord Yuyi either; in fact, he could not release her anyway.
Next, Ye Tian continued killing the other prisoners, but on this day, he encountered a true opponent. The other side was definitely a master at hiding strength—despite having terrifying power, they never revealed it.
