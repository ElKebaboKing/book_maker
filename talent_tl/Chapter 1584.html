Chapter 1583: Eternal Resources!

What a mysterious existence the Void Source Hall was, and Ye Jinpeng never expected that his own ancestor was actually the master of the Void Source Hall?
To be honest, he really couldn't quite believe it.
But if Ye Tian wasn't the master of the Void Source Hall, why could he become a Void Son with just a single word?
At this time, Ye Tian said, "Jinpeng, passing the first floor of the Void Tower makes one a Void Son. Passing the second floor of the Void Tower makes one a Void Divine Son. In the entire Source Sea, there are not many Void Divine Sons, only thirty-nine, and the Tianlin Realm doesn't even have a single Void Divine Son. If one passes the third floor of the Void Tower, they can become a Void Lord, and by becoming a Void Lord, they can become the master of the Void Source Hall. And your ancestor, I, am a Void Lord, and the only Void Lord!"
"Ancestor is too amazing!"
Ye Jinpeng said with incomparable admiration.
Ye Tian said, "Next, you will cultivate in the Void Source Hall. I will disguise you as a Void Divine Son, allowing you to constantly contemplate the Void Source Stele, but I will not give you too many points, only 10,000 points in total. After all, I cannot help you forever, otherwise, letting you develop a dependent character would instead harm you!"
Ye Jinpeng hurriedly said, "Ancestor, you have already helped me a lot!"
In the following days, Ye Jinpeng cultivated in the Void Source Hall, frequently contemplating the Void Source Stele, and his strength improved very quickly.
Perhaps Ye Jinpeng could not become the most top-tier existence within the Source Sea, but becoming a Supreme Being was not difficult.
At that time, if Ye Tian nurtured another Supreme Being, the Dark Forbidden Domain could evolve into an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
And this was also one of his goals.
......
Currently, Ye Tian was a first-class Invincible. What he needed to do was continue cultivating his cultivation base and his Endless Source Body to the Peak realm, then he could charge toward the Supreme Being realm.
Ye Tian could choose to condense the Eternal Tribulation Body at the first-class Invincible realm, or he could choose not to condense the Eternal Tribulation Body and directly break through to the Supreme Being realm.
But condensing the Eternal Tribulation Body still had benefits; at the very least, it could increase his compatibility with Eternal Source Objects to 30%, while the Endless Source Body after its second growth could increase compatibility by 50%.
In other words, after condensing the Eternal Tribulation Body, Ye Tian's compatibility with all Eternal Source Objects could reach a level of 80%.
Although even 100% could not perfectly absorb the essence of Eternal Source Objects, a secret technique for absorbing the Void Source Stele left by the Void Source Ancestor could increase compatibility somewhat. Additionally, there was a treasure that enhanced compatibility with the Void Source Stele—the Void Source Heart, which came from the heart of a Devouring Source Dragon king.
This kind of treasure could increase compatibility by at least 30%, thus allowing Ye Tian to perfectly absorb the essence of the Void Source Stele.
This was why the Void Source Ancestor was confident that his successor could perfectly use the essence of the Void Source Stele along with a large amount of other Eternal Source Object essences to break through to the Supreme Being realm. Therefore, if Ye Tian wanted to break through using a large amount of various Eternal Source Object essences, he had to condense the Eternal Tribulation Body.
The Eternal Tribulation Body was the evolved version of the Invincible Tribulation Body. Although the Endless Source Body was very powerful and had even completed its second growth, it still belonged to a type of Invincible Tribulation Body.
Ordinary Invincible Tribulation Bodies required many special resources to evolve into an Eternal Tribulation Body, and countless first-class Invincibles worked for long ages without being able to collect those special resources.
One could imagine how many special resources Ye Tian would need to evolve the Endless Source Body into the Endless Eternal Tribulation Body?
And these special resources were known as Eternal Resources in the Source Sea.
Treasures similar to Void Source Crystals were not Eternal Resources. The value of a single portion of Eternal Resources did not exceed an Eternal Source Object, but because they were too rare, they simply could not be bought, and no organization would sell them.
Even for a Supreme Being, it was very difficult to collect Eternal Resources.
For example, the Void Source Ancestor, such a powerful existence, did not have many Eternal Resources; the main point was that he had never spent effort collecting them.
After all, the Void Source Ancestor no longer had any use for Eternal Resources, so why would he spend time collecting them!
Currently, the Void Source Hall only had sixteen portions of Eternal Resources.
For an ordinary first-class Invincible, one portion of Eternal Resources was enough. For an ordinary Void Son, they would likely need more than five portions. A first-class Invincible at the level of a Void Divine Son would need more than ten portions, and a Void Lord would need around twenty portions.
But Ye Tian felt he needed at least twenty-five portions of Eternal Resources before he could dare to guarantee that he could improve the Endless Source Body to the level of the Endless Eternal Tribulation Body.
However, the Void Source Hall did not have twenty-five portions of Eternal Resources; it was still short by nine portions.
Therefore, Ye Tian now urgently needed to find Eternal Resources.
· ·········Seeking Flowers··· ·····
"The main body will stay in the Void Source Hall to improve cultivation and the Endless Source Body; the duplicated body should go out to find Eternal Resources. Nine portions of Eternal Resources—I don't know how long it will take to find them. I must look for them in advance, otherwise, when it really comes time to charge toward the Supreme Being realm, there won't be time to look for them!"
Ye Tian muttered.
Regarding the Void Source Ancestor's side, Ye Tian did not count on obtaining any other Eternal Resources. Even if he used points to exchange for them, no one would trade.
The points of the Void Source Hall could not be traded for Eternal Resources; no one would be so foolish as to trade Eternal Resources for Void Source Hall points.
Unless one truly encountered the kind of person who didn't lack Eternal Resources, but was there such a person in the Source Sea?
"Jin Ancestor!"
Ye Tian thought of this fallen powerhouse. Unfortunately, there were no Eternal Resources within the other party's Void Sphere either, but from the other party's memories, Ye Tian learned a useful piece of information.
.... . ...
Although the birth of Eternal Resources had no pattern, they had one thing in common: Eternal Resources would only be born in eternally indestructible places.
For example, Eternal Heaven and Earth, or eternally existing treasure lands and forbidden lands. Of course, the Source Sea was also eternally indestructible, so it was possible for Eternal Resources to be born within the Void of the Source Sea.
However, the Source Sea was too large, so finding Eternal Resources in the Source Sea was too difficult.
Consequently, many first-class Invincibles wanted to go to those treasure lands or even forbidden lands that had existed for countless ages to find Eternal Resources.
Especially forbidden lands; there was a high probability they possessed Eternal Resources because for long ages, few people dared to enter, resulting in Eternal Resources remaining even if they were born.
Whereas even if Eternal Resources were born in treasure lands, they would quickly be snatched away, so there wasn't much hope in going to treasure lands to find Eternal Resources.
Back then, the Tianfeng Source God found a portion of Eternal Resources in the depths of the Yuanji Abyss. Combined with the Eternal Resources found previously, he was finally able to complete the final transformation in one go and condense the Eternal Tribulation Body.
And it was only because the Yuanji Abyss was too dangerous and few first-class Invincibles went there that a portion of Eternal Resources remained.
"Artifact Spirit!"
Ye Tian called out to the Void Source Hall Artifact Spirit.
The Artifact Spirit immediately appeared, waiting for Ye Tian's orders.
Ye Tian said, "You should have some understanding of the many forbidden lands within the Source Sea. List some forbidden lands that are extremely likely to produce Eternal Resources!"
