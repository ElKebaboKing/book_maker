Chapter 1556: The Three Key Steps to Becoming a Supreme Being!

Deep within the Yuanji Abyss.
All six First-Class Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea had gathered here; these were all the First-Class Invincibles the Tianfeng Sea had in the Yuanji Abyss, aside from their leader.
In reality, the Tianfeng Sea naturally had more than just a few First-Class Invincibles, but those within the Tianlin Realm did not dare to come out. One had ventured out previously and was instantly killed by a Supreme Being from the Nether Hall.
For a Supreme Being to kill an Invincible was a shameless act, but that was exactly what the Nether Hall had done. Moreover, since it happened outside the Tianlin Realm and did not involve its internal affairs, even the three Supreme Beings of the Tianlin Realm found it difficult to restrain the Supreme Being of the Nether Hall.
Therefore, the Tianfeng Sea had no reinforcements.
The Nether Hall was different; they had dispatched thirty-two First-Class Invincibles to surround the leader of the Tianfeng Sea. Had the leader not been in such a dangerous location, he would have been dead long ago.
"Charge!"
The six First-Class Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea instantly attacked from one direction, attempting to break through the Nether Hall's blockade and enter the space where their leader was located.
However, they had still underestimated the Nether Hall.
Boom!!!!
The battle between dozens of First-Class Invincibles created massive fluctuations, causing the entire Yuanji Abyss to tremble.
Many Invincibles realized that a battle involving numerous First-Class Invincibles was taking place, prompting First-Class Invincibles from other organizations to wonder what was happening.
Soon, the news spread.
The Invincibles of the Nether Hall and the Tianfeng Sea were clashing.
Many Invincibles in the Yuanji Abyss had heard about the conflict between the Nether Hall and the Tianfeng Sea, so they were curious about the outcome and began to pay close attention.
"They've started fighting!"
Ye Tian guessed that the Tianfeng Sea's treasure must have arrived, but he had no idea what the treasure was or what it was used for.
He had no intention of getting involved; after all, with his strength, he didn't even have the qualifications to participate.
He was only a First-Class Invincible, while the combatants on both sides were also First-Class Invincibles—and from Saint-Name and Eternal-Name level powers at that. They could bring Annihilation to him countless times with a mere wave of their hands.
Although he didn't know exactly what was happening, news traveled fast in the Yuanji Abyss. With the inquiries of interested parties, a secret soon leaked out.
"The leader of the Tianfeng Sea actually obtained an opportunity in the Yuanji Abyss to qualify for promotion to the Supreme Being realm!"
Ye Tian was shocked.
He finally understood why the Nether Hall was targeting the Tianfeng Sea. Since the Tianfeng Sea had snatched away this opportunity, how could the Nether Hall not be furious?
Afterward, Ye Tian spent a significant amount of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals to purchase specific intelligence.
For instance, the treasure being escorted by the Tianfeng Sea this time had been identified: it was the Eternal Source Item, Mind Azure Gold.
To become a Supreme Being, one must rely on an Eternal Source Item.
In the Tianlin Realm, it was nearly impossible to find secrets on how to break through to the Supreme Being realm.
But here in the Yuanji Abyss, it was incredibly chaotic. Any secret, knowledge, or even treasure could be put up for sale. Consequently, the secrets of an Invincible breaking through to become a Supreme Being could also be sold, and many Invincibles were aware of them.
An Invincible who has fused the Physical Tribulation Realm and the Soul Tribulation Realm is a First-Class Invincible, but it is exceptionally difficult for a First-Class Invincible to become a Supreme Being.
First, a First-Class Invincible must create a Supreme Technique. This Supreme Technique is related to their future breakthrough, so it must be chosen carefully.
First-Class Invincibles who possess a Supreme Technique are also extremely rare; they are the ones who can truly be called Invincible among First-Class Invincibles.
After creating a Supreme Technique, a First-Class Invincible needs to temper their body into an eternally indestructible Eternal Tribulation Body. This step is even harder; it is impossible to achieve without sufficiently precious resources. Such resources are so rare that even Eternal-Name level powers might not possess them; it all depends on luck.
This time, the leader of the Tianfeng Sea had found such a resource in the Yuanji Abyss, snatched it from the hands of the Nether Hall's First-Class Invincibles, and successfully transformed his body into an Eternal Tribulation Body.
A Supreme Technique and an Eternal Tribulation Body.
After satisfying these two conditions, only the final condition remained: using an Eternal Source Item to make the breakthrough.
An Eternal Source Item contains a mysterious substance; absorbing this substance allows one to break through their shackles and advance to the Supreme Being realm.
However, if the Eternal Source Item's compatibility with oneself is insufficient, a breakthrough is impossible.
Therefore, when creating a Supreme Technique, one must consider it carefully; it is best to create a Supreme Technique that is compatible with an Eternal Source Item.
For example, the Supreme Technique created by the leader of the Tianfeng Sea was compatible with Mind Azure Gold, being a soul-type Supreme Technique.
However, the Eternal Source Item Mind Azure Gold was of great help to the Tianfeng Sea. Many Invincibles used it to temper their souls and mental states, so it had always remained at the Tianfeng Sea.
After all, no one had expected the leader of the Tianfeng Sea to suddenly complete his Eternal Tribulation Body, let alone become trapped and unable to escape, not even daring to leave the Yuanji Abyss.
Thus, out of necessity, he ordered the Mind Azure Gold to be sent to the Yuanji Abyss. Otherwise, having offended the Nether Hall, the future of the Tianfeng Sea would be truly worrisome.
But if a Supreme Being were born, the Tianfeng Sea would no longer fear the Nether Hall.
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian suddenly understood.
As a member of the Tianfeng Sea, he naturally hoped for the birth of a Supreme Being within their ranks, so he would no longer have to fear the Nether Hall.
At the same time, he was very excited.
Eternal Source Items were incredibly precious. Even the Tianfeng Sea likely had very few, perhaps only the Mind Azure Gold. Thus, he estimated it would be impossible to obtain an Eternal Source Item from the Tianfeng Sea, but he had an Eternal Source Item of his own.
The Xumi Golden Mountain!
If he reached the First-Class Invincible realm in the future, he could create a Supreme Technique compatible with the Xumi Golden Mountain, refine it, and advance to become a Supreme Being.
Of course.
He would still need to find the treasure resources to transform his body into an Eternal Tribulation Body; otherwise, it would still be difficult to advance to a Supreme Being.
Just then, deep within the Yuanji Abyss, the battle situation underwent a startling change.
The six First-Class Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea were all heavily injured, but at the critical moment, the leader of the Tianfeng Sea charged out from the depths of the abyss.
Boom!!!
A massive figure emerged from the depths of the Yuanji Abyss. A terrifying soul attack instantly caused the bodies of eight or nine First-Class Invincibles to freeze, followed by a hand that slapped them away, displaying an Invincible posture.
Although he couldn't kill these Invincibles, he could rescue the six Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea in time.
"Go!"
Protecting the six First-Class Invincibles, the leader of the Tianfeng Sea entered the depths of the Yuanji Abyss and successfully retreated.
The many First-Class Invincibles of the Nether Hall were furious, but they did not dare to give chase.
There were a large number of Abyss Tribulation Beasts there, including many comparable to those with over 100,000 Tribulations. However, the leader of the Tianfeng Sea specialized in soul-type Invincible Techniques, one of which could awe the souls of the Abyss Tribulation Beasts, making them wary and afraid to attack.
But they lacked such means. Once they entered that area, they would surely be attacked by a massive number of Abyss Tribulation Beasts, which would inevitably lead to heavy casualties.
On the other side.
The leader of the Tianfeng Sea led the six First-Class Invincibles into a relatively narrow area. Surrounded by many Abyss Tribulation Beasts, they did their best to conceal their auras, while the leader constantly maintained the aura of his soul-type Invincible Technique to keep the beasts from approaching.
"Sovereign Tianfeng, the item has arrived!"
Sovereign Chenling took out the box and said.
Sovereign Tianfeng was the leader of the Tianfeng Sea; this was his title.
