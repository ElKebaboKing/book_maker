Chapter 1472: Creating One Ancestral Method After Another
!
Ye Tian chose the Tyrant Strength Ancestral Technique from among his ancestral techniques as the subject of his research and deduction, striving to deduce the Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
After all, he had already successfully created one Ultimate Ancestral Technique and gained experience; thus, while researching a second one remained difficult, it would at least save some time.
Thus, over the following domain years, Ye Tian spent most of his time on creating the Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
As for the affairs of the human race, he almost completely ignored them, leaving them to be managed by the various Supreme-level Soul Ancestors and Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Temple.
In this way, seventeen domain years passed.
On this day.
Ye Tian finally created the Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique, and his strength soared.
Next, Ye Tian spent another dozen or so domain years creating a speed-type Ultimate Ancestral Technique—the Streamer Ultimate Ancestral Technique! Defense, speed, and strength—with all three of these Ultimate Ancestral Techniques created, it could be said that Ye Tian's strength had exploded, and he had no weaknesses.
Coupled with the continuous refining of massive amounts of Nirvana treasures over dozens of domain years, his cultivation had increased significantly.
Consequently, Ye Tian's current strength had already reached the level of an extremely powerful Elite Domain Ancestor; this was a perception and realization he had as an Ancestor, requiring no combat test.
If he utilized the power of the Dark Soul Domain, Ye Tian would absolutely not fear the Holy Ancestor or the Destruction Ancestor.
"Finally, I no longer need to worry about the Destruction Ancestor and the Holy Ancestor! With dozens of domain years remaining, it will be difficult for my clone, the Green Leaf Ancestor, to increase its strength further; it likely won't reach the level of a Top-tier Ancestor. Next, I will focus on comprehending and creating Ancestral Methods!"
Ye Tian decided.
Over dozens of domain years, Ye Tian's main body still hadn't created its Ancestral Method. Had he only wanted to create an ordinary Ancestral Method, he would have done so long ago, but the Ancestral Method he wanted to create was the Endless Ancestral Method, which encompassed all Ancestral Daos.
Although he clearly had significant insights and had even found the right path, he was still just one step away, unable to fully bring it into existence.
Another domain year passed, and Ye Tian's progress stalled.
"This won't do. No progress at all over such a long period—even if I'm only one step away, I must have taken the wrong direction in my comprehension!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
The Endless Ancestral Method couldn't truly contain every single Ancestral Method. Theoretically, Ancestral Methods were infinite, so he had to use basic Ancestral Methods as a foundation to continuously evolve various Ancestral Methods, eventually forming the Endless Ancestral Method.
Creating the Endless Ancestral Method all at once was too difficult; he could first create individual basic Ancestral Methods and then fuse them to fully realize the Endless Ancestral Method.
Furthermore, creating these basic Ancestral Methods would allow some of the human race's peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors or Soul Ancestors to fuse with them and become Ancestors themselves.
This way, the human race would give birth to a large number of Ancestors!
However, even with these cultivation methods, fusion would be difficult and might not be suitable for everyone, requiring an endless amount of time.
Moreover, others didn't have his ability to use Deduction infinitely or to shatter cloned bodies repeatedly; for others, shattering their body once meant death.
Thus, his path could not be replicated by others.
However, having a complete Ancestral Method provided an opportunity; one day, they would eventually become Ancestors.
"Let's start with the Gold Ancestral Method!"
Ye Tian decided.
His understanding of Ancestral Methods was already incredibly profound, even surpassing those of the Ancestors themselves.
Many Ancestors had obtained Ancestral Methods by chance and became Ancestors after fusing with them; while some had created their own, they were generally mediocre Ancestral Methods.
The most impressive was the Spacetime Ancestor, who had created the Spacetime Ancestral Method and thus had high potential; however, due to a short cultivation time, he couldn't surpass the Destruction Ancestor.
But if the Spacetime Ancestor were given endless ages to cultivate, he truly would surpass the Destruction Ancestor and become the number one Ancestor of the Destruction Holy Realm.
Yet, compared to the Endless Ancestral Method Ye Tian would create in the future, the Spacetime Ancestor's Spacetime Ancestral Method was only average.
Furthermore, Ye Tian's foundation was far beyond what the Spacetime Ancestor could compare to.
Three domain years later, Ye Tian was incredibly excited.
At this moment, he had already created a Gold Ancestral Method; although it was based on his own creation, others could also comprehend and cultivate it.
If this Gold Ancestral Method were broken into pieces, it could even allow the human race to give birth to several Half-step Ancestors.
Of course, Half-step Ancestors were of no use to the human race; if they were to achieve anything, they should become full Ancestors.
However, a single Gold Ancestral Method could only allow one peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor or Soul Ancestor to become an Ancestor.
Next, every few domain years, Ye Tian was able to create another Ancestral Method.
Wood Ancestral Method, Water Ancestral Method, Fire Ancestral Method, Earth Ancestral Method, Light Ancestral Method, Dark Ancestral Method... one Ancestral Method after another was created by Ye Tian. If other Ancestors knew of this, they would surely be dumbfounded and find it unbelievable.
Even top-tier Ancestors like the Destruction Ancestor and the Holy Ancestor would be unable to create other Ancestral Methods if asked.
The Destruction Ancestor followed the path of the Destruction Ancestor; the Ancestral Method he created was the Destruction Ancestral Method. If he were asked to create a Gold Ancestral Method, he would be completely bewildered, as he simply didn't understand it.
Only Ye Tian, who followed the Endless Dao, could create so many Ancestral Methods.
Furthermore, since the Destruction Ancestor had already created one Destruction Ancestral Method, he couldn't continue to create a second one; if he tried, he would unconsciously end up back on the same Ancestral Method path.
Because of this, the Destruction Ancestor also had no way to help other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors become Ancestors.
Others who wanted to become Ancestors could only rely on themselves, not on an Ancestor.
Otherwise, many Holy Sects wouldn't have only one or two Ancestors.
But Ye Tian was too monstrous; having created so many Ancestral Methods now, it was enough to allow the human race to produce Ancestors one after another.
"Only the Space Ancestral Method and Time Ancestral Method are left!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Although he possessed a Space Ancestral Method, it belonged to someone else.
To create his own Space Ancestral Method without being influenced by the Space Ancestral Method of his clone, the Green Leaf Ancestor, Ye Tian's main body directly blocked its connection with the clone and erased the memory of that Space Ancestral Method from its mind before beginning the creation process.
Otherwise, once influenced, the creation of the Space Ancestral Method would not be successful.
