Chapter 1567: Becoming a Void Divine Son!

"The Void Source Bodies created by other Void Sons should be far less powerful and perfect than mine. Therefore, once I condense the Endless Source Body, my strength will become even greater, and I might truly have hope of passing the second floor of the Void Source Tower!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
With the Endless Source Body created, Ye Tian prepared to begin his cultivation.
However, to truly condense the Endless Source Body, he needed some auxiliary materials. Other ordinary materials aside, there were two main materials he had to have.
The first main material was the Void Source Crystal. This treasure was extremely precious; even within the Void Source Hall, it was worth 100 points.
During the 22 Great Tribulation Eras Ye Tian spent searching for various talents, he had luckily encountered a Void Source Crystal and obtained it after sacrificing several lives.
It was precisely because he possessed this Void Source Crystal that he could add its characteristics while perfecting the Endless Source Body, allowing him to completely finalize it.
The second main material was an Eternal Source Object. The special energy possessed by an Eternal Source Object was the most critical component for training the Endless Source Body. Without an Eternal Source Object, the Endless Source Body could not be formed.
In reality, the Void Source Stele could also be called an Eternal Source Object, and it was one that ranked among the top few. Unfortunately, he could not extract energy from the Void Source Stele; otherwise, the artifact spirit of the Void Source Hall would suppress him.
However, he still had the Xumi Golden Mountain, so he did not lack an Eternal Source Object.
After preparing the other auxiliary materials, Ye Tian began his secluded cultivation of the Endless Source Body on the Xumi Golden Mountain.
After a long time, Ye Tian reached a critical moment in his cultivation. He took out the Void Source Crystal and began to refine it while also starting to extract energy from the Xumi Golden Mountain.
At that moment, Ye Tian's Myriad Source Return to Ruins Invincible Tribulation Body began to disintegrate, gradually evolving into the Endless Source Body.
Initially, Ye Tian was very weak, but as time passed, his Invincible Tribulation Body became increasingly terrifying, soon surpassing the Myriad Source Return to Ruins.
One Dark Calendar!
One Great Tribulation Era!
Unknowingly, Ye Tian remained in seclusion for a full six Great Tribulation Eras. At this moment, he had completely condensed the Void Source Body known as the Endless Source Body. The power of his Invincible Tribulation Body far exceeded that of the Myriad Source Return to Ruins.
However.
"The cost was too high!"
Ye Tian looked at the Xumi Golden Mountain below him; it had now become dim and lusterless. He had absorbed too much energy; even the previous repair of a Rule Heavenly Object had not required nearly as much absorption as this time.
He felt that he had almost sucked this Xumi Golden Mountain dry. Relying on the current state of the Xumi Golden Mountain, it likely wouldn't be enough to help him promote to the rank of an Invincible.
If it were before, he might have felt it was a pity, but now he didn't care.
He was not only a Void Son but might even be promoted to a Void Divine Son. Having condensed the Endless Source Body, his future potential was incredibly terrifying; he no longer cared about a mere Xumi Golden Mountain.
The Xumi Golden Mountain was only ranked in the 100s among Eternal Source Objects; he even looked down on it a bit now.
The Xumi Golden Mountain had fulfilled its duty by creating his Endless Source Body.
After forming the Endless Source Body, Ye Tian began to stabilize it. At the same time, he needed to re-cultivate it to the Perfected stage before he could charge toward the Second-grade Invincible realm.
In the following years, Ye Tian continued his bitter cultivation. Finally, after spending several more Great Tribulation Eras and countless resources, he cultivated the Endless Source Body to the Perfected stage of the Third-grade Invincible realm.
Not only that, but Ye Tian also perfected his various Invincible Ancestral Techniques once again.
The Invincible Ancestral Techniques he had created before were indeed top-tier Invincible techniques, but they were not the absolute best, and they even had some deficiencies.
Now, he had perfected these Invincible Ancestral Techniques, which was enough to increase their power by more than fifty percent while reducing their consumption by over thirty percent.
In this way, his overall strength had also increased by a large margin.
One day.
Ye Tian arrived at the Void Tower of the Void Source Hall. He prepared to pass the second floor of the Void Tower. If he succeeded, he would become a Void Divine Son, with a status far exceeding that of an ordinary Void Son.
On the second floor of the Void Tower.
Ye Tian watched as countless Tribulation Beasts swarmed toward him, and he immediately began to slaughter them.
If it were before, he wouldn't have been able to kill a single Tribulation Beast with one strike and wouldn't have been able to withstand these countless beasts at all.
But now it was different. He could kill a swath of Tribulation Beasts with just his hands; his combat power had increased by an unknown number of times.
As time passed, Ye Tian's consumption was not light, and he sustained injuries, but the number of Tribulation Beasts rapidly dwindled.
Under these circumstances, he had great hope of passing the second floor of the Void Tower.
After a long time, Ye Tian's injuries were severe, but only a few Tribulation Beasts remained. After several more days of hard fighting, Ye Tian finally killed all the Tribulation Beasts, though he himself was in a miserable state.
Had he not perfected those Invincible Ancestral Techniques, he likely would have failed this time.
"Ye Tian has passed the second floor of the Void Tower and officially become a Void Divine Son of the Void Source Hall. Shall this be announced to all Void Sons?"
The artifact spirit's voice rang out.
"No!"
Ye Tian immediately refused.
He did not want to be too high-profile right now. Currently, he was only a Third-grade Invincible; any First-grade Invincible would have the strength to kill him, let alone those Supreme Beings.
If he were too high-profile, in case some Supreme Beings did not want him to grow up, he would be in some trouble. Even though his main body could temporarily hide in the foreign domain world, once he became a Second-grade Invincible, he would have no way to hide in the foreign domain world, and a Supreme Being could crush him with a single hand.
Therefore, it was better to keep a low profile for the time being!
After clearing the second floor of the Void Tower, Ye Tian did not continue to the third floor.
After all, he had paid the price of heavy injuries just to clear the second floor; he had no hope of clearing the third floor.
After coming out of the Void Tower, Ye Tian began to check the permissions and rewards belonging to a Void Divine Son.
The Void Source Hall provided three major rewards.
The first reward was 10,000 points. Since passing the second floor of the Void Tower already rewarded 30,000 points, and becoming a Void Divine Son happened simultaneously with passing the second floor, he was further rewarded with 10,000 points, which was already very good.
The second reward was a Source Artifact. Source Artifacts were weapons that stood above Tribulation Artifacts; almost only Supreme Beings had enough financial resources to forge a Source Artifact.
As a Third-grade Invincible, Ye Tian possessing a Source Artifact was enough to make countless Invincibles green with envy.
The third reward was an Eternal Source Object!
That's right, an Eternal Source Object. Such a precious item was actually one of the rewards for becoming a Void Divine Son.
Of course, the Eternal Source Object given would not be too precious, but its value was still beyond imagination!
In addition to these three major rewards, Ye Tian also possessed several permissions.
First, he could check the basic information of any Void Son; second, he could meditate on the Void Source Stele without queuing, and each meditation only cost 250 points, which was half the points required for other Void Sons; third, he had three opportunities to mobilize the protection of the Void Source Hall's origin power; if he were attacked by an Invincible in the outside world, it could save his life, giving him enough time to return to the Void Source Hall—this was equivalent to three life-saving chances; fourth, he possessed an independent small cultivation space in the Void Source Hall, where cultivation would be very safe. If Ye Tian could no longer stay in the foreign domain world after becoming a Second-grade Invincible in the future, he could practice in the Void Source Hall's cultivation space.
Seeing these rewards and permissions, Ye Tian was incredibly excited.
"Becoming a Void Divine Son truly has too many benefits!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
