Chapter 1529: Refining Tribulation Stones, Physical Body Nine Tribulations Realm!

Following the super-tier power known as the Illusory Dream World, Ye Tian chose the Boundless Wall as his next target.
On this day, Ye Tian arrived at the forbidden land of the Boundless Wall within the Dark Forbidden Domain. Using his formidable strength, he directly captured the strongest experts of the Boundless Wall, thereby threatening this super-tier power. By using these experts as leverage, he obtained ten thousand Tribulation Stones from the Boundless Wall, along with a set of cultivation records, which included information regarding the Eternal Heaven and Earth.
According to the intelligence gathered from the Boundless Wall, the Eternal Heaven and Earth was an otherworldly realm similar to the Dark Forbidden Domain and the Saint Realm, but it was countless times more advanced—it was a realm that truly existed eternally.
Existing eternally meant that the Eternal Heaven and Earth faced no Great Tribulations and would never be destroyed. It remained in existence forever, guarded by countless powerful experts, such that no one dared to invade an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
The Eternal Heaven and Earth was a place many experts dreamed of reaching, but it was not a place of absolute safety; it also harbored slaughter, competition, and more.
However, if one wished to become a truly powerful being, they had to travel to an Eternal Heaven and Earth; this was an unavoidable fact.
But how could a realm become an Eternal Heaven and Earth? Naturally occurring Eternal Heavens and Earths were almost non-existent. They were upgraded step-by-step from ordinary realms. An otherworldly realm would face destruction once every ten thousand Great Tribulation Eras, which was why ten thousand Great Tribulation Eras were referred to as one Kalpa-Era. After enduring one hundred thousand Kalpa-Eras, an otherworldly realm would completely vanish, ceasing to exist entirely.
However, if a Supreme One was born before an otherworldly realm was completely annihilated, they could cause the realm to ascend, extending the limit of one hundred thousand Kalpa-Eras to several hundred thousand.
If it ascended three times, that otherworldly realm could become an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
In other words, an otherworldly realm had to give birth to three Supreme Ones to become an Eternal Heaven and Earth. This was an incredibly difficult feat.
Therefore, Eternal Heavens and Earths were extremely rare. Experts from ordinary otherworldly realms generally could not find them, and many did not even know the name "Eternal Heaven and Earth."
For instance, Ye Tian had not known about the Eternal Heaven and Earth before. If he hadn't learned of its existence from the Illusory Dream World and the Boundless Wall this time, it likely would have taken him a vast amount of time to hear about it.
It had to be said that Ye Tian's luck was quite good, learning about the Eternal Heaven and Earth before even becoming an Invincible.
However, Ye Tian discovered that he did not know how to travel to an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
"It seems I can only learn the method through other super-tier powers," Ye Tian thought to himself.
Ye Tian's third target was the Boundless Demon Sea. Using the same method, he invaded their forbidden land, captured their strongest experts, extorted ten thousand Tribulation Stones, and obtained the method to enter an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
"One must actually become an Invincible to enter an Eternal Heaven and Earth?"
This was the answer Ye Tian received.
Ordinary people, even Supreme Ancestors, could not find the location of an Eternal Heaven and Earth. Rumor had it that they existed in a special space-time, and only a Tier-3 Gate could cross between otherworldly realms to reach one.
However, no power possessed a Tier-3 Gate. Even in an Eternal Heaven and Earth, Tier-3 Gates were incredibly rare and not something an ordinary power could obtain, so one could not rely on them.
But once one became an Invincible, they would vaguely sense the existence of a special place. That special place would be the Eternal Heaven and Earth closest to them. By following that sensation and crossing through various otherworldly realms, one could reach it.
Subsequently, Ye Tian extorted a fourth super-tier power—the Destruction Swamp.
From the Destruction Swamp, Ye Tian obtained another ten thousand Tribulation Stones. The intelligence he gathered was similar to that of the other powers, proving that they had not lied.
At this point, Ye Tian ceased his extortions. He did not go to the forbidden lands of the Firmament Sword Tomb or the Calamity Black Mountain.
This was because these two powers were very formidable, much stronger than the other six super-tier powers. Not only did they have more Invincibles, but their leaders had reached the combat power of a Level 5 Invincible—and they were no ordinary Level 5 Invincibles.
Therefore, extorting these two powers would likely invite a retaliation. If their leaders crossed through a Tier-2 Gate to attack him, even if he hid in the Dark Forbidden Domain, the opponent might still be able to defeat him despite the layers of suppression...
Consequently, he did not dare to take the risk. Unless his strength increased significantly again, rising from the Physical Body Seven Tribulations Realm to the Nine Tribulations Realm, he wouldn't be certain of blocking the attacks of these two super-tier powers.
Besides, he had harvested forty thousand Tribulation Stones this time and gained a lot of intelligence, giving him a clear understanding of future cultivation. He had already gained immensely and had no need to continue risking himself.
In the Mystic Void Ancestral Realm.
Ye Tian took out a Tribulation Stone and performed the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique to absorb and refine the tribulation power contained within.
BOOM!!!!
Countless amounts of tribulation power surged into Ye Tian's body. Refined by his Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique, it rapidly tempered his physical body.
"Such incredible speed!"
Ye Tian was beyond surprised.
For other experts in the Physical Body Tribulation Realm, Tribulation Stones weren't of much use. The tribulation power within the stones was much harder to refine than the power found during a Great Tribulation—the difficulty was at least dozens of times higher. But for Ye Tian, under the effects of the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique and his Eternal Talent 'Tribulation,' there was no difference regardless of the type of tribulation power. Since the power within the Tribulation Stones was many times more concentrated, the tempering effect on his physical body was far superior.
It had to be said that Tribulation Stones were indeed priceless treasures for Ye Tian.
Time flew by like a shuttle.
Ye Tian continuously refined the Tribulation Stones one by one. As a large number of stones were consumed, his physical body grew stronger and stronger.
Finally, after consuming seven thousand Tribulation Stones, Ye Tian finally faced his eighth Physical Body Tribulation.
During this tribulation, Ye Tian was significantly injured, but he still managed to pass the eighth Physical Body Tribulation, becoming an expert of the Physical Body Eight Tribulations Realm. His strength was now much greater than before.
Afterward, Ye Tian continued to refine the remaining stones. He still had over thirty thousand Tribulation Stones left and hoped to use them to temper his body to the Physical Body Nine Tribulations Realm.
In the blink of an eye, dozens of cosmic epochs passed.
Finally, Ye Tian had nearly exhausted all thirty thousand plus Tribulation Stones, and he successfully triggered his ninth Physical Body Tribulation. At the cost of being heavily wounded, he successfully crossed it, becoming an expert of the Physical Body Nine Tribulations Realm.
