Chapter 1658: The Nest of the Void Beast, Void Gold Marrow Wood!

“The Abyssless Land is about to move!”
Lord Gu Heyuan said excitedly.
The other eight-pole realm and Eternal realm experts also all gazed at the Abyssless Land, ready to enter it at any moment.
Rumble!!!!
It took only an instant for the Abyssless Land to go from still to moving.
At the very moment it rapidly moved, Ye Tian and the other eight-pole realm experts, as well as those Eternal realm lords, all darted into the Abyssless Land.
……
Abyssless Land.
This place had floating island terrain, like one floating island after another suspended in a void region.
Moreover, within the void there existed one Void Beast after another. These Void Beasts were in a semi-void state, sometimes revealing their physical true forms, sometimes nearly transparent, constantly switching between the two states.
“Be careful, there are Void Beasts here!”
One Eternal realm expert reminded them.
At this time, one eight-pole realm expert asked curiously, “Didn’t they say the Void Beasts had all been exterminated? I didn’t expect there to still be so many Void Beasts!”
“Exterminated?” Lord Gu Heyuan sneered. “How could the Void Beasts possibly have been wiped out? It’s just that the battle from several million years ago caused the Void Beasts in this void region of the Temporal Domain Land to nearly disappear. But deep within the void region, there are still large numbers of Void Beasts. Even Immortal realm lords dare not rashly enter the depths of the void region. Otherwise, why do you think those lords never develop the land in the depths of the void region?”
That eight-pole realm expert immediately fell silent. Clearly, such a remark had been somewhat embarrassing.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
Those Eternal realm lords flew off in another direction, clearly not intending to stay with Lord Gu Heyuan and the others.
Lord Gu Heyuan said, “According to the contract, we must act together. Although I have never been to the Abyssless Land, I do know a little about it. The Abyssless Land not only gives birth to many rare resources, but there is also one type of material that is definitely present: Void Gold Marrow Wood! Void Gold Marrow Wood can nurture Void Fruits, and Void Beasts are born from Void Fruits. Void Gold Marrow Wood can increase the chance of fusing innate Eternal laws, which is equivalent to increasing the chance of breaking through to the Eternal realm. Let’s first go look for Void Gold Marrow Wood. Does anyone have any objections?”
“No objections!”
Everyone agreed to Lord Gu Heyuan’s proposal.
After all, a treasure like Void Gold Marrow Wood was also something they wanted. For eight-pole realm experts like them, the greatest possibility was to break through to the Eternal realm, so they were extremely eager for resources and treasures that could help with that breakthrough. Even if it could only increase the chance by a little, that was still excellent.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!
Everyone followed Lord Gu Heyuan as he flew toward an island to see whether there was any Void Gold Marrow Wood there.
Suddenly.
Six Void Beasts charged toward Ye Tian and the others.
“Everyone be careful, Void Beasts can neutralize attacks. Their strength is no weaker than that of ordinary Immortal realm lords!”
Lord Gu Heyuan reminded them.
No one underestimated the Void Beasts, after all the legends about them were too terrifying.
Immediately, dozens of eight-pole realm experts joined forces to contend with these six Void Beasts. One attack after another blasted onto the Void Beasts’ bodies, but when these attacks landed, the Void Beasts became illusory, and the power of the attacks was directly neutralized in part; even attacks carrying willpower were neutralized.
It seemed capable of neutralizing any force.
Ye Tian understood very well that this was the Void Beasts’ innate power.
The moment he saw the Void Beasts, Ye Tian used his Copying Talent to investigate their talent conditions and saw a very special talent.
[Phasing: Immortal Ancestor Talent]
“They actually possess an Immortal Ancestor Talent!”
Ye Tian was very surprised.
This was the first time he had encountered a creature possessing an Immortal Ancestor Talent level. His own Immortal Ancestor Talents were formed by continuously fusing talents from the Black Mist Worm into several Immortal Ancestor Talents.
The Void Beasts not only possessed the Immortal Ancestor Talent-level Phasing, but also the three major Immortal Ancestor Talent-level talents of Strength, Defense, and Speed.
It was just that the Void Beasts had low intelligence; otherwise, this race would be far too terrifying. They would probably be able to grow to the Immortal realm level, and by then, countless Temporal Domain Lands and the Source Sea would not be able to contend with the Void Beasts.
“Copy!”
Ye Tian used his Copying Talent to copy one Immortal Ancestor Talent after another from the Void Beasts. Although the copying speed was a bit slow, he could still barely manage to copy them.
Rumble rumble rumble!!!
The six Void Beasts fought fiercely with dozens of eight-pole realm experts. They could unleash wave after wave of destructive light, and each destructive light wave was enough to kill ordinary seven-pole realm powerhouses. Even if an eight-pole realm expert was struck head-on, they would be injured.
But the dozens of eight-pole realm experts had the advantage in numbers, so they still suppressed the Void Beasts.
However, Ye Tian and the others were unable to kill these six Void Beasts.
At this time, Lord Gu Heyuan proposed, “Use formations to trap them temporarily. We can’t let them drag us down.”
The other eight-pole realm experts agreed as well, and so they began setting up formations one after another. They did not seek to trap them completely, only to hold these Void Beasts for a while, and that would be enough.
· ·········求鮮花··· ·····
The eight-pole realm experts were extremely fast at setting up formations. As long as they had materials, they could build a formation in almost a second.
In an instant, one great formation after another enveloped the six Void Beasts.
At the same time, these eight-pole realm experts took out one Eternal Source weapon after another with void-sealing effects and directly sealed off this area, making it so the six Void Beasts would take a little longer to break through the sealing formations.
“Go!”
Lord Gu Heyuan said hurriedly.
Dozens of eight-pole realm experts immediately darted away, while the six Void Beasts were temporarily trapped.
Very quickly, everyone arrived on an island.
Although the island had some resources, there was no Void Gold Marrow Wood.
Thus, everyone immediately left, so as not to waste time.
As everyone continued to go deeper into the Abyssless Land, they encountered Void Beasts again and again, and each time they used the same method to temporarily trap them.
.... ......... 0
Otherwise, in the Abyssless Land, it would be far too difficult to shake off the Void Beasts.
Suddenly.
Everyone discovered that an enormous nest had appeared on an island.
“A nest should be the place where Void Beasts are born. There must be Void Gold Marrow Wood inside. Let’s go in!”
Lord Gu Heyuan said excitedly.
Whoosh!!!
Everyone directly darted into the nest, as if entering another stretch of void.
Here, a leafless golden tree had taken root inside the nest, and on it there were three fruits.
These three fruits were the three Void Fruits that nurtured the Void Beasts.
“Cut down this Void Gold Marrow Tree. Of the Void Gold Marrow Wood obtained, I want one tenth, and the rest goes to you all!” Lord Gu Heyuan said.
“No problem!”
Everyone agreed.
After all, divided in this way, they would still obtain quite a lot. Besides, relying solely on Void Gold Marrow Wood would not truly allow them to break through.
Moreover, Lord Gu Heyuan had brought them here, so they would not reject his distribution plan.
“Five people will cut down the Void Gold Marrow Tree. The other eight-pole realm experts will guard the entrance. Make sure to block those Void Beasts. Otherwise, when we cut down the Void Gold Marrow Wood, the Void Beasts will definitely sense it and come to stop us!” Lord Gu Heyuan reminded everyone.
Immediately, Ye Tian and the others came to the entrance, prepared to intercept the Void Beasts rushing in to rescue it, while Lord Gu Heyuan and the other five eight-pole realm experts began cutting down the Void Gold Marrow Tree.
With a boom!
The felling began, and the Void Beasts born from this nest all sensed it and began gathering toward this place.
