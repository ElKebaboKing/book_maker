Chapter 1636 The Three Great Opportunities of the Homeless Domain!

“The Homeless Domain—is it perhaps a temporal-spatial Domain? Aren’t we unable to enter temporal-spatial Domains?”
Ye Tian raised his doubt.
True River Sword Ancestor explained, “The Homeless Domain is indeed a temporal-spatial Domain, but it is a shattered temporal-spatial Domain. The temporal-spatial ruler has also been shattered and thus embedded in the source sea. Just like fragments of a small world embedded in the main world, there will be a passage that allows one to enter it.”
After hearing this, Ye Tian fully understood.
Then, Ye Tian came to understand the value of the Homeless Domain.
The Homeless Domain had once been the temporal-spatial Domain of the Homeless Ruler, but unfortunately, the Homeless Ruler had fallen. As for how he fell, even True River Sword Ancestor and the others did not know.
However, a huge fragment of the Homeless Domain is now embedded in the source sea and has completely become part of the source sea, which is why these half-step Eternal Realm experts can enter it.
Of course, the Homeless Domain is also extremely dangerous. Within it exists a very terrifying creature—the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python, perhaps once a pet raised and studied by the Homeless Ruler.
But now that the Homeless Ruler has fallen, the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python is no longer under control, yet it still persists in fulfilling its mission of guarding the Homeless Domain. Once anyone enters the Homeless Domain, they will be attacked by the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python.
And the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python is incredibly powerful, basically all of them are half-step Eternal Realm, and the strongest among them can even rival half-step Eternal Realm experts at the Extreme level.
Therefore, Supreme Beings are simply not suited to enter the Homeless Domain; entering means death.
According to information revealed by the other twelve ancestors, the Homeless Domain has three great opportunities.
The first opportunity is the Boundless Sea. The Boundless Sea within the Homeless Domain gives birth to many rare resources, but there is also the main base of the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python. Without absolutely formidable strength, entering there is courting death.
The second opportunity is Rift Wind Valley. This valley can be said to be a place for cultivation, or it can also be said to be a marvel.
Rift Wind Valley constantly blows a strange wind, and this strange wind can affect the will of half-step Eternal Realm experts.
The soul and body of a half-step Eternal Realm expert have fused, but that does not mean they have no will. Will still exists, and it has a great influence.
If one’s will is strong, one can hold fast to one’s original heart, which is of great benefit to breakthroughs, and can even provide some help in breaking through bottlenecks.
Moreover, the will of half-step Eternal Realm experts is already very strong, so tempering it is extremely difficult, yet Rift Wind Valley can help temper the will.
Furthermore, there are no Temporal-Spatial Dragon Pythons in Rift Wind Valley; the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Pythons do not like to go there.
Therefore, Rift Wind Valley is the safest and easiest opportunity to enter, but raising one’s will requires a very long period of time.
More importantly, even half-step Eternal Realm experts find it difficult to stay in Rift Wind Valley for long. Once too much time passes, Rift Wind Valley will cause the will to shatter, and the body will collapse as a result.
Moreover, Rift Wind Valley can only be entered at specific times; at other times, it simply cannot be entered, and no half-step Eternal Realm expert would remain in the Homeless Domain all the time, as that would be far too dangerous.
So those half-step Eternal Realm experts would only stay in Rift Wind Valley for a period of time before leaving.
Ye Tian quietly remembered this place, Rift Wind Valley.
The third opportunity is Homeless Mountain. This was once the residence of the Homeless Ruler. Although it was left in a shattered state after a great battle, many good things still remained, which is why Homeless Mountain is also the greatest opportunity in the Homeless Domain and the place that many half-step Eternal Realm experts all want to enter.
The day the Homeless Domain opens will be three kalpa epochs from now, so the thirteen ancestors agreed to enter the Homeless Domain together after three kalpa epochs.
As for the other inner-circle members, they are not qualified to enter it; even the other half-step Eternal Realm experts among the inner-circle members are not qualified to enter.
This kind of opportunity is a shared opportunity exclusive to the thirteen ancestors. If Ye Tian had not become one of the thirteen ancestors, he would also not have been qualified to enter it.
Of course, if Ye Tian later discovered similar opportunities, he could also tell True River Sword Ancestor and the others, sharing some of the opportunities. This was also one of the purposes for founding the Source God Pavilion.
In the blink of an eye, three kalpa epochs passed.
On this day, the thirteen ancestors gathered at the Source God Pavilion.
“Let’s go!”
The Source God Pavilion teleported the thirteen ancestors, crossing a vast distance and directly arriving at a relatively remote region.
Very soon, the thirteen ancestors arrived at a place, and here several half-step Eternal Realm experts were hidden.
“True River Sword Ancestor, your Source God Pavilion actually brought a Ninth Supreme Being. What kind of background does this Ninth Supreme Being have?” a half-step Eternal Realm expert appeared and asked curiously.
“Rift Wind Source Ancestor, this is the thirteenth ancestor of our Source God Pavilion, Endless Primordial Ancestor.” True River Sword Ancestor casually said a sentence, and then did not say much more; moreover, his tone toward Rift Wind Source Ancestor was not very good.
It could be seen that the relationship between True River Sword Ancestor and Rift Wind Source Ancestor was not good.
At this moment, Wind Ancient Source Ancestor sent a message to Ye Tian, saying: “Rift Wind Source Ancestor is also one of the Extreme-level experts of the source sea, but his relationship with True River Sword Ancestor is not very good. The reason is that he once took away an opportunity that belonged to True River Sword Ancestor. You should be a bit careful of Rift Wind Source Ancestor in the future, but don’t worry too much. He is also only at the first Extreme level, just a little stronger than you.”
Ye Tian nodded and said nothing.
Very soon, one half-step Eternal Realm expert after another arrived, and here gathered as many as several hundred half-step Eternal Realm experts.
“It’s starting!”
The rules of the Homeless Domain and the rules of the source sea collided, and a gap began to tear open.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!
One half-step Eternal Realm expert after another charged into the fissure and entered the Homeless Domain.
Ye Tian, together with True River Sword Ancestor and the others, also charged into the Homeless Domain.
Upon entering the Homeless Domain, bursts of roaring sounds came from all directions.
These sounds were unmistakably those of the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python!
Boom!!!!
One Temporal-Spatial Dragon Python after another that stretched across the sky came threading through the void, each one unimaginably huge and exuding a terrifying aura.
“Kill!”
One half-step Eternal Realm expert after another took action and began battling the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Pythons, but their purpose was not to kill them; it was to escape from here.
Some half-step Eternal Realm experts charged toward Rift Wind Valley, where it was relatively safe and the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Pythons would not go.
Some others did not want to go to Rift Wind Valley and instead went to Homeless Mountain. Although the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Pythons could also force their way into Homeless Mountain, there were certain places in Homeless Mountain that the Temporal-Spatial Dragon Pythons could not enter.
As for the Boundless Sea, no one dared to go there.
Of course, there were even fewer half-step Eternal Realm experts wandering around aimlessly.
True River Sword Ancestor and Wind Ancient Source Ancestor directly chose Homeless Mountain, while Ye Tian chose Rift Wind Valley.
He planned to first experience the will enhancement brought by this opportunity in Rift Wind Valley; as for Homeless Mountain, it would not be too late to go there a bit later.
