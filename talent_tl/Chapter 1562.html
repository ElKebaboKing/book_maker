Chapter 1561: The Loss of the Netherworld Palace!

"Sovereign Qingyang, who is the Invincible you mentioned that is comparable to a Son of the Void? Our Tianfeng Sea doesn't have any Son of the Void at the third-grade Invincible realm in this era!"
A first-grade Invincible Sovereign asked curiously.
Sovereign Qingyang immediately transmitted some information about Ye Tian to the various Sovereigns.
After these Sovereigns received the intelligence regarding Ye Tian, they were instantly shocked.
"A new member, and in such a short time, he was promoted to an elite-level member. Now he has already become a third-grade Invincible. How could he have such a fast promotion speed?"
Many Sovereigns were incredibly shocked.
They were all the top geniuses of the Tianlin Realm, yet they did not have such a fast promotion speed. Compared with Ye Tian's speed of strength improvement, they were practically trash.
Of course, if one were to nurture an Invincible with all their might, disregarding foundation issues, they could also quickly promote to the third-grade Invincible realm, but in that case, their combat power would be very weak.
Yet, while Ye Tian was at the third-grade Invincible realm, he easily killed a third-grade Invincible from the Yongming-level power, the Netherworld Palace. His combat power was suspected to be that of a Son of the Void. This indicated that Ye Tian's foundation was incomparably solid, and his potential far exceeded their imagination.
In other words, Ye Tian had promoted so quickly on the basis of the most perfect foundation. This was simply too staggering.
"Later, when this third-grade Invincible named Ye Tian returns from the battlefield, why don't we have him participate in the Son of the Void assessment? If he can pass, our Tianfeng Sea will effectively have six Sons of the Void, matching the number of Netherworld Sons in the Netherworld Palace!"
"Agreed, but we must ensure Ye Tian's safety. I suggest sending more Invincibles to Jinhe World. Let's make Jinhe World the main battlefield for this war!"
"Let's do it!"
One by one, the Sovereigns reached a consensus and then issued orders for more Invincibles to rush to Jinhe World, effectively turning Jinhe World into the main battlefield between Tianfeng Sea and the Netherworld Palace.
In an instant, a large number of Invincibles flooded into Jinhe World.
--------------------
Ye Tian had no idea that he had attracted the attention of the high-level officials of the Netherworld Palace and Tianfeng Sea, nor did he know that he had drawn out a Son of the Void.
Even if he were told about the Son of the Void, he wouldn't know what it signified.
He continued to slay the Invincibles of the Netherworld Palace. If he returned to the Tianfeng Sea in the Tianlin Realm, he would likely be able to claim a massive amount of contribution points.
On this day.
Ye Tian found another lone third-grade Invincible. As soon as this third-grade Invincible saw Ye Tian, he immediately showed a look of terror.
After all, in the eyes of the third-grade Invincibles of the Netherworld Palace, they already treated Ye Tian as a Son of the Void.
To contend with a Son of the Void was to seek death!
Requesting backup!
This third-grade Invincible didn't want to fight Ye Tian at all. He immediately called for help, requesting other Invincibles from the Netherworld Palace to act together to resist Ye Tian while waiting for the arrival of the Netherworld Palace's Son of the Void.
After all, the Son of the Void of the Netherworld Palace was not in the Tianlin Realm, and it would take time to summon him back.
Ye Tian's speed was very fast. He immediately caught up with this Invincible and disrupted space-time, preventing the other party from tearing through space-time to leave.
Then, Ye Tian heavily injured this third-grade Invincible with a single strike.
Right at this moment, dozens of third-grade Invincibles descended.
Furthermore, they brought heavy treasures.
Of course, they didn't think that so many Invincibles could kill Ye Tian. What they wanted to do was to contend with Ye Tian so that he couldn't kill any more Invincibles.
Rumble!!!
As Ye Tian fought with the many third-grade Invincibles of the Netherworld Palace, the third-grade Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea could not remain indifferent.
Soon, the third-grade Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea arrived to provide support.
With the support of the Tianfeng Sea's third-grade Invincibles, the third-grade Invincibles of the Netherworld Palace used a heavy treasure to protect themselves and jointly retreated into the Jinhe branch. However, that heavy treasure was also smashed to pieces by the Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea.
However, sacrificing a heavy treasure to ensure their safety was not a waste.
Next, the Invincibles of the Netherworld Palace did not leave this branch. This branch was equipped with a grand array, one that had been reinforced over long years, enough to withstand the attacks of any third-grade Invincible. Even some second-grade Invincibles would be unable to break this array.
Since second-grade Invincibles could not enter the foreign world, they were safe for the time being.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian and the other Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea took the opportunity to wipe out the other Invincibles who hadn't made it into the branch in time. Suddenly, many fourth and fifth-grade Invincibles of the Netherworld Palace were slaughtered by the Invincibles of Tianfeng Sea.
In an instant, the Netherworld Palace suffered heavy losses.
However, the Netherworld Palace didn't necessarily care about those weak Invincibles; what they cared about were the lives of the third-grade Invincibles.
Losing one third-grade Invincible was comparable to losing dozens or even more fourth-grade Invincibles!
After all, if a third-grade Invincible took another step forward, they would be a second-grade Invincible.
Even in a Yongming-level power, second-grade Invincibles were the true backbone. After all, first-grade Invincibles rarely acted, as they were all working hard to break through to the Supreme Being realm. Many matters were handled by second-grade Invincibles.
From this, it could be seen how important a combat force a third-grade Invincible was; they could not be lost easily.
Finally, on this day.
A large number of third-grade Invincibles from the Netherworld Palace, along with a Son of the Void, descended upon Jinhe World.
"...Lord Luo Ge, this is Jinhe World!"
A third-grade Invincible stood before a proud young man.
This young man wore a black robe, with a single golden horn protruding from his forehead. His entire body was filled with a noble aura. He was a genius of the Blood Horn Race in the Tianlin Realm, and the Blood Horn Race was also considered one of the top racial powers within the Netherworld Palace.
The Blood Horn Race possessed three first-grade Invincibles, and he was the son of one of those first-grade Invincibles. Furthermore, his talent surpassed his father's, earning him the title of Son of the Void, a name that resounded throughout the entire Tianlin Realm.
Thus, he had the right to be proud.
"Tianfeng Sea, a newly promoted Yongming-level power, actually dares to take action against our Netherworld Palace. I remember that Tianfeng Sea's Son of the Void is not at the third-grade Invincible realm. Even if this Invincible suspected of being a Son of the Void is decent, he is ultimately not a Son of the Void. He doesn't understand the power of a Son of the Void. If Tianfeng Sea had hidden this third-grade Invincible and nurtured him well, perhaps another Son of the Void really could have been born. But now, he is destined to fall here!"
Luo Ge said confidently.
After all, he was not only a Son of the Void, but after becoming one, his strength had improved further. If he hadn't suppressed his strength, he would have already stepped into the second-grade Invincible realm.
Today, he would let the other Invincibles know what a true Son of the Void was!.
