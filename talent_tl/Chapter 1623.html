Chapter 1622 A Black Mist River Formed from a Section of Arm!

Very soon, Ye Tian followed the other black mist worms to a certain place in the Primordial Sea.
In this place, endless black mist worms gathered here, and the density was very high.
Moreover, more black mist worms were arriving.
“This is……”
Ye Tian saw a pitch-black river, almost separated from the Primordial Sea and located in another void.
Above this river, densely packed life forms were hatching, as if the entire river were a place of incubation.
“The hatching place of the black mist worms!”
Ye Tian had always been curious about where the black mist worms were born, because black mist worms had no need to mate, had no distinction between sexes, and had very low intelligence.
Under such circumstances, why were there so many black mist worms, and why was each black mist worm so powerful.
This was not normal!
Yet after spending a long time in the Primordial Sea, he still had not found the place where black mist worms were born.
Now, he could be considered to have found it.
No, it should be said that this place of birth had appeared on its own.
One after another, black mist worms hatched out and began devouring the shells that had hatched them—if those could be called shells, then they were shells. Almost every shell could hatch countless black mist worms.
At this time, the black mist worms from outside also flew in and devoured these shells.
Even Ye Tian himself felt a primal urge to devour them, as if these shells had great attraction and would be of enormous use in increasing his strength.
“Get lost!”
Ye Tian used the aura of the king of black mist worms to force the other black mist worms to scatter; those shells had to be devoured by him alone.
Crash, crash, crash!!!!
One shell after another was devoured and refined by Ye Tian, and the other black mist worms did not dare resist at all.
As these shells were absorbed and refined by him, Ye Tian’s aura grew stronger and stronger.
For black mist worms, these shells were the true treasure resources, while the other resources of the Primordial Sea held no appeal to black mist worms.
Boom!!!!
Ye Tian’s cultivation actually broke through.
This black mist worm clone had actually advanced to the Seventh Stage.
Thus, Ye Tian continued to devour, as if this body had no bottleneck at all.
Other black mist worms could not do this, however; their potential was insufficient and they were restricted. Even if devouring the shells could increase their strength, the increase was not significant.
Perhaps, by instinct they also wanted to break through the shackles and limitations, but without an immortal-level ancestral aptitude, they simply could not do it.
Very soon, the cultivation of the black mist worm clone improved again.
Early Seventh Stage!
Mid Seventh Stage!
Late Seventh Stage!
Peak Seventh Stage!
Logically speaking, it should be very difficult to break through from Seventh Stage to Eighth Stage. Even for Ye Tian’s true body, if he wanted to charge from a Seventh Stage Supreme Being to an Eighth Stage Supreme Being, it would probably be rather difficult.
However, the black mist worm clone easily broke through the shackles and advanced to Eighth Stage, comparable to an Eighth Stage Supreme Being.
But black mist worms were not Supreme Beings after all, and they had no Eternal Body; those Eternal Techniques were also not suitable for them.
Therefore, the black mist worm clone was not very strong in combat.
Of course, black mist worms mainly won by numbers; individual strength did not matter much.
After a long while, the cultivation of the black mist worm clone finally could no longer improve, but it had already reached the Peak of the Ninth Stage.
Unfortunately, such a breakthrough brought no benefit to the true body, and the true body could not draw any reference from it.
After breaking through to the Nine Heavens Stage, Ye Tian’s black mist worm clone also became about the size of a puppy.
After the breakthrough in strength, Ye Tian clearly felt that something was wrong with this river.
The sensory range of an ordinary black mist worm was not very large, but Ye Tian’s perception now covered this entire river.
Under his perception, this was a river resembling an arm, and it was an amputated arm, as if it had crossed over from the depths of endless spacetime, only to be severed here by someone.
At this moment, he had already imagined a scene from long, long ago.
In an age of boundless antiquity, an incomparably terrifying existence had been cut off from one arm by another strong expert, and this section of the arm had remained in this spacetime of the Primordial Sea. Later, through special evolution, black mist worms hatched from this arm.
But this arm was an eternal existence. Even after nurturing endless black mist worms, it still had not completely disappeared; instead, it had taken on the form of a river and continued to exist.
The so-called river form was merely a decayed state.
“Is it really like this?”
Ye Tian guessed inwardly.
But if it was true, then how terrifying must that expert’s strength have been?
Even after the Eternal Realm, that would still be an incomparably terrifying level, right?
Ye Tian thought for a while, then made a decision: he would go deeper into this river.
This river might conceal itself at any moment; by then, it would not be easy to find.
However, once he entered the depths of the river, problems could easily arise.
Therefore, Ye Tian found a nearby island and first stored away the treasures he had collected.
In this way, even if this black mist worm clone perished, the next time he entered the Primordial Sea, he could still take away those resources and would not lose them.
After dealing with the resource treasures, Ye Tian returned once more to this river, which he temporarily called the Black Mist River.
Entering the Black Mist River, Ye Tian plunged in and kept moving deeper and deeper into it.
Boom!!!!
Endless pressure came crashing down, as if trying to stop him from entering the depths of the Black Mist River.
If it were other black mist worms, they would not be able to go deep at all.
But Ye Tian’s black mist worm clone had already reached the Ninth Stage Supreme Being level, and thus could force its way in.
As time passed, Ye Tian went deeper and deeper.
No one knew how much time had passed before Ye Tian finally discovered something different.
A rod-like weapon emitting brilliant golden light appeared within his perception, and in the distance there were even more, larger objects like this, with varying shapes.
Ye Tian came before this brilliant golden rod and examined it carefully.
“This is bone!”
Ye Tian said with certainty.
Strictly speaking, this was a fragment of bone, and a tiny, insignificant fragment at that.
At this point, Ye Tian could confirm that his guess was correct.
The Black Mist River was indeed a section of an arm. Not only had it been severed, but the bones of the arm had been smashed into countless fragments, almost completely shattered into innumerable bone pieces.
And the one before him was one of those bone fragments.
Ye Tian tried attacking it, using the body of his Peak Ninth Stage black mist worm to ram into it.
Under a full-force strike, this bone fragment actually cracked a little.
“It’s probably because it has existed for too long, and black mist worms have hatched here, continuously absorbing the power of the arm. Even the bones of such an existence can be cracked by me!”
Ye Tian sighed.
Of course.
Even so, a fragment of bone like this was priceless.
