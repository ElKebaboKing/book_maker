Chapter 1521: Physical Tribulation! Absorbing Tribulation Power to Temper the Body!

The Supreme Ancestor and the Invincible Ancestor may seem only one realm apart, but the gap in their strength is immense, like the difference between heaven and earth.
Take a One-Tribulation Tribulation Beast, for example. A One-Tribulation Tribulation Beast is comparable to a weaker Invincible Ancestor, yet even so, it required so many Supreme Ancestors to join forces, and many of them lost their lives, just to kill it.
If it were a normal Invincible Ancestor, even that many Supreme Ancestors joining forces would have ended in defeat and death.
This is the power of an Invincible Ancestor!
And the difficulty of breaking through from a Supreme Ancestor to an Invincible Ancestor is more than a hundred times harder than a Top-tier Ancestor breaking through to a Supreme Ancestor.
Among the many exotic domains, the number of Supreme Ancestors is not small; every exotic domain has some to a greater or lesser extent, with the exception of new exotic domains.
But exotic domains that possess Invincible ones are far too few. Only an Invincible one at the level of an Invincible Ancestor can be considered truly free from the shackles of an exotic domain, possessing an infinite future and no longer being a frog at the bottom of a well.
According to the information provided by the Wuhai Sword Ancestor, a Supreme Ancestor must satisfy three conditions to become an Invincible Ancestor.
First is one hundred Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals. If one does not have one hundred Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, even if other conditions are met, the breakthrough is very likely to fail. Furthermore, the deeper one's foundation, the more Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals need to be prepared.
Often, even a single Origin Rule Tribulation Crystal is difficult to collect; trying to collect one hundred is simply too difficult.
But in reality, this is the easiest condition to fulfill. After all, if one is powerful enough, even if they only accumulate one Origin Rule Tribulation Crystal per Great Tribulation, one hundred Great Tribulations would be enough to collect them all.
The second condition is even harder: absorbing Tribulation Power and using the power of an exotic domain's Great Tribulation to undergo a tribulation for oneself.
At this time, a Supreme Ancestor faces two choices. One is to take the path of Physical Tribulation, which is to use the body to absorb Tribulation Power, tempering the body with that power to condense an Invincible Body. The second is Soul Tribulation, to condense an Invincible Undying Soul.
Tribulation Power is terrifying. Ordinary Supreme Ancestors wouldn't dare to absorb and refine it, and to absorb enough Tribulation Power, one must experience many Great Tribulations.
Basically, undergoing Physical Tribulation or Soul Tribulation more than five times gives a high probability of breaking through to the Invincible Ancestor realm.
However, trying to undergo a tribulation more than five times is incredibly difficult.
"Could it be that Tribulation Blood is related to undergoing tribulations?"
Ye Tian speculated.
If Tribulation Blood could help a Supreme Ancestor quickly complete five or more tribulations and do so relatively safely, then it would truly be a priceless treasure, enough to make any Supreme Ancestor fight to the death for it.
The third condition is the most difficult, yet for some Supreme Ancestors, it is also the easiest.
During non-tribulation periods, one enters an exotic domain and uses their body or soul to withstand the exotic rules. Through the stimulation of the exotic rules, they allow themselves to undergo a qualitative change, thereby ascending to the Invincible Ancestor realm.
However, this is incredibly risky; one could die with the slightest mistake.
Supreme Ancestors are almost unable to contend with the rules of an exotic domain. Even if they step into an exotic domain for just one second, they likely won't be able to withstand it and will die instantly.
But if one has enough life-saving items and enters and exits the exotic domain's rules repeatedly to slowly adapt, there is a certain hope of ascending to the Invincible Ancestor realm.
These are all three conditions.
"Hard! It's too hard! No wonder so many Supreme Ancestors have no way to ascend to become Invincible ones!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Currently, he had quite a few Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals in his possession.
Although that Tribulation Beast was only a One-Tribulation Tribulation Beast, it possessed over ten thousand Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals. Because of his great strength, he had managed to seize more than five thousand of them.
However, his foundation was too deep, so the number of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals he needed would definitely far exceed other Supreme Ancestors. One hundred crystals would certainly not be enough; he would need to prepare at least 2,000.
If not for killing the One-Tribulation Tribulation Beast this time, he feared it would have taken him countless ages to accumulate so many Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals seem very precious, but in reality, they are only useful to Supreme Ancestors. For those Invincible ones, they likely aren't of much use and can at most be used as currency.
Ye Tian now satisfied the first condition, leaving only the second and third.
"Absorb Tribulation Power!"
Ye Tian sensed the Great Tribulation power permeating the Dark Forbidden Domain. This power was incredibly chaotic and terrifying, making it fundamentally difficult to absorb and refine.
Suddenly.
He thought of the talent he had copied from the Tribulation Beast. Among them was an Eternal Talent: Tribulation. Perhaps this talent was related to Tribulation Power.
Consequently.
Ye Tian began to integrate the Eternal Talent: Tribulation to see what use this talent had.
After a long time, Ye Tian finally completely integrated the Eternal Talent: Tribulation.
At this moment, he finally knew the use of this talent.
"It's actually for absorbing Tribulation Power to temper the physical body!"
Ye Tian said in surprise.
He finally knew why Tribulation Beasts were all so powerful. They could repeatedly absorb Tribulation Power to temper their bodies; even while sleeping, they were invisibly absorbing Tribulation Power to increase their strength.
If a Tribulation Beast were allowed to survive multiple Great Tribulation Eras, how terrifying would its strength become!
"The Eternal Talent: Tribulation is a talent specifically for the physical body. It seems I can only take the path of Physical Tribulation," Ye Tian thought to himself.
In fact, the path of an Ancestor was already biased toward the physical body, so the Eternal Talent: Tribulation was indeed very suitable for him.
Boom!!!!!
Ye Tian activated the Eternal Talent: Tribulation, absorbing the Tribulation Power from the world at an incredible speed.
This Tribulation Power entered Ye Tian's physical body, quickly tempering it.
As time slowly passed, before long, Ye Tian's physical body had grown much stronger, and he felt the rules of the Dark Forbidden Domain pressing down on him.
At this moment, Ye Tian had a moment of realization.
His physical body was about to undergo a tribulation!
He was an existence that could be called invincible among Supreme Ancestors, and his Endless Ancestor Body was also incredibly powerful, so the power of his physical tribulation was naturally far beyond that of other Supreme Ancestors.
Boom!!!!
The rules of the Dark Forbidden Domain transformed into rule flames, rule wind blades, rule lightning... constantly attempting to annihilate Ye Tian's physical body. But such a tribulation did not cause his body to perish; instead, it grew stronger and stronger.
Not long after, Ye Tian's physical body had passed its first Physical Tribulation.
Next, he continued to cultivate, striving to complete two Physical Tribulations before the Great Tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Domain passed.
In that case, his strength would see a massive increase.
Finally, as the Great Tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Domain was about to end, Ye Tian finally welcomed his second Physical Tribulation and successfully passed it, his physical strength skyrocketing.
