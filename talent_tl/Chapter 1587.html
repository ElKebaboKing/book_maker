Chapter 1586: Invasion of Outsiders, Ye Tian Profits!

Within the Ancient Star Race, the proportion of clansmen possessing the Ultimate Ancestral Talent, Ancient Star Body, was not very large. However, the Ancient Star Race had a vast population, so Ye Tian was not lacking targets to copy from at all.
In a short amount of time, Ye Tian copied a large number of Ancient Star Body Ultimate Ancestral Talents and began merging them in a quiet place.
After some time, Ye Tian successfully merged these Ancient Star Body Ultimate Ancestral Talents and obtained an Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent.
Next, Ye Tian left the Star Ancient Domain and returned to the Void Source Hall. He transferred the Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent to his main body and then returned to the Star Ancient Domain. To him, this was merely a waste of a little time, but he had permanently gained an Eternal Ancestral Talent of extraordinary origin, which was definitely a profit.
One day.
Ye Tian arrived at a star region of the Ancient Star Race, where there were a large number of stars and countless members of the Ancient Star Race.
Suddenly, Ye Tian used his copying talent to sense several Invincibles. These Invincibles did not belong to the Ancient Star Race.
"Outsiders!"
Ye Tian realized.
These people were likely from a certain organization or a temporary group formed to plunder Eternal Resources. They might even have a target and were about to strike; otherwise, they wouldn't be gathered together.
Ye Tian did not show himself but hid in the shadows, waiting silently. If these people made a move, he might be able to seize the opportunity to snatch the Eternal Resources!
Ye Tian didn't have to wait long before these people finally acted.
Boom!!!!
One after another, First-Class Invincibles attacked, instantly killing a large number of Ancient Star Race experts and charging into a certain area.
"Outsiders, how dare you invade our Jingmen!"
A tall member of the Ancient Star Race transformed into a massive body, frantically resisting these First-Class Invincibles while roaring.
Jingmen was a faction within the Ancient Star Race, and it wasn't considered a small one. Although it had no Supreme Beings, it had many First-Class Invincibles. In the outside world, it would be comparable to a powerful Saint-Name level organization.
The physical body and soul of a First-Class Invincible were unified. Since the physical bodies of the Ancient Star Race were already terrifying, after becoming First-Class Invincibles, they had no flaws in either their bodies or souls, and their defensive power was incredibly frightening.
The attacks of several First-Class Invincibles landed on this Ancient Star Race expert, but they couldn't even break through his defense.
With a boom, the Ancient Star Race expert sent several First-Class Invincibles flying with a single punch, displaying an invincible posture.
Suddenly.
This Ancient Star Race member's body was enveloped by a light barrier. In the next moment, the barrier contracted, and the Ancient Star Race member was instantly annihilated.
Then, the light barrier was retracted by a First-Class Invincible, turning into a small golden cover. Clearly, this was a Source Tool.
In the shadows.
"This First-Class Invincible's strength is not weak. To kill a First-Class Invincible of the Ancient Star Race in one blow, he must be a Son of the Void!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
However, there was no information about this Son of the Void in his records. Clearly, the other party had never entered the Void Source Hall.
Not all geniuses desired to become a Son of the Void. Many top-tier organizations knew about the situation of the Void Source Hall, so they would not let their juniors or members participate in the Void Source Hall's assessment.
This was because in the Source Sea, there were many opportunities comparable to the Void Source Tablet; it wasn't strictly necessary to comprehend the Void Source Tablet.
Jingmen was not without experts comparable to a Son of the Void. Soon, an Ancient Star Race expert comparable to a Son of the Void began fighting with that First-Class Invincible.
However, not all members of Jingmen were at the headquarters, so their strength was insufficient. Coupled with the fact that the outsiders were well-prepared, their combat power completely crushed the Ancient Star Race of Jingmen.
Of course, Jingmen only needed to hold out for a while, and high-level members of the Ancient Star Race would surely come to the rescue.
With a boom, Jingmen's treasury was opened. All the invading Invincibles rushed toward the treasury, attempting to seize the Eternal Resources within.
Ye Tian also wanted to enter the treasury and snatch the Eternal Resources.
But after blending in and rushing into the treasury, he found that there were no Eternal Resources here at all.
Eternal Resources were easy to identify, as their aura was difficult to conceal. However, there was no aura of Eternal Resources here, only a faint lingering trace.
"The Eternal Resources have been transferred!" the other Invincibles speculated.
The biggest suspect was the Ancient Star Race expert with combat power comparable to a Son of the Void—Jue Men!
The Eternal Resources were likely on Jue Men, and it would be extremely difficult for them to kill him.
Consequently, these First-Class Invincibles could only plunder other resources in the treasury to compensate for their losses.
After a few breaths of time, these Invincibles began to flee. After all, this mission was considered a failure; obtaining the Eternal Resources was almost hopeless, so they could only leave.
Jue Men saw that the outsiders were about to leave and breathed a sigh of relief. Although Jingmen had suffered great losses this time and many experts had died, as long as he remained, Jingmen would still exist and would eventually recover.
But at that moment, a terrifying force acted upon his body.
The surrounding void shattered, and even his body could not stop crumbling and being annihilated; countless particles collapsed.
Soon, his consciousness vanished, and he was instantly killed.
Beside him, a hand took away his Void Sphere.
The person who struck was naturally Ye Tian. He had performed the Supreme Ancestral Technique level Void Shatter from a distance, instantly pulverizing Jue Men's body.
With his combat power as a Void Lord, killing a Son of the Void was naturally very simple.
Although both were First-Class Invincibles, the gap in their strength was like the difference between heaven and earth.
Soon, Jingmen's reinforcements arrived.
A Supreme Being descended upon the scene. Seeing the state of Jingmen, he was instantly infuriated, and he also saw Jue Men's nearly pulverized body.
"...Jue Men has been killed!"
The Supreme Being was shocked.
Jue Men's strength was not weak; he was famous throughout the Ancient Star Race and had recently entered the Inheritance Land. How could such a genius be killed?
Logically speaking, aside from a Supreme Being, no Invincible should have been able to kill Jue Men, at most only heavily wounding him.
Moreover, the battle had only just occurred; to kill Jue Men in such a short time was definitely the work of a Supreme Being.
"Who was it?"
He suspected it was one of the Ancient Star Race's own, because it was common knowledge that the Supreme Beings of outsiders could not enter the Star Ancient Domain.
On the other side.
Ye Tian obtained Jue Men's Void Sphere and immediately inspected the resources inside.
"So poor!"
Ye Tian looked down on Jue Men's resources. After all, the Ancient Star Race was confined to the Star Ancient Domain, so they naturally couldn't be too wealthy.
Aside from Eternal Resources, Ye Tian didn't care for any other resources.
"There really are Eternal Resources!" Ye Tian said excitedly.
