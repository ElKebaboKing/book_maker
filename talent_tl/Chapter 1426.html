Chapter 1425: Ye Tian Kills the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor
!
"Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor!!!!"
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor roared in rage.
At this moment, he wanted to kill the Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor.
Rumor had it that the Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor was extremely powerful, capable of perfectly borrowing the power of a Holy Realm, making him practically invincible within his own Xuanxu Holy Realm.
He admitted that if they were in the Xuanxu Holy Realm, he would never dare to make a move against the Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor.
However, this was not the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Furthermore, his strength was much greater than before; he had now condensed a Holy Soul, was one of the Holy Gifted, and had mastered two Holy Techniques to their limit.
Although everyone's foundation was different and their strength varied accordingly.
However, he did not believe that he was no match for Ye Tian.
"Die!"
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor attacked Ye Tian.
He wanted to kill Ye Tian! So what if Ye Tian was one of the Holy Gifted? Who wasn't a Holy Gifted? Normally, a Holy Gifted only had one or at most two Holy Talents. Ye Tian had many, so many factions speculated that he wasn't the offspring of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, but rather the child of a Primordial Ancestor.
But this speculation didn't hold up under scrutiny. After all, there were only so many Primordial Ancestors in the Dark Forbidden Zone, and they all knew each other. The possibility of a Primordial Ancestor suddenly appearing was too low.
Therefore, Ye Tian was at most... the offspring of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
If he killed Ye Tian, the Immortal Holy Sect behind him and his parents would be able to protect him.
So, while others were afraid to kill Ye Tian, he was not! "Golden Thorn!"
A top-tier Holy Weapon longsword appeared in the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor's hand, carrying the power of the Holy Technique "Golden Thorn" as it instantly pierced forward.
This sword strike was incredibly fast, and its power was terrifying.
Even a Supreme Domain Ancestor ranked around twentieth on the Supreme Ranking would be killed or heavily injured by such an attack.
After all, in the top twenty of the Supreme Ranking, the gap in strength between each rank was huge. In their eyes, Supreme Domain Ancestors ranked below twentieth were no more than ants.
"Get lost!"
Ye Tian unleashed the Holy Armor technique. He didn't use the Ancestral Armor; a mere Golden Snake Domain Ancestor didn't require him to use an Ancestral Technique.
Clang! The Golden Thorn struck the Holy Armor but failed to break through Ye Tian's defense at all.
Then, Ye Tian struck with a Void Slash, hitting the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor and piercing right through him.
Whoosh! The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor teleported away, looking at his body with a grim expression.
He was injured, and the injury was not light.
"The Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor is this strong?!!!"
This was beyond his expectations. He had thought all of Ye Tian's strength came from the Xuanxu Holy Realm, but he hadn't expected his own combat power to be so terrifying! "Why is it like this? He is also a Holy Gifted. Even if he knows more Holy Techniques, he hasn't condensed a Holy Soul yet. I should be stronger than him!"
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor couldn't understand it.
But he didn't dare to attack Ye Tian anymore. In other words, he had chickened out! However, since he had attacked Ye Tian, Ye Tian would not let him go.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian appeared beside the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor, activating the Time Holy Technique: Eternal Holy Technique.
Buzz! Time in this area was frozen.
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor was also affected. Although he quickly broke free from the time influence, Ye Tian's next round of attacks had begun, and he could no longer dodge.
"Void Slash!"
A near full-power Void Slash struck the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor. It was even more terrifying than the previous hurried Void Slash. This time, Ye Tian had channeled almost all the power of his Soul Holy Light into the technique.
The power of this strike definitely reached the level of a Six-step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
And the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor was not... a Six-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. At most, he could barely reach the level of an Eight-step Extreme Invincible Domain Ancestor, perhaps not even a Seven-step.
Only a Supreme Domain Ancestor like Wu Xiu possessed Seven-step Invincible Domain Ancestor combat power! Thus, the power of this strike...
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor couldn't withstand it. Even with defensive holy items protecting him, he couldn't offset much of the attack's power.
Boom!!!!! Nearly half of the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor's body was annihilated. This time, he was completely and severely injured.
"Quickly, help me!!!"
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor cried out for help to the Domain Ancestors of the Immortal Holy Sect.
However, how could the other Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Immortal Holy Sect dare to intervene? If they did, they would surely die; they couldn't even withstand the aftershocks.
Therefore, they could only call for help from even more powerful Supreme Domain Ancestors, such as the top five geniuses on the Immortal Holy Sect's Supreme Domain Ancestor ranking.
The top five geniuses of the Immortal Holy Sect all possessed combat power of a Seven-step Invincible Domain Ancestor or higher, so only they had a hope of saving the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor.
"This strength..."
In the distance, Wu Xiu showed deep wariness.
He only possessed Seven-step Invincible Domain Ancestor combat power, which was almost the limit, as he had also condensed a Holy Soul and would need to push for the Invincible Domain Ancestor rank once he left.
At the level of a Supreme Domain Ancestor, he no longer knew how to increase his combat power.
It could be said that it was extremely difficult for him to increase his combat power even by a tiny bit.
Among Supreme Domain Ancestors, even those on the Supreme Ranking, Seven-step Invincible combat power was the lifelong limit for most. No matter how they improved, it was hard to step into the Six-step level.
For example, the top five on the Forbidden Holy Sect's Supreme Ranking were basically only at the Seven-step level, merely infinitely close to the Six-step level.
Perhaps one or two of them had reached the Six-step level, but perhaps not.
But right before his eyes, Ye Tian's strength had truly reached the level of a Six-step Invincible Domain Ancestor!
"He cannot be provoked!"
Wu Xiu thought to himself.
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor truly lived up to being a Holy Gifted with a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor for a father; his foundation was deep, and he had many life-saving items.
Without Ye Tian using an Ancestral Technique, it would take at least a minute to kill the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor.
Soon, the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor was on the verge of death.
"Myriad Laws Domain Ancestor, if you kill me, my father, the Golden Desolate War Ancestor, will definitely kill you!!!"
The Golden Snake Domain Ancestor brought up his father.
"Then let him come!"
Ye Tian was fearless.
The Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Dark Holy Region could not be deployed casually; otherwise, the Dark Holy Region would have fallen into chaos long ago.
If a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor from the Immortal Holy Sect tried to kill him, it would be a violation of the rules. The Primordial Ancestor of the War Sect would definitely step in to stop them. This was also the hint that the Fire Pattern War Ancestor had given them this time.
Therefore, within the Holy Soul Realm, everyone could kill freely without worrying about the other party's identity.
At the final moment.
The third-ranked Supreme Domain Ancestor of the Immortal Holy Sect—the Dark Red Domain Ancestor—arrived!
