Chapter 1596: Dongyuan Parting Light!

Dongyuan Mountain.
This is one of the treasure mountains of the Source Sea, existing within the crevices of the Source Sea's void.
The void of the Source Sea is incredibly stable; even a Supreme Being finds it difficult to tear through the Source Sea's void. However, certain places possess naturally unstable void, and within the depths of these voids, treasure lands are born, with Dongyuan Mountain being one of them.
However, Dongyuan Mountain has no master because it completely seals itself off every certain period of time. As for when it will reopen, no one knows for sure, as there seems to be no pattern. Many powerful Supreme Beings do not dare to truly take up residence in Dongyuan Mountain, fearing they might be trapped inside.
Moreover, the treasure land of Dongyuan Mountain only offers slightly better cultivation conditions; the only true opportunity there is the Dongyuan Parting Light.
The Dongyuan Parting Light is highly corrosive to the bodies of Supreme Beings, but it also has one benefit: it can, to a certain extent, break through shackles. Therefore, some Supreme Beings utilize the Dongyuan Parting Light to break through their realms.
Of course, those Supreme Beings who consider themselves to have high potential and hope to reach higher levels will not use the Dongyuan Parting Light to break through.
"The aftereffects of the Dongyuan Parting Light are even greater than the Original Source Liquid, and the effect is quite average. It is only effective for a first-tier Supreme Being attempting to reach the second-tier Supreme Being realm."
Ye Tian murmured.
Consequently, he had no interest in the Dongyuan Parting Light.
As for Tianfeng Source God, he had not been a Supreme Being for a very long time, so he also had no interest in the Dongyuan Parting Light, unless he remained stagnant in the first-tier Supreme Being realm for an infinite amount of time before choosing such a method to break through.
But even if one used the Dongyuan Parting Light to break through, the success rate was not necessarily high.
It had to be said that the Nether Hall Master was taking a great risk this time.
To attempt the breakthrough to the second-tier Supreme Being realm, the Nether Hall Master had spent nearly half of his wealth purchasing various auxiliary breakthrough treasures, along with multiple treasures to heal the injuries caused by the Dongyuan Parting Light. This was how the news had leaked.
Otherwise, Tianfeng Source God would not have known that the Nether Hall Master was going to Dongyuan Mountain to attempt a breakthrough to the second-tier Supreme Being realm.
At this moment, Ye Tian and Tianfeng Source God had arrived at Dongyuan Mountain early, preparing to set an ambush here.
"Ye Tian, when the Nether Hall Master begins his secluded cultivation under the Dongyuan Parting Light, we will strike at his most critical moment. That way, we can heavily injure him and thus kill him," Tianfeng Source God reminded.
"Mm!"
Ye Tian said nothing, nor did he reveal that he could easily kill the Nether Hall Master.
Since Tianfeng Source God had made these arrangements, they would follow them.
Time passed slowly, and finally, on this day, the Nether Hall Master arrived at Dongyuan Mountain.
However, Tianfeng Source God's expression changed.
This was because the Nether Hall Master had not come to Dongyuan Mountain alone this time; he had brought another Supreme Being with him.
"It's him!"
Tianfeng Source God looked at the face of the person displayed on a Source Artifact in his hand and immediately felt troubled.
He quickly put away the Source Artifact and stopped his prying to prevent that Supreme Being from noticing them.
"What's wrong?"
Ye Tian asked.
Tianfeng Source God said, "The Supreme Being accompanying the Nether Hall Master is very powerful. Although he is not a second-tier Supreme Being, his strength is at the top-tier of the first-tier Supreme Being realm. He is not a Son of the Void, but it is said he has an extraordinary background, and his true strength is not inferior to a top-tier Son of the Void. He is much stronger than the Nether Hall Master."
"Is he here to guard him?"
Ye Tian guessed.
"That should be the case!" Tianfeng Source God smiled bitterly. "It seems we underestimated the Nether Hall Master. He must have also feared a surprise attack, so he invited this Chahai Source God to guard him. With Chahai Source God guarding, it will be extremely difficult for us to kill the Nether Hall Master."
"Are we still going to act?"
Ye Tian said.
Tianfeng Source God hesitated for a long time before finally nodding. "We act, but not to kill the Nether Hall Master. Instead, we will disrupt his ascension at the critical moment. Once we strike at the critical moment, the Nether Hall Master will inevitably be heavily injured, and at the very least, he won't be able to attempt a breakthrough to the second-tier Supreme Being realm again for a very long time. Although Chahai Source God is powerful, he cannot kill us."
"Very well!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Somewhere on Dongyuan Mountain, there was an area covered by the Dongyuan Parting Light.
At this moment, six first-tier Supreme Beings were there attempting to break through to the second-tier Supreme Being realm.
Of course, every Supreme Being would set up a defensive array to prevent others from interfering with them.
Under normal circumstances, other Supreme Beings would not come here to disrupt another's ascension.
Additionally, the area covered by the Dongyuan Parting Light was very vast, so they would not affect each other.
The Nether Hall Master and Chahai Source God arrived here.
The Nether Hall Master set up a defensive array and entered the Dongyuan Parting Light area, while Chahai Source God stood guard outside to prevent anyone from interfering with the Nether Hall Master's cultivation.
Upon entering the Dongyuan Parting Light area, the endless Dongyuan Parting Light flooded into the Nether Hall Master's body.
Even though he was wearing defensive Source Artifacts and using defensive Supreme Techniques to protect himself, he still could not completely block the Dongyuan Parting Light.
Moreover, he wouldn't truly block the Dongyuan Parting Light; otherwise, how could he attempt to break through to the second-tier Supreme Being realm?
The Nether Hall Master actively absorbed the Dongyuan Parting Light, allowing it to affect his body.
With the continuous erosion of the Dongyuan Parting Light, the Nether Hall Master's body began to decay rapidly, like a piece of rotting iron.
Immediately, the Nether Hall Master took out a large number of treasures capable of stopping this decay and swallowed them one by one, which made things slightly better.
"I cannot waste time. I cannot endure for too long within the Dongyuan Parting Light; I must break through as quickly as possible!"
The Nether Hall Master murmured.
Then, he refined various auxiliary breakthrough resources and treasures into his body one by one, using the power of the Dongyuan Parting Light to impact the second-tier Supreme Being realm.
BOOM!!!!
The Nether Hall Master's body began to undergo slight changes. Terrifying energy, with the help of the Eternal Rules, struck at the shackles in the unseen. This was the Source Sea's imprisonment of Supreme Beings; only by breaking this imprisonment could one step into the second-tier Supreme Being realm.
A Supreme Being's physical body and soul were fused, making them inherently incompatible lifeforms within the Source Sea, thus making a breakthrough extremely difficult.
Even with so much preparation and coming to Dongyuan Mountain, the Nether Hall Master did not have a hundred percent certainty, but he had at least a seventy percent chance, which was already enough.
"As long as no other Supreme Beings come to disturb me, I have a great hope of breaking through!"
The Nether Hall Master murmured.
Time passed slowly, and after a while, Tianfeng Source God could wait no longer.
"Ye Tian, let's act. We can't keep waiting! You go stall Chahai Source God, and I will attack the Nether Hall Master's array to disrupt his ascension."
Tianfeng Source God said.
"Leave Chahai Source God to me!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Swish!
He stepped out and appeared before Chahai Source God.
