Chapter 1540: First Stage Middle Phase, Strength Skyrockets!

"The effect of the top-grade tribulation stone is simply too good!"
Ye Tian was quite excited.
He activated the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique, and with the assistance of his Eternal Talent: Tribulation, an endless supply of tribulation force surged out from within the top-grade tribulation stone. It was many times more powerful than the thirty-seven tribulation blood.
Of course, Ye Tian was not using the top-grade tribulation stone to increase his cultivation level right now, but rather to cultivate the "Myriad Origins Returning to the Void," an invincible tribulation body technique.
At this moment, Ye Tian's invincible tribulation body seemed to be constantly compressing and condensing under the effect of the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, making his physical body even stronger.
As time passed, Ye Tian gradually reached the entry level of the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, and his progress became faster and faster, but he soon ran into trouble.
*Crack!*
Ye Tian's physical body split open during one cultivation session, and his entire physical form began to become unstable.
However, Ye Tian quickly recovered, though this cultivation session had caused a bit of damage to his invincible tribulation body.
"As expected, danger has appeared, and it has even brought some difficult-to-remove hidden consequences to the physical body. But compared to the gains, these consequences are nothing," Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, he was using a duplicate body to cultivate the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, so it had no impact on his main body.
Subsequently, Ye Tian began to refine the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void to make it more compatible with his own invincible tribulation body.
After many attempts, Ye Tian finally managed to slightly improve the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, ensuring that it would not cause hidden flaws or damage to his invincible tribulation body at the start.
Afterward, the duplicate body continued to cultivate the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void. Whenever he discovered a problem, he would begin to research it. He didn't care about the foundation of the duplicate body, treating it entirely as an experimental subject.
Regardless, the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void was still a terrifyingly powerful invincible tribulation body technique. It wouldn't affect Ye Tian's future breakthroughs; it was just that the hidden consequences it caused would require a long time to repair, and it might have some impact on his attempt to become a Supreme One.
However, not a single Invincible One in the Heavenly Wind Sea had been able to promote to a Supreme One, and the same was true for the entire Tianlin Realm, so no one worried about that point.
If Ye Tian didn't have his duplicate bodies, he wouldn't have dared to cultivate such a top-tier tribulation body technique.
After one domain year had passed, Ye Tian finally finished refining one top-grade tribulation stone. The duplicate body's invincible tribulation body was much stronger than before, having already reached the First Stage Middle Phase.
Whether it was the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void or other invincible tribulation bodies, they were all divided into six stages, which corresponded exactly to the six ranks of Invincible Ones.
If a sixth-rank Invincible One could cultivate their invincible tribulation body to the First Stage Circle, they could promote in a perfected state.
However, very few Invincible Ones could cultivate their invincible tribulation body to the Circle level because it consumed too many resources, and the difficulty increased the further one went.
The top-tier Myriad Origins Returning to the Void was the same; it was almost impossible to cultivate to the Circle stage, but the impact wasn't great. Even if one only cultivated it to the later stages, it could easily crush an ordinary invincible tribulation body at the Circle stage.
...
"It's about time to head back once. I have two top-grade tribulation stones left, and during this time the duplicate body cultivated the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, it went through thirty-three points of incompatibility, three major dangers, and the rest were minor dangers, but they have all been refined one by one. Before the main body cultivates the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void to the First Stage Middle Phase, there's almost no need to worry about flaws in the invincible tribulation body," Ye Tian thought.
Subsequently.
He left the Tianlin Realm.
The Heavenly Wind Sea also knew that Ye Tian was a person from the exotic worlds outside the Tianlin Realm, so they weren't surprised by his departure at all.
Moreover, the Heavenly Wind Sea was very powerful and had branches outside the Tianlin Realm. These branches were established in exotic worlds. Ye Tian could enter those branches to exchange for resources, though those branches could only exchange for very ordinary resources; many rare resources were unavailable for exchange.
This was because the exotic worlds were very dangerous. If too many resources were placed in a branch and it was raided by other forces, the loss would be too great.
Furthermore, it was quite troublesome for the Heavenly Wind Sea to send reinforcements to the exotic worlds.
Not long after.
Ye Tian's duplicate body returned to the Dark Forbidden Zone and handed the tribulation artifacts and top-grade tribulation stones to his main body.
Then, Ye Tian's main body officially began to cultivate the initially refined Myriad Origins Returning to the Void.
In the blink of an eye, another domain year passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had consumed one top-grade tribulation stone and cultivated the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void to the First Stage Middle Phase. His invincible tribulation body had become incredibly powerful.
With his current combat strength, he was already comparable to an ordinary fifth-rank Invincible One.
This was the power of a top-tier invincible tribulation body; it allowed one to fight above their level, even though he still technically belonged to the sixth-rank Invincible Ones because he had not yet comprehended an Invincible Ancestral Technique.
Ye Tian was in no hurry to comprehend an Invincible Ancestral Technique and would not even attempt to perceive one. His invincible tribulation body had not yet reached the First Stage Circle stage; if he accidentally comprehended an Invincible Ancestral Technique now, that would be a bad thing!
Now, Ye Tian only had one top-grade tribulation stone left. If he wanted to exchange for more top-grade tribulation stones from the Heavenly Wind Sea, he had to prepare a large amount of tribulation blood to convert into contribution points.
Thus, Ye Tian had his duplicate body begin searching for tribulation beasts.
After many attempts, including some failures that led to the death of the duplicate body, Ye Tian eventually found a sixty-tribulation beast.
A sixty-tribulation beast was equivalent to a weaker fifth-rank Invincible One. With Ye Tian's current strength, he would have to pay a heavy price to kill it.
But Ye Tian didn't care.
He sent his duplicate body to attack this sixty-tribulation beast. After fighting for hundreds of millions of years, he finally killed the beast at the cost of the duplicate body being heavily injured and on the verge of death, but the harvest was immense.
Sixteen portions of tribulation blood, two hundred thousand Origin Rule tribulation crystals, and fifteen tribulation bones.
Sixteen portions of sixty-tribulation blood plus fifteen sixty-tribulation bones—how many contribution points would these treasures be worth if exchanged at the Heavenly Wind Sea!
On this day.
Ye Tian condensed a new duplicate body and brought these treasures to a branch of the Heavenly Wind Sea in an exotic world—the Yueyang Branch.
The Yueyang Branch was located in the Yueyang World. The master of this world was a fourth-rank Invincible One and an elite member of the Heavenly Wind Sea.
At the same time, thirty-three Invincible Ones were permanently stationed in this exotic world, and no other forces were allowed to open branches here. Only Invincible Ones from the Heavenly Wind Sea were allowed to enter and exit this exotic world freely.
*Boom!!!!*
Ye Tian tore through the barrier of the Yueyang World and arrived. After showing his badge, he faced no obstruction.
In the central void of the Yueyang World, a massive building stood tall.
This was the Yueyang Branch of the Heavenly Wind Sea.
Ye Tian walked in and met one of the people in charge of the Yueyang Branch—Qi Yang. He was also a fifth-rank Invincible One and a high-level member.
The Heavenly Wind Sea had long-term missions to collect tribulation blood and tribulation bones, and there were also options to exchange for them.
However, the price for selling to the Heavenly Wind Sea was definitely lower than the price for buying; otherwise, wouldn't the Heavenly Wind Sea lose money?
For example, if one wanted to exchange for a portion of tribulation blood, it would cost 1,000 contribution points, but if one wanted to sell it, they would only receive 960 contribution points.
Ye Tian didn't care about this; otherwise, if he really wanted to trade tribulation blood for top-grade tribulation stones, he would have nowhere else to go.
"I want to accept the tribulation blood and tribulation bone missions!"
Ye Tian said.
Qi Yang nodded and processed the mission for Ye Tian. Ye Tian immediately submitted the mission, handing over the tribulation blood and bones he had obtained.
"This is sixty-tribulation blood and bones!!!"
Qi Yang exclaimed in shock.
