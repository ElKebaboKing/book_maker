Chapter 1499: Breakthrough, Ultimate Ancestor!

Boom!!!!
Ye Tian's two types of Endless Ancestral Bodies began to fuse. As the degree of fusion between the two Endless Ancestral Techniques grew higher, his Ancestral Body was also rapidly merging.
This process required a massive amount of resources. Ye Tian brought out the countless resources he had accumulated over the long years, as well as the resources obtained from the Sacred Domain World, to prepare for the charge toward becoming an Ultimate Ancestor.
His foundation far surpassed that of other top Ancestors. Once he broke through to the Ultimate Ancestor level, the resources he would need would likely be several times or even more than ten times that of others.
Fortunately, he was well-prepared and thus did not lack resources.
According to Ye Tian's understanding, for a top Sacred Beast to charge toward becoming an Ultimate Sacred Beast, it would take at least one Domain Year to break through. Of course, one could come out of seclusion in the middle without any impact. After all, Ye Tian had technically broken through; now it was just the process of his Ancestral Body's transformation, which would not reverse.
Naturally, if Ye Tian hadn't fully broken through, he wouldn't have come out of seclusion.
In the blink of an eye, a Domain Year passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had already refined a vast amount of resources and finally completed the transformation of his Ancestral Body.
Now, Ye Tian was already an Ultimate Ancestor.
"Ten times! My strength is ten times greater than before!"
Ye Tian was very excited.
Previously, his strength had reached its limit and couldn't be increased by even a tiny bit. Now, having just advanced, he had achieved a tenfold increase, which was a massive leap.
Moreover, he had only just broken through to the Ultimate Ancestor realm, so he still had a period of rapid strength growth ahead of him.
Within a short time, it wouldn't be a problem at all to increase his strength by several more times.
In that case, his strength would likely be dozens of times greater than before his breakthrough.
He wasn't surprised by the strength of an Ultimate Ancestor. After all, although he was incredibly powerful before, he still couldn't defeat a Bone Emperor, only managing to withstand a single blow from one by relying on twenty Ultimate Ancestral Defense Techniques.
In reality, that Bone Emperor had forced his way through the Gate, and his strength was suppressed by the Dark Forbidden Zone. He could probably only exert thirty percent of his power and had likely paid a price, suffering significant injuries. Even under those circumstances, he could easily suppress Ye Tian.
If it were a Bone Emperor in perfect condition, he could have been easily injured.
But now, with his current strength and the many Ultimate Ancestral Techniques he mastered, he was strong enough to contend with any Bone Emperor or Ultimate Sacred Beast.
"I can't move against the Sea Sky Holy Sect yet. I'll wait until I'm even stronger."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he began to frantically absorb and refine countless resources to increase his strength.
Now that he had broken through the shackles, he could continue to improve his cultivation. His strength would naturally skyrocket, and he could continue to temper his Endless Ancestral Body, causing his power to increase at an incredibly fast pace.
Domain Year after Domain Year passed, and in the blink of an eye, several Domain Eras had gone by.
By now, Ye Tian's resources were exhausted.
Originally, he had a vast amount of resources, not only those collected from the Dark Forbidden Zone but also those gathered repeatedly from the Sacred Domain World. However, none of that could withstand the speed at which an Ultimate Ancestor refined resources.
After several Domain Eras of bitter cultivation, those resources simply couldn't hold out and were completely depleted.
It could be said that he was now poor.
However, his strength had increased significantly, becoming several times stronger once again.
With his current strength, he could definitely easily crush the two Ultimate Sacred Beasts of the Sea Sky Holy Sect.
"It's a pity I still haven't obtained the permanent construction method for the Gate!"
Ye Tian sighed.
Not long ago, his duplicated body had entered the Sea Sky Forbidden Land from the Sacred Domain World again. He naturally shared those memories, but unfortunately, the duplicated body still hadn't found an opportunity.
The permanent construction method for the Gate was incredibly precious; it was the foundation of the three major Holy Sects. It was said that in a very distant era, an expert from another realm invaded the Sacred Domain World during its Great Calamity. In the end, the various major forces paid a heavy price to trap him. By the time the calamity ended, that extra-dimensional expert couldn't return to his realm and was directly erased by the laws of the Sacred Domain World, leaving behind a vast amount of inheritance, including the permanent construction method for the Gate.
The Sea Sky Holy Sect was also one of the forces at that time. Unfortunately, after a series of battles, only three forces eventually produced Ultimate Sacred Beasts.
This was the information the duplicated body had gathered through investigation.
It could be said that besides the three major Holy Sects, almost no other force possessed the permanent construction method for the Gate; otherwise, they would have certainly tried to build one.
"Relying on luck won't get me the permanent construction method for the Gate. I can only wait for the Sacred Domain World's Great Calamity... According to the time calculations, the Sacred Domain World has only one Dark Era left before the Great Calamity arrives. The Dark Forbidden Zone still has tens of millions of years before its fourth Dark Era, and five more Dark Eras before the fourth Great Calamity. On the day of the Sacred Domain World's Great Calamity, I can enter the Sacred Domain World directly through the Sea Sky Holy Sect's Gate and charge into the sect. Not only can I seize their resources, but I might also have a chance to obtain the permanent construction method for the Gate!"
Ye Tian planned.
Next, Ye Tian cultivated bitterly while waiting for the Sacred Domain World's Great Calamity.
Meanwhile, the fourth Dark Holy Land of the Dark Forbidden Zone was born. This time, Ye Tian didn't participate much, instead letting the Ancestors of the human race fight for it. He now looked down upon a mere Dark Holy Land.
The human race was very strong now. His family members had all reached the combat power of top Ancestors, and there were many other Ancestors, including many elite ones. Other forces simply couldn't compete with the human race.
Therefore, he didn't need to worry, unless those Ancient Ancestors woke up and participated.
But those Ancient Ancestors were all single-mindedly planning to become Ultimate Ancestors and couldn't be bothered with a mere Dark Holy Land.
----------------
Somewhere in the Dark Forbidden Zone, the fourth Dark Holy Land was born, and Ancestors appeared one after another.
The Sacred Domain, Destruction Ancestor, Realm Ancestor, and other Ancestors appeared one by one. Several Ancestors of the human race also appeared, led by Ye Tian's son, Ye Xingchen. He had also reached the level of a top Ancestor and, with the help of Ye Tian 2.0, had mastered three Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. This was naturally due to Ye Tian's dedicated guidance and demonstrations of his own Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, which finally allowed him to create his own.
Otherwise, relying on himself, there was no way he could have mastered three Ultimate Ancestral Techniques in such a short time.
"The Myriad Law Ancestor didn't come?"
The Destruction Ancestor was very surprised.
This was a Dark Holy Land, yet the Myriad Law Ancestor actually had no interest in it.
"My father looks down on a mere Dark Holy Land!"
Ye Xingchen sneered.
At this moment.
The other Ancestors understood Ye Tian's pursuit. It must be the same as those Ancient Ancestors, working hard to charge toward the Ultimate Ancestor realm.
Unfortunately, they had no idea that Ye Tian had already stepped into the Ultimate Ancestor realm.
