Chapter 1417: Twenty Percent Soul Holy Light
!
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor took action; today would be his battle for fame.
He did not believe that even after breaking the shackles of an Invincible Domain Ancestor and transforming his Invincible Domain Body three times, he would still be no match for Five Thunders Domain Ancestor! "Ancient Shaking Punch!"
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor threw a punch toward Five Thunders Domain Ancestor, shaking the entire Void Territory and causing it to tremble incessantly.
If it were an ordinary first-step Invincible Domain Ancestor, they would likely be blown apart if they tried to take this punch head-on.
However, at that moment, a lightning spear appeared in Five Thunders Domain Ancestor's hand. This lightning spear was not a physical weapon but was condensed from a certain type of power.
"A Saint Technique? No, it surpasses Saint Techniques; it's an Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian recognized it.
He had previously seen the Supreme Domain Ancestor, Fire Pattern War Ancestor, take action. Fire Pattern War Ancestor had performed an Ancestral Technique, so he recognized the fluctuations of its aura.
The move Five Thunders Domain Ancestor performed clearly contained the aura of an Ancestral Technique; it was obviously... an Ancestral Technique.
Five Thunders Domain Ancestor thrust the lightning spear forward with immense force. In an instant, it shattered Gu Heng Domain Ancestor's Saint Technique, the Ancient Shaking Punch, and broke through his defenses in a single strike, directly piercing through the Invincible Domain Body that Gu Heng had transformed three times.
Pfft! Pfft! Pfft! Gu Heng Domain Ancestor spat out blood repeatedly and retreated rapidly, his face filled with utter horror.
"You... you've comprehended an Ancestral Technique? How is this possible?"
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor said in disbelief.
The other Domain Ancestors were also incredibly shocked, as if all of this was too abnormal.
How many... Supreme Domain Ancestors had never even comprehended a single Ancestral Technique? On what basis did Five Thunders Domain Ancestor manage to do so? For an Invincible Domain Ancestor to master an Ancestral Technique, the difficulty was simply too great!
It was practically harder than breaking through to become a Supreme Domain Ancestor! Gu Heng Domain Ancestor had broken the shackles of an Invincible Domain Ancestor and was destined to become a Supreme Domain Ancestor in the future, which was already impressive. But Five Thunders Domain Ancestor was even more formidable, having mastered an Ancestral Technique while still an Invincible Domain Ancestor. How heaven-defying was that!
"This is Five Thunders Domain Ancestor's Five Thunders Saint Spear. It was originally a Saint Technique. It seems Five Thunders Domain Ancestor has not only pushed his Saint Body past its limits to possess a trace of Ancestral Body characteristics but has also pushed his Saint Technique past its limits, advancing it to the level of an Ancestral Technique!"
Gu Hai Domain Ancestor thought to himself.
He had to admit that he greatly admired Five Thunders Domain Ancestor; at the very least, he could not achieve this himself.
Having mastered an Ancestral Technique, Five Thunders Domain Ancestor's strength had already broken the limits of a first-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. His combat power was even comparable to a weaker Supreme Domain Ancestor.
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor was naturally no match for Five Thunders Domain Ancestor. If they truly fought to the death, he would likely be killed directly.
"I concede!"
Gu Heng Domain Ancestor said helplessly.
With Gu Heng Domain Ancestor's surrender, the other Invincible Domain Ancestors did not dare to challenge Five Thunders Domain Ancestor either.
The Dark Saint Domain certainly possessed Invincible Domain Ancestors who could rival Five Thunders Domain Ancestor, but none were present here.
"Five Thunders Domain Ancestor won!"
Ye Tian breathed a sigh of relief.
Now, these Invincible Domain Ancestors had no way to pressure the Human Race or forcibly seize the Saint Tablet slots.
"Thank you, Five Thunders Domain Ancestor!"
Ye Tian felt...
"Just doing my duty!"
Five Thunders Domain Ancestor said indifferently.
The Invincible Domain Ancestors of the various major powers did not dare to act recklessly. Those Paramount Domain Ancestors who wanted Saint Tablet slots could only purchase them.
Consequently, one by one, the Paramount Domain Ancestors of the Saint Gates almost exhausted their entire fortunes, and some even had to borrow resources and treasures from Invincible Domain Ancestors to barely afford a single Saint Tablet slot.
It could be said that Ye Tian made a huge profit this time!
Because the price Ye Tian set was too high, many Paramount Domain Ancestors were unwilling to buy and could only choose to give up.
In the end, Ye Tian found that out of the 100 Saint Tablet slots, quite a few were still left. He distributed some to the War Gate, giving them to several Invincible Domain Ancestors of the War Gate for them to allocate, which served as a form of repayment to them.
The remaining Saint Tablet slots were given by Ye Tian to a newly selected group of Domain Ancestors from the Human Race.
Just like that, all the Saint Tablet slots were fully distributed.
Afterward.
Ye Tian entered one of the Saint Tablet worlds he had selected and began to refine the world core.
Before long, the refinement of the world core was complete.
"Close the world entrance!"
With a thought from Ye Tian, the world entrance closed. This way, outsiders would not be able to enter unless they broke the Saint Tablet.
However, the Saint Tablet was incredibly sturdy. Unless one possessed the power to shatter the Profound Void Saint Realm, it was impossible to break the Saint Tablet.
Only an Invincible Domain Ancestor like Five Thunders Domain Ancestor had a hope of breaking the Saint Tablet; almost no other Invincible Domain Ancestor could do it.
Therefore, going into seclusion here was very safe, with no worries about being disturbed.
The Saint Tablet world was like a starry sky, and Ye Tian sat cross-legged there to begin his cultivation.
Wisps of starlight fell into Ye Tian's soul, helping him temper the Soul Holy Light.
This starlight was naturally not actual starlight but the power of the Saint Tablet, which aided in tempering the Soul Holy Light.
"The cultivation speed is so fast!"
Ye Tian was overjoyed.
Immediately, he circulated the Endless Domain Law, frantically tempering his soul and continuously generating Soul Holy Light.
Furthermore, in this place, his comprehension would also receive a significant boost.
"No wonder everyone wants to enter the Saint Tablet world. The benefits of cultivating here are too numerous. Even a third-tier Paramount Domain Ancestor who comes here to cultivate could eventually become a first-tier Invincible Domain Ancestor!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Day after day passed, and Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light continued to be born, increasing in quantity.
Initially, Ye Tian's entire soul had only a small amount of Soul Holy Light, but now the Soul Holy Light gradually covered the surface of his soul, already occupying a certain proportion.
In the blink of an eye, 300,000 years had passed.
After such a long period of bitter cultivation, Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light had increased greatly.
"Soul Holy Light covering one-fifth of the soul!"
Ye Tian looked into his soul sea at the Soul Holy Light that covered one-fifth of his soul and was immediately very satisfied.
It could be said that his Soul Holy Light had increased tenfold during this cultivation session.
Previously, when he cultivated bitterly on his own without the help of the Saint Tablet world for a hundred million years, he had only barely increased the original total amount of Soul Holy Light by thirty percent. But now it was tenfold, showing how effective the Saint Tablet world was.
Soul Holy Light covering one-fifth of the soul was equivalent to twenty percent Soul Holy Light. Moreover, his twenty percent Soul Holy Light was likely stronger than the Saint Soul of an ordinary Paramount Domain Ancestor; after all, his soul was too powerful.
Unfortunately, having cultivated to this extent, it was difficult for the Soul Holy Light to grow further because the boost from the Saint Tablet world now had a very average effect on him.
In terms of strength, Ye Tian's current power was much greater than in the past. After all, with the massive increase in Soul Holy Light, his strength naturally skyrocketed.
"A single Saint Tablet world can only allow my Soul Holy Light to grow to twenty percent. At this rate, tempering will become harder and harder later on. I'm afraid I'll need seven or eight Saint Tablet worlds, or even more, before my soul can completely transform into a Saint Soul!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Furthermore, the same Saint Tablet could only provide an effect once. For example, if he were to go to another Saint Tablet world within this same Saint Tablet, it would be of no use. In fact, the 100 Saint Tablet worlds of this Saint Tablet were all essentially the same.
"There are very few Saint Tablets in the Dark Saint Domain, and the slots for many Saint Tablet worlds have long since been used up. With my status, obtaining one Saint Tablet world slot from the War Gate is likely the... limit. Expecting to condense a Saint Soul with the help of Saint Tablets is probably a pipe dream!"
Ye Tian shook his head.
Therefore, he had to rely on other opportunities to have any hope of condensing a Saint Soul!
