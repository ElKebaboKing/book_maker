On this day.
Ye Tian found a treasure that even made him unable to help but cry out in surprise.
“Taichen Sacred Flower!”
The Taichen Sacred Flower was an incomparably precious treasure, extremely rare in the Source Sea. Every time it appeared, it would trigger countless fights among Supreme Beings and half-step Eternal Realm experts.
Once, a Taichen Sacred Flower appeared in the world. Too many half-step Eternal Realm experts fought over it, and in the end it drew the attention of an Eternal Realm expert, who took away that Taichen Sacred Flower.
This showed that even Eternal Realm experts would not ignore the Taichen Sacred Flower.
Because the Taichen Sacred Flower could allow a living being to easily reach half-step Eternal Realm, and its foundation would become incomparably strong. It could even reverse the tribulation body; even without condensing an Eternal Body, it could still raise the tribulation body to a level comparable to a lower Eternal Body.
In other words, if he obtained this Taichen Sacred Flower, he could directly raise his wife to half-step Eternal Realm, and her cultivation would reach the Extreme of half-step Eternal Realm.
In addition, during a period after the breakthrough, her comprehension and insight would erupt, and she could easily comprehend all kinds of Eternal techniques.
Thus, it would not be a problem for his wife to directly reach the Extreme of the top Eternal Realm.
Of course, he did not recommend that his wife step into the Extreme Realm, otherwise it would be somewhat difficult for her to break through to Eternal Realm.
“Take it!”
Ye Tian immediately took the Taichen Sacred Flower, then had the clone immediately bring it back to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Otherwise, once an Eternal Realm expert learned of it, they probably would not mind erasing him and snatching this Taichen Sacred Flower away.
After all, even Eternal Realm experts probably had relatives who had not reached half-step Eternal Realm and also needed the Taichen Sacred Flower.
