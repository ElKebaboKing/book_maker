Chapter 1555: The Eternal Source Object Has Arrived!

The Yuanji Abyss is a shattered Eternal Heaven and Earth, formerly known as the Yuanji Realm. After it shattered, it became known as the Yuanji Abyss.
At its Peak, the Yuanji Realm was even more powerful and ancient than the Tianlin Realm. During its Peak, nearly a hundred Supreme Beings were born there.
However, during a great war, the eternal Yuanji Realm was shattered, multiple Supreme Beings fell, and ultimately, the situation of the Yuanji Abyss was created.
After the Yuanji Abyss was formed, the rules changed, making it impossible for Supreme Beings to remain in the Yuanji Abyss. If they forcibly entered the Yuanji Abyss, they might even fall.
Thus, the Yuanji Abyss became a treasure land for Invincibles, but also a forbidden land.
The Yuanji Abyss is very dangerous, not only due to the slaughter between Invincibles but also because of the threat from Abyss Tribulation Beasts.
Eternal Heavens and Earths do not have Tribulation Beasts, but the Yuanji Abyss gave birth to Abyss Tribulation Beasts. These Abyss Tribulation Beasts are different from the Tribulation Beasts of foreign heavens and earths. The Tribulation Beasts of foreign heavens and earths only appear when a foreign heaven and earth is destroyed and return to slumber when the next Tribulation Era arrives.
However, the Abyss Tribulation Beasts of the Yuanji Abyss are always awake and roaming, and among them are many Tribulation Beasts comparable to 100,000 Tribulations, possessing incredibly powerful strength.
Consequently, once weak Invincibles enter the Yuanji Abyss, it is almost certain death. This is why everyone forms teams to enter the Yuanji Abyss.
There are many opportunities in the Yuanji Abyss, such as Rule Heavenly Objects and precious resources; even Eternal Source Objects have been born there before.
Furthermore, when the Yuanji Realm shattered, countless powerhouses fell. Therefore, there are many relics and inheritances of powerhouses there, even Supreme Being inheritances.
Some Invincibles have also obtained opportunities in the Yuanji Abyss and become Supreme Beings.
For the Invincibles of the nearby foreign heavens and earths, the Yuanji Abyss is undoubtedly where their hope of becoming powerhouses lies.
There are two routes to enter the Yuanji Abyss: one is to enter directly from the Source Sea, and the other is to enter through the Third-tier Gate.
Currently, the foreign heaven and earth where Ye Tian is located is a modified foreign heaven and earth that has integrated the rules of the Yuanji Abyss, making it one of the subordinate foreign heavens and earths of the Yuanji Abyss. The benefit of this is that it can accommodate First-class Invincibles.
However, Supreme Beings still cannot enter this foreign heaven and earth.
Through this foreign heaven and earth, Invincibles can easily enter the Yuanji Abyss. There are many passages here, but to use these passages, one must pay some Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals. This is a rule set by the organizations that occupy this place.
It is said that this place was jointly built by several Eternal-name level and numerous Saint-name level forces, and no one dares to break the rules here.
After Ye Tian roughly understood the situation, he paid some Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals and entered the Yuanji Abyss through a void passage.
-------------------
Boom!!!!
Endless suppression swept over, making Ye Tian feel rules similar to those of the Source Sea, but unlike the Source Sea, this place was transformed into countless spaces.
"The Yuanji Abyss, I've arrived!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
His current location was the outermost layer of the Yuanji Abyss. There were no resources here, as they had long been divided up. Coupled with the restrictions of the major forces, this layer of space was very safe. If anyone wanted to leave the Yuanji Abyss, they could leave through the void passages here and return to the various foreign heavens and earths.
Afterward, Ye Tian began to go deeper into the Yuanji Abyss.
According to the mission instructions, they needed to place the mysterious box at a designated location in the Yuanji Abyss. As for where to place it, he needed to contact a certain powerhouse from Tianfenghai within the Yuanji Abyss.
"It's this layer!"
Ye Tian followed the instruction map to a certain layer of the outer Yuanji Abyss. After confirming the coordinates were in this layer, he took out a communicator and began to message the powerhouse from Tianfenghai.
Soon, a voice came from the other end of the communicator.
"Leave the item where it is and then leave this place. Do not return to the Tianlin Realm for now; you can wait in a nearby foreign heaven and earth."
Ye Tian followed the instructions, put down the mysterious box, and left.
Clearly, Tianfenghai was also worried that these Invincible members performing the mission might betray them and set a trap. Therefore, if the other party sensed he was still there, they would not come to take the mysterious box, and his mission would not be considered complete.
After Ye Tian departed, a First-class Invincible arrived at the spot, reached out to pick up the mysterious box, glanced at it, and casually stowed it away before tearing through layers of space and vanishing.
……
Ye Tian did not leave the Yuanji Abyss. Instead, he remained in the outermost layer of the Yuanji Abyss, constantly copying the talents of various Invincibles. The number of Invincibles here was immense; there were always Invincibles entering and leaving.
If he copied their Ultimate Ancestral Talents and fused them into Eternal Ancestral Talents, he would possess even more Eternal Ancestral Talents.
As for treasure hunting, he had no interest for now. After all, his strength really didn't amount to much here, and it would be very easy to fall.
Once he fell, his losses would not be small. At the very least, the talents he had copied would truly be gone unless he dispatched another duplicated body to copy them again.
As time passed, one Fourth-class Invincible after another from Tianfenghai arrived with mysterious boxes.
Finally, a squad descended into the Yuanji Abyss.
This was the secret squad secretly cultivated by Tianfenghai. The strength of every Invincible was powerful; they could all kill Third-class Invincibles as Fourth-class Invincibles. They were geniuses among geniuses, and all were Invincibles who had promoted from the Body Nine Tribulations Realm.
However, only six people remained of the ten-man squad. Four genius Invincibles had fallen, and the other six were not lightly injured.
It couldn't be helped; the strength of the ten was too great. No matter how they tried to hide their abilities, something was discovered to be amiss, and they were subjected to even more encirclement and suppression by the Nether Hall.
Once, they were surrounded by dozens of Third-class Invincibles. Even though they were suppressed miserably, those were Third-class Invincibles after all, and besides those Third-class Invincibles, there were many Fourth-class Invincibles.
If Tianfenghai hadn't bestowed powerful weapons and secret treasures upon them, only then could the six of them have finally escaped; otherwise, they would all be dead.
After paying a heavy price, the six of them finally broke through the encirclement and arrived here.
After entering the Yuanji Abyss, they immediately contacted a First-class Invincible from Tianfenghai.
Soon, that First-class Invincible appeared.
"Greetings, Chenling Supreme!"
The six Invincibles said respectfully.
"Four of you have fallen?" Chenling Supreme frowned.
"Yes!" the six Invincibles replied.
"You've worked hard!" Chenling Supreme sighed.
In his eyes, these six Invincibles all had hope of being promoted to First-class Invincibles in the future. Now that four had fallen, it was indeed a massive loss for Tianfenghai, but compared to the plan, this loss was still worth it.
"Give all the boxes to me!"
The six Invincibles nodded and handed over ten boxes. Although the other four had fallen, the boxes had still been handed over to them.
Because they didn't know which box contained the mission item, they could only protect them all.
Chenling Supreme took the ten boxes, told the six to hide in a nearby foreign heaven and earth, and then headed alone into the depths of the Yuanji Abyss.
There, the leader of Tianfenghai was being besieged by many First-class Invincibles of the Nether Hall. He needed to unite with the First-class Invincibles of Tianfenghai here to charge toward the leader and deliver the Eternal Source Object to him, helping the leader complete his final breakthrough to become a Supreme Being.
