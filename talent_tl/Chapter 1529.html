Chapter 1528: The Six Levels of the Invincible Ones

"One portion of Tribulation Blood!"
Ye Tian demanded an exorbitant price.
"Impossible!" Tong Zu immediately refused.
One portion of Tribulation Blood—how could that be possible!
The value of these members was not necessarily worth a portion of Tribulation Blood. They were merely Supreme Beings; they could be cultivated given enough time. Unless it was a junior he personally favored, he would never be willing to pay the price of a portion of Tribulation Blood. Otherwise, these Supreme Beings simply weren't worth it.
After all, Tribulation Blood was too precious. One had to slay a Tribulation Beast to obtain a single portion.
However, Tribulation Beasts were far too powerful. Even he only dared to target beasts of the first or second tribulation. Once a Tribulation Beast reached the third tribulation level, even he would be no match for it, unless their Leader personally took action.
Therefore, the Illusory Dream World could not possibly pay the price of a portion of Tribulation Blood for these Supreme Beings.
Ye Tian didn't truly expect Tong Zu to pay a portion of Tribulation Blood to ransom these Supreme Beings; he was merely testing the waters.
"I want a type of treasure that contains a large amount of Tribulation Power. Does your Illusory Dream World possess such a treasure?" Ye Tian asked.
Tong Zu really didn't want to deal with Ye Tian, but since the Supreme Beings of the Illusory Dream World were in Ye Tian's hands and he had no way to deal with him, he could only answer: "Aside from treasures like Tribulation Blood, there is another type—Tribulation Stones. They are a resource occasionally born when a foreign world is destroyed. Compared to Tribulation Blood, Tribulation Stones are naturally much more numerous, but their value is far lower. Tribulation Stones also contain terrifying Tribulation Power, which should satisfy your requirements."
"Let me see one!"
Ye Tian demanded.
"Fine, I happen to have a few Tribulation Stones on me!"
Tong Zu took out a Tribulation Stone from his storage treasure and tossed it to Ye Tian.
The Tribulation Stone was six-sided, black, and contained incredibly dense Tribulation Power. If an ordinary Ancestor were to even glance at it, their mind or even their physical body might be affected by the stone's power; the Tribulation Power it contained was simply too terrifying.
Without activating his Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique or invoking his Eternal Talent 'Tribulation,' Ye Tian attempted to refine the Tribulation Stone using his own strength. He found it very difficult to refine; instead, the stone's Tribulation Power began to invade his physical body, wreaking havoc within.
Under such circumstances, there was no way to utilize the Tribulation Stone.
"Tong Zu, Supreme Beings shouldn't be able to use Tribulation Stones to temper their bodies for tribulations, right?" Ye Tian inquired.
"It is possible, but it requires many precious auxiliary items and secret techniques. Even then, the speed is much slower than the Tribulation Power during a Great Tribulation. However, a Great Tribulation only lasts for a short time, whereas with Tribulation Stones, one can refine Tribulation Power continuously. It's just a massive waste of resources and time. In short, Tribulation Blood cannot be given to you; only Tribulation Stones are available. As for whether you want them or not, that is your business!" Tong Zu said coldly.
"I want the Tribulation Stones, but their value is far inferior to Tribulation Blood. You must provide a sufficient quantity of Tribulation Stones to ransom these Supreme Beings. Additionally, I need detailed cultivation data regarding the Invincible Ones," Ye Tian demanded.
"You are too greedy!" Tong Zu snorted, but he did not refuse Ye Tian.
The two continued to negotiate, eventually settling on the number of Tribulation Stones, and Tong Zu also agreed to give Ye Tian the cultivation data for the Invincible Ones.
10,000 Tribulation Stones, plus a set of cultivation data.
Tong Zu had the Illusory Dream World prepare for a while before handing these two items over to Ye Tian.
Ye Tian did not go back on his word. Otherwise, if the Illusory Dream World decided to retaliate at all costs, the Human Race and the Dark Forbidden Domain would not be able to withstand their vengeance. Being able to extort so many good things from the Illusory Dream World was already enough.
When Ye Tian left this forbidden land of the Illusory Dream World, he saw the forbidden land vanish, and that Second-Tier Gate disappeared without a trace.
It was clear that the Illusory Dream World had also abandoned the Dark Forbidden Domain, not wishing to suffer any more such losses.
Human Race, Profound Void Ancestral Realm.
Ye Tian returned and began to browse the cultivation data on the Invincible Ones from the Illusory Dream World.
Supreme Beings were divided into three levels—one, two, and three—and their strength improvement was closely related to Supreme Ancestral Techniques. Above the third level was the Physical Body Tribulation Realm or the Soul Tribulation Realm, followed by the Invincible One realm. The classification of an Invincible One's strength was quite complex.
First, the weakest Invincible Ones were those who had just broken through. They still used Supreme Ancestral Techniques and did not possess a single Invincible Ancestral Technique.
Next were the Invincible Ones who had mastered one Invincible Ancestral Technique. Such Invincible Ones were naturally much stronger than those who had just broken through; their strength would undergo an earth-shattering transformation.
As for how to comprehend an Invincible Ancestral Technique? Ye Tian didn't know before, but now he understood.
· ········· Seeking Flowers ··· ·····
Merging an Ultimate Ancestral Technique from one foreign realm with an Ultimate Ancestral Technique from another, combining the rules of two foreign realms to form a Supreme Ancestral Technique—that was the origin of Supreme Ancestral Techniques. However, Invincible Ancestral Techniques were not formed through fusion; rather, they were comprehended from the rules of the Eternal World, and there was only an extremely low probability of comprehending one.
"Eternal World?"
Ye Tian didn't know what such a place represented, but did the word 'Eternal' have something to do with Eternal Talents?
The Illusory Dream World hadn't revealed too much information, leaving him somewhat confused. Clearly, the Illusory Dream World had withheld it on purpose.
Basically, these two levels of Invincible Ones were referred to as Sixth-Grade and Fifth-Grade Invincible Ones. They belonged to the bottom tier of Invincible Ones, yet the majority of Invincible Ones resided within these two levels.

A Fourth-Grade Invincible One had to master at least three Invincible Ancestral Techniques, and their cultivation had to far exceed that of an ordinary Invincible One to be called such.
A Third-Grade Invincible One required a perfected Invincible Tribulation Body, mastery of multiple Invincible Ancestral Techniques, and the requirement of having ascended as an Invincible One through the sixth tribulation of either the physical body or the soul. Only such an Invincible One could be called Third-Grade.
As for Second-Grade Invincible Ones, the requirements were even higher. If an Invincible One ascended through the Physical Body Tribulation Realm, they were required to have their physical body feed back into their soul, allowing the soul to also undergo tribulation cultivation. By crossing tribulations repeatedly until the soul reached the same level of tribulation, they would become a Second-Grade Invincible One.
As for First-Grade Invincible Ones, that involved the fusion of the Physical Body Tribulation Realm and the Soul Tribulation Realm into one, becoming an Eternal Tribulation Realm Invincible One. That was a First-Grade Invincible One.
"Invincible Ancestors, six grades. The classification is more detailed and has more levels than that of Supreme Ancestors. According to this classification, Tong Zu is likely only a Sixth-Grade Invincible One. As for the Leader of the Illusory Dream World, perhaps they are also just a Sixth-Grade Invincible One, albeit with profound cultivation, infinitely approaching the Fifth-Grade," Ye Tian speculated.
Of course.
There were still many things Ye Tian didn't understand, but he could continue to extort the other five super-grade forces, using the same method to make them provide a certain amount of Tribulation Stones and data.
"That's the plan. No rush to cultivate yet. I'll continue to extort these super-grade forces. They've taken resources from the Dark Forbidden Domain time and again; it's time for some payback!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
