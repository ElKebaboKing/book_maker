Chapter 1690 Eternal Supreme Treasure, the Primordial Order!

What appeared before Ye Tian was a gray plaque. Having lost its master, its original aura as an Eternal Supreme Treasure spread out, even exerting a certain amount of suppression on Ye Tian.
Its aura was incomparably grand and majestic, filled with mysterious and towering power. This power diffused outward, forming a special Domain.
“It really is an Eternal Supreme Treasure, but it should be a very ordinary one!”
Ye Tian murmured.
He had obtained a fragment of an Eternal Supreme Treasure, and from the image in its aura, the green giant bell he had seen had far greater might than the Eternal Supreme Treasure before him.
Eternal Supreme Treasures all possess power that cannot be touched, and this gray plaque was the same. Surrounding it were endless space-time, which Ye Tian could sense but could hardly reach.
But he was very clear that there must be a way to touch an Eternal Supreme Treasure. Otherwise, how had Lord Mu Chen obtained this Eternal Supreme Treasure and refined it?
Yet after trying many times, he was still far from being able to reach this Eternal Supreme Treasure.
So, one of his clone bodies came to Lord Yuyi’s prison to seek advice from
Lord Yuyi.
Lord Yuyi did not want to die, so when Ye Tian sought her advice, she did not dare refuse to answer.
“Lord Yuyi, the Boundless Sea should have quite a few Eternal Supreme Treasures, right? If one finds an Eternal Supreme Treasure, how does one take it away? I discovered that Eternal Supreme Treasures seem to be deep within endless space-time, completely out of reach, as if they do not exist between heaven and earth, yet in fact they do exist.” Ye Tian asked.
Lord Yuyi replied: “The Boundless Sea does indeed possess Eternal Supreme Treasures, but the number is not many. Basically only Lords in the Legendary realm, or a few top-tier Lords in the Legendary realm, possess Eternal Supreme Treasures. And it is indeed not easy to take away an ownerless Eternal Supreme Treasure. Even if we Lords in the Immortal realm really encounter an Eternal Supreme Treasure, it is extremely difficult to take it away. Even some weaker Lords in the Legendary realm may not be able to take away an Eternal Supreme Treasure. But if you ask whether there is a method, there really is.”
“What method?”
Ye Tian asked hurriedly.
“In fact, any Eternal Supreme Treasure represents the ultimate existence of one or more Eternal Daos. For example, the Quiet Void you mentioned earlier—Quiet Void possesses an Eternal Supreme Treasure, the Azure Void Bell, and the Eternal Dao represented by the Azure Void Bell is the two Eternal Daos of Controlling the Void and Traversing the Void. Space, time, and the Daos of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth are actually the foundation. The true Eternal Dao is not a single foundation. Take the Traversing the Void Eternal Dao, for instance: it is mainly based on the Space Dao, while also containing many innate Eternal laws such as speed, light, and darkness. It is said that if one comprehends an Eternal Supreme Treasure and can comprehend one of its Eternal Daos to the Entry Stage, then there is hope of obtaining that Eternal Supreme Treasure. Of course, the prerequisite is that it must be an ownerless Eternal Supreme Treasure. If it already has an owner, then even if one comprehends it for endless Eternal years, there is no way to obtain that Eternal Supreme Treasure.” Lord Yuyi explained.
“So that’s how it is.”
Ye Tian nodded, then left this prison.
……
In Lord Mu Chen’s prison, this clone body of Ye Tian had never left. Now that he knew the method, he began to comprehend the Eternal Dao contained within this gray plaque.
Compared with the sword in the ruin where the True Void Legendary Tower was located, the feeling this plaque gave Ye Tian was much clearer.
At this moment, after comparing the two, Ye Tian was certain in his heart that the sword should not actually be on the True Void Legendary Tower side. It was probably still in the Boundless Sea, and the sword in that ruin was merely the space-time projection of the Eternal Supreme Treasure, while what everyone was comprehending was only that projection of the Eternal Supreme Treasure.
Comprehending a projection might have some effect, but one could never truly grasp its real Eternal Dao. At most, one could use the projection’s aura to cultivate a little, or use it to assist in comprehending and creating Eternal techniques or some secret arts.
And at this moment.
Ye Tian felt a powerful aura of Eternal Dao from this Eternal Supreme Treasure, and deeply sensed one of the Eternal Daos contained within it.
After a long while, he came to understand what this Eternal Dao represented.
Primordial Dao!
This was the Primordial Dao among the Eternal Daos, capable of blending various powers into one, forming an eternal Primordial Domain.
Compared with the others, the Primordial Dao was somewhat suited to Ye Tian, so he had a great chance of comprehending it to the Entry Stage within a certain period of time.
And the foundation of the Primordial Dao was various innate Eternal laws. Ye Tian used these innate Eternal laws to comprehend the Primordial Dao, and with the help of this Eternal Supreme Treasure, his understanding of the Primordial Dao grew deeper and deeper.
Year after year of Eternal time passed. Ye Tian’s cultivation was still at the Six-Step Immortal Lord realm, but his strength had been steadily increasing. For example, he had been continuously opening up his will world.
By now, his will world had already reached more than eighty thousand.
Moreover, he had once again entered the Void Legendary Tower, breaking through all the way to the nineteenth layer in one go, and then cultivated for a period of time in the Void Legendary Land, slightly improving the degree of fusion between his will and body. He was very close to becoming a Seven-Step Immortal Lord, but this step was not so easy to cross.
About ten thousand Eternal years passed.
On this day.
Ye Tian finally cultivated the Eternal Primordial Dao to the Entry Stage, and to his surprise, he discovered that the Eternal Primordial Dao was actually helpful in fusing will and body. However, the prerequisite was that one needed various precious resources, drawing out various powers to enhance the fusion of will and body, similar to fusion and transformation.
“Lord Mu Chen’s Eternal Primordial Dao must also have reached the Entry Stage. If he had not been captured by Mo Shan, he absolutely could have grown into a powerhouse; becoming a Lord in the Legendary realm would not have been very difficult.” Ye Tian thought to himself.
With the Entry Stage Eternal Primordial Dao, together with this Eternal Supreme Treasure, his cultivation speed in the future would be much faster. It definitely would not take long before he could advance to the Seven-Step Immortal Lord realm.
“Let’s see if I can take this Eternal Supreme Treasure away!”
Ye Tian reached out to grab this Eternal Supreme Treasure. The power of the Eternal Primordial Dao formed a weak Domain, blessing his hand as it plunged into endless space-time, passed through layer after layer of space-time, as if crossing an immeasurably distant distance, yet also as if it only crossed a few meters.
Finally, Ye Tian’s hand touched this Eternal Supreme Treasure.
Whoosh!
The Eternal Supreme Treasure was grasped in Ye Tian’s hand.
Then, Ye Tian began refining this Eternal Supreme Treasure, only then could he use it normally.
Time slowly passed. About several calamity cycles later, Ye Tian finally refined this Eternal Supreme Treasure.
At this point, he also learned the name of this Eternal Supreme Treasure—Primordial Order!
“With the Primordial Order, my strength has increased by more than tenfold!” Ye Tian said excitedly.
Moreover, it would not be long before he could advance to the Seven-Step Immortal Lord realm.
Very soon, another piece of good news arrived.
