Chapter 1435: Successful Evolution, Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body
!
The Ancestral Body is the evolved version of the Holy Body. Under normal circumstances, it is many times more difficult for an Invincible Territory Ancestor to master an Ancestral Body than to master Ancestral Techniques.
Within the Battle Sect, only the Five Thunder Territory Ancestor had mastered a half-step Ancestral Body, and he was still some distance away from a true Ancestral Body.
"If I want the Eternal Indestructible Holy Body to break through its limits, ordinary resources are useless. I can only rely on the Evolution trait!"
Ye Tian murmured.
The Evolution trait, deducing the Eternal Indestructible Holy Body—this was more reliable than any resource.
Perhaps the Five Thunder Territory Ancestor had obtained a rare supreme treasure to break the Holy Body's limit, but Ye Tian didn't have such a treasure, nor did he know where to find one.
All he had was the Evolution trait.
Of course, Ye Tian knew very well that even with the Evolution trait, breaking the Holy Body's limit would likely take a very long time and require countless body replications.
Fortunately, Ye Tian had broken through to the Invincible Territory Ancestor realm. His Holy Soul was many times stronger, enough to support the Evolution trait's deductions for a long time, with much better results than before.
"Replicate body!"
Ye Tian replicated his body once more, and the replicated body began to evolve, deducing the Eternal Indestructible Holy Body.
He deduced the Eternal Indestructible Holy Body toward the Ancestral Body realm step by step. Previously, Ye Tian had no clue, but with the help of the Evolution trait and the continuous consumption of his Holy Soul's origin, he deduced it bit by bit.
After a long time, Ye Tian's Holy Soul origin was completely exhausted, and the replicated body died instantly.
"The progress is about one-ten-thousandth!"
Ye Tian estimated.
In other words, he needed to replicate his body ten thousand times and exhaust his Holy Soul origin ten thousand times through the Evolution trait to truly deduce the Ancestral Body and break the Holy Body's limit.
What did it mean to exhaust the Holy Soul origin ten thousand times? If anyone else had obtained the Evolution trait, they only had one life; they wouldn't dare exhaust their origin. Even a slight depletion would take ages to recover.
Therefore, even if others obtained the Evolution trait, it wouldn't be of much help. Only for Ye Tian was the Evolution trait incredibly powerful.
Time passed, and in the blink of an eye... a billion years had gone by.
The human race had over a dozen Saint Talent users, and after a billion years, these dozen or so individuals had naturally risen to prominence.
Many human Territory Ancestors, after reaching the peak of the Supreme Territory Ancestor realm, could exchange for a grain of Star Soul Sand from the Saint Hall at a very low cost to give birth to Soul Holy Light and charge into the Invincible Territory Ancestor realm.
Now, after a billion years, the human race had produced hundreds of Invincible Territory Ancestors.
This was mainly because reaching the Supreme Territory Ancestor realm was no simple feat, and growing the soul to a certain level at the peak of that realm was also difficult.
Additionally, Ye Tian was strict with the exchange requirements for Star Soul Sand; it wasn't... given away freely.
Thus, only about a thousand people had obtained Star Soul Sand. Some human Supreme Territory Ancestors, even after birthing Soul Holy Light, wanted to birth a Holy Soul first and weren't in a hurry to charge into the Invincible realm; otherwise, the human race would have already produced over a thousand Invincible Territory Ancestors.
The ones who made the most progress were undoubtedly Ye Tian's family members.
With the help of Saint Blood Marrow, they all established their foundations. With the massive resources Ye Tian provided, they also gained Saint Talents and mastered Saint Techniques one by one.
Moreover, they had Star Soul Sand and Saint Gold Sand. Ye Tian was not stingy with these resources and supplied them all to his family.
Consequently, Xiao Yue, Ye Xingchen, and the others all condensed Holy Souls and underwent a second qualitative change.
Now, they had all broken through to the Invincible Territory Ancestor realm and mastered several Saint Techniques. Although their cultivation hadn't reached the peak of the Invincible realm yet, they had reached the peak of the early stage, not far from the middle stage.
Their combat power had also reached the level of a Four-Step Invincible Territory Ancestor, which wasn't considered weak even in the Dark Saint Domain.
...."I've finally understood it!"
Just as his replicated body was about to exhaust its origin, Ye Tian's eyes snapped open, filled with joy.
By now, he had completely understood the key mysteries of breaking the shackles of the Eternal Indestructible Holy Body to reach the Ancestral Body realm.
"Then, let's break through!"
Ye Tian's main body began to charge toward the Ancestral Body realm.
Boom!!!! The Holy Body cracked bit by bit. Through repeated cracking, reconstruction, and more cracking and reconstruction, the interior of his body underwent countless evolutions and reorganizations. After trillions of reorganizations, the shackles were finally broken.
This was a method Ye Tian had deduced himself; it was only applicable to him and not to others.
Because everyone's body is different, and since he evolved based on himself, it could only be used by him.
The shackles were broken; the limit of the Holy Body was shattered.
Ye Tian's physical body began evolving directly toward the Ancestral Body, but many steps remained to complete the evolution.
And now, Ye Tian had already condensed a half-step Ancestral Body! Countless amounts of energy flooded into Ye Tian's body, strengthening the half-step Ancestral Body.
Ten thousand years! A million years! Ten million years!
This transformation continued for a hundred million years before the qualitative change was finally completed.
Ye Tian's half-step Ancestral Body far surpassed that of the Five Thunder Territory Ancestor. After all, Ye Tian's Holy Body was the number one Holy Body in the Dark Saint Domain. Even as an Ancestral Body, it would naturally be the number one Ancestral Body in the Dark Saint Domain, incredibly terrifying.
"The Five Thunder Territory Ancestor condensed a half-step Ancestral Body by relying on the effects of a treasure. It would likely take him a very long time to fully condense an Ancestral Body. But I am different; I broke the limit by relying on myself and even have a complete method for condensing the Ancestral Body.
It will probably take a few hundred million more years. With more resources prepared, I can fully condense the Ancestral Body!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
If he condensed the Ancestral Body and then mastered his physical strength—not even needing to master it many times over, just several dozen times—he might actually have a chance to defeat a Supreme-level Territory Ancestor.
This was no joke, but a fact.
After all, his current strength already far exceeded a One-Step Invincible Territory Ancestor; he could even defeat the Five Thunder Territory Ancestor in a single move.
"Continue secluded cultivation and condense the Ancestral Body as soon as possible!"
Ye Tian murmured.
In this way, countless years slowly passed, and in the blink of an eye, another few hundred million years had gone by.
This day.
Ye Tian emerged from seclusion. His Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body was finally condensed. His aura was incredibly terrifying; if fully released, it would be enough to suppress and kill an ordinary Supreme Territory Ancestor.
Standing in the Profound Void Saint Realm, Ye Tian clearly felt that the power of the realm could no longer amplify him.
He felt that he could blow up the Profound Void Saint Realm with a single punch, and even shake the Dao Mountain Territory.
Of course, this was an illusion, but it was enough to prove how terrifying his strength had become.
