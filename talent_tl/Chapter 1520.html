Chapter 1519: A Great Discovery, the Appearance of an Eternal Ancestral Talent!

"Copy Talent!"
Ye Tian immediately used his Copy Talent to investigate the creature's talents.
This creature was called a Calamity Beast, and it possessed multiple Ultimate Ancestral Talents.
However, one specific talent shocked Ye Tian beyond measure, because it wasn't an Ultimate Ancestral Talent, but something called an Eternal Ancestral Talent.
This was the first time he had ever seen an Eternal Ancestral Talent.
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Calamity]
Without a second thought, Ye Tian directly copied all those Ultimate Ancestral Talents, and he copied this Eternal Ancestral Talent as well.
However, while copying this Eternal Ancestral Talent, Ye Tian clearly felt his Copy Talent struggling. It took ten seconds to successfully copy this Eternal Ancestral Talent.
This would have been impossible in the past, as he usually copied any talent in an instant, yet copying the Eternal Ancestral Talent took ten seconds.
Clearly, the Copy Talent had its limits, and copying an Eternal Ancestral Talent was currently close to that limit.

Unless the Copy Talent could be upgraded again, there was no hope of breaking this limit.
Due to time constraints, he couldn't fuse the Eternal Ancestral Talent: Calamity right now, so he didn't yet know its use. He could only wait until he returned to slowly learn more about it.
Ye Tian's arrival naturally couldn't be hidden from the Supreme-level experts of the six super-renowned powers.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, how about helping us hold back this Calamity Beast?"
One Supreme-level expert said.
Although only one expert at the third tier of the Supreme Ancestor realm had arrived, and his help wouldn't be massive, having another Supreme Ancestor to stall for time and share the burden was still good.
As for whether Ye Tian would perish, that was none of their concern.
However, Ye Tian did not act immediately.
"Everyone, tell me what a Calamity Beast is and what its uses are. I know you came specifically for it, so it must be of great value. If you provide me with information, I will help you," Ye Tian said.
It was just a bit of information, so the six super-renowned powers didn't hide it and told Ye Tian directly.
Immediately, a stream of information entered Ye Tian's mind.
There wasn't much content; after all, these six powers wouldn't reveal too much to him, only giving him some basic information.
For example, the origin of the Calamity Beast.
A Calamity Beast is a creature born at the moment an exotic world is completely destroyed. An exotic world does not exist forever; after ten thousand great tribulations, it will completely collapse and be destroyed, and that is when a Calamity Beast is born.
After absorbing the energy of an exotic world's destruction, the Calamity Beast falls into a deep sleep. After long ages, a new exotic world will be born in this region, starting the cycle anew.
A Calamity Beast that has experienced one world destruction is a One-Calamity Beast; if it has experienced two, it is a Two-Calamity Beast; and three makes it a Three-Calamity Beast.
The Dark Forbidden Zone had been destroyed once before, thus giving birth to a Calamity Beast. This beast had been sleeping and was very difficult to find.
If left undisturbed, it would sleep until the day the Dark Forbidden Zone was destroyed again.
But now, it had been disturbed.
Calamity Beasts were of great value because they contained Origin Rule Calamity Crystals. Killing one would yield many of these crystals.
As for exactly how many Origin Rule Calamity Crystals could be obtained, the six powers didn't say.
But Ye Tian guessed the amount must be significant, otherwise, it wouldn't have attracted these six super-renowned powers.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, you've received the information. Hurry and help us stop it!"
The exotic experts shouted one after another.
"Alright!"
Ye Tian replied.
Immediately, he attacked the Calamity Beast.
Of course, Ye Tian wouldn't show his full strength, only displaying power slightly greater than an average third-tier Supreme Ancestor.
Even so, it surprised the six super-renowned powers.
Because Ye Tian had been cultivating for such a short time, yet possessed such strength, if he had enough opportunities, there might be a sliver of hope for him to promote to an Invincible Ancestor.
As Ye Tian attacked the Calamity Beast, it noticed him and slammed its tail toward him.
The power and speed of this tail strike were terrifying. Furthermore, the Calamity Beast was a creature that ignored time and space; even though Ye Tian was proficient in the Time-Space Ancestral Path and Time-Space type Supreme Ancestral Skills, it was difficult to dodge this blow.
Boom!!!!
The Calamity Beast's tail strike slammed into Ye Tian.
Just as the Supreme-level experts of the six powers thought Ye Tian would be killed in one hit, several defensive Supreme Ancestral Skills appeared on his body.
Golden Body Supreme Ancestral Skill!
Holy Armor Supreme Ancestral Skill!
Primordial Chaos Astral Shield Supreme Ancestral Skill!
One-Element Shield Supreme Ancestral Skill!
……
Six defensive Supreme Ancestral Skills were activated simultaneously. Combined with his own physical defense and a defensive Supreme Ancestral Object, the stacked defenses finally withstood the blow...
Of course, all six layers of defense shattered, the defensive Supreme Ancestral Object was damaged, and Ye Tian's body sustained minor injuries.
But at the very least, he blocked it, merely being sent flying.
"What!!!"
"What!!!"
The Supreme-level exotic experts were dumbfounded, unable to believe that Ye Tian had actually blocked a strike from the Calamity Beast and only suffered minor injuries.
How was this possible?
The Calamity Beast was incredibly powerful. If they hadn't formed a battle array to attack collectively and share the damage, they would have been heavily injured.
Yet Ye Tian was just one person; how could he withstand a strike from the Calamity Beast?
"This human Supreme Ancestor has cultivated six defensive Supreme Ancestral Skills. How afraid of death is he?"
One Supreme-level exotic expert remarked.
Others might not have noticed in that split second, but he did; those six defensive Supreme Ancestral Skills were the reason Ye Tian survived.
"What, six defensive Supreme Ancestral Skills? How terrifying. So that means he sacrificed speed, soul defense, and other Supreme Ancestral Skills to specialize in defense and a single offensive skill. I see!"
The other Supreme-level exotic experts suddenly understood and no longer doubted.
A Supreme Ancestor with such amazing defensive power, though reaching the third tier, had major flaws and could easily be targeted. It was only because the Calamity Beast wasn't good at soul attacks; otherwise, an equivalent Invincible Ancestor could have killed Ye Tian with a single soul attack.
Unfortunately, they would never know how many Supreme Ancestral Skills Ye Tian knew. In fact, he didn't just have six defensive skills, but ten.
If he hadn't wanted to hide his strength, the Calamity Beast wouldn't have even been able to break through all his defenses, let alone injure him.
However, Ye Tian alone couldn't kill the Calamity Beast because its defense was truly terrifying.
As time passed, Ye Tian and the experts of the six powers finally received reinforcements. By this time, the Supreme-level experts were heavily injured, and Ye Tian also pretended to be badly hurt.
Boom!!!!
One after another, third-tier Supreme-level experts arrived, all belonging to the six super-renowned powers. These reinforcements were all at the third tier, numbering twenty-five in total.
With twenty-five more third-tier Supreme-level experts, the group was filled with confidence in surrounding and killing the Calamity Beast.
