Chapter 1581: Yin-Yang Qishan, A Very Special Treasure Land!

"Yin-Yang Qishan?"
Ye Tian had never heard of this name, so he asked the spirit tool for information regarding Yin-Yang Qishan.
The spirit tool introduced: "Yin-Yang Qishan is a very special treasure land that only allows Invincibles to enter; Supreme Beings cannot enter Yin-Yang Qishan. Furthermore, to enter Yin-Yang Qishan, one must pass through one of its several entrances; there is no other way. The entrances to Yin-Yang Qishan are not fixed and only exist for a very short time before disappearing, only to reappear in another location later. Because of this, many Invincibles cannot enter Yin-Yang Qishan even if they want to, which has led to Yin-Yang Qishan being rarely known."
"Spirit tool, then how do I enter Yin-Yang Qishan?"
Ye Tian asked.
The spirit tool said: "A strange tree grows within Yin-Yang Qishan. This strange tree has many green leaves, and over long periods of time, a type of golden leaf is born. This golden leaf contains a trace of Yin-Yang Qishan's power. By holding the golden leaf, one can sense the location of the nearest entrance. The Void Source Hall happens to possess a golden leaf. When the time comes, Master can hold the leaf to sense the nearest entrance and have the Void Source Hall teleport Master to the entrance's location to enter Yin-Yang Qishan."
"Give the golden leaf to me!"
Ye Tian commanded.
"Yes, Master!"
The spirit tool took out a golden leaf and handed it to Ye Tian.
Ye Tian held the golden leaf, wanting to study it, but he could not see through it. He only felt a faint, ethereal special aura emanating from it.
"There are too many wondrous things in the Source Sea!"
Ye Tian sighed, but he did not study the golden leaf further.
Afterward, Ye Tian's duplicated body took the golden leaf and left the Void Source Hall, using the golden leaf to sense the location of the Yin-Yang Qishan entrance.
"I sensed it!"
Ye Tian looked into the distance. One of the entrances was right there. Although it was the nearest entrance, it was not close to him.
If he flew there at his own speed, the entrance might disappear by the time he arrived. Thus, Ye Tian had the Void Source Hall teleport him, instantly crossing an endlessly vast region of the Source Sea to approach the entrance.
"I've arrived!"
Ye Tian saw a black vortex; this black vortex was the entrance to Yin-Yang Qishan.
It existed right there, yet there wasn't a single living being around. Perhaps no Invincible had ever discovered this entrance. After all, the Source Sea was too large, and the sensing range of an Invincible within the Source Sea was too small. If they were even slightly further away, they wouldn't be able to sense the black vortex entrance here.
Ye Tian put away the golden leaf and flew into the black vortex.
……
Yin-Yang Qishan.
This was an incomparably massive mountain range. In the void above, there were two suns: one golden sun and one black sun.
Even the earth was half light and half dark.
Here, only two opposing rules existed.
The Rule of Yin and the Rule of Yang, the Rule of Light and the Rule of Darkness.
Because no other rules existed, this was strictly speaking an incomplete world. Once an Invincible entered, they would be out of place; their own rules would conflict with the rules here. Therefore, an Invincible could not stay here for long, otherwise, their physical body and soul would collapse.
However, the Rule of Yin and the Rule of Yang here were fused together, the Rule of Light and the Rule of Darkness were also fused together, and even the void and the earth were faintly fused together, making this place incredibly eerie and special.
By contemplating the mysteries here and comprehending the profundity of fusion, Invincibles could even receive a certain amount of help in merging their soul and physical body.
Consequently, the Invincibles who came here to contemplate were basically all second-class Invincibles; it was of little help to other Invincibles.
At this moment, second-class Invincibles were cultivating on various peaks of Yin-Yang Qishan. However, some second-class Invincibles were trembling all over as if enduring immense pain. Clearly, they were about to reach their limit; this was the backlash and conflict of the rules.
Obviously, they had been contemplating here for a long time but were still unwilling to give up, as they might not have another chance to enter Yin-Yang Qishan next time.
Ye Tian randomly chose a small peak and observed the changes in the rules here.
"To think such a place exists!"
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised.
After arriving here, he realized the value and use of this place. For a second-class Invincible, it was truly an incomparably rare treasure land, providing immense help in merging the physical body and soul.
Ye Tian also felt the conflict between his own rules and Yin-Yang Qishan, but his physical body was very powerful, so he could endure for a long time.
Moreover, he could enter Yin-Yang Qishan time and time again, unlike others who had to rely on luck to enter even once.
Therefore, he wasn't worried at all.
At this time, Ye Tian contemplated the profundity of the world's fusion and repeatedly attempted to merge his physical body and soul, combining them with the mysteries of this world to find a method and path for fusion, so as not to be completely lost.
As time passed, one second-class Invincible after another was forced to leave. Occasionally, second-class Invincibles would enter, but it would take a very long time for new ones to arrive. Usually, many would enter at once.
This situation was clearly because an entrance had been discovered and other second-class Invincibles were notified, leading them to enter together in a short period.
In the blink of an eye, three Dark Eras of time passed.
"I've almost reached the limit!"
Ye Tian muttered.
The time others could endure was nowhere near as long as his. Being able to persist for three Dark Eras was already a very long time.
Through these three Dark Eras of contemplation, Ye Tian had already figured out how to complete the fusion of the Physical Tribulation Realm and the Soul Tribulation Realm. However, this contemplation would at most reduce the time required by two-thirds; he still needed to continue his contemplation.
And so, Ye Tian left Yin-Yang Qishan. After returning to the Void Source Hall, he dissolved this duplicated body and condensed another one, letting this new duplicated body enter Yin-Yang Qishan again.
For this entry, Ye Tian changed his aura and appearance, and added a mask to hide his identity so that he wouldn't be easily recognized by others. Otherwise, if others saw him entering Yin-Yang Qishan twice, they would definitely find it strange.
He didn't want to cause any trouble, at least not for now.
In the following years, Ye Tian entered Yin-Yang Qishan time and time again, comprehending the mysteries of fusion here.
Gradually, Ye Tian's comprehension grew deeper. He now had full confidence that he could merge the Physical Tribulation Realm and the Soul Tribulation Realm, and it wouldn't take too long.
On this day.
After Ye Tian emerged from Yin-Yang Qishan, he did not plan to enter again; it was no longer of much help to him.
He prepared to enter seclusion to charge toward the first-class Invincible realm.
