Chapter 1452: Nirvana Crystals, A Great Harvest
!
Shortly after, the War Ancestor spoke with the other Ancestors. Many Ancestors agreed to let Ye Tian become one of the patrolmen, but many others disagreed.
Almost all the Ancestors who disagreed were from the Dark Destruction Domain. They already had conflicts with the Dark Holy Domain and were only reluctantly cooperating here due to the threat of the Nirvana Clan.
If the threat of the Nirvana Clan were removed in the future, the two sides would likely be at each other's throats again.
Therefore, it was normal for them to disagree with Ye Tian becoming a patrolman.
However, it was enough that some Ancestors agreed; it didn't matter if the others didn't.
Regardless... the War Ancestor allowed Ye Tian to become a patrolman, and there was nothing they could do about it.... "Myriad Law War Ancestor, you can now become a patrolman. This is a communication treasure specifically for patrols; it can be used here!"
The War Ancestor gave Ye Tian a black bracelet and said.
"Understood!"
Ye Tian nodded and accepted the bracelet.
From then on, he was one of the patrolmen of this region of space-time, patrolling various defense lines. Once the Nirvana Clan appeared anywhere, he had to go and provide support.
After becoming a patrolman, Ye Tian patrolled the defense lines one by one, which was quite easy.
In the blink of an eye, hundreds of thousands of years passed.
During this period, Ye Tian rescued various defense lines multiple times. He even fell three times—of course, this was because he attacked the Nirvana Clan frantically, even sacrificing his duplicated body to kill a one-mark Nirvana Clan member.
Although he fell three times, he killed a total of five one-mark Nirvana Clan members, earning a significant amount of contribution points. He exchanged these for two more stalks of Nirvana Grass. Additionally, during his patrols, he found two Nirvana treasures. Although they were very low-level treasures, they were still good gains.
He sent these Nirvana treasures back to the Profound Void Holy Realm for his main body to refine. His Ancestral Body and Supreme Territory Body made considerable progress, though they were still far from advancing.
One day.
While Ye Tian was on patrol, he sensed fluctuations of Nirvana energy.
Other Supreme-level Territory Ancestors, or even Ancestors, were likely inferior to him in sensing Nirvana energy fluctuations.
This was because he possessed the Nirvana Ancestral Talent, while other Supreme-level Territory Ancestors and Ancestors did not.
Swish! Ye Tian flew over. He felt that it was definitely a sign of a Nirvana treasure being born, and it wasn't very far from his current position, just slightly close to the Space-Time Corridor.
Soon, Ye Tian arrived at the place where the Nirvana energy fluctuations were abnormal.
In this area, Nirvana energy surged, and gray crystals were slowly condensing.
"This is..."
Ye Tian recognized these gray crystals.
These gray crystals were on the exchange list; they were called Nirvana Crystals and were a very common type of Nirvana treasure in this space-time.
Although common, they were very precious—far more precious than Nirvana Grass....
If Nirvana treasures were categorized, Nirvana Grass would at most be a low-level Nirvana treasure, while Nirvana Crystals were mid-level Nirvana treasures. One Nirvana Crystal was worth at least dozens of stalks of Nirvana Grass.
And there wasn't just one or two Nirvana Crystals about to be born here; there were at least a hundred.
"The Nirvana energy fluctuations are too large and are still expanding. Once the Nirvana Clan approaches this place, with their Nirvana Clan talents, they will definitely be able to sense it easily!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Immediately, he activated his Nirvana Ancestral Talent and began to absorb the Nirvana energy here, attempting to keep the fluctuations under control.
Soon, the Nirvana energy fluctuations were controlled by Ye Tian at a very stable level, so the Nirvana Clan could no longer sense them.
Of course, he had to maintain control at all times. Once he lost control, the Nirvana energy fluctuations would spread out.
As time passed, the Nirvana Crystals gradually began to condense.
In the blink of an eye, a thousand years passed.
Fortunately, there were no Nirvana Clan invasions during this time; otherwise, if he couldn't leave, the Ancestors would definitely blame him.
Rumble!!!! The Nirvana Crystals were completely condensed, turning into brilliant gray crystals. The fluctuations instantly affected a vast area, and even Ye Tian could no longer suppress them.
"Collect!"
Ye Tian quickly collected these Nirvana Crystals and then used the Void Evasion Ancestral Technique to return toward the Holy Domain continent at maximum speed.
Just a few seconds after Ye Tian left, a terrifying figure appeared there.
It was an eight-mark Nirvana Clan member! He looked around but found no Nirvana treasures. He snorted coldly, "I'm too late. I don't know who took them!"
The Ancestors were even slower to find out; by the time they arrived, there was nothing left to see.
These Ancestors thought the Nirvana treasures had been taken by the Nirvana Clan, unaware that Ye Tian had taken them.
After obtaining these Nirvana Crystals, Ye Tian immediately applied to return to the Dark Holy Domain.
Soon, he was sent back by the War Ancestor.
Afterward, he delivered the Nirvana Crystals to his main body and stayed for a few days before returning to the Holy Domain continent to resume his patrols.
Profound Void Holy Realm.
Ye Tian held a Nirvana Crystal in his hand and began to absorb its energy.
Boom!!!! Incredibly pure Nirvana energy flooded into Ye Tian's body, tempering his Supreme Territory Body and Ancestral Body.
"Such a powerful effect!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
With so many Nirvana Crystals, he could likely temper his Supreme Territory Body to a level close to the middle stage, and even his Ancestral Body would be tempered significantly.
In the blink of an eye, ten million years passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had already refined these Nirvana Crystals, and his Supreme Territory Body was, as he had guessed, very close to the middle stage.
His strength also surged significantly, bringing him closer to having half-step Ancestor combat power.
....Unknowingly, Ye Tian's duplicated body had been guarding the border of the Dark Forbidden Zone for a full billion years. During this time, he had died countless times, but he had also harvested many Nirvana treasures and exchanged for some as well.
Unfortunately, he didn't have the same luck as he did with the Nirvana Crystals, so the Nirvana treasures he obtained were quite ordinary.
But even so, these Nirvana treasures allowed his strength to soar.
Now, he was using these Nirvana treasures to charge toward the middle stage of the Supreme Territory Body realm.
Once he succeeded, he would possess half-step Ancestor combat power.
