Chapter 1428: Detonating the World Source Core, Mass Death of Supreme Domain Ancestors
!
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, this is a World Source Core. It is a special treasure refined from an entire domain by a Half-step Primordial Ancestor. Once detonated, it is equivalent to the explosion of an entire domain. Even you would likely perish. If you continue to hunt me, we will all die together!"
Dark Red Ancestor had gone mad.
A treasure like the World Source Core was something even ordinary Half-step Primordial Ancestors wouldn't bother to refine. She had obtained it from the ruins of a Half-step Primordial Ancestor she once entered; it was her most important trump card.
However, this thing could not be controlled. Once detonated, she herself would be caught in the blast.
"Then detonate it!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
He was well aware of what a World Source Core was. Once detonated, it was powerful enough to kill an ordinary Five-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. However, it was still far from enough to kill him.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian continued his attack. A golden light pierced through, instantly impaling Dark Red Ancestor.
On the brink of death, Dark Red Ancestor detonated the World Source Core.
In the distance.
One by one, the Supreme Domain Ancestors fled frantically, escaping toward the distance at their maximum speed.
"Run, quickly! Dark Red Ancestor has gone mad, she’s actually going to trigger a World Source Core!"
"The range of a World Source Core is too vast! If we can't escape, we're dead!"
The Supreme Domain Ancestors who were relatively far from Ye Tian and Dark Red Ancestor were still able to flee, but those nearby were stunned.
The detonation took almost no time at all; it exploded instantly.
Buzz!!!!! More than half of the treasure land was covered by the power of the World Source Core, and it happened almost in a flash.
Immediately, countless spaces were annihilated. Supreme Domain Ancestors who didn't have time to dodge had their Supreme Domain Bodies obliterated. Even their souls, holy objects, and holy weapons could not be preserved, as if they were instantly erased from this world.
Naturally, Ye Tian wasn't foolish enough to block it head-on. He performed the Void Escape Saint Technique to dive into the deep layers of the void.
Of course, even after diving into the deep void, the power of the World Source Core's explosion would still penetrate through the layers of space to strike his physical body.
But Ye Tian possessed the Ancestral Armor Ancestral Technique, and his defensive power was terrifying, so he would be fine.
The explosion of the World Source Core continued to annihilate this area for several hours before it gradually dissipated.
Whoosh! A figure walked out from the depths of the void; it was Ye Tian.
At this moment, he looked a bit disheveled and was injured. After all, this was a lethal weapon capable of killing those with the combat power of an ordinary Five-step Invincible Domain Ancestor. It naturally posed a threat to him. If not for the help of the Void Escape Saint Technique and the Ancestral Armor, he likely would have been heavily wounded.
Now, he was merely injured.
"Undying Saint Technique!"
Under the recovery of the Undying Saint Technique, Ye Tian healed at an extremely fast speed.
He looked around. Not a single Supreme Domain Ancestor remained in this area, but the void of this treasure land was rapidly repairing itself. After all, even he couldn't destroy the source of this void; a mere World Source Core was naturally incapable of destroying this treasure land.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!! One by one, Supreme Domain Ancestors re-entered the treasure land, but their numbers were significantly reduced. It was clear how many... Supreme Domain Ancestors had died.
Shortly after, several powerful auras descended upon the treasure land.
"Where are the Supreme Domain Ancestors of our Immortal Holy Sect?"
"Where are the Supreme Domain Ancestors of our Dark Holy Sect?"
"Damn it, why can't we contact many of the Supreme Domain Ancestors of our Firmament Holy Sect? Who killed them?"
These Supreme Domain Ancestors were powerful existences ranked in the top five of their respective Holy Sects, and some were even the number one or two Supreme Domain Ancestors.
At this time, the remaining Supreme Domain Ancestors from the various Holy Sects hurriedly reported to these top-five powerhouses.
"It was the Ancestor of Myriad Laws who killed Ancestor Wu Xiu!"
A Supreme Domain Ancestor from the Forbidden Holy Sect sent a message.
When these top-five Supreme Domain Ancestors learned of the situation, they all turned to look at Ye Tian in anger.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, aren't you being a bit too excessive? You've slaughtered so many Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Holy Sects!"
Yuanlong Ancestor, the second-ranked Supreme Domain Ancestor of the Forbidden Holy Sect, spoke coldly.
Ye Tian smiled and said, "I only killed a few Supreme Domain Ancestors. The others all died under the World Source Core of the Immortal Holy Sect's Dark Red Ancestor! It was she who sacrificed the World Source Core, killing a massive number of Supreme Domain Ancestors and herself in the process!"
Shengyun Ancestor, the top-ranked Supreme Domain Ancestor of the Immortal Holy Sect, said angrily, "Ancestor of Myriad Laws, if you hadn't been hunting Dark Red Ancestor, would she have sacrificed the World Source Core?"
"Regardless... she is the one who killed those Supreme Domain Ancestors. That is a fact! According to your logic, if two people are fighting and one can't win and knows they are doomed, they can just go on a mass slaughter? Then, if an Invincible Domain Ancestor of our War Gate offends a Primordial Ancestor of your Immortal Holy Sect and knows he's going to die, can he slaughter countless living beings in the Dark Holy Domain, or even slaughter Supreme Domain Ancestors, Saint-Talented ones, and descendants of Primordial Ancestors from other Holy Sects, and then not be held responsible?"
Ye Tian used an analogy.
"That is a fallacy!"
Shengyun Ancestor quickly replied.
"The logic is... exactly that. I am not wrong. Dark Red Ancestor wanted to kill me, and I fought back; there was nothing wrong with that to begin with.
She was no match for me, so she used the World Source Core to perish together with me, wiping out a large number of Supreme Domain Ancestors in the process. In the end, instead of blaming Dark Red Ancestor, you blame me. How ridiculous!"
Ye Tian continued.
At this point, many Supreme Domain Ancestors from other Holy Sects, swayed by Ye Tian's words, began to look toward the Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Immortal Holy Sect.
Of course, they didn't have a good attitude toward Ye Tian either and were very angry with him, as some Supreme Domain Ancestors from other Holy Sects had indeed fallen by Ye Tian's hand.
However, the real reason these Supreme Domain Ancestors didn't dare to attack Ye Tian was undoubtedly his strength.
