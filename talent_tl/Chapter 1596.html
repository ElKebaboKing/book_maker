Chapter 1595: Bringing Relatives into the Void Source Hall!

Ye Tian had previously broken through to the Triple Supreme Being realm as a duplicate body. Although he had forced the breakthrough regardless of his foundation, he at least gained experience.
Therefore, if Ye Tian wanted to break through quickly, he could naturally reach the Triple Supreme Being realm rapidly.
However, he could not break through like that. He had to break through with a perfect foundation and would not use treasures like the Original Source Liquid; otherwise, it would be difficult to break the boundary of a Nine-fold Supreme Being in the future.
In fact, if the Original Source Liquid was refined too many times, it might even be difficult to become a Nine-fold Supreme Being.
Therefore, Ye Tian would rather have his cultivation strength increase a bit slower and was in no hurry to charge toward a higher realm.
After the Eternal Body fused, Ye Tian duplicated another Xu Chen at the First-class Invincible realm. He then had Xu Chen take the Ancient Star Stone into the Star Ancient Domain to begin secluded cultivation in a remote place, soon charging into the Supreme Being realm.
Then, using some resources, he prepared to charge into the Double Supreme Being realm, or even the Triple Supreme Being realm, at the fastest speed.
This way, he could continue to govern the Star Ancient Domain.
After all, he did not want to give up the Star Ancient Domain yet. It could still provide him with a large amount of resources. It would be a pity to give it up just like that.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian called Ye Jinpeng over.
"Ancestor!"
Ye Jinpeng said respectfully.
At this time, Ye Tian gave some Son of the Void tokens to Ye Jinpeng and instructed: "My true body cannot enter the Dark Forbidden Domain now. You are still at the Third-class Invincible realm and can still enter the Dark Forbidden Domain. Give these Son of the Void tokens to your Star Ancestor and the others. Let them refine the tokens, and they can enter the Void Source Hall as Sons of the Void to contemplate the Void Source Monument for cultivation. As for the remaining Son of the Void tokens, bestow them upon the monstrous geniuses of the human race in the future."
"Yes!"
Ye Jinpeng replied.
Subsequently, Ye Jinpeng returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain and gave these tokens to Xiao Yue, Ye Xingchen, Ye Yu, and the other ancestors.
Soon, Xiao Yue and the others also came to the Void Source Hall. Since other Sons of the Void were not allowed to enter here in the future, it was fine to treat the Void Source Hall as a precious land for the human race.
"Husband!"
Xiao Yue was quite excited to see Ye Tian.
Ye Tian's children were also very happy to see their father. They quickly asked about Ye Tian's recent situation and were naturally overjoyed to learn that Ye Tian had already broken through to the Supreme Being realm.
In the following days, Ye Tian's family members cultivated here. Additionally, Ye Jinpeng selected several monstrous geniuses from the human race to enter the Void Source Hall. With the combat training of the Void Tower and the contemplation of the Void Source Monument, their progress would be very fast.
Of course, it was unclear whether they could break through to become Second-class or higher Invincibles, let alone break through to the Supreme Being realm.
But regardless, as generations of human geniuses entered the Void Source Hall, there would eventually be one who could become a Supreme Being. After all, the human race's bloodline foundation was becoming stronger and stronger, and they were no longer the ordinary race they once were.
Rather than counting on the human race to give birth to a third Supreme Being, Ye Tian felt it was more reliable to help his own relatives break through to the Supreme Being realm at all costs.
Of course.
To help his relatives break through to the Supreme Being realm, it would likely take a very long time—not measured in Great Tribulation Eras, but in Tribulation Units.
To give his relatives a greater hope of promoting to Supreme Beings, Ye Tian also specifically spent effort to find Eternal Ancestral Talents that suited their cultivation paths and then bestowed the Eternal Ancestral Talents upon them. However, this would be difficult to explain.
Nevertheless, he believed his relatives would not ask too much.
Thus, he spent some years fusing several Eternal Ancestral Talents and bestowed them upon Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, Ye Yu, and Xiao Yue. As for others, they did not receive such treatment; even Ye Jinpeng did not receive such treatment.
Ye Tian also exhorted his wife and children not to leak this secret, and he did not say too much.
It had to be said that after obtaining the Eternal Ancestral Talents, their progress was very fast. With the assistance of the Void Source Monument, the progress of their Eternal Rules was extremely rapid. At this rate, becoming a Third-class Invincible would be very easy.
However, promoting to a Second-class Invincible was still somewhat difficult and required sufficient resources, potential, time, and so on.
Unknowingly, over a thousand Great Tribulation Eras passed.
By this time, the human race had produced many Invincibles, and as for Progenitors, they were countless.
The human race's territory spanned many foreign worlds, making them a top-tier power among the foreign worlds.
And Ye Jinpeng did not disappoint Ye Tian. He finally became a true Son of the Void, passing the first floor of the Void Tower with his own ability and creating a Void Source Body. Although it was a relatively low-quality Void Source Body with significant flaws, it was enough to be ranked as a God Source Body.
Ye Jinpeng passed the first floor of the Void Tower and condensed a very ordinary Void Source Body. He did not continue to accumulate foundation; he was already very satisfied. In terms of potential, he was already stronger than the Sky Wind Source God and second only to the Autumn Leaf Source Ancestor.
Therefore, he did not stay in the Third-class Invincible realm any longer and began to charge toward Second-class Invincible.
Not long after, he successfully stepped into the Second-class Invincible realm.
Having broken through to the Second-class Invincible realm, Ye Jinpeng could no longer return to the Dark Forbidden Domain. Thus, he entered the Tianlin Realm and would spend most of his time in the Sky Wind Sea in the future.
Ye Tian's family members had also stepped into the Third-class Invincible realm long ago. If they broke through to the Second-class Invincible realm in the future, before the Dark Forbidden Domain promoted to an Eternal World, they would likely only be able to stay in the Tianlin Realm.
One day.
Ye Tian's cultivation finally reached the Limit of a First-fold Supreme Being, only one step away from the Second-fold Supreme Being. However, if he did not refine the Original Source Liquid, breaking through to the Second-fold Supreme Being realm would not be that easy.
Ye Tian did not want to continue secluded cultivation. Coincidentally, not long after, the Sky Wind Source God came to find him.
The Sky Wind Source God knew that Ye Tian had broken through to the Supreme Being realm. After all, it was impossible for Ye Tian to never return to the Sky Wind Sea, and a breakthrough in cultivation was a normal occurrence.
But in the entire Sky Wind Sea, except for the Sky Wind Source God, no one else knew that Ye Tian had already broken through to the Supreme Being realm.
This was a secret!
"Sky Wind, why have you come to find me?"
Ye Tian asked.
The Sky Wind Source God said solemnly: "The Nether Hall Master has hope of breaking through to the Second-fold Supreme Being realm. Once he breaks through, his strength will far exceed mine. At that time, the Sky Wind Sea will likely be unable to contend with the Nether Hall. Therefore, I want to stop him and need your help."
"How?"
Ye Tian said.
The Sky Wind Source God explained the plan.
His plan was simple because the Nether Hall needed an opportunity in a precious land to charge toward the Second-fold Supreme Being realm.
Therefore, they would besiege the Nether Hall Master in that precious land, disrupt his promotion, and ideally kill him.
"Alright, I understand!"
Ye Tian agreed. After all, when he was still weak, the Sky Wind Sea had protected him; otherwise, he might not have been able to grow to this stage. Therefore, helping the Sky Wind Source God was a matter of course.
