Chapter 1547: The Secretive Yuhua World!

In Ancestor Mingli’s view, the fall of Ancestor Blood Shadow and the others was definitely related to Ye Tian, the Ancestor of Myriad Laws. Perhaps Ye Tian hadn't acted personally, but another Invincible had.

Regardless of the circumstances, the Blood Mist Alliance had offended the Ancestor of Myriad Laws.

If the Ancestor of Myriad Laws brought fifth-grade Invincibles to invade the Blood Mist Alliance, they might discover this place.

Essentially, without a thorough investigation of this foreign world, its secrets would remain hidden. But once an invasion occurred, those secrets would surely be uncovered.

When that time came, he wouldn't be able to protect this place at all.

“I shouldn't have provoked the Ancestor of Myriad Laws!”

Ancestor Mingli said regretfully.

He had thought it would be a very simple matter—three Invincibles could easily kill an ordinary sixth-grade Invincible—but he hadn't expected it to fail.

After all, he had originally thought that if they didn't act, it would arouse suspicion from the outside world, and someone might come to investigate. It seemed better to strike first.

But now, the situation was a catastrophic mistake.

“I hope the fall of those three Invincibles from the Blood Mist Alliance was just an accident, and that the Ancestor of Myriad Laws or other Invincibles don't plan to invade the Blood Mist Alliance!” Ancestor Mingli thought to himself.

……

Ye Tian did not immediately move against the Blood Mist Alliance because the transformation of the Sacred Domain World was not yet complete; he couldn't leave at this time.

He would deal with the Blood Mist Alliance once he had handled the affairs of the Sacred Domain World.

And so, time slowly passed.

Finally, on this day, the Sacred Domain World was completely transformed. The rules here were now consistent with the rules of the Dark Forbidden Zone. Creatures from the Dark Forbidden Zone could enter without being affected in the slightest, and there were no obstacles to their cultivation.

Subsequently.

Ye Tian began a great migration of the creatures from the Dark Forbidden Zone. He even personally moved countless members of the human race to the Sacred Domain World.

The migration of the human race only truly concluded once the great tribulation of the Sacred Domain World ended.

By this time, human figures had appeared in many parts of the Sacred Domain World. It could be said that the Sacred Domain World had become the second-largest foreign world for the human race.

With this, the human race had become a super-tier power possessing two foreign worlds.

“It’s time to deal with the Blood Mist Alliance!”

Ye Tian said coldly.

The Blood Mist Alliance had dispatched three Invincibles to attack him, but he had killed them.

He wasn't sure if the Blood Mist Alliance had any more Invincibles left, but if left unchecked, they might cause trouble for the human race in the future, which was not something he wanted to see.

Therefore, he prepared to directly wipe out the Blood Mist Alliance. With his strength, he could certainly achieve this.

Soon.

Ye Tian descended upon the headquarters of the Blood Mist Alliance—the Blood Mist World.

The Blood Mist Alliance was powerful, with the number of Paragons reaching over a thousand. Among them were over a dozen Paragons in the Physical Tribulation Realm. However, these Physical Tribulation Realm Paragons almost never concerned themselves with trivial matters, focusing all their efforts on breaking through to become Invincibles.

After all, once these Physical Tribulation Realm experts reached the Invincible stage, the strength of the Blood Mist Alliance would skyrocket. Thus, becoming an Invincible was their greatest priority; everything else was minor.

However, when Ye Tian descended upon the Blood Mist Alliance, these Physical Tribulation Realm Paragons were startled.

Whoosh!

A Physical Tribulation Realm Paragon was seized by Ye Tian with one hand and interrogated directly: “How many Invincibles does your Blood Mist Alliance have?”

The Physical Tribulation Realm Paragon did not dare to hide anything and revealed: “My Lord, our Blood Mist Alliance had four Invincibles in total. Three fell not long ago, leaving only one Supreme Elder at the Invincible level. His name is Ancestor Mingli.”

“Ancestor Mingli? What is his strength?” Ye Tian asked again.

“Ancestor Mingli seems to be a fifth-grade Invincible!” the Physical Tribulation Realm Paragon replied.

“I see!”

With a surge of power, Ye Tian directly blasted the Physical Tribulation Realm expert to death.

Following that, Ye Tian began a slaughter. Countless experts comparable to Ancestors were annihilated one after another.

They wanted to flee, but no matter how they traversed time and space, they couldn't escape the Blood Mist World. Some were clever and tried to escape through the various Gates, but Ye Tian destroyed those Gates immediately, causing their plans to fail.

The Blood Mist Alliance had a total of six foreign worlds, and the one where Ancestor Mingli resided was the Yuhua World.

In the Yuhua World.

One Paragon after another arrived before Ancestor Mingli, pleading: “Ancestor Mingli, please save the headquarters! An Invincible has descended upon the Blood Mist World and quickly destroyed the Gates. We were only able to escape because we were guarding the Gates. We beg Ancestor Mingli to reinforce the headquarters and slay the enemy!”

Upon hearing this, Ancestor Mingli trembled all over.

He knew that the revenge from the Ancestor of Myriad Laws had arrived. He didn't dare to gamble; if he lost, everything would be gone.

Consequently.

Ancestor Mingli immediately destroyed the Gates connecting the Yuhua World to the other worlds, including all the second-tier Gates. In this way, the Yuhua World was no longer connected to any other foreign worlds.

Even if Ye Tian, the Ancestor of Myriad Laws, destroyed the headquarters of the Blood Mist Alliance, he wouldn't necessarily go out of his way to search for the Yuhua World. He might even assume Mingli had fled and leave it at that.

He had no other choice; he could only hope for this outcome.

In fact, he could have chosen to flee, and Ye Tian truly wouldn't have been able to find him.

However, he was reluctant to leave this place and unwilling to give up this opportunity.

After all, fifth-grade Invincible was his limit, but if he didn't abandon this fortuitous chance, he might even reach the fourth, third, or even second or first-grade Invincible realms in the future.

Therefore, he would not leave.

As time passed, the Paragons and other Fog Ancestors in the Blood Mist World were all killed by Ye Tian.

As for the endless living beings in the Blood Mist World, he didn't have the time to kill them; that would take far too long.

For Ye Tian, killing that Ancestor Mingli was the most important task.

“This Ancestor Mingli hasn't come to reinforce them. Could he have fled?”

Ye Tian thought to himself.

But a fifth-grade Invincible shouldn't be that cowardly.

Through his previous interrogations and investigations, he knew that Ancestor Mingli was in a foreign world called the Yuhua World. That world seemed to be a relatively mysterious one within the Blood Mist Alliance. Not many people from the alliance were there, and Paragons were usually not allowed to enter or leave freely; only a few Paragons stood guard there.

Furthermore, the Paragons of the Blood Mist Alliance did not have the coordinates for the Yuhua World.

Ye Tian prepared to travel to the Yuhua World through the Gates, only to find that they had been destroyed.

At this moment, Ye Tian sensed that something was wrong.

Ancestor Mingli hadn't appeared, yet the Gates were destroyed.

For an Invincible, if they wanted to flee, it would be very difficult to hunt them down. Since Invincibles could traverse various foreign worlds, they were impossible to track without special means.

If Ancestor Mingli wanted to flee, there was no need to destroy the Gates leading to the Yuhua World.

Unless the Yuhua World held a secret, and Ancestor Mingli didn't want him to know about it.

“It seems I must make a trip to the Yuhua World no matter what!”

Ye Tian thought to himself.
