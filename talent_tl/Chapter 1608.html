Chapter 1607: The Limitless Eternal Body has reached Minor Stage!

Ye Tian came out of Fenggu River and immediately chose to enter the Source God Pavilion. Although his injuries were severe, they were not enough to cause him to fall. Right now, he wanted to exchange for resources as quickly as possible, otherwise, if the resources suitable for Fenggu Domain Ancestor to break through were all exchanged away, he would have no choice but to exchange for other resources.
So, he began to submit the mission, taking out one Eternal Tribulation Race corpse after another to hand in as proof.
Source God Pavilion naturally had a way to verify the time of death of these Eternal Tribulation Race members, and could also infer whether they had fallen within Fenggu River. This mission could not be faked.
Very soon, the appraisal was completed, and Ye Tian finished the mission.
After that, Ye Tian began exchanging for some scarce resources.
For example, the Water Marsh Cold Leaves and the Other Shore Three Flower Fruit, both of which are very suitable for a Second-Tier Supreme Being breaking through to the Third-Tier Supreme Being realm. Their value is quite high, even somewhat better than Eternal Ancient Pine Leaves, but these two treasures are only found in certain special blessed lands, so they are also relatively rare.
Ye Tian immediately exchanged for these two treasures, and finally exchanged for another rare treasure that could help a Third-Tier Supreme Being冲击 the Fourth-Tier Supreme Being—Undying Source Heart Liquid. This treasure is obtained from the heart-blood essence of the Undying Source Bird, condensed into a single drop of Undying Source Heart Liquid. Its value is extremely high because the Undying Source Bird is very powerful, a special lifeform of the Source Sea. Upon adulthood, it can become a Supreme Being, and among them, the outstanding ones can even become Seventh-Tier Supreme Beings.
To obtain one drop of Undying Source Heart Liquid, one has to kill an Undying Source Bird, which is why its value is so high.
Undying Source Heart Liquid can help a Third-Tier Supreme Being break through to Fourth-Tier Supreme Being, and it has no side effects, but once used, it will have no further effect in the future.
It could be said that this kind of resource can only be used once.
After exchanging for these resources, the contribution points Ye Tian had obtained from completing the mission were also almost exhausted, so he left Source God Pavilion and soon returned to Void Source Hall.
……
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian let the copied body automatically collapse, because recreating a copied body did not consume much, but healing the injuries of a copied body would consume even more, and take a very long time.
Rather than that, it was better to simply let the copied body collapse.
With the copied body collapsed, Ye Tian retrieved that section of Eternal realm finger, then began organizing his gains.
This time, after killing those Eternal Tribulation Race members, the other resources obtained could be set aside; the Wind Source Gold Crystal was the real great harvest.
Whoosh!
A large amount of Wind Source Gold Crystal was placed on the floor of Void Source Hall, piling up into a huge mountain.
“This time my luck was not bad; I obtained so much Wind Source Gold Crystal!” Ye Tian was in a very good mood.
If others knew he had obtained so much Wind Source Gold Crystal, they would probably be jealous to death.
--------------------
Eternal Tribulation Race, the only Supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain.
A Ninth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race member in charge of the battle at Fenggu River heard that a Sixth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race member comparable to a Void Divine Son had been killed, and that a large amount of Wind Source Gold Crystal had been seized, and was instantly extremely furious.
Unfortunately, he could not enter; otherwise he would definitely have intercepted and killed Ye Tian.
Afterward, he relayed the news to an existence of the Eternal Tribulation Race at half a step from the Eternal realm—Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor!
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor was also an incomparably powerful existence among those at half a step from the Eternal realm, ranking among the top three in the Eternal Tribulation Race. It was he who had advocated annexing Fenggu River and carried out this plan.
When he learned that Ye Tian had displayed strength comparable to a strike from the Eighth Heaven, he immediately frowned.
“That Supreme Being’s own cultivation is not high; he should have relied on outside power to erupt with the strength of an Eighth-Tier Supreme Being. He definitely paid a heavy price, but it’s very possible that it was only a clone. If it were his true body, he wouldn’t have paid such a great price!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor thought to himself.
Losing some Wind Source Gold Crystal was nothing. Fenggu River was not limited to just that one source of Wind Source Gold Crystal.
Besides, the Eternal Tribulation Race’s real goal was not to seize these Wind Source Gold Crystals.
A bit of Wind Source Gold Crystal was not enough to make him risk offending Fenggu Domain Ancestor. He had a much larger purpose, and even Fenggu Domain Ancestor might not know this secret.
“If that Supreme Being can continue to strike, then he could easily threaten my plan. I must send a few Sixth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race members with powerful trump cards—at the very least, they must have a way to withstand a strike from an Eighth-Tier Supreme Being. From the situation at the time, the other side likely only had the power of one strike. As long as they withstand that strike, the other side will have no power to resist.”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor decided.
Very quickly, he summoned two Sixth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race members.
Not long after, the two Sixth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race members arrived before Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor and respectfully said, “We greet Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor directly ordered, “I am sending you to Fenggu River. There is an important mission for you.”
“Yes!”
The two Sixth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race members immediately responded.
Whoosh whoosh!!
Two treasures fell into their hands.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor introduced them: “These two treasures are the Indeterminate Armor and the Void Water. The Indeterminate Armor defends against everything; even a strike from an Eighth-Tier Supreme Being would find it hard to kill you. However, once you use it, it will consume your own potential. As for the Void Water, it can turn your body into a void state. Even an attack from an Eighth Stage Supreme Being can be weakened by more than ninety-nine percent, but it will also initially affect the Eternal Body. Therefore, unless you are in a desperate situation, you must not use these two treasures. But once you are in a desperate situation, you must use them!”
“Yes, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor!”
The two Sixth Stage Eternal Tribulation Race members nodded repeatedly in agreement.
Afterward, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor transmitted the details of this mission to the two Eternal Tribulation Race members, telling them to keep it absolutely secret, and that even if they fell, they could not speak of it.
Then, the two Eternal Tribulation Race members headed for Fenggu River.
On the other side.
Ye Tian began refining the special substance Wind Source Gold Liquid from within the Wind Source Gold Crystal. Essentially, only a small drop of Wind Source Gold Liquid could be refined from a large piece of Wind Source Gold Crystal.
Although there seemed to be a great deal of Wind Source Gold Crystal, after all of it was refined, it would probably amount to about 100 milliliters of Wind Source Gold Liquid.
Years passed one after another, and the refining process went very smoothly.
One day, Ye Tian finally refined all of the Wind Source Gold Crystal into Wind Source Gold Liquid.
After that, he began absorbing the Wind Source Gold Liquid to enhance his Limitless Eternal Body.
The first drop of Wind Source Gold Liquid entered his mouth and turned into a vast surge of energy that swept through his entire body.
Boom!!!!
The Limitless Eternal Body seemed to have turned into a monstrous maw, devouring that drop of Wind Source Gold Liquid.
The Limitless Eternal Body, which had originally been at the peak of Entry Stage, actually broke through the shackles of the Limitless Eternal Body with the help of this drop of Wind Source Gold Liquid.
Crack!!!!
The bottleneck shackles were broken, and Ye Tian’s Limitless Eternal Body just like that entered Minor Stage.
