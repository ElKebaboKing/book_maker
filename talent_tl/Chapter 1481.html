Chapter 1480: Scaring Away the Destruction Primordial Ancestor
!
The promotion of the Xuanxu Holy Realm to the Xuanxu Ancestral Realm was something that simply could not be hidden.
Although the Xuanxu Holy Realm was completely sealed off and under the sole control of the human race, there were a large number of factions outside the Xuanxu Holy Realm, and even branch divisions of the Dark Holy Region's forces.
Therefore, when the Xuanxu Holy Realm was promoted to an Ancestral Realm, these factions naturally found out immediately and promptly notified the powers within the Dark Holy Region.
Even if Ye Tian were to wipe out these faction branches with a single thought, he could indeed ensure that the news would not leak for a time, and he could even seal off the Daoshan Domain to prevent anyone from entering or leaving.
But that would only treat the symptoms, not the root cause.
If those branch divisions were destroyed, the Dark Holy Region would certainly send people to investigate, and it would still be exposed eventually.
Consequently, Ye Tian stopped caring.
If it was exposed, so be it. He had already advanced to top-tier Primordial Ancestor combat power, so he naturally did not fear other Primordial Ancestors, not even the two top-tier ones: the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Primordial Ancestor.
....Dark Holy Sect.
At this moment, the Holy Ancestor was cultivating when he suddenly received a piece of intelligence.
"The Xuanxu Holy Realm has been promoted to an Ancestral Realm?"
The Holy Ancestor was shocked.
In the past, he wouldn't have even known what the Xuanxu Holy Realm was because he didn't pay attention to it.
But now, he knew the origin of the Xuanxu Holy Realm; it was the place of origin for the human race and the territory of the Primordial Ancestor of Myriad Laws.
It was said that the Xuanxu Holy Realm was initially just an ordinary small universe, which then promoted step by step until it reached the level of a Holy Realm.
Promoting to a Holy Realm was not difficult; he himself could nurture a world to promote to a Holy Realm, provided he spent some precious treasures.
But promoting to an Ancestral Realm—how was that possible? He couldn't do it at all, and neither could other Primordial Ancestors.
If it were possible to nurture an Ancestral Realm, he would have done so long ago.
A single Dark Holy Land could only give birth to one Original Ancestral Realm and ten Ancestral Realms; this was a restriction.
Places that were not Dark Holy Lands simply could not give birth to an Ancestral Realm.
For example, the headquarters of the Eternal Cult was actually just a treasure land within the Dark Forbidden Zone, but it was not an Ancestral Realm.
The Daoshan Domain was merely a domain; how could an Ancestral Realm be born there? This was abnormal! If this situation could be replicated, even if it took a long time, the Dark Holy Sect would be able to nurture one Ancestral Realm after another. What kind of concept was that? Once he controlled the resources of multiple Ancestral Realms, he could take another step forward.
Whoosh! The Holy Ancestor crossed a vast distance in a single step and descended outside the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
At the same time, other Primordial Ancestors arrived one after another. Even the Destruction Primordial Ancestor from the Dark Destruction Domain received the news immediately and arrived here.
...."It really is an Ancestral Realm!"
The Destruction Primordial Ancestor was shocked.
Just then, Ye Tian emerged from the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
"Fellow Primordial Ancestors, what brings you to my place?"
Ye Tian asked.
"Primordial Ancestor of Myriad Laws, your Daoshan Domain is just a domain. Why would an Ancestral Realm be born here, and specifically an Ancestral Realm promoted from a Holy Realm? I have never heard of a Holy Realm being able to promote to an Ancestral Realm!"
The Holy Ancestor questioned.
"I don't know either. It suddenly promoted, and I find it very strange as well!"
Ye Tian feigned ignorance.
"How about letting us go inside to take a look?"
The Destruction Primordial Ancestor suggested.
Boom!!!! A dense killing intent radiated from Ye Tian's body.
"Everyone, the Xuanxu Holy Realm is the origin of our human race. Naturally, no one is allowed to enter. Otherwise, if our human race has any secrets, wouldn't they be exposed to your investigation? Which faction doesn't have a few small secrets? Therefore, I refuse your suggestion."
"Furthermore, even if the Xuanxu Holy Realm has promoted to the Xuanxu Ancestral Realm and there really is some secret behind it, that is a matter for our human race. What does it have to do with you?"
Ye Tian said coldly.
The Destruction Primordial Ancestor said with a grim face, "Primordial Ancestor of Myriad Laws, the fact that a Holy Realm can promote to an Ancestral Realm is too significant. We must know the reason. If you have a way to elevate a Holy Realm to an Ancestral Realm, please sell this method to us. Otherwise, you will offend all the Primordial Ancestors!"
"Based on just you lot?"
Ye Tian said disdainfully.
The Holy Ancestor said, "Primordial Ancestor of Myriad Laws, it's better to hand over the method for nurturing an Ancestral Realm. Otherwise, with the Destruction Primordial Ancestor and me—two top-tier Primordial Ancestors—you won't be able to stop us.
This is not the Dark Soul Domain. If it were the Dark Soul Domain, you and your clone, the Qingye Primordial Ancestor, working together would be equivalent to two top-tier Primordial Ancestors. But here, your Qingye Primordial Ancestor clone can only exert elite Primordial Ancestor combat power. You cannot contend with us!"
The Holy Ancestor stepping in to deal with Ye Tian was equivalent to allying with the Destruction Primordial Ancestor.
At this time, even if the World Ancestor and the War Ancestor of the War Sect helped, they wouldn't be able to contend with so many Primordial Ancestors.
"Is that so?"
Ye Tian sneered.
Rumble!!!! Another Primordial Ancestor appeared, standing right beside Ye Tian.
This Primordial Ancestor was naturally Ye Tian's duplicate body, with identical combat power.
"This..."
The Destruction Primordial Ancestor and the Holy Ancestor were instantly dumbfounded upon seeing this duplicate body.
They knew Ye Tian possessed a special clone, but they thought there was only one, which was the Qingye Primordial Ancestor.
They never expected another clone to appear, and one with top-tier Primordial Ancestor combat power at that.
It was said that even if Ye Tian's clones died in battle, they could be condensed again.
This was equivalent to having a top-tier Primordial Ancestor clone that could never truly perish!
How could they fight this? Immediately, some Primordial Ancestors became extremely wary and left at once to avoid being caught in the crossfire and potentially killed.
Just then, a figure emerged; it was the World Ancestor.
Rumble!!! The aura of a top-tier Primordial Ancestor radiated from the World Ancestor. Although it was slightly weak, as he had just promoted, he was still a top-tier Primordial Ancestor.
Clearly, the World Ancestor had gained immense benefits from the Dark Soul Domain, allowing him to break through his shackles and advance to top-tier Primordial Ancestor combat power.
Now, the Dark Forbidden Zone had given birth to a total of four true top-tier Primordial Ancestors.
The Primordial Ancestor of Myriad Laws, the World Ancestor, the Destruction Primordial Ancestor, and the Holy Ancestor.
Among them, Ye Tian alone could be counted as two true top-tier Primordial Ancestor combat units. Combined with the World Ancestor, the War Sect's side was equivalent to three top-tier Primordial Ancestor combat units. If the Qingye Primordial Ancestor could exert top-tier combat power outside, it would be even more terrifying.
"Holy Ancestor, Destruction Primordial Ancestor, if you want to deal with our War Sect's Primordial Ancestor of Myriad Laws, then let's have a fight!"
The World Ancestor declared.
Three against two? And among the opponent's three top-tier combat units, one was not afraid of death. This was a fight they couldn't win.
Immediately, the Holy Ancestor said, "Hahaha, I was just joking!"
Clearly, the Holy Ancestor did not want to offend the War Sect. After all, his Dark Holy Sect was located in the Dark Holy Region. If he offended the current War Sect, the Dark Holy Sect's days would not be easy.
The Destruction Primordial Ancestor also lost his nerve. If Ye Tian and the others incited the Holy Ancestor to turn around and besiege him, there might actually be a chance they could keep him there forever.
Consequently, the Destruction Primordial Ancestor also left immediately.
