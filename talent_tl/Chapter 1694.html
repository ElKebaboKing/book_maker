Chapter 1693: A Fierce Battle with Lord Dark Mist!

“An Eternal Supreme Treasure, huh!”
Lord Dark Mist was filled with excitement and greed. This was very likely an Eternal Supreme Treasure!
As one of the six legendary lords, he naturally knew very well that the relic in the Time-Space Domain did not contain a true Eternal Supreme Treasure at all. That sword was merely a projection of time and space; the real Eternal Supreme Treasure was not in the Time-Space Domain at all.
He had never encountered a true Eternal Supreme Treasure before, and now one had finally been born in the Source Sea.
“The Source Sea may be relatively young, but it also has the potential to give birth to an Eternal Supreme Treasure. I waited countless ages, and in the end I finally waited for this!”
Lord Dark Mist wanted to monopolize this Eternal Supreme Treasure and did not want to share it with the other legendary lords.
He had always stationed subordinates in the Source Sea, which was why he could arrive there at the first possible moment. As for the other five legendary-realm lords, they were not paying much attention to the Source Sea.
However, this Eternal Supreme Treasure had not yet fully formed. It still needed some time, and obtaining it would be extremely difficult and take a very long time. He simply could not hide it from the other five legendary-realm lords.
“Seal off this area. Those half-step Eternal Realm beings in the Source Sea are all nearly ignorant; they basically don’t even know what an Eternal Supreme Treasure is. If I occupy this area and do not allow any living being to approach, the news won’t spread.”
Lord Dark Mist thought to himself.
Of course, there was still one matter that needed handling right now, and that was the Lord of Endlessness before him.
This was a Lord of the Immortal Realm, and he even possessed a clone. He had to be subdued, otherwise if the Lord of Endlessness spread the news, he would be unable to stop it.
“Lord of Endlessness, submit to me and become a lord under my line. In the future, I will help you break through to the legendary-realm lord level. How about it?”
Lord Dark Mist said.
“Not good!” Ye Tian said coldly.
Lord Dark Mist still wanted to subdue him. How ridiculous.
“Lord of Endlessness, do you know the gap between us? Although you have a clone, I also have a clone. If I go all out against you, do you think you can still escape? And your foundation is the Source Sea and the Bloodbone Domain, right? You also have a race called the human race. If you offend me, you should know the consequences!” Lord Dark Mist began to threaten him.
Although he knew that threatening Ye Tian would make Ye Tian very angry, and even if he reluctantly agreed, it would still become a hidden danger in the future.
But he could not care that much anymore. He did not want news of the Eternal Supreme Treasure being born here to spread. He needed to buy time. The longer he delayed, the greater his chances of seizing this Eternal Supreme Treasure.
In the future, if he held an Eternal Supreme Treasure, then throughout the entire Source Sea and the Time-Space Domain, he would be an invincible existence.
By then, even if he offended all Eternal Realm beings, he would have nothing to fear.
“You’re threatening me?”
Ye Tian looked coldly at Lord Dark Mist. His aura turned cold and sharp, and strands of killing intent boiled through the void. His will erupted, causing even the void to begin shattering.
“Fourth-tier will!”
Lord Dark Mist was somewhat surprised.
Only legendary-realm lords possessed fourth-tier will, yet Ye Tian had not yet reached the level of a legendary-realm lord and had actually already attained fourth-tier will in advance. That difficulty was truly immense!
Even so, Lord Dark Mist still did not take Ye Tian seriously.
No matter how powerful an Immortal Realm lord was, he was still far from being a match for a legendary-realm lord.
“Lord of Endlessness, I’ll ask you again. Will you submit to me or not?”
Lord Dark Mist pressed.
“Scram!”
Ye Tian did not want to waste any more words and directly said coldly.
Boom!!!
Lord Dark Mist’s aura erupted, and countless billions upon countless billions of regions shattered. At this moment, he truly wanted to kill Ye Tian.
But in the end, he still held himself back.
If he really made a move, then he would undoubtedly stand on opposite sides from Ye Tian, and the news here would probably no longer be containable.
In an instant, Lord Dark Mist’s aura restrained itself, and his entire demeanor also became mild as he said, “Lord of Endlessness, you are a peak Immortal Realm lord. It’s quite normal that you don’t want to join my faction. But the opportunity here now belongs to me. As long as you swear an oath of will and heart not to leak the news here, and not to allow anyone to approach this place, I won’t attack you. Otherwise, you and I will be mortal enemies.”
Ye Tian sneered. “Lord Dark Mist, you want to monopolize the Eternal Supreme Treasure here and don’t want the other five legendary-realm lords to know about it, but aren’t you underestimating me a little too much? Let me tell you, this Eternal Supreme Treasure belongs to me. You should be the one to scram.”
The façade had been torn apart!
Lord Dark Mist could no longer endure the anger in his heart. If it were any other Immortal Realm lord in the Time-Space Domain, who would dare speak to him like this? Which one would not be deferential and respectful?
“Lord of Endlessness, this clone of yours is dead for sure. I will even keep hunting down your other clones until I completely kill you!”
As soon as his words fell, Lord Dark Mist made his move.
“Three Thousand Dark Mist Shadow Bodies!”
One gray shadow after another emerged, filling the surroundings.
In an instant, these gray shadows all attacked Ye Tian, and each one possessed strength comparable to an elite nine-step Immortal Realm lord.
If it were an ordinary nine-step Immortal Realm lord, they would probably be quickly blasted to death by Lord Dark Mist.
Facing a legendary-realm lord, Ye Tian would not underestimate his opponent.
Void True Body!
Will Barrier Light!
These two legendary Eternal Techniques had now also been perfected by Ye Tian to the level of top-tier legendary Eternal Techniques, and their power was extremely formidable.
Bang bang bang!!!
The attacks of the Three Thousand Dark Mist Shadow Bodies were all blocked by Ye Tian’s Will Barrier Light, unable to injure him at all.
The next moment.
Ye Tian unleashed the legendary Eternal Technique Ten Thousandfold Void, stacking countless layers of void to suppress Lord Dark Mist.

Boom!!!
Lord Dark Mist’s expression changed, clearly affected by the Ten Thousandfold Void, and even his body began to tremble.
Layers upon layers of void compressed into a single layer, and stretches of region compressed into a tiny fragment, all in order to suppress Lord Dark Mist.
However, Lord Dark Mist was still very powerful. He forcibly tore through the suppression of the countless layers of void, and vast swathes of void shattered into nothingness.
Boom!!!!!
The two sides kept colliding and slaughtering each other, but overall, Ye Tian was still at a disadvantage.
If not for the Void True Body, Ye Tian in his normal state would not be a match for Lord Dark Mist at all.
“You actually possess the ability of a Void Beast. How did you do that?”
Lord Dark Mist asked in confusion.
But Ye Tian would not answer him.
Lord Dark Mist took out a top-tier Void Sacred Soldier specifically designed to counter Void Beasts, but under repeated strikes from Ye Tian’s will Severing Void Sword, that Void Sacred Soldier also gradually shattered.

From beginning to end, Ye Tian still had not activated the Eternal Supreme Treasure, the Primordial Token.
But after a long while, he had no choice but to use it.
Hum!!!!!
Another of Lord Dark Mist’s clones appeared in the Source Sea and directly headed for the Dark Forbidden Domain. This action completely enraged Ye Tian.
Lord Dark Mist had a total of two clones, plus his true body, making three major combat powers.
Now, aside from his true body, both clones had been deployed, and he even wanted to strike at the Dark Forbidden Domain. It was clear that he truly wanted an irreconcilable fight with Ye Tian.
Just as Lord Dark Mist’s second clone was about to descend upon the Dark Forbidden Domain, one of Ye Tian’s copied bodies intercepted him.
When Lord Dark Mist saw yet another body of Ye Tian, he thought it was Ye Tian’s true body and paid it no mind.
But very quickly, he was dumbfounded.
Whoosh whoosh!!
Two copied bodies appeared. At this moment, Ye Tian had already dispatched three major bodies for battle.
More importantly, one of Ye Tian’s copied bodies directly activated the Primordial Token.
Hum!
The Eternal Primordial Domain enveloped one of Lord Dark Mist’s copied bodies, and Ten Thousandfold Void was unleashed.
With the help of the Primordial Token, Ten Thousandfold Void had already fused with the power of the three great forces, reaching the might of a half-step legendary Eternal Technique.
Crack, crack, crack!!!!!
Lord Dark Mist’s Three Thousand Dark Mist Shadow Bodies all shattered, and that legendary Eternal Technique simply could no longer be sustained.
Then, Ye Tian’s Will Extreme Fist smashed directly into Lord Dark Mist, blasting him into the depths of time and space with one punch. Countless layers of time and space shattered, and Lord Dark Mist’s body also began to crack. This punch had truly injured him.
However, it was still somewhat difficult for Ye Tian to kill Lord Dark Mist.
