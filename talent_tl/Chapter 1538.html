Chapter 1537: Saint-Name Level Power—Tianfeng Sea!

Blood Wolf Pavilion.
Ye Tian walked into this shop. As soon as he entered, he encountered a Supreme Powerhouse clerk, and one in the Physical Body Tribulation Realm at that, with a cultivation of approximately the Third Physical Body Tribulation Realm.
In the Tianlin Realm, only Invincibles held a certain level of status. Supreme Powerhouses, even those in the Physical Body Tribulation Realm, had little status and it was quite normal for them to serve as clerks.
Blood Wolf Pavilion not only sold various treasures but also purchased them.
In the center of the Blood Wolf Pavilion was a massive screen displaying various treasures and their prices.
Nine-Leaf Blood Qi Grass: 1,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Yang Cloud Stone: 8,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Tribulation Stone: 100 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
First Tribulation Blood: 80,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Tenth Tribulation Blood: 200,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Hundredth Tribulation Blood: 3,000,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Thousandth Tribulation Blood: 50,000,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
...
"The value of Tribulation Blood is actually this high!"
Ye Tian was incredibly surprised.
If his strength grew in the future and he could easily slaughter Thousandth Tribulation Beasts, wouldn't he be able to effortlessly obtain countless Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals?
However, he then thought that once his strength increased, perhaps a hundred million Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals wouldn't mean much to him anymore.
367
The screen also listed Tribulation Bones for sale. Based on their value, a Thirty-Seventh Tribulation Bone was worth about 300,000 each, which was cheaper than Tribulation Blood, as Thirty-Seventh Tribulation Blood was worth roughly 800,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Of course, if Ye Tian were to sell Tribulation Bones, he certainly wouldn't get such a high price, as the Blood Wolf Pavilion also needed to make a profit.
Ye Tian did not sell the Thirty-Seventh Tribulation Bone; instead, he sold those Twenty-Eighth Tribulation Bones.
He didn't sell all of them, only three. Each bone sold for 180,000, totaling 540,000 Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
This caused the number of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals on Ye Tian's person to skyrocket instantly.
"It seems that when hunting Tribulation Beasts, the real money is in the Tribulation Blood and Tribulation Bones. The Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals found on the beasts themselves are actually of the lowest value!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
The Blood Wolf Pavilion had many treasures, including many that were helpful to Ancestors and Supreme Ancestors. There was also a treasure called the Emperor Blood Soul. This type of treasure was a supreme rarity for Ancestors who had not ascended by creating their own Ancestral Laws. It could make the foundation of the physical body even stronger and remove the barrier between the body and the Ancestral Law, increasing their compatibility.
This way, those Ancestors could rival those who had created their own Ancestral Laws, giving them hope of ascending to higher realms in the future.
Ye Tian's wife and children had all become Ancestors by merging with his Ancestral Law and were thus affected by this limitation. However, if they refined the Emperor Blood Soul, this influence would cease to exist.
Of course, the Emperor Blood Soul was also incredibly precious, valued at 2 million Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Ye Tian needed four pieces of Emperor Blood Soul, which would cost a total of 8 million Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Ye Tian couldn't afford it right now; even if he sold all the Tribulation Bones he had, he still wouldn't be able to afford it.
Therefore, Ye Tian planned to purchase the Emperor Blood Souls the next time he came to the Tianlin Realm.
...
Over the following period, Ye Tian remained in Juewu City to understand the situation. Not only did he learn about the uses of many treasures and greatly expand his knowledge, but he also learned a secret: once the Supreme Ones of the Eternal Heaven and Earth leave the Eternal Heaven and Earth and enter a Foreign World, their strength is severely suppressed.
Because of this, it is incredibly difficult for Supreme Ones from the Eternal Heaven and Earth to enter Foreign Worlds to hunt Tribulation Beasts. Even an incredibly powerful First-Class Supreme One, under conditions where their strength is infinitely suppressed, could very likely perish if they went to hunt Tribulation Beasts.
For this reason, very few Supreme Ones from the Eternal Heaven and Earth go to hunt Tribulation Beasts. Only powerful Supreme Ones who rose from the Foreign Worlds dare to hunt them, and even then, they need to investigate thoroughly, sometimes even using weaker individuals as bait to understand the beast's strength before daring to strike.
Since more than ninety-nine percent of Supreme Ones come from the Eternal Heaven and Earth, Supreme Ones who go to hunt Tribulation Beasts are extremely rare.
This is also why the value of Tribulation Blood and Tribulation Bones is so high.
Furthermore.
If one kills too many Tribulation Beasts, they will carry the aura of the beasts' resentment. Once this aura accumulates to a certain level, it will attract the attention of some terrifying Tribulation Beasts.
Tribulation Beasts with over a hundred thousand Tribulations can leave their Foreign World and enter others. Once noticed by such a beast, even a First-Class Invincible has the possibility of falling.
Therefore.
Many Invincibles, despite their great strength, do not dare to hunt Tribulation Beasts in large numbers. Only some Invincibles who are proficient in clone abilities rely on clones to hunt them, as the resentment on the clone will not transfer to the main body. However, this requires sacrificing clones repeatedly, and the cost is too high. Those proficient in clone abilities do not hunt Tribulation Beasts frequently either; if they use too many clones, it becomes a losing venture.
However, Ye Tian did not need to worry about this problem.
Currently, what Ye Tian wanted most was to join a powerful organization. After some investigation, he learned that the eight Eternal-Name level powers almost never recruit outside Invincibles.
This is because six of the eight Eternal-Name level powers are race-based organizations, similar to the Human Race. Would the Human Race recruit experts from outside the race?
Although the other two Eternal-Name level powers do recruit outside Invincibles, their requirements are incredibly high, and they place immense importance on loyalty. One must swear an incredibly harsh Eternal Oath never to leave the Eternal-Name level power. Furthermore, they do not value ordinary Invincibles and only recruit those at the Fifth-Class Invincible level or higher.
Therefore, none of these eight Eternal-Name level powers were suitable for Ye Tian.
Consequently, Ye Tian lowered his sights and chose a Saint-Name level power.
Some Saint-Name level powers were also incredibly strong. Aside from lacking a Supreme One, they were not necessarily weaker than Eternal-Name level powers at the Invincible level.
After repeated investigations and screening, Ye Tian felt there was one Saint-Name level power he had a hope of joining.
This Saint-Name level power was the Tianfeng Sea. This organization was very powerful and incredibly ancient. Although it had no Supreme One, it possessed several First-Class Invincibles, and its reputation was very prominent in the Tianlin Realm.
Crucially, this power recruited outsiders and had few restrictions, so many Invincibles wanted to join the Tianfeng Sea.
However, joining the Tianfeng Sea was not easy. The entry threshold was being a Sixth-Class Invincible, and one must have passed six Physical Body Tribulations during the Physical Body Tribulation Realm; otherwise, they were ineligible to join.
Most Invincibles had originally broken through with five Physical Body Tribulations, and thus were ineligible to join the Tianfeng Sea.
Additionally, one needed to complete an entry mission for the Tianfeng Sea. Only after completing it could one become a member.
"I'll choose the Tianfeng Sea!"
Ye Tian decided.
