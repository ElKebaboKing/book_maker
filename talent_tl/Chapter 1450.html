Chapter 1449: I Can Absorb Annihilation Energy
?
"Block that Supreme-level Domain Ancestor!"
That six-mark Annihilation Clan member transmitted the message while clashing with the Vajra War Ancestor.
Immediately, a four-mark Annihilation Clan member rushed toward Ye Tian.
Meanwhile, the other Annihilation Clan members fighting Ye Tian retreated one after another, refusing to engage him.
But Ye Tian directly unleashed the Five Elements Sealing. Even though the effect of the Five Elements Sealing was less than ideal in this region, it still managed to trap these Annihilation Clan members in time.
"Kill!"
Ye Tian unleashed the Void Slash Ancestral Technique, striking a one-mark Annihilation Clan member. The opponent had no way to resist and was once again killed instantly.
After all, the recovery ability of the Annihilation Clan was poor, which was one of their flaws. If it were any other Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, they might have survived, but an Annihilation Clan member could not.
Just then, the four-mark Annihilation Clan member arrived, tearing through Ye Tian's Five Elements Sealing with a single hand and engaging in a fierce battle with him.
Rumble!!! Ye Tian relied on his Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body and various Ancestral Techniques to completely suppress this four-mark Annihilation Clan member.
"Damn it, this Supreme-level Domain Ancestor clearly only has the cultivation of an early-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, but his strength is somewhere between our four-mark and five-mark Annihilation Clan members!"
The four-mark Annihilation Clan member said in surprise.
A five-mark Annihilation Clan member was comparable to a Half-Step Primordial Ancestor; this gap was simply too large.
A monster! The four-mark Annihilation Clan member thought to himself.
"Retreat!"
The six-mark Annihilation Clan member transmitted the order.
These Annihilation Clan members didn't originally intend to break the defensive line; they just wanted to see if they could kill one or two Supreme-level Domain Ancestors. They hadn't expected to lose two one-mark members instead.
Soon, all the Annihilation Clan members retreated.
.... "Myriad Law War Ancestor, I didn't expect you to actually kill two one-mark Annihilation Clan members. I've been guarding this place for so long and have only ever killed one!"
Peng Ancestor said in surprise.
"It was just luck. They didn't know my strength, which is why they sent some weaklings to besiege me. If they knew my true strength, I wouldn't have been able to kill even one one-mark Annihilation Clan member so easily!"
Ye Tian said modestly.
The other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors nodded. This time, the Annihilation Clan had indeed been careless; otherwise, killing two of them wouldn't have been so easy. Even for a Primordial Ancestor, killing an Annihilation Clan member was somewhat difficult.
After all, once a Primordial Ancestor made a move, those Annihilation Clan members with seven marks or more would also take action.
Furthermore, in this kind of space-time, a Primordial Ancestor's strength was suppressed even more severely. It was impossible for them to crush a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor with a single thought as they could in the outside world.
Vajra War Ancestor did not linger and left immediately, while everyone else returned to their respective guard posts. Unless one was severely injured and their resources were exhausted, making it impossible to replenish energy, they were not allowed to return to the Holy Domain Continent to recover.
Otherwise, leaving this place was forbidden. The reason Supreme-level Domain Ancestors didn't want to stay here was simply because it was too boring, and they were in a state of constant mental tension.
If it weren't for the fact that Supreme-level Domain Ancestors had all experienced countless hardships and possessed strong mental states, they would have truly broken down.
Back at his guard post.
Ye Tian began to integrate the Ancestral Talents he had obtained from the Annihilation Clan. One by one, the Ancestral Talents merged into Ye Tian's soul, becoming his own.
"Now that I have the Time Ancestral Talent, I can go and practice Time Ancestral Techniques. And since I have the Annihilation Ancestral Talent, I don't have to worry about energy issues anymore!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Only after integrating the Annihilation Ancestral Talent did he realize the use of this talent: it was to control and refine Annihilation Energy, and even use it to influence space-time. This was the effect of the Annihilation Ancestral Talent.
Boom!!!! Annihilation Energy surged into Ye Tian's body, completely without harming him.
Previously, if he had dared to absorb Annihilation Energy, his physical body would have gradually disintegrated, as only Primordial Ancestors dared to absorb it.
But now, with the help of the Annihilation Ancestral Talent, Ye Tian could refine it.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian was overjoyed. Annihilation Energy could actually rapidly increase his cultivation and quickly temper his Ancestral Body.
It was as if... it was supplementing the elements his body lacked.
This brand-new energy was like a supreme elixir for increasing his strength, far more useful than any other energy.
"The reason the Primordial Ancestors are willing to guard this place is probably because Annihilation Energy helps them temper their Primordial Bodies faster and increase their cultivation."
Ye Tian thought.
The Primordial Ancestors hadn't revealed this news because only they could refine it, while Supreme-level Domain Ancestors could not. To a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, Annihilation Energy was... poison.
If this secret were revealed, many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors would likely try it desperately, leading to a large number of them being heavily injured, which would make it impossible to hold this defensive line.
Therefore, the Primordial Ancestors had hidden this secret.
"It's a pity my main body isn't here!"
Ye Tian murmured.
However, his main body wouldn't dare come here anyway; it was too dangerous. If the main body fell, it would be a true death.
If a duplicated body fell, it could be condensed again.
"If I can obtain some Annihilation treasures, I can take them back. Moreover, the Ancestral Talents copied by the duplicated body can be stripped away and bestowed upon the main body. That way, the main body can obtain the Annihilation Ancestral Talent without coming here! However, I can't go back right now; my guard duty isn't over, and I haven't obtained any Annihilation treasures yet!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In the following days, Ye Tian continuously absorbed and refined Annihilation Energy, trying to understand it while also tempering his Ancestral Body.
In short, he would increase his strength by whatever margin he could.
.... Annihilation Clan Territory.
A group of Annihilation Clan members was holding a meeting, led by an eight-mark Annihilation Clan member.
At this moment, these members were reporting the situation.
"Tell me about the battle situation over the last billion years!"
The eight-mark member spoke.
"Thirty-one clansmen were killed, while the opponent lost eight Supreme-level Domain Ancestor warriors!"
One Annihilation Clan member reported.
"They only lost eight, while we lost thirty-one. You have disappointed me too much.
By the way, wasn't it twenty-nine deaths just a short while ago? Two more died during this period?"
The eight-mark member said coldly.
"Just recently, two one-mark clansmen fell. The opponent was an early-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, but his strength was even greater than a four-mark clansman!"
A six-mark Annihilation Clan member said.
"What? An early-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor is stronger than a four-mark clansman? They have such a monster?"
The eight-mark member said solemnly.
"Lord Ao Long, that early-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor practitioner must be killed. Otherwise, if he grows up in the future, he might become another Primordial Ancestor-level figure! He might even grow to a realm like yours, Lord Ao Long!"
The six-mark member suggested.
"That practitioner must be killed, but we must plan it carefully!"
Ao Long decided.
