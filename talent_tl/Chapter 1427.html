Chapter 1426: Legendary Item
: Holy Gold Sand!
Dark Red Domain Ancestor was ranked third on the Immortal Holy Sect's Ultimate List. Her strength was incredibly formidable; she had condensed her Holy Soul long before entering the Holy Soul Realm, and her Holy Soul was much stronger than average. Her own foundation was also heaven-defying.
Additionally, she was a very ancient Ultimate Domain Ancestor who had practiced countless secret techniques and even obtained numerous opportunities, tempering her physical body to an incredibly terrifying degree. Possessing multiple holy artifacts, her combat power was immense, far beyond what Golden Snake Domain Ancestor could compare to.
In terms of strength, she had definitely reached the peak of Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor combat power. As for whether she had reached... Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor combat power, no one could say for sure.
After all, on the surface, no one had been heard to have stepped into the realm of Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor combat power.
And Ye Tian was the first! "Domain Ancestor Wanfa, give me some face and release Golden Snake Domain Ancestor, will you?"
Dark Red Domain Ancestor said hurriedly.
"No!"
Ye Tian refused.
"No!" Golden Snake Domain Ancestor let out a terrified roar, but it was to no avail. Under another strike from Ye Tian, Golden Snake Domain Ancestor perished completely.
Seeing the fall of Golden Snake Domain Ancestor, Dark Red Domain Ancestor was extremely furious.
This was a monstrous genius in the top ten of the Immortal Holy Sect's Ultimate List; losing even one was a massive loss.
Furthermore, she had known Golden Snake Domain Ancestor for countless ages and had a decent relationship with him. Ye Tian had actually killed Golden Snake Domain Ancestor right in front of her, showing her no respect at all.
However, in the end, she did not make a move.
Golden Snake Domain Ancestor was already dead; that was a fact that could not be changed.
Although the Ancestors had the ability to reverse the life and death of an Ultimate Domain Ancestor, the price was too high and they wouldn't do it. Thus, dead meant... dead.
She didn't want to fight to the death with Ye Tian. Moreover, Ye Tian possessed the combat power of a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor; unless she used her trump cards, she was simply no match for him.
She endured it! "She didn't move?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
If Dark Red Domain Ancestor had attacked, he would have killed her as well while he was at it.
In the following days, Star Soul Sands appeared one after another. Ye Tian led the Ultimate Domain Ancestors of the War Sect to seize several grains. Some Star Soul Sands were too far away, and other Ultimate Domain Ancestors had extraordinary means, so they were seized instantly. Even with Ye Tian's speed, he couldn't snatch them all in time.
But even so, many Ultimate Domain Ancestors of the War Sect obtained several grains of Star Soul Sand.
But they were well aware that this was all thanks to Ye Tian and had little to do with them.
"Everyone, I plan to act alone!"
Ye Tian suggested.
The others had no objections. After all, they had already gained a huge advantage, and they would feel embarrassed to continue like this.
The Ultimate Domain Ancestors of the War Sect agreed, and Ye Tian separated from them, heading to another area of this treasure land to avoid competing with them for Star Soul Sand later.
As time slowly passed, grains of Star Soul Sand appeared, and Ye Tian, relying on his strength and speed, managed to snatch quite a few.
These Star Soul Sands were very effective at tempering Ye Tian's Soul Holy Light. If taken to the outside world, they could create many Invincible Domain Ancestors, but such a great opportunity only occurred once in countless ages.
The Primordial Ancestral World only produced this once in such a long time, making it an opportunity for the various Holy Sects to rise.
After this event, the various Holy Worlds would likely produce a large number of Invincible Domain Ancestors, but such a golden age had nothing to do with ordinary holy lands or sects.
"With so many treasures that can help Ultimate Domain Ancestors break through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, the number of Invincible Domain Ancestors in the various Holy Sects must be staggering!"
Ye Tian suddenly thought.
In fact, he didn't even know how many Invincible Domain Ancestors the War Sect had.
Many Invincible Domain Ancestors were not in the War Ancestor World but were out adventuring in the outside world, some even leaving the Dark Holy Region to enter the boundless Dark Forbidden Region to seek other opportunities.
Otherwise, if they stayed in the Dark Holy Region, they would have few chances to break through to the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm.
Suddenly.
Not far away, spatial fluctuations emerged.
The gazes of many Ultimate Domain Ancestors turned toward that spot.
"Has another grain of Star Soul Sand appeared?"
Ye Tian began to warp over, but strangely, although the spatial fluctuations had emerged, the Star Soul Sand did not appear immediately.
Previously, the spatial fluctuations and the Star Soul Sand had always appeared simultaneously. This situation was somewhat special.
Boom!!!! A grain of golden Star Soul Sand, many times smaller than the regular Star Soul Sand, appeared. Its size was only about that of a small mountain, but its mass was terrifying, and the fluctuations it emitted were incredibly powerful.
"What kind of Star Soul Sand is this?"
Many Ultimate Domain Ancestors were dazed.
But someone still recognized it and exclaimed in shock: "This is Holy Gold Sand! It's a rare treasure rumored to allow the Holy Soul to undergo a second qualitative transformation. Even within the Holy Sects, there are very few such treasures!"
"Holy Gold Sand!!!!"
Many people went wild, their eyes filled with greed.
To allow the Holy Soul to undergo a second qualitative transformation—what kind of concept was that? Although the current Holy Sects had similar treasures, they were too few, and they were reluctant to give them to the disciples. Even the Holy Gifted were not qualified to use them, unless they were the offspring of an Ancestor. However, many Ancestors were unwilling to produce offspring, so Ancestral scions were exceptionally rare.
But what if one day an Ancestor wanted to have offspring? Then the incredibly rare resources within the Holy Sect would be given to that Ancestral scion.
There were Holy Gifted among them, but legendary items like Holy Gold Sand were beyond their wildest dreams.
At this moment, Ultimate Domain Ancestors like Wu Xiu and Dark Red Domain Ancestor went mad.
At the same time, those Ultimate Domain Ancestors from all over the Holy Soul Realm who had previously looked down on this treasure land also rushed over.
Holy Soul Sand wasn't very effective for those Ultimate Domain Ancestors who had already condensed their Holy Souls, so if there were other opportunities, those Ultimate Domain Ancestors wouldn't come to this treasure land.
But now, Holy Gold Sand had appeared, and those Ultimate Domain Ancestors immediately abandoned other opportunity sites and rushed here one by one.
"The Holy Gold Sand is mine!"
Wu Xiu's eyes were bloodshot as he directly revealed his Forbidden Body.
Boom!!!! Wu Xiu transformed into a massive ape hundreds of millions of feet tall, his aura incredibly terrifying, very close to a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor. He had hidden a portion of his strength before, but now he revealed almost all of it.
And Dark Red Domain Ancestor was no exception, her aura fully unleashed as if she had already stepped half a foot into the level of Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor combat power.
They all reached out to seize the Holy Gold Sand.
But a treasure like Holy Gold Sand also had an automatic self-protection ability; streaks of golden light shattered the powers and techniques of the various Ultimate Domain Ancestors.
Clearly, taking the Holy Gold Sand would require some time.
