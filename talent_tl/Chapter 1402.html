Chapter 1401: Eternal Undying Sacred Body, Extreme Realm
!
The Sacred Soul is the ultimate pursuit of the ultimate domain ancestors. The ultimate domain ancestors on the Top Ten Sacred Gates' Ultimate Power List, as well as those four-star 44 kings of the Ghost Fox Organization, their pursuit is... to condense a Sacred Soul.
But in reality, how many can condense a Sacred Soul? Countless ultimate domain ancestors cultivated arduously for endless years, almost falling into madness, yet still failed to condense a Sacred Soul, and even on the path of seeking opportunities, one by one they perished.
Even if they didn't perish, it was only after endless years of despair that they abandoned this idea, choosing instead to break through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm and give up on condensing a Sacred Soul.
Therefore, among the ultimate domain ancestors, those who can condense a Sacred Soul are too few. Even Sacred Gifted Ones find it difficult to condense a Sacred Soul.
After all, Sacred Gifted Ones only have an advantage in cultivating Sacred Skills, but in condensing a Sacred Soul, they have no advantage.
Every genius who can condense a Sacred Soul at the ultimate domain ancestor level, after becoming an Invincible Domain Ancestor, is not weaker than a Sacred Gifted One, and may even surpass ordinary Sacred Gifted Ones.
Ye Tian also somewhat didn't expect that the Ghost Fox Organization actually had such a talented individual.
"This pressure is finally a bit interesting!"
Ye Tian smiled, without the slightest hint of panic.
"Smile"
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor frowned, certain that things might be somewhat unexpected, and therefore he didn't want to waste time.
Immediately.
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor activated the projection sword of the Grand Dawn Sword.
The Grand Dawn Sword is not an ordinary sacred artifact; it is a powerful sacred artifact ranked among the top hundred on the Sacred Artifact List. Even its projection sword possesses power comparable to sacred artifacts ranked within the top thousand on the Sacred Artifact List.
What's more, the Grand Dawn Sword projection can unleash a terrifying strike, powerful enough to slay some mighty Invincible Domain Ancestors.
Boom!!!! The golden Grand Dawn Sword emerged from Azure Sun Domain Ancestor's body, like a ten-thousand-zhang giant sword, its might intimidating the endless territories.
"This is.... the Grand Dawn Sword!!!!"
"No, wait, it's not the real Grand Dawn Sword, it's the projection sword of the Grand Dawn Sword!"
Ye Tian was surprised.
This kind of projection is similar to the War Realm Tower projection in the Profound Void Sacred Realm; it is actually a physical sword and can exist independently.
Basically, sacred artifacts in the top hundred of the Sacred Artifact List, by using some special secret methods and paying some price, can cultivate and give birth to a projection artifact.
For example, Ye Tian's Primordial Creation Well, if he knew the secret art of cultivating projection artifacts, after paying some price, he could also create a projection version of the Primordial Creation Well.
Unfortunately, he doesn't know such secret arts, nor would he create such sacred artifact projections, because there's no need.
Even if he created one, he wouldn't dare to use it, for fear that the secret of the Primordial Creation Well would be exposed. At that time, it wouldn't be just some ultimate domain ancestors that would be attracted, but Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, or even Ancestors.
The appearance of the Grand Dawn Sword's projection sword indeed made Ye Tian feel a true crisis.
Just at this moment, Ye Tian unleashed a Time Sacred Skill — Time Shards! He wanted to glimpse into the future, otherwise, he would easily stumble.
Crack!!!! A corner of the future appeared before Ye Tian.
In the future vision, Ye Tian failed to dodge the Grand Dawn Sword's strike; even the Eternal Sacred Skill couldn't freeze the Grand Dawn Sword, and the Grand Dawn Sword shattered space-time with a single strike, blasting into his body.
This sword strike, despite all his efforts, still heavily wounded him.
A projection sacred artifact like the Grand Dawn Sword, Azure Sun Domain Ancestor could only use once in a short period.
Ye Tian prepared to escape into the depths of the void to heal his injuries, and after his injuries recovered, he would immediately fight Azure Sun Domain Ancestor again.
But just as he was about to escape into the depths of the void, a gray light emanated from Azure Sun Domain Ancestor's body; that light transformed into a small knife, instantly piercing through his body.
Then, that gray light obliterated his soul, and severed the connection in the ethereal realm, crossing endless dark forbidden domains, striking towards an unknown place.
That place was—"the Profound Void Sacred Realm!"
Ye Tian was extremely surprised; this corner of the future showed him being struck down.
Of course, this corner of the future is not fixed; it is merely the most likely scenario to occur if there is no interference.
"That is a Karma Sacred Skill — Causality Annihilation!!!!"
Ye Tian said in surprise.
Karma Sacred Skills are extremely rare. Although Ye Tian possesses many Sacred Gifts, he does not have a Karma Sacred Gift, therefore, he hasn't tried to cultivate any Karma Sacred Skills.
Moreover, Karma Sacred Skills are not that easy to cultivate; they are too difficult to get started with.
Without a Karma Sacred Gift, it's likely even Invincible Domain Ancestors would find it difficult to cultivate a Karma Sacred Skill to the introductory or minor accomplishment realm.
"Azure Sun Domain Ancestor actually knows a Karma Sacred Skill, and that Karma Sacred Skill is probably cultivated to the major accomplishment realm; it seems he must have had an astonishing encounter,"
Ye Tian thought.
From the future vision, when Azure Sun Domain Ancestor fully unleashed his power, his Sacred Soul appeared.
The Sacred Soul, the Grand Dawn Sword's projection sword, plus the Karma Sacred Skill — Causality Annihilation, ultimately slew this clone body of his, and then attacked his main body from afar.
Although separated by vast territories, the power of the Karma Sacred Skill was somewhat weakened. Moreover, his main body was protected by the Profound Void Sacred Realm and the Primordial Creation Well, so even if Azure Sun Domain Ancestor's power was stronger, he couldn't kill him through the Primordial Creation Well, but he could still injure him.
In addition, this clone body of his would definitely perish.
But the premise of all this was that Ye Tian didn't know Azure Sun Domain Ancestor's plan. Now that he knew, it would be somewhat difficult for Azure Sun Domain Ancestor to kill him.
Ye Tian was very clear that his strength simply couldn't contend with Azure Sun Domain Ancestor.
Even if he was a monstrous genius, he had to admit that his current strength was far inferior to Azure Sun Domain Ancestor.
Therefore, he must complete a breakthrough to contend with Azure Sun Domain Ancestor, and even slay Azure Sun Domain Ancestor.
"Then break through!"
Ye Tian murmured.
"Eternal Undying Sacred Body, break through for me!"
Buzz!!!! Ye Tian's body suddenly shattered, endless power of forbidden grand dao protected Ye Tian, and endless rules rippled, allowing Ye Tian's body to undergo a perfect transformation.
At this moment, his body was slowly reassembling; this was a breakthrough of breaking down to rebuild! And this process simply couldn't be interrupted.
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor could only watch Ye Tian break through, without any means to stop him.
"This is the Eternal Undying Sacred Body, currently breaking through to the extreme realm!"
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor didn't know much about the Eternal Undying Sacred Body, only knowing that it was the number one Sacred Body in the Dark Sacred Domain. As for how powerful the Eternal Undying Sacred Body at the extreme realm was, he also didn't know.
"It probably won't boost his strength too much; I should still be able to kill him!"
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor murmured in his heart.
However, his confidence suddenly dropped significantly. Even though he told himself he could still kill Ye Tian, his body was somewhat tense.
Soon.
Ye Tian completely finished his breakthrough; his body had already undergone a qualitative change, completely reaching the extreme realm of the Eternal Undying Sacred Body.
It was at this moment that Ye Tian truly understood the reason why the Eternal Undying Sacred Body was called the number one Sacred Body.
