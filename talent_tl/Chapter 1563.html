Chapter 1562: Supreme Beings Clash!

"Be careful, Luo Ge, the Son of the Void from the Nether Hall, is here!"
A third-class Invincible of the Tianfeng Sea said nervously.
Although Ye Tian's strength was comparable to a Son of the Void, he was not a true Son of the Void after all. No one knew exactly how powerful a Son of the Void was. What if Ye Tian was not his match?
Perhaps Luo Ge alone could not stop so many Invincibles, but Luo Ge would not fight them single-handedly. Instead, he would lead the many third-class Invincibles of the Nether Hall to crush them together. If no one could block Luo Ge, their confidence would be destroyed, and they might even suffer a crushing defeat.
Unless the Tianfeng Sea paid a great price to sacrifice many heavy treasures to severely wound Luo Ge.
Luo Ge arrived too quickly, leading a large number of third-class Invincibles to charge over, intending to tear through the Tianfeng Sea's siege of the Jinhe Branch.
Over a dozen third-class Invincibles from the Tianfeng Sea immediately clashed with Luo Ge, but they were all sent flying by a single strike from him, demonstrating a combat power that was nearly invincible.
"I'll be the one to deal with him!"
Ye Tian blocked Luo Ge and spoke.
The other Invincibles nodded. They indeed could not stop Luo Ge unless they deployed too many Invincibles, but if they did that, how could they resist the other third-class Invincibles of the Nether Hall?
If the Nether Hall Invincibles from the Jinhe Branch rushed out at this time and attacked them from both inside and out, the Tianfeng Sea might suffer heavy losses this time.
Boom!!!!
Ye Tian and Luo Ge began their fierce battle.
As soon as they clashed, Ye Tian found himself slightly at a disadvantage.
After all, he had only recently become a third-class Invincible, while Luo Ge was already incredibly close to being a second-class Invincible. Furthermore, Luo Ge's Invincible Tribulation Body was also incredibly powerful, not even weaker than Ye Tian's current physical body.
Additionally, Luo Ge's combat techniques were incomparably refined, as if he had experienced countless eons of slaughter. He had turned every Invincible Technique into combat instinct, and every move was a set of Invincible Techniques. The transitions between various Invincible Techniques were incredibly fluid, as if he had turned Invincible Techniques into basic attacks.
Ye Tian had not yet mastered such methods.
After all, his opportunities for actual combat were indeed few, and his cultivation speed was too fast. He usually spent his time in silent, bitter cultivation, so where would he get many opportunities for battle?
He did not know how Luo Ge had trained his combat abilities, but it made him realize that there is always someone better. Luo Ge was indeed very powerful.
Luo Ge was also surprised. He had to admit that Ye Tian's strength had already reached the threshold of a Son of the Void. If Ye Tian were to take the assessment for a Son of the Void, he would have nearly a fifty percent chance of passing. If he cultivated bitterly for another period of time, he would definitely not be weaker than himself.
Currently, he could only suppress Ye Tian in terms of combat rhythm, but killing Ye Tian would be very difficult.
Ye Tian realized that continuing the fight would not allow them to wipe out the Nether Hall's Jinhe Branch, so he suggested a temporary retreat.
Upon hearing this, the other third-class Invincibles immediately followed Ye Tian's suggestion and withdrew one by one.
Subsequently.
The many Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea returned to a temporary base established by the Tianfeng Sea in the Jinhe World to rest for a while.
......
Luo Ge entered the Jinhe Branch, and one Invincible after another came to pay their respects to this Son of the Void.
When Luo Ge heard the loss report from the Jinhe Branch, his expression turned quite ugly.
"A bunch of trash! To think so many Invincibles have fallen!"
Luo Ge cursed angrily.
On the other side.
After Ye Tian entered the base, he asked the other third-class Invincibles about the Son of the Void.
"Everyone, what is a Son of the Void?"
Ye Tian asked sincerely.
A third-class Invincible explained, "Lord Ye Tian, 'Son of the Void' is a title and also an honor. Basically, every Eternal-name level power or a few Saint-name level powers have Sons of the Void. Our Tianfeng Sea has a total of five Sons of the Void, while the Nether Hall has six. As for how to obtain the title of Son of the Void, we aren't very clear either. We only know that once a Son of the Void is born, all powers will recognize it."
After listening, Ye Tian became even more curious about the Son of the Void.
------------------
In the following years, the Tianfeng Sea used the Jinhe World as a battlefield to clash with the Nether Hall time and time again. Ye Tian and the Son of the Void Luo Ge also fought many times. Ye Tian remained at a disadvantage but could still barely contend with Luo Ge.
As long years passed, Invincibles fell one after another. Whether it was the Tianfeng Sea or the Nether Hall, the losses were relatively large.
However, such losses were still within the acceptable range for both sides. After all, the core combat power had not been lost. No matter how many fourth or fifth-class Invincibles, or even third-class Invincibles were lost, it would not shake their foundation.
However, on this day, the Nether Hall was enraged.
Tianlin Realm, Nether Hall Headquarters.
The Nether Hall Master's roar shook the heavens as he took a step across the void, directly seeking out the Tianfeng Sea.
Knowing that the Nether Hall Master was coming, the Tianfeng Supreme directly engaged in a fierce battle with him in the void of the Tianlin Realm.
"Tianfeng Source God, you are shameless! You actually killed a Son of the Void and thirteen first-class Invincibles of my Nether Hall!!!"
Source Ancestor is a title for a Supreme Being, representing that even within the Source Sea, they can be called gods.
The Nether Hall Master was incredibly furious at this moment, nearly losing his mind.
Thirteen first-class Invincibles and one Son of the Void had all fallen at once. This was practically shaking the foundation of the Nether Hall!
He had originally thought that the Tianfeng Sea's goal was to kill those third-class Invincibles and those below them, just small-scale skirmishes that wouldn't truly trigger a war between Eternal-name level powers.
Even if the Nether Hall lost some in the foreign void, it wouldn't matter.
But he never expected that the Tianfeng Sea's true target was the first-class Invincibles and a Son of the Void at the first-class Invincible level.
That was an important branch of the Nether Hall, located somewhere in the Source Sea.
The Tianfeng Source God had used a special treasure to condense a temporary avatar, disguising it as his true body to make him believe that the Tianfeng Source God's true body was in the Tianlin Realm.
However, at the critical moment, the Tianfeng Source God's true body appeared at that Nether Hall branch and killed the thirteen first-class Invincibles and the Son of the Void with extreme speed before quickly returning to the Tianlin Realm.
He didn't know how that branch had been exposed, but things had already reached this point; investigating the exposure was useless.
He now only wanted a life-and-death battle with the Tianfeng Source God; otherwise, he could not swallow this anger.
The Tianfeng Source God sneered, "Nether Hall Master, didn't you also assassinate one of our Tianfeng Sea's first-class Invincibles before? I am merely following your example."
"This is retaliation!"
The Nether Hall Master roared in rage.
The Tianfeng Source God admitted, "That's right, I am retaliating. So what?"
The commotion from their clash was too great, as if the entire Tianlin Realm was vibrating.
Just then, a man in white appeared in the void where they were fighting.
Seeing this person, the Tianfeng Source God and the Nether Hall Master immediately stopped.
"Greetings, Qiu Ye Source Ancestor!"
