Chapter 1632 Star Demon Source Ancestor, Fallen!

“Star Demon Source Ancestor, you have committed too many killings and slaughtered so many forces. You deserve to die!”
Absolute Ice Ancestor appeared.
She was a woman, wearing translucent battle armor, with grains of ice crystals floating around her. Those ice crystals were not real ice crystals, but Eternal source weapons—Infinite Ice Sand!
“Absolute Ice Ancestor, aren’t you afraid of the Eternal Being behind me? Moreover, you clearly had time to stop me just now, but you didn’t stop me. Instead, you set up a great array. I want to ask, what grudge is there between us?” Star Demon Source Ancestor questioned.
“You killed Lanyue!”
Absolute Ice Ancestor said coldly.
“Lanyue Source Ancestor, you have a relationship with her?”
Star Demon Source Ancestor was stunned.
How could Lanyue Source Ancestor be related to Absolute Ice Ancestor? Why didn’t Lanyue Source Ancestor say so back then?
No.
Even if Lanyue Source Ancestor had said so back then, he would not have believed it. He would only think that Lanyue Source Ancestor was deliberately saying that in order to save her life.
Moreover, in order to hide his identity, he was also impossible to listen to whatever Lanyue Source Ancestor said. He would not waste even a little time. Even if Lanyue Source Ancestor had said it, it would have been useless.
Precisely because of this, Lanyue Source Ancestor did not say it out.
“Lanyue is my junior. I never revealed it, and all my focus was on breaking through to the Eternal Realm, so I didn’t really manage Lanyue. Originally, I thought Lanyue’s strength was that of a powerful half-step Eternal Realm expert, and since she didn’t get involved in fights, there shouldn’t have been much danger in the source sea, so there was no need to rely on my influence. I never expected that you would actually kill her.”
Absolute Ice Ancestor was incomparably furious.
She had only been in seclusion for a little longer, and then she suddenly sensed that her junior Lanyue had fallen and died, which was what woke her up and made her come out of seclusion.
After coming out, she learned that Star Demon Source Ancestor had killed Lanyue.
In order not to kill the wrong person, she did not act immediately, but instead kept watch over Star Demon Mountain.
In the end, Star Demon Source Ancestor attacked again, and she confirmed the target, but she was too lazy to save the Ancient God Palace. Instead, she chose to directly set up a killing array in this area of the Ancient God Palace.
This time, she would definitely kill Star Demon Source Ancestor.
“Kill!”
Infinite Ice Sand activated, and the spacetime of this region directly shattered. Those half-step Eternal Realm experts could finally no longer hold on either. One by one, their bodies shattered and died, leaving only Star Demon Source Ancestor alone.
Star Demon Source Ancestor also had no time to feel sorry for the fall of these half-step Eternal Realm experts. If he could not escape today, he would also have to die.
Boom!!!
Star Demon Source Ancestor’s defenses collapsed inch by inch.
And Infinite Ice Sand transformed into a sword, piercing in.
No matter how Star Demon Source Ancestor defended, he was still pierced through by a single strike, and the injured area even showed signs of freezing. Even though his recovery ability was terrifying, the injured area was still shattering at a frequency of hundreds of millions of times per breath.
“Run!”
Star Demon Source Ancestor did not want to fight Absolute Ice Ancestor.
The other side was a half-step Eternal Realm expert at the Seven Extreme Stage, while he was only at the Three Calamities Extreme Stage; there was a huge gap in strength between them.
Moreover, as one of the six extreme experts of the source sea, she had many trump cards. He had not a shred of hope of winning.
Suddenly.
Star Demon Source Ancestor activated the Time-Space Grain Sand Pearl, shrinking the suppression range, wanting to suppress Absolute Ice Ancestor and thereby create a chance for himself to escape.
However, after all, the Time-Space Grain Sand Pearl was only the lowest-grade Eternal source weapon.
In just one breath, the Time-Space Grain Sand Pearl was forcibly shattered by Absolute Ice Ancestor.
In the source sea.
Star Demon Source Ancestor kept fleeing, while Absolute Ice Ancestor relentlessly pursued and killed him.
At this point, all the countless forces in the source sea knew that Star Demon Source Ancestor was being hunted, and each of them was extremely shocked.
……
“Star Demon Source Ancestor is being hunted by Absolute Ice Ancestor?”
Ye Tian was truly surprised upon learning this news.
He had thought that no half-step Eternal Realm expert would go and kill Star Demon Source Ancestor, but he hadn’t expected a strong expert to act so quickly.
As for Absolute Ice Ancestor, Ye Tian knew very little. He only knew that she was one of the six extreme experts of the source sea.
These six extreme experts were basically all half-step Eternal Realm experts at the Seven Extreme Stage. A few might have reached the Eight Extreme Stage, but there were no half-step Eternal Realm experts at the Nine Extreme Stage.
These six extreme experts were all trying to break through to the Eternal Realm.
However, strength had no necessary connection with breaking through to the Eternal Realm. Some weaker half-step Eternal Realm experts instead managed to break through, while half-step Eternal Realm experts like Absolute Ice Ancestor found it even harder to do so.
But once such a half-step Eternal Realm expert did break through, then after becoming an Eternal Realm expert, their strength would also be incomparably terrifying.
Time slowly passed, and many half-step Eternal Realm experts in the source sea were all paying attention to Star Demon Source Ancestor’s fate.
Although Star Demon Source Ancestor had an Eternal Being behind him, whether that Eternal Being would come or not was still uncertain.
Moreover, they were almost unable to contact Eternal Beings, and Star Demon Source Ancestor was probably the same.
Perhaps, even if Star Demon Source Ancestor were killed, that Eternal Being still would not know.
However, Star Demon Source Ancestor was after all a Three Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm expert. For a Seven Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm expert to kill a Three Extreme Stage half-step Eternal Realm expert still required some time; it was not something that could be instantly done with a single move.
……
In a spacetime ruin in the source sea.
Star Demon Source Ancestor’s entire body had been crystallized with ice, and his injuries were already extremely severe.
After all, this place was covered and completely sealed by Infinite Ice Sand.
Star Demon Source Ancestor had already used up many of his treasures, and coupled with his severe injuries, he simply could not break out and leave.
“Absolute Ice Ancestor, do you really want to kill me? If you kill me, my disciple, the Eternal Being Linghe Lord, will not let you go. You are already a Seven Extreme Stage expert, with an unlimited future. There’s no need to be killed by an Eternal Being because of this.”
Star Demon Source Ancestor tried to persuade her.
“Even if I am hunted by an Eternal Being, I will still kill you!”
Absolute Ice Ancestor said angrily.
And all around, the gazes of one half-step Eternal Realm expert after another were being cast over, as if they were watching the show.
And Star Demon Source Ancestor knew he could not persuade Absolute Ice Ancestor, and also feared that he would not be able to wait for Linghe Lord to arrive in time, so he could only ask for help from the surrounding onlooking half-step Eternal Realm experts.
“Whoever saves me will be able to obtain the friendship of Linghe Lord, and even receive Linghe Lord’s guidance!” Star Demon Source Ancestor’s voice spread across the surrounding region.
However, no one helped.
Who would dare offend Absolute Ice Ancestor? Are they courting death?
Unless it was the other five of the six extreme experts, but experts of that level were simply disdainful of Linghe Lord’s guidance, and Linghe Lord also simply could not provide guidance to them.
Soon.
Star Demon Source Ancestor’s body was pierced through again and again by Infinite Ice Sand, and the speed at which his body crystallized with ice became faster and faster. Moreover, the heavily injured Star Demon Source Ancestor simply could no longer recover from his injuries.
After a long time, Star Demon Source Ancestor finally could no longer resist, and his body was completely crystallized.
Then, after a crisp crack, Star Demon Source Ancestor completely shattered into countless powder, and his vitality as well as consciousness completely dissipated.
At this point, Star Demon Source Ancestor had fallen and died.
And at this moment, a terrifying aura transmitted over.
An Eternal Being had arrived!.
