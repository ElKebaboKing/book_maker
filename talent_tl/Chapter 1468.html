Chapter 1467: The Copy Body Has Become an Ancestor!
!
Whether one is a Supreme-level Soul Ancestor or a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, the method for breaking through to become an Ancestor is largely the same: one must use an Ancestor Law to achieve the breakthrough.
An Ancestor Law is... the ultimate of the Dao, the origin of the Dao, and the incarnation of the Dao.
To break through using an Ancestor Law is to... transform oneself into the Dao.
The Space Ancestor Law involves transforming the body into a Space Origin Ancestor Body, shattering the limits of a Supreme-level Soul Body or a Supreme-level Domain Body to achieve an Ancestor physique.
The Ancestor physique achieved by a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor is a physical body, while the Ancestor physique achieved by a Supreme-level Soul Ancestor is a soul body.
In reality, however, they are much the same. Once an Ancestor Body is achieved, the soul is the body and the body is the soul; both are incomparably powerful in every aspect.
That said, the accumulation of a peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor or Soul Ancestor at the Supreme level varies. Once they break through, their strength will naturally differ as well.
Ye Tian’s copy body had accumulated enough; although it was not as powerful as his main body, it far surpassed other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
Therefore, once Ye Tian became an Ancestor, his strength would inevitably surpass that of ordinary Ancestors.
....Boom!!!! Ye Tian’s body began to reshape itself according to the profound mysteries of the Space Ancestor Law, evolving toward a Space Ancestor physique.
Having long since mastered the Space Ancestor Law, Ye Tian could naturally condense a Space Ancestor physique with ease.
In the midst of this, the origin power of the Dark Forbidden Domain poured into Ye Tian’s body.
The Space Ancestor physique Ye Tian condensed was different from that of other Ancestors; thus, the Dao he practiced did not conflict with others.
A single Dao only allows one person to become an Ancestor. Of course, the Space Ancestor Dao is divided into many types, but if one becomes an Ancestor using the exact same Ancestor Law, it counts as the same Ancestor Dao. During a breakthrough, repulsion occurs, and one's own Ancestor Dao would be shattered by the opponent's Ancestor Dao.
Therefore, once he broke through to the Ancestor realm, others who practiced this specific Space Ancestor Law in the future would be unable to become Ancestors, though they could still merge with a fragmented Ancestor Dao to become Half-step Ancestors.
Endless energy surged into Ye Tian’s copy body, helping him achieve his Ancestor physique.
Outside the Dark Soul Domain.
One Ancestor after another looked toward the Dark Soul Domain, their faces filled with shock.
"Someone is charging for the Ancestor realm? How can it be so fast?"
The Ancestors exclaimed in shock.
They had speculated that some peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors would attempt to break through to the Ancestor realm. Once prepared, there was hope of becoming an Ancestor within the Dark Soul Domain. Perhaps due to the nature of the Domain Ancestor Dao, they might not be able to take control of the Dark Soul Domain, but once an Ancestor was born, they could influence the domain's direction.
If a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor from the Dark Destruction Domain became an Ancestor, they could use their Ancestor lineage to continuously influence the Dark Soul Domain, creating countless destructive races to destroy regions of the Dark Soul Domain and establish a second Destruction Domain.
If an Ancestor was born from the Dark Holy Domain, they would tear into the Domain Ancestor Dao within the Dark Soul Domain, allowing the Domain Ancestors to cultivate faster.
But logically, peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors shouldn't be able to promote to the Ancestor realm so quickly; the suppression of a Dark Holy Land was no joke.
"Wait, it’s not a Supreme Domain Ancestor charging for the Ancestor realm—it’s a Supreme-level Soul Ancestor!!!!"
The Ancestors realized.
Soon, Supreme-level Domain Ancestors went to investigate, and before long, they locked onto the target.
Before the Destruction Ancestor, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor emerged from the domain and reported: "Lord Destruction Ancestor, the person charging for the Ancestor realm is the Human Race's Green Leaf Soul Ancestor!"
"The Human Race, Green Leaf Soul Ancestor? That human genius who entered the Soul Domain? In such a short time, he transformed from a Half-step Domain Ancestor to a Half-step Soul Ancestor, and now he’s charging for the Ancestor realm? How is this possible?"
Even the Destruction Ancestor was shocked, his expression changing.
In a period as short as a trillion years, someone had risen from the beginning of cultivation to the Ancestor realm? Even he couldn't have done it back then, and the Holy Ancestor of the Dark Holy Domain couldn't have done it either.
The Holy Ancestor of the Dark Holy Domain broke through to the Ancestor realm in the 28th Realm Year, and that was with no one obstructing him and the providence of the Dark Holy Domain concentrated upon him.
A mere trillion years was nothing more than a fraction of a single Realm Year.
To do it in such a short time, and as a genius of the Human Race rather than a disciple under the Holy Ancestor, was simply unbelievable to him.
On the side of the Dark Holy Domain, the Holy Ancestor and a group of other Ancestors were also stunned.
The War Ancestor and Realm Ancestor of the War Gate were also completely bewildered. How did the Human Race suddenly produce such a monstrous existence? Setting aside combat power and looking only at the achievement of becoming an Ancestor, he was even more monstrous than Ye Tian! At the very least, Ye Tian had cultivated for so many long years and had yet to become an Ancestor.
...."Myriad Law War Ancestor, what exactly is going on with that Green Leaf Soul Ancestor of your Human Race? How did he suddenly charge into the Ancestor realm?"
The War Ancestor asked one of Ye Tian’s avatars.
When Supreme-level Domain Ancestors entered the Dark Soul Domain, they would leave an avatar behind to report on the situation inside at any time.
"He probably encountered a fortuitous opportunity!"
Ye Tian replied.
He couldn't say that it was his own copy body; otherwise, if other Ancestors specifically targeted his main body, it would be disastrous! Once the main body perished, it was a true death!
At this moment, the War Ancestor was thoroughly envious. The Human Race had already produced a genius as monstrous as Ye Tian, who was almost certain to become an Ancestor in the future. He hadn't expected them to produce an even more monstrous Green Leaf Soul Ancestor—who would be called the Green Leaf Ancestor in the future.
Crucially, once the Green Leaf Ancestor successfully broke through, he could take control of a portion of the Dark Soul Domain! In that case, the Green Leaf Ancestor would be truly invincible within the Dark Soul Domain!
As time passed.
Ye Tian’s transformation grew faster and faster. Some tried to interrupt Ye Tian’s breakthrough, but Ye Tian’s main body was nearby. Whenever a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor made a move, they were all suppressed and killed by Ye Tian.
Even those ancient peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were no match for Ye Tian’s main body.
Although Ye Tian’s main body was not yet a match for an Ancestor, even a Half-step Ancestor comparable to the Six-Mark Silence Clan was no match for him.
A million years finally passed.
On this day, Ye Tian’s copy body finally fully condensed its Ancestor physique, stepping into the Ancestor level and becoming a Space Ancestor.
Outside the Dark Soul Domain, at the War Gate station.
Ye Tian’s avatar was discussing a matter with the War Ancestor and the Realm Ancestor.
"War Ancestor, Realm Ancestor, there are currently two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors in the Dark Soul Domain attempting to break through to the Ancestor realm. One is an Ancestor from the Dark Destruction Domain, and the other is a Domain Ancestor from the Myriad Things Holy Sect of the Dark Holy Domain. Should I move to kill them?"
"Kill them!"
The Realm Ancestor nodded.
Although they were in an alliance before, now that the Silence Clan had been wiped out, everyone was an enemy.
Therefore, the War Gate naturally did not want other forces to produce an Ancestor.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor, your Human Race's Green Leaf Ancestor can only act once. This is because Ancestors are not permitted to act against Supreme-level Domain Ancestors; this is a tacit rule.
The Green Leaf Ancestor has just broken through and does not know this rule, so he can act, and the other Ancestors won't be able to say anything.
But once he is informed, he can no longer act.
Therefore, the Green Leaf Ancestor must act immediately."
The War Ancestor reminded him.
"Understood!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Inside the Dark Soul Domain, Ye Tian’s copy body, through his control over the domain, sensed the locations of the two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors who were attempting to break through.
In the next instant.
With a single thought, Ye Tian arrived beside the two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and reached out with a hand.
No matter how they resisted, it was in vain.
Bang!!! The two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were easily wiped out by Ye Tian, annihilated without any power to resist.
