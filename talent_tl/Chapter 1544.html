Chapter 1543: Multiple Eternal-level Talents!

In the Tianfeng Sea, there were some Invincibles who possessed Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents. Once Ye Tian detected them with his copying talent, he immediately copied the Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents of these Invincibles.
Of course, if there were other Ultimate Ancestral Talents, he would copy them all together as well.
Afterward, Ye Tian left the Tianfeng Sea headquarters at Wufeng Mountain and wandered through various regions of the Tianlin Realm. Any region where cultivation flourished was visited by him.
Countless giant cities similar to Juwu City, various trading markets, and gathering points—Ye Tian constantly traveled to places where Invincibles gathered. Although the harvest each time was small and it was nearly impossible to find practitioners with the Ultimate Power Ancestral Talent,
encountering one occasionally was already very good.
As time slowly passed, the Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents Ye Tian copied grew in number, and other types of Ultimate Ancestral Talents became quite numerous as well.
Finally, after the span of one Domain Era, Ye Tian had copied enough Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents and other Ultimate Ancestral Talents to begin fusing them.
Fusing Ultimate Ancestral Talents was extremely painful and the speed was very slow, somewhat like when he had first obtained the copying talent.
But Ye Tian was very excited, because with every additional Eternal talent, his potential and foundation received a massive boost.
For example, when he previously obtained the Eternal Talent "Calamity" from the Calamity Beast, he had instantly surpassed other geniuses by a wide margin. Even among Invincibles, he was considered an extraordinary monster.
If he didn't have an Eternal talent, he wouldn't have much of an advantage after breaking through to the Invincible level.
One could imagine what an Eternal talent meant to an Invincible; it was a symbol of invincibility.
In the vast Tianlin Realm, he had yet to see a single practitioner who possessed an Eternal talent. Perhaps there were none, or they were so rare that there were only a handful, and they weren't people he could easily meet.
Regardless, Ye Tian was about to possess multiple Eternal talents.
After a long period of time, Ye Tian fused a large number of Ultimate Power Ancestral Talents, finally ushering in the ultimate qualitative change.
BOOM!!!!
The Eternal Power Talent, or rather, the Eternal Ancestral Talent: Power, was born!
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Power]
Seeing this talent, Ye Tian was incredibly excited.
An Eternal Ancestral-level talent was very close to the limit of the copying talent. At the Invincible level, a talent of this caliber was of immense help to him. It was an Eternal-level talent, and since the Eternal level was even linked to the Eternal Heaven and Earth, it might very well be the highest grade of talent.
Subsequently, Ye Tian activated this Eternal Power Talent, and his overall strength surged violently, comparable to the effect of performing a supreme ancestral technique. And this was just the effect of activating the talent itself.
"With the Eternal Ancestral Talent: Power, I should be able to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique very soon!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
He did not immediately head to the Tianfeng Sea to meditate on the Nine Yang Fish. Instead, he continued to fuse other Ultimate Ancestral Talents. After all, he had copied so many this time, including Ultimate Defense Ancestral Talents, Ultimate Speed Ancestral Talents, and Ultimate Five Elements Ancestral Talents.
Therefore, he could still obtain several more Eternal-level talents.
Fuse!
Fuse!
Fuse!
Ye Tian continued to fuse. Although he had no time to cultivate and spent it all on fusion, he was extremely happy with every successful fusion.
After a long time had passed, one Eternal-level talent after another was born.
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Speed]
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Defense]
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Gold]
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Fire]
...
With the birth of each Eternal-level talent, Ye Tian truly transformed into a supreme genius who stood above any other genius in the Tianlin Realm.
Of course, if he wanted to fully grow into his potential, he still needed a very long time.
After all, talent was just talent; it didn't represent his current realm or strength, only his potential.
One day.
Ye Tian once again arrived at the Nine Yang Fish cultivation and meditation space on Nine Yang Mountain in Wufeng Mountain, Tianfeng Sea.
Here, Ye Tian sat cross-legged on a lotus platform, once again meditating on the Eternal Power Rules on the body of the Nine Yang Fish.
In the past, meditating on the Eternal Power Rules felt as if there were an endless barrier and a rejection from the rules themselves. It wasn't just him; everyone else felt the same.
But now, Ye Tian possessed the Eternal Ancestral Talent: Power. The Eternal Power Rules no longer rejected him; instead, they were perfectly compatible with each other, allowing him to meditate on them freely.
Furthermore, the Eternal Ancestral Talent: Power gave Ye Tian an incredible boost in his comprehension of the Eternal Power Rules. In a short amount of time, Ye Tian's gains surpassed what he had achieved over a long period in the past.
"Such a fast comprehension speed! At this rate, I'm afraid it won't be long before I can grasp the Eternal Power Rules and attempt to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique!"
·········Seeking Flowers·········
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, attempting to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique immediately after grasping the Eternal Rules was not necessarily a good thing. If the created technique was too weak, the boost in strength wouldn't be very significant.
Therefore, many geniuses would choose to spend more time meditating to grasp more of the Eternal Power Rules. That way, the Invincible technique they created would be incredibly powerful.
Invincible techniques created by ordinary Invincibles were classified as Common Invincible Techniques. Naturally, those above Common Invincible Techniques were Top-tier Invincible Techniques. There were only these two classifications.
What Ye Tian wanted to create was a Top-tier Invincible Technique; that was his pursuit.
... ........ 0
Time slowly passed, and in the blink of an eye, one Domain Year had gone by.
Such a short amount of time for other Invincibles was probably just the length of a nap. If it were before, Ye Tian definitely wouldn't have made much progress.
However, now, he actually—
"I've grasped it!!!!"
Ye Tian was also somewhat surprised, even shocked.
One Domain Year! How much time had passed? He had only spent a tiny bit of contribution points, and he had actually grasped the Eternal Power Rules!
Other Invincibles who meditated on the Nine Yang Fish would probably need tens of millions of contribution points to have a hope of grasping the Eternal Rules!
This comprehension speed had simply increased by countless times!
Having grasped a trace of the Eternal Power Rules, Ye Tian did not leave, nor did he attempt to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique. Instead, he continued his meditation.
He still had over 200,000 contribution points, so he was in no hurry. He planned to squander all 200,000 points here. This way, his mastery of the Eternal Power Rules would be very high, and when he went to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique, it would surely be the most top-tier one.
Time flew by, and in the blink of an eye, one Domain Era passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had consumed 120,000 contribution points, and his control over the Eternal Power Rules was many times greater than before—the difference was like a drop of water compared to a river.
"This should be enough. My realm is too low; continuing to meditate on the Eternal Power Rules will become quite difficult. It's time to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique and promote myself to the Fifth-tier Invincible Ancestral Realm!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
