“True River Sword Ancestor, have you all challenged the Wuhen Tower?”
Ye Tian asked curiously.
“We already did!” True River Sword Ancestor said. “I only made it through the fifth level, the same as last time, so there wasn’t much reward. We’re waiting for Qingxu Source Ancestor. Last time he only made it through the fourth level, but this time he broke through to the Extreme, so he should be able to pass the fifth level. The reward for the fifth level is still quite good.”
Ye Tian did not wait for Qingxu Source Ancestor. He directly went to challenge the first level of the Wuhen Tower.
The first level of the Wuhen Tower was very easy to pass. As long as one’s strength among the Ninefold Supreme Beings was at the top, there was a certain chance of passing the first level.
Inside the first level.
A spatiotemporal dragon python actually appeared.
“It’s not a real spatiotemporal dragon python, it’s a spatiotemporal dragon python condensed by a formation!”
Ye Tian recognized it at once.
Boom!
Ye Tian threw a punch and directly blasted the spatiotemporal dragon python apart.
Just like that, the first level was passed!
After passing the first level, Ye Tian obtained a reward.
“Dragon Whisk Sacred Stone!”
This reward was not cheap. After refining the Dragon Whisk Sacred Stone, it could be worn on the body to temper the physique at all times. It was of little help to Ninefold Supreme Beings, but for those Supreme Beings with lower cultivation, the value of the Dragon Whisk Sacred Stone was precious indeed.
Originally, Ye Tian would not have recognized the Dragon Whisk Sacred Stone, but after becoming the thirteenth ancestor of the Source God Pavilion, he naturally obtained a list of treasures from the twelfth ancestor, which included treasures born within the Time Domain. And there was no Dragon Whisk Sacred Stone in the Source Sea; it was born in the Time Domain.
“The Dragon Whisk Sacred Stone isn’t of much help to me, but I can give it to Yue’er!” Ye Tian thought.
After that, Ye Tian went to challenge the second level of the Wuhen Tower, and the result was still an easy pass.
The requirement corresponding to the second level was half-step Eternal Realm. If one had only the strength of an ordinary half-step Eternal Realm, they could not even enter the second level.
The third level corresponded to the strength of a powerful half-step Eternal Realm.
The fourth level corresponded to the strength of a top-tier half-step Eternal Realm.
The fifth level required the strength of the Extreme.
Ye Tian challenged the Wuhen Tower level by level and quickly passed the fourth level, and the reward he obtained was not low. In particular, the reward after passing the fourth level was actually a Void God Leaf.
The Void God Leaf was a lifesaving treasure. It could instantly pierce through void and escape with one’s life. It could also conceal aura and block any probing power. Even a Ninefold Supreme Being, once using the Void God Leaf, could escape pursuit from an Extreme level powerhouse.
Of course, for Eternal Realm beings, the Void God Leaf was of no use.
But even so, in the Source Sea, the Void God Leaf was enough to rank among the top ten lifesaving treasures. It could not be traded at all, because it could only be used at most three times. Thus it was too rare and too scarce; no one would be foolish enough to take it out for trade.
After passing the fourth level, Ye Tian actually obtained the Void God Leaf, and his luck was definitely not bad.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian arrived at the fifth level of the Wuhen Tower.
The fifth level required Extreme strength to pass. Ye Tian had not brought an Eternal Realm finger into the Wuhen Domain, so originally he would have been unable to pass here.
But he had first gone to Donghui Mountain and increased his strength.
Now, his strength had surged, and he was almost at the Extreme. Coupled with the lifesaving ability of the Endless Eternal Body, he should have some hope of passing the fifth level.
Even True River Sword Ancestor felt that the reward for the fifth level was extremely precious, so it was certainly very good. Thus, of course he wanted to pass the fifth level.
Very soon, the fifth level was reached!
Boom!!!!!
A spatiotemporal dragon python comparable to a First Extreme appeared.
Perhaps its true combat strength was a bit weaker than a First Extreme, but it was definitely not much weaker.
For a half-step Eternal Realm powerhouse at the First Extreme level, passing the fifth level would probably require a very long time, perhaps even a protracted battle lasting ages.
Ye Tian knew very clearly that he could not kill this spatiotemporal dragon python in a short time at all.
“Then I’ll wear it down to death!”
Ye Tian decided.
Rumble!!!!!
The battle between Ye Tian and this spatiotemporal dragon python began.
As soon as they exchanged blows, Ye Tian was slightly at a disadvantage, but he barely held on and did not suffer any injuries.
Time slowly passed. Relying on the Endless Eternal Body, Ye Tian forcibly dragged out the battle, while from time to time he would land a blow on the spatiotemporal dragon python, inflicting some injuries upon it.
And although the spatiotemporal dragon python could also injure Ye Tian, Ye Tian’s recovery ability was too terrifying, and he recovered almost instantly.
Time slowly passed. Although Ye Tian’s consumption was great, he also absorbed resources from his inner world to recover energy. This did not count as cheating, and the Wuhen Tower would not care.
No one knew how much time had passed before Ye Tian finally wore this spatiotemporal dragon python down to death and passed the fifth level.
By this time, the injuries on this clone body of Ye Tian were also relatively light. After a while, all of the injuries recovered.
This time, Ye Tian seemed to have passed the fifth level without paying much of a price.
But he knew very clearly that if he had not improved his cultivation and strengthened his power, he probably would not even have been able to injure the spatiotemporal dragon python. If he could not injure it, then no matter how long the battle dragged on, he still would not be able to kill it.

Obviously, the decision he made earlier was very correct.
After passing the fifth level, the reward from the Wuhen Tower also appeared.
Whoosh!
A treasure appeared before him.
It was a legacy orb!
“Inheritance!”
Ye Tian was somewhat looking forward to it. What exactly was the inheritance from an Eternal Realm lord?
If it were an ordinary inheritance, he would not be interested, but what if it could help him?
Ye Tian directly accepted the inheritance. Very soon, the inheritance ended, and the legacy orb also shattered directly. Clearly, it was a one-time legacy orb. If it were a legacy orb that could inherit continuously, then its value would be enough for a force to rise completely.
But the more advanced the inheritance, the more troublesome it was to create, and it could even not be tolerated by heaven and earth, so naturally it could not be inherited too many times. This was a limitation.
.... ....... .......
“World of Will!”
This was an inherited secret art, a secret art that helped increase willpower.
“Looks like the Wuhen Lord had a deep study of willpower. Not only does his domain have the Rift Wind Valley that tempers willpower, he also created this World of Will secret art!”
Ye Tian thought.
But after reading this World of Will secret art, he felt that the Wuhen Lord was a madman.
Willpower transformed into a world, forming countless billions upon countless billions of worlds of will throughout the body. When willpower becomes strong enough, it can even allow the body to erupt with explosive power and combat strength that surpasses the limit.
Even more, willpower can affect innate Eternal rules.
Once the World of Will reaches the ninth level, willpower can directly shatter innate Eternal rules and shatter the fleshly body of an Eternal Realm being.
But this was an incomplete secret art, and every step of cultivation was extremely dangerous.
More importantly, willpower was far too difficult to increase. It was almost impossible to reach the ninth level of the World of Will; this was a secret art conceived by the Wuhen Lord.
Moreover, ordinary avatars simply could not cultivate the World of Will, because an avatar is not the true body. It does not possess some of the abilities the true body has, nor can it bear the World of Will.
But who would dare use the true body for experimentation?
“Could it be that the Wuhen Lord had many people cultivate the World of Will, using them as experiments, causing many deaths, and even capturing many geniuses, ultimately enraging some powerful Eternal Realm being, and finally being killed.”
Ye Tian guessed.
Then he smiled.
This secret art was very suitable for him!
His clone body was no different from his true body, so he could use the clone body to cultivate the World of Will. Even if it collapsed, nothing would happen.
