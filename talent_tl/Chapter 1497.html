Chapter 1496: The Way to Break the Shackles
!
The duplicate beast body passed through the gate, pretending to seek reinforcements, while Ye Tian's original body quickly left the Haitian Forbidden Land.
Although he felt that the Haitian Sacred Sect was not particularly powerful, he had to be careful in case the intelligence was incorrect. Therefore, he had to remain cautious.
....Haitian Sacred Sect, Base.
When Ye Tian's duplicate body passed through the gate, appearing heavily injured and on the verge of death, it was immediately discovered by the Haitian Sacred Sect beasts guarding the other side.
"An Ancestor has invaded!"
Ye Tian immediately called out.
"What happened?"
One powerful beast after another appeared, questioning Ye Tian for information.
At the same time, some beasts gave Ye Tian special healing substances and life-saving items to consume, preventing him from falling.
Ye Tian reported the situation truthfully, saying that a powerful Ancestor had invaded, one whose strength was comparable to a Supreme Ancestor. They were simply no match, and almost everyone had been blasted to death.
He had paid a massive price to escape back.
Soon, a beast comparable to a Supreme Ancestor appeared and personally headed over.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian returned to the Haitian Sacred Sect to recover. The sect did not suspect him at all; even this world recognized him and did not attempt to erase him.
Furthermore, since all the beasts within the Haitian Forbidden Land had perished, it was almost impossible for him to be discovered.
It could be said that Ye Tian's plan was a success.
Haitian Sacred Sect.
Ye Tian had been back for some time. He even had several servants; after all, as a powerful beast comparable to a Top Ancestor, his status in the Haitian Sacred Sect was naturally high.
During this time, he used his servants to inquire about recent events. Since he had been guarding the Haitian Forbidden Land and was unfamiliar with the situation here, it was perfectly normal to ask.
Through these inquiries and the records of the Haitian Sacred Sect, he gradually came to understand this sacred land.
This world was not dominated by the Haitian Sacred Sect alone; there were three major Sacred Sects, of which the Haitian Sacred Sect was the weakest.
Beasts comparable to Ancestors were called Sacred Beasts here, and they were divided into Ordinary Sacred Beasts, Elite Sacred Beasts, Top Sacred Beasts, and Supreme Sacred Beasts.
The Haitian Sacred Sect possessed two Supreme Sacred Beasts, while the entire world possessed ten. It was naturally much stronger than the Dark Forbidden Domain.
There were even more Top Sacred Beasts; the Haitian Sacred Sect alone had over seventy.
In total, the Haitian Sacred Sect had several thousand Sacred Beasts, which was equivalent to several thousand Ancestors.
From this, it was clear how powerful the Haitian Sacred Sect was; even ten Dark Forbidden Domains combined could not contend with a single Haitian Sacred Sect.
And the Haitian Sacred Sect was still one of the relatively weaker forces.
Ye Tian had now infiltrated the world of the Haitian Sacred Sect, which they referred to as the Sacred Domain.
The Sacred Domain was infinitely vast. Aside from Sacred Beasts, ordinary beings could not cross the entire domain, a situation similar to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian currently had two goals.
The first goal was to seek a breakthrough and step into the Supreme Ancestor realm.
The second goal was to obtain the method for constructing a Gate. Although the Haitian Sacred Sect was weak, it controlled a Gate, and he wanted to master the construction method so that a Gate could exist eternally.
"I'll complete the first goal first. Once that's done, it will be easier to achieve the second!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
After all, the Gate was the most important asset of the Haitian Sacred Sect. The construction method was bound to be in the hands of those two Supreme Sacred Beasts and would be very difficult to obtain.
Ye Tian currently only knew that by entering another world, one could break the shackles of a Top Ancestor and promote to the Supreme Ancestor realm.
But as for the specific method of breaking through, Ye Tian was still unclear.
Logically, the Top Sacred Beasts of the Haitian Sacred Sect should know.
However, Ye Tian was a fake Top Sacred Beast and did not possess the memories of that beast.
He didn't rush to learn this information at first, but instead focused on healing while gathering general knowledge.
During this period, various Sacred Beasts came to visit and offer their regards.
Of course, since he had nearly lost the Haitian Forbidden Land, the punishment from the Sacred Sect arrived as well, deducting several Sacred Beast Years' worth of his stipend.
A Sacred Beast Year was equivalent to a Dark Era, so this was considered a relatively heavy punishment.
After several hundred million years, another group of Sacred Beasts was stationed in the Haitian Forbidden Land. To prevent its loss, three of the highest-ranking Top Sacred Beasts of the Haitian Sacred Sect were sent to guard it, and one Supreme Sacred Beast went into secluded cultivation right at the edge of the Gate. If anything happened, they could descend upon the Haitian Forbidden Land immediately.
This showed how much importance they placed on the Gate and the Forbidden Land.
As time passed, Ye Tian gradually became familiar with some of the Sacred Beasts of the Haitian Sacred Sect, ensuring he wouldn't make any mistakes.
With increasing familiarity, Ye Tian discovered that the Sacred Domain was somewhat similar to the Dark Forbidden Domain; at the very least, the creatures here possessed various talents, and the Sacred Beasts possessed Sacred Talents.
However, this duplicate body did not contain the Copy Talent, so he couldn't check the talents of other Sacred Beasts. But from the Sacred Beasts in the Haitian Forbidden Land, Ye Tian had seen a uniform array of Sacred Talents, and one even possessed an Ancestral Talent—the very body Ye Tian had copied.
However, these talents were of no use to Ye Tian.
After becoming acquainted with the Sacred Beasts of the Haitian Sacred Sect, Ye Tian, using the identity of the Sacred Beast named He Yuan, frequently interacted with others, even displaying a longing for the Supreme Sacred Beast rank.
Through various means, he discussed topics like Supreme Sacred Beasts, the Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain, and breakthroughs with other Top Sacred Beasts. Gradually, he uncovered the secrets of the breakthrough.
Just as Ye Tian had guessed, all Top Sacred Beasts knew how to charge toward the Supreme Sacred Beast rank.
Thus, he finally obtained this secret.
"I didn't expect it to be so difficult for a Top Sacred Beast to break through to a Supreme Sacred Beast!"
Ye Tian murmured.
According to what he learned, for a Top Sacred Beast to break through to a Supreme Sacred Beast, they must enter another world during a Great Calamity and comprehend the rules of that world. Once their comprehension reaches a certain level, they must create a "Sacred Beast Body" technique. Once this Sacred Beast Body reaches perfection, the stimulation between the two different Sacred Beast Bodies allows them to break the shackles and promote to a Supreme Sacred Beast. The two Sacred Beast Bodies then fuse into one, becoming a Supreme Sacred Beast Body.
Creating a Sacred Beast Body was difficult enough, but to cultivate it to the perfection stage required an immense amount of resources—specifically, resources from the other world. This necessitated plundering countless resources to elevate oneself.
Furthermore, the act of charging toward the Supreme Sacred Beast rank itself required a sea of resources.
Because it was so difficult, the Haitian Sacred Sect only had two Supreme Sacred Beasts to this day.
"So that's it. For a Top Ancestor to charge toward the Supreme Ancestor rank, they must comprehend the rules of another world, create an Ancestor Law that fits that world, condense an Ancestor Body, and then cultivate that Ancestor Body to the peak of the Top Ancestor realm. Only then can they break the shackles, fuse the two Ancestor Bodies, and step into the Supreme Ancestor realm!"
Ye Tian realized.
For other Ancestors, completing such a breakthrough would be incredibly difficult.
But for him, it didn't seem difficult at all!
