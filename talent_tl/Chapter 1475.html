While Ye Tian's main body was in seclusion creating the Endless Ancestor Method, the progress of his clone, Qingye Ancestor, began to slow down.
After all, the Qingye Ancestor clone was raised by Ye Tian for the sake of speed; it couldn't compare to the foundation of his main body. Its potential was far inferior. Furthermore, the Qingye Ancestor clone had integrated the Space Ancestor Method of the Void Ancestor.
Back then, to pursue a rapid promotion to Ancestor, he naturally couldn't consider too much, nor did he have the time to slowly create an Ancestor Method. Otherwise, if someone else had been the first to promote to Ancestor, they might have targeted the Human Race or even his main body.
If that had happened, the Human Race wouldn't have its current prosperity.
Since he didn't promote through a self-created Ancestor Method, Qingye Ancestor's potential was limited and was now nearing its limit. Unless he obtained a better opportunity, he couldn't break through that limit.
And those Nirvana treasures could only increase cultivation; they couldn't help the Qingye Ancestor clone break its limits.
"The Qingye Ancestor clone is currently estimated to only reach the level of a powerful Elite Ancestor, second only to a Top Ancestor. Only by relying on the Dark Soul Domain can it contend with a Top Ancestor.
In the future, no matter how many Nirvana treasures are refined, the increase in strength will be limited!"
Ye Tian muttered.
Of course, the Qingye Ancestor clone could still progress, just many times slower than before, so he looked down on it a bit.
If other Ancestors knew of Ye Tian's progress speed, they would likely be incredibly jealous.
The progress speed Ye Tian looked down upon was something even Top Ancestors like the Saint Ancestor and Destruction Ancestor would envy.
....Domain year after domain year passed.
While the eyes of many Ancestors and factions were focused on the Dark Soul Domain, almost no one cared about a place like the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Even if something happened here, no one would care.
One of Ye Tian's incarnations had been stationed in the Xuanxu Holy Realm, monitoring its changes at all times.
By now, the Xuanxu Holy Realm was many times larger than in the past. It had even birthed many rare resources and holy items, as well as treasures that only appeared in Ancestral Realms.
Additionally, the Celestial Realm within the Xuanxu Holy Realm had produced a large number of treasures. The Star Forbidden Tree had once again borne Star Forbidden Fruits, enough for many Invincible Domain Ancestors of the Human Race to temper their Invincible Domain Bodies to the extreme.
Although compared to the Dark Soul Domain, the resources of the Xuanxu Holy Realm were low-end and far inferior.
However, the potential here was very high, not lower than the Dark Soul Domain. In the future, it even hoped to become the fourth Dark Holy Land.
"The Xuanxu Holy Realm is about to become an Ancestral Realm. However, I can't let it become one yet, or it will surely attract the attention of the Ancestors.
My main body and Ancestor clone are both guarding the Dark Soul Domain. I can't manage the Xuanxu Holy Realm for now. I must suppress its promotion!"
Ye Tian thought.
He planned to wait until his main body created the Endless Ancestor Method and promoted to Ancestor before letting the Xuanxu Holy Realm evolve into an Ancestral Realm.
That way, he could withstand the prying eyes of other Ancestors. He could even explain that he obtained a treasure from the Dark Soul Domain that allowed the Xuanxu Holy Realm to promote. This would shift the Ancestors' attention to the Dark Soul Domain instead of the secrets of the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
On this day.
Something happened to the Human Race.
An Invincible Domain Ancestor promoted to the Supreme Level Domain Ancestor realm in the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Consequently, many Invincible Domain Ancestors who couldn't promote in the Dark Soul Domain and felt it wasn't suitable for their cultivation returned to the Xuanxu Holy Realm one by one.
Gradually, the Dark Soul Domain was almost entirely populated by Human Soul Ancestors, while the Domain Ancestors returned to the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Unknowingly, the 100-domain-year mark since the birth of the Dark Soul Domain was approaching. The Ancestors of various factions were becoming restless.
War Ancestor Realm.
The two Ancestors of the War Gate had also returned long ago, leaving only incarnations outside the Dark Soul Domain.
At this moment, the War Ancestor and Realm Ancestor were chatting with one of Ye Tian's incarnations.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor, the 100-domain-year mark is coming soon. The Destruction Ancestor will likely move against your Human Race's Qingye Ancestor. Give me the truth—how is Qingye Ancestor's strength now? With the help of part of the Dark Soul Domain's power, is there... hope of resisting the Destruction Ancestor? If not, we must give up territory in the Dark Soul Domain and join forces with the various Holy Gates of the Dark Holy Territory to resist the Ancestors of the Dark Destruction Domain.
As long as the benefits are large enough, they will still ally with us."
Ye Tian smiled and said, "War Ancestor, Realm Ancestor, rest assured. Qingye Ancestor's strength has reached the peak of the Elite Ancestor level, second only to Top Ancestors. With the boost from the Dark Soul Domain, he can definitely exert the combat power of a Top Ancestor. Blocking the Destruction Ancestor is no problem! However, even so, we must give up some territory, otherwise we won't be able to block all the Ancestors!"
Ye Tian never thought about ruling the entire Dark Soul Domain completely, but occupying the best area was no problem. Giving up other areas to other Holy Gates and the Ancestors of the Dark Destruction Domain wasn't a big issue either.
"That's good then!"
The War Ancestor and Realm Ancestor nodded slightly.
Although it was abnormal for Qingye Ancestor's strength to increase so quickly, they weren't surprised. After all, since Qingye Ancestor could promote to Ancestor in such a short time, having such strength was normal! Back then, the Saint Ancestor was the same.
However, they never imagined that Qingye Ancestor was actually Ye Tian's clone.
Ye Tian didn't reveal a thing, otherwise some secrets would be leaked.
Dark Soul Domain.
Ye Tian was at a critical moment; he was about to create the Endless Ancestor Method.
More years passed.
On this day.
Countless flashes of inspiration appeared in Ye Tian's mind. The essences of various Ancestor Methods merged together. Combined with dozens of domain years of comprehension and research, these insights finally fused into one.
The Endless Ancestor Method was finally created.
Boom!!!! Deep in his soul, Ancestor Method characters merged into a sphere, and endless Ancestor mysteries permeated out.
This sphere was... the inheritance of the Endless Ancestor Method, officially manifesting now.
The creation of the Endless Ancestor Method wasn't completely finalized yet; it was still evolving, manifesting many Ancestor mysteries, eventually evolving into three thousand Ancestor Method mysteries, and even more—infinite Ancestor Method mysteries.
It was like a collection of countless Ancestor Methods.
This was the true Endless Ancestor Method! This evolution lasted for trillions of years before finally completing, turning into the most perfect Endless Ancestor Method.
Now that the Endless Ancestor Method was created, Ye Tian only needed to fully comprehend and integrate it to become an Ancestor.
Since Ye Tian created the Endless Ancestor Method himself, even though it had evolved into a more perfect form, he could still quickly comprehend it and easily integrate it.
