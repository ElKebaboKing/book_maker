Chapter 1660 Can the Copy Talent Actually Be Upgraded?

“A Void Beast at the Indestructible Realm!”
Ye Tian also received transmissions from the other eight extreme realm half-step Eternal Realm experts, and immediately felt how troublesome it was.
If he encountered a Void Beast at the Indestructible Realm, he would definitely die.
Although he had only brought a copied body this time, once it perished, all those gains would be lost as well.
“This copied body can’t move. Let the clone act instead. That way, even if the clone is destroyed, the loss won’t be too great!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus.
His copied body hid itself and stopped moving, while the clone began searching for resources. Once it found a certain amount of resources, it would return to the copied body, leave the resources there, and then continue acting.
As time passed, some eight extreme realm experts still chose to leave. After all, they did not dare face a Void Beast comparable to the Indestructible Realm. If they encountered one, there would be no escaping at all.
They were not certain they could gain any more benefits, and thus did not want to lose the treasures already in hand.
For example, the Void Gold Marrow Wood was far too valuable; they did not want to lose it.
Therefore, even if it was only a clone, they were unwilling to take the risk.
In this way, more and more eight extreme realm experts left the Abyssless Land, and even some Eternal Realm experts left as well.
Among Eternal Realm experts, those with clones were a minority. For ordinary half-step Eternal Realm experts, the Origin Tree heart could help them condense a clone, but it had no effect at all on Eternal Realm experts.
Even for some half-step Eternal Realm experts with Eternal clones, once they broke through to the Eternal Realm, the clone would be very difficult to maintain and would simply stop functioning.
Unless such a clone stayed within its own domain all the time, it could still be maintained. Otherwise, once it left its own domain and entered a Void Region or some other spacetime domain, the clone would collapse.
And within one’s own domain, maintaining a clone was no different from condensing an avatar or an consciousness clone.
Truly having a clone at the Eternal Realm was simply too rare among domain lords.
So in a situation where their lives were in danger, these Eternal Realm experts dared not take risks and thus chose to leave.
At the same time, news about the Abyssless Land began spreading through the major spacetime domains, and even some Immortal Realm domain lords learned of it.
However.
These Immortal Realm domain lords were not very interested. Clearly, they looked down on the treasures of the Abyssless Land, but they still sent some Indestructible Realm powerhouses under them to the Abyssless Land.
For Indestructible Realm experts, some things in the Abyssless Land still had value. Besides, it had been countless ages since anyone last entered the Abyssless Land; it was likely that many good things had accumulated there, enough to make a trip worthwhile.
……
There were almost no good things left in the outer perimeter of the Abyssless Land. It had been looted by many eight extreme realm and Eternal Realm experts.
Ye Tian went a long time without any gains, so he prepared to enter the depths of the Abyssless Land.
“The depths of the Abyssless Land have Void Beasts comparable to the Indestructible Realm. The other eight extreme realm experts, and even domain lords of the Eternal Life Realm, probably wouldn’t dare go in. But I dare. At worst, my clone will perish and I’ll condense another one.”
Ye Tian thought.
Unfortunately, the Void Beasts were at a level comparable to Eternal Realm experts, so he had no way to copy a Void Beast’s body. Otherwise, he could have copied one and mixed into the depths of the Abyssless Land.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian’s clone moved rapidly, heading toward the depths of the Abyssless Land.
At first, Ye Tian did not encounter any Void Beasts at the Indestructible Realm, so there was not much danger.
Moreover, in the depths of the Abyssless Land, there were surprisingly no ordinary Void Beasts. He flew for a long time without seeing even a shadow of one, which was very abnormal compared to the outer perimeter of the Abyssless Land.
“I suppose the depths of the Abyssless Land are the domain of Void Beasts comparable to the Indestructible Realm. Ordinary Void Beasts probably don’t dare approach such powerful beasts’ territory,” Ye Tian guessed.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian felt a pull, an extremely long-lost attraction, as if his entire soul were rejoicing.
“This is…”
Ye Tian became excited.
This was the copy talent warning him that a treasure capable of helping the copy talent evolve had appeared.
All along, he had thought that the copy talent had already evolved to the extreme, but now he discovered that the copy talent could still evolve.
“From the perspective of rank, my copied body now is comparable to an immortal ancestral talent. If it continues to evolve, wouldn’t it be able to surpass an immortal ancestral talent?”
Ye Tian murmured.
Although he still did not know what kind of treasure could attract the copy talent’s evolution, he had to obtain that treasure no matter the cost.
Not to mention that there was a Void Beast comparable to the Indestructible Realm here; even if there were Void Beasts comparable to the Immortal Realm, he would still have to charge in. Otherwise, he would not be willing to accept it.
Immediately, Ye Tian followed the pull toward the treasure and rapidly moved forward.
However, before he reached the treasure’s location, a humanoid Void Beast appeared.
Boom!
The other side threw a punch, shattering endless void.
Ye Tian unleashed one Eternal technique after another, trying to withstand the punch.
However, the power of that punch was too terrifying. The Eternal techniques he used shattered one after another, and the innate Eternal rules were directly Annihilated.
Next, he was blasted away by a single punch, his body covered in cracks.
But in an instant, he recovered.
In terms of life-preserving ability, he was far stronger than True Yang Saint Ancestor, and even stronger than some Immortal Realm domain lords.
Although he was far from a match for an Indestructible Realm expert, he would not be killed in just a few moves.
Seeing that Ye Tian had not suffered much injury, this Void Beast comparable to the Indestructible Realm instantly grew enraged.
Its body became incomparably huge, and it hammered at Ye Tian with one punch after another.
Ye Tian wanted to shake off this Void Beast, but his speed was not as fast as the other side’s, and his attack power was also far inferior.
He had almost no ability to resist.
Time passed bit by bit, and Ye Tian’s injuries grew more and more severe.
In the end, Ye Tian’s body was completely Annihilated by the other side, and even his will was shattered.
Somewhere in the Abyssless Land.
Ye Tian’s copied body let out a sigh: “The clone was killed. As expected, it still can’t escape from the hands of an Indestructible Realm Void Beast.”
Next, he copied another clone and tried sending it there again.
As a result, he once again encountered that Void Beast and perished there again.
After two consecutive deaths, Ye Tian pondered for a while.
“The Abyssless Land should soon be completely exposed. There may even already be domain lords of the Indestructible Realm, or even the Immortal Realm, arriving. I must follow behind them and slip into the depths of the Abyssless Land, otherwise there’s no way to get past that Void Beast and obtain the treasure I need.”
So, Ye Tian waited silently.
Finally, on this day.
One domain lord after another of the Indestructible Realm descended upon the Abyssless Land.
