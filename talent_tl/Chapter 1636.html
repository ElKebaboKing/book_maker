Chapter 1635 Time-Space Territory and the Eternal Realm Lord!

Not long after, the members of the Source God Pavilion all received a transmission, and the Source God Pavilion gave birth to the Thirteenth Ancestor.
And when they learned that the Thirteenth Ancestor was Ye Tian, who was at the ninth level of the Supreme Being realm, all of them were very puzzled, and even dissatisfied. They wondered whether the Twelfth Ancestors had given Ye Tian special treatment on purpose, deliberately making Ye Tian the Source God Pavilion’s Thirteenth Ancestor.
Although the Source God Pavilion was only a loose force, such behavior would still make them very angry.
However, some Half-Step Eternal Realm experts seized the opportunity and felt that if they applied to challenge now, and applied to become the Source God Pavilion’s Fourteenth Ancestor, they might just pass.
Thus, six Half-Step Eternal Realm experts successively applied to become the Source God Pavilion’s Fourteenth Ancestor.
It was obvious that they felt Ye Tian’s strength was weak, and as long as they could hold on in Ye Tian’s hands for one hour, or even defeat Ye Tian, then they could become the Fourteenth Ancestor.
“Interesting. Someone actually thinks the Endless Founding Ancestor is very weak and wants to take this opportunity to apply for the Fourteenth Ancestor!”
True River Sword Ancestor smiled.
However, since these Half-Step Eternal Realm experts had applied, he naturally could not refuse.
So, he approved the applications of these six Half-Step Eternal Realm experts.
……
The first Half-Step Eternal Realm expert arrived.
“Greetings, Thirteenth Ancestor!”
The Half-Step Eternal Realm expert, Eternal Night Source Ancestor, respectfully said.
“Eternal Night Source Ancestor, long ago you applied to challenge the Thirteenth Ancestor. You know the rules, so begin by choosing.” True River Sword Ancestor said.
Eternal Night Source Ancestor smiled faintly and said, “I know I am not a match for the Twelfth Ancestors, and it will be hard for me to hold on for one hour without falling behind. I’ll choose one then, and that is the Thirteenth Ancestor, the Endless Founding Ancestor.”
Anyway, as long as one could hold on for one hour without falling behind in the hands of one Thirteenth Ancestor, one would win, so choosing Ye Tian would be enough; there was no need to choose again.
“The rules are the rules, you must choose!”
True River Sword Ancestor said sternly.
“Fine!”
Eternal Night Source Ancestor casually chose two of the Twelfth Ancestors, and the assessment began.
Eternal Night Source Ancestor was still planning to defeat Ye Tian, dreaming of becoming the Source God Pavilion’s Fourteenth Ancestor.
However, as soon as they exchanged blows, Ye Tian’s Shatter Void Sword completely injured him.
In the blink of an eye, Eternal Night Source Ancestor was already seriously injured.
“I admit defeat!”
Eternal Night Source Ancestor immediately said.
“There are still two more; continue the assessment!”
True River Sword Ancestor said.
However, as soon as the remaining two Twelfth Ancestors took the stage, Eternal Night Source Ancestor immediately conceded defeat. He simply did not want to fight, or rather, did not want to keep making a fool of himself.
He only wanted to leave immediately; otherwise it would be too awkward.
After Eternal Night Source Ancestor left, the other five Half-Step Eternal Realm experts successively underwent the assessment, but all of them were defeated by Ye Tian.
After that, the inner-circle members of the Source God Pavilion all knew that Ye Tian’s strength was greater than theirs, and naturally no longer dared to mock him; they also acknowledged Ye Tian’s status.
Moreover, they also knew very clearly that Ye Tian’s potential in the future would surely surpass the other Twelve Ancestors.
After all, Ye Tian was only at the ninth level of the Supreme Being realm now. If he became a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert, wouldn’t his strength be comparable to Limit existences?
Of course, there were some heaven-defying prodigies who were brilliant from the very start, but ultimately could not break through to the Half-Step Eternal Realm. Such situations also existed.
Thus, if one was not as good as a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert, one could not enter the eyes of those true powerhouses.
This was also why Ye Tian could fight above his level, yet very few people feared him, while the lofty Eternal Realm experts had never even paid attention to him.
-------------------
In the Source God Pavilion’s most core space, inside a spacious room.
The Thirteen Ancestors sat on chairs like ordinary people, gathered around a table covered with all kinds of rare fruit resources.
They chatted while eating, looking nothing like Half-Step Eternal Realm existences at all.
Ye Tian quickly blended into this atmosphere as well; everyone said whatever came to mind.
“Everyone, I have already become the Source God Pavilion’s Thirteenth Ancestor. I wonder if some secrets can be known?” Ye Tian asked.
“Endless Founding Ancestor, ask whatever you want. If we know it, and if it does not involve our own secrets, we will tell you!” True River Sword Ancestor said.
“I’m quite interested in Eternal Realm existences. Why is it that the Eternal Realm is looked down upon in the Source Sea? Could it be that Eternal Realm existences are in areas outside the Source Sea? But the Source Sea seems boundless, and there are no records or legends of other places.”
Ye Tian asked this question.
“Hahaha!!!!” Wind Ancient Source Ancestor laughed and said, “Endless Founding Ancestor, I guessed that you would ask this question too. In fact, many Supreme Being experts, and even some slightly weaker Half-Step Eternal Realm experts, are curious about this. This is not particularly confidential information, so I can tell you.”
“The Source Sea is indeed boundless. Around the Source Sea are the places where the projections of time and space extend. You could say they are places outside the Source Sea, or you could say they are part of the Source Sea. If the Source Sea is compared to a main world, then there are many small worlds around the main world, and although some of these small worlds are not as large as the main world, the resources and cultivation environment they contain are even better than the main world.”
“That’s right. The Source Sea seems to be the origin of everything, but in the dark it is still constrained, which makes it very difficult to change here. The time-space territories surrounding the Source Sea, however, are varied, unlike the Source Sea, which remains eternally unchanged. Those time-space territories may even give birth to all kinds of precious treasure resources, which are beneficial to Eternal Realm cultivation and development. If some time-space territory develops for a long enough time, the number of top experts it gives birth to may even exceed that of the Source Sea. For example, in the Source Sea, Limit existences are relatively rare, but some time-space territories have many Limit existences.”
“Of course, the Source Sea has one advantage: its innate Eternal Rules are complete. Only the Source Sea can truly give birth to Half-Step Eternal Realm experts at the Nine-Extreme level, while time-space territories can at most give birth to Half-Step Eternal Realm experts at the Eight-Extreme level.”
“Thus, the other Eight-Extreme Half-Step Eternal Realm experts in time-space territories may even pay a huge price to enter the Source Sea in order to break through to the Nine-Extreme level. But exactly how many prodigies can truly break through to Nine-Extreme existences, we also are not too sure.”
“Almost all Eternal Realm existences control a time-space territory, and thus they are also called lords. Between the Source Sea and the time-space territories there is a huge Source Sea membrane, which can only be broken by Eternal Realm lords. Therefore, we Half-Step Eternal Realm experts have no way to leave the Source Sea and go to those time-space territories, unless we are Nine-Extreme Half-Step Eternal Realm experts, who can forcibly tear apart the Source Sea membrane to descend upon those time-space territories.”
“Likewise, if Half-Step Eternal Realm experts from the time-space territories want to come over, they also have to ask an Eternal Realm expert for help.”
After the Twelfth Ancestors finished speaking, Ye Tian more or less understood this secret.
“I know that in order to become an Eternal Realm expert, one must fuse with the Eternal Rules. Then why is it that Limit experts have such a hard time breaking through to the Eternal Realm? Logically speaking, shouldn’t it be easier?”
Ye Tian asked this question.
Many Supreme Being experts knew that the stronger a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert was, it did not necessarily mean the greater their hope of breaking through to the Eternal Realm. Many experts who broke through to the Eternal Realm had never even stepped into the Limit.
Thus, he was quite puzzled by this question and still had not figured it out.
At this moment, True River Sword Ancestor and the others sighed.
Wind Ancient Source Ancestor said, “Because of the innate Eternal Rules. Endless Founding Ancestor, you have also comprehended the innate Eternal Rules, haven’t you? We could sense it.”
“Yes. Isn’t comprehending the innate Eternal Rules a good thing? It means one’s control over the rules is deeper!” Ye Tian said.
“No!” True River Sword Ancestor shook his head and explained, “If you only comprehend the Eternal Rules, and have not understood the innate Eternal Rules, then you only need to fuse with the Eternal Rules to advance to the Eternal Realm. But once you accidentally comprehend the innate Eternal Rules, then you can no longer fuse with ordinary Eternal Rules. You must fuse with the innate Eternal Rules before you can advance to the Eternal Realm. This is the real reason why Limit experts have such a hard time breaking through to the Eternal Realm.”
“What? One must fuse with the innate Eternal Rules to advance to the Eternal Realm?”
Ye Tian was learning of such a secret for the first time.
“Endless Founding Ancestor, this secret must not be spread. It is a rule set by the Eternal Realm experts!” True River Sword Ancestor reminded him, fearing that Ye Tian would leak it.
“Why hide this secret?” Ye Tian was puzzled.
True River Sword Ancestor guessed, “There may be two reasons. The first is that they do not want more Eternal Realm experts to be born. Many geniuses originally had a great chance of advancing to the Eternal Realm, but because they did not know this secret and instead comprehended the innate Eternal Rules, the difficulty of breaking through became many times greater. But it may also not be this reason, because although Eternal Realm experts restrict us from spreading it, they have not completely forbidden it, and they did not make us swear an oath. Thus, there is also the possibility of the second reason.”
“The second reason is that Eternal Realm experts also hope to give birth to more Eternal Realm experts who advanced by way of the Limit. Such Eternal Realm experts have greater potential, a higher future, and can step into even higher levels.”
“Endless Founding Ancestor, you have already comprehended the innate Eternal Rules, so you must also advance to the Limit before fusing with the innate Eternal Rules, and only then can you advance to the Eternal Realm.”
Ye Tian suddenly understood. In his heart, he was not panicking at all; he was very confident in himself and believed that he could certainly fuse with the innate Eternal Rules and thus advance to the Eternal Realm.
Next, Ye Tian chatted continuously with the other Twelve Ancestors, learning more and more secrets.
Suddenly.
True River Sword Ancestor suggested, “In some time, the Wuhen Territory will open, and we are preparing to go there again this time.”.
