Chapter 1455: Fighting the Seven-Mark Oblivion Race
!
"When will the total offensive be launched?"
The Destruction Ancestor asked.
"In ten million years. We will recall the Supreme Realm Ancestors roaming the Dark Forbidden Zone. Additionally, the hidden Supreme Realm Ancestors and Half-step Ancestors of our respective forces should also be deployed. At that time, we will strike the Oblivion Race with everything we have.
Our mission is... to slay the Oblivion Race members with seven marks or more, while the Half-step Ancestors and Supreme Realm Ancestors will... slay those from one to six marks."
The Saint Ancestor proposed.
"I have no objections!"
The Eternal Cult Leader spoke.
The Eternal Cult Leader was... the Ancestor of the Eternal Cult, also comparable to the top-tier Eight-Mark Oblivion Race. Although he was not as strong as a Nine-Mark Oblivion, he possessed unique methods that made it difficult even for the Saint Ancestor and Destruction Ancestor to kill him.
"Furthermore, there is no need to leak the fact that we deliberately refrained from harvesting the Oblivion Race. We only need to say that our Ancestors have made breakthroughs in strength, surpassing the Oblivion Race, and are ready to launch a counter-offensive.
Some Half-step Ancestors and Supreme Realm Ancestors might have guessed it, or perhaps some Ancestors leaked the news, but regardless, as long as it isn't officially stated, the Supreme Realm Ancestors will still fight with all their might and won't retreat at a critical moment."
The Destruction Ancestor said.
"No problem!"
The World Ancestor nodded.
In this way, the six Great Ancestors arranged their plan to invade the Oblivion Race.
In the blink of an eye, ten million years passed.
On this day.
A large number of Supreme Realm Ancestors flooded into the Saint Domain Continent and the Destruction Continent, and one Ancestor after another appeared.
"What's going on?"
Ye Tian was very surprised.
He sensed many more Ancestor auras than usual, and many Supreme Realm Ancestors had arrived. Even the Fire Pattern War Ancestor had come here, seemingly under a forced mobilization.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor!"
"Fire Pattern War Ancestor!"
Ye Tian and the Fire Pattern War Ancestor greeted each other.
Soon after, he also greeted several newly arrived Supreme Realm Ancestors from the War Gate.
Just then, the voices of the Ancestors entered the minds of every Supreme Realm Ancestor.
"In a hundred years, we invade the Oblivion Race territory!"
"In a hundred years, we invade the Oblivion Race territory!"
"In a hundred years, we invade the Oblivion Race territory!"
Hearing this voice, Ye Tian was somewhat stunned.
Many Supreme Realm Ancestors were also stunned. They were going to invade the Oblivion Race? Was this a joke! Weren't they the weaker side? Wasn't the Oblivion Race their nightmare? Even the Ancestors could only barely contend with the Seven-Mark and Eight-Mark Oblivions, and the Oblivion Race even had Nine-Mark Oblivions who were nearly invincible! But now, the Ancestors had decided to invade the Oblivion Race.
If they fought like this, how many of them, the Supreme Realm Ancestors, would die... Immediately, many Supreme Realm Ancestors reported the situation, saying it was too risky.
The Ancestors then gave all the Supreme Realm Ancestors an explanation: several Ancestors had achieved new breakthroughs, and their combat power was now definitely stronger than the Oblivion Race.
Moreover, the Ancestors had mobilized a massive number of Supreme Realm Ancestors for an all-out war, and the success rate of wiping out the Oblivion Race was over ninety percent.
Hearing this, the Supreme Realm Ancestors didn't object as much.
After all, once the Oblivion Race was truly wiped out, they would no longer need to guard this place or face the Oblivion Race for endless ages; they could enjoy a better life.
This place was simply too dull. No Supreme Realm Ancestor was willing to guard it, but they could not resist the orders of the Ancestors and thus had to obey.
Now, with a ninety percent hope of wiping out the Oblivion Race, they naturally had to try.
A hundred years later.
One Ancestor after another stood at the very front, followed by a massive number of Supreme Realm Ancestors. Gathered here were the Supreme Realm Ancestors of various forces from the Dark Saint Domain, the Destruction Saint Ancestor and many Evil Race Supreme Realm Ancestors from the Dark Destruction Domain, the Eternal Cult Supreme Realm Ancestors, and some loose Supreme Realm-level taboo creatures from the Dark Forbidden Zone, and so on...
Such a massive force gathered almost most of the combat power of the Dark Forbidden Zone.
"Cross the Spacetime Corridor and invade the Oblivion Race!"
The Saint Ancestor spoke.
Boom!!!!! A large number of Ancestors and Supreme Realm Ancestors crossed the Spacetime Corridor, entering the Oblivion Race territory for the first time.
The Oblivion energy here was extremely dense. For the Supreme Realm Ancestors, fighting here was very disadvantageous.
But if it was just a short-term battle, the impact wouldn't be too great.
For Ye Tian, however, this place was like a treasure land.
At this moment.
The Oblivion Race members guarding the Spacetime Corridor were stunned. They never expected that the creatures of the Dark Forbidden Zone would dare to invade the territory of their great Oblivion Race.
Consequently, reinforcements did not arrive in time.
Boom boom boom!!!!! One Oblivion after another was wiped out. Among them, a Seven-Mark Oblivion was even instantaneously killed by the combined efforts of the Destruction Saint Ancestor and the Saint Ancestor.
Such a powerhouse, comparable to an Ancestor, didn't even have a chance to flee before falling instantly.
"How powerful the Destruction Ancestor and Saint Ancestor are, to actually insta-kill a Seven-Mark Oblivion!"
Ye Tian was quite surprised.
He wasn't doing nothing either. While invading this place, his Copy Talent enveloped the area, copying the talents of all the Oblivion Race members.
After all, once the Oblivion Race was wiped out, he wouldn't be able to copy their talents in the future. Thus, he would copy them all at once now and integrate them slowly later.
The territory of the Oblivion Race was like individual spacetimes; each spacetime was a territory. Some territories were weaker, while others were stronger.
There were tens of thousands of territories here. One could imagine how many Oblivion Race members there were; their numbers were significantly higher than the Supreme Realm Ancestors and the Ancestors.
However.
The Destruction Ancestor brought out a black longsword. With one slash, he wiped out territory after territory. No one knew how many Oblivion Race members died under that single strike.
"Destruction Ancestor, you seek death! You dare invade our Oblivion Race territory and kill so many of our clansmen!"
A roar came from afar.
Boom!!!!! A Nine-Mark Oblivion arrived.
He was the Patriarch of the Oblivion Race, the current strongest of the Oblivion Race—Ji!
Ji transformed into a terrifying figure billions upon billions of feet tall, reaching out with his bare hands toward the Supreme Realm Ancestors, seemingly wanting to kill a batch of them.
However, the Destruction Ancestor and Saint Ancestor teamed up to block Ji and pulled him into another spacetime to fight.
Meanwhile, the World Ancestor, the Eternal Cult Leader, the Spacetime Sovereign, and several other powerhouses comparable to Eight-Mark Oblivions also took action, engaging in fierce combat with the Eight-Mark Oblivions.
As for Ancestors of the War Ancestor's level, they clashed with the Seven-Mark Oblivions.
An earth-shattering battle had begun!
