Chapter 1592: Original Source Liquid!

Another long period of time passed.
With the help of the Ancient Star Race's resources and Star Ancestor's guidance, Ye Tian's cultivation improved very quickly. He was now approaching the 2nd level Supreme Being realm.
Breaking through each level of the Supreme Being realm is extremely difficult. Without sufficient potential, resources, and opportunity, it is very hard to break the shackles and promote to the next realm.
Even though Ye Tian's talent was monstrous, the difficulty of promoting to a 2nd level Supreme Being was not small.
Star Ancestor did not want to wait slowly for the descent, so he spent a bit of power to forcibly send out a treasure—Original Source Liquid!
This was a cultivation resource that existed at the beginning of the Source Sea. It could increase a Supreme Being's breakthrough chance by nearly 100%, but it only provided significant help to Supreme Beings below the 4th level. For Supreme Beings above the 4th level, the help was not great, at most increasing the breakthrough chance by 10% or 20%.
After Star Ancestor spent the cost to send out some Original Source Liquid, his consciousness remaining within the Ancient Star Tower became somewhat blurred. He looked at Ye Tian and instructed: "Xu Chen, this is Original Source Liquid. It can help you break the shackles and promote to a 2nd level Supreme Being. It can continue to help you break through until the 4th level Supreme Being realm 413. Once you break through to the 4th level Supreme Being realm, its help will not be great, but at that time, I will help you promote faster to the 5th and 6th level Supreme Being realms."
Xu Chen, who was actually Ye Tian, took the Original Source Liquid, his heart quite shocked.
He also hadn't expected there to be a treasure like Original Source Liquid. If word of this spread, it would likely provoke the greed and contention of countless Supreme Beings.
However, were there any drawbacks to consuming Original Source Liquid?
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian asked the artifact spirit: "Artifact spirit, do you know of Original Source Liquid?"
The artifact spirit immediately replied: "Master, Original Source Liquid is a resource that appeared at the beginning of the Source Sea. It is extremely rare and no longer exists today. Only some ancient Supreme Beings possess Original Source Liquid. It can help Supreme Beings below the 4th level break shackles and increase their realm, but it has some flaws. If Master encounters Original Source Liquid, it is best not to consume it, but rather choose to trade it away."
"Flaws? What flaws exactly?"
Ye Tian asked with some curiosity.
The artifact spirit truthfully said: "The only drawback of Original Source Liquid is that it consumes the Supreme Being's potential. It cannot be seen normally, but once one cultivates to the Peak 9th level Supreme Being realm, wanting to cross that half-step becomes as difficult as reaching the heavens. In the past, many ancient and powerful Supreme Beings consumed too much Original Source Liquid. Although their cultivation realm progressed quickly, when they cultivated to the Peak 9th level Supreme Being realm, no matter how startling their combat power was—even being able to contend with existences that had crossed the half-step beyond Supreme Being—they were still unable to break through. Finally, the Supreme Beings learned that Original Source Liquid had this flaw. Of course, many Supreme Beings will never be able to break through to the 9th level Supreme Being realm, so they do not care about this flaw. Therefore, many Supreme Beings still matchlessly crave Original Source Liquid. But you are different, Master. You will certainly be able to easily reach the 9th level Supreme Being realm in the future, and even have hope of surpassing the previous master, so you must not consume Original Source Liquid!"
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian completely understood.
Star Ancestor was trying to trap him. Star Ancestor was once a powerhouse who used Original Source Liquid to break through to the 9th level Supreme Being realm, and was thus trapped in this realm forever.
Now, he wanted to escape, so he wanted to make Ye Tian promote quickly, not caring about his potential issues, and even not wishing for Ye Tian to surpass him.
For Star Ancestor, as long as he could escape as quickly as possible, what did sacrificing the potential of a descendant clansman matter?
"What a pity, Star Ancestor. You underestimated me. That is nothing more than a cloned body!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Since it was a cloned body, Ye Tian naturally did not care about potential.
Since Star Ancestor gave him Original Source Liquid, then he would use it to quickly increase his strength, thereby having enough strength to sweep through the Ancient Star Race.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian used the Original Source Liquid to begin impacting the 2nd level Supreme Being realm. As a result, he broke the shackles very easily.
After his cultivation broke through to the 2nd level Supreme Being realm, Ye Tian's strength surged. He was now afraid he could defeat the ten Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race with a casual move.
However, Ye Tian did not act immediately, but continued to cultivate.
In any case, this body's potential was already consumed, so he collected various potential-consuming methods and forbidden arts to increase his strength. Combined with the various resources of the Ancient Star Race, in just a few short Great Tribulation Eras, he raised his own cultivation to the Peak of the 2nd level Supreme Being, and then used the Original Source Liquid again to break through the realm.
Just like that, Ye Tian broke through to the 3rd level Supreme Being realm.
But breaking through from the 3rd level Supreme Being realm to the 4th level Supreme Being realm was relatively difficult, so Ye Tian did not plan to delay any longer.
With his current strength, killing the ten Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race would not be difficult at all.
On this day.
Ye Tian summoned the ten Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race, inviting them to attend a meeting.
Regarding Ye Tian's invitation, the ten Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race naturally would not refuse to give face, and they all arrived at Ye Tian's palace one by one.
"Xu Chen, do you have something to discuss with us?"
Donghe asked.
Seeing the ten Supreme Beings arrive one by one and sit down, Ye Tian suddenly activated a formation and urged a clan-suppressing Source Tool of the Ancient Star Race—the Void-Suppressing Star River!
This was a top-tier Source Tool for sealing space-time, which could seal this place so the ten Supreme Beings would have no way to escape.
Seeing that Ye Tian had sealed space-time, Donghe was instantly furious: "Xu Chen, what is the meaning of this?"
"The Ancient Star Race will be controlled by me, and there is no need for you all to exist!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
Immediately, the aura of a 3rd level Supreme Being erupted from Ye Tian's body.
Compared to Ye Tian, the ten Supreme Beings were simply like ants, fundamentally too weak.
"Xu Chen, if you do this, are you not afraid of the Ancestor?" Gu Chen brought up Star Ancestor.
"You mean Star Ancestor?" Ye Tian sneered: "He is still counting on me to rescue him, but I will not rescue him. Let him be suppressed for a lifetime!"
"You're crazy! If the Ancestor comes out, we can also leave the Ancient Star Domain. If you stay in the Ancient Star Domain for a lifetime, you won't be able to promote to a higher realm either!" Donghe roared.
"No, I don't care about those things!"
Ye Tian said.
Seeing that they could not persuade Ye Tian, the ten Supreme Beings attacked together; they could not just sit and wait for death.
And Ye Tian no longer wasted words, directly taking action.
As soon as he moved, they discovered just how powerful Ye Tian was. One powerful Supreme Technique after another was unleashed as if by instinct, his combat experience many times richer than theirs.
Moreover, every one of Ye Tian's strikes was enough to heavily injure them.
Very quickly, one Supreme Being was blasted to death by Ye Tian.
