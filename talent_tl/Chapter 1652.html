Chapter 1651: Truth Mountain, the attention of the Eight Extreme Stage!

The Eternal Compression Secret Art of the Doomsday Tower was not expensive. Ye Tian spent only a modest price to buy such an Eternal Compression Secret Art.
This Eternal Compression Secret Art was the Wheel Secret Art. It transformed one’s innate Eternal Law into a law wheel, with oneself within the law wheel, and then began compressing the semi-Eternal power inside the body.
After obtaining the Wheel Secret Art, Ye Tian spent a fairly long period of time comprehending and cultivating this Wheel Secret Art within the Maple Leaf Territory.
As time passed, the semi-Eternal power within Ye Tian’s body was compressed to become increasingly powerful and pure, and naturally, more semi-Eternal power could be fused within his body.
Therefore, although his cultivation seemed unchanged, the semi-Eternal power he contained had increased greatly, and its power was even stronger, so his strength naturally improved rapidly.
Compressing semi-Eternal power fivefold was not difficult, but after compressing it fivefold, it became somewhat difficult to continue compressing it further.
After many era-years, Ye Tian still successfully compressed his semi-Eternal power fivefold.
His semi-Eternal power had originally been very powerful, so after a fivefold compression, the increase in his strength was still quite large.
At this moment, Ye Tian felt that his strength had roughly reached the combat power of a top seven-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm, and it was not far from the first boundary barrier.
After that, Ye Tian inquired about how to improve his will or compress his own semi-Eternal power. These were also the two ways Ye Tian could currently improve his strength quickly.
Very soon, he really got the information he wanted.
Among the many spacetime territories, there were still quite a few blessed lands comparable to or surpassing Rift Wind Valley. Entering those places could slowly improve one’s will.
As for how to quickly compress one’s own semi-Eternal power, aside from a few resources that could assist in compressing semi-Eternal power, one could only rely on Eternal Compression Secret Arts.
This was also why top-tier Eternal Compression Secret Arts were incomparably precious. If Ye Tian obtained an Eternal Compression Secret Art that could compress power by 100 times, then he could easily compress his semi-Eternal power tenfold, and even spend some time to compress it to 20 times.
But an Eternal Compression Secret Art like the Wheel Secret Art could far from do that.
“Top-tier Eternal Compression Secret Arts are impossible to obtain unless one joins some Eternal realm force. And those Eternal realms would not help semi-Eternal realm cultivators for no reason. Unless one becomes their servant and joins their faction, it is very hard to obtain benefits.”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He planned to create his own Eternal Compression Secret Art.
After all, Eternal Compression Secret Arts were also created by others, and those top-tier Eternal Compression Secret Arts came from those powerful Eternal realms.
Since those Eternal realms could create them, so could he.
He could fully use his copied bodies to try again and again. With countless attempts, he would eventually create an Eternal Compression Secret Art with good effects.
And this method was something only he could do, while other semi-Eternal realm cultivators could not imitate it, unless they were courting death.
Thus.
Ye Tian’s clones self-annihilated, and his main body condensed them out again, beginning repeated attempts to create Eternal Compression Secret Arts in the Source Sea.
Of course, Ye Tian also purchased a large number of Eternal Compression Secret Arts. Even those very trashy Eternal Compression Secret Arts, he bought some.
With so many Eternal Compression Secret Arts as reference, his chances of successfully creating a top-tier Eternal Compression Secret Art were naturally much greater.
At the same time, Ye Tian’s main body headed to a blessed land—Truth Mountain!
Truth Mountain was located in the Shaoyang Territory, and the Shaoyang Territory was occupied by a top eight-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm cultivator. Although the other party was not the territory lord and could not fully control the territory, his own strength was no weaker than that of a relatively weak Eternal realm cultivator, so he was qualified to occupy the Shaoyang Territory.
Truth Mountain was naturally also occupied by that eight-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm cultivator, but he also knew very well that he could not completely occupy this blessed land, so he openly made it available to other semi-Eternal realm cultivators. Any semi-Eternal realm cultivator could enter Truth Mountain to cultivate.
Of course.
To enter Truth Mountain, one still had to pay some resources, and these resources did not all fall into the hands of that eight-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm cultivator; the Territory Lord Alliance also took away more than half.
Otherwise, without the recognition and protection of the Territory Lord Alliance, other Eternal realm cultivators would definitely seize this blessed land.
Very soon.
Ye Tian arrived at the Shaoyang Territory through the spacetime teleportation array of the Maple Leaf Territory, making several transfers along the way.
Afterwards, Ye Tian came to Truth Mountain in the Shaoyang Territory.
This place was set up with a grand array by the owner of the Shaoyang Territory, True Yang Holy Ancestor, and many semi-Eternal realm cultivators were also stationed here to guard it. One of them was even a seven-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm cultivator who had already touched the first boundary barrier, the number one expert under True Yang Holy Ancestor.
Therefore, no one dared to act recklessly here. Even if an Eternal realm cultivator wanted to cultivate here, they still had to show True Yang Holy Ancestor some respect.
“Pay 200,000 spacetime coins, and you can enter to cultivate. The time limit is one eternal year!”
The semi-Eternal realm cultivator guarding here stopped Ye Tian and said.
Ye Tian took out 200,000 spacetime coins and entered Truth Mountain.
As soon as he entered Truth Mountain, he immediately sensed a will assault coming at him. This will seemed to be suppressing his will at every moment. Its method was different from the will impact of Rift Wind Valley, but both were generally aimed at tempering the will.
Moreover, the effect of will enhancement here was quite good, even having some effect on Eternal realm cultivators.
It was precisely for this reason that Ye Tian came here to cultivate.
Although the one he came here with was not his main body, if the will of the copied body improved, the main body’s will would also improve somewhat, and opening up a will world would naturally become much easier.
So, there was no real difference between the main body and the copied body.
Ye Tian walked step by step toward the depths of Truth Mountain, quickly drawing the attention of the other semi-Eternal realm cultivators on Truth Mountain. When Ye Tian surpassed the positions of some seven-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm cultivators and reached the summit of Truth Mountain, even True Yang Holy Ancestor was alarmed.
Somewhere in the Shaoyang Territory.
True Yang Holy Ancestor’s gaze penetrated layer upon layer of void and looked at Ye Tian on the summit of Truth Mountain.
“．‖Eternal realm?”
“No, this person is not an Eternal realm cultivator, only a seven-extreme stage cultivator. But a seven-extreme stage cultivator simply cannot reach the summit of Truth Mountain. That is a place only Eternal realm cultivators can reach. If a semi-Eternal realm cultivator dares to cultivate there, their will will be shattered, and they will directly perish!”
True Yang Holy Ancestor said in astonishment.
He quickly guessed the reason why Ye Tian could enter the summit of Truth Mountain: Ye Tian’s will had broken through the will boundary and stepped into the second-tier Eternal will, comparable to the will level of an ordinary Eternal realm cultivator.
“The second tier is Eternal will, and Eternal will helps break through the first boundary barrier. This person has not yet reached the first boundary barrier. If he breaks through it, he will at least have an 80% chance of breaking the shackles. If his semi-Eternal power continues to be compressed by some more times, then he will have a 100% chance of breaking through to the level of an eight-extreme stage semi-Eternal realm cultivator.”
“This young man is qualified for me to make friends with!”
True Yang Holy Ancestor said with a smile.
However, he did not go to meet Ye Tian immediately. It would not be too late to see Ye Tian after Ye Tian finished his closed-door cultivation.
