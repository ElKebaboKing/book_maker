"Our Ghost Fox Organization concedes, the challenge ends here!"
Sparrow War Domain Ancestor said angrily.
"Sorry, the challenge cannot just end like that!"
Blood Gang Domain Ancestor shook his head, "Besides, whether the challenge ends or not, I have no right to decide, that's the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor's business!"
Sparrow War Domain Ancestor looked at Ye Tian: "Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, end the challenge immediately!"
The reason he was so eager to end the challenge was because Azure Sun Domain Ancestor was in danger.
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor had condensed a Saint Soul, and a Saint Soul-level supreme Domain Ancestor was too important to the Ghost Fox Organization.
Even if he was displeased with Azure Sun Domain Ancestor, he absolutely dared not let a Saint Soul-level supreme Domain Ancestor die, otherwise, once the leader got angry, he wouldn't be able to bear the wrath.
Therefore, Azure Sun Domain Ancestor could not fall.
Ye Tian heard Sparrow War Domain Ancestor's message, but ignored it, not even pausing his movements, still attacking Sparrow War Domain Ancestor, vowing to kill Sparrow War Domain Ancestor.
As time passed, Sparrow War Domain Ancestor's injuries became increasingly severe.
Just then, Sparrow War Domain Ancestor once again activated the projection sword of the Grand Dawn Sword, but still failed to break through Ye Tian's Eternal Shield.
He even used the Karma Saint Skill 'Fruitless Annihilation', but still couldn't harm Ye Tian.
Karma Saint Skills are very effective against those weaker than oneself, even able to ignore the distance between a clone and the main body, directly slaying the main body.
But if the opponent's strength is greater than one's own, Karma Saint Skills become useless.
Therefore, Karma Saint Skills are also known as the most useless, and also the most terrifying Saint Skill.
Seeing that Azure Sun Domain Ancestor was about to collapse, Sparrow War Domain Ancestor was also very nervous.
But he dared not act, if he did, he could instantly rescue Azure Sun Domain Ancestor, not even Blood Gang Domain Ancestor could stop him.
However, once he did that, the War Gate would have a reason to act.
Perhaps the War Gate wouldn't destroy the Ghost Fox Organization, but killing an invincible Domain Ancestor like him wouldn't be difficult.
"Sparrow War Domain Ancestor, save me!"
Azure Sun Domain Ancestor cried for help.
"You told me to act, now I'm in danger, you can't just stand by and watch me die, and I've condensed a Saint Soul, the leader will protect me once he knows!"
However, no matter how Azure Sun Domain Ancestor pleaded for help, Sparrow War Domain Ancestor dared not act.
"Azure Sun Domain Ancestor, just hold on a little longer, the leader is almost here!"
Sparrow War Domain Ancestor said.
He had already sent a message to the leader, and the leader, upon learning that Azure Sun Domain Ancestor had condensed a Saint Soul, was now rushing over.
He dared not act, but if the leader acted to save Azure Sun Domain Ancestor, even the War Gate wouldn't kill their leader.
Suddenly.
A suppressive aura descended.
At this moment, Blood Gang Domain Ancestor's expression became extremely solemn.
"Could he have come?"
Blood Gang Domain Ancestor said in shock.
This "he" was the leader of the Ghost Fox Organization—Ghost Fox Emperor, Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, who was also a legendary existence, extremely powerful. Although he couldn't be considered one of the most heaven-defying among Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, he had been a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor for a long time.
Legend has it, he was a descendant of a Progenitor, merely without a Saintly Talent.
Both Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and Progenitors have a certain chance of giving birth to offspring with Saintly Talents, it's just that Progenitors find it more difficult to have offspring.
And Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor was not born to that Progenitor while the latter was in the Progenitor realm, but was one of the many offspring born when the Progenitor was at the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor stage.
Of course, this was a rumor, its authenticity could not be verified.
Blood Gang Domain Ancestor had also never met this... Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, after all, their circles were different. Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor's circle was that of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, while Blood Gang Domain Ancestor's circle was limited to Invincible Domain Ancestors.
Therefore, he had never met Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor.
But now, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor had descended, his aura was very unfamiliar. If not Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, then who could it be? Crack! The void split open, and an eerily handsome man walked out from nothingness.
He ignored Blood Gang Domain Ancestor, and looked at Ye Tian instead.
With just one glance, Ye Tian felt as if he had been frozen.
At this moment, Blood Gang Domain Ancestor also couldn't help much.
"This battle ends here!"
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor said.
"No!"
Ye Tian refused.
"You dare to defy me?"
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor sneered.
"So what if I do?"
Ye Tian said.
He wasn't afraid of Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, not to mention the War Gate backing him, just the fact that he wasn't his main body right now made him unafraid of Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor.
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor didn't have Karma Talent, so he probably wouldn't have Karma Saint Skills, therefore, Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor couldn't kill him.
Unless Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor personally went to the Profound Void Saint Realm to kill him, but in that case, the War Gate couldn't possibly ignore it....
.
Therefore, he was fearless.
"You're very bold, do you think I dare not kill you? Even if I really kill you, the War Gate won't care! Do you believe me?"
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor threatened coldly.
"Ghost Fox, who said our War Gate wouldn't care....
"
A figure descended.
Then, a figure appeared, it was none other than one of the War Gate's Vice-Leaders whom Ye Tian had seen before, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor—Fire Mark War Ancestor.
"Fire Mark War Ancestor, didn't you go to the Primeval Progenitor Realm?"
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor frowned, then smiled, "So it's just a clone, your clone only has one-third of your main body's combat power. I didn't expect that while your main body entered the Primeval War Realm, you still sent out a clone to protect this little fellow, it seems your War Gate truly values him greatly! What a pity, only a clone of you has arrived, do you really think you can contend with me with just a clone?"
"Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor, if it weren't for your father, what do you think you are?"
Fire Mark War Ancestor scoffed.
Fire Mark War Ancestor's words also confirmed that Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor was indeed the son of a Progenitor.
At this moment, the rumor was no longer a rumor, but a fact.
"Fire Mark War Ancestor, do you want to fight me?"
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor asked.
"If you dare to interfere, then I dare to act, even if it's just a clone, suppressing you won't be a problem!"
Fire Mark War Ancestor said disdainfully.
"Hmph, who can't boast? Although you and I haven't fought before, I've been famous longer than you. You're just the weakest of the War Gate's thirteen Vice-Leaders, with just a clone, you're still no match for me!"
Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor said coldly.
"Ignorance is bliss!"
Fire Mark War Ancestor made his move.
And before he made his move, he instantly teleported Ye Tian and Blood Gang Domain Ancestor away.
And Ghost Fox Emperor Ancestor also teleported the Ghost Fox Organization's Domain Ancestors away, otherwise, the battle between Supreme-level Domain Ancestors could easily affect other Domain Ancestors, causing them to be instantly annihilated.
Boom!!!!! The auras of the two Supreme-level Domain Ancestors spread out, the entire Roaring Sea began to churn, countless Domain Ancestors became terrified, and quickly moved away from this area, to avoid being caught in the battle of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
Although Ye Tian and the others had been teleported away, they still observed the battle from a great distance.
