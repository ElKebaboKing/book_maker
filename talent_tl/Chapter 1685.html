Chapter 1684: Refining the Territory and Becoming the Lord!

Blood Bone Territory.
After Ye Tian parted ways with the Watcher Lord, he headed straight here and arrived in the void region outside Blood Bone Territory.
At this moment, his gaze penetrated the spacetime barrier of Blood Bone Territory and saw the interior of Blood Bone Territory.
He saw some Half-Step Eternal Realm experts slowly gathering resources within Blood Bone Territory.
Blood Bone Territory had abundant resources. Even though it was very dangerous, there were still quite a few Half-Step Eternal Realm experts willing to come here and take the risk.
“This one is the Lord of Endless, and I am about to purge Blood Bone Territory. To prevent you from being affected, leave Blood Bone Territory immediately.”
Ye Tian’s voice entered Blood Bone Territory and was heard by many Half-Step Eternal Realm experts.
Immediately, some Half-Step Eternal Realm experts left Blood Bone Territory, but there were still a very small number of Half-Step Eternal Realm experts who did not leave.
As for these people unwilling to leave, Ye Tian no longer tried to persuade them. If any special changes were caused during the battle, or if they were affected and died, that would be their own choice.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian entered Blood Bone Territory and headed straight for the Black Sea.
Boom!!!
A large number of black-scaled beasts sensed Ye Tian’s arrival and all rushed to attack him.
These black-scaled beasts varied in strength. There were black-scaled beasts comparable to Supreme Beings, and there were also black-scaled beasts comparable to Half-Step Eternal Realm experts, but the black-scaled beast comparable to an Immortal Realm expert did not appear. It was still sleeping deep within the Black Sea.
“Die!”
Ye Tian uttered a single word, and the large number of black-scaled beasts turned into corpses, one by one falling into the Black Sea. The Black Sea within an area of billions of domains instantly fell into deathly silence.
At the same time.
Ye Tian unleashed the legendary eternal technique—Will-Slaying Void Sword, and with one sword strike he slashed at the black-scaled beast sleeping deep within the Black Sea.
With a splurt!
This legendary eternal technique of will directly struck the other party, even erasing part of its consciousness.
Roar!!!!
The Immortal Realm black-scaled beast let out a miserable scream and awoke from its slumber.
Immediately, the entire Black Sea boiled, and even Blood Bone Territory began to quake. A blood-colored force swept across Blood Bone Territory.
Those Half-Step Eternal Realm experts who were still staying in Blood Bone Territory were eroded by this blood-colored force and immediately turned into corpses, dying one after another. Only a few powerful Half-Step Eternal Realm experts barely saved their lives by relying on some secret treasures and escaped in time.
“Comparable to a Four-Step Immortal Realm lord!”
Ye Tian truly understood the strength of this black-scaled beast, and he was no longer worried at all.
Next, he used Void True Body and Will Barrier Light to place himself in an invincible position, then repeatedly unleashed various legendary eternal techniques to attack, beating this Immortal Realm black-scaled beast until it had no ability to resist at all.
When the Immortal Realm black-scaled beast was heavily injured, Ye Tian unleashed his most top-tier legendary eternal technique—Myriad Layers of Void!
Buzz!!!
Layer after layer of void began to stack and suppress the Immortal Realm black-scaled beast, as if endless void had been compressed into a single layer.
In the blink of an eye, the Immortal Realm black-scaled beast was completely suppressed into countless fragments, and countless body particles lost all vitality.
Such an Immortal Realm black-scaled beast was instantly killed by Ye Tian in one move.
After killing this black-scaled beast, the greatest threat in Blood Bone Territory was basically gone, but Ye Tian still needed to understand what that blood-colored force came from.
He could tell that although the black-scaled beast had some connection to that blood-colored force, the blood-colored force was not unleashed by the black-scaled beast.
Deep within the Black Sea.
Ye Tian came here, and his will kept probing the area.
Very soon, his will detected a red mountain range. This red mountain range was an underwater mountain range, but it was not very large.
In truth, this was not a real underwater mountain range, but something transformed from a bone.
More importantly, this bone still had a faint trace of vitality.
As Ye Tian approached this bone, he felt a terrifying pressure. This was definitely a bone from a legendary lord-level creature.
When Ye Tian touched this bone, a crimson force struck over, but it was blocked by Ye Tian’s Will Barrier Light.
Even so, Ye Tian still suffered an impact, but he was not injured.
“The blood-colored force in Blood Bone Territory should come from this!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
After examining it, he discovered that this bone was almost connected to Blood Bone Territory itself. It would be somewhat difficult to separate it from Blood Bone Territory.
Or rather, the source of the blood bone power was this bone. If it were stripped away, then even if Blood Bone Territory could remain, it would become an extremely ordinary territory with almost no value.
During the years that followed, Ye Tian began studying this bone of a legendary lord-level creature that still emitted a trace of vitality.
He did not want that blood-colored force to erupt frequently; otherwise, Blood Bone Territory would not be suitable for cultivation, and he also would not dare to bring human Half-Step Eternal Realm experts here.
After a long period of study, Ye Tian discovered that the bone’s power was simply too strong, and Blood Bone Territory was unable to absorb this power in time, causing it to leak out........
At present.
Ye Tian had two methods to resolve this situation. The first was to set up a great formation to absorb the bone’s power, so that no blood-colored force shockwave would be formed; the second was to upgrade Blood Bone Territory into an Eternal Spacetime Territory. In that case, Blood Bone Territory’s need to upgrade would increase its absorption of the bone’s power, thereby completely solving this trouble.
Therefore, the second method was the most suitable.
“Then let’s use the second method!”
Ye Tian decided.
However, for a spacetime territory like Blood Bone Territory to advance into an Eternal Spacetime Territory, the difficulty was slightly greater, more arduous than other spacetime territories.
But he was an Immortal Realm lord. If he refined Blood Bone Territory, he could accelerate Blood Bone Territory’s evolution.
It was just like some Immortal Realm lords occupying a spacetime territory; even without treasures that could help the spacetime territory advance, after countless eternal years, the spacetime territory would still slowly evolve into an Eternal Spacetime Territory.
This was the feedback an Immortal Realm lord gave to a spacetime territory.
For example, when Ye Tian broke through in the Dark Forbidden Domain, the Dark Forbidden Domain had been expanding ever since. Although the expansion speed had now slowed by many times, it was still gradually growing larger, and the resources born there had increased as well.
On this day.
Ye Tian began refining Blood Bone Territory.
The way an Eternal Realm expert refined a spacetime territory was to use their own innate eternal rules to refine the innate eternal rules within the spacetime territory, thereby completely replacing the innate eternal rules here, and then refine the origin of the spacetime territory to become a lord.
Boom!!!!
The innate eternal rules of Blood Bone Territory simply could not contend with an Immortal Realm lord, and Ye Tian very easily refined the innate eternal rules here.
Very soon, Ye Tian’s innate eternal rule power eroded the origin of Blood Bone Territory and began refining its origin.
As time passed, Blood Bone Territory was gradually refined by Ye Tian.
Finally, on this day, Blood Bone Territory completely became Ye Tian’s territory.
He became the lord of Blood Bone Territory.
Afterwards, he reported to the Lord Alliance that Blood Bone Territory had become his territory, declaring the ownership of this place.
In other words, starting from today, no Half-Step Eternal Realm expert was allowed to enter Blood Bone Territory to privately mine resources; all the resources here would henceforth belong to Ye Tian alone.
