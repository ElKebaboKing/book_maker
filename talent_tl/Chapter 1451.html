In the area Ye Tian was guarding, they had just repelled a group of Oblivion clansmen not long ago, but he soon sensed another attack, and the scale was even larger than the last time.
"Request reinforcements!"
Ye Tian, the Peng Ancestor, and the others all called for help.
However, other directions were also under attack, so very few people could come to support, and it would take time to allocate manpower; they couldn't provide immediate assistance.
Boom!!!! The two sides clashed, and Ye Tian blocked three Four-Mark Oblivion clansmen and several Three-Mark Oblivion clansmen.

Another Oblivion clansman appeared—a Six-Mark Oblivion member—and he went straight for Ye Tian.

Many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors cried out in alarm.
At this moment, a Half-step Primordial Ancestor rushed over, but just as he was about to save Ye Tian, a Five-Mark Primordial Ancestor blocked him.
Everyone could see it—the Oblivion Clan was determined to kill Ye Tian!
"Escape!"
Ye Tian utilized his Void Escape Ancestral Technique to retreat rapidly.
He didn't want this duplicated body of his to perish like this, so he fled if he could.
"You cannot escape!"
The Six-Mark Oblivion member activated an Oblivion Clan talent, Space-Time, causing the effectiveness of Ye Tian's Void Escape Ancestral Technique to drop drastically.
Boom!!! One of the Six-Mark Oblivion member's hands clawed into space-time, reaching toward Ye Tian's position.
Crack, crack, crack!!!! The space-time around Ye Tian shattered.
The terrifying strike landed directly on Ye Tian's body. In an instant, Ye Tian's various defenses were shattered, and a claw pierced straight through his torso.
Had he not possessed the Oblivion Ancestral Talent, Ye Tian would not have been able to withstand such an attack; the erosion of Oblivion energy alone would have been enough to kill him.
But now that he had the Oblivion Ancestral Talent, although the attack annihilated ninety percent of his body, the Oblivion energy caused him no harm. Furthermore, he activated the Undying Ancestral Technique, managing to preserve his life just in time.
"Impossible, you're not dead?"
The Six-Mark Oblivion member was stunned.
His strength had reached the peak of a Half-step Primordial Ancestor among Forbidden Zone creatures. To fail to kill a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor who hadn't even reached the Half-step Primordial Ancestor level—especially in this space-time field that was completely advantageous to them—was unthinkable. One strike had actually failed to kill a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
"If you're not dead, then I'll do it again!"
The Six-Mark Oblivion member was determined to kill Ye Tian.
Such a monstrous existence, and so difficult to kill—if Ye Tian were allowed to grow, he would become a major threat to the Oblivion Clan.
Just as he was about to deliver the second strike, a Primordial Ancestor arrived.
At this point, if the Six-Mark Oblivion member retreated, he could still get away; even if the Primordial Ancestor pursued him, their Seven-Mark Oblivion members could intervene in time to save him.
But the key was that he wanted to kill Ye Tian at all costs. Therefore, he did not retreat but struck out again to crush Ye Tian.
"Was that really necessary?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Another claw fell, and Ye Tian's physical body was completely pulverized. This duplicated body was considered fallen.
Meanwhile, the Primordial Ancestor arrived and crushed the Six-Mark Oblivion member with one hand. Although the Oblivion Clan's Seven-Mark member had arrived, it was too late to save him.
With a bang! The Six-Mark Oblivion member was directly crushed to death by the Primordial Ancestor!
The gap between a Half-step Primordial Ancestor and a Primordial Ancestor was... just that large, almost impossible to bridge.
"Retreat!"
The Seven-Mark Oblivion member gave the order, and they began to withdraw.
In the blink of an eye, the Oblivion Clan had completely retreated.
The Primordial Ancestor also departed quickly, without lingering for a moment.
