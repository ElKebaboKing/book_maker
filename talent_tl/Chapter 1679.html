Chapter 1678 A Mysterious Ruin in the Domain of Void Beasts!

“I’ve attracted the attention of the Void Beasts!”
Ye Tian encountered several Void Beast squads. Each squad was led by a second-tier Void Beast. Even though Void Beasts had very low intelligence and poor coordination, there must be a powerful Void Beast controlling them from behind the scenes.
Therefore, once he encountered them, he was quickly besieged.
“This area must be abandoned!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Immediately after, he left this Void region and went to other Void regions to continue searching for Void Beasts to hunt.
However, he did not know this Void region very well, so he soon broke into the inner perimeter of a Void Beast group. Even with his copying talent used to scout ahead, he was still surrounded.
So, Ye Tian simply abandoned this copy body and did not meet these Void Beasts in battle.
After that.
Ye Tian used one copy body after another to probe the way, and only then did he find the correct route and arrive at another Void region.
Here, Void Beasts did not appear in groups.
Then, Ye Tian began hunting Void Beasts in this region.
However, after killing some Void Beasts, Ye Tian drew the attention of the local ruler here, so he immediately left.
After changing several regions, Ye Tian had basically touched the bottom line of the Void Beasts.
A fifth-tier Void Beast descended here, and this Void Beast was going to personally kill the Eternal Realm being who had infiltrated this place.
Just how terrifying a fifth-tier Void Beast comparable to a legendary ruler was, Ye Tian quickly experienced it firsthand.
Just as Ye Tian was about to instantly kill a first-tier Void Beast, the surrounding Void region shook, and a figure crossed the Void from far away.
Zzzzt zzzzt zzzzt!!!!
The Void region shattered into pieces, and an incomparably gigantic Void Beast descended. It was clearly a fifth-tier Void Beast.
“Life from the Space-Time Domain, you are seeking death!”
The fifth-tier Void Beast swiped a claw at him.
No matter how Ye Tian resisted, it was useless. The three copy bodies were instantly frozen in place, then blasted apart by one strike.
Another place.
Ye Tian’s last copy body here immediately condensed three new copy bodies again, then dispersed them, to avoid this place also being tracked by the fifth-tier Void Beast.
After a long while, Ye Tian confirmed that the fifth-tier Void Beast could no longer track him, and only then did he let out a breath of relief.
“It looks like it’s very hard to continue hunting Void Beasts!”
Ye Tian thought.
However, he had also gained a lot. By now, he had raised the degree of fusion between his will and body to the realm of twenty-five percent. This was a realm that many Immortal Realm rulers could not even reach, and he had only cultivated for such a short time.
“I’ve already gone deep into the Void Beast’s domain, haven’t I?”
Ye Tian suddenly thought of this.
He estimated that the Void Beasts were already completely enraged. If he were to return along the same path, he might not necessarily be able to retreat safely.
Besides, even if all of these copy bodies were destroyed, it would not be much of a loss. After all, his strength had increased, and he did not have many resources on him. If they were lost, then so be it.
Thus, Ye Tian prepared to go even deeper into the depths of the Void Beasts’ domain. Perhaps there would be other gains.
In this way, Ye Tian’s four copy bodies split up and headed toward the depths of the Void Beasts’ domain.
Along the way, copy bodies kept being discovered by Void Beasts, and then killed by fifth-tier Void Beasts or fourth-tier Void Beasts.
As time passed, Ye Tian discovered that the resources on him had been exhausted, and he could no longer condense copy bodies.
Condensing copy bodies required energy, and there was no energy supply in this Void region. With his energy exhausted, no matter how powerful the copying talent was, it still could not condense new copy bodies.
Unless he found a blessed land and collected some resources, he would be able to continue condensing new copy bodies.
After some more time, even though Ye Tian was extremely careful, two of the four copy bodies still fell, leaving only two copy bodies remaining in the Void Beasts’ domain.
On this day.
Ye Tian was quite lucky. He actually found a shattered world in the Void region, which could also be called a ruin.
Around this ruin, there were still quite a few Void Beasts.
Ye Tian did not sense the existence of any third-tier Void Beasts. The strongest Void Beast here was only a second-tier Void Beast.
“Why would a ruin world appear in the Void Beasts’ domain?”
Ye Tian was very curious.
This was definitely not a Void Beast nest, because on this world ruin, he clearly felt the fluctuations of a formation, and Void Beasts did not know formations. Their meager intelligence simply could not create a cultivation civilization.

So, this ruin world definitely did not belong to Void Beasts.
“Could it be a ruin from the Space-Time Domain?”
Ye Tian speculated in secret.
But this possibility was not very high, because this place was too far from the Space-Time Domain. If it really were a ruin from the Space-Time Domain, it could not have run all the way here, and even Void Beasts would not have been able to destroy it.
As far as he knew, the Space-Time Domain did not yet have such a powerful ruin.
“Or could it be a ruin similar to the one where the True Void Legendary Tower is located?”
As soon as Ye Tian thought of this, he became somewhat tempted.
If that were really the case, then this would be a great opportunity!
He observed carefully. This ruin world was fairly damaged, with many cracks. Through these cracks, one could enter this ruin world.
..... 0 .......
“First let a copy body go in and take a gamble.”
Ye Tian decided.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian activated the legendary Eternal technique Infinite Passage, crossing the Void region in an instant and arriving at a crack.
At this time, several second-tier Void Beasts sensed Ye Tian’s presence and immediately attacked him.
The other Void Beasts also launched attacks, reacting quite quickly.
“Void True Body!”
“Will Barrier Light!”
With these two methods, the Void Beast attacks were weakened by more than ninety percent, and could not cause Ye Tian any substantial harm at all.
Immediately after, Ye Tian passed through the crack and entered this ruin world.
Not long after.
A fifth-tier Void Beast descended, staring at this ruin with eyes full of fury.
“An ant actually broke in here. Who is he, and why does he have so many clones?”
This fifth-tier Void Beast was very curious.
Unfortunately, it could not enter this ruin world, and neither could the other Void Beasts. It seemed this ruin world forbade Void Beasts from entering, but allowed other life forms to enter.
Boom!!!!
The fifth-tier Void Beast struck the ruin world with both claws, attacking again and again, but it only caused ripples one after another, and could not damage the ruin world in the slightest.
