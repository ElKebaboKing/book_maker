Chapter 1518: Calamity Beast! The True Goal of the Six Great Powers!

Ye Tian couldn't figure it out for the time being, so he stopped thinking about it.
It was a good thing that those six super-tier powers weren't making a move against the human race.
Although he didn't fear the Supreme-level beings who had currently entered the Dark Forbidden Zone, the six super-tier powers did, after all, possess Invincible-level beings. If he killed too many of their Supreme-level experts, an Invincible-level being would inevitably descend upon the Dark Forbidden Zone.
In that case, even if he could escape, the human race wouldn't be able to.
Therefore, he didn't want to provoke the six super-tier powers for the time being.
As time passed, the first Origin Rule Calamity Crystal appeared in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
The Dark Forbidden Zone was the human race's home base, and Ye Tian was extremely familiar with it. Combined with his great strength, he was able to arrive at the destination in time to seize the Origin Rule Calamity Crystal.
Although Ye Tian didn't manage to snatch the first Origin Rule Calamity Crystal, he did get his hands on the second one.
Unbeknownst to many, more than twenty Origin Rule Calamity Crystals had already been born in the Dark Forbidden Zone, and Ye Tian had snatched six of them, which was definitely a very large number.
During this period, Ye Tian also encountered Supreme-level experts from the six super-tier powers. They had also snatched some Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, but since they were primarily searching for something, most of their manpower didn't participate in the scramble for the crystals. Thus, the six powers hadn't snatched many.
Currently, in the Dark Forbidden Zone, except for the human race and the Battle Gate, almost all other forces had been wiped out. Only a few Ancestors remained hidden and were lucky enough to still be alive.
One Dark Holy Land after another had long since been destroyed, and the entire Dark Forbidden Zone had become incredibly empty.
One day.
Somewhere in the Dark Forbidden Zone, a Supreme-level foreign expert was investigating the area and suddenly sensed a special space-time node.
"There should be an Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Zone hiding here, right?"
This foreign expert thought to himself.
So, he attacked that space-time node.
*Crack!!!*
This patch of space-time shattered instantly, revealing a figure—it was a Top-tier Ancestor.
If Ye Tian were here, he would have recognized this Ancestor's identity: Ancestor Hundred Souls, the master of the Saint Ancestor.
At this moment, Ancestor Hundred Souls looked desperate.
This place was very well-hidden, with no resources or treasures in the surrounding area. It just so happened that there was a concealed space-time node here. By hiding inside, as long as no Ancestor was in the vicinity, it would be very difficult to detect this node.
And given how vast the Dark Forbidden Zone was, even a Supreme Ancestor couldn't investigate a very large area. Therefore, he still had a high probability of avoiding this great calamity again.
But he didn't expect that his luck would be so bad this time that he would actually be discovered.
"Hahahaha, a Top-tier Ancestor! By killing you, I can obtain the resources on your body. You Top-tier Ancestors are each incredibly wealthy; killing one of you is more profitable than slaughtering an entire territory!"
The foreign expert said excitedly.
Ancestor Hundred Souls didn't want to die. As an ancient Ancestor, he had lived for a long time and possessed many trump cards, giving him a glimmer of hope for escape.
Immediately, he sacrificed a bead.
This bead was a Primordial Ancestral Object he had once discovered by accident. It could cause space-time vibrations and isolate a patch of space-time, buying him a chance to escape.
*Buzz!!!!*
As the bead was sacrificed, this patch of space-time began to vibrate, isolating him from the foreign expert.
Then, he began to flee.
However, an accident occurred.
The space-time vibration caused a resonance in a certain special space-time, and a terrifying aura emanated from that unknown space-time.
This wisp of aura not only made Ancestor Hundred Souls feel chilled to the bone, but even the Supreme-level foreign expert trembled all over.
"What is this..."
He felt death; the owner of that aura far exceeded his imagination.
*Crack!*
Space-time shattered, and a claw reached out from the depths of space-time. Ancestor Hundred Souls bore the brunt of it and was instantly killed.
The foreign expert wanted to flee, but he was stared down by a pair of pupils, as if he were directly imprisoned.
Then, the claw grabbed him and squeezed gently, turning the foreign expert into ashes.
......
"It's appeared!"
A man with three eyes and golden wings on his back was holding a compass, the needle pointing to where the claw had appeared.
This man was an Illusion Ancestor from the Illusory Dream World, one of the six super-tier powers. He was skilled in illusions and was named Illusion Ancestor Zi Ling, a third-tier Supreme Illusion Ancestor.
*Whoosh!*
The man broke through space-time and shuttled over.
At the same time, one Illusion Ancestor after another from the Illusory Dream World shuttled through space-time, descending upon the place where Ancestor Hundred Souls had fallen.
Additionally, the other five major powers also sensed it and descended upon the location one after another.
The experts from the six super-tier powers tacitly refrained from fighting each other. With a glance at one another, they decided to cooperate.
"Let's cooperate. The Calamity Beast is extremely powerful. Even if it's only a First Calamity Beast, it's comparable to the weakest Invincible-level beings. We must unite to have a chance of killing it."
"Fine, then let's cooperate. We'll decide how to distribute the benefits after the Calamity Beast is killed!"
"No problem!"
"We agree as well!"
After choosing to cooperate, the six super-tier powers joined forces to attack that unknown space-time.
*Boom!!!!*
The unknown space-time barrier was torn apart, and the eyes of many experts fell upon a massive figure.
It was a terrifying beast so massive it was beyond imagination. Infinite rules permeated its surroundings, and the aura on its body crushed even Supreme Ancestors.
It could be said that any Supreme-level being facing this Calamity Beast might be instantly killed.
... ..... 0
Consequently, the Supreme-level beings from each power immediately deployed battle arrays, and six battle arrays surrounded the Calamity Beast.
The slaughter began!
As soon as the fighting started, they realized they had underestimated the Calamity Beast's strength.
"Not good, this Calamity Beast is one of the stronger ones even among First Calamity Beasts. We won't be able to kill it; we must request reinforcements!"
The Supreme-level beings thought to themselves.
Requesting reinforcements was a last resort; they didn't want to do it.
Because once they requested reinforcements, it meant the benefits they would receive would be reduced.
After all, even within the same power, no one wanted to share the benefits with others. If they had a way to solve it themselves, no one would be willing to call for help.
But now, they had no choice but to call for help; otherwise, not only would they fail to kill the Calamity Beast, but they would also lose manpower.
Soon, the battle spread over a very long distance. Additionally, Ye Tian had many thought incarnations searching for Origin Rule Calamity Crystals in the Dark Forbidden Zone, and one of them sensed the battle fluctuations and noticed the area.
Thus, Ye Tian's true body shuttled over and saw the experts of the six major powers besieging the Calamity Beast.
The moment he saw the Calamity Beast, Ye Tian was also shocked.
The rules emanating from this Calamity Beast were actually the rules of the Dark Forbidden Zone. It was clearly a creature of the Dark Forbidden Zone, yet the Dark Forbidden Zone actually possessed such a terrifying creature.
"What on earth is this creature?"
Ye Tian was filled with curiosity.
But he had guessed it: the true goal of the six super-tier powers was likely this creature and...
