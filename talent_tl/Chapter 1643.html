Chapter 1642 The Opportunity of One Hundred Thousand Eternal Years!

“In the future, I still need to collect more resources!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
With his current strength, collecting resources would not be too slow either. After all, he would soon be able to reach the Half-Step Eternal Realm Stage of the Four Extremes Realm, and in the Source Sea, Half-Step Eternal Realm powerhouses above the Four Extremes Realm were exceedingly rare, numbering no more than fifteen.
Coupled with his life-preserving ability, it could be said that in the Source Sea, he was almost not afraid of anyone; no one could kill him unless an Eternal Realm expert came over from the Time-Space Territory.
In the following years, Ye Tian worked hard to improve his cultivation, strengthen his comprehension of the Innate Eternal Rules, and at the same time cultivate the Will World.
In the hundreds of thousands of calamity eras since coming out of the Wuhen Territory, Ye Tian's progress in the Will World had not been great. After all, without a treasure land like the Rift Wind Valley to assist him, and with the Will World having already stepped into the second tier, it became very difficult to increase his will.
And because increasing will was very difficult, opening up the Will World became very slow. In the later stages, if will did not improve, then he simply could not continue opening up the Will World.
Now, although Ye Tian's Will World had far exceeded 1,000, reaching 5,289 Will Worlds, it was still a long way from breaking through to 10,000 Will Worlds. Only by reaching 10,000 Will Worlds could he advance to a third-tier Will World and usher in yet another transformation of his will.
But no matter what, Ye Tian's will was still slowly improving now. This was the improvement brought about by the Will World secret art. Before reaching a bottleneck, the improvement of his will would not stop; it would only become slower and slower.
After another long period of time passed, Ye Tian's cultivation had improved quite a bit, and he had passed the rapid improvement period of the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
At this point, his combat strength had improved considerably and had already reached the Half-Step Eternal Realm Stage of the Four Extremes Realm.
It could be said that he was now already the top combat strength of the entire Source Sea, standing above the other twelve ancestors of the Source God Pavilion. If he displayed his strength, he would be enough to become the first ancestor among the thirteen ancestors.
However, that had no real meaning.
-------------------
Source Sea, True Extremity Palace.
True Extremity Palace was one of the top major powers in the Source Sea, even much stronger than a loose power like the Source God Pavilion, yet it was extremely low-key and did not accept ordinary disciples. As a result, its numbers were not many, but every single one was an elite among elites, a prodigy among prodigies.
Just the Extreme Realm alone numbered nine, even more than the Source God Pavilion; the most important thing was quality.
True Extremity Palace had Half-Step Eternal Realm powerhouses of the Four Extremes Realm, while before Ye Tian, the Source God Pavilion did not have a Four Extremes Realm expert at all, not even a Second Extremes Realm one.
Therefore, True Extremity Palace was much stronger than the Source God Pavilion.
Moreover, True Extremity Palace was incomparably united, a true power rather than a loose one.
At this moment, the palace master of True Extremity Palace, Qingming Sacred Ancestor, a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert at the Four Extremes Realm, was entertaining three guests.
“Qiyuan Source Ancestor, Fengling Source Ancestor, Wulong Source Ancestor, the three of you have descended upon my True Extremity Palace. Could it be that Lord Beihai Territory Lord has some order to issue?”
Qingming Sacred Ancestor asked.
The Beihai Territory Lord he mentioned was the Eternal Realm he served, and in fact the Beihai Territory Lord was once a palace master of True Extremity Palace. After breaking through to the Eternal Realm, he left the Source Sea, occupied a Time-Space Territory, established the Beihai Territory, and became the Beihai Territory Lord.
But True Extremity Palace had always been serving the Beihai Territory Lord, gathering intelligence and supplying talent.
However, the geniuses born from True Extremity Palace were not many, and compared with the Beihai Territory, they fell far short. Thus the Beihai Territory Lord did not pay much attention to True Extremity Palace and rarely sent people over.
But this time, he had sent three Four Extremes Realm experts, which showed that the task was not simple.
Qiyuan Source Ancestor said solemnly, “Qingming Sacred Ancestor, Lord Beihai Territory Lord sent us here because of the opportunity of one hundred thousand eternal years!”
“The opportunity of one hundred thousand eternal years?”
Qingming Sacred Ancestor was first stunned for a moment, but then he immediately reacted to what Qiyuan Source Ancestor meant.
An eternal year was a unit of time used by Eternal Realm experts; one eternal year was equivalent to one million calamity eras. How long was one hundred thousand eternal years?
Even he, Qingming Sacred Ancestor, had not yet lived through one hundred thousand eternal years; he was only seven or eight thousand eternal years old, and that was already an incomparably great age.
The era in which he was born could be called the beginning of the Source Sea. The Source Sea had abundant resources, and all kinds of rare resources were born one after another.
But for Eternal Realm experts, seven or eight thousand eternal years ago was merely the beginning of one Source Sea cycle. When the next cycle began, the resources of the Source Sea would explode again, just like the scene at the beginning of the Source Sea.
And exactly how many eternal years the Source Sea had truly existed for, no one was too sure.
Although Qingming Sacred Ancestor had not been born in the era of one hundred thousand eternal years ago, he had heard of a secret: every one hundred thousand eternal years, the Source Sea would give birth to an astonishing opportunity, even causing slaughter among Eternal Realm experts.
The last time, one hundred thousand eternal years ago, many Eternal Realm experts fought and turned the entire Source Sea into ruins. After many years, the Source Sea finally recovered its vitality and ushered in a great explosion of resources. It was named by later generations of cultivators as the Beginning of the Source Sea Era, which also meant the rebirth of the Source Sea.
In fact, before one hundred thousand eternal years ago, Eternal Realm experts often appeared in the Source Sea. But after that great war, the Territory Lord Alliance issued an order forbidding Eternal Realm experts from staying in the Source Sea. Even if they had business to attend to in the Source Sea, they were not allowed to remain for too long, all to prevent similar things from happening.
After all, the Time-Space Territory Lords were attached to the Source Sea, and they also did not want any problems to occur in the Source Sea.
Once the Source Sea had problems, then the countless Time-Space Territory Lords would also cease to exist.
“The three of you were sent by Lord Beihai Territory Lord to seek that great opportunity?” Qingming Sacred Ancestor asked.
“Yes!”
Qiyuan Source Ancestor nodded. “Lord Beihai Territory Lord asked us to investigate, and True Extremity Palace must also dispatch all its forces. Once we investigate the location of the opportunity and the time of its birth, we will immediately notify Lord Beihai Territory Lord. At that time, Lord Beihai Territory Lord will personally descend upon the Source Sea.”
“I understand. I will fully cooperate with the three of you!”
Qingming Sacred Ancestor replied.
Similar things were unfolding in one power after another, with many Eternal Realm experts sending Half-Step Eternal Realm powerhouses of the Extreme Realm down to the Source Sea.
This kind of news could not be hidden at all, and the Source God Pavilion also learned of it.
Thus.
True River Sword Ancestor transmitted the message to the thirteen ancestors and opened a meeting.
And after Ye Tian received the news, he immediately sent his replicated body to the Source God Pavilion.
