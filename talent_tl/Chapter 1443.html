Chapter 1442: Complete Control of the Physical Body, One Invincible Domain Body Transformation!

"Just one step more and I can completely control my physical body at 365 times, reaching the true pinnacle of physical control!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He continued to condense duplicate bodies, letting them activate the Evolution trait to deduce physical control.
Soon, Ye Tian performed hundreds more evolutions and finally completed this last step.
Boom! The physical body finally reached the level of perfect control.
At this moment, Ye Tian was capable of exerting 365 times the power of his physical body.
A sense of consummate control permeated the air. At this moment, Ye Tian could break the limit of the Invincible Domain Body at any time and undergo the first Invincible Domain Body transformation.
"Then let the transformation begin!"
With a single thought, Ye Tian controlled the infinite cells of his body, each cell comparable to a world.
Infinite worlds evolved under Ye Tian's will at any moment.
Boom! The Invincible Domain Body transformed, evolving toward the Supreme-level Domain Body.
Crack! Ye Tian's physical body instantly shattered into countless particles, each particle equivalent to a world.
These particles transformed, becoming even stronger, and then combined once again to form Ye Tian's body.
This moment seemed to last a long time, but in reality, only an instant had passed.
By now, Ye Tian had completed the first transformation of the Invincible Domain Body. After nine transformations, it would evolve into a Supreme-level Domain Body.
This process was guaranteed to be completed; it just required a long time.
After completing the first transformation of the Invincible Domain Body, Ye Tian frantically absorbed various energies to supplement the needs of the transformation.
Ye Tian took out various holy medicines, forbidden crystals, and source spring water... Under the supply of massive resources, Ye Tian's Invincible Domain Body finally absorbed enough energy and completely stabilized.
At this moment, Ye Tian's physical strength had surged several times over again.
This was only after completing a single transformation, yet his strength had increased so much. If he completed all nine, to what terrifying level would his strength rise?
The transformation of the Invincible Domain Body could be achieved by following the cultivation steps. The reason it took so long was that he needed to regain control over his physical body.
Because his physical body had surged several times in power and undergone a transformation, he could no longer control it at 365 times. It would drop to around 20 times, thus requiring him to regain control, which took time.
Once he regained control to the level of 365 times, he could complete the second transformation.
This would continue until the ninth transformation.
Of course, since he had previously achieved the perfect control of 365 times, regaining it would be much faster than before.
Therefore, for an Invincible Domain Ancestor who had broken through to the transformation stage, they only needed to take their time to eventually step into the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm.
"I have the Evolution trait, so I can speed up each transformation of the Invincible Domain Body!"
Ye Tian thought.
He had nothing else to do for now, so he continued his secluded cultivation to regain control of his body.
....While Ye Tian was in seclusion, the Xuanxu Holy World continued to grow. A large number of holy items were born. Since the human race had complete control over the Xuanxu Holy World and did not allow outsiders to enter, Domain Ancestors outside the human race had no idea about the situation inside.
The Xuanxu Holy World produced a large number of holy items, even holy medicines, holy mineral veins, and Ancestral Script holy items.
Under these circumstances, the human Domain Ancestors were very puzzled.
Nowadays, the human Domain Ancestors also had contact with the outside world, so they knew the situation of ordinary Holy Worlds. The speed and quantity of resource production in the Xuanxu Holy World far exceeded other Holy Worlds, which was too abnormal.
However, they did not ask questions but buried this doubt in their hearts. Regardless, this was a good thing for the human race.
Furthermore, the human race had a large amount of Star Soul Sand, and more and more peak human Domain Ancestors were breaking through to the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
After long years of cultivation, the number of Invincible Domain Ancestors in the human race had reached several thousand, and it was still increasing.
Additionally, the growth speed of those few Holy Talent users in the human race was also very fast. They had long since stepped into the Peak Domain Ancestor realm and had become first-tier Peak Domain Ancestors, soon to reach the combat power level of those on the Peak Rankings.
These Holy Talent users were the ones Ye Tian valued more. In the future, they had the hope of stepping into the combat power of two-step or even one-step Invincible Domain Ancestors, qualifying them to become the foundation of the human race. A large number of ordinary Invincible Domain Ancestors were of little use.
For example, the Qingyuan Holy Land had many Invincible Domain Ancestors, but he could kill them all with a single thought.
Therefore, only having many powerful experts could truly increase the human race's strength and deter others. Ordinary Invincible Domain Ancestors could not achieve such deterrence.
He would often be in seclusion in the future, so the human race still needed to rely on those powerful Invincible Domain Ancestors.
In the blink of an eye, another few hundred million years passed.
One day.
A violent tremor occurred in the Xuanxu Holy World. To prevent any accidents, someone sent a message to Ye Tian.
Ye Tian came directly out of the Holy Stele World.
"Husband!"
Xiao Yue appeared by Ye Tian's side.
"Father!"
Ye Xingchen and Ye Xingyue also arrived.
"Father, what exactly happened?"
Ye Xingchen asked in confusion.
"I'm not sure yet!"
Ye Tian frowned.
Although he was the master of the Xuanxu Holy World, he had just come out and hadn't had time to check the situation.
Hum! Ye Tian's consciousness covered the entire Xuanxu Holy World, sensing its changes.
Soon, he discovered the cause of the tremor.
"That is..."
Ye Tian saw a source spring being born somewhere, and a world was forming in that place.
"Forbidden Ancestral Spring!"
Seeing this ancestral spring, Ye Tian was greatly shaken.
A Forbidden Ancestral Spring was no ordinary thing; it was something that even an Ancestral World might not necessarily produce.
For example, the War Ancestral World did not have a Forbidden Ancestral Spring, but the Forbidden Ancestral World of the Forbidden Holy Sect possessed one.
Among the Ancestral Worlds of the Ten Great Holy Sects, only five possessed a Forbidden Ancestral Spring.
A Forbidden Ancestral Spring was of little use to an individual, but for a power, its value was extremely high.
The forbidden spring water produced by the Forbidden Ancestral Spring could help a Domain Ancestor easily rise to the Peak Domain Ancestor realm, and it also helped in tempering the domain body and purifying internal energy.
But the greatest use of the Forbidden Ancestral Spring was not this, but rather ensuring immortality! In the core of the Forbidden Ancestral Spring, a special kind of forbidden energy could be born. This special energy could keep a person from dying, allowing them to cultivate various dangerous holy bodies and secret techniques to their heart's content inside.
Of course, for a holy body like the Eternal Indestructible Holy Body, once you cultivated it, it would be difficult to recover even with this, but for ordinary holy bodies, even if one died, they could recover again without any side effects.
Therefore, with the Forbidden Ancestral Spring, a large number of top-tier holy body disciples could be produced.
The only pity was that the special energy of the Forbidden Ancestral Spring was limited, accommodating at most a few people at a time. However, accumulated over long years, a power would produce a large number of terrifyingly talented disciples.
Thus, the Forbidden Ancestral Spring was too precious!
