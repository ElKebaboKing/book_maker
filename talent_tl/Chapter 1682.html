Chapter 1681: An Untouchable Eternal Supreme Treasure!

The ruins of the Shixu battlefield.
Several of Ye Tian’s clone bodies had also one by one reached the Immortal Realm, and as the clone bodies grew stronger, they could naturally delve deeper into the battlefield ruins and gain more harvests.
For example, the deeper parts of the Shixu battlefield ruins—Ye Tian had never gone in before, because that place was densely covered with the power of Legendary Lords. With his cultivation at the Nine Extreme Realm before, there was simply no way to get close; once he did, he would immediately be killed across space and time by the power of a Legendary Lord.
But now, Ye Tian had broken through to the Eternal Realm and become an Immortal Realm Lord, and moreover a Three-Step Immortal Realm Lord. His strength was much greater than that of ordinary Immortal Realm experts, so he had some hope of reaching the core of the Shixu battlefield here.
“Let’s try it!”
Ye Tian crossed one area after another of the battlefield ruins in a single step, ignoring the densely scattered void fragments and the many traces of power.
Very soon, Ye Tian arrived at the core region of the Shixu battlefield ruins, a place he had never been able to reach before.
Boom!!!!
The power left behind by a Legendary Lord suppressed Ye Tian across time and space, but Ye Tian still relied on his formidable strength to forcibly withstand that suppression.
However, here, it was almost a void. Even if there had once been many treasures here, they simply could not withstand the attacks of a Legendary Lord; they had all been shattered into fragments, and so everything in sight was nothing but emptiness.
But Ye Tian still sensed one thing.
“That is...”
Ye Tian sensed a fragment. It seemed far away, yet somehow also very close.
“A treasure similar to the True Profound Dao Mark Tree and that mysterious sword?”
Ye Tian murmured.
Such a treasure could not even be touched by a Legendary Lord, and he also could not reach it at all.
But then he thought that the thing here was only a tiny fragment—perhaps he could touch it?
So he forcibly moved through it.
Hum!!!!
Layers upon layers of temporal-spatial barriers blocked Ye Tian, but relying on the power of the Immortal Realm and the powerful force of his will, he extended one hand, shattering layer after layer of temporal-spatial barriers, and finally touched that fragment.
Bang!
Ye Tian seized the fragment directly and pulled it over, then used all his strength to suppress and seal it.
After a long while, the fragment was sealed layer by layer by Ye Tian, and it no longer looked so special.
It was a cyan fragment, about the size of a palm, exuding an aura far beyond the Legendary level.
This fragment was enough to be counted as the most precious item in this ruin world, and it could even be said to be the most precious item on Ye Tian’s person besides the clone talent.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian left this place, then found a safe location and carefully comprehended this fragment.
Following the fragment’s aura, Ye Tian’s perception pierced through endless time and space and saw a great bell.
It was a cyan great bell, filled with endless profound patterns, and that aura was enough to destroy everything and suppress endless time and space.
Dong!
A bell toll rang out, crossing endless time and space and directly causing the clone body Ye Tian had here to crack. Fortunately, this power was relatively weak, so it did not kill this clone body of Ye Tian.
However, Ye Tian still gleaned some information from it.
This fragment was a fragment of that cyan great bell. In a great battle, the cyan great bell was shattered, leaving behind this fragment.
“An Eternal Supreme Treasure!”
Ye Tian slowly uttered these four words.
That cyan great bell was actually an Eternal Supreme Treasure, a weapon far, far more powerful than something like an Eternal Origin Weapon or a Void Saint Weapon.
The Source Sea Space-Time Territory had never given birth to an Eternal Supreme Treasure, so Ye Tian did not know much about them.
But this time, he had gained a great deal of inheritance from the ruins, and among it was an introduction to Eternal Supreme Treasures.
Eternal Supreme Treasures could form an endless temporal-spatial domain. Without sufficient strength, one simply could not touch an Eternal Supreme Treasure. Moreover, Eternal Supreme Treasures could also form projections, so sometimes the Eternal Supreme Treasure one saw was not truly here, but in some unknown place infinitely far away.
In addition, some special things, though not Eternal Supreme Treasures, were still similar treasures and could produce similar effects.
For example, the True Profound Dao Mark Tree was clearly not an Eternal Supreme Treasure, but it was a treasure of this level.
Whether the True Profound Dao Mark Tree and that mysterious sword were truly here in the Time-Space Territory, or merely projections, Ye Tian was not sure, because he had not yet reached that level of strength and could not touch the truth.
However, the fragment before him was indeed a genuine fragment of an Eternal Supreme Treasure, and it was truly in his hands.
An Eternal Supreme Treasure not only possessed tremendous power, but could also have special effects.
As for what special effect this fragment of an Eternal Supreme Treasure had, Ye Tian did not know yet; he would need to slowly comprehend it before he could find out.
Time slowly passed, and Ye Tian continued studying this fragment of the Eternal Supreme Treasure. He even made mistakes several times, causing the power of the fragment to erupt and shatter the clone body.
But through repeated study, he still came to understand the uses of this fragment of the Eternal Supreme Treasure.
It had three uses for Ye Tian.
The first use was that it could briefly form layer upon layer of temporal-spatial barriers, which could be used to block other people’s attacks, but it consumed a great deal of power and could only serve as a trump card.
The second use was that it could break any spatial barrier; for example, he could use it to leave this ruin world, or even enter many places without restriction.
The third use was the real opportunity: Ye Tian could comprehend all sorts of applications of the Eternal Space Dao from this fragment, thereby creating Space Legendary Eternal Techniques, and even stronger Eternal Techniques.
If it were forged into a blessed land, it could even allow the human race to comprehend Space Eternal Techniques in such a blessed land.
“What a pity it’s only a fragment. If it were a complete Eternal Supreme Treasure, how wonderful that would be!” Ye Tian sighed.
But on second thought, if it really were a complete Eternal Supreme Treasure, he would not be able to take it away either.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian began to familiarize himself with this fragment of the Eternal Supreme Treasure.
After a long time, Ye Tian prepared to leave this ruin world.
Thus, he activated this fragment of the Eternal Supreme Treasure, directly tearing apart the barrier of the ruin world and creating a fissure, allowing him to leave the ruin world with ease.
In fact, he could also have left through some other cracks, but there were bound to be layers upon layers of traps arranged by the void beasts there, and there might even be a Tier 5 void beast targeting him.
But now that he had suddenly opened a fissure and charged out of the ruin world, he would certainly be beyond those void beasts’ expectations, thereby giving him time to break out and leave.
Ye Tian only sent one clone body out, and it did not carry the fragment of the Eternal Supreme Treasure.
Once it left the ruin world, this clone body of Ye Tian immediately moved at extreme speed and even used Uninterrupted Space.
Boom!!!!
That Tier 5 void beast made its move and pursued Ye Tian.
Along the way.
Ye Tian kept attacking the void beasts that blocked his way, displaying formidable Immortal Realm battle strength.
When the Tier 5 void beast struck, Ye Tian resisted it with his will-defense Eternal Technique combined with Void True Body. Only then was he not killed instantly, though he still suffered heavy injuries.
“This ability seems a bit like the abilities of our race?”
The Tier 5 void beast was extremely puzzled.
But Ye Tian was clearly not a void beast, so it showed no mercy.
Ye Tian had no intention whatsoever of fighting the Tier 5 void beast; he only kept fleeing, at any cost, as far as he could.
In the end, however, this clone body was still killed.
But all of this had been within Ye Tian’s expectations.
After killing this clone body of Ye Tian, the Tier 5 void beast left, clearly believing that Ye Tian was no longer in that ruin world.
And this region also returned to tranquility. What the Tier 5 void beast did not know was that another clone body of Ye Tian, which had always remained in this area, appeared and arrived at a spot along Ye Tian’s escape route.
Hum!
A small void region cracked open, and a void sphere was pulled out.
Ye Tian directly took this void sphere away, and it disappeared without a trace.
The real purpose of having one clone body leave the ruin world this time was not to escape. A clone body was nothing; there was no need to run away. His true purpose was to send out resources.
The clone body outside lacked resources and could not continue condensing clone bodies. It was also completely impossible to replicate a void beast’s body to hunt other void beasts.
Therefore, he had to send out the massive resources collected in the ruin world.
Just now, the Tier 5 void beast had been focused only on chasing Ye Tian, and coupled with Ye Tian’s deliberate concealment, this void sphere had been hidden away.
Now that the resources were in hand, he only needed to leave one clone body in the ruin world to comprehend the fragment of the Eternal Supreme Treasure. The other clone bodies could be condensed outside, as part of the plan to hunt void beasts.
This was his true perfect plan!.
