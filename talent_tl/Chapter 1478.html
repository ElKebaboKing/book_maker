Chapter 1477: Slaying the Ancestor of All Things
!
"An elite Ancestor, and a top-tier elite Ancestor at that, second only to the combat power of a peak Ancestor... This is impossible! You've only just broken through to the Ancestor realm!"
The Ancestor of All Things was dumbfounded.
Who among them wasn't a monstrous genius? In the Dark Forbidden Zone, those strong enough to break through to the Ancestor realm were all peerless figures who had swept through their respective eras without rivals; they were true monsters.
Therefore, since everyone was a monster, everyone became an ordinary Ancestor after breaking through. Although there were differences in strength, the gap wasn't huge.
But what about Ye Tian? What kind of genius was this? To possess combat power second only to a peak Ancestor immediately upon breaking through—this was too exaggerated! "Ancestor Wanfa, I was just joking with you!"
The Ancestor of All Things chickened out.
So what if he possessed an Innate Ancestral Artifact? So what if he had the Barrier Ancestral Crystal? He still couldn't beat Ye Tian! He was only at the weaker level of elite Ancestors, while Ye Tian was at the level just below peak Ancestor combat power. The gap between the two was too vast.
"Ancestor of All Things, I am going to kill you. Let me tell you, this is no joke!"
Ye Tian's killing intent was laid bare, not hidden in the slightest.
"Ancestor Wanfa, do you really want to do this? Even if my strength is inferior to yours, it's not like I don't have the power to fight back. You don't have an Innate Ancestral Artifact. Although you can wound me, it will be difficult to obliterate my Ancestral Body. Killing me won't be easy!"
The Ancestor of All Things reminded him.
But in reality, he was still panicking; he was merely forcing himself to remain calm.
"Is that so? From what I know, the attacks of ordinary Ancestors are indeed difficult to obliterate an Ancestral Body. Only Innate Ancestral Artifacts and Ultimate Ancestral Techniques can cause significant damage to an Ancestral Body!"
Ye Tian spoke.
"Exactly. You don't have an Innate Ancestral Artifact, and since you've just broken through, it's impossible for you to have an Ultimate Ancestral Technique. Although I don't know how you possess the strength of an elite Ancestor, it will still be difficult for you to kill me.
How about we reconcile? Let's just forget everything that happened before!"
The Ancestor of All Things suggested.
"I indeed do not have an Innate Ancestral Artifact, but who told you I haven't mastered an Ultimate Ancestral Technique?"
Ye Tian sneered.
Boom!!!! Ultimate Ancestral Technique: Ancestral Armor, activated! Ultimate Ancestral Technique: Flowing Light, unleashed!
In an instant, Ye Tian charged toward the Ancestor of All Things.
Then, Ye Tian threw a punch at the Ancestor of All Things, employing the Ultimate Ancestral Technique: Overlord Strength.
For... Ancestors, other than Innate Ancestral Artifacts, other weapons were trash, far less powerful than an Ancestral Body. Even Saint Techniques weren't as powerful as a casual punch from an Ancestor.
Therefore, without an Innate Ancestral Artifact or an Ultimate Ancestral Technique, an Ancestor could choose to wear nothing and fight directly with their physical body; that was their strongest method.
"An Ultimate Ancestral Technique!!!!"
The Ancestor of All Things' pupils shrank violently as he immediately poured all his power into the Innate Ancestral Artifact longsword in his hand.
Boom!!!! The Innate Ancestral Artifact longsword collided with Ye Tian's Nine Extremes Ancestral Technique: Overlord Strength. The longsword was sent flying, and the punch landed directly on the Ancestor of All Things' body.
Crack!!! The Ancestor of All Things' physical body was shattered by the punch, but it healed in an instant.
Although the Ancestor of All Things recalled his Innate Ancestral Artifact, it couldn't pierce Ye Tian's Boundless Ancestral Body at all.
With the Boundless Ancestral Body combined with the Nine Extremes Ancestral Technique, and since the Ancestor of All Things' strength was already inferior to Ye Tian's, if he could have broken Ye Tian's defense with an Innate Ancestral Artifact, his strength would have reached peak Ancestor combat power long ago.
Bang! Bang! Bang! Ye Tian struck again and again, blowing apart the Ancestor of All Things' Ancestral Body repeatedly.
"Each strike actually obliterated one-thousandth—0.1%—of my Ancestral Body!!!"
The Ancestor of All Things was terrified.
This meant Ye Tian only needed a thousand attacks to kill him.
For... Ancestors, a thousand attacks wouldn't take long at all.
"Saint Ancestor, save me quickly!!!"
The Ancestor of All Things pleaded.
The Saint Ancestor frowned and spoke, "Ancestor Wanfa, spare the Ancestor of All Things!"
Ye Tian acted as if he hadn't heard and continued attacking the Ancestor of All Things.
Seeing that Ye Tian wasn't giving him face, the Saint Ancestor was also somewhat annoyed and prepared to break the barrier.
However, just as he moved, Ye Tian's clone, Ancestor Qingye, took action.
Boom!!!! Ancestor Qingye and the Saint Ancestor collided, neither gaining the upper hand.
"Ancestor Qingye, you are Ancestor Wanfa's clone, aren't you!"
The Saint Ancestor spoke.
The other Ancestors weren't surprised, simply because Ye Tian had just used the same Ultimate Ancestral Technique as Ancestor Qingye.
Ultimate Ancestral Techniques couldn't be taught; they could only be created by oneself. They naturally didn't believe that two people could create the exact same Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
Therefore, there was only one possibility: Ancestor Qingye was Ye Tian's clone.
Rumor had it that Ye Tian's clones were special, just like his main body, and it didn't matter how they died.
But now, this clone could even become an Ancestor without affecting the main body. This was too terrifying! "That's right!"
Ancestor Qingye spoke.
Once he acted, there was no way to hide it anymore, but he no longer needed to hide it. In the Dark Forbidden Zone, no one could threaten him anymore.
"As expected!"
The Ancestors thought to themselves.
"As expected of Ancestor Wanfa. I previously thought the Human Race was truly monstrous to produce two such terrifying geniuses, but now I know they are the same person.
Ancestor Wanfa, you sent your clone into the Dark Soul Realm; that is against the rules!"
The Saint Ancestor said.
"Against the rules? If you had the ability, you should have sent your clones into the Dark Soul Realm back then too!"
Ye Tian mocked.
Many Ancestors were fuming, but they couldn't do it.
"Ancestor Wanfa, the Ancestor of All Things cannot die. He is an Ancestor of our Dark Saint Realm. If he falls, the strength of our Dark Saint Realm will drop significantly!"
The Saint Ancestor said hurriedly.
"If one Ancestor dies, I will just train a few more Ancestors for the Dark Saint Realm!"
Ye Tian sneered.
"Are you really not going to give me face?"
The Saint Ancestor said angrily.
Ye Tian continued, "Saint Ancestor, do you really want to be my enemy? My clone can exert peak Ancestor combat power, and my main body will truly reach peak Ancestor combat power before long. Even after leaving the Dark Soul Realm, I will be a peak Ancestor.
Do you want to become my enemy for the sake of a mere weak elite Ancestor?"
The Saint Ancestor's expression changed. Only then did he realize that Ye Tian was not like those weak Ancestors from before, but a being truly equal to him.
The Ancestor of All Things wasn't his subordinate anyway; offending Ye Tian for his sake was indeed not worth it.
