Chapter 1656: The Abyssless Land!

“The limit of lifespan!”
Ye Tian sensed the limit of life from Gu Heyuan Ancestor. He had already gone a very, very long time without seeing anyone face a limit of lifespan. Even though many beings did not have high life cultivation, they could still live for endless ages; in theory, they had no lifespan limit. At most, after passing through endless years, their will would be unable to hold on, they would fall into stillness, and their will would eventually be extinguished, causing them to perish. But that was not a limit of lifespan.
Yet the decaying aura on Gu Heyuan Ancestor’s body proved that his lifespan was running out.
A powerhouse at the Eight Extremities Stage who had half-stepped into the Eternal Stage should still be facing a limit of lifespan—Ye Tian found this unbelievable.
Although he had heard before that those half-step Eternal Stage experts in the Time-Space Land would all have a limit of lifespan, he had never actually seen it. Now that he saw it with his own eyes, he was somewhat shaken.
A half-step Eternal Stage could live for 10 million Eternal Years, yet Gu Heyuan Ancestor was about to face a limit of lifespan. This showed that he had already lived nearly 10 million Eternal Years. What an unimaginably long span of time that was!
Even in the Source Sea, not necessarily every half-step Eternal Stage expert could live that long, because many of them, in order to survive, would inevitably venture into all kinds of precious lands and forbidden lands, and even fight other half-step Eternal Stage experts. They might die at any moment.
Therefore, although the half-step Eternal Stage experts in the Source Sea had unlimited lifespan in theory, very few could truly live beyond 10 million years.
So, many half-step Eternal Stage experts in the Time-Space Land actually did not care too much about lifespan.
For someone like Gu Heyuan Ancestor, who had reached the limit of lifespan, it was truly extremely rare.
“Greetings, fellow Source Ancestors!”
Gu Heyuan Ancestor said with a smile.
“Greetings, Gu Heyuan Ancestor!”
“Greetings, Gu Heyuan Ancestor!”
“Greetings, Gu Heyuan Ancestor!”
Many Eight Extremities Stage experts were quite respectful toward Gu Heyuan Ancestor, because he had truly lived for far too many years. Many Eternal Stage experts were not even as old as he was.
The longer one lived, the more experience Gu Heyuan Ancestor had, and the more secrets he knew.
Moreover, Gu Heyuan Ancestor was also very powerful. He had reached the Peak of the Eight Extremities Stage and had touched the barrier of the second boundary. Among experts who had touched the second boundary barrier, he was also an existence at the very top.
Unfortunately, he still had not stepped into the Nine Extremities Stage of the half-step Eternal Stage, nor had he entered the Eternal Stage.
“Gu Heyuan Ancestor, what is the matter for which you summoned us this time?”
An Eight Extremities Stage expert asked.
Gu Heyuan Ancestor said, “You should all know that my lifespan is nearly exhausted. I have been too cautious all my life, which caused me to miss many opportunities. Now I still want to make one more attempt at the Eternal Stage. However, with my current condition, there is no hope of relying on myself to break through to the Eternal Stage. So I want to go to one place. There is hope there that I can advance to the Eternal Stage, but that place is extremely dangerous. With my strength alone, entering there would be suicide, so I want to ask some Eight Extremities Stage experts to enter that place with me.”
“What place is it?”
Many Eight Extremities Stage experts became interested.
Although that place would surely be very dangerous, if it could give Gu Heyuan Ancestor hope of advancing to the Eternal Stage, then it might also give them hope of advancing to the Eternal Stage.
Although they were at the Eight Extremities Stage and their combat strength could match weaker Eternal Stage existences, they were not true Eternal Stage experts after all!
For an Eight Extremities Stage expert to charge toward the Eternal Stage was simply too difficult, and becoming a Nine Extremities Stage expert was even harder.
So they also had to seize every opportunity.
“It is the Abyssless Land!”
Gu Heyuan Ancestor revealed.
The Abyssless Land.
A small number of Eight Extremities Stage experts immediately became shocked upon hearing this name.
“Gu Heyuan Ancestor, do you know where the Abyssless Land is?” True Yang Saint Ancestor asked in shock.
Another Eight Extremities Stage expert said, “Gu Heyuan Ancestor, 900,000 Eternal Years ago, I even asked you about the Abyssless Land, but you said you didn’t know.”
Gu Heyuan Ancestor said with a smile, “Back then I still didn’t want to enter the Abyssless Land and had not made up my mind. Why would I tell you? Some of you have avatars, but I do not!”
Gu Heyuan Ancestor had no avatar, which was also the reason he had always dared not take risks.
In fact, many Eight Extremities Stage existences had avatars, but avatars differ. Some avatars have weaker strength and can hardly possess the same combat power as the main body. Others, once destroyed, require a great price to condense again.
However, Gu Heyuan Ancestor did not even have the qualification to possess an avatar. It was not that he did not want one, but that the path he cultivated was special and could not support an avatar.
Just like the heart of the Primal Tree: after other half-step Eternal Stage experts refine it, they can form an avatar, but Gu Heyuan Ancestor could not make use of it. He cultivated the Unique Sword Dao, a very powerful sword dao within the Time-Space Land, but this cultivation system could not split will or divide power. Even materials that could normally be used to forge an avatar would be shattered by the power of the Unique Sword Dao, and thus could not form an avatar.
Gu Heyuan Ancestor’s path had already been set, and he had no way to change to another path. He could only keep walking it.
Therefore,
some Eight Extremities Stage experts who had avatars dared to venture into the Abyssless Land, but he did not dare, because he only had one life.
However, the other Eight Extremities Stage experts did not know the location of the Abyssless Land, while Gu Heyuan Ancestor did.
“What kind of place is the Abyssless Land?”
Ye Tian asked curiously.
Not only Ye Tian was unclear about it; many Eight Extremities Stage experts did not understand the Abyssless Land very well either.
Gu Heyuan Ancestor wanted the other Eight Extremities Stage experts to go with him, so he explained, “The Abyssless Land is actually a nest of the Void Beast, and moreover it is a movable nest. About 6 million years ago, many Eternal Stage experts in the Time-Space Land launched a siege against the Void Beast, and the Abyssless Land was affected as well. That battle caused the Void Beast to nearly disappear from the Time-Space Land region, and it also caused huge losses to the Time-Space Land. Several Undying Stage lords fell, and the number of ordinary Eternal Stage experts who died was even greater. Multiple Time-Space Lands were even destroyed. And the Abyssless Land was said by those Eternal Stage experts who took part in the battle to have been Annihilated, but in reality it was not Annihilated. The Abyssless Land seemed to have its own consciousness. After that battle, having been severely damaged, it slipped into the depths of the Void Region. The Undying Stage lords were not very interested in the Abyssless Land, so they would not search for it, and ordinary Eternal Stage experts did not dare enter the depths of the Void Region for fear of getting lost. The reason I know the location of the Abyssless Land is that 3 million years ago, I offended an Eternal Stage expert and was forced to flee into the depths of the Void Region. By chance, I found the Abyssless Land, but I did not dare go in, so I only left a mark and waited for the future to enter it again. 2.8 million years ago, the Eternal Stage expert I had offended fell, and only then did I dare show myself again. Whoever among you wants to go to the Abyssless Land must form a space-time contract with me. Although it may not necessarily bind you, I hope that after everyone enters the Abyssless Land, at the very least you will not harm one another. If you do not form a space-time contract, I will not take you there.”
“I am willing to go!”
“I am willing to go too!”
One after another, Eight Extremities Stage experts wanted to enter the Abyssless Land.
Ye Tian was also willing to enter the Abyssless Land, so he agreed to form the space-time contract. It was only a kind of alliance contract and had little effect on Eight Extremities Stage experts, so no one really cared.
However, there were also some Eight Extremities Stage experts who did not dare to enter the Abyssless Land. These experts who did not dare to enter were basically those without avatars, and thus they did not dare take the risk, after all, they only had one life.
In addition, there were many Eight Extremities Stage experts who had not come here, perhaps cultivating in some Time-Space Land or even in the Source Sea.
After all, many Eight Extremities Stage experts who had touched the second boundary barrier would enter the Source Sea to charge toward the Nine Extremities Stage.
For example, Gu Heyuan Ancestor had spent a very long time in the Source Sea, but he had never broken through, and thus had given up on becoming a Nine Extremities Stage expert.
In the end, 376 Eight Extremities Stage experts were willing to enter the Abyssless Land.
And the time to enter the Abyssless Land was now!
Gu Heyuan Ancestor did not want the news to leak and cause an Undying Stage Eternal Stage lord to come looking for him, asking him to lead the way and ultimately taking away all the benefits.
So he would not waste time. He immediately led these Eight Extremities Stage experts toward the depths of the Void Region.
