Chapter 1489: The Strongest Emperor Artifact!
!
Currently, there were twenty-five Bone Kings in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. The weakest among them was comparable to a Destruction Ancestor, while the most powerful had reached the peak of a top-tier Ancestor. Yet, they were still no match for Ye Tian; otherwise, they wouldn't be so helpless against him.
At this moment, these twenty-five Bone Kings were extremely nervous.
"Everyone, what should we do? That top-tier Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Domain is far too powerful. He likely possesses combat power nearing that of a Strongest Ancestor. A Strongest Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain is comparable to a Bone Emperor, and if a Bone Emperor were to forcibly descend here, they would have to pay a massive price.
But if we leave him be...
Then after some time passes and that top-tier Ancestor refines those Bone Beads, his strength will surge, and we won't be able to stop him. Those Ancestor-level Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals will likely be slaughtered by him in massive numbers, becoming the opportunities for his growth," one Bone King said.
"Currently, there are only three ways. First, evacuate this place. Second, call for reinforcements and request a large number of Bone Kings to station here. Third, have a Bone Emperor pay a certain price to guard this place!" another Bone King spoke up.
"It's impossible to evacuate. Don't even think about the first option!"
The other Bone Kings chimed in one after another.
Evacuate? What a joke. They had paid an incredibly heavy price to establish the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. This was their hope. Just like the Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Domain, once they reached this level, many resources within the Dark Forbidden Domain were useless to them and could no longer increase their strength. Improving their combat power was almost hopeless.
By the same token, the Bone Race was the same; they also had to rely on the resources of the Dark Forbidden Domain to quickly increase their strength.
Because the Bone Sea Forbidden Land was born within the Dark Forbidden Domain, although its rules differed from those of the Dark Forbidden Domain, it was still influenced by them. Consequently, the resources born here had a significant effect on them. Over a long period of time, they could still increase their strength somewhat.
However, they could not withstand the rules of the Dark Forbidden Domain. Once they entered the Dark Forbidden Domain itself, they would surely die.
But the Dark Forbidden Domain would experience Great Calamities time and again, just as the Realm Worlds would.
During a Great Calamity, the origin of the Dark Forbidden Domain would fall into chaos and its rules would weaken. They would only need to pay a certain price to enter the Dark Forbidden Domain, plunder its resources, and thereby strengthen the Bone Race.
Last time, they had broken into the Dark Forbidden Domain and obtained immense benefits.
The Dark Forbidden Domain was relatively young, so its strength was weak, and it didn't have many Ancestors... it was unable to compete with them. Only other places with foundations similar to theirs could pose a threat.
The Bone Race's discovery of the Dark Forbidden Domain was a massive opportunity for them.
Last time, the Bone Race plundered the resources of the Dark Forbidden Domain, leading to the birth of several Bone Emperors.
Therefore, how could they possibly abandon such a great opportunity? Even if it meant paying the price of a large number of Bone Soldiers, they could not abandon the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. Once abandoned, they would have no hope of entering the Dark Forbidden Domain again, or they would have to pay an even more staggering price.
"Then let us call for reinforcements!"
The Bone Kings reached a decision.
As for having a Bone Emperor guard this place, that was the final resort. Unless they were driven into a desperate situation, they would not employ such a method.
After all, once they did so, the price the Bone Emperor would pay would be too great. Their strength would plummet, and it might even affect their origin, preventing them from making any progress for countless ages.
Thus, requesting some Bone Kings to guard this place was something that could be done.
Soon.
The Bone Race dispatched another twenty Bone Kings, including one extremely powerful top-tier Bone King who was not even weaker than the combat power Ye Tian had previously displayed.
The Bone Race felt that by dispatching so many Bone Kings to guard the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, even if Ye Tian dared to enter again, they would be certain to suppress and kill him.
...At this time, Ye Tian was in the Profound Void Ancestral World refining Bone Beads.
This time, he had obtained many Bone Beads. After refining them, his strength continued to rise.
Nearly a million years passed, and he had refined all thirty-plus Bone Soldier Beads, allowing his strength to increase significantly once more.
But at this point, Ye Tian felt that his cultivation had still not reached its limit.
Consequently, Ye Tian continued to refine those two Bone General Beads.
The energy contained within a Bone General Bead naturally far exceeded that of a Bone Soldier Bead. The effect of these two Bone General Beads was likely comparable to those thirty-plus Bone Soldier Beads.
After all, Ye Tian had refined many Bone Soldier Beads, so their effectiveness would naturally decrease, whereas he had never refined a Bone General Bead before. Once he refined them, his strength would likely increase by a large margin.
By then, his strength would definitely allow him to kill a Bone General in a single strike, or even heavily injure a Bone King in one blow.
If it were a top-tier Ancestor like the Destruction Ancestor, he could kill them within a few moves.
"Refine!"
Ye Tian spent a full million years to completely refine the two Bone General Beads, and his strength surged tremendously.
Without treasures like Bone Beads, simply absorbing and refining Nirvana treasures was of no use to Ye Tian anymore. Bitter cultivation also held no meaning unless he could create a more powerful ultimate ancestral technique, or perhaps an ancestral technique above the ultimate level; otherwise, his cultivation would not be able to increase his strength.
"I still have to target the Bone Beads!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
One day.
Ye Tian duplicated his body and sent the duplicate into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land once again.
When Ye Tian descended upon the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, forty-five Bone Kings appeared and joined forces to besiege him, with a large number of Bone Generals assisting them.
Otherwise, if the forty-five Bone Kings failed to stop him, wouldn't he be able to charge into their home base? At that time, even more Bone Soldiers would perish, and there was even a possibility that he might damage the Bone Race's most important treasure—the Gate! Therefore, the large number of Bone Generals forming a formation was able to withstand Ye Tian's offensive.
However, they still underestimated Ye Tian's determination.
Ye Tian attacked the Bone Generals desperately, not caring about his own injuries in the slightest.
In the end, at the cost of being heavily injured and on the verge of death, Ye Tian killed six Bone Generals, took their Bone Beads, and escaped from the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
If it were a real top-tier Ancestor possessing strength like Ye Tian's, they would never act so recklessly. Even if they truly wanted to deal with the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, they would surely combine the strength of all Ancestors to invade, rather than using such a life-risking method.
It was only because Ye Tian did not worry about the death of his duplicate body that he dared to disregard his life so completely.
After obtaining the six Bone General Beads, Ye Tian began secluded cultivation to refine them, and his strength continued to improve.
Meanwhile, the Bone Kings of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land had fallen into a predicament.
They were well aware that forty-five Bone Kings were still unable to quickly kill Ye Tian.
Of course, they believed that Ye Tian's strength had definitely reached the absolute limit of a top-tier Ancestor and could not be improved further. Therefore, even if he obtained six Bone General Beads, he would definitely be unable to break through the shackles and become a Strongest Ancestor.
Thus, they still did not invite a Bone Emperor to guard the place. Instead, they mobilized another dozen or so Bone Kings and brought along a Strongest Emperor Artifact.
Even the Bone Race did not possess many Strongest Emperor Artifacts. By deploying one this time, it could be said they placed immense importance on the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
With so many Bone Kings plus a Strongest Emperor Artifact, once Ye Tian entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Land again, they would absolutely be able to suppress and kill him.
