Chapter 1443: The Immortal Sacred Sect Strikes!
!
"The Taboo Ancestral Spring is far too precious. Back then, the Battle Sect lagged behind significantly in the Holy Wars precisely because they lacked a Taboo Ancestral Spring. If I hadn't appeared out of nowhere, the Battle Sect's performance in the Holy Wars would likely have been pathetic!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In fact, it wasn't just once; in many Holy Wars, the Battle Sect's track record was poor. Because they lacked a Taboo Ancestral Spring, the Battle Sect was relatively mediocre when it came to cultivating Holy Sons.
However, at the level of Invincible Domain Ancestors, the Battle Sect was quite formidable, possessing many treasure lands capable of cultivating Invincible Domain Ancestors.
Of course, those treasure lands were of no use to the current Ye Tian, so he didn't bother going to them.
After all, his strength was already greater than that of an ordinary Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, so those treasure lands naturally had no effect on him.
"A Taboo Ancestral Spring has been born in the Profound Void Sacred World. Once this news leaks, even other Sacred Sects might be tempted to come and seize it!"
Ye Tian mused.
Indeed, a Taboo Ancestral Spring could be stolen; in essence, it was a mobile treasure land.
A Supreme-level Domain Ancestor could cut out a section of the void and carry the Taboo Ancestral Spring away.
"I'll try to keep the news hidden as much as possible. If it really is discovered, I'd like to see which Sacred Sect's Supreme-level Domain Ancestor dares to make a move," Ye Tian said coldly.
With the birth of the Taboo Ancestral Spring, Ye Tian began to vigorously cultivate the geniuses of the human race.
Because of the spring's existence, many top-tier Holy Body inheritances were established.
Furthermore, many human Domain Ancestors easily reached the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm with the help of the Taboo Ancestral Spring.
In a mere hundred million years, the number of human Supreme Domain Ancestors increased a hundredfold. What a terrifying improvement! Meanwhile, Ye Tian also made great progress, once again gaining control over 365 times his physical body, completing his second Invincible Domain Body transformation, and causing his strength to skyrocket once more.
...Human practitioners could not stay in the Profound Void Sacred World forever; they often practiced in the outside world, and some even traveled to the Dark Sacred Domain.
Some human practitioners perished outside and were captured by other experts, who interrogated them and accidentally extracted information about the Taboo Ancestral Spring.
Even though Ye Tian repeatedly emphasized the importance of the Taboo Ancestral Spring to the human race and forbade its disclosure, not everyone could keep the secret.
In the past, when they said the Profound Void Sacred World had many resources, it didn't attract much attention.
But a Taboo Ancestral Spring was different; once leaked, it would inevitably cause a massive uproar.
On this day.
The fact that the Profound Void Sacred World possessed a Taboo Ancestral Spring was exposed. As for who leaked it, Ye Tian could not find out.
The Ten Great Sacred Sects soon learned of this and immediately sent people to verify it.
The Battle Sect was the first to receive the news because they could directly question Ye Tian.
At the Battle World Tower.
Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor questioned Ye Tian.
"Universal Law Domain Ancestor, is it true that a Taboo Ancestral Spring has been born in your Profound Void Sacred World?"
Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor asked.
"It is true!"
Ye Tian admitted.
The news had already leaked, so there was no point in denying it. Moreover, since it was the Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor of the Battle Sect asking, he naturally couldn't refuse to answer.
"Universal Law Domain Ancestor, you should be very clear about the value of a Taboo Ancestral Spring. It doesn't help an individual much, but it is incredibly precious for an organization.
Other Sacred Sects that lack a Taboo Ancestral Spring will likely try to seize the one in the Profound Void Sacred World.
I suggest you move the Taboo Ancestral Spring to the Battle Ancestor World. That way, other Sacred Sects won't come to snatch it."
Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor suggested.
Ye Tian refused directly: "Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor, this Taboo Ancestral Spring belongs to humanity. I cannot place it in the Battle Ancestor World.
As for any Supreme-level Domain Ancestors from other Sacred Sects who come to attack, I will block them myself!"
Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor knew he couldn't persuade Ye Tian, so he stopped trying.
"Universal Law Domain Ancestor, be careful. If you find yourself unable to win, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Battle Sect will come to support you!"
Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor said.
After all, Ye Tian also belonged to the Battle Sect. If Ye Tian ran into trouble, the Battle Sect naturally couldn't ignore it...
.
However, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Battle Sect were also very busy, needing to guard the Battle Ancestor World and focus on their own cultivation. It was impossible for them to be stationed in the Profound Void Sacred World at all times.
This was also why Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor had suggested that Ye Tian move the Taboo Ancestral Spring to the Battle Ancestor World.
"Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor, I will be careful!"
Ye Tian said.
...Immortal Sacred Sect, Immortal Ancestral World.
The Immortal Ancestral World happened to be... one of the Ancestral Worlds that had not birthed a Taboo Ancestral Spring. Now, news of the Taboo Ancestral Spring in the Profound Void Sacred World had arrived.
Subsequently, the Immortal Ancestral World sent people to investigate, and the news was quickly confirmed.
"The news is true!"
Upon learning this, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Immortal Sacred Sect were stirred.
In the core area of the Immortal Ancestral World, within an ancient Immortal Ancestral Hall.
One towering figure after another appeared—they were the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Immortal Sacred Sect.
Soon, thirteen Supreme-level Domain Ancestors had gathered.
In reality, the Immortal Sacred Sect had more Supreme-level Domain Ancestors than this, but some were busy fending off the Nirvana Race. The ones present here were those with their true bodies available; otherwise, coming here would be useless.
"Everyone, a Taboo Ancestral Spring has been born in the Profound Void Sacred World. Although the master of that world belongs to the Battle Sect, our Ten Great Sacred Sects frequently clash, and seizing their treasures is nothing out of the ordinary.
I suggest we move immediately to seize the Taboo Ancestral Spring, lest the Universal Law Domain Ancestor moves it to the Battle Ancestor World. If that happens, we won't be able to take it! The Battle Ancestor World is guarded by an avatar of the Progenitor; no matter how many of us go, we'd just be seeking death!"
The Supreme-level Domain Ancestor—Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor—spoke up.
"We must go, but who should we send? If we all move out, it will definitely alert the Battle Sect. They will likely try to stop us or even take the opportunity to cause trouble for our Immortal Sacred Sect.
Therefore, I suggest sending one person to seize the Taboo Ancestral Spring with the greatest possible speed!"
Another Supreme-level Domain Ancestor said.
They discussed for a long time and finally decided to send Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor.
Although Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor was only at the middle stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm, he had comprehended Ancestral Techniques. Thus, his combat power was far beyond that of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor like Golden Star Saint Ancestor.
If he went to seize the Taboo Ancestral Spring, a mere Universal Law Domain Ancestor would be no match for him and would have no way to stop him.
"Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor, rush there as fast as you can. Don't waste time, or else if another Sacred Sect beats us to it, we'll suffer a great loss!"
The various Supreme-level Domain Ancestors urged.
"Fine, I'll be back shortly!"
Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor said with a smile.
Immediately, he activated a secret treasure, instantly piercing through time and space to cross the Dark Sacred Domain.
Battle Sect.
One by one, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors looked toward the location of the Immortal Sacred Sect.
"Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor has left!"
Fire-Pattern Battle Ancestor noted.
Just as he was preparing to go support the Profound Void Sacred World, several Supreme-level Domain Ancestors from the Immortal Sacred Sect crossed through time and space and arrived at the Battle Ancestor Domain.
Should any Supreme-level Domain Ancestor from the Battle Ancestor World attempt to leave, they would engage them in "conversation."
As long as they could delay them for just a short while, Black-Pattern Saint Ancestor would be able to complete his mission.
