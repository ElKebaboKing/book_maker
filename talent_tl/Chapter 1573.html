Chapter 1572: Mysterious Consciousness!

Ye Jinpeng thought very clearly. If he lost the light orb, he would likely become very ordinary, so he was unwilling to give it up.
As for the fragmented expert's consciousness within the Eternal Source Object origin, he indeed did not dare to keep it on him. Therefore, having the Ancestor extract that expert's consciousness was naturally the best situation.
Soon after, Ye Tian began to act.
Strands of Eternal Rules transformed into chains, drilling into Ye Jinpeng's body. At the same time, Ye Tian mobilized the power of the Eternal Source Object, Wanwu Yang, to suppress the mass of Eternal Source Object origin within Ye Jinpeng's body.
In terms of grade, the Eternal Source Object origin on Ye Jinpeng's body absolutely far surpassed Wanwu Yang and even far surpassed Xumi Golden Mountain. It definitely came from a very powerful Eternal Source Object, but unfortunately, only a tiny bit of origin remained, so it posed no threat to Ye Tian.
Boom!!!!
Numerous Eternal Rules transformed into a palm, grabbing into the Eternal Source Object origin.
Ye Tian's control over Eternal Rules far exceeded other Invincibles and even far surpassed many Supreme Beings, reaching an incredible level.
Thus, while other Supreme Beings might not necessarily be able to handle Ye Jinpeng's problem well, he could.
Within Ye Jinpeng's body, inside the Eternal Source Object origin, a consciousness finally fully awakened in a crisis situation within a certain area.
Previously, it had only instinctively guided Ye Jinpeng's cultivation but had not truly awakened. After all, it was only a fragmented consciousness, and its main body had already fallen.
But now, it had no choice but to wake up.
"Who is it?"
The mysterious consciousness was incomparably furious.
But when it saw Ye Tian's hand of Eternal Rules grabbing toward it, it immediately panicked.
"To think I've been discovered!"
The mysterious consciousness was in utter despair.
It had finally found a target with great difficulty, and it was clearly only located in a foreign world. How could it be discovered by a powerful Supreme Being?
Moreover, even an ordinary Supreme Being would find it very difficult to discover it!
Indeed, an ordinary Supreme Being might not necessarily be able to discover it, but Ye Tian had condensed the Void Source Body. He was inherently too sensitive to the aura of Eternal Source Object origins and had too deep an insight into Eternal Rules.
Furthermore, Ye Jinpeng was his descendant, and they were related by bloodline, which was why he could perceive the situation in Ye Jinpeng's body.
It had to be said that this mysterious consciousness was somewhat unlucky.
In a mere consciousness form with not much power, it simply could not stop Ye Tian.
Swish!
The large hand of Eternal Rules pulled the mysterious consciousness out of the Eternal Source Object origin and then sealed it.
"It's done!"
Ye Tian said.

Ye Jinpeng now felt incomparably refreshed all over, as if that mysterious light orb could be completely under his control. He could also feel himself merging with the mysterious light orb step by step.
Currently, he had only merged to a degree of less than one ten-thousandth.
"It seems the mysterious light orb was previously controlled by that mysterious consciousness. Now that the mysterious consciousness has been taken by the Ancestor, the light orb truly belongs to me. The Ancestor is truly too amazing!" Ye Jinpeng thought to himself.
"Thank you, Ancestor!"
Ye Jinpeng said gratefully.
Ye Tian smiled slightly. "Alright, from now on, you can properly go and comprehend the Eternal Source Object origin in your body. If any special circumstances occur, remember to come and inform me in time."
"Yes, Ancestor!"
Ye Jinpeng responded.
Suddenly, Ye Tian reminded him again, "The Eternal Source Object in your body leans toward the Soul Rules system. In the future, you can focus on Soul Rules. You are currently walking the Body Path, which, strictly speaking, is not very suitable. The Human Race Holy Temple has a complete Soul Path. After you step into the third step of Supreme, you can walk the Soul Tribulation Path and promote to the Invincible Ancestor realm through the Soul Tribulation Realm."
Ye Jinpeng's expression shifted. No wonder it was so easy for him to practice soul secret techniques, soul holy techniques, and soul ancestor techniques.
It turned out he should walk the Soul Path.
As for why that mysterious consciousness did not guide him to walk the Soul Path, it was likely that the mysterious consciousness did not understand the situation either, and since that mysterious consciousness also walked the Body Path, it instinctively guided him to walk the Body Path.
"Ancestor, I understand!"
Ye Jinpeng nodded and then left.
After Ye Jinpeng left, Ye Tian looked at the sealed mysterious consciousness in his hand.
For him, that Eternal Source Object origin was not of high value; what was truly valuable was this mysterious consciousness.
Moreover, he really wanted to know why his descendant could merge with the origin of an Eternal Source Object.
Xumi Golden Mountain.
Ye Tian came here and placed the mysterious consciousness here. Otherwise, without the nourishment of an Eternal Source Object origin, the mysterious consciousness simply could not be preserved for long.
Of course.
Even Xumi Golden Mountain could not preserve a mysterious consciousness for too many ages. After all, Xumi Golden Mountain could not preserve fragmented consciousnesses indefinitely.
But for the duration of one Great Tribulation Era, he could guarantee that the mysterious consciousness would not dissipate.
Boom!!!!
Ye Tian entered the consciousness space of the mysterious consciousness and saw a humanoid figure.
Seeing Ye Tian, this humanoid figure roared in anger, "Was it you who ruined my plan?"
"Yes!"
Ye Tian nodded.
The mysterious consciousness stared at Ye Tian for a while and said coldly, "I thought some powerful Supreme Being had captured me, but I didn't expect it to be a Third-tier Invincible? For a Third-tier Invincible to have reached such an incredible level of mastery over Eternal Rules, surpassing many Supreme Beings, it seems your opportunities are not small."
Ye Tian did not deny it but continued, "Who are you, and how did you make the Eternal Source Object origin merge with my descendant? Which Eternal Source Object did that Eternal Source Object origin come from?"
"Unless you help me return to that person and help me merge with him, only then will I tell you everything you want to know. Otherwise, I won't say a word!" the mysterious consciousness said.
"This consciousness of yours cannot survive forever. If you speak out, I will extend your consciousness for you. But wanting to occupy my descendant's body is impossible," Ye Tian refused.
The mysterious consciousness seemed very angry and roared again, "Extend my consciousness? Do you know how difficult it is to extend consciousness? Even that mysterious Eternal Source Object could not help me extend my consciousness indefinitely. I finally found a vessel after searching for endless ages, and I only found one. There might not even be a second one, yet you ruined it. Besides merging with your descendant, there is no other way to extend my consciousness."
Ye Tian was too lazy to continue communicating with this mysterious consciousness. The other party clearly would not answer any of his questions.
Now only one method remained, and that was to forcibly strip the mysterious consciousness's memories.
But that mysterious consciousness was very powerful. It could manage to fall for countless ages and still maintain its memories and sanity. One could imagine how powerful the other party was when alive.
Therefore, to strip the other party's consciousness and ensure they had no time for self-annihilation, a special method was required.
"I must trade for a Soul Source Tool that can instantly strip the other party's consciousness!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
But Soul Source Tools were so precious that even many Supreme Beings did not have them.
"I can only choose to trade. I'll use the Void Source Pavilion points to post a bounty for a Soul Source Tool that can strip a powerful expert's consciousness," Ye Tian decided.
Memories from someone who was at least a Supreme Being were inherently of immense value, so if he did this, he definitely would not lose out in the end.
Void Source Pavilion.
In the name of the Void Godchild, Ye Tian released information to all the Void Children, publicly offering a bounty to collect a Soul Source Tool, with the price set at 50,000 points.
Soon, within the Source Sea, many Void Children were shocked by this news.
