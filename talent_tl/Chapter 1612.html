Chapter 1611: Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor Who Severed His Own Cultivation!

At the meeting.
The Calamity Tribulation Ancestor ordered, “Tianxu, according to the current progress, it’s probably impossible to completely transform Wind Ancient River into an Eternal Tribulation Domain, so I order you to immediately enter Wind Ancient River and take away the Innate Eternal Lifeform when it is about to be born. In that case, no one will be able to stop you. Once you bring the Innate Eternal Lifeform here, even an Eternal Realm expert will find it very difficult to snatch the Innate Eternal Lifeform from us.”
“Calamity Tribulation Ancestor, I am at Half-Step Eternal Realm and can’t enter Wind Ancient River!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor said in a deep voice.
“No, you can enter. You know that!” Calamity Tribulation Ancestor said.
Suddenly.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor realized what Calamity Tribulation Ancestor meant by those words.
“Calamity Tribulation Ancestor, you want me to sever my cultivation?”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor was furious.
“That’s right!” Calamity Tribulation Ancestor said. “Your cultivation is already very difficult to advance any further. Severing your cultivation is not necessarily a bad thing, and after severing it, you can still continue cultivating. It won’t take long before you can ascend back to Half-Step Eternal Realm.”
“But if I sever my cultivation, my own potential and foundation will also be severed, and I will never be able to advance any further in the future!” Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor said angrily.
“This is for the sake of the clan!”
Calamity Tribulation Ancestor said.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor was unwilling to accept it. Although his hope of further progress was very slim, he was at least the third Tribulation Ancestor of the Eternal Tribulation Race!
But he did not dare oppose Calamity Tribulation Ancestor, because Calamity Tribulation Ancestor was truly powerful. If not for Calamity Tribulation Ancestor, the Eternal Tribulation Race would have been destroyed long ago.
“Calamity Tribulation Ancestor, I will sever my cultivation and enter Wind Ancient River!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor reluctantly agreed.
That’s right, everything was for the sake of the clan.
An Innate Eternal Lifeform, if used properly, would be enough to bring enormous benefits to the Eternal Tribulation Race, and could even allow the Eternal Tribulation Race to completely soar and rise, no longer having to cower within the Supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain.
In the future, the Eternal Tribulation Race would even dare to appear openly in the Source Sea.
Time passed——
On this day, Ye Tian clearly sensed changes in the outside world.
Although he was sealed, he could still initially sense the situation outside.
At this moment, the phenomenon of the Innate Eternal Lifeform’s birth had already begun to appear.
“It’s time to prepare!”
Ye Tian immediately Annihilated this clone body of his.
He did not want to waste any more time. If he missed the opportunity and let the Eternal Tribulation Race instantly take away the Innate Eternal Lifeform, then he would have no time to encounter the Innate Eternal Lifeform.
After all, he was sealed now and could not copy the Innate Eternal Lifeform, so he could only come in again.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian accelerated time and soon condensed a clone body, then had the clone body carry that severed finger of an Eternal Realm mighty figure. After all, once he got close to the Innate Eternal Lifeform, he would probably have to fight the Eternal Tribulation Race.
No one knew what force the Eternal Tribulation Race would dispatch at the final moment, so he had to go there in his strongest state.
Very quickly, Ye Tian arrived at Wind Ancient River, entered it, and then traversed at top speed to the vicinity of the place where the Innate Eternal Lifeform was about to be born.
At this moment, the phenomenon here had already begun to expand. It probably wouldn’t be long before the entire Wind Ancient River was covered by it.
Boom!!!!!
A phantom of a one-horned creature, unimaginably vast, crouched and roared in the void, its voice rippling and shaking the sky.
This was the phantom of the phenomenon.
Very soon.
Several nearby Supremes sensed it and immediately reported it.
Thus, countless Supremes learned that an Innate Eternal Lifeform had been born here and rushed over.
At the same time, the Eternal Tribulation Race’s densely packed armies also entered this place.
Since it could no longer be hidden, there was no need to hide it anymore.
Supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain.
Under the order of Calamity Tribulation Ancestor, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor began severing his cultivation.
In the void, blades formed from eternal laws cut into his body from the nothingness.
Waves of screams came from Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor’s mouth, and his aura kept dropping.
Ninefold Realm!
Eightfold Realm!
Sevenfold Realm!
It kept dropping until the peak of the Sixfold Realm, and only then did Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor stop.
Only the peak of the Sixfold Realm could enter Wind Ancient River, so reducing his cultivation to this level was enough.
At this time, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor quickly recovered from the injuries caused by severing his cultivation by consuming a precious healing treasure.
“Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor, although your cultivation has dropped to the peak of the Sixfold Realm, you have after all mastered an Eternal Technique, and your grasp of eternal laws far exceeds that of other Sixfold Realm experts. Moreover, your Eternal Body has reached the Perfected level, and your body is incomparably strong. You are enough to display strength comparable to the peak of the Eightfold Realm. With the aid of the clan’s treasures, you can even raise your strength to the Ninefold Realm. In Wind Ancient River, you will be an Invincible existence. I hope you won’t let the Eternal Tribulation Race down.” Calamity Tribulation Ancestor said.
“Don’t worry, I will definitely bring the Innate Eternal Lifeform back!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor guaranteed, though he was in a very bad mood. He casually dealt with the reply, then prepared to head to Wind Ancient River, not wanting to talk much more with Calamity Tribulation Ancestor here.
Very soon, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor descended upon Wind Ancient River.
-----------------------
The place where the Innate Eternal Lifeform was born.
Countless Supremes poured into this place and saw the phenomenon, their hearts filled with excitement.
This was an Innate Eternal Lifeform; its value was beyond imagination.
Moreover, outside, one Half-Step Eternal Realm expert after another had already offered sky-high prices. If they obtained the Innate Eternal Lifeform, even if they could not keep it themselves, handing it over to a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert would still earn them enough resources to cultivate to the Ninefold Realm.
“Kill the Eternal Tribulation Race!”
The army of Supremes and the army of the Eternal Tribulation Race began slaughtering each other.
Suddenly.
A gray palm emerged from the void and, with one strike, smashed countless Supremes into pieces.
The power of this strike was terrifying beyond compare!
“No!!!”
One Supreme after another was filled with terror, their bodies crushed into pieces under a terrifying force.
At this moment, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor appeared.
Some Supremes recognized Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor and immediately revealed expressions of disbelief.
“It’s the Eternal Tribulation Race’s third ancestor—Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor. How can he appear here? Only Eternal Tribulation Race members at the Sixfold Realm are allowed in here, and he is at Half-Step Eternal Realm!”
“No, he severed his cultivation. He is now at the Sixfold Realm!”
“Run! Even Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor at the Sixfold Realm is far from something we can deal with!”
A large number of Supremes fled, but Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor did not pursue them, because protecting the Innate Eternal Lifeform was the most important thing!
At this very moment, the Innate Eternal Lifeform was formally born.
And Ye Tian also approached here at the first possible moment, while activating his Copying talent.
