Chapter 1598: Source God Pavilion!

"What great opportunity?"
Ye Tian was somewhat curious. He stared at this black statue, feeling that its material was quite special, but he didn't discover any use for it.
The artifact spirit pointed at the black statue and said, "Master, do you know what this is?"
"I don't know!" Ye Tian shook his head.
The artifact spirit said excitedly, "This is the credential for the Source God Pavilion. By relying on this black statue, one can join the Source God Pavilion."
"Source God Pavilion? What kind of power is this?" Ye Tian asked hurriedly.
The artifact spirit said, "Strictly speaking, the Source God Pavilion is not a power, but a loose organization or a chamber of commerce. Within the Source God Pavilion, Supreme Beings can trade items with each other, post missions, and purchase intelligence. In the Source Sea, it is often incredibly difficult to find a treasure that suits oneself or is relatively rare; sometimes it's even impossible to buy them. But in the Source God Pavilion, they can be purchased. Therefore, many Supreme Beings want to join the Source God Pavilion. However, the requirements for entering the pavilion are very high. Besides being invited by the Source God Pavilion, one must obtain a Source God Statue to join."
"When the previous master was still alive, the Source God Pavilion invited him, but the previous master was too arrogant and did not want to join. Otherwise, if the master had the protection of the Twelve Ancestors of the Source God Pavilion at the end, he likely wouldn't have fallen."
The Twelve Ancestors of the Source God Pavilion?
Ye Tian continued listening and soon learned what level of strength the Twelve Ancestors of the Source God Pavilion possessed.
Half-step Eternal Realm!
The realm above Supreme Being is the Eternal Realm, but the artifact spirit was unsure if there were actually any Eternal Realm beings within the Source God Pavilion. However, Half-step Eternal Realm beings who had surpassed the Ninth Tier Supreme Being did exist, and there were quite a few of such experts.
The Void Source Ancestor was a Half-step Eternal Realm, but belonged to the weaker side among them. The Twelve Ancestors of the Source God Pavilion, however, were top-tier existences among Half-step Eternal Realms. Furthermore, when the Twelve Ancestors united, they were enough to deter countless forces in the Source Sea.
The Twelve Ancestors did not like conflict but were very fond of trading, which was why they established the Source God Pavilion, though the requirements for joining were very high.
It was conceivable that obtaining a Source God Statue to join the Source God Pavilion was indeed a great opportunity.
Subsequently, under the instructions of the artifact spirit, Ye Tian began to refine the statue.
After a long time, Ye Tian finally succeeded in refining it.
"Master, this statue is a credential. You can now use it to cross the Source God Void and enter the Source God Pavilion hidden deep within the void, allowing you to trade with other Supreme Beings within the Source God Pavilion," the artifact spirit said.
"Mm!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Immediately, he had his duplicated body carry a large amount of resources and head toward the Source God Pavilion.
Buzz!!!!!
The Source God Statue enveloped Ye Tian, instantly traversing an incredibly vast distance and arriving directly at the Source God Pavilion.
This method of traversal was even more convenient than the Void Source Hall, but it could not be used during battle. Because the void ripples during combat, the Source God Statue cannot be successfully activated.
Otherwise, the previous Tea Sea Source God would not have fallen.
...
Source God Pavilion.
Ye Tian entered, and as soon as he arrived, he sensed dozens of Supreme Beings.
The members of the Source God Pavilion were not many, and furthermore, these Supreme Beings could not stay in the Source God Pavilion all the time. Therefore, unless there was a gathering, the number of Supreme Beings here would not be very high.
"New member detected, please register your information!"
The voice of the Source God Pavilion's artifact spirit appeared in Ye Tian's mind.
Ye Tian immediately followed the artifact spirit's instructions to register his member identity in the Source God Pavilion, using "Endless Ancestor" as his new title.
Shortly after.
He began to wander around the Source God Pavilion.
Shops could be opened within the Source God Pavilion. These shops were not actual physical storefronts, and no one was present, but they would list what treasures were for sale. If one was interested, they could communicate with the shop owner.
This was also a unique form of trading.
Ye Tian also saw many missions in one area of the Source God Pavilion. Most were trade-type missions, such as someone collecting Original Source Liquid, but these missions were almost all bartering items for items.
Ye Tian saw many good things among the trade missions, some of which would even be of great help to him.
By questioning the artifact spirit, Ye Tian learned about the situation of the Source God Pavilion.
For example, the Source God Pavilion would hold a large-scale trade fair every one hundred Great Tribulation Eras. Many Supreme Beings would prepare treasures to come here and trade.
The time for the next large-scale trade fair was twenty-three Great Tribulation Eras away.
Ye Tian planned to enter the Source God Pavilion to trade after twenty-three Great Tribulation Eras; only then could he trade for good things.
During these twenty-three Great Tribulation Eras, Ye Tian used his identity as Xu Chen of the Ancient Star Race to scour a round of resources from the Ancient Star Race to prepare for the Source God Pavilion's large-scale trade fair.
·········Seeking Flowers··· ·····
In the blink of an eye, twenty-three Great Tribulation Eras passed.
On this day.
Ye Tian entered the Source God Pavilion once again. Upon his arrival this time, he saw a large number of Supreme Beings.
His realm as a First Tier Supreme Being was quite inconspicuous here. Even Ninth Tier Supreme Beings were numerous, and there might even be Half-step Eternal Realm experts present.
There were many trading areas within the Source God Pavilion, and any Supreme Being could use a shop for free to sell their own items.
Ye Tian also set up a shop, listing the things he didn't need. He didn't require bartering; they could be purchased with Eternal Coins.
Eternal Coins were a type of currency used for trading between Supreme Beings. This currency was not a simple currency, but one condensed from the essence of Eternal Source Materials combined with some special treasure materials.
.... ... 0
Depending on the value of the Eternal Source Materials, Eternal Coins were divided into three types: Low-grade Eternal Coins, Mid-grade Eternal Coins, and High-grade Eternal Coins.
Ye Tian exchanged his unneeded items for Eternal Coins to make future trading more convenient.
Moreover, Ye Tian's pricing was very cheap, so many Supreme Beings entered Ye Tian's shop to buy. He only left an avatar here to manage the shop, while his true body went to find suitable trading partners.
Before long, Ye Tian actually found a trading partner.
The other party was a Third Tier Peak Supreme Being who requested to trade for Original Source Liquid. Among the many treasures listed, one was a treasure that Ye Tian needed.
"Eternal Ancient Pine Leaf!"
The Eternal Ancient Pine Leaf had an effect similar to an Enlightenment Leaf, but it was suitable for First and Second Tier Supreme Beings. If one consumed an Eternal Ancient Pine Leaf, it could increase the chance of breaking through a bottleneck by a certain margin. After all, it could instantly elevate a person's comprehension and their perception of Eternal Rules to an unimaginable degree.
More importantly, the Eternal Ancient Pine Leaf would not affect one's foundation and had none of the side effects of Original Source Liquid.
However, while Original Source Liquid could 100% help a First Tier Supreme Being break through to a Second Tier Supreme Being, the Eternal Ancient Pine Leaf could not guarantee it.
But Ye Tian didn't care for Original Source Liquid; he did, however, value the Eternal Ancient Pine Leaf.
Immediately, he walked up to this Third Tier Supreme Being and requested to trade.
