Chapter 1413: Cooperation and Transaction with the War Gate
!
The Sacred Stele.
Ye Tian first learned of the existence of this treasure from Saintess Qingyi, which could be called the most precious opportunity in a Sacred Realm.
Later, he also learned about it from within the War Gate, gaining a deeper understanding of the Sacred Stele.
The Sacred Stele was useless to Invincible Domain Ancestors, but for Supreme Domain Ancestors, it was a truly great opportunity, helping them condense Sacred Souls. Even if they couldn't condense a Sacred Soul, it could still greatly boost their soul's sacred light.
Since this Sacred Stele had not yet fully formed, Ye Tian couldn't be sure how many... Sacred Stele slots it contained; it could be dozens, hundreds, or even more.
Ye Tian estimated that this Sacred Stele would take approximately another 3 million years to truly form.
"Millions of years isn't a long time, I must plan carefully!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He knew very well how strong the Sacred Stele's appeal was to.. Supreme Domain Ancestors. Once the news of the Sacred Stele's emergence spread, those Sacred Lands and even Sacred Gates would likely be alarmed, perhaps coming to seize Sacred Stele slots.
At that time, other Sacred Gates would not fear the War Gate and would undoubtedly acquire Sacred Stele slots at all costs! After all, Sacred Realms were too few, and Sacred Steles were also rare, and each Sacred Stele slot used meant one less. If they occupied many Sacred Stele slots, they might be able to cultivate several more Sacred Soul-level Supreme Domain Ancestors for their forces, which would be enough to strengthen the power of their forces! Even Sacred Gates wanted more Sacred Soul-level Supreme Domain Ancestors! Perhaps the Peerless Domain Ancestors didn't care about this matter, but those Invincible Domain Ancestors would likely strive for this opportunity for their disciples or descendants.
Once many Invincible Domain Ancestors descended upon the Profound Void Sacred Realm, Ye Tian would also be unable to contend with them. If he refused them and didn't give them slots, humanity would suffer.
Therefore—"It seems I must discuss this with the War Gate!"
Ye Tian murmured.
If the War Gate could dispatch a large number of Invincible Domain Ancestors to guard the Profound Void Sacred Realm for a short period, then there would be nothing to worry about.
Moreover, he could also sell these slots to the War Gate, and those Supreme Domain Ancestors who wanted to obtain slots could do so, but would need to pay some resources and treasures.
This way, he would also make a huge profit.
War Gate.
Ye Tian's clone sought an audience with the Peerless Domain Ancestor, Fire-Pattern War Ancestor.
Soon, Fire-Pattern War Ancestor allowed Ye Tian to enter his mansion.
"Greetings, Lord Fire-Pattern War Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said respectfully.
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, what is the matter?"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor asked.
"Fire-Pattern War Ancestor, the Profound Void Sacred Realm where I reside is about to give birth to a Sacred Stele!"
Ye Tian said.
"Oh, a Sacred Stele!"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor showed some interest.
Although the Sacred Stele was useless to him, it was a great opportunity for Supreme Domain Ancestors.
If there were enough Sacred Stele slots, it would be enough to have a huge impact on a Sacred Gate and could enhance the core strength of the Sacred Gate.
After all, when Peerless Domain Ancestors didn't make a move, Invincible Domain Ancestors were revered, and once a Sacred Soul-level Supreme Domain Ancestor advanced to an Invincible Domain Ancestor, they would certainly become a Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor in the future, which was why the War Gate had so many Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors.
"You want the War Gate to send some Invincible Domain Ancestors to guard the Profound Void Sacred Realm, don't you?"
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor saw through Ye Tian's thoughts at a glance and said.
"Yes!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor pondered for a moment and said: "I can guard it, and dispatch some Invincible Domain Ancestors to guard the Profound Void Sacred Realm, but you still need to provide some Sacred Stele slots, ten, 10 slots, this is the War Gate's requirement.
If there are enough Sacred Stele slots, those Supreme Domain Ancestors of the War Gate can also pay a huge price to purchase slots. How many exactly you sell is up to you, how about it?"
"That works!"
Ye Tian agreed.
Subsequently.
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor summoned some Invincible Domain Ancestors, informed them about the Sacred Stele matter, and had them go guard the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
Regarding.. Fire-Pattern War Ancestor's command, these Invincible Domain Ancestors naturally wouldn't object and agreed one by one.
At the same time, the matter of the Sacred Stele also spread within the War Gate, and some Supreme Domain Ancestors on the Supreme Ranking sought out Fire-Pattern War Ancestor, requesting to obtain a Sacred Stele slot.
Fire-Pattern War Ancestor then led these people to Ye Tian, letting them directly inquire with Ye Tian.
On this day.
A Supreme Domain Ancestor came to find Ye Tian.
"Hello, Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, I am Wulong Domain Ancestor, ranked 58th on the War Gate's Supreme Ranking. I wish to purchase a Sacred Stele slot, I wonder if that is possible?"
Wulong Domain Ancestor said expectantly.
"That is indeed possible!"
Ye Tian said.
Immediately, he stated some requirements, which included two sacred artifacts and a pile of resources, and even many domain technique inheritances.
It could be said that ordinary Supreme Domain Ancestors simply couldn't produce the things he demanded, and even the most top-tier Supreme Domain Ancestors would find it difficult to produce them.
Even if an Invincible Domain Ancestor on the Supreme Ranking could produce them, their net worth would shrink considerably.
After Wulong Domain Ancestor finished listening, he was very hesitant, but this was an opportunity.
Moreover, Sacred Stele slots were limited; once he hesitated for a few days, the slots might be gone.
"Alright, I agree!"
Wulong Domain Ancestor quickly said.
Immediately, the two signed an agreement, which was considered a preliminary completion of the transaction.
In the following days, one after another, Invincible Domain Ancestors on the Supreme Ranking sought out Ye Tian, and Ye Tian agreed to each of them.
But when the number of slots reached fifty, he stopped selling these slots.
Firstly, he had already obtained enough resources, and furthermore, humanity also needed Sacred Stele slots, so he couldn't sell all the slots.
After the matter was settled, the Invincible Domain Ancestors of the War Gate, along with this group of Supreme Domain Ancestors who had obtained Sacred Stele slots, began their journey to the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
They did not use treasures like the Myriad Void Star Leaf to rush over; after all, it was still very early, there was no need to.... waste treasures.
So, they slowly flew like this, and their strength was very great, so their flying speed was relatively fast.
Tens of thousands of years later, these Domain Ancestors arrived at the Daoshan Domain and entered the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
And Ye Tian's main body immediately received these Domain Ancestors.
This time, the War Gate dispatched a total of eight Invincible Domain Ancestors. These eight Invincible Domain Ancestors were not weak; all of them were among the Thirty-Six War Emperors, including the Invincible Domain Ancestor Ye Tian had met before—Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor.
Besides Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, the strength of the other eight Invincible Domain Ancestors was not inferior in the slightest.
However, they all restrained their aura, so Ye Tian couldn't determine what level they had reached.
Perhaps they were all Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, or perhaps all First-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors.
In short, with these Invincible Domain Ancestors guarding, unless other Sacred Gates dispatched a large number of First or Second-Step Invincible Domain Ancestors, otherwise, there would be no way to threaten Ye Tian.
