Chapter 1470: The Power of Ultimate Ancestral Techniques
!
Boom!!!! Ye Tian's Supreme Domain Body underwent a qualitative change under the tempering of a large amount of Primordial Ancestral Spring water.
The peak of the Supreme Domain Body was originally the limit of domain bodies; an Ancestor's body was no longer considered a domain body.
Originally, no treasure could temper a peak Supreme-level domain body, but with the help of the Primordial Ancestral Spring, Ye Tian was finally able to break through the peak of the Supreme-level domain body.
Crack!!!! Ye Tian's Supreme-level domain body shattered into countless fragments, but it quickly restored itself.
At this moment, he had already broken through the shackles and began to frantically absorb the energy here to push his Supreme-level domain body toward a breakthrough.
Time passed slowly, and the second transformation of Ye Tian's Supreme-level domain body was completely finished.
"Ten times!"
Ye Tian felt his brand-new Supreme-level domain body; it was ten times stronger than before.
While a ten-fold increase in the Supreme-level domain body didn't mean his overall combat power reached ten times its previous level, it had still increased by several times! At the peak stage of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, every bit of progress usually required endless years, and sometimes there was no progress at all.
But now, having increased his strength several times over in such a short period, it was already terrifying.
After his breakthrough, Ye Tian looked at his family again.
His son and daughter's cultivation levels were also rising very quickly, already reaching the late stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm. Moreover, their speed of absorbing the spring's energy was faster than that of ordinary Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, as Ye Tian had bestowed upon them many Saint Talents and even Ancestral Talents.
Therefore, other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors simply couldn't compare to them.
It could be said that before the Primordial Ancestral Spring was completely depleted, they would definitely be able to break through to the peak stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm.
In the blink of an eye, another several hundred thousand years passed.
On this day.
The Primordial Ancestral Spring was completely depleted; the spring water inside had been exhausted.
Boom boom boom!!! One after another, powerful peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and Supreme Soul Ancestors revealed their auras. For instance, several peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the Battle Gate had also completed their second transformations at this time, their strengths greatly increased, though they were still like ants compared to Ye Tian.
As for the other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, almost all of them had improved to the late or peak stages of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm.
The same was true for the Human Race; the Supreme Soul Ancestors had all reached the late stage of the Supreme Soul Ancestor realm.
It was a pity that the energy of the Primordial Ancestral Spring was limited, and with a massive energy consumer like Ye Tian present, it wasn't possible for all the Supreme Soul Ancestors and Supreme Domain Ancestors to break through to the peak stages of their respective realms.
However, they were still very satisfied.
Because after the tempering of the Primordial Ancestral Spring, their future cultivation would be much easier, and they had full confidence in reaching the peak stages of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor and Supreme-level Soul Ancestor realms in the future.
In the following days.
Ye Tian moved practitioners from the Profound Void Sacred Realm to the Dark Soul Domain. The Battle Gate naturally wouldn't stop him, and the other forces no longer had the heart to interfere.
Consequently, the number of humans in the Dark Soul Domain grew larger and larger.
With a massive supply of resources, the number of Invincible Domain Ancestors in the Human Race increased, but it remained very difficult to give birth to a Supreme Soul Ancestor.
This was because the initial benefits of the Dark Soul Domain were gone, and all practitioners now cultivated just as they did in the outside world.
For example, in the Dark Sacred Realm, even the descendants of Ancestors found it very difficult to become Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
The current Dark Soul Domain was the same; it was too hard to give birth to a new Supreme Soul Ancestor.
Ye Tian didn't mind this; as long as the Human Race could produce a large number of Invincible Soul Ancestors, that was enough.
With a large number of Invincible Soul Ancestors, some would eventually break through to the Supreme Soul Ancestor realm in the future.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian himself continued his secluded cultivation, constantly using his duplicated bodies to evolve Ancestral Laws.
He prepared to start creating his own Ancestral Law, using it to charge toward the Ancestor realm.
His Ancestor clone wasn't idle either; the clone also needed to cultivate to improve its strength. Otherwise, after one hundred domain years when the Ancestors descended, they definitely wouldn't let him off.
Especially the Destruction Ancestor, who certainly wouldn't mind killing this Ancestor clone of his.
It wouldn't matter much if the Ancestor clone fell, but without its protection, the Human Race would be in trouble. Even his main body might be forced to hide within the Battle Gate.
After all, the Human Race had offended almost every force except for the Battle Gate this time by occupying the resources of the Dark Soul Domain—resources so vast that even Ancestors would be moved.
Of course.
To win over the Ancestors of the Battle Gate, Ye Tian also yielded a portion of the Dark Soul Domain's resources to them, which would allow the Battle Gate to produce a large number of Invincible Domain Ancestors and some Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
At the very least, after one hundred domain years, the two Ancestors of the Battle Gate would definitely help him.
...."It is very difficult for an Ancestor to improve their strength. Although the Dark Soul Domain has produced resources useful even to Ancestors, the resources that will truly cause Ancestors to fight to the death will only appear after one hundred domain years..."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Therefore, he had to find a way to improve the strength of his Ancestor clone.
He had communicated with the two Ancestors of the Battle Gate. For an Ancestor to improve their strength, they mainly needed to increase their Ancestral Power, strengthen their Ancestor Body, and bolster their Ancestor Soul.
This was similar to Domain Ancestors—it was simply... an increase in cultivation.
Ancestors didn't have specific realm divisions, only combat power classifications.
Ordinary Ancestors, Elite Ancestors, and Top-tier Ancestors.
Most Ancestors were Ordinary Ancestors; even the Battle Ancestor was an Ordinary Ancestor, while the Realm Ancestor was a powerful Elite Ancestor, second only to the Top-tier Ancestors.
Improving cultivation required time... but there was another way to significantly increase strength, and that was to master an Ultimate Ancestral Technique!
However, no more than five Ancestors had mastered an Ultimate Ancestral Technique. Of course, this was public information; if any Ancestor had hidden their strength, others would have no way of knowing.
The Realm Ancestor mastered one Ultimate Ancestral Technique. The Saint Ancestor, the Destruction Ancestor, the Eternal Sect Leader, and the Space-Time Ancestor—these five Ancestors each mastered an Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
It was precisely because of these Ultimate Ancestral Techniques that they were so powerful.
If Ye Tian could master an Ultimate Ancestral Technique, even if his Ancestor cultivation was lower, he would immediately possess the combat power of a weaker Elite Ancestor.
By then, with a slight increase in his Ancestor cultivation and by wielding a portion of the Dark Soul Domain's power, he would definitely be confident in fighting the Destruction Ancestor.
