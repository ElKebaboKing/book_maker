Chapter 1492: Attempting to Trade with the Bone Clan
!
"Hahaha, the harvest this time is simply too massive!!!"
Ye Tian was incredibly excited.
Three Bone King beads and one hundred and twenty-three Bone General beads.
What did this signify? It was equivalent to him killing three Top Ancestors and over a hundred Elite Ancestors in this single excursion.
And how many were there in the entire Dark Forbidden Domain... at least on the surface, there weren't even a hundred Elite Ancestors; at most, there were no more than ten.
It had to be said that the Bone Clan was incredibly powerful. This level of loss wouldn't shake their foundation, but for him, it was a monumental gain.
Once he refined all these bone beads, his strength would definitely rise to the absolute pinnacle of the Top Ancestor level.
And his pinnacle as a Top Ancestor would likely be comparable to an ordinary Supreme Ancestor.
"Refine!"
One by one, the bone beads were absorbed and refined by Ye Tian, tempering his body and strengthening his Ancestral power. His cultivation continued to rise, and his strength grew steadily.
In the blink of an eye, a hundred million years passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had finished refining all of the hundred-plus Bone General beads, and it was becoming difficult for his strength to increase further.
"There are still two Bone King beads left. Let's continue refining!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Boom!!!! The energy from the Bone King beads surged into Ye Tian's body, frantically tempering his Ancestral physique.
Under these circumstances, Ye Tian began to progress again, but the speed of improvement remained slow.
Ye Tian knew very well that his strength had reached the pinnacle of the Top Ancestor level. Even after refining these two Bone King beads, it would be difficult for his strength to increase by much...
In the blink of an eye, another few tens of millions of years passed.
Ye Tian had completely refined these two Bone King beads, but his cultivation could no longer progress even a single bit. His combat power had reached an absolute limit.
"I can't progress any further!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Even if he obtained more Bone King beads, he wouldn't be able to increase his strength.
Unless he could break through this shackle and promote to the Supreme Ancestor level.
However, he was well aware that the transition from Top Ancestor to Supreme Ancestor was not something that could be crossed simply by increasing cultivation. It surely involved some secret. Otherwise, there wouldn't be a total lack of legends regarding Supreme Ancestors in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Even Ancestors like the Realm Ancestor only knew of ancient Ancestors in deep slumber, but those... ancient Ancestors were also only at the Top Ancestor realm. No Ancestor had ever stepped into the Supreme Ancestor level.
If there truly were Supreme Ancestors, they would have swept through the Bone Sea Forbidden Land long ago.
Since his cultivation could not improve and his Ultimate Ancestral Techniques were already at the peak of what Ancestral Techniques could be, he had no way to continue improving them. Once an Ultimate Ancestral Technique is created, it is the limit; that is why it is called "Ultimate."
With neither cultivation nor Ancestral Techniques able to improve, he had no way to increase his strength. He didn't even know how to go about increasing it.
Even if his Copying Talent was incredible, without a method to increase his strength, he naturally couldn't progress.
"When I left the Bone Sea Forbidden Land before, the Bone Clan dispatched a terrifying powerhouse. That aura definitely surpassed a Top Ancestor. Even a Supreme Ancestor might not be able to defeat them. That person was definitely comparable to a top-tier Supreme Ancestor.
Therefore, the Bone Clan must know the secret of how to break through to the Supreme Ancestor level!"
Ye Tian thought.
But would the Bone Clan tell him this secret? Suddenly.
Ye Tian took out a bone sword.
This... bone sword was a Supreme Imperial Weapon of the Bone Clan. He had already refined it, but he was unable to unleash its full power.
After all, this was a bone sword of the Bone Clan and was not suited for him.
However, to the Bone Clan, the value of this Supreme Imperial Weapon was definitely high. Perhaps he could use this... Supreme Imperial Weapon to exchange for the secret of reaching the Supreme Ancestor level.
Ye Tian did not bring this... Imperial Weapon with him, but instead sent a duplicated body to the location.
Even if this duplicated body perished in the Bone Sea... the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
As soon as Ye Tian stepped into this place, he immediately drew the attention of the Bone Kings in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
At this moment.
The Bone Kings did not attack Ye Tian. The other Bone Generals and Bone Soldiers all retreated to the center of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
At the same time, arrays rose up one after another, protecting the center of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
Clearly, they did not dare to fight Ye Tian anymore, fearing the loss of too many clansmen.
Ye Tian went deep into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land and locked eyes with the Bone Kings.
"I am here to negotiate!"
Ye Tian spoke.
"Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain, what do you want to negotiate?"
A Bone King spoke.
The reason he replied to Ye Tian was not... because he actually wanted to talk, but to stall for time, waiting for a Bone Emperor to descend.
"I want to trade with the Bone Clan. I will return your clan's Supreme Imperial Weapon, and in exchange, you tell me how to step into the Supreme Ancestor level. How about it?"
Ye Tian said.
"We will consider it!"
The Bone King replied.
But deep down, the Bone King sneered: "You want to break through to the Supreme Ancestor realm? Simply wishful thinking!"
A Supreme Imperial Weapon was indeed very precious, and the Bone Clan did not want to lose it, but that didn't mean they had no way to snatch it back.
Once the Great Calamity of the Dark Forbidden Domain arrived, the Bone Emperor could enter the Dark Forbidden Domain. At that time, suppressing Ye Tian would be easy.
Even if Ye Tian's clones could be condensed again and again, if his original body perished, he would be dead for good.
At that time, Ye Tian's original body wouldn't be able to run.
So, they only needed to wait for a while. There was absolutely no need to trade with Ye Tian.
But he wouldn't tell Ye Tian this.
"He's here!"
The Bone Kings thought excitedly.
Rumble!!!! A Bone Emperor descended, his aura instantly sealing off the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
At the same time, the Bone Kings moved, attempting to intercept Ye Tian and prevent him from escaping.
However, Ye Tian had no intention of leaving. He looked directly at this Bone Emperor.
Crack!!!! A hundred-meter-tall Bone Emperor wearing golden battle armor appeared before Ye Tian, emitting a terrifying aura.
However, Ye Tian also felt the suppression of this world upon this Bone Emperor.
Clearly, this Bone Emperor could not exert his full strength within the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
"The Bone Sea Forbidden Land only allows Bone Kings to act at most. This Bone Emperor must have paid a price to force his way in!"
Ye Tian guessed.
Boom!!!! The Bone Emperor attacked, giving Ye Tian no chance to negotiate.
However, the opponent didn't seem prepared to kill Ye Tian, but rather wanted to capture him.
After all, killing Ye Tian would only destroy a clone, which was of little significance. If he could capture Ye Tian, he might be able to study the secrets of the clones through this body or use a secret technique to kill Ye Tian across space.
A bone finger pierced through the air, its power several times stronger than that Supreme Imperial Weapon.
Moreover, such an attack wasn't just a one-off. Previously, that Bone King could only activate the Supreme Imperial Weapon for a single attack. Thus, the gap between the two was massive.
Swish swish swish!!!! One Ultimate Ancestral Defense Technique after another was instantly deployed. Coupled with the fact that Ye Tian's strength was much greater than before, the power of these Ultimate Ancestral Defense Techniques was naturally even stronger.
Crack!!!! Layer after layer of defensive shields were pierced, but in the end, the Bone Emperor only pierced through thirteen layers of defensive shields before his power was spent.
"Your Excellency, if you continue to attack, I will also take action. When that time comes, you won't be able to protect these Bone Generals and Bone Kings. As for me, I am merely a clone and do not fear death.
Shall we negotiate, or continue the slaughter?"
Ye Tian said coldly.
The Bone Emperor stopped and looked directly at Ye Tian, saying, "You are very strong. Your realm is only equivalent to a Bone King, but no Bone King could withstand a single blow from me without being injured. Your strength is not much different from a weaker Bone Emperor, and in terms of defense, you are comparable to some powerful Bone Emperors."
"Thank you for the praise!"
Ye Tian smiled.
The Bone Emperor said in a heavy voice, "What do you want to negotiate?"
"A Supreme Imperial Weapon in exchange for the secret of reaching the Supreme Ancestor level. The Supreme Ancestors of our Dark Forbidden Domain are equivalent to your Bone Emperors. Although the paths we walk are different, there might be similarities in some areas. Therefore, I want to know how to break through the bottleneck!"
Ye Tian requested.
The Bone Emperor fell silent and did not answer immediately...
"Not enough... the Supreme Imperial Weapon is only temporarily in your hands. When the day of the Great Calamity comes, my clan will still have a way to take it back!"
The Bone Emperor said.
"Is that so?"
Ye Tian sneered. "If I attack this Bone Sea Forbidden Land again and again, how many... clansmen will your Bone Clan lose? Unless you guard this place forever, once you leave, I will even do my best to destroy that—Gate!"
At the mention of the Gate, the Bone Emperor's expression changed drastically, and his aura began to turn violent.
"You dare!!!"
The Bone Emperor roared in rage.
"If you don't agree to this trade, I will dare!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
