Chapter 1481: The Six Great Forbidden Lands of the Dark Forbidden Zone, the Ancestors' Graves
!
As the Ancestors departed one by one, the crisis of the Profound Void Ancestral World naturally vanished.
At this time, the Realm Ancestor and the War Ancestor had not yet left.
In truth, the Realm Ancestor and the War Ancestor were extremely curious about the promotion of the Profound Void Holy World into the Profound Void Ancestral World. Of course, they did not directly inquire about the secrets of the Profound Void Ancestral World, but instead asked in a different way.
"Myriad Law Ancestor, the promotion of the Profound Void Holy World into the Profound Void Ancestral World—can this method be replicated?"
the Realm Ancestor inquired.
If this method could be replicated, then the War Gate could create one Ancestral World after another.
If that happened, how powerful would the War Gate become! "It cannot be replicated!"
Ye Tian replied, "In truth, this was a treasure I obtained from the Dark Destruction Domain back then. By integrating it into the Profound Void Holy World, I was able to allow the Profound Void Holy World to promote into the Profound Void Ancestral World!"
"A treasure obtained from the Dark Destruction Domain?"
The Realm Ancestor was shocked.
He did not think Ye Tian was lying. At Ye Tian's level, he was already at the most powerful tier of the Dark Forbidden Zone; there was no need to lie.
Therefore, he believed him.
"In that case, if the Destruction Ancestor knew the truth, he would probably regret it to death. A treasure they failed to discover was discovered by you!"
The War Ancestor laughed heartily.
The three of them chatted for a long time before dispersing.
In the days that followed, news of the Profound Void Holy World's promotion to the Profound Void Ancestral World spread. Furthermore, word got out that the Human Race's Myriad Law Ancestor possessed top-tier Ancestor combat power and even had a clone that also possessed top-tier Ancestor combat power.
This time, countless experts in the Dark Holy Domain, the Dark Destruction Domain, and the Dark Soul Domain were beyond shocked. Even the Eternal Cult and countless forces within the Dark Forbidden Zone were stunned by Ye Tian's strength.
Some even ranked Ye Tian among the top experts of the Dark Forbidden Zone. After all, with Ye Tian's main body and clone together, his combat power might even exceed that of the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor.
Of course, without a true life-and-death struggle, no one knew whose true strength was superior.
What if the Destruction Ancestor and the Holy Ancestor were hiding their strength? That was also a possibility! In the blink of an eye, several million years passed.
On this day, the Profound Void Holy World finally completed its promotion into the Profound Void Ancestral World.
Just as the promotion finished, a massive amount of treasures were born, all of which belonged to the Human Race.
Moreover, Ye Tian only collected a portion of the top-tier resources of the Profound Void Ancestral World. He did not touch the rest, allowing the practitioners of the Human Race to search for them; whoever found them owned them, and they were free to compete for them.
Even some of the weak were lucky enough to obtain treasures, rising to prominence in a single bound.
Under these circumstances, many more experts and lucky geniuses were born within the Human Race.
Perhaps after a long period of time, the Human Race would see the birth of more Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
After another period of time, Ye Tian's duplicated body left the Profound Void Ancestral World and began wandering through the Dark Forbidden Zone.
He had been an Ancestor for so long, yet he had hardly traveled through the Dark Forbidden Zone and truly didn't know much about it.
Therefore, this time he planned to learn more about the Dark Forbidden Zone.
As he traveled, he discovered that the Dark Forbidden Zone was truly vast, far exceeding his imagination. If one did not use the Ancestor's ability to traverse time and space and relied solely on flying, even an Ancestor would likely need millions of years to cross the entire Dark Forbidden Zone.
It was truly strange that such a massive Dark Forbidden Zone had only birthed three Dark Holy Lands to date.
Furthermore, there were places in the Dark Forbidden Zone that even Ancestors did not dare to enter.
For example, the forbidden land in front of Ye Tian.
At this moment, Ye Tian stood before a massive black mountain. This mountain sat right in the void of the Dark Forbidden Zone. The giant black mountain was equivalent to the volume of a Holy World, and it gave Ye Tian a very dangerous aura, as if he would perish upon entry.
"What place is this?"
Ye Tian was very curious. He did not enter immediately but instead contacted the Realm Ancestor.
"Realm Ancestor, I have discovered a massive black mountain in the Dark Forbidden Zone. What place is this?"
Ye Tian inquired.
Soon, the Realm Ancestor's Ancestor Avatar traversed time and space to arrive at Ye Tian's side. He looked at the giant black mountain before them with great trepidation and said, "This is the Calamity Black Mountain. Even Ancestors will perish if they enter. As far as I know, a total of three Ancestors have entered the Calamity Black Mountain. Two perished directly, and the other was an Elite Ancestor who perished shortly after coming out. Moreover, he was already maddened when he emerged. To make an Ancestor go mad, one can only imagine what terrifying things he encountered.
Myriad Law Ancestor, although you have a clone, I suggest you still do not go. Some attacks even ignore the connection between the clone and the main body, directly striking down the original self.
Ancestors also have Ancestor Avatars whose combat power is still very fearsome, yet no one dares to use an Ancestor Avatar to enter the Calamity Black Mountain precisely because... they worry about this problem."
Ye Tian also knew that his duplicated bodies were not omnipotent, which was why he hadn't been reckless; otherwise, he wouldn't have asked the Realm Ancestor.
At this time, the Realm Ancestor continued, "There are currently six places in the Dark Forbidden Zone that are forbidden lands for Ancestors. Even top-tier Ancestors do not dare to enter them.
There are also dozens of other places where ordinary Ancestors will perish upon entry, while top-tier Ancestors have a certain chance of survival."
Subsequently, the Realm Ancestor named those six true Ancestor Forbidden Lands.
The Calamity Black Mountain, the Destruction Swamp, the Boundless Wall, the Firmament Sword Tomb, the Illusory Dream World, and the Boundless Demon Sea.
These six great forbidden lands are known as the Ancestors' Graves, having claimed the lives of many Ancestors.
Before the birth of the first Dark Holy Land—the Dark Destruction Domain—the Dark Forbidden Zone had actually birthed the first batch of the most ancient Ancestors. Most of these ancient Ancestors... perished in these six forbidden lands.
Another portion of Ancestors disappeared; as for where they went, no one knows.
Some say they sealed themselves and entered a deep sleep, while others speculate they left the Dark Forbidden Zone.
When the Realm Ancestor mentioned leaving the Dark Forbidden Zone, Ye Tian was stunned and couldn't help but ask, "Isn't the Dark Forbidden Zone everything? Is it possible to leave the Dark Forbidden Zone? Could there be another world outside the Dark Forbidden Zone?"
"I'm not sure!"
The Realm Ancestor shook his head. "Rumor has it that every long period of time, a phantom portal appears in the Dark Forbidden Zone. Once an Ancestor enters that phantom dream, they disappear completely.
It is said that this phantom portal is... the passage to another world, but these are just rumors, and no Ancestor has ever returned.
We Ancestors have eternal lifespans. Unless we truly have no more room for improvement, only then would we think about leaving."
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian nodded.
He had thought that by breaking through to top-tier Ancestor combat power, the Dark Forbidden Zone would hold no more secrets for him. He hadn't expected the secrets to be even greater.
(End of Chapter)
