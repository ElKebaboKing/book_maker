Chapter 1476: Battle of the Ancestors, Innate Ancestral Artifact
!
Rumble!!! Somewhere in the Dark Soul Domain, an Ancestral Realm was slowly being nurtured. At the same time, the Primordial Ancestral Realm was also undergoing a transformation, giving birth to a large number of treasures.
Ye Tian and his clone, the Qing Ye Ancestor, were both in the Primordial Ancestral Realm, so they immediately collected the resources that were born. These resources were useful even to Ancestors.
It was here that the Dark Soul Domain's resistance toward Ancestors vanished.
Swish! Swish! Swish! One Ancestor after another descended upon the Dark Soul Domain. They did not go to the second Ancestral Realm, as it would take some time for that one to be fully born; their target was the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
At this moment, the Primordial Ancestral Realm was birthing a large number of treasures every second. For the Ancestors, seizing even a few of these treasures was a massive gain.
In an instant, the Ancestors descended upon the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
Some Ancestors went searching for resources, while others sought out the Qing Ye Ancestor.
"Qing Ye Ancestor, die!"
The Destruction Ancestor made his move against the Qing Ye Ancestor.
The World Ancestor wanted to help but was directly blocked by another Ancestor.
"World Ancestor, don't even think about helping the Qing Ye Ancestor!"
The Space-Time Ancestor blocked the World Ancestor.
As for the War Ancestor, his strength was too weak to intervene, and he didn't dare to block the Destruction Ancestor; otherwise, the Destruction Ancestor could easily kill him.
.... "The Destruction Ancestor really did make a move against me!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
At this moment, the Saint Ancestor had the ability to stop the Destruction Ancestor, but he did not. Instead, he was busy collecting the treasures of the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
Thus, at this moment, Ye Tian could only rely on himself.
Currently, the true combat power of Ye Tian's clone, the Qing Ye Ancestor, was that of an Elite Ancestor, second only to Top-tier Ancestors. If he mobilized the power of the Dark Soul Domain, he could exert the combat power of a Top-tier Ancestor.
Moreover, Ye Tian possessed the Ultimate Ancestral Technique: Ancestral Armor, the Ultimate Ancestral Technique: Flowing Light, and the Ultimate Ancestral Technique: Tyrant Strength.
Furthermore, Ye Tian had later deduced his Time Ancestral Technique, the Eternal Ancestral Technique, to the level of an Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
Consequently, Ye Tian was extremely well-rounded, with almost no weaknesses.
At this time.
The Destruction Ancestor did not underestimate Ye Tian's clone, the Qing Ye Ancestor. After all, to be able to promote to an Ancestor at such a rapid speed—even more monstrous than the Saint Ancestor to a certain extent—he had to strike with full force to have any chance of heavily wounding the Qing Ye Ancestor.
He was confident he could slay the Qing Ye Ancestor within a short time.
Since the Saint Ancestor wasn't acting and the World Ancestor was blocked by the Space-Time Ancestor, no one could save the Qing Ye Ancestor.
Therefore, the Destruction Ancestor used his full strength to unleash his Ultimate Ancestral Technique—Destruction Ultimate Punch! Boom!!!!! A punch came crashing in, and he even wore an Innate Ancestral Artifact glove, allowing the power of this punch to reach a terrifying level.
"Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique!"
"Ancestral Armor Ultimate Ancestral Technique!"
The clone, Qing Ye Ancestor, also went all out and mobilized the power of the Dark Soul Domain to collide with the Destruction Ancestor's strike.
Boom!! The terrifying shockwave swept across the entire Primordial Ancestral Realm, causing it to tremble.
This was a collision of Top-tier Ancestor combat power! "What? An Ultimate Ancestral Technique!"
The Destruction Ancestor was shocked.
The Qing Ye Ancestor's attack was actually an Ultimate Ancestral Technique. Although its offensive power wasn't as high as his, it wasn't much weaker either.
Moreover, the Qing Ye Ancestor also possessed a defensive Ultimate Ancestral Technique. Thus, even if his attack was inferior, he wouldn't be injured.
On the contrary, the Destruction Ancestor did not have a defensive Ultimate Ancestral Technique, and after the collision just now, he actually felt a bit uncomfortable.
"I can't kill him!"
The Destruction Ancestor gave up.
He didn't have the ability to kill a Top-tier Ancestor. If the strength gap between Ancestors wasn't too large, it was impossible to kill one. After all, an Ancestor's life-saving capabilities were too strong; they weren't defective products like those of the Oblivion Race.
On the other side.
The All-Things Ancestor sought out Ye Tian's main body.
The human race was the greatest enemy of the All-Things Holy Sect because the human Qing Ye Ancestor had killed a Quasi-Ancestor of the All-Things Holy Sect just as he was about to promote.
Therefore, the All-Things Ancestor hated the human race deeply.
He knew he couldn't kill the Qing Ye Ancestor, but he had hope of killing Ye Tian.
No matter how monstrous Ye Tian's talent was or how strong his combat power had been, once he became an Ancestor, his combat power wouldn't immediately reach the Elite Ancestor level.
If ordinary Ancestors were divided into three tiers, Ye Tian would at most be in the second or third tier.
Besides, Ye Tian had just promoted to Ancestor, and his realm was still unstable, making this the best stage to kill him.
The All-Things Ancestor also knew very well that if Ye Tian were given some time to cultivate and raise his cultivation level, his strength would definitely step into the Elite Ancestor realm.
However, he had no idea of Ye Tian's true strength, because he couldn't even imagine it.
"Myriad Law Ancestor, today you are doomed!"
The All-Things Ancestor appeared before Ye Tian, his entire aura erupting.
"Not good, the All-Things Ancestor is actually an Elite Ancestor!"
The World Ancestor and War Ancestor both sensed it and were greatly shocked.
All along, the All-Things Ancestor had displayed the combat power of an ordinary Ancestor, at most at the third tier of ordinary Ancestors, similar to the War Ancestor.
But now, the All-Things Ancestor had revealed Elite Ancestor combat power. Although he was only a weaker Elite Ancestor, he was still much stronger than an ordinary Ancestor.
The War Ancestor wanted to support Ye Tian. If two ordinary Ancestors joined forces, they might be able to contend with the All-Things Ancestor for a while.
Moreover, Ancestors were very hard to kill, so there wasn't too much to worry about.
But the next scene caused the War Ancestor's expression to shake violently.
Swish! A golden crystal suddenly transformed into a barrier, enveloping Ye Tian and the All-Things Ancestor.
Seeing the barrier formed by this golden crystal, the War Ancestor cried out in shock, "A Barrier Ancestral Crystal!"
Such a thing was extremely precious even to Ancestors; even he didn't have one. Where did the All-Things Ancestor get a Barrier Ancestral Crystal? The All-Things Ancestor indeed didn't have one originally; he had obtained it through a trade with the Destruction Ancestor.
When the Destruction Ancestor learned that the All-Things Ancestor wanted to kill the Myriad Law Ancestor, he was immediately willing to trade.
Once a Barrier Ancestral Crystal deployed its barrier, it was very difficult for the outside world to break it. Unless a Top-tier Ancestor acted, it simply couldn't be shattered.
Furthermore, the Barrier Ancestral Crystal could suppress the opponent's strength by more than thirty percent.
"Myriad Law Ancestor, you can't get out now. Under the suppression of the Barrier Ancestral Crystal, your strength is suppressed by thirty percent. Moreover, you've just become an Ancestor, so I doubt you've even obtained a single Innate Ancestral Artifact.
But I, however, have an Innate Ancestral Artifact."
The All-Things Ancestor took out a silver longsword, which radiated an incredibly terrifying aura, somewhat similar to the aura of an Ancestor.
This was an Innate Ancestral Artifact, arguably the most powerful weapon in the Dark Forbidden Zone. Only the weapon-type Sacred Objects in the top few ranks of the Sacred Object List could barely compare to an Innate Ancestral Artifact.
"An Innate Ancestral Artifact! Myriad Law Ancestor, do not collide with that Innate Ancestral Artifact! That thing can cut through an Ancestor's body and inflict heavy damage!"
The War Ancestor hurriedly warned.
Ancestors usually didn't engage in true life-and-death battles, so they rarely brought out Innate Ancestral Artifacts.
Moreover, average Ancestors didn't even possess an Innate Ancestral Artifact.
For example, the War Ancestor was very poor and lacked sufficient strength; he only had one defective Innate Ancestral Artifact and a few top-tier Sacred Objects that were slightly inferior to Innate Ancestral Artifacts.
Within the War Gate, only the World Ancestor possessed an Innate Ancestral Artifact, and he only had one.
An Innate Ancestral Artifact could only enhance combat power by a few dozen percent, but it could cut through an Ancestor's body. If the strengths were similar, the impact of an Innate Ancestral Artifact wasn't huge.
But if one side was stronger and held an Innate Ancestral Artifact, they could very quickly slay a weaker Ancestor.
The War Ancestor and World Ancestor were panicking, but Ye Tian did not panic.
Looking at the All-Things Ancestor, Ye Tian smiled: "I just happened to be lacking an Innate Ancestral Artifact. I didn't expect you to deliver one, All-Things Ancestor!"
Boom!!!! An aura second only to a Top-tier Ancestor diffused from Ye Tian's body.
At this moment, many Ancestors were shocked beyond measure, revealing expressions of utter disbelief!
