Chapter 1689 Eternal Supreme Treasure, Obtained!

Mo Mountain Prison.
Ye Tian entered Lord Muchen’s prison and fought him for a while, severely injuring him. Just as he was about to kill him, a terrifying aura suddenly burst forth from Lord Muchen.
This Lord Muchen was a Seven-Step Immortal Realm Lord, while Ye Tian was a Six-Step Immortal Realm Lord. Even though he could unleash his full strength in this prison void, his cultivation was still inferior to the other party; after all, the prison void would only suppress cultivation, not enhance it.
However, Ye Tian could fight above his level, so he was not afraid.
But now, Lord Muchen had truly revealed his strength.
Buzz!
An invisible Domain enveloped the entire prison void, and in the next moment a beam of light instantly pierced through.
Facing that beam, it was as if all things were frozen. Even Ye Tian could not evade it; his will barrier was pierced through, and even his Void True Body could hardly withstand it. His body was directly penetrated.
Not only that, but a terrifying power was also destroying Ye Tian’s copied body.
“True My Myriad Hands!”
Boom!!!
One hand after another of Lord Muchen appeared in the void, bombarding Ye Tian. The power contained in each hand was comparable to the full-force strike of a legendary eternal technique, and when joined together they shattered all innate eternal rules, even crushing will itself.
This strike and the one before it both seemed to contain eternal power, will, and innate eternal rules, fused together as if the three powers had merged into a qualitatively new force.
Very quickly.
Ye Tian suffered a severe injury and simply could not contend with Lord Muchen.
This was also the first time Ye Tian had encountered such a terrifying Immortal Realm Lord, one who was far stronger than him by far. Even if he possessed more legendary eternal techniques and an even stronger foundation, he still could not contend with him.
In the end, Ye Tian’s copied body chose to leave this prison.
Outside the prison.
Ye Tian stood before the Stele and did not leave, but instead fell into thought.
“Lord Muchen has hidden himself very deeply. If I had not tried to kill him, he would not have exposed his strength. Perhaps even Mo Mountain doesn’t know Lord Muchen’s true power?” Ye Tian murmured.
After fighting Lord Muchen, although he lost, he still saw some clues.
However, he needed to fight Lord Muchen many more times before he could verify his conclusions.
After some time, Ye Tian could once again enter this prison, so he went in again and fought Lord Muchen, only to suffer another crushing defeat.
As the long years passed, Ye Tian fought Lord Muchen many times, and gradually came to understand his strangeness.
“The power Lord Muchen uses is a force produced after fusing will, body, and innate eternal rules. It is a power only a Legendary Realm Lord should possess, yet he is merely an Immortal Realm Lord. If he truly fused body, will, and innate eternal rules, then he could not possibly be an Immortal Realm Lord; he would be a Legendary Realm Lord.”
Ye Tian thought.
Moreover, he also sensed a special aura.
The reason Lord Muchen could use that kind of eternal technique was entirely because a Domain had descended. In other words, it was that Domain that granted Lord Muchen such means.
“That Domain should be an effect attached to an Eternal Supreme Treasure.”
Ye Tian guessed.
The special aura he sensed was precisely the aura of an Eternal Supreme Treasure. He had obtained fragments of an Eternal Supreme Treasure before, so he was somewhat familiar with its aura. Lord Muchen very likely possessed an Eternal Supreme Treasure, and this Eternal Supreme Treasure could allow him to create a special Domain. Within such a Domain, he could forcibly fuse body, will, and innate eternal rules, forming a brand-new power and thereby unleashing one legendary eternal technique after another.
Such a legendary eternal technique could in fact no longer be considered a legendary eternal technique, because the power it contained belonged to a level only a Legendary Realm could unleash. Therefore, such an eternal technique could be called a half-step legendary eternal technique.
Ye Tian’s strongest eternal techniques were only top-tier legendary eternal techniques; how could they compare to half-step legendary eternal techniques?
Moreover, Ye Tian’s cultivation was weaker than the other party’s, and his combat experience was even somewhat inferior.
Under such circumstances, Ye Tian naturally was far from his match.
But that did not mean Ye Tian could do nothing to him.
An Eternal Supreme Treasure, one that might even be complete, was enough to drive any Legendary Realm Lord mad, let alone him.
Therefore, he had to kill this prisoner at all costs and obtain the Eternal Supreme Treasure from him.
Compared with Lord Muchen, Ye Tian’s advantage was that he was free, while Lord Muchen was a prisoner in confinement, unable even to cultivate normally, and his recovery speed was very slow as well.
“If Lord Muchen is not given time to recover, his combat strength will actually slowly decline!”
Ye Tian thought.
Immediately after, he prepared to copy bodies one after another, and of different people at that, so that he could enter the prison without any time gap and keep fighting Lord Muchen. After all, different Immortal Realm Lords entering the prison would not be stopped by Mo Mountain Prison.
In this way, Lord Muchen would have no time to rest or recover.
As battle after battle continued, Lord Muchen’s power would grow weaker and weaker. Then Ye Tian would be able to kill him.
This was a flaw of Mo Mountain Prison, but it was a flaw aimed at prisoners, and prisoners had no status in Mo Mountain to begin with. Even if it knew of this flaw, Mo Mountain simply would not care.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian copied the body of a Six-Step Immortal Realm Lord, then entered Lord Muchen’s prison and fought with him. Very quickly, this copied body was killed in battle.
After that, Ye Tian continued copying another body to enter this prison, fighting Lord Muchen nonstop.
Just like that, Lord Muchen killed one Six-Step Immortal Realm Lord after another.
“Damn it, what is going on? Even if these Immortal Realm Lords are all clones and their fall only means losing a clone, why are they all willing to sacrifice their clones so willingly?”
Lord Muchen said angrily.
He had guessed that his Eternal Supreme Treasure might have been exposed, but in such a situation, he had no choice but to expose it. If he did not activate that Eternal Supreme Treasure, he simply could not defeat Ye Tian. He had not encountered many Immortal Realm prodigies as monstrous as this one.
So he had to activate that Eternal Supreme Treasure.
However, he roughly guessed the situation outside. Mo Mountain was very likely no longer there. After all, for countless ages, he had not encountered any Mo Mountain disciples. This time, however, it was strange: many Six-Step half-Immortal Realm experts had joined the siege, and some were even Immortal Realm experts he knew, who had once been captured by Mo Mountain and imprisoned here, yet now had run into his prison void to fight him.
Could it be that those Immortal Realm experts had been released and were now serving another force?
He tried to communicate with those Immortal Realm experts, but the other side simply ignored him and instead fought him to the death at all costs.
Gradually, his power was consumed greatly, and he had no way to recover in time. The energy provided by the prison void was too little, and restoring his strength would take a very long time.
Over a long period of time, he began to suffer injuries, and they were difficult to recover from.
At last, he became heavily injured and could no longer even defeat a Six-Step Immortal Realm Lord.
After he killed one Six-Step Immortal Realm expert at the cost of grave injury and near death, the familiar Ye Tian descended once again.
This time, Ye Tian descended into Lord Muchen’s prison void with a copied body modeled on his own true body.
Lord Muchen knew he had no way to kill Ye Tian again, and his face revealed despair.
“My lord, I can surrender and serve the force behind you. Please don’t kill me, all right?”
Lord Muchen begged for mercy.
“Do you think that by begging for mercy, I won’t kill you? You should know very well why I am killing you.” Ye Tian sneered.
Lord Muchen naturally understood: it was for the Eternal Supreme Treasure.
He was extremely reluctant to part with this Eternal Supreme Treasure. Back when he was captured by one of Mo Mountain’s Legendary Realm Lords, he had not exposed the Eternal Supreme Treasure, nor had he even resisted much, because he knew very well that once he activated the Eternal Supreme Treasure, he would surely die. Mo Mountain would definitely kill and seize the treasure.
And once he was captured, Mo Mountain would have difficulty searching out his Eternal Supreme Treasure, so even if he were imprisoned, he would still have hope of leaving in the future.
But this time, he truly had no hope left.
“I am willing to hand over the Eternal Supreme Treasure in my possession!”
Lord Muchen said.
“I don’t trust you! After killing you, the Eternal Supreme Treasure will still be mine!”
Ye Tian used Myriadfold Void and directly blasted the heavily injured, dying Lord Muchen to death.
After Lord Muchen was killed, the Eternal Supreme Treasure on his body could no longer be hidden and was easily found by Ye Tian.
