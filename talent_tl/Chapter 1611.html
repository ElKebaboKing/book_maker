Chapter 1610 Found It!

Fenggu River.
Ye Tian kept activating his copying talent at all times to probe the surrounding situation. Although the innate Eternal Life did not yet fully come into being, so its talent would not be apparent, and he might not necessarily be able to detect it, there would certainly be many Eternal Tribulation Race members stationed near the innate Eternal Life.
Therefore, this method of probing was still useful.
However, Fenggu River was too large, and this way of searching would probably take a very long time.
During this period, Ye Tian was also discovered by the Eternal Tribulation Race. Although he did not have that section of a finger from an Eternal Realm powerful being with him, he still displayed not weak combat power as well as terrifying recovery and survival abilities. Even when surrounded by several Eternal Tribulation Race members of the sixth level, he could easily escape.
And as news of the innate Eternal Life spread, a large number of Supreme Beings poured into Fenggu River, bringing great pressure to the Eternal Tribulation Race in an instant.
A large number of Eternal Tribulation Race members were slain and became the gains of the Supreme Beings.
At the same time, the Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor of the Supreme Eternal Tribulation Domain grew anxious.
If things continued to develop like this, Fenggu River was likely to be lost.
If that happened, the price paid by the Eternal Tribulation Race would all go to waste.
Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!!!
One after another half-step Eternal Realm Eternal Tribulation Race members appeared before Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor, among them the Eternal Tribulation Race’s number one half-step Eternal Realm being—Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor.
“Greetings, Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor said respectfully.
As soon as Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor arrived, he asked, “Tianxu, does Fenggu River possess innate Eternal Life? The battle for Fenggu River was entirely a plan initiated by you, and we did not interfere. But this time, the Eternal Tribulation Race has suffered heavy losses, and rumors outside say that innate Eternal Life has been born in Fenggu River, so I had no choice but to come and ask you.”
The other half-step Eternal Realm beings were also waiting for Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor’s answer.
After a moment of silence, Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor replied, “Yes!”
He did not dare to lie; otherwise, Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor would certainly be able to find out. After all, Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor had greater authority than he did. With a single order, the situation in Fenggu River would certainly be placed before him.
Therefore, he could only answer truthfully.
“The matter of innate Eternal Life is very important, but you should have discussed it with me!” Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor said coldly.
“Yes!”
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor lowered his head, not daring to retort.
“Forget it, you did not do anything wrong in this matter. Anyone else would have done the same. Right now, we must completely capture Fenggu River, and absolutely cannot allow the innate Eternal Life to be exposed. At present, the outside world only suspects it, but has no definite evidence.” Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor said.
Afterward, Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor issued orders, dispatching more Eternal Tribulation Race members into Fenggu River and granting down all kinds of powerful treasures, deciding to protect the innate Eternal Life at all costs.
As a large number of Eternal Tribulation Race members reinforced Fenggu River, an even greater war began.
The entire Fenggu River turned into a huge battlefield, which made it harder for Su He to search for the innate Eternal Life.
At this time, even if he transformed into the body of an Eternal Tribulation Race member, he still had no way to find the innate Eternal Life.
Because the Eternal Tribulation Race members who knew about the innate Eternal Life were certainly very few; perhaps only a small number of Eternal Tribulation Race members throughout Fenggu River knew.
“Search slowly and depend on luck!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Time passed, and the slaughter in Fenggu River grew increasingly fierce. Ye Tian’s gains were not small either, and another Wind Source Gold Crystal mine vein appeared. Ye Tian also once again snatched a bit of Wind Source Gold Crystal.
In the blink of an eye, a Dark Age of time had passed.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian’s copied body detected some Eternal Tribulation Ancestors.
These Eternal Tribulation Race members were located at the bottom of Fenggu River and were extremely concentrated.
Even when he came here, these Eternal Tribulation Race members did not attack him.
Moreover, these Eternal Tribulation Race members were hidden very secretly. If he did not use his copying talent to probe, he would have had no idea that there were still Eternal Tribulation Race members here.
“Could this be it?”
Ye Tian speculated.
He prepared to go down and take a look; even if this copied body were to fall, it would not matter much.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian moved to the bottom of Fenggu River and sensed a large formation.
This formation covered a region at the bottom of Fenggu River, which was why no one could discern what was going on here.
Ye Tian did not say another word and immediately attacked the formation.
Rumble rumble rumble!!!!!
With just a single strike, the formation shattered.
After all, such a concealment formation could not possibly have much defensive power; if its defense were too strong, it would be easily discovered.
When the formation shattered, two furious gazes from Eternal Tribulation Race members shot over.
The next moment, they attacked Ye Tian.
As soon as the fight began, Ye Tian was completely suppressed.
These two Eternal Tribulation Race members were very strong, no weaker than the top Void Son, and their distance from the Void Lord who had passed through the third level of the Void Tower was definitely not far. They were absolutely the most top-tier geniuses of the Eternal Tribulation Race in this era.
During the exchange, Ye Tian spied on the situation here.
He sensed a vast life aura, incomparably dense, and it emanated an exalted and supreme Eternal aura.
“It really is innate Eternal Life. Although it has not yet been fully nurtured, the day it is born is not far off!”
Ye Tian said in surprise.
At this moment.
Those two Eternal Tribulation Race members were also tense, while the other Eternal Tribulation Race members were not strong enough to be of help.
They could indeed defeat Ye Tian, but Ye Tian’s survival ability was too terrifying.
“He is hard to kill. Don’t kill him, seal him instead!”
The two Eternal Tribulation Race members decided.
443 Ye Tian was very clear that he probably would not be able to escape, after all, these two Eternal Tribulation Race members were truly very strong, so he did not make much resistance. Soon, the two Eternal Tribulation Race members sealed him up.
Of course, Ye Tian could at any time annihilate this copied body himself.
But he did not do so.
“The innate Eternal Life has not yet fully come into being. Even if I let the copied body bring that section of the Eternal Realm powerful being’s finger and destroy these two, I still would not be able to completely take over this place. By then, the Eternal Tribulation Race would certainly dispatch a large number of their clansmen to encircle and attack me. It would be better to wait until the final moment and then send the copied body over!”
Ye Tian thought to himself like this.
The Eternal Tribulation Race sealed Ye Tian, thinking the problem was solved, and immediately began to reestablish the formation to conceal this place.
Time passed slowly like this—
The Eternal Tribulation Race was also very anxious, because the day the innate Eternal Life would be born was drawing closer and closer. If they still could not completely occupy Fenggu River, then when the innate Eternal Life was born, it would draw the attention of a large number of Supreme Beings.
By then, the Eternal Tribulation Race would likely have to pay a very heavy price to have any hope of taking away the innate Eternal Life, and they might not even succeed.
On this day.
Tianxu Tribulation Ancestor and Natural Disaster Tribulation Ancestor held a meeting.
They had a new plan!.
