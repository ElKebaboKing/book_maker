Chapter 1420: The Primordial Ancestor Emerges
!
The Red Maple Holy Realm's Holy Stele World no longer had any effect on Ye Tian, so he emerged from seclusion and returned to the War Ancestor Realm.
He inquired and found that the opening date for the Holy Soul Realm had not yet been decided.
After all, to the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and even the Primordial Ancestors of the Holy Gates, a mere tens of millions or even hundreds of millions of years was nothing more than the time it took to take a nap.
In this way, another few million years passed before the opening date of the Holy Soul Realm was finally set.
"Eight million years from now!"
This was the time now decided.
At this moment, the Ten Great Holy Gates and a few Holy Lands were all preparing, planning to send some Peak Domain Ancestors into the realm. However, in reality, many Holy Lands were unwilling to send people in, because if a disciple fell, the loss would be devastating.
And the possibility of such a fall was very high.
Although the Holy Soul Realm held countless opportunities, if one could not survive, no amount of opportunity would matter! Therefore, those Holy Lands did not force their disciples to enter the Holy Soul Realm; if a disciple truly wanted to enter, they would be given a slot, but if they didn't want to, they didn't have to go.
Of course, if one's luck was good, it was truly possible to obtain great benefits.
But the probability of such good luck was far too low! In the blink of an eye, eight million years passed.
On this day, the Fire-Pattern War Ancestor notified Ye Tian and the other Peak Domain Ancestors that the Holy Soul Realm had opened.
In the War Ancestor Realm, at a world entrance:
A massive warship was docked there. When Ye Tian arrived, he felt a terrifying aura emanating from the ship, making him feel like a mortal facing a terrifying prehistoric beast.
"Domain Ancestor Wanfa, this is our War Gate's Emperor-grade warship—the Green Dragon Ship. The power of this warship alone can reach the level of a One-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
It is a warship forged by the Primordial Ancestors. It is said to have been crafted using the bones of Supreme-level creatures, which is why it retains a trace of the pressure of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor creature!"
Qingyu Domain Ancestor introduced.
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!!! One by one, the Peak Domain Ancestors flew into the warship, and Ye Tian also entered the vessel.
They needed to use this Green Dragon Ship to travel to the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
The location of the Primordial Ancestral Realm was no ordinary place; it was a highly dangerous forbidden zone. Even an ordinary Invincible Domain Ancestor could not travel to the Primordial Ancestral Realm and might accidentally perish within the forbidden zone.
Therefore, these Peak Domain Ancestors could not possibly fly to the Primordial Ancestral Realm on their own, but by taking the Green Dragon Ship, they could reach it with ease.
This time, a total of over a thousand Peak Domain Ancestors from the War Gate were heading to the Primordial Ancestral Realm. Every Peak Domain Ancestor was an elite, but with so many entering the Primordial Ancestral Realm, many would likely perish—not just from the dangers within the Holy Soul Realm, but more so from the dangers posed by other factions.
In the Holy Soul Realm, where there were no restraints, ancestors would likely slaughter each other frequently. Thus, if one's strength was insufficient, encountering Peak Domain Ancestors from other factions would certainly lead to a fight.
On the Green Dragon Ship.
The Peak Domain Ancestors sat cross-legged one by one, when they suddenly heard the voice of the Fire-Pattern War Ancestor.
"After entering the Holy Soul Realm, watch out for the Peak Domain Ancestors of the Taboo Holy Gate, the Darkness Holy Gate, the All Things Holy Gate, and the Firmament Holy Gate. Once you encounter Peak Domain Ancestors from these four Holy Gates, kill any that you are able to kill. You must also be wary of disciples from other Holy Gates. You are not permitted to kill each other, understood?"
"Yes, Lord Fire-Pattern War Ancestor!"
The many Peak Domain Ancestors nodded in response.
Soon, the Peak Domain Ancestors of the War Gate, riding the Green Dragon Ship, crossed a vast distance and entered a forbidden zone of the Dark Holy Territory—the Primordial Forbidden Zone.
This Primordial Forbidden Zone contained terrifying killing intent. As the Green Dragon Ship entered, countless killing strikes hammered against it, but they were all blocked by the ship.
Before long, the Green Dragon Ship entered the central area of the forbidden zone.
Here, a safe zone had long since been carved out by the Primordial Ancestors.
Soon, Ye Tian and the others flew out from the Green Dragon Ship and landed in this safe area.
"Is that the Primordial Ancestral Realm?"
Ye Tian gazed at an incredibly massive world in the distance. This world was even larger than the War Ancestor Realm; it was naturally the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
However, this Primordial Ancestral Realm was marked by three cracks. These three cracks ran through the entire Primordial Ancestral Realm, and it was only thanks to the realm's inherent strength that it had not disintegrated.
The aura of the Primordial Ancestral Realm was much stronger than that of the War Ancestor Realm, and its aura was incredibly violent, exerting immense pressure.
Furthermore.
The Primordial Ancestral Realm seemed to have no entrance whatsoever, and it didn't even allow for divine sense investigation.
When Ye Tian's divine sense extended toward it, it was blocked by a terrifying power. This power seemed to transcend his scope of understanding; likely even a One-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor would find it difficult to tear through this power.
"I suppose only Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, or even Primordial Ancestors, can send us in!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
The War Gate was not the first faction to enter this place; several other Holy Gates had already arrived.
Subsequently, several more Holy Gate Domain Ancestors arrived.
Then, the Peak Domain Ancestors of the Holy Lands arrived under the escort of their Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, though some Holy Lands were not qualified to enter this place.
After about several years, all the factions had finally gathered.
"Let it open!"
One Supreme-level Domain Ancestor after another spoke.
Everyone's tone was quite unpleasant. After all, they had likely all fought each other not long ago, and some factions had lost many Domain Ancestors; who would have a pleasant tone? Suddenly.
A massive claw, comparable in size to a small domain, emerged and tore through the invisible barrier outside the Primordial Ancestral Realm.
As this claw emerged, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors all took on solemn expressions and retreated repeatedly, fearing they might be caught in the aftermath.
"A Primordial Ancestor!"
This thought surfaced in everyone's mind.
Only a Primordial Ancestor could possess such terrifying strength, and the entity was likely not a humanoid Primordial Ancestor.
"Is it that Primordial Ancestor from the Taboo Holy Gate?"
Ye Tian speculated.
Next, another Primordial Ancestor made a move, breaking through the world barrier of the Primordial Ancestral Realm with a single finger.
Following that, a staff was thrust directly into the Primordial Ancestral Realm, forming a passage that connected to the Holy Soul Realm within.
"Let these juniors enter!"
A series of voices filled with supreme majesty permeated the air.
These were the voices of the Primordial Ancestors.
"Yes!"
The many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors responded in unison.
Before Ye Tian could even react, a terrifying force lifted him and carried him through the passage, entering the Holy Soul Realm.
