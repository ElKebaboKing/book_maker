Chapter 1471: The First Ultimate Ancestral Technique
—Ultimate Ancestral Armor!
"Ultimate Ancestral Techniques are... very difficult for other Ancestors to master, but for me, although it is also difficult, the hope is very high!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
This was because he possessed several Ultimate Ancestral Talents, all of which were formed by copying the ancestral talents of the Nirvana Clan and continuously merging them.
Currently, he possessed five major Ultimate Ancestral Talents: the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent, the Ultimate Defense Ancestral Talent, the Ultimate Speed Ancestral Talent, the Ultimate Nirvana Ancestral Talent, and the Ultimate Time Ancestral Talent.
Therefore, he had hope of mastering these five types of Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, and the difficulty was at least a thousand or ten thousand times lower than for other Ancestors.
Of course, Ye Tian could not place all his hopes on Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. What if one hundred domain years was not enough? Therefore, he also had to increase his cultivation.
"Increasing an Ancestor's cultivation is not easy; one must rely on Nirvana treasures!"
Ye Tian thought.
Now that the human race controlled the Dark Soul Domain, if he created a large area of Nirvana Spacetime and used many high-level resources to evolve it, he could produce more Nirvana resources. He could then give them all to his clone, Ancestor Qing Ye, to increase the clone's cultivation.
One hundred domain years would be enough for Ancestor Qing Ye's cultivation to surge, and his strength would very likely reach the Elite Domain Ancestor realm. If he mastered an Ultimate Ancestral Technique, his combat power would soar to rival that of a World Ancestor.
Only then could he use the power of the Dark Soul Domain to contend with top Ancestors like the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor.
On this day.
Ye Tian used his Ultimate-level Nirvana Ancestral Talent to begin creating a Nirvana Spacetime. Such a Nirvana Spacetime was larger than previous ones and more likely to give birth to high-quality Nirvana treasures.
After all, even the Nirvana Clan did not possess a Nirvana Ancestral Talent at the Ultimate level! Both the clone Ancestor Qing Ye and Ye Tian's main body worked hard to manage this Nirvana Spacetime. The main body's task was to... deduce Ancestral Laws and Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, while the clone Ancestor Qing Ye's task was to... refine Nirvana treasures and increase cultivation.
As the long years passed, the human population in the Dark Soul Domain saw a terrifying increase. The humans born later were considered native residents of the Dark Soul Domain and received its recognition.
With ample resources and almost exclusive control over a Dark Holy Land, the human race's strength had long since surpassed that of ordinary Holy Sects.
Under these circumstances, various Holy Land-level forces emerged within the human race.
Of course, the most powerful force was the Human Race Temple; any human force had to be under the Temple's jurisdiction.
Even those Supreme Domain Ancestors, if they established their own forces, had to register their names with the Temple.
These Supreme Domain Ancestors were almost all nurtured by Ye Tian, so naturally, they did not dare to have any ulterior motives.
One domain year quickly passed.
For... many humans, this was a long period of time, yet in such a long time, the human race only gained three more Supreme Domain Ancestors.
To Ye Tian, this was not a fast speed, but for those Holy Sects, gaining three Supreme Domain Ancestors in one domain year was incredibly fast. If that speed could be maintained, how terrifying would that be! But later on, it might not even be possible to birth a single Supreme Domain Ancestor in a domain year.
This was not just a limitation of a race's providence, but also a limitation of a Dark Holy Land.
In one domain year, Ye Tian refined a massive amount of Nirvana treasures. If given to Supreme Domain Ancestors capable of refining them, they could have created hundreds of peak Supreme Domain Ancestors.
Yet so many Nirvana treasures only increased Ancestor Qing Ye's strength several times over, and the further he progressed, the slower the improvement became.
"Fortunately, the quality of the treasures and resources recently born in the Dark Soul Domain has become higher, so the Nirvana treasures nurtured in the future will also be of higher quality.
Moreover, the longer the Nirvana Spacetime is managed, the higher the quality of the Nirvana treasures born will be.
And the Nirvana Spacetime here is different from the one at the edge of the Dark Forbidden Zone back then. The Nirvana Spacetime there had a large number of Nirvana Clan members, and with the Ancestors 'harvesting' them, the Nirvana energy there could not grow.
But I am constantly pouring in resources, so the Nirvana energy is constantly skyrocketing.
At this rate, I can definitely nurture top-tier Nirvana treasures, and in large quantities.
In one hundred domain years, I can definitely stack my cultivation and strength to the Elite Domain Ancestor level!"
Ye Tian said with full confidence.
However, after such a long time, Ye Tian still had not pushed a single Ancestral Technique to the Ultimate level, though he had found some leads.
Furthermore, Ye Tian had also made some progress in creating his own Ancestral Laws, but it was only a lead and required a longer time to create and deduce.
Two domain years passed! Three domain years passed! Ten domain years passed! Unknowingly, eighteen domain years had passed in the Dark Soul Domain.
On this day, Ye Tian let out a great laugh, his voice spreading throughout the entire Primordial Ancestral World.
The reason he was so excited was that he had finally created an Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
This technique was deduced based on the Ancestral Armor. After eighteen domain years of deduction and countless instances of his duplicate bodies shattering, he finally evolved the Ultimate Ancestral Technique—Ultimate Ancestral Armor!
"I possess the Ultimate Strength Ancestral Talent at the Ultimate level, and I repeatedly used the Holy Soul of my duplicate bodies to deduce and evolve, exhausting the power of the Holy Soul countless times. It took eighteen domain years to deduce an Ultimate Ancestral Technique. If the Destruction Ancestor, the Holy Ancestor, and the World Ancestor do not have great opportunities, they probably wouldn't be able to create an Ultimate Ancestral Technique even in a hundred thousand or a million domain years.
And the Dark Holy Territory seems to have only existed for several hundred thousand domain years, yet they actually mastered Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. They must have encountered a great opportunity beyond my imagination!"
Ye Tian speculated.
However, he did not bother guessing the great opportunities of the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor; in short, no matter how great the opportunity, it could not be greater than the talent of copying.
Now that he had created the Ultimate Ancestral Armor, his defense alone had reached a terrifying level. Except for the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor, other Ancestors could not harm him in the slightest. If he borrowed the power of the Dark Soul Domain, even the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor would be unable to kill him.
"In the Dark Soul Domain, the Holy Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor cannot kill me, but they can kill the human race. I must also master an offensive Ultimate Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he began to research a Strength-type Ultimate Ancestral Technique.
Once he created an offensive Strength-type Ultimate Ancestral Technique, with the amplification of the Dark Soul Domain, his combat power would soar to a level comparable to the Destruction Ancestor and the Holy Ancestor. Even if he were slightly weaker, he wouldn't be much weaker.
