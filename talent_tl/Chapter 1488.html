Chapter 1487: Harvesting Eighteen Bone Beads
!
The information Ye Tian pried from that Bone General was that the Bone Soldier was currently in seclusion to heal his injuries, so he wouldn't be coming out or performing any missions for a short while.
Thus, he would not be exposed in the short term.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian hid there, secretly scouting for information.
The Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals would chat normally; they didn't believe any Ancestor could infiltrate this place, so they were completely unguarded and talked about everything.
Gradually, Ye Tian learned about the situation.
Among the things he heard, one word appeared most frequently—the Gate! These Bone Race members came to this Bone Sea Forbidden Land through a Gate. The Bone Sea Forbidden Land was vast, and occasionally, Bone Mountain treasure lands would be born. Every Bone Mountain treasure land would produce resources that were extremely precious to the Bone Race.
Therefore, the Bone Sea Forbidden Land was a treasure land for the Bone Race, which was why they stayed here, occasionally coming across precious resources.
Furthermore, whoever discovered a precious resource owned it; even the Bone Kings could not seize their treasures.
Of course.
Every Bone Race member could leave this place at any time, and a new Bone Race member would take their place.
From this, it was evident that there were many Ancestor-level Bone Race members, their numbers likely exceeding Ye Tian's imagination.
If so many Bone Race members were to all appear in the Dark Forbidden Domain, the major factions wouldn't be able to stop them and would be wiped out in an instant.
"How can the Bone Sea Forbidden Land be so powerful, yet it isn't even counted among the Six Great Forbidden Lands!"
At this moment, Ye Tian truly realized the terror of the forbidden lands.
No wonder the Realm Ancestor was so fearful whenever forbidden lands were mentioned, advising him not to enter, even if he had a special clone.
Additionally.
He also learned about the Bone Race's currency, which was Bone Stones.
Bone Stones were divided into many grades, and the Bone Stones used by these Ancestor-level Bone Soldiers were Ancestral Bone Stones. They could restore combat consumption and slowly increase cultivation, so they were used as currency.
However, Ye Tian currently didn't have any bone currency.
Shortly after, Ye Tian left this Bone Mountain and went to the outskirts of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. He found an uninhabited Bone Mountain and began cultivating in seclusion within it.
Ye Tian's current strength was very limited; after all, he had copied a Bone Soldier, not a Bone General.
He also wanted to copy a Bone General, but Bone Generals were rare, and once he copied one, the risk of exposure would be too great.
The risk of a Bone Soldier being exposed was smaller, but their strength was too weak, making it unrealistic to try and kill other Bone Soldiers.
Thus.
Ye Tian wanted to have this Bone Soldier body master Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. He already knew Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, so while mastering them again with a different body was somewhat difficult, it wasn't impossible.
Time passed slowly, and in the blink of an eye... several hundred million years went by.
On this day.
Ye Tian's Bone Soldier body had already mastered several Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, and his strength had reached the level of an Elite Ancestor's combat power.
But even so, if Ye Tian wanted to instantly kill a Bone Soldier, he still couldn't do it.
Moreover, once a battle broke out, it would inevitably attract the attention of other Bone Race members, making it impossible to keep hidden.
Ye Tian's current plan was... to obtain enough Bone Beads or other resources that would be of great help to him, allowing his own strength to reach a terrifying level so that he would no longer... need to use this sneaky method.
At that time, he would sweep through the Bone Sea Forbidden Land with absolute strength, slaughtering large numbers of Bone Soldiers to obtain Bone Beads.
"I'll continue to wait, wait for a Bone Mountain to be born. At that time, with my strength, it will be enough to snatch enough treasures to exchange for Ancestral Bone Stones, then buy the resources useful to me before leaving the Bone Sea Forbidden Land!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In a flash, another several billion years passed.
On this day.
A Bone Mountain was finally born.
Immediately, many Bone Soldiers and some Bone Generals rushed toward this Bone Mountain.
Ye Tian naturally rushed over at the first opportunity.
At the same time, he was also keeping an eye on the Bone Soldier whose body he had copied. Seeing that the other party wasn't there, he dared to show his face.
Clearly, that Bone Soldier's injuries were not light, and he was still: healing.
On the nameless Bone Mountain.
One Bone Soldier after another rushed in and began searching for resources.
A large number of blood-colored flowers appeared within a valley on the Bone Mountain, and many Bone Soldiers fought for them.
"Get lost!"
Ye Tian made his move.
He performed the Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique and the Flowing Light Ultimate Ancestral Technique. One made his speed reach a terrifying level, and the other made his strength reach a terrifying level, and both were very suitable for the Bone Race.
Boom!!!! One Bone Soldier after another was sent flying by Ye Tian. He was the first to rush into the valley and seize these blood-colored flowers.
These Bone Soldiers were stunned, not knowing why Ye Tian had become: so powerful. Aside from his physical body not having transformed, his strength had already reached the level of a Bone General's combat power!
These Bone Soldiers were stunned, but he was not. He continued to snatch resources at extreme speed.
Before long, the resources on this Bone Mountain were completely snatched up by these Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals.
And Ye Tian, relying on his terrifying speed and powerful strength, had snatched many treasures.
Ye Tian didn't recognize these treasures, nor did he want to understand their functions.
He only knew that he didn't have much time left. Having suddenly exploded with such terrifying strength, he would definitely be suspected, and perhaps before long, his identity would be exposed.
Therefore, he had to immediately sell these treasures and resources to the Bone Race shops and then buy the resources he needed.
Not all Bone Race resources were very effective for him; some resources overlapped with those in the Dark Forbidden Domain, so their effects were poor.
Currently, he only knew that Bone Beads were of great help to Ancestors.
Furthermore, while Bone Beads weren't of much help to Bone Ancestors, they were still somewhat useful.
....At a shop on the core Bone Mountain of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
Ye Tian arrived here and immediately took out his treasures, saying, "Sell all of these. How many Ancestral Bone Stones can you give me?"
Ye Tian asked.
The shop owner weighed them and estimated, "30,000 Ancestral Bone Stones. Selling?"
"Selling!"
Ye Tian replied.
Then, Ye Tian added, "Do you have Bone Beads here?"
"What, you want Bone Beads?"
The shop owner's expression changed.
Ancestor-level Bone Soldiers, Bone Generals, and Bone Kings all possessed Bone Beads; these were the source of a Bone Bead's power. The Bone Race was forbidden from killing each other in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, so wanting to obtain Bone Beads in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land was simply unrealistic.
If one wanted Bone Beads, they could only be imported from other places.
And Bone Beads were a very taboo treasure; no one dared to sell them openly.
However, there were definitely people selling Bone Beads privately.
It was just that Bone Beads weren't of high value to the.. Bone Race, but they were of very high value to.. other races. Therefore, some Bone Race members would buy them privately and sell them at high prices to other races.
But what was Ye Tian buying Bone Beads for? There were no other races here.
"Just asking if you're selling. I'm willing to buy at a high price!"
Ye Tian asked.
"Selling, but I don't have many Bone Beads in my shop, only five. If you want them, I'll give them to you for 10,000 Ancestral Bone Stones each!"
The shop owner said.
"Deal!"
Ye Tian purchased five Bone Beads.
Afterward, he took the remaining 250,000 Ancestral Bone Stones and made a round of the other shops. In the end, he only managed to acquire thirteen more Bone Beads. Adding these to the five Bone Beads, that made eighteen Bone Beads.
Eighteen Bone Beads plus 120,000 Ancestral Bone Stones—this was Ye Tian's current harvest.
"I must leave. Those shops are probably suspicious. Although they sold me the Bone Beads, they'll likely investigate me. Once they investigate, I will definitely be exposed."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he immediately left the area and headed toward the edge of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
Soon, he reached the edge of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, where Ye Tian's main body had already been waiting outside.
Boom!!!!! Ye Tian's main body stepped into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land and took all eighteen Bone Beads and 120,000 Ancestral Bone Stones brought by the copied body.
At the same time.
Ye Tian shattered the defenseless copied body with a single punch, destroying it.
When the copied body shattered, it simply dissipated; it didn't have a Bone Bead at all.
"As expected, no Bone Bead!"
Ye Tian sighed.
This was his experiment. In reality, he had known long ago that it was impossible to have a Bone Bead this time. The copied body seemed identical to the original, but once it died, it would dissipate, so it was impossible for a Bone Bead to be born.
Otherwise, if he could repeatedly copy bodies and continuously produce Bone Beads, why would he need to steal Bone Beads from the Bone Race?
But there are no such easy things in this world! Boom!!! Ye Tian instantly left the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, and the Bone Kings of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land appeared there, one step too late.
"What's going on? That Ancestor's aura... didn't he perish?"
The Bone Kings wondered in confusion.
Last time, one of Ye Tian's hands had reached across and quickly disappeared; they thought it was an illusion, but this time it was definitely not an illusion.
That Ancestor who had been besieged and killed by them had appeared again.
"Perhaps it's a special clone!"
The Bone Kings speculated.
But soon, the Bone Kings were enraged.
Because the Bone Soldier who had just been killed hadn't actually perished; he was clearly still: in the Bone Sea Forbidden Land in seclusion healing.
What was the deal with the Bone Soldier who had just been slain? Soon, the Bone Kings investigated and found out.
A fake Bone Soldier had mixed into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, disguised himself as a Bone Soldier, and purchased many Bone Beads, and they hadn't even noticed.
Immediately, the Bone Kings were beyond furious and began ordering a thorough investigation, absolutely refusing to allow such a thing to happen again.
