Chapter 1469: The Human Race Controls the Dark Soul Domain
!
After Ye Tian’s clone became a Primordial Ancestor, many Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Destruction Domain withdrew one after another. Even many Saint Gates of the Dark Saint Domain withdrew their Supreme Domain Ancestors.
There was no other way; the Human Race had birthed a Primordial Ancestor who now controlled a portion of the Dark Soul Domain.
Although a Primordial Ancestor could not easily kill a Supreme Domain Ancestor, it did not mean they couldn't trap or injure them.
It could be said that as long as Primordial Ancestor Qing Ye caused even a slight bit of trouble for those Supreme Domain Ancestors or locked onto their positions, Ye Tian alone could sweep through them and easily slay them.
In the Dark Soul Domain, no Supreme Domain Ancestor could evade the detection of Primordial Ancestor Qing Ye.
To prevent further losses of their Supreme Domain Ancestors, these factions naturally did not dare let them remain in the Dark Soul Domain; otherwise, they could easily fall.
The only Supreme Domain Ancestors who truly dared to stay in the Dark Soul Domain were almost all from the War Gate or those with good relations with the War Gate. After these factions greeted the Primordial Ancestors of the War Gate and Ye Tian, Ye Tian naturally would not make a move against them.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian took control of the Primordial Ancestral World within the Dark Soul Domain. With the strength of his Primordial Ancestor clone, it was not difficult to seal off the Primordial Ancestral World; he could even drive out all the Supreme Domain Ancestors and Supreme Soul Ancestors.
He would not show excessive mercy. He only allowed the Domain Ancestors and Soul Ancestors of the Human Race, as well as those from the War Gate, to enter the Primordial Ancestral World. It was impossible for other factions to enter.
In the Dark Soul Domain, no one could resist Primordial Ancestor Qing Ye.
This was much like when the Dark Saint Domain was first born; the Dark Saint Gate was the oldest and most powerful Saint Gate, and all the best resources fell into its hands.
This was solely because the Dark Saint Gate possessed the Saint Ancestor, the most powerful Primordial Ancestor.
Of course, the current Dark Saint Gate had two other Primordial Ancestors besides the Saint Ancestor, but unfortunately, those two were of average strength. Thus, aside from the Saint Ancestor, the Dark Saint Gate's strength did not hold a dominant advantage among the Ten Great Saint Gates, though they did have more Supreme Domain Ancestors.
Furthermore, the current Dark Saint Domain could no longer provide a strength boost to the Saint Ancestor.
Coupled with the other Primordial Ancestors working together to restrain him, even the Saint Ancestor could no longer dominate the Dark Saint Domain.
The same would eventually happen to the Dark Soul Domain. Sooner or later, Ye Tian would also lose the strength boost from the Dark Soul Domain, as the boost had a time limit—specifically, one hundred thousand domain years.
If Primordial Ancestor Qing Ye’s own strength was not great enough after one hundred thousand domain years, it was inevitable that other Primordial Ancestors would carve away parts of his territory.
....On this day.
Ye Tian brought his family into the domain. After all, the Dark Soul Domain was currently safer than the Profound Void Saint Realm.
Moreover, Ye Tian hoped to let his family enjoy the benefits of the Primordial Ancestral Spring. After all, Ye Tian’s wife, daughter, son, and sister had all ascended to the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm under his full cultivation, though they were only at the early stage.
Once they entered the Primordial Ancestral Spring, they could easily advance to the peak of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm.
Combined with several other Human Race Supreme Domain Ancestors also breaking through to the peak stage, the Human Race’s strength would become terrifying, potentially even stronger than a single Saint Gate.
More time passed.
On this day, the Primordial Ancestral Spring within the Primordial Ancestral World was born. Ye Tian’s main body, along with the Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Human Race and the War Gate, arrived here. They could all enter the Primordial Ancestral Spring to obtain its benefits.
Peak Supreme Domain Ancestors could obtain a second transformation of their Supreme Domain Body, which offered the greatest benefit.
Other Supreme Domain Ancestors could also rapidly temper their Supreme Domain Bodies, and their cultivation would increase swiftly, which was also a significant advantage.
"Wanfa War Ancestor, it is all thanks to your Human Race’s Primordial Ancestor Qing Ye this time. Otherwise, if our War Gate’s Supreme Domain Ancestors wanted to enter the Primordial Ancestral Spring, we would likely have had to go through a tragic and bloody battle. A Supreme Domain Ancestor like me wouldn't even have been qualified to enter!"
Fire Pattern War Ancestor said with a smile.
Indeed, he was only at the middle stage of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm; his strength was too weak, and there were far too many Supreme Domain Ancestors stronger than him.
But this time, he was lucky. He could enter the Primordial Ancestral Spring. Once tempered by its waters, while he might not have hope of completing a second transformation to break the limits of the Supreme Domain Body, he could still raise his cultivation to the peak of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm.
By then, he would be among the most powerful group of Supreme Domain Ancestors in the Dark Saint Domain.
"Everyone, go in and cultivate. Who gains the most will depend on your own abilities!"
Ye Tian spoke.
After all, an opportunity as terrifying as the Primordial Ancestral Spring only appeared when a Primordial Ancestral World was born. It would not exist forever; it was a one-time consumable resource. Once the spring water was exhausted, it would not be replenished.
In the past, when the Dark Saint Domain was born, the Saint Ancestor and several other Primordial Ancestors used the spring water to complete their accumulation from the early to the peak stage of the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm, allowing them to break through to the Primordial Ancestor realm in a short amount of time.
Otherwise, the Saint Ancestor would not have been able to break through before the Primordial Ancestors of the Dark Destruction Domain descended.
The Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Human Race and the War Gate entered the Primordial Ancestral Spring one after another.
Boom!!!! The energy of the Primordial Ancestral Spring surged into their bodies, helping them temper their Supreme Domain Bodies.
While these Supreme Domain Ancestors were cultivating, many Primordial Ancestors outside the Dark Soul Domain departed, and many Supreme Domain Ancestors left as well.
After all, with Primordial Ancestor Qing Ye present in the Dark Soul Domain, the various factions could not gain any benefits. Even if they started a war outside, it wouldn't affect the situation inside the Dark Soul Domain.
Thus, they simply left.
They would wait until the 100 domain years passed and the Primordial Ancestors could descend; they would settle the scores then.
From now until that 10-domain-year mark, the Dark Soul Domain was almost entirely under the control of the Human Race.
Time flowed slowly, and the energy of the Primordial Ancestral Spring continuously decreased.
In particular, the amount of energy Ye Tian absorbed was immense. At this rate of absorption, many Supreme Domain Ancestors might not be able to raise their cultivation to the peak stage.
Everyone was working hard to absorb it, but some Supreme Domain Ancestors had low strength, and their absorption speed was too slow.
"It's almost time. My Supreme Domain Body can undergo one more transformation and break the ultimate limit of the Supreme Domain Body!"
Ye Tian murmured.
