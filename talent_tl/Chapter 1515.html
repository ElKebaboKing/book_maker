Chapter 1514: Only One Dark Era Left!

"Origin Rule Calamity Crystal?"
Upon hearing the name of this treasure, Lingze River Ancestor's expression shifted instantly. He then said helplessly, "My Lord, where would we get an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal? They are far too precious. Our Jinyang River Palace failed to seize a single Origin Rule Calamity Crystal in the Dark Forbidden Domain, and in our own foreign world, our strength isn't particularly great, so we couldn't compete with others. We did manage to snatch one once, but news of it leaked by accident, so we refined it immediately for fear of it being stolen by other powerful forces."
"A force like our Jinyang River Palace is too weak. We don't even have a single second-tier Supreme River Ancestor. We could be wiped out at any moment; how could we possibly keep an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal in stock?"
"No Origin Rule Calamity Crystals?" Ye Tian was somewhat disappointed.
Judging that Lingze River Ancestor was likely telling the truth, he decided not to pursue the crystals with them.
"Since you don't have Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, then let's trade for some resources. If you dare to refuse, then don't expect this base to continue existing, and I will destroy this gate as well!" Ye Tian threatened.
Faced with Ye Tian's threat, Lingze River Ancestor naturally did not dare to refuse and agreed to the trade.
Thus, Ye Tian brought out many resources and treasures, demanding that Lingze River Ancestor mobilize resources from their main headquarters. If the resources weren't delivered, Lingze River Ancestor wouldn't be allowed to leave.
Of course, Ye Tian wasn't interested in an equal exchange; it was essentially a trade where he gave one portion of resources for ten of theirs. Otherwise, how could it be called a "Great Calamity Trade"?
Soon, countless resources were sent from the Jinyang River Palace, and the trade with Ye Tian was completed.
Afterward.
Ye Tian left the Jinyang River Forbidden Area and continued to other forbidden areas.
After Ye Tian departed, Lingze River Ancestor murmured, "During the last Great Calamity, the Dark Forbidden Domain hadn't even given birth to a single Supreme Progenitor. Now, before this Great Calamity has even arrived, a third-tier Supreme Progenitor has suddenly emerged. How did he do it?"
To become a Supreme Progenitor, one must fuse Progenitor Laws. This requires going to other foreign worlds to create Progenitor Laws or obtaining Origin Rule Calamity Crystals from other foreign worlds to use them to comprehend foreign world rules, thereby creating a Progenitor Law that fits those rules.
Therefore, it was absolutely impossible for Ye Tian to become a Supreme Progenitor relying solely on himself.
Clearly, Ye Tian had someone behind him, or a powerful organization.
"To be able to easily create a third-tier Supreme Progenitor... is it one of those six Beyond-Famous grade forces? Or has an even more powerful force intervened in the Dark Forbidden Domain?"
Lingze River Ancestor speculated.
However, this matter had nothing to do with him. Jinyang River Palace was too weak and had no desire to get involved in anything.
Over the following days, Ye Tian continuously visited various forbidden areas, specifically seeking out weak forces to "trade" with.
These forces did not dare to resist Ye Tian's arrival at all.
After trading with these forces, Ye Tian obtained a massive amount of foreign world resources. These were not only helpful to him but also to the Progenitors of the human race, helping them reach the level of top-tier Progenitors faster.
Through multiple trades, Ye Tian finally managed to acquire two Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, replacing the ones he had previously used.
After obtaining the two new Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, Ye Tian returned to closed-door cultivation to comprehend and create Supreme Ancestral Techniques.
It must be said that with the new Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, Ye Tian's speed in fusing ultimate ancestral techniques was incredibly fast, but it was still somewhat affected. It wasn't as fast as the very first time he used an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal to create a Supreme Ancestral Technique.
For the thirteenth Supreme Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian spent a full two Realm Eras to create it successfully, which wasn't particularly fast.
For the fourteenth Supreme Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian spent a full six Realm Eras.
To create the fifteenth Supreme Ancestral Technique, Ye Tian spent twenty Realm Eras.
Subsequently, he spent sixty Realm Eras on the sixteenth Supreme Ancestral Technique.
At this point, he switched to the other Origin Rule Calamity Crystal for comprehension and creation.
As a result, it took him three Realm Eras to create the seventeenth Supreme Ancestral Technique. Clearly, the difficulty of creation and fusion had increased again.
Unknowingly, the Dark Forbidden Domain passed its eighth Dark Era, leaving only one Dark Era of time remaining.
By now, Ye Tian had created a total of nineteen Supreme Ancestral Techniques. His strength was incredibly terrifying, far surpassing that of an ordinary third-tier Supreme Progenitor...
Now, Ye Tian found that for every new Supreme Ancestral Technique he created, the increase in his strength was very, very weak.
Unless he found a way to break through to become an Invincible Progenitor, it would be impossible for his strength to increase significantly!
However, he did not know how to become an Invincible Progenitor, and likely many third-tier Supreme experts didn't know either.
Furthermore, becoming an Invincible Progenitor was likely even harder than becoming a Supreme Progenitor.
He had previously threatened some Nameless-grade forces to learn the secrets of reaching the Invincible Progenitor level, but none of them knew.
Therefore, he temporarily set aside the idea of pushing for the Invincible Progenitor stage.
"There is only one Dark Era of time left. Even if I go trade for more Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, it would be difficult to create many more Supreme Ancestral Techniques, and I wouldn't be able to increase my strength much. It would be better to help my family members create Progenitor Laws that fit foreign world rules and then pass them on, helping them push toward becoming Supreme Progenitors!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
Indeed, through his research, Ye Tian now knew that becoming a Supreme Progenitor didn't necessarily require creating one's own foreign Progenitor Law. Just like becoming a Progenitor, one could fuse an ownerless Progenitor Law.
Becoming a Supreme Progenitor was the same. If one obtained two Progenitor Laws from a fallen Supreme Progenitor, they could fuse those two laws to push for the Supreme Progenitor level.
In fact, because of the experience left behind by the Supreme Progenitor, the difficulty of fusing to become a Supreme Progenitor was actually lower.
For instance, most of those Nameless-grade forces had luckily become Supreme Progenitors by stumbling upon the treasures or inheritances of fallen Supreme Progenitors.
Otherwise, many Nameless-grade forces were too weak and couldn't snatch Origin Rule Calamity Crystals. During a Foreign World Great Calamity, they could only stay for one Realm Year. How could they possibly create a Progenitor Law in such a short time?
Thus, inheriting two Progenitor Laws from a fallen Supreme Progenitor gave one a certain chance of becoming a Supreme Progenitor.
It was a "certain chance," not a certainty. If the compatibility between the Progenitor Law and oneself was not high, the chance of success would not be great.
Furthermore, a Supreme Progenitor created this way would be very weak, inferior to one who achieved it through their own efforts. But no matter how weak, a Supreme Progenitor was still a Supreme Progenitor.
