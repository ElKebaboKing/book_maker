Chapter 1508: The Blood Mist Alliance Strikes!

When the second Origin Rule Calamity Crystal appeared, Ye Tian received the news relatively early. Thus, he arrived at the scene immediately, heavily injured three Supreme Ancestors, and ultimately snatched this Origin Rule Calamity Crystal.
After seizing this Origin Rule Calamity Crystal, Ye Tian returned to the Sea Sky Holy Sect to observe it.
"So this is an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal!"
Ye Tian carefully sensed the rules upon it. They indeed belonged to the rules of the Sanctuary World, and they were the most fundamental origin rules. Comprehending an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal was naturally better and faster than cultivating within the Sanctuary World itself, and the crystal contained powerful origin power.
Ye Tian could sense that if he refined this Origin Rule Calamity Crystal, it would be enough to raise his cultivation level slightly and even temper his Supreme Ancestor Body.
However, compared to the benefits brought by creating a Supreme Ancestral Technique, a mere Origin Rule Calamity Crystal was nothing.
Yet, not just anyone could create a Supreme Ancestral Technique.
For instance, in the Sanctuary World, over such a long period of time, there had only been one second-tier Supreme Holy Beast.
"It is more cost-effective to use this Origin Rule Calamity Crystal in the future to comprehend and create a Supreme Ancestral Technique or to give it to the Human Race!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Consequently, he sent the Origin Rule Calamity Crystal back to the Dark Forbidden Zone. Through the hands of a Human Ancestor, it passed through the door and was delivered to his main body.
Following that, every so often, an Origin Rule Calamity Crystal would be born within the Sanctuary World.
However, his luck was poor afterward, and the subsequent Origin Rule Calamity Crystals were all snatched away by other Ancestors.
As time passed, a total of nearly thirty Origin Rule Calamity Crystals were born in the Sanctuary World.
"I only managed to snatch two!"
Ye Tian sighed.
He also wanted to seize more Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, but once other Ancestors obtained one, they could return to their respective foreign worlds at the fastest possible speed. He had no way to pursue them.
After all, the other foreign worlds had not yet reached the time of their Great Calamity. If he pursued them there, he would be directly obliterated by the foreign world's power.
Unless he was willing to copy the body of a powerhouse from that foreign world to give chase, but that would expose his ability, so it wasn't worth it.
Nevertheless, among so many Supreme Ancestors, being able to snatch two Origin Rule Calamity Crystals was already quite good.
He did not keep either of these two Origin Rule Calamity Crystals for himself; he sent them both back to his human main body.
At this time, the Great Calamity of the Sanctuary World had reached its late stages. Except for the territories of the three Great Holy Sects, all other regions had become a void. Not a single resource remained; everything had been plundered. Countless worlds had been reduced to fragments, and the living beings within them had naturally all perished.
Myriad Beasts Holy Sect.
Wan Yuan Holy Beast was somewhat flustered at this moment because this Great Calamity was far too different from those in the past.
In the past, the True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance would collect resources on a large scale and would even be attacking the three Great Holy Sects by now. The three Great Holy Sects would have to unite with other foreign worlds to keep the Blood Mist Alliance in check.
But now, the Blood Mist Alliance had hardly made a move. Even for collecting resources, they had only dispatched a few Supreme True Emperors. This was not the style of the Blood Mist Alliance.
Furthermore, through investigation, the Blood Mist Forbidden Land of the Blood Mist Alliance had been shrouded. Even Supreme-level powerhouses could not peer inside, and no one knew what they were doing.
Under such circumstances, all Supreme-level foreign powerhouses knew the Blood Mist Alliance had some issues, but the other foreign powerhouses weren't worried. Their bases were not in the Sanctuary World; at worst, they could just withdraw. They weren't afraid of the Blood Mist Alliance at all.
But the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect was afraid!
The Sanctuary World was the very foundation of the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect. They couldn't just run away.
"I hope the Blood Mist Alliance just has problems at their headquarters and lacks the strength to participate in this calamity, rather than it being some kind of conspiracy!"
Wan Yuan Holy Beast thought secretly.
However, not long after, a Holy Beast from the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect sent word that a large number of True Emperors from the Blood Mist Alliance were attacking, including many Supreme True Emperors.
Shortly after the message arrived, that Holy Beast went silent. Clearly, they had been discovered and obliterated by the True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance.
……
One of the three Great Holy Sects of the Sanctuary World, the Gilded Holy Sect.
This sect was even stronger than the Sea Sky Holy Sect, possessing three Supreme Holy Beasts, and its other Holy Beasts were far more numerous than those of the Sea Sky Holy Sect.
But suddenly, many True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance arrived here. It was all too abrupt, leaving the Gilded Holy Sect no time to react.
Hum!!!!
Countless rays of light swept across the region occupied by the Gilded Holy Sect. Countless worlds were annihilated, and countless beings perished. Even many Holy Beasts had no power to resist and were instantly killed.
·········Asking for flowers··· ·····
If the Ancestors in the Dark Forbidden Zone saw this scene, they would likely fall into despair.
After all, Holy Beasts with combat power comparable to Ancestors had become like ants at this moment.
"Blood Mist Alliance, you've gone too far!!!!!"
The three Supreme Holy Beasts of the Gilded Holy Sect shouted in rage.
They didn't think about fighting to the death with the Blood Mist Alliance because they knew they couldn't win. Instead, they tried their best to break through the encirclement to unite with the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect and then with other foreign forces to contend with the Blood Mist Alliance.
However, a giant hand reached across time and space, instantly grabbing one of the Supreme Holy Beasts.
With a gentle squeeze, that Supreme Holy Beast was crushed.
Of course, this Supreme Holy Beast still hadn't perished, but before he could fully heal and recover, another strike landed. This time, every trace of the Supreme Holy Beast's life was completely wiped out, erased entirely from the world.
... .. 0
There was no power to resist!
"A third-tier Supreme True Emperor!!!"
The other two surviving Supreme Holy Beasts cried out in despair.
They had never seen such a powerhouse; in the past, the Blood Mist Alliance had never dispatched such an expert!
Why would such a powerhouse appear in the late stages of the Great Calamity? This wasn't normal!
However, they couldn't escape either.
Soon, they also died at the hands of that third-tier Supreme True Emperor of the Blood Mist Alliance—the White Turtle True Emperor.
The destruction of the Gilded Holy Sect naturally reached the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect and the Sea Sky Holy Sect.
Immediately, Ye Tian had all the human powerhouses withdraw from the Sea Sky Forbidden Land. Then, Ye Tian's copied body, taking his clone He Yuan Holy Beast, evacuated the Sea Sky Holy Sect at once. He abandoned the Sea Sky Holy Sect.
He knew very well that since the Blood Mist Alliance had wiped out the Gilded Holy Sect in an instant, their next target would likely be the Sea Sky Holy Sect.
He had no way to contend with the Blood Mist Alliance, so he didn't want to lose his clone or his copied body.
The powerhouses of the Blood Mist Alliance could enter the Sea Sky Forbidden Land through the door, so the humans naturally couldn't stay to guard it. They had to evacuate to avoid losses.
It must be said that Ye Tian's decision was very clear.
Soon, the Blood Mist Alliance arrived at the Sea Sky Holy Sect and destroyed it with a wave of a hand.
