Chapter 1673: Break Through the Ninth Layer!

Pfft!
A spear pierced toward Ye Tian, directly punching through the void. It even failed to withstand one of Ye Tian’s defensive Eternal arts; the spear stabbed straight into Ye Tian’s body. But at the critical moment, Ye Tian activated the legendary defensive Eternal art—Eternal Wall.
The spear’s attack struck the Eternal Wall, and only then was most of its force neutralized. Ye Tian then activated the legendary Eternal art in a phasing state—Void True Body, and only then did he remain unharmed.
“An Intent Eternal art, huh? This Eternal art contains a massive amount of willpower, along with some Eternal power and the power of the innate Eternal rules. After being combined, it gives birth to an Intent Eternal art focused mainly on willpower attacks!”
Ye Tian had heard of Intent Eternal arts before, but they belonged to the legends among Eternal arts. He had never heard of anyone creating one.
Although many Eternal realm cultivators had studied willpower deeply and developed quite a few willpower secret techniques, they were still only secret techniques, not Eternal arts, and their power could not compare with Eternal arts.
The threat posed by ordinary Intent Eternal arts had already surpassed that of ordinary top-notch Eternal arts, and were even second only to legendary Eternal arts. After all, the Eternal realm cultivators in the Space-Time Territory really had no especially good way to deal with Intent Eternal arts.
Pfft, pfft, pfft!!!!
Spears formed from Intent Eternal arts shot through toward Ye Tian one after another. Ye Tian kept blocking these Intent Eternal arts, while also using legendary Eternal arts such as Eternal Extreme Fist to counterattack.
Rumble!!!!
Ye Tian and the three Immortal realm lords fought fiercely. After spending quite some time, he finally defeated them one by one.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian arrived at the eighth layer.
The three Eternal realm lords on the eighth layer were not Immortal realm cultivators, but Undying realm cultivators, and they too had mastered means such as Intent Eternal arts.
This battle lasted for many years before Ye Tian finally defeated the three Undying realm lords, and he himself had also suffered considerable injuries.
At last, Ye Tian reached the ninth layer as well.
“Star Gazing Sword Ancestor only cleared the ninth layer. I’m afraid I won’t be able to get through the ninth layer!”
Ye Tian guessed.
As expected.
After fighting for only a short while, Ye Tian was completely suppressed by the three Eternal realm cultivators on the ninth layer. Even though his survival ability was formidable, his Endless Eternal Body had little effect in resisting willpower attacks.
When his injuries became quite severe, True Void Legend Tower judged him to have failed and teleported him out.
Thus, Ye Tian’s current record was the eighth layer of True Void Legend Tower.
For the first five layers of True Void Legend Tower, clearing each layer granted one calamity era of cultivation and comprehension time in True Void Legend Land. From the sixth layer to the tenth layer, clearing each layer granted five calamity eras of cultivation and comprehension time in Void Legend Land.
Now, having cleared the eighth layer, Ye Tian obtained 20 calamity eras of cultivation time.
Right after finishing the tower challenge, Ye Tian became very interested in this True Void Legend Land, so he asked True Void Legend Tower to teleport him there.
Buzz!!!!
Ye Tian’s body disappeared and instantly arrived in a mysterious void—True Void Legend Land.
……
“I’m in!”
Ye Tian entered True Void Legend Land, and the very first moment he did so, he felt how extraordinary it was.
This place was filled with a special kind of willpower energy, constantly urging his body and will to merge.
In the distance, a towering sacred tree stood there.
Ye Tian wanted to fly toward this sacred tree, but after flying for a long time, he still could not reach its location, and in fact he had not flown very far at all, as if there was something wrong with the distance in this void.
Suddenly.
A voice entered his ears.
“No need to try. No one can reach this True Void Dao Mark Tree. Back then, even six legendary lords tried. No matter how they flew, they could not get there. We even suspected whether this True Void Dao Mark Tree truly existed. But in any case, cultivating here really can accelerate the fusion of will and body.”
The one transmitting the message to Ye Tian was an Undying realm cultivator.
“Thank you for the reminder, sir!”
Ye Tian said gratefully.
The other party said nothing more and continued cultivating.
Thus, Ye Tian found a random spot and began secluded cultivation.
Compared with the other Eternal realm cultivators who had entered here, Ye Tian’s only advantage was that he could continuously use his clones to verify the fusion of will and body, so the fusion effect and speed were certainly much faster than those of the other Eternal realm cultivators.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian diligently studied the fusion of will and body, so that this opportunity would not be wasted.
Time slowly passed, and one calamity era after another went by.
In True Void Legend Land, there were not many Eternal realm cultivators. After all, many Eternal realm cultivators above the Undying realm had almost used up all their comprehension and cultivation time, and if they wanted to increase their strength and then clear another layer, the difficulty was far too great.
· ·········求鲜花··· ·····
Therefore, there were not many Eternal realm cultivators here normally. Unknowingly, several more Eternal realm cultivators left, leaving only three Eternal realm cultivators and Ye Tian, this Nine Extremes realm half-step Eternal realm cultivator, still here comprehending and cultivating.
Finally, the other Eternal realm cultivators all left, leaving only Ye Tian. It was not until several more calamity eras had passed that two more Eternal realm cultivators arrived.
“Time’s up!”
Ye Tian opened his eyes, and in the blink of an eye, True Void Legend Land teleported him out.
However, Ye Tian did not leave True Void Legend Tower. Instead, he prepared to continue challenging the tower.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian entered the ninth layer of True Void Legend Tower. This time, he was highly confident that he could clear the ninth layer of True Void Legend Tower.
.... ......... 0
Rumble!!!
Ye Tian fought with the three Eternal realm cultivators on the ninth layer of True Void Legend Tower, displaying far greater strength than before.
During these twenty calamity eras of cultivation in True Void Legend Land, the degree of fusion between his will and body had increased tremendously. Previously, his fusion degree had only reached one ten-thousandth, but now it was already close to one-tenth.
In terms of the degree of fusion between will and body, he was probably even stronger than Star Gazing Sword Ancestor.
If he comprehended for another five calamity eras, he would have hope of raising the fusion of body and will to above one-tenth.
If that happened, and he chose to break through to the Eternal realm, his cultivation would immediately break through to the Undying realm.
“Kill!”
Ye Tian displayed one powerful legendary Eternal art after another. No matter how strong the opponent’s Intent Eternal arts were, they could not defeat him.
The increase in the fusion of will and body made his own physical strength reach an inconceivable level, and even his half-step Eternal power surged by a great margin.
Thus, he was completely crushing the three Eternal realm cultivators.
“Eternal Extreme Fist!”
Ye Tian shattered one Eternal realm cultivator’s body with a single punch, and the other party immediately collapsed.
After that, he quickly dealt with the remaining two Eternal realm cultivators and cleared the ninth layer.
Next, Ye Tian wanted to clear the tenth layer, but his strength was still a little lacking, causing him to fail the challenge.
Ye Tian felt that if he wanted to clear the tenth layer, he would probably need to raise the fusion of will and body to above one-tenth and undergo a small transformation before there would be hope of clearing the tenth layer.
