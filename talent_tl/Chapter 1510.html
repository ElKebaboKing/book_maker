Chapter 1509: Authority of Heaven and Earth!

Ye Tian knew he was an invader, but that didn't stop him from cooperating with the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect. After all, even the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect had no way to contend with the Blood Mist Alliance.
Therefore, it was the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect that truly and urgently needed other forces to cooperate with them. Ye Tian's arrival would only make the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect overjoyed.
As for Ye Tian occupying the Hai Tian Holy Sect, it wasn't a significant issue at all.
Swish! Swish! Swish!!!
One after another, Supreme-level foreign experts descended upon the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect. Clearly, they had all received transmissions from the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast. Coupled with the threat of the Blood Mist Alliance, they had no choice but to unite; otherwise, they would be harvested one by one by the Blood Mist Alliance.
Moreover, unless those foreign forces destroyed their Gates now and completely severed their connection with the Sanctuary World, the Blood Mist Alliance would eventually build Gates in the future to link to their own foreign worlds, and they would face invasion from the Blood Mist Alliance as well.
So, to protect their Gates, they had to unite and resist the Blood Mist Alliance.
Unless it was a desperate situation, these foreign experts would not easily give up on the Sanctuary World.
In almost a few moments, a large number of Supreme-level foreign experts gathered at the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect.
Just at the second tier of the Supreme level, thirteen experts had gathered here, and there were dozens more at the first tier of the Supreme level.
With so many foreign experts present, the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast breathed a slight sigh of relief.
Boom!!!!!
The Supreme True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance arrived, nearly surrounding the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect.
"Blood Mist Alliance, what exactly do you want to do?"
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast questioned.
The White Turtle True Emperor smiled and said, "No special thoughts. Our goal is simple: to destroy the Sanctuary World and take complete control of it. I know you Supreme-level beings are not from the Sanctuary World. If you leave now, our Blood Mist Alliance will not act against you. Otherwise, we will treat you as part of the Sanctuary World and wipe you all out!"
After saying this, the White Turtle True Emperor displayed his strength.
The pressure of a Tier 3 Supreme True Emperor spread out, causing the expressions of the many experts present to change drastically.
"A Tier 3 Supreme True Emperor!!!!"
Ye Tian was also incredibly surprised.
Such an expert was only one step away from being an Invincible Ancestor. He was no match for the opponent and simply couldn't win.
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast also panicked. He looked around and saw many foreign experts hesitating.
The Blood Mist Alliance had sent too many Supreme True Emperors this time, and with a Tier 3 Supreme True Emperor descending, they were clearly prepared to destroy the Sanctuary World at any cost.
They were not creatures of the Sanctuary World; there was no need for them to risk their lives for it.
Although losing the Sanctuary World meant they couldn't enter it again in the future, they at least wouldn't perish. There was still hope of finding other foreign worlds, and some forces were connected to more than one, so they didn't need to fight to the death.
Immediately, one foreign expert after another departed, and as expected, the Blood Mist Alliance did not stop them.
Before long, the number of foreign experts at the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect had significantly decreased.
Following this trend, other foreign experts, seeing the situation was dire, would not stay either.
By then, the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect would have no hope of survival.
No matter how one looked at it, the Myriad Beasts Holy Sect seemed to be in a desperate situation.
Ye Tian did not leave, because there was no need to.
His clone, the He Yuan Holy Beast, would definitely be treated as a creature of the Sanctuary World by the Blood Mist Alliance, so they wouldn't let it leave.
As for Ye Tian's own duplicated body, if it perished, it would only be a loss of some energy; the cost wasn't high.
Therefore, he would not leave.
"Blood Mist Alliance, do you truly intend to destroy our Sanctuary World?"
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast roared.
"Correct!" The White Turtle True Emperor sneered. "If those foreign experts hadn't left, your combined strength might have actually caused us some trouble, and perhaps you could have even killed a few Supreme True Emperors of our Blood Mist Alliance. But now, almost all the foreign experts are gone. With your meager strength, you are far from being able to contend with us."
Just as the White Turtle True Emperor was about to make a move, the aura on the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast changed.
Suddenly, he took out at least ten Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals and swallowed them all at once.
These were Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals belonging to the Sanctuary World, not foreign ones, so they weren't of much help to the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast's cultivation.
However, swallowing so many at once seemed useless; it couldn't possibly allow him to step into the third tier of the Supreme Holy Beast level.
Yet, the aura on the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast became more and more terrifying, while cracks spread across his body, and a deathly aura emanated from him.
At the same time, the rules of heaven and earth underwent a change.
The He Yuan Holy Beast didn't feel much, but Ye Tian's duplicated body felt the surrounding rules suppressing him...
"Not good, retreat quickly!!!"
The White Turtle True Emperor cried out in terror.
"Tribulation Power, Disperse!"
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast spoke.
In an instant, several Supreme True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance were covered by a terrifying rule. Their bodies were directly annihilated, leaving not a single trace behind.
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast himself was not faring well either; a portion of his body was permanently annihilated.
"You actually mastered a sliver of the Sanctuary World's Authority? How do you know such a secret technique?"
The White Turtle True Emperor was terrified.
The Authority of Heaven and Earth could be used to drive, modify, and perfect the rules of a world. It might seem like it doesn't help much in increasing one's combat power.
However, the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast was able to modify the rules, changing the rules of a designated area back to the state before the Great Tribulation occurred, which was equivalent to erasing the Tribulation.
Without the Great Tribulation, how could foreign experts dare to invade the Sanctuary World? They would be erased by the world itself.
And those Supreme True Emperors just now were exactly like that. The area they were in had the Tribulation erased, and without the protection of the Tribulation environment, they were directly wiped out by the Sanctuary World.
Never mind these Tier 1 Supreme True Emperors; even a Tier 3 Supreme True Emperor who was infinitely close to the Invincible True Emperor realm could not contend with the power of the Sanctuary World.
"Hahaha, even if I sacrifice this life, I will deal a heavy blow to your Blood Mist Alliance!!!!"
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast went mad.
He knew he couldn't survive. With his strength, he naturally couldn't perform such a secret technique without paying with his life.
A Tier 2 Supreme True Emperor who failed to escape in time was directly erased by the world.
As these Supreme True Emperors fled, the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast pursued them, and one by one, the Supreme True Emperors of the Blood Mist Alliance fell.
At this moment, the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast knew he couldn't hold on much longer and descended directly upon the Blood Mist Forbidden Land.
No one dared to stop him, and no one could.
"Shatter for me!"
The Myriad Yuan Holy Beast exhausted all his strength, striking at the Tier 2 Gate.
However, even with his full effort, he couldn't destroy the Tier 2 Gate.
With his final strike, the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast sacrificed his life to break off a corner of the Tier 2 Gate.
Subsequently, the Myriad Yuan Holy Beast perished.
Only then did the White Turtle True Emperor dare to appear here. Another Tier 3 Supreme True Emperor also stood to the side, both of their faces incredibly grim.
