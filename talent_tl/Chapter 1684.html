Chapter 1683: Territory Selection, Bloodbone Territory!

There are many territories in the Time-Space Domain that are ownerless, and any lord can occupy them, and they will be recognized by the Lord Alliance.
But if you are not a lord, you can only occupy a place here, but you cannot claim the title of lord. If another lord occupies it, you must give it up; this is the rule of the Lord Alliance.
Territories vary in quality. Some territories give birth to more resources, while others give birth to fewer resources.
What Ye Tian most wanted to occupy was naturally an Eternal Time-Space Territory. The value of an Eternal Time-Space Territory far exceeded that of ordinary Time-Space Territories.
For ordinary Eternal Realms, an Eternal Time-Space Territory was an enormous opportunity.
For example, when the Source Sea gave birth to the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree back then, so many Eternal Realms fought over it because the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree could allow an ordinary territory to evolve into an Eternal Time-Space Territory over a certain span of years.
In the end, that Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree was taken away by the Void Sword Lord.
Unfortunately, the Time-Space Domain today did not have any extra Eternal Time-Space Territories. On top of that, Void Beast materials could enhance the fusion of will and body, causing some Eternal Realm lords who were at the half-step Immortal Realm to break through their shackles one after another and advance to the Immortal Realm.
Right now, there were still several Immortal Realm experts who did not have Eternal Time-Space Territories.
Ye Tian could of course seize someone else’s Eternal Time-Space Territory, but once he did that, he would become the target of public criticism.
“Then I’ll occupy a Time-Space Territory with abundant resources and slowly find a way to evolve it into an Eternal Time-Space Territory!”
Ye Tian murmured.
Afterward, he began examining the conditions of one Time-Space Territory after another, selecting a relatively good one.
However, the relatively good Time-Space Territories had already been chosen by those Eternal Realms, so the remaining ones were bound not to be very good.
On this day.
Ye Tian came to the Time-Space Territory of the Star-Observing Lord—Star-Observing Territory.
This was a territory occupied by the Star-Observing Lord long ago. It was fairly good, and no other Eternal Realm would come to choose the Star-Observing Territory, otherwise they would be offending him, because it had always been in an ownerless state.
Only after he had broken through to the Eternal Realm previously did he name the territory Star-Observing Territory.
“Lord of the Endless, welcome to my territory as a guest!”
The Star-Observing Lord personally came forward and welcomed Ye Tian into the Star-Observing Territory.
The two sat in the void within a huge palace and chatted casually.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian asked, “Star-Observing Lord, I came to ask for your guidance. There are too many territories in the Time-Space Domain—not exactly endless, but certainly numbering in the billions. I want to choose a decent Time-Space Territory, but I’ve been unable to make up my mind.”
The Star-Observing Lord smiled and said, “Lord of the Endless, there really are not many good territories left now. Unless a territory with enormous value suddenly appears, you can only choose one that looks passable. Moreover, every territory has its own characteristics. It may have little value to other Eternal Realms, but to some Eternal Realms it has great value. For example, my Star-Observing Territory contains more sword dao laws, making it suitable for cultivating sword dao, which is why I chose it.”
“Actually, I want to choose a Time-Space Territory with more resources, or one with enormous potential,” Ye Tian said frankly.
“Lord of the Endless, Time-Space Territories that can give birth to many resources have always been highly sought after. Last time, many Eternal Realms fell in our Time-Space Domain, but the Lord Alliance has a rule: once an Eternal Realm falls, its territory is inherited by its relatives. As long as a new Eternal Realm is born within ten million Eternal years, the territory can be fully inherited. If not for this rule, one could simply occupy a resource-rich territory whose lord had fallen.” The Star-Observing Lord said.
“Forget it, that would be a bit too much!” Ye Tian waved his hand, having no such intention.
The Star-Observing Lord pondered for a while and said, “Lord of the Endless, there is indeed one Time-Space Territory that is very mysterious, and it also gives birth to quite a few resources, but no one has ever dared to refine it into their own territory.”
“Oh? Which territory?”
Ye Tian was curious.
“Bloodbone Territory!” said the Star-Observing Lord.
“Bloodbone Territory?” Ye Tian had vaguely heard of this name, but there were too many territories in the Time-Space Domain, and he spent much of his time cultivating, so he was not very clear about it.
At this, the Star-Observing Lord introduced it: “Bloodbone Territory is famous in the Time-Space Domain for one sea and one mountain, but very few Eternal Realms enter Bloodbone Territory. The sea in Bloodbone Territory refers to the Black Sea, and the mountain refers to Purple Mountain. The Black Sea is formed from the blood of an unknown creature, and it is very likely to be comparable to the level of a legendary lord, or even stronger than a legendary lord. Purple Mountain, on the other hand, is formed from the bones of that unknown creature. The Black Sea gave birth to Black Scale Beasts, and these creatures are important resources for Supreme Beings or ordinary half-step Eternal Realms. As for the bone mountain, it produces all kinds of mineral veins, all of them worth a great deal.”

“Then why hasn’t any lord refined it into a territory?”
Ye Tian asked in confusion.
“Because every so often, Bloodbone Territory produces a special blood-colored force. Even ordinary Eternal Realms have difficulty withstanding it. Although it is not enough to cause them to fall, it is extremely painful. Moreover, mining the resources of the Black Sea and Bloodbone Territory is also rather troublesome. Immortal Realms look down on such a territory, while ordinary Eternal Realms do not dare to refine it. Of course, the real reason is not this, after all, a territory with such abundant resources would certainly tempt someone.”
“The real trouble is that an ancient Black Scale Beast is slumbering in the Black Sea, and its strength is probably at the Immortal Realm. It is precisely because of this Immortal Realm unknown creature that many Eternal Realms do not dare to rashly mine the resources. Occasionally, some half-step Eternal Realms enter to mine resources, but they do not dare stay for too long, because the unknown blood-colored force follows almost no pattern. Some Eternal Realms have speculated that if that Black Scale Beast were killed, perhaps the blood-colored force would also be resolved, and this territory would return to normal. However, Eternal Realms below the lord level of Immortal Realms simply do not dare to trouble that Black Scale Beast, and Immortal Realm lords all have Eternal Time-Space Territories. Compared with Eternal Time-Space Territories, Bloodbone Territory is still far inferior. Thus, the problem of Bloodbone Territory has never been resolved.”

“Previously, I had indeed set my sights on Bloodbone Territory, but after thinking it over carefully, the resources in Bloodbone Territory could not improve my strength, and I still prefer Star-Observing Territory more. So I gave up on Bloodbone Territory. Lord of the Endless, your strength is now much greater than mine, so perhaps you can give it a try. If you can solve that Black Scale Beast and resolve the problem of the blood-colored force, the potential of this territory would probably be extraordinary.”
After the Star-Observing Lord finished speaking, Ye Tian fell into thought.
It had to be said that Bloodbone Territory indeed met his requirements, except that Bloodbone Territory still had some trouble and needed to be dealt with.
That Black Scale Beast clearly did not have much intelligence and could not be considered an Eternal Realm lord; otherwise, it would have refined Bloodbone Territory itself.
Slaying such a Black Scale Beast would not be stopped by the Lord Alliance.
“Then I’ll choose Bloodbone Territory!”
Ye Tian made his decision and.
