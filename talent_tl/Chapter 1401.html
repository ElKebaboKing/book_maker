Chapter 1400: He has already condensed a Saint Soul
?
"Damn it, he's impossible to kill!"
Ancestor Xuangui said helplessly.
This was also his first time encountering such a difficult opponent as Ye Tian; if it were other supreme domain ancestors, even if their strength was comparable to Ye Tian's, they would be besieged and killed by these four-star 44 kings, or worn down to death.
But Ye Tian could escape into the depths of the void and easily recover all his strength, and possessed the Undying Saint Skill, healing his injuries too quickly; even their trump cards couldn't instantly inflict heavy damage on Ye Tian.
Therefore, they couldn't do anything to Ye Tian.
If this continued, Ye Tian wouldn't die, but they would instead be severely wounded and fall one by one.
"What should we do?"
Ancestor Xuangui asked via message.
Ancestor Yuanfeng was also quite helpless; in this situation, he had no solutions either.
Unless they possessed a top-tier sacred artifact that could instantly kill an invincible domain ancestor-level being, only then would there be hope of killing Ye Tian in one blow.
But they didn't have such a top-tier sacred artifact! "Let's send a message to Lord Ancestor Quezhan, he was the one who told us to act. We can't kill Ancestor Wanfa; if he doesn't care, once Ancestor Wanfa targets us, we won't be able to resist him, unless we stay in the branch forever and never come out!"
Ancestor Yuanfeng said.
"This is the only way!"
Ancestor Xuangui and the others responded one by one.
In the distance.
Ancestor Quezhan received messages from Ancestor Yuanfeng, Ancestor Xuangui, and the others.
"A bunch of good-for-nothings!"
Ancestor Quezhan cursed under his breath.
However, he had already anticipated all of this, he was just a little unwilling.
Now, Ancestor Yuanfeng and the others had sent messages asking for help; clearly, they were truly at their wit's end.
If Ancestor Yuanfeng and the others continued to fight Ye Tian, it was likely that a few more four-star 44 kings would fall.
Four-star 44 kings were not... cabbages; they were the foundation of the Ghost Fox Organization's future! Although the final decision was made by the leader, if too many four-star 44 kings fell, he would also suffer severe punishment.
Therefore, no more four-star 44 kings could be allowed to fall.
"Ancestor Qingyang, it's your turn to act. Once you successfully kill Ancestor Wanfa, all the benefits you ask for will be given to you.
Remember, you must use all your strength to kill Ancestor Wanfa, do not hold anything back.
I know you've always hidden your strength, I'm not sure exactly what level it has reached, but I hope you don't get careless, understand?"
Ancestor Quezhan reminded him.
"Lord Ancestor Quezhan, rest assured, Ancestor Wanfa is just a mere trifle, it won't be difficult for me to kill him!"
Ancestor Qingyang said with a smile.
However, what Ancestor Qingyang didn't know was that Ye Tian's Eternal Undying Saint Body had reached its most critical moment.
If the shackles of the Eternal Undying Saint Body's perfection and ultimate realm were likened to a barrier, then this barrier was now covered in cracks and could shatter completely at any moment.
If Ye Tian wished, he could even forcefully break through to the ultimate realm of the Eternal Undying Saint Body right now.
However, Ye Tian did not do so.
He thought that the Ghost Fox Organization might still have trump cards or hidden kings who hadn't made their move; he planned to wait until that person made their move before breaking through to the ultimate realm of the Eternal Undying Saint Body.
As the number one Saint Body in the Dark Saint Domain, the Eternal Undying Saint Body was incredibly powerful; once it reached the ultimate realm, its power would skyrocket many times over.
Only then would the Eternal Undying Saint Body truly be the Eternal Undying Saint Body.
Suddenly.
Ancestor Yuanfeng and Ancestor Xuangui retreated one after another, while a figure rapidly streaked from a distance, arriving before Ye Tian in an instant, blocking Ye Tian and preventing Ye Tian from pursuing the other four-star 44 kings.
Ye Tian did not continue his pursuit, but instead stared at Ancestor Qingyang.
From the investigation of his replication talent, Ye Tian learned Ancestor Qingyang's name and general background.
"A four-star 44 king of the Ghost Fox Organization, it seems to be a hidden four-star 44 king!"
Ye Tian thought to himself:.
Ancestor Qingyang was not a Saint-gifted one, but his strength was probably far from simple; he put pressure on Ye Tian.
Clearly, Ancestor Qingyang was very powerful! "Ancestor Qingyang, it's him, hasn't he broken through to the invincible domain ancestor realm yet?"
Ancestor Xuangui and the other four-star 44 kings recognized Ancestor Qingyang's identity.
The outside world didn't know Ancestor Qingyang, but they knew that the Ghost Fox Organization had such a four-star 44 king, and an incredibly ancient four-star 44 king; when they first joined the Ghost Fox Organization, Ancestor Qingyang was already a four-star 44 king.
Moreover, Ancestor Qingyang had been out of contact for a long time; they even thought Ancestor Qingyang had already broken through to the invincible domain ancestor realm, unexpectedly, he was still in the supreme domain ancestor realm.
"Ancestor Qingyang, let's besiege Ancestor Wanfa together!"
Ancestor Xuangui suggested.
"A bunch of good-for-nothings, get out of my way, don't interfere with me!"
Ancestor Qingyang said very impolitely.
"You...."
Ancestor Xuangui and the others were extremely annoyed.
They were all four-star 44 kings, yet Ancestor Qingyang actually called them good-for-nothings; it was simply too detestable! Just as Ancestor Xuangui and the others were about to retort, Ancestor Quezhan's voice descended: "Everyone retreat, let Ancestor Qingyang deal with Ancestor Wanfa alone!"
"Yes!"
Ancestor Xuangui and the others obediently retreated; they dared not defy Ancestor Quezhan's orders, otherwise, they would be seeking death.
In reality, it would have been the best approach for them to assist Ancestor Qingyang against Ye Tian, but Ancestor Qingyang was too arrogant and believed those few four-star 44 kings were good-for-nothings, perhaps even interfering with his attacks during battle.
Therefore, he decided to fight Ancestor Wanfa alone.
"Ghost Fox Organization, Ancestor Qingyang, I ask for your guidance!"
Ancestor Qingyang introduced himself.
"War Gate, Ancestor Wanfa, I ask for your guidance!"
Ye Tian also simply said.
As soon as his words fell, the battle began.
Ancestor Qingyang, holding the Qingyang Sword, charged towards Ye Tian.
The Qingyang Sword was not a sacred artifact, but a sacred artifact had been integrated into the weapon, creating this type of weapon that could enhance combat power; such weapons were called Saint Soldiers! In reality, Saint Soldiers could not unleash the full power of a sacred artifact, but some sacred artifacts were truly unsuitable for combat; for example, Ancestor Qingyang was skilled in sword techniques, so if he didn't have a sword-type sacred artifact, he naturally could only choose this method.
This was also a type of weapon commonly used by invincible domain ancestors; transforming sacred artifacts into Saint Soldiers, the effect was quite good.
"Saint Skill: Sword Star!"
Ancestor Qingyang thrust his sword.
With a tearing sound, the void was pierced through, as if ignoring space and time, directly hitting Ye Tian's body.
*Puff puff!!* Ye Tian's Golden Body Saint Skill and Saint Armor Saint Skill both failed to block this sword; all were broken, and even the somewhat damaged Golden Star Armor was pierced, leaving a sword hole on his body.
This time, Ye Tian was injured, and not lightly.
If Ye Tian hadn't used the Undying Saint Skill to quickly heal his injuries in time, he would probably have been severely wounded.
"So strong!"
Ye Tian said solemnly.
Compared to Ancestor Xuangui and the others, Ancestor Qingyang's strength had completely risen to another level.
Moreover, he couldn't perceive the strength of Ancestor Qingyang's Soul Saint Light; as if it had returned to its original simplicity, the Soul Saint Light and his own power were completely integrated, only then could it be completely imperceptible.
"Could it be that he has already condensed a Saint Soul!!!"
