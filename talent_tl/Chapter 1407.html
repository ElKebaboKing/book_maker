Chapter 1406: Saint-grade Ore Vein
!
On this day.
Ye Tian was cultivating when he suddenly felt a special fluctuation.
"A great treasure has been born!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Swish! Ye Tian moved at top speed towards the source of the fluctuation, arriving at his destination in the blink of an eye.
In a dim void.
Ye Tian arrived, and some nearby Domain Ancestors also came here immediately.
Countless energies surged here, nurturing something, and ordinary Domain Ancestors found it difficult to discern what the energy core was.
But Ye Tian, as a Supreme Domain Ancestor, naturally saw through everything and perceived what was at the core of the energy.
"A mountain range!"
Ye Tian was surprised.
"No, it's not an ordinary mountain range, but an ore vein!"
Ye Tian thought to himself:.
If it were an ordinary ore vein, it wouldn't emit such fluctuations; it must be extremely precious.
As for what kind of ore vein it was, if it couldn't truly form, he wouldn't be able to discern it.
As time passed, this mountain range gradually revealed itself, transforming into a treasure land, standing firm in this area.
At the same time, many Domain Ancestors also learned about the situation of this ore vein.
"It's a Saint-grade ore vein!"
A Supreme Domain Ancestor exclaimed in shock.
"Saint-grade ore vein!"
Ye Tian was also quite surprised.
The value of a Saint-grade ore vein is far greater than that of a single Saint Artifact; it's truly a treasure mine! The ore materials produced by Saint-grade ore veins can be used to refine Saint Weapons. Many Supreme Domain Ancestors desire to possess a Saint Weapon that suits them. Saint Artifacts specifically for combat are relatively rare, so refining Saint Weapons using ordinary Saint Artifacts combined with Saint-grade materials is the mainstream approach.
However, Saint-grade materials are too scarce. Without Saint-grade materials, using only ordinary materials makes it impossible to transform a Saint Artifact into a Saint Weapon.
One can imagine how high the value of a Saint-grade ore vein is.
For example, a Saint-grade ore vein like the one before them is enough to supply a major power with materials to refine a large number of Saint Weapons, without worrying about a shortage of Saint-grade materials.
One must know that ordinary sects are not qualified to possess a Saint-grade ore vein; only Saint Land-level powers have one.
Moreover, Saint-grade ore veins are not... sustainable ore veins. Once they are depleted, they are truly depleted. Therefore, many Saint Lands may not necessarily have many Saint-grade ore materials.
The many Domain Ancestors did not rush to seize it, because Saint-grade materials are difficult to mine, and even if they fought, it would be a waste of time.
Furthermore, other Domain Ancestors would not simply sit by and watch them slowly mine the Saint-grade ore vein.
More importantly, with the appearance of this Saint-grade ore vein,
in the Saint Realm, the human race would not allow a Saint-grade ore vein to be plundered and mined by others.
"This Saint-grade ore vein belongs to our human race. Everyone, please leave." Ye Tian appeared on the Saint-grade ore vein, addressing the non-human Domain Ancestors.
As for the Domain Ancestors from outside the human race, although they were unaware of Ye Tian sweeping through the Ghost Fox Organization's four-star 44 kings, the matter of Ye Tian previously slaying two four-star experts outside the Human Realm had spread far and wide.
Therefore, knowing how powerful Ye Tian was, they dared not provoke him.
Unless they were Supreme Domain Ancestors ranked among the top of the Ten Great Sacred Sects' Supreme Powerhouses list, they wouldn't dare to contend with Ye Tian even slightly.... otherwise, they wouldn't dare to act presumptuously.
Consequently, these non-human Domain Ancestors retreated one by one, without uttering a single word.
Ye Tian then began to set up a grand formation, enveloping this Saint-grade ore vein to prevent other Domain Ancestors from secretly infiltrating and mining it.
In addition, Ye Tian dispatched dozens of Domain Ancestors to guard this place, including several top-tier Domain Ancestors.
Should anyone intrude here, they would immediately send a message to him, and he would rush over at once.
....Dark Saint Domain, Qingyuan Sacred Land.
Qingyuan Sacred Land is a declining Sacred Land, ranked last among the seventy-two Sacred Lands, and might even have its title as a Sacred Land revoked.
Although there have always been seventy-two Sacred Lands, in reality, it doesn't mean they cannot be replaced; rather, they are frequently replaced.
For instance, if some sects become very powerful and give birth to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, while the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of certain Sacred Lands fall, those Sacred Lands will be replaced by such sects.
In the past, Qingyuan Sacred Land was merely a sect, known as Qingyuan Sect. Later, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor was born, and it seized a Saint Realm, thus entering the ranks of Sacred Lands.
But now, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of Qingyuan Sacred Land is severely injured and has been in secluded cultivation for recovery. Furthermore, there are no outstanding powerhouses at the Invincible Domain Ancestor level in Qingyuan Sacred Land.
Apart from that Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, Qingyuan Sacred Land's strength is even inferior to some top-tier sects. The sects ranked among the top few actually conceal Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, but without a Saint Realm as their headquarters, they cannot become Saint Land-level powers.
One Saint Realm and one Supreme-level Domain Ancestor are the two necessary conditions to become a Sacred Land.
One can imagine that once something happens to the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of Qingyuan Sacred Land, Qingyuan Sacred Land will immediately be expelled from the ranks of Sacred Lands and replaced by those top-tier sects.
Therefore, Qingyuan Sacred Land's situation is extremely perilous, and it has always sought opportunities to strengthen its power.
At this moment, the news of a Saint-grade ore vein being born in the Xuanxu Saint Realm became known to Qingyuan Sacred Land, immediately drawing its attention.
Qingyuan Sacred Land is quite poor among the Sacred Lands. The Saint Realm it possesses is also a resource-scarce Saint Realm, considered an incredibly ancient Saint Realm where resources have been over-mined, and even its origin has been damaged, making it difficult for Saint-grade ore veins to form.
Once they acquire a Saint-grade ore vein, they can forge many Saint Weapons, thereby arming Qingyuan Sacred Land's Invincible Domain Ancestors.
In that case, Qingyuan Sacred Land's strength would directly rise by a notch.
Inside Qingyuan Sacred Land.
An elder of Qingyuan Sacred Land, Elder Qingxu, and the contemporary Sacred Lord of Qingyuan Sacred Land, Sacred Lord Qingfeng, were discussing the matter of the Saint-grade ore vein in the Xuanxu Saint Realm.
"Sacred Lord, a Saint-grade ore vein has appeared in the Xuanxu Saint Realm. Once our Qingyuan Sacred Land obtains this Saint-grade ore vein, our strength will surely greatly increase!"
Elder Qingxu said excitedly.
"Our Qingyuan Sacred Land's strength is too weak; are we qualified to intervene?"
Sacred Lord Qingfeng said worriedly.
"Sacred Lord, rest assured. Those Sacred Sects shouldn't covet a mere Saint-grade ore vein to the extent of fully contending for it. At most, some Domain Ancestors within the Sacred Sects might be interested.
As for other Sacred Lands, they might be wary of the War Sect's strength and might not act immediately.
Therefore, as long as we act quickly and immediately seize this Saint-grade ore vein, and as long as we don't kill any Domain Ancestors from the War Sect, we won't offend the War Sect.
This is an opportunity. We only need to dispatch a few Invincible Domain Ancestors, and then, by understanding our Sacred Land's Saint Artifact—the Qingyuan World Pearl—we can directly sever the void where that Saint-grade ore vein is located, seal it within the Qingyuan World Pearl, and then instantly depart from the Xuanxu Saint Realm."
Elder Qingxu suggested.
"It's a bit risky!"
Sacred Lord Qingfeng hesitated.
"Sacred Lord, this is a great opportunity! If a Saint-grade ore vein appeared in the Dark Saint Domain, where would our chance be? Moreover, the attention of various major Sacred Sects and some top Sacred Lands is currently drawn to the Primordial Ancestral Realm. They don't have time to pay attention to the situation in the Xuanxu Saint Realm, which makes this our best opportunity.
Could it be that the Sacred Lord wishes for our Qingyuan Sacred Land to fall out of the ranks of Sacred Lands?"
Elder Qingxu quickly tried to persuade him.
"Alright, let's do it!"
Sacred Lord Qingfeng gritted his teeth and decided.
