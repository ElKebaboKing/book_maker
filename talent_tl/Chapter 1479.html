Chapter 1478: Huaxia Ancestral Realm
!
The Progenitor of All Things was dead! The other Progenitors had not expected this at all. When the Progenitor of All Things brought out the Boundary Ancestral Crystal and the Innate Ancestral Artifact, they thought that Ye Tian—this newly ascended Progenitor—would fall.
However, they were wrong. The one who fell was the Progenitor of All Things.
A Progenitor! How long had it been since one had fallen? The number of Progenitors who had fallen in the Dark Forbidden Zone was far more than just one or two—it even exceeded double digits—but it had been a very long time since the last Progenitor fell. Thus, the fall of a Progenitor this time truly shocked them.
Particularly for the several newly ascended Progenitors, they realized that even Progenitors could fall.
For a moment, everyone was wary of Ye Tian.
His main body possessed top-tier elite Progenitor combat power and would definitely be able to promote to a top-tier Progenitor before long, while his clone possessed top-tier Progenitor combat power within the Dark Soul Domain.
How terrifying was such a Progenitor? He was even more fearsome than the Destruction Progenitor or the Saint Progenitor! Whoosh! Whoosh!!! The Battle Progenitor and the Realm Progenitor appeared beside Ye Tian one after another. Suddenly, the Battle Sect effectively possessed four Progenitors and held top-tier Progenitor combat power. In an instant, they surpassed the Dark Holy Sect and could be called the number one sect of the Dark Sacred Domain.
Of course.
The Battle Sect did not yet have top-tier Progenitor combat power in the outside world. After all, the combat power of the clone, Progenitor Qingye, was inferior to the main body in the outside world. Therefore, they needed to keep a low profile for the time being.
If Ye Tian's main body broke through to top-tier Progenitor combat power and then copied another body, it would be equivalent to two top-tier Progenitors, plus a clone capable of exerting top-tier Progenitor combat power in the Dark Soul Domain. That would be equivalent to three top-tier Progenitor combat forces.
At that time, the Battle Sect would have even greater confidence, and the human race would have even greater backing.
For now, Ye Tian was still unable to directly contend with so many Progenitors at once; if all those Progenitors joined forces, they would be no match for them.
Consequently, as these Progenitors searched for resources in the Original Ancestral Realm, Ye Tian did not stop them.
The other Progenitors left one by one to search for resources.
Ye Tian did not waste time either, searching for resources at extreme speed.
Progenitor Qingye also mobilized the power of the Dark Soul Domain to search for the resources of the Original Ancestral Realm.
Perhaps because a Progenitor had fallen, the Progenitors did not continue fighting afterward. Unless they encountered particularly precious resources, they only occasionally exchanged blows, and it would end quickly.
In the hundreds of millions of years that followed, the Dark Soul Domain was in a stage of resource explosion. A large number of Domain Ancestors flooded into the Dark Soul Domain to snatch resources and fight.
The Progenitors did not act; this was a rule among the Progenitors, not allowing interference in the affairs of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
Although the human race was powerful now, they could not contend against all the forces of the entire Dark Forbidden Zone. Therefore, they could only give up most of their territory and concentrate on resource-rich areas.
Gradually, the division of territory in the Dark Soul Domain became clear.
Almost every force occupied a territory, and even the experts of the Dark Destruction Domain occupied enough territory.
As for the local forces of the Dark Soul Domain, they were quite miserable. Because no Progenitor had been born among them, their status was too low. Many forces were wiped out or annexed by other forces.
As time passed, those local forces would cease to exist, gradually merging into the forces of the Dark Soul Domain and the Dark Destruction Domain.
In reality, the Dark Soul Domain had originally been controlled by the human race, and there weren't many local forces to begin with. Now, after this upheaval, the local forces naturally collapsed.
It could be said that the Dark Soul Domain had completely become the territory of the Dark Soul Domain, the Dark Destruction Domain, the Eternal Cult, and other forces.
They did not have the luck of the Dark Sacred Domain, which could control its own Dark Sacred Land.
Time passed slowly, and in the blink of an eye, three hundred million years had gone by.
At this point, the resource explosion stage of the Dark Soul Domain had ended. More resources might be born later, but it was almost impossible for resources to be born in such a concentrated manner as they were now.
Additionally.
The Original Ancestral Realm was also sealed off. After all, the previous battles had damaged the Original Ancestral Realm, and an Original Ancestral Realm of a Dark Sacred Domain had to be well-preserved so that more good resources could be obtained in the future.
If it wasn't sealed, the damage to the Original Ancestral Realm would be too great, which would affect its operation in the future and make it difficult for precious treasures to be born.
More importantly, no single force could completely occupy the Original Ancestral Realm; that was the real reason.
At this time, the second Ancestral Realm besides the Original Ancestral Realm was born.
According to the birth rules of the Dark Sacred Land, every interval of one Realm Year, the Dark Soul Domain would give birth to an Ancestral Realm. A total of ten Ancestral Realms could be born, though these ten Ancestral Realms did not include the Original Ancestral Realm.
For the sake of the Ancestral Realm, the Progenitors began to deliberate.
As soon as the meeting began.
Ye Tian demanded: "Our human race will occupy one Ancestral Realm!"
"On what grounds?"
The Destruction Progenitor said angrily.
The other Progenitors also disagreed one after another.
"On the grounds that my clone holds a portion of the authority of the Dark Soul Domain and can exert top-tier Progenitor combat power, and that I am about to promote to a top-tier Progenitor!"
Ye Tian's main body revealed an aura that was even more powerful than it had been three hundred million years ago.
During these three hundred million years, Ye Tian had also been secretly refining Nirvana treasures. Although the actual time spent practicing wasn't long, he had repeatedly used time acceleration arrays to cultivate. Thus, he had refined a massive amount of Nirvana treasures, and his strength was about to step into the level of top-tier Progenitor combat power.
"In at most one hundred million years, the Progenitor of Myriad Laws will inevitably promote to a top-tier Progenitor!"
The Destruction Progenitor and the Saint Progenitor were both wary.
But they could not kill Ye Tian, because he was incredibly close to top-tier Progenitor combat power and possessed an ultimate defensive Ancestral Technique. Such a Progenitor was too difficult to kill.
In addition, Ye Tian had a clone with top-tier Progenitor combat power, which made it impossible to kill him off.
"Agreed!"
"Agreed!"
The Destruction Progenitor and the Saint Progenitor discussed it and decided to give the human race one Ancestral Realm.
However, for the remaining Ancestral Realm slots, the human race would not be allowed to compete again.
Nevertheless, there were still nine Ancestral Realms left. There certainly wouldn't be enough to go around for so many forces, so it was inevitable that several forces would simultaneously control one Ancestral Realm, using it as a public world.
Subsequently, Ye Tian refined this Ancestral Realm and renamed it the Huaxia Ancestral Realm.
The human race originated from Huaxia, so using this name served as a way to remember the Huaxia of Earth.
Following this, a large number of human experts entered the Huaxia Ancestral Realm, and countless humans were moved here.
An Ancestral Realm was incredibly vast and could accommodate a very large population.
Furthermore, with the Huaxia Ancestral Realm as the core, the human race was distributed here.
Basically, humans from other regions of the Dark Soul Domain were almost all moved here to avoid being wiped out by other forces.
Before the Profound Void Sacred Realm was promoted to an Ancestral Realm, the Huaxia Ancestral Realm would serve as the core world of the human race for a period of time to come.
