Chapter 1411: Slaying Two Invincible Domain Ancestors
!
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor was a Two-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, and the Crying Blood Emperor didn't think he was his match.
Unless his main body was here, only then could he contend with the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor; otherwise, he simply couldn't defeat him.
Moreover, he wasn't even sure if the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor had... advanced to a One-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, as it had been a very long time since he last saw him make a move.
“If you, Crying Blood Emperor, can appear here, why can't I?”
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor said with a smile, but did not explicitly answer the Crying Blood Emperor's question.
The Crying Blood Emperor knew very well that he had no way to rescue Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng today.
“Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, I just came to join in the fun today, I'll leave now!”
With that, the Crying Blood Emperor prepared to depart.
He certainly didn't want this clone of his to perish here; after all, this clone was also a part of his main body's power, and once it perished, it would take a long time to recover.
Although he also really wanted the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus, he simply couldn't complete the Azure Edge Saint Lord's mission now, so he might as well give up, otherwise the loss would be too great.
However, he wanted to leave, but the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor wouldn't let him.
“Crying Blood Emperor, since you've come, then don't leave. You once killed a Domain Ancestor of our War Gate; on the War Gate's wanted list, you are ranked ninth!”
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor instantly made his move.
One saw the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor holding a top-tier Saint Weapon-grade staff, into which nine holy artifacts were integrated, demonstrating the immense power of this supreme Saint Weapon.
Boom!!!! The supreme Saint Weapon staff fiercely struck towards the Crying Blood Emperor, causing the entire Profound Void Saint Realm to tremble slightly, a testament to the terrifying power of that single strike.
“Not good!”
The Crying Blood Emperor was greatly alarmed, fully unleashing a defensive Saint Skill in an attempt to resist the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor.
However—with a crack! The defensive Saint Skill shattered, and the defensive Saint Weapon on his body also shattered.
With a bang! The Crying Blood Emperor's body was blown to pieces; although it healed back together, the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor's attacks were relentless.
Countless staff shadows enveloped the Crying Blood Emperor, blowing him up again and again.
Soon.
This clone of the Crying Blood Emperor was truly and completely blown apart, no longer able to heal, and even its soul was annihilated.
Before this clone died, the Crying Blood Emperor angrily declared, “Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, one day, I will kill you!”
“You're always welcome!”
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor said with a smile.
....After the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor slew this clone of the Crying Blood Emperor, many Domain Ancestors breathed a sigh of relief.
At the very least, the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor was an invincible figure of the War Gate, one of its thirty-six War Emperors, so it was impossible for him to attack them.
At this moment.
Ye Tian was still attacking Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng, showing no intention of stopping.
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor did not intervene; there was no need for him to forcefully intervene, and moreover, Ye Tian had the strength to slay these two invincible Domain Ancestors.
“It's over, completely over. Even if the Saint Lord came here, he wouldn't be able to save us, unless the Grand Elder made a move!”
Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng said in despair.
However, would the Grand Elder make a move? Impossible! The Grand Elder's injuries had not yet recovered; how could he dare to leave the Azure Origin Saint Realm? If he were to leave, there might be a risk of perishing if he were attacked by a hostile Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
And by staying in the Azure Origin Saint Realm, other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors wouldn't dare to invade the Azure Origin Holy Land, otherwise it would be provoking the Holy Land Alliance! As one of the Holy Lands, the Azure Origin Holy Land was naturally protected.
Therefore, it was impossible for the Grand Elder to come to their rescue.
“Even if we die, we'll take you with us!”
Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng said angrily.
Immediately, the two unleashed forbidden techniques, and their strength greatly increased.
However, no matter how desperately they fought, they still couldn't harm Ye Tian.
As they unleashed forbidden techniques, the duration they could hold on for was much shorter. As the forbidden technique's effect wore off, they became extremely weak, completely unable to resist Ye Tian's attacks, and their injuries fully flared up.
Not long after, Domain Ancestor Mili was blasted to death by Ye Tian.
Afterwards, Domain Ancestor Wansheng also completely perished.
The demise of two invincible Domain Ancestors here deeply shocked the many onlookers.
At this moment, many Supreme Domain Ancestors looked at Ye Tian with eyes full of awe.
After Ye Tian killed Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng, he collected their treasures, and even the Azure Origin World Pearl fell into his hands.
However, he didn't have time to inspect these treasures; instead, he flew to the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor.
“Many thanks to Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor for your assistance!”
Ye Tian said gratefully.
If it weren't for the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor's intervention, he might have been fine this time, but Domain Ancestor Mili and Domain Ancestor Wansheng would definitely have escaped, and the human race's Saint-grade ore vein would naturally be lost.
“Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, let's find a place to chat!”
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor said.
“Alright!”
Ye Tian invited the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor to the Human Realm of the human race and personally hosted him.
Human Realm.
“Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, you must have come out of the War Realm Tower, right?”
Ye Tian said.
“Oh, I thought I had hidden myself deeply enough. I didn't expect you to have sensed it during the battle. It seems your control over the Profound Void Saint Realm is very, very high!”
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor said in surprise.
“Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, you should have entered from the War Realm Tower of the Dark Saint Domain. How are you able to come out from the War Realm Tower of our Profound Void Ancient Realm? This is too incredible, isn't it?
If the War Realm Tower could freely traverse, wouldn't it be comparable to the Space-Time Gate of the Dark Destruction Domain?”
The Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor explained.
“Hahaha, Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, you're thinking too much. The War Realm Tower is naturally far from comparable to the Space-Time Gate.
In reality, the War Realm Tower can allow a Domain Ancestor to traverse to another War Realm Tower, but there's no way to leave the War Realm Tower too far.
For example, I can currently only stay in the Profound Void Saint Realm at most; once I leave too far, the War Realm Tower will suck me back in.
In fact, if I stay a little longer..., the War Realm Tower's consumption is very great.
You can understand it as me still being inside the War Realm Tower; it's just that the War Realm Tower allows me to manifest in the outside world in a special way.
Of course, only invincible Domain Ancestors can do this; Supreme Domain Ancestors cannot, otherwise they wouldn't be able to withstand the War Realm Tower's power, and their physical bodies would instantly turn to dust! Therefore, the War Realm Tower's limitations are too great, far from comparable to the Space-Time Gate!”
“So that's how it is!”
Ye Tian understood.
“Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, be careful. This time you killed those two invincible Domain Ancestors. I don't know which force is behind them, but if they are from the Saint Gate, they might continue to retaliate against you.
If you truly encounter a desperate situation, seek help from the War Gate immediately, understand?”
“Many thanks, Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, I understand!”
Ye Tian nodded.
In reality, he knew that those two invincible Domain Ancestors were from the Azure Origin Holy Land, but he didn't say it.
Even if he said it, the one suffering severe losses this time was the Azure Origin Holy Land. The War Gate wouldn't destroy the Azure Origin Holy Land; at most, they would suppress it.
