Chapter 1639 The Forbidden Stele That Disappointed Ye Tian!

For Ye Tian, obtaining the secret art of the world of will this time was the greatest gain, even more precious than all kinds of rare resources.
Ye Tian did not challenge the sixth tier. To pass the sixth tier, even the strength of a second-tier half-step Eternal Realm was not enough; it probably required a third-tier one.
Therefore, Ye Tian gave up challenging the sixth tier and came out directly.
When he came out, True River Sword Ancestor and the others were already no longer here; clearly, they had left.
Mistless Mountain was not limited to East Return Mountain and the Mistless Pagoda as places of opportunity; there were also other blessed places. Therefore, True River Sword Ancestor and the others did not want to waste time.
Very soon, Ye Tian arrived at another blessed place on Mistless Mountain—the Forbidden Stele!
The Forbidden Stele was the greatest opportunity on Mistless Mountain. Comprehending the Forbidden Stele did not improve one’s control over the Eternal Law or the Innate Eternal Law, but instead allowed one to comprehend the mysteries of the fusion between the body and the Innate Eternal Law.
The fusion of the body with the Innate Eternal Law was too difficult. Many peak half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses could not even figure out the direction, but the Forbidden Stele could offer some help.
Of course, this so-called help could only raise the success rate by less than one percent, but that was still quite good.
If there were more such opportunities, would that not mean there would be hope of advancing to the Eternal Realm?
Therefore, the Forbidden Stele was extremely precious to half-step Eternal Realm cultivators, unfortunately they could not take it away.
As for Eternal Realm cultivators, they likely looked down on the Forbidden Stele, or perhaps they could not take it away either, which was why the Forbidden Stele had always remained on Mistless Mountain in the Mistless Territory.
The Forbidden Stele was located at the summit of Forbidden Peak on Mistless Mountain, where there were many Space-Time Dragon Pythons.
At this moment.
One after another, half-step Eternal Realm cultivators set up formations to resist the attacks of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons, allowing the other half-step Eternal Realm cultivators to peacefully comprehend the Forbidden Stele. Everyone worked together to comprehend it.
“They’re actually collaborating to comprehend the Forbidden Stele!”
Ye Tian had not expected these half-step Eternal Realm beings to cooperate. But when he thought about it, if they did not cooperate, they would simply be unable to comprehend it in peace. The Forbidden Stele did not prohibit Space-Time Dragon Pythons from approaching, so without anyone to protect them, comprehending it alone would be courting death.
After that.
Ye Tian also joined in, but since he had joined later, he naturally was not qualified to comprehend the Forbidden Stele immediately; he had to wait for the next batch.
After a long while, it was finally Ye Tian’s group of powerhouses’ turn to comprehend the Forbidden Stele.
The Forbidden Stele was not very large, only about a hundred meters tall. It was purple in color, surrounded by special purple halos, and within the range of the halo, one could use the Forbidden Stele to comprehend the mysteries of the fusion between the body and the Innate Eternal Law.
At this moment.
Many half-step Eternal Realm cultivators took out various resources to assist their comprehension, helping themselves comprehend quickly.
However, this clone of Ye Tian did not bring much in the way of resources, so he could only rely on himself.
Boom!!!!
In his perception, strands of Innate Eternal Law continually evolved and slowly merged with life, demonstrating the process of the fusion of various forms of life with the Innate Eternal Law.
Very soon, one lifeform after another collapsed and perished during the fusion; the fusion attempts of billions upon billions of lives all ended in failure, but a few lives successfully fused and advanced into the Eternal Realm.
“The Forbidden Stele is actually showing the fusion process in a special way, allowing half-step Eternal Realm cultivators to comprehend it!”
Ye Tian finally understood why the Forbidden Stele had the effect of assisting fusion.
Just by watching this, even a fool could gain something!
However, this straightforward method was indeed effective, and very useful.
Ye Tian also quickly became immersed in his comprehension, even placing himself into the situation, though unfortunately these lives fusing with the Innate Eternal Law were not using their own bodies as experimental subjects.
And everyone’s body was different; the Innate Eternal Law each comprehended was also different, and the methods and difficulty of fusion were different as well.
Therefore, these examples of fusion could not be fully combined; they could only provide some limited gain.
Of course.
The Forbidden Stele still allowed half-step Eternal Realm cultivators to gain a deeper understanding and insight into their own Eternal Law or Innate Eternal Law, and that was also one of its benefits.
“Too bad it isn’t of much help to me!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He would be better off using cloned bodies in the future to repeatedly attempt fusion, finding experience through countless failures; that would definitely be more useful than comprehending the Forbidden Stele.
But other half-step Eternal Realm powerhouses did not have methods like his, so naturally they could not do that. They could only rely on treasures like the Forbidden Stele, seeking hope again and again.
After comprehending it for some time, Ye Tian gave up on the Forbidden Stele and chose to leave.
Afterward, at various blessed places on Mistless Mountain, he also gained some things.
For example, in one place on Mistless Mountain there were traces of an Eternal Realm battle; those traces still remained, and one could still feel the profound mysteries of Eternal Realm techniques.
Another example was the rows of stone statues on Mistless Mountain. These stone statues contained some inheritances, and if one carefully comprehended them, one could still gain some Eternal techniques.
However, this was only helpful to ordinary half-step Eternal Realm cultivators. Those ordinary half-step Eternal Realm cultivators had not comprehended any Eternal techniques before, which was why they wanted to obtain some Eternal technique inheritances and use other Eternal techniques to create their own.
But Ye Tian already possessed a large number of Eternal techniques, so he looked down on such inheritances.
One blessed place after another was rather disappointing to Ye Tian.
It had to be said that his vision was now higher, and he no longer looked favorably upon the benefits here.
“Mistless Territory was discovered long ago. I don’t know how many half-step Eternal Realm cultivators have entered here; the benefits that can be left behind are too few........”
Ye Tian thought.
But this time, his greatest gains still came from the Rift Wind Valley and the world of will; next were treasures like the Void God Leaf.
After some more time passed, True River Sword Ancestor and the others invited Ye Tian to go to certain territories of the Mistless Territory to collect resources. As long as they could withstand the attacks of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons, they could seize some of the resources that were born here.
As long as they did not go to Endless Sea, there was actually still a great chance of surviving.
But there would definitely be powerhouses who went to Endless Sea, such as those half-step Eternal Realm cultivators of the fifth and sixth tiers. They probably would join forces to force their way into Endless Sea and seize those extremely precious resources.
Ye Tian rejected True River Sword Ancestor’s suggestion; he had no interest in this now.
At this moment.
Ye Tian was trying to cultivate the world of will in Rift Wind Valley, and his clone had also returned here.
In fact, clones and copy bodies both possessed the Copying Talent. Even if one of them fell, the Copying Talent could copy another one again.
Therefore, he could continuously try to cultivate the world of will without fear of falling.
And the environment of Rift Wind Valley was perfectly suited to the world of will; it was the best place.
The wind of Rift Wind Valley tempered the will, while the world of will strengthened the will. The two combined perfectly, but without many lives to spare, even the Mistless Territory Lord would not dare to cultivate so crazily—yet Ye Tian did.
On this day.
When Ye Tian was cultivating the world of will, a problem arose with his will, causing a problem with his body.
Coupled with the wind of Rift Wind Valley blowing over, his will collapsed and his flesh began to disintegrate.
Instantly, this clone dissipated.
“The clone has fallen; copy another one!”
Ye Tian accelerated time, and very soon he could condense another body and begin cultivating again.
No one knew how long had passed when True River Sword Ancestor sent a transmission.
“Endless Ancestor, the entrance is about to close. We have to leave!”
Ye Tian immediately replied to him: “True River Sword Ancestor, what I entered with was a clone, and I didn’t bring any treasures. I’m not planning to leave. I intend to cultivate here for a few more days!”
True River Sword Ancestor did not try to persuade Ye Tian anymore; he guessed that Ye Tian was cultivating in Rift Wind Valley.
But if one cultivated for too long, the clone would not be able to hold on and would still have to come out. Once it came out, it would have to withstand the attacks of the Space-Time Dragon Pythons, and still would not survive.
Therefore, doing this would only let him stay a few more days; it was of little use.
However, he also knew that Ye Tian was only a clone, so the loss was not great, and thus he did not try to persuade him.
Very soon, one after another half-step Eternal Realm cultivators left the Mistless Territory, leaving only Ye Tian behind.
