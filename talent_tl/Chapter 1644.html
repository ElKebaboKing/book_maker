Chapter 1643 Five Extreme Stage, the Sea of Silence!

Inside the Source God Pavilion.
Ye Tian and the other twelve ancestors all came and took their seats one by one.
Ye Tian also roughly understood the situation, but not much.
When True River Sword Ancestor and the other twelve ancestors saw Ye Tian and sensed Ye Tian’s cultivation, they were immediately shocked.
“Congratulations to the Endless Founding Ancestor on advancing to the Extreme Stage!”
“Congratulations to the Endless Founding Ancestor on advancing to the Extreme Stage!”
“Congratulations to the Endless Founding Ancestor on advancing to the Extreme Stage!”
The twelve ancestors all congratulated him one after another.
They even felt that Ye Tian’s strength was more terrifying than theirs, but it was hard to say exactly how many Extreme Stages he had reached; but he was definitely above the first Extreme Stage.
For a time, everyone’s attitude toward Ye Tian changed slightly, and perhaps even they themselves did not notice it—there was a faint trace of respect.
Very quickly, everyone returned to the main topic.
True River Sword Ancestor said, “Everyone, the main topic of this meeting is to discuss a major event that happened recently. I believe all of you know that many temporal lords have dispatched many Extreme Stage experts to the Source Sea, all for the sake of seeking an opportunity.”
Wind Ancient Source Ancestor followed up, “I don’t know much about this matter. I only know that it is related to an opportunity from one hundred thousand Eternal Years ago, but I myself have not existed for even sixty thousand Eternal Years, so I don’t know much about such distant ages.”
“I know even less!”
Ye Tian shook his head.
One Eternal Year was equivalent to a million Tribulation Yuan years. How many Eternal Years had he lived for?
So, he had no idea about such a secret at all.
Seeing this, True River Sword Ancestor said, “In fact, in the Source God Pavilion, I should be the only Half-step Eternal Being who truly experienced one hundred thousand Eternal Years. One hundred thousand Eternal Years ago, that was the era known as before the beginning of the Source Sea. Many Eternal Beings fought to seize an opportunity. The number of fallen Eternal Beings alone reached double digits or more; countless Eternal Heavens were blasted apart, and endless small heavens were shattered into nothingness. As for the Supreme Beings who were affected and killed, there is no way to know how many there were. I count as one of the lucky Half-step Eternal Beings who survived.”
“It is said that once every one hundred thousand Eternal Years, the Source Sea will give birth to an opportunity, and the opportunity from last time was too precious, which made many Eternal Beings tempted, and that was why such a terrifying war erupted. I learned from a secret channel that the last opportunity was a supreme treasure that could greatly increase the strength of even an Eternal Being, and even gave hope of standing above other Eternal Beings. However, I don’t know exactly what it was, and I don’t even know whose hands it fell into.”
“However, after the last war among Eternal Beings, the Lord Alliance stipulated that Eternal Beings were not allowed to stay in the Source Sea for long periods of time. This time, it should not become as great a war as last time. Still, once that opportunity appears, there will definitely be no shortage of Eternal Beings taking part in the battle.”
True River Sword Ancestor spoke at length, allowing Ye Tian and the others to basically understand the situation.
At this time, they were also very interested in such an opportunity.
If they were to obtain such an opportunity, could they become Eternal Beings?
Of course, the greater possibility was that while fighting over the opportunity, they would be casually crushed to death by an Eternal Being.
Unless they were lucky enough for the opportunity to be born without much commotion, and just happened to be discovered by them.
But based on what they had learned, when the opportunity first appeared, there would be an anomaly, and although the range covered by the anomaly was not large, the chance of it being discovered was very, very high.
Especially now, when the major forces had all deployed countless people, once the opportunity appeared, it would definitely be discovered.
At the meeting.
Some Half-step Eternal Beings proposed not participating in this matter, while others proposed taking a gamble—what if they were lucky?
It was obvious that quite a few Half-step Eternal Beings still yearned desperately for such an opportunity; perhaps this was their only chance to charge toward Eternal Being.
In the end.
True River Sword Ancestor said, “I don’t really recommend that you participate, and in today’s era, it is very easy to come into conflict with those Extreme Stage experts who have arrived from the temporal lands. If you don’t plan to participate, then cultivate in seclusion during this period. If there are any unexpected situations, everyone can share information. If the opportunity appears and the Eternal Beings’ battle becomes too tragic, I will provide a place for everyone to take shelter.”
Very quickly, the meeting ended.
One after another, the Half-step Eternal Beings left, and Ye Tian also left.
……
“What exactly is the opportunity that appears once every one hundred thousand Eternal Years?”
Ye Tian was somewhat curious.
If it did not endanger his own life or affect the Dark Forbidden Domain, then he would not mind joining in. If he truly gained a bit of benefit, perhaps it would be enough for him to grow into an Eternal Being.
Perhaps that was also the thinking of many Half-step Eternal Beings.
Time passed, and more and more Extreme Stage experts from the various temporal lords came to the Source Sea, naturally bringing about some conflicts with the major forces.
News of Supreme Beings being killed was often heard, and most of it involved Extreme Stage and Half-step Eternal Being powerhouses from the temporal lands.

Meanwhile, Ye Tian remained very low-key, cultivating all the while, and the Dark Forbidden Domain side remained very peaceful.
Unknowingly, several hundred thousand Tribulation Yuan years passed again.
The opportunity born once every one hundred thousand Eternal Years had still not appeared. Even those Eternal Beings were anxious now; some Eternal Beings even personally came to the Source Sea to investigate certain regions, but they quickly left again.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian ended his cultivation.
At this time, he had already cultivated to the Five Extreme Stage. After all, before the Seven Extreme Stage, he had basically no bottleneck; he only needed to increase his cultivation, and his strength would rise accordingly.
However, after reaching the Five Extreme Stage, he had almost used up the resources on him. Although he still had a few treasures left, even if he took them out to trade for resources, they would not fetch many valuable resources.

On this day.
The artifact spirit of the Void Source Hall came to inform Ye Tian of a major matter.
“Master, some changes have occurred outside the Void Source Hall.”
The artifact spirit reported.
Changes outside the Void Source Hall?
Ye Tian’s expression changed.
The Void Source Hall was located in a forbidden zone of the Source Sea, and this forbidden zone was the Sea of Silence.
Even a Half-step Eternal Being like Void Source Ancestor did not dare to step into the Sea of Silence, and had thrown the Void Source Hall into the Sea of Silence. The enemies of Void Source Ancestor also did not dare to enter the Sea of Silence to search for the Void Source Hall.
This shows how terrifying the Sea of Silence was.
Even a Half-step Eternal Being at the first Extreme Stage would not dare to risk entering the Sea of Silence, because the Sea of Silence possessed a peculiar power. Those with unsteady wills would sink into total silence and become a floating corpse in the Sea of Silence.
Although the Void Source Hall could resist the power of the Sea of Silence, once it went outside, it still would not be enough.
There had once been an Extreme Stage expert who conducted experiments. Basically, one needed at least the strength of the third Extreme Stage to walk in the Sea of Silence, but even then there was still some danger, and only Half-step Eternal Beings above the sixth Extreme Stage could completely ignore the dangers of the Sea of Silence.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian’s duplicate body directly walked out of the Void Source Hall and arrived in the Sea of Silence.
Buzz!!!!
Waves of power surged in, but Ye Tian’s will had already reached the second level of the Will World, and his will power was almost comparable to an Eternal Being, so he ignored those strange powers.
