Chapter 1505: This Gate Belongs to the Human Race Now!

"Suppression?"
Ye Tian attempted to step through the gate and enter the Sanctuary World, but he felt a sense of suppression emanating from the gate.
This gate allowed top-tier Ancestors to enter easily, but Ye Tian's duplicate body had reached the level of a Supreme Ancestor, and thus was restricted by the gate.
Of course, Ye Tian could also force his way in, though it would require paying a certain price.
Whoosh!!!!!
Ye Tian brought out a massive amount of energy resources and infused them into the gate, causing it to glow brilliantly.
At the same time, Ye Tian forced his way through. He suffered the gate's backlash; even if his body was injured and his strength declined after passing through, it didn't matter.
With his current power, even with a decline in strength, he could still suppress ordinary Supreme Saint Beasts.
Boom!!!!!
Ye Tian forcibly crossed the gate and entered the Sanctuary World.
The Hai Tian Saint Sect was currently preparing for the Great Calamity. Although many Saint Beasts guarded the gate, the two Supreme Saint Beasts were not present.
Upon arriving in the Sanctuary World, Ye Tian instantly slaughtered these Saint Beasts and a large number of ordinary Hai Tian Saint Sect disciples. Even top-tier Saint Beasts could not withstand a single blow from him.
Toward these exotic beasts and creatures of a foreign realm, Ye Tian felt no pity.
The Dark Forbidden Domain had faced three Great Calamities, and in each one, the creatures of the domain were wiped out by foreign worlds, with only a few top-tier Ancestors surviving. Why should he feel pity for other foreign worlds? Even if all the living beings in the Sanctuary World were wiped out, it would be of little consequence.
The headquarters of the Hai Tian Saint Sect was not far from the gate. At this moment, the Wolf Bone Saint Beast and the Violent Ape Saint Beast became aware of Ye Tian's arrival, feeling both shocked and bewildered.
They didn't know much about the Dark Forbidden Domain of this era, but they were well aware that there should absolutely be no Supreme Ancestors in the Dark Forbidden Domain right now. After all, those beings couldn't travel to other foreign worlds, nor did they possess the Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals of other worlds. How could they possibly break through to the Supreme Ancestor realm?
Yet now, a Supreme Ancestor had actually come from the Dark Forbidden Domain?
"Kill him! This gate must not be lost. With the Great Calamity approaching, we cannot let a Supreme Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Domain destroy everything we have!"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast and the Violent Ape Saint Beast thought to themselves.
They believed that with the three Supreme Saint Beasts of the Hai Tian Saint Sect working together, there was a great chance they could slay a Supreme Ancestor who had just crossed the gate and was in a weakened state.
"He Yuan Saint Beast, let us strike together and kill this Supreme Ancestor!"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast sent a telepathic message.
Boom!!!!
The three Supreme Saint Beasts instantly charged from the Hai Tian Saint Sect headquarters toward Ye Tian.
Meanwhile, the other Saint Beasts retreated one after another. They could not interfere in a battle at the level of Supreme Saint Beasts and Supreme Ancestors; even the shockwaves alone could claim their lives.
Right at that moment, the He Yuan Saint Beast changed his line of attack and struck the weakest one, the Wolf Bone Saint Beast.
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast never expected the He Yuan Saint Beast to attack him, so he had no defenses prepared.
Instantly, the Wolf Bone Saint Beast was sent flying by the blow, suffering significant injuries.
And at that moment, Ye Tian's attack arrived.
Ye Tian did not hold back at all, unleashing an attack at the second tier of the Supreme Ancestor level. Although his strength had been weakened by the gate's backlash, his offensive power still reached the second tier.
"Supreme Ancestral Technique: Tyrant Force!"
This blow landed squarely on the Wolf Bone Saint Beast.
In an instant, the Wolf Bone Saint Beast was blown apart. Although he managed to reform his body, the wounds on his form continued to crack and could not heal at all.
At this moment, he was incomparably heavily injured.
"Second tier of the Supreme Ancestor level!"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast was dazed.
In the entire Sanctuary World, there was only one Supreme Saint Beast who had reached the second tier. The Dark Forbidden Domain hadn't even had a single Supreme Ancestor before, yet now a second-tier Supreme Ancestor had suddenly appeared. This was too surreal.
"Violent Ape Saint Beast, run!"
The Wolf Bone Saint Beast shouted.
He knew he couldn't escape. Being heavily injured, neither Ye Tian nor the He Yuan Saint Beast would let him go.
On the other hand, the Violent Ape Saint Beast still had a hope of escaping.
The Violent Ape Saint Beast knew deep down that he was no match for Ye Tian, so he didn't dare stay. He immediately began to flee, while the Wolf Bone Saint Beast risked his life to stall Ye Tian.
In the end, the Violent Ape Saint Beast managed to escape, while the Wolf Bone Saint Beast was killed by Ye Tian.
Ye Tian didn't care in the slightest about the Violent Ape Saint Beast's escape.
He wasn't a third-tier Supreme Ancestor; otherwise, he could have easily killed both Supreme Saint Beasts.
Next, Ye Tian had the He Yuan Saint Beast take over the management of the Hai Tian Saint Sect. Naturally, there were many who resisted.
But Ye Tian showed no mercy. Whoever resisted was killed instantly.
In a short amount of time, nearly half of the Saint Beasts in the Hai Tian Saint Sect were slaughtered. Some fled, while only a few remained to pledge loyalty to the He Yuan Saint Beast. As for the beasts below the Saint level, most of them surrendered. They didn't dare resist, and with the Great Calamity arriving, they couldn't survive outside. They had to rely on a Supreme Saint Beast to have any hope of living, so naturally, they didn't dare rebel.
In this way, Ye Tian had effectively taken control of the Hai Tian Saint Sect.
...
In the Sanctuary World, in a silent stretch of void.
The Violent Ape Saint Beast struck the void in fury, roaring madly.
"He Yuan Saint Beast, WHY????"
He could not understand why the He Yuan Saint Beast would help a Supreme Ancestor from the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Even if the He Yuan Saint Beast had some deal with the people of the Dark Forbidden Domain, would he have turned against his own kind?
Even if that Supreme Ancestor was powerful, if the three of them had joined forces, they might have been able to barely contend with him. They wouldn't have ended up in such a state.
"Damn that He Yuan Saint Beast! Even if you side with the Supreme Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain, they won't value you. They might even kill you one day! Why are you so foolish?! They are an alien race—why are you of one mind with them?"
The Violent Ape Saint Beast couldn't understand, nor could he find a way to make sense of it.
However, he had no idea that the He Yuan Saint Beast was actually Ye Tian's clone. It was essentially Ye Tian himself. Helping oneself was, of course, perfectly normal.
At the Hai Tian Saint Sect.
Ye Tian began to draw upon the power of the gate to create a forbidden zone within the Sanctuary World, preparing for the future entry of the human race's Ancestors.
Previously, Ye Tian didn't know how to construct a gate, nor did he know how to build a forbidden zone.
But once the He Yuan Saint Beast became a Supreme Saint Beast, Wolf Bone and the others had informed him. Combined with the construction methods plundered from the Wolf Bone Saint Beast—and confirming nothing was hidden—he now set out to build a forbidden zone base.
A so-called forbidden zone base involved drawing power from the gate to alter the rules of an area, turning it into a forbidden land. As long as the gate wasn't destroyed, the forbidden zone would not dissipate.
Ye Tian could also build a gate himself, but the cost was too high. He would have to capture a phantom gate of a world and fuse it with countless materials to create an eternal gate.
One phantom gate is born every Dark Era; nine gates are born over nine Dark Eras.
Previously, the Dark Forbidden Domain had also birthed phantom gates, but he didn't know the construction methods then, so he hadn't occupied them.
Additionally, which foreign world a gate connects to is normally uncontrollable. Only by locking onto the coordinates of a specific foreign world can a gate be temporarily modified to connect to a designated realm.
That would be extremely troublesome.
Right now, Ye Tian didn't have time to wait. He could only utilize this gate of the Hai Tian Saint Sect.
In short, this gate now belonged to him—it belonged to the human race!.
