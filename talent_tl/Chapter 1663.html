Chapter 1662: The Purple Stone Is in Hand!

For those half-step Eternal Realm experts, Void Beasts did not have any clear equivalent classification; they were merely compared to Eternal Realm experts according to their strength.
This was because they had hardly ever come into contact with Void Beasts, whereas those ancient Eternal Realm experts had, and thus had assigned the Void Beasts one rank after another.
A first-tier Void Beast was comparable to the Immortal Life Realm, a second-tier Void Beast was comparable to the Undying Realm, while a third-tier Void Beast had not yet reached the Immortal Realm. It was stronger than a half-step Immortal Realm expert, yet not as strong as an Immortal Realm expert, but its difficulty to deal with was comparable to that of an Immortal Realm expert.
According to the comparison and analysis based on past clashes between Immortal Realm experts and third-tier Void Beasts, a newly advanced Immortal Realm expert could roughly handle six third-tier Void Beasts, while a fourth-tier Void Beast was somewhat stronger than a newly advanced Immortal Realm expert, comparable to an established Immortal Realm lord.
“Retreat!”
Those Undying Realm lords all withdrew, not daring to fight this third-tier Void Beast, because they truly could not beat it.
The third-tier Void Beast was extremely fast, and it immediately locked onto the Chenhai Lord.
The Chenhai Lord’s expression was also grave. Although he had once severely injured an Immortal Realm existence, he had done so by using a forbidden land and paying the price of three avatars to heavily wound that Immortal Realm expert, and there had also been an element of carelessness on the other side’s part.
And here was the Void Beast’s home ground. Even if all three of his avatars attacked together, he would be unable to do anything to this third-tier Void Beast, and might even be killed by it.
Only to see a blade appear in the Chenhai Lord’s hand as he continuously slashed at the third-tier Void Beast.
However, when those attacks landed on the third-tier Void Beast, most of the attack power was offset by the beast, and the remaining force was unable to deal much damage to it.
On the other hand, when faced with the Void Beast’s attacks, the Chenhai Lord had to deal with them with full force; a moment’s carelessness would leave him injured.
And the third-tier Void Beast had no intention of letting the other Undying Realm lords go either. Countless black tentacles emerged from its body and attacked the many Undying Realm lords, intending to leave them all here.
“Join forces together, or we won’t get out!”
the Undying Realm lords said.
Boom, boom, boom!!!!!
The Undying Realm lords all joined forces to contend against the third-tier Void Beast.
However, their attacks could not injure the third-tier Void Beast even in the slightest; they were completely nullified by the Void Beast. One Eternal technique after another blasted onto the third-tier Void Beast’s body, as though merely tickling it.
Soon after.
Some Undying Realm experts began to deploy formations, trying to trap this third-tier Void Beast.
However, even after the formations were set up and a large number of Eternal Source Weapon-grade items were used as the foundation, they still could not trap the third-tier Void Beast.
With a ripping sound!
One great formation after another was shattered and torn apart, and large numbers of Eternal Source Weapons were destroyed.
In no time at all, many Undying Realm lords were badly injured.
Suddenly.
The Chenhai Lord’s second avatar appeared, but he did not have the two avatars join forces to block the third-tier Void Beast. Instead, he had another avatar search for opportunities on this black island.
Whenever he encountered some top-grade resources, the Chenhai Lord would collect them all.
Perhaps because the Chenhai Lord’s two bodies put pressure on the third-tier Void Beast, it did not vigorously pursue and intercept the other Undying Realm lords, and thus some of them still managed to escape successfully.
Ye Tian also arrived here, waiting for an opportunity all along.
He did not dare to waste too much time. If he kept waiting silently like this and the item he needed that could upgrade the Copying Talent was taken away by some Undying Realm lord, then it would be very hard for him to get it back.
Therefore, he had to act as soon as possible.
But before acting, he planned to first fuse one Void-Forming Immortal Ancestor Talent.
He had not previously fused this Immortal Ancestor Talent called Void-Forming. If he fused it, then by using the ability of void-forming, he could also offset part of the attacks, and perhaps he could withstand one strike from a third-tier Void Beast without dying.
Immediately afterward, he began the fusion, and had his avatar accelerate time nearby.
Very soon.
The Void-Forming Immortal Ancestor Talent was successfully fused into Ye Tian’s copied body.
Next, Ye Tian’s copied body began entering this black island.
Upon entering the black island, Ye Tian followed his sense of the mysterious treasure and rapidly traversed through.
Perhaps because there were quite a number of Undying Realm experts on the black island, the third-tier Void Beast did not attack Ye Tian immediately, allowing Ye Tian to truly pass through.
How fast was the speed of the Eight Extremes Realm, not to mention that he had a clear target? Thus, he swiftly traversed to the location of that treasure.
“Purple stone!”
On a mountain peak, Ye Tian saw a purple stone. Without another word, he directly took away the purple stone, then began traversing out of this black island.
At the very moment he disappeared through traversal, a streak of black light pierced over.
Ye Tian activated many layers of defense, and even the Void-Forming Immortal Ancestor Talent was used as well.
With a puff!
The black light pierced through Ye Tian’s body, and Ye Tian desperately concealed his aura. Combined with the fact that his own strength was very weak, the low-IQ Void Beast thought that Ye Tian would definitely die from this strike, so it did not immediately follow up with another blow.
And that gave Ye Tian the chance and hope to escape.
He continued traversing, fleeing toward the outside of the black island.
And just as he left the black island, one finger of the third-tier Void Beast struck to kill, and a strike from afar crushed Ye Tian’s body.
Instantly, the flesh body of Ye Tian’s copied body was Annihilated, and even the will within that copied body was shattered as well, leaving only some matter behind.
At this time, however, Ye Tian’s avatar did not waste any time. It quickly arrived here, took the things Ye Tian had left behind, and fled again.
This was the benefit of having two bodies: after being killed, there was still a way to take the things away.
The third-tier Void Beast did not notice this, and thus did not immediately move against Ye Tian’s avatar, allowing Ye Tian’s avatar to successfully escape.
In fact, if it had pursued, it naturally could have killed Ye Tian.
However, there were still other Undying Realm experts and the Chenhai Lord’s two avatars here, so it would not abandon this place to chase Ye Tian.
Very soon.
Ye Tian’s avatar escaped from deep within the Abyssless Land and arrived in the outer region.
He found an island, hid himself, and then began to accelerate time. When he could once again condense a copied body, he condensed one.
After that.
Ye Tian’s copied body remained here, while his avatar carried the purple stone back outside, returning to the vicinity of his true body and handing it over to it.
Only in this way could he truly rest assured.
