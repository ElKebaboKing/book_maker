Chapter 1447: Initial Understanding of the Silent Annihilation Clan
!
War Ancestor Realm.
Ye Tian's duplicate body arrived here to request an audience with the War Ancestor.
Soon, Ye Tian met the War Ancestor. "War Ancestor, I wish to go and guard the edge of the Dark Forbidden Domain to resist the Silent Annihilation Clan!"
Ye Tian stated his purpose.
"You actually want to take the initiative to resist the Silent Annihilation Clan?"
The War Ancestor was incredibly shocked.
He knew exactly how terrifying the Silent Annihilation Clan was. Even he was unwilling to go and resist them, but he had no choice.
Otherwise, if the Silent Annihilation Clan invaded, they would eventually reach the Dark Holy Sanctuary. By then, even the Dark Holy Sanctuary would be unable to stop their invasion.
Furthermore, as the area occupied by the Silent Annihilation Clan grew larger, their numbers would also increase.
Therefore, the Silent Annihilation Clan had to be stopped.
However, no one was willing to take the initiative to resist them; it was always a move of desperation.
As for the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, Ye Tian, he was a newly ascended Supreme-level Domain Ancestor and would not need to resist the Silent Annihilation Clan for a very long time.
"War Ancestor, I truly wish to go and resist the Silent Annihilation Clan. This body is my special clone; it is no different from my main body. Even if it dies in battle, I will not truly perish!"
Ye Tian said.
"A clone? If you hadn't said so, I would have truly thought it was your main body.
Very well, since you want to go, then go. It just so happens you can replace a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of the War Sect!"
The War Ancestor said.
Subsequently, the War Ancestor told Ye Tian many things regarding the Silent Annihilation Clan.
First, the Dark Holy Sanctuary and the Dark Destruction Domain had each established a continent at the places where they resisted the Silent Annihilation Clan. One was the Sanctuary Continent, and the other was the Destruction Continent. The Primal Ancestors and Supreme-level Domain Ancestors resided on these two continents, resisting the Silent Annihilation Clan at all times.
The place where the Silent Annihilation Clan was located was too far from the Dark Holy Sanctuary. If a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor tried to fly there, it was unknown how many... Domain Eras it would take to arrive; it would be an unimaginable figure.
Thus, only Primal Ancestors could send Supreme-level Domain Ancestors there.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor, prepare yourself. I am going to send you to the Sanctuary Continent!"
The War Ancestor said.
The Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the War Sect liked to use the title of "War Ancestor." Since Ye Tian had broken through to the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor rank, he was called the Myriad Law War Ancestor.
If Ye Tian truly reached the Primal Ancestor level in the future, to avoid having the same title as the War Ancestor, he could take another Primal Ancestor name.
"Alright!"
Ye Tian responded.
Boom!!!! A powerful force enveloped Ye Tian's space-time, arriving at the Space-Time Source of the Dark Forbidden Domain, crossing an infinite distance in an instant.
This transmission took a full day before Ye Tian finally arrived at the Sanctuary Continent.
At this moment.
The connection between Ye Tian's duplicate body and his main body had become very weak. The distance was too great; even for information to pass between the two, it would likely take several thousand years.
"Something's not right, the environment here..."
Ye Tian sensed his surroundings. The spatial pressure here was immense, and time was extremely chaotic. Without the mind of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, one's consciousness would likely be eroded by the space-time power here.
Furthermore, it was difficult for him to exert his power.
"I'm afraid I won't be able to exert much of my strength here...!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Boom!!!! A Supreme-level Domain Ancestor appeared before Ye Tian, one whom Ye Tian did not recognize.
"You must be the Myriad Law War Ancestor?"
The newcomer asked.
"You are the Blood Dragon War Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said.
He had never met the Blood Dragon War Ancestor, but his Copy Talent had detected the name, thus revealing the Blood Dragon War Ancestor's identity.
The Blood Dragon War Ancestor was also one of the Supreme-level War Ancestors of the War Sect and one of its Vice Sect Masters.
However, this so-called Vice Sect Master title was actually just a title for the outside world. Within the War Sect, there was no such thing as a Vice Sect Master among Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
"It is I. The War Ancestor notified me of your arrival. I'll introduce you to the situation of the Sanctuary Continent and the things you need to watch out for. These matters are quite complex, and the War Ancestor didn't have the time to explain them to you one by one, so he let me be your guide!"
The Blood Dragon War Ancestor said.
Afterward, the Blood Dragon War Ancestor brought Ye Tian to a manor.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor, you shall live here from now on!"
The Blood Dragon War Ancestor said.
Then, the two sat down, and the Blood Dragon War Ancestor explained the situation to Ye Tian.
"Myriad Law War Ancestor, deep within the space-time in front of our Sanctuary Continent lies the Space-Time Corridor. The other end of the Space-Time Corridor is... the territory of the Silent Annihilation Clan. Their power is invading this region of space-time. Our goal is to... hold them back. Otherwise, the position of the Space-Time Corridor will keep moving, and their territory will grow larger.
Additionally, this place is different from the Dark Holy Sanctuary. You should have felt that you cannot exert your full power, right? This is normal; it is the same for everyone!"
"There is no Taboo Power here, nor any other power. There is only the Silent Annihilation energy of the Silent Annihilation Clan.
Strictly speaking, the Silent Annihilation energy of the Silent Annihilation Clan fills this... space-time. Unfortunately, it is very difficult for us to refine their energy; only Primal Ancestors have the ability to refine Silent Annihilation energy.
Therefore, once we fight here and consume too much power, it is very difficult to replenish energy.
So, once the energy resources on our person are depleted, we need to exchange for energy resources from the Primal Ancestors."
"Furthermore, this region of space-time also gives birth to many rare treasures and even some opportunities. There was once a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor who obtained an opportunity in this space-time and eventually ascended to become a Primal Ancestor.
Additionally, there are many Taboo creature races on the Sanctuary Continent. They are also Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, and almost all of them are under the command of the Taboo Holy Gate."
"The Silent Annihilation Clan launches a small attack almost every few tens of thousands of years. We try our best to fight the weaker members of the Silent Annihilation Clan. Do not seek out the powerful ones, or you won't even know how you died!"
"The strength of the Silent Annihilation Clan is easy to judge. You only need to look at the grey stripes on their bodies. One grey stripe means a One-Stripe Silent Annihilation member, whose strength is equivalent to a general Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. Then there are Two-Stripe, Three-Stripe... once they reach Five-Stripe, they are comparable to a Half-step Primal Ancestor.
If it is a Seven-Stripe Silent Annihilation Ancestor, that is Primal Ancestor combat power. The most powerful is the Nine-Stripe Silent Annihilation Ancestor. Currently, only one has been discovered, but it requires the strongest Primal Ancestor of our Dark Holy Sanctuary and the strongest Primal Ancestor of the Dark Destruction Domain to join forces just to barely contend with it."
The Blood Dragon War Ancestor said many things, and Ye Tian recorded them all one by one.
