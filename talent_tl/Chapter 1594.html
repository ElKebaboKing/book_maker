Chapter 1593: Taking Charge of the Ancient Star Race!

After the first Supreme Being was killed, the other Supreme Beings could no longer withstand Ye Tian's attacks.
Soon, the second Supreme Being was killed, followed by the third.
The power gap between them and Ye Tian was too vast, like the difference between heaven and earth.
Otherwise, if the gap were small, it would have been extremely difficult for Ye Tian to kill them.
Very quickly.
Of the ten great Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race, only Donghe and Gu Chen remained, but they were also heavily wounded and could not hold on for much longer.
"Why?"
Gu Chen questioned.
He truly did not understand. What benefit was there in killing them? Even if he truly was unwilling to release the Star Ancestor, it shouldn't have come to killing them!
Yet Ye Tian had deliberately planned to kill them and gave them no chance to surrender.
Gu Chen and the others could not think of a reason, and Ye Tian would not explain it to them.
Rumble!!!!!
During their battle, the ancient star that completely belonged to Ye Tian was thoroughly reduced to dust.
Space-time shattered, rules were annihilated, and endless shockwaves swept across the surrounding territories. However, under the suppression of the Suppressing Void Star River, their battle was confined to a small area and was difficult to transmit outward. Thus, no other Supreme Beings came to support them.
However, even if they had come to support, they could not have saved them.
After all, even the ten great Supreme Beings had been defeated; what could those ordinary Supreme Beings do?
After a while longer, Gu Chen's body shattered into pieces. He could no longer heal, and the vitality in his body completely dissipated.
Gu Chen had fallen.
At this moment, Donghe was truly terrified. He did not want to die.
After all, he was an eternal existence with infinite lifespan, and as one of the ten great Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race, he held a high position and power. His life was very comfortable, so he naturally did not want to perish.
"Xu Chen, I beg you to spare me! I am willing to serve you forever!"
Donghe pleaded.
However, Ye Tian ignored Donghe's pleas for mercy.
Boom!!!!
With a single punch, terrifying power directly shattered Donghe's body, killing the heavily wounded and dying Donghe in one strike.
At this point, all ten great Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race were dead.
After these Supreme Beings perished, Ye Tian put away the Suppressing Void Star River.
This source tool was bestowed upon him by the Ancient Star Race after he became a Supreme Being. It must be said that he owed a lot to this source tool this time; otherwise, a few of the ten great Supreme Beings might have escaped. If they had caused him trouble later, it would have been a bit difficult to completely rule the Ancient Star Race.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian summoned the other Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race as well as a large number of first-class Invincibles. These members of the Ancient Star Race were all high-level officials, and he summoned them to declare his status.
......
Ancient Star Race Headquarters.
One Supreme Being after another and a large number of first-class Invincibles arrived. Seeing only Ye Tian alone and none of the other ten great Supreme Beings, they were extremely curious.
Logically, a meeting like this should not have only Ye Tian present.
Moreover, Ye Tian was sitting on the most central throne, and his meaning was already very clear.
Some members of the Ancient Star Race began to secretly contact the ten great Supreme Beings. They didn't suspect anything yet; they just wanted to ask why the ten great Supreme Beings had not come to attend the meeting.
But soon, their expressions changed.
They actually could not contact the ten great Supreme Beings. If they couldn't contact one, it could be said they were in secluded meditation and had closed off their senses, ignoring external affairs.
But being unable to contact any of them meant a major problem had occurred.
At this time, the Supreme Being of the Ancient Star Race, Keluo, asked, "Lord Xu Chen, where are Lord Donghe and the others? I tried to contact them just now but couldn't reach them."
Ye Tian said indifferently, "They are dead!"
"What? How could Lord Donghe and the others die?"
Many members of the Ancient Star Race were shocked, each one unable to believe it.
At this time, Ye Tian continued, "The ten of them wanted to surround and kill me, but they underestimated my strength and were killed by me."
"I don't believe it!" Keluo said angrily. "Lord Donghe could not have attacked you!"
"Could it be that I killed them intentionally?"
Ye Tian said coldly.
Keluo stopped speaking, but the expression on his face clearly showed he did not trust Ye Tian.
This was also normal. After all, Ye Tian had only risen to power a short time ago, while he and Supreme Beings like Donghe had lived together for endless ages.
In comparison, Keluo and the others trusted Donghe more than they trusted Ye Tian.
"Donghe and the others have perished. From this day forward, the Ancient Star Race shall be controlled by me. I am the Patriarch of the Ancient Star Race!"
Ye Tian announced.
"I do not agree!" Keluo retorted.
"Who else disagrees?" Ye Tian asked coldly.
One by one, powerful members of the Ancient Star Race stood up, expressing their disagreement.
Right at that moment, Ye Tian acted.
Boom!!!!
A terrifying force swept across the field, locking onto those Ancient Star Race experts who disagreed.
In the next instant, these Ancient Star Race experts were crushed into fine powder by the terrifying power.
Even a Supreme Being like Keluo was crushed by Ye Tian's hand, perishing on the spot.
"Who else dares to oppose? This is the consequence!"
Ye Tian stood up and said.
·········Seeking Flowers··· ·····
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
One by one, the members of the Ancient Star Race knelt down and bowed, saying, "Greetings, Patriarch!"
That's right, they cowered and did not dare to resist Ye Tian.
Just like that, Ye Tian forcibly gained the recognition of the Ancient Star Race's high-level officials.
In the following years, the news of Ye Tian becoming the Patriarch of the Ancient Star Race spread. Many members of the Ancient Star Race were somewhat indignant, but their strength was too low, so they could not resist.
As time passed, Ye Tian's prestige grew greater and greater, and thus the entire Ancient Star Race tacitly accepted this matter, as they could not resist anyway.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian announced another matter, which was to seal off the Ancient Star Stone space and forbid other clansmen from entering it.
This regulation caused many people to object, but after Ye Tian killed another batch of Ancient Star Race members, the regulation was enforced.
.... . ...
The Star Ancestor's consciousness had been staying inside the Ancient Star Tower, so he did not understand external affairs.
But as time passed and other outsiders stopped entering the Ancient Star Stone space, the Star Ancestor also began to suspect something.
On this day.
Ye Tian came to the Ancient Star Tower to seek guidance, and the Star Ancestor directly asked, "Xu Chen, why have no members of the Ancient Star Race entered the Ancient Star Stone space during these years?"
Ye Tian smiled and said, "I discussed it with the other Supreme Beings and decided that we will no longer open the Ancient Star Stone space in the future. Furthermore, I am preparing to take the Ancient Star Stone away. Since the Star Ancestor has already chosen me, there is no need to continue nurturing other geniuses of the Ancient Star Race. After all, the resources of the Ancient Star Domain are limited and must be appropriately arranged."
"So that's how it is!"
The Star Ancestor nodded slightly but said nothing.
Ye Tian stayed in the Ancient Star Tower for a few days, asking some questions before leaving the Ancient Star Tower.
When Ye Tian left, the Star Ancestor's expression became incredibly dark.
"Damn Xu Chen, I nurtured you, yet you dare to have other thoughts!"
The Star Ancestor was beyond furious.
Although he wasn't clear on what had happened outside, it was definitely no small matter. Xu Chen was very likely to betray him and might not even release him.
Xu Chen was his only hope of escaping his predicament; he could not afford to offend Xu Chen, otherwise he would have dropped all pretenses just now.
Not long after.
Ye Tian took the Ancient Star Stone away from the Ancient Star Stone space. After all, he planned to spend the coming years researching the fusion of two Eternal Bodies. If he succeeded, he would have to re-cultivate his duplicated body to ascend to the Supreme Being realm with the most perfect foundation. Therefore, he needed the Ancient Star Stone to re-cultivate the Ancient Star Eternal Body and ascend with a perfect, flawless Ancient Star Eternal Body.
Thus, he could not do without the Ancient Star Stone.
