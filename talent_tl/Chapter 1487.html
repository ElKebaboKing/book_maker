Chapter 1486: Turning into a Bone Soldier, Infiltration
!
"Bone Soldiers are not allowed to exist in the Dark Forbidden Domain. I didn't expect it to be like this!"
Ye Tian said in surprise.
He had thought that the Bone Sea Forbidden Land was what restricted those Bone Soldiers, Bone Generals, and Bone Kings. He hadn't expected that it wasn't the Bone Sea Forbidden Land restricting them; they could actually leave the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. However, the sole price of leaving was... instant annihilation.
Consequently, they did not dare to come out.
"It seems I cannot replicate a Bone Soldier in the Dark Forbidden Domain. In that case, my main body will go to the Bone Sea Forbidden Land, thrust one hand into it, and then replicate the Bone Soldier body so that it appears directly inside the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. That should work fine."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
And so.
Ye Tian's main body arrived at the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. He instantly thrust one hand into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land and replicated a Bone Soldier's body.
In the next instant, Ye Tian withdrew his hand.
The Bone Soldier body appeared within the Bone Sea Forbidden Land and immediately moved away from that area. Soon, a powerful divine sense swept over to investigate. Finding no trace of an outsider, it paid no further mind.
Some Bone Kings sensed Ye Tian's Bone Soldier body, but they didn't care, as it was a genuine Bone Soldier.
...."I must be careful next. Otherwise, if this Bone Soldier body falls, it will be easily discovered if I try this method again!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he did his best to control his aura and did not enter the depths of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. He only wandered the outskirts to understand the situation here.
The Bone Sea Forbidden Land contained massive bone mountains. On each bone mountain, there were Bone Soldiers cultivating, and even some Bone Generals.
Therefore, Ye Tian did not dare to approach these bone mountains. After sensing the auras of the Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals from a distance, he would move far away.
There were no Bone Kings in the outer perimeter, and he didn't know why the Bone Kings didn't guard the outskirts.
In the blink of an eye, ten years passed.
Ye Tian didn't encounter any trouble during these ten years. After all, he was extremely cautious, and these Bone Soldiers rarely perished; most were constantly in bitter cultivation.
There were fewer Bone Soldiers in the outer perimeter, while there were more Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals in the interior of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. However, if he approached, it would be easy to run into the actual Bone Soldier he had replicated.
It wasn't that he hadn't thought about replicating a deceased Bone Soldier, but that would be even more dangerous.
Because the Bone Soldiers of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land likely all knew which of their comrades had died in battle. If one suddenly reappeared, it would be strange if they weren't investigated. His replicated body might even be discovered by the Bone Kings the moment it appeared, leading to the discovery of the problem.
That was why he didn't dare choose a deceased Bone Soldier as a replication template.
"I can't obtain much intelligence on the outskirts of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land. I should head deeper!"
Ye Tian decided.
Although this decision was risky, at worst, this replicated body would just perish, and he would have to find other ways to obtain Bone Beads later.
Thus, Ye Tian cautiously ventured deep into the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
Ye Tian's luck seemed good; without much trouble, he easily entered the depths of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
At this time, he saw a massive bone mountain. This bone mountain was covered with buildings.
Ye Tian directly approached this bone mountain and entered it.
This bone mountain seemed to be a trading mountain. Many Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals were trading here, and there were even some shops.
Seeing this bizarre scene, Ye Tian found it somewhat hard to believe.
It was just a small forbidden land, yet it actually had a place for trade. This was just like the Ancestors of the Dark Forbidden Domain; with so few Ancestors living in one area, how could it develop into a place of trade? "The number of Bone Soldiers here is likely very large, far beyond my imagination!"
Ye Tian realized.
Sure enough, he saw many, many Bone Soldiers. Just the number of Bone Soldiers he could see right now was in the thousands.
Among them, some Bone Soldiers seemed to recognize him and greeted him, but he did not respond, fearing he would say the wrong thing.
But those Bone Soldiers didn't seem to care, as if they didn't have much of a friendship.
If this were the outside world, the Ancestors would perceive everything with a single thought, and he would likely be discovered quickly.
But here, things were heavily suppressed. Even an Ancestor couldn't know everything. Divine sense couldn't probe very far, and there were many Bone Soldiers. Many Bone Soldiers stayed in their residences or were even constantly in seclusion, so their auras wouldn't spread out.
Therefore, as long as Ye Tian's Bone Soldier body didn't come into close contact with that specific Bone Soldier, it wouldn't be easily discovered.
The greater possibility was that the Bone Soldier was currently cultivating, hadn't come out, and wasn't sensing the outside situation. Thus, Ye Tian would not be exposed for the time being.
However, over a long period, he would definitely be exposed.
Soon, Ye Tian arrived at a shop.
The owner of the shop was a Bone General. Seeing Ye Tian arrive, he said, "Xiao Zai, I heard you were injured while following the Bone Kings to besiege an Ancestor of the Dark Forbidden Domain. I didn't expect you to recover so quickly! What, have you come to my place to buy some treasures?"
"Just looking!"
Ye Tian said indifferently.
"These are newly arrived goods, take a look!"
This Bone General took out a treasure orb. Inside the orb were various treasures.
Ye Tian didn't know what these treasures were. Moreover, he knew nothing about this place and had no local currency, so he naturally wouldn't actually buy anything.
He currently wanted Bone Beads, but even more, he wanted to know the situation here.
He felt that this place was far from simple. Having so many Bone Soldiers, Bone Generals, and Bone Kings comparable to Ancestors was too abnormal! Furthermore, this Bone General mentioned the Dark Forbidden Domain, indicating they knew about the situation outside.
"Sigh, I don't know what's up with that Ancestor lately. He's entered several times, and I almost died!"
Ye Tian lamented.
The Bone General nodded: "Indeed, this didn't happen in the past. The Dark Forbidden Domain is very weak, and those Ancestors are few in number and value their lives more. I didn't expect a top-tier Ancestor to enter our base multiple times and kill several Ancestor-level Bone Soldiers. However, that top-tier Ancestor still died!"
Clearly, the Bone Race of the Bone Sea Forbidden Land thought Ye Tian's replicated body was his main body, completely unaware that it was merely a replicated body.
Ye Tian continued to chat with this Bone General, gathering intelligence through indirect means. Although he made the Bone General feel suspicious several times in the middle—feeling that Ye Tian's speech was somewhat abnormal—the other party didn't doubt Ye Tian's identity. He only thought that Ye Tian's frequent injuries hadn't fully healed, making him a bit muddled.
But in the end, Ye Tian still obtained some useful intelligence.
For example, this shop occasionally went to get stock. As for where they got the stock, he wasn't entirely sure, but it was definitely not the Bone Sea Forbidden Land.
This indicated that the Bone Sea Forbidden Land was connected to another place.
Second, the Bone Ancestors comparable to Ancestors were called Ancestor-level Bone Soldiers, Ancestor-level Bone Generals, and Ancestor-level Bone Kings, and they were called the War Bone Race.
Third, the War Bone Race had once entered the Dark Forbidden Domain, but they couldn't enter now. Moreover, the War Bone Race desperately needed the resources of the Dark Forbidden Domain. It was said that the resources of the Dark Forbidden Domain were of great help to the War Bone Race, significantly enhancing their cultivation and strength.
Just as Bone Beads were of great help to Ye Tian.
But he couldn't fish for any more intelligence, because if he continued to probe indirectly, he feared something would go wrong.
Thus, he hurriedly left the place.
