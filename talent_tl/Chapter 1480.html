Chapter 1479: The Xuanxu Holy Realm is Finally About to Advance!
!
Time flowed by, and ten domain years passed.
By this time, all ten Ancestor Realms of the Dark Soul Domain had been born, and each had been partitioned.
Among them, the Destruction Holy Race occupied one Ancestor Realm, the Dark Holy Gate occupied one, and the War Gate occupied half of an Ancestor Realm.
Ye Tian occupied one Ancestor Realm in the name of the Human Race. Since the War Gate did not have a quota for this particular Ancestor Realm, they could continue to occupy their own share, and other forces could do nothing about it even if they disagreed.
Consequently, the War Gate seized an additional half of another Ancestor Realm.
For the War Gate, half an Ancestor Realm was sufficient; any more would be unnecessary.
As the various major powers each occupied their own territories within the Dark Soul Domain, the region gradually began to settle down.
At the very least, the Ancestors went into seclusion one by one to absorb and refine the resources they had obtained from the Primordial Ancestor Realm and the new Ancestor Realms.
Perhaps after a long period of time, their strength would increase once more.
For instance, some ordinary Ancestors who were very close to becoming Elite Ancestors might be able to break through their shackles and promote to Elite Ancestors. Likewise, top-tier Elite Ancestors like Jiezu might also have hope of breaking through their limits to reach top-tier Ancestor combat power.
Of course, the probability of such a breakthrough was relatively low.
Ye Tian, however, had utilized these ten domain years to refine countless Nirvana treasures, and he had long since broken through to top-tier Ancestor combat power.
This was a breakthrough for his true body; therefore, it was a genuine advancement.
Even in any part of the Dark Forbidden Zone, he could now exert top-tier Ancestor combat power, fearing neither the Destruction Ancestors nor the Holy Ancestors.
"The Dark Soul Domain has finally calmed down!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Over these ten domain years, the Dark Soul Domain had been extremely chaotic. Only the regions held by the Human Race remained peaceful, while the slaughter in other places had almost never ceased.
Now, the Dark Soul Domain was much better, roughly resembling the state of the Dark Holy Territory—local skirmishes were unavoidable, but the overall situation was stable.
Furthermore, the Dark Soul Domain imitated the practices of the Dark Holy Territory, constructing a large number of giant cities to manage the countless practitioners through these hubs.
However, the regions governed by the Dark Destruction Domain were not like this. They were frantically terraforming and cultivating a large number of races, forcing them to slaughter one another like raising Gu.
After all, many races of the Dark Destruction Domain grew through slaughter and destruction. It was only upon reaching the levels of Great Domain Ancestor, Invincible Domain Ancestor, Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, and Ancestor that they became almost like regular practitioners, increasing their strength through cultivation, as destruction could no longer provide much... improvement to their strength.
The Three Thousand Evil Races and the Destruction Holy Race only had their ordinary clansmen increase their cultivation by destroying domains and powerful worlds to absorb the power of destruction.
By conducting this "Gu-style" cultivation within a region of the Dark Destruction Domain, and given the recovery speed and resource generation rate of the Dark Soul Domain, they could mass-produce Domain Ancestors and experts above the level of Great Domain Ancestor.
This was the goal of the Ancestors from the Dark Destruction Domain. In reality, they also wanted to occupy the Dark Holy Territory, but the Dark Holy Territory had now become a monolithic block. The Destruction Ancestors could not easily invade it and could only occasionally launch an invasion war to cultivate their "Gu" through slaughter, sacrificing the majority of their Domain Ancestors to focus on nurturing experts at the Supreme level and above.
The Ancestors of the Dark Holy Territory were also aware of the cultivation methods of the Dark Destruction Domain and thus did not interfere. After all, the Domain Ancestors of the Dark Holy Territory could also temper themselves through these battles.
Ye Tian did not care about the domains occupied by the Dark Destruction Domain. The other party had sealed off that area, and for the time being, it would not affect other regions, which was enough.
"My true body has advanced to top-tier Ancestor combat power. Next, I can return to the Xuanxu Holy Realm and promote it to an Ancestor Realm!"
Ye Tian decided.
As for Xiao Yue and the others, they remained in the Huaxia Ancestor Realm.
Xiao Yue and the others had been: meditating on Ancestor Laws. With their comprehension and talent, it would likely take another hundred domain years before they had hope of completely integrating the Ancestor Laws and advancing to the Ancestor level.
This speed was not slow. After all, when other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors obtained Ancestor Laws, it would take them at least several hundred domain years to have any hope of a breakthrough.
Furthermore, the Ancestor Laws that Xiao Yue and the others were integrating were top-tier Ancestor Laws, which were much harder to merge. Therefore, achieving such a speed was quite excellent.
*Whoosh!* Ye Tian left the Huaxia Ancestor Realm. His departure was sensed by the avatars of other Ancestors, but they did not pay it much mind.
With a single thought, Ye Tian crossed a vast distance, traversing time and space to arrive at the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
If a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor wanted to fly there without the help of an Ancestor, it would take at least tens of millions of years at their fastest speed to reach the Xuanxu Holy Realm. It was... that exaggerated, because the distance was truly too vast.
If it were the speed of an Invincible Domain Ancestor, they might fly for an eternity and still be unable to cross such a staggering distance.
Therefore, in the Dark Forbidden Zone, aside from Ancestors, other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were unable to traverse extremely long distances.
The Dark Soul Domain was considered relatively close to the Dark Destruction Domain, but the Dark Soul Domain itself was too far from other places.
The birth of a Dark Holy Land was random, so no one knew where one might appear. It was possible that one day, a Dark Holy Land might be born right next to the Dark Soul Domain.
Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Ye Tian returned and immediately duplicated his true body.
This way, the Xuanxu Holy Realm would be guarded by two top-tier Ancestor combat powers. Once the Xuanxu Holy Realm advanced to the Xuanxu Ancestor Realm, even if the Destruction Ancestors and Holy Ancestors came, he could block them and prevent them from probing the Xuanxu Ancestor Realm.
"Let the Xuanxu Holy Realm advance!"
Ye Tian no longer suppressed the origin of the Xuanxu Holy Realm. Under the stimulation of the Creation Primordial Well, the Xuanxu Holy Realm finally began its advancement.
*Rumble!!!!!* The volume of the Xuanxu Holy Realm began to skyrocket, doubling in size with every breath.
Endless territories were born, a sight that completely shocked the Human Race.
Consequently, one Supreme-level Domain Ancestor after another came to inquire about the situation.
"Lord Ancestor of Myriad Laws, what is happening to the Xuanxu Holy Realm?"
Facing the inquiries of these Domain Ancestors, Ye Tian gave a unified answer: "The Xuanxu Holy Realm is about to advance into the Xuanxu Ancestor Realm!"
"What? The Xuanxu Holy Realm is advancing to an Ancestor Realm? Does this mean our Human Race will possess two Ancestor Realms?"
Countless Domain Ancestors of the Human Race were stunned, followed by... wild joy! Once the Xuanxu Holy Realm was born as an Ancestor Realm, how many... rare resources would be produced! Perhaps the number of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors in the Human Race would increase again, and the strength of the Human Race would skyrocket.
The vast number of Invincible Domain Ancestors were even more excited upon hearing this news; they now had hope of advancing to the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor rank.
*Rumble!!!* The Xuanxu Holy Realm continued to expand. Given its condition, it would take roughly several million years to evolve into an Ancestor Realm.
At the same time, the entire Daoshan Domain was affected by the advancement of the Xuanxu Holy Realm and began to transform. The area of the Daoshan Domain continuously swallowed the Dark Forbidden Zone, and its territory expanded frantically.
This time, while the Daoshan Domain might not evolve to the size of a Dark Holy Land, it would at least be equivalent to a small-scale Dark Holy Land.
