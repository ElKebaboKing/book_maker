Chapter 1482: Ten Realm Years, Strength Skyrockets
!
"So, there are more Ancestors in the Dark Forbidden Zone than the ones I've seen,"
Ye Tian asked curiously.
"Correct, there are indeed more than just these Ancestors. As for exactly how many there are, even I am not sure!"
The Realm Ancestor explained: "If an Ancestor hides, no one can find them.
Moreover, at the dawn of the Dark Forbidden Zone, no one knows exactly how many... good things were born.
It is said that before the Dark Destruction Zone was born, a significant portion of those sleeping Ancestors were Top Ancestors. Having lived through endless ages and reaching a point where they could no longer progress, yet unwilling to leave the Dark Forbidden Zone, they chose to enter a self-induced slumber to wait for new opportunities! The birth of the Dark Holy Land isn't of much value to them. After all, compared to the entire Dark Forbidden Zone, the Dark Holy Land is merely a treasure land. While it is highly valuable to... ordinary Ancestors, its value isn't necessarily high for those Top Ancestors.
The Saint Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor belong to the weaker tier of Top Ancestors. If we were to classify the level above Top Ancestors as Supreme Ancestors, the Destruction Ancestor and Saint Ancestor are still very far from that level. Therefore, some treasures within the Dark Holy Land are still quite useful to them and can improve their cultivation, which is why they are so keen on fighting for the treasures and opportunities of the Dark Holy Land.
If they reach the limit of Top Ancestors and cannot promote to Supreme Ancestors, or if they find themselves unable to progress for a long time in the future, perhaps one day they too will enter a self-induced slumber, leave the Dark Forbidden Zone, or perhaps charge into the Six Great Forbidden Grounds to gamble for a chance!"
"So that's how it is!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Indeed, if a Top Ancestor remained unable to break through after living for too long, life would lose its meaning for such an Ancestor.
At that point, if they didn't want to seek death by entering the Six Great Forbidden Grounds, they could only sleep and wait for the long ages to pass before waking up. Perhaps then, a Supreme Ancestor would be born in the Dark Forbidden Zone, giving them hope for a breakthrough.
"Ancestor Wanfa, you have only recently broken through to the Ancestor realm, yet you already possess the combat power of a Top Ancestor. You can continue to cultivate. Once your strength approaches that of a Supreme Ancestor, you can go to some of the forbidden areas beneath the Six Great Forbidden Grounds. Although dangerous for Top Ancestors, there is still a high hope of survival, and you might obtain some opportunities.
For now, whatever you do, do not enter the Six Great Forbidden Grounds!"
The Realm Ancestor warned.
"I understand!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Afterward, the Realm Ancestor departed.
Ye Tian took a deep look at the Calamity Black Mountain before leaving the area.
In the following days, Ye Tian visited each of the Six Great Forbidden Grounds, but he only observed them from the outside without entering.
Just by observing from the exterior, he felt a terrifying aura of death, as if he would perish the moment he stepped inside.
...."I should still focus on increasing my Ancestor cultivation. It hasn't been long since I broke through to the Ancestor realm, so I can continue to improve. First, I'll nurture the Nihility Spacetime. By using the Profound Void Ancestral World to nurture the Nihility Spacetime and sealing its aura, other Ancestors shouldn't be able to sense it.
Besides, the Profound Void Ancestral World belongs entirely to the Human Race, and other Ancestors won't come here to investigate, so it should be very safe!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Although he didn't fear the Saint Ancestor or the Destruction Ancestor, if news of him nurturing a Nihility Spacetime leaked and caught the attention of some sleeping Top Ancestors, it wouldn't be good for him.
Therefore, it was better to be cautious for now.
Thus.
Ye Tian nurtured a top-tier Nihility Spacetime within the Profound Void Ancestral World, pouring a massive amount of resources into it to cultivate Nihility treasures.
Then, Ye Tian refined these Nihility treasures to continuously improve his cultivation.
In this way, Ye Tian's strength grew steadily.
One Realm Year after another passed by.
As his cultivation increased, he also attempted to create more Ultimate Ancestral Techniques.
For instance, defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques could be stacked.
Ye Tian had created the Ancestral Armor Ultimate Ancestral Technique, but he could also create a second defensive Ultimate Ancestral Technique, such as a Dao Robe Ultimate Ancestral Technique—condensing a robe to cover the body and block attacks, or other similar techniques.
The same applied to speed-type Ultimate Ancestral Techniques; a movement-type technique combined with a wing-type speed technique could also be stacked.
Similarly, for time-based Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, he could create one to accelerate his own time, which would significantly boost his speed, reaction time, and attack power.
Once he mastered more Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, his own strength would become even more formidable.
And so.
Ye Tian continuously created one Ultimate Ancestral Technique after another.
In the blink of an eye, ten 10 Realm Years had passed.
By this time, Ye Tian had created five 55 Ultimate Ancestral Techniques.
These five 55 Ultimate Ancestral Techniques were the Golden Wings, the Dao Robe, the Extreme Arm, the Dream Butterfly, and the Hand of Nihility.
Among them, the Extreme Arm technique allowed one arm to exert a terrifying effect, even more fearsome than the Tyrant Strength technique, and the two techniques had a slight stacking effect.
The Dream Butterfly technique was a time-based Ultimate Ancestral Technique that could rapidly accelerate his own time, making his speed and reactions incredibly fast. With increased speed, his attack power naturally became stronger. Thus, this technique was a huge help to Ye Tian's strength. If used in conjunction with the Eternal technique to freeze spacetime, the effect would be even better.
The Hand of Nihility was a Nihility-type Ultimate Ancestral Technique that Ye Tian created from scratch. It was only because he possessed the Ultimate Nihility Talent that he could create such a technique; it was likely the only one of its kind in the entire Dark Forbidden Zone.
With these five 55 new techniques, combined with his previous ones and the Nihility treasures he had refined over the ten 10 Realm Years, his strength had undergone a world-shaking transformation.
"My current strength is at least twenty times 20 greater than when I first broke through to the Top Ancestor level. I should have reached the peak of the Top Ancestor rank now. I could easily defeat the Destruction Ancestor or the Saint Ancestor!"
Ye Tian murmured.
If he used his duplicated bodies in coordination, he would be enough to kill the Destruction Ancestor and the Saint Ancestor.
Furthermore, his Green Leaf Ancestor clone's cultivation had also improved slightly. With the boost from several Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, it had barely reached the level of Top Ancestor combat power.
However, at that level, the power boost from the Dark Soul Realm had decreased significantly, and even with that boost, its combat power was far inferior to his main body.
"Yue'er and the others are about to break through to the Ancestor realm soon!"
Ye Tian murmured.
