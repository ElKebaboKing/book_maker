Chapter 1464: Supreme-level Soul Ancestor, A Bold Idea
!
The fall of the three Saint Talent Users—the Scorpio Domain Ancestor, the Dark Eagle Domain Ancestor, and the Evil Wind Domain Ancestor—was immediately known to their parents.
At this, the three evil races of the Dark Destruction Domain were enraged.
Through divination, they learned that all the Domain Ancestors they had sent into the Dark Soul Domain had perished. They had fallen without serving much of a purpose at all.
This was a massive loss! Furthermore, losing three Saint Talent Users was a blow so heavy they could barely withstand it.
More importantly, the fact that all their clansmen had fallen meant total failure; the benefits of the Dark Soul Domain now had nothing to do with them.
It would be another trillion years before it was the turn of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors like them to enter the Dark Soul Domain.
During this time, they would have no way to obtain any benefits from the Dark Soul Domain, which was the true loss.
....After Ye Tian wiped out the three evil race Domain Ancestors from the Dark Saint Domain, he continued to consolidate the Human Race's territory.
Whenever a precious treasure was born in a certain location, he would head there to seize it.
In this way, the long years slowly passed. Under Ye Tian's leadership, the Human Race grew stronger and stronger within the Dark Soul Domain, seizing many treasures, some of which were useful even to Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
A portion of these treasures obtained by the Human Race was used for themselves, while another portion was taken out of the Dark Soul Domain and sent to the Human Race headquarters in the Profound Void Saint Realm.
It could be said that with the resources from the Dark Soul Domain, the strength of the Human Race developed extremely rapidly.
In the blink of an eye, ten billion years passed.
By this time, Ye Tian's duplicated body in the Dark Soul Domain had already grown to the Invincible Soul Ancestor realm and had reached the peak of that realm.
The path for an Invincible Soul Ancestor to promote to a Supreme Soul Ancestor had also been deduced by now.
For an Invincible Domain Ancestor to promote to a Supreme Domain Ancestor, one must master the physical body and complete nine transformations. The path for an Invincible Soul Ancestor was similar.
An Invincible Soul Ancestor must... control the soul. After mastering 365 times the soul power, they would undergo nine soul transformations, at which point they could step into the Supreme-level Soul Ancestor realm.
Currently, there were many experts in the Dark Soul Domain who had reached the Invincible Soul Ancestor level. Most were native Soul Ancestors, as the natives were favored by providence and their cultivation was very smooth, while outsiders faced a certain degree of suppression.
Ye Tian's duplicated body had also cultivated to the peak of the Invincible Soul Ancestor realm and was beginning to master the soul.
Although mastering the soul was different from mastering the physical body, Ye Tian's realm was simply too high. Furthermore, by occasionally using his Derivation Trait and consuming a tiny bit of Saint Soul power to deduce how to master the soul, the impact on his main body was negligible.
Consequently, Ye Tian's speed in mastering the soul was many times faster than other Invincible Soul Ancestors.
Although Supreme-level Domain Ancestors could not enter for a trillion years, and the Primordial Ancestors certainly couldn't enter either...
Ye Tian wanted to raise his cultivation to the peak of the Supreme Soul Ancestor realm within that trillion-year period.
That way, even when the other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors entered the Dark Soul Domain, they would still be unable to contend with him.
Moreover, Ye Tian knew that at the one-trillion-year mark, a world-shaking opportunity would be born in the Dark Soul Domain, which would also be the time of the fiercest competition.
Right now, the Dark Soul Domain was only in its initial stages.
The grade of the treasures being born was limited.
The years flowed by, 100 million years at a time.
In the 70th billion year, Ye Tian finally completed his nine soul transformations and stepped into the Supreme-level Soul Ancestor realm.
BOOM!!!!! Endless soul energy surged from all corners of the Dark Soul Domain into the Ten Directions Saint Realm, where it was absorbed and refined by Ye Tian.
The commotion of his breakthrough was too massive, attracting the attention of many experts. Even the Primordial Ancestors noticed the disturbance caused by Ye Tian's breakthrough.
"A Supreme Domain Ancestor has been born in the Dark Soul Domain. Such speed! Who is it?"
The Primordial Ancestors were all incredibly curious.
Soon, they found out.
"The Human Race, Qingye Soul Ancestor. It's him!"
The Primordial Ancestors were dumbfounded.
In order to nurture Invincible Soul Ancestors, they had even brought out some of their own treasures. However, the path of the Soul Ancestor was, after all, an unfamiliar one, and it was difficult for them to quickly help Soul Ancestors promote to the Supreme level.
Yet even so, with their resources and guidance, they were beaten to it by a Soul Ancestor of the Human Race.
What was the Human Race? They only had a single Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. To them, this faction was practically an ant.
But it was such a small faction that had nurtured a terrifying genius whom their own descendants and disciples could not surpass.
Once Ye Tian's duplicated body entered the Supreme-level Soul Ancestor realm, he no longer had any reservations.
In his identity as the Qingye Soul Ancestor, Ye Tian began to sweep through everything. He stepped into the headquarters of various factions, seizing all their resources. Even the outposts of the Destruction Saint Race and the various Saint Sects of the Dark Saint Domain were visited by him.
Of course, he did not kill indiscriminately. After all, the Human Race headquarters and his main body were in the outside world; he naturally couldn't afford to excessively offend these Primordial Ancestors.
Therefore, Ye Tian only seized resources and did not kill.
In this way, many resources of the Dark Soul Domain were gathered into the hands of the Human Race, and many precious lands were occupied by them.
Many factions united, wanting to resist Ye Tian, but they were still no match for him.
"Forget it. The real battlefield begins after the Dark Soul Domain has existed for a trillion years. The treasures born then will be truly precious. What does it matter if we yield these current resources to the Human Race? Is it possible that this Qingye Soul Ancestor can grow to a level where he can contend with those peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors in a mere trillion years?"
The Primordial Ancestors thought to themselves.
The Primordial Ancestors gave up on competing during this period; thus, in this era, the Dark Soul Domain was dominated and controlled by the Human Race.
Gathering the resources of the Dark Soul Domain, Ye Tian nurtured several Soul Ancestors of the Human Race to the Invincible Soul Ancestor realm.
However, it remained extremely difficult for them to promote to the Supreme-level Soul Ancestor realm.
Still, with his guidance and lectures, by the time the trillion-year mark arrived, there was hope for the Human Race to give birth to several Supreme-level Soul Ancestors.
Ye Tian's duplicated body was also cultivating diligently, refining countless resources just so he could raise his strength to the peak of the Supreme Soul Ancestor realm.
But this step was not easy.
"Time is insufficient. A mere trillion years is indeed not long. Moreover, during this period, the Dark Soul Domain cannot produce resources that allow a Supreme Soul Ancestor's cultivation to progress rapidly. My main body has also finished refining the Nirvana treasures there. Without more Nirvana treasures, there's no way to quickly increase my cultivation!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
After thinking it over, Ye Tian could only think of one solution.
"The Nirvana Clan was wiped out, but strictly speaking, I can be considered a member of the Nirvana Clan. After all, I possess all the Ancestral Talents of the Nirvana Clan. If I am willing, I could also create a region of Nirvana Space-Time!"
Ye Tian suddenly realized.
