Chapter 1627 Ten Thousand Tribulation Yuan, Eighth Supreme Being!

Ge Fei Domain Ancestor fled, but to Ye Tian, that was not a big deal at all.
In the future, once his strength became greater, Ge Fei Domain Ancestor would be even less daring to show up; otherwise, showing up would mean death.
Therefore, he did not care much about Ge Fei Domain Ancestor.
In addition, through this matter, Ye Tian's strength was considered exposed.
The outside world now knew that he possessed the strength of a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert, and that he was a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert with the ability to create clones.
Even an incomparably powerful Half-Step Eternal Realm expert would not offend Ye Tian without enormous benefits.
The Primal Tree was indeed very precious, but that was only speculation, and they had no confidence in seizing the Primal Tree, so they would not make a move.
Otherwise, once they offended a Supreme Being like Ye Tian, whose prospects were limitless, if Ye Tian's cultivation reached the Ninth Supreme Being, or even Half-Step Eternal Realm, then their trouble would be enormous.
Therefore, the other Supreme Beings, and even the Half-Step Eternal Realm experts, gave up the idea of dealing with Ye Tian.
The Dark Forbidden Domain also became calm, and the Invincible experts of the human race gradually dared to leave the Dark Forbidden Domain and venture out into the outside world.
Ye Tian also began to cultivate in closed seclusion, raising his cultivation.
In any case, he was not short of resources now, so naturally he wanted to raise his cultivation to the Eighth Supreme Being realm as soon as possible.
The stronger one's strength was, the more treasure lands and good places one could go to.
There were far too many mysterious places in Source Sea. With his current strength, he was not yet qualified to touch the true secrets of Source Sea; even the Void Source Ancestor of the Void Source Hall back then had only been an ordinary Half-Step Eternal Realm expert, a truly invincible existence in the eyes of ordinary Supreme Beings, and a top combat power in Source Sea.
But in truth, Void Source Ancestor was really not qualified.
Only after seeing the existence of Eternal Realm did Ye Tian truly realize that a powerhouse like Void Source Ancestor really was nothing much.
Even now, if he borrowed the finger of an Eternal Realm expert, he would be enough to exchange blows with Void Source Ancestor for a while.
“Back then, the number of Void Sons and Void Divine Sons was so small. In fact, many Invincible experts from powerful forces simply looked down on the Void Source Stele of the mere Void Source Hall; only the Invincible experts of some weak Eternal Heavens longed to become Void Sons!” Ye Tian murmured.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian continued cultivating.
And in the years that followed, one Supreme Being after another was born among the human race.
For example, Ye Tian's daughter and younger sister also successively broke through to the Supreme Being realm.
Moreover, Ye Tian provided the human race with a large amount of Eternal resources. For at least a very long period of time, anyone who reached the First Supreme Being would be qualified to obtain some Eternal resources.
This was a benefit Ye Tian gave to the human race.
Unknowingly, one Tribulation Yuan after another passed.
Ten thousand Tribulation Yuan!
Twenty thousand Tribulation Yuan!
Thirty thousand Tribulation Yuan!
……
Ye Jinpeng, Ye Xingchen, and the other Supreme Beings gradually advanced to the Second Supreme Being realm, and the number of Supreme Beings born in the human race had already reached more than ten, gradually catching up to the Tianlin Realm.
Before they knew it, the ten-thousand-Tribulation-Yuan protection period of the Dark Forbidden Domain was almost over.
And at this time, Ye Tian had also cultivated to the peak of the Seventh Supreme Being realm, and could step into the Eighth Supreme Being realm at any moment.
Not only that, his comprehension of the Eternal Laws had reached an incomparably terrifying level, because he had repeatedly copied Innate Eternal Lifeforms and comprehended the Innate Eternal Laws.
Therefore, Ye Tian understood the Innate Eternal Laws very well, and the Eternal techniques he created were naturally even more powerful.
In addition, Ye Tian's foundations were incomparably solid.
It was also because of the issue of foundations that he spent so long cultivating; otherwise, he would have already broken through to the Eighth Supreme Being realm, and would not have waited until today.
On this day.
Ye Tian began to charge toward the Eighth Supreme Being realm. After consuming several treasures and resources that aided breakthrough, Ye Tian easily broke through to the Eighth Supreme Being realm, and his strength once again crossed a limit.
At this point, his combat strength was already comparable to an ordinary Half-Step Eternal Realm expert, rather than the weakest Half-Step Eternal Realm expert.
However, at this level, it was not possible for Ye Tian to rapidly cultivate to the Ninth Supreme Being realm.
Unless he obtained the resources on the Black Mist Worm clone from the Primal Sea, but the Primal Sea could only be opened once every long span of time; ten thousand Tribulation Yuan was nothing at all.
According to what he knew, the interval between each opening of the Primal Sea was ten million Tribulation Yuan.
Even so, many Supreme Beings were still waiting for it, but Ye Tian could not afford to wait.
He had no way to wait through such a long stretch of time; perhaps by then, he would no longer need the resources of the Primal Sea.
Therefore, he wanted to leave the Primal Sea as soon as possible.
The Primal Sea was not without a way out; for example, those two Eternal Realm experts could casually tear apart the Primal Sea void and take away so many Black Mist Worms from the Primal Sea.
Therefore, he could also leave the Primal Sea, as long as his strength was strong enough, or he had special methods.
During these years, Ye Tian's Black Mist Worm clone had already cultivated to the extreme of the Ninth Stage, and could step into the Half-Step Eternal Realm at any time.
Moreover, the Black Mist Worm clone had also comprehended a number of Eternal techniques suitable for the Black Mist Worm clone. Combining these with the Black Mist Worm race, he even thought of an excellent method to raise his strength, but that would have a major impact on the foundation.
But this was only a clone; even if this clone was sacrificed, as long as the treasures were brought out, it would all be worth it.
On this day.
Ye Tian's Black Mist Worm clone arrived at the Primal Tree, and he uprooted the Primal Tree and stored it in his inner space.
Afterward, he also took away the newly born resources of the Primal Sea.
“I have to leave now, otherwise if I'm discovered by the Eternal Realm powerhouses, it would be a small matter if this clone perishes. But if those resources are taken away, the loss would be too great!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he began to charge toward the Supreme Being realm.
Boom!!!
Ye Tian executed an Eternal technique that devoured other lifeforms—Black Mist Worm Devouring Art!
Immediately, a large number of Black Mist Worms were swallowed by Ye Tian's Black Mist Worm clone and absorbed into his body, then refined by other means, turning into streams of power to help him charge toward the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
Very quickly, the shackle was broken!
The biggest difference between the Half-Step Eternal Realm and the Supreme Being realm was that Supreme Beings had unified flesh and soul, inseparable from each other, but the body and the Eternal Laws were not yet unified.
The Half-Step Eternal Realm was when the body and the Eternal Laws reached a state of compatibility, using that compatibility to break the shackle and thus advance to the Half-Step Eternal Realm, but in truth they still had not truly fused, only blended together.
It was like mixing two kinds of wine together; on the surface it looked like one wine, but upon careful distinction, it was still a mixture of two wines.
Precisely because of this, the Half-Step Eternal Realm was called “Half-Step.”
And the Black Mist Worm clone was forcefully using a large number of Black Mist Worms as energy, forcibly allowing the Eternal Laws within its body to blend with its body, thereby crossing into the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
This method was detrimental to the foundation, and future potential would be affected, with even no hope of becoming an Eternal Realm expert.
But Ye Tian did not care about that.
After breaking through to the Half-Step Eternal Realm, Ye Tian continued devouring other Black Mist Worms, and his strength grew stronger and stronger.
