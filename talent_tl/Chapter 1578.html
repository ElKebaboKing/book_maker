Chapter 1577: Silver Horn Source Race, Silver Copper Bead

Ye Tian possessed the memories of the Jin Ancestor, and thus understood that various Source Sea lifeforms had a 100% compatibility with certain Eternal Source Items.
Based on his screening, Ye Tian selected a relatively weak type of Source Sea lifeform—the Silver Horn Source Race. They were a race within the Source Sea that was not considered very powerful, yet they still possessed nearly ten Supreme Beings.
The Silver Horn Source Race had a 100% compatibility with the Eternal Source Item known as the Silver Copper Bead. However, the Silver Horn Source Race's physical bodies were compatible with the Silver Copper Bead, while their souls were not.
More importantly, the soul talent of the Silver Horn Source Race was too poor. This resulted in many members of the Silver Horn Source Race being unable to cultivate to the Second-tier Invincible realm in their entire lives, with their limit being the Third-tier Invincible realm.
But if they could not become First-tier Invincibles, even a 100% compatibility with the Silver Copper Bead was useless. The Silver Horn Source Race would not allow those members without potential to absorb and refine the Silver Copper Bead's origin.
Therefore, there were not many Supreme Beings in the Silver Horn Source Race.
Perhaps this was also a restriction of the Source Sea's Highest Rules.
The reason Ye Tian targeted the Silver Horn Source Race was not only because they were weaker, but also because it would be easier to obtain the Silver Horn Source Race's origin this way.
According to his understanding and investigation, the Silver Horn Source Race did not have a First-tier Invincible in this era. Thus, they currently had no need to consume the Silver Copper Bead's origin, giving him a complete opportunity to trade for a portion of it.
As for seizing the Silver Copper Bead by force, he had no such thoughts, as it was impossible to achieve.
The Supreme Beings of the Silver Horn Source Race guarded the Silver Copper Bead at all times. Even if he replicated the body of a Silver Horn Source Race member, he would have no way to snatch the Silver Copper Bead.
Therefore, he could only conduct a trade.
What the Silver Horn Source Race valued most were soul treasures. If they could trade for a treasure that could change soul aptitude, they would likely be willing to trade the Silver Copper Bead's origin.
And Ye Tian just happened to have a type of treasure that could change soul aptitude—the Eternal Soul Bead!
Eternal Soul Beads originated from the Eternal Soul Wood. The Eternal Soul Wood was an Eternal Source Item ranked within the top few dozen. Naturally, it could not only preserve consciousness; its most powerful effect was the ability to condense Eternal Soul Beads.
Over the long years since the Jin Ancestor obtained the Eternal Soul Wood, he had obtained a total of ten Eternal Soul Beads. However, he had consumed or traded several of them in the past, leaving only three Eternal Soul Beads remaining within his Void Sphere.
"I used my authority as a Void Son of God to investigate the identities of those Void Sons. One of the Void Sons at the Third-tier Invincible realm comes from the Silver Horn Source Race. I shall contact him!" Ye Tian decided.
...
"That Void Son of God is contacting me?"
Yin Ren, who came from the Silver Horn Source Race, received a message from within his Void Son badge. The other party did not mention much, but did mention soul treasures.
It had to be said that soul treasures were indeed the type of treasure the Silver Horn Source Race needed most.
Furthermore, the other party was a Void Son of God with a noble status, so he naturally had to meet him.
Thus, Yin Ren came to the Void Source Palace to meet with Ye Tian.
This time, Ye Tian still concealed his identity, but Yin Ren did not care.
"What business does Your Excellency have with me?"
Yin Ren asked.
"A trade!" Ye Tian spoke. "I wonder if Yin Ren, the Void Son, has any interest in an Eternal Soul Bead?"
"What, an Eternal Soul Bead!!!!"
Yin Ren's expression changed drastically, and he suddenly became extremely excited.
That was an incredibly rare soul treasure, an Eternal Soul Bead condensed from the Eternal Soul Wood. It was a priceless treasure. Even if the Silver Horn Source Ancestor went to purchase one, it would be impossible to find.
This was because experts who possessed Eternal Soul Wood did not look highly upon ordinary treasures. Unless they were offered something that truly moved them, they would not trade an Eternal Soul Bead.
The Silver Horn Source Race was not considered a very powerful race in the entire Source Sea. Coupled with poor luck, they had never been able to purchase an Eternal Soul Bead, and they did not even know who possessed the Eternal Soul Wood.
"I am interested!"
Yin Ren said immediately.
Before Ye Tian could continue speaking, Yin Ren said, "If Your Excellency is willing to trade one Eternal Soul Bead to me, I am willing to pay any price."
The value of an Eternal Soul Bead might not be very high to other Void Sons, as it could only strengthen soul intensity. But for him, it was enough to change his future.
He had been a Third-tier Invincible for a very long time. However, due to the issue of soul aptitude, his soul cultivation had seen no progress, and he was simply unable to reach the Soul Nine Tribulations Realm. How could he then step into the Second-tier Invincible realm?
But if he had an Eternal Soul Bead to improve his soul aptitude, he would have the confidence to become a Second-tier Invincible, or even a First-tier Invincible.
As a member of the Silver Horn Source Race, if he became a First-tier Invincible, he would almost certainly be able to become a Supreme Being. He would not even need any Supreme Techniques or an Eternal Tribulation Body; he could break through directly by absorbing the Silver Copper Bead's origin.
This was the advantage of the Silver Horn Source Race.
"Listen to me first!" Ye Tian said. " I only need one thing to trade the Eternal Soul Bead to you."
"What thing?"
Yin Ren asked.
Ye Tian said very seriously, "The origin of the Silver Copper Bead. I do not need too much origin; one-twentieth of the complete Silver Copper Bead's origin will suffice."
For an Eternal Source Item, as long as one did not extract one-third of its origin, it could slowly recover. One-twentieth of the origin was indeed not much.
"What, you want the origin of our Silver Horn Source Race's race-stabilizing Eternal Source Item, the Silver Copper Bead?"
Yin Ren frowned.
If anyone else had said this, they would be making an enemy of the Silver Horn Source Race and he would have turned hostile on the spot.
However, this time Ye Tian possessed an Eternal Soul Bead, which was the treasure he desired most.
"I cannot make the decision on this matter for now!" Yin Ren admitted frankly.
"I understand!" Ye Tian said with a smile. "Therefore, you need to go back and strive for it. I will wait for your reply. I believe that if you cannot strive for it, someone else in your Silver Horn Source Race might be able to. At that time, the person trading with me might not necessarily be you."
Yin Ren's expression changed, and he felt a sense of tension.
Indeed, he was not the only member of the Silver Horn Source Race who needed an Eternal Soul Bead, even if other Invincibles were not as talented as he was.
However, his failure to break through until now had already disappointed many of his clansmen.
Thus, he felt a great sense of urgency.
If Ye Tian traded with another member of the Silver Horn Source Race, he could not imagine what his future would become.
Perhaps this was his only chance, and he had to seize it.
"I must ensure that you truly possess an Eternal Soul Bead!" Yin Ren said.
Ye Tian took out an Eternal Soul Bead and let Yin Ren take a look.
Yin Ren recognized it at a glance; it was indeed a genuine Eternal Soul Bead.
Immediately, he promised, "Your Excellency must not trade with anyone else. I will give you a reply as soon as possible."
After speaking, Yin Ren left through the Void Source Palace and returned to his race territory.
