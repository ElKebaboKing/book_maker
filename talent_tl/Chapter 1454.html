Chapter 1453: Half-Step Ancestral Realm, Star Taboo Fruit!

Chapter 1453: Half-Step Ancestral Realm, Star Taboo Fruit
!
BOOM!!!!! One by one, Annihilation Crystals were consumed by Ye Tian, and the Supreme Domain Body within his body began to undergo a transformation.
At the same time, Ye Tian brought out countless energy treasures. After all, the advancement of the Supreme Domain Body required a massive amount of energy. Annihilation treasures only allowed Ye Tian's Supreme Domain Body to be rapidly tempered and break through its shackles, but energy replenishment could not rely entirely on Annihilation treasures.
Moreover, he wasn't so extravagant as to use Annihilation treasures solely for energy replenishment.
Soon.
Ye Tian's Supreme Domain Body officially broke through, and countless energy treasures were consumed by him.
At this moment, Ye Tian's body was transforming. Once the transformation was complete, he would reach the middle stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm, completely crossing a bottleneck that other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors found difficult to overcome even over endless eons.
And since his foundation far surpassed other Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, the increase in strength from advancing a minor stage was far greater than the magnitude of improvement for others.
It was conceivable how exaggerated the boost from Annihilation treasures was for his strength.
Approximately several hundred thousand years passed before Ye Tian finally completed the breakthrough.
"I've finally reached the middle stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm. My strength has skyrocketed. Now, I am capable of fighting a true Half-step Progenitor without falling into a disadvantage!"
Ye Tian possessed such confidence.
However, after his cultivation reached the middle stage of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm, advancing to the late stage would be even more difficult, requiring even more Annihilation treasures.
His duplicated bodies could slowly collect Annihilation treasures; there was no need for his main body to worry about it.
Furthermore, he couldn't place all his hopes solely on Annihilation treasures.
Within the Dark Forbidden Zone, many precious treasures were no less effective than Annihilation treasures, and some were even more precious than average Annihilation treasures. Otherwise, the Dark Forbidden Zone wouldn't have given birth to so many Progenitors.
For instance, if a new Ancestral Realm were born, it would be enough to tempt even a Progenitor.
After all, when an Ancestral Realm is first born, many Innate items are created. The Innate items of an Ancestral Realm far surpass those of ordinary worlds, enough to drive even Supreme-level Domain Ancestors mad. Obtaining just one would be enough to significantly increase one's strength.
The Xuanxu Holy Realm was naturally still a long way from becoming an Ancestral Realm, but it now far surpassed any Holy Realm.
If one were to describe its level, it would be a Half-step Ancestral Realm.
Time flowed by.
On this day.
A special world was born within the Xuanxu Holy Realm. As soon as this world appeared, Ye Tian immediately sealed it, forbidding anyone from entering. Even the Invincible Domain Ancestors of the human race were not allowed to enter, nor were they even allowed to know the situation of this world.
Regarding this, the Invincible Domain Ancestors of the human race did not dare to have any objections.
After all, everything the human race possessed now was given by Ye Tian. Even if they knew, they wouldn't dare to have any designs on it. Ye Tian's strength had reached a height they could never hope to attain; no one dared to oppose him.
At this moment, Ye Tian appeared within this special world.
"This world is actually giving birth to Ancestral Qi!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He sensed wisps of Ancestral Qi permeating the void. Although very faint, it definitely existed.
Ancestral Qi only appeared in Ancestral Realms. For Domain Ancestors, Ancestral Qi was a higher level of energy. Therefore, cultivating in an Ancestral Realm far surpassed cultivating in a Holy Realm.
But a Holy Realm could not produce Ancestral Qi.
Now, if the news that the Xuanxu Holy Realm was producing Ancestral Qi leaked out, trouble would follow. Even Progenitors might come to investigate.
The situation of the Xuanxu Holy Realm could not withstand a Progenitor's investigation. Back then, a mere thought from the Progenitor of War had arrived at the Xuanxu Holy Realm and almost frightened him, fearing the secrets of the Xuanxu Holy Realm would be exposed. However, a single thought from a Progenitor didn't have that much power, and the Progenitor of War hadn't intentionally investigated the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
Thus, the secrets of the Xuanxu Holy Realm remained hidden.
But if a Progenitor were to investigate intentionally, even if they couldn't find the Creation Primordial Well, they would easily discover the extraordinary and unique nature of the Xuanxu Holy Realm.
"Ancestral Qi has been born. The Xuanxu Holy Realm is evolving toward an Ancestral Realm, and this world is... the starting point of that evolution!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He could feel the Ancestral Qi in this world slowly increasing. In the future, it would surely become the core of the Xuanxu Holy Realm, and the core of the future Xuanxu Ancestral Realm.
Because of this, he sealed this world. He would stall for as much time as possible. Once his strength grew, he would fear nothing.
Even if the Progenitors knew the Xuanxu Holy Realm would become an Ancestral Realm, so what? If he possessed combat power comparable to a Progenitor, then owning an Ancestral Realm would be perfectly normal.
"This world will become the core of the Xuanxu Holy Realm and even the Xuanxu Ancestral Realm in the future. Let's call it the Heavenly Human Realm!"
Ye Tian gave the world a name.
In the blink of an eye, another hundred million years passed.
On this day.
A change occurred in the Heavenly Human Realm; a small tree was born.
The birth of this small tree caught Ye Tian's attention.
"An Innate item!"
He revealed a look of pleasant surprise.
Moreover, this was not a Holy Realm Innate item, but one equivalent to an Ancestral Realm Innate item, which could also be called an Innate Ancestral Item, distinct from ordinary Innate items.
At this moment, this small tree was frantically absorbing the Ancestral Qi of the Heavenly Human Realm.
BOOM!!!! Ye Tian's consciousness touched the small tree, and after some deduction, he learned part of its information.
Innate Ancestral Item—Star Taboo Tree!
According to his deductions, the Star Taboo Fruit produced by this Star Taboo Tree could temper the body and even had a certain effect on tempering the Supreme-level Domain Body. For Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, its value was incredibly precious.
If an Invincible Domain Body were to refine a Star Taboo Fruit, just one would be enough to allow an Invincible Domain Ancestor to advance directly from the early stage to the perfection stage of the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
Of course.
Such a Star Taboo Tree required a sufficiently long time to grow from birth to maturity.
Thus.
In the following days, Ye Tian set up a time acceleration array around the Star Taboo Tree and mobilized countless resources to supply its growth.
Under Ye Tian's strenuous cultivation, after three hundred million years had passed, the Star Taboo Tree reached maturity, transforming into a divine tree billions of miles tall, and it even grew 108 Star Taboo Fruits.
Each Star Taboo Fruit was as large as a star, emitting an enticing, refreshing fragrance.
