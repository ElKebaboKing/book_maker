Chapter 1695: Dark Mist Lord flees for his life!

Near Dark Mist Territory, Ye Tian’s two replicated bodies were locked in fierce combat with Dark Mist Lord’s true body.
At this time, Ice Container Lord was watching, and several other Legendary Realm Lords were observing this battle as well.
“Lord of Endlessness actually possesses such powerful strength!”
Saint Peak Lord was shocked.
Although Dark Mist Lord’s strength was not the strongest among the six Legendary Realm Lords, it was not the weakest either, and he had already been a Legendary Realm Lord for a very long time, so his strength was much greater than that of a newly broken-through Legendary Realm Lord.
Moreover, an Immortal Realm Lord was far too weak compared with a Legendary Realm Lord. Normally speaking, a Legendary Realm Lord could heavily injure or even kill the most top-tier Immortal Realm Lords.
Yet Ye Tian was actually able to contend with a Legendary Realm Lord like Dark Mist Lord. This was simply unbelievable.
“Lord of Endlessness can already match a true Legendary Realm Lord!”
Ice Container Lord muttered.
They did not participate in the battle between Ye Tian and Dark Mist Lord. Although they had known Dark Mist Lord for a longer time, they were also in a competitive relationship and would not offend Ye Tian just for Dark Mist Lord’s sake.
In addition, they could see that Ye Tian, while still at the Immortal Realm Lord level, already possessed battle strength comparable to a Legendary Realm Lord. If Ye Tian were to break through to the Legendary Realm Lord level, his strength would absolutely surpass theirs.
Therefore, they did not want to offend Ye Tian.
If Dark Mist Lord had learned of Ye Tian’s strength earlier, he would not have wanted to become enemies with Ye Tian either. But now that they were already enemies, there was naturally no room for reconciliation.
Time slowly passed. The battle between Ye Tian and Dark Mist Lord had already spread throughout the entire Time-Space Territory. Many, many Eternal Realm beings came to observe this battle, but none dared to approach; they watched from a distant area instead.
If they came closer, they might be blasted to death by the shockwaves from Ye Tian and Dark Mist Lord’s battle.
At first, they did not know the reason why Ye Tian and Dark Mist Lord were fighting, but it was soon exposed.
After all, Ye Tian and Dark Mist Lord’s avatars were fighting in the Source Sea, and those few Legendary Realm Lords only had to investigate a little to know the truth.
Immediately, the other five Legendary Lords became excited.
This was an Eternal Treasure, a true Eternal Treasure, not a temporal projection of an Eternal Treasure. It was also the first Eternal Treasure in the Source Sea, which made its significance enormous.
If they obtained this Eternal Treasure, their strength would surely soar.
At this point, the five Legendary Lords finally understood why Ye Tian was fighting Dark Mist Lord—it must have been for this Eternal Treasure.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!
The avatars of the five Legendary Lords arrived at the Source Sea, silently waiting at the place where that Eternal Treasure was being born. Even though Ye Tian and Dark Mist Lord were fighting nearby, they did not care at all.
……
“Lord of Endlessness, the other five Legendary Realm Lords have already discovered the Eternal Treasure here. There is no point in continuing this battle!”
Dark Mist Lord said.
He no longer wanted to keep fighting because he was at a disadvantage.
If the battle continued, he really might fall.
Even if only an avatar perished, that would still be a huge loss, so he wanted to call a truce with Ye Tian.
“Dream on!”
Ye Tian directly refused.
Dark Mist Lord wanted to stop, but Ye Tian did not.
Dark Mist Lord had dared to threaten him and even wanted to act against the Dark Forbidden Zone. That was equivalent to touching his reverse scale.
As for the Eternal Treasure being discovered, so be it. In any case, the Eternal Treasure had not truly finished forming yet, and he was not in the least anxious.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian crazily surrounded and killed Dark Mist Lord’s three bodies.
As time went on, the avatar of Dark Mist Lord that was besieged by Ye Tian’s three bodies could no longer hold on. After all, those three bodies also had the assistance of the Primordial Order Token, so Dark Mist Lord was naturally far from their match.
At this time,
Dark Mist Lord’s avatar was covered in cracks, and countless particles inside its body had been shattered by the Half-Step Legendary Eternal Technique.
Finally, on this day, this avatar of Dark Mist Lord was completely blasted to death by Ye Tian.
One avatar killed—that was a catastrophic disaster for Dark Mist Lord!
Whoosh!!!
Ye Tian’s three bodies tore through the spacetime barrier of the Source Sea and headed toward Dark Mist Territory, preparing to join the other two bodies and use all five bodies to surround and kill Dark Mist Lord’s true body.
Once Dark Mist Lord’s true body was killed, the other avatar would also collapse.
Boom boom boom!!!
The arrival of Ye Tian’s three bodies caused Dark Mist Lord’s true body to tremble all over.
Although the true body possessed the most weapons and trump cards, it was not much stronger than the other two avatars. It simply could not withstand Ye Tian’s five bodies. Once surrounded, there would truly be a risk of falling.
Losing an avatar was one thing. Although the loss was severe, it was not unbearable. But if the true body were to fall, then it would truly be dead.
After all, Dark Mist Lord was not like Ye Tian, whose six bodies could all be counted as true bodies.
“Run!”
Dark Mist Lord panicked and fled at all costs.
Very quickly, Dark Mist Lord fled into a more distant region of void, while Ye Tian’s five bodies pursued him.
But at this moment, the avatar of Dark Mist Lord left in the Source Sea began charging madly toward the Dark Forbidden Zone.
Ye Tian’s strength was indeed very powerful, but he could not completely stop Dark Mist Lord from attacking the Dark Forbidden Zone. If Dark Mist Lord fought desperately, he could still heavily injure or even destroy the Dark Forbidden Zone.
Helplessly, Ye Tian had to withdraw several replicated bodies to assist the replicated body remaining in the Source Sea, thereby stopping Dark Mist Lord.
In that case, Dark Mist Lord’s true body would have a great chance of escaping.
It was obvious that Dark Mist Lord was sacrificing his second avatar in order to gain a chance to flee.
“He still let Dark Mist Lord’s true body escape!”
Ye Tian said helplessly.
Dark Mist Lord’s speed was not particularly fast, but he had many trump cards, and he also had a special Eternal Treasure that directly helped him flee into the depths of the void region, making it impossible to lock onto his position for the time being.
Since his position could not be locked down in a short time, with Dark Mist Lord’s speed, he naturally escaped to who-knew-where.
Afterwards,
Ye Tian’s six bodies joined forces to surround the avatar Dark Mist Lord had left in the Source Sea, and soon killed it.
By this point, only Dark Mist Lord’s true body had escaped, while his two avatars had perished in the Source Sea.
As for Dark Mist Lord’s outcome, the other five Legendary Realm Lords were very shocked, and thus all became extremely wary of Ye Tian.
Not long after, the Eternal Treasure in the Source Sea was finally fully formed.
At this point, it would depend on who could take away this Eternal Treasure.
