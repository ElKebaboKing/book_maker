Chapter 1475: Condensing the Endless Ancestor Body, The Main Body Finally Becomes an Endless Ancestor
!
Outside the Dark Soul Domain.
One Ancestor after another emerged. These Ancestors were not... incarnations, but their true bodies descending here.
This was because in another few tens of thousands of years, the Dark Soul Domain would have existed for a hundred domain years.
At that time, the Dark Soul Domain would no longer restrict Ancestors from entering.
Furthermore, at that time, a second Ancestral World would be born within the Dark Soul Domain, and the Ancestral World would also produce more rare resources, even resources that were of great use to the Ancestors themselves.
The Ancestors didn't care about the previous resources, as those were of little help to them, but now it was different. What they were waiting for was... today.
For these opportunities and the territory of the Dark Soul Domain, they would even slaughter one another.
At this moment.
The Destruction Ancestor radiated an extremely dense aura of destruction, and the faintly revealed killing intent made many Ancestors tremble with fear.
At this time, various Ancestors were communicating in secret.
"The human race's Green Leaf Ancestor is likely finished. In a mere hundred domain years, how much could he possibly grow? Back then, the Dark Soul Domain was able to survive the crisis because the Destruction Ancestor was only an Elite Ancestor, but now the Destruction Ancestor possesses Top-tier Ancestor combat power. Green Leaf Ancestor still hasn't fully mastered the power of the Dark Soul Domain; at most, he can exert the combat power of an ordinary Elite Ancestor.
Once the Destruction Ancestor makes a move against Green Leaf Ancestor, I'm afraid Green Leaf Ancestor won't survive!"
"Who told Green Leaf Ancestor to dare kill a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of the Destruction Saint Clan who was about to promote to Ancestor? Naturally, he enraged the Destruction Ancestor, the Space-Time Ancestor, and the others!"
"A Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of the All-Things Saint Sect who was about to promote to Ancestor also died at the hands of Green Leaf Ancestor. The All-Things Ancestor will likely make a move against Green Leaf Ancestor as well, right?"
"The All-Things Ancestor is merely an ordinary Ancestor and probably isn't a match for Green Leaf Ancestor, but he will definitely target the human race where Green Leaf Ancestor resides. For example, the human race's Myriad Law War Ancestor definitely has hope of promoting to Ancestor, and his combat power is terrifying. Perhaps the All-Things Ancestor will seek revenge by killing the Myriad Law War Ancestor."
"It's not very realistic for the All-Things Ancestor to want to kill the Myriad Law War Ancestor. The War Ancestor and the Domain Ancestor won't just stand by; they will definitely stop him!"
"They might not be able to stop him. Once they enter the Dark Soul Domain and many Ancestors engage in battle, in such a chaotic situation, anything could happen!"
....Time passed slowly, and the day for the Ancestors to enter the Dark Soul Domain drew closer and closer.
In the past, hundreds of millions of years were just the time for a nap to these Ancestors, but now, a mere ten thousand years seemed incredibly long.
On this day.
Abruptly.
The Dark Soul Domain shook. Someone was charging toward the Ancestor realm, and the commotion was incredibly massive, many times greater than when Green Leaf Ancestor broke through to the Ancestor realm.
"Who? Who is charging toward the Ancestor realm?"
One Ancestor after another was shocked.
At this critical juncture, someone was suddenly striking for the Ancestor realm; it was too strange.
"It's the human race's Myriad Law War Ancestor. No need to guess, he is the only one in the Dark Soul Domain qualified to strike for the Ancestor realm and capable of creating such a terrifying commotion!"
One Ancestor spoke up.
"There are only a few thousand years left before we enter the Dark Soul Domain. For the Myriad Law War Ancestor to break through now, he probably won't be able to fully enter the Ancestor realm. Isn't he afraid of his breakthrough process being interrupted?"
An Ancestor asked curiously.
Indeed, for a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor to charge into the Ancestor realm, without a million years, it was impossible to thoroughly break through.
Moreover, ordinary time formations simply couldn't withstand the aura of an Ancestor; it was impossible to break through inside a time formation.
A mere few thousand years was simply not enough time.
However, they had overlooked the existence of the Green Leaf Ancestor.
BOOM!!!! Green Leaf Ancestor mobilized the power of the Dark Soul Domain and began to accelerate time in the area where Ye Tian was in seclusion.
10,000 times! 100,000 times! Accelerating time by ten thousand times for Ye Tian, who was currently breaking through, and frantically mobilizing the energy of the Dark Soul Domain to assist Ye Tian's breakthrough came at a great cost.
At this moment, Green Leaf Ancestor's energy was being continuously consumed. To restore energy, massive amounts of Nirvana Treasures were being consumed and refined by Ye Tian.
Outside the Dark Soul Domain.
The Ancestors were all stunned; they also sensed Green Leaf Ancestor's actions.
"He actually accelerated the time of the Dark Soul Domain to fully assist the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor's breakthrough. Isn't he afraid that after a few thousand years, his state will be unstable?"
An Ancestor said in surprise.
While the many Ancestors were surprised, the Destruction Ancestor opened his eyes and looked toward the Dark Soul Domain.
"Hmph, Myriad Law War Ancestor, no matter how monstrous your talent or how deep your foundation, at most your combat power will rival an Elite Ancestor upon breakthrough. But the Myriad Law Domain Ancestor isn't the first person to break through to Ancestor in the Dark Soul Domain, so he can't borrow the power of the Dark Soul Domain. Thus, his combat power will only be that of an ordinary Elite Ancestor, nothing to fear!"
The Destruction Ancestor thought to himself.
However, he had underestimated Ye Tian.
Ye Tian not only had an incredibly deep foundation and had completed a second transformation at the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor level, but he also mastered several Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. His combat power was not... only that of an ordinary Elite Ancestor as they imagined.
In the blink of an eye, several hundred years passed in the outside world, but under the time acceleration, Ye Tian had spent a full several million years breaking through.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian completely finished his breakthrough, condensed the Endless Ancestor Body, and stepped into the Ancestor realm.
Upon breaking through to the Ancestor realm, Ye Tian's strength increased by an unknown number of times. Furthermore, the Endless Ancestor Body far surpassed other Ancestor bodies, and could be called truly immortal and indestructible.
Although he didn't have time to refine that large amount of Nirvana Treasures now and couldn't rapidly increase his cultivation level.
But he did possess Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, and once he broke through to the Ancestor realm, he could fully exert the power of those Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. His combat power was even stronger than Green Leaf Ancestor borrowing the power of the Dark Soul Domain.
According to Ye Tian's estimation, his current strength was likely infinitely close to Top-tier Ancestor combat power.
Moreover, relying on the Endless Ancestor Body, even a Top-tier Ancestor couldn't kill him.
If given enough time, he could easily step into the Top-tier Ancestor combat power level.
At this time, with a few thousand years remaining in the outside world, Ye Tian had his clone, Green Leaf Ancestor, continue to accelerate time in this area. He began to frantically refine those Nirvana Treasures, striving to increase his cultivation a bit more.
BOOM BOOM BOOM!!!! Countless Nirvana Treasures were swallowed and refined by Ye Tian, and his cultivation rose bit by bit.
Unfortunately, no matter how much he practiced, he could only train for a few tens of millions of years more. He couldn't increase his strength too much; increasing it by thirty percent was already good, making him even closer to Top-tier Ancestor combat power.
But that was enough! When there were only a hundred years left, Ye Tian controlled his clone, Green Leaf Ancestor, to stop the time acceleration and instead refined a large number of Nirvana Treasures to recover his state.
Additionally, Ye Tian destroyed the Nirvana Time-Space; at the very least, he couldn't let... other Ancestors know about it, waiting to slowly cultivate the Nirvana Time-Space again in the future.
Soon, a hundred years passed.
The Ancestors were about to descend upon the Dark Soul Domain.
