Chapter 1698: Break Through to Become a Legendary Lord!

In the secluded cultivation site.
Ye Tian began forcibly merging the power of the will law with his body. This was equivalent to merging the body, will, and innate eternal law in all three aspects, a kind of cultivation that only a Legendary Lord had the qualification to touch.
At this moment, Ye Tian was also undergoing such cultivation.
Buzz!!!
An odd power seemed about to be born, but Ye Tian's body could no longer bear it; it seemed about to shatter.
At this time, Ye Tian had been paying attention to the condition inside his body.
If his body were turned into an endless world of particles, then these three powers at this moment would be interwoven across countless particle worlds, appearing extremely clear.
As time passed, Ye Tian's body gradually collapsed, but he did not care in the slightest and still carefully observed the changes inside his body.
No one knew how much time had passed when Ye Tian suddenly sensed something unusual somewhere in his body.
At a certain spot in the barrier of the will source sea within his body, the will and body here had not fully merged; they were still lacking the slightest bit. However, this place was on the will source sea itself and covered by countless will powers, so it was extremely difficult to detect.
But now, during the process of forcibly merging the will law power with the body, this place was exposed.
With a boom!
Ye Tian's body exploded along with it, but he had already found the source of the problem. Afterward, he only needed to completely merge this spot, and he could break through the shackle, fully fuse his will and body to 100%, which also meant stepping into the realm of a Legendary Lord.
His true self.
“Begin!”
Ye Tian found the location and began merging the will and body there. This final bit was very difficult to merge, to the point that he could not even feel that it had not yet fused. At first, there was almost no reaction. If he had not been certain, he would have already given up.
Time passed little by little, and Ye Tian gradually sensed the problem here, doing his utmost to merge will and body.
In this way, one tribulation era after another passed, but this final trace remained unbelievably stubborn.
It took a full six eternal years before Ye Tian finally completely fused that last trace.
The moment the fusion succeeded, Ye Tian's entire body began to transform.
Boom!!!!!
The leap began!
Ye Tian's Immortal Stage body was transforming into a Legendary Stage body, his eternal power was also surging, and his willpower was rapidly increasing.
Endless source energy poured from the source sea into his body, spurring him to break through even faster.
At the same time.
One after another, the Legendary Lords in the Time-Space Territory sensed Ye Tian's breakthrough. After all, the commotion was too great, and there was no concealment at all, so it was hard for them not to know.
“The Endless Ancestor broke through just like that?”
Lord Bing Han was shocked.
Previously, Ye Tian had come to ask her about breaking through, and she had been unable to give any useful advice. She had even thought that Ye Tian would remain at this level for at least tens of millions of eternal years, or even hundreds of millions of eternal years, which was all very normal.
If luck were bad, it was not impossible for him to remain stuck at the Immortal Stage Lord realm forever.
Yet only a few eternal years had passed, and Ye Tian had already broken through. This speed was simply too fast.
The other Legendary Stage Lords were even more shocked, because there had been no sign of it at all before. They had not even known Ye Tian had reached the level where he could attempt to impact the Legendary Lord realm, and it felt as if, all of a sudden, Ye Tian had broken through. Everything had happened too abruptly.
Ye Tian's breakthrough was not exactly a good thing for them. Although it strengthened the Time-Space Territory, from their individual perspective, there was now a Legendary Stage Lord far above them, and their future actions would be restricted, as they would be afraid of offending Ye Tian.
However, they had no way to stop Ye Tian from breaking through; everything was already settled.
After a long stretch of time, Ye Tian's realm finally stabilized.
-
“My strength has actually increased so much!”
Ye Tian was incomparably excited.
At this moment, his body was more than ten times stronger, his eternal power had increased by several dozen times, and his willpower had also become several times stronger.
Now, even without relying on the Primordial Token, he could easily beat and even suppress Dark Mist Lord to death. If he used the Primordial Token, he could probably kill Dark Mist Lord within a short time.
More importantly, he had now become a Legendary Stage Lord, allowing him to merge his will and innate eternal law, and since his will itself had already merged with his body, he could attempt to fuse all three powers together.
By then, he would be able to create a legendary eternal technique of his own.
Once he mastered a legendary eternal technique, his strength would rise even further.
Of course, the Primordial Token could now also raise his legendary eternal technique to the level of a weaker legendary eternal technique, but it would definitely not be as good as a true legendary eternal technique when used.
In short, during this period just after breaking through to the Legendary Stage Lord realm, his strength would still rise enormously.
Thus, Ye Tian continued cultivating and improving, while his other clone bodies appeared in public.
At the first opportunity, the five Legendary Stage Lords of the Time-Space Territory came to the Bloodbone Territory to congratulate Ye Tian.
“Congratulations to Lord Endless on ascending to the Legendary Stage Lord realm!”
“Congratulations to Lord Endless on ascending to the Legendary Stage Lord realm!”
“Congratulations to Lord Endless on ascending to the Legendary Stage Lord realm!”
· ·········Ask for flowers··· ·····
One Legendary Stage Lord after another offered their congratulations.
“Thank you, everyone!”
Ye Tian was also quite happy, and immediately invited the five Legendary Stage Lords into his palace, where a feast began.
During the feast, everyone discussed the Dao and exchanged insights, and only after a long time did they disperse.
By exchanging with these five Legendary Stage Lords, Ye Tian gained quite a lot. Before, he had only had a rough idea of the division among Legendary Stage Lords, but after discussing it with these five, the distinctions in strength among Legendary Stage Lords became much clearer to him.
Immortal Stage Lords are divided according to the degree of fusion between will and body into first through ninth-step Immortal Stage Lords, but the classification of Legendary Lords is different.
On the Time-Space Territory side, Legendary Lords have their will and body fused, and their body and innate eternal law fused. In theory, the will and innate eternal law are also fused, but that is not actually the case. It is like an egg with two yolks: the egg is the body, and the two yolks are respectively the will and the innate eternal law.
..... .. 0
However.
The will and innate eternal law themselves have no limit. Even if the will and innate eternal law inside the body have fused, they can still continue to fuse without any end.
However, once the will and innate eternal law within the body's particles begin to fuse, such a Legendary Lord is called a Initial Stage Legendary Lord; if the will and innate eternal law within the body are all fused, such a Legendary Lord is called a Perfected Stage Legendary Lord; and if one continues to fuse will and innate eternal law, although they can no longer fuse with the body because the body has already been fully fused, the will and innate eternal law are infinite, allowing one to accumulate endless will-law power. When that will-law power accumulates to a certain degree, a will-law domain can be formed, and such a Legendary Lord is called a Domain-level Legendary Lord. For many Legendary Lords, a Domain-level Legendary Lord is almost the peak of a Legendary Lord, but there is still one more level of Legendary Lord, and that is the Transcendence Stage Legendary Lord. As for how to reach that level, even the five Legendary Lords did not know.
Ordinary Legendary Lord, Initial Stage Legendary Lord, Perfected Stage Legendary Lord, Domain Stage Legendary Lord, and Transcendence Stage Legendary Lord.
These are the five major realms of Legendary Lords.
And a newly broken-through Legendary Lord belongs to the category of Ordinary Legendary Lord, while Ye Tian was in fact not yet considered a Legendary Lord, because he had not yet fused the three powers. However, for him, that could be accomplished very easily, after all, he had already begun fusing his will and innate eternal law long ago.
