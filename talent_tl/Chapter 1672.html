Chapter 1671 Breakthrough, becoming a Nine Apex Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm!

“Next, I’ll create a speed-type legendary Eternal technique!”
Ye Tian was extremely excited in his heart.
He was very clear that once he created a speed-type legendary Eternal technique, he would definitely be able to shatter the Second Boundary Barrier and completely step into the level of Nine Apex Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm.
Before he knew it, another ten Eternal years had passed.
On this day.
The speed-type legendary Eternal technique was finally created by Ye Tian.
Ye Tian named this speed-type legendary Eternal technique Wujian, meaning that for him, distance had already lost all meaning, representing extreme speed.
With the Wujian Eternal technique created, Ye Tian’s strength increased quite a bit, and the Second Boundary Barrier that existed in the dark finally began to completely shatter.
Boom!!!!
With the Second Boundary Barrier shattered, a source void entirely composed of the origin of innate Eternal rules appeared in Ye Tian’s perception.
At this moment.
Ye Tian’s will transformed into a giant sitting in this source void, comprehending this endless origin of innate Eternal rules at all times, and in this place of comprehension, it seemed that everything was very easy to understand thoroughly.
The origin of all dao was crystal clear, and any doubts Ye Tian had could be answered here.
Yet in the Time-Space Territory, he had not sensed the source void formed by the origin of innate Eternal rules.
At this moment, Ye Tian understood why one could not break through to the Nine Apex Realm in the Time-Space Territory; thus, one could not comprehend the source void of the origin of innate Eternal rules in the Time-Space Territory, and only in the Source Sea could one comprehend it.
This place seemed to have no time limit, and Ye Tian did not know how long he had comprehended it.
Only at some point did he emerge from the source void.
At this moment.
He had already comprehended the innate Eternal rules to the true Extreme, far beyond comparison with the Eight Apex Realm, and he could fuse the innate origin rules at any time, thereby advancing to the Eternal Realm.
But like the Nine Apex Realm expert in the Eight Apex Tower, he did not go on to assault the Eternal Realm.
“So the Nine Apex Realm can already allow the body and will to merge, similar to how the Immortal Realm assaults the Immortal Realm.”
Ye Tian was somewhat surprised by this.
Once the fusion of will and body exceeds ten percent, an Immortal Realm expert can break through to become an Immortal Realm expert, and the Nine Apex Realm is the same. If, at the Nine Apex Realm, one merges will and body to more than ten percent, then after breaking through to the Eternal Realm, one becomes an Immortal Realm directly.
Moreover, the physical body of the Nine Apex Realm is far weaker than that of the Immortal Realm, and the body has not yet fully merged with the innate Eternal rules, making it easier to fuse with will.
Therefore, fusing will at the Nine Apex Realm is relatively easy.
This is also why the Nine Apex Realm is not in a hurry to break through. If one breaks through prematurely, even if one becomes a Half-Step Immortal Realm expert, it would still take a very long time to become a true Immortal Realm expert. That would be a foolish thing to do.
And after becoming a Nine Apex Realm expert, the strength does not undergo a qualitative change, but once one becomes a Nine Apex Realm expert, there will be a huge period of improvement in strength.
However, after that improvement period passes, it becomes very difficult for strength to improve, and one can only walk the path of fusing will; will fusion also increases strength.
Thus, a truly grown Nine Apex Realm expert will be many times stronger than an Eight Apex Realm expert, but a freshly broken-through Nine Apex Realm expert will not be that powerful.
After breaking through to the Nine Apex Realm, Ye Tian’s will improved considerably, and he could continue cultivating the will world, opening up one will world after another.
Moreover, his body could continue to grow, and his Half-Step Eternal power could also continue to be compressed, and it was still very easy to compress.
As time slowly passed, Ye Tian’s Half-Step Eternal power had already been easily compressed to 80 times, and it could still be compressed further. The layered secret technique was already of little use to Ye Tian, but relying on his grasp of the innate Eternal rules, compressing Half-Step Eternal power was too simple for him.
Not only that, Ye Tian’s body had also become much stronger than when he had just broken through, but this had also consumed nearly all of his resources.
Afterward, Ye Tian created one legendary Eternal technique after another, making his strength even more powerful.
In the blink of an eye, a thousand Eternal years passed.
Such a long period of time had completely carried Ye Tian through the improvement period.
And at this moment, Ye Tian had already opened up thirty thousand will worlds, and his third-tier Eternal will was probably even a bit stronger than that of many Immortal Realm lords.
In addition.
Ye Tian also began merging his will, but fusing will was indeed very difficult; at present, only one ten-thousandth of his body had fused with his will, and there was still a long way to go before reaching more than ten percent fusion.
Of course, there was also a shortcut to will fusion, and that was the Void Beast.
Materials from Void Beasts could fuse with will, but he had previously used Void Beast materials to enhance his will, which was also in order to raise his Eternal will to third tier, so it did not count as waste.
At present, he still had a little of those Void Beast materials left, but their effect was not very great.
To fuse the body and will to more than ten percent, a large amount of Void Beast material was required.
However, everyone in the Time-Space Territory knew how precious Void Beast materials were, and no one was willing to trade them, unless it was the six legendary lords. They did not have much need for Void Beast materials, but they also would not take them out to sell; giving them to their own family or loyal subordinates was also a good choice!
“．‖Perhaps I can ask that Nine Apex Realm expert from the Eight Apex Tower for guidance—Star-Observing Sword Ancestor!”
Star-Observing Sword Ancestor was in the depths of the Eight Apex Tower, but normally no one would disturb him.
Last time, Star-Observing Sword Ancestor also took part in the hunt for Void Beasts, but not together with Ye Tian and the others. After all, as a Nine Apex Realm existence, Star-Observing Sword Ancestor had the qualification to join forces with some Immortal Realm lords to encircle and kill Void Beasts.
It could be said that Star-Observing Sword Ancestor’s circle had already reached the Immortal Realm and even the Immortal Realm, and he would not have too much interaction with those Eight Apex Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm experts in the Eight Apex Tower.
On this day.
Ye Tian’s clone entered the Eight Apex Tower and immediately announced that he had stepped into the Nine Apex Realm.
This news quickly exploded throughout the entire Eight Apex Tower, shocking the other Eight Apex Realm experts one after another.
True Yang Saint Ancestor arrived first and exclaimed in shock: “Boundless Ancestor, you... you actually broke through to the Nine Apex Realm!!!!”
The other Eight Apex Realm experts arrived one after another, sensing the Nine Apex Realm aura on Ye Tian’s body and knowing that Ye Tian had not lied.
It had only been a little over a thousand Eternal years since they last met, and Ye Tian had actually broken through the Second Boundary Barrier. Wasn’t this breakthrough speed too fast?
“Lord Gu He luckily broke through to the Immortal Realm, and Boundless Ancestor broke through to become a Nine Apex Realm Half-Step Eternal Realm expert. In these more than a thousand Eternal years, two major events have happened in our Eight Apex Tower!”
An Eight Apex Realm expert said with emotion, his tone full of envy.
Whether it was becoming an Eternal Realm expert or becoming a Nine Apex Realm expert, these were the goals they had pursued all their lives!
Just then, a figure filled with endless sword dao rules walked out from the depths of the Eight Apex Tower.
“Greetings, Star-Observing Sword Ancestor!”
The other Eight Apex Realm experts greeted him one after another.
“Congratulations, Boundless Ancestor!”
Star-Observing Sword Ancestor congratulated him.
Afterward.
Ye Tian and Star-Observing Sword Ancestor entered a void deep within the Eight Apex Tower, and the two sat down and chatted.
