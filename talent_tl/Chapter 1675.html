Chapter 1674 A Sword That Cannot Be Reached!

After entering the True No Legendary Land, Ye Tian continued cultivating.
As time passed, the degree of fusion between his will and body grew higher and higher, getting closer and closer to ten percent.
Finally, after the fourth kalpa era, Ye Tian’s will-body fusion reached ten percent.
It was at this moment that his body began to undergo a qualitative transformation.
Boom!!!!!
Every particle in Ye Tian’s body began to transform, becoming increasingly powerful. Endless source energy from the outside poured into his body, driving his physical strength to grow stronger and stronger.
Moreover, Ye Tian’s will also improved quite a bit.
After a long time, Ye Tian’s transformation was complete.
At this point, he was much stronger than before.
Now, if he went to break through to the Eternal Realm, then after the breakthrough he would become an Immortal Realm lord.
But Ye Tian was not in a hurry to break through yet. After all, once he broke through to the Immortal Realm, continuing to fuse his will and body would become much more difficult than it was now. So there was no need for him to rush. It would be better to stay a while longer in the Half-Step Eternal Realm of the Ninth Extreme Realm.
In any case, his lifespan was infinite, unlike those Half-Step Eternal Realm cultivators in the Space-Time Territory, whose lifespan limit was ten million eternal years. Thus, they would not keep lingering at the Half-Step Eternal Realm forever; they would definitely advance to the Eternal Realm earlier.
Another kalpa era passed, and Ye Tian came out of the True No Legendary Land.
This time, he began challenging the tenth level of the True No Legendary Tower.
Once he cleared this level, he would be able to obtain ten kalpa eras of cultivation time, which was an enormous temptation to him!
Tenth level.
Ye Tian fought against three Eternal Realm opponents, and their battle caused the void on the tenth level to shake violently.
The three Eternal Realm opponents on the tenth level were indeed very powerful, and each of them knew more than one will Eternal technique. However, Ye Tian’s strength was also incomparably powerful, and he forcibly held off the three Eternal Realm opponents.
Suddenly.
The three Eternal Realm opponents jointly unleashed a will Eternal technique. If they were true Eternal Realm cultivators, they naturally would not have been able to do this, but these Eternal Realm opponents were merely condensed by the True No Legendary Tower, so anything was possible.
Buzz!!!
A will void sphere appeared in the void, enveloping Ye Tian within it.
Immediately afterward, the will void sphere began to compress; the void inside kept collapsing, and even Eternal laws were breaking apart, unable to withstand the power of this will void sphere at all.
Such a will Eternal technique sealed off all of Ye Tian’s escape routes, leaving him nowhere to dodge.
“Void True Body!”
“Eternal Extreme Fist!”
Ignoring his injuries, Ye Tian set out to shatter this will void sphere.
Boom!!!!
The will void sphere kept cracking, but Ye Tian also suffered many will injuries all over his body.
Suddenly.
The will void sphere exploded, and the entire region was directly shattered into nothingness along with it.
Within the distorted and shattered nothingness, Ye Tian walked out, covered in blood. If not for the Void True Body, he truly would not have been able to withstand it.
It had to be said that this will Eternal technique jointly unleashed by the three Eternal Realm cultivators was indeed terrifying.
However, Ye Tian had already figured out their move, and with a countermeasure in hand, he would not give them another chance to join forces and unleash it again.
Next.
Ye Tian fought the three Eternal Realm cultivators at extreme speed, bringing out the full power of the legendary Eternal technique, Interruption.
Immediately, the three Eternal Realm cultivators had no way to jointly unleash the will void sphere. Whenever they tried, Ye Tian would use the legendary Eternal technique Interruption to cut them off.
The battle continued like this until, after a long time, Ye Tian finally wore down and killed the three Eternal Realm cultivators one by one, clearing the tenth level, though he also paid a heavy price in injuries.
“If I encountered three such Eternal Realm cultivators outside, I would surely die!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
After all, the Eternal Realm cultivators condensed by the True No Legendary Tower did not possess the recovery abilities of true Eternal Realm cultivators. Otherwise, he would not have been able to wear down and kill the three of them.
But that was the challenge of the True No Legendary Tower. If the Eternal Realm cultivators here could also recover, then clearing the tenth level would be far too difficult; at the very least, he would be nowhere near capable of doing it.
Having obtained ten kalpa eras of cultivation time this time, Ye Tian did not keep it for later. He entered the True No Legendary Land again and continued improving the fusion between his will and body.
In the blink of an eye, another ten kalpa eras passed.
By now, his will-body fusion had improved somewhat again, but it was still far from the point of qualitative transformation, and the increase in strength was not too large.
He went to challenge the eleventh level, but unfortunately failed.
Therefore, in the short term, he would not be able to clear the eleventh level.
This ruin was not without other opportunities besides the True No Legendary Tower.
Through chatting with Star Ancestor, he also learned that there was one place here that counted as an opportunity, but not every Eternal Realm cultivator could gain any harvest or benefit from it, so not many Eternal Realm cultivators took it seriously.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian walked through the shattered void of the ruin and soon arrived at a broken palace in the ruin.
After entering this palace, he came to a special place. .........
Here, there were many Immortal Realm beings, and even a legendary lord—namely, Lord Binghan.
Ye Tian cupped his hands in greeting, but did not speak, so as not to disturb them.
In fact, these Immortal Realm beings and the legendary lord, Lord Binghan, did not pay him any mind either. They were still silently comprehending and cultivating.
Ye Tian’s gaze was soon drawn to a sword as well.
In the void, there was a silver long sword. It seemed illusory, yet was incomparably real. Looking at it carefully, it was as if one were seeing a sword Dao world stretching across endless heaven and earth, where every wisp of sword qi seemed capable of shattering an Immortal Realm being.
Compared with the sword Dao of this long sword itself, the sword Dao among the many Great Dao Ye Tian had comprehended was simply not worth mentioning. He had also comprehended the innate Eternal sword Dao laws, but compared with the sword Dao aura here, they were simply far too crude.
It existed at the source of endless spacetime, existing in every heaven and earth. It seemed very near, yet also as if it were not in this stretch of spacetime, not in the Space-Time Territory.
Ye Tian’s will wanted to touch this sword, but even as his will crossed spacetime, he still felt it to be out of reach, as if his will would never reach the place where this sword was located.
“It really is like the True No Dao Mark Tree, impossible to touch!”
Ye Tian was quite surprised.
He had heard from Star Ancestor before that there was such a sword here, and that Star Ancestor had been comprehending it here all along, but he had not known that this sword could not be touched.
“That makes sense. If it could be touched, the six legendary lords would have taken it away already!”
Ye Tian thought.
Although sword Dao was not Ye Tian’s main path of cultivation, it was still a part of the boundless Dao, so comprehending it here would also bring him some benefits.
However, the reason Ye Tian had come here was not to comprehend sword Dao, but to create an even more powerful Eternal compression secret art.
Star Ancestor had said that this sword could affect Eternal power, and even more so Half-Step Eternal power.
Its sword Dao aura was far too condensed, far surpassing Eternal power, which was why many Eternal Realm cultivators tried to comprehend the sword Dao aura and use this sword to create a secret art that could compress the Eternal power or Half-Step Eternal power within their bodies.
In fact, quite a few people had succeeded.
For example, the six legendary lords had created very powerful Eternal compression secret arts, but ordinary people could not obtain them.
Although Ye Tian had also created an Eternal compression secret art capable of eightyfold compression, it was of no use to him now.
So, he wanted to借 help from this sword to create a more powerful Eternal compression secret art.
