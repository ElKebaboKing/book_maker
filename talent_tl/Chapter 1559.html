Chapter 1558: Can the Xumi Golden Mountain Repair Rule Heavenly Object Fragments?

"Safe at last!"
Ye Tian breathed a sigh of relief when he saw that he had already entered the foreign world.
Although Abyss Tribulation Beasts could also enter the foreign world, they definitely did not dare to enter easily; otherwise, they would surely die.
In the Yuanji Abyss, Supreme Beings could not enter, which led to some Abyss Tribulation Beasts being almost Invincible existences. However, if they were in the outside world, the Abyss Tribulation Beasts would be hunted by Supreme Beings.
An Abyss Tribulation Beast with over a hundred thousand tribulations was of no small value. Even Supreme Beings were very willing to kill such Abyss Tribulation Beasts.
Sure enough, although one of the Abyss Tribulation Beast's claws reached toward the void passage, it ultimately did not charge through.
For Ye Tian, the harvest this time was great. Not only did he learn the key to impacting the Supreme Being realm, but he also obtained the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent.
Additionally, he completed the mission for the Tianfeng Sea.
"It's impossible for the Tianfeng Sea to truly give everyone a Rule Heavenly Object. They probably give damaged Rule Heavenly Objects. Otherwise, if multiple Invincibles successfully delivered the boxes to the Yuanji Abyss this time, wouldn't the Tianfeng Sea have to produce a large number of Rule Heavenly Objects?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, it wasn't that the Tianfeng Sea couldn't produce them, but they wouldn't give them out easily.
Rule Heavenly Objects were not ordinary treasures. The Tianfeng Sea only set such a high reward to attract Invincibles to take the mission; it was just a deliberate move to lure them in.
"I'll know once I get back!"
Ye Tian thought.
The way back was safe. After all, the Tianfeng Sovereign had already broken through to become a Supreme Being, so the Nether Hall naturally wouldn't dare to go and assassinate the Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea again. It would be good enough if they weren't hunted themselves.
After several million years, Ye Tian crossed countless foreign worlds and finally arrived at the Tianlin Realm.
Subsequently, Ye Tian returned to the Tianfeng Sea to submit the mission.
However, this time it was not the Qingyang Sovereign in charge of the matter, but a second-tier Invincible named Qi Hai.
Qi Hai said, "Ye Tian, it has been confirmed that you did indeed deliver the box to the Yuanji Abyss. This is the Rule Heavenly Object for you."
Ye Tian took a ring from Qi Hai. The ring was a void ring, and inside it was a Rule Heavenly Object.
Ye Tian's consciousness entered the void ring, where a tree branch floated in the void.
This tree branch was the Rule Heavenly Object!
At first glance, one might think this tree branch was a complete Rule Heavenly Object, but Ye Tian discovered that the aura of this tree branch was too far removed from that of a complete Rule Heavenly Object.
"This should be a branch cut from a tree-shaped Rule Heavenly Object. The Tianfeng Sea couldn't have cut off many branches and treated each one as a Rule Heavenly Object for the reward, right?" Ye Tian said in shock.
If that were truly the case, the higher-ups of the Tianfeng Sea were too stingy and deceptive.
However, the Tianfeng Sea had now become a Saint-Name level power. Even if the surviving Invincibles knew they had been cheated, they wouldn't complain. Instead, they would be very happy. After all, being able to become a member of a Saint-Name level power was an incredibly rare opportunity.
Qi Hai also seemed to feel that the reward this time was a bit of a scam, so he said, "The Tianfeng Sea has also decided to give each Invincible who completed the mission 30 million contribution points!"
30 million contribution points was indeed quite a lot. Although Ye Tian did not lack contribution points, his mood improved significantly.
……
Feathering World.
At this time, Ye Tian's main body was here, so his duplicated body had also returned.
Ye Tian duplicated the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent from the duplicated body, which was equivalent to permanently obtaining the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent.
Subsequently, Ye Tian took out the Rule Heavenly Object tree branch and examined it carefully.
"A very trashy Rule Heavenly Object. It contains the Wood Eternal Rule, but only a tiny bit, equivalent to one-ten-thousandth of a normal Rule Heavenly Object's Eternal Rule. To rely on such a Rule Heavenly Object fragment to comprehend the Wood Eternal Rule would take at least hundreds of thousands of times longer or even more."
Ye Tian shook his head.
In short, the value of such a Rule Heavenly Object fragment was not low; it was worth at least a hundred million contribution points. But compared to a true Rule Heavenly Object, there was no comparison at all.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian discovered an incredible scene. This tree branch was actually absorbing the aura of the Xumi Golden Mountain and was slowly recovering.
Of course, the recovery speed of the tree branch was very slow. If one didn't look closely, it was impossible to notice.
As a result.
Ye Tian inserted the tree branch into the Xumi Golden Mountain, and the Eternal Xumi Golden Mountain began to repair the tree branch at an even faster speed.
· ·········
At this speed, perhaps after a few Darkness Eras, this section of the tree branch would be completely repaired, or perhaps it would turn into a brand-new Rule Heavenly Object.
"Can an Eternal Source Object like the Xumi Golden Mountain repair Rule Heavenly Objects?"
Ye Tian was very puzzled.
He realized he knew too little about Eternal Source Objects.
However, the Xumi Golden Mountain was ranked over a hundred among Eternal Source Objects, which was not a low rank.
In fact, there were quite a few types of Eternal Source Objects discovered in the Source Sea, totaling tens of thousands. Therefore, being ranked over a hundred was a very high rank. Even an Eternal Source Object like Soul Blue Gold was only ranked over a thousand, which was inferior to the Xumi Golden Mountain.
... ......... .......
Perhaps it was precisely because the Xumi Golden Mountain had special abilities that it could be ranked so high, and repairing Rule Heavenly Objects was one of its abilities that had not been publicized to the outside world.
Of course, repairing a Rule Heavenly Object would consume the energy of the Xumi Golden Mountain itself. The value of an Eternal Source Object was far higher than that of a Rule Heavenly Object, so ordinary Invincibles would not use an Eternal Source Object for such a thing.
"I'm not in urgent need of the Xumi Golden Mountain right now. Besides, repairing just one Rule Heavenly Object shouldn't take much of its energy, right?"
Ye Tian thought so.
After thinking about it, Ye Tian decided to use the Xumi Golden Mountain to repair this Rule Heavenly Object fragment.
Time passed slowly—
While Ye Tian paid attention to the Xumi Golden Mountain repairing the Rule Heavenly Object tree branch, he also comprehended and created Invincible Ancestral Techniques and cultivated the Invincible Tribulation Body technique, Wan Yuan Gui Xu.
Gradually, Ye Tian's Wan Yuan Gui Xu Invincible Tribulation Body was refined to the third stage Minor, Major...
And Ye Tian's Invincible Ancestral Techniques also gradually reached over a dozen, but he still had not reached the Third-tier Invincible realm.
Perhaps others would have already stepped into the Third-tier Invincible realm, but he had cultivated Wan Yuan Gui Xu and reached the third stage, which meant his Invincible Tribulation Body was not yet Perfected. Therefore, he had to cultivate it to the third stage Perfected for his current realm's Invincible Tribulation Body to be considered Perfected, only then could he step into the Third-tier Invincible realm.

Ye Tian's Wan Yuan Gui Xu reached the third stage Perfected realm, and his entire being underwent an incredible qualitative change and.
