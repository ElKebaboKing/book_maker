Chapter 1696: The Five Legendary Domain Lords Were Dumbfounded!

Source Sea.
The place where the Eternal Treasure was born, Ye Tian and the five Legendary Domain Lords of the Time-Space Territory were all here.
At this moment, Domain Lord Bing Han proposed, “Everyone, the Eternal Treasure is extremely rare, and it is very difficult to take away. Whoever takes it away gets it. We must not kill one another. How about that?”
“I agree!”
Domain Lord Holy Peak immediately agreed.
The other Legendary Domain Lords also agreed one after another, and Ye Tian naturally agreed as well. He had no way of contending against the combined strength of the other five Legendary Domain Lords. This time, when dealing with Domain Lord Dark Mist, if even one Legendary Domain Lord had helped, he would not have been able to kill Dark Mist Lord’s clone.
He did not want to become the target of everyone, so he agreed to Domain Lord Bing Han’s proposal.
However, everyone clearly stated that Immortal Domain Lords were not allowed to approach this place. If an Immortal Domain Lord took away the Eternal Treasure, then it would be difficult to deal with.
Although the possibility of an Immortal Domain Lord taking away the Eternal Treasure was too small, it could not be ignored.
Of course, if other Immortal experts also possessed combat strength on par with Ye Tian’s, then they would naturally be qualified to participate as well. However, within the Time-Space Territory, there was only one Immortal Domain Lord like Ye Tian.
Next, Ye Tian and the five Legendary Domain Lords sealed off this area, not allowing any living beings to approach.
Ye Tian also arranged a few clone bodies in the Dark Forbidden Domain to prevent Domain Lord Dark Mist from running back and destroying the Dark Forbidden Domain.
The chance of that happening was very small, but Ye Tian still had to guard against it.
……
Ye Tian knew that if he wanted to take away the Eternal Treasure, he had to comprehend the corresponding Eternal Dao, and only after reaching Entry could he take away the Eternal Treasure. Otherwise, he would need the strength of a Legendary expert. None of the Legendary Domain Lords present had that kind of strength, so they could only obediently comprehend the Eternal Dao.
However, the five Legendary Domain Lords still tried once.
Boom！！！！
The five Legendary Domain Lords all rushed toward the location of the Eternal Treasure, but after flying for a long time, they still could not get close to it.
Afterward, they all obediently sat down and began comprehending the Eternal Dao.
Clearly, they also knew the method of obtaining the Eternal Treasure. After all, they had been Legendary Domain Lords for a very long time, and they had obtained who knew how many inheritances. Knowing this secret was not strange.
Ye Tian also immediately began comprehending the Eternal Dao contained within this Eternal Treasure. His will continuously approached the Eternal Treasure, sensing its aura in order to comprehend its Eternal Dao.
After a long time, Ye Tian roughly understood what kind of Eternal Dao this leaf-like Eternal Treasure contained.
“All-Life…”
“Division!”
These were two kinds of Eternal Dao.
The All-Life Eternal Dao could heal injuries, cure the injuries of all things, or restore the vitality of all things. As for Division, it could divide into one fake clone after another. It was similar to Domain Lord Dark Mist’s Dark Mist Shadow Body; in fact, the effect was about the same. If the Division Eternal Dao were cultivated to the Extreme, it could permanently form a true clone.
For many Immortal experts, if they wanted to add another clone, they could cultivate the Division Eternal Dao.
However, comprehending a true Eternal Dao was difficult to begin with, and cultivating it to the Extreme was naturally even harder.
“I’ll still cultivate the All-Life Eternal Dao. If I can cultivate it to Entry, I can take away this Eternal Treasure!”
Ye Tian decided.
Ye Tian’s realm was only Immortal, and compared with the Legendary Domain Lords, his realm was much lower. But he possessed many Legendary Innate Abilities and Immortal Innate Abilities, so he had a huge advantage in comprehending the Eternal Dao.
Moreover, Ye Tian could also use clone bodies to study the All-Life Eternal Dao, using his own body as an experiment, which would further increase his comprehension speed.
Therefore, Ye Tian was fully confident that he would be the first to comprehend the All-Life Eternal Dao to Entry and thus take away this Eternal Treasure.
As for the five Legendary Domain Lords, they were destined to end up with nothing.
Time slowly passed, and before anyone knew it, ten thousand Eternal Years had passed in the Source Sea.
Ye Tian and the five Legendary Domain Lords had been in closed-door cultivation here for such a long time.
The five Legendary Domain Lords appeared very patient. Perhaps to them, a mere ten thousand Eternal Years was only a very short time. They remained calm and unhurried, seemingly not believing that anyone could cultivate the Eternal Dao contained within the Eternal Treasure to Entry in such a short time.
What they did not know was that Ye Tian had really cultivated the All-Life Eternal Dao to Entry.
“All-Life Eternal Dao, Entry!”
Ye Tian opened his eyes, his gaze filled entirely with joy.
Immediately after, he walked toward that Eternal Treasure. As the power of the All-Life Eternal Dao unfolded, the endless space-time was pushed aside by Ye Tian.
Very soon, Ye Tian reached the front of that Eternal Treasure and took it in one hand.
Buzz！！！！
The leaf-like Eternal Treasure landed before Ye Tian and truly became an object in his hand.
“It really is an Eternal Treasure!”
Before, there had been a ninety-nine percent chance, but now that it was in his hand, it was one hundred percent certain.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh！！！！
One clone body after another of Ye Tian descended here; this was to ensure the safety of the Eternal Treasure.
After all, facing an Eternal Treasure, he was not afraid that the other five Legendary Domain Lords would be unable to resist making a move.
If they really did make a move and he could not contend with them, then he would have no choice but to take his family and flee the Source Sea.
At that time, the five Legendary Domain Lords would have to face Ye Tian’s retaliation in the future.
“W-what, the Invincible Domain Lord took away the Eternal Treasure?”
“How could he comprehend the All-Life Eternal Dao and one of the Division Eternal Dao so quickly to Entry? We are Legendary Domain Lords, and we have not even cultivated them to Entry at all?”
“Unless he had already come into contact with the All-Life Eternal Dao and Division Eternal Dao before, which is why he entered so quickly this time!”
At this moment, the five Legendary Domain Lords all looked unconcerned on the surface, yet they were itching to rush up and seize it immediately.
But they hesitated. If they robbed him, they would completely offend Ye Tian, and they might not even be able to seize it successfully.
If Ye Tian escaped, then when Ye Tian broke through to Legendary Domain Lord and returned to the Time-Space Territory, that would be their doom.
After all, Ye Tian was too monstrous; they had to admit that in terms of talent and aptitude, they were far inferior to Ye Tian.
So they did not dare take the risk.
“Congratulations, Invincible Domain Lord!”
Domain Lord Bing Han was the first to offer congratulations. Her words indicated her stance; it was tantamount to completely giving up on seizing the Eternal Treasure and acknowledging that this Eternal Treasure belonged to Ye Tian.
Afterward, the other Legendary Domain Lords also congratulated Ye Tian one after another and did not make a move. After all, the price of making a move was too great. They were Legendary Domain Lords, the true rulers of the Time-Space Territory, eternal and indestructible existences; there was no need to take such a risk.
Now that the first Eternal Treasure had been born in the Source Sea, perhaps a second or even a third Eternal Treasure would be born in the future, and perhaps it would land in their hands.
Thus, they suppressed the urge to act.
“Thank you, everyone!”
Ye Tian said gratefully, and at the same time, he also let out a sigh of relief. After all, as long as they did not break ties, the Dark Forbidden Domain and his family would be safe. Otherwise, he could not guarantee their safety.
