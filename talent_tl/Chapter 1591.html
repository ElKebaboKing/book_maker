Chapter 1590: Eternal Body Classification!

"Greetings, Ancestor!"
Ye Tian assumed a respectful manner.
After all, he was currently using the identity of the Ancient Star Race, so he naturally had to be respectful toward this mysterious existence. Otherwise, how would he obtain any benefits?
"You are very good!"
The mysterious consciousness spoke.
"Thank you for the praise, Ancestor!" Ye Tian said.
At this moment, the mysterious consciousness began to introduce itself: "My name is Xing. Other Supreme Beings call me Star Ancestor. I am the oldest life form from the beginning of the Source Sea, and my constitution is incomparably powerful. Although I am at the Ninth-tier Supreme Being realm, my combat power far exceeds other Ninth-tier Supreme Beings. Only those who have taken a half-step beyond the Supreme Being realm are qualified to compete with me. Unfortunately, I encountered a great enemy. That enemy was not only a half-step beyond the Supreme Being realm but had also walked that path for a long time, being only one step away from crossing the Supreme Being realm and stepping into an entirely new realm. In truth, even that great enemy could not kill me because my body was too powerful and my recovery ability too strong, making me nearly immortal. Thus, he sealed me."
As Ye Tian listened, he was inwardly shocked.
This ancient existence named Star Ancestor was actually this powerful, and his life-preserving abilities were so formidable. Setting aside combat power, his survival capability was unknown how many times greater than that of the Void Source Ancestor. After all, the Void Source Ancestor had fallen and died, while Xing was still alive.
But in terms of actual combat power, the Star Ancestor should be roughly on par with the Void Source Ancestor.
Supreme Beings were divided into nine tiers, with the ninth being the highest. According to his understanding, the Void Source Ancestor had stepped beyond the Ninth-tier Supreme Being realm, taking a half-step beyond the Supreme Being realm, thus possessing immense strength. However, the Star Ancestor's body was exceptionally powerful.
Therefore, the combat power of both sides should be about equal.
Star Ancestor said a lot, but it was all about how powerful he was and his hatred for his enemy.
"Ancestor, how can I rescue you?" Ye Tian spoke these words. If he didn't say them, he wouldn't be valued by Star Ancestor and might even be suspected.
Seeing Ye Tian so eager to rescue him, Star Ancestor was very gratified and said: "To rescue me, the minimum requirement is to have the Seventh-tier Supreme Being realm. Every tier of improvement for a Supreme Being is incomparably difficult. The ten Supreme Beings of the Ancient Star Race are only at the Third-tier Supreme Being realm, and their ultimate potential is at most the Fourth-tier Supreme Being realm, so they cannot save me. But you are different. You possess an Eternal Ancestral Talent level Ancient Star Body; in the future, there is hope for you to reach the Seventh-tier Supreme Being realm."
Ye Tian asked: "Ancestor, can a single talent determine the future?"
"Of course it can't!" Star Ancestor said with a smile: "But the Ancient Star Body is not simple. Originally, I did not have the Ancient Star Body, but there were many good things at the beginning of the Source Sea. I obtained a treasure and thus created the Ancient Star Body, eventually creating the Ancient Star Source Physique. There are many types of Source Physiques in the Source Sea, and the Source Physiques of each faction are different. However, Source Physiques are generally divided into Ordinary Source Physiques, Top-tier Source Physiques, Divine Source Physiques, and Eternal Bodies! And the Ancient Star Source Physique can be ranked as an Eternal Body, specifically a High-rank Eternal Body!"
Ye Tian listened. An Ordinary Source Physique was like the Invincible Tribulation Physique formed by the Invincible Tribulation Physique techniques of the Heavenly Wind Sea. A Top-tier Source Physique was equivalent to the Myriad Source Return to Ruin. A Divine Source Physique was equivalent to the Void Source Physique achieved by an ordinary Son of the Void. Meanwhile, a Perfect Void Source Physique or even Ye Tian's Endless Source Physique belonged to the Eternal Body category. The Void Source Ancestor's Void Source Physique should be a Low-rank Eternal Body, while Ye Tian's Endless Source Physique should belong to the Mid-rank Eternal Body.
Since the Ancient Star Source Physique was a High-rank Eternal Body, if he could elevate a High-rank Eternal Body like the Ancient Star Source Physique to the level of an Eternal Tribulation Physique, how powerful would that be?
"Ancestor, is it possible to cultivate two Eternal Bodies at the same time?"
Ye Tian inquired.
He did not want to give up the Endless Source Physique he had created. Moreover, the Endless Source Physique was the Eternal Body most suitable for him. If he abandoned it, it would be a great pity.
Therefore, if he could cultivate two Eternal Bodies simultaneously, he wouldn't have to worry.
"Yes!" Star Ancestor gave a definitive answer. "It is naturally possible to cultivate two Eternal Bodies, but the difficulty is immense. First, you must split off a clone and let the clone cultivate the other Eternal Body. Once the clone has mastered the other Eternal Body, you can attempt to fuse the clone and the main body into one. However, the clone itself will be influenced by the main body, making it inherently difficult to master another Eternal Body. The even greater difficulty is the fusion. If the fusion of two Eternal Bodies fails, both the main body and the clone will suffer damage—the kind that harms one's foundation. In the Source Sea, there are treasures that can help fuse two Eternal Bodies, such as the Void Holy Crystal. This treasure can assist in the fusion of two Eternal Bodies and increase the success rate by half, but even I have not managed to obtain such a treasure."
Void Holy Crystal!
Ye Tian didn't think he could obtain such a treasure either, but that didn't mean he had no way to succeed.
He could let this duplicated body cultivate the Ancient Star Source Physique. Furthermore, he had an Invincible Progenitor clone, and along with his main body, he had a total of three bodies.
To ensure the foundation of his main body remained undamaged, he could let the Invincible Progenitor clone and the duplicated body undergo fusion time and time again. Even if they failed, the price paid wouldn't be too high.
Once he thoroughly mastered the knack for fusion and the success rate reached nearly one hundred percent, he could then fuse the duplicated body with his main body. This would allow his main body to possess a brand-new, powerful Eternal Body resulting from the fusion of two Eternal Bodies.
In that case, his Eternal Body would likely surpass ordinary High-rank Eternal Bodies and reach the level of the most Peak Eternal Bodies.
He did not reveal this secret to Star Ancestor, nor did he ask further about the fusion of Eternal Bodies, as asking too much could easily arouse suspicion.
Subsequently, Star Ancestor began to guide Ye Tian's cultivation and passed on the true Ancient Star Source Physique technique to him.
The true Ancient Star Source Physique technique required an Eternal Ancestral Talent level Ancient Star Body talent to condense the true Ancient Star Source Physique. The other experts of the Ancient Star Race only condensed a fragmented and weakened version of the Ancient Star Tribulation Physique, though it still surpassed the Top-tier Tribulation Physique techniques of the outside world.
Star Ancestor had no idea that Ye Tian was an outsider. Instead, he treated Ye Tian as his hope for escape, helping him wholeheartedly by explaining and guiding the Ancient Star Source Physique technique repeatedly, even having the Ancient Star Race provide a massive amount of resources to help Ye Tian grow.
Unknowingly, dozens of Great Tribulation Eras passed.
With the help of Star Ancestor and the Ancient Star Race, Ye Tian's Ancient Star Race duplicated body had already reached the First-class Invincible realm. The Ancient Star Source Physique had also completed its second growth. With the eternal resources provided by the Ancient Star Race, the Ancient Star Source Physique had also completed its promotion, becoming the Ancient Star Eternal Tribulation Physique.
Coupled with the fact that Ye Tian had also created a Supreme Technique compatible with the Ancient Star Race, he could now absorb the origin of the Ancient Star Stone at any time and use it to absorb the origins of other Eternal Source Materials, thereby charging toward the Supreme Being realm.
Indeed, the Ancient Star Stone origin was like the Void Source Monument, allowing Ye Tian to absorb the origins of other Eternal Source Materials simultaneously. This was also where the power of the Ancient Star Stone lay.
On this day.
Ye Tian prepared to charge toward the Supreme Being realm. Once this duplicated body reached the Supreme Being realm, he would choose to Annihilation his clone in the Dark Forbidden Domain and transform the Ancient Star Race duplicated body into a clone to permanently guard the Starry Ancient Domain, and even completely occupy the Starry Ancient Domain in the future.
As for Star Ancestor, he wouldn't actually go and help him escape.
With the resources provided by the Ancient Star Race, he would not lack eternal resources in the future, and he would have enough resources to perform the fusion of two Eternal Bodies over and over again.
