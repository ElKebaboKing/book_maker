Chapter 1498: About to Break Through

Dark Forbidden Domain, Haitian Forbidden Land.
Ye Tian's clone passed through the Gate from the Haitian Sacred Sect and arrived here.
Immediately, Ye Tian's main body shared memories with the clone. After a brief fusion, the main body and the clone completely shared their memories.
At this moment, Ye Tian's main body naturally obtained the various top-tier Ancestor Laws and Endless Ancestor Laws created by the clone in the Sacred Realm.
"It's time to go to the Haitian Forbidden Land and bring back those resources from the clone!"
Ye Tian tore through the void in one step, arriving near the Haitian Forbidden Land, and then stepped across.
At the same time, Ye Tian's clone was also nearby, and the main body made contact with the clone.
Ye Tian's clone immediately handed those resources to the main body and then began to fake a battle.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian's clone was sent flying by a punch. Then, other exotic beasts rushed over, wanting to besiege Ye Tian, but Ye Tian easily departed.
"Damn Ancestor, invading our base again!"
One exotic beast roared.
Ye Tian's main body would invade every once in a while. In reality, he didn't do much; it was just to prepare for this moment.
If he never invaded the Haitian Forbidden Land and the clone suddenly appeared during an invasion, it would be too suspicious. If it aroused the suspicion of those two Supreme Sacred Beasts, it would be bad.
Therefore, Ye Tian occasionally invaded the Haitian Forbidden Land.
This way, the clone He Yuan would not be suspected.
In the following days, Ye Tian's clone, the sacred beast He Yuan, stayed in the Haitian Forbidden Land for a period of time before leaving and returning to the Sacred Realm to work on obtaining the Eternal Construction Method for the Gate.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian's main body also had things to do, which was to practice the Endless Ancestor Laws created in the Sacred Realm and charge toward the Supreme Ancestor realm.
Once he reached the Supreme Ancestor realm, his combat power would far exceed his current level. At that time, he wouldn't have to worry about anything, nor would he need to fear the future revenge of the Bone Race.
However, when Ye Tian practiced the Endless Ancestor Laws of the Sacred Realm, he found it very difficult.
After all, these were Endless Ancestor Laws from the Sacred Realm, which were incompatible with the rules of the Dark Forbidden Domain. Thus, they were hard to practice. If not for the countless resources from the Sacred Realm, he wouldn't even be able to merge the Endless Ancestor Laws.
In the blink of an eye, more than a thousand domain years passed.
On this day, Ye Tian finally merged the Endless Ancestor Laws from the Sacred Realm. In this specific Ancestor Law, he reached the combat power of an elite Ancestor, not far from a top-tier Ancestor.
Subsequently, Ye Tian began to refine the resources from the Sacred Realm. This was the accumulation of the clone He Yuan's endless years of effort, and these resources were very effective for Ye Tian, many of them surpassing the Bone Beads.
Gradually, Ye Tian's second Endless Ancestor Law also rose to top-tier Ancestor combat power and continued to improve.
One domain era passed, and Ye Tian's Sacred Realm Endless Law was practiced to the limit, his cultivation reaching a point where no further progress could be made.
Two types of Endless Ancestor Laws allowed Ye Tian's strength to reach an incredibly terrifying level.
Now, he had touched the invisible shackles. Once he merged the two Endless Ancestor Laws, he could break the shackles, and his two Ancestor bodies would merge into one, transforming into a Supreme Ancestor Body.
This step also took time and was not easily accomplished.
According to what he knew, one of the Supreme Sacred Beasts of the Haitian Sacred Sect spent a full Great Tribulation's time to finally complete the breakthrough.
Strictly speaking, that Supreme Sacred Beast of the Haitian Sacred Sect entered the Dark Forbidden Domain during the first Great Tribulation. Until the second Great Tribulation, he still hadn't completed the breakthrough and entered the Dark Forbidden Domain again. In fact, he had already created the second Sacred Law by then, but it was difficult to merge.
After the second Great Tribulation passed, it took a full six Dark Eras of time before he broke through to the Supreme Sacred Beast realm.
From this, it could be seen that merging two Endless Ancestor Laws was a very difficult task and could not be completed all at once.
If it were that easy to break through, experts at the level of Supreme Ancestor, Bone Emperor, and Supreme Sacred Beast would not be so rare.
After all, during each Great Tribulation, there were many experts from other domains, but among so many experts, very few could truly break through.
Currently, the Dark Forbidden Domain had passed through three Dark Eras and was now in the period between the third and fourth Dark Eras.
Ye Tian's requirements for himself were not high; he only needed to complete the breakthrough before the next Great Tribulation arrived.
Of course, he didn't believe his talent would be so poor as to require that much time. At most, within one Dark Era, he would be able to complete the breakthrough.
