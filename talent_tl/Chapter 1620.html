Chapter 1619: This Is Not Black Mist, It Is Black Mist Insects!

Although the body of that Eighth-Tier Supreme Being was crushed by Ye Tian with a single finger, he was not killed instantly.
After all, this Eighth-Tier Supreme Being was very powerful. Even a top Ninth-Tier Supreme Being could not kill a Peak Eighth-Tier Supreme Being with one strike, but this Eighth-Tier Supreme Being was still severely injured.
“Shattering Void Sword!”
Ye Tian immediately unleashed another Eternal technique. Although he did not activate the power of the Eternal Stage finger, his ordinary combat strength had also reached the level of a top Eighth-Tier Supreme Being.
Coupled with the fact that this Eighth-Tier Supreme Being had not much combat strength left, he was simply no match for Ye Tian.
Very soon, this Eighth-Tier Supreme Being was killed by Ye Tian.
“This guy found quite a lot of good things!”
After Ye Tian inspected the other party’s void sphere, he could not help but say excitedly.
Killing an Eighth-Tier Supreme Being was far more satisfying than slowly searching for resources.
Of course, he would not deliberately go around looking for other Supreme Beings to kill. As long as they did not provoke him, he would not make a move either. If he developed the habit of hunting others, one day he would also be hunted by other half-step Eternal Stage experts, and by then all his gains would end up belonging to someone else.
Just like this Eighth-Tier Supreme Being—he had thought he could hunt others, but in the end he died at Ye Tian’s hands.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian became even more cautious.
After all, this time he had only encountered an Eighth-Tier Supreme Being. If he encountered a half-step Eternal Stage expert, he would not be that person’s opponent.
Thus, Ye Tian kept his copying talent activated at all times.
In a place like the Primordial Sea, the copying talent was more useful than perception. Once he detected a half-step Eternal Stage expert approaching, he would flee at the first possible moment.
Only this way could he ensure his own safety.
During the following period, whenever Ye Tian detected a half-step Eternal Stage expert, he would leave at the fastest speed possible to avoid meeting them, and thus encountered no danger.
Some Supreme Beings, after obtaining certain benefits, resisted the temptation and immediately chose to leave the Primordial Sea, fearing that if they fell here, they would be left with nothing.
However, most Supreme Beings could not control this temptation. They all wanted to obtain more treasures and were unwilling to leave even if it was very dangerous.
As time passed, one Supreme Being after another fell in the Primordial Sea. Most of the fallen Supreme Beings died at the hands of other powerful experts, and only a small number accidentally touched the black mist, thereby perishing within it.
And on this day, Ye Tian finally had the bad luck to encounter the black mist as well.
……
“This is Yuanqing Eternal Stone. It’s actually such a huge piece!”
Ye Tian passed by a small island and saw Yuanqing Eternal Stone, and was immediately startled.
Yuanqing Eternal Stone was an extremely valuable resource. It could be refined into Yuanqing Eternal Liquid, which could help Eternal Bodies grow, and was one of the top auxiliary cultivation resources for Eternal Bodies.
Ye Tian flew down, preparing to take the Yuanqing Eternal Stone away.
But he discovered that the Yuanqing Eternal Stone was connected to the island and had to be severed before it could be removed.
So he struck at the base of the Yuanqing Eternal Stone, directly separating it from the ground.
Boom!!!!!!
The Yuanqing Eternal Stone was severed and detached from the island.
But the next second, he was stunned.
From the place where the Yuanqing Eternal Stone connected to the ground, there was actually a small space, and at this moment countless black mist were surging out from within that small space.
The speed of this black mist was too fast, even slightly faster than Ye Tian.
“Retreat!”
Ye Tian wanted to escape, but one of his arms was corroded by the black mist.
Just as his body was about to be corroded by the black mist, the Eternal Stage finger fused into his finger suddenly sensed danger, because the black mist also wanted to corrode the finger of the Eternal Stage mighty figure. It had touched the seal on the finger, directly triggering the power of the Eternal Stage.
Boom!!!!!!
The black mist was blasted apart, and although Ye Tian was also injured by the backlash from the Eternal Stage finger’s power, he at least saved his life and promptly withdrew from this area.
The black mist could not spread infinitely; it could only spread across this island, so Ye Tian no longer needed to worry.
He stood outside the island, looking at that mass of black mist, feeling extremely shocked in his heart.
“Good thing it was me. If it had been any other Supreme Being, they’d probably be dead for sure. This corrosion speed is too fast!”
Ye Tian muttered.
Suddenly.
His expression changed as his gaze toward the black mist became suspicious.
“This…”
Ye Tian discovered that this was not true black mist at all, but black mist formed from countless Black Mist Insects.
The so-called corrosion was simply the body being invaded by Black Mist Insects and devoured by them.
Each Black Mist Insect was very tiny and could even shield perception and divine sense probing. When a Supreme Being’s divine sense entered this black mist, it would be devoured, and thus even a half-step Eternal Stage expert might not necessarily know what the black mist was, only feeling that it was incomparably terrifying........
But Ye Tian’s copying talent had detected the talents of these black mist. Endless talents covered his data panel, all of them were the talents of Black Mist Insects.
Type: Black Mist Insect
Strength: Eternal Ancestor Talent
Speed: Eternal Ancestor Talent
Defense: Eternal Ancestor Talent!
Devouring: Eternal Ancestor Talent
Undying: Eternal Ancestor Talent!
……
The talents of these Black Mist Insects were all very luxurious, but the key was that their numbers were too terrifying.
Suddenly, Ye Tian burst out laughing.
“So many Eternal Ancestor Talents. If I copy them one by one, even if it takes a long time, once countless Eternal Ancestor Talents are fused together, could they surpass Eternal Ancestor Talent?”
Thinking of this, Ye Tian became excited.
Looking for resources?
No, he no longer cared about that.
For him, the talents of these Black Mist Insects were the greatest opportunity.
Copy!
Copy!
Copy!
Copy!
……
In order to speed up the copying, each time he copied an Eternal Ancestor Talent, he used time acceleration to pass through the time required for copying.
Just like that, Ye Tian did not spend too long before copying all the talents contained in these Black Mist Insects.
However.
Fusing them would take far too long.
Therefore, Ye Tian did not fuse these talents; he would wait until after leaving to fuse them slowly.
He now had an idea: if he self-annihilated the clone outside, then he could once again expend a body to serve as a clone.
Thus, he prepared to copy the body of a Black Mist Insect.
In this way, even after the source tide passed, he could let the Black Mist Insect clone remain in the Primordial Sea.
Outside.
Ye Tian’s clone self-annihilated.
After that, the copied body located in the Primordial Sea could use the copying ability to begin copying a Black Mist Insect.
Whoosh!
Copy successful.
A Black Mist Insect appeared in Ye Tian’s hand, its body so tiny that it was tens of thousands of times smaller than an ant.
Immediately after, Ye Tian’s Black Mist Insect clone flew toward the island and mixed in with the other Black Mist Insects.
