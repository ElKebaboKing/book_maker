Chapter 1699 Legendary Eternal Technique!

After Ye Tian broke through to the Lord of the Legendary Realm, he spent the next ten Eternal years consolidating his realm, silently increasing his Eternal power and physical strength, and did not immediately attempt to break into the Initial Stage Lord of the Legendary Realm.
Very quickly, ten Eternal years passed.
Thus, Ye Tian began to break into the Initial Stage.
The gap between an ordinary Lord of the Legendary Realm and an Initial Stage Lord was not very big, but compared with the top existences among the Initial Stage, an ordinary Lord of the Legendary Realm fell far behind.
For other Lords of the Legendary Realm, how to fuse willpower with the innate Eternal law was an incomparably difficult step. For example, a Legendary Lord like Lord Bing Han had not taken this step, which was why he kept searching for all kinds of cultivation secret arts that fused willpower with the innate Eternal law, in order to seek a breakthrough point.
Among the five Legendary Lords in the Time-Space Territory at present, Ye Tian estimated that only Lord Sheng Feng had truly broken through to the Initial Stage, but that was only a suspicion, because Lord Sheng Feng hid himself very deeply. If he did not reveal his true strength, then with Ye Tian’s current cultivation realm, it would still be difficult to see through his realm at a glance.
Ye Tian began to merge his willpower, body, and innate Eternal law, and this process did not encounter any bottleneck at all. Very quickly, the three aspects began to fuse, producing a power of a higher level.
In the Time-Space Territory and the Source Sea, there was no description or definition of this power, but in the Boundless Sea, the power formed after the fusion of body, willpower, and innate Eternal law was called Eternal Original Power, a higher-level version of Eternal power.
With the birth of Eternal Original Power, Ye Tian’s body underwent a slight change, but because the degree of fusion was not high, this transformation was not very obvious.
This degree of fusion, like the Lord level of the Immortal Realm, was also divided into ten parts. Once the fusion reached ten parts, it meant a Perfected Stage Legendary Lord, while from zero to 99 percent all belonged to an Initial Stage Legendary Lord.
Since Ye Tian himself already had a considerable degree of fusion between his willpower and innate Eternal law, cultivation was very easy at the beginning of the Initial Stage Legendary Lord realm, with no bottleneck or obstacle at all.
In less than one Eternal year, Ye Tian’s cultivation had reached the third step of the Initial Stage Legendary Lord, which meant the fusion degree of body, willpower, and innate Eternal law had reached thirty percent, and thirty percent Eternal Original Power had been born within his body.
Such strength was naturally much stronger than that of a newly broken-through Initial Stage Legendary Lord.
Moreover, Ye Tian was also trying to create a Legendary Eternal Technique, and he already had a rudimentary form of it; it was not far from truly creating it.
This day.
Ye Tian came to the Black Mountain Prison, prepared to challenge those Legendary Realm Lord prisoners in the Black Mountain Prison.
Not only could he fight these Legendary Realm Lord prisoners to increase his combat experience, but he also had a chance to gain benefits and opportunities from them, so sending a clone body here to fight for a long time was not a problem.
Thus, Ye Tian entered one of the prisons holding a Legendary Realm Lord prisoner.
This Legendary Realm Lord was called Lord Shu Hai. Ye Tian entered Lord Shu Hai’s prison and saw this Lord Shu Hai.
“Initial Stage Legendary Lord!” Ye Tian saw through the other’s cultivation realm.
To be more specific, this Lord Shu Hai’s cultivation was at the fifth step of the Initial Stage Legendary Lord, with fifty percent Eternal Original Power contained within his body, which was even higher than Ye Tian’s cultivation.
However, a higher cultivation did not mean greater combat strength.
For example, Ye Tian had a very solid foundation, had created many top-tier Legendary Eternal Techniques, and was even about to create a Legendary-grade Eternal Technique. If he borrowed the Hunyuan Token, he could even match a Perfected Stage Legendary Lord.
However, Ye Tian did not activate either the Hunyuan Token or the Jialan Leaf, these two Eternal supreme treasures, but instead relied on his own strength to battle the other party.
Fighting Lord Shu Hai, Ye Tian discovered that the other party’s use of Eternal Original Power had reached a very exquisite level, worthy of his study.
In addition, Lord Shu Hai also possessed several top-tier Legendary Eternal Techniques and was skilled in many kinds of Eternal Paths.
Without the Hunyuan Token and the Jialan Leaf, Ye Tian could only barely contend with the other party a little; it was unexpectedly somewhat difficult to completely defeat him.
Ye Tian also knew that the Legendary Lords imprisoned here for endless ages were all far from simple, so he did not find this strange at all.
Just like that, over the long years that followed, Ye Tian’s clone body sought out one Legendary Realm Lord prisoner after another, using them to temper his combat strength and his adaptation to the Lord level of the Legendary Realm, and his use of Eternal Original Power became even more skillful.
After several Eternal years, Ye Tian’s cultivation did not increase much, but his combat strength was already enough to suppress Lord Shu Hai.
Before he knew it, tens of thousands of Eternal years passed.
After enduring such a long period of bitter cultivation, Ye Tian’s cultivation greatly increased.
Now, he had already reached the seventh step of the Initial Stage Lord, with seven percent Eternal Original Power contained throughout his body, and his physical toughness had also far surpassed the past. ..........
In addition.
He had also created a Legendary-grade Eternal Technique, and not just one, but basically they had all been perfected based on his previous top-tier Legendary Eternal Techniques.
For example, Void Step, Ten-Thousandfold Void, Void True Body, Will Wall Light, and Will Severing Void Sword had all become Legendary-grade Eternal Techniques.
If he borrowed the Hunyuan Token, these Legendary-grade Eternal Techniques could all obtain a certain boost, and by using the Hunyuan Token he could also form an willpower law domain similar to that of a Domain Ancestor Legendary Lord, making his strength naturally even more powerful.
With Ye Tian’s current strength, he could easily suppress any Initial Stage Legendary Lord.
“My regular combat strength should now be enough to match a Perfected Stage Legendary Lord. If I borrow the Hunyuan Token and the Jialan Leaf, these two Eternal supreme treasures, I even have a chance to contend with a Domain Ancestor Legendary Lord a little.” Ye Tian said confidently.
Even in the Boundless Sea, Domain Ancestor Legendary Lords were not many, and were definitely counted as overlords of a region.
-
Somewhere in the Void Region.
A group of people was rapidly traversing here, and one of them was none other than the Dark Mist Lord whom Ye Tian knew.
At this moment, the Dark Mist Lord was respectfully communicating with a Legendary Realm Lord.
“Young Master Qin Yu, in not much time, we will be able to reach the Time-Space Territory of the Source Sea.” Dark Mist Lord said.
The Legendary Realm Lord known as Young Master Qin Yu said coldly, “Dark Mist Lord, you said your Source Sea is very weak, with only six Legendary Lords and one or even two Eternal supreme treasures. If this information is wrong and has delayed me for so long, I will not spare you.”
“Young Master Qin Yu, I would not dare to deceive you. Everything I said is true. I only hope that Young Master Qin Yu can help me kill that Nine-Step Immortal Realm Lord.” Dark Mist Lord hurriedly said.
“Rest assured, it’s merely an Immortal Realm Lord. I can kill him with a wave of my hand!” Young Master Qin Yu said with full confidence.
Dark Mist Lord knew that Qin Yu was not bragging, but truly had that strength.
Qin Yu was a peak Initial Stage Legendary Lord from the Boundless Sea, only one step away from the Perfected Stage, and he also possessed an Eternal supreme treasure. His own strength was even greater than that of an ordinary Perfected Stage.
By accident, Dark Mist Lord found his way to the Boundless Sea, and was seized by Qin Yu. Only then did he exchange the information about the Source Sea and the Eternal supreme treasure for his life, and now he had completely thrown in his lot with Qin Yu.
“Limitless Lord, once I descend upon the Source Sea, you are dead for sure!”
Dark Mist Lord thought darkly.
