Chapter 1579: Lord of the Void, Taking Charge of the Void Source Palace!

"This time, I should be able to succeed, right?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He had once again arrived at the third floor of the Void Tower, where seemingly endless and powerful Tribulation Beasts swarmed toward him.
Each of these Tribulation Beasts possessed strength comparable to a top-tier second-class Invincible, and they even knew how to coordinate with one another. If he made even a single mistake, Ye Tian would suffer defeat.
Ye Tian unleashed Invincible techniques with every move, occasionally performing a Supreme technique.
After all, Supreme techniques imposed a heavy burden on his consumption; he could not use them continuously. Otherwise, if his power were exhausted, he would also be defeated. Thus, Supreme techniques could only be unleashed at critical moments.
Boom!!!!
One Tribulation Beast after another was killed and subjected to Annihilation by Ye Tian in an instant, but even more Tribulation Beasts surged forward.
This was a bitter struggle.
During his previous attempts to clear the third floor of the Void Tower, he had failed to persevere, but this time, there was at least hope.
He did not know how much time had passed; Ye Tian had forgotten the existence of time. He only remembered one thing, and that was to kill!
He fell into a state of frenzied slaughter, slaying more Tribulation Beasts at the lowest possible cost.
The originally seemingly endless number of Tribulation Beasts rapidly thinned out until one day, only a few Tribulation Beasts remained, and Ye Tian’s appearance was also incredibly wretched.
At this moment, his power was nearly exhausted, more than half of his body was gone, and he had no surplus power to repair his injuries.
Furthermore, his soul strength was also nearly depleted.
It could be said that for an ordinary person, it would be very difficult to clear the floor in such a state.
But Ye Tian was very happy; only a few dozen Tribulation Beasts remained, and these beasts were also injured.
Although he had little power left, he was confident in clearing it.
When these Tribulation Beasts charged over, Ye Tian activated the power of his Space Eternal Talent.
The consumption of talent power was very low, far less than that of an Invincible technique, but the effect was quite good.
However, talent power could not be used indefinitely either. He had not used his talent power before, and now it represented a glimmer of hope.
One by one, the Tribulation Beasts were heavily injured by Ye Tian’s Space Eternal Talent, halting this wave of their offensive.
Immediately after, Ye Tian activated various Eternal Talents, finally blasting and killing all of these Tribulation Beasts before his power was completely exhausted.
At this point, Ye Tian had cleared the third floor of the Void Tower.
"Void Divine Son Ye Tian has cleared the third floor of the Void Tower and officially becomes the Lord of the Void!"
The Spirit Tool congratulated.
At the same time, a surge of power poured into Ye Tian’s body, quickly repairing his injuries.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian appeared in a source void within the Void Source Palace and saw a figure of light and shadow; this figure was the Spirit Tool.
"Greetings, Master!"
The Spirit Tool spoke.
"Master?" Ye Tian was stunned for a moment, then said: "By becoming the Lord of the Void, can I become the master of the Void Source Palace?"
He had learned from Jin Zu's memories that by becoming the Lord of the Void, one could obtain the inheritance of the Void Source Ancestor, but he did not know that one could also become the master of the Void Source Palace.
"Yes!" the Spirit Tool said. "Once you clear the third floor of the Void Tower, you can become the master of the Void Source Palace and also obtain some of the previous master's legacy, which includes the Void Source Tablet!"
"I want to refine and take control of the Void Source Palace first!"
Ye Tian requested.
This was the most urgent matter; other things could be put aside for later.
Only by controlling the Void Source Palace would this place truly belong to him.
"Yes, Master!" The Spirit Tool handed the core of the Void Source Palace to Ye Tian, allowing him to refine it slowly.
The Void Source Palace was no ordinary place, so refining it would require a long time.
Ye Tian continued to refine the core of the Void Source Palace for a total of six Great Tribulation Eras. Had he reached the Supreme Being realm, he likely would have succeeded in refining it very quickly.
Having refined the Void Source Palace, Ye Tian could truly be considered its master. At the same time, he also realized the extraordinary nature of the Void Source Palace.
The Void Source Palace was actually a Supreme Source Tool refined by the Void Source Ancestor using dozens of powerful Eternal Source Items plus countless rare materials, and the Spirit Tool could control the Void Source Palace to exert a certain level of offensive power.
Furthermore, the true body of the Void Source Palace was not in the Source Sea but within a Forbidden Land. The Void Source Palace in the Source Sea was actually just a special projection; otherwise, it would have been taken away by those powerful Supreme Beings long ago.
Precisely because the Void Source Palace was located in a Forbidden Land, and the Spirit Tool could still exert a portion of its power, the Void Source Palace had remained in an ownerless state...
Otherwise, there were Supreme Beings back then who were comparable to the Void Source Ancestor, and even they had no way to take the Void Source Palace away.
In other words, although Ye Tian had refined the Void Source Palace, he also had no way to take it away unless he could emerge from that Forbidden Land. However, his current strength was insufficient, so he could not do it.
Subsequently, Ye Tian went to the Inheritance Hall of the Void Source Ancestor, preparing to accept the inheritance.
At Ye Tian's level, his path was already fixed. Therefore, the inheritance of the Void Source Ancestor could at most serve as a reference. Additionally, there were some special secret techniques, knowledge, and so on, which were all highly valuable things.
Spending only a few million years, Ye Tian completely integrated the inheritance of the Void Source Ancestor.
This inheritance from the Void Source Ancestor was still helpful to Ye Tian. For example, he now knew the realm divisions of a Supreme Being and how to take every step so as not to go down the wrong path.
Moreover, he also learned how to refine the source of the Void Source Tablet, thereby using the source of the Void Source Tablet to absorb other sources to charge toward the Supreme Being realm.
"Master, you have now taken control of the Void Source Palace. Shall I expel the other Void Sons and Void Divine Sons?"
The Spirit Tool inquired.
"No need!" Ye Tian shook his head. "I am in no hurry to use the Void Source Tablet to charge toward the Supreme Being realm right now. There is no need to reveal that the Void Source Palace has a master. Furthermore, if those Void Sons want to obtain more points, they must complete the tasks of the Void Source Palace, which is also accumulating various resources for me. Why expel them?"
"Yes, Master!"
The Spirit Tool understood.
Then, Ye Tian asked: "How many Eternal Source Items and other resources does the Void Source Palace currently possess?"
The Spirit Tool immediately replied: "The Void Source Palace currently possesses a total of 276 Eternal Source Items, 136,394 Void Source Crystals..."
The Spirit Tool reported the quantities of various resources. After Ye Tian finished listening, he was incredibly excited.
What a massive fortune this was; he simply could not imagine it.
He was certain that other Supreme Beings likely only possessed a fraction of his current net worth.
Having obtained the Void Source Palace, his wealth had already joined the ranks of the very top tier in the Source Sea.
