Chapter 1551: A Mysterious Mission with a Rule Heavenly Object as Reward!

Ye Tian applied to become an elite-level member of Tianfeng Sea, and high-level officials of Tianfeng Sea immediately conducted a review.
His cultivation met the standard, and his contribution points met the standard.
Thus, Ye Tian officially became an elite-level member of Tianfeng Sea. Elite members surpassed a full ninety-nine percent of Tianfeng Sea's members; this level was considered the middle layer of Tianfeng Sea.
After becoming an elite-level member of Tianfeng Sea, Ye Tian could indeed exchange for treasures like the Xumi Golden Fruit. However, upon making a slight inquiry, he learned that the Xumi Golden Fruit was currently out of stock. Furthermore, tens of thousands of Invincibles were already in line to purchase it, some even having submitted one billion contribution points.
From this, it was evident just how many Invincibles desired the Xumi Golden Fruit.
Consequently, Ye Tian abandoned the idea of obtaining the Xumi Golden Fruit through Tianfeng Sea.
On this day.
Ye Tian spent a large amount of contribution points to purchase a vast quantity of precious materials. These materials were all used for the purpose of sealing Yuhua World.
Sealing an exotic world and treating it as one's own backyard was something many powerful experts were willing to do, but the cost was too great, far beyond what an ordinary Invincible could afford. Fortunately, Ye Tian had now accumulated enough contribution points, allowing him to afford these materials to set up a Void Source Array to seal Yuhua World.
Once the Void Source Array was set up, even within the Source Sea, this exotic world would be hidden. Only by observing this exotic world from a very close distance would one sense its presence.
But the probability of such an occurrence was too low, so it would almost never be discovered.
Sealing an exotic world to serve as one's base was also relatively common among the high-level officials of Tianfeng Sea. Therefore, even if Ye Tian were discovered, no one would suspect anything. After all, an exotic world didn't have much value and couldn't produce anything good; no one would suspect that an exotic world would give birth to an Eternal Source Object.
Within a short period, Ye Tian gathered all the materials for the Void Source Array to seal Yuhua World. He took these materials and left the Tianlin Realm, transferring multiple times to ensure no one was following him before arriving at Yuhua World.
...
"Let's begin the setup!"
Ye Tian melted countless materials and began refining array bases according to the layout of the Void Source Array. He integrated them into the various territories of Yuhua World, perfectly merging the 108 array bases with Yuhua World.
Spending several hundred years and going through countless setups, Ye Tian finally completed the Void Source Array.
"Activate!"
Ye Tian triggered the Void Source Array.
In the next moment, countless amounts of source power from the Source Sea poured into Yuhua World, merging into those array bases to serve as the energy for the Void Source Array. This was the brilliance of the Void Source Array—it could utilize the energy of the Source Sea.
It was precisely because of this that the Void Source Array could seal an exotic world.
Additionally, the Void Source Array could isolate and block entry; even an incredibly powerful Invincible would have no way to tear through coordinates to enter the Void Source Array.
Furthermore, as long as Ye Tian wished, he could even slowly move the exotic world to change its coordinates.
Time slowly passed, and eventually, the Void Source Array began to operate eternally. As long as the Source Sea did not dry up, the Void Source Array would not collapse, and Yuhua World would not be easily discovered.
The moment the Void Source Array was fully operational, Ye Tian breathed a sigh of relief.
"Xumi Golden Mountain will not be discovered; it completely belongs to me!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
-----------------
In the days that followed, Ye Tian entered bitter cultivation again, beginning to create various Invincible Ancestral Techniques.
He had comprehended many Eternal Rules, so creating Invincible Ancestral Techniques was not difficult. Coupled with the fact that he had consumed the Xumi Golden Fruit, his comprehension had improved significantly, allowing him to create several Invincible Ancestral Techniques in a short time.
Unknowingly, Ye Tian already possessed nine Invincible Ancestral Techniques, and his cultivation had steadily improved a great deal. Even Ten Thousand Sources Returning to the Void was gradually perfected by Ye Tian to the peak of the third level's Entry stage.
Originally, Ye Tian planned to remain in bitter cultivation until his realm reached the level of a Third-tier Invincible.
But this time, Tianfeng Sea issued a very important mission.
No one knew what the mission content was, and there was a warning that this mission was incredibly dangerous with a high probability of perishing. However, the reward offered was so generous that even Ye Tian was moved.
A Rule Heavenly Object!
That was the reward!
The value of a Rule Heavenly Object was so high that it was enough to cause Saint-name level forces to slaughter each other.
Many Invincibles had to spend tens of millions or even several tens of millions of contribution points just to comprehend Eternal Rules. With so many Invincibles seeking to comprehend them, one could imagine how high the value of a Rule Heavenly Object was.
To a certain extent, even the value of a Xumi Golden Fruit was far inferior to a Rule Heavenly Object.
However, Xumi Golden Fruits were rare, while Rule Heavenly Objects were not quite as rare.
Eternal Source Objects were of high value, but the Xumi Golden Fruit was not an Eternal Source Object; it was merely a fruit born from the Eternal Source Object, Xumi Golden Mountain. As long as there was enough time to wait, Xumi Golden Mountain could produce countless Xumi Golden Fruits.
Therefore, what was truly priceless was the Eternal Source Object, not the Rule Heavenly Object.
Moreover, for an individual, the Xumi Golden Fruit held greater value, while for an organization, the value of a Rule Heavenly Object was immense.
Many Invincibles in Tianfeng Sea possessed contribution points far exceeding one billion, yet they could not exchange them for a Xumi Golden Fruit, which was why they craved it even more.
However, by comparison, although the Rule Heavenly Object had a higher value, its attraction to them was not as great as the Xumi Golden Fruit.
But Ye Tian wanted the Rule Heavenly Object more. Once he obtained a Rule Heavenly Object, even if it was the most mediocre one, if he brought it back to the Dark Forbidden Domain, many human Supremes would have the chance to comprehend Eternal Rules upon promoting to Invincible in the future.
However, all Invincibles were very clear that for Tianfeng Sea to offer a Rule Heavenly Object as a reward, this mission was likely incredibly perilous; perhaps not even one in ten thousand Invincibles would survive.
Furthermore, this mission stipulated that experts above the level of Third-tier Invincible were not allowed to take it. Ultimately, only Fourth-tier Invincibles were permitted to accept the mission.
In other words, Ye Tian met the criteria.
"Accept the mission!" Ye Tian immediately decided.
In any case, this body was a duplicate body; even if it perished, the loss would not be great. If he happened to succeed, he could obtain a Rule Heavenly Object. With Tianfeng Sea's strength, they naturally wouldn't cheat an elite-level member like him.
After accepting the mission, Ye Tian was led into a secret space by a Third-tier Invincible.
Here, there were many other Fourth-tier Invincibles, clearly also elite-level members who had accepted this mission.
As time passed, more and more Invincibles accepted the mission.
Finally, on this day several decades later, the mission posted by Tianfeng Sea disappeared, meaning other Invincibles could no longer accept it.
