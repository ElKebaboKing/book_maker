Chapter 1533: Slaying the Calamity Beast, A Massive Harvest!

The Cloud Mist World.
Ye Tian and the other Invincible Ones descended upon this place, but the others were secretly preparing their retreat, faintly tearing at the world barrier to ensure they wouldn't lack the time to escape.
These were necessary preparations; after all, one could never be too careful when hunting a Calamity Beast.
"Ancestor of Myriad Laws, to think you are an Invincible One with the ability to create clones. As far as I know, aside from a few special Invincible Ones, others have no way of condensing a clone," remarked the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity, the Vice Mountain Lord of the Calamity Black Mountain.
"It's just a mere clone, of no great use!"
Ye Tian replied evasively, not revealing much.
The Ancestor of Dragon Calamity did not press further; their mission this time was to hunt the Calamity Beast.
The primary combatants this time were not Ye Tian, but rather the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity from the Calamity Black Mountain and the Ancestor of Ten Thousand Origin Swords from the Firmament Sword Tomb. These two Invincible Ones were much stronger than Ye Tian. Although Ye Tian could possess top-tier 5th-grade Ancestor combat power—which was indeed terrifying for someone who had just broken through to become an Invincible Ancestor—the gap between a newly ascended Invincible Ancestor and a true 5th-grade Invincible Ancestor was immense. It wouldn't be an exaggeration to call it a hundredfold difference.
Some Invincible Ones, despite not having created an Invincible Ancestral Technique and thus being unable to officially become 5th-grade Invincible Ones, could still possess 5th-grade combat power or even surpass ordinary 5th-grade Invincible Ones if their cultivation was profound enough.
The Ancestor of Dragon Calamity and the Ancestor of Ten Thousand Origin Swords both possessed combat power at the threshold of the 5th-grade, far beyond what an ordinary newly ascended Invincible One could compare to.
However, a 37-Calamity Beast also possessed combat power infinitely close to a 5th-grade Invincible One. A 6th-grade Invincible One would find it impossible to kill such a beast. After all, if the disparity in strength wasn't massive, it was very difficult for a battle between Invincible Ones to result in death.
For instance, while the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity was stronger than the 37-Calamity Beast, he still wouldn't be able to kill it on his own.
This was only possible now because the Cloud Mist World was currently undergoing a Great Calamity. Once the Great Calamity passed, they would have no way to hunt such a beast.
Furthermore, a 37-Calamity Beast was very likely to grasp a certain level of World Authority, allowing it to temporarily disperse the power of the Great Calamity. If that happened, the Invincible Ones would have to endure the suppression of the world's rules, and their strength would be greatly diminished.
At that point, under the suppression of the Cloud Mist World's rules, even the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity would be no match for the Calamity Beast.
Therefore, they needed to unite to surround and kill the 37-Calamity Beast; otherwise, they would never succeed.
"It's right here!"
Ye Tian pointed to a space-time node not far away.
The Invincible Ones from the Calamity Black Mountain and the Firmament Sword Tomb did not approach. Instead, they had Ye Tian strike the space-time node first. Only after confirming that this beast was indeed a 37-Calamity Beast would they take action.
Thus, Ye Tian struck without hesitation.
Boom!!!
The space-time node shattered, and a Calamity Beast carrying heaven-shaking rage appeared. Its massive body occupied the entire area, and its terrifying aura made even ordinary Invincible Ones tremble.
Thirty-seven calamity markings were visible on the beast's body, proving its strength.
"It truly is a 37-Calamity Beast!"
The Invincible Ones from the Calamity Black Mountain and the Firmament Sword Tomb exclaimed in pleasant surprise.
"Attack together!"
The Ancestor of Dragon Calamity shouted.
Rumble!!!!!
Ten Invincible Ones attacked simultaneously, easily overwhelming the Calamity Beast.
In a short time, the beast's body was covered in craters and wounds, with countless streams of blood flowing out. Its injuries were extremely severe.
Suddenly.
The Calamity Beast invoked the Authority of the Cloud Mist World, dispersing the power of the Great Calamity.
In an instant, several Invincible Ones were struck by the rules of the Cloud Mist World, and their strength was immediately and severely compromised.
However, they were Invincible Ones after all; their strength was merely suppressed.
Abruptly.
Ye Tian's duplicated body was suppressed by the rules, and at the same time, half of its body was blown apart by the beast's desperate strike, resulting in significant injuries.
As the duplicated body was injured, it retreated slightly, letting the clone take its place to give the duplicated body time to recover.
In this manner, one Invincible One after another was injured and retreated. Only the Ancestor of Dragon Calamity and the Ancestor of Ten Thousand Origin Swords remained completely uninjured. After all, their strength was superior to the beast's; even with the sudden suppression of the rules and the resulting drop in power, they were strong enough to ensure they weren't hurt.
Wielding World Authority was not without cost, and soon the Calamity Beast could no longer control it. Combined with its worsening injuries, its strength continued to plummet.
Finally.
The Calamity Beast was blasted to death, its massive corpse lying motionless in the void.
Now that the Calamity Beast was dead, the Invincible Ones breathed a sigh of relief, their faces lighting up with excitement.
The value of a 37-Calamity Beast's corpse was immense.
Swish, swish, swish!!!!
The beast's physical body began to disintegrate, and a dense cluster of Origin Rule Calamity Crystals flew out from its remains.
. . . . . . . . .
The number of these Origin Rule Calamity Crystals far exceeded those of a 1-Calamity Beast. Back then, a 1-Calamity Beast yielded over ten thousand crystals, but this 37-Calamity Beast yielded over a hundred thousand.
In addition to the hundred thousand plus Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, thirteen portions of Calamity Blood flew out, along with several bones emitting powerful auras.
"Let's divide the Origin Rule Calamity Crystals first!"
The Ancestor of Dragon Calamity suggested.
"Agreed!" Ye Tian concurred.
After some discussion, since Ye Tian contributed the combat power of two Invincible Ones and was the planner and discoverer of this hunt, he naturally received a larger share of the crystals, obtaining twenty thousand Origin Rule Calamity Crystals.
. . . . . . .
This was a massive harvest for Ye Tian. He had previously exhausted his supply of Origin Rule Calamity Crystals in order to break through to the Invincible level.
Originally, he had obtained over five thousand crystals, nearly six thousand in total. Combined with the crystals he had acquired from various factions, his travels to different foreign worlds, his conflicts with other powers, and the slaying of various supreme experts, he had amassed over six thousand.
However, between his main body and his clone both breaking through to the Invincible realm, those six thousand-plus crystals were just barely enough and had been completely consumed.
But now, suddenly acquiring over twenty thousand crystals, his wealth was once again substantial.
For an Invincible One, Origin Rule Calamity Crystals were of significant value, serving as a form of currency. If he went to the Eternal World in the future, he wouldn't be able to buy anything without them.
After dividing the crystals, the group began to distribute the thirteen portions of Calamity Blood.
"I want two portions of Calamity Blood! That's not asking too much, is it?"
Ye Tian spoke up.
With ten Invincible Ones dividing thirteen portions of Calamity Blood, and him counting as two Invincible Ones, asking for only two portions was definitely a modest request.
But he was well aware that the Calamity Blood of a 37-Calamity Beast was far superior to that of a 1-Calamity Beast. Its benefit to an Invincible One was immense, far exceeding that of the Origin Rule Calamity Crystals.
His current strength was still relatively low, so there was no need to fight these powerful Invincible Ones for more blood.
"That's acceptable!"
Seeing that Ye Tian only wanted two portions, the other Invincible Ones readily agreed.
