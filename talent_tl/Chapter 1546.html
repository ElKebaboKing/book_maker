Chapter 1545: Occupying the Holy Region World!

The weakest of the Calamity Beasts Ye Tian hunted were over a hundred-calamity level, making their value much higher than the sixty-calamity beasts he had hunted before.
A sixty-calamity beast was worth at least thirty thousand contribution points, while a beast of over a hundred calamities was worth at least a hundred thousand points.
The thirteen Calamity Beasts Ye Tian hunted were ultimately exchanged for 2.01 million contribution points. Since he hadn't spent much time doing so, his speed of accumulating contribution points was incredibly fast. If the other Invincibles of the Heavenly Wind Sea knew of this, they would likely be driven mad with envy.
With so many contribution points, Ye Tian not only lacked no resources, but he could also spend a long time at Nine Yang Mountain to comprehend the Nine Yang Fish.
As time passed, Ye Tian comprehended various Eternal Rules, slowly refining them as his mastery over the different Eternal Rules continued to improve.
Additionally, Ye Tian used his contribution points to purchase various treasures that could enhance his cultivation, among which top-grade Calamity Stones could also be used for that purpose.
As for the top-tier Invincible Calamity Body technique, "Ten Thousand Origins Returning to Ruins," Ye Tian's progress was not fast. This was because he frequently encountered flaws or dangers, requiring him to perfect it time and again to ensure it was perfectly compatible with himself before allowing his main body to practice it.
Consequently, his cultivation speed in this regard was much slower.
However, compared to others, Ye Tian's cultivation speed was already countless times faster.
One domain epoch after another passed. Unknowingly, Ye Tian's current seclusion had exceeded the duration of one Dark Era.
Ye Tian's contribution points had not decreased; instead, they grew more numerous. From the initial two million-plus points, they had reached over ten million points, and most of them remained unused, as he had only spent a small fraction.
In terms of strength, Ye Tian's progress was immense.
"The Ten Thousand Origins Returning to Ruins has finally reached minor success! My cultivation has also increased significantly compared to the past, equivalent to the late stage of a Fifth-tier Invincible!" Ye Tian said happily.
As for Invincible Ancestral Techniques, he could have easily created several, but he was in no hurry.
If he raised his cultivation to the point where it was difficult to improve further and brought his Invincible Calamity Body to the second stage of perfection, he could then create several Invincible Ancestral Techniques to step into the Fourth-tier Invincible realm, maximizing the benefits he would receive.
Although he hadn't continued creating Invincible Ancestral Techniques, his insights into them were not low. Many techniques were only one step away from being created, but he held back. Once his cultivation and Calamity Body reached their peak, he could immediately bring those unformed Invincible Ancestral Techniques to life.
This was his plan.
……
Dark Forbidden Zone.
The human race had already become the sole race of the Dark Forbidden Zone. Other races could no longer survive and had all perished. Furthermore, the humans occupied the vast majority of the Dark Forbidden Zone, abandoning only a few areas that were truly devoid of resources.
As time passed, the human population became too large, leading to the Dark Forbidden Zone becoming somewhat insufficient. If they forced themselves to stay within its confines, internal conflict would become inevitable.
Therefore, occupying other foreign worlds and transforming them to be suitable for human life was currently an urgent task for the human race.
In fact, any Super-Famous power would take this step; for instance, the Blood Mist Alliance had done the same in the past.
Additionally, powers like Calamity Black Mountain and the Firmament Sword Tomb also possessed more than one foreign world; they had several, all under their control.
As a member of the Heavenly Wind Sea, Ye Tian naturally knew how to transform foreign worlds, so he prepared to take action against one.
"I'll choose the Holy Region World!"
Ye Tian decided.
On this day.
The Great Calamity of the Holy Region World arrived. Ye Tian led a group of human Ancestors and a few Supreme Ancestors as they descended upon the Holy Region World.
The human race now had quite a few Supreme Ancestors. After all, Ye Tian could exchange for many treasures from the Heavenly Wind Sea, some of which helped Ancestors break through to the Supreme Ancestor level. Thus, the number of human Supreme Ancestors was constantly increasing and had already reached several dozen.
After Ye Tian descended upon the Holy Region World, he immediately set about wiping out its local powers.
In a short time, the forces of the Holy Region World were completely eradicated. Even those foreign powers could not withstand the human race; their gates were destroyed one by one, and they abandoned the Holy Region World, leaving only the Super-Famous power, the Blood Mist Alliance.
------------------------
Blood Mist World.
The high-level members of the Blood Mist Alliance were holding a meeting.
The several vice-leaders of the Blood Mist Alliance were not qualified to attend this meeting. Those eligible were the Alliance Leader and the three Grand Elders. The three Grand Elders almost never interfered with the alliance's internal affairs, spending most of their time cultivating in the Tianlin Realm.
But this time, all three Grand Elders had returned.
The Blood Mist Alliance Leader, Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor, spoke: "Three Grand Elders, the Holy Region World was originally one of the worlds we planned to occupy, but unexpectedly, our previous plan failed. Now, a human power is opposing us, and this time they have gone too far, actually wanting to seize the Holy Region World as well, even killing some of our Supreme experts."
"What is the opponent's strength?" asked Mingli Mist Ancestor, one of the three Grand Elders.
"There aren't many Supreme experts, and almost all of them are at the first level. We haven't seen any third-level Invincibles. The truly powerful one is the human leader, the Myriad Law Ancestor, who should be an Invincible," Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor said.
An Invincible!
The three Grand Elders frowned, finding the situation somewhat difficult.
Although the Blood Mist Alliance had a total of four Invincibles, and Mingli Mist Ancestor was even a Fifth-tier Invincible, Invincibles varied in strength, and a Fifth-tier Invincible wasn't considered particularly high.
Moreover, it was just one Holy Region World; it wasn't an exceptionally precious asset. At worst, they could just switch to another foreign world.
At this moment, Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor added: "According to our information, our alliance's Supreme experts once fought that Myriad Law Ancestor. At that time, he was still in the Supreme realm. He hasn't been an Invincible for long; he's a newly ascended Invincible. If we let this go, it would be equivalent to our Blood Mist Alliance backing down. We absolutely cannot let it go!"
The three Grand Elders agreed; they definitely couldn't let it go.
If the opponent were an ancient Invincible, they shouldn't provoke him, but a newly ascended Invincible was definitely not that strong. No matter how monstrous one's talent, it still took time to grow!
Therefore, the Blood Mist Alliance could easily kill a newly ascended Invincible.
However, Mingli Mist Ancestor then said: "This time, the three of you will take action. I cannot go. You should be very clear that in one of the foreign worlds we occupy, that specific item is about to appear. I must stay there at all times; I cannot lose the greater for the smaller!"
"Understood!"
Blood Shadow Mist Ancestor and the others nodded one after another.
