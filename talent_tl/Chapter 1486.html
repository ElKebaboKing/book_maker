Chapter 1485: The Effect of the Bone Beads
!
In front of the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
Ye Tian did not hesitate for long before resolutely stepping into the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
Boom! Endless pressure surged forth, many times stronger than the pressure outside. If an ordinary Domain Ancestor entered this place, they would likely be crushed to pieces by the pressure in an instant.
In such a place, even a Progenitor would find it difficult to exert the same destructive power they possessed in the outside world.
Of course, while he was suppressed, the Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals within the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone were also suppressed, making it quite fair.
As soon as Ye Tian entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, he immediately attracted the attention of the Bone Soldiers inside.
Suddenly, a large number of Bone Soldiers swarmed toward him.
Ten Bone Generals and over a thousand Bone Soldiers charged to surround and kill him, while the Bone Kings did not move.
The Bone Kings were likely too lazy to act. After all, if they had to act every time a Progenitor entered, they would be annoyed to death if Progenitors constantly sent avatars to harass them. Moreover, ten Bone Generals plus a thousand Bone Soldiers were enough to surround and kill a top-tier Progenitor.
If they truly couldn't win, the Bone Kings would naturally step in.
Ye Tian's goal this time was clear: kill a few Bone Soldiers, seize the Bone Beads, and then leave.
He wasn't greedy; otherwise, he wouldn't be able to obtain even a single Bone Bead.
"Kill!"
Ye Tian performed several Ultimate Ancestral Techniques. With the Ancestral Armor technique and the Dao Robe Ultimate Ancestral Technique, the two could be stacked, naturally causing his life-preserving capabilities to soar.
Immediately after, Ye Tian unleashed the Golden Wings Ultimate Ancestral Technique and the Streaming Light Ultimate Ancestral Technique, pushing his speed to the limit as he charged toward the Bone Soldiers. He then executed the Tyrant Strength Ultimate Ancestral Technique and the Extreme Arm Ultimate Ancestral Technique, throwing a punch at a Bone Soldier! With a loud boom, the Bone Soldier was shattered by Ye Tian's punch, leaving behind a Bone Bead.
Ye Tian immediately collected the Bone Bead.
At the same time, the thousand Bone Soldiers and ten Bone Generals attacked together. Even though Ye Tian was very fast, he was still hit by some of the attacks.
However, his defense was too powerful. With the two defensive Ultimate Ancestral Techniques stacked, the Dao Robe technique was broken, but the second layer, the Ancestral Armor technique, held firm. Consequently, he only suffered minor injuries, which were of little concern.
"Kill!"
Ye Tian struck back, shattering another Bone Soldier and obtaining a second Bone Bead.
"You seek death!!!"
Roars echoed from the depths of the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
Clearly, Ye Tian's killing of two Bone Soldiers had enraged the Bone Kings.
Rumble! Ten Bone Kings traveled from the depths of the Bone King Forbidden Zone. Each Bone King was incredibly powerful; in Ye Tian's eyes, they were all stronger than the Destruction Progenitor, at least three or four times as powerful.
There was no telling how many Bone Kings were here. Once surrounded by ten Bone Kings, it would be very difficult for him to escape.
Although he wasn't worried about the safety of his duplicated body, if the duplicated body fell, he wouldn't be able to bring the Bone Beads back.
Thus, while fleeing, Ye Tian killed one more Bone Soldier before quickly escaping the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
These Bone Kings did not dare to chase him out of the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone. There seemed to be some kind of restriction, though the exact nature of it was unclear.
"Three Bone Beads, not bad at all!"
Ye Tian said excitedly.
However, this was his first time entering the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, and the Bone Kings had been careless, which allowed him to obtain three Bone Beads at once.
Next time, he would likely not have such an opportunity.
Because once he entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone again, the Bone Kings would surely take action immediately.
"Whatever, I'll refine these three Bone Beads first and see the effects!"
Ye Tian's duplicated body returned to the Mystic Void Ancestral Realm and handed the three Bone Beads to his original body.
Subsequently, Ye Tian's original body began to absorb and refine one Bone Bead.
Boom! Countless amounts of pure energy flooded into Ye Tian's Endless Progenitor Body, rapidly tempering it, nourishing his soul, and strengthening his Progenitor power.
"This effect..."
Ye Tian was shocked.
Compared to the effect of the Bone Beads, those Nirvana treasures were simply trash!
"Top-tier Progenitors don't dare to enter the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone because they could easily perish, and even if they do enter, it's very difficult to kill a single Bone Soldier.
Otherwise, the Progenitors would all take the risk.
But for me, my duplicated body isn't afraid of death. The Bone Sea Forbidden Zone is a treasure land for me, not a forbidden zone!"
Ye Tian murmured.
In the following days, Ye Tian continued to refine the Bone Beads.
His cultivation continued to rise, and his strength was also rapidly increasing.
It took him nearly ten thousand years to refine one Bone Bead. Now that thirty thousand years had passed, all three Bone Beads were completely refined.
At this moment, Ye Tian's strength had increased by ten percent.
Do not underestimate a ten percent increase in strength; to gain ten percent in just thirty thousand years—what if he had enough Bone Beads? Moreover, Ye Tian had already reached the pinnacle of top-tier Progenitor combat power. To be able to increase his strength by ten percent at such a level was truly terrifying!
"I must get more Bone Beads! Perhaps with my foundation, I can possess combat power comparable to a Supreme Progenitor while still at the top-tier Progenitor level!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Soon, Ye Tian's duplicated body arrived at the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone once again.
This time, as soon as Ye Tian entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, ten Bone Kings moved out to surround and kill him.
All around him, there were also a large number of Bone Soldiers and Bone Generals assisting.
It could be said that Ye Tian was trapped in a heavy siege.
In the end, Ye Tian fought desperately. At all costs, he managed to kill one Bone Soldier and obtain a Bone Bead, escaping with a body that was nearly annihilated.
Such injuries would be fatal for an ordinary Progenitor because their Progenitor source would be almost exhausted.
But since this was a duplicated body, Ye Tian didn't care.
After obtaining another Bone Bead, Ye Tian's cultivation improved significantly.
However, Ye Tian knew very well that the next time he entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone, he would likely face not ten Bone Kings, but even more.
So, Ye Tian used his duplicated body to test the strength of the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone.
When Ye Tian entered the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone again, he indeed encountered twenty Bone Kings and was brutally killed.
"It's no use. If the duplicated body enters again, the result will still be death by siege. I can only resort to... another method!"
Ye Tian murmured.
His other method was... to duplicate the body of a Bone Race member and enter the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone with a Bone Race identity.
"I can't duplicate a Bone King. Bone Kings have high status in the Bone Sea Forbidden Zone; if a strange Bone King suddenly appeared, it would be easily recognized.
If I duplicate the body of a Bone Soldier, the possibility of exposure will be lower."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, even if he duplicated a Bone Soldier's body, he wouldn't appear in front of other Bone Soldiers. Instead, he would hide and investigate the situation of the Bone Race.
After all, if the Bone Soldier he duplicated met the original person, he would be exposed immediately.
Ye Tian had fought those Bone Soldiers for a long time, so he could naturally duplicate their bodies.
Ye Tian randomly chose a Bone Soldier and prepared to begin the duplication.
However.
When he performed the duplication, an accident occurred.
Boom! The duplicated Bone Soldier body was instantly annihilated.
It was forcibly annihilated by the source of the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Bone Soldiers were not allowed to appear in the Dark Forbidden Domain.
This was the reason why Bone Soldiers, Bone Generals, and Bone Kings did not dare to come out; they would be annihilated the moment they emerged.
