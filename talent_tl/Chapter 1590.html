Chapter 1589: The Star Ancient Domain is a Prison!

"The Star Ancient Domain is a prison!"
Ye Tian said in shock.
He originally thought that the Holy Land would contain the inheritance of some powerful being or some shocking Eternal Source Item, but he hadn't expected the truth to be like this.
The Star Ancient Domain was a prison, and the so-called Holy Land was the core of that prison.
At this moment, many geniuses of the Ancient Star Race became panicked one after another.
At this time, Donghe said, "Everyone, there is no need to worry. Although our Star Ancient Domain is a prison, nothing can go wrong. Furthermore, what this prison imprisons and seals is the ancestor of our Ancient Star Race. Our Ancient Star Race is a race evolved from the ancestor's aura. If not for this ancestor, we would not have been able to develop to our current scale. Our ultimate goal is to release this ancestor. Of course, this is extremely difficult. But if we do not release this ancestor, we will be forever trapped in this prison of the Star Ancient Domain, never able to leave this place."
Listening to these words, Ye Tian finally understood why the Ancient Star Race could not leave the Star Ancient Domain.
This Star Ancient Domain was a prison, and the Ancient Star Race were the descendants of the prisoner. Naturally, they had no way to leave and would be blocked by the Star Ancient Domain.
This place was a prison and a forbidden land, but there were still opportunities here.
And that sealed powerful being was where the opportunity lay.
What a mysterious place the Star Ancient Domain was; even the Void Source Ancestor had not entered the Star Ancient Domain, and yet such a place was a prison. It was evident that the strength of the imprisoned being must be extremely powerful, definitely not weaker than the Void Source Ancestor, and perhaps even stronger.
Soon, Donghe led the geniuses of the Ancient Star Race to a mysterious space within the Holy Land.
This mysterious space was created by the ancestor of the Ancient Star Race, who had spent a long time finally projecting some power to forge it, and within this mysterious space was a treasure left behind by that mysterious being—the Ancient Star Stone.
Under the influence of the Ancient Star Stone, this space had transformed into a treasure land.
The geniuses of the Ancient Star Race could use their Ancient Star Body talent to absorb the power of the Ancient Star Stone, strengthening both their physical bodies and souls. It even had a certain effect on First-Tier Invincibles, but there was a Limit to the power each member of the Ancient Star Race could absorb.
"The Ancient Star Stone is an Eternal Source Item, and the compatibility between the Ancient Star Race members and the Eternal Source Item Ancient Star Stone is one hundred percent!"
At this moment, Ye Tian completely understood.
Strictly speaking, the Ancient Star Stone was the true clan-protecting Eternal Source Item of the Ancient Star Race.
Absorbing the Ancient Star Stone to strengthen the physical body and soul was a minor opportunity, not considered a truly great opportunity. The truly great opportunity was an Ancient Star Tower within the Ancient Star Stone space, where a wisp of the Ancient Star Race ancestor's consciousness was hidden.
If one could gain his recognition, they could obtain his guidance and a portion of his inheritance.
And the ultimate goal of the Ancient Star Race was to nurture a Supreme Being who could rescue the ancestor, but they had failed to do so until now.
At this moment, Ye Tian was pondering one thing: should he or should he not attract the attention of that imprisoned Ancient Star Race ancestor?
If he didn't attract the other party's attention, he feared he wouldn't gain any benefits. Although the opportunity of the Ancient Star Stone was good, this was a duplicated body and could not bring benefits to his main body!
After thinking it over, Ye Tian decided to attract the attention of that Ancient Star Race ancestor.
Near the Ancient Star Stone.
Ye Tian activated his Ancient Star Body Eternal Ancestral Talent and began to absorb the source power dissipating from the Ancient Star Stone.
Boom!!!!
A massive amount of Ancient Star Stone source power surged into Ye Tian's body, being absorbed and refined by him, forming a source vortex.
This was something that had never happened since the opening of the Ancient Star Stone space, and it immediately attracted the attention of many Ancient Star Race members.
As time passed, the strength of Ye Tian's duplicated body grew more and more powerful, with his physical body and soul soaring frantically.
"What's going on?"
Many Invincibles were bewildered. How could an Invincible absorb the source power of the Ancient Star Stone so quickly? This was far too abnormal.
Soon, the Supreme Being Donghe outside also learned of the situation in the Ancient Star Stone space.
Thus, the Ancient Star Stone immediately summoned several other of the Top Ten Supreme Beings.
"Donghe, has something happened in the Holy Land?"
"Donghe, why did you call me here? I was still cultivating."
"Donghe, has an outsider broken into the Holy Land?"
One Supreme Being after another asked.
"You will know once you project your consciousness into the Ancient Star Stone space!" Donghe said.
Swish, swish, swish!!!
The consciousnesses of the Supreme Beings probed into the Ancient Star Stone space and sensed Ye Tian's situation; they were also instantly stunned.
"The speed of absorbing the Ancient Star Stone source power is many times faster than ours was back then!"
Gu Chen said in shock.
"Could it be..." Another Supreme Being, Chi Yang, speculated, "Could it be that his Ancient Star Body talent is above all of ours?"
Gu Chen said, "Many of our Ancient Star Race possess the Ancient Star Body talent, and the best among them have an Ancient Star Body at the Ultimate Ancestral Talent level, but this clansman at the Fourth-Tier Invincible realm, his Ancient Star Body talent likely surpasses the Ultimate Ancestral Talent..."
The other Supreme Beings looked shocked, and a talent level surfaced in their minds—Eternal Ancestral Talent!
The Ancient Star Race had never given birth to an Ancient Star Body of the Eternal Ancestral Talent level; this was the first time!
One could imagine what a monumental event this was for the Ancient Star Race.
"Perhaps if we nurture this person well, there is hope of helping the ancestor escape!" Donghe murmured.
...
Ancient Star Stone space.
Ye Tian continued to absorb the Ancient Star Stone source power. He knew he had already attracted the attention of those Supreme Beings, and perhaps even the gaze of the Ancient Star Race ancestor.
However, he was not panicked at all.
After all, from his body to his soul, he was currently a genuine member of the Ancient Star Race; no one could detect his identity.
Time slowly passed. Ye Tian absorbed more and more Ancient Star Stone source power. Even if he couldn't take it with him, he wouldn't waste this absorption; otherwise, it would arouse the suspicion of other Ancient Star Race members. Therefore, he would absorb until his own Limit.
Finally, on a certain day, Ye Tian felt his body's Limit. If he continued to absorb to the extreme, it would affect his future attempt to break through to the Supreme Being realm, so he stopped the absorption.
By this time, the strength of his duplicated body had also increased significantly.
"It's time to go to the Ancient Star Tower!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Swish!
Ye Tian flew toward the Ancient Star Tower, took a deep breath secretly, and then stepped into the Ancient Star Tower.
Ye Tian was not clear about what was inside the Ancient Star Tower, and other Ancient Star Race members who had come out of the tower would not reveal much.
He only knew that entering the Ancient Star Tower involved undergoing some assessments, but Ye Tian found that he did not experience any assessment.
Boom!!!!
A powerful consciousness arrived before Ye Tian. It was somewhat similar to Jin Ancestor's consciousness from back then, but the consciousness before him was many times more terrifying than Jin Ancestor's.
Upon seeing this consciousness, Ye Tian finally realized who it was.
He must be the ancestor of the Ancient Star Race, that extremely mysterious and powerful being, the mysterious powerhouse who required the entire Star Ancient Domain to be turned into a prison to suppress him.
