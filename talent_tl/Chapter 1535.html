Chapter 1534: Refining Tribulation Blood, Strength Greatly Increases!

Ye Tian took two portions of Tribulation Blood. Ancestor Dragon Calamity and Ancestor Wanyuan Sword, who contributed the most, each took two and a half portions. The remaining six Invincibles each received one portion. Thus, the thirteen portions of Tribulation Blood were distributed, and no one objected.
After all, for them, this opportunity was practically a windfall!
"Next is the distribution of these Tribulation Bones!"
Ancestor Dragon Calamity spoke.
"What are Tribulation Bones?" Ye Tian asked.
Ancestor Wanyuan Sword explained, "Tribulation Bones come from Tribulation Beasts. Generally, Tribulation Beasts of ten tribulations or more will produce Tribulation Bones, but the quantity is uncertain. We're lucky this time; a total of nine Tribulation Bones were produced from this Tribulation Beast. Tribulation Bones have only one use: artifact refining. Weapons suitable for us Invincibles are rare; for instance, Tribulation Artifacts refined from Tribulation Bones are of significant help to us. Tribulation Bones are extremely precious. A single bone from a thirty-seven tribulation beast is worth about thirty thousand Source Rule Tribulation Crystals."
"One bone is worth thirty thousand Source Rule Tribulation Crystals!!!"
Ye Tian was surprised.
He only now realized that the Source Rule Tribulation Crystals he had obtained before weren't actually worth much. Not only were they inferior to Tribulation Blood, they weren't even as valuable as these Tribulation Bones!
No wonder the Invincibles had little interest in one-tribulation beasts. A one-tribulation beast really didn't have much value. Any small item from a thirty-seven tribulation beast far exceeded the value of a one-tribulation beast. Hunting powerful Tribulation Beasts was the real money-making business.
Unfortunately, Tribulation Beasts were too dangerous. Without confirming a beast's strength, Invincibles didn't dare to seek them out, as they could perish with the slightest mistake.
After some discussion regarding the nine Tribulation Bones, Ye Tian received only one, and the others each received one as well.
However, the other Invincibles each gave Ye Tian over 1,000 Source Rule Tribulation Crystals as compensation. Since Ye Tian had discovered the beast, he naturally deserved more benefits.
Next, the flesh and blood of the beast were also distributed. They held some value, worth about several dozen Source Rule Tribulation Crystals.
In this way, the Tribulation Beast was completely divided up.
After the distribution, Ye Tian left the Cloud Mist World and returned to the Dark Forbidden Zone.
The Invincibles of the Calamity Black Mountain and the Firmament Sword Tomb also returned to their respective worlds, bringing the cooperation to an end.
Calamity Black Mountain.
An Invincible asked Ancestor Dragon Calamity, "Lord Ancestor Dragon Calamity, why didn't we just take down that Ancestor of Myriad Laws? With your strength plus ours, there was definitely hope of capturing him."
"Too risky!" Ancestor Dragon Calamity said. "For him to become an Invincible in just one or two Great Tribulation Eras, his talent is too terrifying. Moreover, can you guarantee that these two Invincible bodies are his main body? What if they are both clones? Once we act, it would be equivalent to offending an Invincible who can create clones. Once he grows up, it would be a disaster for our Calamity Black Mountain. Besides, for two portions of Tribulation Blood, one Tribulation Bone, and some Source Rule Tribulation Crystals, it's not worth it. Even if he really came in person, are you sure the people from the Firmament Sword Tomb wouldn't help him, perhaps creating a future enemy for us? Furthermore, this Ancestor of Myriad Laws might be using clones to find Tribulation Beasts, so he isn't afraid of falling. Perhaps in the future, we will have more opportunities to cooperate with him and obtain more Tribulation Blood, so there's no need to offend him."
"Lord Ancestor Dragon Calamity is right!"
The other Invincibles nodded one after another.
Similarly, a similar conversation took place at the Firmament Sword Tomb.
---------------
"They didn't make a move?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He had thought the Invincibles of the Firmament Sword Tomb and Calamity Black Mountain would attack him, but nothing happened. This was naturally a good thing.
After returning to the Dark Forbidden Zone, Ye Tian began secluded cultivation to absorb and refine those two portions of Tribulation Blood.
Meanwhile, the Firmament Sword Tomb and Calamity Black Mountain destroyed those two Second-Tier Gates and no longer established bases in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
From then on, except for the gates controlled by Ye Tian, there were no more gates of foreign forces in the Dark Forbidden Zone.
It could be said that the human race had completely taken control of the Dark Forbidden Zone, with no more external enemies.
Ye Tian absorbed and refined the Tribulation Blood of the thirty-seven tribulation beast. Endless, gentle tribulation power surged into Ye Tian's body, continuously tempering his Invincible Tribulation Body.
Other Invincibles' utilization rate of Tribulation Blood was likely only around ten percent, with most of it being wasted. But Ye Tian, who possessed the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique and the Eternal Talent 'Tribulation', could definitely utilize the Tribulation Blood one hundred percent.
Therefore, two portions of Tribulation Blood in his hands were equivalent to at least twenty portions refined by other Invincibles.
It was conceivable that once he completely refined these two portions of Tribulation Blood, his cultivation would likely increase significantly, and his combat power would naturally surge by a great tribulation.
Time passed slowly. Ye Tian's Tribulation Blood was continuously refined, his Invincible Tribulation Body grew stronger, and his cultivation level rose higher.
Using the Tribulation Transformation Secret Technique, Ye Tian's refining speed was very fast. In just a few domain years, Ye Tian had completely refined one portion of Tribulation Blood. After spending some time stabilizing his realm, he began refining the second portion.
Perhaps due to his increased strength, the refining speed was even faster this time.
About two domain years passed, and the second portion of Tribulation Blood was also refined by Ye Tian.
Afterward, Ye Tian entered the Source Sea to test his strength and found that his combat power had increased by about three times.
Originally, his strength was already very powerful among Sixth-Tier Invincibles. Now that his strength had tripled, although he was still not as strong as Invincibles like Ancestor Dragon Calamity, he wasn't much weaker, roughly equivalent to the strength of a thirty-seven tribulation beast.
In other words, he was not far from the threshold of a Fifth-Tier Invincible.
However, if he didn't create an Invincible Ancestral Technique, the increase in strength from just using treasures and resources to raise his cultivation would be limited, and in the later stages, it wouldn't improve even a tiny bit.
However, to create an Invincible Ancestral Technique, one must go to the Eternal World to comprehend its rules.
The Eternal World had no Great Tribulations, so Source Rule Tribulation Crystals would not be born there; thus, one had to go to the Eternal World in person.
"I'll look for some more Tribulation Beasts. Once my strength reaches a point where it's hard to progress further, I'll go to the Eternal World!" Ye Tian planned.
In the following days, he continued to search for Tribulation Beasts.
This time, with his increased strength, even if he encountered a powerful Tribulation Beast, he wouldn't be easily killed instantly. However, if he encountered a beast with thousands of tribulations, he would still be unable to escape.
Finally, one day, Ye Tian encountered another Tribulation Beast that he had a hope of hunting. Moreover, he didn't need to rely on others; just by relying on his duplicated body and his clone—two Invincible-level combat forces—he had hope of hunting it down, as this Tribulation Beast only had twenty-eight tribulations.
