Chapter 1640: Will World, Second Stage!

Only one person remained in Wuhen Territory.
However, before the entrance of Wuhen Territory disappeared, Ye Tian still sent his clone out for a trip, letting his main body take away some of the treasures carried by the clone.
For example, the Void God Leaf; this item was relatively valuable and could not be lost.
Afterward, the clone self-Annihilated, and the copied body recondensed again in Wuhen Territory.
In this way, Ye Tian could boldly and confidently use Split Wind Valley to cultivate the Will World.
His copied body was at the very edge of Split Wind Valley, where the will impact was the weakest, so the copied body could endure for a very long time without needing to leave Split Wind Valley.
Meanwhile, his clone was within the inner region of Split Wind Valley, enduring the intense will impact, crazily tempering its own will, and cultivating in coordination with the Will World.
Just like that, with the clone collapsing again and again and cultivating again and again, Ye Tian came to understand the Will World more and more.
Will was unlike cultivation; as the will of the copied body and the clone increased, Ye Tian's main body's will would also increase. This was much like accumulating experience and comprehension.
As the clone collapsed time and time again, his will still kept improving, and he also gradually gained a deeper comprehension of this secret art, the Will World, combining it with Ye Tian's own situation to turn it into a secret art belonging to Ye Tian, while steadily perfecting it step by step.
He would not safely and directly cultivate according to Wuhen Territory Lord's secret art method; that would easily lead to collapse. He had to improve it himself and adapt it to his own body before it could become a truly powerful will secret art.
On this day.
The Will World had made new progress, and he was preparing to open up a Will World.
Once a Will World was opened up, it meant that the Will World secret art had reached the first Stage.
Ye Tian continuously condensed will through the Will World secret art, opening up a Will World inside a certain cell of his body. The Will World was not a real world, so even a single cell could bear it.
“Will World, open!”
In the unknown layer of the cell, a Will World was forcibly opened up, and endless will manifested there in tangible form.
At the same time, Ye Tian had complete control over this cell and could erupt with terrifying power.
Moreover, he could use this cell as the core and radiate it throughout his entire body, thereby allowing his body to erupt with far more power than usual.
More importantly, once the Will World was opened up, it could slowly strengthen his will, which was also one channel for will improvement.
One could imagine how fast the will improvement speed would be, and how powerful the will would become, once large numbers of Will Worlds were opened up.
It had to be said that the Will World secret art created by Wuhen Territory Lord was indeed terrifying; it was just a pity that even he himself did not dare cultivate it.
The clone successfully opened up a Will World, but the main body naturally could not automatically open up a Will World.
Thus.
Ye Tian's main body also needed to open up a Will World, and since this process had already been improved, there would be no danger.
Ye Tian then began opening up a Will World inside the Void Source Hall.
Very soon, the main body successfully opened up a Will World.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian's clone stayed in Split Wind Valley. Because a Will World had been opened and his will strengthened, he could remain in Split Wind Valley for a longer time.
Moreover, Ye Tian could go deeper into Split Wind Valley.
Just like that, another period of time passed.
On this day, Ye Tian began to open up the second Will World.
Unfortunately, the opening of the second Will World failed.
Ye Tian quickly found the reason: it was influenced by the first Will World. The first Will World radiated throughout the entire body, and under its influence, if the second Will World were opened in the same way as the first, it would inevitably fail.
The failure to open up the Will World directly dealt a severe blow to Ye Tian's will.
Helplessly, Ye Tian self-Annihilated this clone and then recondensed another one, which was more convenient than slowly recovering his will.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian continuously studied how to create a second Will World and discovered that this had a great deal to do with the chosen cell position.
It could be said that the cell position chosen for each subsequent Will World had to follow a certain pattern in order to avoid interference.
After many attempts, Ye Tian finally found the proper position and opened up the second Will World.
After the second Will World was successfully opened, Ye Tian's main body began to open up the second Will World as well, succeeding in one go without any problems.
Unknowingly, Ye Tian's copied body and clone had stayed in Split Wind Valley for several tribulation eras.
Such a long span of years, with Ye Tian's clone still existing there, would probably be unimaginable in the eyes of people like True River Sword Ancestor and the others, yet no one knew about it.
By now, Ye Tian had already opened more than three hundred Will Worlds.
If he opened one thousand Will Worlds, that would be equivalent to stepping into the second Stage of the Will World secret art.
“According to the introduction of the Will World secret art, once the Will World reaches the second Stage, the will will undergo a transformation!” Ye Tian was not sure whether this was true, but he very much wanted to raise the Will World to the second Stage.
Now, his understanding of the Will World secret art was deepening more and more, and the speed at which he opened up Will Worlds was also getting faster and faster.
Moreover, his clone had already reached the core area of Split Wind Valley.
Few half-step Eternal Realm experts dared to come here; otherwise, their will would be blasted into collapse by the impact.
Ye Tian knew very well that, with his current level of will, he originally would not have been able to reach this place. But more than three hundred Will Worlds radiating through his body had made his resistance to will impact terrifyingly strong, allowing him to come here.
On this day.
Ye Tian truly walked to the most core area of Split Wind Valley, where he saw a huge rock.
This huge rock was gray in color, covered with densely packed patterns that were not engraved onto it, but naturally formed.
“The will impact in Split Wind Valley should be emanating from this rock. In other words, this rock created the anomaly of Split Wind Valley!”
Ye Tian said in surprise.
He wanted to take this rock away, but found that he could not move it.
Clearly, Wuhen Territory Lord had also placed a restriction on this rock, perfectly fusing it with Wuhen Territory so that it could not be taken away.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian sat cross-legged on this will rock, enduring the impact of will while cultivating the Will World.
Unknowingly, another five tribulation eras passed.
At this time, the number of Ye Tian's Will Worlds had reached 999; just one last one remained before he could reach the second Stage of the Will World and trigger a transformation of will.
Boom!!!!
The 1000th Will World was born!
Ye Tian's will began to undergo a qualitative change, as though it had stepped into an entirely new tier.
At this moment.
The countless cells in Ye Tian's body all resonated with the transformation of his will, and his body radiated endless will light, illuminating the surrounding void.
After a long while, the transformation of will came to an end.
“My will has increased tenfold!”
Ye Tian said in shock.
Before, the improvement of his will had been very, very slow, but this time the increase was too terrifying!
Moreover, his will had become even more tenacious.
In addition, Ye Tian discovered that when he infused his will into his fist or weapon, it became incomparably sharp and could easily tear apart the body of a half-step Eternal Realm expert.
Of course, this was tested with Ye Tian's copied body.
Moreover, will could also enhance his defense.
“After will reaches the second Stage, it is actually this terrifying. Perhaps the will of Eternal Realm existences is also like this! Second Stage will should be equivalent to the will of an Eternal Realm existence!”
Ye Tian speculated.
In short, after the transformation of his will reached the second Stage, his strength also transformed; at the very least, he could already match a true First Realm, and was not weaker than True River Sword Ancestor and the others.
