Chapter 1427: Massacre, Supreme Domain Ancestors Fall One After Another
!
The Sacred Golden Sand was firmly fixed in the void of this treasure land. A nearby Supreme Domain Ancestor wanted to collect it into his internal world, but his speed was far too slow.
Boom! Another powerful Supreme Domain Ancestor attacked him, heavily wounding him in an instant.
The slaughter began! And Ye Tian also made his move, killing everything in his path and striking down Supreme Domain Ancestors one by one.
As the Supreme Domain Ancestors fell one after another, the others grew terrified and retreated, no longer daring to compete.
At this moment, only Ye Tian, Wu Xiu, An Hong, and a few other Supreme Domain Ancestors remained. These Supreme Domain Ancestors were by no means weak; they were all monstrous geniuses ranked in the top ten of their respective Sacred Sects' Supreme Rankings. Each possessed combat power comparable to a Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, though some were slightly weaker while others were infinitely close to the combat power of a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Their auras were locked onto Ye Tian, as if they were about to strike him simultaneously.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor!"
Wu Xiu and the others blocked Ye Tian, preventing him from seizing the Sacred Golden Sand.
"You aren't fighting for the Sacred Golden Sand, yet you're blocking me. Aren't you afraid others will take the chance to snatch it away?"
Ye Tian sneered.
An Hong Domain Ancestor said, "The Sacred Golden Sand is firmly connected to the void. It will take some time to remove it, so we aren't afraid of others snatching it.
Wanfa Domain Ancestor, your strength is comparable to a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor. If we don't unite against you, we won't have any chance of seizing the Sacred Golden Sand."
Wu Xiu also added, "Wanfa Domain Ancestor, you have two choices now: retreat immediately and swear an oath not to fight for the Sacred Golden Sand, or we will join forces to kill you!"
For the Sacred Golden Sand, it was worth the risk to kill Ye Tian.
"I give the same warning to you: retreat immediately, or I will kill you all right now!"
Ye Tian threatened back.
"Do it!"
Wu Xiu and the others attacked.
They didn't dare waste time. After all, every second wasted meant a more powerful Supreme Domain Ancestor might arrive. If they delayed too long, even if they killed Ye Tian, their own chances of seizing the Sacred Golden Sand would be greatly reduced.
They weren't... true allies; they were still competitors. It was only because Ye Tian was too great a threat that they were forced to unite.
Therefore, killing Ye Tian and then competing among themselves for the Sacred Golden Sand was the best choice. Time was of the essence.
Nearly ten experts from the top ten of the Supreme Ranking attacked together. Such a deterrent was enough to threaten a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
Among these Supreme Domain Ancestors, some used Soul Sacred Techniques, some used binding-type Sacred Techniques, and others used bizarre Sacred Techniques, but most used offensive Sacred Techniques.
Furthermore, they all utilized Sacred Objects and Sacred Weapons. Under their collective outburst, even a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor would be heavily wounded.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor!"
The Supreme Domain Ancestors of the Battle Sect grew nervous and began to worry.
However, they didn't dare step forward because their strength was too low. If they intervened, they wouldn't be able to help and might even become a burden.
Now, they could only hope that Ye Tian was strong enough or that other top-ten experts from the Supreme Ranking would arrive to support him.
But Ye Tian didn't panic at all.
"I originally wanted to keep a low profile, but since you're seeking death, I'll let you see my true strength!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
"Ancestral Armor!"
Boom!!!!! The Ancestral Technique, Ancestral Armor, protected Ye Tian, allowing him to withstand the numerous attacks without his defense being shaken in the slightest.
Then, with a wave of his hand, Ye Tian sent out countless golden lights that transformed into threads, slicing toward the Supreme Domain Ancestors.
One Supreme Domain Ancestor, whose strength was comparable to a weaker Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor and who was ranked ninth in the Dark Sacred Sect, was instantly disintegrated when sliced by Ye Tian's Golden Silk Ancestral Technique.
In an instant, this Supreme Domain Ancestor fell! It could be said that the strength Ye Tian now displayed was no longer that of a Six-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, but a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor—and not a weak one, but a standard Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor, close to the middle tier.
It was very easy for a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor to kill a Seven-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor. Moreover, Supreme Domain Ancestors were easier to kill than Invincible Domain Ancestors.
Thus, this Supreme Domain Ancestor was insta-killed by Ye Tian without any power to resist.
"No!!!!"
The Supreme Domain Ancestors were terrified. Those with slightly weaker life-saving abilities might have blocked one strike, but the golden light of the Golden Silk Ancestral Technique sliced through again and again, making it impossible to resist.
Soon, the Supreme Domain Ancestors fell one by one.
"Impossible! How can you know Ancestral Techniques, and two of them at that?!!!"
Wu Xiu said in disbelief.
Among the Invincible Domain Ancestors, such as the very top tier within the various Sacred Sects, there were likely very few who had comprehended Ancestral Techniques.
For example, among the Thirty-Six War Emperors of the Battle Sect, only the Five Thunder Domain Ancestor was known to have comprehended an Ancestral Technique.
Yet Ye Tian was only a Supreme Domain Ancestor, and his cultivation time was very short; he was an incredibly young Supreme Domain Ancestor. For such a young Supreme Domain Ancestor to have comprehended Ancestral Techniques—was that even possible? If someone had told him before that a Supreme Domain Ancestor could master an Ancestral Technique, he would have considered them a madman or a fool.
But now, he had seen with his own eyes Ye Tian perform Ancestral Techniques—two of them—with strength comparable to a Five-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor.
This was no longer a monster; this was a taboo existence!
"Escape!"
Wu Xiu prepared to flee, and An Hong Domain Ancestor also prepared to flee; otherwise, death was certain.
They spared no expense, even burning their Domain Bodies and Sacred Souls just to escape.
However, the golden threads of the Golden Silk Ancestral Technique pierced through the void.
Pfft! Wu Xiu's physical body was directly pierced; his so-called defenses were completely useless.
One life-saving item after another was applied to his body, and he even used an Ancestral Medicine to recover from his injuries, but he still couldn't repair the damage.
Soon, the golden threads pierced through again.
Under the repeated attacks, Wu Xiu finally let out a cry of unwilling despair as his massive body turned into a corpse, floating in the void.
Wu Xiu had fallen!
Now, only An Hong Domain Ancestor remained. However, Ye Tian had long since sealed the void. Although the power of the Five Elements Sealing couldn't completely lock down a powerhouse like An Hong Domain Ancestor, it could hinder her. Breaking the seal completely would take time.
And Ye Tian would not give her that time.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor, how about you let me go? I am willing to pay a price!"
An Hong Domain Ancestor said hurriedly.
"No need!"
Ye Tian said coldly.
Since these Domain Ancestors dared to attack him, they had to be prepared to fall.
"Then let's die together!"
An Hong Domain Ancestor went mad.
She took out a treasure, and a terrifying power was faintly about to explode.
