Chapter 1465: Creating Nirvana Spacetime, Cultivation Breakthrough...

Chapter 1465: Creating Nirvana Spacetime, Cultivation Breakthrough to the Peak of Supreme Soul Ancestor!
!
Creating a Nirvana Spacetime was indeed a very bold idea.
If he were in the outside world, the Ancestors would easily find out, and then he would be in trouble.
However, this was the Dark Soul Domain, and currently, the Ancestors could not enter.
Therefore, if he created a Nirvana Spacetime within the Dark Soul Domain, the Ancestors would definitely not be able to discover it.
Moreover, he didn't need to create a large-scale Nirvana Spacetime; he only needed to create a small patch of it.
As long as a single member of the Nirvana Clan existed, a Nirvana Spacetime could persist indefinitely, as the Nirvana Ancestral Talent of the Nirvana Clan could continuously influence it.
Of course, Nirvana Spacetime also required energy to sustain it. If it absorbed a large amount of external energy, it could continuously convert it into Nirvana energy. Nirvana energy had a massive effect on both Supreme Domain Ancestors and Supreme Soul Ancestors.
However, the prerequisite was being able to withstand the erosion of Nirvana energy. Other Supreme Domain Ancestors could not withstand its erosion; only the Ancestors themselves could refine Nirvana energy.
But Ye Tian could refine Nirvana energy.
"The Dark Soul Domain does not lack resources. I can completely create a small patch of Nirvana Spacetime and then integrate a large amount of resources into it.
Back then, the reason the Nirvana Clan frantically invaded the Dark Forbidden Domain was... because they wanted to occupy a large amount of territory and devour the energy resources of the Dark Forbidden Domain to convert them into Nirvana energy, thereby strengthening the Nirvana Clan."
Ye Tian murmured to himself.
Thus, for him now, all of this was very easy to achieve, and he didn't have to worry about being discovered by the Ancestors.
In any case... those low-level resources were useless to him now. It was better to put them to use by converting them into Nirvana energy, which was a way to rapidly increase his cultivation.
"Let's do it!"
Ye Tian decided.
No sooner said than done.
Ye Tian chose the Ten Directions Sacred Realm and then sealed off an area, not allowing anyone to enter—not even the Invincible Domain Ancestors of the human race.
Subsequently, he began to manifest the Nirvana Ancestral Talent.
This duplicated body had also been bestowed with the Nirvana Ancestral Talent by his main body, so it could influence the spacetime here.
Boom!!!!! An area of spacetime was invaded by the Nirvana Ancestral Talent, gradually forming a special spacetime region.
Initially, this spacetime region was very small, but it gradually expanded to the size of a universe.
"That's about enough. A range this large is sufficient!"
Once the Nirvana Spacetime was created, it still needed to be stabilized; otherwise, it would gradually dissipate.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian integrated a sea of resources into this Nirvana Spacetime, using the Nirvana Ancestral Talent to smelt this energy and increase the power of this spacetime region.
From the beginning, the Nirvana energy in this Nirvana Spacetime was extremely thin, but it gradually became concentrated.
Even without Nirvana treasures, cultivating by absorbing Nirvana energy here was many times faster than in the outside world.
Under the accumulation of massive resources, a Nirvana treasure finally began to be born within this Nirvana Spacetime.
Buzz!!!! Somewhere within the Nirvana Spacetime, a treasure was born.
"Nirvana Blood Flower, a mid-grade Nirvana treasure. A good harvest!"
Ye Tian waited for some time, and when the flower bloomed and matured, he plucked it.
In the following days, one Nirvana treasure after another was born, all falling into Ye Tian's hands, with no one to compete with him.
Meanwhile, the Dark Soul Domain continuously produced a large number of treasures, almost all of which fell into the hands of the human race, and the majority of the human race's resources were concentrated on Ye Tian.
Consequently, Ye Tian did not lack resources at all, pouring them all into the Nirvana Spacetime.
With enough Nirvana treasures, Ye Tian began to refine them to temper his soul and increase his cultivation.
After only tens of millions of years, Ye Tian's cultivation rose to the mid-stage Supreme Soul Ancestor realm.
In the blink of an eye, several billion years passed in the Dark Soul Domain.
By this time, a second Supreme Soul Ancestor had still not been born in the Dark Soul Domain, while Ye Tian's cultivation, with the help of Nirvana treasures, had already risen to the peak of the Supreme Soul Ancestor level, consistent with his main body.
"Hahaha, if I didn't care about my foundation and just wanted to become an ordinary Ancestor, I would truly have hope of becoming one within a single Realm Year!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
As for the Ancestors wanting to enter the Dark Soul Domain, it would likely take them a hundred Realm Years.
A hundred Realm Years was not a very long time for the Ancestors—which Ancestor hadn't lived through millions of Realm Years since they began cultivating? But for Ye Tian, within a hundred Realm Years, even if he were mediocre, he would have already become an Ancestor.
Of course, his main body pursued becoming the strongest Ancestor, so he would not rush to break through. He wanted to create the strongest Ancestor Law before attempting to break through to the Ancestor realm.
"The coming days will be spent... deducing a batch of Ancestral Techniques, cultivating them to the Perfection stage, and then beginning to practice the Spatial Ancestor Law!"
Ye Tian decided.
Once he broke through to the Ancestor realm with the help of the Spatial Ancestor Law, he would be a Spatial Ancestor. Moreover, since he would become a Spatial Ancestor within the Dark Soul Domain, even as a Future One, he could control a small portion of the Dark Soul Domain's power and exert formidable strength.
Furthermore, after cultivating for a hundred Realm Years, his strength would be even greater, and he would definitely have hope of contending with the likes of the Destruction Ancestor and the Holy Ancestor.
Once the Dark Soul Domain was secured as a foundation, the strength of the human race would likely reach a terrifying level.
Moreover, the Profound Void Sacred Realm could definitely become comparable to a Dark Sacred Ground in the future.
Time flowed by.
In the blink of an eye, 1 billion years passed.
By this time, the Dark Soul Domain had already given birth to hundreds of Supreme Soul Ancestors. Most of them were native inhabitants; after all, at the beginning of the Dark Soul Domain's birth, it was too easy for natives to cultivate. However, it would be difficult to produce so many Supreme Soul Ancestors in the future; this was a benefit of the birth of a Dark Sacred Ground.
These Supreme Soul Ancestors were all at the early stage. To reach the mid-stage, they would likely have to wait for the great opportunity that would come after a trillion years.
Furthermore, over a trillion years, many treasures would be born that could allow Supreme Domain Ancestors to rapidly increase their strength.
However, what no one knew was that by this time, Ye Tian had already cultivated a large number of Ancestral Techniques to the Perfection stage, and his cultivation had reached the limit of a Supreme Soul Ancestor. He had even begun to temper his physical body, hoping to achieve something in the path of the physical body.
However, this step was too difficult. Once one became a Supreme Soul Ancestor, the physical body was almost entirely soul-energized, making it impossible to possess a physical body as terrifying as that of a Supreme Domain Ancestor.
But this was also quite normal.
Because a Supreme Domain Ancestor also had no way of possessing a soul as heaven-defying as that of a Supreme Soul Ancestor. It was too difficult to completely combine the two.
During these 10 billion years, Ye Tian also began to comprehend the Spatial Ancestor Law, and had currently comprehended one-fifth of it.
By the time a trillion years passed, he would definitely be able to fully comprehend the Spatial Ancestor Law and step into the Ancestor realm with a single thought.
"Then I will wait until the trillion-year mark to break through to the Ancestor realm and shock those Ancestors!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
