Chapter 1680 Breakthrough to the Eternal Realm, Immortal!

“Star Sword Ancestor is finally going to choose to break through!”
When Ye Tian first met Star Sword Ancestor, the degree of fusion between his will and body was already close to ten percent. Now, it was probably already at ten percent, so he would no longer remain in the Nine Extreme Realm and was going to charge into the Eternal Realm.
Only after Star Sword Ancestor advanced to the Eternal Realm could his strength reach the level of the Immortal Realm. And only after becoming an Immortal Realm existence would he be qualified to obtain more resources.
Although in the Nine Extreme Realm it was easier to merge will and body, without other opportunities, the speed would still be very slow.
Moreover, even beings in the time-space territory, even at the Nine Extreme Realm, still had a lifespan limit, so Star Sword Ancestor could not remain in the Nine Extreme Realm forever.
Star Sword Ancestor chose the Eight Extreme Tower as the place for his breakthrough, and invited many friends to come and observe, including quite a few lords of the Immortal Realm.
Eight Extreme Tower.
One Eternal Realm expert after another, along with members of the Eight Extreme Tower, came here to wait for Star Sword Ancestor’s breakthrough.
Not long after, a massive source energy vortex formed around the Eight Extreme Tower, and boundless innate eternal rules poured into Star Sword Ancestor’s body, merging with it. The fusion of innate eternal rules with the body was a necessary condition for becoming an Eternal Realm existence.
In the Nine Extreme Realm, it was very easy for the body to fuse with innate eternal rules, so Star Sword Ancestor would not need much time before breaking through to become an Eternal Realm existence, and an Immortal Realm existence at that, leaping directly over the two great realms of the Longevity Realm and the Immortal Extinction Realm.
Hum hum hum!!!!
A giant sword covering the entire time-space territory appeared, enveloping this time-space territory and the surrounding void region.
And a towering body projection transformed into a heaven-shaking giant, as if one hand could encompass an entire time-space territory.
That towering figure was Star Sword Ancestor, and all of this was merely the overflow of power from his breakthrough.
Within the time-space territory, many beings did not dare look directly at Star Sword Ancestor’s body projection. If anyone dared to look directly, their body and soul would be frozen, and time-space would also solidify. Only Eight Extreme Realm experts dared to directly witness Star Sword Ancestor’s breakthrough.
After a long time, Star Sword Ancestor’s breakthrough was finally complete.
At this moment.
Star Sword Ancestor’s voice spread out.
“Everyone, please come in!”
Very soon, everyone saw Star Sword Ancestor.
At this time, Star Sword Ancestor’s aura still could not be fully restrained, so a trace of the Immortal Realm aura leaked out, causing great suppression upon many Eight Extreme Realm experts. Even Ye Tian felt a faint suppression.
“An Immortal Realm lord!”
Many Eight Extreme Realm experts looked at Star Sword Ancestor with great envy.
There were too few Immortal Realm lords in the entire time-space territory, and now Star Sword Ancestor had also stepped into this level, standing among the very top ranks of the time-space territory.
“Greetings, Star Lord!”
The Eight Extremes all said respectfully.
No matter what, Star Sword Ancestor was no longer a half-step Eternal Realm existence, but an Eternal Realm lord, and moreover one at the Immortal Realm level. Aside from Ye Tian, no one else dared be disrespectful toward Star Lord.
“Today, I have proved the Immortal Path, so I shall give a lecture here. From today onward, I will no longer be a member of the Eight Extreme Tower.”
Star Lord said.
The other Eight Extreme Realm experts remained silent. The Eight Extreme Tower was originally a joint force of Eight Extreme Realm or Nine Extreme Realm experts, and once someone broke through to the Eternal Realm, they had to leave the Eight Extreme Tower. That was the rule.
So they did not try to stop Star Lord.
After that.
Star Lord expounded on his own path, and even spoke of many experiences such as how to charge into the Eternal Realm and how to charge into the Nine Extreme Realm. This lecture lasted a full one hundred thousand years before it finally ended.
Afterward, Star Lord and Ye Tian chatted privately.
“Endless Ancestor, your degree of fusion between will and body has probably long since surpassed ten percent. When do you plan to charge into the Eternal Realm?”
Star Lord asked curiously.
“I’ll wait a bit longer. I myself am a source sea being, so I have no lifespan limit, and there’s no rush!” Ye Tian said with a smile.
“That’s true!” Star Lord nodded slightly, but still reminded him: “However, Endless Ancestor, there’s no need for you to stay in the Nine Extreme Realm for too long. Only after becoming an Immortal Realm existence will your strength become greater, allowing you to obtain more opportunities. In the time-space territory, the Nine Extreme Realm actually isn’t that strong, and it’s hard to gain many benefits.”
“I understand!”
Ye Tian nodded.
……
“Star Sword Ancestor has broken through to the Eternal Realm. In this era of the time-space territory, I’m the only Nine Extreme Realm expert left!”
Ye Tian murmured.
At this time.
His strength had already approached a limit. He had gained many benefits in the ruins of the Silent Void Battlefield, and had also obtained many inheritances, even secret techniques that could improve his will—similar to the Will World, and even stronger than the Will World—which made it much easier for Ye Tian to perfect the secret technique of the Will World.
In addition, Ye Tian had also found some treasures that could strengthen and temper his will. By now, he had already opened up fifty thousand Will Worlds, and his will had fused further with the innate eternal rules.
As for the fusion between will and body, it had finally reached the level of thirty percent.
Not only that, Ye Tian had also created a legendary-level will Eternal Technique, greatly increasing his strength.
Moreover, Ye Tian’s half-step Eternal power had also been compressed almost to its current limit, compressed by nearly 300 times.
After some more years passed, Ye Tian’s strength improvement became slower and slower, to the point of almost stagnating.
Moreover, it had also become difficult for him to hunt Void Beasts now.
In the end, Ye Tian decided to first break through to the Eternal Realm. Then he would be able to copy the body of a Void Beast, making it much easier to hunt Void Beasts. Even after breaking through to the Immortal Realm, it would be somewhat harder to improve the fusion of will and body, but at least he would have a large amount of Void Beast materials, and his cultivation speed would definitely be faster than it was now.
Thus, he no longer hesitated and had his true body break through directly in the Source Sea, without notifying anyone.
Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian chose to break through here. Once he completed the breakthrough, the Dark Forbidden Domain would probably undergo another transformation and surpass the level of Eternal Heavens.
During the breakthrough process, Ye Tian spread the word and ordered the human race not to look directly at his breakthrough process.
After that, Ye Tian officially began his breakthrough.
Boom!!!!!
Boundless innate eternal rules, under Ye Tian’s control, began to merge with his body.
Gradually, every particle of his body completely fused with the innate eternal rules, transforming into innate eternal particles.
At the same time, boundless source energy poured into Ye Tian’s body, helping him complete the final qualitative transformation.
Ye Tian’s aura also became more and more terrifying, spreading throughout the entire Dark Forbidden Domain and even the boundless territories around it.
At this moment, Ye Tian seemed to have transformed into a giant, with one foot enough to crush many Eternal Heavens. Even all the endless living beings in the Source Sea seemed to sense a terrifying existence. They could not look directly at it, could not see it, yet still faintly sensed it in the dark.
After a long time.
Ye Tian’s body completed its final transformation. His body and the innate eternal rules had already completely fused, and from now on, every casual strike of his would be the most perfect utilization of the innate eternal rules, every blow being an attack like innate eternal rules themselves.
Not only that, because his will and body had fused, and the degree of fusion had reached more than thirty percent, his will surged dramatically, and his bodily strength also surged dramatically.
Now, Ye Tian had not only entered the Immortal Realm, but even among Immortal Realm experts, he was considered a relatively powerful one.
Based on the fusion of will and body, Eternal Realm experts regarded a ten percent fusion as just having advanced into the Immortal Realm, known as a One-Step Immortal Realm existence, while a ninety percent fusion was a Nine-Step Immortal Realm existence. Ye Tian had now taken the third step, becoming a Three-Step Immortal Realm existence.
But if one also counted the legendary will Eternal Technique and many other legendary Eternal Techniques, his strength was probably far greater than that of many Four-Step, Five-Step, or even Six-Step Immortal Realm experts.
