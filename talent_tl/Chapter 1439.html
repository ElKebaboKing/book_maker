Chapter 1438: Today, I, Ye Tian, Shall Slay a Supreme Domain Ancestor!
!
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen, a middle-stage Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, was once an invincible Domain Ancestor who roamed the Dark Sacred Domain with great fame. He later ascended to the rank of Supreme-level Domain Ancestor and served as the Holy Lord of the Qingyuan Sacred Ground. Currently, he was the Supreme Elder of the Qingyuan Sacred Ground.
"Domain Ancestor Wanfa, you have killed so many Invincible Domain Ancestors of my Qingyuan Sacred Ground and severed our foundation. Today, I will not let you off!"
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen roared in fury.
At this moment, he would not be deterred by Ye Tian's identity. Even if he killed Ye Tian, so what? It wasn't as if the War Gate would come to kill him. Besides, this time it was Ye Tian who had broken into the Qingyuan Sacred Ground and slaughtered their Invincible Domain Ancestors. Even if there was a cause for it, the Qingyuan Sacred Ground still held the moral high ground.
Therefore, even if he truly killed Ye Tian, there would absolutely be no repercussions.
"Die!"
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen clawed toward Ye Tian. With this single grab, countless laws shattered, reaching an extreme level of terror.
In the shadows.
Invincible Domain Ancestors from other factions also peered into the situation here across vast territories.
Upon seeing Saint Ancestor Jin Chen take action, their hearts trembled.
This was a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor taking action; they could easily obliterate an Invincible Domain Ancestor. Even a One-Step Invincible Domain Ancestor was no match for a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor. Only certain existences who had surpassed the Invincible Domain Ancestor level, such as Domain Ancestor Five Thunders, could rely on terrifying combat power to fight a weaker Supreme-level Domain Ancestor for a few rounds...
And Saint Ancestor Jin Chen was not... the kind of weaker Supreme-level Domain Ancestor who had just ascended; he was at the middle stage and had been at that level for endless ages.
Consequently, no one believed Ye Tian could contend with Saint Ancestor Jin Chen.
Right at this moment, Ye Tian revealed his Ancestral Body.
Rumble!!!! The Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body manifested, bolstered by numerous Ancestral Techniques. He then thrust a spear toward Saint Ancestor Jin Chen.
With a bang! Ye Tian's attack collided with Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's palm.
Instantly, the light of annihilation swept through everything. Some Qingyuan Sacred Ground Domain Ancestors who were watching the commotion and had not yet moved far enough away were caught in the aftermath and instantly turned to ash.
Ye Tian only retreated a short distance, whereas Saint Ancestor Jin Chen was sent flying backward.
In the shadows.
The spying Invincible Domain Ancestors were stunned.
"A Supreme-level Domain Ancestor was sent flying?"
"Domain Ancestor Wanfa's strength is too terrifying; he actually managed to knock back Saint Ancestor Jin Chen! Could it be that Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's injuries were too severe and he hasn't recovered yet, leading him to be careless?"
"It's not carelessness. Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's injuries are likely over ninety percent healed. Look closely at Ye Tian's condition—that is an Ancestral Body, and the aura of various Ancestral Techniques is surfacing on his body!"
"Ye Tian's Saint Body was the Eternal Indestructible Saint Body, the number one Saint Body in the Dark Sacred Domain. Now he has broken through the shackles and condensed the Eternal Indestructible Ancestral Body.
Furthermore, he knows too many Ancestral Techniques, which is why he can erupt with such terrifying combat power!"
....Golden Silk Ancestral Technique! Void Slash! Void Escape Ancestral Technique! Ye Tian unleashed his full combat power, especially the Void Slash technique, which was immensely powerful.
Every Void Slash hit Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's body, breaking through his Supreme-level Domain Body and wounding him.
Meanwhile, Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's attacks could not hit Ye Tian, who possessed the Void Escape Ancestral Technique.
"Saint Ancestor Jin Chen, you don't even have an Ancestral Body and don't know a single Ancestral Technique, yet you want to kill me?"
Ye Tian mocked him mercilessly.
"If I were to step into the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor rank—no, even if I just broke the limit of the Invincible Domain Body and underwent just one transformation, I could easily kill you!"
Ye Tian continued.
"Damn it!"
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen could not refute him because what Ye Tian said was the truth.
He could see that Ye Tian's degree of control over his physical body was not high, only a few dozen times. If he controlled it to 365 times, he would be far from being able to contend with Ye Tian.
Once Ye Tian truly completed one transformation of the Invincible Domain Body, he would truly be killed by him.
Currently, he was only relying on the advantages of a Supreme-level Domain Body and the recovery capabilities of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor to contend with Ye Tian.
However, as the slaughter continued, Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's injuries grew heavier and heavier.
Ordinary attacks actually couldn't cause much damage to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor; their Supreme-level Domain Bodies could directly ignore most attacks. But the attacks brought by Ancestral Techniques and Ancestral Bodies caused far too much damage to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
Whether it was an Ancestral Body or an Ancestral Technique, they actually involved the Progenitor level.
To a Progenitor, a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor was no different from an ant.
Thus, Ancestral Bodies and Ancestral Techniques were extremely terrifying things. Even many Supreme-level Domain Ancestors hungered for them. Anyone who could master an Ancestral Body or an Ancestral Technique would stand above ordinary Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
It was just like the former leader of the Ghost Fox organization, Emperor Ancestor Ghost Fox. Despite being a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor and the scion of a Progenitor, because he had not mastered an Ancestral Body or an Ancestral Technique, he was toyed with by the avatar of War Ancestor Fire Rune.
That Emperor Ancestor Ghost Fox's strength was even inferior to Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian's Void Slash hit Saint Ancestor Jin Chen again. A portion of Saint Ancestor Jin Chen's physical body was annihilated. Although he restored his body instantly, his injuries became even more severe.
Suddenly! Saint Ancestor Jin Chen pleaded for mercy: "Domain Ancestor Wanfa, our Qingyuan Sacred Ground apologizes. How about we call it even for today's matters?"
"Call it even?"
Ye Tian sneered, "I have killed so many Invincible Domain Ancestors of your Qingyuan Sacred Ground; it's impossible for you to let it go. I will not leave any hidden dangers for myself. Today, I shall slay a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor!"
"Domain Ancestor Wanfa, don't be too arrogant! With just you, you can't kill me!"
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen was enraged.
Rumble!!!! The battle continued!
Suddenly, Saint Ancestor Jin Chen took out a piece of paper. A look of heartache appeared on his face; clearly, this piece of paper was extraordinary and incredibly precious, one of his most valuable possessions.
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen activated the paper, and a terrifying creature emerged.
It was a humanoid creature, but with a hideous face like a demon, standing about thirty thousand feet tall, its body covered in scales.
It was merely a phantom, yet it gave everyone a sense of great horror.
Abruptly.
The humanoid phantom extended its claws and grabbed toward Ye Tian. The speed wasn't very fast, but it seemed to ignore time itself, or rather, as if time had stopped.
Ye Tian couldn't dodge; he couldn't even activate the Void Escape Ancestral Technique before his body was pierced through by the claw.
Pfft! Ye Tian spat out blood incessantly, heavily wounded.
"Indestructible Ancestral Technique, Recover!"
Ye Tian activated the Indestructible Ancestral Technique. The wounds on his body healed rapidly, though some lingering power resisted, trying to keep the wounds open. However, under the effect of the Indestructible Ancestral Technique, it was ground away bit by bit.
"What exactly is that phantom? It doesn't seem to be a Taboo Creature, nor does it look like the Destruction Saint Race of the Dark Destruction Domain or the Three Thousand Evil Races! It seems to be a special kind of creature!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
But now was not the time to think about that; he had to kill Saint Ancestor Jin Chen.
At this moment, Saint Ancestor Jin Chen was dumbfounded.
"You actually know the Indestructible Ancestral Technique as well!"
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen showed a look of desperation.
If Ye Tian didn't have the Indestructible Ancestral Technique to recover from his injuries, that strike just now would have surely crippled him, causing him to lose eighty to ninety percent of his combat power.
But now, Ye Tian's injuries were recovering too quickly.
"Flee!"
Saint Ancestor Jin Chen did not want to die; he could only choose to run.
