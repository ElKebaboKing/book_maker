There seemed to be nothing here, as if it were no different from any other place and nothing special at all.
But very soon, one Supreme Being after another gathered here; the lowest cultivation level among them was basically Seventh-Tier Supreme Being, and most were Ninth-Tier Supreme Beings as well as Half-Step Eternal Realm experts.
Ye Tian looked very inconspicuous here, but his strength was definitely not weak, placing him at an intermediate level.

Primal Sea.

“Let’s split up. Entering the Primal Sea requires Primal Leaves, but leaving does not. Follow the origin tide, and you can fly out.”

Ye Tian indicated that he understood. Above them was the origin tide, continuously letting origin flow out of space-time. Flying out along that path was enough to leave.
Supreme Beings or Half-Step Eternal Realm experts who entered here all needed to constantly pay attention to the state of the origin tide. Once there were signs that the origin tide was dispersing, they had to rush out immediately, otherwise they would easily become trapped.

He had already done well enough by bringing Ye Tian and the others into the Primal Sea; naturally, he would not continue to look after them.
Ye Tian casually chose a direction and began slowly searching for resources.
The Primal Sea had no living beings, but that did not mean it had no danger. On the contrary, it was very dangerous; if one lacked sufficient strength, one could easily fall.
One danger came from other Supreme Beings and even Half-Step Eternal Realm experts. If a Eighth-Tier Supreme Being found many treasures and a Half-Step Eternal Realm expert saw it, that expert might casually kill the Eighth-Tier Supreme Being; then there would be no need to search, and they could directly obtain a large amount of resources.
