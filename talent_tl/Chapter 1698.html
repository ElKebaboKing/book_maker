Chapter 1697 Calan Leaf! Auxiliary Eternal Treasure!

The five Legendary Realm lords did not linger in the Source Sea for too long; otherwise, the moment they saw Ye Tian, they would have thought of the Eternal Treasures and could not help but make a move, which would have completely torn their faces with Ye Tian.
Therefore, they still chose to leave the Source Sea with no sight of it, no pain in their hearts, and directly returned to their own eternal temporal-spatial territories.
However, they did send quite a few of their subordinates to garrison the temporal-spatial territories. If the Source Sea continued to give birth to Eternal Treasures, they would be able to know at the first moment.
……
Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian was studying the Eternal Treasure he had just obtained. Through refining it, he learned that this Eternal Treasure was called the Calan Leaf. Judging from its power, the Calan Leaf was much better than the Eternal Treasure Hunyuan Token, and was fully comparable to Qingxu Bell, which belonged to Jingxu.
The Calan Leaf had no small number of uses. It could save his life and also help Ye Tian escape, making it an auxiliary Eternal Treasure.
This kind of Eternal Treasure was extremely popular, and was even rarer than attack-type Eternal Treasures.
Especially when exploring certain ruins, preserving one’s life was particularly important.
Sometimes, having stronger attack power was of no use at all; when facing a desperate situation, one could hardly survive.
The Calan Leaf could allow Ye Tian, when facing a fatal attack, to almost protect all the particles in his body. This method made him nearly immortal, unless the enemy he faced was too powerful.
Moreover, the Calan Leaf could allow Ye Tian to split into countless forms and flee, similar to the Void Light method of a Tier 5 Void Beast. A Tier 5 Void Beast could transform into billions upon billions of strands of Void Light and rush into the depths of the void region; even Legendary Realm lords would not have time to stop it.
The Calan Leaf had a similar effect as well, and even a better one, with greater speed. It did not even require self-mutilation, whereas the Void Light of a Void Beast was in fact a self-damaging method. Although it could save one’s life, the cost was great, so unless a Tier 5 Void Beast was facing utter desperation, it would not use it.
So to a certain extent, the Calan Leaf was too heaven-defying.
“Not bad. Hunyuan Token can enhance my strength, while the Calan Leaf can enhance my life-preserving ability. With these two Eternal Treasures in hand, even if the five Legendary Realm lords of the temporal-spatial territories join forces to besiege me, I still won’t fear them!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In addition, if Ye Tian’s clone body were to conduct experiments in the future, with the help of the Calan Leaf, it would not collapse so easily, which would be of even greater help to Ye Tian.
“My next goal is still to cultivate to the level of a Legendary Realm lord as soon as possible. As long as I do not reach the Legendary Realm lord realm, it will be very difficult to suppress other Legendary Realm lords.”
Thus, Ye Tian worked hard at cultivation, increasing the degree of fusion between his will and body, allowing his cultivation to constantly approach the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord. Only when he reached the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord could he attempt to break through to the Legendary Realm lord realm.
With the help of Hunyuan Token, Ye Tian was naturally drawing closer and closer to the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord. In fact, he was not too far from the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord now; only, the closer one got to the Limit, the harder it became to improve.
For example, going from 96% to 97% fusion was already extremely difficult, and 98% to 99% was also extremely difficult.
The so-called Extreme was ninety-nine point ninety-nine ninety-nine...
This was infinitely close to 100% fusion, and even looked like 100% fusion, but in the dark, there was still that tiny bit that had not fused. This was the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord, and also a bottleneck and shackle. In fact, many Nine-Step Immortal Realm lords at the Extreme could not even find that last unfused trace, and simply did not know how to attempt a breakthrough to the Legendary Realm lord realm.
This was the so-called bottleneck shackle!
If one truly found it, then the shackle would be broken and one would advance to become a Legendary Realm lord, but very few could truly break through.
However, Ye Tian was still very confident in himself; he believed 100% that he could break through.
In the blink of an eye, five hundred thousand Eternal years passed.
At this time, the degree of fusion between Ye Tian’s will and body had already reached more than 99%, and exactly how far it had reached was difficult to estimate.
However, Ye Tian felt that he was still a tiny bit short of the peak of the Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord, because he could still continue to fuse his will and body, albeit very, very slowly.
Finally, after another fifty thousand Eternal years had passed, Ye Tian felt that his entire body had already completely fused with his will, but he still had not broken through to the Legendary Realm lord realm.
At this point, Ye Tian knew clearly that he had already reached the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord, and was only one last thread away from breaking through to the Legendary Realm lord tier.
For a very long time after that, Ye Tian made no progress at all, as if he had been blocked by a bottleneck.
But Ye Tian was not anxious in the slightest. Every clone body was cultivating on its own, preparing for the assault on the Legendary Realm lord realm.
On this day.
Ye Tian discussed the Dao with Lord Bing Han. Among the five Legendary Realm lords, Ye Tian and Lord Bing Han had a rather good relationship.
“Lord Bing Han, how can an Immortal Realm lord break through to the Legendary Realm lord realm?” Ye Tian asked for guidance.
Lord Bing Han smiled slightly and said, “It seems that Lord Endless has cultivated to the Extreme of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord. This speed really makes me ashamed. As for how to break through to the Legendary Realm lord, every Legendary Realm lord’s breakthrough method is different. Some experienced endless battles and finally comprehended it, thereby breaking through to the Legendary Realm lord. Some took certain eternal treasures and thus completed the breakthrough. Some experienced long years of bitter cultivation and, without realizing it, broke through. As for me, I entered a wondrous sight and fell into a long period of enlightenment, which allowed me to completely finish the last trace of fusion and break through to the Legendary Realm lord. If you asked me to give some advice, I really wouldn’t know what to say.”
“So that’s how it is. Many thanks for Lord Bing Han’s guidance!”
Ye Tian said gratefully.
After some time, the Dao discussion between Ye Tian and Lord Bing Han ended.
On this day.
Ye Tian was cultivating, pondering how to break through to the Legendary Realm lord realm.
Suddenly, he thought of one point.
The Legendary Realm lords on the temporal-spatial territories’ side cultivated by fusing their will with the innate eternal rules, and only in this way could they grow step by step and eventually attempt to reach the Lord of Legends realm.
In fact, his attainments in the fusion of will and innate eternal rules had surpassed many Legendary Realm lords.
If he forcibly fused the power after the fusion of will and innate eternal rules with his body, it would be equivalent to stimulating the body. This would count as forced fusion, but it might reveal the final point of inability within the body.
If that were the case, he could break through to the Legendary Realm lord realm.
