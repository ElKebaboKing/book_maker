Chapter 1569: Breakthrough, Second-tier Invincible!

With the Sun of All Things in hand, Ye Tian left the Void Source Hall and returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
As the Dark Forbidden Domain was Ye Tian's birthplace, he naturally placed great importance on this world. In the future, he was very confident in promoting to an Invincible. If he could cultivate two more Supreme Beings, the Dark Forbidden Domain could be promoted to an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
However, under normal circumstances, there was no hope for the Dark Forbidden Domain to give birth to another two Supreme Beings. But the Sun of All Things could change the potential and constitution of the human race, giving them a glimmer of hope.
Coupled with his assistance, it was not impossible for two more Supreme Beings to be born in the future.
Dark Forbidden Domain.
Ye Tian placed the Sun of All Things in the very center of the Dark Forbidden Domain, using its radiance to shine upon the entire Dark Forbidden Domain.
Additionally, he sealed off the area where the Sun of All Things was located, forbidding any humans from approaching, and stationed an Invincible avatar there to guard it.
As for the Xumi Golden Mountain side, he didn't pay much attention to it.
Under the influence of the Sun of All Things, the human race underwent tremendous changes.
Within a world of the human race, a child was born. Right at birth, he was comparable to a powerful martial artist, his body glowing with light, clearly no ordinary person.
The locals thought the child was the reincarnation of some great expert, but soon they discovered that all newborn children were the same.
Furthermore, some ordinary people found their strength advancing by leaps and bounds, their physical bodies becoming stronger and stronger, and even some disabled people returned to normal.
Many were filled with doubt, thinking that a special change had occurred in this world.
But the higher-ups were very clear that this change was not limited to a single world; it affected the entire Dark Forbidden Domain.
Through the human high-level officials, many finally learned the reason for the change: the strongest human, Ye Tian, had brought back a supreme treasure that could change the potential and foundation of the human race, making each generation stronger until they could eventually rival the powerful races within an Eternal Heaven and Earth.
Immediately, the human race was in an uproar. Countless people were beyond excited, even beginning to cultivate descendants in large numbers to make their families stronger.
Moreover, this influence was continuous. As long as they were humans below the level of Ancestor, they would become stronger and stronger under the influence of the Sun of All Things, their potential becoming increasingly terrifying.
……
"It's time to charge for the Second-tier Invincible realm!"
Ye Tian decided.
He had delayed in the Third-tier Invincible realm for a long time; continuing to delay served no purpose.
He was now almost unable to make further progress and had to break through his realm.
As for the safety of the Dark Forbidden Domain, he didn't need to worry much.
Although a Second-tier Invincible could not enter a foreign world, if the Dark Forbidden Domain truly encountered danger, his Invincible avatar had not yet broken through to the Second-tier Invincible realm and could still guard the Dark Forbidden Domain. He planned to keep this Invincible avatar at the Third-tier Invincible realm indefinitely, never promoting it unless the Dark Forbidden Domain one day promoted to an Eternal Heaven and Earth, at which point the Invincible avatar would continue its breakthrough.
Ye Tian did not choose to break through in the Dark Forbidden Domain, but instead chose to do so in his own cultivation space within the Void Source Hall.
Void Source Hall, cultivation space.
Ye Tian began his secluded cultivation here, putting his soul through Tribulation Realm cultivation.
By now, his soul was already incomparably powerful, so undergoing Tribulation Realm cultivation was naturally very easy.
"Begin the first soul tribulation!"
Buzz!!!!
Ye Tian's soul began to endure the Rule Thunder, Rule Flames, and Rule Wind Blades from the heavens and earth... but these attacks were like a mere itch to Ye Tian.
Soon, the first tribulation ended.
At this time, in terms of the Soul Tribulation Realm, he was now at the First Soul Tribulation Realm.
After resting for a while, Ye Tian began the second tribulation.
The second tribulation was equally easy for Ye Tian. After all, his soul was so powerful that it was much stronger than many Invincibles who had promoted from the Soul Tribulation Realm.
Third tribulation!
Fourth tribulation!
……
It didn't take long for Ye Tian to complete nine tribulations, becoming a Ninth Soul Tribulation Realm expert, at the same tribulation level as his previous Ninth Physical Tribulation Realm.
Breaking through from the Ninth Soul Tribulation Realm to the Invincible realm was very simple for Ye Tian, but it also required a large amount of resources.
Ye Tian prepared many Tribulation Stones, absorbing their energy to help himself break through the shackles and promote to the Soul Invincible realm.
Boom!!!!
His soul broke through, crossing into the Invincible realm. At this time, his soul could be called an Invincible Indestructible Soul...
If a Sixth-tier Invincible Indestructible Soul were to promote to a Third-tier Invincible Indestructible Soul, the difficulty would be immense. However, Ye Tian's own realm was already at the Third-tier Invincible realm, so he could quickly raise it to the Third-tier Invincible realm.
Of course, a Soul Invincible could also cultivate Invincible Tribulation Body techniques, as a Soul Invincible Tribulation Body was also a type of Invincible Tribulation Body.
Therefore, Ye Tian had his Soul Tribulation Body cultivate the Endless Source Body, a Void Source Body technique.
Ye Tian had already mastered the Endless Source Body himself, so having his soul cultivate it did not consume additional Eternal Source Substance energy or Void Source Crystals; otherwise, Ye Tian would have had to continue searching for Void Source Crystals.
The following years were very simple, just consuming a large amount of resources to cultivate.
With his contribution points in the Sky Wind Sea, he could exchange for a large amount of resources to meet his cultivation needs, so he would not lack resources at all.
In just one Great Tribulation Era, Ye Tian successfully cultivated his soul path to the Third-tier Invincible Extreme stage, reaching the same level as his physical body path.
Physical Tribulation Realm Third-tier Invincible, Soul Tribulation Realm Third-tier Invincible.
When both Tribulation Realms reached such a level, the transformation began.
Ye Tian's soul and physical body seemed to break through their shackles, and under their mutual influence, their Limit was greatly increased.
Subsequently, Ye Tian absorbed countless resources, and both his physical body and soul surged many times over.
At this moment, he was already a Second-tier Invincible. Once he cultivated his physical body and soul to the Second-tier Invincible Limit, he could attempt to perfectly fuse the Physical Tribulation Realm and Soul Tribulation Realm to step into the First-tier Invincible realm. However, this step would inevitably be difficult and require a very long time.
"With my current strength, I'm afraid even many First-tier Invincibles are no match for me!"
Ye Tian thought.
Since he had just broken through, he still had a lot of room for improvement.
Therefore, Ye Tian did not immediately end his seclusion but continued to cultivate.
Besides, his main body didn't need to go out; his duplicate body could handle matters in the outside world.
