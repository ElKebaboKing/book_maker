Chapter 1676 Success, the Eternal Technique of Will, Another Road!

After Ye Tian took his leave, he returned.
Then.
Ye Tian’s copied body began studying the path of fusing will with innate Eternal rules. He was very clear that in the time-space domain, other Eternal realm experts did not dare to walk this path, so he had almost no information to refer to and could only grope forward step by step on his own.
Buzz！！！！
A large amount of innate Eternal rules were mobilized by Ye Tian, and then the power of will was drawn forth to begin attempting fusion with the innate Eternal rules.
He did not know how to fuse them, so he could only force the fusion, constantly making will and the innate Eternal rules collide.
At a certain moment.
Will shattered, and this copied body turned into a corpse, disappearing in the blink of an eye.
“It failed!”
Ye Tian muttered.
He had long anticipated this failure, yet he still tried it.
If one wants to walk a road no one else has walked before, countless attempts are necessary; this is also an inevitable process.
In the days that followed.
Ye Tian attempted fusion again and again, failing time and time again. After countless failures, Ye Tian finally prepared to try another method of fusion, which was to let the innate Eternal rules attempt to fuse into the will world.
If the will world and the innate Eternal rules were allowed to fuse, it would be equivalent to allowing the will world to give birth to rules. This also counted as a kind of fusion, and the difficulty would be somewhat lower and easier.
Otherwise, to have his own will completely fuse with the innate Eternal rules all at once was almost impossible. This was also an experience Ye Tian had summed up after countless failed attempts.
Thus.
Ye Tian began drawing a wisp of innate Eternal rules into one of his will worlds, starting fusion with the will within the will world, thereby allowing rules to be born inside the will world.
Sizzle sizzle sizzle!!!!
The will world began to collapse during the fusion of rules, and very soon it shattered completely. But Ye Tian’s will was also injured, and his fleshly body suffered severe damage, though this time he did not die.
“Perhaps I’m on the right path!”
Ye Tian said in delight.
Afterwards, he tried to fuse several more times before this copied body finally collapsed completely.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian had the innate Eternal rules fuse with will worlds. After many failed attempts, he gradually accumulated some experience and even began to sketch out a rudimentary path.
Ye Tian was extremely firm in his belief that will and the innate Eternal rules could be fused.
While deducing, Ye Tian also kept trying, and after countless experiments, he finally gained something.
On this day.
As usual, Ye Tian’s innate Eternal rules were fusing with will inside the will world. This time, the innate Eternal rules transformed into strands of threads, forming a seal-character array formation.
This seal-character array formation had the effect of being compatible with all things, so Ye Tian fused with will in this way; the success rate would be higher. He had already modified the array formation and tried it many times.
Under his control, the innate Eternal rules and will began to fuse.
At a certain moment.
The two actually began to fuse slowly.
Success!!!
Though this was only a beginning, for Ye Tian it was the hope of an entirely new path.
Not long after, the innate Eternal rules and the will within this will world slowly fused together.
With this beginning, Ye Tian’s subsequent experiments went much more smoothly.
In the trials that followed, Ye Tian’s innate Eternal rules fused with one will world after another, but the path of fusion between will and the innate Eternal rules was endless; he had merely opened the beginning.
After Ye Tian’s will and the innate Eternal rules initially fused, he truly touched the threshold of the Eternal Technique of Will.
By now, he understood that the Eternal Technique of Will was to study the power formed after fusing will with the innate Eternal rules, then unleash that power through the creation of an Eternal technique. This was the Eternal Technique of Will.
And Ye Tian now possessed this power, and after this power was born, he also learned its name.
Will rule power!
Without the birth of will rule power, it would simply be impossible to create an Eternal technique. It was the power foundation of the Eternal Technique of Will; without this foundation, one could not even step through the threshold.
At this moment, Ye Tian thought of the sword in the ruins that he could not touch. Perhaps he could use that sword’s sword Dao aura, combined with will rule power and the power system of strength, to create a sword Dao Eternal Technique of Will containing overbearing power.
Having thought of it, he acted on it.
Ye Tian began trying to create such a sword Dao Eternal Technique of Will. Since this was only the beginning of creation, he did not set high requirements; as long as he could create an ordinary Eternal Technique of Will, that would be enough.
Because Ye Tian’s talent in power was a legendary ancestor talent, his creation speed was very fast when it came to matters involving strength.
In merely a hundred thousand tribulation yuan of time, he successfully created an Eternal Technique of Will, and it was only slightly inferior to a top-tier Eternal technique.
This Eternal Technique of Will was called Will Severing Void Sword. Although it was only an ordinary Eternal technique, other Eternal realm experts had no means to deal with Eternal Techniques of Will, so an ordinary Eternal Technique of Will could even possess deterrent power comparable to a legendary Eternal technique.
Then.
Ye Tian also created Eternal Techniques of Will in other areas, such as defense. If he created a defensive Eternal Technique of Will, then when challenging the True Void Legend Tower, he could easily withstand the Eternal realm will attacks of those Eternal realm experts, thereby reaching higher tiers.
Unknowingly, several Eternal years passed.
By now, Ye Tian had already created multiple Eternal Techniques of Will.
For example, the defensive Eternal Technique of Will——Will Wall Light!
The speed-type Eternal Technique of Will——Will Shadow Light!
In addition, Ye Tian also created another attack-type Eternal Technique of Will——Will Extreme Path Fist, an offensive Eternal Technique of Will he created based on the Eternal Extreme Path Fist.
Creating these Eternal Techniques of Will greatly increased Ye Tian’s strength.
In the dark, he felt that his body had undergone a special change. Although his power had not increased, his will had increased by a great deal and was full of vitality.
More importantly, Ye Tian’s control over will had increased severalfold.
This made Ye Tian even more convinced that this path was extremely correct.
On this day.
Ye Tian once again arrived at the True Void Legend Tower. He was preparing to challenge the True Void Legend Tower again and see how many tiers he could reach.
He firmly believed that he could easily pass the eleventh tier, and even the twelfth tier.
The eleventh tier of the True Void Legend Tower.
Ye Tian appeared here, and three Eternal realm experts emerged. They all displayed Eternal Techniques of Will to attack Ye Tian.
At this moment, a golden light appeared on Ye Tian’s body, easily blocking the attacks of the three Eternal realm experts.
This was Will Wall Light!
A legendary Eternal technique could not completely block will attacks, but an ordinary Eternal technique like Will Wall Light did block them. This was precisely the restraint of Eternal Techniques of Will against Eternal Techniques of Will.
“．‖ Will Eternal Fist々〃!”
Ye Tian threw out a punch that shattered everything, and behind him appeared the phantom shadows of billions of will worlds, crushing through the ages of space-time and pressing down.
The void shattered, Eternal rules broke, and everything before him turned into nothingness.
Meanwhile, the three Eternal realm experts were all sent flying, each suffering no light injuries.
“Will Severing Void Sword, fall!”
A Will Severing Void Sword slashed out from the void, piercing one Eternal realm expert with a single strike, and the other’s body immediately collapsed.
Very quickly, Ye Tian shattered the other two Eternal realm experts as well, successfully passing the eleventh tier with complete ease.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian arrived at the twelfth tier.
