Chapter 1668: The Eight Extremes Tower Joins Forces to Slay the Void Beast!

Not long after, the Lord Alliance prepared to launch a general assault on the Void Beast.
Although the Lord Alliance did not force other strong cultivators to join the battle this time, many Eight Extremes Stage experts were prepared to take part.
Ye Tian immediately invited the other Eight Extremes Stage experts at the Eight Extremes Tower, and very soon many of them arrived.
“Infinite Ancestor, why have you invited us here?”
one Eight Extremes Stage expert asked.
Ye Tian went straight to the point: “Let’s join forces to kill the Void Beast!”
At this, another Eight Extremes Stage expert said, “The Void Beast is very hard to kill, and if we run into a Second-Tier Void Beast, we may be in danger of losing our lives. The other Eight Extremes Stage experts have all attached themselves to some Undying Realm lord, following behind those Undying Realm lords to hold back the Void Beasts, while the Undying Realm lords personally move in to kill the First-Tier Void Beasts. But even if we Eight Extremes Stage experts join forces, we still can’t kill a Void Beast!”
“I’m confident I can kill Void Beasts!”
Ye Tian said confidently.
“You?”
Many of the Eight Extremes Stage experts present did not believe it.
One of them said, “Infinite Ancestor, you haven’t even touched the barrier of the Second Boundary yet. You’ve only just become an Eight Extremes Stage expert. On what basis should we believe you have the strength to kill Void Beasts?”
“What if I have an Eternal art that can injure a Void Beast in its ethereal state, and the damage it causes isn’t low? Moreover, I can dispatch three clones. With your cooperation, if we find a lone Void Beast, then we’ll have a certain chance of killing a First-Tier Void Beast!”
Ye Tian said earnestly.
“Really?”
Some of the Eight Extremes Stage experts were tempted and asked urgently.
So Ye Tian displayed his Eternal art—Void Piercing Secret Sword.
When the illusory small sword was unleashed, it pierced through the void-time territory and entered the void region, even drilling through the void region itself.
Although Ye Tian did not attack a Void Beast directly, the Eight Extremes Stage experts present were all very experienced, and after a little deduction, they confirmed that this kind of attack was precisely an Eternal art developed specifically to target Void Beasts.
“Infinite Ancestor, you actually managed to create such an Eternal art!”
The other Eight Extremes Stage and half-step Eternal Realm experts were all shocked beyond words.
If they were the ones to create it, even after countless Eternal years they still wouldn’t be able to do so!
However, some Eight Extremes Stage experts did not have clones, and were therefore extremely cautious with their lives.
Even so, quite a few Eight Extremes Stage experts were willing to follow Ye Tian to kill Void Beasts, among them one Eight Extremes Stage expert who had touched the barrier of the Second Boundary—Ancestor Gu He.
Ancestor Gu He had quite good luck; he had not died in the Abyssless Land before, but he had suffered heavy injuries, and so after recuperating until now, he had recovered.
Originally, he had planned to try breaking through to the Eternal Realm, but this time he had heard about the situation with the Void Beasts.
Void Beasts could enhance one’s will, and could even help the will and the body merge. Although they were of greater help to the Undying Realm and Immortal Realm, if one’s will improved, the hope of breaking through would increase a little more.
Therefore, he also wanted to hunt Void Beasts.
But he alone could not kill any Void Beast, so he could only rely on Ye Tian.
In the end, about five hundred Eight Extremes Stage experts were willing to follow Ye Tian to hunt Void Beasts. After everyone was ready, they came to the battlefield in the void region.
……
The battlefield in the void region was very large, and Ye Tian and the others naturally would not go to the central battlefield; otherwise, if they encountered a Second-Tier Void Beast, it would be troublesome. If they encountered Third- or Fourth-Tier Void Beasts, even escaping would likely be a luxury.
Therefore, it was enough to linger at the edge.
Ye Tian’s three cloned bodies scattered out to search for traces of Void Beasts. Once one was found, he would notify the other Eight Extremes Stage experts.
Time slowly passed, and everyone was extremely cautious.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian discovered traces of a First-Tier Void Beast, and immediately notified the other Eight Extremes Stage and half-step Eternal Realm experts to come here. Meanwhile, he fought with this First-Tier Void Beast to prevent it from escaping, because tracking it down later would be troublesome.
Therefore, it had to be held back.
Ye Tian only restrained it and did not immediately use the Eternal art Void Piercing Secret Sword, in case the other side became wary and fled.
Very soon, the other Eight Extremes Stage experts from the Eight Extremes Tower arrived.
Boom!!!!!
One Eight Extremes Stage expert after another sealed off this stretch of void, and one great formation after another was set up, all to trap this Void Beast so that there would be hope of killing it.
This Void Beast sensed the crisis and instinctively released a distress signal. If there were other Void Beasts nearby, they would also instinctively come to support it.
This was one of the reasons Void Beasts were so hard to kill.
Whoosh!!!
Three Ye Tians appeared and began using Void Piercing Secret Sword to attack this Void Beast.
At the same time, the other idle Eight Extremes Stage experts also attacked with all their strength. Even if they could only inflict a little damage, the damage would be decent when stacked together.

Pfft! Pfft! Pfft!!!!
Three Void Piercing Secret Swords struck the Void Beast, causing it some damage and making it scream miserably. Then the other Eight Extremes Stage attacks also landed on it, but the vast majority of the damage was offset by its void state.
Even so, the Void Beast’s injuries still deepened somewhat.
Thud! Thud! Thud!!!
This Void Beast kept slamming against the formations and the sealed void, trying to break free from here.
However, the many Eight Extremes Stage experts would not give it a chance to escape.
Not long after, three more Void Beasts came over.
“Three hundred Eight Extremes Stage experts, go intercept those Void Beasts!”
.... ......... 0
Ye Tian ordered.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
Three hundred Eight Extremes Stage experts went to block them. They absolutely could not allow those three Void Beasts to reinforce this one.
Very soon, those three Void Beasts were also trapped and could not break Ye Tian’s formations here or interfere with Ye Tian.
After a little more time, this Void Beast, which had been attacked by Ye Tian, finally could not withstand the repeated assaults of the many Eight Extremes Stage experts. Its vitality dissipated, and it was completely blasted to death.
Immediately.
Ye Tian collected this Void Beast’s corpse and sent word to the other Eight Extremes Stage experts to withdraw at once. Otherwise, many more Void Beasts would soon arrive here, and if a Second-Tier Void Beast came, it would be a disaster.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!!
The many Eight Extremes Stage experts withdrew quickly and soon disappeared.
Returning to an ownerless void-time territory, the many Eight Extremes Stage experts began to rest and recover, while also dividing up the Void Beast.
Ye Tian had contributed the most, so naturally he received the largest share of the Void Beast materials.
In no time at all, this Void Beast had been divided up completely.
“Everyone, let’s continue hunting Void Beasts!”
Ye Tian said.
In the days that followed, the Eight Extremes Stage experts of the Eight Extremes Tower, under Ye Tian’s leadership, hunted one Void Beast after another. However, there were also setbacks; they encountered a Second-Tier Void Beast and lost one Eight Extremes Stage expert, but that was nothing, and no one chose to withdraw.
On this day.
The six chairmen of the Lord Alliance had trapped seven Fifth-Tier Void Beasts, and the encirclement had reached the most critical moment.
