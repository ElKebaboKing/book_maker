Chapter 1574: The Secret of the Void Source Hall!

The mysterious person was named Jin Zu. He was not a lifeform of the Eternal Heaven and Earth, but was born in the Source Sea; he was a Source Sea lifeform. He had lived for a very long time and was extremely powerful. At the level of a Supreme Being, he was considered to be at the top tier, many times stronger than a Supreme Being like Tianfeng Source God.
However, Jin Zu still encountered danger. He was besieged by many experts and was heavily wounded, to the point where his injuries were almost impossible to recover from.
In a certain-death situation, he extracted a bit of origin from the Eternal Soul Wood on his person, fused his own will into it, and began to wander through various foreign worlds.
Because it was a will, it was not restricted by the foreign worlds. After wandering for endless ages, when his own will could no longer be maintained, he finally found Ye Jinpeng.
"According to the memories, Jin Zu threw the Eternal Soul Wood on his person and his corpse into a forbidden land in the Source Sea—Longze!"
Longze was an incomparably dangerous forbidden land in the Source Sea. Without the strength of the Supreme Being realm, once one dared to enter Longze, their body would be directly subjected to Annihilation. Even Supreme Beings did not dare to step into Longze casually, as it was very difficult to sense directions within it.
Jin Zu did not want his pursuers to benefit, which was why he was so decisive.
Ye Tian naturally wanted to obtain the treasures on Jin Zu's body and the Eternal Soul Wood, but his current strength was insufficient. Even though he could duplicate his body time and again, there was no necessity; it would be a complete waste of time.
Once he stepped into the Supreme Being realm, he could consider going to Longze.
From Jin Zu's memories, Ye Tian also learned about many treasure lands. The information on these treasure lands alone was a priceless treasure.
More importantly, Jin Zu also knew the locations of three Eternal Source Objects, and they were unowned Eternal Source Objects.
The birth of an Eternal Source Object required time. Before they were fully born, they were very difficult to discover. However, as a Supreme Being, Jin Zu could naturally sense the birth of Eternal Source Objects. Therefore, he had immediately sealed those three locations, intending to retrieve them in the future.
Thus, those three Eternal Source Objects should still be there.
For Ye Tian, three Eternal Source Objects were also a great harvest.
But what truly excited Ye Tian was that he learned a secret—a secret about First-class Invincibles breaking through to Supreme Being.
When a First-class Invincible breaks through to Supreme Being, unless they are naturally compatible, they all need to forcibly refine the origin of an Eternal Source Object, and they can only refine one type of Eternal Source Object origin. Therefore, the more powerful the Eternal Source Object, the better.
However, several types of Eternal Source Objects were quite special. Once one's compatibility with them reached 100%, one could easily refine them. Furthermore, one could use them to refine the origins of other Eternal Source Objects, using the origins of multiple Eternal Source Objects to break through to the Supreme Being realm, becoming an incomparably terrifying Supreme Being.
And one of these types of Eternal Source Objects was the Void Source Tablet. It was once the Eternal Source Object of the Void Source Ancestor, and the Void Source Ancestor was an existence who had reached the Peak of the Supreme Being realm, and had even taken a half-step beyond the Peak.
Unfortunately, in the end, the Void Source Ancestor fell, but he still left behind the Void Source Hall.
This was a secret, a secret known to very few people.
Becoming the Void Lord was the final trial of the Void Source Ancestor. Once one became the Void Lord, they could obtain the inheritance of the Void Source Ancestor and use a special method to increase their compatibility with the Void Source Tablet to 100%. This way, they could absorb the origin of the Void Source Tablet and use it to absorb the origins of other Eternal Source Objects, gathering a large amount of Eternal Source Object origins to step into the Supreme Being realm.
Therefore, if one wanted to become the Void Lord, it had to be before becoming a Supreme Being. Otherwise, no matter how heaven-defying one's combat power was, the Void Source Hall would not allow them to pass.
So, once a Void God Son became a Supreme Being, it was equivalent to losing this opportunity. They no longer needed to go to the Void Source Hall, as they could no longer obtain any benefits.
"No wonder I don't see other Void God Sons in the Void Source Hall, and I've hardly heard any news about them. It turns out those Void God Sons likely already knew they couldn't obtain any benefits, so they completely gave up on the opportunities of the Void Source Hall," Ye Tian murmured.
The Void Source Hall still had no master, so a Void Lord had not yet been born, nor had a Void God Son with a natural 100% compatibility with the Void Source Tablet appeared.
Therefore, Ye Tian still had a chance now.
But becoming the Void Lord was too difficult. He had tried to challenge the third floor of the Void Tower and was defeated very quickly; it was simply very difficult to pass.
However, now that he had obtained the memories of this Jin Zu, he felt that he still had hope.
At his level, if he wanted to continue improving at the same stage, relying on the Endless Source Body would not allow for a significant increase. There were only two ways.
The first way was to create a Supreme Ancestral Technique. Second-class Invincibles could also create Supreme Ancestral Techniques, though the difficulty was even greater.
But relying solely on Supreme Ancestral Techniques would still not allow him to pass the third floor of the Void Tower; otherwise, the previous Void God Sons would have passed it long ago.
Therefore, he also needed to rely on the second way—strengthening his physical body or soul strength.
In the Source Sea, there were many mysterious treasures that could strengthen the physical body and soul strength. For example, the Xumi Golden Fruit was one such treasure, but its effect was not very large, and eating more of it was useless.
If he collected enough of these treasures, his physical strength could still be improved significantly.
However, Ye Tian had an even better way, which was to find several Invincibles with 100% compatibility with Eternal Source Object origins and duplicate their bodies. This would be equivalent to possessing their compatibility, allowing him to study the mysteries of compatibility himself. He could then find a way to fuse Eternal Source Object origins, strengthening his physical body and soul by fusing various types of Eternal Source Object origins.
It was just like Ye Jinpeng; Ye Jinpeng had only fused a tiny bit of the Eternal Soul Wood origin, yet his soul strength was already terrifying.
Therefore, according to this method, he could also significantly strengthen his physical body and soul strength.
Moreover, from Jin Zu's memories, he knew that some races in the Source Sea possessed Clan-Protecting Source Objects, and they were naturally 100% compatible with their Clan-Protecting Source Objects.
At this time.
Ye Tian secretly formulated a plan: first, look for treasures that could strengthen the physical body, and then study the issue of compatibility.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian's duplicated body passed through the Void Source Hall, crossed a vast distance, and arrived at a certain location in the Source Sea.
This time, he had come to take away an Eternal Source Object from this place.
