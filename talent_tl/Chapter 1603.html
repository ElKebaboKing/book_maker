Chapter 1602 Spirit Source Contract Stone!

“Old Ancestor, what do you mean by that?”
Ye Tian pretended to be confused and asked back.
Star Ancestor said coldly, “Xu Chen, Dong He, Gu Chen, and the others have either fallen or been imprisoned by you. What exactly do you want to do?”
Star Ancestor saying this was equivalent to tearing off all pretenses.
Ye Tian could no longer keep pretending to be confused, so he said, “Star Ancestor, since you already know, then I won’t hide it anymore. That’s right, Dong He and those other Supreme Beings have already been killed by me.”
“Why?”
Star Ancestor had no sadness at all, only anger.
“I want to completely take control of the Ancient Star Race. They would obstruct me!” Ye Tian said.
“Ridiculous!” Star Ancestor sneered. “Is your vision really that short-sighted? If you keep staying in the Starry Ancient Domain and don’t have my guidance, you simply can’t become a top powerhouse. You won’t even be able to leave the Starry Ancient Domain. Don’t tell me you want to stay inside the Starry Ancient Domain for your entire life?”
“The Starry Ancient Domain is very good. It isn’t as dangerous as the outside world, so what’s wrong with staying here?”
Ye Tian countered.
“……”
Star Ancestor was furious. He had never seen a Supreme Being so content with the status quo and so lacking in ambition. Moreover, Xu Chen’s talent was so good—443 even after consuming Primal Source Liquid, his future potential was still immense.
Little did he know that Ye Tian was just stringing him along.
If not for squeezing out the last bit of benefit, he would probably have directly annihilated this portion of Star Ancestor’s consciousness.
Suddenly
Star Ancestor said, “Xu Chen, do you think that by living in the Starry Ancient Domain, you will remain eternal and indestructible?”
Ye Tian heard the sarcasm in Star Ancestor’s words and couldn’t help asking, “Why not? Our lifespans are infinite, and the Starry Ancient Domain exists eternally as well. Outer Supreme Beings cannot enter the Starry Ancient Domain. Who would be my match?”
“Hahahaha!!!!” Star Ancestor burst into wild laughter. “You’re too naive. It’s true that your lifespan is eternal, but that doesn’t mean no one can enter the Starry Ancient Domain. The Starry Ancient Domain is the prison that confines me, and that half-step Eternal Realm expert who imprisoned me did not fall. If he comes back and sees you people, he absolutely won’t mind erasing you along the way. As the long years pass, that person may already have stepped into the Eternal Realm. Do you think you can contend with an Eternal Realm expert?”
“Who is that half-step Eternal Realm expert who imprisoned you?”
Ye Tian was somewhat curious.
Thinking of that Eternal Realm expert, Star Ancestor’s expression darkened, and he did not reveal the other party’s identity.
Star Ancestor only reminded him, “Xu Chen, if you help me break the seal, the restrictions of the Starry Ancient Domain will disappear. Only then will you be able to leave the Starry Ancient Domain. At that time, you can simply enter some remote territory and live freely, instead of being casually erased in the future by that half-step Eternal Realm expert.”
Ye Tian pretended to think deeply, but inwardly he was not worried at all.
This was only a clone; he was not afraid of falling.
“What good will it do me if I help you? Although you gave me quite a few good things before, you also cheated me. Primal Source Liquid consumes potential. You think I don’t know that?” Ye Tian exposed it directly.
“You actually know about the flaw in Primal Source Liquid?”
Star Ancestor was somewhat surprised.
The Starry Ancient Domain had little contact with the outside world, and Primal Source Liquid was exceedingly rare. The Supreme Beings inside the Starry Ancient Domain should not have known about it.
However, he did not think too much about it.
Since Ye Tian had already discovered the flaw in Primal Source Liquid, there was no point in hiding it anymore.
“Xu Chen, Primal Source Liquid does indeed have some regrets, but it is still a treasure that countless Supreme Beings dream of. That tiny flaw is not worth mentioning compared to its own value,” Star Ancestor said.
“If there are no other earth-shattering benefits, I won’t help you get out,” Ye Tian said.
“If I give you earth-shattering benefits, what if you don’t help me then?”
Star Ancestor said.
“Believe it or not, that’s up to you!” Ye Tian said indifferently.
Star Ancestor’s expression changed several times before he finally said, “I want to sign a contract with you. Later, I will send out a special contract stone. You will enter into a contract with me. If you violate it, you will suffer consequences beyond imagination.”
“What contract stone?”
Ye Tian was very surprised. What kind of contract could restrain a Supreme Being?
“Spirit Source Contract Stone!”
Star Ancestor said.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian asked the artifact spirit about the Spirit Source Contract Stone.
“Artifact spirit, do you know about the Spirit Source Contract Stone?” Ye Tian asked.
“I do!” the artifact spirit said. “Strictly speaking, the Spirit Source Contract Stone is a kind of Eternal Source Object, but at the beginning of the Source Sea, this Eternal Source Object was shattered into pieces. Each fragment is also equivalent to a Spirit Source Contract Stone. This kind of contract stone can restrain Supreme Beings, but it is ineffective against half-step Eternal Realm experts.”
“If my clone signs a contract with the Spirit Source Contract Stone, can the contract affect my true body?” Ye Tian wanted to know this, so he asked.
“It can’t affect it!” Star Ancestor answered. “For Supreme Beings with special clones, the Spirit Source Contract Stone cannot take effect. This is also one of its flaws. The reason the Spirit Source Contract Stone can affect Supreme Beings is that it releases a special energy that surges into the body of the Supreme Being and completely merges with them, leaving them inseparable. Once the contract is broken, the Supreme Being will suffer the consequences of the vow. But the master’s clone and the master are not the same body, so naturally it won’t be affected.”
“So that’s how it is!”
Ye Tian understood, and no longer worried about this so-called Spirit Source Contract Stone.
……
Starry Ancient Domain.
“Fine, I agree!”
Ye Tian said.
“Good, I’ll send the Spirit Source Contract Stone over right now!”
Boom!!!!!
Even the Starry Ancient Domain began to shake. This was Star Ancestor expending power to send the Spirit Source Contract Stone out from the sealed land.
With a ripping sound!
A gap split open in the void, and a blue stone fell out. It was exactly the Spirit Source Contract Stone.
“Xu Chen, let’s sign the contract!”
Star Ancestor demanded.
“No problem!” Ye Tian nodded.
Then, the two signed the contract.
This contract was drafted by Star Ancestor and was extremely strict. It required Ye Tian to rescue Star Ancestor. If, when capable, Ye Tian did not rescue Star Ancestor, then not only would Ye Tian be unable to advance any further in strength, he would also suffer backlash and pain until the day his body collapsed and he died.
It had to be said that such a vow was extremely cruel, but Ye Tian still signed it.
After the contract was signed, Star Ancestor finally let out a sigh of relief and burst into laughter.
Clearly, he believed that he would definitely be able to break through the seal.
“It’s time to give me the benefits, right? I’ve already signed the contract. I can’t possibly violate it, so you must truly cultivate me!!” Ye Tian demanded.
“Alright, I’ll give you a great opportunity right now!”
Star Ancestor promised.
