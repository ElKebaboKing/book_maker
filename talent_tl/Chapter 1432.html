Chapter 1431: Gray Crystal, Bestowing Spatial Saint Talent
!
The Primordial Ancestral Realm has opened quite a few times since its inception, so the Battle Sect has naturally collected some information, including data on dozens of forbidden zones.
One piece of data piqued Ye Tian's interest. The forbidden zone in that data was somewhat special because the Saint Soul Beasts there possessed spatial abilities, and their strength was terrifying, even including Saint Soul Beasts at the Invincible Domain Ancestor level.
According to the data, without the combat power of a five-step peak Invincible Domain Ancestor, it would be difficult to survive inside; one would die instantly upon entry.
"That forbidden zone should be the territory of that... Saint Soul Beast race. Those Saint Soul Beasts might possess Spatial Saint Talents!"
Ye Tian speculated.
This was an opportunity. If it were true, he could copy a large number of Spatial Saint Talents, thereby elevating his own Spatial Saint Talent to the level of an Ancestral Talent. At that time, he could easily upgrade his Void Slash Saint Skill and Void Escape Saint Skill into Ancestral Skills, and his strength would increase significantly.
Swish! Ye Tian traveled quickly, and after some time, he finally arrived at that forbidden zone.
This forbidden zone was named the Void Realm of Unjust Death by the Ten Great Saint Sects of the Dark Saint Domain.
As soon as Ye Tian approached this forbidden zone, he felt a terrifying aura. However, this aura did not pose much of a threat to him. But for other peak Domain Ancestors, this was indeed an incredibly terrifying forbidden zone.
Naturally, other peak Domain Ancestors would not come to such a dangerous forbidden zone, so Ye Tian was the only one here.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian took a step and entered the Void Realm of Unjust Death.
Just as he entered the Void Realm of Unjust Death, Ye Tian immediately sensed groups of Saint Soul Beasts rushing toward him.
Among them, many Saint Soul Beasts were actually at the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
This wasn't just one or two, but many.
"No wonder this forbidden zone is so dangerous; there are too many Invincible Domain Ancestors!"
Ye Tian murmured.
These Saint Soul Beasts were almost all the same, looking like large plates that could split into countless tentacles.
These Saint Soul Beasts rushed over, their countless tentacles manipulating spatial power to form dense clusters of spatial spears. Each tentacle controlled a spatial spear, piercing toward him.
Ye Tian did not kill these Saint Soul Beasts immediately. Instead, he used his Copy Talent to investigate their talents.
As soon as the Copy Talent investigated, he immediately perceived their talents.
"They really do have Spatial Saint Talents!"
Ye Tian said joyfully.
[Saint Talent: Void Spear] "Copy!"
"Copy!"
"Copy!"
Ye Tian frantically copied these Void Spear Saint Talents. In the blink of an eye, he had copied a large number of them before finally taking action.
"Ancestral Armor!"
"Golden Silk Ancestral Skill!"
"Streamer Ancestral Skill!"
"Golden Body Ancestral Skill!"
"Tyrant Strength Ancestral Skill!"
.... Ye Tian performed one Ancestral Skill after another. His combat power was incredibly terrifying, and a single light strike could slay a group of Saint Soul Beasts.
After a short while, the area was littered with the corpses of Saint Soul Beasts. A significant amount of Saint Soul power dissipated from these corpses and merged into Ye Tian's soul. However, Ye Tian's Saint Soul had already completed its second qualitative change, so this small amount of Saint Soul power was useless to him.
Ye Tian swept through the area. There was even a Saint Soul Beast comparable to a five-step Invincible Domain Ancestor, but under Ye Tian's frantic attacks, it also turned into a corpse.
Day after day of slaughtering, Ye Tian didn't know how long he had been fighting.
There were many Saint Soul Beasts here, and most were at the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, making them relatively difficult to kill. Naturally, it took more time.
A long time later, Ye Tian looked around and saw no more traces of Saint Soul Beasts. He had arrived at the center of the Void Realm of Unjust Death.
Here, he saw a gray crystal, the size of a star, suspended in the void.
"This is..."
Ye Tian observed it carefully but could not recognize the use of this gray crystal.
However, Ye Tian felt the power of space emanating from it. A special spatial power seemed to be influencing Ye Tian's soul, as if it wanted to transform it.
"Could it be that this gray crystal can allow someone to birth a Spatial Saint Talent?"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
If it were true, then this gray crystal was simply too heaven-defying! "Collect!"
Ye Tian tried to collect the gray crystal, but he did not succeed. It seemed to be a part of the Saint Soul Realm itself, making it very difficult to strip away.
Thus, Ye Tian used his full strength to collect it, even mobilizing all the power in his body. Only then did he slowly move the gray crystal into his internal world.
However, as soon as the gray crystal was collected, it actually began to shrink. Furthermore, its light became incredibly dim, and its spatial aura became very weak.
In the end, it turned into a crystal the height of a person, having weakened countless times over.
Holding the gray crystal, Ye Tian obtained a set of information from it.
"It can create nine Void Spear Saint Talent users!"
Ye Tian said in surprise.
Originally, it could probably create many Void Spear Saint Talents; the spatial talent race here was an example.
However, that was due to the special nature of the Saint Soul Realm. If it were taken outside, it would not have such a terrifying effect!
Therefore, after being stripped away, this crystal lost the amplification of the Saint Soul Realm and could only bestow the Void Spear Saint Talent to nine people.
But even so, it was still incredibly terrifying. If the Primordial Ancestors knew about it, they would likely be interested.
This gray crystal was undoubtedly Ye Tian's greatest harvest.
"Now that I've copied so many Spatial Saint Talents, it's time to try fusing them into an Ancestral Talent!"
Ye Tian sat cross-legged and began to fuse that large number of Spatial Saint Talents.
Under time acceleration, Ye Tian fused one Spatial Saint Talent after another. After a long time, he successfully fused all of them.
And the Spatial Saint Talent successfully transformed into a Spatial Ancestral Talent.
[Ancestral Talent: Space] Seeing these words, Ye Tian was extremely excited.
Subsequently, he began to duplicate his body and had the duplicates perform derivations to deduce and comprehend the Void Slash Saint Skill and the Void Escape Saint Skill.
Just like that, more years passed. The Void Slash Saint Skill and Void Escape Saint Skill finally became the Void Slash Ancestral Skill and Void Escape Ancestral Skill.
How powerful was the Void Slash Saint Skill? The Void Escape Ancestral Skill almost made Ye Tian invincible.
Therefore, with these two additional Ancestral Skills, Ye Tian's strength surged once again.
If he could cultivate these Ancestral Skills to the minor completion stage, Ye Tian's strength would likely reach the peak of a four-step Invincible Domain Ancestor's combat power.
"Time to check out the other forbidden zones!"
Ye Tian cast the Void Escape Ancestral Skill and vanished.
