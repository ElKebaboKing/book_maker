Chapter 1620: The Immortal Ancestor Talent!

“It’s done!”
Ye Tian was extremely excited.
The black mist worm clone had been staying in the Primordial Sea all this time. Who could enjoy such treatment?
Suddenly.
Ye Tian thought of a problem: if the black mist worm clone was too weak, then it would be of no use even in the Primordial Sea.
Moreover, once the origin tide of the Primordial Sea ended, he would be unable to enter this place, and the black mist worm would also be unable to leave. In reality, it would not gain too many benefits either.
The only gain was that he had copied a large number of Eternal Ancestor Talents.
Now, he faced two choices.
The first was to continue staying here and not wait until after leaving to fuse the Eternal Ancestor Talents, but instead begin fusing them directly here.
The second choice was to wait until after leaving before fusing these Eternal Ancestor Talents, and use the time to search for resource treasures himself.
After thinking for a while, Ye Tian decided to choose the first.
He planned to fuse them right here, use the fastest speed to fuse out the talents above the Eternal Ancestor Talents, and then bestow them upon the black mist worm clone, letting the black mist worm clone rise completely and become the king of the black mist worms.
In that case, the black mist worm clone would be able to hold a hegemonic position in the Primordial Sea and naturally collect more good things. At worst, the next time he came in, he would be able to gain countless resources.
Moreover, he was also very curious: what exactly was the talent above the Eternal Ancestor Talents?
“Begin fusing!”
Ye Tian set up a time acceleration great array around him and first began by devouring the Eternal Ancestor Talents.
One by one, the devouring Eternal Ancestor Talents fused together, but the fusion speed was rather slow, and even somewhat laborious. After all, the level of the copied talent was not much higher than the Eternal Ancestor Talents, so fusing the Eternal Ancestor Talents was naturally very strenuous.
But in the end, the devouring Eternal Ancestor Talents still fused together one by one.
During this time.
Some Supreme Beings passed by here, but Ye Tian concealed his figure. As soon as these Supreme Beings sensed the black mist on the island, they were so frightened that they fled immediately, not daring to investigate carefully at all.
Thus, Ye Tian had not been discovered to this day.
Time slowly passed—
Ye Tian did not know how much time had passed, but he had stayed within the time acceleration great array for a very long period, and he had also fused many Eternal Ancestor Talents.
Gradually, after the devouring Eternal Ancestor Talent fused, its power became stronger and stronger, as if a qualitative change could occur at any moment.
Finally, on this day, the devouring Eternal Ancestor Talent transformed completely.
【Devouring: Immortal Ancestor Talent】
This was a talent level above the Eternal Ancestor Talents, and it was also the first Immortal Ancestor Talent Ye Tian possessed.
The Immortal Ancestor Talent was almost completely on the same level as the copy talent, and Ye Tian also clearly sensed that the Immortal Ancestor Talent could no longer continue to upgrade. At the very least, the current copy talent was unable to fuse the Immortal Ancestor Talent any further.
Immediately.
Ye Tian stripped the Immortal Ancestor Talent and bestowed it upon the black mist worm clone, and thus the black mist worm clone also possessed the Immortal Ancestor Talent.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian fused the other Eternal Ancestor Talents one by one.
In this way, the Immortal Ancestor Talents of strength, defense, speed, and indestructibility were born one after another, and all of them were bestowed by Ye Tian upon the black mist worm clone.
Gathering these Immortal Ancestor Talents, the black mist worm clone finally underwent a qualitative change.
Rumble!!!!!
The black mist worm clone welcomed its transformation. Not only did its body size begin to surge, but its strength also skyrocketed rapidly, as if it had broken through some kind of boundary.
After all, in the Primordial Sea, the black mist worm was subject to some kind of restriction. If it were outside, there could not possibly be such a tiny creature capable of threatening a Ninth-Tier Supreme Being, but it existed in the Primordial Sea, so it was definitely the Primordial Sea’s rules that had restricted the black mist worm.
And now, Ye Tian was effectively breaking this restriction.
In no time at all, the black mist worm’s body size became comparable to that of a black-armored worm, making it stand out too much among the countless black mist worms.
Moreover, Ye Tian clearly felt that the black mist worm clone could command the other black mist worms, like a king.
In terms of cultivation realm, the black mist worm clone was roughly equivalent to a Sixth-Tier Supreme Being, but it could lead countless black mist worms, and its threat level was comparable to that of a powerful half-step Eternal realm expert.
Moreover, the black mist worm clone could leave the island and was no longer restricted.
Not long after.
Ye Tian discovered that the origin tide was about to end.
“It’s time to leave, otherwise this copied body won’t be able to return, and those talents won’t be taken away either.”
Ye Tian murmured.
Whoosh!
He flew into the void and left the Primordial Sea along with the origin tide.
Not long after.
Ye Tian returned to the Source Sea, and when he carefully tried to sense the location of the Primordial Sea again, he could sense it, but could not enter it.
This time, Ye Tian’s gains were not very great. He definitely could not compare with those half-step Eternal realm experts, but he had obtained some Immortal Ancestor Talents, and that was his biggest gain.
Ye Tian took a look, and the Twelve Ancestors and the other Supreme Beings of Source God Pavilion had also come out.
Very soon, the Supreme Beings of Source God Pavilion returned to Source God Pavilion.
Internal meeting of Source God Pavilion.
One Supreme Being after another sat in their seats, and Ye Tian was also here.
But soon, he discovered that quite a few Supreme Beings had not returned.
Time slowly passed until the origin tide completely disappeared.
At the same time, Ye Tian’s black mist worm clone discovered that the restrictions of the Primordial Sea had completely disappeared, and countless black mist worms flew out, spreading across the entire Primordial Sea.
……
“The remaining Supreme Beings didn’t come back. Even if they’re still alive, they must have been swallowed by the black mist by now.”
“This time, our Source God Pavilion lost thirty-seven inner-circle members!”
True River Sword Ancestor announced.
Thirty-seven inner-circle members, and among those fallen Supreme Beings, at the very least all of them were at the Eighth-Tier Heavenly realm.
Eighth-Tier Supreme Beings—what a powerful realm, yet so many had fallen all at once.
“Everyone, begin trading!”
Fenggu Source Ancestor spoke.
The other Supreme Beings immediately took out the resources they did not need, hoping to exchange them with other Supreme Beings for resources.
Ye Tian also took out some resources, mainly to exchange for resources that could help the Eternal Body grow and develop.
He wanted to raise his Endless Eternal Body to the Perfected realm, and thus needed a large amount of related resources.
Many of the Supreme Beings here had their Eternal Tribulation bodies, Eternal divine bodies, or Eternal bodies reach the Perfected realm, so even if they obtained treasures like the Yuanqing Eternal Stone, they did not need them much, and were therefore willing to exchange them for other resources.
Very quickly, Ye Tian traded for quite a few resources similar to the Yuanqing Eternal Stone.
Not long after, the trading ended, and one Supreme Being after another left this place to go back into seclusion.
Ye Tian also hurriedly returned to the Void Source Hall and disappeared from Source God Pavilion.
