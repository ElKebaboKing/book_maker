Chapter 1576: Eternal Soul Wood Obtained!

Longze was indeed a forbidden land that even a Supreme Being would not dare to enter, unless they were a top-tier Supreme Being at the level of the Void Source Ancestor.
However, such Supreme Beings did not care about Longze and would not go there.
Ye Tian was currently an Invincible. Once he entered Longze, he would be annihilated by its special power. However, if he copied a creature from within Longze and entered as that creature, he would not be annihilated by Longze.
Were there living creatures within Longze?
Previously, Ye Tian truly did not know, but he had been inquiring about Longze's situation over these years, and thus his understanding of it grew. There were indeed lifeforms within Longze called Longyou. This kind of lifeform was not very strong; its only advantage was its extreme speed.
Some powerful experts captured Longyou to use as mounts for traveling through the Source Sea.
Of course, because Longyou lived within Longze, they were very difficult to capture. To this day, the number of captured Longyou could be counted on one's fingers.
But Ye Tian still managed to track down the whereabouts of one Longyou. Furthermore, that Longyou's strength was not great, only at the level of a second-class Invincible. It was the mount of a first-class Invincible, but that first-class Invincible had a top-tier Supreme Being as a backer.
Ye Tian did not intend to snatch the Longyou; he only needed to get close to it to copy its body.
......
Dust World.
This was also an Eternal Heaven and Earth, but it was much more ancient than the Tianlin World.
The number of Supreme Beings in the Dust World was far greater than in the Tianlin World. While the Tianlin World was not very famous in the Source Sea, the Dust World was different; this Eternal Heaven and Earth was quite well-known throughout the Source Sea.
Ye Tian arrived at the Dust World and approached Molong Mountain very low-key, as that Longyou was located there.
This Longyou belonged to the second-class Invincible Chen He, and Chen He was a member of Molong Mountain.
Ye Tian could not enter Molong Mountain, and it was difficult for his copying talent to extend far enough to investigate the internal situation of Molong Mountain, so he could only wait patiently.
Time slowly passed, and finally, after waiting for a long time.
Ye Tian finally saw Chen He appear. The other party was riding the Longyou, preparing to leave, and Ye Tian captured this moment.
The moment his copying talent detected the Longyou, Ye Tian could copy its body.
Thus, Ye Tian collapsed this current copied body.
Not long after, Ye Tian re-condensed a copied body, but this time he copied the body of the Longyou.
After copying the Longyou's body, Ye Tian personally brought the Longyou body toward Longze. Otherwise, if he let the Longyou fly there by itself and it happened to be caught by a Supreme Being in the Source Sea, wouldn't that be awkward?
Ye Tian used the Void Source Palace to quickly move to an area near Longze and then flew to the edge of Longze.
At this moment, his Longyou body instantly entered Longze.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian's main body returned to the Void Source Palace, focusing all his mind on controlling his Longyou body.
Longze.
As soon as the Longyou body entered this place, it felt the warmth of home and was not suppressed in the slightest.
"Back then, Jin Zu threw his corpse and the Eternal Soul Wood in from that exact position. Based on that speed and power, combined with the resistance of Longze, it should be in that area!"
Ye Tian flew toward that area and began searching for Jin Zu's corpse.
Along the way, Ye Tian also discovered some good things. They were likely residual items left behind by Invincibles or Supreme Beings who had perished here in the past. However, they were able to withstand the power of Longze without being destroyed, showing that these items were of no small value.
Ye Tian was truly poor right now, so he collected all of these items.
Soon, Ye Tian arrived at that area. During this time, he sensed a terrifying aura coming from the depths of Longze that far surpassed his current strength. However, the owner of that powerful aura did not attack Ye Tian, clearly treating him as an ordinary Longyou within Longze.
Longze's area was too vast, so Ye Tian searched for tens of thousands of years before finally finding Jin Zu's corpse.
It had to be said that Jin Zu's strength was very powerful. Even though he had been dead for countless ages, his corpse still showed no signs of damage.
However, because Jin Zu's strength was too great, it was also very difficult for Ye Tian to take the corpse away.
Fortunately, Jin Zu's treasures were not stored in an internal world—he didn't have one—but were placed inside a Void treasure. This Void treasure was located inside Jin Zu's finger, which Ye Tian knew from Jin Zu's memories.
It took Ye Tian a long time to extend his power into that Void treasure. Only after preliminary refining it did he successfully take it out and carry it away.
Afterward, Ye Tian headed out of Longze, encountering no danger along the way.
Ye Tian's main body had been waiting outside Longze for a long time. When the Longyou body emerged, Ye Tian immediately took the Void treasure from the Longyou body and then immediately returned to the Void Source Palace.
As for the Longyou body, Ye Tian naturally collapsed it at the first opportunity.
--------------
Void Source Palace.
Ye Tian began to re-refine this Void treasure, which was a Void Sphere.
Void Spheres were very common storage treasures in the Source Sea, with a vast area comparable to a domain.
After refining the Void Sphere, Ye Tian began to investigate the situation inside.
It had to be said that Jin Zu was indeed wealthy. Just within this Void Sphere, there were six Eternal Source Items, as well as some rare treasures like Void Source Crystals. There were also some rare treasures that could temper Ye Tian's body.
Of course, the Eternal Soul Wood that Ye Tian wanted most was also among them.
"The Eternal Soul Wood is in my hands!"
Ye Tian was quite excited.
In the following days, Ye Tian began to refine all the rare treasures within Jin Zu's Void Sphere that could improve his body, using them to enhance his strength.
Only then did he begin to absorb and refine the source power of the Eternal Soul Wood.
Buzz!!!
The source power of the Eternal Soul Wood was absorbed and refined by Ye Tian's soul. This refining speed was much faster than Ye Jinpeng's because Ye Tian's strength was great, and his understanding of affinity far surpassed Ye Jinpeng's.
Of course, Ye Tian was not currently pushing for the Supreme Being realm, so he would not absorb too much of the Eternal Soul Wood's source power. Otherwise, if it caused a qualitative soul leap change that could not be reversed, the loss would outweigh the gain.
As the source power of the Eternal Soul Wood was absorbed and refined bit by bit by Ye Tian, his soul strength grew more and more powerful.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian felt he had reached his Limit and could not continue absorbing and refining the source of the Eternal Soul Wood, so he stopped.
Having stabilized his soul strength, Ye Tian's power had increased significantly. He went to challenge the third floor of the Void Tower again, but he still failed, though he was now very close to success.
"It seems I still need a second type of source affinity and to absorb a second type of Eternal Source Item source!"
Ye Tian murmured.
