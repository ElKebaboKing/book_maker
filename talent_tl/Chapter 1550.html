Chapter 1549: Xumi Golden Mountain, Xumi Golden Fruit!

"Your Invincible Tribulation Body is actually this powerful!"
Ming Li Fog Ancestor’s entire body trembled.
His Invincible Tribulation Body had just been blasted apart once, and nearly thirty percent of his physical body had been annihilated. If this continued, he would soon perish.
Both were fifth-grade Invincibles, and he had been a fifth-grade Invincible for countless ages, possessing strength far greater than an average fifth-grade Invincible and having comprehended two Invincible Techniques. He never expected to be so far inferior to Ye Tian.
It was evident how terrifyingly powerful Ye Tian's Invincible Tribulation Body was, far exceeding his imagination.
Furthermore, the Invincible Technique Ye Tian performed was definitely no ordinary technique, but a top-tier Invincible Technique. Thus, the power it unleashed far surpassed his own.
Had Ye Tian not been suppressed by the rules of the Feathered Heaven, only able to exert a small fraction of his strength, Ming Li Fog Ancestor would have been killed instantly.
"Escape!" Ming Li Fog Ancestor was no longer prepared to guard this opportunity. No matter how great the opportunity, if he perished, everything would be for naught.
However, he simply could not escape.
Ye Tian had no intention of letting him go, and he had no time at all to break through the barrier of the Feathered Heaven.
Soon, Ming Li Fog Ancestor’s life force was completely annihilated, turning into a corpse that floated above the golden mountain.
The battle just now had not caused even a shred of damage to the golden mountain, which was enough to show the miraculous nature of this place.
Ye Tian felt no pride in slaying a fifth-grade Invincible like Ming Li Fog Ancestor.
In reality, Ming Li Fog Ancestor was quite weak. Though he seemed to have comprehended two Invincible Techniques, both his foundation and his Invincible Tribulation Body were incredibly fragile. Any random fifth-grade Invincible from the Tianfeng Sea could easily crush him.
Therefore, killing such a fifth-grade Invincible naturally required little effort.
Swish!
Ye Tian arrived at Ming Li Fog Ancestor’s palace on the golden mountain. There wasn't much inside; it was merely a simple residence.
Subsequently, Ye Tian found a golden scroll among Ming Li Fog Ancestor’s belongings. This golden scroll recorded a piece of information.
Xumi Golden Mountain: Ranked 128th among Eternal Source Objects. One Xumi Golden Fruit is born every thirty thousand Kalpa Eras. Consuming the Xumi Golden Fruit can strengthen the physical body, condense the soul, and permanently enhance comprehension.
"It’s actually the Xumi Golden Fruit!"
Ye Tian was immensely shocked.
During his years in the Tianfeng Sea, he hadn't just been cultivating bitterly; he had also learned about various treasures through the Tianfeng Sea, and the Xumi Golden Fruit was among them.
However, high-level members were not qualified to exchange for the Xumi Golden Fruit; only elite members had that privilege.
According to his understanding, a single Xumi Golden Fruit required one billion contribution points.
If one point of contribution from the Tianfeng Sea were converted into Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, it would be equivalent to at least 4,000 crystals, and likely even more.
This was because Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals were easy to obtain, but they couldn't be used to exchange for many rare treasures, whereas Tianfeng Sea contribution points could.
Therefore, what did one billion contribution points represent? It was equivalent to trillions of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals!
For Ming Li Fog Ancestor and the Blood Mist Alliance, their entire net worth was likely far less than a hundred million Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals. It was clear how massive the attraction of the Xumi Golden Fruit was to them.
Whether they traded it away or refined it themselves, it was an unimaginable opportunity.
Unfortunately, all of this now belonged to Ye Tian.
"No wonder Ming Li Fog Ancestor was unwilling to leave. Who would give up a treasure worth trillions of Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals? Moreover, the Xumi Golden Fruit is just one type of treasure produced by the Xumi Golden Mountain. The value of the mountain itself is even greater, simply incalculable. If it were me, I wouldn't be able to abandon this place either!" Ye Tian thought to himself.
The Xumi Golden Mountain was an Eternal Source Object. Ye Tian had some knowledge of Eternal Source Objects; though limited, he knew they were higher in tier than Rule Heavenly Objects.
Rule Heavenly Objects were treasures born from an Eternal Heaven, representing its rules. However, Eternal Source Objects corresponded to the Source Sea. Most Eternal Source Objects were born within the Source Sea. Once they appeared, even the lowliest Eternal Source Object would trigger a bloodbath among numerous first-grade Invincibles.
And if a powerful Eternal Source Object emerged, perhaps even Supreme Beings would take action.
An Eternal Source Object like the Xumi Golden Mountain was something even the Tianfeng Sea might not be able to protect.
"This kind of treasure cannot be reported. Furthermore, I had best completely seal off the Feathered Heaven. Otherwise, if word leaks out like it did for Ming Li Fog Ancestor today, it will be disastrous!"
Ye Tian thought secretly.
Completely sealing off an alien heaven was possible, but it required paying a heavy price...
Ye Tian currently had no way to completely seal an alien heaven, so he had his Invincible Progenitor Avatar descend into the Feathered Heaven to stand guard there.
Later, through research, Ye Tian discovered that the day of the Xumi Golden Fruit's birth was not far off.
"In six Dark Eras at most, the Xumi Golden Fruit will appear!" Ye Tian estimated.
This was the maximum time; it was also possible it could be born in the next second.
After all, it was an Eternal Source Object, so there was no way to estimate accurately.
It had to be said that Ming Li Fog Ancestor simply lacked luck; he perished just as the Xumi Golden Fruit was about to be born.
Time passed slowly, and in the blink of an eye, five Dark Eras had gone by.
During this time, Ye Tian made great progress.
With ample resources, he raised his cultivation to the limit of a fifth-grade Invincible. He also cultivated the Invincible Tribulation Body technique, "Ten Thousand Origins Returning to the Void," to the late stage of the second layer. After refining and perfecting it, his main body finally reached the late stage of the second layer of Ten Thousand Origins Returning to the Void.
However, going from the late stage of the second layer to full completion would likely take another full Kalpa Era.
On this day.
The Xumi Golden Fruit was born!
Hum!!!!
Upon the Xumi Golden Mountain, an illusory golden fruit appeared.
Just like the Xumi Golden Mountain itself, divine sense and other methods could not detect it; it could only be seen with the naked eye.
As time passed, the Xumi Golden Fruit absorbed the power of the Xumi Golden Mountain and slowly became solid.
After approximately several hundred million years, this Xumi Golden Fruit finally matured.
Immediately, Ye Tian plucked the fruit.
Now, Ye Tian had two choices. The first was to hand the Xumi Golden Fruit over to the Tianfeng Sea, which would net him at least ten million contribution points.
However, if he did that, the Xumi Golden Mountain could no longer be kept a secret.
Furthermore, he wasn't currently lacking contribution points, and with his abilities, earning them wasn't difficult.
Therefore, he would not choose that path.
"I'll just eat it directly!"
Ye Tian decided.
Immediately, he brought the Xumi Golden Fruit back to the Dark Forbidden Zone and had his main body swallow it in one gulp.
