Chapter 1554: About to Arrive at the Yuanji Abyss!

"Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea, you won't be able to leave!"
One Invincible after another descended upon this foreign domain world, surrounding Ye Tian and the others.
At the same time, an Invincible sacrificed a special treasure, instantly sealing the void of the entire foreign domain world. Even if Ye Tian and the others wanted to tear through the foreign domain world to go to another, it would be very difficult to achieve.
"Eighteen Fourth-tier Invincibles, and one Third-tier Invincible!"
Ye Tian's expression turned solemn.
Although a Third-tier Invincible would have over ninety percent of their strength suppressed by the foreign domain world—more so than a Fourth-tier Invincible—a Third-tier Invincible was inherently very powerful. Even with heavy suppression, they were still much stronger than a Fourth-tier Invincible.
Furthermore, there were eighteen Fourth-tier Invincibles here, and more Invincibles could arrive at any time. Thus, they were in an incredibly dangerous situation; a single mistake could lead to their total annihilation.
"Who are you, and why are you targeting us?" Ye Tian asked.
"Hahaha, it seems you know nothing. We are Invincibles of the Ghost Underworld Palace. Your Tianfeng Sea offended the Ghost Underworld Palace. Otherwise, why do you think the Tianfeng Sea only sent you to carry out this mission?" The Third-tier Invincible from the Ghost Underworld Palace sneered.
It was actually the Ghost Underworld Palace!
Ye Tian and the others were shocked, and several Invincibles were even filled with terror.
They didn't have abilities like Ye Tian; they only had their original bodies. Once they fell, they truly died.
Although Paragon Qingyang had said this mission was extremely dangerous, they had felt he was being hyperbolic, and that with good luck, there might not be any danger.
But now, learning they had offended the Ghost Underworld Palace, they were filled with regret.
The Ghost Underworld Palace was one of the eight Eternal-name level powers in the Tianlin Realm. Although it was ranked last, it was still an Eternal-name level power, possessing Supreme Beings.
The Tianfeng Sea was only a Saint-name level power, and its strongest experts were only First-tier Invincibles.
"I surrender!" one of the Tianfeng Sea Invincibles hurriedly shouted.
"Fine. As long as you kill the others, you can join our Ghost Underworld Palace!" the Third-tier Invincible of the Ghost Underworld Palace promised.
In reality, he had no intention of letting any Invincible from the Tianfeng Sea go. Orders from above were to kill them all, leaving no one alive.
If an Invincible pretended to surrender while secretly continuing the mission, wouldn't they be played for fools? To avoid this, they wouldn't leave any Invincible alive.
The other nine Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea weren't stupid; they didn't believe the words of the Ghost Underworld Palace's Third-tier Invincible.
"Break through!"
After exchanging glances with the others, Ye Tian decided.
In terms of strength, he far surpassed ordinary Fourth-tier Invincibles. Even among the Third-tier Invincibles of the Tianfeng Sea, few were likely his match.
However, the opponents this time were from the Eternal-name level Ghost Underworld Palace. Their Fourth-tier Invincibles were bound to be stronger than him, and that Third-tier Invincible might be very powerful. Moreover, they had reinforcements, while the Tianfeng Sea likely had none.
Therefore, he didn't want to delay or show off his strength; he only thought about escaping immediately.
Boom!!!!
The slaughter began!
Everyone had a box. Ye Tian also carried a box as he broke through. Three Fourth-tier Invincibles attacked him.
Ye Tian only displayed a portion of his strength, blocking the attacks of the three Fourth-tier Invincibles. He then attempted to break the void seal. Though it was slow, there was hope of breaking it.
The other Invincibles were in a miserable state. Before long, a Fourth-tier Invincible from the Tianfeng Sea died at the hands of that Third-tier Invincible.
Just then...
Rip!
The void was torn open, leading to another foreign domain world.
The Invincibles of the Ghost Underworld Palace looked at Ye Tian in disbelief, clearly not expecting him to break the seal so quickly.
"Quick, stop him!"
The Third-tier Invincible personally moved to kill Ye Tian, but he was clearly a step too late.
With one strike, Ye Tian heavily injured the three Fourth-tier Invincibles. With no one left to stop him, he turned into a streak of light and escaped into another foreign domain world. He then broke through that world at maximum speed to reach yet another.
Soon, Ye Tian had shaken off the Invincibles of the Ghost Underworld Palace.
...
"Captain Qingzi, three Invincibles escaped!" a Fourth-tier Invincible reported to the Third-tier Invincible, Qingzi.
Qingzi was also quite annoyed. His strength was decent among the Third-tier Invincibles of the Ghost Underworld Palace. Although his strength was suppressed in the foreign domain world, leading so many Fourth-tier Invincibles to intercept a ten-man squad of Fourth-tier Invincibles and still letting three escape...
"It's all because of that man!"
Qingzi thought of Ye Tian. If he hadn't gone to hunt Ye Tian down, he wouldn't have given the other two Fourth-tier Invincibles the chance to escape.
However, he had to admit that Ye Tian's strength was indeed great, perhaps not much weaker than his own.
Unfortunately, the opponent had fled too quickly. He couldn't lock onto the location in a short time. Otherwise, he could have invited some other Third-tier Invincibles to ensure the target's death.
Next, Ye Tian became even more cautious and traveled alone.
He didn't know if the others had survived, but even if they had, he couldn't regroup with them. What if it was a trap?
Thus, he could only carry out the mission alone.
Of course, he could also abandon the mission, but if he did, he would likely face punishment upon returning to the Tianfeng Sea. Before the Tianfeng Sea was destroyed by the Ghost Underworld Palace, it was indeed a great backer.
Moreover, he felt that the Tianfeng Sea wasn't without hope of surviving the crisis; otherwise, this mission wouldn't exist. The Ghost Underworld Palace was likely very wary of the Tianfeng Sea; otherwise, they wouldn't have sent so many Invincibles to stop the mission.
Time passed slowly—
Relying on his powerful strength and a bit of luck, Ye Tian finally successfully approached his destination.
In the final foreign domain world before the destination, Ye Tian arrived.
Upon arriving, he was initially panicked because there were many Invincibles here. He thought he had been surrounded by the Ghost Underworld Palace, but he soon realized that wasn't the case.
There were giant cities here, with many Invincibles coming and going. Third-tier Invincibles and even Second-tier Invincibles could be seen. A Fourth-tier Invincible like Ye Tian was undoubtedly at the bottom here.
Ye Tian entered a city named Guyuan City.
"Teaming up for the Yuanji Abyss! Only Third-tier Invincibles. Those below or above Third-tier, don't bother!" a Third-tier Invincible shouted.
"Fourth-tier Invincibles teaming up! Need a hundred people. Once full, we enter the Yuanji Abyss!"
Voices filled the air, and Ye Tian couldn't help but notice.
"Yuanji Abyss!"
Ye Tian guessed that this Yuanji Abyss should be that shattered eternal world.
Afterward, he gathered some general information and finally learned about the Yuanji Abyss.
