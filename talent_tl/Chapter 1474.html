Creating a Space Ancestor Law was naturally much more difficult than creating other Ancestor Laws.
However, Ye Tian still had plenty of time, so he wasn't worried about running out...
In any case... his clone, Ancestor Qing Ye, had already grown to a level where he could rival the Destruction Ancestor, so there was no need to worry about safety.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian devoted himself wholeheartedly to creating the Space Ancestor Law.
One domain year! Two domain years! Three domain years! ...Unknowingly, thirteen domain years passed.
On this day, Ye Tian finally created the Space Ancestor Law.
Moreover, the Space Ancestor Law he created was much better than the one he had obtained from the Void Ancestor.
If one were to become an Ancestor using his Space Ancestor Law, they would definitely become a powerful Space Ancestor.
"Only the Time Ancestor Law is left. I'll continue creating!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
It took Ye Tian thirteen domain years to create the Space Ancestor Law, and the Time Ancestor Law was even more terrifying than the Space Ancestor Law, making it even more difficult.
This time, it took Ye Tian a full eighteen domain years to finally create the Time Ancestor Law.
At this point, all the basic Ancestor Laws had been created.
Next, Ye Tian needed to fuse these basic Ancestor Laws into the Endless Ancestor Law.
This step was also very difficult and would likely take a very long time, but at least there was a clear direction, so he wouldn't fall into a bottleneck that couldn't be broken.
"Before going into seclusion, I need to pass these Ancestor Laws to Yue'er, Xingchen, and the others!"
Ye Tian thought.
His wife, son, daughter, and sister were all at the peak of the Supreme-level Domain Ancestor realm. Their cultivation could no longer progress; they could only continue to improve by practicing Ancestor Techniques and tempering their Ancestor Bodies.
They also needed to fuse an Ancestor Law to break through to the Ancestor realm. With their own comprehension and talent, it wasn't impossible for them to create their own Ancestor Laws, but the hope was slim. Even if they could truly create one, it would take an endless amount of time.
Instead of that, it was better to fuse his Ancestor Laws. In the future, they would still have the hope of growing to the Elite Ancestor realm.
Therefore, Ye Tian wanted to pass these Ancestor Laws to them.
"Yue'er, Xingchen, Xingyue, Xiao Yu, come here for a moment!"
Ye Tian sent a divine sense transmission to them.
*Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!* Xiao Yue, Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, and Ye Yu all tore through space and arrived before Ye Tian.
"Husband!"
"Father!"
"Brother!"
The four of them called out one by one.
Ye Tian looked at the four of them and said, "I have created some Ancestor Laws, all of which are top-tier. I want to ask for your opinions.
Will you choose to fuse with the Ancestor Laws I created to become Ancestors, or will you choose to create your own?"
"Some Ancestor Laws?"
The four of them widened their eyes, revealing looks of disbelief.
How precious were Ancestor Laws? In the Dark Forbidden Territory, countless monstrous geniuses were unable to create their own Ancestor Laws, and many couldn't even obtain a complete, masterless Ancestor Law, thus remaining unable to promote to the Ancestor realm for their entire lives.
Even some broken Ancestor Laws became treasures that many peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors fought over, hoping to use such broken laws to become Half-step Ancestors.
Complete Ancestor Laws could no longer be created even by Ancestors. Therefore, if a complete, masterless Ancestor Law appeared, even Ancestors would fight and kill for it to secure a chance for their descendants to become Ancestors.
There were very few Ancestors in the Dark Forbidden Territory simply because Ancestor Laws were too rare and too difficult to create.
But now, Ye Tian said... he had created some Ancestor Laws, clearly more than one.
If these words were to spread, it would definitely cause a massive shock throughout the entire Dark Forbidden Territory, and all the Ancestors would have their eyes on Ye Tian.
"Husband, I choose to fuse with the Ancestor Law you created!"
Xiao Yue made her choice.
She had no confidence in herself. Other peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors were unable to create Ancestor Laws, and she had always relied on Ye Tian to grow to this level. Where would she find the potential to create an Ancestor Law of her own? Therefore, being able to obtain an Ancestor Law that suited her was already excellent; she couldn't ask for more.
"Good. Choose one of these Ancestor Laws to fuse with. Ancestor Laws are very dominant; as long as you can successfully fuse with one, it will forcibly shift the path of your cultivation. Therefore, it doesn't matter much whether it perfectly suits you; the impact won't be that great.
Of course, it's naturally not as good as an Ancestor Law that perfectly suits you!"
Ye Tian said.
*Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!* One Ancestor Law inheritance after another appeared before Xiao Yue.
Xiao Yue examined these Ancestor Laws and finally made a decision: "Husband, I choose the Light Ancestor Law!"
"Good, this Light Ancestor Law is yours, Yue'er!"
Ye Tian fused the Light Ancestor Law into Xiao Yue's soul. Once Xiao Yue fused with the Light Ancestor Law, even if others obtained this Light Ancestor Law in the future, they would not be able to become an Ancestor with it; they could only use it to comprehend Light-type Saint Techniques and Ancestor Techniques.
Ye Yu, Ye Xingchen, and Ye Xingyue also chose to fuse with Ancestor Laws instead of creating their own.
They also felt that the hope of self-creation was slim, so they didn't want to waste time.
Besides, they never dared to imagine becoming Ancestors before; being able to become Supreme-level Domain Ancestors was already satisfying, so they didn't have high demands.
"It's your turn, Xiao Yu!"
Ye Tian let Ye Yu pick second.
As for the two juniors, Ye Xingchen and Ye Xingyue, they would naturally pick third and fourth; the two of them certainly had no objections.
Ye Yu chose the Ice Ancestor Law without hesitation: "Brother, I choose the Ice Ancestor Law!"
Ye Tian nodded and fused the Ice Ancestor Law into Ye Yu's soul.
Subsequently, it was Ye Xingchen's turn.
Ye Xingchen pondered for a moment and finally chose the extremely powerful Space Ancestor Law.
In this way, after he became an Ancestor, his strength would be greater than that of ordinary Ancestors.
Ye Xingyue was not to be outdone and chose the Time Ancestor Law. It happened to be on the same level as her brother Ye Xingchen's choice, and since Time and Space were related and they were siblings, it was perfectly suitable.
Of course.
If they wanted to completely master the Time Ancestor Law and the Space Ancestor Law, it would likely take a very long time, but eventually, there would come a day when they could comprehend them thoroughly and become Ancestors.
Ye Tian's family had chosen their Ancestor Laws. As for the other Ancestor Laws, he would not give them to others for the time being, unless someone truly met his requirements; only then would he bestow these Ancestor Laws.
"It's time to continue my seclusion, fuse these Ancestor Laws, and create my own Endless Ancestor Law!"
Ye Tian entered the cultivation room and continued his seclusion to create.
