Chapter 1578: Strength Increases Once Again!

Silver Horn Source Race.
After Yinren returned, he immediately sought an audience with a Supreme Being of the Silver Horn Source Race, who was also the ancestor of his lineage.
"Greetings, Ancestor!"
Yinren respectfully said to the Supreme Being, Silver Eagle Source God.
"Yinren, what business do you have with me?" Silver Eagle Source God said indifferently.
Yinren immediately said: "Ancestor, I have heard that an Invincible possesses an Eternal Soul Pearl. I wish to trade for that Eternal Soul Pearl, but the other party's requirement is one-twentieth of the source of our race's stabilizing Eternal Source Object, the Silver-Copper Pearl."
"That Invincible actually dares to covet our race's stabilizing object!"
Silver Eagle Source God said angrily.
Immediately, he suggested: "Yinren, tell me who that Invincible is, and I will go kill him!"
Yinren hurriedly said: "Ancestor, I do not know who the other party is either, but it is best if you do not offend him. He is a Void Divine Son!"
Void Divine Son!
Silver Eagle Source God's expression changed. A Son of the Void was already monstrous enough, but a Void Divine Son was even more terrifying. He had heard of the battle achievements of a Void Divine Son, which were truly horrifying, simply unstoppable.
The Silver Horn Source Race currently could not afford to offend a Void Divine Son; otherwise, there would be a danger of extermination in the future.
Yinren hurriedly pleaded: "Ancestor, this is a great opportunity for me. If I can obtain the Eternal Soul Pearl and improve my soul aptitude, I will surely have a great hope of becoming a Second-tier Invincible, or even a First-tier Invincible. At that time, with the help of the race's stabilizing Eternal Source Object, the Silver-Copper Pearl, I can easily become a Supreme Being. I am a Son of the Void; if I become a Supreme Being, my strength will surely surpass other Supreme Beings, which will be enough to greatly enhance the strength of the Silver Horn Source Race."
Silver Eagle Source God remained silent for a long time before saying: "Yinren, this is a major matter. I need to discuss it with the other Supreme Beings in the race. If they also agree, then we will consent. Rest assured, I will try my best to persuade the other Supreme Beings, but you will likely have to pay a price."
"I understand. I am willing to bear any price!"
Yinren stated.
Subsequently, Silver Eagle Source God met with several other Supreme Beings of the Silver Horn Source Race to discuss Yinren's matter.
"I want to let Yinren use one-twentieth of the Silver-Copper Pearl's source to exchange for an Eternal Soul Pearl. What do you all think?"
"I disagree. This is too risky. Once one-twentieth of the Silver-Copper Pearl's source is consumed, how many long years will it take to replenish it? We would even need to pay many resources to let the Silver-Copper Pearl recover."
"Yes, I also disagree. What if Yinren fails? Besides, there is more than one clansman in the race who urgently needs an Eternal Soul Pearl. Why must it be given to Yinren?"
"Based on the fact that Yinren is a Son of the Void. If Yinren breaks through to the Supreme Being realm, it will be a great thing for our race, enough to greatly enhance our strength."
After some discussion, the other Supreme Beings of the Silver Horn Source Race finally agreed.
However, they also made several demands of Yinren.
First, regarding the matter of quickly recovering the Silver-Copper Pearl's source in the future, Yinren would need to bear ninety percent of the burden.
Second, after Yinren breaks through to the Supreme Being realm in the future, he must guard the Silver Horn Source Race for at least one million Kalpa Eras.
This was also to prevent Yinren from running around everywhere. If he were to perish in some forbidden land or stay away indefinitely to increase his strength, wouldn't the Silver Horn Source Race have wasted resources cultivating him for nothing?
And the third point: after Yinren breaks through to become a Supreme Being, he must take a clansman from within the race as a personal disciple and do his utmost to cultivate them, striving to cultivate another Supreme Being.
It had to be said that the conditions proposed by the several Supreme Beings were very harsh.
But there was no choice. It was fine to let clansmen refine the Silver-Copper Pearl's source, but trading it to an outsider was a first.
That was why they were so opposed and proposed such harsh conditions.
After hearing these conditions, Yinren was also very annoyed, but for the sake of the Eternal Soul Pearl, he still agreed.
Thus.
The several Supreme Beings extracted one-twentieth of the source and gave it to Yinren, letting Yinren bring this Silver-Copper Pearl source to the Void Source Hall to trade with Ye Tian.
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian and Yinren met once again.
"My race has agreed. Let us begin the trade!"
Yinren said.
"Alright!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Immediately, Ye Tian took out the Eternal Soul Pearl, while Yinren took out the Silver-Copper Pearl source.
Under the witness of the Void Source Hall's artifact spirit, Ye Tian and Yinren completed the trade.
After the trade was completed, Yinren asked: "Does Your Excellency have a second soul pearl? Our Silver Horn Source Race is willing to continue trading with Your Excellency."
· ········· Seeking Flowers··· ·····
This was also a requirement of the Supreme Beings in the race. If they could obtain a second Eternal Soul Pearl, they were willing to relinquish another one-twentieth of the Silver-Copper Pearl's source.
"No more!" Ye Tian explained: "I only killed a powerful enemy and obtained this Eternal Soul Pearl from his body. I happened to know that your Silver Horn Source Race might need an Eternal Soul Pearl, and I needed the Silver-Copper Pearl source, so I traded with you."
He would not actually continue trading with the Silver Horn Source Race because he only needed this much Silver-Copper Pearl source; any more would be useless.
Furthermore, the value of the Eternal Soul Pearl was very high, and it wasn't just the Silver Horn Source Race that needed it.
He was not stupid and naturally would not take out a second Eternal Soul Pearl.
"I see!"
.... . ...
Yinren said in a low voice.
Perhaps what Ye Tian said was true, or perhaps it was false, but he could not prove it and could only leave it at that.
In any case, he had obtained an Eternal Soul Pearl, so he didn't care much about the rest.
Subsequently, Yinren left.
Meanwhile, Ye Tian took the Silver-Copper Pearl source and entered his cultivation space, immediately copying Yinren's body.
He wanted to properly study the physical body of the Silver Horn Source Race and search for special particles within the body, just like studying soul compatibility.
Since he had already found the method, this research should not take too long.
As expected.
In just three Dark Eras, Ye Tian researched the special physical particles that could be compatible with the Silver-Copper Pearl source. He then used the copied body to research how to evolve these special physical particles over and over again, which took another five Dark Eras before he finally succeeded.
On this day.
Ye Tian began to let his body's physical particles evolve into the special physical particles compatible with the Silver-Copper Pearl source. After some time, the evolution was successful.
In this way, Ye Tian's body could also be 100% compatible with the Silver-Copper Pearl source.
Next, Ye Tian took out the Silver-Copper Pearl source.
"Absorb!"
Ye Tian's physical body began to absorb the Silver-Copper Pearl source without any obstruction.
As Ye Tian's physical body continuously absorbed the Silver-Copper Pearl source, his body became stronger and stronger.
Finally, the Silver-Copper Pearl source was completely absorbed by Ye Tian, and his physical body improved significantly, his strength increasing once again.
