Chapter 1412: The Sacred Stele Appears
!
Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor didn't stay long in the Profound Void Sacred Realm and soon left.
And when he left, he did so quietly.
After all, the War Realm Tower allows people to temporarily descend to another War Realm Tower's area, which is also a secret within the War Gate, unknown to outsiders.
If Ye Tian hadn't discovered this secret, the War Gate wouldn't have told him, unless he had reached the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm and held a relatively high status, only then would he be qualified to know this secret.
....Azure Origin Sacred Land.
Azure Edge Sacred Master learned about the situation in the Profound Void Sacred Realm and immediately went to question the Crying Blood Emperor.
He immediately sent a message to the Crying Blood Emperor.
"Crying Blood Emperor, you failed to rescue our two Invincible Domain Ancestors from the Azure Origin Sacred Land, what do you have to say?"
Azure Edge Sacred Master questioned.
The Crying Blood Emperor angrily said: "Azure Edge Sacred Master, encountering the Ancient Sea Domain Ancestor, how can you blame me? To save your two Invincible Domain Ancestors from the Azure Origin Sacred Land, I lost a clone and suffered heavy losses. It's good enough that I'm not bothering you, and you still dare to question me?"
Azure Edge Sacred Master snorted coldly: "Forget it, but since you didn't complete the task I gave you, the Thousand Blood Treasure Lotus and the Phoenix Blood Soul Fruit will not be given to you!"
"Azure Edge Sacred Master, I paid the price of a clone for your Azure Origin Sacred Land, and you're not offering any compensation?"
The Crying Blood Emperor said.
"None!"
Azure Edge Sacred Master replied directly.
"Fine, don't even think about cooperating with me in the future!"
The Crying Blood Emperor said furiously.
The two immediately cut off contact.
Azure Edge Sacred Master stopped thinking about the Crying Blood Emperor; he was now very troubled.
The Azure Origin World Pearl was lost, and Ye Tian would probably soon know that the Azure Origin Sacred Land was behind it. If he informed the War Gate, the War Gate would definitely strike at the Azure Origin Sacred Land.
"I hope Myriad Law Domain Ancestor doesn't inform the War Gate!"
Azure Edge Sacred Master thought to himself.
Afterward, he stopped thinking about this matter and immediately entered seclusion.
If he could break through to the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm, and the Azure Origin Sacred Land had two Supreme Domain Ancestors, their strength would surely greatly increase. Then they wouldn't fear other Sacred Lands, nor would they fear their status being jeopardized. Even the War Gate wouldn't be able to casually let their Azure Origin Sacred Land decline.
Human Realm.
Ye Tian began to sort through the treasures of those two Invincible Domain Ancestors. It had to be said that Invincible Domain Ancestors were... quite wealthy, far wealthier than Supreme Domain Ancestors.
Soon, Ye Tian found the Azure Origin World Pearl.
"This sacred artifact..."
Through the Azure Origin World Pearl, Ye Tian saw a void within it. The mineral vein was sealed inside this Azure Origin World Pearl.
"A very powerful sacred artifact, it actually sealed a void directly and took away a sacred-grade mineral vein.
Such a sacred artifact is not of low value!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Afterward, he investigated a bit and knew what this sacred artifact was.
"The Azure Origin World Pearl, ranked 219th on the Sacred Artifact List, belongs to the Azure Origin Sacred Land. Indeed, the information obtained from their personal introductions, when using the replication talent to investigate those two Invincible Domain Ancestors, was correct.
This time, it was... the Azure Origin Sacred Land coveting the sacred-grade mineral vein!"
Ye Tian was very displeased with the Azure Origin Sacred Land.
However, the opponent was, after all, a Sacred Land. Even if it ranked at the bottom among Sacred Lands, it still had its foundations. To overthrow the Azure Origin Sacred Land all at once was almost unrealistic.
He also couldn't be bothered to inform the War Gate, so as to avoid provoking the Azure Origin Sacred Land's frantic retaliation.
After all, once the Azure Origin Sacred Land was in a desperate situation, it would truly retaliate against humanity.
Humanity was too weak now and still needed to develop.
"I'll destroy the Azure Origin Sacred Land when I get the chance!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Thus, he put away the Azure Origin World Pearl. This single Azure Origin World Pearl was enough to cause the Azure Origin Sacred Land heavy losses.
Subsequently, Ye Tian unsealed the sacred-grade mineral vein from the Azure Origin World Pearl and placed it in a void in the Human Realm, allowing the human Domain Ancestors to mine it.
For humanity, sacred-grade mineral veins were not currently needed, but they could be traded with other Domain Ancestors, or even taken to the War Realm Tower for trade, thereby acquiring a large amount of resources humanity needed.
Moreover, once humanity produced sacred-weapon-grade artifact refiners, these sacred-grade mineral veins would be of great use.
In short, sacred-grade mineral veins were strategic resources of immense importance to a power.
In the days that followed, the Profound Void Sacred Realm passed through a period of peace.
Ye Tian also continuously cultivated in seclusion, constantly improving his Soul Sacred Light.
However, Soul Sacred Light was difficult to improve. Without great opportunities, the progress speed would be very slow.
In the blink of an eye, a hundred million years passed in the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
For Domain Ancestors, a hundred million years was not that long.
But for humanity, a hundred million years was extremely long.
Now, humanity had changed too much.
Not to mention that the number of human Domain Ancestors had increased dozens of times, even the Supreme Domain Ancestors had reached double digits.
For example, Ye Tian's relatives had basically all stepped into the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm, and several talented geniuses among humanity had also entered the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm.
Besides Supreme Domain Ancestors, humanity also had many more top-tier Domain Ancestors.
The Profound Void Sacred Realm was extremely rich in resources, and there were many opportunities that could help Domain Ancestors break through to the top-tier Domain Ancestor realm. Therefore, if human Domain Ancestors obtained even a few good opportunities, they could break through to the top-tier Domain Ancestor realm.
This led to humanity having a great many top-tier Domain Ancestors, even more than many sects in the Dark Sacred Domain.
In terms of overall strength, humanity was already comparable to the sect-level powers of the Dark Sacred Domain.
At this time, Ye Tian also made some progress, but unfortunately, the progress was not very fast; his Soul Sacred Light only increased by thirty percent.
A hundred million years, and the Soul Sacred Light only increased by thirty percent. This speed was simply too slow compared to his previous progress.
"No wonder those Supreme Domain Ancestors in the Dark Sacred Domain all desire various opportunities, even fighting desperately for a single treasure. The progress from arduous cultivation is simply too slow!"
Ye Tian lamented.
This was still with the cultivation amplification from the Profound Void Sacred Realm and the Primordial Creation Well, allowing him to increase his Soul Sacred Light by thirty percent over a hundred million years. If it were other Supreme Domain Ancestors, a hundred million years would probably only yield a maximum of one percent increase in Soul Sacred Light.
On this day.
As Ye Tian was cultivating, he clearly sensed that an astonishing treasure was about to be born deep within the void of the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
This treasure had not yet been born, but it had already caused tremors in the origin of the Profound Void Sacred Realm.
Others might not have noticed, but as the master of the Profound Void Sacred Realm, his perception was too obvious.
Boom!!!! Ye Tian's gaze pierced through the depths of the void, seeing into the infinite depths.
Soon, the phantom of that treasure appeared before Ye Tian.
It was an incredibly ancient stone stele, upon which fluctuations of various worlds were displayed.
Seeing this, Ye Tian immediately recognized it.
"The Sacred Stele!"
