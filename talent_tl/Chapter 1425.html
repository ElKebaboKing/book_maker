Chapter 1424: Star Soul Sand
!
"Next, while practicing the Golden Silk Ancestral Technique, I will strive to break the shackles of the Holy Armor Holy Technique and turn it into the Ancestral Armor Ancestral Technique!"
Ye Tian arranged his next cultivation plan.
With the Defense Ancestral Talent, Ye Tian was not worried about being unable to upgrade the Holy Armor Holy Technique into an ancestral technique.
In the blink of an eye, another several hundred thousand years passed.
During these years, besides constantly copying holy talents and slaying holy soul beasts to strengthen his soul holy light, Ye Tian had been using the Holy Armor Holy Technique to fight in close quarters with numerous holy soul beasts, tempering the technique.
Countless times, Ye Tian's Holy Armor was shattered by the holy soul beasts, but between the breaking and repairing, Ye Tian's comprehension of the Holy Armor Holy Technique grew deeper and deeper. Moreover, because of the Defense Ancestral Talent, Ye Tian faced no bottlenecks in holy techniques; as long as the opportunity arrived, he could upgrade the holy technique into an ancestral technique. This was his advantage.
On this day.
While Ye Tian was fighting a group of holy soul beasts, he finally completed the transformation of the Holy Armor Holy Technique.
Buzz!!! A set of black-gold battle armor replaced Ye Tian's previous Holy Armor. This was a fundamental qualitative change in the technique; it was no longer a holy technique, but an ancestral technique! Ancestral Technique: Ancestral Armor! The power of this Ancestral Armor technique was terrifying, its defensive power many times stronger than the Holy Armor. Ye Tian stood there, allowing a large number of holy soul beasts to attack him, yet they could not break his defense.
Such defense could only be described as horrifyingly formidable! With the Golden Silk Ancestral Technique for attack and the Ancestral Armor Ancestral Technique for defense, Ye Tian's strength could be described as having surged exponentially.
If he could obtain a speed-type ancestral technique, he would be even more well-rounded.
"Next, I'll focus on fully upgrading the Flowing Light Holy Technique!"
Ye Tian murmured.
Originally, Ye Tian wanted to cultivate peacefully like this. After all... he could stay in the Holy Soul Realm for a long time and had plenty of time to enhance his soul holy light and practice holy techniques.
However, on this day, a transmission arrived.
"A transmission from Qingyu Domain Ancestor!"
Ye Tian was surprised.
According to the transmission, the supreme domain ancestors of Zhanmen and those from other factions had discovered a treasure land, a place of true great opportunity. They were currently in a dispute, and a battle could break out at any moment, so they sent a message asking him to come and support them.
Subsequently, other supreme domain ancestors of Zhanmen also sent transmissions to Ye Tian, requesting his support.
"A place of true great opportunity!"
Ye Tian was somewhat curious about this place, but the supreme domain ancestors of Zhanmen did not specify what the opportunity was. He didn't ask further and instead rushed over at his fastest speed.
In the Holy Soul Realm, within a void that resembled a starry sky, this was the interior of the treasure land where a group of supreme domain ancestors had appeared.
Suddenly.
A black sphere the size of a star appeared, immediately triggering a scramble among the supreme domain ancestors.
"This grain of Star Soul Sand is mine!"
Shouted a supreme domain ancestor on the Supreme Rankings.
However, a supreme domain ancestor on the Supreme Rankings was nothing here. A ten-meter-tall giant crushed that supreme domain ancestor to death with one hand.
This giant was not a cultivator but a forbidden creature appearing in human form.
He was the sixth-ranked supreme domain ancestor of the Forbidden Saint Gate—Wu Xiu! Wu Xiu's strength intimidated the other supreme domain ancestors. He instantly snatched the grain of Star Soul Sand and stored it in his soul sea, making it difficult for others to steal it back.
It wasn't that there were no supreme domain ancestors here comparable to Wu Xiu, but this treasure land's void was too vast, and the location where the Star Soul Sand appeared was unpredictable, so it was snatched by Wu Xiu.
"What a pity!"
One powerful supreme domain ancestor after another lamented.
"Wu Xiu, you dare kill a supreme domain ancestor from our Immortal Saint Gate's Supreme Rankings!"
An angry supreme domain ancestor who had just arrived shouted.
He was the seventh-ranked supreme domain ancestor on the Immortal Saint Gate's Supreme Rankings—Golden Snake Domain Ancestor! "Golden Snake Domain Ancestor, if you're not satisfied, then let's have a fight!"
Wu Xiu sneered.
"Hmph!"
Golden Snake Domain Ancestor snorted coldly but did not make a move.
Fighting Wu Xiu without any benefit was completely unnecessary... if he got injured, it would only hinder him from snatching treasures later.
"Coward!"
Wu Xiu chuckled.
Just then, Ye Tian arrived.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor, you've arrived!"
The supreme domain ancestors of Zhanmen greeted Ye Tian upon seeing him.
Among them, Qingyu Domain Ancestor was also present but was significantly injured and was currently healing.
After all, Qingyu Domain Ancestor's strength was not particularly great. Even though his strength had improved over these years, he still couldn't compare to the top-tier supreme domain ancestors on the Supreme Rankings, hence his injuries.
"What kind of treasure land is this?"
Ye Tian asked.
Having just arrived, he didn't understand the situation and was curious about the circumstances here.
"This treasure land is very special; it can produce Star Soul Sand!"
Xihe Domain Ancestor, the thirteenth-ranked supreme domain ancestor of Zhanmen, introduced.
"Star Soul Sand!!!!!"
Ye Tian had naturally heard of this treasure. Though named "sand," its size was comparable to a small star, but in the context of the Dark Holy Domain, it was indeed just... a grain of sand. However, it was a treasure that could strengthen soul holy light.
The value of a single grain of Star Soul Sand was even more precious than a Soul Holy Realm Flower. Thus, one grain of Star Soul Sand was enough for a supreme domain ancestor to cultivate for a long time; after all, its size was large and difficult to consume entirely.
"No wonder it attracted so many supreme domain ancestors. If I obtain enough Star Soul Sand, it will definitely allow me to condense a Holy Soul!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
"Wanfa Domain Ancestor, once Star Soul Sand is discovered, we will work together to snatch it.
A grain of Star Soul Sand is born here almost every day, but it only appears for ten minutes. Therefore, it must be snatched within ten minutes, or it will disappear!"
Xihe Domain Ancestor suggested.
"Agreed!"
Ye Tian nodded.
In reality, he wanted to act alone, but since Xihe Domain Ancestor and Qingyu Domain Ancestor had alerted him to this place, it wouldn't be right to refuse cooperation.
If he helped them snatch enough Star Soul Sand, he could then propose to act independently without it being considered excessive.
Soon, the next day arrived.
At this time, at a location somewhat distant from Ye Tian and the others, a grain of Star Soul Sand appeared.
Wu Xiu wanted to snatch this grain of Star Soul Sand, but the distance was a bit far, while the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor of the Immortal Saint Gate was closer to it.
"Hahaha, I am the closest, no one can beat me to it!"
Golden Snake Domain Ancestor said excitedly.
However, a figure flickered—it was Ye Tian.
Ye Tian utilized the Space Holy Technique, Space Escape. The limit-level Space Holy Technique made Ye Tian's speed incredibly fast, arriving there in an instant.
At the same time, Ye Tian directly used the Five Elements Sealing to lock down the space around himself and the Star Soul Sand, isolating the Golden Snake Domain Ancestor.
"Whoosh!"
The Star Soul Sand was collected by Ye Tian.
