Chapter 1692 Four-Stage Eternal Will!

Inner World.
At this moment, Ye Tian had already turned this place into nothingness. The countless continents originally built within the Inner World had all become nothingness, everything returning to its original state. This was preparatory work for opening up the sea of the source of will.
Immediately afterward, countless will powers poured into this world. According to the operation of the new Will World secret art, the power of will continuously merged into the Inner World, beginning to form a source spring of will, constantly transforming this Inner World and turning it into a sea of the source of will.
However, the Inner World was not an ordinary particle cell, so to transform such a world into a sea of the source of will required endless resources and time.
Fortunately, Ye Tian had obtained too many treasures from those Mo Mountain prisoners. He could take out a few treasures at will and trade with the six great legendary lords or other Immortal Realm lords, thereby obtaining a large amount of resources, enough to complete this cultivation.
Time passed.
One Eternal Year after another went by. The time spent opening up the sea of the source of will was many times greater than the time spent opening up the Will World. Even though this was only an experiment, this time had to be spent. This was a crucial experiment affecting Will World cultivation.
If successful, he would not only break through the shackles of will and advance to Four-Stage Eternal Will, but could even continue cultivating to Five-Stage Will, and even higher will levels.
If he failed, he would be unable to break through and would have to create a new Will World from scratch.
After about ten Eternal Years, the sea of the source of will was finally opened up by Ye Tian.
At this time.
The sea of the source of will was filled with endless will power, but Ye Tian’s current condition was not particularly good. For this breakthrough, nearly all of the other will worlds within his body had collapsed. Still, the sea of the source of will had been opened up, and that was success.
Next, Ye Tian began testing again and again, striving to make the cost of opening up the sea of the source of will as small as possible and ensure there would be no side effects. This required multiple tests and thus a very long time.
However, for Ye Tian, spending a bit more time was worthwhile.
And so, Ye Tian opened up the sea of the source of will again and again. His experience became increasingly skilled, and the price he paid became smaller and smaller.
After about 3,000 Eternal Years, Ye Tian had tried 300 times in succession and could already open up the sea of the source of will at a very small cost. The consequences could all be slowly recovered from, without affecting his foundation.
Thus, on this day, Ye Tian began to formally condense the sea of the source of will.
Boom!!!!!!
The sea of the source of will was opened up, and then Ye Tian began repairing the side effects. Once fully repaired, he could reopen new Will Worlds.
Brand-new Will Worlds surrounded the sea of the source of will, and their number gradually increased.
After many years, the Will Worlds had once again returned to 99,999, and soon another one was condensed, reaching 100,000 Will Worlds.
At that moment, the transformation began.
Ye Tian’s will power continuously became more refined, and it grew stronger at an unbelievable speed. This was the breaking of the will shackles from Three-Stage Eternal Will to Four-Stage Eternal Will.
After a long while, all of Ye Tian’s will had undergone transformation and had already become Four-Stage Eternal Will.
Four-Stage Eternal Will was nearly ten times stronger than Three-Stage Eternal Will. It could even crush ordinary Immortal Realm lords using will alone. That was the power of Four-Stage Eternal Will.
With his will power raised to the fourth stage, Ye Tian’s strength greatly increased. Even without using the Eternal treasure Hunyuan Token, his true combat power could be compared to a relatively weak legendary lord. If he were to cooperate with the Hunyuan Token, among the six legendary lords of the spacetime territory, he might even rank in the middle.
Moreover, now that Ye Tian found it easier to open up Will Worlds, within a certain period of time his Four-Stage will would improve greatly, and his strength still had room to grow for some time.
As for the degree of fusion between will and body, that was also rapidly increasing. Perhaps it would not take too long before he could fuse ninety percent of his body with will and step into the realm of a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord.
In the years that followed, Ye Tian devoted himself fully to raising his cultivation, accelerating the fusion of will and body, striving to reach the Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord realm as soon as possible.
At the same time, he was also helping some human geniuses, opening up many blessed lands, allowing the human race to gradually give birth to several Eternal Realm lords.
It could be said that the human race was now almost the number one race in the source sea.
From beginning to end, Ye Tian had never abandoned the Dark Forbidden Domain. Perhaps many forces did not value the source sea very much, thinking that it had no real resources and was merely vast in area.
But Ye Tian valued the source sea greatly. After all, no matter how good the spacetime territory was, it had to rely on the source sea, and only the source sea possessed the most complete innate eternal rules.
Thus, Ye Tian’s true body had always been in the Dark Forbidden Domain, using the Hunyuan Token to comprehend and improve his cultivation.
One hundred thousand Eternal Years!
Two hundred thousand Eternal Years!
Three hundred thousand Eternal Years!
Finally, on this day, the degree of fusion between Ye Tian’s will and body reached ninety percent, and he became a Nine-Step Immortal Realm lord. However, to completely fuse his body with his will and thereby step into the realm of a legendary lord would require even more time.
Yet Ye Tian did not care in the slightest about this time and cultivated with full force.
At the very least, he could see hope, whereas the spacetime territory also had several Nine-Step Immortal Realm lords who still had no hope of stepping into the level of legendary lord to this day. This showed just how difficult that step was.
If it were not difficult, the spacetime territory would not have only six legendary lords.
On this day.
Ye Tian was cultivating in the Dark Forbidden Domain when a Half-Step Eternal Realm human reported an item of information: a strange anomaly had appeared somewhere in the source sea, but they could not find the location of the anomaly, and they could not even fly to that place.
After receiving this news, Ye Tian rushed to that location immediately.
Source Sea, Shulong Blessed Land.
Ye Tian arrived here and saw a world. In the center of the world, a leaf was being nurtured.
His will tried to approach this place, but it could not extend into it.
“An Eternal treasure?”
Ye Tian guessed.
If that were truly the case, then it would be extraordinary. This might very well be a real Eternal treasure, not a spacetime projection.
With a tearing sound!
Space split open, and a figure appeared.
This person was completely shrouded in black mist and emitted a sinister aura.
“Greetings, Dark Mist Lord!”
Ye Tian said politely.
The person before him was one of the six legendary lords, and quite mysterious at that.
The Dark Mist Lord paid no attention to Ye Tian at all, merely staring intensely at the leaf within the anomaly.
