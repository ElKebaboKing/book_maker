Chapter 1553: Escorting the Eternal Source Object!

Ye Tian and the other nine Invincibles in his squad each received a box and a route map. The final destination of this route map was a mysterious place.
No standard name was listed, but it was very far from the Tianlin Realm. Even following this route, it would likely take millions of years of travel, passing through countless foreign worlds.
He did not know what kind of place was so distant.
Soon, Ye Tian's squad set off, leaving the Tianlin Realm through a Tier 3 gate in Tianfeng Sea and appearing in a foreign world. They then began advancing along the route map through that foreign world.
On the way.
A Rank 4 Invincible named Qing Ou asked curiously, "Do you know what item we are escorting this time? And where is our destination?"
"No idea!"
Everyone shook their heads one by one.
"I might have guessed the destination, but as for what the item is, I'm not sure!" Another female Invincible, Qiong Yu, spoke up. "My parents are members of Tianfeng Sea, but I was born in a foreign world, and I've stayed in Tianfeng Sea for a very long time, at least tens of thousands of kalpa eras. Therefore, I know many pieces of information that other Invincibles don't. For example, this place, if I haven't guessed wrongly, should be the location of an Eternal Realm!"
"Another Eternal Realm?" Everyone was surprised.
Qiong Yu immediately added, "To be precise, it's a shattered Eternal Realm. Eternal Realms can exist eternally and won't undergo annihilation under normal circumstances, but that Eternal Realm was shattered during a great war, resulting in a special situation that caused it to evolve into a special area. It seems to contain great opportunities, and even Supreme Beings will enter there to seek them. Therefore, it is extremely cruel, not as peaceful as the Tianlin Realm. Even Rank 3 and Rank 4 Invincibles are just cannon fodder there and don't dare to step in easily; otherwise, they could easily be killed by stronger Invincibles."
"So, our mission is to deliver the box to a place in this shattered Eternal Realm and hand it over to someone?"
Ye Tian said.
"It should be so!" Qiong Yu nodded. "We just don't know what the item is. Is it in the boxes in our hands? However, this mission is very dangerous. The enemy might have deployed more Rank 4 Invincibles or even Rank 3 Invincibles. If we are unlucky enough to be discovered by the enemy, it's almost certain death. Therefore, we must slow down, not reveal our aura, and slowly tear through the barriers of the foreign worlds, not letting other Invincibles discover us."
……
Wufeng Mountain.
Supreme Qingyang of Tianfeng Sea and several other Supremes were discussing.
"Supreme Qingyang, is the plan arranged?"
Another Rank 1 Invincible of Tianfeng Sea, Supreme Tulong, asked.
Supreme Qingyang nodded. "It has been arranged. Ten Invincibles per team, all have been sent out. The Eternal Source Object, the Mind Azure Gold, has been carried by ten Rank 4 Invincibles we secretly cultivated. Each of their strengths is comparable to a Rank 3 Invincible. Without the enemy knowing the specific situation, there is over a 90% chance of delivering the Mind Azure Gold to the Yuanji Abyss. Once the leader obtains the Mind Azure Gold, not only can he heal his injuries, but he can also break through to the Supreme Being realm in one fell swoop. At that time, we won't fear other Eternal-level forces!"
"During this time, let's not go out; otherwise, once we encounter an assassination by a Supreme Being, none of us will survive."
"Yes, we can't go out. In the Tianlin Realm, there are constraints from the three Great Supreme Beings, so the Supreme Beings of the Nether Hall won't break the rules to attack our Tianfeng Sea. But outside the Tianlin Realm, they will have no scruples, and the three Great Supreme Beings won't be able to control them."
"Supreme Beings cannot enter the Yuanji Abyss. The people we dispatched can enter the Yuanji Abyss directly through the foreign worlds. Although Supreme Beings can enter foreign worlds, they need to pay a certain price. Moreover, foreign worlds are endless, and they don't know our plan, so it's hard to find people. Therefore, our success rate is still very high."
"Humph, the Supreme Beings of the Nether Hall are truly too much. The leader was able to find an opportunity in the Yuanji Abyss to break the shackles and has hope of promoting to a Supreme Being. The Rank 1 Invincibles of the Nether Hall couldn't compete, so they want to kill the leader and even want to wipe out our Tianfeng Sea!"
"The Nether Hall only has one more Supreme Being than us. At the Invincible level, they are not much stronger than us."

"It's a pity for these Rank 4 Invincibles; they are likely to suffer heavy losses this time. If any Invincibles survive, we will give them enough benefits!"
Ye Tian and the others were not yet clear that Tianfeng Sea had offended an Eternal-level force and a Supreme Being; otherwise, everyone would be terrified.
After all, this matter involved a Supreme Being. If they knew the truth, who wouldn't panic? They might even be scared into surrendering.
This was also why Tianfeng Sea kept it a secret, made them swear oaths, and had them set off immediately without giving them time to prepare—to prevent them from having a chance to defect. Once the Invincibles of the Nether Hall contacted them, the plan would be ruined.
... ......... .......
At the same time.
An order was issued within the Nether Hall, and dense crowds of Invincibles set out, heading to various foreign worlds to search for the whereabouts of the Tianfeng Sea Invincibles.
The Nether Hall also had informants within Tianfeng Sea, so they knew immediately who had taken this mission and collected the data on these Rank 4 Invincibles. Once encountered, they would be killed immediately to reclaim those boxes.
While the Nether Hall and Tianfeng Sea were gaming each other, the Invincibles of many foreign worlds suffered, accidentally being killed by mistake.
Ye Tian also felt the change in the situation and led a group of human elites directly into the Yuhua World. He had already treated this place as a secret world for the human race, and it was now hidden, so it would not be easily discovered.
As for the Forbidden Dark Zone, there were no longer any Invincibles there, so it naturally wouldn't attract the attention of the Nether Hall.
It could be said that Ye Tian's move prevented the human race and the Forbidden Dark Zone from being affected by the Nether Hall, which was a fortunate thing.
As time slowly passed, and with the Nether Hall searching for the traces of Tianfeng Sea Invincibles at all costs—even mobilizing many investigative treasures to search across foreign worlds—they really did find some Invincibles.
Consequently, one Invincible after another fell in battle with the Invincibles of the Nether Hall.
Ye Tian and his group did not escape the pursuit either and were intercepted by a group of Rank 4 Invincibles in a foreign world.
