Chapter 1541: The First Stage of the Invincible Calamity Body Reaches Perfection!

A 60-Calamity beast was nothing special; Qi Yang could easily slay one.
However, finding a 60-Calamity beast was no easy task.
Even with the strength of a top-tier fifth-rank Invincible, he wouldn't dare go looking for Calamity Beasts recklessly, or he would have been dead long ago.
Yet, Ye Tian had handed over so much 60-Calamity blood and bone, clearly indicating he had slain a 60-Calamity beast.
Although Qi Yang was shocked by Ye Tian's harvest, he didn't ask any questions. After all, how Ye Tian managed to kill a 60-Calamity beast was his own business. Since he wasn't close to Ye Tian, he naturally wouldn't pry.
Afterward, he processed the task submission for Ye Tian.
Sixteen portions of 60-Calamity blood plus fifteen 60-Calamity bones earned Ye Tian a total of 30,000 contribution points. This was a significant amount, considering a single top-grade Calamity Stone only cost fifty contribution points.
One could imagine how exaggerated a value 30,000 contribution points was.
Originally, Ye Tian only had a bit over a hundred contribution points, which he had earned by turning in a single portion of Calamity blood. Now, with 30,000 points added all at once, it was enough to exchange for a vast amount of good items.
In reality, if other members of the Tianfeng Sea obtained Calamity blood or bones, they would rarely turn them in for contribution points. They would use the blood and bones that were useful to them and give those that weren't to their descendants.
A sixth-rank Invincible like Ye Tian, who turned in all of his 60-Calamity blood and bones, was absolutely rare.
"I want to exchange for top-grade Calamity Stones. Are there any here?"
Ye Tian asked.
Qi Yang checked and said, "There are top-grade Calamity Stones, but only six!"
"I'll take them all!" Ye Tian said.
One top-grade Calamity Stone cost 50 contribution points, so six only cost 300 points.
In truth, these six top-grade Calamity Stones were far more helpful to Ye Tian than the 60-Calamity blood, but because others couldn't utilize top-grade Calamity Stones, they were almost entirely ignored.
After obtaining the six top-grade Calamity Stones, Ye Tian left and returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian's duplicate body used the top-grade Calamity Stones to cultivate the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, gradually perfecting the technique and making this Invincible Calamity Body method perfectly compatible with himself.
On the other hand, he would occasionally send his duplicate body to hunt suitable Calamity Beasts.
Time flew by, and one Dark Era passed.
During this Dark Era, Ye Tian hunted a total of six Calamity Beasts, yielding a great harvest. He also traveled to the Tianlin Realm again and exchanged for several hundred top-grade Calamity Stones from the Tianfeng Sea.
At Ye Tian's request, these Calamity Stones came from different foreign realms, thus containing the rules of various foreign worlds, which met the requirements for cultivating the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void.
After all, the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void required the rules of different foreign realms to temper the Invincible Calamity Body for it to progress.
Different Calamity Stones possessed different properties, allowing them to serve as substitutes for Calamity blood.
Through these hundreds of top-grade Calamity Stones, combined with his duplicate body, Ye Tian perfected the first stage of the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void Invincible Calamity Body technique.
If it were anyone else, they would have had no way to perfect this Invincible Calamity Body method.
It was also fortunate that Ye Tian possessed the Calamity Transformation Secret Technique and the Eternal Talent: Calamity. Otherwise, if he had to rely solely on absorbing and refining Calamity blood, he would likely have needed a massive amount of it to reach this step.
It had to be said that cultivating a top-tier Invincible Calamity Body required far too many resources. Moreover, since he had to repair the flaws of the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void, his resource consumption was several times, if not ten times, higher.
One by one, the top-grade Calamity Stones were consumed and refined by Ye Tian. Finally, on this day, Ye Tian's main body successfully cultivated the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void to the perfection of the first stage. Furthermore, it was a perfect first-stage completion without any flaws.
While ordinary Invincible Calamity Body methods only made a sixth-rank Invincible three times stronger than one who hadn't cultivated such a body, the top-tier Myriad Origins Returning to the Void made him nine times stronger.
More importantly, Ye Tian had laid a supreme foundation.
Once Ye Tian stepped into the fifth-rank Invincible realm and cultivated the Myriad Origins Returning to the Void to the perfection of the second stage, which would also be a nine-fold increase, the stacking effect might not reach eighty-one times, but it would at least reach forty to fifty times.
An Invincible Calamity Body forty to fifty times stronger—what kind of concept was that? He could easily insta-kill ordinary Invincibles of the same rank.
However, top-tier Invincible Calamity Bodies had flaws. If others couldn't resolve them, it was difficult to reach the perfection of the first stage. By the later stages, they might reach the minor success of the third stage and then be unable to progress further.
At that point, if each stage was only at minor success or entry-level, it would only be slightly stronger than an ordinary Invincible Calamity Body method, which wasn't very useful.
But Ye Tian could perfect the top-tier Invincible Calamity Body, reaching perfection at every stage. Once stacked to the first-rank Invincible realm, his strength would likely allow him to insta-kill those first-rank Invincibles, truly becoming invincible among Invincibles, and perhaps even fighting against Supremes.
...
"The Invincible Calamity Body has reached the perfection of the first stage. My current combat power has actually surpassed many fifth-rank Invincibles. It's time to try and create an Invincible Ancestral Skill!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Subsequently.
Ye Tian spent a few contribution points to exchange for a secret manual on comprehending Invincible Skills from the Tianfeng Sea. This information was very comprehensive and would save Ye Tian from taking many detours.
Whether it was a sixth-rank Invincible from a foreign realm or the Eternal Realm of Tianlin, if they wanted to advance to the fifth-rank Invincible realm, they had to comprehend an Invincible Skill, such as an Invincible Ancestral Skill or an Invincible Battle Skill. The names varied, but the essence was the same.
An Invincible Skill was an application of the Eternal Realm's rules—a higher level of rule that stood above ordinary foreign realm rules.
Under normal circumstances, it was nearly impossible for a sixth-rank Invincible to comprehend an Invincible Skill.
Because of this, countless sixth-rank Invincibles were stuck at this level, unable to advance to the fifth-rank Invincible realm.
There were three common ways for a sixth-rank Invincible to advance to the fifth rank. The first was through long periods of meditation while staying in the Eternal Realm. As long as one had potential, they would eventually comprehend the rules of the Eternal Realm and create an Invincible Skill.
The second way was through Rule Heavenly Objects born from the Eternal Realm. By using these to comprehend the rules of the Eternal Realm and create an Invincible Skill, the speed of comprehension was very fast. As long as one's talent wasn't poor, they could almost certainly create an Invincible Skill.
However, Rule Heavenly Objects were too precious. Ordinary Invincibles simply couldn't obtain them. Only powerful Saint-Name level organizations possessed Rule Heavenly Objects, and often more than one.
The third way was to refine an Eternal Rule Pill. These were created by Supremes or a few first-rank Invincibles through alchemy, using countless rare and precious materials to melt the rules of the Eternal Realm and forcibly integrate a wisp of those rules into a pill. After refining an Eternal Rule Pill, one could master that specific Eternal Realm rule and gradually create an Invincible Skill.
This third method was the most common in the Tianlin Realm. Countless sixth-rank Invincibles sought an Eternal Rule Pill, accumulating resources for ages just to buy one.
Measuring the value of an Eternal Rule Pill in Origin Rule Calamity Crystals, it was worth a full 10 million crystals, which was not something an ordinary Invincible could produce.
However, Ye Tian would not choose the third method. While it could lead to the fifth-rank Invincible realm, future progress would be difficult, making it far inferior to the second method.
In the past, Ye Tian naturally had no way to use the second method to advance, but now that he had joined the Tianfeng Sea, and the Tianfeng Sea possessed Rule Heavenly Objects, he only needed to pay a certain amount of contribution points to meditate on them.
This was also one of the reasons why countless Invincibles wanted to join Saint-Name level organizations.
