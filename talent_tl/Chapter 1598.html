Chapter 1597: An Unexpected Opportunity?

Chahai Source God noticed Ye Tian's arrival and immediately said, "Excellency, please leave this place. This area has already been occupied by my friend. Please show some respect."
He didn't want to fight because it would easily affect the Nether Hall Master. Otherwise, if they were in the outside world, he wouldn't have been so polite from the start.
"Chahai Source God, I have a conflict with the Nether Hall Master. If you choose to leave, I will not stop you!"
Ye Tian said.
Hearing Ye Tian's words, Chahai Source God was instantly infuriated.
"You have a lot of nerve. Get lost immediately, or die!"
Chahai Source God said angrily.
Boom!!!!
Ye Tian's aura enveloped the area, crushing down toward Chahai Source God. His intention was clear: he was going to make a move.
Chahai Source God no longer had any reservations and attacked directly. As long as he was careful, he wouldn't disrupt the Nether Hall Master's promotion.
He was already prepared to kill Ye Tian; only then could his anger be appeased.
"Supreme Technique: Nether World!"
A dark world manifested around Ye Tian. Terrifying black hands, formed entirely from the Eternal Rules of Darkness, appeared. The power of each strike was enough to heavily injure an ordinary First-Tier Supreme Being.
These black hands grabbed at Ye Tian simultaneously, as if they wanted to tear him to pieces.
"A decent Supreme Technique, but unfortunately, its power is too weak!"
Ye Tian sneered.
In the next moment, an even more terrifying power directly shattered the Nether World.
Then.
The Five Elements Eternal Rules were mobilized by Ye Tian, transforming into a world.
"Supreme Ancestral Technique—Five Elements Eternal World!"
This was a Supreme Ancestral Technique evolved and created by deducing from the Five Elements Sealing step by step and merging it with other Five Elements-type Invincible techniques.
The Five Elements Eternal World was a Supreme Ancestral Technique that combined suppression, sealing, and defensive effects, making it comparable to the top-tier Supreme Techniques.
Rumble!!!!
The power of the Five Elements Eternal World acted upon Chahai Source God, causing his strength to plummet instantly, making it difficult for him to exert even half of his power.
At this moment, Chahai Source God finally realized that Ye Tian's strength was above his own, far beyond that of an ordinary First-Tier Supreme Being.
"Escape!"
He wanted to flee, but he couldn't tear through the void here. The void had been completely suppressed by the Five Elements Eternal World.
More importantly, this place was a treasure land in the depths of the void. Even many escape-type treasures couldn't leave this place; one had to exit through the entrance.
In other words, he couldn't escape.
On the other side, Tianfeng Source God attacked the defensive array set up by the Nether Hall Master.
Rumble!!!!
Attack after attack slammed into the defensive formation, causing the void inside the array to vibrate. The Nether Hall Master's breakthrough was affected.
"Someone is attacking the formation?"
The Nether Hall Master knew someone was hindering his breakthrough and had even guessed who it was.
It had to be his arch-rival, Tianfeng Source God. He had been very careful and even invited Chahai Source God, yet Tianfeng Source God still found out and made a move against him.
He didn't know why Chahai Source God hadn't stopped Tianfeng Source God, but at this moment, he had to focus all his mind on the breakthrough.
But the void vibrations were becoming more and more severe. Under such circumstances, how could he focus entirely on breaking through?
Pfft!
The Nether Hall Master suddenly spat out blood, and his body began to crack.
His attempt was interrupted, counting as a failed breakthrough. Furthermore, his foundation was affected, and his body suffered severe injuries.
"Tianfeng Source God!"
The Nether Hall Master was furious.
Whoosh!
The Nether Hall Master rushed out of the array and indeed saw Tianfeng Source God attacking the formation.
"Tianfeng Source God, I'll fight you to the death!"
The Nether Hall Master forcibly performed a Forbidden technique to quickly recover from his injuries and began a life-and-death struggle with Tianfeng Source God.
......
"It seems the Nether Hall Master's breakthrough failed. Then I'll stop playing around!"
Ye Tian didn't intend to let Chahai Source God leave. After all, he had already offended him, and given Chahai Source God's temperament, he would definitely seek revenge and might even involve the human race.
Therefore, he would just kill him.
"Void Shatter!"
Ye Tian casually struck with the Void Shatter Supreme Technique, blasting Chahai Source God.
In the next moment, Chahai Source God's body was smashed to pieces, his life force dissipating. He was actually killed in a single blow.
Next, Ye Tian collected Chahai Source God's Void Sphere and began to watch the battle between Tianfeng Source God and the Nether Hall Master.
He didn't go up to help; in fact, his help wasn't needed.
The Nether Hall Master had forcibly recovered from his injuries and couldn't hold out for long. The final victory would inevitably belong to Tianfeng Source God.
The Nether Hall Master also knew that Chahai Source God had fallen. His heart grew increasingly desperate; under these circumstances, how could he be a match for Tianfeng Source God?
Soon, his injuries relapsed, and he had no time to stabilize them.
Suddenly.
Tianfeng Source God landed a blow on the Nether Hall Master, heavily injuring him once again.
In a short while, the Nether Hall Master was already mortally wounded and dying, with no hope of survival left.
Tianfeng Source God didn't leave the Nether Hall Master any hope, not stopping for a moment.
Ultimately, the Nether Hall Master died at the hands of Tianfeng Source God.
"Tianfeng, let's go!"
Ye Tian urged.
Tianfeng Source God nodded and quickly left the place.
Shortly after, the two returned to the Tianlin Realm.
......
"Ye Tian, I really owe it to you this time. Otherwise, I couldn't have killed the Nether Hall Master!"
Tianfeng Source God said excitedly.
"I am also a member of the Tianfeng Sea, so naturally, I should help!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Although Tianfeng Source God didn't know to what extent Ye Tian's strength had reached, the fact that Ye Tian could kill Chahai Source God meant his power was likely far beyond his imagination.
He didn't ask too many questions to avoid displeasing Ye Tian.
The two chatted for a while and then parted ways.
In the following days, the Tianfeng Sea prepared to encroach upon the Nether Hall's territory. While they wouldn't necessarily destroy the Nether Hall, they could at least take the opportunity to gain some benefits.
Ye Tian also indicated that he would be going into secluded cultivation next, so Tianfeng Source God would not disturb him.
------------------
Void Source Hall.
Ye Tian returned and organized Chahai Source God's Void Sphere.
He originally had no interest in Chahai Source God's Void Sphere. After all, what good things could be inside the Void Sphere of a First-Tier Supreme Being?
But soon, he realized he had underestimated Chahai Source God. The other's net worth was truly extraordinary, at least much higher than that of the ten great Supreme Beings in the Star Ancient Domain.
Item after item was sorted out by Ye Tian, categorized, and placed in the treasury of the Void Source Hall.
The artifact spirit also helped with the organizing. After all, the artifact spirit had broader knowledge and could often identify good things that Ye Tian didn't recognize.
Suddenly.
The artifact spirit picked up a black statue and immediately showed a shocked expression.
"Master, you have obtained a great opportunity!"
The artifact spirit said.
