"With these Invincible Domain Ancestors from the War Gate guarding it, the Profound Void Holy Realm is safe!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
He was also very clear that while the Holy Stele was of high value, it wasn't to the extent of being... terrifying, not enough to make the Holy Gates dispatch a large number of first and second....,....step Invincible Domain Ancestors to fight the War Gate.
Once a large number of first and second....,....step Invincible Domain Ancestors were involved in a battle, it would definitely involve the struggle of Supreme-level Domain Ancestors, and then the conflict would completely escalate.
Moreover, the Supreme-level Domain Ancestors of the various Holy Gates still had their eyes on the Primeval Ancestral Realm; any treasure casually obtained there would be more valuable than a Holy Stele.
Even the Fire-Patterned War Ancestor, his main body was in the Primeval Ancestral Realm, with only a clone staying in the War Ancestor Realm.
Therefore, Ye Tian was not worried that Supreme-level Domain Ancestors would come.
....As time passed, the day of the Holy Stele's birth drew closer and closer.
On this day, the Holy Stele was finally born.
Boom!!!! An incredibly massive Holy Stele stood in the central region of the Profound Void Holy Realm, its power continuously spreading, and countless Domain Ancestors became aware that a supreme treasure had been born in the Profound Void Holy Realm.
Thus, the gazes of one Domain Ancestor after another turned towards the direction of the Holy Stele.
Most Domain Ancestors were unaware of the Holy Stele's existence, but a few Supreme Domain Ancestors recognized it.
"The Holy Stele, it's finally born!"
Azure Fish Domain Ancestor and the other Supreme Domain Ancestors on the War Gate's Supreme Ranking were all incredibly excited.
With the Holy Stele born, they could obtain Holy Stele slots, enter the Holy Stele World to cultivate, and quickly enhance their Soul Holy Light.
Especially a few Supreme Domain Ancestors who were on the verge of condensing their Holy Souls, if they entered the Holy Stele World to cultivate, they would have a ninety percent or higher chance of condensing a Holy Soul.
In that case, they could immediately charge towards becoming Invincible Domain Ancestors, and their strength would reach an incredibly terrifying level.
They had finally waited for such a great opportunity.
Whoosh whoosh whoosh!!!! One Supreme Domain Ancestor after another flew towards the Holy Stele, and Ye Tian had also arrived at the Holy Stele long ago.
"worlds!"
Ye Tian looked at the 100 world entrances displayed on the Holy Stele and was immediately surprised.
Normally, many Holy Steles only had dozens of slots, better Holy Steles had hundreds of slots, but Holy Steles with thousands of slots were extremely rare.
"Originally, I only sold dozens of slots, and there are still over 900 slots. Most of them can be given to the Human Race's Domain Ancestors, and the remaining ones can continue to be sold to the War Gate or other Supreme Domain Ancestors!"
Ye Tian said with a smile.
Of course, the price set now was definitely very high; even a first-tier Supreme Domain Ancestor, even if they exhausted all their assets, might not be able to afford this price.
In short, he didn't care whether others could afford it or not; he had set it that way.
No matter....
how expensive the price, there were definitely many Supreme Domain Ancestors who wanted a Holy Stele slot.
"This is the Holy Stele!"
Some foreign Supreme Domain Ancestors saw the stele and immediately went crazy.
They also saw Ye Tian and were very clear that Ye Tian would definitely not give them Holy Stele slots.
However, once they rushed into the worlds within the Holy Stele and refined the Holy Stele World's core, it would be equivalent to forcibly obtaining a slot, and by then, even Ye Tian would be unable to strip them of their slots.
So, they rushed towards the Holy Stele recklessly.
"Courting death!"
Ye Tian made his move.
"Golden Silk Holy Art!"
Pfft pfft pfft!!!! In the void, countless golden threads cut towards those Supreme Domain Ancestors, instantly killing them one by one, leaving them with no power to resist whatsoever.
The strength of this group of Supreme Domain Ancestors was merely that of ordinary Supreme Domain Ancestors or third-tier Supreme Domain Ancestors, with not even a few second-tier Supreme Domain Ancestors among them. They were simply... a bunch of ants, yet they dared to rush into the Holy Stele. Even an Invincible Domain Ancestor, if they dared to do so at this moment, would be suppressed and killed by those few first and second....,....step Invincible Domain Ancestors of the War Gate who were secretly present.
Every Holy Stele slot was extremely precious, and the War Gate naturally wouldn't casually allow these slots to be snatched away.
"Supreme Domain Ancestors of the War Gate who have obtained Holy Stele slots, you may now enter!"
Ye Tian said.
"Thank you, Myriad Law Domain Ancestor!"
Azure Fish Domain Ancestor, Oolong Domain Ancestor, and others flew into the Holy Stele one by one, each choosing a world and flying in through its world entrance.
As long as they completely refined the world core and closed the world entrance, others would have no way to enter or disturb their cultivation.
"Yue'er, you, our son, daughter, and my sister, choose a world and go in!"
Ye Tian sent a message.
"Okay, husband!"
Xiao Yue replied.
Subsequently, Xiao Yue, Ye Xingchen, Ye Xingyue, and Ye Yu each entered a world.
After that, the top-tier Domain Ancestors of the Human Race, who had already been designated, also entered the Holy Stele World one by one.
Before this, Ye Tian conducted an assessment and selected a total of 158 talented Domain Ancestors. Now, all of them had entered the Holy Stele World, making countless Domain Ancestors envious.
After all, these people were just top-tier Domain Ancestors! Entering the Holy Stele World was simply a waste! The Holy Stele World was of great use only to Supreme Domain Ancestors who had already condensed their Soul Holy Light! Of course, top-tier Domain Ancestors or ordinary Supreme Domain Ancestors, once they entered the Holy Stele World, their strength would naturally improve very quickly.
Especially top-tier Domain Ancestors, if they cultivated in the Holy Stele World, they would have a hundred percent chance of breaking through to the Supreme Domain Ancestor realm.
"Myriad Law Domain Ancestor, I should be able to go in, right?"
Light-Clothed Saintess appeared, asking with a smile.
"Light-Clothed Saintess, you can have a slot!"
Ye Tian nodded.
Whoosh! Light-Clothed Saintess smiled and flew into the Holy Stele World, making all the other Domain Ancestors envious.
Next, Ye Tian sent out a clone to continue selecting a batch of Domain Ancestors from among the many Human Race Domain Ancestors. But no matter what, to obtain a Holy Stele slot, even for the Human Race, one must at least possess the cultivation of a top-tier Domain Ancestor, otherwise giving them a Holy Stele slot would truly be a waste.
After all, the Holy Stele World's cultivation effect was best at the beginning, then it would be equivalent to developing resistance, and the effect would become worse and worse, until in the later stages, it would only be considered a decent cultivation treasure land.
Otherwise, the value of the Holy Stele would be more than just this.
At this moment.
The various Holy Gates and Holy Lands of the Dark Holy Domain all learned that a Holy Stele had been born in the Profound Void Holy Realm, and it even had as many as 100 slots.
It was normal for a Holy Realm to give birth to a Holy Stele, but they never expected it to be born so quickly, and with so many slots.
For a time, numerous forces were shaken and dispatched people to the Profound Void Holy Realm one after another.
