Chapter 1605: The Battle of Fenggu River!

It was more difficult for a Second Supreme Being to advance to a Third Supreme Being than for a First Supreme Being to advance to a Second Supreme Being, but the difficulty was only somewhat greater.
However, last time, in order to break through to the Second Supreme Being, Ye Tian had used up all of the Eternal Ancient Pine Leaves and the other auxiliary materials. Thus, if he were to continue striving for the Third Supreme Being realm now, the success rate would not be very high.
So Ye Tian entered the Source God Pavilion again.
However, there were too few resources that could help a Second Supreme Being break through to a Third Supreme Being. Even within the Source God Pavilion, it would be very difficult to trade for such resources unless one happened to possess resources desired by other Supreme Beings, in which case one could trade for the resources one needed.
“Eh, there’s actually a mission posted by one of the twelve ancestors of the Source God Pavilion!”
Ye Tian said in surprise.
This mission concerned the Battle of Fenggu River. Fenggu River was one of the properties of Fenggu Source Ancestor, one of the twelve ancestors of the Source God Pavilion. Fenggu River could also be said to be a treasured land, continuously giving birth to Fengyuan Crystal Sand.
Fengyuan Crystal Sand was one of the materials used to refine source weapons, and it held great value in the Sea of Sources. One could imagine how much Fengyuan Crystal Sand the entire Fenggu River could produce.
However, not long ago, the Eternal Tribulation Race had set its sights on Fenggu River and used special means to drag Fenggu River into the depths of the void, preparing to transform Fenggu River into an Eternal Tribulation Domain, and an advanced Eternal Tribulation Domain at that.
This time, the Eternal Tribulation Race had mobilized a large number of its clansmen, even six Half-Step Eternal Realm experts, joining forces to lay out the scheme.
Because it was a sudden move and the defenders of Fenggu River were wiped out instantly, the news never spread. By the time Fenggu Source Ancestor learned of it, Fenggu River had already been half transformed.
At present, Fenggu River had not yet been fully transformed and was only equivalent to a mid-level Eternal Tribulation Domain, allowing at most Sixth Supreme Beings to enter.
Fenggu Source Ancestor was now preparing to take Fenggu River back.
To that end, Fenggu Source Ancestor had dispatched a large number of Supreme Beings into Fenggu River to slaughter the Eternal Tribulation Race. If they could kill a large number of Eternal Tribulation Race members, or even wipe out the Eternal Tribulation Race within Fenggu River, then the Eternal Tribulation Race would be unable to transform Fenggu River, and there would be hope for Fenggu Source Ancestor to reclaim it.
Ye Tian was aware that many Eternal Tribulation Domains were transformed from treasured lands. He just had not expected the Eternal Tribulation Ancestor to be so bold as to actually strike against Fenggu Source Ancestor, one of the twelve ancestors of the Source God Pavilion. Was it really worth offending such a powerful being?
If Fenggu Source Ancestor were to spare no cost, he would definitely bring enormous losses to the Eternal Tribulation Race.
Ye Tian did not dwell on the reason. For him, this was also a good thing.
At the very least, in order to gather more Supreme Beings, Fenggu Source Ancestor had posted this mission within the Source God Pavilion, and the rewards were extremely generous.
Among these lists were many resources that could help Supreme Beings break through their realm. Some of them had no side effects at all, and there were even top-tier resources that could help Sixth Supreme Beings break through to Seventh Supreme Beings.
Of course, if one wanted better treasures, one had to kill more members of the Eternal Tribulation Race.
If Ye Tian were to kill an Eternal Tribulation Race member at the level of a Sixth Supreme Being, it would already be enough to exchange for resources sufficient for him to break through to the Third Supreme Being realm.
Unfortunately, it was very difficult to obtain too many resources from Star Ancestor. After all, Star Ancestor was sealed and was also consuming resources himself. On top of that, he had been sealed too early, so the resources accumulated on him were not very many. Otherwise, if Ye Tian squeezed him a few more times, he would also be able to gather enough resources, and there would be no need to obtain resources from Fenggu Source Ancestor here.
“I’ll take this mission!”
Ye Tian decided.
Although his cultivation was only at the Second Supreme Being realm, even without relying on the finger of an Eternal Realm mighty figure, he was already capable of displaying combat strength comparable to an ordinary Fifth Supreme Being. If he used the power of the Eternal Realm finger, one strike would be enough to heavily injure or even kill a relatively weak Eighth Supreme Being.
But compared with Ninth Supreme Beings, he was still far from enough. After all, at most he could unleash one strike, and he still could not harm a Ninth Supreme Being.
Yet Fenggu River only allowed Sixth Supreme Beings to enter. What did he have to fear?
After taking the mission, Ye Tian dispatched his clone body, carrying a section of an Eternal Realm finger, to Fenggu River.
……
Fenggu River.
Ye Tian arrived here, and after reporting his identity, he entered Fenggu River.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian passed through a spatial rift and arrived above a boundless stretch of river.
This was Fenggu River.
Fenggu River was composed of islands and river water, and Fengyuan Crystal Sand existed at the bottom of Fenggu River.
However, without enough manpower and time, not much Fengyuan Crystal Sand could be collected, so Ye Tian had little interest in collecting it.
Whoosh!
Ye Tian moved through Fenggu River without the slightest concealment of his aura. If he could attract the Eternal Tribulation Race to attack him, that would be even better.
If the other side underestimated him and only thought he was an ordinary Second Supreme Being, then that would be sending him heads on a platter.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian’s clone talent sensed a talent’s existence, and an Eternal Tribulation Race member appeared in his perception.
The other side had concealed its form and aura and was approaching him.
Boom!
The other side suddenly launched a sneak attack. It was an Eternal Tribulation Race member comparable to the Fifth Supreme Being realm.
The other side was very excited, believing that it would certainly be able to kill Ye Tian with one strike.
However, after Ye Tian added many Supreme techniques for defense to himself, he immediately executed the Destruction Severing Finger.
Of course, Ye Tian did not fully activate the Destruction Severing Finger, but only unleashed part of its power, displaying a strike comparable to the Seventh Supreme Being level.
That Eternal Tribulation Race member at the Fifth Supreme Being realm had completely not expected Ye Tian’s strength to be so terrifying. Only at the Second Supreme Being realm, yet he actually unleashed a strike at the Seventh Supreme Being realm—this was unreasonable!
However, he had no time to think further.
With a puff!
His body was pierced with a huge hole, and all vitality was annihilated, turning into a corpse.
Ye Tian forcefully suppressed the discomfort in his body, immediately put away the corpse of that Eternal Tribulation Race member, and then quickly left the area.
Much later.
Ye Tian found an island, hid within a grain of sand, and did his utmost to conceal his aura before finally breathing a sigh of relief.
Then, he began to heal the injuries brought about by activating the Eternal Realm finger.
Much later, the injuries finally recovered.
“Continue the operation!”
Ye Tian muttered.
Whoosh!
He left this place and continued to use this method to lure members of the Eternal Tribulation Race into attacking him, then counterkill them.
Of course, Ye Tian was also extremely cautious. Once he sensed two or more members of the Eternal Tribulation Race, he would immediately leave.
Just like that, tens of thousands of years passed.
In total, Ye Tian had killed thirteen members of the Eternal Tribulation Race: six at the Fourth Supreme Being realm, five at the Fifth Supreme Being realm, and two at the Sixth Supreme Being realm.
In order to kill those Sixth Supreme Beings, Ye Tian even activated the Eternal Realm finger twice, and the injuries were not light.
