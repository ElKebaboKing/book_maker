Chapter 1614 First Eternal Skill!

“It’s time to completely settle the matters in the Starry Ancient Domain. It’s no longer of much value to me there, and it’s not worth having a clone permanently stationed there!”
Ye Tian muttered.
After all, his regular combat strength could already reach the Ninth Supreme Being, and Star Ancestor’s strength was at about that level too. Although he was a bit stronger than Ye Tian, he definitely wasn’t much stronger.
Therefore, Star Ancestor was not of much value to him either.
Besides, Star Ancestor wouldn’t have many treasures on him, and there was no need for him to keep squeezing him for more benefits.
On this day, Ye Tian’s Ancient Star Ancestor clone arrived at the Ancient Star Tower.
“Xu Chen, you’re here!”
Seeing Ye Tian arrive, Star Ancestor showed himself.
“Star Ancestor, I’m here to bid you farewell!”
Ye Tian said.
When Star Ancestor heard Ye Tian’s tone, his expression immediately changed.
“Xu Chen, what do you mean by this?” Star Ancestor said coldly. “There is a contract between us.”
“That’s right!” Ye Tian nodded. “There is indeed a contract between us, but what if this is a special clone?”
“A special clone!”
Star Ancestor’s expression changed drastically.
If the one who had signed the contract with him was Ye Tian’s special clone, then if Ye Tian destroyed this body himself, the contract would no longer count, and it would be impossible to restrain Ye Tian anymore.
“Who exactly are you?”
Star Ancestor roared.
He finally understood why Ye Tian had killed the ten Supreme Beings, and why everything he had done was fundamentally not beneficial to the development of the Ancient Star Race.
The reason for all of it was that Ye Tian was a lifeform from the outside world. He had created this Ancient Star Race clone and sent the clone into the Starry Ancient Domain, allowing it to grow to this day.
He hated it!
“Damn it, why did I not see that this was a clone?”
Star Ancestor said in regret.
If it had been an ordinary clone, he would definitely have seen through it.
But Ye Tian’s clone was actually no different from the main body, so Star Ancestor could not tell.
“Star Ancestor, I’ve told you the reason, so you no longer have any doubts. I’ll seal this place, and your consciousness will no longer be able to break the seal. Moreover, I’ll take away most of the Ancient Star Race’s resources.”
Ye Tian said.
“Xu Chen, if I can get out, I’ll definitely kill you!”
Star Ancestor roared.
“Star Ancestor, do you think that when you come out, you’ll still be my opponent?” Ye Tian sneered.
Then Ye Tian began sealing this space.
He exerted almost all of his comprehension of the Eternal Rule, setting up an incomparably complex restriction. Even if the outside Ancient Star Race wanted to communicate with Star Ancestor, they would not be able to do so.
After setting up the restriction, Ye Tian, under his own identity, took away all the treasure vaults accumulated by the Ancient Star Race over countless ages. He also entered one Ancient Star Race force after another and forcibly took the treasures from their treasure vaults.
In almost no time at all, the Ancient Star Race was completely looted by Ye Tian, and the entire Ancient Star Race fell into chaos.
After that, Ye Tian copied a body of a First-Tier Invincible Being and entered the Starry Ancient Domain, bringing back all the resources plundered by the Ancient Star Race clone.
Then Ye Tian directly Annihilated this clone.
The identity of Xu Chen would completely disappear from this world.
Inside the Ancient Star Tower.
Star Ancestor looked at the restriction sealing this place and deeply felt how difficult the situation was.
Apart from the fact that the sealing power was not very strong, the profundity of the seal itself was so troublesome that even he found it extremely hard to deal with.
“Xu Chen’s mastery of the Eternal Rule has actually risen to this level, absolutely comparable to half-step Eternal Realm. Could he be the clone of some half-step Eternal Realm expert?”
Star Ancestor was utterly despairing.
The Ancient Star Race could not save him. He was probably never going to get out for all eternity.

“The Ancient Star Race’s resources are pretty good, but unfortunately they’re not very useful to me. Let’s give them to human race development!”
Ye Tian then had these resources sent to the Human Race Sacred Hall, where they would serve as resources for the future development of the human race.
And his main task now was to help his son Ye Xingchen break through to the Supreme Being realm. Once Ye Xingchen made the breakthrough, then the Dark Forbidden Domain could evolve into Eternal Heaven and Earth, and it would become the true base of the human race.
By then, he could also return to the human race’s base and truly guard the human race.
Moreover, with his own Eternal Heaven and Earth, the human race would truly be able to rise.
In addition, Ye Tian had another goal, and that was to create an Eternal Skill.
A truly top-notch half-step Eternal Skill not only mastered multiple half-step Eternal Skills, but also at least one Eternal Skill. Previously, Ye Tian had been unable to master an Eternal Skill, not only because his cultivation was too low, but more importantly because his understanding of the Eternal Rule was not profound enough, and he did not understand Eternal Skills sufficiently.
But now, he was constantly comprehending the innate Eternal Rule within the innate Eternal lifeform, and his mastery of the Eternal Rule had reached an unimaginable level. Therefore, he had hope of creating an Eternal Skill........
This time, Ye Tian planned to begin with the Destroying Finger, and create an Eternal Finger Skill.
In order to create this finger skill, Ye Tian did not care about time, and endless ages passed.
One hundred great tribulation eras!
One thousand great tribulation eras!
One tribulation era!
Ten tribulation eras!
……
It had to be said that creating an Eternal Skill was indeed incomparably difficult. Many half-step Eternal Realm experts did not know how many tens of thousands of tribulation eras they had spent before they luckily created an Eternal Skill. Naturally, Ye Tian could not create one easily either.
Finally, in the fifty-sixth tribulation era, Ye Tian created a finger skill at the level of an Eternal Skill.
He named this finger skill the Ruinous Finger, meaning to ruin everything.
After the Eternal Skill, Ruinous Finger, was created, Ye Tian underwent a transformation. Not long after that, his cultivation rose to the peak of the Fifth Supreme Being, only one step away from the Sixth Supreme Being.
After some more years passed, news came from Ye Xingchen’s side.
He was about to begin his charge toward the Supreme Being realm.
Under Ye Tian’s vigorous cultivation and pressure, Ye Xingchen had also lived up to expectations and not disappointed him. He finally broke through to the peak of First-Tier Invincible and created an Invincible Skill that suited the Ancient Star Physique.
Now, Ye Xingchen was preparing to condense the Eternal Tribulation Body. Once he succeeded, he would be able to absorb the origin of the Ancient Star Stone and charge toward the Supreme Being realm.
After receiving the massive amount of Eternal resources Ye Tian had bestowed upon him, Ye Xingchen successfully condensed the Eternal Tribulation Body.
Then he began absorbing the origin of the Ancient Star Stone to break through to Supreme Being.
“I must succeed!”
Ye Xingchen calmed himself down and said.
He knew his own potential very well. If not for his father’s support, he would probably never be able to step into the Supreme Being realm, let alone advance to Supreme Being with such a solid foundation now.
This was his fortune!
So, he could not let his father down.
Time passed. After a long while, a Supreme Being aura spread from the space where Ye Xingchen was in seclusion.
Ye Xingchen had successfully broken through to the Supreme Being realm.
And the Dark Forbidden Domain had also given birth to three Supreme Beings, and was about to undergo a transformation.
