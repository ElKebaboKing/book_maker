Chapter 1664 Six Bodies! Powerful Copying Talent!

“The copying talent really underwent such a huge change!”
Ye Tian was quite excited and thrilled.
For him, the greatest change was undoubtedly the transformation of the copied bodies. Before, although he could condense two copied bodies, they were actually all considered a type of clone. But now, after the copying talent had been upgraded and advanced, there was no longer any distinction between a copied body and a clone.
Even if the main body were to fall, a copied body would become the main body.
In other words, it would be very hard to kill him. As long as any copied body survived, he could not be killed.
Moreover, he now did not just copy two bodies, but five.
That meant that, including his main body, he could now form six bodies in total. His enemies would have to kill all six of his bodies at the same time in order to truly kill him, and accomplishing such a feat was extremely difficult—one could even say impossible.
In addition.
After the copying talent was upgraded, it had already condensed onto the Immortal Ancestor Talent. He could now easily copy other people’s Immortal Ancestor Talent, succeeding instantly, and he could also fuse similar Immortal Ancestor Talents together. As long as he had a large number of Immortal Ancestor Talents, he could fuse them into an even more powerful talent.
And the range covered by the copying talent had also increased dramatically, expanding by roughly ten times.
It had to be said that the current copying talent was indeed far stronger than before.
At the first moment.
Ye Tian condensed a new copied body, which was also equivalent to a true main body.
One main body remained in the Dark Forbidden Domain, namely the original main body, while one copied body entered the other side of the Source Sea and went into seclusion in a blessed land.
On the side of the Abyssless Land, he also condensed copied bodies, and all at once condensed two of them.
In this way, the Abyssless Land side effectively had three copied bodies. The three copied bodies entered various parts of the Abyssless Land to search for Void Beasts.
He had not looked for Void Beasts before because they were not very useful to him.
But now he could fuse Immortal Ancestor Talents, so naturally he had to look for Void Beasts and copy the Immortal Ancestor Talents on them.
As time passed, Ye Tian’s three copied bodies found quite a few Void Beasts in the Abyssless Land. Once they copied the Immortal Ancestor Talents on them, they would immediately leave and not fight them.
Ye Tian could not kill Void Beasts anyway, so there was no need to tangle with them; it was purely a waste of time.
Unfortunately, the number of Void Beasts in the Abyssless Land was not very large, so it was almost impossible for Ye Tian to use the talents on these Void Beasts to fuse into a higher-level talent.
However, at least this was a good beginning. In the future, if he found some more Void Beasts, wouldn’t he be able to fuse a higher-grade talent?
This day.
A being of the Immortal Realm arrived in the Abyssless Land. He came here for a very simple purpose: to deal with the Tier Three Void Beast on the black island. Without an Immortal Realm lord making a move, no one could shake that Tier Three Void Beast.
Therefore, he came.
In the void not far from the black island, one after another Lord of the Undying Realm arrived here. The reason they dared to come here again was naturally because they relied on that Immortal Realm lord.
“Lord Xu Jian, this is the place!”
A Lord of the Undying Realm said respectfully.
Lord Xu Jian was one of the Immortal Realm lords, and also the Immortal Realm being who had once gone to the Source Sea and taken the Eternal Knowledge Golden Sea Tree.
In the Endless Time Domain, many Immortal Realm lords no longer appeared very often, or rather, they were simply not in the same circle as other Eternal Realm and Half-Step Eternal Realm beings, and were hard to see in ordinary times.
But Lord Xu Jian was very active, and was considered one of the more easily seen Immortal Realm lords.
Logically speaking, Immortal Realm lords would not care about the resources in the Abyssless Land, but Lord Xu Jian did. He wanted everything, because he wanted to cultivate many concubines, so even resources that were of no use to the Immortal Realm, he would collect them.
Besides, the Abyssless Land posed little danger to him, which was why he came here to harvest a wave of resources.
Whoosh!
Lord Xu Jian took one step and arrived on the black island.
Immediately, that Tier Three Void Beast made a move.
Boom!!!!
The Tier Three Void Beast charged toward Lord Xu Jian, with Void Rules protecting its entire body and making its body become illusory. Its hands turned into one black wheel after another, suppressing toward Lord Xu Jian.
This was the true move of the Tier Three Void Beast, going all out against Lord Xu Jian alone.
If it were another Lord of the Undying Realm who came here, facing a Tier Three Void Beast alone, they would probably be blasted to death very quickly.
“Slash!”
Lord Xu Jian condensed his own eternal will together with the innate Xu Jian Eternal Rule, merging it into his sword, and with one strike he pierced through the Tier Three Void Beast’s body.........
But that sword seemed to stab into nothingness, yet it still caused some injuries to the Tier Three Void Beast.
However, the Tier Three Void Beast healed very quickly.
Rumble rumble rumble!!!!
Lord Xu Jian and the Tier Three Void Beast fought continuously. Though it seemed that Lord Xu Jian held the advantage, in reality it was very difficult to kill the Tier Three Void Beast.
Suddenly.
Lord Xu Jian created an eternal domain rule world, trapping the Tier Three Void Beast and fighting it continuously.
At this time, the Undying Realm beings under Lord Xu Jian immediately poured onto the black island and began collecting the many resources there.
That was Lord Xu Jian’s real plan.
He never intended to kill this Tier Three Void Beast, because he had no confidence in doing so. What he valued was only the resources here.
Very soon, all the resources on this black island were collected clean, and even some mineral veins were dug up from the roots.
“Leave!”
Lord Xu Jian urged his subordinates of the Eternal Realm to depart.
Swish, swish, swish!!!
One by one, the Lord of the Eternal Realm left in succession, so as not to be affected by the pursuit of the Tier Three Void Beast.
The Tier Three Void Beast had a very strong sense of belonging to its territory. Seeing its territory destroyed so completely, it went completely mad and lost even its last bit of rationality.
Even so, it still was no match for Lord Xu Jian. It was completely suppressed by Lord Xu Jian. If not for its virtualization talent being too terrifying, it would already have been heavily injured.
But after fighting for a while, Lord Xu Jian also did not want to continue fighting.
Thus, Lord Xu Jian turned into a streak of sword light, tore through time and space, and fled. In an instant, he broke free of the Tier Three Void Beast’s sensing range.
Boom!!!!
The Tier Three Void Beast left this island and began its revenge. Whenever it encountered an Eternal Realm or Half-Step Eternal Realm being, it would begin killing them.
After Ye Tian got the news, he prepared to leave.
Not only Ye Tian, but other Half-Step Eternal Realm or Eternal Realm beings were also preparing to leave.
But when Ye Tian left the Abyssless Land and had only just come out not long ago, the void area in the distance began to ripple.
Next, he saw an astonishing scene.
