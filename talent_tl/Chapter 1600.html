Chapter 1599: Eternal Tribulation Domain!

"Does Your Excellency want Original Source Liquid?"
Ye Tian sent a message to the other party.
That Triple Supreme Being replied, "Yes, does Your Excellency have Original Source Liquid on you?"
"A trading space to discuss in detail?"
Ye Tian suggested.
"Sure!"
*Swoosh! Swoosh!!*
The two entered a trading space, and only the two of them were allowed to enter. In such a trading space, other Supreme Beings could not spy, participate, or interfere.
"I need Eternal Pine Leaves!"
Ye Tian spoke directly.
That Triple Supreme Being nodded and said, "I have Eternal Pine Leaves on hand. One drop of Original Source Liquid for one Eternal Pine Leaf."
"The value is not equal!" Ye Tian shook his head and said, "Original Source Liquid allows a First-tier Supreme Being to 100% break through to a Second-tier Supreme Being, whereas Eternal Pine Leaves cannot guarantee that."
The other party said in a deep voice, "Original Source Liquid has flaws; it reduces potential, while Eternal Pine Leaves do not."
Ye Tian smiled and said, "That slight flaw of Original Source Liquid is not a flaw at all for a Supreme Being who wants to use it to break through. If Your Excellency wanted to advance to the Half-step Eternal Realm, you wouldn't be trading for Original Source Liquid."
"Then what price are you suggesting?" The other party yielded.
Ye Tian thought for a moment and said, "One drop of Original Source Liquid for two Eternal Pine Leaves plus 10,000 low-grade Eternal Coins."
"This price is too high!"
The other party frowned.
Ye Tian maintained an indifferent attitude and said, "Trade if you want to; if not, then forget it!"
Although Eternal Pine Leaves were rare, they could still be found nowadays. However, Original Source Liquid would no longer be born. Only some ancient Supreme Beings still had a little in their hands, and even that wasn't certain.
If Ye Tian hadn't extorted some Original Source Liquid from Star Ancestor, he wouldn't have been able to obtain it now.
Therefore, he wasn't afraid the other party wouldn't trade.
If the other party really was unwilling to trade, he could easily trade the Original Source Liquid for other treasures similar to Eternal Pine Leaves.
"Fine, I agree!"
The other party said.
Under the witness of the Source God Pavilion's artifact spirit, Ye Tian and the other party completed the transaction.
After the transaction was completed, Ye Tian emerged from the trading space and walked around again. He traded for some treasures beneficial for breaking through without side effects, but their grade was inferior to the Eternal Pine Leaves.
Suddenly.
A Supreme Being said, "I know the entrance to a low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain. Who wants to enter? However, the entrance to that low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain is somewhat unstable, so it can only allow fewer than 100 First, Second, or Triple Supreme Beings to enter at once; otherwise, it could easily cause the entrance to collapse. To prevent the entrance from collapsing, I will only take 90 Supreme Beings below the Fourth-tier into it this time. I am now starting to sell slots."
"What? You actually discovered the entrance to a low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain."
Many Supreme Beings were shocked.
Ye Tian heard it too, and his interest was immediately piqued.
The Eternal Tribulation Domain was no ordinary place. It was formed when Tribulation Beasts of over 100,000 Tribulation Eras broke through a certain limit and became Eternal Tribulation Races. These Eternal Tribulation Races were similar to Supreme Beings. Some Eternal Tribulation Races would unite to manifest an Eternal Tribulation Domain in the depths of the void.
Low-level Eternal Tribulation Domains only allowed First, Second, and Triple Supreme Beings to enter. Mid-level Eternal Tribulation Domains only allowed Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth-tier Supreme Beings to enter. High-level Eternal Tribulation Domains allowed Seventh, Eighth, and Ninth-tier Supreme Beings to enter.
Highest Eternal Tribulation Domains allowed those in the Half-step Eternal Realm to enter.
It was precisely because of the existence of Eternal Tribulation Domains that Tribulation Beasts comparable to Supreme Beings were almost never seen in the Source Sea.
This was because Tribulation Beasts comparable to Supreme Beings were almost all in Eternal Tribulation Domains.
Tribulation Beasts comparable to Supreme Beings were called Eternal Tribulation Races. They were different from regular Tribulation Beasts. Regular Tribulation Beasts did not have very high intelligence, but the Eternal Tribulation Races had developed into a race. Their strength was not only powerful, but they also had high comprehension, were able to create Supreme Techniques, and could cultivate without needing to rely on Great Tribulations to grow.
The strength of the Eternal Tribulation Races was very powerful, and they were quite united. If it weren't for the fact that the Eternal Tribulation Races grew too slowly and were constantly hunted by Supreme Beings, they would have definitely developed into the top racial power in the Source Sea long ago.
Any single member of the Eternal Tribulation Race was comparable in value to an Eternal Source Object because their bodies contained not only Tribulation Blood, Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, and Tribulation Bones, but even a very precious substance—Eternal Tribulation Crystal.
Eternal Tribulation Crystal was one of the rare treasures capable of tempering an Eternal Body.
Supreme Beings who had condensed an Eternal Body, or those with ordinary Eternal Tribulation Bodies, all needed to continue tempering them.
For example, Ye Tian's Endless Eternal Body was currently at the Entry stage.
Entry, Minor, Major, Perfected. These were the four great realms of the Eternal Body!
To temper an Eternal Body, ordinary resources were of no use at all, but Eternal Tribulation Crystals had such an effect...
However, Eternal Tribulation Crystals were not only effective for Eternal Bodies but also had a huge effect on ordinary Eternal Tribulation Bodies. Therefore, this resource was almost always in short supply and could not be purchased at all.
Therefore, to obtain Eternal Tribulation Crystals, one could only choose to hunt the Eternal Tribulation Race.
But the Eternal Tribulation Race was almost always hidden within Eternal Tribulation Domains and could not be found at all.
Because of this, hearing about the existence of a low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain was a major opportunity for some First, Second, and Triple Supreme Beings, especially for Triple Supreme Beings.
Of course, Eternal Tribulation Domains were also very dangerous; one could fall if they weren't careful.
For instance, First-tier Supreme Beings hardly dared to enter an Eternal Tribulation Domain. After all, it was the territory of the Eternal Tribulation Race, and if they were surrounded, they would have no way to request support.
However, a few Triple Supreme Beings were prepared to take the risk and sought out that Supreme Being to buy a slot.
That Supreme Being was also a Triple Supreme Being and would therefore also enter the Eternal Tribulation Domain.
The price for a slot wasn't expensive, requiring only 10,000 Eternal Coins.
But ninety slots meant 900,000 Eternal Coins!
But such a business could only be done once. After all, other Supreme Beings already knew the entrance and location and would definitely spread the word. How could he sell slots then?
Many Supreme Beings were actually waiting for the next opportunity, not wanting to waste Eternal Coins.
Ye Tian didn't want to waste time. What if the number of Eternal Tribulation Race members inside wasn't high? If he waited for the next opportunity, wouldn't he fail to get any benefits?
Even if it was truly dangerous inside, he was not afraid. After all, he was entering with a duplicated body and had no fear of danger.
Thus, Ye Tian paid 10,000 Eternal Coins and purchased a slot.
The time to enter the Eternal Tribulation Domain was after this gathering, and this gathering would last for millions of years.
Ye Tian immediately left the Source God Pavilion. He prepared to immediately charge toward the Second-tier Supreme Being realm. If he succeeded in breaking through, his strength would soar, and his harvest would be even greater upon entering the low-level Eternal Tribulation Domain.
To ensure he could break through in time before heading to the Eternal Tribulation Domain, he activated a time acceleration array.
Immediately after, he took out the two Eternal Pine Leaves and other treasures, preparing to charge toward the Second-tier Supreme Being realm right there in the Void Source Hall.
