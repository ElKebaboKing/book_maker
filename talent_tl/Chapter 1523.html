Chapter 1522: After the Great Tribulation!

If one were to consider physical body tribulation crossing as a realm, it would stand above the third-tier paramount powerhouses.
Thus, Ye Tian was now a Physical Body Second Tribulation Realm paramount powerhouse. Every time the physical body crossed a tribulation, its strength would skyrocket. It was precisely because of this that a Paramount Ancestor would become so incredibly powerful after becoming an Invincible Ancestor.
Above the Physical Body Fifth Tribulation Realm or the Soul Fifth Tribulation Realm, one could travel to foreign domains to confront foreign laws head-on. Relying on various life-saving items, one would attempt to make the physical body undergo a qualitative change time and again, at least ensuring they would not perish under the suppression of foreign laws. Only then would they be considered qualified.
Afterward, one would use a hundred Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals to charge toward the realm of an Invincible One, such as an Invincible Ancestor.
Once successful, one would be a true Invincible One.
These three steps became increasingly difficult with each stage. Many paramount powerhouses did not know how many Great Tribulation Eras they had to endure or how many life-threatening dangers they had to face. Even with the support of major forces, there was only a sliver of hope to promote to the Invincible One realm.
If others knew that Ye Tian had raised his physical body to the Second Tribulation Realm in such a short time, they would find it absolutely unbelievable!
Because for any paramount powerhouse, the absorption of tribulation force was extremely slow, making it almost impossible to improve. However, Ye Tian had obtained the Eternal Talent: Tribulation, which allowed him to quickly absorb and refine tribulation force, thus raising his physical body to the Second Tribulation Realm in a short period.
If he visited a few more foreign domains undergoing a Great Tribulation, he could definitely raise his physical body to the Ninth Tribulation Realm.
Soon, the Great Tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Domain passed.
From beginning to end, the Invincible Ones of the six super-tier forces did not descend upon the Dark Forbidden Domain, allowing Ye Tian to breathe a sigh of relief.
From the information gathered from the six forces, it seemed Invincible Ones could enter foreign domains during non-tribulation periods. Their strength was terrifying, and the Invincible Ones of the six super-tier forces were definitely not newcomers to the realm; thus, they were certainly much stronger than Tribulation Beasts.
Therefore, he was definitely no match for those Invincible Ones.
But now, since the Invincible Ones had not come, it was likely they had no interest in a few thousand Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals, or perhaps they were occupied with other matters or were in secluded cultivation, which was why they had not descended upon the Dark Forbidden Domain.
Now that the Great Tribulation of the Dark Forbidden Domain was over, even if an Invincible One came, he would no longer be afraid.
After all, during a non-tribulation period, even if an Invincible One came, their strength would inevitably be suppressed severely, perhaps even becoming weaker than him. Coming here would be seeking death.
In this Great Tribulation, the Human Race's strength suffered almost no losses, but other forces were nearly annihilated, with only a few Ancestors remaining.
Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!
One by one, the Ancestors of the Human Race emerged from human territory, crossing time and space to reach various regions of the Dark Forbidden Domain, only to see a landscape of desolation and silence.
In the short term, those treasure lands and domains of the Dark Forbidden Domain likely would not be born. It would take a certain amount of time for these domains, worlds, treasure lands, and Dark Holy Lands to emerge.
Therefore, the practitioners of the Human Race would likely have to live within human territory for the coming years. Although it would be crowded, it was sufficient for the time being.
Moreover, the Ancestors of the Human Race could create worlds in the surrounding territories and even accelerate the evolution of domains, so the Human Race did not lack territory.
On this day.
Ye Tian summarized and organized the entire evolutionary path of the Human Race from Ancestor to Paramount Ancestor and Invincible Ancestor. He used special means to seal this knowledge within the Human Race Holy Temple. Any Supreme Domain Ancestor would be eligible to inherit this secret knowledge, ensuring they would not take the wrong path and fail to promote to Paramount or Invincible Ancestor in the future.
In the following time, the Human Race produced several Supreme Domain Ancestors at regular intervals. Successive Supreme Domain Ancestors with deep foundations even promoted to Ancestors, all by creating their own Ancestor Methods.
Because everyone's comprehension had skyrocketed after surviving the Great Tribulation; even Ye Tian's comprehension had improved significantly. This was the benefit of surviving a Great Tribulation.
It was foreseeable that before long, the Human Race would produce a large number of Ancestors, and Supreme Domain Ancestors would truly be as numerous as the hairs on an ox.
Additionally, since Ye Tian had obtained many Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals this time and did not need them all himself, he could place them in the Human Race Holy Temple for the top Ancestors to slowly contemplate.
It was unlikely that a Paramount Ancestor would be born in a short time, but over long ages—even if one was not born in a single Great Tribulation Era (nine Dark Calendars)—after several Great Tribulation Eras, Paramount Ancestors would definitely emerge, and their numbers would only increase.
...
With the Great Tribulation gone from the Dark Forbidden Domain, Ye Tian could no longer absorb tribulation force. Not knowing which foreign domain would undergo a Great Tribulation next, he began to focus on creating Ultimate Ancestral Techniques and then fusing them into Paramount Ancestral Techniques.
He already had several Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals from other foreign domains, obtained by killing paramount powerhouses from those worlds.
Furthermore, he could continue to trade for Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals. With his current strength, he could easily trade for foreign Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
Thus, he did not lack foreign Origin Rule Tribulation Crystals.
As time passed, Ye Tian created more and more Paramount Ancestral Techniques. Although their effects on him were weakening, creating more still enhanced his strength.
Occasionally, he would help his wife and son create Ultimate Ancestral Techniques, guiding them to fuse two Ultimate Ancestral Techniques to comprehend and create a Paramount Ancestral Technique, saving them countless years of time.
Domain year after domain year passed. The Dark Forbidden Domain slowly became prosperous. Densely packed domains were born, ensuring the entire Dark Forbidden Domain was no longer empty.
Moreover, many taboo creatures were born, but they only served as resources for the Human Race, being hunted or raised in captivity by human experts.
At the same time, countless humans were distributed among super-large and large domains, and even small domains were filled with human presence.
Additionally, Ye Tian spent time building a Space-Time Gate at the level of a Paramount Ancestral Artifact. It could project countless Space-Time Gate phantoms, allowing humans to travel easily throughout the Dark Forbidden Domain. Otherwise, even a Supreme Domain Ancestor would find it difficult to cross such vast territories.
Unknowingly, the Human Race had produced many Ancestors and countless Supreme Domain Ancestors.
Ye Tian now possessed nearly forty Paramount Ancestral Techniques, to the point where they could no longer amplify his physical body. There was no longer any need to continue creating them.
At this point, aside from crossing physical body tribulations, he had no other way to increase his strength.
Thus, he began to look for ways to find out which foreign domain was about to undergo a Great Tribulation.
