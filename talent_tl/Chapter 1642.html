Chapter 1641 Breakthrough, Half-Step Eternal Realm!

Very quickly.
Ye Tian’s true body and clone body both stepped into the second stage of the will world, each condensing 1000 will worlds, and both completed their transformation.
After the transformation of his will, Ye Tian discovered that the Rift Wind Valley could no longer create any impact on his will at all.
In other words, Rift Wind Valley could no longer help him.
He could continue cultivating his will world, but he could no longer rely on Rift Wind Valley.
“Forget it. I’ve already gained such great benefits this time; I should be satisfied!”
Ye Tian muttered.
During this long period, Ye Tian had claimed that his avatar was always in the Traceless Territory, so the other twelve ancestors did not look for him, and he had not even attended any meetings.
But from time to time, some important notices were still sent to him by message.
The Twelve Ancestors also made one move, but Ye Tian did not go to participate.
For experts like True River Sword Ancestor at the Extreme Stage level, they had already been stuck at the Half-Step Eternal Realm for countless years, and in their dreams they all wanted to break through and become Eternal Realm beings.
Therefore, they would participate in any opportunity and never give up. What if they really happened to break through by chance?
On this day.
Ye Tian dissolved his clone body and avatar by himself, leaving the Traceless Territory in this way.
And the several treasures he had obtained from the Traceless Tower back then, after True River Sword Ancestor and the others left, also had their avatars walk out of the entrance, allowing the true body to take away treasures such as the Void God Leaf.
Thus, these treasures were not lost either.
Void Source Hall.
The clone body and avatar were once again condensed. The avatar cultivated the will world, while the clone body comprehended the Innate Eternal Rule, and as for the true body, it mainly focused on improving its cultivation.
What Ye Tian most wanted to do right now was raise his cultivation to the Extreme of the Ninth-Stage Supreme Being. Only in that way would his strength advance by leaps and bounds, even becoming much stronger than True River Sword Ancestor, perhaps giving him hope of matching a Second Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm being.
However, cultivating from Ninth-Stage Supreme Being to the Extreme of Ninth-Stage Supreme Being also required enormous resources and time; the deeper the foundation, the longer it naturally took.
Ten calamity eras!
One hundred calamity eras!
One thousand calamity eras!
Unknowingly, tens of thousands of calamity eras had passed.
After such a long period, and after expending many resources, Ye Tian finally raised his cultivation to the Extreme of Ninth-Stage Supreme Being.
At this moment, he was only one step away from the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
To break through from Ninth-Stage Supreme Being to the Half-Step Eternal Realm, the Eternal Rule needed to fit with the body, and there was no need for perfect fusion; if one could truly fuse, that would be the Eternal Realm.
But even merely fitting with the body was extremely difficult, and if one’s bodily potential was insufficient or one’s foundation inadequate, there would be no way to break through the shackle.
Only with sufficient potential and foundation could one borrow the moment of fitting with the Eternal Rule to break the shackle and advance into the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
It was not too difficult for Ye Tian to advance into the Half-Step Eternal Realm, but he currently had one trouble: he had comprehended the Innate Eternal Rule.
In other words, while others only needed to fit with the Eternal Rule, he needed to fit with the Innate Eternal Rule in order to borrow that power of compatibility to break the shackle and advance into the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
Thus, the difficulty had increased by more than tenfold.
But once he broke through, the increase in strength would surely be the greatest as well.
“If the true body directly attempts the breakthrough, the success rate won’t be very high. Let the clone body try breaking through repeatedly first! Even if it fails, it can still accumulate some experience. There’s no need to rush the matter of breaking through to the Half-Step Eternal Realm!”
Ye Tian thought to himself.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian occasionally let the clone body attempt a breakthrough, but at the beginning, it failed every time.
After all, the shackle from Ninth-Stage Supreme Being to the Half-Step Eternal Realm was very difficult to overcome, and many Half-Step Eternal Realm cultivators had relied on external objects when they first broke through, more or less damaging their foundations.
Ye Tian did not want to rely on external objects. Otherwise, he still had the Void True Liquid in his possession; if he took the Void True Liquid, he would be able to break through to the Half-Step Eternal Realm with one hundred percent certainty.
But if that were the case, it would have a huge impact on his future attempt to impact the Eternal Realm.
Therefore, he did not want to use Void True Liquid to break through.
After many attempts, Ye Tian gradually mastered quite a bit of breakthrough experience, and finally on this day successfully broke through to the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
Of course, this was only the clone body breaking through, not the true body.
Afterwards.
Ye Tian dissolved the clone body and began breaking through again.
Gradually, Ye Tian could almost guarantee that he would break through with one hundred percent certainty.
-------------------
“Break through!”
Ye Tian’s true body began to choose to assault the Half-Step Eternal Realm.
Only to see that his incomparably powerful body and the Innate Eternal Rule reached a certain degree of compatibility in a short time, as though the Innate Eternal Rule was fusing with the body on another level, but it was not a true fusion.
And the shackles in the dark, under this state of compatibility, began to become very easy to assault.
Thus, Ye Tian went all out to assault the shackles.
Crack!!!!
The shackles were shattered, the bottleneck was broken, and Ye Tian officially stepped into the Half-Step Eternal Realm level.
At this moment, his body began to transform, and even the power within him began to convert into Half-Step Eternal power.
Not only that, Ye Tian’s will also rose by a great margin.
After a long time, Ye Tian’s breakthrough was finally completely finished.
“Far, far stronger power than before!”
Ye Tian said excitedly.
At this moment, he could already be considered Extreme Stage.
Because he had reached the Half-Step Eternal Realm, created many Eternal Techniques, and also comprehended the Innate Eternal Rule, there was no real difference from Extreme Stage.
The only somewhat weak point was that his Half-Step Eternal power was still relatively weak.
However, the Half-Step Eternal Realm did not emphasize cultivation level, so he could be considered an Extreme Stage-level Half-Step Eternal Realm.
Ye Tian closed his eyes and sensed the state of his body, immediately realizing what level his current strength was probably at.
Three Extreme Stage!
Before the breakthrough, he had been between One Extreme Stage and Two Extreme Stage, or rather, the weakest Two Extreme Stage. But now, he could already be considered a top-tier Three Extreme Stage, only one step away from Four Extreme Stage.
As long as he stabilized his realm and increased his Half-Step Eternal power, his strength would formally step into the level of a Four Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm.
And the cultivation of Extreme Stage was to strengthen the Innate Eternal Rule, using the compatibility between the Innate Eternal Rule and the body to stimulate the body to strengthen step by step, thereby reaching a higher Extreme Stage.
With Ye Tian’s current potential and standard, before becoming Seven Extreme Stage, he would practically not encounter any bottleneck at all.
As for other Half-Step Eternal Realm cultivators, such as True River Sword Ancestor, they found it very difficult to continue improving already at the One Extreme Stage level, because what he could improve had basically already been improved, and the remaining parts were very hard to improve.
After breaking through to the Half-Step Eternal Realm, Ye Tian quickly discovered a problem: his cultivation consumed too much. Although he had once obtained massive resources in the Primordial Sea, even that could not withstand such consumption.
In addition, condensing clone bodies also required resources, and the resources consumed by countless condensations and dissolutions were an astronomical number.
According to his estimate, by the time he became a Five Extreme Stage Half-Step Eternal Realm, he would probably have no resources left.
