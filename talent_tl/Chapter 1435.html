Chapter 1434: Hunyuan Ancestral Water, Invincible Domain Ancestor Perfection
!
Boom!!!! Fire Pattern War Ancestor appeared in the War World Tower and came before Ye Tian.
"Ye Tian, were you looking for me?"
Fire Pattern War Ancestor asked.
He saw that Ye Tian's cultivation was no longer that of a Supreme Domain Ancestor, but an Invincible Domain Ancestor.
He then continued, "It shouldn't be about your breakthrough to the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, right?"
"Lord Fire Pattern War Ancestor, it is naturally not about my breakthrough. Rather, I have a treasure I wish to trade to the War Gate. However, this treasure is quite valuable, so I needed Lord Fire Pattern War Ancestor to step forward!"
Saying this, Ye Tian took out the Space Saint Talent Crystal.
As soon as Fire Pattern War Ancestor saw this Space Saint Talent Crystal, he was instantly shocked. "A Saint Talent Stone, and a Space Saint Talent Stone at that? You actually have such a treasure?"
"Is its name the Saint Talent Stone?"
Ye Tian felt that his naming was quite accurate, having only added the word 'crystal.'
"This Space Saint Talent Stone of yours has had some of its Saint Talents used up; there's only one quota left, right?"
Fire Pattern War Ancestor suddenly asked.
"Yes!"
Ye Tian admitted, "I bestowed Space Saint Talents upon some geniuses of our Human Race, leaving only one slot. I plan to trade it to the War Gate. The War Gate should be interested, right?"
"Hahaha, the War Gate is certainly interested. Even I am interested. Not a few of my descendants lack Saint Talents. Why don't you trade it directly to me? How about it?"
Fire Pattern War Ancestor asked.
"That is fine, but I hope Fire Pattern War Ancestor can spread the news of this trade!"
Ye Tian suggested.
Fire Pattern War Ancestor was startled for a moment, then smiled.
He understood Ye Tian's purpose and nodded. "Fine, I will let it be known. You don't need to worry about the matter of so many Saint Talent users appearing in your Human Race!"
"Then, many thanks to Lord Fire Pattern War Ancestor!"
Ye Tian said gratefully.
Subsequently, Ye Tian and Fire Pattern War Ancestor completed the trade.
The Space Saint Talent Stone now belonged to Fire Pattern War Ancestor, while Ye Tian obtained a very precious treasure from Fire Pattern War Ancestor—Hunyuan Ancestral Water.
Hunyuan Ancestral Water was a very precious cultivation resource that was of great help to Invincible Domain Ancestors, allowing them to quickly temper their Invincible Domain Bodies.
As long as one refined the Hunyuan Ancestral Water, they could advance from the initial stage of the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm to the perfection stage, without needing endless ages to slowly temper the Invincible Domain Body and increase their cultivation.
Therefore, for a newly promoted Invincible Domain Ancestor, once they possessed Hunyuan Ancestral Water, they could rapidly increase their strength and raise their cultivation to the perfection stage of the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm.
Other resources were not of much help to Ye Tian. Perhaps others would be more eager to obtain treasures that could add a hint of breaking the physical limit, such as turning a Saint Body into an Ancestral Body or a Saint Skill into an Ancestral Skill.
But Ye Tian did not need those treasures; he needed a treasure like Hunyuan Ancestral Water more.
Once he used the Hunyuan Ancestral Water to raise his cultivation, his strength would increase to a terrifying degree.
For example, having just broken through to the Invincible realm, his combat power had directly jumped to the peak of a Step Three Invincible Domain Ancestor, only one step away from Step Two combat power.
If he broke through to the Perfection realm, his combat power would likely be comparable to the Five Thunders Domain Ancestor, which was combat power that surpassed a Step One Invincible Domain Ancestor!
After the trade, Ye Tian used his Life Ancestral Talent to look at Fire Pattern War Ancestor's Ancestral Skill.
[Ancestral Skill: Fire Pattern War Body]
Regarding this Ancestral Skill, Ye Tian had seen it before.
Back then, Fire Pattern War Ancestor had manifested this Ancestral Skill, turning into a terrifying existence comparable to the size of a domain, defeating a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor of the same rank in a single blow. Its power was incredibly fearsome.
Of course, this Ancestral Skill was actually quite ordinary; it was only because Fire Pattern War Ancestor's cultivation was high that it could erupt with such terrifying power.
"Copy it. It might be useful in the future!"
Ye Tian casually copied this Ancestral Skill.
Afterward, Ye Tian left the War World Tower, returned to the Saint Tablet World, and began his cultivation.
He took out a small bottle of Hunyuan Ancestral Water, extracted a single drop, swallowed it, and began to refine it.
Boom!!!!! Terrifying energy tempered Ye Tian's Invincible Domain Body, and his body was rapidly tempered and improved.
"What a terrifying improvement effect. Even Ancestral Medicines are useless to me now, but Hunyuan Ancestral Water has such a terrifying effect. The value of just this one drop is more precious than an ordinary Saint Artifact!"
Ye Tian murmured.
He continued to cultivate, refining drop after drop of Hunyuan Ancestral Water, and his cultivation progressed rapidly!
Initial Invincible Domain Ancestor! Middle Invincible Domain Ancestor! Late Invincible Domain Ancestor! Great Perfection Invincible Domain Ancestor!
When Ye Tian had completely raised his cultivation to the perfection stage of the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm, about 10% of the Hunyuan Ancestral Water remained. He could save it for his wife and children to use in the future.
Boom!!!! An endless pressure spread out, but it was blocked by the Saint Tablet World. Otherwise, this pressure alone could have blanketed the entire Dao Mountain Domain.
"I am very strong!"
Ye Tian discovered that he was far, far stronger than when he was at the initial stage of the Invincible Domain Ancestor realm... He could easily crush his former self with a casual hand.
Moreover, his Saint Soul had also strengthened dozens of times. After all, as his cultivation rose, his Saint Soul naturally improved along with it.
However, this was not yet the true limit!
Many Invincible Domain Ancestors who cultivated to the perfection stage only had the combat power of a Step Three or Four Invincible Domain Ancestor, but they could slowly improve to Step Two or even Step One combat power in the future.
That was because the physical body of an Invincible Domain Ancestor was too terrifying; many Invincible Domain Ancestors were unable to fully exert their own power.
It might seem like they were exerting 100% of their power, but in many cases, they could continue to gain more control.
After an Invincible Domain Ancestor reached the perfection stage, it was a process of achieving complete control.
And this kind of control was very difficult to cultivate. Controlling the physical body to exert its inherent power was just ordinary control. If one could exert ten times their own power, that was impressive control. If one could control a hundred times their own power, it could be called peerless genius.
If one could control 365 times their own power, they would reach their own limit.
At that point, one could attempt to break the physical limit and undergo a transformation of the Invincible Domain Body.
Back then, Gu Heng Domain Ancestor had completed three transformations of the Invincible Domain Body. Once nine transformations were completed, one could promote to a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor.
And wanting to control 365 times one's physical strength was very difficult. For example, Five Thunders Domain Ancestor had not achieved it, though that also had to do with him reaching the Half-step Ancestral Body.
In short, the stronger the physical body, the harder the control. If one was monstrous enough to have cultivated to the level of an Ancestral Body, it would be even harder to control.
This was also why Five Thunders Domain Ancestor was much stronger than Gu Heng Domain Ancestor but had not yet broken the limit.
But for an Invincible Domain Ancestor like Five Thunders, once they broke the physical limit in the future, their strength would be terrifying.
"I expect I will try to control my physical strength after I have refined the Ancestral Body!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
After all, he wanted to become the strongest Invincible Domain Ancestor, Supreme-level Domain Ancestor, and Progenitor-level being. Therefore, he naturally had to reach an incredibly terrifying level in every realm.
