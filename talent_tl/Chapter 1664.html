Chapter 1663 Heavenly Extreme Origin Pearl, Transformation of the Copy Talent

After the avatar left the Land of the Abyss, it shuttled through the Void Region at extremely high speed.
He still had not relaxed until the avatar returned to his true body’s side; only then did he truly feel at ease.
As time passed, the situation in the Land of the Abyss changed greatly.
Lord Chen Hai and the others could not do anything to that third-tier Void Beast, but Lord Chen Hai still snatched quite a few good things.
The information Ye Tian had sent out was not wrong. Indeed, many top-tier resources had been born on that black island, enough to tempt even Lords of the Undying Realm, and they were even useful to Lord Chen Hai.
However, there were not many Eternal Realm cultivators who truly managed to snatch good things, and even several Lords of the Undying Realm were nearly killed.
Now, they no longer dared step onto that black island.
After a long while.
Ye Tian’s avatar finally returned to the Time-Space Territory, then passed through one Time-Space Territory after another, and arrived at the Eight Extremes Tower.
The Eight Extremes Tower had great influence in the Time-Space Territory, and naturally had channels to enter the Source Sea.
For example, the Eight Extremes Tower cooperated with many Lords of the Eternal Life Realm, and could ask them to take members of the Eight Extremes Tower to the Source Sea, as long as some Time-Space Coins were paid.
Thus, through the Eight Extremes Tower’s connections, Ye Tian contacted a Lord of the Eternal Life Realm—Lord Wolf Fang.
This lord had a fairly good relationship with the Eight Extremes Tower mainly because an Eternal Realm member who had risen from the Eight Extremes Tower had taken good care of Lord Wolf Fang, which was why Lord Wolf Fang had grown close to the Eight Extremes Tower.
Ye Tian immediately contacted this Lord Wolf Fang, and then arrived at Lord Wolf Fang’s Wolf Fang Territory.
Swish!
Ye Tian walked out from the time-space passage and entered the Wolf Fang Territory, soon meeting Lord Wolf Fang.
Lord Wolf Fang did not look like a human, but rather like a giant humanoid wolf.
“Greetings, Lord Wolf Fang!”
Ye Tian greeted him very politely.
“The Endless Ancestor of the Eight Extremes Tower, you want to go to the Source Sea?” Lord Wolf Fang asked with some puzzlement.
Because in his view, Ye Tian should not have yet touched the second boundary barrier, so he did not need to break through to the level of Nine Extremities half-step Eternal Realm, right? Why go to the Source Sea at this time?
Ye Tian saw Lord Wolf Fang’s confusion, and explained, “Lord Wolf Fang, I am originally a being from the Source Sea, so I want to go back and take a look.”
“A being from the Source Sea!”
Lord Wolf Fang’s attitude immediately changed, and he said with a smile, “I didn’t expect that the Endless Ancestor would actually be a being from the Source Sea. Many Eight Extremities Realm cultivators are extremely envious of Source Sea beings. As Source Sea beings, their lifespan is endless, while the Eight Extremities Realm cultivators in the Time-Space Territory can only live like half-step Eternal Realm cultivators—they can all only live for 10 million Eternal Years. Yet the Eight Extremities Realm cultivators from the Source Sea have enough time to break through to the Nine Extremities Realm or the Eternal Realm.”
Ye Tian shook his head and said with a bitter smile, “Although Source Sea beings have unlimited lifespans, it is extremely difficult to grow to the Extreme realm. In this era of the Source Sea, there are fewer than a hundred Extreme realm beings, while any single territory in the Time-Space Territory has more than that number.”
“That is indeed the case. The Source Sea’s rules are too complete, so cultivation is difficult. The Time-Space Territory’s rules are incomplete, which is equivalent to having loopholes, and thus treasures and resources that break free from the shackles of the rules are born, along with various blessed lands, and even powerful constitutions and races. These are all situations that cannot appear in the Source Sea.” Lord Wolf Fang said.
The two chatted for a while before getting down to business.
Lord Wolf Fang handed Ye Tian a token and said, “This is a special communication token. It can penetrate the Source Sea barrier and send messages into the Time-Space Territory. Although the speed of receiving information is slow, once I receive your message, I can enter the Source Sea and bring you out, without needing to go through the Time-Space Island again.”
“Thank you, Lord Wolf Fang. These are one million Time-Space Coins!”
Ye Tian casually handed one million Time-Space Coins to Lord Wolf Fang.
This was the fee for entering the Source Sea, but if he were to come out, it would only require 100,000 Time-Space Coins.
“The Endless Ancestor is too courteous. Take these one million Time-Space Coins back; let’s just consider it making a friend!” Lord Wolf Fang did not accept the Time-Space Coins.
Ye Tian roughly guessed Lord Wolf Fang’s thoughts. He probably valued him highly and wanted to make friends with him.
If it were an Eight Extremities Realm cultivator from the Time-Space Territory, there was a high chance that, like Gu He Domain Ancestor, they would reach the end of their lifespan without breaking through to the Nine Extremities Realm or the Eternal Realm, and could only take a risk by entering some forbidden grounds or death grounds to gamble on a chance.
And many Eight Extremities Realm cultivators perished completely in some forbidden ground during that gamble.
Ye Tian was different. He had a long lifespan and could slowly endure until the end, eventually shedding his cocoon and becoming a butterfly, breaking through into the Eternal Realm.
Once an Eight Extremities half-step Eternal Realm cultivator breaks through to the Eternal Realm, at the very least they are a Lord of the Undying Realm, and not a weak one at that; among the Undying Realm, they would be considered powerful.

Therefore, a mere 1 million Time-Space Coins compared with making friends with a future Lord of the Undying Realm was nothing at all.
Ye Tian did not insist that Lord Wolf Fang accept the Time-Space Coins, and instead took them back.
Then, Lord Wolf Fang began to take Ye Tian to the Source Sea.
With a tearing sound!
Lord Wolf Fang tore open the space-time barrier, quickly forming a time-space passage that reached the Source Sea barrier.
With a thunderous boom!
The Source Sea barrier was shattered. Lord Wolf Fang’s power wrapped around Ye Tian, taking Ye Tian into the Source Sea.

Source Sea.
Ye Tian appeared in an unfamiliar region, with Lord Wolf Fang right beside him.
At this moment, Lord Wolf Fang said, “Endless Ancestor, I have already brought you here, so it would be inconvenient for me to linger here.”
Afterward, Lord Wolf Fang left.
And Ye Tian soon returned to the Dark Forbidden Domain, back to his true body’s side, and took out that purple stone.
Ye Tian touched the purple stone and felt that the treasure, which even the Copy Talent longed for, was located at the core of the purple stone.
But the purple stone was extremely hard, so he slowly melted it little by little. After spending a long time, he finally caused the purple stone to crack open.
Crack!
The purple stone split open, and a tiny purple pearl appeared in the void.
At this moment, the Copy Talent’s desire became incomparably intense.
The Copy Talent wanted him to devour this purple pearl; this was an instinct.
“Heavenly Extreme Origin Pearl!”
This name appeared in Ye Tian’s mind.
He picked up the Heavenly Extreme Origin Pearl and swallowed it in one gulp, beginning to refine it.
Immediately, a surge of energy poured into the world of talent imprints transformed by the Copy Talent, prompting the Copy Talent to begin its transformation.
This transformation far surpassed any previous one, as if it were a transformation that crossed the rules. Even the Source Sea origin began to tremble, as though declaring the birth of a heaven-defying existence.
No one knew how much time passed before Ye Tian’s transformation truly finished.
