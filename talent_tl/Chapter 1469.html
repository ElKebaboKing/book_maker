Chapter 1468: The Ancestor-level Copied Body Becomes a Permanent Clone!
!
The two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors who were in the process of breaking through to the Ancestor level had fallen, and their incarnations outside the Dark Soul Domain naturally dissipated immediately.
Consequently, the Ancestors backing these two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors also found out instantly.
In an instant, the Ancestors of the two factions were enraged.
These two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors had an eighty to ninety percent chance of becoming Ancestors. For the sake of this opportunity in the Dark Soul Domain, they had been holding back and refraining from breaking through.
It could be said that these two factions had invested far too many resources and opportunities into these two peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors.
Now, everything had come to naught! Boom!!! The Destruction Ancestor and the other Ancestors of the Destruction Holy Race turned their gazes toward the War Sect, and the All-Things Ancestor of the All-Things Holy Sect could no longer suppress his killing intent.
The All-Things Holy Sect only had him as an Ancestor. If that peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor had been willing, the All-Things Holy Sect would have long since birthed a second Ancestor.
But now, the hope for the All-Things Holy Sect's rise was shattered, and he wished he could wipe out the Human Race.
"Wanfa Zhanzu, your Human Race's Qingye Ancestor killed our All-Things Holy Sect's Guanghong Holy Ancestor!!!"
The All-Things Ancestor roared in fury.
He didn't even need to investigate to know it was Qingye Ancestor's doing.
After all, Guanghong Holy Ancestor was a peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor with immense combat power, no weaker than a weaker half-step Ancestor. Furthermore, he possessed an Ancestor-level defensive weapon bestowed upon him; even a powerful half-step Ancestor would find it difficult to kill him in a short time.
Yet just now, Guanghong Holy Ancestor's incarnation didn't even have the chance to call for help before his main body was slain and his incarnation dissipated.
This indicated that Guanghong Holy Ancestor had been insta-killed, and the only person capable of doing that was the Qingye Ancestor, who had just successfully broken through to the Ancestor level.
"I don't know anything about that. Besides, it's just the death of a Supreme-level Domain Ancestor! In the battles of the Dark Soul Domain, isn't it normal for one or two Supreme-level Domain Ancestors to die? What, does your All-Things Holy Sect intend to go to war with our War Sect?"
Ye Tian said coldly.
"Ancestors are not permitted to take action against peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors; this is a regulation among Ancestors!"
The Destruction Ancestor also spoke with great fury.
After all, the Destruction Holy Race had also lost a quasi-ancestor. If he hadn't died, an Ancestor from the Destruction Holy Race would have appeared within the Dark Soul Domain. This would have been great news for the Destruction Holy Race, enough to seize control of vast territories within the Dark Soul Domain and even obtain massive amounts of resources.
But now, that peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor had fallen.
How could he not be angry? If Ye Tian's true body were here, he likely would have crushed him with a single thought.
Unfortunately, the Ye Tian here was merely an incarnation.
Boom!!!!! Jiezu blocked the enraged All-Things Ancestor and the Ancestors of the Dark Destruction Domain, explaining: "Qingye Ancestor was initially only a half-step Domain Ancestor and rapidly ascended to the Ancestor realm within the Dark Soul Domain. He is unaware of the Ancestor agreements. I have already had Wanfa Zhanzu notify Qingye Ancestor; Qingye will no longer kill Supreme-level Domain Ancestors or Supreme-level Soul Ancestors!"
"Is our All-Things Holy Sect's quasi-ancestor just supposed to have died for nothing?"
The All-Things Ancestor said angrily.
"Quasi-ancestor? That is merely your claim. Who knows if he was just an ordinary peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestor? Besides, life and death are determined by fate. Since he entered the Dark Soul Domain, if he's dead, he's dead."
Jiezu said coldly.
The All-Things Ancestor snorted coldly and said no more.
In terms of strength, he was far from being Jiezu's match, and there was no benefit in making an enemy of Jiezu.
Thus, he could only let the matter drop.
Although the Destruction Ancestor was furious, it was impossible for him to kill Zhanzu or Jiezu over this. Moreover, while Jiezu was not his match, he had many means of escape, and there was no way to kill him.
Furthermore, the Holy Ancestor of the Dark Holy Domain would definitely not allow the Dark Holy Domain to lose a powerful combatant like Jiezu and would certainly intervene to stop him.
"Once the Dark Soul Domain allows Ancestors to enter, I will surely kill that Qingye Ancestor!"
The Destruction Ancestor thought to himself.
Although Qingye Ancestor held authority over a portion of the Dark Soul Domain, he was not a true native resident after all and could not completely control the Dark Soul Domain.
If Ancestors were divided into three tiers: Ordinary Ancestors, Elite Ancestors, and Top-tier Ancestors.
Then, when the Dark Holy Domain was first born, the Holy Ancestor, relying on the power of the Dark Holy Domain, could exert the combat power of a peak Elite Ancestor. At that time, he was also only an Elite Ancestor, so he could do nothing to the Holy Ancestor.
But now, he was a Top-tier Ancestor, and the Holy Ancestor was also a Top-tier Ancestor.
Qingye Ancestor, with the help of the Dark Soul Domain, could at most exert the combat power of a weaker Elite Ancestor. He was confident he could kill such a Qingye Ancestor.
He believed the Holy Ancestor would not stop him. After all, if Qingye Ancestor were allowed to live, once he used the Dark Soul Domain to grow into an Elite Ancestor, he could then exert Top-tier Ancestor combat power with its help.
In that case, the Dark Soul Domain would fall into the hands of the Human Race, which was equivalent to falling into the hands of the War Sect.
The Holy Ancestor would protect the powerful Ancestors of the Dark Holy Domain, but he also suppressed them to a certain extent.
Therefore, if he killed Qingye Ancestor, it was impossible for the Holy Ancestor to intervene.
If the Holy Ancestor didn't intervene, the other Ancestors would be even less able to stop him.
....Ye Tian did not know that the Destruction Ancestor wanted to kill Qingye Ancestor, and even if he did, he wouldn't be worried. After all, Qingye Ancestor was merely his copied body.
Moreover, there was still a very long time before the hundred-domain limit. Qingye Ancestor could continue to increase his strength, and by then, there was much hope of growing to a level where he could contend with the Destruction Ancestor.
But at this moment, Ye Tian was completely immersed in the new changes of his Copying Talent.
No, it should be said he was immersed in the changes of his copying ability.
Ye Tian's copying ability could copy creatures with cultivation equal to or lower than his own, but he could only copy one.
But now it was different. When the copied body broke through to the Ancestor realm, this copied body was no longer just a copied body; it had become solidified, turning into a special clone that no longer occupied the slot for a copied body.
In other words, the body of Qingye Ancestor existed independently as a special clone that could exist permanently and cultivate on its own, while Ye Tian could continue to use his copied body ability.
However, he could still only copy peak Supreme-level Domain Ancestors and could not copy Ancestors. After all, the ability of the copied body was determined by his main body, not by his clones.
"Not bad, it's equivalent to having an extra Ancestor clone! Hahaha, this way, I can continue to use the copied body ability to copy bodies, and then continuously use the Evolution ability to deduce and create Ancestor Laws suitable for myself! I can even help the Ancestor clone deduce more powerful Ancestral Techniques."
Ye Tian thought to himself.
