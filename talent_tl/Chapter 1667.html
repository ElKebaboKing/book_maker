Chapter 1666: Legendary Ancestor Gift!

“Void Beasts, do you want to start a war?”
The Six Yang Lord stared coldly at the five-tier Void Beast and questioned it.
The five-tier Void Beast spoke, its voice echoing throughout this void region.
“So what if we do? You Eternal Realm beings have invaded the Abyssless Land. The Abyssless Land is one of our Void Beasts’ lairs. Invading the Abyssless Land is equivalent to declaring war on us Void Beasts.”
The five-tier Void Beast was full of momentum, as if it did not fear the Eternal Realm beings of the Time-Space Territory in the least, and as if it had also forgotten how it had once been driven into the depths of the void region.
“Looks like your Void Beasts are very confident in your strength this time, but just one five-tier Void Beast like you is still not enough to contend with our Lord Alliance,” the Six Yang Lord sneered.
“Then add a few more!”
Boom!!!!
One after another, five-tier Void Beasts appeared, and all around them, large numbers of Void Beasts surged forth as well.
“So many!”
Even the Six Yang Lord’s expression turned grave.
Back then, the Lord Alliance had driven the Void Beasts out of this region, allowing countless time-space territories to connect with one another and communicate freely.
Otherwise, with Void Beasts roaming the void region outside the time-space territories, even time-space passages would not be able to open, and they could be destroyed by Void Beasts at any moment.
And in that battle, there had not been too many Void Beasts, only one five-tier Void Beast.
Yet now, how could so many five-tier Void Beasts have appeared?
“Could it be that these are Void Beasts from the depths of the void region, and they have formed an alliance?”
the Six Yang Lord guessed.
If that was truly the case, then it would not be strange.
After all, no one knew exactly how many Void Beasts existed in the depths of the void region.
If the Void Beasts were divided into factions, then the Void Beasts outside the time-space territories before had belonged to one faction, while now it was multiple Void Beast factions joining forces to invade.
They had probably wanted to invade for a long time, but had never fixed on the timing. This Abyssless Land incident had directly prompted them to act.
“Since you want war, then come on!”
the Six Yang Lord said fearlessly.
This time, the Void Beasts had mobilized a total of seven five-tier Void Beasts, attempting to break through the Lord Alliance’s many time-space territories in one fell swoop.
Immediately, they made their move.
The Six Yang Lord fought while retreating, and the other Immortality Realm lords also withdrew rapidly.
Very soon, the Six Yang Lord was injured.
After all, facing seven by himself, he was naturally no match for them.
Just then, one of the six chairmen of the Lord Alliance, another legendary lord, the Aurora Lord, appeared.
Buzz!
A golden ray of light tore through the space-time of the void region and directly struck a five-tier Void Beast. Even though a five-tier Void Beast could make its body completely void and exist nowhere between heaven and earth.
But the Aurora Lord’s attack could distort the void to that extent, and thus it struck the five-tier Void Beast, sending it flying directly.
Even so, it still could not severely injure the five-tier Void Beast.
Back then, when the six legendary lords had joined forces to surround and attack one five-tier Void Beast, they still could not kill it, only heavily injure it, and even then it had escaped.
Time passed slowly. The battlefield line was drawn back to a stretch of void region closer to the time-space territories. Large numbers of Eternal Realm beings participated in the war under the Lord Alliance’s orders, and some Eight Extremes Realm beings were also forced to join in.
As for the weaker existences, there was no need for them to participate. Otherwise, once they entered the void region, they would not even be able to stay for long, and would not even be qualified to serve as cannon fodder.
As for this war, Ye Tian was the happiest, because he could replicate many Void Beasts’ Immortal Ancestor Gifts.
Thus, he frequently participated in the war. With his ability to preserve his life, his clone body had perished only once; all the other times, he returned safely and brought back a large number of Immortal Ancestor Gifts.
Ye Tian did not know when this war would end, so he had to replicate enough Immortal Ancestor Gifts before it would be easier to fuse them into a talent beyond Immortal Ancestor Gifts.
This day.
One of Ye Tian’s clone bodies began to fuse those replicated Immortal Ancestor Gifts. The first one to be fused was the voidification ancestor gift. This Immortal Ancestor Gift was extremely terrifying, absolutely Ye Tian’s strongest life-preserving talent at the moment.
Therefore, he naturally attached great importance to it.
As one voidification gift after another fused together, the power of this Immortal Ancestor Gift also became stronger and stronger.
But fusing Immortal Ancestor Gifts still took quite a long time. Even though he accelerated time, he still could not fuse these Immortal Ancestor Gifts to a higher level in a short period.
After a long time, huge changes had already taken place in the war, and the voidification gift that Ye Tian had fused finally underwent a qualitative change.
Boom!!!!
The voidification gift transformed, becoming a talent that surpassed Immortal Ancestor Gifts.
“Legendary Ancestor Gift!”
This was a talent beyond Immortal Ancestor Gifts, far stronger than Immortal Ancestor Gifts.
Ye Tian discovered that just by using the Legendary Ancestor Gift-level voidification gift, even the Immortal Lord realm lords found it very difficult to injure him.
If he created the corresponding Eternal Technique and developed the power of the voidification gift to the Extreme, then such an Eternal Technique would probably be enough to make him unafraid of the attacks of Immortal Realm lords even when facing Immortal Realm lords.
“Legendary Ancestor Gifts are too powerful!”
Ye Tian exclaimed.
Immediately after, he continued fusing other Immortal Ancestor Gifts.
For example, the strength Immortal Ancestor Gift, which every Void Beast possessed as well, and he had therefore replicated many of them too.
In the days that followed, Ye Tian fused out the Legendary Strength Ancestor Gift, the Legendary Defense Ancestor Gift, and the Legendary Speed Ancestor Gift in succession. The emergence of these Legendary Ancestor Gifts raised Ye Tian’s strength by quite a bit.
More importantly, his potential had risen dramatically.
Moreover, Legendary Ancestor Gifts gave him hope of creating a Legendary Eternal Technique.
Eternal Techniques were divided into ordinary Eternal Techniques, top-tier Eternal Techniques, and Legendary Eternal Techniques. It was said that only legendary lords had any hope of creating Legendary Eternal Techniques. If Ye Tian were to create a Legendary Eternal Technique, his strength would definitely increase greatly, and might even directly reach the second boundary barrier.
Of course, he would need a very long time to create it; it could not be created all at once.
This day.
A new change occurred in the war between the time-space territories and the Void Beasts. The upper echelon of the Lord Alliance held a meeting, preparing to heavily injure the Void Beasts in one stroke.
And the Eternal Realm beings participating in this meeting were at least Immortal Realm, with the highest being the six legendary lords.
Of course.
They did not attend in their true bodies, but participated in the meeting as mind projections, so it would not affect the battle.
Six Yang Lord.
Aurora Lord Army!
Holy Peak Lord!
Great Desolation Lord!
Dark Mist Lord!
Ice Veil Lord!
The six legendary lords and nearly a hundred Immortal Realm lords gathered here.
The meeting began!.
