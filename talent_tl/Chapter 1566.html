Chapter 1565: Void Source Body!

Ye Tian had also heard of Source Sea life forms like the Devouring Source Dragon. This was a very rare type of Source Sea life form that reached the Supreme Being realm upon adulthood and could even swallow foreign worlds, but very few people had ever seen one.
Moreover, if he truly encountered a Devouring Source Dragon, he wouldn't dare to hunt it; he wouldn't even be able to flee.
As for Void Source Crystals, they were also incredibly rare treasures that only formed in certain special treasure lands within the Source Sea. He did not know where such treasures could be found.
"Condensing the Void Source Body!"
Ye Tian stared at this mission, his heart stirred.
Although he wasn't clear on what the Void Source Body was, the reward was extremely generous—a full 30,000 points.
In other words, the Void Source Body was extremely important.
"I'll head back first and ask Tianfeng Source God. Perhaps he knows what the Void Source Body is!"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Of course, before leaving, he applied to comprehend the Void Source Tablet, but he was ranked behind enough people that it would take 22 Great Tribulation Eras. This showed just how many Void Sons wanted to comprehend the Void Source Tablet.
After becoming a Void Son, Ye Tian had already obtained the Void Son badge. Through this badge, he could enter the Void Source Hall and leave it easily.
He could even use the badge to condense a temporary token for others, allowing them to participate in the Void Son assessment.
Tianfeng Source God had given him such a token to enter the Void Source Hall for the assessment.
Clearly, Tianfeng Source God was also a Void Son.
Swish!
Ye Tian left the Void Source Hall and returned to the Tianfeng Sea.
The moment Tianfeng Source God saw Ye Tian, he knew that Ye Tian had succeeded.
There was a resonance between Void Sons. He could sense the aura of the Void Son badge on Ye Tian, so Ye Tian must have become a Void Son.
"Ye Tian, congratulations!"
Tianfeng Source God offered his congratulations.
"Thank you, Leader!" Ye Tian said gratefully.
"You have just become a Void Son, so there are likely many things you don't understand. You can ask me whatever you wish to know. In a while, I will be entering seclusion, and if you want to ask me then, I won't be able to answer you!" Tianfeng Source God said with a smile.
Ye Tian quickly asked, "Leader, I want to know what the Void Source Body is. The Void Source Hall actually gives a reward of 30,000 points for it!"
"The Void Source Body!" Tianfeng Source God said with a hint of regret, "You can consider it an incomparably powerful Tribulation Body. Unfortunately, it must be condensed into a Void Source Body while at the Third-tier Invincible level. Otherwise, once you become a Second-tier Invincible and the physical Tribulation Body begins to merge with the soul Tribulation Body, it will be too late. Because of this, many Void Sons remain at the Third-tier Invincible realm to this day, wanting to take that gamble. For example, the Void Son Luo Ge you fought before also wants to condense the Void Source Body, which is why he hasn't pushed for the Second-tier Invincible realm."
"As for how to cultivate the Void Source Body? There are no cultivation techniques. One can only comprehend the Void Source Tablet. The Void Source Tablet allows one to comprehend various Eternal Rules and can also allow one to perfect their Invincible Tribulation Body. The most powerful Invincible Tribulation Body in the Tianlin World is only a Top-tier Invincible Tribulation Body. For instance, the Wan Yuan Gui Xu Tribulation Body you cultivate was actually an Invincible Tribulation Body that a Void Son comprehended himself through the Void Source Tablet. Unfortunately, it fell far short of the standard for a Void Source Body and was considered a failed creation."
"According to our speculations, to create a Void Source Body, one must comprehend enough Eternal Rules, and each Eternal Rule must reach a very profound level. This requires accumulating enough points at the Third-tier Invincible realm to comprehend the Void Source Tablet, and points are now incredibly difficult to obtain. In our Tianlin World, only Lord Qiu Ye Source Ancestor has condensed a Void Source Body to date. This was only achieved with the help of two other Supreme Beings to accumulate a massive amount of points. It is said they hunted a large number of Devouring Source Dragons, but such opportunities no longer exist today."
After Tianfeng Source God's explanation, Ye Tian finally understood the power of the Void Source Body; it far surpassed ordinary Tribulation Bodies.
Perhaps only after condensing the Void Source Body would he have hope of passing the second floor of the Void Tower and becoming a Void Divine Son.
Afterward, he left the Tianfeng Sea.
For now, he still had to wait 22 Great Tribulation Eras before he could comprehend the Void Source Tablet.
Compared to other Void Sons, he had a massive advantage: he possessed Eternal Talents, and far more than just one.
Through his Eternal Talents, his speed in comprehending Eternal Rules was countless times faster than others. Therefore, he might only need to spend a small amount of points to condense the Void Source Body.
Of course, the more Eternal Talents he obtained, the greater his hope of condensing the Void Source Body.
"Currently, I don't have a Time-type Eternal Ancestral Talent. It seems I'll have to spend a lot of time copying Time Ultimate Ancestral Talents and fusing a large number of them into a Time Eternal Ancestral Talent!" Ye Tian murmured.
Thus, he used the intelligence of the Tianfeng Sea to inquire about the existence of Time Ultimate Ancestral Talents. As it turned out, there were indeed some life forms that possessed them.
Some were Source Sea life forms, while others came from races in other Eternal Worlds.
Ye Tian could travel through the Void Source Hall, which saved him an immense amount of time.
As time flowed, Ye Tian ventured to various parts of the Source Sea again and again. He encountered some dangers and even risked his life by entering many desperate lands. His copied bodies died 397 times, but compared to his gains, that was not worth mentioning.
Unknowingly, one Great Tribulation Era passed.
Finally, on this day, Ye Tian copied enough Time Ultimate Ancestral Talents and fused them together, finally completing the transformation.
[Eternal Ancestral Talent: Time]
"Success!"
Ye Tian was extremely excited. He had previously obtained the Space Eternal Ancestral Talent, and now he had the Time Eternal Ancestral Talent. Combined with his other Eternal Ancestral Talents, he now possessed many.
However, Ye Tian did not stop at these Eternal Ancestral Talents.
He continued to collect other rare Ultimate Ancestral Talents, striving to obtain even more Eternal Ancestral Talents.
Just like that, era after era passed.
Ye Tian's strength didn't increase by much, as he had already reached the Extreme of the Third-tier Invincible level, but his combat experience improved significantly.
He frequently went to the Void Tower to fight, using the second floor of the Void Tower to hone his combat awareness and combat rhythm.
Consequently, his current combat experience was gradually approaching that of Void Sons like Luo Ge.
On this day.
Ye Tian headed to the Void Source Hall, and this time, his main body entered.
He had applied to comprehend the Void Source Tablet, and now it was his turn.
