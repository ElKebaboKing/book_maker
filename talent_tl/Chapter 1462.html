Chapter 1461: Soul Ancestor, Rise of the Human Race
!
"The Soul Dao specifically cultivates the soul but neglects the physical body; this path is also incomplete.
However, in the future, I can combine the Soul Dao with the Physical Body Dao to create a more perfect path,"
Ye Tian thought to himself.
Therefore, he prepared to use this cloned body to walk the path of the Soul Dao.
Through his understanding, the Soul Dao in this Dark Soul Domain had currently developed to the Soul Ancestor level, which was equivalent to the Domain Ancestor realm.
The cultivation of a Soul Ancestor also involved soul transformations time and again. Furthermore, the soul strength of a newly promoted Soul Ancestor was already comparable to that of a peak Domain Ancestor.
Otherwise, there would be no way to match the combat power of a Domain Ancestor using soul power alone.
In the following days, a portion of the human race began to follow the Soul Dao, while another portion continued with their previous cultivation paths.
However, they discovered that it was very difficult to increase their cultivation here using their old paths; even attempting to break through to the Domain Ancestor realm felt like an insurmountable chasm.
Consequently, the human Half-step Domain Ancestors abandoned the path of the Domain Ancestor and began to follow the path of the Soul Ancestor.
But soon, Ye Tian discovered a problem: their souls were too weak to become Soul Ancestors.
Now, not only were the human Half-step Domain Ancestors stunned, but even the Supreme Half-step Domain Ancestors from other factions were bewildered.
If they couldn't become Soul Ancestors, how would they obtain more opportunities in the future, or fight against the native soul practitioners of the Dark Soul Domain? Currently, the Dark Soul Domain also had many Half-step Soul Ancestors; though their individual strength wasn't great, their numbers were vast.
Once these Half-step Soul Ancestors promoted to the Soul Ancestor realm, if the outsiders couldn't become Soul Ancestors themselves, they wouldn't be able to defeat them, let alone occupy this place in the future.
"I must create a cultivation technique to re-cultivate the Soul Dao and transform the bodies of human Half-step Domain Ancestors into Half-step Soul Ancestors!"
Ye Tian decided.
This step was extremely difficult, one could even say it was like an insurmountable chasm.
But at this time, Ye Tian noticed that Half-step Domain Ancestors from many factions were emerging from the Dark Soul Domain, bringing out a large number of Soul Dao techniques.
Thus, Ye Tian also had his cloned body exit the Dark Soul Domain.
Afterward, Ye Tian dispelled the clone, began to duplicate his original body, and used his Evolution characteristic to deduce techniques over and over again.
By consuming Sacred Souls to deduce techniques, his efficiency was many times faster than even an Ancestor.
Time and again, his cloned bodies collapsed, only for him to clone again and continue the evolution.
In this way, time passed, and a million years went by in the blink of an eye.
During these million years, the Ancestors were all deducing techniques to achieve the transformation from Half-step Domain Ancestor to Half-step Soul Ancestor.
While the other Ancestors had yet to complete their technique transformations, Ye Tian, relying on countless evolutions, finally created a technique—the Body-Merging Soul Technique!
The so-called Body-Merging Soul Technique was... to convert the power of a Half-step Domain Ancestor into soul power, thereby achieving a qualitative change in the soul, stepping into the Half-step Soul Ancestor realm, and embarking on the Soul Dao in one fell swoop.
Ye Tian had only created the Body-Merging technique at the Half-step Domain Ancestor level and had not created one for the Domain Ancestor level; therefore, it was only suitable for Half-step Domain Ancestors.
After creating the Body-Merging Soul Technique, Ye Tian once again cloned that... Half-step Domain Ancestor body and sent it back into the Dark Soul Domain.
Upon returning to the Dark Soul Domain, Ye Tian, in his capacity as the leader of the ten geniuses, gathered the 100,000 geniuses.
During these million years, the 100,000 human geniuses had made little progress in their cultivation, and some had even fallen. They had obtained a large amount of resources and treasures, but unfortunately, since their cultivation couldn't improve, they couldn't utilize them.
"The Myriad Law Combat Ancestor has created a technique that can allow us to become Half-step Soul Ancestors. However, everyone must swear an oath not to leak this technique. Since other factions do not have such a technique yet, once it is leaked, our human race's advantage will be gone,"
Ye Tian commanded.
"Yes!"
One by one, the geniuses swore their oaths—the most serious and harsh Life Oaths—before receiving the inheritance of the Body-Merging Soul Technique.
With the Body-Merging Soul Technique, the human geniuses transformed into Half-step Soul Ancestors one by one. Then, utilizing the resources and soul treasures they had acquired, they broke through to the Soul Ancestor realm.
After 100,000 years, all the human geniuses had stepped into the Soul Ancestor realm. At this point, they could no longer leave; otherwise, once they exited the Dark Soul Domain, they wouldn't be able to return.
After becoming Soul Ancestors, the human geniuses began to expand their territory, even establishing a faction called the Sacred Hall, recruiting native residents of the Dark Soul Domain on a large scale to continuously expand their influence.
With territory and more resources, the human Soul Ancestors' strength increased very rapidly.
In just a few million years, the human Soul Ancestors had all reached the level of top-tier Soul Ancestor combat power, while Ye Tian's cultivation improved the fastest, already reaching the Peak Soul Ancestor realm.
The path of the Soul Ancestor and the path of the Domain Ancestor seemed different, but they still shared similarities. Furthermore, since Ye Tian was at the peak of the Supreme Domain Ancestor level, reverse-engineering the Soul Ancestor path was naturally not difficult—at least not before reaching the Supreme Soul Ancestor realm.
Additionally, Ye Tian created several Sacred Skills suitable for Soul Ancestors, as well as a pile of soul techniques at the Domain Ancestor level, sufficient for the human Soul Ancestors to practice.
Dark Soul Domain, Sacred Hall Headquarters.
As the Master of the Sacred Hall, Ye Tian gathered the human Soul Ancestors.
"Everyone, it is time for us to occupy a more vast territory. This place is a remote area of the Dark Soul Domain, but now, with our human race's strength, occupying the core region would pose no problem.
I plan to seize a Holy Realm within the Dark Soul Domain!"
Ye Tian planned.
"A Holy Realm!"
Everyone was excited upon hearing this plan.
Even the Daoshan Domain only had one Holy Realm, the Xuanxu Holy Realm. Now, they were about to conquer a Holy Realm in the Dark Soul Domain. If they succeeded, the human race would effectively possess two Holy Realms.
Subsequently, they began planning which Holy Realm to attack.
Currently, the Dark Soul Domain possessed dozens of Holy Realms, each occupied by numerous factions; no single faction yet held ownership over an entire Holy Realm.
This was because completely occupying a Holy Realm was extremely difficult.
"Let's conquer the Shifang Holy Realm!"
Ye Tian said, pointing to a Holy Realm on the map.
The Shifang Holy Realm contained many factions. According to the human race's investigation, most of these factions belonged to certain races from the Dark Destruction Domain.
The Sacred Hall left behind only a small portion of the human race, but there were still many native Soul Ancestors stationed there. These Soul Ancestors had joined the Sacred Hall when they were very weak and had no idea that the Sacred Hall consisted of outsiders; even if they knew, they wouldn't have any particular thoughts about it.
After all, they were all nurtured by the Sacred Hall, so they naturally wouldn't betray it.
Moreover, the human race had not done anything excessive. Only factions like those from the Dark Destruction Domain aimed to destroy everything, which was why they were hated and had faced the full resistance of the Dark Holy Domain in the past.
Ye Tian leading tens of thousands of top-tier Soul Ancestors, crossed a vast distance. It took a full several months before they reached the core region of the Dark Soul Domain and arrived before the Shifang Holy Realm.
