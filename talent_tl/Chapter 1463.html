Chapter 1462: Occupying the Ten Directions Sacred Realm
!
Before the Ten Directions Sacred Realm.
Ye Tian led the human army as they descended, directly entering the Ten Directions Sacred Realm.
"From this day forth, the Ten Directions Sacred Realm belongs to our Human Race!"
Ye Tian's voice permeated the entire Ten Directions Sacred Realm.
Such an arrogant declaration immediately incensed many factions within the Ten Directions Sacred Realm.
....Ten Directions Sacred Realm, Destruction Dragon Mountain.
This was the territory conquered by the geniuses of the Dragon Evil Clan, one of the three thousand evil clans of the Dark Sacred Domain.
The Dragon Evil Clan held a very respectable position among the three thousand evil clans, possessing three Supreme-level Realm Ancestors and several Invincible Realm Ancestors.
At this moment, the gaze of the Dragon Evil Clan's genius leader, Gu Luolu, pierced through layers of void, looking toward the direction of the Human Race.
By now, the various major powers of the entire Dark Sacred Domain had long since obtained techniques to transform into Half-Step Soul Ancestors. After the Dragon Evil Clan members transformed into Half-Step Soul Ancestors, they had one by one ascended to become Soul Ancestors.
Moreover, the Ten Directions Sacred Realm was rich in resources. Coupled with the Dragon Evil Clan's heavy support and full efforts to nurture Gu Luolu, his cultivation had been raised to the level of a Paramount Soul Ancestor. It could be said that the strength of an entire clan was concentrated on nurturing one person.
Within the Ten Directions Sacred Realm, Gu Luolu's strength was sufficient to rank in the top ten.
"The Human Race? Is that the race the Myriad Law War Ancestor belongs to?"
Gu Luolu said coldly, "The Myriad Law War Ancestor's strength is indeed powerful, comparable to a Half-Step Primordial Ancestor. However, the Human Race has only produced one Myriad Law War Ancestor; everyone else is an ant. For a group of humans to dare claim they will occupy the Ten Directions Sacred Realm is truly arrogant!"
Currently.
His Dragon Evil Clan also occupied a region in the Ten Directions Sacred Realm and had nurtured a large number of subordinates, possessing immense strength.
"Hear my command: exterminate the Human Race!"
Gu Luolu ordered.
Boom!!!!!! One Soul Ancestor after another set out, and a large number of Half-Step Soul Ancestors were also mobilized, charging toward the Human Race.
This time, the Human Race had angered the factions of the entire Ten Directions Sacred Realm. Numerous powers attacked one after another, swearing to slaughter the humans.
"They're here!"
Ye Tian sensed the arrival of a large number of Soul Ancestors, as terrifying tides of soul energy surged forward.
Compared to so many Soul Ancestors, the Human Race was indeed lacking in numbers.
However, the Human Race's Soul Ancestors were all top-tier Soul Ancestors. Combined with Ye Tian, a nearly invincible Paramount Soul Ancestor, ordinary Paramount Soul Ancestors were like ants in his eyes.
The first to arrive was the Dragon Evil Clan.
"Kill them!"
Gu Luolu commanded.
As one of the evil clans, they knew nothing of hesitation; they killed upon encounter without any nonsense.
"Kill!"
With Ye Tian's command, the top-tier Soul Ancestors of the Human Race struck out together.
As soon as they clashed, Gu Luolu was stunned. "They are all top-tier Soul Ancestors? How can this be? Why has the Human Race progressed so quickly?"
Gu Luolu said in disbelief.
He watched as the two sides collided, and the Soul Ancestors of the Dragon Evil Clan were blasted apart one after another.
Gu Luolu did not dare to engage so many top-tier human Soul Ancestors. A Paramount Soul Ancestor like him was not truly powerful; after all, his cultivation time was short. He had only reached the level of a Paramount Soul Ancestor in cultivation but had not even mastered a few soul techniques at the Soul Ancestor level. His combat power was only comparable to an ordinary Paramount Realm Ancestor, and he had no way to resist tens of thousands of top-tier Soul Ancestors by himself.
Suddenly.
Ye Tian made his move. A Soul Spear instantly pierced through the air.
With a soft "puchi" sound, Gu Luolu was killed in a single strike, without even the slightest power to resist.
Soon, the Dragon Evil Clan was wiped out.
Subsequently, one faction after another encountered the Human Race.
Ye Tian led the Human Race to annihilate faction after faction, virtually sweeping through the Ten Directions Sacred Realm.
Finally, dozens of Paramount Realm Ancestors in the Ten Directions Sacred Realm united, preparing to assassinate Ye Tian together.
The result, however, was that Ye Tian instantly killed these dozens of Paramount Soul Ancestors.
Several decades later, the Human Race unified the Ten Directions Sacred Realm, occupying it completely.
Outside the Dark Soul Domain.
The Supreme-level Realm Ancestors of the Dragon Evil Clan already knew that their clan's geniuses had been wiped out by the Human Race, and they were instantly furious.
But they did not dare to vent their anger. After all, their strength was very weak, far inferior to Ye Tian's; they had no right to be angry with him.
However, the fact that the Human Race had occupied the Ten Directions Sacred Realm caught the attention of the Primordial Ancestors.
After all, the Dark Soul Domain currently only had a few dozen Sacred Realms. If the War Sect had occupied a Sacred Realm, it would have been relatively normal.
But now, a single Human Race had occupied a Sacred Realm all by itself; this was far too shocking.
Especially when they learned that a Paramount Soul Ancestor had swept through everything and was unstoppable, they suspected the Human Race had cheated.
But no matter how they investigated, they could not find any problems.
"From now on, pay close attention to the Human Race!"
The Primordial Ancestors of the Dark Sacred Domain and the Eternal Cult issued this directive one after another.
Other Sacred Sects of the Dark Sacred Domain also began to take the Human Race seriously. After all, since the Human Race occupied a Sacred Realm, their future development speed would inevitably be extremely fast, making them a major threat.
After the Human Race occupied the Ten Directions Sacred Realm, Ye Tian concentrated a large amount of resources to continuously advance his Soul Ancestor cultivation. At the same time, his main body placed no restrictions on itself, continuing to create various soul techniques and even Sacred Techniques suitable for Soul Ancestors.
In the blink of an eye, one hundred million years had passed since the Human Race entered the Dark Soul Domain.
At this time, the Dark Soul Domain underwent its second transformation, and Realm Ancestors below the Paramount level were now permitted to enter.
Consequently.
A large number of top-tier Realm Ancestors entered, including many at the Holy Son level who could kill ordinary Paramount Realm Ancestors across levels. There were even Holy Talent Possessors among them.
These top-tier Realm Ancestors entering the Soul Domain naturally would not follow the path of a Soul Ancestor. Moreover, the Primordial Ancestors had no way to deduce techniques that would turn a Realm Ancestor into a Soul Ancestor.
These top-tier Realm Ancestors were fully confident in entering the Paramount Realm Ancestor stage. Even though following the path of a Realm Ancestor in the Dark Soul Domain was extremely difficult, they still had hope of forcibly breaking through the shackles and ascending to become Paramount Realm Ancestors.
Once they ascended to Paramount Realm Ancestors, their strength would be immense, even crushing the Paramount Soul Ancestors of the Dark Soul Domain.
In this way, the landscape of the Dark Soul Domain would change, and the territories of various powers would also shift.
For instance, the Dragon Evil Clan and other factions dispatched a large number of top-tier Realm Ancestors. The Dragon Evil Clan even sent a Holy Talent Possessor, the offspring of one of the three Supreme Realm Ancestors of the Dragon Evil Clan, the Hundred Ghost Evil Ancestor. Named the Scorpio Holy Son, his strength was immense. Once he entered the Dark Soul Domain, he could immediately charge toward the Paramount Realm Ancestor stage.
Once the Scorpio Holy Son stepped into the Paramount Realm Ancestor stage, there was no telling how many times stronger he would be than an ordinary Paramount Realm Ancestor.
Combined with the resources of the Dragon Evil Clan, his strength would rise once more.
At that time, they would launch an attack against the Human Race in the Ten Directions Sacred Realm.
