Chapter 1456: The Nirvana Race Has Been Wiped Out Just Like That!
!
Ye Tian did not engage any members of the Nirvana Race above five marks. Although his strength was comparable to a five-mark Nirvana member, he was unable to actually kill one.
Therefore, he directly targeted those below five marks. This way, he could not only slaughter a large number of them but also harvest some Nirvana treasures.
This time, his primary goal was to obtain a batch of Nirvana treasures.
At the same time, his Copy Talent continuously enveloped the surrounding area, copying the talents of one Nirvana member after another.
Only in this way could he maximize his gains.
Boom! A two-mark Nirvana member was strangled by a casual Golden Silk Ancestral Technique from Ye Tian, and its corpse was collected by him.
The Nirvana members here were different from those who attacked the defense line. Once the Nirvana Race attacked the defense line, they were not allowed to carry any resources except for their own weapons.
This was to prevent resources from being seized by the creatures of the Dark Forbidden Zone after the Nirvana members died in battle.
But now, this was the Nirvana Race's territory, and they carried their resources with them. Thus, killing a single Nirvana member allowed him to obtain a certain amount of Nirvana treasures and resources, which was why Ye Tian was so keen on killing them.
The other Supreme Domain Ancestors didn't seem to know the value of Nirvana treasures, so they didn't have the same idea.
Puff, puff, puff! One after another, one-mark and two-mark Nirvana members died under Ye Tian's Golden Silk Ancestral Technique.
Ye Tian also continuously utilized his Void Escape Ancestral Technique. His speed was incredibly fast as he crossed one Nirvana territory after another, constantly copying a vast number of Nirvana talents while searching for weaker members to kill, occasionally saving a few Supreme Domain Ancestors along the way.
However, even so, some Supreme Domain Ancestors still fell, and the number was not small.
Yet, compared to the Nirvana Race, the number of fallen Supreme Domain Ancestors was almost negligible.
Boom! A seven-mark Nirvana member was blasted to death, triggering a chain reaction.
Following that, one seven-mark Nirvana member after another was slain.
It had to be said that the life-saving capabilities of the seven-mark Nirvana members were too poor, whereas Ancestors were not easily killed.
Strictly speaking, the Ancestors were perfect lifeforms, while the Nirvana Race possessed significant flaws.
Otherwise, not a single Ancestor would have fallen to this day, while several seven-mark Nirvana members had already perished.
The same applied to the Supreme Domain Ancestors. Although many were eroded by Nirvana power and suffered heavy injuries, they simply wouldn't die.
In contrast, once a Nirvana member suffered the same level of injury, they were doomed.
As time passed, Ye Tian copied an immense number of Nirvana talents.
After all, he could easily traverse various territories. Essentially, by staying in a territory for a single second, he could copy the talents of every Nirvana member in that area.
The other Supreme Domain Ancestors didn't know Ye Tian was copying talents; they thought he was rushing around to provide support, so they didn't pay much attention.
Before long, Ye Tian had visited every territory and had copied the talents of almost every Nirvana member.
Only then did he begin to attack with full force, even breaking into the buildings within the Nirvana territories to plunder resources.
Although many Supreme Domain Ancestors didn't know the exact value of the Nirvana treasures and resources, they began to collect them upon seeing them. After all, the Ancestors offered options to exchange for Nirvana treasures, so they were definitely valuable. There was no reason to leave them behind.
Ten days! One month! One year! Ten years! This battle lasted for a full hundred years. A massive number of Nirvana members fell, and finally, the Ancestors were able to free themselves up.
This was because the Ancestors had killed almost all of the seven-mark and eight-mark Nirvana members.
Once these Ancestors joined the battlefield against the six-mark and lower Nirvana members, it became a total massacre.
In a short while, the Nirvana Race was completely wiped out.
Subsequently, the Ancestors began to collect the corpses of these Nirvana members and started plundering the resources within their territories.
Ye Tian naturally couldn't compete with the Ancestors, but he entered some smaller territories to begin gathering Nirvana resources.
Some Supreme Domain Ancestors seemed to realize something and joined the ranks of those plundering resources.
Suddenly.
From the depths of endless time and space came the voice of the nine-mark Nirvana member, Ji: "Saint Ancestor, Destruction Ancestor, the two of you hid your strength so deeply! I am unwilling!"
Accompanied by a loud bang.
The depths of endless time and space seemed to be shattered, and the nine-mark Nirvana member, Ji, was blasted to death.
Crack! The Saint Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor stepped out from the depths of time and space. They looked slightly disheveled, but their condition was excellent.
"Even a nine-mark Nirvana member was killed by the combined efforts of the Saint Ancestor and the Destruction Ancestor. They really were hiding their strength," Ye Tian thought to himself.
Why would the Destruction Ancestor and the Saint Ancestor hide their strength? Based on their power, they probably could have solved the Nirvana Race problem long ago. Why wait until today? "Were they harvesting leeks?"
Ye Tian thought of this possibility.
Perhaps the Ancestors also needed Nirvana treasures, which was why they kept the Nirvana Race around. Regardless, the Nirvana Race wasn't a threat to them. Even if they needed to guard this place, couldn't they cultivate anywhere? Therefore, they had never wiped out the Nirvana Race.
As for why they chose to destroy them now, something must have happened that he wasn't aware of.
"The Ancestors probably all know the reason, but the Supreme Domain Ancestors likely don't. Otherwise, those Supreme Domain Ancestors wouldn't be so terrified of the Nirvana Race! Even the Fire-Mark War Ancestor was afraid. Clearly, even veteran Supreme Domain Ancestors like him were kept in the dark," Ye Tian mused.
He could go ask the War Ancestor, but the War Ancestor likely wouldn't tell him; otherwise, such news would have leaked out long ago.
At this moment.
Many Supreme Domain Ancestors also felt that something was off, but since the Nirvana Race had already been wiped out, what could they say? To question the Ancestors would be seeking death. In short, they had survived, and that was enough.
As for the fallen Supreme Domain Ancestors, they were mostly those with insufficient strength. Not a single truly powerful Supreme Domain Ancestor had perished, though some were heavily injured.
"Everyone, leave!"
The Ancestors spoke.
Immediately.
One by one, the Supreme Domain Ancestors found themselves being teleported out of this space-time by the Ancestors.
When Ye Tian regained his senses, he found himself back in the War Ancestor Realm. The other Supreme Domain Ancestors of the War Ancestor Realm had also returned.
This time, not a single Supreme Domain Ancestor from the War Ancestor Realm had fallen. The Supreme Domain Ancestors from the Dark Holy Region who died mostly belonged to those Holy Lands.
One could imagine that the Seventy-Two Holy Lands would likely undergo a major reshuffle.
But Ye Tian no longer cared about such trivial matters.
At this moment, he immediately returned to the Profound Void Holy Realm. He wanted to deliver the Nirvana treasures he had obtained to his main body so that his main body could refine them and increase his strength.
Once those Nirvana treasures were refined, his strength would likely receive a terrifying boost.
